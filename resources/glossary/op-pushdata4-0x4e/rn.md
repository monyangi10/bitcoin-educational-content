---
term: OP_PUSHDATA4 (0X4E)

definition: Opcode ishiraho amakuru menshi cane ku mupfundikizo (ntikunze gukoreshwa).
---

Iremeza gusunika amakuru menshi cane ku kirundo. Ikurikirwa n’ibice bine (little-endian) vyerekana uburebure bw’amakuru yo gusunika (bishika nko kuri 4,3 GB). Iyi opcode ikoreshwa mu kwinjiza amakuru menshi cane mu nyandiko, naho nyene ikoreshwa ryayo ari gake cane kubera imipaka y’ibikorwa ku rugero rw’ibikorwa.
