---
term: AOPP
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikirundi "*Address Ownership Ivyemezo vy'Itegeko*". Ni umurongo utera impaka wagenewe kwemeza ubwo nyene Ownership y’amaderesi ya Bitcoin. Ubwo buryo buratuma abakoresha berekana ko bashobora kugenzura Address yihariye biciye kuri porogarama yabo ihuye na Wallet. Mu ntango, AOPP yashinzwe mu ntango kugira ngo yorohereze ukugenzura kwa Address Ownership, ikintu gitegekanijwe n’amategeko ku bakiriya bipfuza kwimurira amafaranga yabo ya bitcoins hanze y’ibibanza vya Exchange mu bihugu bimwebimwe, nk’Ubusuwisi.


Ariko rero, iyo porotokole yaranezwe cane mu muryango wa Bitcoin, kuko yoshobora gushinga intahe aho abayikoresha bategerezwa gusaba uruhusha rwo kwirinda amahera yabo bwite. Mu kwishura ivyo bivugwa, porogarama nyinshi za Wallet zahisemwo kudafata iyo porotokole.
