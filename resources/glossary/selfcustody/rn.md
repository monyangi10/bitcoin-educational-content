---
term: Self-custody
definition: Uburyo bwo kugumya kugenzura imfunguruzo zawe bwite utisunze uwundi muntu.
---

Yerekeza ku ngeso umukoresha akoresha mu kuguma afise ububasha butaziguye ku mfunguruzo ziwe z’ibanga, gutyo rero n’ama bitcoins yiwe, ata kwizigira ikigo co hanze co gucunga itunga ryiwe.
