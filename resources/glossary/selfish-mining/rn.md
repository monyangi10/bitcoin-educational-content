---
term: UBWIKUNDA Mining
---

Ingamba (canke igitero) muri Mining, aho Miner canke umugwi w’abacukuzi bafata n’ibigirankana amabuye afise Proof of Work ibereye ataco baciye bayarekura ku rubuga. Intumbero ni uguguma imbere y’abandi bacukuzi mu bijanye na Proof of Work, bikaba bishobora kubashoboza gukora Miner amabuye menshi akurikiranye maze bakayasohora yose icarimwe, gutyo bakaronka inyungu nyinshi. Mu yandi majambo, uwo mugwi w’abacukuzi batera ntucukura ku gice ca nyuma cemejwe n’urubuga rwose, ahubwo ucukura ku gice bo ubwabo baremye, kikaba gitandukanye n’ico cemejwe n’urubuga.


Ivyo bituma haba ubwoko bw’ishami ry’ibanga rya Blockchain, riguma ritamenyekana ku rubuga rwose gushika aho uru ruhererekane rushobora kurenga Blockchain y’ukuri. Igihe uruhererekane rw’ibanga rw’abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro batera rumaze kuba rurerure (ni ukuvuga ko rurimwo ibikorwa vyinshi vyirundanijwe) kuruta uruhererekane rw’ukuri, rwa bose, ruca rumenyeshwa ku rubuga rwose. Muri iki gihe, uturongo turi mu ruzitiro dukurikira uruzitiro rurerure kuruta izindi (n’igikorwa kirundanijwe cane) tuzokorana n’urwo ruzitiro rushasha. Rero hariho ugusubira gutunganya.


Ubwikunzi bwa Mining burababaza abakoresha, kuko bugabanya umutekano wa sisitemu mu gutakaza ubushobozi bumwe bumwe bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none. Iyo bishoboka, bituma kandi habaho ugusubira gutunganya Blockchain, bikagira ico bikoze ku kwizigirwa kw’ivyemezo vy’amafaranga ku bakoresha. Naho ari ukwo, uwo mugenzo uracari akaga ku mugwi w’abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro batera, kuko akenshi bifise akamaro kanini kuri Miner mu bisanzwe hejuru y’ibarabara rya nyuma rizwi na bose kuruta guha ububasha bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none ishami ry’ibanga kumbure ritazokwigera rirenga Blockchain y’ukuri. Uko umubare w’amabuye mu gusubira gutunganya urushiriza kuba mwinshi, ni ko n’amahirwe yo gutera neza aba make.
