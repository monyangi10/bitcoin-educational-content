---
term: Bitcoin pooled mining (BPM)
definition: Method of calculating rewards in pools valuing shares based on the time elapsed in the cycle.
---

Another name for the Score Based Method used to calculate miners' compensation within mining pools. It's a proportional reward method, but weighted by the time at which the share is submitted. BPM values shares based on the time elapsed since the beginning of the mining cycle. The later a share is submitted in the cycle, the higher its value. This method encourages miners to remain connected: when a miner stops mining, their score remains stagnant while those of others continue to grow more rapidly.

"Bitcoin Pooled Mining" was also the name of Slush’s mining pool in 2011, which originated this reward calculation method.
