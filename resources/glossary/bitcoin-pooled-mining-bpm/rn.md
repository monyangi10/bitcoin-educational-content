---
term: Bitcoin ISHIRANYE Mining (BPM)
---

Iyindi zina ry’uburyo bushingiye ku manota bukoreshwa mu kubara indishi y’abacukuzi mu bidengeri vya Mining. Ni uburyo bwo gutanga impembo zihuye, ariko zigereranywa n’igihe umugabane ushikirizwa. BPM itanga agaciro k’imigabane ishingiye ku gihe cacitse kuva mu ntango z’ingendo ya Mining. Uko umugabane ushikirizwa mu nyuma mu nzinguzingu, ni ko agaciro kawo karushiriza kuba kanini. Ubwo buryo buraremesha abacukuzi kuguma bafitaniye isano: iyo Miner ihagaritse Mining, amanota yabo aguma ahagaze mu gihe ay’abandi abandanya gukura cane.


"Bitcoin Pooled Mining" ni ryo zina rya Slush’s Mining pool mu 2011, ryatanguje ubu buryo bwo kubara impembo.
