---
term: AMAHERA YO GUTERA
---

Igitero aho abacukuzi barondera gusubira kwandika igice giherutse kwemezwa kugira ngo basabe amafaranga y’ugucuruza kirimwo, mu gihe bongerako amafaranga menshi y’ugucuruza yashitse muri ico gihe muri Mempool. Intumbero nyamukuru y’iki gitero ku ba Miner ni iyo kwongerera inyungu zabo. Guca amafaranga y’amabuye y’agaciro birashobora gutuma habaho inyungu nyinshi uko Block reward igabanuka kandi amafaranga y’ugucuruza agereranya igice kinini c’amahera abacukuzi baronka mu mabuye y’agaciro. Bishobora kandi kuba vyiza iyo amafaranga ari mu gice c’imbere ari menshi cane kuruta ayo mu gice gikurikira c’abahiganwa beza. Mu kworosha, Miner ihanganye n’iri hitamwo mu bijanye n’inzego zitera intege:


- Ivyanje mu buryo busanzwe bikurikira igice ca nyuma, n’amahirwe menshi yo gutsinda impembo ntoyi;
- Kugerageza gucukura igice c’imbere (fee sniping), n’amahirwe make yo gutsinda impembo nini.


Ico gitero kiratera ingorane ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin, kuko uko abacukuzi barushiriza kubufata, ni ko n’abandi bacukuzi, mu ntango bari abanyakuri, bavyurirwa umutima wo kubigira. Nkako, igihe cose Miner nshasha yifatanije n’abo bagerageza gutera amahera, amahirwe y’uko umwe mu bacukuzi batera ashobora kuroranirwa aragwira, kandi amahirwe y’uko umwe mu bacukuzi b’inzirabugunge yoshobora kwagura uruzitiro aragabanuka mu gusubiza. Iyo ico gitero gikozwe cane kandi kigakomeza mu gihe kirekire, ivyemezo vy’amabuye ntivyoba bikiri ikimenyetso co kwizigirwa c’ukudahinduka kw’isoko rya Bitcoin. Ivyo vyoshobora gutuma iyo sisitemu idashobora gukoreshwa.


Kugira ngo ushobore guhangana n’ico kibazo, porogarama nyinshi za Wallet zica zizuza `nLocktime` kugira ngo zishireho ivyemezo vy’ugushingira intahe ivy’ugucuruza kugira ngo zishirwe mu burebure bw’ibarabara rikurikira. Gutyo, bica bidashoboka gushiramwo iyo nzira mu kwandika gusubiramwo igice c’imbere. Iyo ikoreshwa ry’abantu benshi rya `nLocktime` ryemejwe n’abakoresha Bitcoin, bigabanya cane inkurikizi zo gufata amafaranga. Nkako, biraremesha ugutera imbere kwa Blockchain aho gusubira kwandika mu kugabanya inyungu zishobora kuva muri yo. Ku bikorwa vya Taproot, BIP326 isaba gukoresha umwanya wa `nSequence` mu buryo busa n'ubwo kugira ngo haboneke ingaruka zingana n'iz'umurima wa `nLocktime` ku bindi bikorwa. Iryo koreshwa ryokwica inyoni zibiri n’ibuye rimwe mu gutuma kandi ubuzima bwite bw’amasezerano ya kabiri ya Layer akoresha umurima umwe butera imbere.
