---
term: Pizza day
definition: Umunsi mukuru kw'igenekerezo rya 22 Rusama wibutsa urugendo rwa mbere rwa Bitcoin rwakoreshejwe ku mapiza mu 2010.
---

Ivyo birori bihimbazwa buri wa 22 Rusama n’umuryango wa Bitcoin kugira ngo bibuke igikorwa ca mbere co gukoresha amafaranga y’ama bitcoins ku kintu ciza c’umubiri. Mu mwaka w’2010, Laszlo Hanyecz, umuhinga mu bijanye n’ivy’ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin, [yasavye ku rubuga rwa BitcoinTalk] kugura pizza zibiri nini ku madolari 10.000 BTC, hanyuma akagura amadolari 10.000. Ku wa 22 Rusama, yemeje ko iyo nzira yemewe n’umunyeshure w’imyaka 19 wo muri Californie yitwa Jeremy Sturdivant, azwi kw’izina ry’uruyeri rya Jercos. Yarasavye maze aratuma izo pizza ziva kwa Papa John zijanwa i Laszlo muri Floride. Uyu musi ni igihe gihambaye kuri Bitcoin kuko yerekana ubushobozi bwayo nk'ifaranga rya Exchange. Buri mwaka, abanyabubasha barahimbaza ico kintu mu kurya pizzas zihembwa mu ma bitcoins.
