---
term: GUHINDURA ATOMI
---

Ikoranabuhanga ryemerera Exchange y’amahera y’amahera hagati y’abantu babiri, ata kwizigira canke abahuza bisaba.  Ivyo biganiro vyitwa "atome" kuko bishobora gutuma habaho ingaruka zibiri gusa:


- Canke iyo swap iraroranirwa, kandi abo bompi bari muri iyo nama barahinduye neza amafaranga yabo y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none;

*Canke Exchange irananirwa, abo bompi bagakomeza amahera yabo y'intango.*


Atomic Swaps zishobora kubaho haba mu mafaranga amwe (yitwa *coinswap*) canke hagati y’amafaranga atandukanye. Mu mateka, barizigiye "Hash Time-Locked Contracts" (HTLC), uburyo bwo gufunga igihe butuma ibice vyose bibiri vy'ubudandaji biheza canke ko ata na kimwe muri vyo kirangira, gutyo bikaba bizigama ubutungane bw'amahera y'uwugira uruhara. Ubwo buryo bwasaba amasezerano ashobora gukorana n’inyandiko n’igihe.


Ariko mu myaka iheze, hariho uguhinduka kw’ugukoresha *Adaptor Signatures*, zitanga inyungu nyinshi:



- Bikuraho ivy’ugukenera inyandiko, bikagabanya amahera yo gukoresha;
- Ntibakeneye gukoresha ama hashes asa ku mpande zompi z’uguhinduranya, bigatuma ubuzima bwite butera imbere mu guca ihuriro ryose riboneka hagati y’ivyo bicuruzwa bibiri.
