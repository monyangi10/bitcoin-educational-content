---
term: Chaumian coinjoin
definition: Uburyo bwa coinjoin bukoresha imikono ya Chaum mu guhisha uwashizeko umukono kugira ngo bikingire abakoresha.
---

Itegeko rya CoinJoin rikoresha imikono y’impumyi ya David Chaum na Tor ku bijanye n’uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’abaje mu nama n’umuhuzabikorwa. Intumbero ya Chaumian CoinJoin ni ugutuma abaje mu nama bamenya neza ko umuhuzabikorwa adashobora kwiba ama bitcoins, canke ngo ahuze ivyo yinjiza n’ivyo asohoka.


Kugira ngo ivyo bishoboke, abakoresha bararungika ivyo bashizeko be n’inyandiko y’ukwakira blinded Address ku muhuzabikorwa. Iyi Address, yahora ari unblinded, igenewe kwakira ama bitcoins nk'umusaruro uva kuri CoinJoin. Umuhuzabikorwa arashira umukono kuri ivyo bimenyetso maze akabisubiza ababikoresha. Abakoresha baca bongera kwifatanya ata mazina yabo na server y’umuhuzabikorwa n’akaranga gashasha ka Tor maze bagahishura amaderesi yabo y’isohoka mu nyandiko zisanzwe ku bw’ubwubatsi bw’ibikorwa. Umuhuzabikorwa arashobora kugenzura ko izo aderesi zose zo kwakira abantu zikomoka ku bakoresha mu buryo bubereye, kuko yari yarashize umukono kuri verisiyo yabo ya blinded akoresheje urufunguzo rwiwe rw’ibanga. Ariko rero, ntashobora gufatanya igisohoka kinaka Address n’ukoresha w’inyungu yatanzwe. Nta sano rero riri hagati y’ivyo umuntu yinjiza n’ivyo asohora, mbere n’aho umuhuzabikorwa abona ibintu. Iyo iyo nzira y’ubudandaji imaze kwubakwa n’umuhuzabikorwa, arayirungikira abayigize bariko barayishirako umukono kugira ngo bafungure ivyo bashizemwo, amaze kugenzura ko ivyo basohora biri muri iyo nzira vy’ukuri. Abaje muri iyo nama boherereje umuhuzabikorwa umukono. Iyo imikono yose imaze gukusanywa, umuhuzabikorwa arashobora gutangaza ivy’ugucuruza CoinJoin ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.





Ubwo buryo buratuma umuhuzabikorwa adashobora guhungabanya amazina y’abaje mu nama canke ngo yibe ama bitcoins mu kiringo cose c’igikorwa ca CoinJoin.


Biragoye kumenya neza uwabanje gushiramwo iciyumviro ca CoinJoin kuri Bitcoin, n’uwagize iciyumviro co gukoresha imikono ya David Chaum y’impumyi muri ivyo. Kenshi vyiyumvirwa ko Gregory Maxwell ari we yabanjirije kubivuga muri [ubutumwa bwashizwe kuri BitcoinTalk mu 2013](279249.0):


> *"Mu gukoresha imikono y'impumyi ya Chaum: Abakoresha barahuza kandi bagatanga ivyinjizwa (no guhindura amaderesi) hamwe n'uburyo bw'ibanga bwa blinded bwa Address bipfuza koherezako amafaranga yabo bwite; ibisohoka vyose vyashizweko umukono na we kandi ko, rero, ibisohoka vyose biva ku bari muri iyo nama bafise uburenganzira Mu nyuma, abantu barasubira guhura bagashirako umukono."*

Maxwell, G. (2013, 22 Myandagaro). *CoinJoin: Bitcoin ubuzima bwite bw'isi nyakuri*. Ihuriro ry'Ibiganiro vya Bitcoin. 279249.0

Ariko rero, hariho n’ibindi vyavuzwe imbere y’aho, ku bijanye n’imikono ya Chaum mu bijanye n’uguvanga, be no ku bijanye n’ama coinjoins. [Muri Ruheshi 2011, Duncan Townsend yashize ahabona ku rubuga rwa BitcoinTalk) igikoresho co guvanga gikoresha imikono ya Chaum mu buryo busa cane n’ubw’amahera y’iki Chaum yo muri iki gihe. Muri iyo nkuru nyene, hariho [ubutumwa buvuye kuri hashcoin mu kwishura Duncan Townsend](htts. Ubu butumwa buratanga neza neza ivyo bisa cane n’ama coinjoins. Hari kandi n’ukuvuga uburyo busa n’ubwo muri [ubutumwa bwa Alex Mizrahi mu 2012](htts.github.com/killerstorm/6f843e1d38191aebca67d483bd88#file-laundry), igihe yariko aragira inama abaremye Tebrix ya Ijambo "CoinJoin" ubwaryo ntiryari guhingurwa na Greg Maxwell, ariko ryari rivuye mu ciyumviro ca Peter Todd.
