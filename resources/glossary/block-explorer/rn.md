---
term: Umuraba w'icitunza
definition: Igikoresho gihindura amakuru nyayo y'uruhererekane mu buryo busomwa neza bufise n'inyanduruko y'ivyonderwa.
---

Igikoresho co kuri interineti canke co mu karere gihindura amakuru atagiramwo ivyatsi ya Bitcoin Blockchain mu buryo butunganijwe kandi bushobora gusomwa n’abantu. Block explorer mu bisanzwe irimwo igikorwa co kurondera gituma abakoresha bashobora kwihuta kuronka amabarabara yihariye, amafaranga canke amaderesi.
