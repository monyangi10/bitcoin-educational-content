---
term: Daemon
definition: Porogaramu ya muduga ikora mu manyege, nka bitcoind kuri Bitcoin Core.
---

Ubwoko bwa porogaramu ya mudasobwa ikora mu nyuma, idashingiye ku kugenzura kw’uwuyikoresha. Izo porogarama zikora ibikorwa nk’ugucungera ibikorwa vy’urubuga, kugenzura ubuhinga, no kwishura ku bibazo ataco bisaba ko umuntu abigiramwo uruhara. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin core, daemon yitwa "bitcoind". Igereranya verisiyo ya Bitcoin core ata mukoresha Interface, ikora gusa biciye ku murongo w'amabwirizwa.
