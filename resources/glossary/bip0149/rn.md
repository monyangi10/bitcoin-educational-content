---
term: BIP0149
definition: Isubirira rya BIP148 rishikiriza SegWit biciye kuri BIP8 mu buryo buke buke kandi butarimwo amatati menshi.
---

Iciyumviro ca Shaolin Fry co gukoresha SegWit (BIP141, BIP143, na BIP147) hakoreshejwe uburyo bwo gukoresha BIP8 n'`LOT=ukuri`, iyo gukoresha SegWit biciye kuri BIP9 kwananiwe gukoresha imbere y'igenekerezo rya 15 Ugushyingo 2019.

Ubutandukanye n’uburyo bwa BIP9, aho ukunanirwa kw’ikimenyetso bituma umuntu aheba gukora, BIP149 yari ifise intumbero yo gukoresha SegWit ku wa 4 Nyakanga 2018, abacukuzi boba bashitse ku rugero rw’ikimenyetso rwa 95% canke atarivyo.

Mu kiringo c’amezi umunani hagati y’ukwezi kwa 11 n’ukwezi kwa ndwi, ama node yari kuronka akaryo ko gushitsa BIP149, kugira ngo haboneke ko SegWit ikora n’ubutunzi bwinshi bw’urubuga iyo Miner idakora (UASF).

Igihe ihinduka rya mbere ry’ingorane ryari rishitse inyuma y’igenekerezo rya 4 Nyakanga 2018, ugukoresha kwobaye kuri `LOCKED_IN`, kandi SegWit yari gukoreshwa mu gihe c’uguhindura gikurikira.

Udakunze BIP148, yashizeho gukoresha SegWit biciye ku bakoresha canke benshi mu bacukuzi, BIP149 yasavye uburyo bwo gukoresha buhoro buhoro kandi bugereranywa, naho bwagumye butera isoni, hakurikijwe ingingo ngenderwako za BIP8.

Mu gihe BIP148 yari ishobora gutera ingorane yo gucapura Blockchain, BIP149 yaririnze ivyo mu kwemera amabuye adatanga ikimenyetso ca SegWit (kiretse Miner yakoze ukundi n’ibigirankana).

Ku bw’ivyo, BIP149 yari uburyo bwo gukoresha SegWit butagira amakimbirane menshi kuruta BIP148, bukaba bwari bufasha gukoreshwa buhoro buhoro kandi ata ngorane nyinshi.

Nta BIP148 canke BIP149 vyashizwe mu ngiro, SegWit ikoreshwa biciye ku MASF, cane cane kubera BIP91.
