---
term: Hydro cooling
definition: Uburyo bwo gukonjesha ASICs bukoresha umuzenguruko ufunze urimo amazi.
---

Uburyo bwo gukonjesha ama ASIC bukoresha umurongo ufunze aho amazi agendagenda aciye mu bice vy’ugukonjesha, amatiyo hanyuma akaja mu radiator. Intumbero ni ugukura ubushuhe mu bihimba no kubungabunga ubushuhe bubereye kugira ngo bikore.
