---
term: Bitcoin Inquisition
definition: Fork ya Bitcoin Core yo kugerageza ivyiyumviro bishasha vy'iterambere ku miyoboro y'igerageza.
---

Bitcoin Inquisition ni Fork ya porogarama ya Bitcoin core yagenewe kugerageza gushiramwo ivyifuzo bishasha vyo gutegura neza Bitcoin (BIPs) mu bidukikije bigenzurwa, ahanini ku nzira z’igerageza nka Signet. Harimwo, nk’akarorero, ugushigikira BIP118 (ICO COSE KIRI MU GIHE) na BIP119 (GUSUZUMA IKIGANIRO).

Intumbero y’Inama Nkuru y’Igihugu ya Bitcoin ni ukugerageza, gusuzuma no gutahura neza ingene ayo mahinduka ashobora gukoreshwa, akamaro, ingorane n’ingene ayo mahinduka ashobora guhindurwa.

Bitcoin Inkizisiyo irongowe na Anthony Towns.
