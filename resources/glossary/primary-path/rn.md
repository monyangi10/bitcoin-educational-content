---
term: INZIRA Y'IBANZE
---

Mu porogaramu ya Wallet ikoresha Miniscript, nka Liana nk’akarorero, inzira ya mbere yerekeza ku rutonde rw’imfunguruzo zituma amahera akoreshwa ubwo nyene, ata n’imwe ishingiye ku gihe. Iyi nzira yama iboneka kandi ikoreshwa mu gucunga bitcoins ku musi ku musi, ataco bisaba kurindira (timelock) bitandukanye n’inzira zo gusubirana. Fata akarorero k’inyandiko irimwo imfunguruzo 2 zitandukanye: urufunguzo A, rwemerera gukoresha amafaranga bitcoins ubwo nyene, n’urufunguzo B, rwemerera kuyakoresha inyuma y’ugucerezwa gusobanurwa n’igihe c’amabuye 52.560. Muri aka karorero, urufunguzo A ruva mu nzira y’intango, mu gihe urufunguzo B ruva mu nzira yo gusubirana.
