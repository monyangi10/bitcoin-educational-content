---
term: OP_CODESEPARATOR (0XAB)

definition: Opcode igabura inyandiko mu bice kugira ngo hashobore kubaho ibimenyetso vyigenga kuri buri gice.
---

Ihindura inyandiko y'isohoka ry'ubu, yerekana ko ibikorwa bikurikira iyi opcode vyonyene bizofatwa mu mutwe mu kugenzura imikono y'ibintu vyinjijwe bihuye. Ivyo bituma umuntu ashobora gucapura inyandiko igoye mu bice vyinshi, aho igice cose gishobora gushirwako umukono ku giti caco.
