---
term: PPLNSG
definition: Uburyo bwo kuriha bwa PPLNS buhuza imigabane mu migwi.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikirundi ngo "*Ishura ku migabane N ya nyuma yashizwe hamwe*". Ni uburyo bwo kubara indishi y’abacukuzi mu bidengeri vya Mining. PPLNSG ikora nka PPLNS, ariko ikoranya imigabane mu matsinda. Iyo migwi y’imigabane rero irahembwa hamwe.
