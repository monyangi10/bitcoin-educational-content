---
term: BIP0013
---

Iyi BIP Itanga uburyo bumwe bwo guhingura amaderesi ya P2SH. BIP13 yasobanuye uburyo bwa P2SH Address, butangurana n'intango `3`, kandi burimwo Hash y'inyandiko ya Redeem aho kuba Hash y'urufunguzo rwa bose. Uwo murongo waracitse urugero rwo gukoresha mu gukoresha amasakoshi y’amasinya menshi mu myaka myinshi.
