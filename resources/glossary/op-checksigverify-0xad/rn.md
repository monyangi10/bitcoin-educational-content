---
term: OP_CHECKSIGVERIFY (0XAD)

---

Ikora igikorwa kimwe na `OP_CHECKSIG`, ariko iyo igenzura ry'umukono rinaniwe, inyandiko ica ihagarara ubwo nyene n'ikosa maze rero igikorwa kikaba kitagira akamaro. Iyo igenzura rishoboye, inyandiko irabandanya ata gusunika agaciro ka `1` (ukuri) ku kirundo. Mu ncamake, `OP_CHECKSIGVERIFY` ikora igikorwa `OP_CHECKSIG` ikurikiwe na `OP_VERIFY`. Iyi opcode yahinduwe muri Tapscript kugira ngo isuzume imikono ya Schnorr.
