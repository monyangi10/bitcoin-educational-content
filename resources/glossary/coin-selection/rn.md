---
term: Coin GUHITANIRA
---

Uburyo porogarama ya Bitcoin Wallet ihitamwo UTXOs izokoresha nk’ivyo yinjiza kugira ngo ihaze ibisohoka mu gicuruzwa. Uburyo bwo guhitamwo Coin burahambaye kuko bugira ingaruka ku giciro c’ugucuruza no ku buzima bwite bw’uwukoresha. Akenshi bigamije kugabanya umubare w’ibintu bikoreshwa, kugira ngo bigabanye ubunini bw’ibikorwa n’amahera ajanye, mu gihe bigerageza kubungabunga ubuzima bwite mu kwirinda gufatanya ibiceri biva mu bibanza bitandukanye (CIOH). Hari uburyo bwinshi bwo guhitamwo Coin, nk' *Knapsack Solver* canke *Ishami-n'Imbohe*. Iyo uguhitamwo Coin gukorwa n’amaboko n’uwukoresha, vyitwa “*Coin Control*”.


> ► *Mu congereza, ryitwa "Ihitamwo rya Coin".*
