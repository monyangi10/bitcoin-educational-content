---
term: Icisebe
definition: Inyubako nshasha ishobora kwimurwa irimwo ASIC nyinshi n'uburyo bwo gufuta imbere.
---

Mu Mining, igikoresho ni igikoresho gikoreshwa mu gushiramwo no gukoresha umubare munini w’ibikoresho vya ASIC. Ivyo bikoresho vyakozwe kugira ngo bikoreshe neza umwanya, ubushuhe n’ububasha bwa Supply. Bifise ubuhinga bwo gukonjesha umuyaga. Inyungu nyamukuru zifise ni uko zishobora gutembera n’uko zishobora gukoreshwa ningoga, akenshi zikaba ziri hafi y’aho zishobora gukoreshwa n’amashanyarazi atari menshi.
