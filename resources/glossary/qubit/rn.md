---
term: QUBIT

---

Igiharuro c’ishimikiro c’amakuru kuri mudasobwa y’ubuhinga bwa quantum. Izo qubits zishobora gufata agaciro ka 0, agaciro ka 1, canke ugushira hamwe kwa 0 na 1. Mu gukoresha ukwo gushirwa hamwe kw’ibintu n’ibindi bimenyetso vy’ubuhinga nk’ugufatanya n’ugutera imbere kw’ubuhinga, mudasobwa y’ubuhinga irashobora guhuza imigenderanire y’ubuhinga, gutyo igatorera umuti ingorane zimwe zimwe zidasanzwe vyihuta cane.
