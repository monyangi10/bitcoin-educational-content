---
term: SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUTANYSCRIPT

definition: Uburyo bwa SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT butuma kandi uhindura script y'isohoka ijyanye naryo.
---

Ihinduka ry'Ibendera rya SigHash `SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT` muri Bitcoin. Iyi SigHash ikora nka `SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT`, ariko yongerako guhindura inyandiko y'isohoka ifatanye na UTXO.
