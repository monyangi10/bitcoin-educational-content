---
term: HACHEUR
---

In the context of mining pools, participants are often referred to as "miners". The main task of these individual miners is to hash template blocks supplied by the pool server, looking for hashes that satisfy the difficulty target defined for the shares, and not that of Bitcoin. The rest of the mining process, which includes actual block construction, transaction selection or proof-of-work search according to Bitcoin's own difficulty, as well as distribution, is carried out directly by the pools.
