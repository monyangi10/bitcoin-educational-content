---
term: KWIHANGANIRA AMAKOSA YA BIZANTINE (BFT)
---

Iciyumviro gikoreshwa mu mice ikwiragijwe kugira ngo habeho ukwiyumvikana kwizigirwa kandi gutekanye, mbere n’aho hariho utugingo ngengabuzima dufise amakosa canke dufise ububi.


Ico ciyumviro gifitaniye isano n’[Ikibazo c’Abajenerali b’Abanyabizantine](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/ingorane y’Abajenerali b’Abanyabizantine), ikigereranyo kigaragaza ingorane zo guhuza ibikorwa no gufata ingingo mu nzira y’ubuhinga busanzwe aho abakinyi bashobora kutizigirana.


> ► *Ubuhinga bwa Proof-of-Work (PoW) bukoreshwa na Bitcoin ni uburyo bwo kwumvikana bushika ku "Kwihanganira amakosa ya Bizansi (BFT)." Ushaka kumenya vyinshi, raba insobanuro ya **[IKIBAZO C'ABAGENERALA BA-BIZANTINE](https://planb.academy/it/ibikoresho/urutonde rw'amajambo/ingorane-y'abakuru-ba-bizantine)**.*
