---
term: SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT

definition: Icabazwa ca SigHash gituma umukono utaboshwa na UTXO idasanzwe.
---

Iciyumviro co gushirwa mu ngiro kw'ihindura rishasha ry'ibendera rya SigHash muri Bitcoin, ryashizweho na BIP118. `SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT` iremesha guhindura cane mu gucunga amafaranga, cane cane ku bikorwa biteye imbere nk'imihora yo kwishura kuri Lightning Network n'ivugurura rya Eltoo. `SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT` ishoboza umukono kudaboheshwa na UTXO iyo ari yo yose y'imbere (*Igisohoka cose c'imbere*). Iyo ikoreshejwe hamwe na `SIGHASH_ALL`, yotuma umuntu ashobora gusinya ku bisohoka vyose vy’ugucuruza, ariko nta na kimwe mu vyo yinjiza. Ivyo vyotuma uwo mukono wosubira gukoreshwa mu bikorwa bitandukanye, igihe cose hari ibintu bimwebimwe vyategekanijwe bishitse.


> ► *Iki gihindura SigHash SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT kiva ku ciyumviro ca SIGHASH_NOINPUT catangujwe na Joseph Poon mu 2016 kugira ngo yongere iciyumviro ciwe ca Lightning Network.*
