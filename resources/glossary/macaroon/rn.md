---
term: Macaroon
definition: Uburyo bwo kwemeza umuntu bukoreshwa kuri Lightning kugira ngo bakingire kwinjira mu bikorwa vyo kure.
---

Uburyo bwo kwemeza ko umuntu ari umunyakuri bugenewe gukingira uburenganzira bwo kuronka ibikorwa ku bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none. Macaroons zikoreshwa cane cane kuri Lightning kugira ngo zimenyeshe abakoresha iyo bashitse ku bikorwa vyashinzwe. Nk’akarorero, ukoresheje node ya Lightning, birashoboka ko generate i macaroon yemerera gukora ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji ukoresheje telefone yawe ngendanwa biciye ku node yawe iri kure. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibifungurwa, ama macaroons arafise akamaro ko kwemezwa n’uwuyatanga canke akarungikwa kugira ngo asuzumwe.
