---
term: UBUTUNZI BENSHI
---

Yerekeza ku gice kinini c’ibikorwa vy’ubutunzi bijanye n’amafaranga ya Bitcoin, akomoka ku bacuruzi. Umucuruzi asobanurwa nk’ikigo cose gifatika ku mubiri canke gitegekanijwe n’amategeko gishaka gutanga Exchange ikintu canke igikorwa ku bwa BTC. Abo bacuruzi, harimwo abacuruzi, abakoresha, ama platforms ya Exchange, n’abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro, baratandukanye mu bunini no mu butunzi. Bamwebamwe ni abakinyi bakuru, bagatuma haba ibikorwa vyinshi vy’ubutunzi, mu gihe abandi ari abafise uruhara runini. Ivyinshi mu vy’ubutunzi rero bisobanurwa n’abo igikorwa cabo c’ubutunzi gihurikiye hamwe giserukira umugabane munini muri ayo mafaranga. Ivyo vyinshi biragira ico bikoze ku mategeko y'uguhurizako, cane cane iyo habaye Fork.
