---
term: URUNYUNGURO RWO MU NDA
---

Mu gukuraho amasakoshi y’ibintu vy’agaciro n’ivy’ubukuru, uruzitiro rw’imbere ni ishami ry’ugukuraho rikoreshwa kuri generate kwakira amaderesi agenewe kwakira amahera avuye kuri Wallet nyene, ni ukuvuga guhindura amaderesi gusa. Inkuru yose nk'uko isobanurwa mu burebure bwa 3 ifise iminyororo ibiri mu burebure bwa 4: urudodo rw'inyuma n'urudodo rw'imbere (rwitwa kandi "urudodo rwo guhindura"). Uruhererekane rw'imbere rukomoka ku rutonde rwa `/1/`.
