---
term: OP_HASH160 (0XA9)

definition: Opcode ihahinga ikintu k’inyuma ikoresheje SHA256 hanyuma RIPEMD160.
---

Ifata ikintu co hejuru mu kirundo ikagisubiriza hash yaco, ikoresheje ibikorwa vyose `SHA256` na `RIPEMD160` icarimwe. Ivyo bikoresho bifise intambwe zibiri bituma umuntu agira ikimenyetso c’urutoke c’ibice 160.
