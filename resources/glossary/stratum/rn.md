---
term: Stratum

definition: Protocolo y'urusobe rw'itumanaho rworohereza itumanaho hagati y'abaminer b'abantu ku giti cyabo n'ama server y'ama pools y'ubumeneri.
---

Itegeko ry’urubuga ryashizwe hejuru ya Bitcoin ryateguwe canecane kugira ngo rituma abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bavugana neza hagati y’abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro n’abakozi ba mining kugira ngo bongere inyungu. Stratum yatangajwe mu mpera z'umwaka w'2011 na Marek Palatinus, azwi cane kw'izina ry'uruyeri "Slush" akaba n'uwashinze ikidengeri ca mbere ca mining, Slush Pool, ubu citwa Braiins. Stratum yaje gusubirira umurongo wa kera wa Getwork, wari waracitse igihe.


Ni ngombwa gutahura ko Stratum itashizwemwo nk’itegeko muri Bitcoin, ahubwo ari porogarama yihariye ikoreshwa n’ibidengeri. Naho ibidengeri vya mining bifise ubuntu bwo kutabikoresha, Stratum yarishizeho nk’ikigereranyo ca Bitcoin mining mu myaka irenga cumi. Igitabu caco ca kabiri, Stratum V2, ubu kiriko kirategurwa. Igamije gutuma Stratum itungana neza no kugabanya ivyiyumviro vyo gushiramwo ibintu vyose hamwe bijanye n’ibidengeri vya mining ivyo vyavuyemwo.
