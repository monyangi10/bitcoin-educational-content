---
term: UMUGONGO WA ELLIPTE
---

Mu bijanye n’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru, umurongo w’uruzitiro ni umurongo w’uruzitiro usobanurwa n’ingero y’uburyo $y^2 = x^3 + ax + b$. Ivyo bipimo bikoreshwa muri Elliptic Curve Cryptography (ECC), ari bwo buryo bwo gupfuka urufunguzo rwa bose bushoboza guhingura ubuhinga bwo gupfuka, imikono y’ubuhinga bwa none, n’uburyo bwo gupfuka urufunguzo Exchange. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ECDSA (*Igiharuro c'Umukono w'Igiharuro*) canke umurongo wa Schnorr bikoreshwa n'igiharuro ca `secp256k1`. Iyi curve yatowe kubera ubushobozi bwayo n’imiterere y’umutekano wayo. Izo algorithmes zikoreshwa ku mfunguruzo za bose za generate zivuye ku mfunguruzo z’abantu ku giti cabo, hamwe no gusinyana amafaranga, gutyo zigafungura bitcoins.
