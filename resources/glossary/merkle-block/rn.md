---
term: Merkle block
definition: Inyubako itanga ikimenyetso gito c'uko ihererekanya ryashizwe mu mubumbe ku bakoresha uburyo bworoheje.
---

Igishushanyo c’amakuru gikoreshwa mu bijanye na BIP37 (*Transaction Bloom Filtering*) kugira ngo hatangwe ikimenyamenya gikomeye c’ugushiramwo ibikorwa vyihariye mu gice. Ikoreshwa cane cane ku ma wallet ya SPV. Merkle Block irimwo imitwe y’amabuye, amafaranga ayunguruwe, n’igice ca Merkle Tree, bituma abaguzi b’umuco bashobora kwihuta kugenzura nimba amafaranga ari ay’amabuye batakuye amafaranga yose.
