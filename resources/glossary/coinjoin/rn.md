---
term: CoinJoin
---

CoinJoin ni ubuhinga bukoreshwa mu guca ubushobozi bwo gukurikirana ama bitcoins. Ishingiye ku gucuruza kw’ubufatanye gufise imiterere yihariye y’izina rimwe: ugucuruza kwa CoinJoin. Ibikorwa vya CoinJoin birafasha mu gutuma ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha Bitcoin bukingirwa neza mu gutuma abarorerezi bo hanze bagorwa cane no gusuzuma ibikorwa. Uwo mubumbe utuma umuntu ashobora kuvanga ibiceri vyinshi mu gikorwa kimwe, bikaba bigoye kumenya amasano ari hagati y’amaderesi y’injiza n’ay’isohoka.


Ibikorwa rusangi vya CoinJoin ni ibi: abakoresha batandukanye bipfuza guvanga bashiramwo amahera nk’inyungu y’ugucuruza. Ivyo bizosohoka nk’ibisohoka bitandukanye vy’umubare umwe. Ku mpera y’ugucuruza, ntibishoboka ko umuntu amenya igisohoka ari ic’uwukoresha. Mu buryo bw’ubuhinga nta sano riri hagati y’ivyo yinjira n’ivyo asohora mu bikorwa vya CoinJoin. Ihuriro hagati y’ukoresha wese na UTXO yose riracitse, nk’uko nyene amateka ya Coin yose ari.

Kugira ngo CoinJoin ishobore gukoreshwa ata mukoresha n’umwe atakaje ububasha ku mahera yiwe igihe cose, iyo nzira y’ugucuruza ibanza kwubakwa n’umuhuzabikorwa hanyuma igashikirizwa umukoresha wese. Umwe wese aca ashira umukono ku ruhande rwiwe rw’ivyo agurisha amaze kugenzura ko bimubereye, hanyuma imikono yose igashirwa ku vyo agurisha. Iyo umukoresha canke umuhuzabikorwa agerageje kwiba amahera y’abandi mu guhindura ibiva mu gikorwa ca CoinJoin, rero imikono izoba idafise akamaro kandi igikorwa kizokwangwa n’ibice. Iyo kwandika ibisubizo vy'abaje mu nama bikozwe hakoreshejwe imikono ya Chaum kugira ngo ntihagire isano n'ivyo bashizemwo, ivyo vyitwa "Chaumian CoinJoin".


Ubwo buryo bwongerera ibanga ry’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji ataco bisaba guhindura mu masezerano ya Bitcoin. Ishirwa mu ngiro ryihariye rya CoinJoin, nka Whirlpool, JoinMarket, canke Wabisabi, ritanga inyishu zo kworohereza uburyo bwo guhuza ibikorwa hagati y’abaje mu nama no kwongerera ubushobozi igikorwa co gucuruza CoinJoin. Aha ni akarorero k’ugucuruza kwa CoinJoin:


```text
323df21f0b0756f98336437aa3d2fb87e02b59f1946b714a7b09df04d429dec2
```


Biragoye kumenya neza uwabanje gushiramwo iciyumviro ca CoinJoin kuri Bitcoin, n’uwagize iciyumviro co gukoresha imikono ya David Chaum y’impumyi muri ivyo. Kenshi vyiyumvirwa ko Gregory Maxwell ari we yabanjirije kubivuga muri [ubutumwa bwashizwe kuri BitcoinTalk mu 2013](279249.0):

Gukoresha imikono y’impumyi ya Chaum: Abakoresha barahuza kandi bagatanga ivyo bashiramwo (no guhindura amaderesi) hamwe n’uburyo bw’ibanga bwa blinded bwa Address bipfuza koherezako ibiceri vyabo vy’ibanga; umukozi ashira umukono ku bimenyetso maze akabigarura. Abakoresha barasubira kwifatanya ata mazina yabo, bagakuraho amaderesi yabo y’isohoka, maze bakayarungika kuri server. Serveri ishobora kubona ko ibisohoka vyose vyashizweko umukono na yo kandi ko, kubera ivyo, ibisohoka vyose biva ku baje mu nama babereye. Mu nyuma, abantu barasubira guhura bagashira umukono.

Maxwell, G. (2013, 22 Myandagaro). *CoinJoin: Bitcoin ubuzima bwite bw'isi nyakuri*. Ihuriro ry'Ibiganiro vya Bitcoin. 279249.0


Ariko rero, hariho ivyo vyavuzwe imbere y’aho, ku bijanye n’imikono ya Chaum mu bijanye n’uguvanga, be no ku bijanye n’ama coinjoins. [Muri Ruheshi 2011, Duncan Townsend yashize ahabona ku rubuga rwa BitcoinTalk] (Ikiganiro c’Ivyabona vya Yehova) igikoresho co guvanga gikoresha imikono y’Abachaum mu buryo busa cane n’ubw’ibiharuro vy’Abachaum bo muri iki gihe. Muri iyo nkuru nyene, hariho [ubutumwa buvuye kuri hashcoin mu kwishura Duncan Townsend] (htts. Ubu butumwa buratanga ivyo bisa cane n’ama coinjoins. Hari kandi n’ukuvuga uburyo busa n’ubwo muri [ubutumwa bwa Alex Mizrahi mu 2012](htts.github.com/killerstorm/6f843e1d38191aebca67d483bd88#file-laundry), igihe yariko aragira inama abaremye Tebrix ya Ijambo "CoinJoin" ubwaryo ntiryavumbuwe na Greg Maxwell, ariko ryavuye mu ciyumviro ca Peter Todd.


> ► *Ijambo "CoinJoin" nta nsobanuro y'igifaransa rifise. Bamwe mu bacuruzi ba bitcoins na bo nyene bakoresha amajambo "vanga", "vanga", canke "vanga" kugira ngo bavuge ivy'ugucuruza kwa CoinJoin. Guvanga ni uburyo bukoreshwa mu mutima wa CoinJoin. Kandi, birahambaye ko tutavanga ukuvanga biciye ku coinjoins n’ukuvanga biciye ku mukinyi nyamukuru afata bitcoins mu gihe c’urugendo. Ivyo ntaco bihuriyeko na CoinJoin aho uwuyikoresha adatakaza ububasha ku bitcoins vyiwe mu gihe c’ivyo bikorwa.*
