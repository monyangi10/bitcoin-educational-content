---
term: Percolation
definition: Uburyo bwerekana ikwiragizwa ry'amakuru mu muryango w'innode za Bitcoin.
---

Yerekeza ku citegererezo gifasha gutahura ukwiragira kw’amakuru (ibikorwa n’amabuye) ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin. Ivyiyumviro vy’ugusesa amazi mu ntango ni ikigereranyo c’imibare n’ivy’umubiri ciga ukuntu amazi agenda n’ukuyungurura biciye mu bikoresho bifise utunogo. Irasuzuma ingene, iyo umuntu arengeje urugero runaka, uruzitiro rufatanye rutuma amazi aguma atemba aciye muri ivyo bintu. Ivyo bishobora gukoreshwa ku nzira za mudasobwa kugira ngo umuntu abone ingene amakuru akwiragizwa mu kwihweza izo nzira nk’imbuga zishobora kuba zikora canke zidakora. Mu Bitcoin, utugingo ngengabuzima rero turagira uruhara rw’ibihimba vy’umubiri mu vyiyumviro vy’uguca mu mazi. Igikoresho cose gikora kirakira kandi kikarungika amakuru ku bindi bice, ivyo bikaba bizobandanya gutanga amakuru canke bikabibuza. Ugukwiragira kw’ubwoko bumwe bumwe bw’ibikorwa birashobora gusuzumwa mu bijanye n’ibipimo vy’ugusenyuka, aho ijana ry’ibihimba bikora rikenewe kugira ngo umuntu ashike ku Miner azoyishira mu gice. Iyi nyigisho itanga urutonde rwo gusuzuma ingene amahinduka mu rubuga, nk’uguhindura amategeko y’ugushinga urugero n’ibice bimwebimwe, bigira ico bikoze ku buryo bwo gukwiragiza amafaranga kugira ngo umuntu ashike ku Miner.
