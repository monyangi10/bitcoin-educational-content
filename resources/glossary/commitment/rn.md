---
term: Commitment
---

Commitment (mu buryo bw'ibanga) ni ikintu c'imibare, kigaragazwa $C$, kiva ku gikorwa ku makuru atunganijwe $m$ (ubutumwa) n'agaciro k'imburakimazi $r$. Twandika :


$$
C = \text{commit}(m, r)
$$


Ubu buryo bugizwe n'ibikorwa bibiri nyamukuru:




- Commit: dukoresha igikorwa c'ibanga ku butumwa $m$ n'imburakimazi $r$ kugira ngo tubone $C$ ;
- Genzura: dukoresha $C$, ubutumwa bwa $m$ n'agaciro ka $r$ kugira ngo tugenzure ko iyi Commitment ari ukuri. Igikorwa kigarura `Ukuri` canke `Ikinyoma`.


Commitment itegerezwa kwubahiriza ibintu bibiri:




- Binding: bitegerezwa kuba bidashoboka kuronka ubutumwa bubiri butandukanye butanga $C$ imwe :


$$
m' : \, | \, : m' \neq m \quad \text{and} \quad r' : \, | \, : r' \neq r \quad
$$


Nka nka :


$$
\text{verify}(m, r, C) = \text{verify}(m', r', C) \rightarrow \text{True}
$$




- Kwihisha: ubumenyi bwa $C$ ntibutegerezwa guhishura ibirimwo $m$.


Ku bijanye n’amasezerano ya RGB, Commitment ishirwa mu gikorwa ca Bitcoin kugira ngo yerekane ko hari amakuru kanaka ariho mu gihe kinaka, ata makuru ubwayo atangajwe.
