---
term: OP_CAT (0X7E)

definition: Opcode yakuweko yemerera gufatanya ibintu bibiri vya mbere kurutonde rw'ibintu.
---

Kwemerera gufatanya ibintu bibiri vyo hejuru ku ruganda (ni ukuvuga, kubifatanya kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera). Iyi opcode yarazimye, bituma ubu idashobora gukoreshwa. Ariko rero, ubu nyene ryarasubiye kugaragara cane. Bamwe bipfuza kuvyongera kuri Tapscript kugira ngo bishobore gukoranya ibintu biri ku rutonde, gutyo bigatuma uru rurimi rushobora guserura neza. Iki gikoresho coroshe co kwongerako coshobora kwemera:


- Ikoreshwa ry’imikono ya Lamport, ishobora kuba ishobora guhangana n’ibitero vy’ubuhinga bwa quantum;
- Ishirwa mu ngiro ry’Ivyagezwe;
- Ikoreshwa ry’amasezerano;

*Canke mbere, gukoresha amasezerano atagiramwo uburinganire.*
