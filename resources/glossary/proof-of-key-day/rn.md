---
term: Proof-of-key day
definition: Umugambi ngarukamwaka wo kw’igenekerezo rya 3 Nzero wo gukura abitcoin mu mashengero ukayashira mu buryo bwa self-custody.
---

Intambwe y’umwaka ihimbazwa buri wa 3 Mukakaro (ku musi w’isabukuru y’ibarabara rya Genesis) iremesha abafise Bitcoin gukura itunga ryabo ku bibanza vya Exchange mu gihe kimwe maze bakarirungika mu gutorera umuti ibibazo vyo kwibungabunga. Intumbero ni ukugerageza ubushobozi bw’ama platforms ya Exchange, bikabahatira kwemeza ko vy’ukuri bifise amafaranga y’abakoresha bavyo kandi ko bitagira uruhara mu bikorwa vy’ububiko bw’amabanki. Ariko rero, uwo mugenzo uragenda uragenda ukurikizwa cane muri iki gihe, ivyo bikaba bigabanya ubushobozi bwawo. Mu mpera, uburyo bwiza bwo kwikingira gusenyuka kwa Exchange ni ukuguma ufise amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri (bitcoins) mu kwikingira umwaka wose.
