---
term: PROOF-OF-KEY DAY
---

An annual initiative observed every January 3 (on the anniversary of the Genesis block) that encourages bitcoin holders to withdraw their assets from exchange platforms at the same time and transfer them to self-custody solutions. The aim is to test the solvency of exchange platforms, forcing them to prove that they are indeed holding their users' bitcoins and not engaging in fractional reserve banking. However, this practice is less and less followed today, which reduces its effectiveness. Ultimately, the best way to protect yourself against exchange bankruptcy is to keep your bitcoins in self-custody all year round.
