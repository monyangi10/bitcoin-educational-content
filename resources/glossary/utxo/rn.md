---
term: UTXO
definition: Ibisohoka biva mu itunshwa ry'amafaranga bitarakoreshwa biba byerekana igice cy'abitcoin gishobora gukoreshwa nka vin.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikirundi *Igisubizo c'Ibikorwa Bitakoreshejwe*. UTXO ni umusaruro w’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji utarakoreshwa, bisobanura ko utakoreshejwe nk’inyungu y’ibikorwa bishasha. UTXOs zigereranya igice c’ama bitcoins umukoresha afise kandi ubu araboneka kugira ngo akoreshe. Buri UTXO ifatanye n’inyandiko y’isohoka yihariye (`scriptPubKey`), isobanura ivyangombwa bikenewe kugira ngo ukoreshe ama bitcoins. Ibikorwa vyo gucuruza muri Bitcoin birya izo UTXO nk’ibintu vyinjizwa kandi bikarema UTXO nshasha nk’ibintu bisohoka. Ico kigereranyo ca UTXO ni co gihambaye kuri Bitcoin, kuko gituma umuntu ashobora kugenzura vyoroshe ko amafaranga atagerageza gukoresha amafaranga y’ama bitcoins atahari canke amaze gukoreshwa.
