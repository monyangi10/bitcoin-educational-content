---
term: IGAKOPO RY'UMWIZA
---

Porogaramu Bitcoin Wallet yibanze ku buzima bwite, yatangujwe na Amir Taaki na Cody Wilson mu 2014, ikora nk’ukwagura umucukumbuzi wa Google Chrome. DarkWallet yari ifise ibikorwa vyo kwongereza ubuzima bwite bw’ukoresha Bitcoin, nk’ukwishura mu buryo bw’ubujuju n’ugushiramwo amahera. Iterambere ryayo ryarahagaze kuva muri Mukakaro 2015.
