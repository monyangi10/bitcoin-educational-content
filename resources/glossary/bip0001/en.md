---
term: BIP0001
---

Document that defines the process for developing and implementing proposed improvements to the Bitcoin protocol. Written by Amir Taaki in August 2011, it establishes a standard methodology for proposing and documenting potential modifications. BIP proposals are then classified into three categories:
* *Standards Track*: Direct modifications to the Bitcoin protocol and its interoperability.
* *Informational*: General information that does not directly affect the protocol.
* *Process*: Non-technical changes, such as procedures and guidelines.

This framework helped systematize Bitcoin’s development, ensuring a coordinated and transparent approach to its evolution. The methodology introduced by BIP1 was later superseded by BIP2.
