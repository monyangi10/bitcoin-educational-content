---
term: Wallet.dat

definition: Idosi rya Bitcoin Core ryapfunditswe ririmo inyifunguzo z'ibanga n'amakuru y'agashakoshi k'umukoresha.
---

Dosiye muri Bitcoin Core ibika amakuru yerekeye wallet y'ukoresha, nk'imfunguruzo z'ibanga n'ibikorwa vyakozwe. Dosiye `wallet.dat` irashizwemwo amakuru kugira ngo amafaranga agire umutekano. Kuva kuri verisiyo 0.16.0, iyi dosiye yimuriwe muri dosiye `/wallets`.
