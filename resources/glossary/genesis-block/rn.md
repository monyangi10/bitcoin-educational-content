---
term: Genesis UBUGABO
---

Igipande ca Genesis ni co gipande ca mbere c’urutonde rwa Bitcoin. Bisobanura ugutangura kwa Bitcoin. Ico kibanza ca Genesis cashinzwe n’uwashinze Bitcoin atamenyekanye, Satoshi Nakamoto, kw’igenekerezo rya 3 Mukakaro 2009. Hash yaco ni:


```text
000000000019d6689c085ae165831e934ff763ae46a2a6c172b3f1b60a8ce26f
```


Iyi block irimwo gusa Coinbase Transaction itanga ama bitcoins 50 nk’impera ya Miner (muri iki gihe, Satoshi Nakamoto ubwiwe). Iyi block irimwo ubutumwa bushizwe muri Coinbase Transaction:


```text
The Times 03/Jan/2009 Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks
```


Ivyo bivugwa ni ivyerekeye ingingo yo mu kinyamakuru *The Times*. Ubutumwa busobanurwa nk’ugunegura uburyo bw’ivy’ubutunzi bwa kera n’ibirenze urugero bwabwo, ivyo bikaba ari vyo vyatumye habaho Bitcoin nk’ubundi buryo bwo gukoresha amahera.


Kubera ko irimwo igice ca mbere cane ca Bitcoin Blockchain, biragaragara ko igice ca Genesis kitagira umurima urimwo Hash y’igice c’imbere (kuko ata n’imwe iriho). Ikindi kandi, ama bitcoins 50 yashizweho nk’impera muri iyi block ntashobora gukoreshwa ku rugero rwa protocole.
