---
term: Nonce
definition: Inomero ikoreshwa rimwe gusa, ihindurwa n'abacukuzi kugira baronke hash y'ikibonwa yemewe.
---

Mu bijanye n'ubuhinga bwo guharura, ijambo "Nonce" ryerekeza ku mubare ukoreshwa rimwe gusa hanyuma ugasubirizwa. Akenshi ni ivy’imburakimazi canke ivy’imburakimazi. Nonces zikoreshwa mu mirongo ngenderwako itandukanye y’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru kugira ngo umutekano w’ivyo bikorwa ubeho. Nk’akarorero, imikono ya ECDSA ikoreshwa mu masezerano ya Bitcoin irimwo n’ugukoresha Nonce. Ivyo bisigura ko uwo mubare utegerezwa kuba mushasha ku musinya wose. Niba atari ko biri, birashoboka kubara urufunguzo rw’ibanga rukoreshwa mu kugereranya imikono ibiri ikoresha Nonce imwe.


Nonces na zo nyene zikoreshwa mu nzira ya Bitcoin Mining. Abacukuzi bongera ivyo bipimo bishobora guhindurwa mu bice vyabo vy’abahiganwa. Bahindura agaciro ka Nonce kugira ngo barondere Hash y’ibanga iri hasi canke ingana n’intumbero y’ingorane. Ivyo bisaba ububasha buhambaye bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwo guharura, kuko bisaba gushakashaka bimwe bishitse mu bintu vyinshi bishoboka. Iyo Miner ibonye Nonce iyo ishizwe mu gice cabo, itanga igitabu gihuye n’ingorane, ico gice kiramenyeshwa ku rubuga, Miner agatsinda impembo.


> ► *Mu mwaka w’2010, abashakashatsi barabonye ko PlayStation 3 ya Sony ikoresha Nonce imwe igihe yariko irasinya ama code atandukanye. Ukwo gusubira gukoresha Nonce kwatumye abatera bashobora kubara urufunguzo rw’ibanga rwakoreshejwe mu gusinya iyo porogarama. Iyo abatera bafise urufunguzo rw’ibanga, barashobora gukora imikono ibereye kuri kode iyo ari yo yose, ivyo bikaba bituma bashobora gukoresha porogarama zitemewe, harimwo n’imikino y’ubusuma canke ubuhinga bwo gukoresha busanzwe, ata guca ku ruhande kuri PS3.*

> ► *Hariho ivyiyumviro bitari vyo ku bijanye n'inkomoko y'ijambo "Nonce." Bamwe bavuga ko igereranya insiguro ngufi y'"umubare ukoreshwa rimwe gusa." Mu vy’ukuri, inkomoko y’iryo jambo yatanguye mu kinjana ca 18 kandi ikomoka ku guhinduka kw’insobanuro y’ijambo ry’icongereza rya kera ryitwa “then anes,” risobanura “ku bw’ico kintu.”*
