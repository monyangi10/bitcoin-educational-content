---
term: ISEZERANO
---

Uburyo bwemerera gushinga amategeko yihariye ku buryo amafaranga kanaka yoshobora gukoreshwa, harimwo no mu bikorwa vyo muri kazoza. Uretse ibintu bisanzwe vyemewe n’ururimi rw’inyandiko ku UTXO, iryo sezerano rishiraho izindi ngingo zijanye n’ingene iyo Bitcoin ishobora gukoreshwa mu bikorwa bikurikira. Mu buryo bw'ubuhinga, ugushinga isezerano bishika iyo `scriptPubKey` ya UTXO isobanura uburenganzira ku `scriptPubKey` y'ibisohoka vy'ugucuruza bikoresha iyo UTXO. Mu kwagura urugero rw’inyandiko, amasezerano yoshobora gutuma habaho iterambere ryinshi kuri Bitcoin nk’ugushiramwo amabarabara y’ibinyabiziga, gushirwa mu ngiro kw’ibibanza vy’ububiko, canke gutegura neza uburyo bwo gupfuka nk’umuravyo. Ivyiyumviro vy’isezerano biratandukanywa bishingiye ku ngingo zitatu:

*Igipimo cabo;*

*Ivyo bishirwa mu ngiro;*

*Ugusubira inyuma kwabo.*


Hariho ivyiyumviro vyinshi vyotuma hakoreshwa amasezerano kuri Bitcoin. Ivyateyimbere cane mu nzira yo gushirwa mu ngiro ni: `OP_GUSUZUMA IGITEGEKO` (CTV), `GUSUZUMA_ICO COSE` (APO), na `OP_UBUBIKO`. Mu bindi vyifuzo, harimwo: `OP_TX`, `OP_GUSUZUMA GUSUBIZA` (TLUV), `OP_KWIRUKA`, `OP_GUSUZUMA BIMENYETSO BIVA KU GUSUZUMA` (CSFSV), `OP_CAT`, n'ibindi.


Kugira ngo utahure neza iciyumviro c’isezerano, rimbura iki kigereranyo: wiyumvire isandugu irimwo 500€ mu bice bitobito. Niwashobora gufungura iyo safe ukoresheje urufunguzo rwiza, rero urafise umwidegemvyo wo gukoresha ayo mahera uko wipfuza. Ivyo ni vyo bisanzwe kuri Bitcoin. None, wiyumvire ko iyo safe nyene itarimwo amafaranga 500€ y’amafaranga y’amafaranga, ahubwo ko harimwo amafaranga y’ibifungurwa afise agaciro kangana. Niwashobora gufungura iyo safe, urashobora gukoresha iyo nsiguro. Ariko rero, umwidegemvyo wawe wo gukoresha amahera urabujijwe, kuko ushobora gukoresha izo mpapuro gusa kugira ngo wishyure mu maresitora amwamwe. Gutyo, hariho ikintu ca mbere umuntu ategerezwa gukora kugira ngo ayo mahera akoreshe, ni ukuvuga gushobora gufungura iyo safe akoresheje urufunguruzo rukwiye. Ariko kandi hariho ikindi kintu co kwongerako ku bijanye n’ikoreshwa ry’ayo mahera muri kazoza: ategerezwa gukoreshwa gusa mu maresitora y’abafatanyabikorwa, atari ku buntu. Uwo murongo wo guhagarika ibikorwa vyo gucuruza muri kazoza ni co citwa isezerano.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, nta jambo rishobora gusobanura neza insobanuro y'ijambo "isezerano". Umuntu yovuga ivy'"ingingo", "isezerano", canke "Commitment", ariko ivyo ntivyohuye neza n'ijambo "isezerano". Iri jambo ryakuwe mu majambo yo mu vy’amategeko yemerera kudondora ingingo y’amasezerano itegeka ko umutungo ugira inshingano zidahera.*
