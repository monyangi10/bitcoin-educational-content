---
term: Imahera rya crypto
definition: Ijambo rusangi ryerekana amahera yose canke itunga ry'ubuhinga rikoresha cryptography mu guhana.
---

Ijambo rusangi rihabwa uburyo bwose bw’amahera, umutungo, inguzanyo canke igice c’ubuhinga bwa none kiri muri sisitemu ya mudasobwa aho ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru bukoreshwa mu guhanahana no gukorana hagati y’abakoresha.
