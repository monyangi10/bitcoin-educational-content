---
term: Shitcoin

definition: Ijambo riteye isoni rikoreshwa ku biceri bitagira agaciro canke altcoins zose.
---

Ijambo ry’agasuzuguro rikoreshwa kenshi mw’isi y’amahera y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none kugira ngo ridondore amahera y’ubuhinga bwa none (altcoins) abonwa ko ata co amaze canke ko ata kazoza afise. Iri jambo rikoreshwa mu bihe bibiri bitandukanye: mu bidukikije rusangi vy’amahera y’ibanga, ryerekeza ku mugambi udafise ubuhinga bushasha, udatanga ikibazo nyaco co gukoresha, canke ahanini ari ingaruka y’ugutekereza ku buryo burenze urugero. Ku ba Bitcoin, ijambo ririmwo amafaranga yose y'ivy'ubuhinga atari Bitcoin (BTC), gutyo bigatuma "shitcoin" iba imwe na "altcoin." Ijambo ni ihuriro ry'ijambo "shit," risobanura kimwe mu congereza, n'ijambo "coin." Ikoreshwa rya mbere ry’iryo jambo ryakomotse ku butumwa bwashizweho n’uwukoresha Ribuck kw’igenekerezo rya 8 Ugushyingo 2010, ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin Talk.
