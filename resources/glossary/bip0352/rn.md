---
term: BIP0352
definition: Silent Payments, uburyo bukoresha aderesi zidahinduka kugira ngo bakire amapayementi hatabayeho kwongera gukoresha aderesi bigaragara.
---

Iciyumviro co gutera imbere ca Josibake na Ruben Somsen kizana Silent Payments, uburyo bwo gukoresha aderesi za Bitcoin zidahinduka kugira ngo umuntu aronke amahera ata Address isubira gukoresha, ata gukorana, kandi ata n’ihuriro riboneka rya On-Chain hagati y’amahera atandukanye. Ubwo buryo burakuraho ivy’uko umuntu akeneye gutanga amaderesi mashasha, atakoreshwa yo kwakira generate ku bijanye n’ugucuruza kwose, gutyo hakaba umuntu yirinda imigenderanire isanzwe muri Bitcoin aho uwuronka ategerezwa gutanga Address nshasha ku wuyitanga.


Muri iyo nzira, uwuriha akoresha urufunguzo rwa bose rw’uwuronka n’urufunguzo rwiwe bwite ku generate Address nshasha ku kwishura kwose.

Uwuronka, afise urufunguzo rwiwe rw'ibanga, ni we wenyene ashobora guharura urufunguzo rw'ibanga rujanye n'uru Address. Ubwo buryo bushingiye kuri ECDH (*Elliptic‐Curve Diffie‐Hellman*), urufunguzo rw’ibanga Exchange, kugira ngo hashirweho ibanga rusangi rikoreshwa mu gukura Address yakira n’urufunguzo rwayo rw’ibanga (ku ruhande rw’uwukira gusa).

Kugira ngo bamenye amahera y’uguhembwa mu gacerere abagenewe, abayaronka bategerezwa gucapura urupapuro rwa Blockchain maze bagasuzuma igikorwa cose gihuye n’ingingo ngenderwako z’amahera y’uguhembwa mu gacerere.

Udakunze BIP47, ikoresha igikorwa co kumenyesha kugira ngo ishinge umurongo wo kwishura, Silent Payments irakuraho iyo ntambwe, ikazigama igikorwa kimwe.

Ariko rero, ikintu co gusubiza inyuma ni uko abaronka amafaranga bategerezwa gusuzuma amafaranga yose yoshobora gukoreshwa maze bagakoresha ECDH kugira ngo bamenye nimba ari bo bagenewe guhabwa amafaranga.
