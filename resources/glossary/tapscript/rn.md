---
term: Tapscript
definition: Ururimi rwa script rwahinduwe rwatangijwe na BIP342 ku bijanye n'ikoreshwa rya P2TR mu rugero rwa Taproot.
---

Ivugurura rigamije guhindura opcodes zimwe zimwe z’ururimi rwa kera rwa Bitcoin, kugira ngo hasobanurwe ururimi rushasha rwo kwandika rukoreshwa mu gukoresha P2TR. Inyandiko yashizweho na BIP342, ishirwa mu ngiro na Taproot Soft Fork.


Kugira ngo hashirwe mu ngiro ivyo bihinduka bitandukanye bijanye na Taproot, vyari ngombwa ko basubira gusuzuma ururimi rwo kwandika. Iyi ni yo ntumbero ya Tapscript, ishobora guhagarika canke guhindura ama opcode amwe amwe, maze ikazana ayandi mashasha.
