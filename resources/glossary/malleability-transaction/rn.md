---
term: UBUSHOBOZI BWO GUKORA (GUGURIZA)
---

Yerekeza ku bushobozi bwo guhindura gatoyi imiterere y’isoko rya Bitcoin, ata guhindura ingaruka zaryo, ariko mu gihe uhindura ikimenyetso c’isoko (*txid*). Uwo mutungo urashobora gukoreshwa nabi kugira ngo uzimize abafatanyabikorwa ku bijanye n’ingene igikorwa giteye, gutyo bikazana ibibazo nk’ugukoresha amahera kabiri. Ugukomera kwashobotse kubera uguhinduranya umukono wa digitale wakoreshwa. SegWit Soft Fork yashizweho cane cane kugira ngo ntihagire iyo nzira yo guhindura ibikorwa vya Bitcoin, bituma gushirwa mu ngiro kwa Lightning Network bigorana. Ivyo ibishikako mu gukuraho amakuru ashobora guhinduka mu bijanye n’ugucuruza mu biharuro vya txid.


> ► *Naho ari gake, ijambo "mutability" rimwe na rimwe rikoreshwa mu kwerekeza ku ciyumviro kimwe.*
