---
term: Mining Porogarama yo gucungera
---

Porogaramu zigenewe gucunga no gutuma ibikorwa vya Mining bigenda neza. Ubwo bwoko bwa porogarama buratuma umuntu ashobora kugenzura mu gihe nyaco ibikorwa vya ASIC, ingene ikoresha amashanyarazi, ubushuhe n’ibipimo vya Hash. Itanga kandi uburyo bwo gucunga amatongo manini manini ya Mining, n’imiterere yikora (nk’ugukoresha overclocking), gutahura amakosa no gucunga ibikoresho biri kure.
