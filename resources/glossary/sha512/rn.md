---
term: SHA512
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikirundi "*Umutekano wa Hash Algorithme 512 bits*". Ni igikorwa c’ubuhinga bwa Hash gitanga igiharuro c’ibice 512. Yari yateguwe n'ikigo *kijejwe umutekano w'igihugu* (NSA) mu ntango z'imyaka ya 2000. Ku Bitcoin, igikorwa ca `SHA512` ntigikoreshwa mu buryo butaziguye mu masezerano, ariko gikoreshwa mu bijanye no gukura imfunguruzo z'abana ku rugero rw'ikoreshwa, hakurikijwe BIP32 na BIP39. Muri izo nzira, ikoreshwa incuro nyinshi mu `HMAC` algorithm, no mu `PBKDF2` urufunguzo rwo gukuraho. `SHA512` igikorwa ni igice c'umuryango wa SHA 2, nka `SHA256`. Ivyo ikora bisa cane n’ivyo vya nyuma.
