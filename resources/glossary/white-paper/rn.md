---
term: White paper

definition: Icetegeko ryanditswe na Satoshi Nakamoto mu mwaka wa 2008 risigura ishingiro rya Bitcoin.
---

Raporo canke ubuyobozi bumenyesha abasomyi mu buryo butomoye ikibazo gikomeye. Ku bijanye na Bitcoin, yerekeza ku nyandiko y'ubuhinga yitwa "*Bitcoin: Uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera mu buryo bw'ubuhinga bwa none*" yasohowe na Satoshi Nakamoto ku ncuro ya mbere ku wa 31 Gitugutu 2008. Iyi nyandiko irerekana ingingo ngenderwako z'ishimikiro za Bitcoin. Igitabo cera cashikirijwe muri iki gihe ni ica kabiri casohowe ku wa 24 Ntwarante 2009.
