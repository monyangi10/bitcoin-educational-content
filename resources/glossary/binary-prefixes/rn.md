---
term: INTANGAMARARA Y'IBIBARI
---

Ivyiyumviro bikoreshwa mu guharura kugira ngo umuntu apime incuro nyinshi z’ubunini bw’amakuru ashizweho ku bubasha bwa 2. Bitandukanye n’intango z’urutonde rw’ubuhinga bw’ubuhinga, zishingiye ku bubasha bwa 10, intangiriro z’ubuhinga bwa metiri, nka kibi (Ki), mebi (Mi), gibi (Gi), na tebi (Ti), zigereranywa n’ububasha bwa $2^{$}0, {$}0 $2^{30}$, $2^{40}$ uko bikurikirana). Ivyo bimenyetso biva ku buryo bwa kera bwo gupima ubunini bw’amakuru mu gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none.


Rimwe na rimwe ziboneka muri Bitcoin, nk'akarorero, kugira ngo zigaragaze urugero rw'ubunini bwa dosiye y'amadosiye `BLOCKS/BLK*.DAT` akoreshwa mu kubika amakuru ya Blockchain atagiramwo ivyatsi muri porogarama ya Bitcoin core. Izo dosiye zifise ubushobozi bwo gukora ama mebibyte 128 (MiB), ivyo bikaba bingana n’ama megabyte arenga gatoyi 134 (MB).
