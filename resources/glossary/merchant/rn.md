---
term: Umudandaza
definition: Umuntu canke ishirahamwe ryemera guhana ibidandazwa canke serivisi na bitcoin.
---

Umuntu wese w’umubiri canke w’amategeko yemera Exchange ikintu canke igikorwa c’ama bitcoins. Abo bacuruzi ni bo baha Bitcoin akamaro kayo. Uko amafaranga yemerwa n’abadandaza benshi, ni ko arushiriza kugira akamaro ku bantu ku giti cabo. Kubera ko abacuruzi bafise ubushobozi bwo kumenya akamaro k’ifaranga mu kwemera kuri Exchange ku bicuruzwa n’ibikorwa, ku bijanye na Bitcoin, na bo nyene baragira ico bakoze cane ku guhitamwo amategeko y’uguhurizako. Umwe wese afise ububasha bumwe bumwe bujanye n’igikorwa c’ubutunzi ashobora kuzana kuri Fork. Mu badandaza, biragaragara ko hariho ubudandaji, ariko kandi hariho n’ama platforms ya Exchange, abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro, n’abakoresha.
