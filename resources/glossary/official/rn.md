---
term: UMUKOZI
---

Mu rwego rwa Liquid Sidechain, abayobozi ni ama node akoreshwa n’inzego zijejwe gucunga iyo sisitemu. Bafise mbere na mbere uruhara rubiri: gushinga amasezerano no gushitsa ibikorwa nk’abasinya ku mabarabara (*blocksigners*), hamwe no gucungera ama bitcoins afiswe n’urubuga kugira ngo habeho ugushikama kw’ibihugu bibiri (*watchmen*).
