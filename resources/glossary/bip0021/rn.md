---
term: BIP0021
---

Iciyumviro canditswe na Nils Schneider na Matt Corallo, gishingiye kuri BIP20 yanditswe na Luke Dashjr, iyo ubwayo ikaba yava mu yindi nyandiko yanditswe na Nils Schneider.

BIP21 isobanura ingene amaderesi y’ukwakira akwiye gushirwa mu ma URI (*Indangamuntu y’Itunga rimwe*) kugira ngo bishobore kwishura. Nk'akarorero, URI ya Bitcoin ikurikira BIP21 isaba 0.1 BTC munsi y'ikimenyetso "Pandul" yosa n'iyi:


```text
bitcoin:bc1qmp7emyf7un49eaz0nrxk9mdfrtn67v5y866fvs?amount=0.1&label=Pandul
```


Iryo tegeko rituma umuntu ashobora gukoresha neza amafaranga y’ibikorwa vya Bitcoin mu gutuma umuntu ashobora kwishura mu gufyonda gusa ku nzira canke mu gucapura kode ya QR. Muri iki gihe, ingingo ngenderwako ya BIP21 irakoreshwa cane muri porogarama ya Bitcoin Wallet.
