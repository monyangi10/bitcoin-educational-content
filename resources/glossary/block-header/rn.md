---
term: UMUTWE W'IBUBUKO
---

Umutwe w'ibarabara ni umubumbe w'amakuru ukora nk'igice nyamukuru c'ibarabara rya Bitcoin. Igipande cose gifise umutwe n’urutonde rw’ibintu bikoreshwa. Umutwe w'ibara urimwo amakuru ahambaye yemeza ubutungane n'ukuri kw'ibara muri Blockchain.

Umutwe w'ububiko ufise ubunini bwa bytes 80 kandi urimwo ibi bikurikira:

*Verisiyo y'ububiko;*


- Hash y’ibarabara ry’imbere;
- Umuzi wa Merkle Tree w’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji;
- Igipande ca Timestamp;
- Intumbero y’ingorane;

*Igitabu ca Nonce.*


Akarorero: Umutwe w’ibarabara 785.530 (ryacukuwe na Foundry USA ku wa 15 Ndamukiza 2023) mu buryo bw’ibice cumi na bitandatu:


```text
00e0ff3f5ffe3b0d9247dc437e18edc19252e4517cee941752d501000000000000000000206b
de3a10826e2acb2f28fba70463601c789293d0c9c4348d7a0d06711e97c0bcb13a64b2e00517
43f09a40
```


Gusenyuka kw'umutwe:

**Ivyo:**


```text
00e0ff3f
```


**Ivyo Hash vya kera:**


```text
5ffe3b0d9247dc437e18edc19252e4517cee941752d501000000000000000000
```


**Ivya Merkle Root:**


```text
206bde3a10826e2acb2f28fba70463601c789293d0c9c4348d7a0d06711e97c0
```


**Ivya Timestamp:**


```text
bcb13a64
```


**Intego:**


```text
b2e00517
```


**Ivya Nonce:**


```text
43f09a40
```


Kugira ngo igice kibe gifise akamaro, umutwe waco, iyo ushizwemwo kabiri hakoreshejwe `SHA256d`, utegerezwa gutanga Hash iri munsi canke ingana n’intumbero y’ingorane.
