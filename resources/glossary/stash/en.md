---
term: Stash
definition: In the RGB protocol, set of data stored client-side for one or more contracts, including history and proofs.
---

In the context of the RGB protocol, Stash refers to all customer-side data stored by a user for one or more contracts. This data includes transition history, logs and proofs of validity, which are essential for the customer-side validation process (*Client-side Validation*). Each holder retains only the relevant historical fragments (*shards*) needed to verify and update his or her contracts.
