---
term: Stash
---

Mu bijanye n’amasezerano ya RGB, Stash yerekeza ku makuru yose yo ku ruhande rw’umukiriya abitswe n’ukoresha ku masezerano imwe canke menshi. Aya makuru arimwo amateka y’ihinduka, ibitabo n’ibimenyamenya vy’ukuri, ari vyo bihambaye mu bijanye n’ugushingira intahe ku ruhande rw’umukiriya (*Client-side Validation*). Buri mufise amasezerano wese agumana gusa ibice vy’amateka bibereye (*shards*) bikenewe kugira ngo asuzume kandi ahindure amasezerano yiwe.
