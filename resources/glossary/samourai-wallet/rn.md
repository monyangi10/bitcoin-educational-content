---
term: SAMOURAI Wallet.
---

Bitcoin Porogarama ya Wallet y’ibikoresho vy’amatelefone ngendanwa vy’i Android yibanze ku buzima bwite. Itanga ibintu biteye imbere nk’ibice vy’amahera vya Whirlpool, ivyuma vy’amabuye, ivyuma vy’amabuyeX2, ivyuma vy’amabuye, n’ivyuma vy’amabuye (PayJoin). Samourai kandi ishira mu ngiro uburinzi bwinshi kugira ngo ifashe abakoresha kurinda ubuzima bwite bwabo ku bijanye n’ugusesangura uruhererekane.

> ► *Ku wa 24 Ndamukiza 2024, abo 2 bashinze Samourai Wallet barafashwe ataco bazira kubera kwandika kode gusa. Ndashaka kubaserura ko ndabashigikiye ata gukeka. Commitment yabo yo kurinda ubuzima bwite n'umwidegemvyo irimwo ingingo ngenderwako nyamukuru za Bitcoin. Abo baterambere nta caha bakoze; barakoze gusa kugira ngo batange ibikoresho bituma umuntu wese ashobora kwemeza uburenganzira bwiwe bw’akameremere. Urubanza rwabo ni rwo ruhambaye cane kuri Bitcoin no ku bidukikije vyayo, kandi cane cane ku mwidegemvyo mu bihugu vyacu vya demokarasi. Kugira ngo mbashigikire muri iyo ngorane, ndatumiye umuntu wese gutanga umusanzu wo kwiregura mu vy’amategeko. Ivyo mutanga bizotuma abo baterambere bakora buri musi kubera umwidegemvyo wacu bashobora kwikingira neza. Bazoshiraho kandi akarorero kazotuma abahinguzi b’ibintu batera ubwoba mu gihe kizoza, gutyo bakabuza ko ubuhinga bushasha bugenda buhoro ku Bitcoin no ku bidukikije vyayo. **Ugutanga: https://uburenganzira.org/**.*
