---
term: Bitcoin YESU
---

Izina ry’akataraboneka ry’umunyamigambi akaba n’umushoramari Roger Ver, uwa mbere mu guteza imbere Bitcoin mu ntango z’imyaka ya 2010. Yaronse iryo zina ry’akataraboneka kubera uruhara runini yagize mu gukwiragiza Bitcoin mu gihe yariko iratangura kwemerwa. Roger Ver kandi azwi cane kubera ugushigikira cane Bitcoin Cash (BCH) Hard Fork, abona ko ari yo idahemuka cane ku ciyumviro ca mbere ca Bitcoin.
