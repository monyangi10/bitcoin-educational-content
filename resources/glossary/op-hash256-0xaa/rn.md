---
term: OP_HASH256 (0XAA)

---

Ifata ikintu co hejuru mu kirundo ikagisubiriza hash yaco ikoresheje igikorwa ca `SHA256` incuro zibiri. Ivyo winjiza bifatwa rimwe na `SHA256`, hanyuma ivyashizwemwo bifatwa ubwa kabiri na `SHA256`. Ivyo bikoresho bifise intambwe zibiri bituma umuntu agira ikimenyetso c’urutoke c’ibice 256.
