---
term: Opcodes
definition: Amategeko y’ururimi rwa Script akoreshwa mu kwerekana amategeko yo gukoresha bitcoins.
---

Ivyiyumviro bikoreshwa muri sisitemu y’inyandiko ya Bitcoin. Script ni ururimi rwo gukora porogarama rushingiye ku rutonde rukoreshwa mu gushinga ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha amahera, kandi gutyo, mu buryo butaziguye gukingira bitcoins. Amabwirizwa akoreshwa mu rurimi rw'inyandiko yitwa "opcodes". Ivyo ni ibikorwa vy'ubwenge n'amabwirizwa yo gukoresha ibirundo. Aya mabwirizwa yihariye ashirwa mu ngiro n'ibihimba vy'urubuga iyo wongeyeko igikorwa kuri Blockchain. Script ni ururimi rwuzuye rutari rwa Turing. Ishobora gushirwa mu rwego rw’ururimi rwo hagati (hafi y’urugero rwo hasi) rwahumekewe na Forth.
