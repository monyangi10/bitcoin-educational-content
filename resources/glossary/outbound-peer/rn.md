---
term: URUGANZI RWO HANZE
---

Igikoresho ico igikoresho cawe ca Bitcoin gishiraho ubufatanye. Ku mburabuzi, urudodo rugerageza kwifatanya n'abagenzi 8 basohoka. Ivyo bihuza birafise agateka kandi bifatwa nk’ivyizigirwa kuruta ivy’urunganwe binjira, kuko bitorwa n’urudodo. Abagenzi binjira n’abasohoka barasangira ubwoko bumwe bw’amakuru. Itandukaniro rikomeye hagati y’urunganwe rwinjiye n’urwo gusohoka ntiri mu bwoko bw’amakuru bahanahana, ahubwo riri mu buryo ayo mahuriro ashingwa.


> ► *Impinduro y'icongereza y'ijambo "pair sortant" ni "umugenzi asohoka" canke "ihuriro risohoka".*
