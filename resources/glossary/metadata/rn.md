---
term: AMAKURU
---

Mu bijanye n’ubuhinga rusangi, ivyo vyerekeye amakuru atanga amakuru yerekeye ayandi makuru. Iradondora ibiranga, ibirimwo, uburyo, uburyo n’imiterere y’amakuru ajana na yo. Gutyo, haratandukanywa hagati y’umuzigo w’inyungu, ugereranya umushinge w’amakuru, n’amakuru y’imbere. Nk’akarorero, ku nyandiko, amakuru ashobora kubamwo umwanditsi, itariki yaremeweko, ubunini bwa dosiye, n’amajambo y’ingenzi ajanye. Muri Bitcoin, amakuru y'imbere aboneka muri Elements nyinshi. Ikoreshwa mu kwakira aderesi, imfunguruzo zagutse, amabuye, n'ibindi.
