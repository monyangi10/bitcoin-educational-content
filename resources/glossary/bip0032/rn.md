---
term: BIP0032
---

BIP32 yashizeho iciyumviro c’uburongozi bw’ubukuru Wallet (HD Wallet). Ico ciyumviro kiremesha uguhingura urutonde rw'ibice bibiri vy'ingenzi bivuye ku `master seed` rusangi, hakoreshejwe ibikorwa vy'ugukuraho inzira imwe. Buri rufunguzo rwavutse rushobora ubwarwo kuba umuvyeyi w'izindi mfunguruzo z'abana, zigahingura umubumbe umeze nk'igiti (w'ubukuru).

Inyungu nyamukuru ya BIP32 ni uko ituma abayikoresha bashobora gucunga amafunguro menshi mu gihe bakeneye gusa kubika seed imwe kugira ngo bayasubire gukora. Ivyo bishasha vyatumye Wallet ikoreshwa neza cane kandi ikagira umutekano, kandi vyafashije no kugabanya ugusubira gukoresha Address, ivyo bikaba bitera ingorane zikomeye z’ubuzima bwite ku bakoresha. Ikindi, BIP32 irashoboza kurema amashami menshi muri Wallet imwe, bikaba bituma habaho uguhinduranya imitunganirize n’uburongozi bwa Wallet.
