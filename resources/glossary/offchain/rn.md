---
term: off-chain
---

Yerekeza ku bikorwa canke ibikorwa bijanye na Bitcoin bibera hanze ya Blockchain nyamukuru ariko bifise uruja n’uruza canke Anchor na yo. Ntibica vyandikwa kuri Blockchain kandi bisaba uburyo bwo kwongerako kugira ngo bibe vyiza kandi bibe ivy’iherezo. Ivyo bikorwa akenshi bishingira intahe icipfuzo co guca kure y’imipaka y’ubuhinga iri muri Bitcoin, kugira ngo habeho amafaranga afise iherezo ryihuta, amafaranga make, ubushobozi bwongerekanye, canke ibikorwa vyinshi.
