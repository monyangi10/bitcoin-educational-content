---
term: UMUNSI
---

Igikoresho cakozwe na Lightning Labs gigenewe gukura amakuru mu nzira ya LND no kuyasesangura kugira ngo ifashe uwuyikoresha. Itanga impanuro zo gufunga imihora idakora neza kandi itanga amakuru arambuye ku nyifato y’inzira y’uruzitiro. Faraday afasha kumenya imirongo y’ijwi rito n’iyo ifise ibibazo vyo kuronka (igihe co gukora). Ico gikoresho kigamije gufasha abakoresha ama node mu gutanga umutungo wabo mu mihora yabo.
