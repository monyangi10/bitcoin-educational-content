---
term: BIP0093
---

Amakuru BIP atanga iciyumviro co kuzigama no kugarura seed ya Wallet y’ubukuru (bishingiye kuri BIP32) hakoreshejwe Ugusangira Ibanga rya Shamir.


Iryo tegeko risobanura uburyo bwa “codex32”, bwahumekewe na bech32, buzana urudodo rutunganijwe rugizwe na:


- intango,
- umurongo w'imbuga,
- ikimenyetso,
- urutonde rw'imigabane,
- umuzigo w'inyungu, na
- igiharuro c’igenzura (BCH).


Ubwo buryo butuma seed igabanywamwo imigabane igera kuri 31, muri iyo migabane hakaba hakenewe urugero rwasobanuwe (hagati ya 1 na 9) kugira ngo seed ishobore gusubirana neza.
