---
term: PRNG
definition: Uburyo bukora urukurikirane rw'inomero zenda kuba iz'amahirwe bishimikiye ku mbuto.
---

Insiguro ngufi y'"Igikoresho c'umubare w'ibinyoma". GNPAs ni umugwi w’ubuhinga bukoreshwa mu gukurikirana generate kw’imibare hafi y’imburakimazi, guhera ku rwego rw’intango rwitwa seed (seed). Mu gukora amakuru y’ibanga, GNPA ikoreshwa ku mfunguruzo za generate, ama vecteurs y’intango n’izindi Elements zisaba gukora ataco zivuze. GNPA nziza itegerezwa kuba ifise ibintu nk’uguhuza umusaruro, ukudashobora kumenya imbere y’igihe no kunanira ibitero vy’imbere y’igihe. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibikoresho vy’ukuri vy’imibare y’imburakimazi, GNPAs zishobora gushingwa kandi zishobora gusubirwamwo. Ku Bitcoin, GNPAs zishobora gukoreshwa kuri porogarama zo gucunga ibiharuro canke ku bikoresho, ku generate ijambo ryo gusubirana rishigikira ibiharuro vy’ibiharuro vy’ubuhinga n’ivy’ubukuru.
