---
term: Shards (rgb)
definition: Ishami ritandukanye mu gishushanyo ca DAG rikurikirana amateka y'ihinduka ry'amasezerano ya RGB.
---

Mu bijanye n’amasezerano ya RGB, Shard igereranya ishami ritandukanye mu gicapo c’ibara ry’umubiri (DAG) gikurikirana amateka y’Impinduka za Leta za Contract. Igize igice gihuye c'umugwi w'ihinduka, bihuye, nk'akarorero, n'urutonde rw'ibikorwa bisabwa kugira ngo umuntu yemeze ukuri kw'umutungo kanaka kuva Genesis. Ubwo buryo buratuma bishoboka gutandukanya ibice vyihariye vy’amateka yose, kugira ngo bishobore gusuzuma ku ruhande rw’umukiriya.
