---
term: BITCOIN CASH (BCH)
---

Cryptocurrency system resulting from a hard fork of Bitcoin (BTC) that occurred on August 1, 2017, at block 478,558. 
The fork arose from disagreements within the Bitcoin community over how to address the protocol’s scaling issues (the Blocksize War). 
While Bitcoin implemented SegWit (a soft fork) to achieve a modest, indirect increase in block capacity, Bitcoin Cash opted for a direct increase in block size, raising it from 1 MB to 8 MB.
The goal was to reduce transaction fees and improve confirmation times.
