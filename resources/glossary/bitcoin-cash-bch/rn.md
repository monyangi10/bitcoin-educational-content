---
term: Bitcoin Amafaranga (BCH)
---

Uburyo bw’amahera y’ibanga buva kuri Hard Fork ya Bitcoin (BTC) yabaye ku wa 1 Myandagaro 2017, ku nzu 478.558.

Fork yavyutse ku kutumvikana mu muryango wa Bitcoin ku buryo bwo Address ibibazo vy’ugupima amasezerano (intambara y’ubunini bw’amabarabara).

Mu gihe Bitcoin yashize mu ngiro SegWit (a Soft Fork) kugira ngo ishike ku kwiyongera gutoyi, kutaziguye kw’ubushobozi bw’amabuye, Bitcoin Cash yahisemwo kwongerera ubunini bw’amabuye mu buryo butaziguye, ikayiduza kuva kuri 1 MB gushika kuri 8 MB.

Intumbero yari iyo kugabanya amafaranga y’ugucuruza no gutuma ibihe vyo kwemeza bigenda neza.
