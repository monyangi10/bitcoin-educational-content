---
term: FIBRE

---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikirundi risobanura "*Internet yihuta Bitcoin Moteri y'Igihugu*". Ni umurongo washizweho na Matt Corallo mu 2016 kugira ngo wihutishe gukwiragiza amabuye ya Bitcoin kw’isi yose. Intumbero yayo yari iyo kugabanya igihe co gukwiragira hafi y’imipaka y’umubiri uko bishoboka kwose. FIBRE yari ifise intumbero yo gutuma amahirwe ya mining agabanywa mu buryo bubereye, mu kumenya neza ko igice c’amabuye yacukuwe n’uwuriko arakora kigaragaza neza uruhara rwayo mu bijanye n’ububasha bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none, ataco yitaye ku kibanza afise mu rubuga.


Nkako, uguteba mu gutanga amakuru mu bice bishobora gutuma imigwi minini ya mining ihuriye neza, akenshi ikaba iri hafi y’iyindi, bikaba bitera ingorane imigwi mitoyi. Ico kintu coshobora, mu gihe c’igihe, kwongerera ugushira hamwe kwa mining no kugabanya umutekano muri rusangi w’urwo rutonde. Kugira ngo ico kibazo gitorerwe umuti, FIBRE yarashizeho amakode yo gukosora amakosa no gutanga amakuru y’inyongera kugira ngo umuntu ashobore guhangana n’ugutakaza amapakete, hamwe n’ugukoresha amabuye akomeye asa n’ayo adondora muri BIP152, vyose bikoreshwa biciye ku UDP kugira ngo umuntu arenge imipaka imwe imwe ya TCP. Naho biri ukwo, FIBRE yarahebwe mu 2020, ahanini bivuye ku kuba yari ishingiye ku muntu umwe ayibungabunga be n’uko kwemera BIP152 vyatumye mwene iyo nzira idakenewe cane.
