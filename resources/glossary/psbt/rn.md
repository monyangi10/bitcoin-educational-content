---
term: PSBT
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera "Partially Signed Bitcoin Transaction". Ni ivyerekeye vyashizweho na BIP174 kugira ngo bihuze uburyo amafaranga atarangiriye yubatswe muri porogarama zijanye na Bitcoin, nka porogarama ya Wallet. PSBT ipfutse ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji aho ivyinjijwe bishobora kutashirwako umukono ku buryo bushitse. Harimwo amakuru yose akenewe kugira ngo uwuriko aragira uruhara ashobore gusinya ku bijanye n’ugucuruza ata makuru y’inyongera asaba. Gutyo, PSBT irashobora gufata uburyo 3 butandukanye:


- Igitabu c’ubudandaji cubatswe neza, ariko kitarashirwako umukono;
- Ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji vyashizweko umukono igice, aho ibintu bimwe bimwe vyinjijwe bishirwako umukono mu gihe ibindi bitarashirwako umukono;

*Canke igikorwa ca Bitcoin gishizweko umukono wuzuye, gishobora guhindurwa kugira ngo kibe giteguye gutangazwa ku rubuga.*


Uburyo bwa PSBT burafasha gukorana neza hagati ya porogarama zitandukanye za Wallet n’ibikoresho vy’umukono (Hardware Wallet). Ubu, verisiyo 0 ya PSBT irakoreshwa, nk'uko bisobanurwa muri BIP174, ariko verisiyo 2 iriko irashikirizwa biciye kuri BIP370.


> ► *Igitabu ca mbere ca PSBT ntaco kiriho. Kubera ko verisiyo 0 ari yo yari verisiyo ya mbere, verisiyo ya kabiri yitwa mu buryo butamenyekanye verisiyo 2. Ava Chow, uwanditse BIP370, rero yaciye afata ingingo yo guha inomero 2 iyo verisiyo nshasha kugira ngo ntihagire uwuyitera urujijo.*
