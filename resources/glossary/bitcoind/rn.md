---
term: bitcoind
definition: Bitcoin Daemon, iporogaramu yo muri komande yemerera gukoresha nodu ya Bitcoin no gukorana na RPC.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikigiriki "*Bitcoin daemon*".

Ni porogaramu y’umurongo w’amabwirizwa (itagira igishushanyo Interface) ishira mu ngiro umurongo wa Bitcoin, ishoboza abakoresha gukoresha urudodo rwuzuye rwa Bitcoin no gukorana na rwo biciye ku guhamagara kw’uburyo bwo kure (RPC) Interface.

Ikora nka daemon, bisobanura inzira y'inyuma abakoresha bashobora gukorana nayo biciye ku bikoresho vy'umurongo w'amabwirizwa nka bitcoin-cli.

`bitcoind` yari igice c’umukiriya wa mbere wa Satoshi Nakamoto. Bamwe babona ko ari umukiriya wa kabiri mu mateka ya Bitcoin, inyuma ya Satoshi ya mbere, kuko verisiyo 0.2.6 yashizeho ubushobozi bwo gukora nka daemon ata Interface y'igishushanyo. Mu nyuma yashizwe hamwe na Bitcoin QT mu 2011 yahinduwe izina "Bitcoin core" mu 2014. Ubu, `bitcoind` iri mu mukiriya wa Bitcoin core.
