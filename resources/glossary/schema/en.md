---
term: Schema
---

In the context of the RGB protocol, a Schema is a declarative piece of code that defines all the variables, rules and business logic (*Business Logic*) governing the operation of a contract. It specifies the structure of the state, including the initial configuration established during Genesis, and details the types of transitions allowed, as well as the strict conditions for their validation. Schema serves as a normative framework, ensuring that any evolution of the contract state conforms to the rules established by the issuer.
