---
term: CAHOOTS

---

Mu bijanye n’ivyo Samourai Wallet n’izindi porogarama za wallet ziyishira mu ngiro, Cahoot yerekeza ku bwoko bwose bw’ugucuruza bukorwa mu bufatanye hagati y’abakoresha benshi.

Kwifatanya na Cahoot bisigura gufatanya gukora igikorwa mu guhana amakuru y’ibikorwa yasinywe igice. Ivyo biganiro bishobora gukorwa n’amaboko, hakoreshejwe amakode ya QR, canke bikaba bihita biciye ku nzira y’itumanaho ya Soroban.


Ubwoko bw’amafaranga y’i Cahoot harimwo:


- Ivy’ugucuruza ibintu (Payjoin);

**Ibikorwa vy'amabuye x2;**

*Imigenderanire ya Joinbot.*
