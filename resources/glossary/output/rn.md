---
term: ISOHOKA
---

Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, umusaruro uri mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji yerekeza ku _Ibisohoka mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji bitakoreshejwe_ (UTXOs) biremwa nk’amahera y’aho umuntu ashobora kwishura. Mu buryo bubereye, ni uburyo igikorwa co gucuruza gikoresha mu gutanga amahera. Ibikorwa bifata UTXOs, ni ukuvuga ibice vy'ama bitcoins, nk'"ivyinjizwa" maze bikarema UTXOs nshasha nk'"ibisohoka". Ivyo bimenyetso bivuga umubare kanaka w’amahera y’ibiceri, akenshi ashirwa ku Address yihariye, hamwe n’ingene ayo mahera yoshobora gukoreshwa mu nyuma.


Uruhara rw’isoko rya Bitcoin rero ni ugukoresha UTXO nk’ibintu vyinjizwa, no kurema UTXO nshasha nk’ibintu bisohoka. Itandukaniro hagati y’ivyo bibiri rihuye n’amahera y’ugucuruza ashobora gusubirwamwo n’uwatsinze Miner y’ibarabara. UTXO ni, mu vy’ukuri, umusaruro w’isoko ry’imbere y’aho ritarakoreshwa. Ivyiza biva mu bikorwa rero ni uguhingura ama UTXO mashasha, na yo nyene azoshobora gukoreshwa nk’ibintu vyo kwinjiza mu bikorwa vyo gucuruza muri kazoza.


Uvuye mu buryo bwagutse, mu buhinga bwa mudasobwa, ijambo "igisohoka" muri rusangi ryerekeza ku makuru ava ku gikorwa, ku buhinga canke ku buryo. Nk'akarorero, iyo amakuru aciye mu gikorwa c'ubuhinga bwa Hash, ayo makuru yitwa "input", kandi igisubizo citwa "output".
