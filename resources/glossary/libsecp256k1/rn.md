---
term: LIBSECP256K1

definition: Isomero rya kriptografiya mu rurimi rwa C rikoreshwa mu gushira imikono n'ibindi bikorwa kuri secp256k1 ikoreshwa na Bitcoin.
---

Igikoresho gikomeye, gifise umutekano mwinshi c'ibitabo vy'imikono ya digitale n'ibindi bikoresho vya kera vy'ubuhinga bwa none ku `secp256k1` umurongo w'uruzitiro. Kubera ko iyo nzira itarigeze ikoreshwa cane hanze ya Bitcoin (itandukanye n’inzira `secp256r1` ikunda gukundwa cane), iri soko ry’ibitabu rigamije kuba ari ryo ry’ugukoresha ryinshi kuruta ayandi yose. Iterambere rya libsecp256k1 ryari rishingiye ahanini ku bikenewe na Bitcoin, kandi ibiranga bigenewe ibindi bikorwa bishobora kuba bigeragejwe canke bigenzurwa bike. Gukoresha neza iri soko ry’ibitabu bisaba kwitwararika cane, kugira ngo ribe ryiza ku ntumbero zidasanzwe z’ibikorwa bitari Bitcoin.


Ico kigo c'ibitabu libsecp256k1 gitanga ibintu bitandukanye, harimwo:




- ECDSA-secp256k1 umukono n’ugusuzuma, n’uguhingura urufunguzo rw’ibanga ;
- Ibikorwa vy'inyongezo n'ivy'ugukubita ku mfunguruzo z'ibanga n'iza bose ;
- Gukurikiranya no gusuzuma imfunguruzo z’ibanga, imfunguruzo za bose n’imikono ;
- Igihe gihoraho urufunguzo rwa bose gusinya n’uguhingura n’ugushika ku kwibuka guhoraho;
- Kandi n’ibindi vyinshi vy’intango vy’ubuhinga bwa none.
