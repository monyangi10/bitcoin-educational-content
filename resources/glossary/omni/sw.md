---
term: Omni
definition: Jukwaa la kuunda na kudhibiti mali kwenye blockchain ya Bitcoin, lililojulikana zamani kama Mastercoin.
---

Omni Layer (zamani Mastercoin) ni chanzo huria, jukwaa lililogatuliwa la kuunda na kudhibiti mali kwenye Blockchain Bitcoin. Mali za Omni ni pamoja na Tether (USDT) na MaidSafeCoin (MAID).
