---
term: Gukwira
definition: Uburyo bwo guhana ivunjisha n'ibice ku nkingi imwe ku yindi biciye ku rurere rwa Bitcoin.
---

Inzira amakuru, nk’ibikorwa n’amabuye, arungikwa kuva ku nzira imwe gushika ku yindi biciye ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin. Iyo umukoresha akoze igikorwa, kibanza kugenzurwa n’urudodo bahuriyeko. Inyuma y’ugushingira intahe, iyo nzira irarungikwa ku zindi nzira zihuye na yo, na zo zigaca ziyigenzura hanyuma zigakwiragizwa. Mu buryo bwihuse, igice kinini c’ibihimba vy’urubuga kizomenya ivy’ugucuruza. Iyo itanga amahera ahagije, abacukuzi bazoyishira mu gice cabo c’abashaka kwitoza. Iyo igice kirimwo iyo nzira y’ugucuruza cemejwe, kiraheza kiremezwa.
