---
term: Résistance au partitionnement
definition: Ubushobozi bw'umuryango wa Bitcoin bwo kuguma bwunze ubumwe naho hoba ukugerageza kuwucamwo ibice.
---

Ubushobozi bw’urubuga rwa Bitcoin bwo kuguma rumwe no kuguma rwumvikana hagati y’abarukoresha mu kuguma rufise amasano no kubuza gutandukanya uturongo tumwetumwe n’urubuga rwose, naho hoba hari ukugerageza kuruca ibice. Kugira ngo urudodo rugumye ruhurizako n’urubuga, rutegerezwa kuguma rufise n’imiburiburi ubucuti bumwe bukorana n’umugwi w’urunganwe rusangiye amategeko y’uguhurizako.
