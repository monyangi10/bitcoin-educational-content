---
term: Contract Rights
---

Uburenganzira bwa Contract buvuga uburenganzira butandukanye bushobora gukoreshwa n'abakinyi muri RGB Contract. Bica mu mice myinshi:




- uburenganzira bwa Ownership, bujanye n’uburenganzira bwa Ownership bwa UTXO kanaka (biciye kuri Seal Definition);
- Uburenganzira bw’ubutegetsi, ni ukuvuga ubushobozi bwo kwubaka Inzira y’Igihugu imwe canke nyinshi zihuye n’Itegeko rya Schema;
- Uburenganzira bwa bose, iyo Schema yemereye gukoresha ibintu bimwe bimwe vya bose, nk'akarorero kurema State Extension biciye mu gucungura Valency.
