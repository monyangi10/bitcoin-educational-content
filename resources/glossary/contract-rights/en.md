---
term: Contract Rights
---

Contract Rightss refer to the various rights that can be exercised by players in a RGB contract. They fall into several categories:


- Ownership rights, associated with the ownership of a particular UTXO (via a Seal Definition);
- Executive rights, i.e. the ability to build one or more Schema-compliant State Transitions;
- Public rights, when Schema authorizes certain public uses, for example the creation of a State Extension via the redemption of a Valency.
