---
term: DOS (KWANKA UBURYO)
---

Igitero co kuri internet kigamije gutuma umutungo (urubuga, node, service yo kuri internet...) udashobora gukoreshwa n’abawukoresha mu buryo bubereye. Abatera bararengeje urugero amakuru canke ibisabwa vyinshi cane ku muntu batera, ivyo bikaba bituma ubuhinga bwo gukoresha ubuhinga n’ubuhinga bw’urubuga bw’uwo batera buhungabana, bigatuma agenda buhoro canke agahagarara burundu. Uburyo bwa DoS burashobora guhinduka, ariko intumbero iguma ari imwe: kubuza umuntu kuronka ibikorwa canke amakuru. Mu bijanye n’ivya Bitcoin, igitero ca DoS gishobora kuba kigamije kwuzuza urubuga canke ama node n’ibintu vyinshi cane bisabwa kugira ngo bibuze gukora neza. Intumbero akenshi ni ugukomeretsa umukoresha w’uruzitiro canke ukuntu urubuga rushobora kuboneka ku bakoresha b’inzirabugunge.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, rishobora guhindurwa ngo "attaque de déni de service".*
