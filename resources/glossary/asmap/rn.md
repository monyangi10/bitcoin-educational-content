---
term: ASMAP
---

Igikoresho cavumbuwe na Gleb Naumenko kandi gikoreshwa na Bitcoin core kugira ngo yongere umutekano n’ubuhinga bw’urubuga rwa Bitcoin mu guhindura amasano hagati y’ibice. Ni ikarita ya IP Address ku mibare ya Sisitemu yigenga (ASN), ishobora gukwiragiza neza amahuzu asohoka ashingiye kuri ASN aho gushingira ku ntango za IP. Ivyo bifasha gukingira ibitero vya Eclipse (harimwo n’ibitero vya Erebus) mu gutuma bigorana cane ko uwutera kwigana amanode menshi.
