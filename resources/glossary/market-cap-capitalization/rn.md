---
term: Market cap
definition: Agaciro kose hamwe ka bitcoin zose ziri mu buzima, gaharurwa mu kugwiza igitigiri cazo n'igiciro.
---

Iserukira agaciro kose k'umutungo uriko urakoreshwa, nka Bitcoin. Iharurwa mu kugwiza umubare wose w’ibiceri biriko birakoreshwa n’igiciro c’ubu c’ikintu kimwekimwe cose. Uwo mubare uratanga ikimenyetso c’ubunini n’agaciro rusangi k’isoko rya Bitcoin.
