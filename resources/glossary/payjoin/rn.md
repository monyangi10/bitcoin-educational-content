---
term: PayJoin
---

Uburyo bwihariye bw’ugucuruza bwa Bitcoin bwongerera ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha mu gihe c’ugukoresha amahera mu gukorana n’uwuronka amahera. Ubudasa bwa PayJoin buri mu bushobozi bwayo bwo gukora generate igikorwa kimeze nk’igisanzwe iyo ukibonye ubwa mbere ariko mu vy’ukuri kikaba ari mini CoinJoin hagati y’abantu babiri. Ku bw’ivyo, imiterere y’ugucuruza ishiramwo uwuronka amahera mu vyo yinjiza iruhande y’uwurungika vy’ukuri. Gutyo, uwuronka ashiramwo amahera yishura hagati mu vyo akora bituma ashobora kwishurwa. Nk'akarorero, iyo uguze baguette ku `6.000 Sats` ukoresheje UTXO ya `10.000 Sats`, maze ugahitamwo PayJoin, umutetsi wawe azokwongerako UTXO ya `15.000 Sats` ari iyabo nk'inyungu, iyo bazosubiramwo nk'inyungu yuzuye mu,000. Sats`.


Iryo soko rya PayJoin rirangura intumbero zibiri. Ica mbere, kigamije kuzimiza uwubibona wo hanze mu kurema uruyeri mu gusesangura uruhererekane ku bijanye n’Ivyiyumviro rusangi Ownership Heuristic (CIOH). Kenshi, iyo igikorwa co gucuruza kuri Blockchain gifise ibintu vyinshi vyinjizwa, bifatwa ko ivyo bintu vyose bishobora kuba ari ivy’ikigo kimwe. Gutyo, iyo umuhinga mu vy’ugusesangura asuzuma ivy’ubudandaji bwa PayJoin, barajana mu kwemera ko ivyo vyose bishirwamwo biva ku muntu umwe. Ariko rero, ivyo vyiyumviro ntivyiza kuko uwuronka amahera na we nyene atanga umusanzu ku vyo yinjiza iruhande y’uwutanga amahera nyawe. Ica kabiri, PayJoin na yo nyene irahenda uwuyibona wo hanze ku bijanye n’ingero nyayo y’amahera yarishwe. Mu gusuzuma ingene iyo nzira y’ugucuruza iteye, uwo musesanguzi yoshobora kwizera ko iyo nkunga ingana n’ingero y’imwe mu nzira zisohoka. Mu vy’ukuri, amahera yo kwishura nta n’imwe mu nkuru zisohoka ihuye. Mu vy’ukuri ni itandukaniro riri hagati ya UTXO y’uwuronka mu vyo asohora n’ivyo uwuronka UTXO mu vyo yinjiza. Muri ivyo, igikorwa ca PayJoin kigwa mu rwego rw’ubuhinga bwa steganography. Bituma umuntu ashobora guhisha umubare nyawo w’ibintu bigurishwa mu gihe c’ibintu bigurishwa mu buryo bw’ikinyoma bikora nk’uruyeri.
