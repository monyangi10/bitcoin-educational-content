---
term: OP_CHECKSIG (0XAC)

definition: Opcode igenzura ko ikimenyetso ari co kishimikije urufunguruzo rusangi.
---

Igenzura ukuri kw'umukono ku rufunguzo rwa bose rwatanzwe. Ifata ibintu bibiri vyo hejuru mu rutonde: umukono n'urufunguzo rwa bose, maze igasuzuma nimba umukono ari wo ku hash y'ibikorwa n'urufunguzo rwa bose rwavuzwe. Iyo igenzura ryiza, risunika agaciro `1` (ukuri) ku kirundo, ahandi ho `0` (ikinyoma). Iyi opcode yahinduwe muri Tapscript kugira ngo isuzume imikono ya Schnorr.
