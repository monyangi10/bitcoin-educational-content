---
term: Izigigwa bigize uburyo bw'amafaranga
definition: Uburyo aho igice gito c'amahera yabitswe arico gicungwa mu nzu ibika amahera, ayandi agakoreshwa mu bindi.
---

Ingingo ngenderwako y’ivy’ubutunzi ya kera ivuga ko amabanki abika gusa agace k’amahera y’abaguzi bayo nk’ububiko buboneka, agakoresha ayasigaye mu gutanga inguzanyo n’inyungu za generate. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, naho amafaranga ubwayo adashobora gushirwaho n’inguzanyo za banki, iciyumviro c’ububiko bw’imice gikora iyo urubuga rwa Exchange rugumya gusa igice ca BTC yashizwemwo n’abarukoresha, rugakoresha ibindi mu yindi ntumbero. Ivyo bitera ingorane y’ugutakaza amahera n’ugutakaza amahera iyo abakoresha bose bafashe ingingo yo gukura amahera yabo icarimwe. Ivyo ni vyo cane cane bituma ari ngombwa ko umuntu aguma afise amahera yiwe yitwa bitcoins mu kwizigama.
