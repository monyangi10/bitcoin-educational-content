---
term: BIT

---

Ijambo “bit” ni ugucapura kw’ijambo “binaire” n’“umubare.” Mu vy’ubuhinga bw’ibarabara n’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru, bit ni co kintu nyamukuru c’amakuru y’ubuhinga bwa none kandi igereranya amakuru makeyi cane ashoboka. A bit ishobora gusa gufata agaciro kabiri gatandukanye: `0` canke `1`. Ivyo bipimo, bizwi nk'ibihe bibiri, bishobora guserukira ivyiyumviro bitandukanye nka `egome` canke `oya`, `ukuri` canke `ikinyoma`, na `ku` canke `kuzima`. Bits ni zo zigize umushinge w’ubuhinga bwa none kandi zikoreshwa mu kubika no gutanga amakuru muri mudasobwa no mu mihora. Izina “Bitcoin” rishobora kuba rikomoka ku gufatanya “Bit”, ryerekeza ku buryo ubuhinga bwo kwishura bukoreshwa mu buryo bw’ubuhinga bwa none, na “Coin”, ryerekeza ku gikorwa cayo c’amahera.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, ijambo "bit" rikoreshwa kenshi ataco rihinduye. Ijambo ry'icongereza ryoshobora guhindurwa ngo "umubare ubiri".*
