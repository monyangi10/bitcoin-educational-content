---
term: Invoice LIGHTNING
---

Lightning payment request generated by the recipient, containing all the information needed to complete the transaction.

A Invoice Lightning contains the payment destination in the form of the recipient node's public key, but also a `LN` prefix, the amount, a time to expiration, the hash of the secret used in HTLCs, and other metadata, some of which is optional, such as routing options. These invoices are defined by the BOLT11 standard. Once paid for, a Invoice Lightning cannot be reused.

> ► *In French, we could translate "Invoice" as "facture", but we generally use the English term even in French.*
