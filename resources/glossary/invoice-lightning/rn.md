---
term: Invoice UMURABO
---

Igisabwa co kwishura nk’umuravyo cavuzwe n’uwuronka, kirimwo amakuru yose akenewe kugira ngo umuntu arangize igikorwa.


Invoice Lightning irimwo aho kwishura mu buryo bw’urufunguzo rwa bose rw’uruzitiro rwakira, ariko kandi n’intango `LN`, umubare, igihe co guhera, Hash y’ibanga rikoreshwa muri HTLCs, n’ibindi bimenyetso, bimwe muri vyo bikaba ari ivy’uguhitamwo, nk’amahitamwo y’inzira. Izo fagitire zisobanurwa n’ingingo ya BOLT11. Iyo umuntu amaze kwishura, umuravyo wa Invoice ntushobora gusubira gukoreshwa.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, twoshobora guhindura "Invoice" ngo "facture", ariko muri rusangi dukoresha ijambo ry'icongereza mbere no mu gifaransa.*
