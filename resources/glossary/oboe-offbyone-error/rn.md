---
term: OBOE (IKOSA-KURI-RImwe)
---

Ikosa ry'ubwenge aho uruzitiro rusubiramwo incuro imwe nyinshi cane canke incuro imwe nke cane, kenshi bitewe n'ugukoresha nabi abakozi bo kugereranya canke index zitari zo mu gucunga imiterere y'amakuru. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, iki gikoko kiboneka mu gihe ca "*ikintu c'ikinyoma*" muri `OP_CHECKMULTISIG`, aho ikintu c'inyongera gikoreshwa mu makosa.


> ► *Mu congereza, iri jambo rishobora guhindurwa ngo "ikosa ry'uguhagarika-ku-rimwe".*
