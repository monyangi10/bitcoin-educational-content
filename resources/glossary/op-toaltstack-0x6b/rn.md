---
term: OP_TOALTSTACK (0X6B)

definition: Opcode yimura ikintu kiri k’inyuma ku mupfundikizo mukuru ikagishyira mu mupfundikizo w’inyongera.
---

Ifata hejuru y'ikirundo nyamukuru (*ikirundo nyamukuru*) ikayijana ku kirundo gisubirira (*ikirundo gishasha*). Iyi opcode ikoreshwa mu kubika amakuru ku ruhande kugira ngo azokoreshwe mu nyuma mu nyandiko. Ico kintu cimuriwe rero kirakurwa mu kirundo nyamukuru. `OP_FROMALTSTACK` izoca ikoreshwa mu kuyisubiza hejuru y'ikirundo nyamukuru.
