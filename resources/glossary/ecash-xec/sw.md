---
term: Ecash (xec)
definition: Sarafu ya kripto inayotokana na hard fork ya Bitcoin Cash mnamo Novemba 2020.
---

Mfumo wa Cryptocurrency, ambao hapo awali ulijulikana kama Bitcoin Cash ABC (BCHA), unaotokana na Hard Fork ya Bitcoin Cash (BCH). ECash Fork ilitokea mnamo Novemba 15, 2020, kwenye block 661,647, iliyotokana na mzozo ndani ya jumuiya ya Bitcoin Cash.
