---
term: Ecash (xec)

definition: Cryptocurrency yavuye ku ihinduka (hard fork) rya Bitcoin Cash muri Munyonyo 2020.
---

Uburyo bw’amahera y’amahera, mbere bwari buzwi kw’izina rya Bitcoin Cash ABC (BCHA), buva ku fork ikomeye y’amahera ya Bitcoin (BCH). Ico gitero ca eCash fork cabaye kw’igenekerezo rya 15 Ugushyingo 2020, ku nzu 661.647, kivuye ku ntambara yo mu muryango wa Bitcoin Cash.
