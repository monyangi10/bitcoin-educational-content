---
term: Dust
definition: Ingere z'ama-bitcoins ntoya cane ku buryo kuzikoresha bitahenda bitashoboka kubera amahera y'ivunjisha.
---

Yerekeza ku mahera make cane ya Bitcoin ari make cane ku buryo adashobora kwoherezwa mu gucuruza, kuko amahera y’ugucuruza asabwa kugira ngo ashirwe mu gice c’ububiko yoba ari menshi cane kuruta agaciro kayo. Insobanuro nyayo ya "Dust" irashobora guhinduka bivanye n'aho ivuga, ariko muri rusangi yerekeza ku gisubizo cose c'ubudandaji gisaba amahera menshi kugira ngo ukoreshe kuruta uko bibereye. Ku bakoresha Bitcoin, birahambaye ko bacungera UTXO zabo no kwimenyereza kuzishiramwo kugira ngo ntizibe Dust.
