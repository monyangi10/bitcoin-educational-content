---
term: BDK (Bitcoin Dev Kit)
---

Igikoresho co gutegura porogarama (SDK) y’amasakoshi ya Bitcoin. BDK ni ihuriro ry’amasomero n’ibikoresho vyagenewe abahinguzi kugira ngo borohereze uguhingura ibikorwa vya Bitcoin Wallet. BDK itanga ibice vy’ibikorwa vy’ingenzi nk’uburongozi bwa Wallet, kwubaka amasezerano, gusinyana amasezerano, n’uburongozi bw’ingenzi. Abahinguzi barashobora rero kwubaka kuri ivyo bice kugira ngo bashobore guhingura porogarama zabo bwite. Ibice vya BDK vyateguwe bishimikiye cane ku kuyoroshe no ku guhinduka, bikaba bituma bishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo bwinshi. Intumbero y’iki gikoresho ni ugushira hamwe iterambere rya Bitcoin Wallet kugira ngo utwigoro twibande.


> ► *BDK mbere yari izwi kw'izina rya "Bitcoin y'amageza".*
