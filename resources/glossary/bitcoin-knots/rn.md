---
term: Bitcoin Knots
---

Ishirwa mu ngiro ry’amasezerano ya Bitcoin n’abantu bake. Bitcoin Knots ni ubundi buryo bwo gukoresha porogarama y’ishingiro ya Bitcoin core, itanga amategeko makeyi atandukanye n’ibintu bitandukanye, mu gihe iguma ihuye neza n’izindi nzira ziri ku rubuga.

Amapfundo ategurwa kandi akabungabungwa na Luke Dashjr.
