---
term: BIP
definition: Bitcoin Improvement Proposal. Uburyo buzwi bwemerera umuryango gushikiriza, kuyaga no kwandika ivyiza bishasha ku mategeko ya Bitcoin.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'"Iciyumviro co guteza imbere Bitcoin." Iciyumviro co guteza imbere Bitcoin (BIP) ni uburyo butegekanijwe bwo gutanga no kwandika ivyo gutegura no guhindura amasezerano ya Bitcoin n’ingingo zayo. Kubera ko Bitcoin ata bubasha buhambaye ifise bwo gufata ingingo ku bijanye n’ivyo guhindura, BIPs ireka abarundi bagatanga ivyiyumviro, baganira, bagashira mu ngiro ivyo guhindura mu buryo butunganye kandi buboneye.


Buri BIP iradondora neza intumbero z’ivyo bizotuma habaho iterambere, imvo n’imvano, ingaruka zishobora kugira ku bijanye n’uguhuza, hamwe n’ivyiza n’ibibi. BIPs ishobora kwandikwa n’umuntu wese wo mu muryango, ariko itegerezwa kwemezwa n’abandi bategura n’abahinduzi bacungera urutonde rw’amakuru rwa Bitcoin core: Bryan Bishop, Jon Atack, Luke Dashjr, Mark Erhardt (Murch), Olaoluwa Osuntokun, na Ruben Somsen. Uruhara rwabo rwo kwandika ntirusobanura ko ari bo bagenzura Bitcoin ubwayo. Naho igitekerezo coba kitemewe biciye mu nzira ya BIP, gishobora gushikirizwa umuryango wa Bitcoin canke kigashirwa mu ngiro mu nzira itandukanye ya Fork.

Uburyo bwa BIP buratanga uburyo busanzwe n’inzira y’ugushingirako kugira ngo bishobore gutuma habaho impaka no kugabanya uguca ibice hagati y’abakoresha Bitcoin, bigamije gushirwa mu ngiro ivyagezwe bishasha biciye mu kwumvikana. Mu mpera, ubutunzi bwinshi ni bwo bugena ububasha n’ingingo zifatwa mu masezerano.


BIPs zishizwe mu mice itatu nyamukuru:

**Ivyiyumviro vy’Ikurikirana BIPs**: Ivyerekeye impinduka zigira ingaruka zitaziguye ku gushirwa mu ngiro kwa Bitcoin, nk’amategeko agenga ivy’ugucuruza n’ugushingira intahe;

**Ivyerekeye amakuru BIPs**: Gutanga amakuru canke impanuro ata guhindura ataco bimaze mu masezerano;

**Imigenderanire ya BIPs**: Gusobanura amahinduka mu migenderanire ikikuje Bitcoin, nk’imigenderanire y’ubutegetsi.


Ivyagezwe vy'Ikurikirana n'Amakuru BIPs navyo nyene bishirwa mu rwego rwa "Layer":

**Isezerano Layer**: Ivyiyumviro bihindura amategeko y’ihuriro ya Bitcoin (nk’akarorero, amategeko y’uguhagarika canke kwemeza ibikorwa). Ivyo bishobora gushirwa mu ngiro nk’amaforogo ya Soft (amahinduka ahuye n’inyuma) canke amaforogo ya Hard (amahinduka adahuye n’inyuma). SegWit na Taproot BIPs zigwa muri uwo mugwi.

**Ibikorwa vy’urunganwe**: Ivyiyumviro bijanye n’ingene ama node abona kandi akavugana n’ayandi biciye ku rubuga.

**API/RPC**: Impinduka ku bikoresho vyo gukora porogarama (APIs) no ku guhamagara ubuhinga bwo kure (RPCs) bikoreshwa mu gukorana n’ibindi bikoresho.

**Ibikorwa Layer**: Ivyagezwe vy’ibikorwa vyubatswe hejuru ya Bitcoin, nk’imiterere ya Wallet.


Gutanga BIP bitangura no gutanga iciyumviro no kuganira ku ciyumviro kiri ku rutonde rw’abarungika ubutumwa rwa Bitcoin-dev. Niba ari umuhango, umwanditsi arategura BIP akurikije uburyo busanzwe maze akayirungika nk’Igisabwa co Gukurura ku bubiko bwa GitHub. Abanditsi barasubiramwo ico ciyumviro kugira ngo bamenye nimba gihuye n’ingingo zose. BIP itegerezwa kuba ishoboka mu buryo bw’ubuhinga, ifise akamaro ku bijanye n’amategeko, ihuye n’imiterere isabwa, kandi ikaba ihuye n’ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin. Iyo BIP ihuye n'ivyo bisabwa, ishirwa mu bubiko bwa GitHub bwa BIPs. Hanyuma igahabwa umubare. Uwo mubare muri rusangi ufatwa n’umwanditsi, akenshi Luke Dashjr, kandi ushirwaho mu buryo bubereye: BIPs ivuga ku bintu bisa n’ivyo akenshi ironka imibare ikurikirana.


BIPs rero zica mu bihe bitandukanye mu kiringo c’ubuzima bwazo. Ivyo ubu biriko biragaragazwa mu mutwe wa buri BIP:

**Umugambi:** Ico ciyumviro kiracari mu rwego rwo gutegura no guhanahana ivyiyumviro;

**Ivyiyumviro:** BIP ifatwa ko yuzuye kandi yiteguriye gusubirwamwo n’abanyagihugu;

**Ivyasubitswe:** BIP irahagarikwa kugira ngo izosohoke mu nyuma n’umwanditsi canke umunyamakuru;

**Ivyanse:** BIP ifatwa nk’iyanse iyo imaze imyaka 3 ataco ikora. Umwanditsi wayo ashobora guhitamwo kuyisubiramwo mu nyuma, ivyo bikaba vyotuma isubira mu rwego rw’umugambi;

**Ivyakuweho:** Igitabo ca BIP cakuweho n’umwanditsi waco;

**Iherezo:** BIP yemewe kandi irashirwa mu ngiro cane kuri Bitcoin;

**Ikora:** Ku bijanye n’igikorwa ca BIPs gusa, iki kibanza gihabwa iyo hashitse ku ntumbero imwe imwe;

**Yasubirijwe / Yaciye:** BIP ntikigikora canke yasubirijwe n’iciyumviro gishasha gituma idakenewe.
