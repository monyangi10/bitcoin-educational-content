---
term: BIP0151
definition: Uburyo bwa guhisha itumatumanako rya P2P hagati y'ama peers, ubu bwasubirijwe na BIP324.
---

Itanga umurongo wo gupfuka amakuru ya P2P hagati y’urunganwe ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, kugira ngo haboneke umutekano n’ubuzima bwite. Intumbero yayo nyamukuru yari iyo gukingira abantu gukoresha nabi imiduga be n’ibitero vy’ugucungera abantu benshi. Amaherezo, BIP151 yasubiriwe na BIP324 (*P2P gutwara V2*), ubu ikaba ikoreshwa mu buryo bw'ubuhinga muri Bitcoin core.
