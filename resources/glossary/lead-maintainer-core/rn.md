---
term: Lead maintainer (core)
definition: Icapu rya kera ryo kurongora umushinga wa Bitcoin Core, ryakuweho muri Ruhuhuma 2023.
---

Uwurongoye ubuzima yari afise uruhara mu burongozi bwa Bitcoin core. Uwo murimo ntaco ukiriho kuva muri Ruhuhuma 2023. Uwo muntu yari afise inshingano yo kurongora uwo mugambi, rero yari afise ububasha kuruta abawubungabunga. Uruhara rwo kubungabunga uburongozi rwari rwarafashwe na Satoshi Nakamoto gushika aho agenda mu ntango z’umwaka w’2011. Hanyuma, Gavin Andresen, yari amaze gutanga umusanzu iruhande ya Satoshi, ni we yatwaye uburongozi bw’iyo porogarama gushika mu ntango z’umwaka w’2014. Gashyantare 2023. Kuva ico gihe, nta n’umwe arongoye ivyo gucungera uwo mugambi.
