---
term: BLOCK REWARD
---

The block reward is the total number of bitcoins received by a miner when they successfully mine a valid block. 
It consists of two components: 
- Block subsidy: a fixed amount of bitcoins newly created by the miner, which decreases over time through scheduled halving events.
- Transaction fees: an amount of bitcoin newly created by the miner and limited to the amout of cumulative fees paid by users for the transactions to be included in the block. A transaction fee represent the difference between the transaction’s total inputs and total outputs. Therefore, the amount of newly created bitcoin correspond to the total bitcoins destroyed as fees in the transactions of the block.

The block reward is issued through a special transaction called the coinbase transaction. Bitcoins received from a coinbase transaction are subject to a maturity period of 100 blocks before they can be spent.

> ► *The French translation is "Récompense de bloc".*
