---
term: Block reward
---

Block reward ni umubare wose w’ama bitcoins yaronswa na Miner iyo ashoboye gucukura neza igice gifise akamaro.

Gigizwe n’ibice bibiri:


- Block subsidy: umubare udahinduka w’ama bitcoins aherutse kuremwa na Miner, agabanuka uko igihe kigenda kirarenga biciye mu bikorwa vya Halving vyategekanijwe.
- Amafaranga y’ibikorwa: umubare wa Bitcoin uherutse kuremwa na Miner kandi ugarukira ku mubare w’amafaranga y’urugero yishurwa n’abakoresha ku bikorwa vyo gucuruza bizoshirwa mu gice. Amafaranga y’ugucuruza agereranya itandukaniro hagati y’ibintu vyose vyinjijwe mu gucuruza n’ivyo vyose bisohoka. Ku bw’ivyo, umubare w’amahera Bitcoin aherutse kuremwa uhuye n’amahera yose ya bitcoins yasambutse nk’amahera mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji vy’ibarabara.


Block reward isohorwa biciye ku nzira idasanzwe yitwa Coinbase Transaction. Bitcoins zaronswa na Coinbase Transaction zishobora gukoreshwa igihe co gukura c’ama blocks 100 imbere y’uko zishobora gukoreshwa.


> ► *Impinduro y'igifaransa ni "Récompense de bloc".*
