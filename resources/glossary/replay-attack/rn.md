---
term: GUSUBIRAMWO IGITERO
---

Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, igitero co gusubiramwo kiba iyo igikorwa gifise akamaro kuri Blockchain imwe gisubiwemwo mu buryo bubi ku kindi Blockchain gifise amateka y’igikorwa nk’ayo. Mu yandi majambo, igikorwa co gutangaza amakuru ku muhora umwe gishobora gusubirwamwo ku wundi ata kwemera kw’uwarungitse igikorwa ca mbere.


Reka dufate akarorero k'ivyiyumviro vya Hard Fork biva kuri Bitcoin, vyitwa "*BitcoinBis*". Iyo utanga amahera mu bitcoins kugira ngo ugure baguette ku mutetsi w’imikate kuri Blockchain Bitcoin nyayo, uwo mutetsi nyene yoshobora gusubiramwo iyo nzira yemewe kuri *BitcoinBis* kugira ngo uronke ayo mahera nyene mu mafaranga y’amahera kuri iyi Fork, ata gikorwa na kimwe ku ruhande rwawe.


Ubwo bwoko bw’igitero bushobora gushika gusa iyo Blockchain igira amashami afise iminyororo 2 yigenga igumaho mu gihe kirekire. Mu bisanzwe, ivyo ni ko vyoba kuri Hard Fork. Kugira ngo igitero co gusubiramwo gishoboke, izo 2 blockchains zitegerezwa kuba zifise amateka amwe, kandi igikorwa co gusubiramwo gitegerezwa kunywa nk’inputs UTXOs zaremwe zivuye mu bikorwa vya kera vyabaye imbere y’uko iyo minyororo ibiri igabanywa, canke zivuye mu bikorwa vya kera vyabaye ubwavyo vyari vyaramaze gusubirwamwo mu gitero co gusubiramwo ca kera.


Muri rusangi, mu vy’ubuhinga bwa none, igitero co gusubiramwo gishingiye ku gufata no gusubira gukoresha amakuru akwiriye kugira ngo umuntu abeshe ubuhinga mu gusubiramwo amakuru y’intango. Ivyo bishobora rimwe na rimwe gutuma umuntu yiba akaranga kawe ku rubuga.


> ► *Mu gihe c'igitero co gusubiramwo ku giciro ca Bitcoin, ivyo rimwe na rimwe vyitwa gusa "ugusubiramwo igicuruzwa. "*
