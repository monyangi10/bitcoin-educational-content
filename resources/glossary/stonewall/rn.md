---
term: Stonewall
definition: Transaction ya Bitcoin itari iy'ubufatanye yigana coinjoin kugira yongere ibanga mu gihe co gukoresha.
---

Uburyo bwihariye bw’ugucuruza Bitcoin bugamije kwongerera ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha mu gihe c’ugukoresha amahera mu kwigana CoinJoin hagati y’abantu babiri, ataco mu vy’ukuri ari umwe. Nkako, iyo nzira y’ugucuruza si iyo gukorana. Uwukoresha arashobora kuvyubaka wenyene, akoresheje gusa UTXO ziwe nk’ibintu vyinjizwa. Rero, urashobora gukora igikorwa ca Stonewall ku musi mukuru uwo ari wo wose, udakeneye gukorana n’uwundi mukozi.


Ibikorwa vy’ugucuruza Stonewall ni ibi: ku nzira y’ugucuruza, uwurungitse akoresha UTXO 2 ziri izabo. Iryo soko rero ritanga ibisohoka 4, 2 muri vyo bikaba bingana neza na neza. Abandi 2 bazoba ari bo bazogira ihinduka. Mu bisohoka 2 vy’amahera amwe, kimwe gusa ni co kizoja mu vy’ukuri ku wuzoronka amahera.


Gutyo, hariho gusa uruhara 2 mu gucuruza Stonewall:


- Uwurungitse, ni we ariha vy’ukuri;

*Uwuronka, ashobora kuba atazi uburyo bwihariye bw'ugucuruza, akaba arindiriye gusa ko uwumurungitse amwishura.*




Ivy’ugucuruza vy’uruhome rw’amabuye biraboneka kuri porogarama ya Samourai Wallet no kuri porogarama ya Sparrow wallet.


Igishushanyo ca Stonewall congera entropie nyinshi ku bikorwa kandi kigapfuka inzira y’isesengura ry’uruzitiro. Uvuye hanze, mwene ukwo gucuruza gushobora gusobanurwamwo ko ari CoinJoin ntoyi hagati y’abantu babiri. Ariko mu vy’ukuri, nk’uko nyene vyagenze ku bijanye n’ugucuruza Stonewall x2, ni ukwishura. Ubwo buryo rero buratuma habaho ukudakeka mu gusuzuma uruhererekane, canke mbere bukajana ku nzira z’ibinyoma. Naho umuntu wo hanze yoshobora kumenya ingene ivyo bicuruzwa vya Stonewall vyagenda, ntibazogira amakuru yose. Ntibazoshobora kumenya uwuhe muri izo UTXO zibiri z’amahera amwe ahuye n’ivyo kwishura. Ikindi kandi, ntibazoshobora kumenya nimba izo UTXO zibiri ziri ku nzira y’injiza zikomoka ku bantu babiri batandukanye canke nimba ari iz’umuntu umwe yazihuje. Iyi ngingo ya nyuma iterwa n’uko amafaranga y’i Stonewall x2 akurikira neza na neza uburyo bumwe n’ubw’amafaranga y’i Stonewall. Kuva hanze kandi ata makuru y’inyongera yerekeye ivy’imbere, ntibishoboka gutandukanya igikorwa ca Stonewall n’igikorwa ca Stonewall x2. Ariko rero, ivya mbere si ibikorwa vy’ugukorana, mu gihe ivya nyuma ari vyo. Ivyo birongereza mbere n’ugukekeranya ku bijanye n’ayo mahera akoreshwa.
