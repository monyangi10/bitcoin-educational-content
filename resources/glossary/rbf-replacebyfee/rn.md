---
term: RBF (Replace-by-fee)
---

Uburyo bwo gukorana butuma uwurungitse ashobora gusubirira igikorwa kimwe n’ikindi mu kwishura amahera menshi, kugira ngo vyihute kwemezwa. Iyo igikorwa gifise amafaranga make cane gifatanye, uwurungitse arashobora gukoresha *Replace-by-fee* kugira ngo yongere amafaranga yongere ashire imbere igikorwa cabo co gusubirira mu ma mempools.


RBF irakoreshwa igihe cose igikorwa kiri mu mempools; iyo iri mu bubiko, ntishobora gusubira gusubirwamwo. Ku ntumbero yo kohereza, igikorwa gitegerezwa kugaragaza ukuboneka kwaco kugira ngo gisubirizwe mu guhindura agaciro ka `nSequence` ku mubare uri munsi ya `0xfffffffe`. Ivyo bizwi nka "ibendera" rya RBF. Ivyo bigaragaza ko bishoboka ko umuntu ashobora guhindura amafaranga amaze gutangazwa, ivyo bikaba bituma habaho RBF. Ariko rero, rimwe na rimwe birashoboka gusubirira igikorwa kitagira ikimenyetso ca RBF. Ivyuma bikoresha umurongo w'imiterere `mempoolfullrbf=1` vyemera uku gusubirizwa naho RBF yoba itagaragajwe mu ntango.


Udakunze CPFP (*Umwana ariha umuvyeyi*), aho uwuronka ashobora gukora kugira ngo yihutishe amafaranga, RBF (*Replace-by-fee*) iremesha uwurungitse gufata ingingo yo kwihutisha amafaranga yiwe bwite mu kwongera amafaranga.
