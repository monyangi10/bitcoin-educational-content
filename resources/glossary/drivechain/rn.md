---
term: UMUGORORO
---

Uburyo bwihariye bwa Sidechain aho abacukuzi b'amabuye y'agaciro bo muri Blockchain (Bitcoin) bafise uruhara rudasanzwe mu gutwara umurongo w'inzira zibiri no mu buryo bwo kwumvikana bwa Sidechain. Iryo tegeko ryavumbuwe na Paul Sztorc kandi ryoshobora gushirwa mu ngiro biciye ku nzira y’impaka BIP300, yoshobora gutuma habaho ubucuti bw’inzira zibiri n’abacukuzi, na BIP301, yoshobora gutuma hakoreshwa neza Mining ihuriweko.
