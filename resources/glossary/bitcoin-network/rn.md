---
term: Umuhora wa Bitcoin
definition: Ibikorwa remezo vy'ama-nodu ajanye akoresha porotokolo ya Bitcoin kugira ngo akwirakwize kandi yemeze ivunjisha n'ibice (blocks).
---

Yerekeza ku bikorwa remezo vyo kw’isi yose vy’ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin. Iryo shirahamwe rigizwe n’ibihimba vyose (ordinateri) bikoresha porogarama zishira mu ngiro umurongo wa Bitcoin kandi zigafatanya. Buri nzira ivugana n’izindi nzira kugira ngo: ishobore gukuraho no kugenzura Blockchain, kwemeza no gutangaza amabuye mashasha, no kwemeza no gutangaza amafaranga mashasha.
