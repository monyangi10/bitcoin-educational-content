---
term: GUHINDURA AMAFARANGA
---

Porotokole yo gutanga Ownership mu mpisho hagati y’abakoresha. Ubu buryo bufise intumbero yo gukura ubutunzi bw’ama bitcoins ku muntu umwe bukabujana ku wundi, n’ibihushanye n’ivyo, ata Exchange iboneka neza kuri Blockchain. Coinwap ikoresha amasezerano y’ubwenge kugira ngo ikore iyo nzira ata kwizigirana hagati y’ababiri.


Reka twiyumvire akarorero k'ubujuju (katakora) na Alice na Bob. Alice ifise BTC 1 icungiwe n’urufunguzo rw’ibanga $A$, na Bob nayo ifise BTC 1 icungiwe n’urufunguzo rw’ibanga $B$. Mu vy’impwemu, boshobora Exchange imfunguruzo zabo z’ibanga biciye ku nzira y’itumanaho yo hanze kugira ngo bakore igikorwa co gutanga amakuru mu mpisho. Ariko rero, ubwo buryo bw’ubujuju burazana ingorane nyinshi mu bijanye n’ukwizigirana. Ntaco kibuza Alice kubika kopi y’urufunguzo rw’ibanga rwa $A$ inyuma ya Exchange maze akarukoresha mu nyuma kugira ngo yibe ama bitcoins, urufunguzo rumaze kuba mu minwe ya Bob. Ikindi, nta cizere ko Alice atazoronka urufunguzo rw'ibanga rwa Bob $B$ kandi ntazokwigera atanga urufunguzo rwiwe rw'ibanga $A$ muri Exchange. Iyi Exchange rero yizigiye ukwizigirana gukaze hagati y'ababuranyi, kandi ntaco imaze mu gutuma Ownership irungikwa mu buryo butekanye kandi mu mpisho.


Kugira ngo dutorere umuti ivyo bibazo no gutuma habaho uguhinduranya amafaranga hagati y'abatemeranya, tuzokoresha ubuhinga bwa Smart contract butuma Exchange iba "atome". Ivyo bishobora kuba HTLC (*Amasezerano y’Igihe Hash*) canke PTLC (*Amasezerano y’Igihe c’Intonde*). Izo porotokole zibiri zikora mu buryo busa n’ubwo, zikoresha uburyo bwo gufunga umwanya butuma Exchange iheza neza canke igakurwaho burundu, gutyo bikaba birinda ubutungane bw’amahera y’impande zompi. Itandukaniro rikomeye hagati ya HTLC na PTLC ni uko HTLC ikoresha hashes n’amashusho y’imbere kugira ngo ikingire ivy’ugucuruza, mu gihe PTLC ikoresha Adapter Signatures.


Mu gihe co guhindura amafaranga hakoreshejwe HTLC canke PTLC hagati ya Alice na Bob, Exchange iba mu buryo butekanye: canke iraroranirwa maze umwe wese akaronka BTC y’uwundi, canke irananirwa maze umwe wese akaguma afise BTC yiwe. Ivyo bituma uwundi wese adashobora guhenda canke kwiba BTC y’uwundi.


Ikoreshwa ry'Imikono y'Ibimenyetso riraryoshe cane muri iki gihe, kuko rituma bishoboka kureka inyandiko za kera (uburyo rimwe na rimwe bwitwa "_inyandiko zitagira inyandiko_"). Ivyo bigabanya amahera ajanye n’ivyo Exchange ikoresha. Ikindi ciza gikomeye c’Imikono y’Ibikoresho ni uko idasaba gukoresha Hash imwe ku mpande zompi ziriko ziragurisha, gutyo bikaba bitarinda guhishura isano ry’ukuri hagati yabo mu bwoko bumwebumwe bwa Exchange.
