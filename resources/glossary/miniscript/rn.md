---
term: Miniscript
definition: Uburyo bworohereza ukurema, ukusuzuma no kwemeza ibisomwa (scripts) vya Bitcoin.
---

Inzego zagenewe gutanga inzego zo gukora porogarama z'inyandiko zitekanye kuri Bitcoin. Ururimi kavukire rwa Bitcoin rwitwa inyandiko. Biragoye cane gukoresha mu bikorwa, cane cane ku bikorwa bikomeye kandi bihuye n’ivyo umuntu ashaka. Ikiruta vyose, biragoye cane kugenzura aho inyandiko igarukira. Miniscript ikoresha igice c’inyandiko za Bitcoin kugira ngo yorohe kurema, gusuzuma no kugenzura. Inyandiko yose y’ikirundi ingana na 1 kuri 1 n’inyandiko y’ikirundi. Ururimi rw’amategeko rukoreshwa neza, ruca rushirwa mu nyandiko ntoyi, kugira ngo amaherezo rujane n’inyandiko y’ikirundi.





Miniscript rero iremesha abayikora kwubaka inyandiko zikomeye mu buryo butekanye kandi bwizigirwa. Ivyiza vy’ingenzi vya Miniscript ni ibi bikurikira:


- Iremeza isesengura ry’inyandiko ridahinduka, harimwo n’ingene ryemerera gukoresha amahera be n’igiciro caryo mu bijanye n’ubutunzi;
- Bishoboza kurema inyandiko zijanye n’ivyo abantu bose bahuriyeko;
- Bituma habaho isesengura ry’uko inzira zitandukanye zo gukoresha amahera zihuye canke zidahuye n’amategeko agenga ivy’ibihimba;

*Ishiraho urugero rusangi, rutahurwa kandi rushobora gukorana, ku maporogarama yose ya Wallet n'ibikoresho vyose.*


Umugambi wa Miniscript watangujwe mu mwaka w’2018 na Petero Wuille, Andrew Poelstra, na Sanket Kanjalkar, biciye mu kigo citwa Blockstream. Miniscript yongewe kuri Bitcoin core Wallet mu buryo bwo kuraba gusa mu kwezi kwa kigarama 2022 na verisiyo 24.0, hanyuma yuzuye muri Rusama 2023 na verisiyo 25.0.
