---
term: CHAMPION (BIP)
---

In the context of Bitcoin Core development (the majority implementation of Bitcoin), the Champion is the person who writes and manages a BIP (Bitcoin enhancement proposal). The Champion is responsible for writing the BIP following the formal process, defending it to the community, answering questions and guiding discussions to refine the proposal.
