---
term: GUKONYERA MU KWIBIZA
---

Uburyo bwo gukonjesha bukoreshwa muri Mining, aho ASICs zibizwa muri Liquid idashobora gutwara umuriro kugira ngo zishiremwo ubushuhe generate. Kwibiza mu mazi biratanga ivyiza vyinshi kuruta gukonjesha umuyaga. Ica mbere, nta nkeka ko ukoresha ama ventilateur, ivyo bikaba bigabanya inguvu zikoreshwa. Vyongeye, amashini arakingirwa na Dust, ivyo bikaba bituma akora neza kandi akamara igihe kirekire. Kwibika mu mazi na vyo nyene bisigura gukuraho ubushuhe neza, kuko ibihimba bikorana ata guca ku ruhande n’amazi akonjesha.
