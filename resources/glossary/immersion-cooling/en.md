---
term: IMMERSION COOLING
---

A cooling technique used in mining, where ASICs are immersed in a non-conductive liquid to dissipate the heat they generate. Immersion offers several advantages over air cooling. Firstly, there's no need to use fans, which reduces energy consumption. Also, machines are protected from dust, which improves their performance and longevity. Immersion also means better heat dissipation, as components are in direct contact with the cooling fluid.
