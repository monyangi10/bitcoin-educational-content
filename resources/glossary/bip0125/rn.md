---
term: BIP0125
definition: Uburyo bwa Replace-by-Fee (RBF) bwemera gusubiriza itunshwa ritaremezwa n'irindi ririko amahera menshi.
---

Isobanura iciyumviro ca *Replace-by-fee* (RBF), iremesha uwurungitse gusubirira igikorwa kitaremejwe n’iyindi verisiyo irimwo amafaranga menshi y’ugucuruza.

Ishiraho kandi urutonde rwo gutanga ikimenyetso ca RBF mu gucuruza no kwemerwa n’ibihimba vy’uruja n’uruza.
