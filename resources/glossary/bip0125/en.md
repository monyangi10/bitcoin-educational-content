---
term: BIP0125
definition: Replace-by-Fee (RBF) standard allowing an unconfirmed transaction to be replaced by a version with higher fees.
---

Defines the concept of *Replace-by-Fee* (RBF), allowing the sender to replace an unconfirmed transaction with another version that includes higher transaction fees. 
It also establishes a framework for signaling RBF in a transaction and for its acceptance by network nodes.
