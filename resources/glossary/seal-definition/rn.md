---
term: Seal definition
definition: Muri RGB, igice gihuza ukwemera na UTXO kugira ngo hashingwe nyen'itunga.
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, Seal Definition ni igice ca Assignment gihuza mu buryo butomoye Commitment na UTXO ifiswe na nyen’iyo nshasha. Igaragaza aho Leta iri mu kugaragaza UTXO irimwo, gutyo igashinga Ownership y’umutungo canke uburenganzira kuri RGB.
