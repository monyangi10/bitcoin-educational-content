---
term: Seal definition
definition: In RGB, component linking a commitment to an UTXO to establish ownership of an asset.
---

In the RGB protocol, the Seal Definition is the component of a Assignment that explicitly links a Commitment to a UTXO held by the new owner. It specifies the location of the state by identifying the UTXO in which it is held, thus formally establishing ownership of an asset or right on RGB.
