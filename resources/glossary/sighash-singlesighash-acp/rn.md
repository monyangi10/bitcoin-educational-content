---
term: SIGHASH_SINGLE/SIGHASH_ACP

---

Ubwoko bw'Ibendera rya SigHash (`0x83`) rifatanijwe n'umuhinduzi w'ibara ry'umubiri (`SIGHASH_ACP`) rikoreshwa mu mikono y'ibikorwa vya Bitcoin. Iryo huriro rigaragaza ko umukono ukoreshwa gusa ku nkuru imwe yihariye kandi gusa ku gisohoka gifise urutonde rumwe n'urwo rutonde. Ibindi vyinjizwa n’ibisohoka birashobora kwongerwa canke guhindurwa n’abandi bantu. Iyi ntunganyo ni ngirakamaro ku bikorwa vy’ubufatanye aho abaje mu nama bashobora kwongerako ivyo bashiramwo kugira ngo bashobore gutanga amahera y’umusaruro wihariye.
