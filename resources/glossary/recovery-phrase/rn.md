---
term: Recovery phrase
definition: Urutonde rw'amajambo 12 canke 24 yemerera kuzigama no kugarukana i wallet ya Bitcoin.
---

Invugo yo gusubirana, na yo rimwe na rimwe yitwa invugo ya Mnemonic, invugo ya seed, canke invugo y’ibanga, ni urutonde rusanzwe rugizwe n’amajambo 12 canke 24, ruvugwa mu buryo bw’ikinyoma buva ku isoko y’uburemere bw’ibintu. Urutonde rw'ibinyoma rwama rwuzuye n'umubare w'igenzura. Invugo Mnemonic, hamwe n'iyindi passphrase, ikoreshwa mu gukuraho imfunguruzo zose zijanye n'i HD (Ikigereranyo c'ubukuru) Wallet. Ivyo bisigura ko kuva muri iri jambo, bishoboka ko umuntu ashobora gufata generate mu buryo bw’ubuhinga no gusubira kurema imfunguruzo zose z’ibanga n’iza bose za Bitcoin Wallet, maze bigatuma umuntu ashobora kuronka amahera ajanye na yo. Intumbero y’ijambo ry’ugusubirana ni ugutanga uburyo bwo gusubiza inyuma no gusubiza amafaranga y’ama bitcoins atekanye kandi yoroshe gukoresha.


Ni ngombwa ko iri jambo riguma ahantu hatagira umutekano, kuko umuntu wese afise Mnemonic yoshobora kuronka amahera y’iyo Wallet ihuye. Iyo ikoreshejwe mu bijanye n’igitabu ca kera ca Wallet, kandi ata gitabu ca passphrase co guhitamwo, akenshi iba ari SPOF (Single Point Of Failure). Iryo jambo ry’ugusubirana rero ni ugushiramwo urutonde rw’ibintu bitari vyo be n’urutonde rw’ibintu bitari vyo mu majambo ya misi yose kugira ngo abantu bashobore kuyandika no kuyasoma. Yubatswe hakurikijwe ingingo ngenderwako ya BIP39, isobanura kandi igategeka urutonde rw’amajambo 2048 akoreshwa muri iyo nzira yo gushiramwo amakuru.


> ► *Urutonde rw'amajambo 2048 yo muri BIP39 ruraboneka mu ndimi nyinshi, ariko rero, birakenewe ko ukoresha ururimi rw'icongereza gusa, kuko ari rwo rushigikirwa cane na porogarama ya Wallet.*
