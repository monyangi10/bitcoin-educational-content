---
term: State Extension
---

Mu bijanye n’amasezerano ya RGB, State Extension ni igikorwa c’amasezerano gikoreshwa mu gutanguza guhindura uko Contract imeze biciye mu gucungura ama Valencies yari yatangajwe mbere. Ubu buryo bukoresha uburenganzira bwa bose bwanditswe muri Contract kugira ngo buvyure ihinduka, ritangura gukora iyo rimaze gupfungwa na State Transition. Ivyo vya nyuma birimwo n’ivugurura ry’ukuri rya Contract State.
