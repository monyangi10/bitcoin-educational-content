---
term: Liquid network
definition: Sidechain yafederatwe ya Bitcoin itanga ihererekanya ry'amafaranga ryihuta kandi ryiherereye.
---

Bitcoin Sidechain yateguwe na Blockstream kugira ngo itange ivyihuta na Confidential Transactions. Udakunze Blockchain nyamukuru ya Bitcoin, Liquid ikoresha uburyo bwo kwumvikana bushingiye ku gisata c’ubumwe (umugwi watoranijwe w’abakoresha ama node, akenshi ubucuruzi bufitaniye isano na Bitcoin), gutyo igasubirira uburyo bwo kwumvikana bwa Nakamoto. Ubwo buryo buratuma amafaranga yihuta cane kandi bukagabanya n’ibiciro, mu gihe butanga ibintu vyiza cane. Liquid kandi iremeza gutanga amafaranga y’ubuhinga bwa none, harimwo n’ibimenyetso bigereranya ayandi mafaranga y’ubuhinga bwa none. Bitcoins kuri Liquid, izwi nka L-BTC, iboshe na BTC biciye mu nzira zibiri zishingiye ku gice c’ubumwe. Abaje muri iri shirahamwe bitwa "abakozi," kandi bashobora gukora nk'"watchmen" n'"abasinya amasezerano."
