---
term: Bitcoin Inzahabu (BTG)
---

Bitcoin Inzahabu (BTG) ni amafaranga y'ibanga yaremwe na Hard Fork ya Bitcoin (BTC) ku wa 24 Gitugutu 2017, ku 491.407.

Fork yatangujwe n’umugwi w’abahinguzi n’abashoramari batanezerewe n’inzira Bitcoin yariko irafata, cane cane ukuntu ububasha bwa Mining bwariko buragwira mu maboko y’amatongo manini manini makeyi akoresha ASIC.

Bitcoin Gold yari ifise intumbero yo gutuma uburyo bwa Mining bugira demokarasi mu kwemera ubuhinga bushasha bwa Proof-of-Work bwitwa Equihash bushobora guhangana na ASICs rero ikunda Mining ifise amakarita y’ibishushanyo (GPU).

Intumbero yari iyo gutuma uruhara rwa Mining rushikira abantu benshi, bikongera ukwegereza ubutegetsi abaturage no kugabanya ingorane zo kwegereza ubutegetsi ubutegetsi hagati. BTG mu ntango yakoresheje ubuhinga bwa Mining nka Zcash (ZEC) imbere y’uko ibuhindura gatoyi kugira ngo ikore verisiyo yayo bwite ya Equihash.


> ► *Iyumvire, inzahabu ya Altcoin Bitcoin (BTG) ntikwiye kuvyivangako n’inzahabu y’ibice, iciyumviro ca Nick Szabo.*
