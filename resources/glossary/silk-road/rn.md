---
term: SILK ROAD

---

Silk Road yari urubuga rw’umwiza rwashinzwe na Ross Ulbricht, kandi azwi kw’izina ry’uruyeri ry’umunyagihugu w’umunyagihugu. Iryo soko ryo kuri Internet ryatangujwe mu 2011, ryatuma abakoresha bashobora kugura no kugurisha ibintu n’ibikorwa, vyemewe n’amategeko canke bitemewe n’amategeko, mu gihe baguma bazigama amazina yabo biciye mu gukoresha uburyo bwo kwishura Tor na Bitcoin. Silk Road yatanga ibintu vyinshi, ahanini ibiyayuramutwe n’imiti yo kwa muganga, ariko ntiyashiramwo ibikorwa bibonwa ko biteye kubiri n’ingingo ngenderwako z’amategeko kavukire, nk’ugucuruza ibikorwa vy’abantu bica abantu canke ibintu vyerekana amasanamu y’ibiterasoni y’abana.


Iryo koraniro ryari rigaragaza ivyiyumviro vya Ross Ulbricht vy’abanyagihugu, ritera imbere isoko ry’ubuntu kandi ritagira amategeko, gutyo rigahunga ubugenzuzi bwa Leta n’imisoro. Silk Road yarafise uruhara runini mu kwemera kwa mbere Bitcoin mu ntango z’imyaka ya 2010, bituma imenyekana kandi ikoreshwa birenze imigwi ya cypherpunk.


Silk Road yafunzwe mu mwaka w’2013.Ross Ulbricht yafashwe muri uwo mwaka nyene, acirirwa ibihano bibiri vy’ubuzima bwiwe bwose yongerako imyaka 40, ata n’ubushobozi bwo kurekurwa ataco akora, hamwe n’ihazabu y’amadolari imiliyoni 183. Ico gihano kiravyura ibibazo ku bijanye n’ingene ibihano vy’ubugizi bwa nabi bitarimwo ubukazi biri kuri Internet bingana. Ross aracari mw’ibohero n’ubu.
