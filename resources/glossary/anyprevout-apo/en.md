---
term: Anyprevout (apo)
definition: A proposal (BIP118) adding new SigHash flags allowing the creation of signatures that do not cover any specific input of the transaction.
---

Name given to BIP118, which proposes adding two new SigHash flag modifiers, named `SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT` and `SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUTANYSCRIPT`. The term "*AnyPrevOut*" comes from the contraction of "*Any Previous Output*".
