---
term: Imfuvyi
definition: Ibloc ikomeye itagumijwe mu muryango mukuru canke itaramenyekana umuvyeyi wayo.
---

Mu vy’impwemu, Orphan block yerekeza ku gice gifise akamaro cakiriwe n’urudodo rutararonka igice c’umuvyeyi, ni ukuvuga ica mbere mu ruhererekane. Naho ari ngirakamaro, iyo block iguma iri ukwayo mu karere nk’impfuvyi.


Ariko rero, mu gukoresha abantu benshi, ijambo "Orphan block" akenshi ryerekeza ku gice kitagira umwana: igice gifise akamaro, ariko kitagumye mu ruhererekane nyamukuru rwa Bitcoin. Ivyo bishika iyo abacukuzi babiri babonye ibuye ry’agaciro riri ku burebure bw’uruzitiro rumwe mu kiringo gitoyi maze bakaritangaza ku rubuga. Amaherezo ama Nodes ahitamwo igice kimwe gusa co gushiramwo mu ruhererekane, ashingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako y'uruhererekane rufise ibikorwa vyinshi vyirundanijwe, bigatuma uwundi "impfuvyi."





> ► *Ku bwanje, ndakunda gukoresha ijambo "Orphan block" mu kuvuga ivy'ibarabara ritagira umuvyeyi n'ijambo "stale block" kugira ngo mvuge ivy'ibarabara ritagira umwana. Ivyo ndabibona bihuye n’ubwenge kandi bitahurwa, naho benshi mu bakoresha ama bitcoin badakurikiza iyo nkoresha.*
