---
term: Batched spending
definition: Uburyo buhuriza hamwe amariha menshi ku baronswa batandukanye mu gikorwa kimwe ca Bitcoin kugira ngo amande agabanuke.
---

Gukoresha amafaranga mu buryo bw’imigwi ni ubuhinga bukoreshwa cane cane n’ibigo bifise amafaranga menshi, nk’ibibanza vya Exchange, kugira ngo bishobore gutuma amafaranga y’amafaranga akoreshwa neza kandi agabanywa. Mu gufatanya amahera menshi yishurwa abayihabwa batandukanye mu gikorwa kimwe ca Bitcoin, gukoresha amahera mu buryo bw’imigwi bikoresha umwanya mutoyi w’amabarabara, gutyo bikagabanya amahera ajanye n’ivyo. Amafaranga akoreshwa mu migwi arashobora kumenyekana bitagoranye mu gihe c’isesengura ry’uruhererekane. Uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera mu buryo bw’imigwi burangwa n’umubare mutoyi w’ama UTXO y’injiza (kenshi imwe gusa) n’ama UTXO menshi y’isohoka. Kwihweza iyo nzira mu gihe c’isesengura vyerekana ko inyungu UTXO ishobora kuba ari iy’ikigo gifise ibikorwa vy’ubutunzi bikomeye, kandi ko UTXO zisohoka zizokwiragira nk’uko bibereye. Ivyiza bimwe bimwe bizoba ivy’abaguzi b’ishirahamwe, ibindi bishobora kuja mu mashirahamwe afatanije, kandi mu bisanzwe n’imiburiburi ivyiza bimwe bizoba bigereranya ihinduka rigaruka ku kigo catanguriyeko.
