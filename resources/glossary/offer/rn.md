---
term: Offer
definition: Ibiranga QR bidahinduka bishobora gukoreshwa kenshi mu kuronka amahera menshi kuri Lightning (BOLT12).
---

Muri BOLT12, *amashimwe* ni amakode ya QR adahinduka yo kwishura kenshi kuri Lightning Network. Udakunze amafagitire asanzwe, *amashimwe* arashobora gusubira gukoreshwa. Bishobora gukoreshwa ku generate ibisabwa vyinshi vya Invoice. Iyo umuntu akoresheje scanner ya QR code irimwo *offer*, yohereza ubutumwa busaba Invoice nshasha ku nzira ya Lightning ijana. Ico gice gisubiza n’ikete Invoice uwuriha ashobora gukoresha. Ivyo *bitanga* rero bitanga ikimenyetso kidasanzwe co kwakira amahera menshi avuye ku bakoresha batandukanye kuri Lightning.
