---
term: Dandelion
definition: Uburyo bw'ibanga buhisha inkomoko y'ivunjisha biciye mu gice co gukwiragira mu giti hanyuma mu rurabo.
---

Iciyumviro kigamije gutuma ubuzima bwite bw’inzira y’amafaranga bugenda neza mu rubuga rwa Bitcoin kugira ngo harwanywe ugukuraho amazina y’abantu. Mu bikorwa bisanzwe vya Bitcoin, amafaranga ahita atangazwa ku bice vyinshi. Ico kintu gishobora gutuma abarorerezi bashobora guhuza amafaranga, mu bisanzwe atamenyekana, n’amaderesi ya IP. Intumbero ya BIP156 ni ugutuma Address iki kibazo. Kugira ngo ivyo bishoboke, rizana ikindi gice mu bijanye n’ugutangaza amakuru kugira ngo umuntu ntamenyekane imbere y’uko bikwiragizwa ku bantu bose. Gutyo, Dandelion ibanza gukoresha igice ca "stem" aho igikorwa coherezwa biciye mu nzira y'ibihimba, imbere y'uko kimenyeshwa ku rubuga rwose mu gice ca "fluff". Uruti n’ubwoya ni ibimenyetso vyerekana inyifato y’ugukwiragira kw’isoko biciye ku rubuga, bisa n’imiterere y’igiti citwa dandelion. Ubwo buryo bwo gutanga inzira burazibira inzira ijana ku nzira y’inkomoko, bikaba bigoye gukurikirana igikorwa biciye ku rubuga rwo gusubira aho cavuye.
