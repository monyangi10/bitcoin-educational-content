---
term: SPOF
definition: Igice kimwe c'uburyo iyo kigize ikibazo bituma uburyo bwose buhagarara canke bugakora nabi cane.
---

Igiharuro kimwe co kunanirwa (SPOF) mu bijanye n’ubuhinga bw’ibarabara kivuga igice canke ikintu co muri sisitemu iyo kinaniwe cotuma sisitemu yose ihagarara burundu canke igatakaza cane ubushobozi bwo gukora. Ivyo bishobora kuba ari igice c’ibikoresho, amakuru, porogarama canke igice c’urubuga. Nk'akarorero, mu bijanye n'amasakoshi ya HD Bitcoin, ijambo ry'ugusubirana ry'amajambo 12 canke 24 akenshi rikora nk'i SPOF ya Wallet. Iyo ibanga ryayo ritagumyeho, Wallet yose yoshobora kwibwa. Na vyo nyene, ukubura kwayo kworoheje kwoshobora gutuma ugutakaza ama bitcoins yose ari muri Wallet.
