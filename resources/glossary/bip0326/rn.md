---
term: BIP0326
---

Iciyumviro co gutera imbere ku bategura porogarama za Bitcoin Wallet zifasha mu bikorwa vya Taproot.

Intumbero yayo nyamukuru ni ugutuma ubuzima bwite bw’amasezerano ya kabiri ya Layer akoresha PTLCs (*Amasezerano y’Igihe c’Igice*), nka CoinSwap, Lightning Network, canke DLCs (*Amasezerano y’Inyandiko y’Igihe*).

Ico ciyumviro kirazana ukwihakana gushobora kwemerwa mu guhindura ubwavyo `nSequence` field y'ibikorwa vya Taproot nk'uko `nLocktime` field yashizweho mbere mu bindi bikorwa kugira ngo ntihagire ibitero vy'ugutera amafaranga.

Mu yandi majambo, BIP326 iremesha porogarama ya Wallet gukoresha umwanya wa `nSequence` aho gukoresha `nLocktime` ku ntumbero zo kurwanya amafaranga, gutyo igatuma ubuzima bwite butera imbere ku masezerano yose atari mu ruhererekane akoresha uwo mwanya mu buryo busa n’ubwo.

Nk’inyishu, igikorwa ca Taproot gifise agaciro kadasanzwe mu kibanza ca `nSequence` coshobora guserukira igikorwa gisanzwe ca Wallet canke igikorwa ca kabiri c’ugutanga amahera ca Layer gifise igihe co gufunga, bikaba bituma ivyo bibiri bidashobora gutandukanywa.

Iyo iki ciyumviro co gutera imbere cemerwa cane n’abahinguzi ba porogarama za Bitcoin Wallet, vyotuma ubuzima bwite n’ubushobozi bwa Bitcoin muri rusangi butera imbere cane.
