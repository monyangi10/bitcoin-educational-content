---
term: ATLC
definition: Anchor Timelock Contracts. Ikinini cyerekabyage gikoreshwa mu nkuru ya Ark, kijwanya na HTLC ku Lightning.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikigiriki "*Amasezerano y'Igihe ca Anchor*". Ni ukwishyura kw'ivyangombwa gukoreshwa mu masezerano ya Ark kugira ngo ushireho urutonde rw'ukwishyura kw'atome ku nzira, biciye ku bihuza bikora ico bita "txlock". Intumbero ya ATLC ni imwe cane n'iya HTLC kuri Lightning Network.
