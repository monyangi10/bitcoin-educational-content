---
term: GNU
---

Umugambi watangujwe mu mwaka w’1983 na Richard Stallman wo guhingura ubuhinga bwo gukoresha ku buntu, bujanye n’ubuhinga bwa Unix. Uwo mugambi warateguye porogarama nyinshi z’ubuntu zishobora gukoreshwa nk’uburyo bwo gukoresha canke zikongerako ubundi buryo bwo gukoresha. GNU ni yo ishimikiyeko umuhari w’ubuhinga bwa none, wemerwa cane mu muryango wa Bitcoin. Iyo ifatanijwe n’umurongo wa Linux, igira ubuhinga bwo gukoresha GNU/Linux.


> ► *Izina "GNU" ni ijambo ry'inyongera risobanura "GNU's Not Unix," risobanura "GNU si Unix."*
