---
term: BIP0386
definition: Imirimo ya tr() yo gusobanura ibisohoka bya Taproot mu ma-descriptors.
---

Itangaza ibikorwa vya Descriptor vya Taproot. Isobanura ibikorwa `tr(KEY)` na `tr(KEY, TREE)` vyo kurondera ibisohoka vya Taproot, aho `KEY` ari urufunguzo rw'imbere na `TREE` ari igiti c'inzira y'inyandiko y'ubusa. BIP386 yashizwe mu ngiro muri Bitcoin core verisiyo 22.0.
