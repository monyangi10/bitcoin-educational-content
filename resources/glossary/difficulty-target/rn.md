---
term: Icipimo c'ingorane
definition: Iharuro rya bit 256 ryerekana ingere ntarengwa yo kwemererwa hashing y'imitwe y'ibice.
---

Ikibazo c'ingorane, kizwi kandi nk'intumbero y'ingorane, ni ikigereranyo gikoreshwa mu buryo bwo kwumvikana na Proof of Work (Proof of Work, PoW) kuri Bitcoin. Ico ciyumviro kigereranya agaciro k’imibare kagena ingorane abacukuzi bashobora gutorera umuti ingorane yihariye y’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru, yitwa Proof of Work, igihe barema igice gishasha kuri Blockchain.


Intumbero y'ingorane ni umubare ushobora guhindurwa w'ibice 256 (64 bytes) ugena umupaka wo kwemerwa ku bijanye n'ugushiramwo imitwe y'amabuye. Mu yandi majambo, kugira ngo igice kibe gifise akamaro, Hash y’umutwe waco ufise `SHA256d` (ibiri `SHA256`) itegerezwa kuba iri hasi canke ingana n’intumbero y’ingorane mu mibare. Proof of Work igizwe no guhindura `Nonce` field y'umutwe w'ibarabara canke Coinbase Transaction gushika Hash ivuyemwo iri hasi y'agaciro k'intumbero.


Iyi ntumbero ihindurwa buri 2016 (nk'indwi zibiri), mu gihe c'ikintu citwa "uguhindura." Ico kibazo c’ingorane kirasubirwamwo hakurikijwe igihe vyatwaye kugira ngo umuntu acukure amabuye y’agaciro yo mu 2016 aheze. Iyo igihe cose kiri munsi y’indwi zibiri, ingorane irarushirizaho mu guhindura intumbero ikaja hasi. Iyo igihe cose kirenze indwi zibiri, ingorane iragabanuka mu guhindura intumbero ikaja hejuru. Intumbero ni ugukomeza umwanya wa Mining w’iminota 10 ku bubiko. Ico gihe kiri hagati y’ibarabara rimwe rimwe kirafasha mu gukingira amacakubiri y’uruja n’uruza rwa Bitcoin, ivyo bikaba bituma haba ugutakaza ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none. Intumbero y'ingorane iboneka mu mutwe w'ibarabara ryose, mu murima wa `nBits`. Kubera ko uwo mwanya ugabanywa ukagera ku bice 32 kandi mu vy’ukuri ikintu kigamijwe kikaba ari ibice 256, ico kintu kirashirwa mu buryo butari bwiza cane mu buryo bwa siyansi.





> ► *Ikibazo c'ingorane rimwe na rimwe citwa kandi "ikibazo c'ingorane." Mu kwagura, rishobora kwerekezwa n'ugushiramwo amakuru mu mitwe y'ibice n'ijambo "nBits."*
