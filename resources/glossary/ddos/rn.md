---
term: DDOS
---

Uburyo bwa DOS aho igitero kiva ahantu henshi icarimwe, bigatuma ukwikingira bigorana cane. Abatera kenshi bakoresha imirongo y’amaordinateri yandujwe n’imigera (botnets) kugira ngo batanguze gusaba vyinshi ku muntu umwe. Iryo tegeko rituma igitero kigira akamaro kanini mu gutuma ubushobozi bw’urwego rwo gutera burengerwa kugira ngo buhagarike ibikorwa.


> ► *Mu congereza, rishobora guhindurwa ngo "Igitero co kwanka ibikorwa".*
