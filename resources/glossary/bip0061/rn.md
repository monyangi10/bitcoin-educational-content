---
term: BIP0061
---

Yashizeho ubutumwa bwo kwanka mu nzira y’uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’ibice. Intumbero yayo yari iyo gutanga uburyo bwo gutanga inyishu kugira ngo iyo node yaronse igikorwa canke ububiko ibona ko ata co imaze, ishobore kumenyesha uwuyirungitse imvo y’uko yanse.

Ubwo bwoko bw’uguhanahana amakuru bwari bugamije gutuma habaho ugukorana neza hagati y’abaguzi batandukanye be n’uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’ibice vyuzuye n’abaguzi ba SPV.

Ivyo bikoresho vyazanywe na BIP61 vyaciye biheba guhera kuri verisiyo 0.20.0 ya Bitcoin core, kuko vyabonwa ko bidakenewe mu gihe vyari birimwo ivy’ugukenera uburebure bw’uruja n’uruza.
