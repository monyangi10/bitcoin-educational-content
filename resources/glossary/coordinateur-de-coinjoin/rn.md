---
term: Umuhuzabikorwa wa Coinjoin
definition: Imeza ihuza coinjoins itashobora guhura inyinjira n'inyasohoka z'abakoresha.
---

Serveri nyamukuru ihuza abakoresha bipfuza gukora coinjoins kandi igahuza inyubako y’ibikorwa vy’ubufatanye, ataco ishobora gufatanya ivyo yinjiza n’ivyo umukoresha wese asohoka. Ivyo bishoboka biciye ku gukoresha imikono y’impumyi ya Chaum be n’urubuga rwa Tor. Udakunze umuhuza wa Bitcoin, umuhuzabikorwa wa CoinJoin nta na rimwe afata amafaranga y’abaje mu nama kandi ntashobora gufatanya ivyo yinjiza n’ivyo asohoka.
