---
term: Addrv2
definition: Inzira y'ubutangazamahoro bushya (BIP155) yemeza kuhereza ayo mibotoro ya Bitcoin. Ishema ayo magara marefu nka Tor v3 cyangwa I2P.
---

Iterambere ryashizweho na BIP155 ku butumwa bwa `addr` ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin. Ubutumwa bwa `addr` bwakoreshejwe mu gutangaza amaderesi y'ibice vyemeza amahuzu yinjira, ariko bwari bushingiye ku maderesi y'ibice 128. Ubu bunini bwari buhagije ku ma aderesi ya IPv6, IPv4, na Tor V2, ariko bwari bugufi cane ku yindi mirongo ngenderwako. Verisiyo ya vuba `addrv2` yagenewe gushigikira amaderesi maremare, harimwo n’ibikorwa vyihishijwe vya Tor v3 vy’ibice 256, hamwe n’ibindi bikorwa vy’uruja n’uruza nka I2P n’ibindi bishobora kubaho muri kazoza.
