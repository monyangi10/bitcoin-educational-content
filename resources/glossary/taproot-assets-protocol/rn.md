---
term: Taproot AMASEZERANO Y'UMUTUNGA
---

Itegeko ryakozwe na Lightning Labs ryemerera gutanga umutungo ku Bitcoin Blockchain nyamukuru, hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bushasha bwa Taproot. Itunga rya Taproot rishoboza kurema itunga rishobora guhinduka nk’ama stablecoins n’itunga ridahinduka nk’ama NFT. Itunga rya Taproot rishobora kwimurirwa biciye mu bikorwa vya kera vya Bitcoin canke biciye mu bikorwa vya Lightning Network. Iyi porotokole ikoresha *Ibiti vya Merkle bifise umubare muto* (MS-SMT), ubwoko bw’uguhuza MST na SMT, kugira ngo bimenyekane ko ivy’umutungo bifise ukuri kandi bishobora gusuzumwa.


> ► *Itegeko ry'Itunga rya Taproot ryari ryitwa mbere "TARO".*
