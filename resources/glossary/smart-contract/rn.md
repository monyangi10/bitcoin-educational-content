---
term: Smart contract
---

Porogarama yikora ubwayo iyo hari ibintu bimwebimwe vyategekanijwe imbere y’igihe bishitse. Smart contract rero ni urutonde rw’ingingo ziri hagati y’abantu benshi zishobora gushirwa mu ngiro ata muntu agira gatatu yizigirwa. Aya masezerano akenshi atuma haba ibikorwa vyihariye nk’uguhindura Bitcoin.


> ► *Mu congereza, ryitwa "Smart contract". Mu gifaransa, rimwe na rimwe ryitwa kandi "ubwigenge bw'amasezerano".*
