---
term: Bcoin
definition: Inyandiko mbererekanyi y'amategeko ya Bitcoin yubatswe muri JavaScript ku bwa Node.js, igenewe abahinga n'amashirahamwe.
---

Iyindi nzira yo gushirwa mu ngiro kw’umurongo wa Bitcoin, wateguwe muri JavaScript ya Node.js. Udakunze Bitcoin core, ariyo nzira nyamukuru y’ugushirwa mu ngiro ku nzira za Bitcoin (hafi 99% vy’urubuga), Bcoin yibanze cane ku bigo n’abahinguzi bafise intumbero yo kwinjiza ibintu vyihariye bijanye na Bitcoin mu mirongo yabo. Bcoin itanga urutonde rw’amasomero yose yo gushirwa mu ngiro Full node, node ya SPV, HD Wallet, indexer n’ibindi bifitaniye isano.
