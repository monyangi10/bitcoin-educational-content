---
term: JOINMARKET

---

Ishirwa mu ngiro rya Chaumian CoinJoin ryaremewe mu 2015 na Adamu Gibson na Chris Belcher. JoinMarket yitandukanya n’izindi nzira za CoinJoin biciye ku citegererezo cayo kidasanzwe co guhuza abakoresha. Ikora nk'isoko rya P2P aho "abahinguzi" batanga ama bitcoins yabo kugira ngo bavange kandi baronke amafaranga, mu gihe "abafata" bakoresha ayo mafaranga mu guhindura amafaranga yo gukora coinjoins.
