---
term: IHUZA rya ZERO
---

Itegeko rya Chaumian CoinJoin rigamije guca amahuzu yose hagati y’ibice bitandukanye vy’ibiceri biciye mu buhinga buteye imbere bwo kubivanga. Iryo tegeko rya ZeroLink ritandukanye n’ubushobozi bwaryo bwo kurinda amazina y’abakoresha ku buryo butandukanye bwo gukorana n’ugusesangura uruhererekane rw’urugero rw’urubuga. ZeroLink itanga uburyo bwo gukoresha amasakoshi ya CoinJoin, hakoreshejwe amakonti atandukanye imbere y'uguvanga n'inyuma y'uguvanga, hamwe n'ubuhinga bwihariye bwo guvanga: Chaumian CoinJoin. Kugeza ubu, ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa ZeroLink ni Whirlpool, ku rubuga rwa Samourai Wallet na Sparrow wallet. Iyi porotokole [yashizweho na nopara73 na TDevD mu 2017].
