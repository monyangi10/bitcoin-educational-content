---
term: Bitcoin Unlimited
definition: Icifuzo ca hard fork mu 2016 cakundira ingano y'ibice (blocks) ihindagurika, ariko ntiyashizwe mu bikorwa.
---

Iciyumviro co guhindura amasezerano ya Bitcoin yasohotse mu mpera z'icuma 2016, kigamije kwongera ubunini bw'amabuye biciye ku Hard Fork.

Ishigikiwe n’ababujije bakuru mu gihe c’intambara y’ubunini bw’amabuye, cane cane Roger Ver, iyo nzira yari itandukanye n’iya Bitcoin Classic mu kudashiraho umupaka wo hejuru ukomeye ku bunini bw’amabuye. Ahubwo, vyatuma abakoresha bashobora gutunganya ivyabo bwite: ubunini bw’uruvyaro rwo hejuru, ubunini bw’ibice vy’inyongera, n’uburebure bwo kwemera.

Naho nyene iyo nzira yo guhindura ibintu, Bitcoin Unlimited yaraneguwe kubera ubuhinga bwayo buteye akaga be n’ubugoyagoye bwinshi bw’umutekano.

Ico ciyumviro amaherezo nticigeze cemerwa.
