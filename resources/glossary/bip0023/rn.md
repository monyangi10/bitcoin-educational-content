---
term: BIP0023
---

Iyi BIP ni ukwaguka kwa BIP22, igamije guhimiriza kwemerwa kwayo n’ubuhinga bukoreshwa n’ibidengeri vya Mining, cane cane n’umurongo wa Getwork, uwabanjirije Stratum. Iyi nzira yashikirijwe na Luke Dashjr, irondera kwinjiza ingingo ya BIP22 muri Getwork ku buryo busanzwe, kugira ngo yorohereze kwemerwa n’ibidengeri vya Mining.

Intumbero ihambaye ya BIP23 ni ugutanga urugero rwose rw’ibarabara ku bacukuzi, bikabafasha gukora igenzura kandi bishoboka ko bahindura imiterere y’ibarabara ryashikirijwe n’ikidengeri. Ubwo buryo bwari bugenewe gutorera umuti ivyiyumviro vya Address ku bijanye no gushiramwo Mining mu guha abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro ku giti cabo ububasha bwinshi n’uguserukira inzira yo kurema amabuye.
