---
term: BIP0050
---

BIP50 ni BIP y'amakuru yerekana ikibazo gifitaniye isano n'uguhinduka kuva kuri Berkeley DB gushika kuri LevelDB, vyatumye uruzitiro ruca mu Bitcoin Blockchain n'ugusubira gutunganya amabarabara 24 ku wa 12 Ntwarante 2013.

Iyi BIP idondora ivyo bintu mu buryo burambuye be n’ibikorwa vyo kubikosora vyashizwe mu ngiro.
