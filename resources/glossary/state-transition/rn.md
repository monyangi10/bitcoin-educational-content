---
term: State Transition
---

Mu nzira ya RGB, State Transition ni igikorwa gikoreshwa mu guhindura uko Contract imeze ngo ibe mu buryo bushasha. Ishobora guhindura amakuru ya Global State n’ay’Ibihugu vy’Igihugu. Ihinduka ryose risuzumwa hakurikijwe amategeko asobanuwe muri Schema ya Contract, ivyo bikaba vyemeza ko ivyo bihinduwe bihuye n’ingingo zashizweho muri Genesis. Ivyo bikorwa bishingiye kuri Blockchain Bitcoin biciye ku Commitment.
