---
term: Mtp (median time past)
definition: Impuzandwi y'ibihe vy'imibumbe 11 yaheruka, ikora nk'iciro c'igihe c'umuyoboro.
---

Ico ciyumviro gikoreshwa mu masezerano ya Bitcoin kugira ngo hamenyekane uruhande ku masezerano y'urubuga Timestamp. MTP isobanurwa nk’umurongo w’imbere w’ibimenyetso vy’igihe vy’amabuye 11 ya nyuma yacukuwe. Gukoresha iki kigereranyo birafasha kwirinda ukutumvikana hagati y’ibihimba ku bijanye n’igihe nyaco mu gihe hoba hariho ukutumvikana. MTP yakoreshejwe mu ntango mu kugenzura ukuri kw’ibimenyetso vy’igihe vy’amabarabara n’ivya kera. Kuva BIP113, ryakoreshejwe kandi nk'ishingiro ry'igihe c'urubuga kugira ngo hasuzumwe ukuri kw'ibikorwa vy'ugufunga igihe (`nIgihe co Gufunga`, `OP_GUSUZUMA IGIHE`, `nUrukurikirane`, na `OP_GUSUZUMA URUTONDE`).
