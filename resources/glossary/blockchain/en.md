---
term: BLOCKCHAIN
---

The blockchain is the commonly used term for Bitcoin’s distributed timestamp server.  It is literally a chain of blocks, where each block is linked to its predecessor through its cryptographic hash. 
To prevent double spending on Bitcoin without relying on a central authority, each participant must be able to verify that a transaction has not already been spent.The only way to ensure this is to know all past Bitcoin transactions. To achieve this, transactions are grouped and timestamped inside blocks, and each participant maintains access to the entire blockchain.

> ► *Following the numerous marketing misuses of the term "Blockchain," especially towards the end of the 2010s, many bitcoiners refuse to use this word. Some prefer to talk about "TimeChain" to evoke this concept. Others, referring to Satoshi Nakamoto's White Paper, mention a "Proof-of-Work Chain." In French, the English term "Blockchain" is generally accepted. The translation "chaîne de blocs" can also be used.*
