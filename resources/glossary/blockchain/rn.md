---
term: Urinyenyeri rw'icintunza
definition: Uruhererekane rw'ibice (blocks) bifise igihe vyashiriweko bikundira kwemeza kahise k'ivunjisha no kwirinda gukoresha amafaranga kabiri.
---

Blockchain ni ijambo rikoreshwa cane ku bijanye n’umurongo wa Bitcoin wa Timestamp.  Ni uruhererekane rw’ibice, aho igice cose gifatanye n’icabanjirije biciye ku gicapo caco c’ibanga Hash.

Kugira ngo umuntu ntakoreshe amahera kabiri ku Bitcoin ata kwizigira ubuyobozi bukuru, umwe wese mu bari muri iyo nama ategerezwa kuba ashoboye kugenzura ko amahera yagurishijwe ataramaze gukoreshwa.Uburyo bumwe rudende bwo kumenya ivyo ni ukumenya amahera yose ya Bitcoin ya kera. Kugira ngo ivyo bishoboke, amafaranga akoreshwa arashirwa mu matsinda kandi agashirwako ikidodo c’igihe mu bice, kandi umwe wese mu bari muri iyo nama araguma afise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha Blockchain yose.
