---
term: Wallet UBUSHOBOZI BWO KWINYANZA (WIF)
---

Uburyo bwo gushiramwo urufunguzo rw’ibanga rwa Bitcoin mu buryo bushobora kwinjizwa canke gusohoka mu buryo bworoshe hagati y’ibipapuro bitandukanye. WIF ishingiye ku `Base58Check` encoding, irimwo amakuru yerekeye verisiyo, gufatanya urufunguzo rwa bose rujanye n'ivyo, n'umubare wo gusuzuma amakosa mu kwandika. Urufunguzo rw'ibanga rwa WIF rutangura n'inyuguti `5` ku mfunguruzo zitapfutse, canke `K` na `L` ku mfungurwa zipfutse, kandi rurimwo inyuguti zose zikenewe kugira ngo urufunguzo rw'ibanga rw'intango rusubire kwubaka. Uburyo bwa WIF butanga uburyo bumwe bwo gutanga urufunguzo rw’ibanga hagati ya porogarama zitandukanye za Wallet.
