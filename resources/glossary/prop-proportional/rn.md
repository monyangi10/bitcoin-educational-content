---
term: PROP (IBIGERANE)
---

Uburyo bwo kubara impembo y’abacukuzi mu rwego rw’ibidengeri vya Mining. PROP gusa itanga Block reward mu bacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bivanye n’intererano yabo mu migabane. Ibara ry’imigabane ritangura ku gice ca nyuma kibonetse n’ikidengeri, rigahera iyo habonetse igice gishasha. Buri bubiko bushasha busubiramwo igiharuro c'imigabane kuri zero. Ubwo buryo bwo guhembwa buratuma umuntu wese mu bari muri iyo nama ashobora kwerekana ata guca ku ruhande igikorwa ciwe.
