---
term: Modèle temporel
definition: Isubumiriza ry'ibihe itunshwa ry'amafaranga ryashiriweko kugira hamenyekane akaranga cyangwa aho umuntu akorera muri blockchain aherereye.
---

Inyifato zimwe zimwe z’abantu ziramenyekana On-Chain. Kumbure ikintu gifise akamaro kuruta ibindi vyose mu gusuzuma uruhererekane ni ukuntu usinzira! Ego cane, iyo usinziriye, birashoboka ko udatangaza amakuru y’ubudandaji bwa Bitcoin. Muri rusangi, uryama nk’amasaha amwe amwe. Ni co gituma isesengura ry’igihe rikoreshwa cane mu gusesangura uruhererekane. Birimwo gusa kwandika ibihe amafaranga y’ikigo kinaka atangazwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin. Gusuzuma ivyo bigereranyo vy’igihe biratuma dushobora gukuramwo amakuru menshi cane.


Ica mbere, isesengura ry’igihe rimwe na rimwe rirashobora kugaragaza kamere y’ikintu gikurikiranywe. Nitwabona ko amafaranga atangazwa ata guhinduka mu masaha 24, ivyo rero vyoshobora kwerekana igikorwa gikomeye c’ubutunzi. Ikigo kiri inyuma y’ivyo bikorwa gishobora kuba ari ubudandaji, bushobora kuba ari ubw’isi yose kandi kumbure bukaba bufise uburyo bwikoresha imbere mu gihugu. Ku rundi ruhande, nitwabona ko urugero rw’igihe rukwiragiye mu masaha 16 yihariye, rero twoshobora kugereranya ko turiko turakorana n’umuntu ku giti ciwe, canke kumbure ubucuruzi bwo mu karere bivanye n’ibiharuro vy’ibintu bihindurwa.


Uretse kamere y’ikintu cihwejwe, urugero rw’igihe rurashobora kandi kwerekana hafi aho uwukoresha ari biciye mu bice vy’amasaha. Ivyo bituma dushobora guhuza ibindi bikorwa, no gukoresha Timestamp y’ivyo nk’inyongera y’ubuhinga bushobora gushirwa mu gusesangura uruhererekane.


Mu gitabu gitandukanye, ni ubwo bwoko bw’isesengura ry’igihe bwatumye habaho iciyumviro c’uko Satoshi Nakamoto itariko irakora ivuye mu Buyapani, ariko vy’ukuri ivuye muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika: [_The Time Zones of Satoshi]. Nakamoto_](ivyo-bice-vy'amasaha-vya-Satoshi-nakamoto-aa40f035178f).
