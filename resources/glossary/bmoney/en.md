---
term: B-MONEY
---

Prototype of a decentralized cryptocurrency designed by Wei Dai in 1998. It envisioned a network in which participants would be identified solely by public keys, and every transaction would be digitally signed by the sender. 
Unlike Bitcoin’s UTXO model, B-money was based on an account-based ledger. It proposed the creation of money through a form of proof of work tied to a basket of goods, making it an early conceptual precursor to what we now call stablecoins.

The concept, however, was never implemented.
