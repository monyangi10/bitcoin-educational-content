---
term: ESMPPS
definition: Uburyo bwo guha agashimwe abacukuzi bugabura utushimwe ku buryo buringaniye mu migabane (shares) yose yatanzwe.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikirundi risobanura "*Umushahara munini w'umugabane ungana ku mugabane*". Ni uburyo bwo kubara impembo y’abacukuzi bo mu bidengeri vya Mining. ESMPPS ifise intumbero yo gutanga impembo mu buryo bubereye mu migabane yose yashikirijwe, ata kuraba igihe yashikirijwe canke amahirwe y’ikidengeri. Mu vy’ukuri ikora nka SMPPS, ariko n’iciyumviro congereweko c’uburinganire ku migabane yashikirijwe.
