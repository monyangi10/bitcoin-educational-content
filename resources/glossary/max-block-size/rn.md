---
term: MAX_BLOC_SIZE

definition: Ukuri gusobanura ubunini ntarengwa umubumbe ushobora kugira kuri Bitcoin.
---

Igiharuro kigaragaza ubunini burengeye ubunini bushobora kugira kuri Bitcoin. Mu mateka, iyo nzira yashizwe kuri 1 MB, ingingo yashizweho na Satoshi Nakamoto mu 2010, kugira ngo hakingirwe spam no kugira ngo hagumyeho urugero runaka rw’ugusenyura urubuga.
