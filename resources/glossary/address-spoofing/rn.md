---
term: Address spoofing
definition: Ikibazo aho ubwiganze bubi bumwe ipinina ikinini k'inyubako ryo mu bwiyunge bwikinini rya muribwa kugira ngo muribwa abe mu bwiyunge bwa gisubizo.
---

Igitero aho umukinyi w’ububisha atanga Address (canke ikindi kimenyetso c’ukwishura) gisa cane n’ica Address c’uwagirizwa. Intumbero ni uguhenda uwukoresha ngo akope Address idakwiriye igihe akora igikorwa, ivyo bikaba bituma ayo ma bitcoins yoherezwa ku wuyitera aho kurungikwa ku muntu yashaka kuyaronka.



Uwo muterabwoba akoresha ubwihuta bw’uwukoresha, uwo na we akashobora gukopa Address idakwiriye iyo akoze iyo nzira atasuzumye neza ko ari ukuri. Uburyo busanzwe bwo gukora ico gitero ni ukurungika amahera makeyi kuri Wallet y’uwagirizwa, gutyo bagashira iyo Address yihenda mu mateka yabo y’ibikorwa. Ubwo bwoko bw’igitero burasanzwe cane ku ma altcoins, aho gusubira gukoresha Address ari vyo vyinshi. Muri Bitcoin, gusubira gukoresha Address biracika intege kandi gukoresha amaderesi mashasha ni vyo bimenyerewe cane, ivyo bikaba bifasha kugabanya ingorane. Ariko rero, abakoresha Bitcoin ntibashobora gukingira ubwo bwoko bw’igitero.



Uburyo bw'inyongera bwo gushira aderesi itari yo imbere y’uwagizwe ingorane ni ugukoresha porogaramu z’uburiganya zo gucunga amapfuko zigereranya porogaramu zemewe, canke guhindura aderesi igihe mudasobwa yinjiriwe, hagati y’igihe ikopororwa n’igihe igikorwa co kohereza gikozwe. Ivyo rimwe na rimwe vyitwa '"*address swapping*"'.



Kugira ngo umuntu yikingire ivyo bitero, birahambaye ko asuzuma inyuguti nyinshi zo muri Address, canecane checksum yayo (ku mpera), ku gicapo c’igikoresho co gusinya imbere y’uko asinya ku giciro.



Iki gitero rimwe na rimwe citwa kandi '"*address poisoning*"'.
