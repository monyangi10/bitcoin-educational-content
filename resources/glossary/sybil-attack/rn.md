---
term: Sybil Attack
---

Igitero co kuri interineti aho umuntu canke ishirahamwe rirema akaranga k’ikinyoma kenshi muri sisitemu kugira ngo rigire akosho kadakwiriye canke rironke inyungu idatemerewe. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ico gitero kigamije kuronka ububasha budakwiriye kuri iyo nzira, gutyo kigakoresha nabi uburyo bwo kwumvikana. Kugira ngo barwanye ibitero vya Sybil, Satoshi Nakamoto yariyumviriye uburyo bwa Proof of Work bushiraho igiciro c’inyuma kitari co ku kurema amajwi menshi.
