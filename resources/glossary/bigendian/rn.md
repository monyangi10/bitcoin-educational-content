---
term: INDIYA NINI
---

Uburyo bwo kubika amakuru muri mudasobwa aho ama bytes ahambaye cane ("iherezo rinini") ashirwa imbere mu rutonde rwa Address. Ivyo bisigura ko mu rukurikirane rufise ama byte menshi, byte ifise insiguro nini (nk’akarorero, imibare y’ibubamfu cane mu hexadecimal) ni yo ibanza kubikwa.
