---
term: SIGHASH_SINGLE (0X03)

definition: SigHash Flag ifatanije buri cinjiye basinye n'isohoka ijyanye nawe ku giharuro kimwe.
---

Ubwoko bwa SigHash Ibendera rikoreshwa mu mikono y’ibikorwa vya Bitcoin kugira ngo ryereke ko iyo sinyatire ikoreshwa ku vyinjizwa vyose vy’ibikorwa no ku gisohoka kimwe gusa, bihuye n’urutonde rw’ivyo nyene vyashizweko umukono. Gutyo, ikintu cose gishizweko umukono na `SIGHASH_SINGLE` gifitaniye isano n’igisohoka kinaka. Ibindi bisohoka ntibishingwa n’umukono kandi rero birashobora guhindurwa mu nyuma. Ubwo bwoko bw’umukono burakenewe mu bikorwa bikomeye, aho ababigiramwo uruhara bashaka guhuza ibintu bimwebimwe bishirwamwo n’ibintu bimwebimwe bisohoka, mu gihe basiga uburyo bwo guhindura ibindi bisohoka mu bikorwa.
