---
term: UDP (ITEGEKO RY'AMAKURU Y'UMUKORESHA)
---

Itegeko ry’itumanaho rikoreshwa kuri Internet rituma umuntu ashobora kwohereza ubutumwa (datagrams) hagati ya mudasobwa ata n’umwe abanza gukorana (bitandukanye na TCP). UDP ni uburyo bwo gutanga amakuru vyihuta, ariko ata n’ivyemezo vy’ugushikana, ugusaba amapakete, canke gucungera amakosa. Ikoreshwa canecane ku bikorwa bisaba gutangaza amakuru vyihuta kandi mu gihe nyaco. Iryo tegeko ryakoreshejwe mu mugambi wa FIBER (*Internet yihuta Bitcoin Relay Engine*) kugira ngo vyihute gukwiragiza amabuye ya Bitcoin.
