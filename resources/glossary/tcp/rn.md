Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikigiriki "*Itegeko ry'Igenzura ry'Itangazamakuru*". Ni uburyo bwo guhanahana amakuru bugenewe gutuma amakuru ashobora gushikirizwa biciye kuri Internet mu buryo bwizigirwa. Irashinga uruja n’uruza, igatanga urutonde rw’amakuru yoherejwe, igacungera ugusubira gutanga amakuru iyo amapakete yatakaye, kandi igacungera ukuntu amakuru yoherezwa.
