---
term: Witness transaction
definition: Muri RGB, iterambere rya Bitcoin rirangiza Single-use Seal kugira rihame imiterere y'isezerano on-chain.
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, Witness Transaction yerekeza ku gikorwa ca Bitcoin gifunga Single-Use Seal hirya no hino y’ubutumwa bushiramwo Multi Protocol Commitment (MPC). Ico gikorwa gishingiye ku gukoresha UTXO iriho, canke guhingura iyindi nshasha, kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gufunga Commitment y’amasezerano yanditswe muri porotokole. Witness Transaction rero ni ikimenyamenya ca On-Chain c’uko ikibanza ca RGB Contract cashinzwe ku gihe kinaka.
