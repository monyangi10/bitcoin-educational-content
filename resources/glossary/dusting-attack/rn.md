---
term: IGITERA C'INKUNGU
---

Igitero co gutera inkungugu ni ukurungika utugingo ngengabuzima dutoduto tw’ama bitcoins ku ma aderesi menshi y’ukwakira. Intumbero y’uwo muterabwoba ni uguhatira abayaronka gufatanya ayo mahera n’ayandi ma UTXO. Uwo muterabwoba aca akurikirana ingene ayo ma bitcoins makeyi azogenda muri kazoza, afise intumbero yo gukora amatsinda y’amaderesi, ni ukuvuga kumenya nimba amaderesi menshi ari ay’ikigo kimwe. Mu guhuza amakuru yaronkejwe mu gihe c’igitero co gutera inkungugu n’ayandi makuru be n’ubuhinga bukoreshwa mu gusesangura uruhererekane, birashoboka ko uwutera amenya ibigo bimwebimwe n’aho biherereye. Ubwo buryo bugereranya gusa ingeramizi ku buzima bwite bw’abakoresha, ariko ntibugira ico bukoze ku mutekano w’amahera yabo.


> ► *Bamwe mu bakoresha bitcoins barasaba ko batazosubira gukoresha ijambo "dusting attack" kuko rishobora kuba rizimiza. Nkako, ijambo "Dust" ridondora ikintu kidasanzwe muri Bitcoin core. Iyo igitero co gutera inkungugu gikoresha vy’ukuri Dust nk’uko vyavuzwe muri Core, igitero coba ata co kimaze. Ku bw’ivyo, bamwebamwe basaba ko bokoresha ijambo ngo “gusubira gukoresha Address ku nguvu” kugira ngo bashobore kudondora neza ico gitero.*
