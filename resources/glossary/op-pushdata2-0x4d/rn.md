---
term: OP_PUSHDATA2 (0X4D)

definition: Opcode ishiraho gushika ku ma KB 65 y’amakuru ku mupfundikizo.
---

Iremeza gusunika amakuru menshi ku kirundo. Ikurikirwa n’ibice bibiri (little-endian) vyerekana uburebure bw’amakuru azosunikwa (bishika nko kuri 65 KB). Ikoreshwa mu kwinjiza amakuru menshi mu nyandiko.
