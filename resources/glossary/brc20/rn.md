---
term: BRC-20
---

Itegeko rishoboza gukorana n'ibimenyetso bitari ivy'imvukira kuri Bitcoin, bishingiye ku nyandiko z'amasezerano ya Ordinals. Yaremwe na Domo muri Ntwarante 2023, iki kigereranyo c’igerageza carateye inyungu nyinshi mu gutekereza ku bintu, bituma habaho ukwiyongera kw’amafaranga y’ugucuruza muri Ndamukiza na Rusama 2023. Ibimenyetso vya BRC-20 bikoresha Bitcoin mu kubika n’ibikorwa vya Timestamp JSON bicungera ivyo bintu. Ivyo bikorwa ni `gushiraho` (token kurema), `mint` (token gutanga), na `kwimurira` (token kwimurira). Kugira ngo iyo porotokole ishobore gushirwa mu ngiro, abantu bategerezwa kubungabunga ama server akora urutonde rw’ibikorwa vyose. Itegeko rya BRC-20 rero ni ugukoresha nabi cane Bitcoin ugereranyije n'izindi nzira zo kurema token nka RGB.
