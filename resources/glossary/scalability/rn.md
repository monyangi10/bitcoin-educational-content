---
term: UBWO GUSHIRA MU RUGERO
---

Yerekeza ku bushobozi bwa Bitcoin bwo guhangana n’ukwiyongera kw’umubare w’ibikorwa mu gihe iguma ifise ingero z’ibikorwa zemewe. Bitcoin irafise ingorane z’ubuhinga zisanzwe, nk’ubunini bw’ibarabara, igihe kiri hagati y’ibarabara rimwe rimwe, n’uko igikorwa gifatwa gusa nk’ikidahinduka inyuma y’aho cemejwe kenshi. Ivyo bibuza Bitcoin gukora neza umubare w’ibikorwa bitagira aho bigarukira. Iyo ugusaba umwanya mu mabarabara gukomeye cane, abakoresha baragoberwa no kwongera amahera batanga kugira ngo amafaranga yabo akorwe. Ugushobora gutera imbere, bisobanura ubushobozi bwa Bitcoin bwo guhangana n'ukwiyongera kw'umubare w'ibikorwa, bishobora guterwa imbere biciye mu guhindura ataco bimaze amasezerano canke biciye mu gutorera umuti ibibazo vyo hanze nka Lightning Network. Ikibazo c’ugushobora gutera imbere cama ari co gitera impaka zikomeye mu kibano. Igerageza rya mbere ryo kwongera ubunini bw’amabuye ryabaye mu kwezi kwa Gitugutu 2010 n’[igice cashikirijwe na Jeff Garzik](insanganyamatsiko=1347.0). Iyi nsiguro y'ugutera imbere cane cane yatumye haba block size war hagati ya 2015 na 2017.


> ► *Ijambo "scalabilité" ni ururimi rw'icongereza. Impinduro ibereye y'ijambo ry'icongereza "scalability" ni "évolutivité" canke "passage à l'échelle". Ariko rero, muri rusangi vyemewe mu kibano gukoresha ijambo "scalabilité" ataco rihinduye.*
