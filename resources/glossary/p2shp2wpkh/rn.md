---
term: P2SH-P2WPKH.
---

P2SH-P2WPKH bisobanura *Ishura Inyandiko Hash - Ishura Icabona Urufunguzo rwa bose Hash*. Ni urugero rw'inyandiko rusanzwe rukoreshwa mu gushinga ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha kuri UTXO, izwi kandi nka "Nested SegWit".


P2SH-P2WPKH yashizweho n’ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa SegWit muri Myandagaro 2017. Iyi nyandiko ni P2WPKH izingirijwe muri P2SH. Ifunga bitcoins ishingiye kuri Hash y’urufunguzo rwa bose. Itandukaniro na P2WPKH ya kera ni uko inyandiko izingiwe muri `redeemscript` ya P2SH ya kera.


Iyi nyandiko yaremewe igihe SegWit yatanguzwa kugira ngo ishobore kwemerwa. Iremeza gukoresha iyo ngingo nshasha, mbere n'ibikorwa canke amasakoshi atarahuye na SegWit. Ni ubwoko bw’inyandiko y’uguhinduka yerekeza ku rugero rushasha. Muri iki gihe rero, ntibigikenewe cane gukoresha ubwo bwoko bw'inyandiko za SegWit zizingiwe, kuko amasakoshi menshi yashize mu ngiro SegWit y'akavukire. Aderesi za P2SH-P2WPKH zandikwa hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwa `Base58Check` kandi zama zitangura na `3`, nk'ibindi vyose vya P2SH Address.


> ► *`P2SH-P2WPKH` nayo rimwe na rimwe yitwa `P2WPKH-ishizwe-mu-P2SH`.*
