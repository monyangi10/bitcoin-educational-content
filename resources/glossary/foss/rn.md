---
term: FOSS
definition: Ubuhinga bwa mudasobwa bwidegemvye kandi bwuguruye bushobora gukoreshwa, kwigwa, guhindurwa no gukwiragizwa ku bwidegemvye.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'"Porogaramu y'ubuntu kandi yuguruye". Ivyo vyerekeye porogarama zifise uruhusha rwemerera umuntu wese kuzikoresha ku ntumbero iyo ari yo yose, kuziga, kuzihindura kugira ngo zihure n’ivyo akeneye, no kuzisubira gukwiragiza ataco zitwaye, zihinduwe canke zitahinduwe. Iyo nsobanuro ivuga ko kode y’inkomoko y’iyo porogarama ishobora gushikirwa na bose.
