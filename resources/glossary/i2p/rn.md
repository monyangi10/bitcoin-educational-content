---
term: I2P
---

Urubuga rwo guhanahana amakuru rutazwi rwateguwe kugira ngo ivyo umuntu avuga kuri Internet bibe ibanga. Cokimwe na TOR, I2P ipfuka amakuru y'urubuga kandi ikoresha uburyo bwo gutanga inzira bwitwa "urubuga rw'uruvange". Ubwo buryo buratuma amakuru atamenyekana mu kuyaca ku bikoresho vy’ubuhuza bihindura amakuru ataco azi ku bijanye n’uwuyarungitse canke uwuyakira. Nka kurya kwa TOR, I2P ishobora gukoreshwa ku nzira ya Bitcoin kugira ngo ikingire ivy’itumanaho ryayo.


> ► *Ijambo ry'inyongera "I2P" risobanura "Umugambi wa Internet utaboneka".*
