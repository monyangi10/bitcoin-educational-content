---
term: Zeroconf

definition: Uburyo buteye inkeke bwo gufata iterambere ritarahamazwa nk'irirangiye, rishobora gukoreshwa kabiri.
---

Igikorwa kiteye akaga co gufata igikorwa ca Bitcoin nk’ica nyuma, no gukomeza gushitsa igikorwa gifitaniye isano n’ico gikorwa mu gusubiza (nk’ugucuruza ikintu canke igikorwa), imbere y’uko igikorwa gishirwa mu vy’ukuri mu gice kiri ku rubuga rwa blockchain. Ivy’ugucuruza bitaremejwe, canke zeroconf, birashobora guterwa n’ibitero vy’ugukoresha amahera kabiri, kuko bitarashobora kwandikwa mu gitabu c’amahera ataco bihinduye. Zeroconf ishobora gufatwa mu bihe vyihariye cane, aho umuvuduko ari wo ubanza, nk’igihe habaye ubucuruzi buto buto canke iyo habaye ubucuruzi hagati y’abantu bamenyana. Muri ivyo bihe, ingorane yo gukoresha amahera incuro zibiri akenshi ibonwa ko yemewe ugereranije n’inyungu yo gukoresha amahera vyihuse. Naho ari ukwo, ku bijanye n’ibikorwa bihambaye, cane cane iyo uwubirungitse atazwi, birahambaye cane ko umuntu arindira ko yemezwa kenshi imbere y’uko abona ko iyo bikorwa idashobora guhinduka. Iciyumviro cemewe muri rusangi ni ugutegerezwa kwemeza 6, bisobanura ko amabuye 5 y’inyongera ategerezwa gucukurwa inyuma y’iryo harimwo n’ugucuruza, kugira ngo rifatwe nk’iry’iherezo.
