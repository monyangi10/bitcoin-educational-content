---
term: P2PK
---

P2PK ni *Ishura ku rufunguzo rwa bose*. Ni urugero rw'inyandiko rusanzwe rukoreshwa kuri Bitcoin kugira ngo hashirweho ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha kuri UTXO. Ishobora gufunga bitcoins ku rufunguzo rwa bose, aho gufunga Address.

Mu buryo bw’ubuhinga, inyandiko ya P2PK irimwo urufunguzo rwa bose n’amabwirizwa asaba umukono wa digitale uhuye kugira ngo ufungure amahera. Iyo nyen’ivyo bikoresho yipfuza gukoresha ayo ma bitcoins, ategerezwa gutanga umukono wakozwe n’urufunguzo rw’ibanga rujanye n’ivyo. Iryo sinyatire rigenzurwa hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwa ECDSA (*Algorithme de la signature numérique de la curve elliptique*). P2PK yakoreshwa kenshi mu bitabu vya mbere vya Bitcoin, cane cane na Satoshi Nakamoto. Hafi y’aho ntikigikoreshwa muri iki gihe.
