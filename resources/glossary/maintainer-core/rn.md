---
term: Maintainer (core)
definition: Abantu bajejwe kwitaho umushinga wa Bitcoin Core no guhuza amahinduka y'ikode.
---

Mu bijanye n’umugambi wa Bitcoin core, ni ukuvuga ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’ibihimba vy’umubiri ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, ababibungabunga ni abantu bashinzwe gucunga umugambi. Ni bo bikorera inshingano yo gushirwa mu ngiro kwayo. Ni bo bajejwe gucungera ububiko bwa Bitcoin core GitHub no gushinga urutonde rwo gusohora verisiyo nshasha. Ni bo bajejwe canecane gukora ivy’uguhuza ibisabwa vyo gukurura (PR) vyashikirijwe n’abatanga.


Mu yandi majambo, iyo umugambi wo guhindura kode uciye mu ntambwe zitandukanye zo kwemeza, abo bacungezi ni bo bifatanya n’inshingano ihambaye yo gufatanya kode nshasha na porogarama ya Bitcoin core. Imbere y’uko abajejwe gucungera iyo ngingo babandanya iyo nzira y’uguhuza, barasuzuma nimba iyo ngingo ngenderwako yubahiriza ingingo ngenderwako z’ishimikiro z’umugambi, nimba yashitse ku rugero rutoyi rusabwa kugira ngo ishirwemwo, kandi bagacira urubanza n’ivyo abatanga intererano bemeranije muri rusangi ku bijanye n’iyo mpinduka. Kuva aho twandika iyi nsobanuro, abacungera Bitcoin core ni batanu gusa ni bo basigaye: Hennadii Stepanov, Mikaheli Ford, Ava Chow, Gloria Zhao na Ryan Ofsky.
