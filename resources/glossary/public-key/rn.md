---
term: URUPFUZO RWA RUSE
---

Urufunguzo rwa bose ni ikintu gikoreshwa mu gukora amakuru y'ibanga. Iva ku rufunguzo rw'ibanga hakoreshejwe igikorwa c'imibare kidashobora gusubirwamwo. Ku Bitcoin, imfunguruzo za bose zikomoka ku mfunguruzo zazo z’ibanga zijanye n’ivyo biciye ku nzira z’umukono w’ibarabara z’umurongo w’uruzitiro ECDSA canke Schnorr. Mu buryo butandukanye n’urufunguzo rw’ibanga, urufunguzo rwa bose rushobora gusangizwa ataco ruhinduye ku mutekano w’amahera. Mu masezerano ya Bitcoin, urufunguzo rwa bose rukora nk'ishimikiro ryo kurema Bitcoin Address, ikoreshwa mu kurema ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha kuri UTXO hakoreshejwe `scriptPubKey`. Imfunguruzo za bose zikunda gutera urujijo n'imfunguruzo nyamukuru canke n'imfunguruzo zagutse (xpub...). Ariko rero, izo Elements ziratandukanye cane.


> ► *Mu congereza, urufunguzo rwa bose rwitwa "urufunguzo rwa bose." Iri jambo rimwe na rimwe rigabanywa ngo "pubkey," canke "PK."*
