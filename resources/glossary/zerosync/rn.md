---
term: ZEROSYNC

---

Umugambi wateguwe kugira ngo ukoreshe ZKP (Zero-Knowledge Proofs) mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin, ugamije kwongereza ubushobozi bwo gukoresha no gukingira ubuzima bwite. Itegeko ryabo ry’ingenzi (ryitwa kandi *ZeroSync*) riremesha gufatanya amateka ya Bitcoin blockchain, kugira ngo vyihute gukorana kw’intango kw’ibihimba n’urubuga biciye mu gukoresha ikimenyamenya gikomeye, ataco bihinduye ku kugenzura.
