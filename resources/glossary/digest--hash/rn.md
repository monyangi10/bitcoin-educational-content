---
term: Condensat
definition: Ibisigwa bifise ingere idahinduka bivuye ku gukoresha hashing kuri amakuru.
---

Mu buhinga bwo gukingira amakuru, bisigura igisubizo (canke igisohoka) kivuye mu gukoresha igikorwa c'ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru Hash ku rutonde rw'amakuru. Ico gice c’umubiri ni uruzitiro rw’inyuguti zifise ubunini butahinduka, muri rusangi zikaba zigereranywa n’urutonde rw’imibare n’inyuguti mu kwandika kw’imibare cumi n’itandatu (ishimikiro rya 16). Ico ciyumviro gifise ubudasa bwo kuba hafi kidasanzwe kandi kidasanzwe ku makuru yinjijwe, ku buryo ihinduka ritoyi mu yinjijwe rizotuma habaho umunyu utandukanye rwose. Ibikorwa vya Cryptographic Hash vyakozwe kugira ngo bibe bifise inzira imwe kandi ntibishobora guhura n’ibintu, bikaba bigoye cane kugarura amakuru y’intango mu mfuruka, canke kuronka ibintu bibiri bitandukanye biva mu mfuruka imwe.
