---
term: PAPER Wallet
---

Offline storage method that involves printing or writing down a pair of cryptographic keys giving access to bitcoins on paper (or possibly another physical medium) only. Paper wallets are considered a cold storage medium, as they are not connected to the Internet, provided they have been produced manually and not printed. Their security relies on the physical protection of the paper against loss, destruction and theft. Today, they are often considered obsolete, as they offer a poorer security model than modern solutions. What's more, their use often involves reusing the same public key, which is highly problematic for user confidentiality.
