---
term: URUPFUZO RUKURU
---

Mu bijanye n'amasakoshi ya HD (Hierarchical Deterministic), urufunguzo rw'ibanga ni urufunguzo rw'ibanga rudasanzwe ruva kuri seed ya Wallet. Kugira ngo uronke urufunguzo rwa mbere, igikorwa ca `HMAC-SHA512` gikoreshwa kuri seed, hakoreshejwe amajambo "*Bitcoin seed*" nk'urufunguzo. Ico gisubizo c'iyi nzira kizana igisohoka c'ibice 512, ibice 256 vya mbere bikaba bigize urufunguzo nyamukuru, ibice 256 bisigaye bikaba bigize urufunguzo nyamukuru chain code. Urufunguzo rwa mbere n'urufunguzo rwa mbere chain code bikora nk'intango yo gukuraho urufunguzo rwose rw'abana rw'ibanga n'urwa bose mu giti ca HD Wallet. Rero, urufunguzo rw'ibanga rw'umukuru ruri ku burebure 0 bw'inkomoko.
