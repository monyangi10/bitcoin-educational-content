---
term: GUSUBIRAMWO
---

Mu bijanye n’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru n’ubuhinga bwa orodinateri, yerekeza ku nzira yo gukoranya ibintu bibiri bikoreshwa mu kubishira ku mpera ku mpera, gutyo bikaba bihingura urudodo rushasha rw’inyuguti canke amakuru. Iyi nzira muri rusangi yerekanwa n'ikimenyetso c'imirongo ibiri ihagaze $\Vert$, canke n'ikimenyetso $\circ$. Nk’akarorero, gufatanya $45$ n’$87$ vyoba bingana $4587$. Tugaragaza: $45 \Igice ca 87 = 4587$. Ivyo bikoresho bibiri vyashizwe ku mpera ku mpera.
