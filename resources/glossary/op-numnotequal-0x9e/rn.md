---
term: OP_NUMNOTEQUAL (0X9E)

definition: Opcode igenzura ko ibintu bibiri biri k’inyuma ku mupfundikizo bitandukanye mu namba.
---

Gereranya ibintu bibiri biri hejuru cane ku rutonde kugira ngo umenye nimba bitangana mu mibare. Iyo agaciro katangana, isunika `1` (ukuri) ku ruganda, ahandi ho, isunika `0` (ikinyoma). Ivyo ni ibihushanye n'ivyo `OP_NUMEQUAL`.
