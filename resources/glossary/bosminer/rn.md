---
term: Bosminer
definition: Isoseti y'ivunjisha ifunguye muri Rust yakozwe na Braiins, yagenewe ASICs ifise uburyo bwa StratumV2.
---

BOSminer ni porogaramu y’inkomoko yuguruye ya Mining yateguwe muri Rust na Braiins (yahora yitwa Slush Pool) nk’iyindi nzira y’ubuhinga bwa none yo gusubirira CGMiner. Ikoreshwa cane cane ku bacukuzi ba ASIC kandi iri mu bidukikije vya BraiinsOS.


BOSminer itanga ubushobozi bwo gucunga ASIC buteye imbere, harimwo no gukoresha igihe kirekire, kugenzura ibikorwa mu buryo burambuye, no gufasha Stratum V2.
