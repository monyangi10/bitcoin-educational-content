---
term: MAST
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'"Igiti c'inyandiko zindi." Ubuhinga bukoresha Merkle Tree kugira ngo buvuge mu ncamake umubare w’ibintu bikoreshwa vyatowe n’uwukoresha mu Address yakira, kimwe muri vyo kigomba gushitswako kugira ngo umuntu akoreshe amahera y’ibice vy’amahera. Merkle Tree iremesha uwuyikoresha guhitamwo ivyangombwa yipfuza gushitsa ataco ahishura ku bindi bisabwa biri kuri Blockchain. Ivyo bifasha kugabanya amahera ajanye n’izo nyandiko, bikazana ibintu bikomeye cane, kandi, uko igihe kigenda kirarenga, bikatuma abakoresha bagira ubuzima bwite (ukwongerako gukoresha imikono ya Schnorr). Ico ciyumviro cari ikintu c'ivyifuzo vyinshi ariko amaherezo congerewe kuri Bitcoin biciye ku Taproot Soft Fork mu 2021.


> ► *Mu ntango, "MAST" yari ihagarariye "Igiti c'Imirongo y'Imirongo." Ikoreshwa ry'inzego za Taproot ntirigifitaniye isano n'"Igiti c'Inyigisho." Ariko rero, abakoresha barabandanije gukoresha ijambo MAST. Anthony Towns rero yasavye ko insobanuro y’intango yohindurwa mu gihe yagumye iryo jambo ry’inyongera rikoreshwa cane nk’iri: “Igiti c’Inyandiko zindi z’Ikirundi.”*
