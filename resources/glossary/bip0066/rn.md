---
term: BIP0066
definition: Gushira mu rugero imiterere y'ibimenyetso hifashishijwe DER encoding ikaze, mu kwirinda ukudahuza hagati y'amasistemu.
---

Yashizeho uburyo bwo guhuza uburyo bwo gusinya mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji.

Yasabwe mu kwishura ku kudahuza mu buryo OpenSSL yakoresha ubuhinga bwo gushiramwo amajambo y’umukono ku mirongo itandukanye, ivyo bikaba vyatuma haba ingorane yo gucapura Blockchain.

BIP66 yashizeho uburyo bwo gushirako umukono ku bikorwa vyose hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwa DER bukomeye (*Amategeko y’Ugushiramwo Ubuhinga*).

Iryo hinduka ryasaba Soft Fork, ikoreshwa hakoreshejwe uburyo bumwe na BIP34:


- Umwanya wa nVersion wongerewe kuri verisiyo ya 3.
- Amabuye yose ya version 2 canke hasi yaranse igihe 95% vy’abacukuzi bamenyesheje ko bashigikiye.

Ico kigero cashitse ku 363.725 ku wa 4 Nyakanga 2015.
