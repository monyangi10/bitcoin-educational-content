---
term: Hot wallet (software)
definition: Software wallet connected to the internet, storing private keys on a non-dedicated device.
---

A hot wallet is a software device dedicated to securing and managing the private keys of a Bitcoin wallet. A hot wallet is when the recovery phrase of a Bitcoin wallet is stored on a computing device, via software, that is not dedicated solely to Bitcoin use and is connected directly to the internet. For example, the Samurai Wallet application on your smartphone would be considered a hot wallet.
