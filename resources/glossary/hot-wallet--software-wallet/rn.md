---
term: Agasanduku gashushe (software)
definition: Igapu ya software ihujije na internet, ibika imfunguzo z'ibanga kuri mudasobwa isanzwe.
---

Hot Wallet ni igikoresho ca porogarama gikoreshwa mu gucungera no gucunga imfunguruzo z'ibanga za Bitcoin Wallet. Hot Wallet ni igihe ijambo ry’ugusubirana rya Bitcoin Wallet ribitswe ku gikoresho co gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none, biciye ku rubuga rwa porogarama, kitari co gusa gikoreshwa na Bitcoin kandi kikaba gifatanye n’internet. Nk’akarorero, porogarama ya Samurai Wallet iri kuri telefone yawe yofatwa nk’iya Hot Wallet.
