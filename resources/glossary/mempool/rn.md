---
term: Mempool
definition: Ikibanza ihererekanya ry'amafaranga ririndiriye gushirwa mu mubumbe ribikwamwo na buri node.
---

Inyuguti z'amajambo "ukwibuka" na "ikidengeri". Ivyo vyerekeye ikibanza c’ukuri aho amafaranga y’ubudandaji ya Bitcoin arindiriye gushirwa mu gice c’ibarabara akoranirizwa hamwe. Iyo igikorwa co gucuruza kiremwe kandi kigatangazwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, kibanza kugenzurwa n’ibihimba vy’urubuga. Iyo bibonwa ko ari vyiza, rero bishirwa muri Mempool y’urudodo rumwe rumwe, aho biguma gushika bitowe na Miner kugira ngo bishirwe mu gice.


Ni ngombwa kumenya ko buri nzira iri mu murongo wa Bitcoin iguma ifise Mempool yayo, kandi rero, hashobora kubaho uguhinduka mu birimwo Mempool hagati y’inzira zitandukanye igihe cose. Biragaragara, `maxmempool` parameter muri dosiye `Bitcoin.conf` ya buri node iremesha abakoresha kugenzura umubare wa RAM (ubuhinga bwo kwibuka) node yabo izokoresha mu kubika amafaranga ategerejwe muri Mempool. Mu kugabanya ubunini bwa Mempool, abakoresha node barashobora kuyibuza gukoresha ibikoresho vyinshi cane kuri sisitemu yabo. Iyi parametere igaragazwa mu megabytes (MB). Agaciro ka Bitcoin core gushika ubu ni 300 MB.


Ivy’ugucuruza biri muri Mempool ni ivy’agateganyo. Ntibikwiye gufatwa nk’ibidahinduka gushika bishizwe mu gice, kandi inyuma y’umubare kanaka w’ivyemezo. Ivyo bishobora kenshi gusubirizwa canke gusukurwa.
