---
term: UBUSHOBOZI BWO GUSOHOKA
---

Yerekeza ku mubare munini w'ama bitcoins node ishobora kwohereza biciye ku muhora wihariye kuri Lightning Network. Bivana n’amahera iyo node yashizeho ku muhora igihe yafungurwa, canke yaronse mu gihe c’ukwishura canke guca mu nzira y’umuravyo.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, rishobora guhindurwa ngo "capacité sortante".*
