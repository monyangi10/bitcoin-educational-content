---
term: BIP0094
---

Defines Testnet 4, a new Testnet network to replace Testnet 3 in 2024. The latter, which had been in service for 13 years, had become vulnerable to various attacks and had been the scene of misuse through fraudulent altcoin airdrops, disrupting the normally free distribution of TBTC. To correct these flaws, Testnet 4 incorporates 3 major adjustments to the consensus rules: modification of the 20-minute exception, a correction to the difficulty adjustment algorithm (*block storm fix*) and the introduction of protection against the "time warp" attack.
