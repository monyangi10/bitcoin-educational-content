---
term: BGP hijacking
definition: Igitero gihindura amayira y'ikure y'isi kugira ngo kiyobye urujya n'uruza rw'amakuru mu muhora mubi, gishobora gukoreshwa mu guheza utunode twa Bitcoin.
---

Igitero aho umukinyi w’umunyaruyeri akoresha amatangazo ya BGP kugira ngo asubire kurungika uruja n’uruza rwa Internet ku rubuga rwabo bwite. Mu kwigira nk’aho ari inkomoko yemewe y’ibice bimwebimwe vya IP Address, uwutera arashobora gufata, gukurikirana canke guhagarika uruja n’uruza ruja kuri izo aderesi.


BGP (*Border Gateway Protocol*) ni umurongo ugena ingene uruja n’uruza ruja hagati y’imihora itandukanye yigenga (AS) igize Internet y’isi yose. Ifata ingingo y’inzira amakuru afata kugira ngo ave ku ntumbero imwe aja ku yindi.


Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, BGP Hijacking ishobora gukoreshwa mu guhindura uruja n’uruza hagati y’ibice biri ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin kugira ngo zimwe muri zo zitandukanye. Ico gitero gishobora kandi gutera ama server ya Stratum akoreshwa n’ibidengeri vya Mining, kigahindura ubushobozi bw’abacukuzi bw’amabuye y’agaciro buja ku kigega c’uwugitera. Kubera ko abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro badakunda gusubira gutangura imashini zabo, iyo nzira yo guhindura inzira irashobora kumara igihe kirekire. Mwene ico gitero gishobora kutagamije gusa kuronka inyungu y’amahera ataco ihinduye mu kwiba impembo z’amabuye, ariko kandi coshobora no kuronka ububasha bwinshi ku rubuga mu gihe gito.
