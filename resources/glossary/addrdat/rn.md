---
term: Addr.dat

definition: Idosiye y'akadeni i Bitcoin Core yatanze amakuru ku mahoro ya mtandao. Yavunditswe na peers.dat kuva ku verisiyo 0.7.0.
---

Izina rya dosiye ya kera ikoreshwa muri Bitcoin Core kubika amakuru yerekeye urunganwe (i.e., amanode) mu rubuga node y'ukoresha yakoranye canke ishobora gukorana na yo. Iyi dosiye yasubiriwe na dosiye peers.dat itangura na verisiyo 0.7.0.
