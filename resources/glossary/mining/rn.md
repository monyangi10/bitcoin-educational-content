---
term: Gucukura
definition: Igikorwa co gukora icerekana igikorwa (proof of work) mu ntumbero yo gukingira umuyoboro no kwemeza ihererekanya ry'amafaranga.
---

Igikorwa co kugira uruhara mu nzira ya Bitcoin Proof-of-Work (Proof-of-Work). Proof-of-Work ni uburyo bwo kurwanya ibitero vya Sybil. Ni umushinge w’uburyo bwo kwumvikana bwa Nakamoto, ari ryo ngingo ngenderwako ikoreshwa mu gushinga amasezerano ku bijanye n’uburyo bumwe bw’ugutanga Ledger hagati y’ibice bitandukanye vy’uruja n’uruza.


Mu buryo bwihariye, Mining ni ugushaka agaciro, iyo kaciye mu gikorwa c’imibare c’imburakimazi, gatanga igisubizo kiri hasi y’umubare w’intumbero. Iyi ntumbero ya Proof-of-Work irahindurwa buri 2016 amabarabara n’ibihimba. Ivyo bizwi kw’izina ry’uguhindura ingorane. Umubare w’intumbero uragabanywa kugira ngo wongere ingorane za Mining, canke ukaduzwa kugira ngo ugabanye ingorane, bivanye n’iterambere ry’ububasha bwo guharura bwakoreshejwe n’abacukuzi mu kiringo c’imbere.





Ico gikorwa gikorwa n’abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro kirahembwa ku bubiko bwose bubereye bubonetse. Miner itsinda ironka impembo y’amahera, igizwe n’infashanyo y’ibarabara (uguhingura ama bitcoins mashasha ex nihilo), n’amahera yo gukoresha. Ubu, ingorane ya Proof-of-Work kuri Bitcoin ni uko Mining isaba ubushobozi buhambaye bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none kugira ngo ishobore gutsinda amabuye. Ivyo bituma kenshi bikenerwa ko umuntu agira ama chips yihariye y’ubuhinga bwa none yo gukora `SHA256d`, azwi nka ASICs, no kugira uruhara mu bidengeri vya Mining.
