---
term: Single-Use Seal
---

Refers to a cryptographic promise of commitment relating to an as yet unknown message, which will be revealed only once in the future and must be accessible to the whole of a specified audience. This mechanism aims to prevent the creation of multiple competing commitments for the same seal, thus guaranteeing the uniqueness of each validation. By imposing this single-use constraint, Single-Use Seal is used on RGB to avoid double-spending.
