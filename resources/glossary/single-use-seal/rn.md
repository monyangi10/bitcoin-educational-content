---
term: Single-Use Seal
---

Yerekeza ku sezerano ry’ubuhinga bwa none rya Commitment ryerekeye ubutumwa butaramenyekana, buzohishurirwa rimwe gusa muri kazoza kandi butegerezwa gushikira abantu bose bamwebamwe. Ubwo buryo bufise intumbero yo kubuza gushinga amasezerano menshi ahiganwa ku Seal imwe, gutyo bikaba vyemeza ubudasa bw’ukwemeza kwose. Mu gushinga iyo nzira yo gukoresha rimwe, Single-Use Seal ikoreshwa kuri RGB kugira ngo yirinde Double-spending.
