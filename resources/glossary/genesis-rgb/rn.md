---
term: Genesis (RGB)
---

Mu bijanye n'amasezerano ya RGB, Genesis yerekeza ku makuru y'intango, ayoborwa na Schema yihariye, ishiraho intango ya Contract iyo ari yo yose. Aya makuru ni Anchor point aho amasezerano y’ubwenge ava n’ibimenyetso vya RGB bicungirwa. Bishobora kugereranywa n’iciyumviro ca *Genesis Block* kuri Bitcoin, canke n’iciyumviro ca Coinbase Transaction, ariko hano ku rugero rwa *client-side* na RGB token.
