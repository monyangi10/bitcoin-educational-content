---
term: Genesis (RGB)
---

In the context of the RGB protocol, Genesis refers to the set of initial data, governed by a specific schema, which establishes the starting state of any contract. This information is the anchor point from which smart contracts evolve and RGB tokens are managed. It can be likened to the *Genesis Block* concept on Bitcoin, or to the Coinbase transaction concept, but here at the *client-side* and RGB token level.
