---
term: Kuremeshwa
definition: Igitigiri c'ibice vyacukuwe inyuma y'icirimo ivunjisha, kikerekana urugero rwo gukingira no kudahinduka kwaryo.
---

Yerekeza ku mubare w’amabuye igikorwa co gucuruza kivyungukirako ku mutekano wavyo. Iyo igikorwa gitangajwe ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, kibanza kuguma kirindiriye mu bice vy’imirongo. Hanyuma igashirwa mu gice gifise akamaro na Miner. Kuri iyi ntambwe, iyo nzira y’ubudandaji iraherutse kwongerwa kuri Blockchain, gutyo iraheza ikangukira ku kwemezwa kwayo kwa mbere. Iyo habonetse igice gishasha hejuru y’igice kirimwo ivy’ugucuruza bivugwa, kizoronka icemezo ca kabiri, n’ibindi. Igipande cose gishasha gicukuwe hejuru y’igipande kirimwo iyo nzira y’ugucuruza, kikaba ari ikimenyamenya gishasha. Mu kubara igitigiri c’ivyemezo vy’ugucuruza, turashobora kugereranya ingorane zoshobora gukurwaho kubera ugusubira gutunganya. Umubare w'ivyemezo utuma dushobora guca urubanza ku rugero rw'ukudahinduka kw'ibikorwa ku Blockchain.
