---
term: RPOW
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'"Ibimenyamenya vy'akazi bishobora gusubirwamwo". Ni uburyo bw’amahera y’ubuhinga bwa none biciye mu gutanga ibimenyetso vyashinzwe ku bimenyamenya vy’ibikorwa, vyateguwe kandi bishirwa mu ngiro na Hal Finney mu 2004. RPoW yashizwe ahantu nk’iterambere ku vyiyumviro vy’inyigisho vy’amahera b n’inzahabu y’ibice. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ivyo, RPoW mu vy’ukuri yarashitse kandi iratangura. RPoW yari gushobora gufata ikibanza Bitcoin ifise uno musi. Ni wo mugambi w’amahera y’ubuhinga bwa none wari uteye imbere cane imbere y’uko Satoshi ivumburwa. Ariko rero, Bitcoin yararengeye RPoW mu gutorera umuti ibibazo bibiri bihambaye cane. Ica mbere, Bitcoin yashizeho uburyo bwo guhindura ingorane za Mining, uburyo bwari butaboneka muri RPoW, gutyo hakaba hariho ugutera imbere kw’ibiciro kubera ubushobozi bwa Mining bwariyongereye be n’umubare w’abacukuzi wariko uragwira. Ubwa kabiri, bitandukanye n’uko RPoW yizigira abakozi bo hagati, Bitcoin yashizeho uburyo bwo kwumvikana busanzwe. Ubwo buryo bushingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako y’uko ama node akorana ku ruzitiro n’ibikorwa vyinshi vyirundanijwe, gutyo bikaba bikuraho ivy’uko hakenewe abakozi bazwi. RPoW ntiyigeze ironka infashanyo ikenewe kugira ngo isohoke kandi yemerwe na bose. Udakunze b-amahera n’inzahabu, Satoshi Nakamoto ntiyigeze avuga RPoW, naho nyene iyo nzira yari hafi cane y’ivyo yahinguye.
