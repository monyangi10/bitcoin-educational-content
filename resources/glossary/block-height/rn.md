---
term: Uburebure bw'icitunza
definition: Inomero y'ukurikirana kw'igice bivanye n'igice ca Genèse, ikoreshwa mu kumenya igice mu ruhererekane.
---

Uburebure bw’ibarabara ni umubare w’urutonde rw’ibarabara ugereranije n’ibarabara rya mbere, rizwi kw’izina rya Genesis Block, rifise urutonde ku burebure bwa 0. Rigereranya umubare w’ibarabara ribanza ibarabara rivugwa.

Nk’akarorero, iyo ibuye rifise uburebure bw’amabuye 650.000, bisigura ko hari amabuye 650.000 yacukuwe imbere yaryo. Uburebure bw'ibuye bukoreshwa mu kumenya ibuye ry'umwihariko muri Blockchain.

Mu buryo butandukanye n’ivyo abantu benshi bemera, uburebure bw’amabuye ntibugena uruzitiro rufise akamaro, kuko uturongo dukorana ku ruzitiro rwarundanije igikorwa kinini, atari ngombwa ko ari uruzitiro rurerure kuruta izindi.
