---
term: AMAKORA BATAREMEZWE
---

Igikoresho ca Bitcoin camenyeshejwe ku rubuga ariko kitarashirwa mu gice ca Miner. Iguma mu mempools z’ibihimba, irindiriye kwakira ivyemezo. Kwemeza bishika iyo igikorwa co gucuruza gishizwe mu gice gifise akamaro congerewe kuri Blockchain. Igihe gifata kugira ngo igikorwa cemezwe kivana n’amahera y’igicuruzwa yishuwe be n’uko iyo sisitemu iguma ipfutse.
