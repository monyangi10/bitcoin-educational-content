---
term: BIP0017
---

Iciyumviro ca Luke Dashjr cari gihanganye na BIP12 na BIP16. BIP17 yashizeho opcode nshasha, `OP_CHECKHASHVERIFY`, yagenewe gushobora kugenzura inyandiko iri muri `scriptSig` n'iyindi Hash iri muri `scriptPubKey` imbere yo gufungura amafaranga. BIP16 (P2SH) yaje gukundwa kuruta BIP17 (CHV) inyuma y’igihe c’impaka zikomeye.
