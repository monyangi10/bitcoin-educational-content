---
term: Redeem
definition: Muri protocol ya RGB, igikorwa co kugarukana canke gukoresha Valency yamenyekanishijwe mw'isezerano.
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, muri State Extension, ijambo Redeem ryerekeza ku gikorwa co gusubiza canke gukoresha Valency yari yatangajwe mbere. Valency, nk’uburenganzira bwa bose bwanditswe muri leta ya Contract, buha uwuyigiramwo uruhara yemerewe ubushobozi bwo gusaba ko iyo leta yongererwa. Ubwo buryo rero buratuma habaho ugukoresha uburenganzira bwihariye busobanuwe mu mugambi wa Contract, bikaba vyerekana ko uruhande rufise ububasha bubereye ari rwo rwonyene rushobora gutanguza iyo mpinduka ihuye n’ivyo no gukoresha ubutunzi bujanye n’ivyo.
