---
term: NO2X

definition: Isekeza ryo mu 2017 rirwanya ituburwa ry'ingano y'ibibonwa ryari ryateguwe na SegWit2x.
---

Izina ry’iyi gahunda yatangujwe muri Myandagaro 2017 inyuma y’ugufunga soft fork SegWit, igamije kwemeza abakoresha ko badashobora gushiramwo incuro zibiri ubunini bw’ibarabara kuva kuri 1 MB gushika kuri 2 MB nk’uko vyategekanijwe mu guhindura SEGWIT2X.
