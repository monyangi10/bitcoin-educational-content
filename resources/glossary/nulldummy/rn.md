---
term: Nulldummy
definition: Itegeko risaba ko icitegerezo muri OP_CHECKMULTISIG kiba ari ubusa.
---

Itegeko ry'ukwemeranya ryashizweho na BIP147 muri SegWit Soft Fork risaba ko ikintu c'ikinyoma gikoreshwa mu `OP_CHECKMULTISIG` na `OP_CHECKMULTISIGVERIFY` kuba urutonde rw'ibice vy'ubusa (`OP_0`). Iyi ngingo yashizwe mu ngiro kugira ngo hakurweho umurongo w'ubushobozi mu kubuza agaciro ako ari ko kose atari `OP_0` k'iki kintu.
