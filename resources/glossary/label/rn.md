---
term: Label
definition: Akimenyetso gihabwa UTXO kugira ngo wibuke iyo yavuye canke iyo ija.
---

Ikete canke ibisobanuro vyashizwe kuri UTXO muri Bitcoin Wallet kugira ngo wibuke inkomoko yayo. Nk’akarorero, nimba mfise UTXO ivuye mu kugura P2P kuri Bisq na Charles, noshobora kuyiha ikimenyetso `Bisq Charles atari KYC`. Ivyo ni ibikorwa vyiza bifasha kwibuka inkomoko canke aho iyo UTXO igamije kuja, ivyo bikaba bifasha gucunga neza amafaranga no gutuma ubuzima bwite bugenda neza. Gushirako amazina birarushiriza kuba ivy’agaciro iyo bikoreshejwe n’ubugenzuzi bwa Coin. Nkako, mu kwemerera abakoresha gutandukanya no guhitamwo neza ama UTXO y’ibikorwa vyabo, iyo ngeso ifasha kwirinda gufatanya ama UTXO ava mu bibanza bitandukanye. Ivyo bigabanya ingorane zijanye n’Igiharuro c’Ivyinjira rusangi Ownership (CIOH), gishobora guhishura Ownership rusangi y’ivyinjizwa mu gikorwa.
