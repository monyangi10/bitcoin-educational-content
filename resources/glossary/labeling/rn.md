---
term: GUSHIRAHO IBIMENYETSO
---

Ivy'ugutanga ibisobanuro canke ikimenyetso ku UTXO yihariye muri Bitcoin Wallet. Nk'akarorero, nimba mfise UTXO iva kuri P2P naguze kuri Bisq na Charles, noshobora kuyindika ngo "`Bisq Charles atari KYC`".


Gushirako amazina ni akamenyero keza gafasha kwibuka inkomoko canke aho UTXO igamije kuja, gutyo bikaba vyorosha gucunga amafaranga no gutuma ubuzima bwite bugenda neza. Gushirako amazina birarushiriza kuba ivy’agaciro iyo bikoreshejwe n’ubugenzuzi bwa Coin. Nkako, mu kwemerera abakoresha gutandukanya no guhitamwo neza ama UTXO y’ibikorwa vyabo, iyo ngeso ifasha kwirinda gufatanya ama UTXO ava mu bibanza bitandukanye. Ivyo bigabanya ingorane zijanye n’Igiharuro c’Ivyinjira Rusangi Ownership (CIOH), gishobora guhishura Ownership rusangi y’Ivyinjizwa mu gucuruza.
