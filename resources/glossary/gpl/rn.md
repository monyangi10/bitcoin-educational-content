---
term: GPL
definition: Uruhusha rw'ubuhinga bwidegemvye rwemeza ubwidegemvye bwo gukoresha, guhindura no gukwiragiza.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera ya "*Uruhusha rusangi rwa GNU*". Ni urutonde rw’uruhusha rwo gukoresha porogarama z’ubuntu rwaremewe n’ishirahamwe ry’ubuhinga bwa porogarama z’ubuntu. Iraha abakoresha umwidegemvyo wo gukoresha, guhindura no gukwiragiza iyo porogarama. Ariko rero, GPL isaba ko porogarama iyo ari yo yose ikomoka canke yahinduwe na yo nyene ikwiragizwa hakurikijwe uruhusha rumwe, kugira ngo umwidegemvyo w’intango uzigame muri verisiyo zose za porogarama. GPL ikoreshwa kenshi mu migambi y'inkomoko yuguruye kandi rimwe na rimwe iboneka mu migambi ijanye na Bitcoin. Ariko rero, uruhusha rwa mbere rw'umukiriya wa Satoshi Nakamoto rwari MIT X11. Ivyo n’ubu ni ko biri kuri Bitcoin core.
