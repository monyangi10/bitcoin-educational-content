---
term: ASICBOOST
---

ASICBOOST ni uburyo bwo gutuma ubuhinga bugenda neza bwavumbuwe mu 2016, bugenewe gutuma Bitcoin Mining ikora neza hafi 20% mu kugabanya umubare w’imibare isabwa ku kigeragezo cose co gukora hashing y’umutwe w’ibarabara. Ubu buhinga bukoresha ikintu kimwe c’igikorwa ca SHA256 Hash, gikoreshwa kuri Mining, gikora amakuru mu bice. AsicBoost igumya kimwe muri ivyo bice bitahindutse mu ngeragezo nyinshi zo gukora hashing, bituma abacukuzi basubira gukoresha igice c'imibare ya kera, gutyo bikagabanya umubare wose w'imibare ikenewe kugira ngo umuntu aronke Hash ibereye.


ASICBOOST ishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo bubiri: ASICBOOST igaragara na ASICBOOST ipfutse. Igishushanyo ca ASICBOOST kigaragara kiboneka kuri bose, kuko kirimwo gukoresha umwanya wa `nVersion` w'ibarabara nk'i Nonce, ata guhindura `Nonce`. Ku rundi ruhande, igitabu Covert kigerageza guhisha ivyo bihinduka hakoreshejwe ibiti vya Merkle. Ariko rero, ubwo buryo bwa kabiri bwaciye bugira akamaro ahanini inyuma y’aho SegWit ikoreshejwe, ivyo bikaba vyatumye habaho Merkle Tree ya kabiri yongera igiciro co guharura, bikaba vyatumye habaho ugutuma ibintu bigenda neza.


Mu ncamake, ASICBOOST iremesha abacukuzi kwirinda gukora ibara ryuzuye rya SHA256 ku kigeragezo cose ca Hash mu gusubira gukoresha igice c’igikorwa, gutyo bigatuma igikorwa ca Mining cihuta.
