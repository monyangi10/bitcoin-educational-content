---
term: OP_TUCK (0X7D)

definition: Opcode yigana ibiri k’inyuma ku mupfundikizo ikabishyira mu kibanza ca gatatu.
---

Kopa ikintu kiri hejuru y'ikirundo maze akagishira hagati y'ikintu ca kabiri n'ica gatatu c'ikirundo. Nk'akarorero, nimba ikirundo ari:


```text
A
B
C
D
```


`OP_TUCK` izosubiramwo ikintu co hejuru `A` maze ikishire mu kibanza ca gatatu. Ivyo bizovamwo bizoba:


```text
A
B
A
C
D
```
