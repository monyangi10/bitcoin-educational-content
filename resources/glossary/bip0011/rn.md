---
term: BIP0011
---

BIP11, yashizweho na Gavin Andresen muri Ntwarante 2012, yasavye uburyo busanzwe bwo gukora amafaranga y'imikono myinshi kuri Bitcoin. Intumbero yayo yari iyo kwongereza umutekano w’ama bitcoins mu gusaba imikono myinshi kugira ngo umuntu yemererwe gucuruza.

BIP11 itanga ubwoko bushasha bw'inyandiko, yitwa "M-of-N Multisig," aho `M` igereranya umubare mutoyi w'imikono isabwa mu mfunguruzo za bose zishoboka `N`. Iryo tegeko ryakoresheje opcode `OP_CHECKMULTISIG` yariho, mbere ntiyari yemerewe hakurikijwe amategeko y'itegeko ry'ibikorwa rya Bitcoin.

Naho ubwo bwoko bw’inyandiko Multisig butagira ikintu butagikoreshwa cane ku bikoresho vy’amasakoshi vy’ukuri vy’amasinya menshi, kuko bwasubiriwe cane na P2SH na P2WSH, buracariho kandi buracari bukoreshwa mu ma meta-protocols amwamwe nk’Ibidodo. Ivyuma birashobora kandi guhitamwo kudatanga izo nzira za P2MS mu gushinga umurongo `permitbaremultisig=0`.


> ► *Muri iki gihe, iyo ngingo izwi kw'izina rya "bare-Multisig" canke "P2MS".*
