---
term: BIP0330
definition: Erlay, itunganywa ry'ikwirakwizwa ry'ivunjisha rigabanya ikoreshwa ry'umuyoboro hafi 40%.
---

Iciyumviro kizwi nka "*Erlay*", kigamije gutuma amafaranga ataremejwe akwiragizwa neza mu rubuga rwa Bitcoin.

Ubu, amafaranga akoreshwa aramenyeshwa ku bagenzi bose b’uruzitiro, ivyo bikaba bituma habaho ugusubira inyuma n’ugukoresha cane uburebure bw’uruzitiro.

BIP330 isaba ko iki kiganiro coba ku bantu 8, hanyuma bagakoresha uburyo bwo gusubiza hamwe kugira ngo bashobore gusangira neza amafaranga abuze.

Erlay igabanya ikoreshwa ry'uburebure bw'uruja n'uruza hafi 40% kandi ibuza ikoreshwa ry'ubwaguke bw'uruja n'uruza gutera imbere mu buryo bujanye n'umubare w'abagenzi bahuye n'urudodo.
