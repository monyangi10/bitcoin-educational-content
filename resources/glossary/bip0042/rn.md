---
term: BIP0042
---

Iciyumviro co kunoza Bitcoin core cakosoye akaga gatoyi mu rutonde rwa Block reward Halving. Iyo iyo nkoko itakosorwa, yari gutuma habaho amafaranga arenga miliyoni 21 z’ama bitcoins yari yateguwe.

Mu buryo bwihariye, inyuma y'aho ibice vyose birangiriye, uruziga rushasha rwo kurema Bitcoin rwari gushobora gutangura nko mu mwaka w'2214.

Itegeko ry’Ishingiro ririko rivugwa ryakoresheje igikorwa co guhindura ibiri ku gaciro k’impera ya Miner. Ico kibazo cavuye mu gukoresha iki gikorwa co guhindura mu gihe inyifato yari itasobanuwe hakurikijwe ingingo ngenderwako z’ururimi rwa C++. Guhindura umubare w'ibice 64 (`int64_t`) birenze ibice 63 iburyo bigwa muri iki kiciro c'inyifato idasobanutse.

Mu kode ya Bitcoin core, ivyo vyari gushobora kuba inyuma y’uguca ibice 64, ku burebure bw’ibarabara no. 13.440.000. Inyuma y'iyi nzira, inyifato y'uguhindura ibice ntiyasobanuwe, bisobanura ko amakoraniro atandukanye yoshobora gusobanura kode mu buryo butandukanye. Ivyo vyari gushobora gutuma habaho ingaruka zitamenyekana, harimwo n’ubushobozi bwo kurema ugutera imbere kw’ibiciro vy’ibiciro vy’ibiciro vy’ibiciro vy’ibiciro vy’ibiciro (Bitcoin) bitagira iherezo. BIP42 yarakosoye ico kibazo mu gutegeka ko Block reward ishirwa kuri zero inyuma y’uguca ibice 64, gutyo bikaba birinda gukoresha igikorwa co guhindura iburyo mu gihe c’inyifato idasobanutse.

Iryo hinduka, ryari Soft Fork, ryaratomoye inyifato y’itegeko rya Bitcoin core. Naho cari gikomeye cane, ico kibazo cakosowe na BIP42 nticaciye gica kigira ico gikoze, kuko nticari kugaragara gushika nko mu mwaka w’2214. Casohowe ku wa 1 Ndamukiza 2014, na Pieter Wuille, BIP42 iratandukanye n’ijwi ryayo ry’ugutwenga.
