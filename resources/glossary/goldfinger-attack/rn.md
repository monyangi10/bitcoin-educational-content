---
term: Igitero ca Goldfinger
definition: Igitero aho uwugize igikorwa agenzura hashrate irenga 50% kugira ngo ahindure isubizahamwe rya Bitcoin.
---

Ivyiyumviro ku bijanye n’ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin aho umukinyi w’umunyaruyeri agenzura ibice birenga 50% vy’ububasha bwose bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa Mining (*Hashrate*). Iyo umuntu afise ubwo butegetsi, arashobora gukoresha nabi uburyo bwo kwumvikana, akareka habaho ibikorwa bibi nk’ugukoresha amahera kabiri, aho ayo ma bitcoins nyene akoreshwa rimwe ku ruzitiro ruheza rugacika uruzitiro, hanyuma agakoreshwa ubwa kabiri ku ruzitiro rufise akamaro. Iyindi ntumbero y’igitero ca Goldfinger ni ugucengera amafaranga. Ariko rero, kugira ngo umuntu akore ico gitero bisaba amahera menshi, abantu, inguvu nyinshi be n’ubuhinga bwinshi, kandi bituma uwo muntu afise ububisha ashobora kuvumburwa imbere y’uko ico gitero kiba. Naho mu vyiyumviro bishoboka, igitero ca Goldfinger kuri Bitcoin kibonwa ko kidashoboka cane kubera ugusenyuka kwa Mining n’ububasha bwinshi bw’ubuhinga bw’ibarabara buriko burakoreshwa ubu.


> ► *Ico gitero na co nyene citwa “Igitero ca 51%”.*
