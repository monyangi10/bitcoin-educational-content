---
term: OP_PICK (0X79)

---

Isubiramwo ikintu ku kirundo maze akagishira hejuru y'ikirundo, bishingiye ku burebure bugaragazwa n'agaciro kari hejuru y'ikirundo imbere y'igikorwa. Nk'akarorero, iyo agaciro kari hejuru y'ikirundo ari `4`, `OP_PICK` izosubiramwo ikintu ca kane kuva hejuru y'ikirundo, kandi izoshira iyo kopi hejuru.
