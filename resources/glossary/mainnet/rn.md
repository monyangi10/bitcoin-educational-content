---
term: Mainnet
definition: Umuyoboro mukuru wa Bitcoin aho ihererekanya ry'amafaranga ry'ukuri rifise agaciro k'ubutunzi ribera.
---

Yerekeza ku rubuga nyamukuru aho amafaranga nyayo ya Bitcoin yandikwa kandi agashirwa mu ngiro. Mainnet ni urusobe rwa Bitcoin gusa. Udakunze testnets, regtests, n’ibimenyetso, Mainnet ikoresha amafaranga y’ibiceri afise agaciro nyakuri mu vy’ubutunzi.
