---
term: KRIPOTONGARAFI
---

Intonde irimwo ingingo ngenderwako, uburyo n’uburyo bwo guhindura amakuru, cane cane hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bw’imibare, kugira ngo umuntu ahishe insobanuro yayo, abuze gukoreshwa ata wemerewe, amenye neza ko ari ay’ukuri, canke abuze guhindurwa ataco amenye. Ivy’ugushiramwo amakuru bikubiyemwo gukoresha ibikorwa vya Hash, imikono y’ivyuma, n’ubuhinga bwo gushiramwo amakuru.
