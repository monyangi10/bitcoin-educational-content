---
term: Blind signature
---

Imikono y’impumyi ya Chaum ni uburyo bw’imikono y’ubuhinga bwa none aho uwuyisinye atamenya ibirimwo mu butumwa buriko burasinywa. Ariko rero, umukono uva muri ivyo urashobora kugenzurwa ugereranije n’ubutumwa bw’intango. Ubu buhinga bwateguwe n'umuhinga mu vy'amabanga David Chaum mu 1983.


Nk’akarorero, rimbura ishirahamwe rishaka ko inyandiko y’ibanga, nk’akarorero Contract, yemezwa ata co ivuze. Iyo sosiyete ikoresha uburyo bwo gupfuka inyandiko y’intango mu buryo bushobora gusubirwamwo. Ico cete gifise igipfukisho coherezwa ku kigo gitanga icemezo, kikaba gikoresha Blind signature kitabona ibirimwo. Iyo sosiyete imaze kwakira iyo nyandiko yashizweko umukono ipfutse, irakuraho umukono. Ico bivamwo ni inyandiko y’umwimerere yemejwe n’umukono w’ubutegetsi, ata butegetsi bwigera bumenya ibirimwo.


Gutyo, imikono ya Chaum ataco abona ituma inyandiko zishobora kwemezwa ata guhishura ibirimwo, gutyo bikaba bituma amakuru aguma ari ibanga be n’uko inyandiko ziguma zitagira aho zigarukira.


Mu Bitcoin, imigambi ya Blind signature ikoreshwa mu gushiramwo amabanki ya Chaumian (Cashu, Fedimint, n’ibindi), na cane cane mu masezerano ya Chaumian CoinJoin, kugira ngo umuhuzabikorwa ntashobore guhuza ikintu yinjijwe n’ico asohotse.
