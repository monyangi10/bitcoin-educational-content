---
term: BIP0009
definition: Uburyo bwo gushira mu ngiro soft forks aho abaminezi bashikiriza ko bashigikiye bakoresheje akamenyetso (bit) mu maburoko, isaba 95% y'ubwemerane kugira ishirwe mu ngiro.
---

Uburyo bwo gukoresha amaforogo ya Soft kuri Bitcoin bwashikirijwe mu 2015. Bwashizeho uburyo abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bagaragaza ko bashigikiye Soft Fork mu gushiramwo igice kinaka mu kibanza c’ihinduka ry’amabuye.

Soft Fork itegurwa hakurikijwe BIP9 irakora iyo 95% vy’amabuye mu kiringo c’amabuye yo mu 2016 (nk’indwi zibiri, bihuye n’igihe cose co guhindura ingorane) vyemejwe ikimenyetso. Inyuma y’ico kiringo co gufunga, igihe c’ubuntu c’inyongera kiratuma abacukuzi bitegurira iyo nkuru nshasha imbere y’uko ikora. Iyo 95% bitashitse mu kiringo c’agaciro kanini categekanijwe, Soft Fork irahebwa.

BIP9 iremeza ko amaforoko menshi ya Soft atangazwa icarimwe ariko igatanga ububasha bwinshi ku bacukuzi: iyo urugero rudashitsweko, Soft Fork itegekanijwe irananirwa gusa gukora. Ubu buryo bwakoreshejwe mu ntango kuri SegWit gushika BIP148 (Soft Fork ikoreshwa n’uwukoresha) ihinduye ubushobozi n’ugukoresha ku nguvu biciye kuri BIP91.
