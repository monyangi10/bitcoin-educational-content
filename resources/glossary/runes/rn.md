---
term: Runes
definition: Uburyo bwemerera gukora no kurungika amatungo ashobora gusubirizwa kuri Bitcoin.
---

Protocole yateguwe n’umugwi umwe na Ordinals ishoboza gukoresha amafaranga y’ubudandaji ya Bitcoin kugira ngo umuntu akoreshe ubuhinga bwo gucapura, gukora amafaranga, no gutanga amafaranga y’ubuhinga bwa none atari BTC. Udakunze inyandiko zisanzwe, aho igice cose ari ikidasanzwe, rune imwe imwe isa n’izindi, kandi rero irashobora guhindurwa, bikaba vyemerera kurema amafaranga y’ibanga atandukanye na BTC kuri Bitcoin. Ubutumwa bw'umurongo wa Rune, bwitwa runestones, bubikwa muri `OP_RETURN`.
