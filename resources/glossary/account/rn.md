---
term: Ikońti
definition: Mu muhasiguro wa HD, urwego rw'ibisubizo (ubugari 3) rubemerera kwinjiza urwego ruva mu kindi hamwe n'ayo mashanyalaze.
---

Mu bikoresho vya HD (Ivyiyumviro vy'ubukuru), konti igereranya igikomoka Layer ku burebure bwa 3 hakurikijwe BIP32. Buri konti igira inomero zikurikirana guhera kuri `/0'/` (inkomoko ikomeye, rero `/2^31/` canke `/2 147 483 648/`). Ni muri ubwo bwimbike bw’inkomoko ni ho `xpubs` zizwi cane ziri. Muri iki gihe, kenshi na kenshi konti imwe gusa ni yo ikoreshwa kuri HD Wallet. Ariko rero, mu ntango vyari vyagenewe gutandukanya ivyiciro bitandukanye vy’ikoreshwa muri Wallet imwe. Nk'akarorero, turavye inzira y'ugukomoka ku Taproot yo hanze (P2TR) yakira Address: `m/86'/0'/0'/0/0`, urutonde rwa konti ni rwa kabiri `/0'/`.
