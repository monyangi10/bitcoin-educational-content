---
term: Wallet
---

Igikoresho ca porogarama cagenewe canecane gukingira no gucunga imfunguruzo z’ibanga z’uwukoresha. Iyo Wallet ibitswe kandi icungiwe ku gikoresho ca porogarama ubwaco gishizwe ku mashini ikora vyinshi, yitwa "Hot Wallet". Ku rundi ruhande, iyo ibitswe muri porogaramu, ubwayo ishizwe ku gikoresho c'ubuhinga gusa c'ico gikorwa kandi idahuye na interineti, rero yitwa "Cold Wallet". Wallet cane cane iremeza gukoresha imfunguruzo z’ibanga kugira ngo umuntu ashire umukono ku bikorwa, gutyo agashitsa ibisabwa vyo gukoresha amafaranga y’ibiceri.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, benshi bakoresha ata guca ku ruhande ubuhinduzi bw'icongereza "Wallet" kugira ngo bavuge Wallet.*
