---
term: SLIP

---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'"Ivyiyumviro vyo gutegura neza amalaboratwari ya Satoshi". Ni urutonde rw’ivyifuzo bigamije kunoza canke guhuza ikoreshwa rya Bitcoin, bikomoka kuri SatoshiLabs, ishirahamwe riri inyuma y’ibikoresho vya Trezor. Ivyo vyiyumviro akenshi bikora nk’ivyo kwagura BIP (Ivyiyumviro vyo guteza imbere Bitcoin), bifise intumbero yo gutuma ingingo mfatirwako zisanzwe zitunganywa. Bidondora neza ingingo z’ubuhinga zafashwe na SatoshiLabs zidahuye na BIPs, ariko ziguma zifise akamaro ku bandi bakora porogarama wallet canke ibikoresho wallet, cane cane mu gufasha mu gutuma imigenderanire igenda neza.
