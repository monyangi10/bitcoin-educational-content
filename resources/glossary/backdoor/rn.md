---
term: URUGIRO RW'INYUMA
---

Urugi rw’inyuma ni uburyo bw’ibanga butanga uburenganzira bwo gushika ku rutonde rwa mudasobwa, porogarama, igikorwa, ubuhinga canke amakuru, ataco uciye mu buryo busanzwe bwo kwemeza canke bwo gucungera umutekano. Mu buryo butandukanye n’akaga k’umutekano, inzugi z’inyuma zishirwa mu kode y’inkomoko n’abahinguzi b’ibintu bibi. Bishobora gukoreshwa mu gutata, gukoresha canke kwiba amakuru y'agaciro.
