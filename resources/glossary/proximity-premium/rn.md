---
term: Proximity premium
definition: Akaryo k'amahera ku bamina biturowe no kugabanya ubutere bw'itumatumanako ryo ku muhora.
---

Mu bijanye na Bitcoin Mining, yerekeza ku nyungu y’amahera abacukuzi baronka mu kugabanya igihe co guhanahana amakuru ku rubuga. Abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bari hafi cane bararonka amabuye mashasha afise akamaro vyihuta, ivyo bikaba bituma bahagarika gutakaza ubutunzi ku butaka bushobora gukoreshwa buri ku ishami rifise ibikorwa bike vyirundanijwe. Ukwo kugabanya igihe co guhagarara bituma abacukuzi bashobora kuronka inyungu nyinshi, ivyo bikaba bituma mu bisanzwe bakorana mu karere kugira ngo bashobore kuronka inyungu nyinshi.


Ico kibanza co hafi ni akaga kavukiye mu bikorwa vya Bitcoin, gakomoka ku buryo bubiri: urutonde rw’amabuye n’uko Block reward ihabwa gusa Miner abona amabuye. Ico kintu kiremesha ibikorwa vya Mining gushirwa hamwe.
