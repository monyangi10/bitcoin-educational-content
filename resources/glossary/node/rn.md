---
term: NODE
---

Mu rubuga rwa Bitcoin, node (canke "node" mu congereza) ni mudasobwa ikoresha umukiriya wa porotokole ya Bitcoin (nk'akarorero Bitcoin core). Igira uruhara mu nzira mu kubungabunga kopi ya Blockchain, igatanga no kugenzura amafaranga n’amabuye mashasha, kandi mu buryo bushoboka, ikagira uruhara mu nzira ya Mining. Igitigiri c’ibihimba vyose vya Bitcoin kigereranya uruja n’uruza rwa Bitcoin ubwarwo.


Hari ubwoko bwinshi bw’ibihimba muri Bitcoin, harimwo ibihimba vyuzuye n’ibihimba vy’umuco. Ivyuma vyuzuye bigumya kopi yuzuye ya Blockchain, bikagenzura ibikorwa vyose n’amabuye hakurikijwe amategeko y’uguhurizako, kandi bikagira uruhara n’umwete mu gukwiragiza ibikorwa n’amabuye ku rubuga rwose. Ku rundi ruhande, amanode y’umuco, canke amanode ya SPV (*Simple Payment Verification*), agumana gusa imitwe y’amabuye kandi yizigira amanode yuzuye kugira ngo aronke amakuru y’ibikorwa.


> ► *Bamwe kandi baratandukanya ivyo bita "pruned nodes" ("pruned node" mu congereza). Ivyo ni ibice vyuzuye, bikura kandi bikagenzura amabuye yose yo mu gice ca Genesis, ariko bikagumana gusa amabuye aherutse gusohoka mu mutwe.*
