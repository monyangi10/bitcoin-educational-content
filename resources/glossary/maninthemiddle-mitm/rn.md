---
term: UMUNTU-WO-HAGATI (MITM)
---

Igitero aho umukinyi w’ububisha yishira mu mpisho hagati y’abantu babiri bariko baravugana, kugira ngo afate kandi ashobore guhindura ubutumwa buhanahana, ata n’umwe muri bo abona ko ariho.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, ryitwa "igitero c'umuntu wo mu kibano" canke "HDM".*
