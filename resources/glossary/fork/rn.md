---
term: Fork
definition: Iyubakwa ry'ururere rw'inkingi canke ihinduka ry'amategeko ya Bitcoin.
---

Ijambo "Fork" rifise insobanuro nyinshi mu bijanye n'ijambo Bitcoin. Bishobora kwerekeza ku gucapura urusobe rw’ibihimba mu migwi myinshi itandukanye, bikajana mu kurema ama blockchains menshi atandukanye, canke guhindura amategeko y’amasezerano, canke vyose icarimwe. Kugira ngo tworohereze, turashobora gutandukanya ivyiciro 4 nyamukuru vy’amaforogo:

**Fork:** kamere ibaho iyo hariho uguhiganwa kw’igihe gito hagati y’amabuye abiri yavumbuwe igihe kimwe ku burebure bumwe. Iyi Fork ishobora gushika ku bibanza vyinshi. Ubwo bwoko bwa Fork buratorera umuti mu buryo busanzwe iyo umwe mu minyororo ubaye muremure kuruta uwundi (hakaba hariho ibikorwa vyinshi vyirundanijwe), bikaba bituma haba ugusubira gutunganya. Ukwo gusubira gutunganya ibintu kugaragara n’aho ama node yose yongera kwemeranya kuri Blockchain imwe;

**Ikode Fork:** irimwo guhingura amafaranga mashasha y’ubuhinga bwa none avuye muri kode y’inkomoko ya Bitcoin, mu gutanguza Blockchain nshasha avuye mu gice ca Genesis;

**Hard Fork:** igereranya uguhindura amategeko ya Bitcoin, adahuye n’amaverisiyo ya kera, mu gukuraho amategeko canke mu kworohereza ayasanzweho. Ivyo bituma habaho iminyororo ibiri itandukanye kandi idahuye iyo ata node zose zivuguruwe. Iryo shirahamwe rero rica rigabanywamwo ibice bibiri: abafata amategeko mashasha n’abagumya aya kera;

**Soft Fork:** irimwo impinduka zijanye n’inyuma zongerako amategeko canke zigatuma ayasanzweho arushirizaho guhagarika, ataco bituma urubuga ruca ibice. Ivyuma bitafata ayo mategeko mashasha birashobora kubandanya bikurikira uruhererekane nk’urw’ayandi, igihe gusa ubushobozi bwinshi bw’urubuga bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none bushigikira uruhererekane rwavuguruwe.
