---
term: ISHAMI (GIT)
---

Mu bijanye na Git, ishami rigereranya ugutandukana n'urugendo rw'ibikorwa nyamukuru, bikaba bishoboza iterambere rihuye ataco rihinduye ku ishami ry'intango (rikenshi ryitwa `master` canke `main`). Amashami atuma abahinguzi bashobora guhindura, kugerageza ibiranga, no kugerageza mu kibanza kiri ukwaco imbere y’uko amaherezo babishira mu mugambi nyamukuru biciye mu gufatanya.
