---
term: Urungano (tx)
definition: Igikorwa canditse muri blockchain kura uburenganzira bw'abitcoin ku muntu umwe cyangwa benshi kikabuha undi cyangwa abandi.
---

Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ugucuruza (mu ncamake "TX") ni igikorwa canditswe kuri Blockchain gihindura Ownership y'ama bitcoins kuva ku kintu kimwe canke vyinshi vyinjizwa gushika ku kintu kimwe canke vyinshi bisohoka. Igikorwa kimwekimwe cose gikoresha Ivyiza Bitakoreshejwe (UTXOs) nk’ibintu vy’injiza, ari vyo biva mu bikorwa vya kera, kandi kikarema UTXOs nshasha nk’ibintu vy’injiza, bishobora gukoreshwa nk’ibintu vy’injiza mu bikorwa vyo muri kazoza.


Injiza yose irimwo ivyerekeye igisohoka kiriho hamwe n'inyandiko y'umukono (`scriptSig`) yuzuza ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha (`scriptPubKey`) vyashinzwe n'igisohoka ivuga. Ivyo nivyo bituma bitcoins zifungurwa. Ivyo bisohoka bisobanura uburyo bushasha bwo gukoresha amahera (`scriptPubKey`) ku bitcoins vyimuriwe, kenshi mu buryo bw’urufunguzo rwa bose canke Address aho bitcoins ubu zifatanye.
