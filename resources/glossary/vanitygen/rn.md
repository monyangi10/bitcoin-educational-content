---
term: Vanitygen
definition: Porogaramu y'ubuntu ikorera muri command-line ituma hashobora gukorwa aderesi zo kwakirirako zahiswemo n'umukoresha (vanity address).
---

Porogaramu ya mbere y’umurongo w’amabwirizwa yuguruye ikoreshwa mu guhingura amaderesi y’ukwakira (*vanity Address*). Ikora mu gutanga no kugerageza amafunguro abiri biciye mu kugerageza no gukosa gushika Address yakira ihuye n'ingingo ngenderwako n'ukoresha (nk'urutonde runaka rw'ibimenyetso vyihariye) ibonetse. Vanitygen isaba ubuhinga bukomeye bwo guharura, cane cane ku bijanye n’urutonde rurerure.
