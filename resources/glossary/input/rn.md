---
term: Izinjira
definition: UTXO ikoreshwa nk'isoko y'amahera mu ihererekanya rya Bitcoin, ikerekana ibyasohotse (output) imbere.
---

Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, inyungu iri mu gikorwa c’ubudandaji yerekeza ku UTXOs (*Ibisohoka mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji bitakoreshejwe*) bikoreshwa nk’amahera y’intango kugira ngo bihaze ibiva mu bikorwa. Ivyo winjiza vyose birimwo ibisobanuro vy’ama UTXO ya kera, azoca afungurwa n’ugucuruza. Ivyo bikoresho bikoreshwa mu kugaburira ama UTXO mashasha azoremwa nk’ibisohoka mu bikorwa, kandi bishobora gukoreshwa mu bikorwa vyo muri kazoza.


Uruhara rw’isoko rya Bitcoin rero ni ugukoresha UTXO nk’ibintu vyinjizwa, no kurema UTXO nshasha nk’ibintu bisohoka. Itandukaniro hagati y’ivyo bibiri rihuye n’amahera y’ugucuruza ashobora gusubizwa na Miner yemeza iyo block.


Uvuye mu buryo bwagutse, mu vy'ubuhinga bwa mudasobwa, ijambo "input" muri rusangi ryerekeza ku makuru atangwa ku gikorwa, ku buhinga canke ku sisitemu nk'ibikoresho canke amakuru asabwa kugira ngo umuntu akore igikorwa canke igiharuro. Muri ubwo buryo, ijambo rikoreshwa cane mu kudondora ikintu cose gihabwa igikorwa kugira ngo umuntu aronke igisubizo canke umusaruro. Nk'akarorero, iyo amakuru aciye mu gikorwa ca Hash, ayo makuru yitwa "input".
