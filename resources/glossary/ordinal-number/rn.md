---
term: Ordinal number
definition: Ikimenyetso kihariwe gihabwa buri sat bivanye n’uko yacukuwe muri porotokore ya Ordinals.
---

Mu rwego rw’amasezerano ya Ordinals, ni ikimenyetso kidasanzwe gihabwa sat yose ishingiye ku rutonde rwayo rwa Mining mu gice. Ivyo biharuro bituma izo Sats zidashobora guhinduka hakurikijwe amategeko ya Ordinals, gutyo bikaba bishoboka ko izo Sats zidashobora guhindurwa.
