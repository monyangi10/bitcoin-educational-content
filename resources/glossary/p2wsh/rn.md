---
term: P2WSH
definition: Script ya SegWit yo mu mizi iziba bitcoin kuri hash ya script, aderesi bc1q.
---

P2WSH ni ijambo ry'ikirundi risobanura *Iriha Inyandiko y'Icabona Hash*. Ni urugero rw'inyandiko rusanzwe rukoreshwa mu gushinga ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha amahera kuri UTXO. P2WSH yatangujwe n'ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa SegWit muri Myandagaro 2017.


Iyi nyandiko isa na P2SH (*Ishura ku nyandiko ya bose Hash*), kuko nayo ifunga ama bitcoins ashingiye kuri Hash y’inyandiko. Itandukaniro riri mu kuntu imikono n’inyandiko bishirwa mu gucuruza. Kugira ngo umuntu akoreshe ama bitcoins kuri ubwo bwoko bw’inyandiko, uwuyaronka ategerezwa gutanga inyandiko y’umwimerere, yitwa `witnessScript` (ingana na `redeemscript`), hamwe n’imikono isabwa. Ubwo buryo buratuma hashirwa mu ngiro uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera buteye imbere cane, nk’ubuhinga bwo gukoresha amahera menshi.


Mu bijanye na P2WSH, amakuru y'inyandiko y'umukono (`scriptWitness`, angana na `scriptSig`) akurwa mu miterere y'ibikorwa vya kera aja mu gice gitandukanye citwa `Witness`. Ivyo ni ikintu kiranga ivugurura rya SegWit (*Icabona Gitandukanijwe*) rifasha gukingira ukuntu umukono ushobora guhinduka. Ibikorwa vya P2WSH muri rusangi birazimvye cane mu bijanye n’amahera ugereranyije n’ibikorwa vya Legacy, kuko igice kiri mu cabona kizimvye cane.


Aderesi za P2WSH zandikwa hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwa `Bech32` n'umubare w'isuzuma mu buryo bwa kode ya BCH. Izo aderesi zama zitangura na `bc1q`. P2WSH ni verisiyo 0 SegWit isohoka.
