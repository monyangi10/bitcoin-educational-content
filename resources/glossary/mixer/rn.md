---
term: Mixer

definition: Igikorwa gishira hamwe bitcoin z'abakoresha benshi mu rucantege kugira ngo basibe amateka yazo.
---

Igikorwa kimwe co guca amateka y’igiceri ca Bitcoin mu kuvanga amahera ava ku bakoresha benshi. Udakunze CoinJoin, aho abakoresha bagumana ububasha ku mahera yabo mu gihe cose c’uguvanga, umuvunji afata mu gihe gito amafaranga y’abakoresha, akayavanga n’ay’abandi bakoresha, hanyuma akarungika ayo mahera ku ma aderesi mashasha.


Ubu, gukoresha ama blenders ya bitcoin biriko birazimangana kubera amafaranga y’i Chaumian, atanga umutekano mwinshi mu gukuraho ivy’ukwizigirana. Udakunze blenders, aho abakoresha bategerezwa kwemera bitcoins zabo umukozi ashobora kuziba canke akabika amakuru ku bijanye n’ingene blender ikora, coinjoins ntizitanga ivyo bibazo.
