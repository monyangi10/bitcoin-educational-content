---
term: Multi-path payments (mpp)
definition: Ubuhinga bwa Lightning bugabura ihererekanya mu bice vyinshi binyura mu nzira zitandukanye.
---

Ijambo rusangi ry’ubuhinga bwose bwo kwishura kuri Lightning bushobora gutuma igikorwa gishobora gucagurwa mu bice bitobito vyinshi maze kigaca mu nzira zitandukanye. Mu yandi majambo, igice cose c’ukwishura gifata inzira itandukanye y’urudodo. Ivyo bituma bishoboka kurenga imipaka y’amahera ku muhora umwe wo mu nzira.


Ukwishura mu nzira nyinshi na kwo nyene kuratanga inyungu ntoyi mu bijanye n’ibanga, kuko bigorana cane ko uwuvyihweza ahuza igice cose c’ukwishura n’ugucuruza kwose. Ariko rero, mu nsiguro yayo y’ishimikiro, ibice vyose birasangiye ibanga rimwe ku ma HTLC, ivyo bikaba bishobora gutuma iyo nzira ishobora gukurikirana iyo umuntu yihweza ariho ku nzira nyinshi. Ikindi, kuko ibanga ari ryo ryonyene ku mice yose y’ukwishyura, si atome. Ivyo bisigura ko ibice bimwebimwe vyo kwishura bishobora gushirwa mu ngiro neza, mu gihe ibindi bishobora kunanirwa. Ivyo bibazo bikosorwa n'"Ukwishura kw'inzira nyinshi z'atome".
