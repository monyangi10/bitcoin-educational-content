---
term: Timelock

definition: Uburyo bw'amasezerano akoresha muduga (smart contract) bushyiraho igihe ntarengwa kugira ngo itunshwa ry'amafaranga rishyirwe mu gitabo.
---

Igikoresho c’amasezerano y’ubwenge gishobora gushinga ivyangombwa bishingiye ku gihe bitegerezwa gushitswako kugira ngo igikorwa co kwongerwa ku gice. Hari ubwoko bubiri bw'ibifungurwa vy'igihe kuri Bitcoin:


- Igihe c’ugufunga, kigaragaza umwanya nyawo imbere y’uko igikorwa co gucuruza kidashobora gushirwa mu gice;

*Igihe c'ugufunga, gishinga igihe co guteba kuva kwemera ibikorwa vya kera.*

Igihe gishobora gusobanurwa nk'itariki yerekanwa mu gihe ca Unix canke nk'umubare w'ibarabara. Ubwa nyuma, igihe co gufunga gishobora gukoreshwa ku gisohoka c’ibikorwa hakoreshejwe opcode yihariye mu nyandiko yo gufunga (`OP_CHECKLOCKTIMEVERIFY` canke `OP_CHECKSEQUENCEVERIFY`), canke ku gikorwa cose hakoreshejwe ivyicaro vy’ibikorwa vyihariye (`nLockTime` canke `nSequence`).
