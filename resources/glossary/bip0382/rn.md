---
term: BIP0382
---

Itangaza ibikorwa `wpkh(URUPFURO)` (Ishura-Icabona-PubKey-Hash) na `wsh(INYANDIKA)` (Ishura-Icabona-Inyandiko-Hash) ku bisobanuro. Ivyo bikorwa bihuza ingene ubwoko bw'inyandiko za SegWit buserukirwa mu bisobanuro. BIP382 yashizwe mu ngiro hamwe n'izindi BIPs zose zijanye na Descriptor (uretse BIP386) muri verisiyo 0.17 ya Bitcoin core.
