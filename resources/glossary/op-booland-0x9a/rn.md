---
term: OP_BOOLAND (0X9A)

definition: Opcode ikora ibijanye n'ubwenge bwa 'NA' (AND) ku gaciro k'ibintu bibiri vya mbere kurutonde rw'ibintu.
---

Yigana inyifato y'irembo ry'ubwenge `AND`. Bifata agaciro kabiri hejuru y'ikirundo maze bikagarura `1` gusa iyo agaciro kose katari zero. Ahandi ho, igarura `0`.
