---
term: Mixing

definition: Uburyo bw'ikintu aho ibintu bishizwe hamwe mu rugero rungana, bukoreshwa mu gupima ubwiza bwa coinjoins.
---

Mu bijanye n’imibare rusangi, guvanga canke guvanga vyerekeye umutungo w’urutonde rw’inguvu aho, haciye igihe kinaka, ibice vyose vy’ikibanza c’intango bishobora mu vyiyumviro guvanga n’ikindi gice ico ari co cose. Guvanga bisigura ko ikibanza c’igice canke uko urutonde ruri bihinduka mu buryo bw’uko ugukwiragira kwaco muri kazoza kutajanye n’ugukwiragira kwarwo kwa mbere, gutyo bigashika ku rwego aho ibiranga urutonde rw’intango bigabanywa mu buryo bumwe mu kibanza cose c’urutonde. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, iki ciyumviro gishobora gukoreshwa mu gusuzuma uburyo bwo kuvanga ibiceri nk’ivy’uguvanga ibiceri.


> ► *Mu congereza, tuvuga ivy'"uguvanga". Bamwe mu ba bitcoiners batandukanya iciyumviro co guvanga n’ingendo yo gukora coinjoin. Nkako, bavuga ko guvanga yerekeza ku kuvanga ibiceri bikorwa n’ishirahamwe rifise ayo mahera, bitandukanye n’ivyo gukora coinjoins aho uwubikoresha yama afise ayo mahera. Ariko rero, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, iyo ntandukaniro nta co ivuga, kuko coinjoin birimwo vy’ukuri ukuvanga mu busobanuro bw’imibare bw’iryo jambo.*
