---
term: Ath (all-time high)
definition: Ibiciro byerekana Bitcoin bitari kandi byigize mu magara yibiciro nka dolare.
---

Yerekeza ku rugero rwo hejuru cane rwashitsweko n’ikintu kiriko kirigwa. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ATH akenshi yerekeza ku giciro kinini kuruta ibindi vyose vyigeze vyandikwa ku bijanye n’amahera y’amahera mu kiringo kinaka.
