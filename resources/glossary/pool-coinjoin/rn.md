---
term: Pool (coinjoin)

definition: Umugwi w'abakoresha bavanga UTXO zabo zifise amafaranga angana mu kuryohora ibanga.
---

Mu bijanye n’ama coinjoins, pool ni umugwi w’abakoresha bemera guvanga UTXO zabo mu buryo bumwe kugira ngo bavune amateka yabo. Igitero ca CoinJoin gishiraho umubare udahinduka ku ma UTXO ari muri iyo nama, ivyo bikaba bituma ivyo kwinjira n’ivyo gusohoka mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji vy’amahera bihuye, gutyo bikaba bibuza guhuzwa. Uko abaje mu kidengeri baba benshi, ni ko n’ibiceri vyinshi bishobora kuronka anonsets nyinshi mu gukora inzinguzingu nyinshi z’ugusubiramwo.
