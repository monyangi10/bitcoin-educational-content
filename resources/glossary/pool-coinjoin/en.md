---
term: POOL (CoinJoin)
---

In the context of coinjoins, a pool is a group of users who agree to mix their UTXOs homogeneously to break their histories. A CoinJoin pool imposes a fixed amount for the participating UTXOs, which ensures that the inputs and outputs of coinjoin transactions are identical, and thus prevents them from being linked. The more participants there are in a pool, the more coins can earn large anonsets by performing multiple remix cycles.
