---
term: BISHIRA MU GIHE (IBUZIMA)
---

Yerekeza ku gice kitagira abana: igice gifise akamaro, ariko kidashizwe mu ruhererekane nyamukuru rwa Bitcoin. Bishika iyo abacukuzi babiri babonye ibuye ry’agaciro riri ku burebure bw’uruzitiro rumwe mu kiringo gitoyi maze bakaritangaza ku rubuga. Amaherezo ama nodes ahitamwo igice kimwe gusa co gushiramwo mu ruhererekane, hakurikijwe ingingo ngenderwako y'uruhererekane rufise ibikorwa vyinshi, bigatuma urundi "rudakoreshwa". Inzira ijana mu guhingura ibuye rya kera ni iyi ikurikira:


- Abacukuzi babiri bararonka ibuye ry’agaciro riri ku burebure bumwe bw’uruzitiro mu kiringo gitoyi. Reka tuvyite `Ibarabara A` na `Ibarabara B`;
- Umwe wese atangaza ububiko bwiwe ku rubuga rwa node Bitcoin. Buri node ubu ifise amabuye 2 ku burebure bumwe. Hariho rero iminyororo ibiri ibereye;
- Abacukuzi barabandanya kurondera ibimenyamenya vy’ibikorwa vy’amabuye akurikira, ariko kugira ngo babikore, bategerezwa guhitamwo ata kabuza ibuye rimwe gusa hagati ya `Ibuye A` n’Ibuye B` bazocukurako;
- Miner amaherezo ibona ububiko bubereye hejuru ya `Ububiko B`. Reka tuvyite `Ibarabara B+1`;
- Bamenyesha `Block B+1` ku nzira z’uruja n’uruza;
- Kubera ko izo nzira zikurikira uruhererekane rurerure kuruta izindi (rufise igikorwa cirundanirijwe cane), zizogereranya ko `Uruhererekane B` ari rwo ruzokurikira;
- Bazoheba `Block A` itagifise uruhererekane nyamukuru. Gutyo ryacitse ibuye ritagikoreshwa.

> ► *Mu congereza, ryitwa "Stale Block". Mu gifaransa, rishobora kandi kwitwa "bloc périmé" canke "bloc abandonné". Naho nyene ntavyemeranya n'iryo koreshwa, bamwebamwe mu bakoresha ijambo "bloc orphelin" kugira ngo bavuge ico mu vy'ukuri ari block itagikoreshwa.*
