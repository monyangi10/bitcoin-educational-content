---
term: ON-GRID
---

Refers to a Bitcoin mining operation that is connected to the general electrical grid. On-grid miners connect to the conventional power grid, also used by residential consumers, to power their ASICs. This method offers a stable power source, but is often more expensive than off-grid mining, due to more limited negotiation margins.

Sometimes, on-grid mining farms participate in demand-side management programs. They adjust their energy consumption according to fluctuations in electricity production and consumption on the grid, to maintain balance and avoid wastage or blackout.
