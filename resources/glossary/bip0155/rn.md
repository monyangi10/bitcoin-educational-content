---
term: BIP0155
definition: Uburyo bushasha bw'ubutumwa addrv2 bushigikiye adresse za network nini nka TorV3 na I2P.
---

BIP155 izana uburyo bushasha bw'ubutumwa bwo gusangira amaderesi y'ibice vyemeza amahuzu yinjira. Ico ciyumviro kiremesha gushigikira ubunini bunini bwa Address, cane cane kugira ngo bishobore kworohereza ugushiramwo amasezerano y’urubuga azoza n’amaderesi ya IP akomeye cane nka TorV3 canke I2P. Ivyo bimenyekana kandi nka `addrv2`.
