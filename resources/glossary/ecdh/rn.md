---
term: ECDH
---

Uburyo bw’urufunguzo rw’ibanga Exchange bushingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako z’urufunguzo rwa Diffie-Hellman Exchange, ariko hakoreshejwe ibigobe vy’uruzitiro kugira ngo hatangwe umutekano wo ku rugero rwo hejuru n’ubunini bw’urufunguzo buto. Iyi porotokole iremesha ababiri gukora generate ibanga basangiye bakoresheje urufunguzo rwabo rwa bose n’urw’ibanga, ata na rimwe bategerezwa gukora Exchange urufunguzo rw’ibanga bonyene. Ibanga ry’abantu bose rishobora rero gukoreshwa mu gupfuka amakuru akurikira. Iyi algorithm rimwe na rimwe iboneka mu vyiyumviro vyo kunoza Bitcoin, cane cane BIP47 canke BIP352 yo gukura amaderesi mashasha y’ukwakira avuye ku kimenyetso kidahinduka.
