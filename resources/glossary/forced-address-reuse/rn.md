---
term: Address GUSUBIRAMWO GUKORESHA
---

Igitero kirimwo kohereza utugingo ngengabuzima dutoduto tw’ama bitcoins ku ma aderesi menshi y’ukwakira. Intumbero y’uwo muterabwoba ni uguhatira abayaronka gufatanya ayo mahera n’ayandi ma UTXO. Uwo muterabwoba aca akurikirana ingene izo nkoko ntoyi zizogenda muri kazoza, afise intumbero yo gukora amatsinda y’amaderesi, ni ukuvuga kumenya nimba amaderesi menshi ari ay’ikigo kimwe. Mu guhuza amakuru yaronkejwe mu gihe c’igitero n’ayandi makuru be n’ubuhinga bukoreshwa mu gusesangura uruhererekane, birashoboka ko uwutera amenya ibigo bimwebimwe n’aho biherereye. Ubwo buryo bugereranya gusa ingeramizi ku buzima bwite bw’abakoresha, ariko ntibugira ico bukoze ku mutekano w’amahera yabo.


> ► *Ijambo ry'intango ryo gusobanura iki gitero ni "Dusting Attack," ariko bamwe mu bakoresha bitcoins barasaba gukoresha ijambo "Address reuse ku nguvu," kuko basanze ijambo "Dust" ritabereye hano.*
