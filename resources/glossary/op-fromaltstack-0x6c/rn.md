---
term: OP_FROMALTSTACK (0X6C)

definition: Opcode yimura ikintu ikiguye mu mupfundikizo w’inyongera ikagishyira mu mupfundikizo mukuru.
---

Ikura ikintu co hejuru mu kirundo c'ibindi (*alt stack*) ikagishira hejuru y'ikirundo nyamukuru (*main stack*). Iyi opcode ikoreshwa mu kugarura amakuru yabitswe mu gihe gito ku rutonde rw'ibindi. Mu mvugo yoroshe, ni igikorwa co guhindura `OP_TOALTSTACK`.
