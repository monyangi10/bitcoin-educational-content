---
term: pruned URUDODO
---

Igikoresho ca pruned, mu congereza "pruned Node", ni igikoresho ca Full node gikora ugukata Blockchain. Ivyo birimwo gukuraho buhorobuhoro amabuye ya kera cane, amaze kuyasuzuma neza, kugira ngo hasigare amabuye aherutse gusa. Igitigiri co kubika kigaragazwa muri dosiye `Bitcoin.conf` biciye ku nzira ya `prune=n`, aho `n` ari ubunini burengeye ubundi bufatwa n'ibice mu megabytes (MB). Iyo `0` igaragajwe inyuma y'iyi parametere, rero gukata birahagarara, kandi urudodo rugumya Blockchain yose uko ingana.


Ivyuma vya pruned rimwe na rimwe bifatwa nk’ubwoko butandukanye bw’ivyuma biva ku vyuma vyuzuye. Ikoreshwa ry’uruzitiro rwa pruned rishobora gushimisha cane cane abakoresha bahanganye n’ingorane z’ubushobozi bwo kubika. Ubu, Full node itegerezwa kuba ifise hafi 600 GB gusa yo kubika Blockchain. Igikoresho ca pruned gishobora kugabanya ububiko busabwa kugera kuri 550 MB. Naho ikoresha umwanya muto kuri disiki, urudodo rwa pruned rugumya urugero rwo kugenzura no kwemeza nk'urwa Full node. Ama node ya pruned rero aratanga icizigiro kinini ku bayakoresha ugereranyije n’ama node y’umuco (SPV).
