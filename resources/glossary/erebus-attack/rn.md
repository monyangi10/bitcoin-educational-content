---
term: Igitero ca Erebus
definition: Igitero cyemerera umuhunzi w'amakuru kuri internet gukura inkingi ya Bitcoin mu rurere.
---

Uburyo buteye imbere cane bwo gutera urubuga rwa Bitcoin bushobora gutuma uwutanga ubuhinga bwa Internet afise ububisha ashobora gutandukanya utugingo ngengabuzima twihariye twa Bitcoin. Gutyo, ni uburyo bwo gutera Eclipse. Igitero ca Erebus gikoresha imiterere y'urubuga rwa Internet, cane cane ahantu hategekanijwe guca (canke "ibizitiro") mu guca mu nzira hagati y'Imirongo y'Ivyigenga (AS). Umuntu atera, mu kugenzura uburyo bwigenga, arashobora gukoresha uruja n’uruza rw’urubuga kugira ngo atandukanye urudodo rwa Bitcoin n’urudodo rwose, gutyo akarutuma rwemera ko Blockchain iri mu kibanza c’ikinyoma (amabarabara canke ibikorwa bitazwi n’urudodo). Ukwo kwitandukanya gushobora gutuma umuntu akoresha amahera incuro zibiri canke agacengera iyo node yitandukanije. Ico gitero caragoye cane kuva hasohoka Bitcoin core version 0.20.0 n’ugushiraho Asmap.
