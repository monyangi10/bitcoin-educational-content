---
term: Contract Operation
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, bisobanura guhindura ivyerekeye Contract bikorwa hakurikijwe amategeko ya Schema. Ibikorwa bikurikira biri muri RGB:




- State Transition;
- Genesis;
- State Extension.


Ico gikorwa cose gihindura raporo mu kwongerako canke mu gusubirira amakuru amwamwe (Global State, Owned State...).
