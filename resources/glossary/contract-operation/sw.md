---
term: Contract Operation
---

Katika itifaki ya RGB, inamaanisha sasisho la hali ya Contract linalofanywa kulingana na sheria za Schema. Operesheni zifuatazo zipo katika RGB:




- State Transition;
- Genesis;
- State Extension.


Kila operesheni hurekebisha ripoti kwa kuongeza au kubadilisha data fulani (Global State, Owned State...).
