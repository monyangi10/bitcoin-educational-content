---
term: Bolt
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ijambo "Ishingiro ry'ubuhinga bw'umuravyo." Ni urutonde rw’ibisobanuro vyagenewe gutuma habaho ugukorana hagati y’ugushirwa mu ngiro gutandukanye kwa Lightning Network (LND, Core Lightning, Eclair...). Ivyo bisobanuro bisobanura amategeko, amasezerano, n’ingingo mfatirwako vyose bitegerezwa gukurikizwa, kugira ngo ama node ya Lightning ashobore gukorana no gukora nk’igice c’urubuga rumwe, rumwe.
