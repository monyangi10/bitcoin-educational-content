---
term: Paiement rond
definition: Uburyo bwo gusuzuma umuhora bwerekana kuriha biciye ku gitigiri c'amahera kiringaniye muri transaction.
---

Ivyiyumviro vy’imbere mu gihugu vy’isesengura ry’uruzitiro kuri Bitcoin bishobora gutuma habaho iciyumviro ku bijanye n’ingene ibiva mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji bimeze bishingiye ku mahera azunguruka. Muri rusangi, iyo umuntu ahanganye n’uburyo bwo kwishura bworoshe (1 input na 2 outputs), iyo kimwe muri ivyo bisohoka gikoresheje amahera azunguruka, rero kigereranya ukwishurwa. Mu gukuraho, iyo igisohoka kimwe kigereranya ukwishyura, ikindi kigereranya ihinduka. Bishobora rero gusobanurwamwo ko bishoboka ko uwukoresha yinjiza igikorwa agifise igisohoka kigaragazwa ko ari co gihinduka.


Birakenewe kumenya ko iyo heuristic idahora ikoreshwa, kuko amahera menshi yishurwa aguma akoreshwa mu mafaranga y’amahera. Nkako, iyo umucuruzi wo mu Bufaransa yemeye Bitcoin, muri rusangi, ntibagaragaza ibiciro bihoraho muri Sats. Bazohitamwo guhindura hagati y’igiciro mu ma euro n’amahera yo mu bitcoins azokwishurwa biciye ku POS yabo (nk’akarorero BTCPay Server). Ntihakwiye rero kubaho umubare uzunguruka mu gisohoka c’ugucuruza. Naho ari ukwo, umuhinga mu vy’ugusesangura yoshobora kugerageza guhindura ivyo mu kuzirikana igipimo ca Exchange cariho igihe iyo nzira yatangazwa ku rubuga. Iyo umusi umwe, Bitcoin iba igice c’ibarabara gikundwa cane mu guhanahana kwacu, iyo heuristic yoshobora kuba ngirakamaro cane mu gusesangura.
