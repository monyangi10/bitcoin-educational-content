term: BIP0119
---

Izana opcode nshasha yitwa `OP_SUZUMA IGITEGEKO` (CTV). CTV yoshobora gutuma habaho amasezerano adasubira inyuma mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji, bikaba vyemeza ko hashirwaho ivyangombwa vyihariye ku buryo Coin ishobora gukoreshwa, harimwo no mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji vyo muri kazoza.

Mu buryo butomoye, vyoshobora kwemera gusobanura inzitizi ku gisohoka c'ibikorwa `scriptPubKey` bishingiye ku `scriptPubKey` ya UTXO iriko irakoreshwa.

CheckTemplateVerify yagenewe kuba yoroshe kandi ata n'ihinduka. Ishirwa mu ngiro ryayo rigamije kwagura ubushobozi bwa Bitcoin bwo kwandika kugira ngo bishobore gukoreshwa mu buryo butandukanye nk'ugucungera umuvuduko w'amafaranga, guhingura uburyo bwo kwishura butakorana, DLCs, amashirahamwe y'ukwishura...

Iyi opcode nshasha yosubirira OP_NOP4 kandi yosaba Soft Fork kugira ngo ikore.
