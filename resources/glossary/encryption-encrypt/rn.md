---
term: GUSHIRAHO (GUSHIRAHO)
---

Uburyo bwo guhindura amakuru atagira ishingiro mu makuru yashizwe mu nzira. Amakuru apfutse insobanuro y’intango y’amakuru kugira ngo ntamenyekane. Gushiramwo amakuru birimwo urutonde rw’ibintu bihindurwa ku makuru y’intango hakoreshejwe urufunguzo. Iyo izo mpinduka zishobora gusubirwamwo, inzira y'ugusubirwamwo ihuye yitwa "gukuraho amakuru," kandi ituma amakuru asubira uko yari ameze.
