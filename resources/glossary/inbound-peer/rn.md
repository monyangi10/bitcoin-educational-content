---
term: Peer yinjira
definition: Innode yo kurubuga rwa Bitcoin itangiza isano n'innode yawe utabigizemo uruhara.
---

Igikoresho kiri mu rubuga rwa Bitcoin gitangura gukorana n’igikoresho cawe ataco winjiramwo. Bitcoin core iremeza abagenzi 125 binjira mu gihugu ku buryo busanzwe, kugira ngo bishobore guhuza mu rubuga. Abagenzi binjira mu gihugu barashikirizwa n’ubwenge, kuko umuntu adashobora kwemera neza ubugororotsi bwabo, kubera ko ayo mahuriro atanguzwa n’abandi bantu. Abagenzi binjira n’abasohoka barasangira ubwoko bumwe bw’amakuru. Itandukaniro rikomeye hagati y’urunganwe rwinjiye n’urwo gusohoka ntiri mu bwoko bw’amakuru bahanahana, ahubwo riri mu kuntu ayo mahuriro ashingwa.
