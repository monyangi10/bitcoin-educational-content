---
term: BIP0014
---

BIP yashikirijwe na Patrick Strateman na Amir Taaki mu 2011 igamije gutandukanya inomero z’abaguzi n’abakoresha. BIP14 isobanura ingene ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’amasezerano ya Bitcoin bikwiye kwigaragaza ku rubuga, isaba gukoresha uburyo bw’umukoresha-umukozi kugira ngo hamenyekane verisiyo n’ubwoko bw’umukiriya wa Bitcoin.

Intumbero ihambaye ya BIP14 ni ugufasha mu gucunga amahinduka no kumenya ukudahuza hagati y’abaguzi batandukanye bariho. Naho mbere vyari bibereye ko umukiriya wa Satoshi ari we de facto protocole ya Bitcoin, ukwiyongera kwa porogarama muri ico gihe kwatumye BIP14 itandukanya neza aba clients n’iyo protocole ubwayo.
