---
term: OP_ELSE (0X67)

definition: Opcode ikora amategeko akurikira namba igisabwa ca mbere kitashitswe.
---

Ihindura urugendo rw'ugushirwa mu ngiro mu nyandiko y'ivyangombwa: yerekana ko ibikorwa biyikurikira bikwiye gushirwa mu ngiro iyo ivyangombwa vy'imbere vyavuzwe na `OP_IF`, `OP_NOTIF`, canke uwundi `OP_ELSE` bidashitse.
