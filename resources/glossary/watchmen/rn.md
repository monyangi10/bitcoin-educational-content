---
term: watchmen
---

Mu bijanye na Liquid (a Bitcoin Sidechain), ivyo ni ibigo bijejwe kubungabunga igiti ca L-BTC, token y’akavukire ya Liquid, mu gucunga no gucungera BTC ikoreshwa nk’ingwati. Barabona ko itunga ryimurirwa hagati ya Bitcoin Blockchain nyamukuru na Liquid Sidechain rikingirwa neza kandi rikafungurwa. Intumbero y’ibikorwa vyabo ni ugukomeza agaciro kamwe hagati ya L-BTC izunguruka kuri Liquid na BTC izunguruka kuri Bitcoin. Muri Liquid, watchmen ni kimwe mu bakuru hamwe n’abashizeko umukono ku mabarabara.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, "watchmen" ishobora guhindurwa ngo "amatongo".*
