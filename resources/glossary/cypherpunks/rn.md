---
term: ABASIPEPUNKI
---

Umuryango w’abantu udasanzwe kandi mpuzamakungu utera imbere ikoreshwa ry’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru nk’uburyo bwo gutuma umuntu agira umwidegemvyo kuri Internet. Aba Cypherpunks bavuganira uburenganzira bw’ishimikiro bw’abantu bwo kurinda ubuzima bwite bwabo, cane cane mu gihe Leta iriko irarushiriza gucungera no gukoresha amakuru n’ibigo vyigenga. Izina ryabo rikomoka ku majambo y'icongereza *cypher*, bisobanura "kode y'ibanga", na *punk*. Ni igikino kivuga ku jambo *cyberpunk*, kidondora ubwoko bw’inyandiko bugaragaza kazoza ka dystopie aho ubuhinga bushasha bufatanye n’ububisha n’ubugizi bwa nabi mu kibano kiriko kirabora.


Ishirahamwe Cypherpunk ryatanguye mu ntango z’imyaka ya 1990 aho imigwi y’abahinga mu vy’amabanga, abahinga mu bijanye n’amaporogarama, n’abaharanira umwidegemvyo batangura kuganira ku ngaruka za politike z’amabanga y’amabanga mu nama zabo za mbere zabereye i Silicon Valley. Umuntu w’agaciro mu muryango ni Tim May, yanditse *Manifesto y’abanyagihugu b’abanyagihugu* mu 1988, igitabu c’ishimikiro c’uwo muhari. Mu bihe bihambaye mu mateka y'aba cypherpunks harimwo ugushinga, mu 1992, "urutonde rw'aba Cypherpunks", urutonde rwo gukwiragiza ubutumwa kuri email rwatumye ivyo biganiro vyoroha. Gusohora *Manifesto ya Cypherpunk* na Eric Hughes mu 1993, inyandiko yerekana intumbero n’ibikorwa vy’aba cypherpunks, na vyo nyene vyari ikintu gihambaye cane.


Iciyumviro c’amahera y’ubuhinga bwa none adashingiye ku kintu nyamukuru, nka Bitcoin, gishingiye ku buhinga bwa filozofiya y’abanyabwenge b’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none. Guhingura Bitcoin akenshi bifatwa nk’ikintu gikomeye cashitsweko muri iyo ntumbero, iruhande ya PGP, Tor, na WikiLeaks.
