---
term: Coinmux
definition: Uburyo bwa kera bwa coinjoin buteguye bushingiye ku kwizigirana hagati y'abayikora.
---

Ishirwa mu ngiro rya CoinJoin ryateguwe mu 2014. Coinmux ni umurongo w’uguvanga Bitcoin wishingikiriza ku kwizigirana hagati y’abaje mu nama, ataco bisaba ko umukinyi nyamukuru yinjiramwo. Iryo koraniro rifatanya amafaranga y’ibice vy’amahera (bitcoins) ava ku bakoresha benshi mu gikorwa kimwe, aho igisohoka cose gifise amafaranga asa, gutyo kigaca amasano hagati y’ivyo umuntu yinjiza n’ivyo asohora. Iryo tegeko rya Coinmux rituma abakoresha bagumana ububasha ku mahera yabo mu gihe cose, mu kugira gusa amafaranga asinywe igihe ivyo binjira n’ivyo bisohoka bihuye neza n’ivyo bemeranije. Ubu, iyo protocole ntikigikoreshwa.
