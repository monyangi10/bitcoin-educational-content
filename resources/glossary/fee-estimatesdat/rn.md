---
term: FEE_ESTIMATES.DAT
definition: Idosi ya Bitcoin Core ibika ivyaharuwe ku mahera y'ivunjisha bishinzwe na mempool.
---

Dosiye iri muri Bitcoin core ibika amakuru agereranywa ku mafaranga y'ibikorwa, akoranijwe na porogarama y'ibikorwa biherutse gukorwa n'ingene Mempool imeze ubu. Ivyo biharuro bifasha uwubikoresha kumenya amahera akwiye gushiramwo mu bikorwa vyiwe kugira ngo yemezwe nk’uko yiteze. Iyi dosiye yariho kuva kuri verisiyo 0.10.
