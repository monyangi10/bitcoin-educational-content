---
term: Banlist.dat
definition: Faili ya zamani ya Bitcoin Core iliyorekodi anwani za IP za nodi zilizopigwa marufuku. Imebadilishwa na banlist.json tangu toleo la 22.0.
---

Jina la faili ya zamani inayotumiwa na programu ya Bitcoin Core kurekodi anwani za IP za nodi ambazo zimepigwa marufuku na mtumiaji. Tangu toleo la 22.0, faili banlist.json inatumiwa badala yake.
