---
term: Satoshi (SAT)
---

Satoshi, akenshi ivugwa mu buryo bugufi ngo "sat," ni igice gitoyi kuruta ibindi vyose ca Bitcoin gishobora kwandikwa kuri Blockchain. Iryo zina ryitwa mu gutera iteka uwahinguye Bitcoin, Satoshi Nakamoto. Bitcoin imwe igabanywamwo 100.000.000 Sats, bisobanura ko Satoshi imwe ingana na 0,00000001 Bitcoin. Kubera agaciro kayo gatoyi, sat ikoreshwa kenshi mu gushinga ibiciro, cane cane mu bikorwa bitobito. Ikoreshwa ryayo rikunda gukundwa kuruta BTC kuri Lightning Network.
