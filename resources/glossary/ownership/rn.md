---
term: Ownership
---

Mu bijanye n'amasezerano ya RGB, ijambo Ownership ryerekeza ku bushobozi bwo kugenzura no gukoresha UTXO nk'uko bigaragazwa na Seal Definition. Iyo Owned State ifatanijwe na UTXO, nyen’iyo UTXO arafise uburenganzira, hakurikijwe amategeko ya Contract, bwo kwimurira canke guhindura igihugu gihuye.
