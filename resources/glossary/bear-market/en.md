---
term: Bear market
definition: Prolonged period of decline in the price of bitcoin relative to fiat currencies like the dollar.
---

A prolonged period during which the price of an asset, such as bitcoin, declines relative to a fiat currency, typically the US dollar. Since its introduction in 2009, the BTC bear market cycles appear to be influenced by halvings, scheduled events that cut miners' reward in half and occur approximately every four years.
