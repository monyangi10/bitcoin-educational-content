---
term: Bear market
definition: Igihe kirekire c'imanuka ry'igiciro ca bitcoin ugereranije n'amafaranga ya reta nk'idola.
---

Igihe kirekire igiciro c’ikintu, nka Bitcoin, kigabanuka ugereranije n’amahera y’amahera, cane cane idolari ry’Amerika. Kuva yatangura mu 2009, inzinguzingu z’isoko ry’idubu rya BTC zisa n’izikozweko n’uguca ibice, ibintu bitegekanijwe bigabanya impembo y’abacukuzi mu bice bibiri kandi bikaba bishika hafi inyuma y’imyaka ine.
