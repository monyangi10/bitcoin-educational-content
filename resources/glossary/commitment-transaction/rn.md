---
term: Commitment Transaction
---

Mu bijanye n’umurongo w’inzira zibiri muri Lightning, Commitment Transaction ni igikorwa abo bompi barema bagashirako umukono, batagitangaje ku ruhererekane nyamukuru. Igereranya uko amafaranga ameze ubu hagati y'abafatanyabikorwa b'umurongo, aho ukwishyura kwose kw'umuravyo kuzana Commitment Transaction nshasha. Ivyo bicuruzwa birafise akamaro ariko bitangazwa gusa iyo umurongo ufunze ku ruhande rumwe. Birimwo ibiva mu ruhande rumwe rumwe, vyerekana ukuntu amafaranga agabanywa hakurikijwe amahera Lightning yatanze kuva uwo muhora ufungurwa. Uburyo bwo guhana burafitaniye isano n’uguhagarika ababuranyi gutangaza amakuru ya kera y’umurongo, ni ukuvuga amafaranga ya kera ya Commitment yerekana ugusangira amafaranga nabi.
