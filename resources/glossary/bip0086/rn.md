---
term: BIP0086
definition: Standard ya derivation ku ma adresse ya Taproot (bc1p...), ikoresha index 86' mu nzira ya derivation.
---

Isobanura urugero rwo gukura SegWit V1 canke Taproot (`bc1p...`) aderesi mu gihe c'ubuhinga n'uburongozi Wallet. Igaragaza urutonde `86'` ubu rutegerezwa gukoreshwa ku `intumbero` uburebure bwa HD Wallet ku bigereranyo vy'inyandiko za P2TR.
