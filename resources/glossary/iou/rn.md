---
term: IOU
definition: Itunga ryo kuri internet ryerekana isezerano ry'agaciro rishinzwe kw'itunga ry'ifatizo.
---

Ijambo ry'inyongera ry'ijambo _I Owe You_, rikoreshwa mu bijanye na Bitcoin kugira ngo ryerekeze ku mitungo y'ubuhinga bwa none ishigikiwe n'imitungo y'ishimikiro iyo mu bisanzwe ikwiye gushirwako. Iri jambo rikoreshwa cane cane ku bice vy’amahera canke ibimenyetso vya BTC ku mirongo iri hanze ya Bitcoin, nk’imirongo y’inyuma, imirongo y’imiduga, imirongo ya Exchange, canke ETFs. Ivyo bintu vy’ubuhinga bwa none bigereranya umuhango w’agaciro kangana n’ak’ibintu vy’ishimikiro.
