---
term: Bitcoin core
---

Bitcoin core ni porogaramu y’inkomoko yuguruye y’ishingiro y’uburyo bwa Bitcoin kandi iguma ari yo nzira nyamukuru y’ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’amasezerano ya Bitcoin gushika n’ubu. Itegurwa kandi ikabungabungwa n’umugwi munini w’abantu batanga amahera y’intahemuka. Mu ntango yitwa "Bitcoin Qt," ni umukiriya wa gatatu mu mateka ya Bitcoin akurikiye Bitcoin na Satoshi Nakamoto, na bitcoind. Yubatswe ivuye kuri kode y’intango ya Satoshi, yashizeho umukoresha w’ibishushanyo Interface (GUI) ku bakoresha, n’ubu nyene yitwa `Bitcoin-qt`.  Kuva kuri verisiyo 0.5, Bitcoin core yarashizwe hamwe na `bitcoind`.

Porogarama ya Bitcoin core ikora intumbero nyinshi. Mbere na mbere, ikora nk’umukiriya wa Full node. Bitcoin core kandi irimwo Wallet ku bakoresha bipfuza kubika, gucunga, no gukora amafaranga ataco baciye kuri Bitcoin core.
