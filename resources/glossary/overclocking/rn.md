---
term: Overclocking

definition: Ingendo yo kwongera inkuru z'ubushobozi bw'imbanyi za ASIC kurenza uko vyagenwe kugira ngo hashrate yongeke.
---

Ivyo kwongera incuro z’ibikorwa vy’ibice vya ASIC bikoreshwa kuri mining birenze ivyo bimenyetso vy’izina. Ivyo bituma haba Hashrate nyinshi kandi bikongera amahirwe yo kuronka ivyuma. Ariko rero, iyo ngeso iratuma kandi amashini akoresha ubushobozi bwinshi, bigatuma haba ubushuhe bwinshi, ivyo bikaba bishobora gutuma ibihimba vy’umubiri bishaza vuba kandi bisaba ko habaho uburyo bwiza bwo gukonjesha, nk’ugukonjesha mu mazi.
