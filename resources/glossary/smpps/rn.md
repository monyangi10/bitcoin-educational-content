---
term: SMPPS
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikirundi risobanura "*Igiciro kinini gisangiye ku mugabane*". Ni uburyo bwo kubara impembo y’abacukuzi mu rwego rw’ibidengeri vya Mining. Ni uburyo butandukanye bw’uburyo bwa PPS. Igabanya amahera yishurwa kugira ngo pool ntiyigere yishura ibirenze ivyo yaronse mu ngororano z’amabuye. Gutyo, naho abacukuzi botanga imigabane ibereye, impembo yose batanga ntishobora kurenga amahera yinjira muri iyo pool. Ubwo buryo bufise intumbero yo kubungabunga uburinganire bw’amahera y’ikidengeri mu gihe hakoreshwa uburyo bwo kwishura ku gikorwa, ivyo bikaba bifasha kugabanya itandukaniro ku bacukuzi ku giti cabo.
