---
term: BCH code
definition: Ibimenyetso bikosora amakosa bikoreshwa mu ma aderesi ya Bech32 na Bech32m kugira ngo bitahure amakosa mu kwinjiza ivyanditswe.
---

Itsinda ry’amakode akosora amakosa akoreshwa mu kumenya no gukosora amakosa mu rutonde rw’amakuru. Mu yandi majambo, amakode akosora amakosa ya BCH atuma amakosa atari yo mu makuru yoherezwa amenyekana kandi akosorwa, ivyo bikaba bituma ayo makuru ashika aho aja ataco akoze. Iryo jambo ry'inyongera "BCH" rikomoka ku nyuguti z'intango z'abahinguzi b'izo kode: Bose, Ray-Chaudhuri, na Hocquenghem.


Izo kode zikoreshwa cane mu vy’ubuhinga bwa none, cane cane mu ma SSD, DVD na QR codes. Nk’akarorero, kubera izo kode zikosora amakosa, naho kode ya QR yoba ipfutse igice, birashoboka ko umuntu ashobora kuronka amakuru yashizwe muri yo.


Mu Bitcoin, amakode ya BCH akoreshwa ku bijanye n’igiharuro c’igenzura mu mice ya Bech32 na Bech32m Address (yashizweho inyuma ya SegWit). Bigereranya uguhuza neza hagati y’ubunini bw’umubare w’igenzura n’ubushobozi bwo kumenya amakosa ugereranyije n’ibikorwa vyoroshe vya Hash vyakoreshwa mbere ku maderesi y’Iragi. Ariko mu bijanye na Bitcoin, amakode ya BCH akoreshwa gusa mu kumenya amakosa, atari mu gukosora amakosa. Porogarama ya Bitcoin Wallet rero, iramenya kandi ikamenyesha abakoresha amakosa yo kwakira amaderesi, ariko ntihita ikosora Address idakwiriye. Iryo hitamwo ni ry’ibigirankana, kuko gushiramwo ubushobozi bwo gukosora amakosa vyogabanya ata kabuza ubushobozi bw’ubuhinga bwo kumenya amakosa.
