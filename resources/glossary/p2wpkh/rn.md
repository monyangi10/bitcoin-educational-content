---
term: P2WPKH
---

P2WPKH bisobanura *Ishura ku Cabona Urufunguzo rwa bose Hash*. Ni urugero rw'inyandiko rusanzwe rukoreshwa mu gushinga ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha amahera kuri UTXO. P2WPKH yashizweho n'ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa SegWit muri Myandagaro 2017.


Iyi nyandiko isa na P2PKH (*Ishura ku rufunguzo rwa bose Hash*), kuko nayo ifunga bitcoins zishingiye kuri Hash y'urufunguzo rwa bose, ni ukuvuga, Address yakira. Itandukaniro riri mu kuntu imikono n’inyandiko bishirwa mu gucuruza. Ku bijanye na P2WPKH, amakuru y'inyandiko y'umukono (`scriptSig`) akurwa mu miterere y'ibikorwa vya kera aja mu gice gitandukanye citwa `Icabona`. Ivyo ni ikintu kiranga ivugurura rya SegWit (*Icabona Gitandukanijwe*) rifasha gukingira ukuntu umukono udashobora guhinduka. Ibikorwa vya P2WPKH muri rusangi birazimvye cane mu bijanye n’amahera ugereranije n’ibikorwa vya Legacy, kuko igice kiri mu cabona kizimvye cane.


Amaderesi ya P2WPKH yandikwa hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwa `Bech32` n'umubare w'isuzuma mu buryo bwa kode ya BCH. Izo aderesi zama zitangura na `bc1q`. P2WPKH ni verisiyo 0 SegWit isohoka.
