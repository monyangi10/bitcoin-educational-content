---
term: Global State
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, Global State yerekana umugwi w'ibintu vya bose vya Contract, vyashinzwe mu gihe ca Genesis. Ikora nk’inyandiko ya bose, ikoranya Elements za leta zishobora gushikirwa na bose kandi zishobora guhindurwa n’uguhindura kwemerewe hakurikijwe amategeko asobanuwe na Contract Schema. Mu buryo butandukanye n’Ibihugu vy’Igihugu, bifitaniye isano n’ibigo vyihariye, Global State ntabwo ari iy’umuhari uwo ari wo wose.
