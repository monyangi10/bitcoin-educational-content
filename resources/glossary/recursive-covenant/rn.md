---
term: IVYO GUSUBIRAMWO (ISEZERANO)
---

Isezerano ry’ugusubira inyuma kuri Bitcoin ni ubwoko bwa Smart contract bushiraho ivyangombwa atari ku bijanye n’ugucuruza gusa ariko no ku bikorwa vyo muri kazoza bikoresha ibiva muri iyo nzira. Ivyo bituma habaho imirongo y’ubudandaji aho umwe wese ategerezwa kwubahiriza amategeko amwamwe asobanurwa n’uwa mbere mu ruhererekane. Recursivity irema urutonde rw'ibikorwa aho umwe wese aragwa uburenganzira buva ku bikorwa vyayo vy'umuvyeyi. Ivyo vyotuma habaho ukugenzura gukomeye kandi kw’igihe kirekire kw’ingene amafaranga y’ama bitcoins ashobora gukoreshwa, ariko kandi vyotuma habaho ingorane ku bijanye no gukoresha umwidegemvyo n’ugushobora gukoreshwa.


Mu ncamake, isezerano ridasubira inyuma ryoba rigarukira gusa ku bijanye n’ugucuruza gukurikira ubwo nyene iryo ryashizeho amategeko. Ku rundi ruhande, isezerano risubiramwo rifise ubushobozi bwo gushinga ibintu vyihariye kuri Bitcoin ibihe bidahera. Ivy’ugucuruza birashobora gukurikirana, ariko Bitcoin ivugwa izokwama igumya ivyangombwa vy’intango biyifatanye. Mu buryo bw'ubuhinga, ugushinga isezerano ridasubira inyuma bishika iyo `scriptPubKey` ya UTXO isobanura uburenganzira ku `scriptPubKey` y'ibisohoka vy'ugucuruza bikoresha iyo UTXO. Ku rundi ruhande, ugushinga isezerano ry'ugusubira inyuma bishika iyo `scriptPubKey` ya UTXO isobanura uburenganzira ku `scriptPubKey` y'ibisohoka vy'ibikorwa bikoresha iyo UTXO, no ku `scriptPubKey`s zose zizokurikira ikoreshwa rya UTXO.


Muri rusangi, mu vy'ubuhinga bw'ibarabara, ico bita "recursivity" ni ubushobozi bw'igikorwa bwo kwihamagara, kigatuma habaho ubwoko bw'uruzitiro.
