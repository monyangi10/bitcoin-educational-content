---
term: KYC (MENYA UMUKIRIYA WAWE)
---

Uburyo bwo gutegeka bukoreshwa n’amashirahamwe amwe amwe akorera kuri Bitcoin kugira ngo asuzume akaranga k’abaguzi bayo nk’igice c’urugamba rwo kurwanya ugukoresha nabi amahera n’ugufasha iterabwoba. KYC ni ugukusanya no kugenzura amakuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Mu bijanye no kugura ama bitcoins, KYC iratera ingorane nyinshi uwuyikoresha, harimwo:


- Ico kibazo co gusohoka kw'amakuru y'umuntu ku giti ciwe ajanye n'igikorwa kiri kuri Bitcoin. Kubika amakuru ku ma server y’amashirahamwe birashobora gutuma amakuru asohoka, bigatuma amakuru y’abakoresha ageragezwa gukoreshwa mu buryo bw’ubuhendanyi, ibitero vy’umubiri, canke ubusuma bw’akaranga, cane cane kubera ko bifitaniye isano n’ibidukikije vya Bitcoin;
- Gushirwa ahabona n’ubugenzuzi bwa Leta. Gugura BTC biciye mu nzego zigenzurwa birashobora guhishurira Leta ko uwuyikoresha yari afise Bitcoin mu gihe kanaka, ivyo bishobora kugira ingaruka muri kazoza iyo habaye imivurungano ya politike canke y’ubutunzi;

**Kworohereza gukurikirana On-Chain.** Kurangiza KYC bituma habaho isano hagati y’akaranga k’ukoresha n’ibikorwa vyiwe kuri Blockchain, bikaba vyemeza ko haba ahantu ho kwinjira kugira ngo hakorwe isesengura ry’uruhererekane.
