---
term: OP_LESSTHAN (0X9F)

definition: Opcode igenzura ko ikintu ca mbere ku mupfundikizo kiri musi y’ica kabiri.
---

Igereranya ibintu bibiri biri hejuru cane ku rutonde maze igenzure nimba ikintu ca mbere ari gitoyi ku ca kabiri. Iyo ikintu ca mbere kiri munsi y'ica kabiri, gisunika `1` (ukuri) ku ruganda, ahandi ho, gisunika `0` (ikinyoma).
