---
term: OP_GREATERTHAN (0XA0)

definition: Opcode igenzura ko ikintu ca mbere ku mupfundikizo kigeretse ku ca kabiri.
---

Igereranya ibintu bibiri biri hejuru cane ku ruganda maze igenzure nimba ikintu ca mbere ari kinini kuruta ica kabiri. Iyo ikintu ca mbere ari kinini kuruta ica kabiri, gisunika `1` (ukuri) ku ruganda, ahandi ho, gisunika `0` (ikinyoma).
