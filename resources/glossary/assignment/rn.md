---
term: Assignment
---

Mu bijanye n’amasezerano ya RGB, Assignment ni ikintu kingana n’igisubizo c’ibikorwa gihindura, kigahindura canke kigahingura ibintu vyihariye mu gihugu ca Contract. Assignment igizwe n’ibice bibiri Elements:




- Seal Definition (ivuga ku UTXO yihariye);
- Owned State (amakuru adondora igihugu gifitaniye isano n’uwo mufise mushasha).


Assignment rero yerekana ko igice ca Leta (nk’umutungo) ubu gihabwa uwufise, kimenyekana biciye ku Single-Use Seal iboshe kuri UTXO.
