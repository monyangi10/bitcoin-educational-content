---
term: BIP0380
---

BIP380 itanga ururimi rusanzwe rwo kudondora amakoraniro y’inyandiko zisohoka zikoreshwa mu bikoresho vya HD Bitcoin, zizwi nka “Ibidondora Inyandiko z’Isohoka.”

Intumbero ni uguhuza ingene inyandiko z’isohoka ziserukirwa no gucungirwa, bigatuma Wallet ishobora gusubirwamwo, yoherezwa hanze, no kwinjiza mu gihugu vyoroha.

Uretse amakuru y'ibanga nk'ijambo ry'ugusubirana, abadondora baratanga amakuru yose akenewe kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kuronka amakuru abiri akoreshwa muri HD Wallet. Naho BIP380 idondora urutonde rusangi rw’abadondora, BIP381–BIP386 idondora ubwoko bw’imvugo za Descriptor ku giti cabo. BIP380 yashizwe mu ngiro hamwe n'izindi BIPs zose zijanye na Descriptor (uretse BIP386) muri verisiyo 0.17 ya Bitcoin core.
