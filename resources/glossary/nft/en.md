---
term: NFT
---

Acronym for *Non-Fungible Token*. An NFT represents a unit of Bitcoin which, by users of a second-layer protocol, is interpreted as non-fungible and associated with the ownership of an asset external to Bitcoin, such as an image or other type of data.

On Bitcoin, second-layer protocols such as Ordinals and Stamps enable the creation and management of these NFTs. These protocols are often controversial, as their activity clutters Blockchain with non-financial data. This can have negative consequences on the system, such as inducing an increased operational load for complete nodes or increasing transaction costs through the influence of external economic factors.
