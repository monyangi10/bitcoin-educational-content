---
term: GUHUZA
---

Mu bijanye na Git, igereranya igikorwa co gushiramwo amahinduka ava kw'ishami rimwe akaja mu rindi, mu bisanzwe akazana iterambere riva ku ishami rya kabiri rikaja mw'ishami ry'ingenzi. Ivyo bituma habaho uguhuza amateka y’ibikorwa vy’amashami afise uruhara no gutorera umuti amakimbirane yose yoshobora kubaho kugira ngo porogarama ikomeze kuba intahemuka.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, "gufatanya" bishobora guhindurwa ngo "gufatanya".*
