---
term: BIP0123
definition: Itondorwa rya BIP mu mice 4 Consensus, Peer Services, API/RPC na Applications.
---

BIP123 ishiraho uburyo bumwe bwo gushikiriza ivyifuzo vyo guteza imbere Bitcoin (BIPs).BIPs ubu itegerezwa gushirwa mu mice 4:

**Ukwemeranya**: yerekeye ivyiyumviro bisaba guhindura ukwiyumvikana kandi bikagira ico bikoze ku guhuza hagati y’imirongo ya kera n’iyo muri kazoza y’amasezerano ya Bitcoin. Ivyo ni amaforogo Soft n’amaforogo Hard;

**Ibikorwa vy’urunganwe**: bireba guhindura ibikorwa n’amasezerano y’itumanaho hagati y’ibice vy’uruja n’uruza, ataco bihinduye ku kwumvikana;

**API/RPC**: irimwo ivyiyumviro bigamije guhindura APIs na RPCs zikoreshwa mu gukorana n’ibihimba vya Bitcoin;

**Ibikorwa**: birimwo ivyifuzo vyo kunoza ibikorwa bikoreshwa hejuru y'urubuga rwa Bitcoin, nk'ingingo ngenderwako zijanye n'ubuhinga bwa Wallet.
