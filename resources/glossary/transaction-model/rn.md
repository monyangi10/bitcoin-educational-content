---
term: Modèle de transaction
definition: Uburyo cyangwa imisubirize y'itunshwa ry'amafaranga ikoreshwa mu gusobanura imiterere yaryo mu gusesengura blockchain.
---

Igishushanyo c’ugucuruza ni gusa ikigereranyo canke imiterere rusangi y’ugucuruza gusanzwe gushobora kuboneka kuri Blockchain, kandi insobanuro yayo ishobora kuba ngirakamaro mu bijanye n’isesengura ry’uruhererekane. Igihe twiga ivyerekanwa, twibanda ku gikorwa kimwe dusuzuma ku rugero rwo hejuru (bitandukanye n’ubuhinga bwo mu mutima n’ubwo hanze bw’isesengura ry’uruhererekane). Mu yandi majambo, tuzorabira gusa umubare w’ama UTXO mu vyo yinjiza n’umubare w’ama UTXO mu vyo asohora, tutagumye ku bintu vyihariye canke ku bidukikije vy’ugucuruza. Dufatiye ku citegererezo twabonye, ​​tuzoshobora gusobanura kamere y’ugucuruza. Tuzoheza turonde ibiranga imiterere yayo maze dufate insobanuro ishoboka.
