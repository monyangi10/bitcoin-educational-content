---
term: Paiement simple
definition: Uburyo bwa transaction ifise ivyasohotse 2, akenshi inkwisho n'isigara.
---

Uburyo bw’ugucuruza (canke urugero) bukoreshwa mu gusesangura uruhererekane burangwa n’ugukoresha UTXO imwe canke nyinshi mu bikoresho vy’injiza n’uguhingura UTXO 2 mu bikoresho vy’injiza. Iyi modele rero izosa n’iyi:





Iyi nzira yoroshe yo kwishura yerekana ko dushobora kuba turi mu gihe hariho igikorwa co kohereza canke co kwishura. Uwukoresha yarakoresheje UTXO yiwe bwite mu vyinjizwa kugira ngo ahaze mu bisohoka UTXO y’ukwishura n’ihinduka UTXO (ihinduka rigaruka ku mukoresha umwe). Turazi rero ko uwukoresha yihwejwe ashobora kuba atagifise imwe muri izo UTXO zibiri mu bisohoka (iyo kwishura), ariko bakaba bakiri mu yindi UTXO (iyo guhindura).
