---
term: SOROBANI
---

Itegeko ry’itumanaho ry’ibanga ryashinzwe kuri Tor ryemerera gukorana n’abandi bakoresha mu bijanye n’ugucuruza kwa Cahoots. Soroban yateguwe n’imigwi yo muri Samourai Wallet, kugira ngo yorohereze Exchange y’Ibikorwa vya Bitcoin Vyashizweko Umukono (PSBT) mu bakoresha bipfuza gukorana n’abandi (*Stowaway*, *Stonewall*, *StonewallX2*...). Iyi porotokole ikoreshwa muri porogarama ya Samourai Wallet no muri porogarama ya Sparrow wallet.
