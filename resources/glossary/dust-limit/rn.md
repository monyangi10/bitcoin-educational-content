---
term: Dust UMUGAMBI
---

Yerekeza ku nzira iri muri Sats munsi yayo UTXO ifatwa nk'"Dust" n'uruzitiro rw'uruja n'uruza. Iryo tegeko ni igice c’amategeko y’ugushinga urugero ashobora guhindurwa yigenga n’urudodo rumwe rumwe. Mu Bitcoin core, iyo nzira igenwa n’igipimo c’amahera yihariye, gishirwaho ku buryo busanzwe ku 3.000 Sats ku kilobyte y’ukuri (Sats/kvB). Ico kigero kigamije kugabanya ukwiragira kw’amafaranga arimwo amafaranga make cane y’ama bitcoins. Nkako, UTXO ikwiriye kuba Dust bisobanura ko gukoresha iyo UTXO ata co bimaze mu vy’ubutunzi: gukoresha iyo UTXO vyotwara amahera menshi kuruta kuyiheba gusa. Nimba gukoresha Dust ataco bimaze, bivuga ko ayo mahera yoshobora gusa guterwa n’ibintu biva hanze, akenshi bikaba ari ivy’ububisha. Ivyo bishobora kuba ingorane iyo umukozi w’ububisha arondera kwuzuza urubuga amafaranga arimwo amahera makeyi, afise intumbero yo kwongera umuzigo w’ibikorwa ku nzira z’ubuhinga kandi bikaba bishobora kubabuza gukora ibindi bikorwa vyemewe. Kugira ngo ntange ikigereranyo (naho ari ikintu giteye isoni gatoyi, ndavyemera), ni nk’umuntu agerageza kwishura igiseke c’ibicuruzwa c’ama€ 100 cose mu biceri vy’isenti 1 kugira ngo ahagarike abandi bakiriya ku giciro.
