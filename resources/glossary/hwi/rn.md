---
term: HWI
definition: Uburyo busanzwe (interface) bwo gukorana hagati ya software y'igapu n'amapagu y'ibyuma (hardware wallets).
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikigiriki "*Hardware Wallet Interface*". Ni Interface igereranijwe yorohereza ugushiramwo no gukorana hagati ya Bitcoin Wallet porogarama y’uburongozi n’ibikoresho vy’amasakoshi. Mu buryo bwihariye, HWI ni ububiko bw'ibitabu bwa Python n'igikoresho c'umurongo w'amabwirizwa. Birorosha uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’ivyo bice hakoreshejwe PSBTs (Ibikorwa vya Bitcoin vyashizweko umukono ku gice) be n’ibisobanuro (ibisobanuro vy’inyandiko y’isohoka). Iyi Interface yatanguye gukoreshwa kuri Bitcoin core, hanyuma, iba ikigereranyo gikoreshwa na porogarama nyinshi za Wallet.
