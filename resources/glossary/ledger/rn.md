---
term: Igitabo c'ibarurwa
definition: Igitabu rusangi kandi gishanzwe (distributed register) candikwamwo ihererekanya ryose rya Bitcoin kuri blockchain.
---

Izina rimwe na rimwe rikoreshwa mu kugaragaza igitabu ca bose, gikwiragizwa kindika amafaranga yose yakozwe kuri Bitcoin, ni ukuvuga Blockchain. Gusohora no kwandika amafaranga akoreshwa kuri Ledger birafasha gukingira gukoresha amahera kabiri kuri Bitcoin, mu kumenya neza ko Coin yose amafaranga ageragejwe gukoresha atari yarakoreshejwe mbere mu kindi gicuruzwa.
