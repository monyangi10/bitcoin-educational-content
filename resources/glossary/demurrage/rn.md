---
term: GUKORA
---

Igiciro gifitaniye isano n’ugufata amafaranga. Mu kwagura, demeurage rimwe na rimwe yerekana uburyo bw’amahera aho Ownership y’ifaranga imara igihe kirekire ituma nyen’ifaranga ahanishwa igihano, nk’akarorero, mu kugabanya buhoro buhoro agaciro kayo. Intumbero akenshi ni iyo gutuma amahera agenda neza mu guca intege abantu kuzigama igihe kirekire.


Ubwo buryo rimwe na rimwe burasabwa gushirwa mu ngiro kuri Bitcoin n’intumbero yo gushigikira isoko ry’amahera y’ugucuruza no gutanga amafaranga y’uburyo mu buryo bubereye. Nkako, igiciro co gukoresha Bitcoin kizoheza kigwe gusa ku bakora ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji, atari ku bazigama bungukira ku mutekano w’urwo rutonde. Ariko rero, umuryango wa Bitcoin uranka cane iciyumviro co gusigaza, kuko gihana abafise igihe kirekire kandi ntibihuye n’akaranga ka BTC ko kubika agaciro. Kubera ko bituma umuntu agenda atakaza agaciro, birashobora gufatwa nk’uguhonyanga uburenganzira bwo kugira ikintu.


Umuntu yoshobora guhakana residurage mu gusigura ko igiciro c’ubuhinga buriho ubu gisanzwe kigwa ku bazigama, kubera ko bategerezwa gukora igikorwa mu gihe kanaka kugira ngo bashobore kuronka amafaranga yabo ya bitcoins. Ikindi, umutekano wa UTXO itangwa urushirizaho cane n’amabuye make ya mbere amaze kuremwa, ariko uko igihe kigenda kirarenga, umutekano w’inyongera uronswa n’ibuye rimwe rimwe urushiriza kuba muto. Rero si ngombwa ngo bibe ububi guhindura igiciro ca Mining ku bafise igihe kirekire.


Cane cane, demeurage yakoreshejwe kuri Altcoin Freicoin, Fork iva kuri Bitcoin yashize mu ngiro uburyo bw’inyungu mbi.
