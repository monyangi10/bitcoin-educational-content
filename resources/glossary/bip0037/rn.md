---
term: BIP0037
---

Iciyumviro cagenewe kwemerera amasakoshi yoroshe (*Igenzura ryoroshe ry'ukwishyura*) gucungera amafaranga ataco akeneye gukura kuri Blockchain yose. Ubu buryo bushingiye ku ciyumviro ca Bloom Filters, imiterere y’amakuru y’ibishoboka igerageza neza nimba ikintu kiri mu mugwi.

Ivyo biyunguruzo bituma abaguzi ba SPV bashobora kwakira gusa amafaranga ajanye na Wallet yabo, kugira ngo bagabanye uburebure bw’uruja n’uruza n’uburebure bw’uruja n’uruza (RAM) bisabwa kugira ngo bashobore gukorana, cane cane kuri telefone ngendanwa. Ivyo biyunguruzo vya Bloom birungikwa kuri Full node, iyo na yo igasubiza n’amabuye ya Merkle. Ivyo bice birimwo gusa amafaranga ayunguruwe, umutwe w’ibice ufise Merkle Root, n’amashami akenewe kugira ngo yerekane ko ayo mafaranga ari igice ca Merkle Tree.

BIP37 yaranezwe kubera intege nke zayo mu vy’ubuzima bwite, kuko ihishura aderesi z’abakoresha n’ibikorwa ku nzira zuzuye ziriko zirabazwa. Kugira ngo umuntu agerageze gutorera umuti iyo nzira, birashoboka ko umuntu ashiramwo ibindi bikorwa vy'ubudandaji, "ivyiza vy'ibinyoma", kugira ngo umuntu ashobore guhakana. Ariko rero, igitigiri c’ibintu vyiza vy’ibinyoma bikenewe kugira ngo umuntu ashike ku rugero rushimishije rw’uguhakana kwemewe ruracari hejuru cane.

BIP37 kandi yaranezwe kubera umuzigo w’ubuhinga ishira ku nzira zishitse no kubera yashizeho uburyo bwo gutera bwo kwanka gukoresha. Nk'inyishu, iki kintu kirazimwa ku buryo busanzwe muri Bitcoin core. Kugira ngo uyishoboze, umurongo `peerbloomfilters=1` utegerezwa kwinjira muri dosiye y'imiterere.
