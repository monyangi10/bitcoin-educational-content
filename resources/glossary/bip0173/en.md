---
term: BIP0173
---

Introduced the bech32 address format for SegWit V0 addresses. This address format is characterized by the prefix `bc1q`. The bech32 format offers several advantages:
* It requires less space in QR codes;
* It is more easily interpretable by humans;
* It features an innovative checksum mechanism that is more efficient and can detect, and even potentially correct, typographical errors automatically.
These characteristics make receiving addresses easier to use while minimizing the risk of errors.
