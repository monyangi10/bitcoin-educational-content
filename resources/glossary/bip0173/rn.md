---
term: BIP0173
definition: Uburyo bw'adresse bech32 kuri SegWit V0 ririko bc1q mu ntango, butuma gusoma vyoroha kandi bukerekana amakosa.
---

Yashizeho uburyo bwa bech32 Address ku ma aderesi ya SegWit V0. Iyi nzira ya Address irangwa n'intango `bc1q`. Uburyo bwa bech32 buratanga ivyiza vyinshi:


- Bisaba umwanya mutoyi mu ma code ya QR;
- Ni vyo vyoroshe gusobanura n’abantu;

*Ifise uburyo bushasha bwo gusuzuma amakuru bukora neza cane kandi bushobora kumenya, mbere no gukosora amakosa yo kwandika ubwabwo.*

Ivyo biranga bituma amaderesi yo kwakira yoroha gukoresha mu gihe bigabanya ingorane zo gukora amakosa.
