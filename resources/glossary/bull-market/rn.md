---
term: Isoko ry'imfizi
definition: Igihe kirekire c'izinduka ry'igiciro ca bitcoin, kenshi na kenshi riterwa na halvings.
---

Igihe kirekire igiciro c’ikintu, nka Bitcoin, kiduga ugereranije n’amahera y’amahera, cane cane idolari ry’Amerika. Kuva yatangura mu 2009, inzinguzingu z’isoko ry’ibimasa rya BTC zisa n’izigira ico zikoze ku guca ibice, ibintu bitegekanijwe bigabanya igice c’impera y’abacukuzi kandi bikaba bishika hafi inyuma y’imyaka ine.
