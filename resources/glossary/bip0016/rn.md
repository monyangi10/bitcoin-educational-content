---
term: BIP0016
definition: Ishirwaho rya P2SH (Pay-to-Script-Hash) ryemera kwishyura ku hash ya script, ryoroshya ikoreshwa ry'itunshwa ryingushanya nka multisig.
---

BIP16 yashizeho iciyumviro ca *Pay-to-Script-Hash* (P2SH), bisobanura "kwishura Hash y'inyandiko". BIP16 yasabwe mu ntango mu 2012, itangura gukora mu 2013, yaroroheje gukoresha inyandiko z’ibikorwa zikomeye, nk’inyandiko z’ibikorwa zifise amasinya menshi, mu kwemerera abakoresha kwishura Hash y’inyandiko ya Redeem aho gushiramwo inyandiko yuzuye mu gisohoka.

Ivyo bishasha vyagabanyije amakuru akenewe mu gucuruza kwa mbere, bica bihindura umuzigo wo gutanga inyandiko yuzuye ku ruhande rukoresha ayo bitcoins. Yaratumye kandi ubuzima bwite butera imbere mu guhishura inyandiko nyayo igihe gusa ama bitcoins yakoreshejwe, aho guhishura igihe yaronka.

BIP16 ni ikintu gihambaye mu mateka nk’imwe mu mpinduka za mbere zikomeye zakozwe ku masezerano ya Bitcoin inyuma y’aho Satoshi Nakamoto akuyeho mu 2011. Ico ciyumviro caratumye habaho impaka zikomeye mbere zituma Gavin Andresen, uwakurikiye Satoshi nk’umuyobozi w’ivy’ugucungera, asubira inyuma mu gihe gitoyi ku ruhara rwiwe. Hariho ivyiyumviro vyinshi vyari bihanganye, kandi bimwe bimwe vyari hafi kwemerwa aho kwemerwa na BIP16.
