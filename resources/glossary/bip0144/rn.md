---
term: BIP0144
---

Isobanura uburyo bushasha bw’ubutumwa bw’urubuga n’uburyo bwo gukurikirana bwo gukwiragiza amafaranga n’ibice birimwo imibumbe y’ivyabona itandukanye (SegWit).

BIP144 ishiraho uburyo bwihariye butuma abo mu runganwe bagaragaza ko bashigikiye SegWit no gutanga imiterere y’ivyabona ataco bihinduye ku bijanye n’uguhuza n’ibihimba bitavuguruwe.
