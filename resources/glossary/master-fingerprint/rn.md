---
term: IKIMENYETSO C'URTOKE
---

Urutoke rwa 4-byte (32-bit) rw'urufunguzo rw'ibanga rw'umukuru mu rwego rw'uburongozi (HD) Wallet. Iboneka mu kubara `SHA256` Hash y'urufunguzo rw'ibanga, ikurikirwa na `RIPEMD160` Hash, uburyo bwitwa `HASH160` kuri Bitcoin. Igishushanyo c'urutoke gikoreshwa mu kumenya HD Wallet, ataco bimaze inzira zikomokako, ariko hakurikijwe ukubaho canke ukutabaho kwa passphrase. Ni amakuru makeyi atuma umuntu ashobora kumenya inkomoko y'imfunguruzo, ata makuru y'agaciro yerekeye Wallet ahishura.
