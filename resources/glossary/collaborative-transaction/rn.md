---
term: Igikorwa co gufashanya
definition: Ivunjisha rya Bitcoin ririmwo abantu batari bake mu bwinjiriro, nka coinjoins.
---

Ibikorwa vya Bitcoin birimwo ibigo vyinshi bitandukanye nk’ibintu vyinjizwa mu bikorwa. Ni n’akarorero k’ugucuruza gashobora gukoreshwa mu gusesangura uruhererekane. Akarorero gasanzwe k’ugukorana n’abandi ni CoinJoin, aho abakoresha benshi bahuriza hamwe amahera angana mu vyo bashiramwo kugira ngo bashobore kuronka amahera yose (bakuyeko amafaranga y’ugukorana) mu bisohoka vy’amahera amwe, kugira ngo ntihagire uwukurikirana mu guca amateka y’ibiceri.
