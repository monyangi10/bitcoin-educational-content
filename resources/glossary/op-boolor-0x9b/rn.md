---
term: OP_BOOLOR (0X9B)

definition: Opcode ikora ibijanye n'ubwenge bwa 'CANKE' (OR) ku gaciro k'ibintu bibiri vya mbere kurutonde rw'ibintu.
---

Yigana inyifato y'irembo `CANKE` ryumvikana. Bifata agaciro kabiri hejuru y'ikirundo maze bikagarura `1` iyo kimwe canke vyose bitari zero. Ahandi ho, igarura `0`.
