---
term: LEVELDB

---

Ububiko bw'agaciro k'urufunguzo bworoshe, bwihuta, kandi bufunguye bwakozwe na Google. Ikoreshwa muri Bitcoin kugira ngo ubike umugwi wa UTXO, urutonde rw’ibikorwa, n’urutonde rw’ibice. Iyi nzira y'urutonde rw'amakuru yashizweho mu 2012 nk'igice c'Igisabwa co Gukura "Ultraprune" kigamije gusubirira BerkeleyDB. Iryo hinduka ryaragize ingaruka zikomeye, harimwo no kurema igice ca mbere c’amabarabara n’ugusubira gutunganya amabarabara 24 ku wa 12 Ntwarante 2013. Ivyo vyabaye vyari vyaravuzwe mu buryo burambuye muri BIP50. Mu nyuma, iyo mpinduka mbere yatumye haba fork ikomeye itagamije ku wa 15 Rusama 2013.
