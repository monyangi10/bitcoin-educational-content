---
term: BIP0043
definition: Standard ishiraho ikibanza ca purpose mu nzira za HD derivation mu kumenya ubwoko bw'iwallet yakoreshejwe (akarorero m/84' kuri SegWit).
---

Iciyumviro co gushiramwo urugero rw'inzira y'ugukuraho kugira ngo hasobanurwe intumbero mu mibumbe ya HD Wallet, nk'uko vyashizweho mu ntango muri BIP32. Dushingiye kuri BIP43, urugero rwa mbere rwo gukuraho HD Wallet, inyuma gato y’urufunguzo nyamukuru rwerekanwa na `m/`, rwabikiwe umubare w’intumbero yerekana urugero rw’ugukuraho rukoreshwa mu nzira isigaye. Uwo mubare wandikwa nk’urutonde rukomeye. Nk'akarorero, iyo Wallet ikurikije urugero rwa SegWit (BIP84), intango y'inzira y'ugukomoka kwayo yoba: `m/84'/`.

BIP43 iratomora ingingo ngenderwako zikurikizwa na Wallet imwe imwe, igatuma habaho ugukorana neza hagati y’ugushirwa mu ngiro gutandukanye. Uguhuza ingero z'inzira zikurikira zivugwa muri BIP44.
