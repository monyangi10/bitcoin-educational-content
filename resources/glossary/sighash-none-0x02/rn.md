---
term: SIGHASH_NONE (0X02)

---

Ubwoko bwa SigHash Ibendera rikoreshwa mu mikono y'ibikorwa vya Bitcoin kugira ngo ryereke ko iyo sinyatire ikoreshwa ku vyinjizwa vyose vy'ibikorwa, ariko nta na kimwe mu bisohoka vyayo. Ikoreshwa rya `SIGHASH_NONE` risobanura ko uwusinya yiyemeza gusa ivyo yinjiza, bikaba vyemeza ko ivyo asohora biguma bitamenyekanye canke bihindurwa inyuma yo gusinya. Ubwo bwoko bw’umukono burakenewe mu bihe aho uwushize umukono yipfuza kwemerera abandi bantu gufata ingingo y’ingene ama bitcoins azokwiragizwa mu gucuruza.
