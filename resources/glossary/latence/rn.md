---
term: GUTEBA
---

Gucererwa hagati yo gutanga amakuru n’ayo kwakira. Mu gukoresha mudasobwa, rigereranya igihe amakuru afata kugira ngo agende hagati ya mudasobwa zibiri. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, latency ishobora kwigwa mu bijanye n’igihe bifata kugira ngo ama blocs amenyekane ku rubuga rwose. Uguteba cane birashobora gutuma abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bari ukwabo baba mu kaga, kuko babandanya gukora ku mabuye yari amaze gukurwaho n’ayandi mashasha gushika baronse ayo mabuye mashasha. Latency rero mu bisanzwe iraremesha abacukuzi gukorana. Bigabanya kandi umutekano wose wa sisitemu mu gutakaza umwanya mu kurondera Proof of Work ibereye. Intumbero yo gushiramwo igihe c’iminota 10 hagati y’ibarabara rimwe rimwe ni ingero yo kugabanya ingaruka z’uguteba kuri sisitemu.
