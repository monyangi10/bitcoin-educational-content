---
term: LATENCE
---

Delay between the transmission and reception of information. In computing, it represents the time taken for data to travel between two computers. In the context of Bitcoin, latency can be studied in terms of the time it takes for blocks to be broadcast across the network. High latency can put isolated miners at a disadvantage, as they continue to work on blocks that have already been invalidated by new ones until they receive the new blocks. Latency therefore naturally encourages miners to group together. It also reduces the overall security of the system by wasting time searching for a valid proof of work. The goal of putting a 10-minute period between each block is a measure to reduce the impact of latency on the system.
