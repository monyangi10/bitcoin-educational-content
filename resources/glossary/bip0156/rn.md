---
term: BIP0156
definition: Dandelion, uburyo bwongereza ibanga mu kurungika amatunshwa mu guhisha inode y'inkomoko.
---

Iciyumviro, kizwi nka Dandelion, kigamije gutuma ubuzima bwite bw’inzira y’ubudandaji butera imbere mu rubuga rwa Bitcoin kugira ngo harwanywe ugukuraho amazina y’abantu. Mu bikorwa bisanzwe vya Bitcoin, amafaranga ahita atangazwa ku bice vyinshi.

Iyo umuntu yihweza ashobora gukurikirana igikorwa cose gishikirizwa n’urudodo rumwe rumwe, yoshobora kwiyumvira ko urudodo rwa mbere rwohereza igikorwa na rwo nyene ari rwo rwakomotseko, bikaba bishobora guhuza amafaranga asanzwe atamenyekana n’amaderesi ya IP.


Ku Address ivyo, BIP156 izana igice c’inyongera imbere y’uko itangazwa ku mugaragaro kugira ngo umuntu ntamenyekane.

Iryo tegeko rikoresha intambwe zibiri:


- igice c’“uruti”, aho ugucuruza gushikirizwa biciye mu nzira y’ibihimba,

*n'igice ca "fluff", aho kimenyeshwa ku rubuga rwose.*


Uruti n’ubwoya ni ibimenyetso vyerekana inyifato y’ugukwiragira kw’isoko biciye ku rubuga, bisa n’imiterere y’igiti citwa dandelion.


Ubwo buryo bwo gutanga inzira burapfukiranya inzira ijana ku nzira y’inkomoko, bikaba bituma bigorana gukurikirana amafaranga asubira aho yava.

Dandelion iratuma ubuzima bwite butera imbere cane mu kugabanya ubushobozi bw’abansi bwo gukuraho izina ry’urubuga. Ubu buryo burakora neza cane iyo igikorwa co gucuruza kijabutse mu gihe c'igice ca "stem" urudodo rupfuka amakuru y'urubuga rwayo, nk'uko bigenda kuri Tor canke *P2P Transport V2*. BIP156 ntirashirwa kuri Bitcoin core.
