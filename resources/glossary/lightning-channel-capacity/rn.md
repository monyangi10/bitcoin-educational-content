---
term: Ubushobozi bwa lightning channel
definition: Igice ntarengwa ca bitcoin gishobora guca kuri canal ya Lightning, gishinzwe iyo ikozwe.
---

Igitigiri c'ama bitcoins yugarijwe mu Address ifise umukono mwinshi iserukira umurongo wo kwishura kuri Lightning Network. Ubushobozi bw’umurongo rero ni umubare munini cane wa Sats (satoshis) ushobora guca muri uwo muhora wihariye. Bisobanurwa igihe umurongo uremwa n’umubare w’amahera umuhari umwe yiyemeje guha umurongo. "Ubushobozi bwo kwinjira" buvuga umubare munini w'ama bitcoins node ishobora kwakira biciye ku muhora. "Ubushobozi bwo gusohoka" bugereranya umubare munini w'ama bitcoins node ishobora kohereza biciye ku muhora wihariye.
