---
term: WABISABI

---

Itegeko ry'ubufatanye ry'i Chaumian n'umuhuzabikorwa wa mbere akoreshwa muri Wasabi 2.0 wallet. Ico kigereranyo cakozwe na Ádám Ficsór (nopara73), Yuval Kogman, Lucas Ontivero, na István András Seres mu 2021, hanyuma gishirwa muri porogarama ya Wasabi 2.0 umwaka wakurikiyeho.
