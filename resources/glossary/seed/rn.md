---
term: INTETE
---

Mu bijanye n’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin, seed ni amakuru y’ibice 512 akomoka ku kintu cabaye ataco kivuze. Ikoreshwa mu gushinga intahe no gukurikirana generate urutonde rw'imfunguruzo z'ibanga, n'imfunguruzo za bose zihuye, ku bijanye n'ibikorwa vya Bitcoin. seed akenshi ivangwa n’ijambo ry’ugukira ubwaryo. Ariko si co kimwe. seed iboneka mu gukoresha igikorwa ca `PBKDF2` ku nvugo ya Mnemonic na passphrase iyo ari yo yose.


seed yahinguwe na BIP32, yasobanura imishinge y’ivy’uburongozi bw’ivy’ubuhinga. Muri iyo ngingo, seed yari ipima ibice 128. Ivyo bituma imfunguruzo zose ziri mu gitabo zishobora gukurwa ku makuru amwe, bitandukanye n’ibitabo vya JBOK (*Just a Bunch Of Keys*), bisaba ko habaho ububiko bushasha ku rufunguzo rwose ruvutse. BIP39 yaciye itanga iciyumviro co gushiramwo amajambo y’iyi seed, kugira ngo abantu bashobore kuyisoma. Iryo kode rifata uburyo bw’ijambo, muri rusangi ryitwa ijambo Mnemonic canke ijambo ry’ugusubirana. Iryo tegeko ririnda amakosa igihe uzigama seed, canecane bivuye ku gukoresha umubare w’igenzura.


Hanze y’ivyo Bitcoin ivuga, mu gupfuka muri rusangi, seed ni agaciro k’intango gakoreshwa ku mfunguruzo z’ugupfuka za generate, canke mu buryo bwagutse, ubwoko bwose bw’amakuru akomoka ku mubare w’ibinyoma. Iryo gaciro ry’intango ritegerezwa kuba ry’imburakimazi kandi ritamenyekana kugira ngo ryemeze umutekano w’uburyo bwo gukingira amakuru. Nkako, seed yinjiza entropie muri iyo nzira, ariko inzira y’uruvyaro ikurikira ni iyo gushinga intahe.


> ► *Mu rurimi rusangi, benshi mu bakoresha bitcoiners bavuga ijambo Mnemonic iyo bavuga "seed", ntibavuga ivy'inkomoko iri hagati y'ijambo Mnemonic n'urufunguzo nyamukuru.*
