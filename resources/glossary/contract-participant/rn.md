---
term: Contract Participant
---

Muri RGB, Contract Participant ni umukinyi agira uruhara mu bikorwa bijanye na Contract. Hariho itandukaniro hagati ya :




- Uwutanga Contract, ari we arema Genesis (inkomoko ya Contract);
- Abagize Contract, ni ukuvuga abafise uburenganzira ku gihugu ca Contract ;
- Amashirahamwe ya Leta, ashobora kwubaka Ama Extensions ya Leta iyo Contract itanga Valencies zishikira abantu bose.
