---
term: URUGANGANI-KU-RUGANZANI (P2P)
---

Yerekeza ku citegererezo co guhanahana amakuru no gukwiragiza amakuru aho abaje mu nama, akenshi bitwa nodes canke peers, basangira ubutunzi bwabo (nk’amadosiye, ubushobozi bwo gukora, uburebure bw’uruzitiro, itunga...) ataco baciye hagati yabo, ata n’umwe akeneye umuhuza ahurikiye hamwe. Mu nzira ya P2P, uwuriko aragira uruhara wese akora icarimwe nk’umukiriya (umukoresha w’ibikoresho) n’umukozi (umutanga ibikoresho).


Uburyo bwa Bitcoin bukora ku buryo bwa P2P, aho ama node ashinzwe kwemeza amafaranga no kubungabunga Blockchain. Ivyo bisigura ko, bitandukanye n’uburyo bwa kera bwo gukoresha amabanki bwishingikiriza ku bigo bihurikiye hamwe, Bitcoin ikora ku buryo busanzwe aho ata kigo kimwe gifise ububasha. Ivyo bihimba biri mu muhora wa Bitcoin biravugana kugira ngo bimenyeshe amakuru n'amabuye, no kugira ngo bironke amasezerano ku bijanye n'ingene Ledger imeze.
