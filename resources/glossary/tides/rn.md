---
term: TIDES

---

Ijambo ry'inyongera rya "*Index y'Imigabane Itandukanye*". Ni uburyo bwo kubara indishi y’abacukuzi mu bidengeri vya mining vyashizweho n’ikidengeri ca Ocean mu 2023. Ubwo buryo butanga impembo zishingiye ku ijanisha ry’igikorwa cagenewe imigabane iherutse kuboneka. Ikimenyamenya cose kirahembwa incuro nyinshi, hakaba n’ibara ry’impera ririmwo amahera y’ugucuruza. Ubwo buryo buratuma habaho ukuri gukomeye mu kwishura abacukuzi, ata nkenerwa y’umuhuza w’abacukuzi kugira ngo akore ivyo kwishura, bitandukanye n’ubundi buryo nka FPPS. TIDES yagenewe guhembwa mu buryo buboneye.
