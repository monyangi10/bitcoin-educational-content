---
term: BTCD (IKIBAZO ca BTC)
---

Ishirwa mu ngiro ry’amasezerano ya Bitcoin n’abantu bake. BTCD ni ubundi buryo bwo gukoresha porogarama ya Bitcoin core (cane cane bitcoind).

Udakunze Bitcoin core, ikoreshwa mu rurimi rwa C++, BTCD yandikwa mu rurimi rwa Go (golang). Intumbero yayo ni iyo kwubahiriza amategeko y’uguhurizako yashizweho na Bitcoin core kugira ngo bihure n’izindi nzira z’uruja n’uruza. BTCD iriko irategurwa nk’igice c’umugambi wa BTC Suite.
