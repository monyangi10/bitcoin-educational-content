---
term: Recovery path
definition: Urutonde rw'indapi zikora inyuma y'igihe ntarengwa (timelock) kugira ngo ugarukane amahera muri Miniscript.
---

Mu porogarama ya Wallet ikoresha Miniscript, nka Liana nk’akarorero, inzira zo gusubirana zivuga imigwi y’imfunguruzo zitangura gukora gusa inyuma y’igihe gitegekanijwe c’ukudakora mu nyandiko ifunga ama bitcoins (timelock). Inzira zo gusubiza amahera zikoreshwa gusa iyo igihe c’ugufunga kirangiye, gutyo bikaba bitanga uburyo bwo gusubiza amahera iyo inzira ya mbere itaboneka. Rimbura akarorero k’inyandiko irimwo imfunguruzo 2 zitandukanye: urufunguzo A, rwemerera gukoresha bitcoins ubwo nyene, n’urufunguzo B, rwemerera kuyakoresha inyuma y’ugucerezwa gusobanurwa n’igihe c’amabuye 52.560. Muri aka karorero, urufunguzo A ruva mu nzira y’intango, mu gihe urufunguzo B ruva mu nzira yo gusubirana.
