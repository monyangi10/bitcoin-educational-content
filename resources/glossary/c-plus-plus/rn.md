term: C++ (KWONGERA KWONGERA)
---

Ururimi rwo gukora porogarama rushobora gukoreshwa mu buryo bwinshi rwavuye muri C, ruzwi kubera ububasha bwarwo n’uguhinduranya. Ikoreshwa cane mu gutegura porogarama zikomeye, ishigikira porogarama zishingiye ku bintu kandi itanga ibintu bikomeye vyo gucunga ubwonko no gukoresha ibikoresho vya sisitemu.


Bitcoin core, ni yo ishirwa mu ngiro ry'umurongo wa Bitcoin, yanditswe mu rurimi rwa C++. Ivyo ni iragi ry’umukiriya w’intango wa Satoshi, na we nyene yanditswe muri uru rurimi rwa porogarama. o kumenya ko inyifato y'ibihimba bihuye neza, bihambaye mu kwirinda amaforogo atagamije, vyafashwe ingingo yo kuguma hakoreshwa C++ kuva mu 2009.
