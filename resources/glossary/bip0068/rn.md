---
term: BIP0068
---

BIP68 yashizeho ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ibihe vy'ugufunga biciye ku mwanya wa `nSequence`.

Ivyo bituma igikorwa gishobora kugaragaza igihe co guteba imbere y'uko gishobora gushirwa mu gice. Ivyo bishobora gusobanurwa n'umubare w'ibice, canke n'incuro nyinshi z'amasegonda 512 (ni ukuvuga igihe nyaco).

Zirikana ko iyi nsobanuro nshasha y'umurima wa `nSequence` ikora gusa iyo umurima wa `nVersion` uruta canke ungana na `2`. Iyi nsobanuro y'umurima wa `nSequence` ibaho ku rugero rw'amategeko y'uguhurizako ya Bitcoin.

Udakunze igihe c'ugufunga, kigaragaza igihe gihoraho imbere y'uko igikorwa kitashobora gushirwa mu gice, igihe c'ugufunga gisobanura igihe co gufunga gitangura igihe igikorwa c'ugucuruza kivuzwe cemejwe.

BIP68 yashizweho biciye ku Soft Fork ku wa 4 Nyakanga 2016, iruhande ya BIP112 na BIP113, vyakoreshejwe ku ncuro ya mbere hakoreshejwe uburyo bwa BIP9.
