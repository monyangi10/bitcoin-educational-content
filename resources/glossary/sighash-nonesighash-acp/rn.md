---
term: SIGHASH_NONE/SIGHASH_ACP

definition: Uburyo bwa SigHash businya ku cinjiye kimwe gusa ata co bwemeye ku vyasohotse.
---

Ubwoko bw'Ibendera rya SigHash (`0x82`) rifatanijwe n'umuhinduzi w'ibara ry'umubiri (`SIGHASH_ACP`) rikoreshwa mu mikono y'ibikorwa vya Bitcoin. Iryo huriro ryerekana ko umukono ukoreshwa gusa ku vyo winjije, ataco wiyemeje gusohoka. Ivyo bituma abandi bari muri iyo nama bongerako mu mwidegemvyo ibindi bintu vy’inyongera no guhindura ivyo vyose biva mu bikorwa.
