---
term: GUISETINGI.INI.BAK
---

Dosiye muri Bitcoin core ikoreshwa mu kubika ububiko bw'imiterere y'umukoresha w'igishushanyo Interface (GUI). Iyi nkuru iremeshwa iyo ukoresheje uburyo bwa `-resetguisettings`, busubizaho amategeko ya GUI ku gaciro kayo ka mbere. Iyi dosiye iremesha uwukoresha gusubiramwo imiterere yiwe ya kera iyo bikenewe.
