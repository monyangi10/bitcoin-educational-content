---
term: Cgminer

definition: Isoseti y'ivunjisha ifunguye kuri CPU na GPU, yashizwe mu mushinga wa BOSminer wa Braiins.
---

Porogaramu y’inkomoko yuguruye mining yakorewe mbere na mbere ku CPU na GPU. CGminer ishoboza abakoresha gucunga no gutuma ibikorwa vyabo vya mining bigenda neza, n’ibintu nk’ugukoresha imirongo myinshi, gucunga amazi menshi n’ugukurikirana ibikorwa mu gihe nyaco. Iyi porogaramu ntaco ikora vy’ukuri muri iki gihe, ariko yatwawe n’umugambi wa Braiins witwa BOSminer.
