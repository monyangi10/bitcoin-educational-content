---
term: ISESENGURA RY'URUNYUNZURO
---

Ivyigwa birimwo uburyo bwose bukoreshwa mu gukurikirana uruja n’uruza rw’ama bitcoins kuri Blockchain. Muri rusangi, isesengura ry’uruzitiro rishingiye ku kwihweza ibiranga mu ngero z’ibikorwa vya kera. Bica birimwo kumenya ivyo bimenyetso nyene mu gucuruza umuntu yipfuza gusuzuma, no gukuraho insobanuro zishobora kwemerwa. Ubwo buryo bwo gutorera umuti ingorane, bushingiye ku buryo bukora bwo kuronka umuti mwiza uhagije, buzwi kw’izina rya heuristic. Kugira ngo vyorohe, isesengura ry’uruzitiro rikorwa mu ntambwe zibiri nyamukuru:


- Kumenya ibiranga bizwi;

*Gukuraho ivyiyumviro.*


Kimwe mu ntumbero z’isesengura ry’uruzitiro ni ugushira hamwe ibikorwa bitandukanye kuri Bitcoin kugira ngo hamenyekane ubudasa bw’uwubikoresha yabikoze. Hanyuma, bizoshoboka ko umuntu agerageza guhuza uwo mugwi w’ibikorwa n’akaranga nyakuri biciye ku nzira y’injira. Ni ngombwa gutahura ko ugusesangura uruzitiro atari ubuhinga nyabwo. Ishingiye ku buhinga bwo gutahura ibintu (heuristics) buva ku vyo umuntu yabonye mbere canke ku nsobanuro zijanye n’ubwenge. Aya mategeko atuma umuntu aronka ibisubizo vyizigirwa, ariko ntabwo yigera agira ukuri gushitse. Mu yandi majambo, isesengura ry’uruzitiro ryama rifise urugero rw’ibishoboka mu nsozero zishikirizwa. Nk’akarorero, birashobora kugereranywa ata gukeka ko amaderesi abiri ari ay’ikigo kimwe, mugabo ukudakeka kwose kuzokwama kutaboneka. Intumbero yose y’isesengura ry’uruzitiro iri mu gukoranya neza ubuhinga butandukanye kugira ngo hagabanuke ingorane zo gukora amakosa. Mu buryo bumwe, ni ukwirundanira ibimenyamenya bituma twegera ukuri. Ivyo bimenyetso bizwi cane bishobora gushirwa mu mice itandukanye:


- Ivyitegererezo vy’ugucuruza (canke ivyitegererezo vy’ugucuruza);

*Heuristics imbere mu gucuruza;*

*Ivyiyumviro biva hanze y'ugucuruza.*


Biragaragara ko izo heuristics zibiri za mbere ku Bitcoin zateguwe na Satoshi Nakamoto ubwiwe. Arabishura mu gice ca 10 c’Igitabu Cera. Birashimishije kubona ko izo nzira zibiri ziguma zifise akamaro kanini mu gusesangura uruhererekane muri iki gihe. Ivyo ni vyo bikoreshwa mu gusubira gukoresha Ownership (CIOH) n’ugusubira gukoresha Address.
