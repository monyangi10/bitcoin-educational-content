---
term: ONION_PRIVATE_KEY

definition: Ikaratasi ya kera ya Bitcoin Core yabika urufunguruzo rwiherereye kuri Tor V2.
---

Dosiye yahora ikoreshwa muri Bitcoin Core kubika urufunguzo rwihariye rufitaniye isano n'umurimo w'ibanga wa Tor V2 ku mahitamwo ya `-listenonion`. Iyi dosiye ntigikoreshwa kuva kuri verisiyo 0.21.0 ku neza ya Tor V3.
