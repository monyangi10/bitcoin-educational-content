---
term: Eclair

definition: Ishirwa mu ngiro ry'amategeko ya Lightning Network mu rurimi rwa Scala, ryakozwe na Acinq.
---

Ishirwa mu ngiro rikomeye ry’umurongo wa Lightning Network wanditswe mu rurimi rwa Scala. Eclair ikoreshwa n’ishirahamwe ry’abafaransa ryitwa Acinq.


> ► *Iciyumviro, "Eclair" ryari kandi izina ry'umuco wallet w'ibikoresho vy'amaboko, vyakozwe n'iyo sosiyete nyene. Muri iki gihe, iyo wallet ntibagitunganirizwa.*
