---
term: UASF
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikirundi "*Soft Fork yakoreshejwe n'ukoresha*". Idondora Soft Fork muri Bitcoin iyo itangujwe kandi igashirwa mu ngiro n’abakoresha urubuga biciye ku nzira zabo, ata kwizigira kwemererwa na Miner. Ivyo bice vy’uruja n’uruza birahindura porogarama zavyo kugira ngo vyemeze amategeko mashasha ya Soft Fork, maze bireke ama chips agwe aho ashobora!


Mu bisanzwe ikoreshwa mu bihe vy’ivyihutirwa, cane cane iyo abacukuzi barwanya cane kwemera Soft Fork, UASF ikora nk’uburyo bwo gutera umukazo kugira ngo ububasha bube burengeje urugero mu bacukuzi. Mu bikorwa, UASF mbere yaracitse igikoresho co guhagarika abantu, gishirwako ikimenyetso n’abakoresha ama node iyo abacukuzi bakoresheje nabi ububasha bwabo. Ariko iyo UASF ishizwe mu ngiro vy’ukuri, iratwara ingorane, harimwo n’uko Blockchain yoshobora guca ibice, bikazana uruhererekane rushasha rushobora kubura agaciro mu vy’ubutunzi n’umutekano. Iciyumviro ca mbere c’uko UASF ikora cavuye ku muhinga Shaolin Fry, yasunikiye BIP148 muri Ntwarante 2017 kugira ngo ahatire abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro banse gutanga ikimenyetso ca SegWit.
