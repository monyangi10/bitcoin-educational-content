---
term: Izinjira nini
definition: Uburyo bwo gusesengura bwerekana ko igisohotse (output) kinini mu ihererekanya ari ayagarutse (change).
---

Heuristic ku bijanye n’isesengura ry’uruzitiro rw’imbere. Iyo habonetse itandukaniro rinini bihagije hagati y’ibiva mu bikorwa bibiri vy’ugucuruza mu buryo bworoshe bwo kwishura, birashobora kugereranywa ko umusaruro munini cane ushobora kuba ari wo mpinduka. Iyi heuristic y’umusaruro munini cane kumbure ni yo idatomoye kuruta izindi zose. Iyo imenyekanye ubwayo, iragoyagoya cane. Ariko rero, ico kiranga gishobora gufatanywa n’ibindi bimenyetso vy’ubuhinga (heuristics) kugira ngo bigabanye ukudakeka kw’insobanuro yacu.
