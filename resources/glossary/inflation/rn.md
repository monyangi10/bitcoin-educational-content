---
term: IBICIRO
---

Yerekeza ku kwiyongera kw’amahera Supply ariko arakoreshwa, bikaba bituma agaciro k’ikintu cose c’amahera kagabanuka, igiciro c’ibintu n’ibikorwa kikaduga. Ku mafaranga ya Leta, ico kintu kiba iyo amabanki akomeye asohoye amafaranga mashasha.


Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ugutera imbere kw’ibiciro kugenzurwa n’itegeko. Bitcoins nshasha ziterwa n’ukwemezwa kw’ibarabara ryose biciye ku nkunga ihabwa Miner yatsinze. Iyi nkunga yari 50 BTC ku butaka, kandi igabanywa igice ku butaka 210.000 mu gikorwa citwa "Halving". Igitigiri cose c’amahera ya Bitcoin gishobora gushika ku miriyoni 21, ivyo bikaba bisobanura ko ugutera imbere kw’amahera ya BTC kuriko kuragabanuka kandi kuzohagarara iyo umubare munini w’amahera ushitse.
