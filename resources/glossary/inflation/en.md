---
term: INFLATION
---

Refers to the increase in the money supply in circulation, leading to a fall in the value of each unit of money and a rise in the price of goods and services. For state-owned currencies, this phenomenon occurs when central banks issue new currency units.

In the Bitcoin context, inflation is controlled by the protocol. New bitcoins are generated with each block validation via the subsidy awarded to the victorious miner. This subsidy was initially 50 BTC per block, and is halved every 210,000 blocks in an event known as "Halving". Total bitcoin issuance is capped at around 21 million units, which means that BTC monetary inflation is decreasing and will stop once the maximum number of units is reached.
