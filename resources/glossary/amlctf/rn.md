---
term: AML/CTF
---

Iterambere ry’ukurwanya ubusuma bw’amahera n’iterabwoba (AML/CTF) ryerekeye ku ngingo z’amategeko zafashwe kugira ngo hatagira Bitcoin ikoreshwa mu bikorwa bitemewe n’amategeko. Izo ngingo zirimwo kumenya no kugenzura akaranga k’abaguzi (KYC), gukurikirana ibikorwa kugira ngo hamenyekane uburyo "buteye amakenga", no gukorana n’ubutegetsi kugira ngo bamenyeshe ibikorwa bibonwa ko bitemewe n’amategeko. Amashirahamwe y’ubudandaji ategekanijwe arakenewe kugira ngo yubahirize izo ngingo kugira ngo akore mu bihugu vyinshi, harimwo n’Ubufaransa.
