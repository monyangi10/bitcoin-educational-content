---
term: BIP0090
---

BIP90 isaba kworohereza gukoresha amaforogo ya Soft ya kera mu gusubirira ikimenyetso ca Miner biciye ku mibare y’amabara n’ugusuzuma uburebure bw’amabara yoroshe.

Iryo hinduka rikuraho ivy’ugucapura amabuye 1.000 ya kera igihe hakoreshwa amategeko y’uguhurizako, gutyo bikagabanya umwenda w’ubuhinga ujanye n’ugushira mu ngiro izo nzira za Soft.
