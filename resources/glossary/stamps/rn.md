---
term: Stamps
definition: Uburyo butuma amakuru y'ibishushanyo ashirwa kuri blockchain ya Bitcoin biciye kuri transaction za P2MS.
---

Itegeko ryemerera gushiramwo amakuru y’amashusho yatunganijwe ataco akora kuri Bitcoin Blockchain biciye mu gukoresha imikono myinshi (P2MS). Ibimenyetso bishiramwo ibirimwo bibiri vy'ishusho mu rwego rwa 64 maze bikavyongera ku mfunguruzo za 1/3 P2MS. Urufunguzo rumwe ni urw’ukuri kandi rukoreshwa mu gukoresha amahera, mu gihe izindi zibiri ari urufunguzo rw’ibinyoma (urufunguzo rw’ibanga rujanye n’urwo ntiruzwi) rubika amakuru. Mu gushiramwo amakuru mu buryo butaziguye nk'imfunguruzo za bose aho gukoresha ibisohoka `OP_RETURN`, amashusho abitswe n'umurongo w'ibimenyetso ni cane cane igikorwa gikomeye ku nzira. Ubu buryo buratuma habaho UTXO nyinshi, ivyo bikaba bituma ubunini bwa UTXO bugira ingorane kandi bikazana ingorane ku nzira zishitse.
