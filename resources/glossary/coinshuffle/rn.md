---
term: GUTERERA AMAFARANGA
---

Itegeko ry’uguvanga Bitcoin ryashikirijwe mu 2014 na Tim Ruffing, Pedro Moreno-Sanchez, na Aniket Kate, ryahumekewe n’iciyumviro cashizweho na Gregory Maxwell. Coinshuffle iremesha guca amateka ya Coin ata muntu wa gatatu yizigirwa asaba. Iryo tegeko rituma mbere n’umuhuza adashobora guhuza uwuriha n’uwuhabwa. Ico ciyumviro nticigeze cemerwa cane, ubuhinga bwo gukingira ubuzima bwite nk’ubwa Chaumian CoinJoin ni bwo bushimwa.
