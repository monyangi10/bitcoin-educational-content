---
term: IKIGEREREZO C'IBUBIRO
---

Ivyiyumviro vy’amakuru bitangwa n’urudodo rwa Bitcoin kuri porogarama ya Mining canke ikidengeri, kirimwo amakuru akenewe kugira ngo wubake igice gishasha c’abashaka gutora. Iyi miterere irimwo urutonde rw'ibikorwa vyatowe kugira ngo bishirwemwo n'umutwe w'ibarabara. Iyo Miner imaze kuronka ayo makuru, barashobora gutangura kurondera Proof of Work ibereye ku bijanye n’ibarabara ry’abashaka gutora.
