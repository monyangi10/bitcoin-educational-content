---
term: SUBIRAMWO
---

Ibidukikije vy'igerageza vy'ibanga vya Bitcoin bishobora gutuma abahinguzi bashobora gukora urubuga rwa Bitcoin rwo mu karere rufise amategeko yihariye. Udakunze Testnet, regtest ni iy’ibanga, bisobanura ko bidashoboka ko umuntu ayifatanya na yo mu mwidegemvyo. Vyongeye, iratanga ububasha bwose ku bijanye n’imiterere y’urubuga. Ivyo bishobora gutuma habaho ubuhinga bwo gukora Mining no kugerageza ibintu vyihariye. Ikoreshwa mu gutegura, gukosora, no kugerageza imbere yo gushirwa mu ngiro kuri Testnet canke Mainnet. Ivyo bitcoins bigendagenda ku regtest nta gaciro bifise.


> ► *Regtest ni ugucapura kw'ijambo "Isubira inyuma" n'"Ikigeragezo". Mu congereza, ryoshobora guhindurwa ngo "Ikigeragezo co gusubira inyuma".*
