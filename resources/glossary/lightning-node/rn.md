---
term: LIGHTNING NODE

---

Mudasobwa ikoresha ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa Lightning Network (Eclair, LND, C-Imiravyo...). Igikoresho ca Lightning gishirwa ku gikoresho ca Bitcoin kandi gishoboza kurema no gucunga imihora y’ukwishura y’inzira zibiri hagati y’abakoresha batandukanye. Ivyuma vy’umuravyo birahuye, bikora uruja n’uruza rw’imihora yo kwishura. Izo nzira zituma habaho umubare hafi w’ibikorwa bitagira aho bigarukira ata nkenerwa yo kubitangaza ku giti cabo ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.
