---
term: Index (key)
definition: Inomero ikurikirana ihabwa urufunguzo rw'umwana (child key) kugira ngo rugaragare mu zindi muri HD wallet.
---

Mu bijanye n'igitabo ca HD, yerekeza ku mubare w'urutonde wahawe urufunguzo rw'umwana rwavuye mu rufunguzo rw'umuvyeyi. Iyi index ikoreshwa hamwe n'urufunguzo rw'umuvyeyi na kode y'urudodo rw'umuvyeyi kugira ngo ubone urufunguzo rw'abana rwihariye. Bishoboza gutunganya neza no gusubiramwo imfunguruzo z’abana b’abavukanyikazi zivuye ku rufunguzo rw’umuvyeyi umwe. Ni umubare wose w'ibice 32 ukoreshwa mu gikorwa co gukuraho `HMAC-SHA512`. Uyu mubare ushobora gukoreshwa mu gutandukanya imfunguruzo z'abana mu gitabo ca HD.
