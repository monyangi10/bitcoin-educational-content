---
term: BIP0034
definition: Soft fork isaba ishirwaho ry'uburebure bwa blok mu itunshwa rya coinbase, ivyemeza ko buri blok itandukanye n'izindi.
---

Soft Fork yatanguye gukora muri Ntwarante 2013, itanguye kuva ku gice ca 227.930, kikaba cashizeho verisiyo ya 2 ku gice ca Bitcoin. Iyi verisiyo nshasha y'ibarabara yasaba ko `scriptSig` ya Coinbase Transaction irimwo uburebure bw'ibarabara ririko riremwa.

Iryo hinduka ryasobanuye ingene urubuga rwemera guhindura imiterere y’amabarabara n’amategeko y’uguhurizako kandi ryashize mu ngiro ubudasa bw’amabarabara yose na Coinbase Transaction yayo.
