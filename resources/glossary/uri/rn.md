---
term: URI
definition: Uburyo bwemejwe butuma hashobora kwandikwa aderesi ya Bitcoin n'ibindi bikenewe kugira ngo itunshwa ry'amafaranga ryoroshe.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikigiriki "*Ikimenyetso c'Itunga rimwe*". Ni uburyo bw’urudodo busanzwe bukoreshwa mu kumenya ikintu kiri kuri Internet. URI ishobora kuba URL (*Uniform Resource Locator*), itanga uburyo bwo gushika ku kintu mu kwerekana aho kiri ku rubuga rwa mudasobwa, canke URN (*Izina ry’Igikoresho Rimwe*), itanga izina ry’ikintu ataco kivuga. URIs ni ngirakamaro mu gukora kwa *World Wide Web*, kuko zituma umuntu ashobora kuronka ibikoresho nk'amapaji y'urubuga, inyandiko, n'ibikorwa.


Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, URI ikoreshwa cane cane kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukora ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji. Iremeza gushiramwo Address, hamwe n'ibindi bipimo vy'ugucuruza nk'amahera, mu buryo busanzwe hakurikijwe BIP21. Ivyo birorosha uburyo bwo kwishura mu gutuma abakoresha bashobora gufyonda ku nzira canke gucapura kode ya QR, iyo kode igaca yinjiza amakuru akenewe mu gikorwa cabo ca Bitcoin Wallet.
