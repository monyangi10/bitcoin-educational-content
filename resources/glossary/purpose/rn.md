---
term: Purpose
definition: Urwego rwo gukomokako mu ma wallets ya HD rwerekana uburyo bw'ama aderesi yakoreshejwe.
---

Mu bipimo vy’ivy’ubuhinga n’ivy’ubuhinga (HD), intumbero, isobanurwa na BIP43, igereranya urugero rwihariye rw’ivy’ubuhinga. Iyi index, iri ku burebure bwa mbere bw’igiti c’inkomoko (`m / purpose' /`), igaragaza urugero rw’inkomoko rwemejwe n’ibiharuro, kugira ngo bishobore guhuza hagati ya porogarama zitandukanye zo gucunga ibiharuro. Nk'akarorero, ku bijanye n'amaderesi ya SegWit (BIP84), intumbero igaragazwa nk'`/84'/`. Ubu buryo buratuma imfunguruzo zishobora gutunganirizwa neza hagati y’ubwoko butandukanye bwa Address mu gice kimwe ca HD. Index zisanzwe zikoreshwa ni :




- Ku bijanye na P2PKH: `44'` ;
- Ku P2WPKH-ishizwe-mu-P2SH: `49'` ;
- Ku bijanye na P2WPKH: `84'` ;
- Ku P2TR: `86'`.
