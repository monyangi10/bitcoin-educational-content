---
term: DBC
---

Short for "Deterministic Bitcoin Commitment". It's a set of rules enabling a commitment to be entered in a Bitcoin transaction in a provable and unique way. In the RGB protocol, there are two main forms of CAD:


- Opret ;
- Tapret.

These mechanisms define precisely how the commitment is encoded in the outputs or structure of a Bitcoin transaction, to ensure that this commitment is deterministically traceable and verifiable.
