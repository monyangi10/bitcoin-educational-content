---
term: Testnet
definition: Uburyo bwa kabiri bwa Bitcoin bukoreshwa gusa mu kugerageza no gutunganya porogaramu, itagira agaciro nyakuri.
---

Iyindi verisiyo ya Bitcoin ikoreshwa gusa mu bijanye no kugerageza no gutegura. Ni urubuga rutandukanye n’urubuga nyamukuru (Mainnet), rufise amabuye yarwo n’ibikorwa vyarwo, bituma abahinguzi bagerageza ibintu bishasha, ibikorwa, n’ibintu bishasha ataco batera ingorane ku rubuga nyamukuru. Testnet kandi iratuma umuntu yirinda amahera y’ugucuruza mu gihe c’igerageza. Ivyo bikoresho bikoreshwa kuri Testnet nta gaciro nyakuri bifise.
