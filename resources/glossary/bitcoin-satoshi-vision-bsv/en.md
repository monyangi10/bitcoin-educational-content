---
term: BITCOIN SATOSHI VISION (BSV)
---

Cryptocurrency system resulting from a hard fork of Bitcoin Cash (BCH), which itself is a fork of Bitcoin (BTC). The Bitcoin SV fork occurred on November 15, 2018, at block 556,766 due to disagreements within the Bitcoin Cash community, particularly regarding block size and the interpretation of Satoshi Nakamoto’s “vision” for Bitcoin. 
Two factions emerged:
* Supporters of the "Bitcoin Cash ABC" fork, which became Bitcoin Cash (BCH), notably backed by entrepreneur Roger Ver;
* Proponents of the Bitcoin Cash Satoshi Vision fork, which created Bitcoin Satoshi Vision (BSV), notably supported by Craig Wright.

Bitcoin SV differs from Bitcoin Cash, and even more from Bitcoin (BTC), by having a significantly higher block size limit, which its supporters claim better reflects Satoshi Nakamoto’s original vision for Bitcoin.
