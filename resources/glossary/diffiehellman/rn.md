---
term: Diffie-hellman
definition: Uburyo bwa cryptography butuma imice ibiri irema ibanga basangiye biciye mu nziry itakingiwe.
---

Uburyo bwa Cryptographic bushobora gutuma abantu babiri bashobora generate ibanga basangiye ku muhora w’itumanaho udatekanye. Iryo banga rishobora rero gukoreshwa mu gupfuka amakuru hagati y’abo babiri. Diffie-Hellman akoresha imibare y’ibice kugira ngo, naho uwutera yoshobora kwihweza ivyo bihindurwa, ntashobora gukuraho ibanga basangiye ata gutorera umuti ingorane igoye y’imibare: logarithme discrete. Mu Bitcoin, uburyo bwa DH bwitwa ECDH bukoresha umurongo w'uruzitiro rimwe na rimwe burakoreshwa, cane cane ku masezerano ya Address nk'Ivyishyurwa bicereje canke BIP47.
