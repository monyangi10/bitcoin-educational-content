---
term: Bitcoin-DEV
---

E-mail mailing list dedicated to the development of the Bitcoin protocol. Bitcoin-dev was created by Jeff Garzik in 2011 and initially hosted on Sourceforge.net. The mailing list was then moved to Linux Foundation, then to Open Source Lab, and finally, since February 2024, it has been hosted on Google Groups: https://groups.google.com/g/bitcoindev

This mailing list is an exchange point for developers and researchers involved in the evolution of Bitcoin. Discussions are strictly technical or academic. In particular, drafts of BIPs (Bitcoin enhancement proposals) should be sent here, before eventually being integrated into the GitHub repository maintained by Bitcoin Core. Its moderators are currently Ruben Somsen, Bryan Bishop and Warren Togami.

Participation in this list is open to all contributors, but is primarily aimed at those with technical expertise in the Bitcoin field. If you'd like to receive these discussions by e-mail, you can subscribe to this mailing list. If you'd rather have a summary of these discussions, you can also subscribe to the Bitcoin Optech newsletter: https://bitcoinops.org/en/newsletters/
