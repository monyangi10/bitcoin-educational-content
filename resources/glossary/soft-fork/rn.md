---
term: Soft fork
definition: Ihinduka ry'amategeko ya Bitcoin ryisunga ibya kera, ata kwishura k'umuhora kubayeho.
---

Guhindura amategeko y’amasezerano mu buryo bujanye n’inyuma. Udakunze Hard Fork, Soft Fork ntituma utugingo ngengabuzima tw’urubuga rwa Bitcoin tugabanywamwo imigwi ibiri itandukanye, igihe gusa ubushobozi bwinshi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none buri ku ruhererekane rwavuguruwe. Iyo vyose bigenda neza, ama node afise ivugurura n'ama node atagira ivugurura aguma kuri Blockchain imwe. Ihinduka rihuye n'inyuma iyo ryongeyeko canke rituma amategeko amwamwe y'amaporotokole arushirizaho guhagarika.
