---
term: Lightning service provider

definition: Ikigo gitanga serivisi z'amahera (liquidity) ku bakoresha Lightning Network.
---

Sosiyete ikoresha Lightning ifise ubucuruzi bwo gutanga amafaranga ku bakoresha.


Ku Lightning, amahera yishurwa off-chain biciye ku nzira zibiri, ubushobozi bwayo bugabanywa n'amahera yabujijwe muri Multisig 2/2 On-Chain. Uruhara nyamukuru rwa LSP ni ugufasha mu gucunga ayo mahera, haba mu gufasha guhindura amafaranga y’i On-Chain ngo abe Lightning, canke mu gufungura inzira nshasha zo kwongera ubushobozi bw’abakoresha bwo kwinjira.


Izo serivisi zikoreshwa kenshi n’amasakoshi atagira ububiko, kugira ngo abakoresha bashobore guca baronka amahera ya Lightning ataco bakeneye gucungera ugufungura no gucunga imirongo bonyene. Mu guhindura, LSP irasaba amahera.
