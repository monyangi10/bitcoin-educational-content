---
term: LIGHTNING SERVICE PROVIDER
---

Company operating on Lightning whose business is to provide liquidity services to users.

On Lightning, payments are made off-chain via two-way channels, whose capacity is limited by the funds blocked in the Multisig 2/2 On-Chain. The main role of an LSP is to facilitate the management of this liquidity, either by helping to convert On-Chain funds to Lightning, or by opening new channels to increase users' incoming capacity.

These services are often used by non-custodial wallets, so that users can immediately receive Lightning payments without having to manage the opening and management of channels themselves. In exchange, the LSP charges a fee.
