---
term: OP_EQUAL (0X87)

definition: Opcode ngeranya agaciro kabiri k’inyuma ku mupfundikizo ikongeraho 1 mu gihe bingana.
---

Gereranya agaciro kabiri ko hejuru ku kirundo maze isunike `1` ku kirundo iyo zingana, ahandi ho isunika `0`. `OP_EQUAL` ikoreshwa mu kugenzura ukungana kw'amakuru mu nyandiko.
