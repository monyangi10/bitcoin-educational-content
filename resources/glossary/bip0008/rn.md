---
term: BIP0008
---

Yateguwe hakurikijwe impaka ku SegWit, yakoresheje BIP9 mu gukora kwayo, BIP8 ni uburyo bwo gukora Soft Fork bushiramwo uburyo bwikora bwa UASF (*User-Activated Soft Fork*). Cokimwe na BIP9, BIP8 ikoresha ikimenyetso ca Miner ariko yongerako `LOT` (*Igihe co Gufunga*) parameter. Iyo `LOT` ishizwe kuri `ukuri`, igihe igihe co gutanga ikimenyetso kirangiye kitashitse ku rugero rukenewe, UASF iratangura gukora, igatuma Soft Fork ikora. Ubu buryo butera umukazo abacukuzi: bafatanye canke bashire mu kaga Soft Fork ikoreshwa n'abakoresha.

Ikindi kandi, bitandukanye na BIP9, BIP8 isobanura igihe co gutanga ikimenyetso gishingiye ku burebure bw’ibarabara, igakuraho ugukoreshwa nabi biciye ku rugero rwa Hash n’abacukuzi. BIP8 kandi iremeza gushinga urugero rw’amatora ruhinduka kandi igashiraho umurongo w’uburebure bukeyi bw’ibarabara kugira ngo ikore, bikaba biha abacukuzi umwanya wo gutegura no gutanga ikimenyetso c’amasezerano yabo imbere y’igihe ataco biteguye. Iyo Soft Fork ikoreshejwe biciye kuri BIP8 ifise `LOT=true` parameter, ivyo bikoresha uburyo bukomeye cane ku bacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro baca bashirwa munsi y’umukazo w’umuntu ashobora kuba UASF. Nkako, bibasiga amahitamwo 2 gusa:


- Nimube abafatanyabikorwa, gutyo mworohereze inzira yo gukora;
- Kuba uwudakorana, muri ivyo abakoresha baca bakora UASF kugira ngo bategeke Soft Fork.
