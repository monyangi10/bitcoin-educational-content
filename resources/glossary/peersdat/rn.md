---
term: PEERS.DAT

---

Izina rya dosiye y'amakuru ikoreshwa na porogaramu ya Bitcoin Core kugira ngo ibike amakuru yerekeye abo mu runganwe (ni ukuvuga, amanode) mu rubuga node y'ukoresha yakoranye canke ishobora gukorana. Irimwo amakuru nk’amaderesi ya IP, inomero z’ivyambu, n’amakuru atandukanye. Ivyuma biri muri uru rutonde ni ivyuma vya seed, bikurikirwa n’ibindi vyuma vyose vyavumbuwe canke vyongeweko n’amaboko. Iyi dosiye akenshi irimwo urutonde runini cane rw'urunganwe aho urudodo ruhitamwo mu buryo butari bwo kugira ngo rushireho amasano yarwo.
