---
term: UBURYO BW'IBIMENYETSO
---

Uburyo bwo kubara impembo y’abacukuzi mu rwego rw’ibidengeri vya Mining. Ubwo buryo bwo kwishura bushingiye ku manota, bugenewe guhangana n’ikintu co gutera imbere mu mazi. Bituma amahera yishurwa ku mugabane wose ushikirijwe aguma ari imwe, ata kuraba igihe co gutanga. Abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bararundanya amanota, bagaharura hakoreshejwe igiharuro c’ukubora, kandi amahera yishurwa agaharurwa ku mpera y’ingendo. Bihuye n’amanota yabo. Ubwo buryo bujanye n’amahera ahinduka kandi adahinduka ku Miner kandi bugabanya itandukaniro ry’amahera yishurwa ku mugabane.
