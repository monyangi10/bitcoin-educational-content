---
term: Address GUSUBIRAMWO GUKORESHA (ISOHOKA)
---

Address gusubira gukoresha bivugwa ko ari "ivyo hanze" iyo bishitse mu bikorwa vyinshi bitandukanye. Muri iyi nzira, gusubira gukoresha Address hanze ni ubuhinga bwo gusesangura Blockchain bushobora gutuma habaho iciyumviro gishoboka : ko izo aderesi zose ari iz'ikigo kimwe.
