---
term: BIP0137
definition: Uburyo bwashizwe mu rugero bwo gushira umukono ku butumwa hakoreshejwe imfungurazo y'ibanga ya Bitcoin kugira ngo hemezwe ko nyen'adresse ari we.
---

BIP137 itanga uburyo bumwe bwo gusinya ubutumwa bukoresheje imfunguruzo z'ibanga za Bitcoin n'amaderesi yazo, bitanga uburyo bwo kwemeza Ownership ya Address.

Igamije gutorera umuti ukutumvikana hagati y’ubwoko butandukanye bwa Bitcoin Address (P2PKH, P2SH, P2WPKH, n’ibindi) igihe umuntu ashira umukono ku butumwa mu gushiramwo uburyo bwo gutandukanya neza izo nzira biciye ku mikono.

Ivyo bimenyetso birakenewe mu bikorwa bitandukanye nk’ikimenyamenya c’amahera, igenzura, n’ibindi bikorwa bisaba kwemeza Address biciye ku rufunguzo rwayo rw’ibanga.

BIP322 kuva ico gihe yashizeho uburyo bushasha bwo gusinya, iragura kandi irashiramwo muri rusangi ico ciyumviro kugira ngo ishigikire ubwoko bwose bw'inyandiko.
