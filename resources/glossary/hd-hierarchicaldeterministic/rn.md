---
term: Hd (hierarchical-deterministic)
definition: Igapu ikora imfunguzo mu buryo bukurikirana kandi bwisubira bivuye ku mbuto (seed) imwe.
---

Yerekeza kuri Bitcoin Wallet ikoresha amakuru yihariye (seed) ku generate ubwinshi bw'urufunguzo rwa bose n'urw'ibanga mu buryo bukurikirana kandi bushobora gusubirwamwo. Ubu buryo bwo gucunga imfunguruzo busobanurwa n'ingingo ngenderwako ya BIP32. Inyungu nyamukuru y’amasakoshi ya HD ni uko atuma abakoresha bagira amafunguro menshi atandukanye, cane cane kugira ngo ntibasubire gukoresha Address, mu gihe bashobora kubisubiramwo vyose bivuye ku makuru amwe. Iyi mibumbe idondorwa nk'iy'ubukuru kuko ishoboza kurema urutonde rumeze nk'igiti rw'imfunguruzo nyinshi n'amaderesi bivuye kuri seed imwe. Kandi ni deterministic mu buryo bw’uko seed yose yama itanga urutonde rumwe rw’imfunguruzo muri Wallet iyo ari yo yose ihuye n’iyi nzira.
