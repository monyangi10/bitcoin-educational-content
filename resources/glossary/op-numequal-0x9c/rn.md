---
term: OP_NUMEQUAL (0X9C)

definition: Opcode igenzura ko ibintu bibiri biri k’inyuma ku mupfundikizo bingana mu namba.
---

Gereranya ibintu bibiri biri hejuru cane ku rutonde kugira ngo umenye nimba bingana mu mibare. Iyo agaciro kangana, isunika `1` (ukuri) ku ruganda; ahandi ho, isunika `0` (ikinyoma).
