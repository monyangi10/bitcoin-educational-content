---
term: Address GUSUBIZA GUKORESHA (INT)
---

Address gusubira gukoresha bivugwa ko ari "imbere" iyo bibaye mu gihe c'ugucuruza kumwe nk'injiza n'isohoka. Muri iyi ntunganyo, gusubira gukoresha Address yo mu mutima ni ubuhinga bwo gusesangura Blockchain bushobora gutuma habaho iciyumviro gishoboka ku bijanye n’ihinduka ry’ugucuruza. Nkako, iyo hariho ibisohoka bibiri kandi kimwe muri vyo gikoresha Address imwe nyene yakira nk’injiza, rero birashoboka ko igisohoka ca kabiri kiriko kirava mu vy’uwukoresha wa mbere. Igisohoka kiri kumwe na Address yasubiwemwo birashoboka ko ari co kigereranya ihinduka.
