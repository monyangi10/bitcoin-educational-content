---
term: Intambara y'ubunini bw'icitunza

definition: Amatati mu muryango wa Bitcoin (2015-2017) ku bijanye n'izinduka ry'ingano y'ibice (blocks).
---

Ivyo bivuga ku kiringo c’impaka n’amatati akomeye mu muryango wa Bitcoin hagati ya 2015 na 2017. Iyo ntambara yari ishingiye ku kuba ubunini bw’ibarabara, bwari bwagabanutse ku megabyte 1 kuva mu 2010 na Satoshi Nakamoto, bwokwongerwa kugira ngo Bitcoin ishobore gukora cane.


Abashigikiye kwongerera ubunini bw'ibibanza (bizwi nka "*big blockers*"), bayobowe n'abahinguzi ba mbere nka Jeff Garzik, Gavin Andresen, na Mike Hearn, abantu b'abanyabubasha mu bidukikije nka Roger Ver, hamwe n'igice kinini c'amashirahamwe akomeye ya mining kugira ngo iyo nzira yongerewe amafaranga yo guhindura Ugushobora gutera imbere kwa Bitcoin. Babona ko iryo hinduka ryoshobora gukorwa ataco rihinduye ku mutekano w’urwo rutonde.

Ku rundi ruhande, abarwanya ukwongerekana kw’ubunini bw’amabuye (bavugwa ko ari “*ababuza buto*”), ahanini abahinguzi ba Bitcoin Core (Pieter Wuille, Peter Todd, Gregory Maxwell, Wladimir van der Laan, Luke Dashjr...) n’igice c’abahinga mu vy’ubuhinga, bavuga ko ata mabuye manini yokwongerwa. Ahubwo, barateje imbere inyishu z’urugero rwa kabiri, nka Lightning Network, zashobora gukora ibikorwa vyinshi ata guhindura amategeko y’ishimikiro ya Bitcoin.


Naho impaka ku bijanye n’uguhindura Bitcoin zari zihari nibura kuva mu 2010, n’ukugerageza kwa mbere kwo kwongerera ubunini bw’ibarabara biciye ku [igice cashikirijwe na Jeff Garzik](https://bitcointalk.org/index.php?topic=1347.0), iyo ntambara yashitse ku rwego rwo hejuru mu gihe c’Ibarabara ry’Ibarabara). ivyagezwe vyinshi vya fork (Bitcoin XT, Bitcoin Classic, Bitcoin Itagira aho bigarukira, SegWit, SegWit2X, Bitcoin Amahera), amasekeza y’ubukazi y’ivy’iterabwoba, eka mbere n’ibitero vy’abantu ku giti cabo.


SegWit2X hard fork yahebwe ku wa 8 Ugushyingo 2017, inyuma y'ukurwanywa gukomeye kw'abanyagihugu ("NO2X"), ivyo bikaba akenshi bibonwa nk'iherezo ry'intambara ya Blocksize. Naho rimwe na rimwe casus belli ifatwa nk’ivugurura 0.11A ry’umukiriya wa Bitcoin XT, ryashize mu ngiro BIP101 ku wa 15 Myandagaro 2015, vyoshobora kandi kuvugwa ko Intambara ya Blocksize yarangiye ku wa 24 Myandagaro 2017, itariki soft fork GW yashizeho camp1 "UASF".
