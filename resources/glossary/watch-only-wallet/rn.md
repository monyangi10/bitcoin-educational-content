---
term: Watch-only wallet
---

Watch-only wallet ni ubwoko bwa porogarama ishobora gutuma uwuyikoresha abona ibikorwa bijanye n’urufunguzo rwihariye rwa Bitcoin canke urutonde rw’urufunguzo, ata n’urufunguzo rw’ibanga rujanye n’urwo afise. Itanga ukuboneka ku bijanye n’amahera asigaye n’amateka y’ibikorwa, ata kwemerera uwukoresha gukoresha amahera ari muri Wallet. Nk'akarorero, igikorwa ca Sentinel ni Watch-only wallet.
