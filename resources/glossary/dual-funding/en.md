---
term: DUAL FUNDING
---

When a Lightning channel is opened, dual funding allows both participants to lock bitcoins into the channel. Unlike the traditional model, where only one node funds the channel, this method allows both parties to fund it, enabling immediate liquidity balancing.

Dual funding is included in version 2 of the channel-building protocol, which uses a negotiation phase. The two parties work together to create the funding transaction, exchanging messages to add or withdraw bitcoins until the transaction is finalized.
