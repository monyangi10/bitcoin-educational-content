---
term: BLOCKSIGNERS
---

In the context of Liquid (a Bitcoin sidechain), blocksigners are the entities responsible for constructing and validating blocks on the network. 
Liquid operates using a federation model, where blocksigners, selected from federation members, work together to confirm transactions and create new blocks, forming the sidechain’s consensus.
The blocksigner role is one of the official functions in Liquid and include the role of watchmen, who act as custodians of the network’s assets.
