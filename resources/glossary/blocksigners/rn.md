---
term: Abashira umukono ku citunza
definition: Imirwi ishinzwe kwubaka no kwemeza ibice (blocks) muri sidechain ya Liquid.
---

Mu bijanye na Liquid (a Bitcoin Sidechain), abashirako umukono ku mabarabara ni bo bajejwe kwubaka no kwemeza amabarabara ku rubuga.

Liquid ikora ikoresheje uburyo bw’ishirahamwe, aho abashira umukono ku mabarabara, batoranijwe mu bagize ishirahamwe, bakorana kugira ngo bemeze amasezerano no guhingura amabarabara mashasha, bikaba ari vyo bituma Sidechain yemeranya.

Uruhara rw’umunyamuryango ni rumwe mu bikorwa vyemewe muri Liquid kandi rurimwo uruhara rwa watchmen, bakora nk’abacungera umutungo w’urubuga.
