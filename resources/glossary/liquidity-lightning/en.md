---
term: LIQUIDITY
---

Refers to the ability of a Lightning payment channel to pass transactions between two nodes based on the bitcoins available on each side of the channel. There are two types of liquidity: incoming and outgoing. Outgoing liquidity represents the funds that the node can send or transfer via the channel. Incoming liquidity represents the funds it can receive or transfer in the opposite direction. Liquidity can also refer to the sum of liquidity available in all a node's channels.
