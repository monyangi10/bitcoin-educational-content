---
term: BIP0112
definition: Ishirwaho rya opcode OP_CHECKSEQUENCEVERIFY (CSV) ryemera iremwa rya timelocks zijanye muri script.
---

Itangaza opcode `OP_SUZUMA URUTONDE GUSUZUMA` (CSV) mu rurimi rw'inyandiko Bitcoin. Iyi opcode iremesha kurema ibikorwa bifise akamaro gusa inyuma y’ugucerezwa kw’ibikorwa vya kera, bisobanurwa n’umubare w’amabuye canke n’igihe cacitse. `OP_CHECKSEQUENCEVERIFY` igereranya agaciro kari hejuru y'ikirundo n'agaciro ka `nUrukurikirane` k'inyungu.

Iyo ari nini kandi ibindi vyose bishitse, inyandiko irafise akamaro. Mu vy'ukuri, `OP_CHECKSEQUENCEVERIFY` igabanya agaciro kemerewe ku mwanya w'inyungu `nSequence`, ivyo navyo bikaba bigabanya igihe igikorwa kirimwo iyo nkuru gishobora kwemezwa mu gice. BIP112 yashizweho biciye ku Soft Fork ku wa 4 Nyakanga 2016, iruhande ya BIP68 na BIP113, vyakoreshejwe ku ncuro ya mbere hakoreshejwe uburyo bwa BIP9.
