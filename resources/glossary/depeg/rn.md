---
term: Depeg
definition: Ikibazo aho itunga ry'ubuhinga ritakaza agaciro kangana n'itunga ry'ifatizo risanzwe rifatanyijwe naryo.
---

Yerekeza ku gihe agaciro k’umutungo w’ubuhinga bwa none, mu bisanzwe igikoresho c’imyenda, kadafitaniye isano n’umutungo w’ishimikiro usanzwe ushirwako urutonde, bikaba bituma igiciro gihinduka kigereranywa n’uwo mutungo. Ivyo bigaragara cane cane ku ma stablecoins, akoreshwa mu kuguma afise uburinganire bukomeye n’amafaranga y’amahera canke ibindi bintu vy’ishingiro. Depegging ishobora kandi kubaho mu gihe c'ibimenyetso vya Bitcoin ku sidechains canke ETFs, aho iyo habaye ingorane, agaciro k'izina gashobora gutandukana n'aka Bitcoin.
