---
term: ISHAMI-N'IMIRONGO
---

Uburyo bukoreshwa mu guhitamwo ivyinjizwa muri Bitcoin core Wallet kuva kuri verisiyo 0.17 kandi bwavumbuwe na Murch. Branch-and-Bound (BnB) irondera umugwi w’ama UTXO ahuye neza n’ibiva mu bikorwa bisabwa, n’intumbero yo kugabanya ihinduka n’amahera ajanye.

Intumbero yayo ni ugugabanya ingero y’imyanda izirikana ibiciro vy’ubu nyene n’ibiciro vyitezwe muri kazoza vyo guhangana n’ihinduka. Ubu buryo burabereye cane mu bijanye no gutuma amafaranga akoreshwa neza ugereranyije n’ubuhinga bwa kera nk’ubwa *Knapsack Solver*. Uburyo bwa *Ishami n’Imirongo* bushingiye ku buryo bwo gutorera umuti ingorane bw’izina nyene, bwavumbuwe mu 1960 na Ailsa Land na Alison Harcourt.


> ► *Ubwo buryo na bwo rimwe na rimwe bwitwa "Algorithme ya Murch" mu kwerekeza ku wabuhinguye.*
