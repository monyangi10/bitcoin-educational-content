---
term: Gukonjesha mu mpeho
definition: Sisitemu y'umushata w'ibijini bya komine ikoresha impumputsi kugira ngo ihambuke ubushyuhe. Inzira ikunze kandi irahitana n'imari.
---

Uburyo bwo gukonjesha bukoreshwa ku ASICs muri Bitcoin Mining. Gukonjesha umuyaga birimwo gukoresha ama ventilateur kugira ngo ubushuhe buva ku bihimba bivemwo. Umuyaga wo mu bidukikije urakwegwakwegwa kugira ngo ukonjeshe izo nkoko imbere y’uko zirukanwa. Ubwo buryo bwo gukonjesha ni bwo bukwiragiye cane, kubera ko mu bisanzwe amaventilateri abanza gushirwa ku mashini igihe umuntu agura. Muri rusangi ni uburyo buzimvye cane, ariko ntibukora neza kuruta ubuhinga nk’ugukonjesha Liquid canke gukonjesha mu kwibiza.
