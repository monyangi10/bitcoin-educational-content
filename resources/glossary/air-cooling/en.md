---
term: Air cooling
definition: A cooling system for mining machines using fans to dissipate heat. The most widespread and least expensive method.
---

A cooling system used for ASICs in Bitcoin mining. Air cooling involves using fans to dissipate the heat generated by the components. Ambient air is drawn in to cool the chips before being expelled. This method of cooling is the most widespread, since fans are typically pre-installed on the machines at the time of purchase. It's generally the least expensive option, but less efficient than techniques such as liquid cooling or immersion cooling.
