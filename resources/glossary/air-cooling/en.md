---
term: AIR COOLING
---

Cooling system used for ASICs in Bitcoin mining. Air cooling involves using fans to dissipate the heat generated by the components. Ambient air is drawn in to cool the chips before being expelled. This method of cooling is the most widespread, since fans are installed directly on the machines when they are purchased. It's generally the cheapest to buy, but less efficient than techniques such as hydro cooling or immersion cooling.
