---
term: Loop
definition: Igikorwa ca Lightning Labs co kuringaniza ubushobozi bw'amafaranga hagati ya Bitcoin on-chain na Lightning.
---

Servisi yateguwe na Lightning Labs yateguwe kugira ngo yorohereze uguhuza amafaranga mu mihora ya Lightning. Loop ituma abakoresha bashobora gutanga amahera hagati ya Bitcoin na Lightning Network ataco bakeneye gufunga canke gufungura umurongo. Gutyo, Loop ifasha mu gutuma amahera agenda neza no kugabanya amahera y’uburongozi bw’imirongo yayo.
