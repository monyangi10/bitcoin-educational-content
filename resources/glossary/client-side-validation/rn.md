---
term: Isubumirwa ku ruhande rw'umukiliya
definition: Uburyo bwo gusuzuma amakuru kuri buri ruhande mu mwiherero, bukoreshwa canecane mu itegeko rya RGB.
---

Uburyo buri ruhande (umukiriya) akoresha mu kugenzura urutonde rw’amakuru yahinduwe mu bwiherero, hakurikijwe amategeko y’amasezerano. Ku bijanye n’umurongo wa RGB, ayo makuru ahindurwa ashirwa hamwe mu vyo bita consignments. Udakunze umurongo wa Bitcoin, usaba ko amafaranga yose akoreshwa asohorwa muri On-Chain, RGB yemera ko amasezerano gusa (ashizweho muri Bitcoin) abikwa mu ruhame, mu gihe amakuru y’ingenzi ya Contract (amahinduka, ivyemezo, ibimenyamenya) aguma muri off-chain, asangiwe gusa hagati y’abacu.
