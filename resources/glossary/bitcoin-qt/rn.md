---
term: Bitcoin QT
---

Bitcoin QT ni umukiriya wa Bitcoin arimwo Interface y’igishushanyo, yasohotse muri Rusama 2011. Ikomeza uruvyaro rw’umukiriya wa mbere wa Satoshi Nakamoto.

Mu mwaka w’2014, Bitcoin QT yahinduwe izina yitwa Bitcoin core, ubu ni ryo shirwa mu ngiro ry’amasezerano ya Bitcoin. Kuva kuri verisiyo 0.5, yashizwemwo bitcoind. Ikindi kandi, n’ubu, igishushanyo Interface ca Bitcoin core citwa `Bitcoin-qt` mu bijanye n’inkomoko ya porogarama.


> ► *Ijambo "QT" rikomoka kw'izina ry'ububiko bw'ibitabu bukoreshwa mu gukora igishushanyo ca Interface. Izina "QT" rimwe na rimwe risobanurwa nk'ugukina ku majambo ku bijanye n'ijwi ry'ijambo "cute".*
