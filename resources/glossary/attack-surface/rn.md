---
term: IGITERA KU RUSO
---

Yerekeza ku rutonde rwose rw’ibibanza bishobora kwinjiramwo umuterabwoba yoshobora gukoresha kugira ngo ashobore kwinjira muri sisitemu. Ico gitero gifise interfaces zose, services, ports, protocols, n’ibindi bikoresho vyo guhanahana amakuru bishobora gushirwa ahabona. Igitero kinini cane kirongereza umubare w’ibitero, bigatuma iyo sisitemu ishobora guterwa.


Kugabanya ahantu ho gutera ni uburyo bwiza bwo gucungera umutekano, akenshi bikaba birimwo guhagarika ibikorwa bidakenewe. Nk’akarorero, mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ama wallet y’ibikoresho afise ahantu hato cane ho gutera kuruta ama wallet ya software. Nkako, amasakoshi y’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none aratandukanya imfunguruzo ziri ku gikoresho kitahuye na Internet, kikaba gifise interface n’ibikorwa bike cane. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ubwo, amasakoshi y’amaporogarama ashirwa kuri orodinateri zikoreshwa muri rusangi, zifasha ibintu vyinshi be n’uguhuza urubuga, gutyo zigatuma umuntu ashobora gutera ahantu hagari cane.
