---
term: ASICBOOST IGISHIKA
---

Verisiyo yuguruye kandi igaragara ya AsicBoost. AsicBoost ni ubuhinga bwo gutuma ibintu bigenda neza bukoreshwa muri Bitcoin Mining. Abacukuzi bakoresha verisiyo ya Overt bakoresha `nVersion` umwanya w'ibarabara ry'abashaka gutora maze bagakoresha iyo mpinduka nk'inyongera Nonce. Ubu buryo busiga umurima nyawo wa `Nonce` w'ibarabara udahindutse mu gihe cose umuntu agerageza gukora hashing, gutyo bikagabanya imibare ikenewe kuri SHA256 yose, mu kugumiza amakuru amwe amwe hagati y'ibigeragezo. Iyi verisiyo iramenyekana ku mugaragaro kandi ntihisha ivyo yahinduye ku rubuga rwose, bitandukanye n’iyindi verisiyo ya Covert ya AsicBoost.
