---
term: PHOENIX

---

Porogaramu y’ubuhinga bwa none yitwa wallet yagenewe kworohereza no gutuma amafaranga akoreshwa neza ku bijanye n’umurongo wa Lightning. Ituma abakoresha bashobora gucunga amahera yabo ataco baciye ku bikoresho vyabo vy’amatelefone ngendanwa ataco bakeneye kwifatanya n’ibindi bikoresho vy’umuravyo. Phoenix mu vy’ukuri ikora nk’umurongo w’umuravyo wigenga wuzuye kuri telefone (ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’umuravyo). Ishigikira ibikorwa vya Bitcoin na Lightning kandi itanga ibintu vyo kworohereza uburongozi bw’inzira, nk’ugucungera umurongo wikorana n’inzira ya Acinq. Udakunze izindi porogarama za Lightning wallet, zikoreshwa cane cane mu gucungera, Phoenix itanga uburyo bushimishije bwo gusenyera ku mugozi umwe mu gufatanya gukoresha urudodo rwa Lightning n’uburyo bwo gukoresha porogarama ya telefone ngendanwa. Ni kimwe mu bisubizo vyiza vyoroshe ku muntu atanguye ashaka gukoresha Lightning mu gihe agumana uburenganzira bwose bw’ama bitcoins yiwe (ukwizigama). Phoenix ni umugambi wateguwe kandi ukabungabungwa n’ishirahamwe ry’Abafaransa ryitwa Acinq.
