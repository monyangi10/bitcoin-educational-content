---
term: BIP0030
---

Iciyumviro cashizeho Soft Fork yashizwe mu ngiro ku wa 15 Ntwarante 2012, co gutorera umuti ikibazo c’ibimenyetso vy’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji vyisubiriza. Imbere ya BIP30, vyari bishoboka mu buryo bw’ubuhinga kugira amafaranga abiri atandukanye afise ikimenyetso kimwe c’amafaranga (txid) muri Blockchain.

Ivyo vyabaye incuro zibiri ku bikorwa vya coinbase:


- Iryo soko ryo mu gice ca 91.880 ryari rifise txid imwe n'iya Coinbase Transaction yo mu gice ca 91.722.
- Iryo soko ryo mu gice ca 91.842 ryari rifise txid imwe n'iya Coinbase Transaction yo mu gice ca 91.812.

BIP30 yarakosoye iyo nzira y’agahomerabunwa mu gushitsa itegeko rishasha ryoroshe: nta gicuruzwa gishasha gishobora kugira txid imwe n’igicuruzwa cari caranditswe mbere kiretse igicuruzwa c’intango cari carakoreshejwe cose (ni ukuvuga, ibisohoka vyaco vyose vyari vyakoreshejwe).

Iyi Soft Fork yakoreshejwe hakoreshejwe uburyo bw’umunsi w’ibendera, bituma iba imwe mu nzira za mbere zakoreshejwe n’abakoresha Soft forks (UASFs) mu mateka ya Bitcoin.
