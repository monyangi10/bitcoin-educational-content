---
term: PASSPHRASE (BIP39)
definition: Ijambo ry'ibanga ryiyongera ku ntekane yo kugarura ikigega kugira ngo HD wallet itekane.
---

Ijambobanga ry’ubuhinga, iyo rifatanijwe n’ijambo ry’ugusubirana, ritanga Layer y’inyongera y’umutekano ku bikoresho vy’ibanga vy’ubuhinga n’ivy’uburongozi. Amasakoshi ya HD akenshi akomoka ku majambo yo gusubirana agizwe n’amajambo 12 canke 24. Iryo jambo ryo gusubizaho rirahambaye cane, kuko rituma umuntu ashobora gusubizaho imfunguruzo zose ziri muri Wallet iyo zitakaye. Ariko rero, rigize ikintu kimwe co kunanirwa (SPOF). Iyo bishizwe mu kaga, ama bitcoins araba mu kaga. Aho niho passphrase yinjira, ni ijambobanga ry’ubuhinga, ryitowe n’uwukoresha, ryongerwa ku majambo yo gusubizaho kugira ngo ryongere umutekano wa Wallet. Kugira ngo ntituvyivangane na kode ya PIN canke ijambobanga risanzwe, passphrase irafise uruhara mu kuronka imfunguruzo z’ibanga.


Ikorana n’ijambo ry’ugusubirana, ihindura seed aho imfunguruzo zikomoka. Gutyo, naho umuntu yoronka ijambo ryawe ryo gusubirana, ata passphrase, ntashobora kuronka amahera yawe. Ikoreshwa rya passphrase mu vy’ukuri rituma haba Wallet nshasha ifise imfunguruzo zitandukanye. Guhindura (mbere gatoyi) passphrase bizoba generate Wallet itandukanye.


passphrase ni ubusa kandi ishobora kuba ihuriro ryose ry'inyuguti zitowe n'uwukoresha. Gukoresha passphrase biratanga ivyiza vyinshi. Ica mbere, bigabanya ingorane zijanye n’ugusenyuka kw’ijambo ry’ugusubirana mu gusaba ikintu ca kabiri kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kuronka ayo mahera. Igikurikira, gishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo bw’ingene umuntu ashobora gukora ama wallets y’uruyeri arimwo amahera makeyi, mu gihe co guhatira umuntu kwiba amahera yawe. Ubwa nyuma, ikoreshwa ryayo riraryoshe iyo umuntu yipfuza kugenzura ukuntu uruvyaro rwa HD Wallet seed rugenda ruhinduka. passphrase itegerezwa kuba ikomeye bihagije kugira ngo ishobore kunanira ibitero vy’inkomezi z’ubukazi kandi itegerezwa gukizwa mu buryo bwizigirwa. Gutakaza passphrase bishobora gutuma umuntu adashobora kuronka amahera, nk’uko nyene gutakaza ijambo ry’ugusubirana.


> ► *Ijambo passphrase rimwe na rimwe ryitwa kandi: "ijambo ry'inyongera rya seed," "ijambobanga," "Ijambobanga rya seed," "ijambo ry'inyongera," canke mbere "ijambo rya 13 canke rya 25." Birabereye kumenya ko hari ubwoko bubiri bw'amajambo y'ibanga kuri Bitcoin. Izwi cane ni iyo twavuze haruguru, ivana na BIP-39, kandi ishobora gukingira HD Wallet yose. Ariko rero, BIP-38 yari yarasobanuye kandi uburyo bwo gukingira urufunguzo rw’ibanga rudasanzwe hakoreshejwe passphrase. Ubwo bwoko bwa kabiri bwa passphrase hafi ntibugikoreshwa muri iki gihe.*
