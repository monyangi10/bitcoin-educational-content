---
term: ALUVM

---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'"Imashini y'ubuhinga". Ni imashini y’ubuhinga bushingiye ku gitabu c’ubuhinga bwa none yagenewe canecane gushitsa ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa none no kwemeza amasezerano y’ubwenge, cane cane mu bijanye n’amasezerano ya RGB. AluVM yatangujwe na Dogiteri Maxim Orlovsky, ubu ikaba icungiwe n’ishirahamwe ry’ingingo mfatirwako za LNP/BP.
