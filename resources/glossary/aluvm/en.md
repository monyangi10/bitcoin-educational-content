---
term: Aluvm
definition: A virtual machine designed for deterministic execution of smart contracts, notably within the context of the RGB protocol on Bitcoin.
---

Acronym for "Algorithmic Logic Unit Virtual Machine". It is a register-based virtual machine specifically designed for the deterministic execution of distributed computation tasks and the validation of smart contracts, particularly in the context of RGB contracts. AluVM was originally developed by Dr. Maxim Orlovsky and is currently maintained by LNP/BP Standards Association.
