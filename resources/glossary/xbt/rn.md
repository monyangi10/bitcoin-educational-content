---
term: XBT
---

Ikimenyetso c’ububiko n’amafaranga (*ticker*) gikoreshwa mu guserukira Bitcoin, bihuye n’ingingo ngenderwako ISO 4217 igenga amakode y’amafaranga. Udakunze "BTC," ari co kimenyetso c'amateka, "XBT" ikurikira amasezerano y'uko amafaranga adashigikiwe na Leta atangura na "X". Rero, naho bitemewe ku mugaragaro na ISO, "XBT" ikoreshwa n'amashirahamwe amwe amwe kugira ngo iserukire ibice vya Bitcoin.
