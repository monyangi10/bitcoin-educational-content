---
term: Custody
definition: Izigama n'icungwa ry'imfunguruzo z'ibanga rya Bitcoin, bikozwe n'umuntu ubwiwe (self-custody) canke n'uwundi muntu.
---

Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, yerekeza ku gufata no gucunga imfunguruzo z’ibanga zishoboza kugenzura amafaranga y’ibiceri. Ugucungera BTC gushobora gukorwa mu buryo bubiri: haba ku giti ciwe n’uwukoresha, afise imfunguruzo z’ibanga zikenewe kugira ngo ashobore gushika ku bitcoins vyiwe (bizwi nka "ukwizigama"), canke n’uwundi muntu, nk’urubuga rwa Exchange, aho iyo sosiyete ifise imfunguruzo z’ibanga kandi igacungera bitcoins user mu izina rya bitcoins. Iryo hitamwo rya nyuma rirateye akaga kuruta kwizigama, kuko rituma amahera y’uwukoresha ashobora gushikirwa n’ingorane zo gufatwa n’abantu, gusenyuka canke inyifato y’ubuhendanyi y’umuyobozi.
