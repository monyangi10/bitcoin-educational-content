---
term: Bitcoin OPTECH
---

Non-profit organization focused on sharing technical information for companies using Bitcoin. Bitcoin Optech offers a wide variety of technical resources on Bitcoin, including an excellent weekly newsletter. This newsletter summarizes the most important discussions and latest news concerning the development of Bitcoin and Lightning Network. To subscribe to the newsletter, go to : [https://bitcoinops.org/en/newsletters/](https://bitcoinops.org/en/newsletters/).
