---
term: Bitcoin UBUTUNGANE
---

Ishirahamwe ritagira inyungu ryibanze ku gusangira amakuru y’ubuhinga ku masosiyete akoresha Bitcoin. Bitcoin Optech itanga ibikoresho vyinshi vy’ubuhinga kuri Bitcoin, harimwo n’ikinyamakuru ciza cane c’indwi ku yindi. Iki kinyamakuru kiraca irya n’ino ibiganiro bihambaye cane n’amakuru mashasha yerekeye iterambere rya Bitcoin na Lightning Network. Kugira ngo wiyandikishe ku kinyamakuru, genda kuri : [https://bitcoinops.org/ru/amakuru/](https://bitcoinops.org/ru/amakuru/).
