---
term: HMAC-SHA512
---

`HMAC-SHA512` bisobanura "Kode y'ukwemeza ubutumwa bushingiye kuri Hash - Ubuhinga bwa Hash butekanye 512". Ni ubuhinga bwo gupima amakuru bukoreshwa mu kugenzura ubutungane n’ukuri kw’ubutumwa bushikirizwa hagati y’abantu babiri. Ihuza igikorwa c'ibanga ca Hash `SHA512` n'urufunguzo rw'ibanga rusangi kuri generate kode yihariye y'ubutumwa bwo kwemeza (MAC) ku butumwa bwose.


Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ikoreshwa ry’akameremere rya `HMAC-SHA512` rirakomoka gatoyi. Iyi algorithme ikoreshwa mu nzira y'ugukuraho n'uburongozi bw'igiti c'urufunguzo rw'ibanga ca Wallet. `HMAC-SHA512` ikoreshwa cane cane mu kuva kuri seed gushika ku rufunguzo rwa mbere, hanyuma ku nkuru yose iva ku muvyeyi umwe gushika ku bana babiri. Iyi algorithme iboneka kandi mu yindi algorithme y'ivyaduka yitwa `PBKDF2`, ikoreshwa mu kuva ku mvugo yo gusubirana na passphrase gushika kuri seed.
