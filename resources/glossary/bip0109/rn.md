---
term: BIP0109
---

Iciyumviro cashikirijwe na Gavin Andresen muri Mukakaro 2016 mu gihe c’intambara y’ubunini bw’amabuye. Yari igamije kwongera ubunini bw’ibice kuva kuri 1 MB gushika kuri 2 MB biciye ku Hard Fork, mu gihe yongerako imipaka imwimwe ku mubare w’ama sigops na sighashs (bitandukanye na BIP102). Gukoresha BIP109 vyasaba ko 75% vy’ububasha bwo gukora hashing bushigikirwa n’idirisha ry’imisi 28. Ico ciyumviro cari igice nyamukuru ca Bitcoin Classic Fork, cari kirondera gutanga umuti wo gupima Bitcoin.

Ico ciyumviro nticigeze cemerwa.
