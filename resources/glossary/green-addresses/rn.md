---
term: Green addresses
definition: Icipantse ca kera c'ama-address yemewe imbere y'igihe n'uwundi muntu wizigwa kugira ivunjisha ryihute.
---

Iciyumviro ca kera ca Bitcoin Address aho amafaranga yoherezwa kuri co abanje kwemezwa n’uwundi muntu yizigirwa. Ico ciyumviro kiratuma abaronka amahera biciye muri iyi Address bayabona ko ari abo kwizigirwa ubwo nyene, ata kurindira ko bashirwa mu gice. Ishingiye ku cizigiro gishizwe mu gikorwa kigenzura Green Address, kuko itanga icemezo c’uko amahera yoherejwe atakoreshejwe kandi ko atazokoreshwa kabiri. Ubwo bwoko bwa serivisi bwahora bukoreshwa mu kwihutisha amafaranga (zero-conf), ariko ubu ntibugikoreshwa.
