---
term: Ikibazo c'abasirikare bakuru ba Byzantine
definition: Ikibazo cerekana intamyi zo guhuza muri sisitemu ikwiragijwe aho abayigize badashobora kwizigirana.
---

Ico kibazo cabanje gutegurwa na Leslie Lamport, Robert Shostak, na Marshall Pease mu kinyamakuru kidasanzwe *ACM Transactions on Programming Langues and Systems, umuzingo wa 4, n° 3* ["Ikibazo c'abajenerali b'Abanyabizantine"](https://lamport.azurewebsites.net/pubs) yerekana ingorane zo gufata ingingo mu buryo busanzwe aho ata muntu n’umwe ashobora kwizigirwa bimwe bishitse.


Muri iyo mvugo ngereranyo, umugwi w’abajenerali b’Abanyabizantine n’ingabo zabo barakambitse bakikuje igisagara bategura gutera canke gugota. Abajenerali baratandukanye n’abandi mu bijanye n’aho baba kandi bategerezwa guhanahana amakuru biciye ku ntumwa kugira ngo bahuze ingamba zabo. Bahitamwo gutera canke gusubira inyuma ntaco bimaze, igihe cose bose bazoba bashitse ku ciyumviro kimwe.


Ibisabwa ni:


- Buri jenerali wese ategerezwa gufata ingingo: gutera canke gusubira inyuma (egome canke oya);

*Ingingo imaze gufatwa, ntishobora guhindurwa;*

**Abajenerali bose bategerezwa kwemeranya ku ngingo imwe bakayishitsa mu gihe kimwe.**


Ariko rero, ubutumwa burashobora guteba, gusenyuka, guhindurwa canke gutakazwa. Naho boba bashikirije neza, abajenerali bamwebamwe boshobora kuba ari abahemu, bagarungika ubutumwa bw’ikinyoma canke buvuguruzanya n’ibigirankana kugira ngo babibe urujijo.


Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, umujenerali wese aserukira urudodo rw’uruja n’uruza rutegerezwa gushika ku masezerano ku bijanye n’ingene urutonde rumeze. Mu yandi majambo, benshi mu bari mu nzira y’ubuhinga busanzwe bategerezwa kwemeranya no gushitsa igikorwa kimwe kugira ngo ntibacike intege rwose. Mu migenzo, kugira ngo umuntu ashike ku masezerano y’ukwihanganira amakosa y’Abanyabizantine bisaba ko n’imiburiburi bibiri vya gatatu vy’ibihimba vy’uruja n’uruza biba inzirabugunge. Iyo benshi bakoze ivy’ububisha, urutonde ruragira ingorane.
