---
term: Nlocktime

definition: Ikibanza c'itunkanirwa ry'amahera kigena igihe ntarengwa imbere y'uko rishirwa mu gitabo c'ibibonwa.
---

Umurima winjijwe mu bikorwa ushinga ivyangombwa bishingiye ku gihe imbere y'uko ibikorwa bidashobora kwongerwa ku gice gifise akamaro. Iyi parametere ishobora kugaragaza igihe nyaco (Unix timestamp) canke umubare wihariye w'ibice nk'itegeko ry'uko igikorwa co gucuruza gifatwa nk'ikibereye. Gutyo, ni igihe c’ugufunga (kitagira aho kigarukira). `nLockTime` igira ingaruka ku bikorwa vyose kandi ishoboza neza kugenzura igihe, mu gihe opcode `OP_CHECKLOCKTIMEVERIFY` yemerera gusa kugereranya agaciro ko hejuru k'ikirundo n'agaciro ka `nLockTime`.
