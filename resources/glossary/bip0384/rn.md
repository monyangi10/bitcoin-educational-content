---
term: BIP0384
definition: Umurimo wa combo() wo gusobanura amoko menshi y'amadosiye ku rufunguzo rumwe mu ma-descriptors.
---

Itangaza `combo(KEY)` igikorwa c'ibisobanuro. Iyi nshingano idondora urutonde rw'inyandiko zishobora gusohoka ku rufunguzo rwa bose, zipfutse ubwoko bwinshi bw'inyandiko icarimwe, harimwo P2PK, P2PKH, P2WPKH, na P2SH‐P2WPKH.

Iyo urufunguzo rwatanzwe rwacitse, ubwoko bwose bw’inyandiko bune burageragezwa; iyo idashizwemwo, ubwoko bubiri bw'inyandiko za Legacy ni bwo bwonyene bugeragezwa. Intumbero ni iyo kworohereza urufunguzo mu gusobanura mu gutanga uburyo bumwe kuri generate ubwoko butandukanye bw'inyandiko z'isohoka zivuye ku rufunguzo rumwe rwa bose. BIP384 yashizwe mu ngiro hamwe n'izindi BIPs zose zijanye na Descriptor (uretse BIP386) muri verisiyo 0.17 ya Bitcoin core.
