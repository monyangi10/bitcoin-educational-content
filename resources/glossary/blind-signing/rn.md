---
term: IKIMENYETSO Impumyi
---

Inzira aho umukoresha asinya ku giciro ca Bitcoin atashobora kugenzura neza neza ivyo ariko arasinya. Ku bikoresho vy’amasakoshi, gusinya ataco ubona birateye akaga, kuko uwubikoresha ategerezwa kwizigira mudasobwa ihuye kugira ngo yerekane amakuru (abayakira, amahera, amahera, n’ibindi).

Ivyo bitera ingorane z’umutekano, kuko iyo mudasobwa ifatanye na internet kandi ifise ahantu hanini cane ho gutera kuruta igikoresho cagenewe gusinya. Iyo orodinateri ishobora gukoreshwa nabi, irashobora gukoresha nabi amakuru yerekanwa maze igaragaza ikintu kibi co gusinyako ata wuyikoresha abizi. Kugira ngo umuntu yirinde iyo ngorane, birakenewe ko akoresha ama wallet y’ibikoresho afise igicapo kirimwo igicapo, kandi akagenzura amakuru y’ivyo akora ataco akora ku gikoresho imbere y’uko ashirako umukono.
