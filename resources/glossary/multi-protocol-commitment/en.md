---
term: Multi protocol commitment
definition: Merkle structure in RGB grouping several contract commitments in a Bitcoin transaction.
---

In the context of the RGB protocol, MPC refers to the Merkle tree structure designed to integrate several Transition Bundles from different contracts into a single Bitcoin transaction. This mechanism makes it possible to group various commitments, corresponding to different contracts or assets, into a single anchor point. By consolidating these transitions into a hierarchical structure, the MPC optimizes the occupation of block space, reducing transaction costs and improving the efficiency of Blockchain utilization.
