---
term: Multi protocol commitment
definition: Inyubako ya Merkle muri RGB ishira hamwe amasezerano menshi mu ihererekanya rimwe rya Bitcoin.
---

Mu bijanye n’amasezerano ya RGB, MPC yerekeza ku mibumbe ya Merkle Tree yagenewe gushiramwo amasezerano menshi y’Impinduka ava mu masezerano atandukanye mu gikorwa kimwe ca Bitcoin. Ubwo buryo buratuma bishoboka gukoranya amasezerano atandukanye, ahuye n’amasezerano canke itunga ritandukanye, mu ntumbero imwe ya Anchor. Mu gushiramwo izo mpinduka mu ntumbero y’ubukuru, MPC iratuma habaho ugukoresha neza umwanya w’amabuye, igabanura amafaranga y’ugucuruza no gutuma ikoreshwa ryiza rya Blockchain rigenda neza.
