---
term: GUKWIZA (WST)
---

Mu gikoresho ca Whirlpool Stat Tool, ugukwiragira ni ikimenyetso gikoreshwa mu gupima ubumwe bw’igikorwa co kuvanga bivuye ku mbonerahamwe y’igice kimwe. Hari ubwoko bubiri bw’ugukwiragira: ivy’imbere n’ivy’inyuma.


Ivyo bishobora gukwiragira biharurwa nk'igipimo kiri hagati y'ivyo bizokwiragira vy'igice cawe n'umubare wose w'ibice vyaremwe inyuma ya Tx0 yawe. Nk’akarorero, nimba hari ibice 100 mu kidengeri cawe kandi igice cawe gifise anonset ya 70, rero ukwiragira kw’igice cawe ni 70%.


Ku rundi ruhande, ugukwiragira kw’inyuma ni igipimo kiri hagati y’ugusubira inyuma kw’igice cawe n’umubare wose wa Tx0 waremwe imbere y’umuvango wa nyuma w’igice cawe. Rero, nimba anonset y’inyuma y’igice cawe ari 95 kandi hariho 100 Tx0 imbere y’uko ukora mix yawe ya nyuma, rero spread y’inyuma y’igice cawe ni 95%.


Ivyo bimenyetso bibiri bikoreshwa mu gusuzuma ubushobozi bw’uguvanga kw’igice cawe ugereranije n’ubushobozi butangwa n’ikidengeri. Ukwiragira kw’ivyizigiro gutoya, nk’ibice 5%, vyerekana ko hariho urugero runini rwo gutera imbere bishoboka biciye mu bivanze vy’inyongera. Ku rundi ruhande, ugukwiragira kw’ivyizigiro vyinshi, nka 97%, bisigura ko anonset nkeyi y’inyongera ishobora kuronswa n’inyongera coinjoins.


> ► *Mu congereza, "gukwiragira" vyoshobora guhindurwa ngo "igipimo co gukwiragira" canke "igipimo co gukwiragira."*
