---
term: ERLAY
---

Ivyiyumviro vy’uruja n’uruza kugira ngo haboneke ubushobozi bwo gutanga amakuru ataremejwe hagati y’ibice vya Bitcoin.


Ubu, igikorwa cose kirakwiragizwa biciye ku buryo aho urudodo rumwe rumwe rutangaza igikorwa ruzi ku bagenzi baco bose. Ikibazo ni uko ivyo bituma habaho ugusubira inyuma kwinshi n’ugukoresha bandwidth ku bisubizo. Ivyerekeye amakuru menshi y’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji ntaco bimaze, kuko uwuyakira asanzwe azi ivyo bikorwa, kandi node yose ikeneye gusa kumenya ivy’ibikorwa vyose rimwe. Erlay asaba guhagarika ku buryo busanzwe umubare w’abagenzi urudodo rwoherezako amafaranga ruzi, hanyuma rugakoresha uburyo bwo gusubiza hamwe bushingiye ku bubiko bw’ibitabo bwa minisketch kugira ngo rusangire amafaranga abuze neza.


Erlay yogabanya ikoreshwa ry’uburebure bw’uruzitiro ku rugero rwa 40%, bigatuma ibikorwa vya Full node bishikira abakoresha bafise ubuhinga buke bwo gukoresha Internet, gutyo bigatuma habaho ugusenyura neza kw’uruja n’uruza. Iyi porotokole yoshobora kandi kuguma ikoresha uburebure bw’uruzitiro hafi buhoraho uko umubare w’amahuza wiyongera. Ivyo bisigura ko vyoba vyoroshe cane ku bakoresha ama node kwemera umubare munini cane w’amahuza ava ku bagenzi babo, ivyo bikaba vyotuma umutekano w’urubuga rwa Bitcoin urushiriza kuba mwiza mu kugabanya ingorane zo gucapura, haba ku bushake canke ku mpanuka. Ikindi, Erlay yotuma bigorana cane kumenya aho igikorwa gikomoka, gutyo bikongera ibanga ku bakoresha inzira zidakora munsi ya Tor.


Erlay irashikirizwa muri BIP330.
