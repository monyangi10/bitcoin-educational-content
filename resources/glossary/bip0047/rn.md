---
term: BIP0047
---

Iyi porotokole yashikirijwe na Justus Ranvier mu 2015, ivuga ku kibazo gikomeye co gusubira gukoresha Bitcoin Address, umugenzo uhungabanya cane ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha. Satoshi Nakamoto, mu gitabu cera ca Bitcoin, yari amaze kugaragaza akamaro ko gukoresha amajambo y’ingenzi atandukanye ku bijanye n’ugucuruza kwose kugira ngo hagumyeho itandukaniro mu bikorwa bitandukanye vy’abakoresha.

BIP47 itanga iciyumviro c’amakode y’ukwishura ashobora gusubirwamwo, atuma uwukoresha ashobora kwakira amahera menshi atakoresheje amaboko generate Bitcoin Address nshasha ku giciro cose. Izo kode zikora nk'ibimenyetso vy'ukuri, zikomoka kuri Wallet seed y'ukoresha kandi ziri ku rugero rwa konti mu miterere y'ikomoka rya HD Wallet.

Mu gufatanya amakode y’ukwishyura y’impande zompi, umwe wese mu bari muri iyo nama arashobora kuronka amaderesi menshi yihariye y’uwundi, ataco asavye ko yongera guhanahana amakuru inyuma ya Exchange ya mbere.

Iryo tegeko ryishimikije ubuhinga bwa ECDH (Elliptic-Curve Diffie-Hellman), ubuhinga butandukanye bw’urufunguzo rwa Diffie-Hellman Exchange rwahuye n’imirongo y’uruzitiro, bikaba bishoboza abo bompi gushinga ibanga basangiye rikoreshwa kuri generate aderesi zidasanzwe zo kwakira.

Naho BIP47 itashizwe muri Bitcoin core kandi ikaronka inyishu zitandukanye zivuye mu kibano, ibikorwa nka PayNym (Samourai Wallet) na Sparrow wallet vyaravyemeye kandi birabishira mu bikoresho vyavyo vy’ubuzima bwite.
