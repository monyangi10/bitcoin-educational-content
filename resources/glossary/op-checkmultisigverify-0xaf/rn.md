---
term: OP_CHECKMULTISIGVERIFY (0XAF)

---

Ihuza `OP_CHECKMULTISIG` na `OP_VERIFY`. Bisaba imikono myinshi n'imfunguruzo za bose kugira ngo ugenzure ko imikono ya `M` mu `N` ari yo, nk'uko `OP_CHECKMULTISIG` ibigira. Hanyuma, nka `OP_VERIFY`, iyo ukugenzura kutashoboye, inyandiko ica ihagarara ubwo nyene n'ikosa. Iyo igenzura ryiza, inyandiko irabandanya ata gaciro na kamwe isunikiye ku kirundo. Iyi opcode yakuweho muri Tapscript.
