---
term: P2P IVYITWARA V2.
---

Verisiyo nshasha y’umurongo w’ivy’ugutwara abantu n’ibintu Bitcoin P2P ushiramwo ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru kugira ngo haboneke ubuzima bwite n’umutekano w’ivy’itumanaho hagati y’ibice. Iryo terambere rigamije Address ibibazo vyinshi bifise verisiyo y’ishimikiro y’amasezerano ya P2P, cane cane mu gutuma amakuru ahindurwa adashobora gutandukanywa n’umuntu yihweza ataco akora (nk’uwutanga ubuhinga bwa interineti), gutyo bikagabanya ingorane zo gucengera n’ibitero biciye mu kumenya uburyo bumwe bumwe buri mu mapakete y’amakuru.


Ivyo gupfuka bishizwe mu ngiro ntibirimwo ivy’ukwemeza kugira ngo ntihagire ivyongerako bidakenewe no kugira ngo ntibihungabanye uburyo butagira uruhusha bw’uguhuza urubuga. Iyi protocole nshasha y’ugutwara abantu n’ibintu P2P naho biri ukwo itanga umutekano mwiza ku bitero bitagira ico bikora kandi ituma ibitero bikora bihenda cane kandi bishobora kumenyekana (cane cane ibitero vya MITM). Gushiraho amakuru y’ibinyoma bitera ingorane igikorwa ku batera bipfuza gucengera canke gukoresha nabi ivy’itumanaho.


P2P Transport V2 yashizwemwo nk’uburyo (buzimye ku buryo busanzwe) muri verisiyo 26.0 ya Bitcoin core, yashizweho mu kwezi kwa kigarama 2023. Ishobora gukoreshwa n’uburyo `v2transport=1` muri dosiye y’imiterere.
