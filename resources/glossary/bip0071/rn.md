---
term: BIP0071
definition: Ishirwaho rya MIME type ku butumwa bw'iyishyurwa rya BIP70. Ntiyigeze yakirwa na benshi.
---

Isobanura ubwoko bw'itangazamakuru rya MIME (*Ivyagutse vy'Iposita ya Internet*), bihuye n'ingingo ya RFC 2046, ku butumwa bwo gusaba kwishura Bitcoin muri BIP70.

MIME ni urugero rwa Internet rwo kwagura uburyo ubutumwa bwo kuri interineti, rugatuma umuntu ashobora gutanga amakuru y’ubwoko butandukanye mu buryo butunganye.

Mu gusobanura ubwoko bwihariye bwa MIME ku butumwa bwo kwishura, BIP71 irabona ko porogarama ya Wallet yohereza ubutumwa bw’amasezerano yo kwishura biciye kuri email canke HTTP ikurikiza ingingo ngenderwako za Internet ku bijanye n’ugushiramwo ubutumwa neza.


Ariko rero, kubera ko iyo nzira yo gutera imbere yari ifatanijwe na BIP70, ntiyigeze yemerwa cane.
