---
term: UMUKEMUKANZA
---

Ivy’ugukoresha neza itandukaniro ry’ibiciro vya BTC (canke ikindi kintu cose) ku mbuga zitandukanye za Exchange kugira ngo ubone inyungu. Arbitrage ni ugugura Bitcoin ku rubuga aho igiciro kiri hasi cane, hanyuma ukayigurisha ku rundi rubuga aho igiciro kiri hejuru. Ugutandukana kw’ibiciro gushobora kubaho kubera ubudasa bw’amahera, ugusaba, ubunini bw’ibikorwa, n’ugucererwa kw’ugutanga amahera hagati y’ibibanza vya Exchange. Arbitrage ifasha guhuza ibiciro ku mbuga zitandukanye.
