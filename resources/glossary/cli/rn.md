---
term: CLI
definition: Uburyo bw'imirongo y'amategeko butuma umuntu avugana na porogaramu akoresheje amategeko y'inyandiko muri terminal.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ijambo "Command Line Interface," ni uburyo bwo gukorana na porogarama zishingiye ku kwinjiza amabwirizwa y'inyandiko mu gikoresho co gukoresha canke mu gikoresho co gukoresha. CLI itandukanye na GUI (Ukoresha w'Igishushanyo Interface), ifise imigenderanire yerekana (n'imbeba) n'imigenderanire y'amashusho Elements.
