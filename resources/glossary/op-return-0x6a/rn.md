---
term: OP_RETURN (0X6A)
definition: Opcode ituma imisohokere itaba iyakoreshwa, kenshi ikoreshwa mu kwandika amakuru ayo ari yo yose.
---

Igaragaza inyandiko idakora, ituma igisohoka kirimwo kigaragara ko kidashobora gukoreshwa. Ivyuma vy’urubuga birashobora rero gukuraho iyo nkuru mu gice cabo ca UTXO. `OP_RETURN` akenshi ikoreshwa mu kwandika amakuru atari yo muri Blockchain.
