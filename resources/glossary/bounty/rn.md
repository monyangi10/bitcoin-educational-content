---
term: UBUTUNGANE
---

Impembo ni impembo itangwa kubera kurangiza igikorwa kinaka, akenshi kikaba gifitaniye isano n’ugutegura neza canke gucungera umugambi wa porogarama. Bounties zikoreshwa cane mu guhimiriza abantu kuvumbura no gukosora ubugoyagoye bwa porogarama, gutegura ibintu bishasha canke gutorera umuti ingorane zikomeye zo mu vy’ubuhinga. Abaje muri iyo nama, akenshi abategura canke abashakashatsi, baronka impembo gusa iyo intererano yabo imaze kugenzurwa no kwemerwa. Uwo murongo w’inkurikizi wihutisha iterambere kandi ugakomeza umutekano mu gukoresha neza umuryango wagutse.


Bounties rimwe na rimwe zikoreshwa n’imigambi ya porogarama zijanye na Bitcoin.
