---
term: Uburyo bwo gukora
definition: Inzira y'uko imiryango ya Bitcoin ibasanga ibishanyo n'uko bisangirwa, habigusisha ubwenge bwacakurikira n'abasebanzi kugira ngo bigire icimpaye cya blockchain.
---

Uburyo bwo gukoresha ni uburyo umuryango w’abakoresha ufata ingingo ku gushirwa mu ngiro kwa Soft Fork ku masezerano ya Bitcoin, ugamije kwirinda ugucapura kwa Blockchain. Ivyo birimwo gukoranya ivyiyumviro vy’abacukuzi kugira ngo bemeze Soft Fork imbere y’uko ikora. Iyo abantu benshi cane bemeye Soft Fork, ingorane zo gucapura Blockchain ziragabanywa. Ukwo kwemeranya ni ikintu gihambaye cane kuko iyo benshi mu bacukuzi banse kwemera iryo hinduka, Soft Fork yoshobora kurema iminyororo ibiri itandukanye: imwe ifise amategeko yahinduwe iyindi idafise. Hariho ivyiciro 2 nyamukuru vy'uburyo bwo gukoresha:


- UASF (Soft Fork Ikoreshwa n’Ukoresha) igihe amanode ashira mu ngiro ivyagezwe;

**MASF (Miner-Ikoreshwa Soft Fork)** igihe abacukuzi bavyura igikorwa.


Uburyo butandukanye bwo gukora bwarageragejwe uko Bitcoin yagenda iratera imbere. Mu gihe ca Satoshi, uburyo bwo gukora ntibwari bwashinzwe ku buryo butegekanijwe. Amahinduka akenshi yakorwa ataco akora, rimwe na rimwe mbere akagira ataco amenyesheje abantu. Mu nyuma, uburyo bwa *Flag Day* burakoreshwa. Satoshi amaze kugenda, haciye hakoreshwa ubundi buryo bukurikiranye, cane cane BIP34, BIP9, BIP8, n’ubwa nyuma, *Speedy Trial*.
