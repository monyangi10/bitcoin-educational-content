---
term: Ipfundo RYUZUYE
---

Mudasobwa ikoresha umukiriya w’umurongo wa Bitcoin, ifata, igenzura kandi ikabika Blockchain yose, ni ukuvuga amateka yose y’ibikorwa kuva ku gice ca Genesis gushika ubu. Igikoresho cuzuye kigenzura ubwaco ibikorwa vyose n’ibibujijwe hakurikijwe amategeko y’uguhurizako Bitcoin. Ubwo bwoko bw’urudodo rero buratanga urugero rwo hejuru rwo kugenzura ku wubukoresha, kandi bugabanura ivy’ukwizigira uwundi muntu. Full node isaba ibikoresho vyinshi vyo kubika, ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none, RAM n’uburebure bw’urugendo kuruta node yoroshe (SPV).
