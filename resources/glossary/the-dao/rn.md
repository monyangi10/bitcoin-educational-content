---
term: DAO
---

Umugambi watangujwe mu 2016 kuri Ethereum, ugamije gushinga ikigega c’ishoramari cigenga gicungiwe n’amasezerano y’ubwenge. Naho ahanini bifitaniye isano na Ethereum, DAO yaragize ingaruka zikomeye ku gutera imbere kwa Bitcoin. Muri Ruheshi 2016, The DAO yarashikiwe n’ikibazo gikomeye cane. Mu kwishura, umuryango wa Ethereum warakoze igikorwa gikomeye, ivyo bikaba vyaciye bihinduka ukunanirwa gukomeye. Ivyo vyabaye vyakomeje ivyiyumviro vy’abanyagihugu batobato mu gihe c’intambara ya Bitcoin Blocksize. Bakoresheje iki kintu kugira ngo barwanye amahinduka yihuta mu bijanye n’amategeko ya Bitcoin, nk’uko vyashigikiwe n’ababujije cane, no kurwanya iciyumviro co gukora ama hardforks, nk’uko vyari kuri Ethereum inyuma y’aho The DAO hack.
