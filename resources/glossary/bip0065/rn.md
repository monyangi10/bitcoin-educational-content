---
term: BIP0065
definition: Ishirwaho rya opcode OP_CHECKLOCKTIMEVERIFY ryemera gufunga bitcoin gushika ku mfungurazo y'italiki canke uburebure bw'iblock.
---

Yashizeho kode nshasha yitwa `OP_CHECKLOCKTIMEVERIFY` ishobora gutuma UTXO ikingirwa kandi iguma idakoreshwa gushika igihe kizoza.

Ishirwa mu ngiro ry'iyi BIP ryasaba Soft Fork, ryabaye ku wa 14 Kigarama 2015. Ryashizeho kandi verisiyo ya 4.
