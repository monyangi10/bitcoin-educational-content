---
term: Gukoresha aderese muli bwinshi
definition: Inzira itavugwa n'inzira y'ikinini k'ikibuye ki-Bitcoin kinyene igisubizo n'amafranga, yica mu mahoro mu buryo bwo gukurikirana igisubizo.
---

Gusubira gukoresha Address vyerekeye umugenzo wo gukoresha Address imwe yakira kugira ngo ufunge UTXO nyinshi, rimwe na rimwe mu bikorwa bitandukanye. Bitcoins zisanzwe zikingirwa hakoreshejwe urufunguzo rw’ibanga rujanye n’urufunguzo rwihariye rwa Address. Kubera ko Blockchain ari iya bose, biroroshe kubona aderesi zijanye n’ingene ama bitcoins angahe. Iyo iyo Address nyene isubiye gukoreshwa mu kwishura kenshi, umuntu arashobora kwiyumvira ko ama UTXO yose afitaniye isano ari ay’ikigo kimwe. Ivyo bigaragaza ikibazo c’ubuzima bwite, kuko bituma habaho amahuzu y’ivy’ubuhinga hagati y’ibikorwa vyinshi n’ama UTXO, bikaba bishoboza gukurikirana amafaranga y’i On-Chain. Satoshi Nakamoto yaramaze kuvuga iyo ngorane mu gitabu cera ca Bitcoin:


> *Nk’iyindi muriro w’umutekano, hakwiye gukoreshwa urufunguzo rushasha rw’ibiri kuri buri gikorwa kugira ngo ntibihuzwe na nyeneyo umwe.*


Nakamoto, S. (2008). "*Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System*". https://bitcoin.org/bitcoin.pdf.

Kugira ngo umuntu azigame ubuzima bwiwe bwite, biraremeshwa cane ko umuntu wese aronka Address akoresha rimwe gusa. Ku bijanye n’ukwishura gushasha kwose, hakwiye kuboneka Address nshasha. Ku bijanye n’ibiva mu guhindura, Address nshasha na yo nyene ikwiye gukoreshwa. Ikintu ciza, kubera amasakoshi y’ibintu vy’agaciro n’ivy’ubukuru, gukoresha amaderesi menshi atandukanye vyaciye vyoroshe cane. Ivyiyumviro vyose bifitaniye isano na Wallet birashobora gusubirwamwo bitagoranye bivuye kuri seed imwe. Ivyo ni vyo kandi bituma porogarama ya Wallet yama itanga Address nshasha, itandukanye iyo ukanda kuri buto ya “Receive”.
