---
term: Simplified payment verification
definition: Uburyo butuma abakoresha boroheje bemeza ama transactions batarinze gukuraho blockchain yose.
---

Uburyo bwo kwemerera abaguzi b'umuco kugenzura amafaranga y'i Bitcoin batakuye kuri Blockchain yose. Node ikoresha SPV ikura gusa imitwe y’amabuye, iyo na yo ikaba iremereye cane kuruta amabuye yose. Iyo bikenewe kugenzura igikorwa, urudodo rwa SPV rusaba ikimenyamenya ca Merkle ku rudodo rwuzuye kugira ngo rwemeze ko igikorwa kiri mu gice kinaka. Ubwo buryo burakora neza ku bikoresho bifise uburyo buke, nk’amatelefone ngendanwa, ariko busobanura ko umuntu akwiye kwizigira ama node yuzuye, ivyo bikaba bishobora kugabanya umutekano no kwongera ukwizigira gukenewe.


> ► *Ijambo ry'inyongera "SPV" ni ryo rikoreshwa kenshi mu kwerekeza kuri ubwo buryo.*
