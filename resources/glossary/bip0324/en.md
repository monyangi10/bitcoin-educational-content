---
term: BIP0324
definition: V2 P2P Transport with opportunistic encryption of communications between Bitcoin nodes.
---

Introduces a new version of the Bitcoin P2P transport protocol incorporating opportunistic encryption to enhance the privacy and security of communications between nodes. 
The P2P V2 transport of BIP324 was included as an option (disabled by default) in version 26.0 of Bitcoin Core, deployed in December 2023. It can be activated with the `v2transport=1` option in the configuration file. This improvement is inspired by BIP150 and BIP151.
