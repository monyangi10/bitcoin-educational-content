---
term: UBUSA (Address)
---

Umugenzo wakira Address urimwo urutonde rw’ibimenyetso vyihariye vyatowe n’uwubikoresha, akenshi kubera imvo z’ubwiza. Izo aderesi ziterwa n’ugukora igikorwa co kubara, aho imfunguruzo nyinshi z’ibanga ziremwa gushika imwe muri zo ihuye n’iyakira Address irimwo urutonde rwifuzwa. Ivyo ntibitera ingorane umutekano wa Address ariko birashobora gusaba umwanya munini n’ubuhinga bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none, cane cane ku bijanye n’urutonde rurerure canke rutomoye. Ni ubwoko bw’inzira y’inguvu z’agahomerabunwa.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, ryitwa "adresse personnalisée."*
