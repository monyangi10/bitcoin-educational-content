---
term: BIP0078
---

Izana umurongo wo gukoresha PayJoin kuri Bitcoin, uburyo bwo gukorana butera imbere mu bijanye n’ubuzima bwite bw’ukwishura mu gushiramwo uwuronka nk’uwutanga inyungu iruhande y’uwutanga.

Iyi BIP ihumekewe na BIP79, yari yaramaze gutanga iciyumviro nk’ico.

Ariko rero, izo BIPs zibiri si zo zikomoka ku ciyumviro ca PayJoin.

Ishirwa mu ngiro rya Stowaway ryakozwe na Samourai Wallet ryari risanzwe ririho imbere y’aho, kandi iyo nzira y’ugucuruza yaravuzwe ubwa mbere na LaurentMT mu 2015 mw’izina ry’“ugucuruza kw’ubuhinga bwa none.”
