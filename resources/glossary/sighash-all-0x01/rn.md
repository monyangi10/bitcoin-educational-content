---
term: SIGHASH_ALL

definition: SigHash Flag yerekana ko umukono ukubiyemwo ivyinjiye n'ivyasohotse vyose vy'iyo transaction.
---

Ubwoko bw'Ibendera rya SigHash rikoreshwa mu mikono y'ibikorwa vya Bitcoin kugira ngo ryerekane ko iyo sinyatire ikoreshwa ku bice vyose vy'ibikorwa. Mu gukoresha `SIGHASH_ALL`, uwusinya apfuka ivyinjizwa vyose n'ibisohoka vyose. Ivyo bisigura ko ata vyo kwinjira canke ivyo gusohoka bishobora guhindurwa inyuma y’umukono ataco bihinduye. Ubwo bwoko bwa SigHash Flag ni bwo busanzwe mu bikorwa vya Bitcoin, kuko butuma ibikorwa bishika ku mpera n’ubutungane.
