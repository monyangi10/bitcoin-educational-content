---
term: GUSANGIZA
---

Igikorwa kivanga Bitcoin catangujwe mu 2013 na Blockchain.info, ariko ntikigikora uno musi. Iyi serivisi yahaye abakoresha uburyo bwo kwongereza ubuzima bwite bwabo kuri Bitcoin mu gufatanya amafaranga yabo n’ay’abandi bantu, bakoresheje ubuhinga bwo guvanga busa n’ubw’ama coinjoins. SharedCoin yatanze uburyo bwo kwirinda ata nkenerwa yo kwizigira umuhuzabikorwa, kuko ibiceri vy’abakoresha vyaguma munsi y’ububasha bwabo mu gihe cose c’ivyo bikorwa. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibikorwa vy’uguvanga vy’ico gihe, ama bitcoins ntiyashobora kwibwa n’umuhuza. SharedCoin yaje guhura n’ingorane zatumye zimwe mu mivumba yabo zitamenyekana mu ntango z’icuma ca 2014.
