---
term: Banlist.json

definition: Idosi ya Bitcoin Core irimwo urutonde rw'ama aderesi ya IP y'atunode twabujijwe utwo itunode ryacu ryanka kwungurana naryo.
---

Izina rya dosiye ikoreshwa na porogaramu ya Bitcoin Core kugira ngo yandike aderesi IP z'ibihimba vyabujijwe n'ukoresha. Iyi dosiye irimwo urutonde rw'ibihimba vyabujijwe iyo nzira itazokwifatanya navyo. Iki kintu kirafasha gukingira imigenderanire n'ibihimba bishobora gutera ingorane canke gutera ingorane.
