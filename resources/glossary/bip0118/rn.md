---
term: BIP0118
---

Iciyumviro co kuzana ibihinduzi bibiri bishasha vy'Ibendera rya SigHash: `SIGHASH_ICO COSE IVYO GUTANGA` na `SIGHASH_INYANDITSWE Y'IBIMENYETSO VYOSE`.

Ivyo vyongeweko biratuma habaho uguhinduranya amasezerano ya Bitcoin, cane cane ku masezerano y'ubwenge n'imiti ya kabiri ya Layer nka Lightning Network.

Imwe mu nzira zihambaye za BIP118 ni ugushoboza uburyo bwo guhindura Eltoo.

Inyungu nyamukuru ya `SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT` ni uko yemerera imikono gusubira gukoreshwa mu bikorwa vyinshi, itanga ubushobozi bwo guhindura. Mu buryo bwihariye, ayo mabendera ya SigHash atuma bishoboka gutanga umukono udashigikiye ikintu na kimwe mu vyinjijwe mu gucuruza.
