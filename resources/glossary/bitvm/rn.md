---
term: BitVM
definition: Amasezerano akundira iharura iryo ari ryo ryose hanze y'uruhererekane rw'ibice (off-chain) hamwe n'uburyo bwo kubigihiriza ku ruhererekane (on-chain), vyongereza ubushobozi bwa Bitcoin.
---

Porotokole yashizweho na Robin Linus mu 2023, igamije kwagura ubushobozi bwo gutegura ibikorwa vya Bitcoin.

BitVM ituma bishoboka gukora igikorwa cose c’ubuhinga bwa none off-chain no gukoresha igisubizo kugira ngo umuntu afate ingingo y’ingene ama bitcoins yugarijwe akoreshwa. Birashoboza kandi gutorera umuti amatati ya On-Chain iyo uruhande rumwe ruvuze ko rwaronse igisubizo c’ubuhendanyi.

Ivyo bituma Bitcoin igira ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwo guharura hafi nk’ubwa Turing ataco bisaba guhindura amategeko y’uguhurizako.

BitVM isubiramwo inyifato y'irembo ry'ubwenge rya `NAND` ikoresheje amakode y'ibikorwa `OP_BOOLAND` (isubiramwo irembo rya `AND`) na `OP_NOT` (isubiramwo irembo rya `NOT`).

Irembo rya NAND ni "irembo ry'isi yose" kuko rishobora guhurizwa hamwe kugira ngo risubiremwo irindi rembo ryose ry'ubwenge, mu kwagura, umurongo wose w'ubuhinga. Iciyumviro kiri muri BitVM ni ugubika izo nzira z'imibare `NAND` nk'amababi muri MAST y'ugucuruza kwa Taproot.
