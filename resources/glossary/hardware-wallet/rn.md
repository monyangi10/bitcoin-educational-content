---
term: Hardware Wallet
---

Hardware Wallet, canke Wallet y’umubiri, ni igikoresho c’ubuhinga bwa none gikoreshwa mu gucungera no gucunga imfunguruzo z’ibanga za Bitcoin Wallet. Ivyo bikoresho vyakozwe kugira ngo bihe umutekano mwiza ugereranije n’ibikoresho vy’amaporogarama biri ku mashini zikoreshwa muri rusangi kandi bihuye ata guca ku ruhande na interineti. Ama wallets y’ibikoresho abika ijambo Mnemonic ata murongo, ku gikoresho gifise ahantu hato cane ho gutera, ivyo bikaba biyitandukanya n’ibidukikije bishobora gutera ingorane. Iyo umuntu akoresheje igikorwa, Hardware Wallet irashira umukono kuri ico gicuruzwa mu gikoresho ubwaco, ata n’urufunguzo rw’ibanga rugaragaje hanze. Iyo iyo nzira imaze gusinywa, irarungikwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin kugira ngo yemezwe kandi ishirwe muri Bitcoin Blockchain. Mu bikoresho bikundwa cane vy’amasakoshi y’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none harimwo: Ledger, Trezor, Coldcard, Pasiporo, BitBox, Satochip, Jade, na SeedSigner (urutonde ntiruheza).


> ► *Ijambo Hardware Wallet rishobora kuvugwa mu buryo butandukanye mu gifaransa. Bamwebamwe bayita "ibintu vy'umukara" canke "ibintu vy'umukara." Bamwe mu bakoresha ama bitcoins barakunda ijambo "périphérique de signature," canke "igikoresho co gusinya" mu congereza, kugira ngo ntibavuge ko ama bitcoins ari mu Wallet.*
