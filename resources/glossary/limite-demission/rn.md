---
term: Akarimbi k'isohoka
definition: Igereranyo ntarengwa c'amahera yose ya Bitcoin, gishinzwe ku bice imiliyoni 21.
---

Igitigiri c’amahera yose ya BTC Supply, yashinzwe ku bice nka miliyoni 21. Ico kigero gisobanurwa n’ingingo ngenderwako y’ugutera imbere kw’ibiciro iri mu masezerano ya Bitcoin. BTC nshasha ziraremwa zigashikirizwa abacukuzi biciye mu nkunga yo kwemeza igice cose. Iyi nkunga igabanywa igice c'amabarabara 210.000, uburyo buzwi nka "Halving". Ubwo buryo buratuma amahera aremwa buhoro buhoro agabanuka gushika ashitse kuri zero.
