---
term: EMISSION LIMIT
---

Ceiling set for BTC's total money supply, established at around 21 million units. This limit is defined by the inflation policy in the Bitcoin protocol. New BTC are created and distributed to miners via the subsidy for the validation of each block. This subsidy is halved every 210,000 blocks, a process known as "Halving". This method ensures that money creation gradually decreases until it reaches zero.
