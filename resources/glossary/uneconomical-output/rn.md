---
term: Sortie non rentable
definition: Ingano y'abitcoin namba cyane ku buryo itokoreshwa kuko amafaranga y'itunshwa ryayo yaba menshi kuruta agaciro kayo (dust).
---

Yerekeza ku mahera make cane ya Bitcoin ari make cane ku buryo adashobora kwoherezwa mu gucuruza kubera ko amahera asabwa kugira ngo ashirwe mu gice c’amahera yoba ari menshi cane kuruta agaciro kayo. Insobanuro nyayo ya "Dust" irashobora guhinduka bivanye n'aho ivuga, ariko muri rusangi yerekeza ku gisubizo cose c'ubudandaji gisaba amahera menshi kugira ngo ukoreshe kuruta uko gifise agaciro. Ku bakoresha Bitcoin, birahambaye ko bacungera UTXO zabo no kwimenyereza kuzishiramwo kugira ngo ntizibe Dust.
