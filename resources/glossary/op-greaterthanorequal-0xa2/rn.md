---
term: OP_GREATERTHANOREQUAL (0XA2)

definition: Opcode igenzura ko ikintu ca mbere ku mupfundikizo kigeretse canke kingana n’ica kabiri.
---

Igereranya ibintu bibiri biri hejuru cane ku ruganda maze igenzure nimba ikintu ca mbere kinini canke kingana n'ica kabiri. Iyo ikintu ca mbere kinini canke kingana n'ica kabiri, gisunika `1` (ukuri) ku kirundo; ahandi ho, isunika `0` (ikinyoma).
