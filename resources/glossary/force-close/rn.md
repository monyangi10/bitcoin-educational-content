---
term: GUFUNGA INGUVU
---

Uburyo bwo gufunga umurongo w’umuravyo udakorana. Iyo abakoresha babiri bafunguye umurongo ufise Multisig 2/2, umwe wese arashobora gufunga uwo muhora ku ruhande rumwe mu gutangaza Commitment Transaction ya nyuma yari yaramaze gusinywa, kugira ngo bashobore kugarura amahera yabo ya onchain bitcoins. Ivyo bizwi nka "ugufunga inguvu".


Ubwo buryo burakoreshwa muri rusangi iyo umwe mu bari muri iyo nama atagisubiza, canke iyo gufunga umuhora mu bufatanye bidashoboka. Niba force close ishobora kwirindwa, ni vyiza, kuko bifata igihe kirekire kugira ngo umuntu asubire kuronka amahera ya onchain kandi bishobora kuba bizimvye cane mu bijanye n’amahera.


Mu gufunga inguvu, umwe muri abo babiri bakoresha atangaza Commitment Transaction, yerekana uko umurongo wa Lightning uri nyuma uzwi. Hariho rero timelock imbere y’uko bitcoins zishobora gusubirwamwo onchain, bikaba biha uwundi mugwi umwanya wo kugenzura ko igikorwa gihuye n’ivyo umuhora uheruka. Iyo uwukoresha agerageje guhendahenda mu gusohora Commitment Transaction yashize igihe, uwundi arashobora gukoresha ibanga ryo gukuraho kugira ngo ahane uwo mubeshi maze asubize amahera yose ari muri uwo muhora.
