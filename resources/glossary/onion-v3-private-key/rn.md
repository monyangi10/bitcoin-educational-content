---
term: ONION_V3_PRIVATE_KEY

definition: Ikaratasi ya Bitcoin Core yabika urufunguruzo rwiherereye kuri Tor V3.
---

Dosiye ikoreshwa muri Bitcoin Core kubika urufunguzo rwihariye rufitaniye isano n'umurimo w'ibanga wa Tor ku mahitamwo ya `-listenonion`. Iyo iyo nzira ikoreshejwe, bitcoind ihita irema igikorwa ca Tor gihishijwe, kigatuma iyo node ishobora kuvugana n’urubuga rwa Tor.
