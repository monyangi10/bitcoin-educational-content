---
term: IBICE (UMURABO)
---

Mu bijanye n’*Inyishu z’inzira nyinshi (MPP)* canke *Inyishu z’inzira nyinshi z’atome (AMP)*, Shard ni agace k’ukwishura kw’isi yose. Shard yose igereranya igice c’amahera yose yishurwa, iyo nzira ikaba irungikwa ukwayo biciye mu nzira itandukanye kuri Lightning.


Muri MPP, ibice vyose bifise ibanga rimwe, mu gihe muri AMP, Shard yose ifise ibanga ry’igice ryihariye. Uwuronka ivyo bice akoranya ivyo bice kugira ngo asubire gukora kandi arangize kwishura vyose. Ubwo buryo burakingira imipaka y’amahera ku muhora umwe.
