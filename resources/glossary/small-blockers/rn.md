---
term: ABABUZA BATO
---

Izina ry’abashigikiye ugukomeza canke kwongera ubunini bw’amabuye ya Bitcoin mu gihe c’intambara y’ubunini bw’amabuye hagati ya 2015 na 2017, kugira ngo hazigame ukwegereza ubutegetsi abaturage n’umutekano w’urwo rutonde. Bavuganira inyishu nka SegWit, bemera ko ugupima bikwiye kuba ku bice vy’imbere nka Lightning Network, kandi bagashigikira ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’ivyiza biciye ku maforogo ya Soft aho gukoresha amaforogo ya Hard.
