---
term: LND
definition: Inyubako nkuru ya protocole Lightning Network yanditswe muri Go na Lightning Labs.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikigiriki "*Lightning Network Daemon*". Ni ugushirwa mu ngiro gukomeye kw’itegeko rya Lightning Network ryanditswe mu rurimi rwa Go. Yateguwe na Lightning Labs, LND ishoboza kurema no gucunga inzira zo kwishura n'ibice kuri Lightning Network.
