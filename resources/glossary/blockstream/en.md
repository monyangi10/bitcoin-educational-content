---
term: BLOCKSTREAM
---

A company specializing in the development of  Bitcoin-related solutions. Blockstream has initiated the Liquid sidechain, the implementation of the Lightning Network Core Lightning, and the Jade and Green wallets. It is also known for employing Bitcoin Core developers. The company is currently led by Adam Back, a cypherpunk and cryptographer best known as the inventor of Hashcash, the proof‑of‑work system that inspired Bitcoin’s consensus mechanism.
