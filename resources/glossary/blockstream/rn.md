---
term: BLOCKSTREAM

---

Ishirahamwe ry’abahinga mu gutegura inyishu zijanye n’ibibazo vya Bitcoin. Blockstream yatanguye uruhererekane rw’inyuma rwa Liquid, ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’umuravyo w’ishimikiro wa Lightning Network, n’amasakoshi ya Jade na Green. Irazwi kandi mu gukoresha abahinguzi ba Bitcoin Core. Ubu iyo sosiyete iyobowe na Adam Back, umuhinga mu vy’ubuhinga bwa none, azwi cane nk’uwahinguye Hashcash, ubuhinga bwo kwemeza ko umuntu akora bwatumye Bitcoin igira uburyo bwo kwumvikana.
