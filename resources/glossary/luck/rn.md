---
term: Luck
definition: Ikimenyetso gipima ingene pool yo gucura ikora ugereranije n'ivyo biteze.
---

Ikigereranyo gikoreshwa mu bidengeri vya Mining kugira ngo umuntu apime ubushobozi bw’ikidengeri kijanye n’ivyo citeze mu vyiyumviro. Nk’uko izina ryaco rivyerekana, risuzuma amahirwe y’ikidengeri mu kuronka ibuye. Amahirwe aharurwa mu kugereranya umubare w’imigabane ikenewe mu vyiyumviro kugira ngo umuntu aronke igice gifise akamaro, bishingiye ku ngorane ziriho ubu za Bitcoin, n’umubare nyawo w’imigabane yasohotse kugira ngo umuntu aronke ico gice. Igitigiri c’imigabane kiri hasi y’ico umuntu yiteze kigaragaza amahirwe, mu gihe igitigiri kinini kigaragaza amahirwe mabi.


Mu guhuza ingorane iri kuri Bitcoin n’umubare w’imigabane yayo isohoka buri segonda be n’ingorane y’umugabane umwe umwe, iyo pool irashobora kubara umubare w’imigabane ikenewe mu vyiyumviro kugira ngo umuntu aronke igice gifise akamaro. Nk’akarorero, dufate ko mu vyiyumviro, bisaba imigabane 100.000 kugira ngo ikidengeri kironke ibuye. Iyo pool mu vy’ukuri itanga 200.000 imbere y’uko ironka block, amahirwe yayo ni 50%:


```text
100,000 / 200,000 = 0.5 = 50%
```


Ku rundi ruhande, iyo iyo pool ironse block ibereye inyuma yo gutanga imigabane 50.000 gusa, rero amahirwe yayo ni 200%:


```text
100,000 / 50,000 = 2 = 200%
```


Amahirwe ni ikimenyetso gishobora guhindurwa gusa inyuma y’aho igice kivumbuwe n’ikidengeri, kigatuma kiba ikimenyetso kidahinduka gihindurwa buri gihe.
