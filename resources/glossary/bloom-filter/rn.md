---
term: IKYUNGURA RY'AMASHURWE
---

Akayunguruzo ka Bloom ni ubuhinga bw'amakuru bukoreshwa mu kugerageza nimba ikintu ari umunyamuryango w'umugwi.

Bishoboza gusuzuma abanywanyi vyihuta cane ata gucapura amakuru yose, bikaba bituma biba ngirakamaro cane cane mu bihe aho ubushobozi bwo kwibuka n’umuvuduko ari ngirakamaro, kandi aho igitigiri gitoyi c’amakosa gicunzwe cemewe.

Ivyunyuro vya Bloom ntivyemeza ko ata binyoma bitari vyo (niba iyunguruzo rivuga ko ikintu kitari muri iyo nkuru, nta gukeka ko atari vyo) ariko vyemeza ko hariho amahirwe make y’ibinyoma (bishobora kwerekana nabi ko ikintu kiriho).

Iyo ikintu congerewe ku kayunguruzo, ibikorwa vyinshi Hash bikora generate mu bibanza vy'ibice. Kugira ngo umuntu asuzume ko ari umunyamuryango, hakoreshwa izo nzira nyene za Hash. Iyo ibice vyose bihuye bishizweho, ikintu gishobora kuba kiri muri iyo nkuru, ariko gifise ingorane y'ibinyoma vy'ibinyoma.

Amayunguruzo ya Bloom akoreshwa cane mu bubiko bw'amakuru no mu mirongo y'urubuga. Nk’akarorero, uburyo bwo gucunga amakuru ya *Google’s Bigtable* burabikoresha. Mu masezerano ya Bitcoin, amayunguruzo ya Bloom akoreshwa ku bikoresho vya SPV (Igenzura ryoroshe ry’ukwishyura) nk’uko bivugwa muri BIP37.


> ► *Igihe tuvuga cane cane ivyerekeye gukoresha amayunguruzo ya Bloom mu bijanye n'ibikorwa vya Bitcoin, rimwe na rimwe turahura n'ijambo "Iyunguruzo ry'amashurwe y'ibikorwa".*
