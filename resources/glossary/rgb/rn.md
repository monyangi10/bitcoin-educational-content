---
term: RGB
definition: Uburyo bw'amasezerano agezweho adafise aho ahengamiye kandi y'ibanga akorera kuri Bitcoin.
---

Uburyo bw'ibanga bwa Smart contract bugenewe gukorana na Bitcoin na Lightning Network. RGB ikora ku citegererezo ca Client-side Validation kandi itandukanya ububiko bwa Contract State na Blockchain, ku buryo amasezerano y’ubuhinga bwo gukingira gusa ari yo aguma kuri yo. Muri ubwo buryo, amateka yose ya Leta aguma hanze y’uruzitiro, bikaba bishoboza gutera imbere cane no kuba ibanga. RGB rero ishoboza gukora amasezerano asobanutse yo kubika ibimenyetso, NFTs, akaranga kashizwe ahantu hamwe canke inyishu za DeFi, hejuru ya Bitcoin.


Ku RGB, ukurwanya Double-spending kuraboneka mu gukoresha Single-Use Seal, uburyo bwo gukora amakuru y’ibanga bukoresha neza ko UTXOs kuri Bitcoin zishobora gukoreshwa rimwe gusa. Naho ku bijanye n’ukuri kwa token, ivyo biremezwa n’ugusuzuma amateka ya Leta ku ruhande rw’umukiriya, kuva Contract iremwa gushika aho iherutse kuba.
