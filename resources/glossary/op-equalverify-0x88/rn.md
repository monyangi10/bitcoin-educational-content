---
term: OP_EQUALVERIFY (0X88)

---

Ihuza ibikorwa vya `OP_EQUAL` na `OP_VERIFY`. Ibanza gusuzuma ukungana kw'agaciro kabiri ko hejuru ku rutonde, hanyuma isaba ko igisubizo kitari zero, ahandi ho, igikorwa c'ugucuruza ntikigira akamaro. `OP_EQUALVERIFY` ikoreshwa cane cane mu nyandiko zo kugenzura aderesi.
