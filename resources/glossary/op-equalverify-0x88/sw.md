---
term: OP_EQUALVERIFY (0X88)
definition: Inachanganya OP_EQUAL na OP_VERIFY, ikibatilisha muamala ikiwa thamani zinatofautiana.
---

Inachanganya utendaji wa `OP_EQUAL` na `OP_VERIFY`. Kwanza hukagua usawa wa thamani mbili za juu kwenye rafu, kisha inahitaji matokeo kuwa si sifuri, vinginevyo, muamala ni batili. `OP_EQUALVERIFY` inatumika haswa katika hati za uthibitishaji za Address.
