---
term: Bitcointalk
definition: Online forum created by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009, dedicated to discussions about Bitcoin.
---

Online forum dedicated to discussions about Bitcoin. It was launched in November 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto, following the earlier Bitcoin discussion space on sourceforge.net. The forum adopted its current name during the migration from bitcoin.org to bitcointalk.org in August 2011. BitcoinTalk serves as a platform for sharing information, news, technical debates, and analyses.
