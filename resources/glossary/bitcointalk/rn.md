---
term: IKIGANIRO C'AMAFARANGA
---

Ihuriro ryo kuri internet ryerekeye ibiganiro ku vyerekeye Bitcoin. Yatangujwe mu kwezi kwa 11/2009 na Satoshi Nakamoto, ikurikije ikibanza c’ibiganiro ca Bitcoin cariho mbere kuri sourceforge.net. Iryo huriro ryafashe izina ryaryo ry’ubu mu gihe c’ukwimuka kuva kuri Bitcoin.org gushika kuri bitcointalk.org muri Myandagaro 2011. BitcoinTalk ikora nk’urubuga rwo gusangira amakuru, amakuru, impaka z’ubuhinga, n’isesengura.
