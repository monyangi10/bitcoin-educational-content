---
term: Dollar cost averaging (dca)
definition: Uburyo bwo kuziganya bugizwe no kugura kenshi amahera akwiye y'itunga kugira ngo uguhindagurika kw'ibiciro ntukugireko ingaruka.
---

Ingamba y’ishoramari irimwo gushiramwo amafaranga y’amahera atari make mu kintu kinaka mu bihe bitandukanye, ata kuraba igiciro c’ico kintu. Ubwo buryo burafasha kugabanya ingaruka z’uguhinduka kw’ishoramari muri rusangi mu kugura amayunite menshi iyo ibiciro biri hasi, make iyo ari hejuru. DCA mu bisanzwe igamije kwubaka ikibanza mu mutungo mu kiringo kirekire mu gihe igabanya ingorane. Ni ubuhinga buzwi cane bwo gushiramwo amahera muri Bitcoin.
