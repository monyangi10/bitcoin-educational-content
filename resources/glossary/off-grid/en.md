---
term: OFF-GRID
---

Refers to a Bitcoin mining operation that operates autonomously, without being connected to the main power grid. Off-grid miners generally use alternative energy sources such as solar, wind or hydroelectric power, or even generators running on fossil fuels (e.g. gas for flaring). This model is often favored to reduce energy costs, as it relies in part on energy that, without mining, would be wasted. Off-grid miners have the advantage of being able to set up in isolated locations, in order to consume this energy too far away from populations.

Off-grid mining often has ecological benefits, as it can, in some cases, help reduce greenhouse gas emissions by limiting flaring. It also makes certain renewable energy projects economically viable, despite their natural intermittency.
