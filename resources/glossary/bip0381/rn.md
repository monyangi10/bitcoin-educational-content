---
term: BIP0381
definition: Imirimo ya pk(), pkh() na sh() yo gusobanura amadosiye ya Legacy mu ma-descriptors.
---

Itangaza ibikorwa vy'ibisobanuro, cane cane `pk(URUPFUZO)` (Ishura-ku-Urufunguzo rwa Pub), `pkh(URUPFUZO)` (Ishura-ku-Urufunguzo rwa Pub-Hash), na `sh(INYANDITSWE)` (Pay-to-Script-Hash). Izo nshingano zihuza ingene ubwoko bw'inyandiko za Legacy zidondora mu ndondoro.

BIP381 yashizwe mu ngiro hamwe n'izindi BIPs zose zijanye n'ivyo Descriptor (uretse BIP386) muri Bitcoin core v0.17.
