---
term: LDK (lightning dev kit)
definition: Ibikoresho vy'abatera imbere vyorohereza ishirwa rya Lightning mu nporogaramu.
---

Igikoresho co gutegura (SDK) c’umuravyo. LDK ni ihuriro ry’amasomero n’ibikoresho vyagenewe abahinguzi kugira ngo bashobore gushiramwo Lightning mu buryo bworoshe muri porogarama zabo canke ngo bareme porogarama za Lightning mu gihe bagabanya uburemere. LDK ikora imice igoranye yo gushiramwo ibintu bijanye n’umuravyo. Uyu mugambi watangujwe na Spiral, ishirahamwe ryashinzwe na Jack Dorsey, kandi ushingiye ku nzira ya Rust-Imiravyo (RL).
