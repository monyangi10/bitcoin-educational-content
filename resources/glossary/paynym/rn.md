---
term: Paynym

definition: Ikiranga ciharije gihujwe n'ikigega ca Bitcoin, gishimikiye kuri BIP47 payment code.
---

Ikimenyetso kidasanzwe gihuye na Bitcoin wallet gishira mu ngiro iyo nzira. Amazina y’amahera araboneka ku rubuga rwa Samourai Wallet na Sparrow Wallet. Mu ntango, ivyo bimenyetso biva ku kode y'ukwishyura ya wallet, hakurikijwe BIP47. Gutyo bitanga ubushobozi bwo kwifatanya n’uwundi muntu kugira ngo generate isukure kandi yihariye amaderesi y’ukwakira, ataco bisaba gukorana ata guca ku ruhande. Ikoreshwa ry’ama Paynyms ryaraguwe mu nyuma kugira ngo rishigikire ibindi bintu bitandukanye vyo mu bidukikije vyo muri Samourai. Bikoreshwa cane cane mu gutuma habaho uguhanahana amakuru mu buryo bupfutse ku nzira y’itumanaho ya Soroban, kugira ngo bishobore gushirwa mu ngiro mu buryo bworoshe ibikorwa vy’ubufatanye. Amazina y’amahera ashobora kandi gukoreshwa mu kwemeza ko umuntu ari we wese kuri sisitemu zihuye n’umurongo wa AUTH47. Buri Paynym irangwa n’ikimenyetso kidasanzwe kandi igereranywa n’ikigereranyo c’ururobo.
