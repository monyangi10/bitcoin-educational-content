---
term: Null data
definition: Uburyo bw'itunkanirwa ry'amahera bwemerera gushiramwo amakuru ayo ari yo yose biciye kuri OP_RETURN.
---

Ubwoko bw'ibikorwa vya Bitcoin vyemerera kwinjiza amakuru makeyi biciye ku `OP_RETURN`. Ivyo bishobora kuba bifitaniye isano n’ubwo bwoko bw’isohoka biragaragara ko bidashobora gukoreshwa, nk’uko `OP_RETURN` yerekana inyandiko idakora.
