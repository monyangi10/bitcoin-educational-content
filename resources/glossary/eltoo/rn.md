---
term: ELTOO

---

Itegeko rusangi ry'ibice vya kabiri vya Bitcoin risobanura ingene bocungera hamwe ubutunzi bwa UTXO. Eltoo yahinguwe na Christian Decker, Rusty Russell, na Olaoluwa Osuntokun, cane cane kugira ngo batorere umuti ingorane zijanye n’uburyo bwo guhanahana amakuru bw’ibihugu vy’imirongo y’umuravyo, ni ukuvuga hagati yo gufungura n’ugufunga. Ubwubatsi bwa Eltoo bworosha inzira y’ibiganiro mu gushiramwo uburyo bwo gucunga Leta bujanye n’umurongo, bugasubirira uburyo bwashinzwe bushingiye ku bihano uburyo bwo guhindura ibintu bushobora guhinduka kandi butagira ibihano vyinshi. Iyi porotokole isaba gukoresha ubwoko bushasha bwa SigHash bushobora kwirengagiza ibintu vyose vyinjijwe mu gusinya kw'ibikorwa. Iyi SigHash mu ntango yitwa `SIGHASH_NOINPUT`, hanyuma `SIGHASH_ANYPREVOUT` (*Igisohoka cose ca mbere*). Ishirwa mu ngiro ryaryo riteguwe muri BIP118.


> ► *Eltoo rimwe na rimwe yitwa kandi "LN-Symétrie".*
