---
term: Consignment TERMINAL
---

Katika itifaki ya RGB, Terminal Consignment, pia inajulikana kama Consignment Endpoint, inarejelea uhamisho wa Consignment unaounganisha hali ya mwisho ya Contract, iliyopatikana na State Transition inayotokana na Invoice ya mpokeaji (mlipaji). Utaratibu huu unawakilisha mwisho wa uhamishaji, kukusanya data zote zinazohitajika ili kuthibitisha kuwa Ownership au hali ya Contract imetumwa na kuthibitishwa ipasavyo.
