---
term: Consignment UMUGAMBI
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, Terminal Consignment, izwi kandi kw’izina rya Consignment Endpoint, yerekeza ku kwimurira Consignment gushiramwo ivy’iherezo vya Contract, bironswa na State Transition ivuye kuri Invoice y’uwuronka (uwuhembwa). Ubwo buryo bugereranya iherezo ry’uguhindura, bugakoranya amakuru yose akenewe kugira ngo yemeze ko Ownership canke ikibanza ca Contract carungitswe neza kandi cemejwe.
