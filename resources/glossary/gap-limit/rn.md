---
term: IKIGANIRO
---

Igikoresho gikoreshwa muri porogarama ya Bitcoin Wallet kugira ngo umenye umubare munini w’amaderesi atakoreshwa akurikiranye kuri generate imbere yo guhagarika ugushaka ibindi bikoresho. Guhindura iyo parametere kenshi birakenewe igihe umuntu ariko aragarura Wallet kugira ngo umenye neza ko amafaranga yose akoreshwa abonetse. Igitigiri c'Ikinogo kidahagije coshobora gutuma umuntu abura amafaranga amwe amwe iyo amaderesi asimbuwe mu bice vy'ugukuraho. Kwongera Gap Limit bituma Wallet irondera cane mu rutonde rwa Address, kugira ngo ibone amafaranga yose ajanye.


Nkako, `xpub` imwe irashobora mu vyiyumviro kuronka amaderesi arenga miliyari 4 z’abakira (amaderesi yo mu mutima n’ayo hanze). Ariko rero, porogarama ya Wallet ntishobora kubikuramwo no kubisuzuma vyose kugira ngo bikoreshwe ataco bitwara amahera menshi cane. Gutyo, zigenda zikurikirana urutonde, kuko ari rwo rutonde rusanzwe porogarama zose za Wallet zitanga amaderesi. Iyo porogarama irandika Address imwe yose ikoreshwa imbere y’uko ija ku yindi ikurikira, kandi irahagarika ubushakashatsi bwayo iyo ihuye n’amaderesi menshi akurikiranye ata co amaze. Uwo mubare ni wo bita Gap Limit.


Niba, nk'akarorero, Igitigiri c'Ikinogo gishizwe kuri `20`, kandi Address `m/84'/0'/0'/0/15/` iri ubusa, Wallet izoronka aderesi zishika kuri `m/84'/0'/0'/0/34/`. Iyo iyo nzira y'amaderesi iguma idakoreshwa, ubushakashatsi burahagarara aho. Ku bw’ivyo, ugucuruza hakoreshejwe Address `m/84'/0'/0'/0/40/` ntivyomenyekana muri aka karorero.
