---
term: UNIX
---

Umuryango w’ibikoresho vy’ibikorwa vyinshi vyateguwe mu myaka ya 1970 na Bell Labs. UNIX yagenewe kuba yoroshe, ishobora guhindurwa, kandi ishobora gutwara, yarahinduye ubuhinga bwinshi bwo muri iki gihe. Ingingo ngenderwako zayo z'ishimikiro zirimwo imiterere ya dosiye, gukoresha inyandiko z'ibikoko, n'ibikoresho vy'ubuhinga. UNIX yaravyaye uburyo bwinshi, harimwo na Linux, kandi iguma ari ikigereranyo c’ugushikama n’ugukora neza.
