---
term: Steganography
definition: Technique consisting of hiding sensitive information in a seemingly harmless medium such as text, image, or video.
---

Steganography is the technique of concealing sensitive information in a seemingly innocuous object or cover medium (typically a signal such as text, sound, image, video, Bitcoin transaction, etc.), which will not arouse suspicion even if it is in plain sight; only a legitimate recipient will know that there is information there for him or her, and where to look for it.
