---
term: Stéganographie
definition: Uburyo bwo kunyegeza amakuru akomeye mu kintu kimeze nk'igisanzwe nka mpanuro, igishushanyo canke video.
---

Steganography ni ubuhinga bwo guhisha amakuru y’agaciro mu kintu canke mu gipfukisho kimeze nk’ikitagira akamaro (mu bisanzwe ni ikimenyetso nk’ivyanditswe, ijwi, ishusho, amasanamu, ugucuruza kwa Bitcoin, n’ibindi), ivyo bikaba bitazotuma umuntu akekeranya naho yoba ari ahantu umuntu abona; uwuronka iyo nkuru mu buryo bubereye ni we wenyene azomenya ko hariho amakuru ashobora kumuronsa, n’aho yoyarondera.
