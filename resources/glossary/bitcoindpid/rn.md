---
term: bitcoind.pid
definition: Idosiye irimwo inomero iranga porosesi (PID) y'ingero ya bitcoind iriko irakora.
---

Dosiye yasohowe na porogarama ya bitcoind (Bitcoin daemon) mu gihe c’ugushirwa mu ngiro kwayo. Iyi dosiye irimwo ikimenyetso c'imigenderanire (PID) y'intangamarara ya bitcoind ikora. Ikoreshwa mu gukurikirana no gucunga ingene porogarama ikora, ivyo bikaba bituma izindi porogarama canke inyandiko zishobora kuyimenya bitagoranye no gukorana na yo iyo bikenewe.
