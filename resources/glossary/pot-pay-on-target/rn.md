---
term: Pot (pay on target)
definition: Uburyo bwo kuriha abamina buhindagurika bivanye n'ubugumyi bw'imigabane yashikirijwe.
---

Uburyo bwo kubara impembo y’abacukuzi mu bijanye n’ibidengeri vya Mining. POT ni uburyo bwo gutanga indishi buhinduka bivanye n’ingorane z’igikorwa coherezwa mu kidengeri aho guhinduka bivanye n’ingorane z’igikorwa gitangwa n’ikidengeri. Muri ubwo buryo, impembo y’umugabane wose ushikirijwe na Miner ishingwa hakurikijwe ingorane z’uwo mugabane wihariye. Ivyo bisigura ko imigabane igoye cane ihembwa neza kuruta iyo idakomeye cane. Ni uburyo butandukanye bwa PPS, ariko mu guhindura impembo ishingiye ku kuntu igikorwa cakozwe gikomeye, POT irimwo ubudasa bwinshi ku Miner.
