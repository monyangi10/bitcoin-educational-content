term: BIP0141
---

BIP141 yashizeho iciyumviro c’Icabona Gitandukanye (SegWit), kikaba ari co catumye citwa SegWit Soft Fork.

Igereranya uguhindura gukomeye kw’itegeko rya Bitcoin ryagenewe gutorera umuti ingorane y’ugukomera kw’ibikorwa.


SegWit itandukanya amakuru y’abashingantahe (imikono) n’ayandi makuru y’ibikorwa mu kuyashira mu ntumbero y’amakuru itandukanye.

Aya makuru y’abashingantahe yemejwe kuri Merkle Tree nshasha, iyo na yo igaca ivugwa mu gice biciye ku Coinbase Transaction, kugira ngo ishobore gusubira inyuma n’amaverisiyo ya kera y’amasezerano.
