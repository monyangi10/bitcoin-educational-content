---
term: IKIBAZO
---

Mu bijanye na Github n’izindi nzira zo kwakira kode, ikibazo ni raporo yerekana ikibazo, itanga iciyumviro co gutera imbere, canke itanga iciyumviro c’ikintu gishasha. Ikora nk’ikiganiro ku batanga intererano kandi igatuma hakurikirana ibikorwa bizoheza canke ingorane zizotorwa umuti mu mugambi. Nk'umukoresha wa Bitcoin, urashobora gufasha porogaramu y'inkomoko yuguruye ukoresha mu kumenyesha ibibazo vyose bishobora kubaho biciye mu bibazo biri ku bubiko bw'umugambi.
