---
term: BLOCK

---

Uburyo bw’amakuru muri sisitemu ya Bitcoin. Igipande kirimwo urutonde rw'ibikorwa vy'ukuri hamwe n'amakuru y'imbere abitswe mu mutwe waco. Buri block ihuzwa n’iyibanjirije mu gushiramwo hash y’umutwe w’iyibanjirije, igahingura blockchain.

Blockchain ikora nk’umurongo w’igihe, ituma uwuyikoresha wese ashobora kugenzura amafaranga yose yakoze kera, akamenya neza ko ata mafaranga yisubiriza, kandi akabuza gukoresha amahera kabiri.

Ibikorwa biri mu gice bitunganijwe mu giti ca Merkle, igikoresho co gukoranya amakuru y’ibanga gitanga hash imwe ivuga mu ncamake ibikorwa vyose, izwi kw’izina ry’umuzi wa Merkle.


Umutwe w'ibubiko urimwo ivyicaro bitandatu:

*Verisiyo y'ibarabara;*


- Hash y’ibarabara ry’imbere;
- Umuzi wa Merkle w’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji;
- Igihe c’ibarabara;
- Intumbero y’ingorane;

*Ivya nonce.*


Kugira ngo igice kibonwe ko gifise akamaro, umutwe waco, iyo ushizwemwo `SHA256d` (igice ca kabiri ca SHA‐256), utegerezwa gutanga agaciro kari munsi canke kangana n’intumbero y’ingorane.
