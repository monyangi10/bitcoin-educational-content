---
term: Dlc (discreet log contract)
definition: Amasezerano y'ubuhinga ya Bitcoin akora yisunze ibintu biba hanze yemejwe n'ivyabona (oracles).
---

Ubwoko bwa Smart contract kuri Bitcoin bushoboza gushitsa amasezerano ashingiye ku ngaruka z’ibintu vyo hanze, vyemezwa n’umuvugizi umwe canke menshi, abo bavugishwa batazi ido n’ido rya Contract. DLCs ​​zavumbuwe na Tadge Dryja mu 2018. Aya masezerano y’ubwenge ni ngirakamaro cane cane mu bikorwa vy’ivy’ubutunzi, atuma habaho uguhingura ibikoresho vy’ubutunzi canke gutera amahera bifise ivyangombwa, mu gihe bigabanya ingorane z’abafatanyabikorwa. Kugira ngo wubake DLC, imigwi myinshi ifunga amafaranga muri Multisig Address. Izo bitcoins zishobora gufungurwa gusa iyo oracle isohoye amakuru yatanzwe ku gihe kinaka.
