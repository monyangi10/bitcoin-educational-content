---
term: BIP0049
definition: Standard ya derivation ku ma adresse ya Nested SegWit (P2SH-P2WPKH), ikoresha index 49' mu nzira ya derivation.
---

BIP49 ni BIP itanga amakuru itanga uburyo bwo gukura bukoreshwa kuri generate Nested SegWit aderesi muri HD Wallet. Inzira y’ugukuraho itegekanijwe ikurikira ingingo mfatirwako za BIP43 na BIP44, n’urutonde `49'` (ugukuraho gukomeye) ku rugero rw’intumbero. Nk'akarorero, Address ya mbere ya konti ya P2SH-P2WPKH yova mu nzira: `m/49'/0'/0'/0/0`. Ivyanditswe vya Nested SegWit vyashizweho igihe SegWit yatanguzwa kugira ngo yorohereze abantu kuyikoresha, bikaba vyatumye abakoresha bashobora kwungukira ku bintu vya SegWit no mu bikoresho vy’amasakoshi bitari bwa SegWit-native.
