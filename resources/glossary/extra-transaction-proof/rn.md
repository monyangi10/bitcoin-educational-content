---
term: IKIMENYETSO C'IBIMENYETSO VYO KWONGERAKO
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, ETP ni igice ca Anchor gishiramwo amakuru y’inyongera asabwa kugira ngo umuntu yemeze Commitment y’ubwoko bwa Tapret (mu bijanye na Taproot). Harimwo, mu bindi, urufunguzo rwa bose rw'imbere rujanye n'inyandiko ya Taproot n'amakuru yihariye asabwa ku *Script Path Spend*. Ico gice rero kiratuma habaho ukugenzura neza kw’imihigo y’ubuhinga bwa none.
