---
term: Private key
definition: Inomero y'ibanga ya 256-bit ikoreshwa mu gusinya inzira z'amafaranga no gukoresha bitcoin.
---

Urufunguzo rw’ibanga ni ikintu nyamukuru c’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru butaringaniye. Ni umubare (256 bits mu bijanye na Bitcoin) ugereranya ibanga ry’ubuhinga bwa none. Uru rufunguzo rukoreshwa mu gusinya ku bikorwa no kwemeza Ownership y'urufunguzo rwa bose rwa Bitcoin (kandi mu kwagura, Address yakira) mu guhaza `scriptPubKey`. Rero, imfunguruzo z’ibanga zituma umuntu akoresha amafaranga y’ibice bitcoins mu gufungura UTXOs zijanye n’imfunguruzo za bose zihuye. Imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitegerezwa kuba ibanga ridasanzwe, kuko kuzitangaza vyoshobora gutuma abandi bantu b’abanyaruyeri bashobora kugenzura amahera ajanye n’ivyo.


Mu nzira ya Bitcoin, urufunguzo rw’ibanga ruhuzwa n’urufunguzo rwa bose biciye ku nzira y’umukono w’ibarabara hakoreshejwe imirongo y’uruzitiro (ECDSA canke Schnorr). Urufunguzo rwa bose ruva ku rufunguzo rw’ibanga, ariko ikinyuranyo n’ikindi ntaco gishobora gushikako kubera ingorane y’ubuhinga bwo guharura iri mu gutorera umuti ingorane y’imibare iri inyuma (ingorane y’imibare itandukanye). Urufunguzo rwa bose rukoreshwa muri rusangi ku generate a Bitcoin Address, rukoreshwa mu gufunga bitcoins hakoreshejwe inyandiko. Mu buhinga bwo gupfuka amakuru, imfunguruzo z’ibanga akenshi ni imibare y’imburakimazi canke y’imburakimazi. Mu bijanye n'ivy'amasakoshi ya Bitcoin, imfunguruzo z'ibanga zikomoka kuri seed. Imfunguruzo z’ibanga zikunda gutera urujijo na seed canke n’ijambo ry’ugusubirana (Mnemonic). Ariko rero, izo Elements ziratandukanye.
