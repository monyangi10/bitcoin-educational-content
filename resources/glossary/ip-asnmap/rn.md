---
term: IP_ASN.MAP

---

Dosiye ikoreshwa muri Bitcoin Core kugira ngo ubike ASMAP, ivyo bikaba bituma habaho ugushiramwo (ni ukuvuga, ugushiramwo) amaderesi ya IP mu kwizigira inomero za Sisitemu Yigenga (ASN). Aho gushiramwo amatsinda y’amahuza asohoka hakurikijwe amajambo y’intango y’urubuga rwa IP (/16), iyi dosiye iremesha guhinduranya amahuza mu gushinga ikarita y’amaderesi ya IP ku ma ASN, ari vyo bimenyetso bidasanzwe vy’urubuga rwose ruri kuri Internet. Iciyumviro ni ugutuma umutekano n’imiterere y’urubuga rwa Bitcoin bitera imbere mu guhindura amahuzu kugira ngo umuntu akingire ibitero bimwebimwe (cane cane igitero ca Erebus).
