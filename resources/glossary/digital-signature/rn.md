---
term: Igikono co kuri rereshi
definition: Icemeza ca cryptography c'ukugumya urufunguruzo rw'ibanga butuma hasinywa kandi hamezwa ivunjisha rya Bitcoin.
---

Ikimenyamenya c’ubuhinga bw’ibanga kigaragaza ko umuntu afise urufunguzo rw’ibanga rwihariye, rujanye n’urufunguzo rwihariye rwa bose, ataco akeneye kurumenyesha. Mu Bitcoin, yubatswe hakoreshejwe urufunguzo rw’ibanga na Hash y’ugucuruza. Ishingira intahe Ownership y'ama bitcoins ariko aravugwa kandi yemerera ukunyurwa kw'inyandiko ifungura UTXO. Ivyara biciye ku nzira y’umukono w’umurongo w’umurongo nk’uwa ECDSA canke umurongo wa Schnorr.
