---
term: BIP0102
---

Pendekezo lililowasilishwa na Jeff Garzik mnamo Novemba 2015 wakati wa Vita vya Blocksize. Ililenga kuongeza ukubwa wa block block kutoka 1 MB hadi 2 MB kupitia Hard Fork. Pendekezo hili baadaye liliongoza BIP109 na utekelezaji wa Bitcoin Classic. Pendekezo hilo halikupitishwa kamwe.
