---
term: BIP0102
---

Iciyumviro cashikirijwe na Jeff Garzik mu kwezi kwa 11/2015 mu gihe c’intambara y’ubunini bw’amabuye. Yari igamije kwongera ubunini bw'ibice kuva kuri 1 MB gushika kuri 2 MB biciye ku Hard Fork. Ico ciyumviro caje gutuma BIP109 n’ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa Bitcoin Classic. Ico ciyumviro nticigeze cemezwa.
