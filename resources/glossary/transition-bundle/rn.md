---
term: Transition Bundle
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, Transition Bundle ni umugwi w'Impinduka za Leta ziri muri Contract imwe, zose zikora icarimwe muri Witness Transaction Bitcoin imwe. Ubwo buryo buratuma bishoboka gushiramwo ibintu vyinshi bishasha canke ivyimurirwa mu ntumbero imwe ya On-Chain Anchor, kugira ngo hakoreshwe neza umwanya w’amabuye.
