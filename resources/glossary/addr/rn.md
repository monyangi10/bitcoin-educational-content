---
term: Addr
definition: Imyiyeho y'intambwe yakuze ya Bitcoin yatanga amahoro ma IP y'ikinini gikoromeza inyongezo. Yavunditswe na addrv2 (BIP155) kugira ngo ishema ikinini kirekire cyane.
---

Ubutumwa bw’urubuga bwakoreshwa mbere muri Bitcoin mu gutangaza amaderesi y’ibice vyemeza amahuzu yinjira. Iyi nzira y’intango, yari igarukiye ku bice 128 kuri Address, yari ikwiye gusa ku maderesi ya IPv6, IPv4, na verisiyo 2 ya Tor. Kubera ukuza kw'amasezerano mashasha nka Tor V3 n'ugukenera gutera imbere mu guhindura amasezerano y'urubuga azoza, uburyo bwa `addr` bwasubiriwe na `addrv2`, bwashizweho muri BIP155.
