---
term: BINARY
---

Uburyo bwo gutanga inomero bushingiye kuri base‐2 bukoresha 0 na 1 kugira ngo bugaragaze amakuru. Buri mubare muri iyi sisitemu witwa bit, mu buryo bugufi ni "umubare wa kabiri". Mu gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none, binary ni uburyo nyamukuru bwo guserukira amakuru mu bwenge. Mudasobwa zikora ubwoko bwose bw’amakuru mu buryo bubiri kubera ko zijanye n’ibintu bibiri bishobora kuba biri mu nzira y’ubuhinga bwa none: zizimye canke zizimye.
