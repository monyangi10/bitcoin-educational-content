---
term: OP_LESSTHANOREQUAL (0XA1)

definition: Opcode igenzura ko ikintu ca mbere ku mupfundikizo kiri musi canke kingana n’ica kabiri.
---

Igereranya ibintu bibiri biri hejuru cane ku rutonde maze igenzure nimba ikintu ca mbere ari gito canke kingana n'ica kabiri. Iyo ikintu ca mbere kiri munsi canke kingana n'ica kabiri, gisunika `1` (ukuri) ku kirundo; ahandi ho, isunika `0` (ikinyoma).
