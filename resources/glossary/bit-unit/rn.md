---
term: Bit (igipimo)
definition: Igice c'ifaranga rya bitcoin bingana na 100 satoshis, kidakoreshwa cyane ugereranije na sats.
---

Muri Bitcoin, ijambo "bit" rikoreshwa kandi mu kwerekeza ku gice c'amahera ca Bitcoin. Biti imwe ingana n’amasatoshi 100, ni ukuvuga igice gitoyi kuruta ibindi vyose kitagabanywa ca Bitcoin.

Gutyo, Bitcoin 1 = ibice 1.000.000 = ibice 100.000.000 vy’ibice vy’umubiri.

Ariko rero, gukoresha “bit” nk’igice c’amahera biratera impaka.

Abenshi mu bakoresha Bitcoin barakunda gukoresha “Sats” canke “BTC,” mugabo ntibakoreshe “bit.”
