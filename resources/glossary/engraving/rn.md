---
term: Engraving
definition: Ukuritonde rw'amakuru canke uburyo bushobora gushirwa mu rutonde rw'ivyahindutse ku masezerano ya RGB.
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, *ugucapura* yerekeza ku rudodo rw'amakuru rwo guhitamwo rushobora gukurikirana kwinjira n'abafise Contract mu mateka y'ihinduka ryabo. Ivyo bishobora gushikwako cane cane biciye ku Interface RGB21, bituma amakuru y’icibutso canke y’insobanuro yongerwako ata guhindura ukuri kw’ibikorwa. Itunga amateka ya Contract mu gushiramwo izindi metadata.
