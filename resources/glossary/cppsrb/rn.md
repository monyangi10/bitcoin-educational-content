---
term: CPPSRB
definition: Uburyo bwo kuriha abacukuzi mu ma pools bushira imbere imigabane ya vuba kuri buri gice gitowe.
---

Inyishu y'inyongera y'ikirundi risobanura "*Ikihembo gitegekanijwe ku giciro c'umugabane n'Ikihembo giherutse kwishurwa*". Ni uburyo bwo kubara impembo y’abacukuzi bo mu bidengeri vya Mining. Muri iyo nzira, iyo pool iriha imigabane myinshi uko ishoboye igihe cose habonetse ububiko, igashira imbere imigabane iherutse gusohoka. Ubwo buryo buratuma Mining pool iguma ihagaze mu vy’ubutunzi, mu gihe itanga impembo ishingiye ku bikorwa no guhimiriza abacukuzi kuguma bafitaniye isano n’ikidengeri kugira ngo ntibatere mu kidengeri.
