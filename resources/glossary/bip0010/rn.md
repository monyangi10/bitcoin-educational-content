---
term: BIP0010
definition: Ishikirizwa rya mbere ku bikorwa vy'imikono myinshi, ryakuweho risubirizwa na BIP11 na BIP16.
---

Itegeko BIP10, ryashikirijwe na Gavin Andresen mu 2011, ryari rigamije gushiramwo iciyumviro co gukoresha amasezerano menshi. Intumbero yayo nyamukuru yari iyo guhuza ingene amahera yo kwishura yoshobora gushingira ku mikono myinshi (Multisig) mu gushiramwo ubwoko bushasha bw’inyandiko.

Ico ciyumviro cari gifise iciyumviro co kwemerera abahingura amasezerano y’ubudandaji gusobanura imfunguruzo nyinshi za bose be n’itegeko risaba umubare usobanuwe w’izo mfunguruzo kugira ngo umuntu ashireko umukono kugira ngo amasezerano yemezwe.

Ariko rero, BIP10 yarakuweho ntiyigeze yinjira muri iyo porotokole. Ivyo vyari vyitezwe vyashikirijwe n'izindi BIPs zakurikiyeho, nka BIP11 na BIP16, zashizeho uburyo buhambaye kandi bushobora guhinduka bwa Multisig ku Bitcoin.
