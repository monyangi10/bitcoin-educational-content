---
term: GUHUZA
---

Porogaramu yakozwe na Chris Belcher kandi yatangujwe muri Myandagaro 2014 yagenewe kworohereza gukoresha coinjoins zifise GUI. Udakunze ibindi bikorwa vya CoinJoin vy’ico gihe vyasaba ko abaje mu nama bashaka gukora CoinJoin icarimwe, CoinJumble yemerera gusangira ibice vy’ubudandaji mu buryo butajanye n’igihe. Abakoresha boshobora Exchange biciye ku nzira z’itumanaho zo hanze kugira ngo basangire ibice vy’ubudandaji vyakozwe. Uyu musi, CoinJumble ntikigikoreshwa.
