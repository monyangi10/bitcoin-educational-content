---
term: CPU (UMUGAMBI WA HAGATI WO GUKORA)
---

Igihimba nyamukuru ca mudasobwa gijejwe gushitsa amabwirizwa y’imashini ya porogarama. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, CPU yakoreshejwe mu ntango kuri Mining n'ibihimba vy'umubiri imbere y'uko irengerwa na GPU (amakarata y'ibishushanyo) Mining, hanyuma ikoreshwa n'ibihimba vy'umubiri bizwi nka "ASICs".


> ► *Mu gifaransa, umuntu yoshobora kuvyita "unité centrale de calcul" canke gusa "umuhinguzi".*
