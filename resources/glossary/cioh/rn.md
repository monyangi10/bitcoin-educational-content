---
term: CIOH
definition: Amayeri avuga ko uburyo bwose bwinjira mu vunjisha ari ubw'umuntu umwe.
---

Incamake y'ijambo "*Inyinjizo rusangi Ownership Heuristic*". Ni ubuhinga bukoreshwa mu bijanye n’isesengura ry’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin ryiyumvira ko ibintu vyose vyinjizwa mu gicuruzwa ari ivy’ikigo kimwe canke umukoresha umwe. Iyo umuntu yihweje amakuru ya bose y’isoko rya Bitcoin, kandi akabona ibintu vyinshi vyinjijwe, rero, iyo ata bigereranyo canke ayandi makuru yo guhakana ivyo, birashobora kugereranywa ko ibintu vyose vyinjijwe muri iyo nzira vyari vy’umuntu umwe (canke ikigo).


Iryo sesangura ry’ubuhinga bwa heuristic ryavumbuwe na Satoshi Nakamoto ubwiwe, akarivuga mu gice ca 10 c’Igitabu Cera:


Na n’ubu, CIOH iguma ari yo heuristic nyamukuru ikoreshwa n’amashirahamwe y’isesengura ry’uruzitiro, hamwe n’ugusubira gukoresha Address.
