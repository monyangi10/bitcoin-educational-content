---
term: OP_SUCCESS

---

`OP_SUCCESS` igereranya urutonde rw'amakode y'ubuhinga bwa none yari yarazimijwe kera kandi ubu akaba yarabitswe kugira ngo akoreshwe muri Tapscript muri kazoza. Intumbero yabo nyamukuru ni ugufasha mu guhindura no kwagura ururimi rw’inyandiko, mu kwemera ko hashirwaho ibikorwa bishasha biciye ku maforogo yoroshe. Iyo imwe muri izo opcodes ihuye n’inyandiko, yerekana ko ico gice c’inyandiko kizororanirwa, ata kuraba amakuru canke ibintu biriho. Ivyo bisigura ko iyo nyandiko ikomeza gukora ataco ipfa, ataco ikora ku bikorwa vya kera.


Gutyo, iyo opcode nshasha yongewe kuri `OP_SUCCESS`, bigereranya vy’ukuri ko hongeweko itegeko ry’uburenganzira kuruta iryo ry’imbere. Ivyuma vyavuguruwe birashobora rero kugenzura ko ryubahiriza iri tegeko, kandi ivyuma bitavuguruwe ntibizokwanka amafaranga ajanye n'ivyo n'amabuye arimwo, kuko `OP_SUCCESS` yemeza ico gice c'inyandiko. Nta fork ikomeye rero iriho.


Mu kugereranya, `OP_NOP` (*No Operation*) na zo nyene zikora nk'ibibanza mu nyandiko, ariko ntaco zigira ku gushirwa mu ngiro kw'inyandiko. Iyo `OP_NOP` ihuye, inyandiko irabandanya gusa ata guhindura uko ikirundo kimeze canke ingene inyandiko itera imbere. Itandukaniro nyamukuru rero, riri mu ngaruka zavyo ku gushirwa mu ngiro: `OP_SUCCESS` iremeza ko umuntu ashobora guca neza muri ico gice c’inyandiko, mu gihe `OP_NOP` ataco ihuriyeko, kandi ntaco ikora haba ku ruganda canke ku nzira y’inyandiko. Izo kode zigaragazwa na `OP_SUCCESSN` aho `N` ari umubare ukoreshwa mu gutandukanya `OP_SUCCESS`.
