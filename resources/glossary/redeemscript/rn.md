---
term: redeemscript
---

Inyandiko isobanura ivyangombwa vyihariye ivyo bikoresho bitegerezwa gushitsa kugira ngo bifungure amahera ajanye n’igisohoka ca P2SH. Mu P2SH UTXO, `Urufunguzo rw'inyandiko` rurimwo Hash ya `redeemscript`. Iyo igikorwa cipfuza gukoresha iyi UTXO nk'inyungu, kigomba gutanga `redeemscript` igaragara ihuye na Hash iri muri `scriptPubKey`. `redeemscript` rero itangwa mu `scriptSig` y'inyungu, hamwe n'izindi Elements zikenewe kugira ngo zishitse ivyangombwa vy'inyandiko, nk'imikono canke imfunguruzo za bose. Iyi nzira ipfutse ituma amakuru y’ivyo gukoresha aguma yihishije gushika amafaranga bitcoins akoreshejwe vy’ukuri. Ikoreshwa cane cane ku bikoresho vy’amasakoshi vy’imikono myinshi vya Legacy P2SH.
