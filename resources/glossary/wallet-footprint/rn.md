---
term: Wallet IKIRENGERO
---

Ivyiza bigaragara mu bikorwa vyakozwe na Bitcoin Wallet nyene. Ivyo bimenyetso bishobora kubamwo ugusa mu gukoresha ubwoko bw'inyandiko, gusubira gukoresha aderesi, urutonde rwa UTXO, gushiramwo ibisohoka vy'ihinduka, gutanga ikimenyetso ca RBF (*Replace-by-fee*), inomero ya verisiyo, umwanya wa `nSequence`, n'umwanya wa `nLockTime`.


Ibirenge vya Wallet bikoreshwa n’abahinga mu gukurikirana ibikorwa vy’ikigo kinaka kuri Blockchain mu kumenya uburyo busubira mu bikorwa vyaco. Nk’akarorero, umukoresha yohereza mu buryo butunganye ihinduka ryiwe ku ma aderesi ya P2TR (`bc1p...`) akora ikirenge kidasanzwe gishobora gukoreshwa mu gukurikirana ibikorwa vyiwe vyo muri kazoza.


Nk’uko LaurentMT abisigura muri Space Kek #19 (ikiganiro kivuga igifaransa), akamaro k’ibirenge vya Wallet mu gusesangura uruhererekane karagwira cane uko igihe kigenda kirarenga. Nkako, ukuntu ubwoko bw’inyandiko bugenda buragwira be n’ukuntu ivyo bintu bishasha bigenda birakoreshwa buhorobuhoro na porogarama ya Wallet, biratuma habaho ubudasa. Birashoboka mbere ko umuntu amenya neza porogarama ikoreshwa n’ico kintu gikurikiranywe. Ni ngombwa rero gutahura ko kwiga ikirenge ca Wallet ari ngirakamaro cane cane ku bikorwa vy’ubudandaji biherutse gukorwa, kuruta ivyo vyatanguye mu ntango z’imyaka ya 2010.
