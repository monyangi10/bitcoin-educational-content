---
term: OP_WITHIN (0XA5)

definition: Opcode igenzura ko agaciro kari hagati y’utundi gaciro tubiri.
---

Igenzura nimba ikintu co hejuru ku kirundo kiri mu rutonde rwasobanuwe n'ikintu ca kabiri n'ica gatatu co hejuru. Mu yandi majambo, `OP_WITHIN` isuzuma nimba ikintu co hejuru ari kinini canke kingana n’ica kabiri kandi kikaba ari gito kuruta ica gatatu. Iyo iyi nzira ari ukuri, isunika `1` (ukuri) ku ruganda, ahandi ho, isunika `0` (ikinyoma).
