---
term: BIP0075
definition: Iyagurwa rya BIP70 ryongerako ugushira umukono ku busabe bw'iyishyurwa no guhisha inyishu. Ntiyigeze yakirwa na benshi.
---

Ukwagura kwongereza ubuhinga bwo kwishura BIP70 mu gushiramwo ibintu bibiri bihambaye bishasha.

Ica mbere, bituma uwurungitse igisabwa co kwishura ashobora gushira umukono kuri ico gisabisho ku bushake, agatanga icemeza ko uwugirungitse ashobora kugenzura akaranga k’uwo yarungitse.

Ubwa kabiri, irashiramwo ubusabe bwo kwishura bwagarutse kugira ngo ntihagire uwuyifata no kugira ngo abaje mu nama bonyene bashobore kubona amakuru y’ukwishura.

Ivyo bintu vyiza bikomeza umutekano w’ibikorwa n’ubuzima bwite mu gihe kandi bizana ibintu vyo kwongereza ubumenyi bw’abakoresha.

Ariko rero, kubera ko BIP75 yubakiye kuri BIP70, ntiyigeze yemerwa cane.
