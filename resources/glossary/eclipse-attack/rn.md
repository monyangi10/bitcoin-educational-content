---
term: Igitero ca Eclipse
definition: Igitero gikura inkingi ya Bitcoin mu rurere mu kugenzura itumatumanako ryayo ryose.
---

Igitero kijanye n’ugutandukanya no kugenzura ivy’itumanaho ry’urudodo ruri mu rubuga mu kurema ibidukikije vy’ubuhinga birukikuje. Intumbero ni ugucungera canke gukoresha amakuru yaronswa n’iyo node yoherezwa, gutyo ikayaca ku bagenzi bayo babereye. Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ubu buhinga burashobora gukoreshwa mu kuzimiza node, mu gucengera canke mu guhindura amakuru yakira canke yohereza, canke mu gukora ibitero vya Double-spending.
