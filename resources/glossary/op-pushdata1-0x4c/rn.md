---
term: OP_PUSHDATA1 (0X4C)

definition: Opcode ishiraho gushika ku ma bytes 255 y’amakuru ku mupfundikizo.
---

Isunika umubare w'amakuru ku kirundo. Ikurikirwa na byte yerekana uburebure bw’amakuru yo gusunika (gushika kuri 255 bytes). Iyi opcode ikoreshwa mu gushiramwo amakuru y'ubunini buhinduka mu nyandiko.
