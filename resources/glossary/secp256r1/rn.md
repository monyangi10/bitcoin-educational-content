---
term: SECP256R1
---

Izina rihabwa umurongo w'uruzitiro usobanurwa n'ingingo ya NIST y'urufunguzo rwa bose. Ikoresha umurima w'intango w'ibice 256 n'ingero y'umurongo w'uruzitiro $y^2 = x^3 + ax + b$ n'ibidahinduka:


```text
a = -3
```


kandi


```text
b = 410583637251521421293261297800472684091144410159937255548542755610749322
77127
```


Igicapo `secp256r1` gikoreshwa cane mu masezerano menshi, ariko ntigikoreshwa muri Bitcoin. Nkako, Satoshi Nakamoto yahisemwo igicapo `secp256k1`, ico gihe cari kizwi cane mu 2009. Imvo nyayo y’iryo hitamwo ntiramenyekana, ariko bishobora kuba vyari ukugira ngo bigabanye ingorane z’inyuma. Ivyagezwe vy’umurongo wa $k1$ vy’ukuri vyoroshe cane kuruta ivy’umurongo wa $r1$, cane cane umurongo udahinduka $b$.


> ► *Iyi nzira rimwe na rimwe yitwa kandi "P-256".*
