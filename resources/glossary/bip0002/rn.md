---
term: BIP0002
---

Inyandiko yanditswe na Luke Dashjr muri Ruheshi 2012 yashizeho ingingo zitomoye zo kwemera ivyifuzo vyo guteza imbere Bitcoin (BIPs) vyo muri kazoza. BIP2 yashizeho ivyiyumviro vy'uko BIPs "yemererwa", "yanka", canke "ikurwamwo", kandi isobanura ibisabwa kugira ngo umuntu agire ikibanza cose.

BIP ifatwa nk'"yemewe" iyo ironse infashanyo ikomeye kuva ku muryango wa Bitcoin, harimwo abayitegura n'abayikoresha. BIP "iranka" iyo idaronka infashanyo ihagije canke igaragaza ingorane zidashobora gutorwa umuti. Ubwa nyuma, BIP irashobora "gukurwamwo" n'umwanditsi wayo igihe cose. BIP2 na yo nyene irasubira gusobanura neza uburyo n'imiterere vyashizweho na BIP1.
