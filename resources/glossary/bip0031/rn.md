---
term: BIP0031
---

Iciyumviro kigamije gutuma uburyo bwo gucunga urubuga rwa Bitcoin node butera imbere. Imbere ya BIP31, ama node ya Bitcoin nta buryo butaziguye yari afise bwo kumenya nimba abo mu runganwe rwabo bari bakihuye, bariko barasubiza, kandi bataremerewe cane. BIP31 yashizeho ikoreshwa ry'ubutumwa bwa `pong`, mu kwishura ubutumwa bwa `ping`, bishoboza gusuzuma ubufatanye hagati y'ibice.
