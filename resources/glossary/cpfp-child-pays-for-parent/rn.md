---
term: CPFP (Child Pays For Parent)
definition: Uburyo butuma uwayironse anyarutsa ivunjisha ryahagaze mu kurema ivunjisha ry'umwana ririmwo amahera menshi.
---

Uburyo bwo gukorana bugamije kwihutisha kwemeza igikorwa ca Bitcoin, busa n’ico Replace-by-fee (RBF) ikora, ariko buva ku ruhande rw’uwuronka. Iyo igikorwa gifise amafaranga make cane ugereranije n’isoko kiguma gifatanye mu ma mempools y’ibihimba kandi ntikivyemeze ningoga bihagije, uwugihabwa arashobora gukora igikorwa gishasha, akoresheje amafaranga y’ama bitcoins yaronse mu gicuruzwa cabujijwe, naho nyene kitaremezwa. Iryo soko rya kabiri risaba vy’ukuri ko rya mbere rizocukurwa kugira ngo ryemezwe. Abacukuzi rero baragoberwa no gushiramwo ivyo bicuruzwa vyose hamwe. Iryo soko rya kabiri rizotanga amahera menshi cane mu mafaranga y’isoko kuruta irya mbere, mu buryo amahera asanzwe aremesha abacukuzi gushiramwo ivyo bikorwa vyose bibiri. Igikorwa kijanye n’umwana (ica kabiri) kiriha igikorwa c’umuvyeyi gifatanye (ica mbere). Ni co gituma yitwa "CPFP."


Gutyo, CPFP ituma uwuyironka ashobora kuronka amahera yiwe ningoga naho amahera y’intango uwuyirungitse ari make, bitandukanye na RBF (*Replace-by-fee*), ituma uwuyirungitse afata ingingo yo kwihutisha ibikorwa vyiwe bwite mu kwongera amahera.
