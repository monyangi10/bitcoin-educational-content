---
term: BIP0148
definition: Icishirwaho ca UASF co guhata SegWit kugira ishikirizwe n'abakoresha mu gihe ibimenyetso vy'abacukuzi vyari vyahagaze.
---

Iciyumviro cashizweho muri Ntwarante 2017 n’umuhinga mu vy’ubuhinga bw’amasanamu yitwa Shaolin Fry. Intumbero ya BIP148 yari iyo guhatira gukoresha ivugurura rya SegWit ku masezerano ya Bitcoin, mu kwishura ku guhagarara kw’ibimenyetso vy’iyi Soft Fork n’abacukuzi biciye ku buryo bwa BIP9. BIP148 yasavye ko hashirwa mu ngiro UASF (*User-Activated Soft Fork*) kugira ngo SegWit ikoreshwe ku nguvu n'ibihimba ku wa 15 Ugushyingo 2017, iyo abacukuzi batazoba bafunze SegWit ku wa 1 Myandagaro 2017.

Iyo yemezwa, ama node akurikira BIP148 UASF yari kwanka ama blocs atagaragaza ko SegWit ishigikiye, gutyo agahatira abacukuzi gukora ivyo guhindura.

Naho iyo BIP y'akahise itakoreshejwe, yarafise uruhara runini mu kwemera neza SegWit, mu guhatira abacukuzi gufunga Soft Fork biciye kuri BIP91. Mu gihe kirekire, BIP148 yashizeho akarorero gahambaye, yerekana ingaruka abakoresha bashobora kugira biciye ku nzira zabo zuzuye ku ngingo z’ubutegetsi z’amasezerano ya Bitcoin.
