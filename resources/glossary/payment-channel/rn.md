---
term: Payment channel
definition: Umuhuza hagati ya innode zibiri za Lightning mu guhana bitcoin bitaciye kuri chaine.
---

Mu rwego rwa Lightning Network, umurongo wo kwishura ni uruja n’uruza rw’inzira zibiri hagati y’ibice bibiri vy’umuravyo bishobora gutuma habaho uguhinduranya off-chain Bitcoin. On-Chain, umurongo wo kwishura ugereranywa n’igitabu Address c’imikono myinshi 2/2 gifise abaje mu nama bompi. Umurongo wo kwishura usaba amafaranga On-Chain kugira ngo ufungurwe, uwundi kugira ngo ufunge. Hagati y’ivyo bintu bibiri, abakoresha imirongo barashobora gukora umubare munini cane w’uguhinduranya off-chain Bitcoin kuri Lightning Network, ata gikorwa ca On-Chain bikenewe. Ku Lightning, birashoboka ko umuntu yohereza amahera biciye mu nzira nyinshi n’ibihimba vyinshi, kugira ngo yohereze ama bitcoins ataco afungura umurongo utaziguye n’uwuyakira.
