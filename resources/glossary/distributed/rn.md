---
term: BITANGAZWE
---

Kamere y’urubuga rwa mudasobwa aho ububasha bwo gufata ingingo n’ubugenzuzi bigabanywa mu buryo buringaniye hagati y’abaje mu rubuga bose. Ukwo gutanga ni kwo gutuma urutonde rushobora gukomera. Ni ryo kandi ryitwa urubuga rw’urunganwe. Ubutandukanye n’urubuga rwigenga, aho ububasha buca ibice kandi bugashira mu bigo vyinshi, ariko aho ubutegetsi bumwe bumwe buguma bufise ububasha burengeye ubw’ababukoresha, urubuga rwiganje rurakuraho ubutegetsi buhambaye mu kwegera uburongozi n’ubugenzuzi ku bakoresha ubwabo. Bitcoin ni akarorero k’uburyo busanzwe. Bitcoin nta n’ubukuru canke ubukuru buhambaye ifise. Ugukomeza amasezerano, ugusuzuma ibikorwa, no gutanga amafaranga mashasha bikorwa n’abakoresha. Uwo mubumbe ugabanywa utuma iyo nzira ishobora gukomera no kunanira gucengera, bikaba bigoye cane ko ikigo kimwe kiyigenzura canke kiyikoresha.


> ► *Abantu bamwe bamwe bavuga ko Bitcoin ari uburyo bwo kwegereza ubutegetsi. Nkako, ntibisanzwe kubona ayo majambo abiri akoreshwa mu buryo bumwe. Ijambo ry'ikigereranyo ry'ikigereranyo "gukwiragizwa" ryoshobora kuba "peer-to-peer," rimwe na rimwe rikaba rigabanywa ngo "P2P," iryo jambo ry'inyongera rivuye mu mpinduro y'icongereza "Peer-to-Peer."*
