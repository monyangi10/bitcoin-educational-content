---
term: ABABUZA BAKURU
---

Ijambo rikoreshwa mu kudondora abashigikiye kwongerera ubunini bw’amabuye ya Bitcoin mu gihe c’intambara y’ubunini bw’amabuye hagati ya 2015 na 2017. Ababuza bukomeye bemera ko kwongera ubunini bw’amabuye ari ngombwa kugira ngo Bitcoin ishobore gutera imbere. Bashigikiye ivyiyumviro nk’ivyo bita Bitcoin XT, Bitcoin Classic, na Bitcoin Bitagira aho bigarukira. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ababujije batobato, bakunda amaforogo ya Soft, ababujije bakuru bari biteguriye gushitsa amahinduka biciye ku maforogo ya Hard.
