---
term: BIP0143
definition: Uburyo bushasha bwa hashing ku bimenyetso vya SegWit, bukemura ukwongereka kwa quadratic kandi buriko agaciro k'ibwinjiriro.
---

Izana uburyo bushasha bwo gukora hashing y'ibikorwa kugira ngo hasuzumwe umukono mu nyandiko z'inyuma ya SegWit. Intumbero ni ugugabanya ibikorwa bitari ngombwa mu gihe co kugenzura no gushiramwo agaciro ka UTXO k’injiza mu mukono. Ibi bitorera umuti ibibazo bibiri bikomeye n'ubuhinga bwo gukorana n'abandi:

**Ukwiyongera kw'ibice bine mu gukora hashing n'umubare w'imikono uriko uragwira**

**Ukutagira agaciro k'inyungu mu sinya, vyatumye haba ingorane ku bikoresho vy'amasakoshi, cane cane mu kugenzura amafaranga y'ugucuruza akoreshwa.**

Kubera ko ugusubiramwo kwa SegWit (kwasiguwe muri BIP141) kwashizeho ubwoko bushasha bw’inyandiko y’ugucuruza iyo node za kera zitazosuzuma, BIP143 yarakoresheje ako karyo kugira ngo itore umuti ivyo bibazo ataco isaba Hard Fork.


BIP143 rero yashizwe mu gice ca SegWit Soft Fork.
