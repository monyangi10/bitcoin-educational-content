---
term: Relais
definition: Icakoreshwa canke ishengero rishinzwe gukwiragiza amablock masha yemewe ku muhora.
---

Mu bijanye na Proof-of-Work ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, bisigura igikoresho gifise igikorwa nyamukuru co gutanga amabuye mashasha afise akamaro yabonetse. Rimwe na rimwe habaho itandukaniro hagati y’uruhara rwa Miner, afise uruhara mu nzira yose ya Proof-of-Work, kuva ku kwubaka igice c’umukandida gushika ku gukwiragiza igice c’umubiri cemejwe, n’uruhara rw’umucapura, rugarukira ku kugira uruhara mu gukora hashing y’igice ataco yinjiye mu guhitamwo canke gukwiragiza igice. Ku rundi ruhande, uruhara rw’ugutanga amabuye mashasha abonwa n’igikoresho co gucapura. Ku bijanye n’ibidengeri vya Mining, uwo murimo wo gutanga amakuru ukorwa n’ikidengeri ubwaco. Uwo murimo ni wo uha ikidengeri ububasha bwinshi ku ba choppers ku giti cabo. Ijambo "relayer" rishobora kandi kwerekeza ku muntu canke ikigo gikoresha relay.
