---
term: ENTROPIE (ISESENGURA)
---

Mu bijanye n’isesengura ry’uruzitiro, entropi ni izina ry’ikigereranyo, rikomoka kuri entropi ya Shannon, yavumbuwe na LaurentMT. Ico kigereranyo kiremesha gupima ubukene bw’ubumenyi abasesangura bafise ku bijanye n’imiterere nyayo y’ugucuruza kwa Bitcoin. Mu yandi majambo, uko entropie y’ugucuruza igenda irakura, ni ko bigorana ko abasesangura bamenya ingene ama bitcoins agenda hagati y’ivyo yinjiza n’ivyo asohoka.


Mu bikorwa, entropi ihishura nimba, mu kubona kw’umuntu wo hanze, igikorwa gitanga insobanuro nyinshi zishoboka, zishingiye gusa ku rugero rw’ibintu vyinjizwa n’ibisohoka, ata kwihweza ibindi bigereranyo vyo hanze canke vyo mu mutima n’ubuhinga bwo kumenya ibintu. Entropie nini rero ni co kimwe n’ibanga ryiza ry’ugucuruza.


Entropie isobanurwa nk’umubare w’ibintu bishobora gukorana. Aha niho hari formule ikoreshwa na $E$ igereranya entropie y'ugucuruza na $C$ umubare w'insobanuro zishoboka:


$$
E = \log_2(C)
$$


Hakurikijwe agaciro k’ama UTXO ari mu bikorwa, umubare w’insobanuro $C$ ugereranya umubare w’uburyo ivyinjizwa bishobora gufatanywa n’ibisohoka. Mu yandi majambo, ni ryo rigena igitigiri c’insobanuro igikorwa gishobora gutanga bivuye ku mbonerahamwe y’umuntu wo hanze ariko aragisuzuma.
