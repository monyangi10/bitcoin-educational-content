---
term: POOL

---

Ibikorwa vyateguwe n’Ibikorwa vy’Imiravyo. Ni ubwoko bw’isoko ry’amahera mu mihora ya Lightning. Pool ihuza abakoresha bakeneye kuronka amafaranga kuri LN n’abafise amafaranga yo gukoresha ku rubuga. Abazoja muri iyo nama barashobora kuronka ama satoshis mu gufungura imihora mishasha ku barondera kuronka amahera kuri Lightning mu kiringo kinaka. Pool kandi iremesha gukodesha umurongo kugira ngo umuntu ahite yemera amahera ya Bitcoin.
