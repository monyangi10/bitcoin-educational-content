---
term: Bitcoin XT
definition: Fork yo mu 2015 yashikirije kwongera ingano y'ibice (blocks) gushika kuri 8 MB, inyanduruko ya Blocksize War.
---

Fork ya Bitcoin yatangujwe mu 2015 n’umuhinga mu vy’ubuhinga Mike Hearn kandi ishigikiwe na Gavin Andresen (uwahoze ari umuyobozi mukuru w’ivy’ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin inyuma y’aho Satoshi ivuye).

Mu ntango, Bitcoin XT yari ugushirwa mu ngiro guhuye n’umurongo wa Bitcoin core.

Ariko rero, muri Myandagaro 2015, verisiyo 0.11A ya Bitcoin XT yemeye BIP101: igitekerezo ca Hard Fork co kwongera ubunini bw'ibarabara kuva kuri 1MB gushika kuri 8MB, n'ugutegura gukubita kabiri inyuma y'imyaka ibiri gushika bishike ku 8GB ku ibarabara mu 2036.

Ico ciyumviro cari kimwe mu vyatumye haba intambara ya Blocksize yabaye hagati ya 2015 na 2017. Cari gishigikiwe n’umugabane munini w’abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro be n’amashirahamwe akomeye nka BitPay, Blockchain.info, na Circle.

Amaherezo, Bitcoin XT ntiyashoboye gukoranya infashanyo ihagije y’abanyagihugu. Mike Hearn amaherezo atangaza ko azova mu kibano no kugurisha amafaranga yiwe y’ibiceri, yongera aserura ukuntu ataco akora mu kiganiro yanditse ku rubuga rwiwe, cane cane avuga ko “Bitcoin yananiwe.”
