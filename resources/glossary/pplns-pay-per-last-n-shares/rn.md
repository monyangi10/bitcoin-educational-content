---
term: Pplns (pay per last n shares)
definition: Uburyo bwo kuriha bishimikiye ku migabane yashikirijwe igihe ibloc yatorwa.
---

Uburyo bwo kubara impembo y’abacukuzi mu rwego rw’ibidengeri vya Mining. PPLNS ihemba abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bivanye n’intererano batanga y’imigabane mu kiringo kinaka. Muri PPLNS, amahera yishurwa gusa iyo pool ibonye ububiko kandi agenwa n’umubare w’imigabane yashikirijwe na Miner ugereranije n’imigabane yose yashizwe hamwe mu kiringo c’ivyigwa. Ubwo buryo burafasha abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro badahinduka kandi bakora cane mu kiringo kirekire, kuko buca intege abacukuzi b’ibidengeri (guhindura ibidengeri kenshi). Impembo irahindagurika bivanye n’uko umuntu ashobora kuronka igice, ivyo bishobora gutuma Miner igabanuka mu bijanye n’uguhuza inyungu (uguhinduka kwinshi kuruta kwishura ku mugabane).
