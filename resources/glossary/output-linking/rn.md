---
term: Output linking
definition: Ingendo yo gusubira gukoresha aderesi zonona ibanga mu guhuza UTXO nyinshi.
---

Rimwe na rimwe rikoreshwa mu buryo bumwe kugira ngo ryerekeze ku gusubira gukoresha Address. Ihuza ry’ibisohoka ryerekeye ku mugenzo wo gukoresha Address imwe yakira kugira ngo ufunge UTXO nyinshi, rimwe na rimwe mu bikorwa bitandukanye. Bitcoins zisanzwe zikingirwa hakoreshejwe urufunguzo rw’ibanga rujanye n’urufunguzo rwihariye rwa Address. Kubera ko Blockchain ari iya bose, biroroshe kubona aderesi zijanye n’ingene ama bitcoins angahe. Mu gihe co gusubira gukoresha Address imwe nyene ku kwishura kenshi, birashobora kwiyumvirwa ko UTXO zose zijanye n’ivyo ari iz’ikigo kimwe. Ku bw’ivyo, gusubira gukoresha Address biratera ingorane ubuzima bwite bw’uwuyikoresha. Ishobora gutuma habaho amasano hagati y’ibikorwa vyinshi n’ama UTXO, hamwe no gukomeza gukurikirana amafaranga y’i On-Chain.
