---
term: BTC
---

Ikimenyetso c’ububiko canke c’amahera (*ticker*) gikoreshwa mu guserura igice ca Bitcoin ku bibanza vya Exchange. Ikoreshwa cane mu kumenya ningoga Bitcoin mu bindi bintu n’amahera. Igitigiri kimwe ca Bitcoin (1 BTC) kingana n’amasatoshi 100.000.000 (canke “Sats”).


> ► *"BTC" ni ikimenyetso c'amateka ca Bitcoin, ariko ntikijanye n'ingingo ya ISO 4217, igenzura amakode y'amafaranga. Dushingiye kuri iyo ngingo, "BT" igenewe Bhutan. Amafaranga adashigikiwe na Leta, nka Bitcoin, akwiye gutangura na "X". Ni co gituma amashirahamwe amwe amwe akoresha ikimenyetso "XBT" kuri Bitcoin. Naho "XBT" itararemezwa ku mugaragaro n'ingingo ngenderwako y'ISO 4217, naho biri ukwo irahuye n'ingingo zayo.*
