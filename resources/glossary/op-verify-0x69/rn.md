---
term: OP_VERIFY (0X69)

definition: Opcode isaba ko ibiri k’inyuma ku mupfundikizo bitaba zeru, ahandi ihererekanya riba ritari ryo.
---

Bisaba ko agaciro k'ikirundo co hejuru katari zero (ukuri). Ivyo bikoresho ntibigira akamaro nimba atari ko biri. `OP_VERIFY` ikoreshwa mu kwemeza ivyangombwa vy'inyandiko.
