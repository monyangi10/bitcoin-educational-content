---
term: Hexadecimal
definition: Uburyo bwo kubara bukoresha imibare 16 bukoreshwa mu kwerekana amakuru muri mudasobwa.
---

Uburyo bwo gutanga imibare buri mu rwego rwa 16, rero bukoresha ibimenyetso 16 bitandukanye kugira ngo bugereranye imibare. Ibimenyetso 10 vya mbere ni imibare kuva kuri 0 gushika kuri 9, isa n’iyo mu rutonde rw’ibiharuro cumi (ishimikiro 10), kandi ibimenyetso bitandatu bikurikira bigereranywa n’inyuguti A gushika kuri F. Gutyo, A igereranya umubare 10, B igereranya 11, gushika kuri F igereranya 15. imibare (ishimikiro rya 2), kandi umubare wose w’icumi na gatandatu ugereranya neza na neza ibice 4, ivyo bikaba bituma guhindura vyoroha. Ubu buryo bwo gutanga inomero burakoreshwa kenshi cane mu guserura amakuru yerekeye Bitcoin.
