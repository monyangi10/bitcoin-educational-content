---
term: Unix (heure)
definition: Igitigiri c'amasegonda amaze guhera itariki ya 1 Nzero 1970 isaha sita z'ijoro (UTC), gikoreshwa mu gucunga igihe muri Bitcoin.
---

Igihe ca Unix canke Unix Timestamp kigereranya umubare w’amasegonda yaheze kuva kw’igenekerezo rya 1 Mukakaro 1970, isaha zibiri z’ijoro UTC (Unix Epoch). Uyu murongo ukoreshwa mu mirongo y’ibikorwa ya Unix n’ibiva kuri yo kugira ngo ushire ikimenyetso ku gihe mu buryo busanzwe kandi buhuye. Bituma amasaha ashobora guhuzwa neza be n’ugucungera ibintu bishingiye ku masaha, ata kuraba amasaha.


Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ikoreshwa ku isaha y’aho hantu y’ibihimba, kandi rero ku kubara NAT (Igihe gihinduwe n’urubuga). Igihe gihinduwe n'urubuga ni igihe c'imbere c'ibihe vy'urudodo biharuwe mu karere n'urudodo rumwe rumwe, kandi iyo nzira ikoreshwa mu kugenzura ukuri kw'ibimenyetso vy'igihe vy'amabuye. Nkako, kugira ngo igice cemerwe n’urudodo, Timestamp yaco itegerezwa kuba iri hagati ya MTP (Median Time Past y’amabuye 11 ya nyuma yacukuwe) na NAT yongeweko amasaha 2:


```text
MTP < Valid Timestamp < (NAT + 2h)
```


Igihe ca Unix gikoreshwa kandi mu gushinga timelocks iyo zishingiye ku gihe nyaco, aho gushingira ku mubare w'ibice.
