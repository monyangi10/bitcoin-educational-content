---
term: Zkp (zero-knowledge proof)
definition: Uburyo bwa kiritografiya bwerekana ko amakuru ari ay'ukuri uterekanye ayo makuru ubwayo.
---

Uburyo bwo gukingira amakuru (cryptographie) butuma uruhande rumwe (uwushingira intahe) rwemeza uwundi (uwugenzura) ko amakuru ari ukuri, ataco ahishura ayo makuru ubwayo canke umuce uwo ari wo wose wayo. ZKP iratuma amajambo agira ukuri mu gihe izigama ibanga ry’amakuru ashingiyeko.
