---
term: Gusubira gukoresha
definition: Igitero kigerageza gukoresha ama-bitcoins amwe incuro nyinshi mu kuryarya abo mukorana.
---

Igitero aho umukoresha w’ububisha agerageza gukoresha UTXO imwe (*Unspent Transaction Output*) incuro zirenga imwe kugira ngo yitunganirize ku giciro c’abagira uruhara mu bikorwa. Mu ngingo ngenderwako, iyo igikorwa cemejwe mu gice c’ibarabara maze kigashirwa kuri Blockchain, ikoreshwa ry’ayo ma bitcoins rirandikwa ubudasiba, rikabuza gukoresha ayo ma bitcoins nyene. Gukingira gukoresha amahera kabiri ni co kintu nyamukuru gifasha Blockchain.


Mu gihe c’igitero co gukoresha amahera kabiri, uwutera abanza gukora ubucuruzi bubereye n’umucuruzi, hanyuma agakora ubucuruzi bugira kabiri buhiganwa akoresheje ivyo biceri nyene, haba mu kubirungika kugira ngo basubize ayo mahera canke mu kubikoresha mu kugura ikindi kintu canke igikorwa ku mucuruzi atandukanye.


Hariho ibintu bibiri nyamukuru bishobora gutuma ico gitero gishoboka. Ica mbere, kandi coroshe ku wutera, ni ugushitsa iyo nzira y’ubuhendanyi imbere y’uko iyo nzira yemewe ishirwa mu gice. Kugira ngo ivy’ubuhendanyi vyabo vyemezwe ubwa mbere, uwubitera afatanya amahera menshi cane y’ugucuruza kuruta ayo gucuruza kwemewe. Iyi ni ubwoko bw’ubuhendanyi RBF. Ivyo bishoboka gusa iyo umucuruzi yemeye guheza ugurisha muri "zeroconf," bisobanura ko ata vyemezo vy'ugutanga amahera. Ni co gituma ari vyiza cane kurindira ivyemezo vyinshi imbere y’uko umuntu abona ko igikorwa co gucuruza kidahinduka. Igisubizo ca kabiri, gikomeye cane, ni ic’igitero c’ibice 51 kw’ijana. Iyo uwo muterabwoba agenzura igice kinini c’ububasha bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none, barashobora gucukura uruzitiro rwo guhiganwa n’urwo rurimwo ivy’ugucuruza vyemewe, ariko harimwo n’ugucuruza kwabo kw’ubuhendanyi. Iyo umucuruzi yemeye ugurisha kandi uwumuteye ashoboye kurema uruzitiro rurerure (rufise ibikorwa vyinshi vyirundanijwe) kuruta uruzitiro rwemewe, barashobora rero gutangaza uruzitiro rwabo rw’ubuhendanyi, ruzokwemerwa n’ibihimba vy’uruja n’uruza ko ari rwo rubereye.
