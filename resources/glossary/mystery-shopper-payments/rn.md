---
term: Mystery shopper payments
definition: Igitero aho ihererekanya rito ryemewe rishobora gukurikirana UTXO n'ibikorwa vy'uwo bashaka gutera.
---

Igitero aho uwutera yohereza amahera makeyi kandi yemewe mu bice vy’amahera (bitcoins) ku muntu atera kugira ngo aronke amakuru yongere agerageze guhungabanya ibanga ryiwe. Ubwo buryo burakoreshwa kenshi ku bacuruzi. Reka dufate akarorero ka Alice, afise iduka ry’imikate ryemera amahera ya Bitcoin. Eva, uwo muterabwoba, yari gushobora kugura croissant kugira ngo aronke Alice yakira Address. Eva rero yari gushobora gukurikirana ingendo z’iyi UTXO mu kugerageza gukuraho ayandi makuru yerekeye Alice akoresheje ubuhinga bwo gukurikirana.
