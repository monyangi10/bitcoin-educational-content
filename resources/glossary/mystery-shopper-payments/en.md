---
term: MYSTERY SHOPPER PAYMENTS
---

Attack in which an attacker sends a small, legitimate payment in bitcoins to its target to obtain information and attempt to compromise its confidentiality. This technique is often used against merchants. Let's take the example of Alice, who owns a bakery that accepts bitcoin payments. Eve, the attacker, could buy a croissant to obtain Alice's receiving address. Eve could then track the movements of this UTXO in an attempt to deduce further information about Alice using tracing heuristics.
