---
term: Merkle Root
---

Digest canke "top Hash" ya Merkle Tree, igereranya incamake y'amakuru yose ari mu giti. Merkle Tree ni umubumbe w'ivyuma vy'ibanga, rimwe na rimwe vyitwa "igiti ca Hash". Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ibiti vya Merkle bikoreshwa mu gutunganya ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji mu gice kimwe no kugira ngo bishobore gusuzuma vyihuse ko hariho ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji vyihariye. Gutyo, mu mabarabara ya Bitcoin, Merkle Root ironswa mu gukurikirana amafaranga y’ibikorwa bibiri bibiri gushika hasigaye Hash imwe gusa (Merkle Root). Ivyo rero bica bishirwa mu mutwe w’igice gihuye. Ico giti ca Hash kiraboneka kandi muri UTREEXO, ubuhinga bushobora gutuma habaho uguteranya umugwi w'ibihimba vya UTXO, no muri MAST Taproot.
