---
term: BIP0091
---

Iciyumviro ca James Hilliard (injeniyeri muri Bitmain) co kworohereza gukoresha SegWit Soft Fork, isobanuwe muri BIP141, BIP143, na BIP147, biciye mu MASF ataco ishitse ku rugero rukenewe rwa 95% rw'ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none biciye mu bit 1.

Ahubwo, BIP91 yaremeye abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro gutanga ikimenyetso c’uko bashigikiye SegWit mu gushiramwo igice ca 4 mu bice vyabo vy’amabuye y’agaciro.

Igihe amabuye 269 avuye mw'idirisha ry'amabuye 336 (80% y'ububasha bwa Hash) yerekanwa hakoreshejwe igice ca 4, BIP91 yofunga. Inyuma yo gufunga, amabara yose ahuye yoshobora kwanka amabuye atagaragaza SegWit akoresheje igice ca 1.

Ubwo buryo bwari bufise intumbero yo gutuma BIP148 (UASF) itagikoreshwa no kwirinda gucapura Blockchain ku wa 1 Myandagaro 2017.

BIP91 yaje gutangura gukora kw’igenekerezo rya 23 Nyakanga 2017 (ku nzu 477.120), imbere gato y’itariki y’agaciro y’itariki 1 Myandagaro yashizweho muri BIP148.

Ivyo vyatumye abacukuzi b'amabuye y'agaciro batanga ikimenyetso c'uko SegWit, yaje gupfungwa ku wa 9 Myandagaro ku nzu 479.808, hanyuma itangura ku wa 24 Myandagaro ku nzu 481.824.

Mu ncamake, BIP148 (UASF) yaremewe mu kwishura abacukuzi batatanga ikimenyetso gihagije ca SegWit, ariko amaherezo ntiyigeze ishirwa mu ngiro.

BIP91 (MASF) yaremewe mu kwishura BIP148 kugira ngo ihagarike amaboko y'abacukuzi, ata ngorane UASF ya BIP148. BIP91 ubwayo igereranya Soft Fork, izoheza ihagarike abacukuzi gufunga SegWit Soft Fork biciye ku buryo bw’ishimikiro (MASF BIP9).
