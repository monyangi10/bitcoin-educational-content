---
term: Knapsack solver
definition: Uburyo bwa kera bwo guhitamo ibiceri muri Bitcoin Core, bwasubirijwe na Branch-and-Bound.
---

Uburyo bwa kera bwakoreshwa mu guhitamwo ibiceri muri Bitcoin core Wallet imbere ya verisiyo 0.17. Knapsack Solver igerageza gutorera umuti ingorane yo guhitamwo Coin mu guhitamwo UTXOs mu buryo busubiramwo kandi mu buryo butandukanye, no kuzishira hamwe mu bice bitobito, n’intumbero yo kugabanya amafaranga n’ubunini bw’ugucuruza. Ubwo buryo kuva ico gihe bwasubiriwe na *Ishami-n'Imbohe*.
