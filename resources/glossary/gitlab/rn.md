---
term: GITLAB
---

Urubuga rwo gucunga kode y’inkomoko n’urubuga rwo kwakira abashitsi rworohereza ubufatanye hagati y’abahinguzi. GitLab ni yo nzira nyamukuru yo gusubirira GitHub. Ico kibanza kirasa cane, ariko kandi kiratanga ubushobozi bwo kwiyakira. Nk’uko izina ryayo rivyerekana, GitLab yubatswe ku buryo bwo kugenzura verisiyo ya Git. Iyi nzira rero irashoboza gukurikirana amahinduka ya kode, gucunga verisiyo, no guteza imbere ubufatanye biciye ku bikoresho nk’ibisabwa n’ibibazo. Imigambi imwe imwe ijyanye na Bitcoin nka Samourai Wallet, Whirlpool, canke RoninDojo ikoresha GitLab.
