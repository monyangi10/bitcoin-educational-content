---
term: Shares
definition: Ikimenyetso gipima uruhara rw'umucuzi umwe mu mugwi w'abacuzi.
---

Mu bijanye n’ibidengeri vya Mining, umugabane ni ikimenyetso gikoreshwa mu gupima umusanzu wa Miner ku giti ciwe mu bidengeri. Ico gipimo ni co gishingiyeko mu kubara impembo iyo pool isubira guha Miner yose. Buri mugabane uhuye na Hash ihaza intumbero y’ingorane iri hasi y’iy’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin.


Kugira ngo ubisigure dukoresheje ikigereranyo, rimbura igiti c’impande 20. Ku Bitcoin, dufate ko Proof of Work isaba gutera umubare uri munsi ya 3 kugira ngo umuntu yemeze ububiko (ni ukuvuga gushika ku ciyumviro ca 1 canke 2). Ubu rero, wiyumvire ko mu Mining pool, intumbero y’ingorane y’umugabane ishirwa kuri 10. Gutyo, ku Miner imwe imwe iri mu kidengeri, uruzitiro rwose rw’amadayimoni ruvamwo umubare uri munsi ya 10 ruharurwa nk’umugabane ubereye. Ivyo bice bica birungikwa mu kidengeri na Miner, kugira ngo bisabe impembo yavyo, naho vyoba bidahuye n'igisubizo gikwiriye c'ibarabara kuri Bitcoin.


Ku Hash yose iharuwe, Miner imwe imwe iri mu kidengeri irashobora guhura n’ibintu bitatu bitandukanye:

**Nimba agaciro ka Hash karuta canke kangana n’intumbero y’ingorane y’ikidengeri ku bijanye n’umugabane, rero iyo Hash ntaco imaze. Miner rero ihindura Nonce yabo kugira ngo bagerageze Hash nshasha:** `Hash > gusangira > guhagarika`.


- Iyo Hash iri hasi y’intumbero y’ingorane y’umugabane, ariko ikaba nini canke ingana n’intumbero y’ingorane ya Bitcoin, rero iyo Hash igize umugabane ufise akamaro, ariko rero, ntuhagije kugira ngo wemeze ububiko. Aba Miner bashobora kohereza iyo Hash mu kidengeri cabo kugira ngo basabe impembo ijana n'umugabane: `umugabane > Hash > ububiko`.
- Iyo Hash iri hasi y’intumbero y’ingorane y’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin, ifatwa nk’umugabane ubereye be n’ibara ry’ukuri. Miner irungika iyo Hash ku kidengeri cabo, kigaca cihuta kugitangaza ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin. Iyi Hash nayo iharurwa nk'umugabane ubereye kuri Miner: `umugabane > umugwi > Hash`.





Ubu buryo bwo gusangira bukoreshwa mu kugereranya igikorwa gikozwe na Miner imwe imwe mu kidengeri, ataco umuntu wese akeneye gusubira guharura ama hashes yose yashizweho na Miner, ivyo bikaba bidakora neza na gato ku kidengeri.


Ivyuma vya Mining biratunganya ingorane z’imigabane kugira ngo bihuze umuzigo wo kugenzura no kumenya neza ko Miner yose, yaba nto canke nini, itanga imigabane hafi buri masegonda makeyi. Ivyo bituma habarwa neza Hashrate ya Miner yose n’ugusangira impembo hakurikijwe uburyo bwatowe bwo kubara indishi (PPS, PPLNS, TIDES...).
