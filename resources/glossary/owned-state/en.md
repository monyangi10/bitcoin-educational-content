---
term: Owned State
---

In the context of the RGB protocol, Owned State refers to the portion of Contract State encapsulated in a Assignment and explicitly associated with a particular holder via a Single-Use Seal pointing to a UTXO. This component represents, for example, a digital asset or a specific contractual right assigned to an individual. By isolating the state linked to each holder, Owned State guarantees precise and secure property management within RGB smart contracts.
