---
term: GUHINDURA
---

Mu bijanye na Bitcoin, yerekeza ku nzira yo guha amaverisiyo yihariye Elements zitandukanye z’amasezerano, kugira ngo bishobore gukurikirana, kuzicungera no kuzisubiramwo. Verisiyo ziharurwa zikurikirana, ivyo bikaba bituma umuntu ashobora kumenya ivyo yahinduye, ibintu bishasha canke ivyo yakosoye. Nk’akarorero, igice cose kiri muri Blockchain kirimwo inomero ya verisiyo, yerekana amategeko y’uguhurizako n’imiterere y’amakuru yakoreshejwe igihe caremwa. Versioning iboneka ku bwoko bw'inyandiko, ku bikorwa, ku ma block, ku porogaramu ya Bitcoin core, ku guhanahana amakuru ku rubuga, n'ibindi.
