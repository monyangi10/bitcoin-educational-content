---
term: CISA
definition: Icifuzo c'ubuhinga co guhuriza hamwe imikono y'inyinjira zose z'ivunjisha mu mukono umwe, kugira ngo hagabanuke ubunini n'amahera yo kuriha.
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'"Ihuriro ry'Isinya ry'Inyinjizo". Ico ni iciyumviro c’ubuhinga gigenewe gutuma ubunini bw’amafaranga y’ubudandaji bwa Bitcoin bugenda neza mu kugabanya umubare w’imikono isabwa kugira ngo yemezwe.


Ubu, kuri Bitcoin, ikintu cose cinjijwe mu gucuruza kigomba kuba gifise umukono w’umuntu ku giti ciwe imbere y’uko gishobora gukoreshwa. Ivyo vyerekana ko nyen’iyo UTXO ivugwa ari we yemereye iyo nzira. Muri CISA, intumbero ni uguhuza imikono y’ibintu vyose vyinjijwe mu gikorwa kimwe mu gucuruza umukono umwe upfutse ibintu vyose vyinjijwe. Ivyo vyotuma bishoboka kugabanya ubunini bw’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji bifise ibikoresho vyinshi, gutyo bikagabanya n’ibiciro vyavyo. Ivyo vyoba vyiza cane cane ku bakora coinjoins canke consolidations, mu gihe bishobora gutuma amafaranga menshi yemezwa kuri Bitcoin ata guhindura ubunini bw’amabuye canke ibihe. CISA ishingiye ku nzira ya Schnorr, ishobora gutuma imikono ihurizwa hamwe mu buryo bujanye n’umurongo. Ivyo bisigura ko umukono umwe ushobora kwemeza ko umuntu afise imfunguruzo nyinshi zigenga.


Ariko rero, gushirwa mu ngiro CISA kuri Bitcoin biragoye cane, kuko bisaba amahinduka akomeye mu buryo inyandiko zikora. Ubu, ugusuzuma inyandiko kuri Bitcoin bikorwa input by input. Kwimukira ku citegererezo aho igikorwa cose gisuzumwa rimwe, nk’uko vyasabwe na CISA, si kure y’ihinduka ry’agaciro gatoyi.
