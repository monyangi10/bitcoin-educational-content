---
term: Bitcoin (INDOME NTO B)
---

Bitcoin (yanditswe n'inyuguti nto "b") yerekeza ku rugero rw'amahera akoreshwa mu guhinduranya amafaranga kuri sisitemu y'amahera y'ubuhinga bwa none Bitcoin (n'inyuguti nini "B"). Bitcoin akenshi ivugwa mu buryo bugufi ngo "BTC" canke "XBT" kandi ikora nk'umurongo wa Exchange, ububiko bw'agaciro, n'igice ca konti kiri mu rubuga. Bitcoin yose igabanywamwo ibice bitobito vy'imiliyoni 100, vyitwa "satoshis" canke "Sats", mu gutera iteka uwayiremye, Satoshi Nakamoto. Ivyuma vy’ama Bitcoins bisohoka biciye mu nzira ya Proof of Work (Mining). Igitigiri cose c’ama bitcoins kigera ku miriyoni nka 21.
