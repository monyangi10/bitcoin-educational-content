---
term: INGINGO YO KWIJIRA
---

Amakuru yemerera guhuza igikorwa ca On-Chain (Address, igikorwa, igice...) n’uburyo bw’akaranga k’umukoresha canke ikigo. Nk’akarorero, iyo ushize Address yawe yakira kuri Twitter mw’izina ryawe, umuhinga mu vy’ugusesangura yoshobora kuyironka akayifatanya n’akaranga kawe. Muri ivyo, iyo tweet yoba ari ikibanza co kwinjiramwo mu gusesangura uruhererekane. Kugira ngo umuntu amenye aho yinjira, abahinga bashobora gukoresha OSINT, ariko uburyo bwakwiragiye cane bwo gufatanya igikorwa ca On-Chain n’akaranga buguma ari KYC.
