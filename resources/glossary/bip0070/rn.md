---
term: BIP0070
---

Amasezerano yo kwishura akorana na Bitcoin.

Bituma abacuruzi bashobora kohereza ibisabwa vyo kwishura no kwakira amahera mu buryo butekanye kandi buhuye.

Muri iyi porotokole, umukiriya akanda kuri Bitcoin URI (BIP21) yagutse n’iyindi parametere (idondora muri BIP72). Igisabwa co kwishura gishirwako umukono n'icete ca SSL c'umucuruzi. Igihe Wallet yakira kandi yemeza ico gisabisho, amakuru y'ukwishyura aca ahita ashirwa muri Wallet Interface y'umukiriya.

Iyi porotokole itanga ivyemezo vy’uko umuntu yishuye kandi igatuma umutekano n’ubumenyi bw’abakoresha bigenda neza mu kumenya neza uwuzokwishurwa.

Ariko rero, BIP70 ntiyigeze yemerwa cane n’abadandaza.
