---
term: Sweep transaction
definition: Uburyo bw'itunshwa ry'amafaranga rikoresha UTXO imwe yinjiye n'imwe isohotse, biranga ukwihereza amafaranga.
---

Igishushanyo canke urugero rw’ugucuruza rukoreshwa mu gusesangura uruhererekane kugira ngo hamenyekane kamere y’ugucuruza. Ico kigereranyo kirangwa n’ugukoresha UTXO imwe nk’inyungu n’uguhingura UTXO imwe nk’isohoka. Insobanuro y’iki kigereranyo ni uko turi mu kubaho kw’ukwihindura. Uwukoresha yari yimuriye ama bitcoins yiwe, akayashira ku yindi Address afise. Kubera ko ata mpinduka ibaho mu bijanye n’ugucuruza, birashoboka cane ko tuba turiko turakorana n’ugutanga amahera. Nkako, iyo umuntu yishuye, biragoye cane ko uwuyishuye agira urupapuro rwa UTXO rujanye n’amahera asabwa n’uwugurisha, uretse n’amahera y’ugucuruza. Muri rusangi, uwuriha rero agoberwa n’ugutanga umusaruro w’ihinduka. Turamenya rero ko uwukoresha iyo UTXO yihwejwe ashobora kuba agifise iyo UTXO. Mu bijanye n’isesengura ry’uruhererekane, nitwamenya ko UTXO ikoreshwa nk’inyungu mu gucuruza ari iya Alice, twoshobora kwiyumvira ko UTXO mu gusohora na yo nyene ari iyawe.
