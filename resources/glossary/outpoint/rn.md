---
term: ISOZO
---

Igiharuro kidasanzwe c’umusaruro w’ibikorwa bitakoreshejwe (UTXO). Igizwe n’ibice bibiri Elements:


- `txid`: ikimenyetso c'isoko ryaremye umusaruro;
- `vout`: urutonde rw'ibisohoka mu gucuruza.


Ivyo bibiri Elements bihuriye hamwe bigaragaza neza na neza UTXO. Nk'akarorero, iyo igikorwa gifise `txid` ya `abc...123` kandi urutonde rw'isohoka ari `0`, isohoka rizomenyekana nk'iri:


```text
abc...123:0
```


Igiharuro gikoreshwa mu vyinjizwa (`vin`) vy'ugucuruza gushasha kugira ngo vyerekane UTXO iriko irakoreshwa.


> ► *Ijambo "outpoint" rikoreshwa kenshi mu buryo bumwe n'ijambo "UTXO."*
