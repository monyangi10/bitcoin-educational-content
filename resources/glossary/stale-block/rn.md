---
term: IGIHE C'IKIBAZO (IBUBU)
---

Yerekeza ku gice kitagira abana: igice gifise akamaro, ariko kidashizwe mu ruhererekane nyamukuru rwa Bitcoin. Bishika iyo abacukuzi babiri babonye ibuye ry’agaciro riri ku burebure bw’uruzitiro rumwe mu kiringo gitoyi maze bakaritangaza ku rubuga. Amaherezo ama Nodes ahitamwo igice kimwe co gushiramwo mu ruhererekane, ashingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako y'uruhererekane rufise igikorwa kinini cane, agatuma uwundi "impfuvyi," "yashaje," canke "yashaje."
