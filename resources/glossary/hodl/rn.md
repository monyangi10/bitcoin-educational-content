---
term: Hodl
definition: Ijambo risobanura kugumya bitcoin zawe mu gihe kirekire naho haba hari ihindagurika ry'agaciro.
---

Ijambo rizwi cane mu muryango wa Bitcoin ryerekeza ku ngeso yo gufata amafaranga y’umuntu igihe kirekire, naho isoko ryoba rihinduka, no kutayagurisha. Ijambo ryakomotse ku makosa yo kwandika mu [ubutumwa bwashizwe mu 2013 ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin Talk](https://bitcointalk.org/index.php?topic=375643.msg4022997#msg4022997#msg4022997#msg4022997) bwakoreshejwe *Gamexicaeared into* "I AM HODLING" aho kuvuga ngo "I AM HOLDING", bisobanura "gufata" mu congereza. Iri jambo ryaciye rihinduka ningoga meme n’invugo.
