---
term: LNURL
---

Itegeko ry’itumanaho risobanura urutonde rw’ibintu vyagenewe kworohereza imigenderanire hagati y’ibihimba vya Lightning n’abaguzi, hamwe n’ibikorwa vy’abandi. Iyi porotokole ishingiye kuri HTTP kandi ituma amahuzu ashobora kuremwa ku bikorwa bitandukanye, nk’ugusaba kwishura, gusaba gukura amahera, canke ibindi bikorwa bituma umuntu ashobora gukoresha neza. Buri LNURL ni URL yashizwe muri bech32 n'intango `lnurl`, iyo isuzumwe, ivyura urutonde rw'ibikorwa vyikora kuri Lightning Wallet.


Nk’akarorero, LNURL-Withdraw (LUD-03) iragufasha gukura amahera mu gikorwa mu gucapura kode ya QR, ataco ukeneye gukoresha n’amaboko generate kuri Invoice. Canke LNURL-auth (LUD-04) iragufasha kwifatanya n’ibikorwa vyo kuri interineti ukoresheje urufunguzo rw’ibanga kuri Lightning Wallet yawe aho gukoresha ijambobanga.
