---
term: Atomic multi-path payments
definition: Inyungu y'ikinini ya Lightning aho igipande gikoreshwa rihamwe rya serikuri, gikubwira ikinini kigizwa tubahire cyangwa atakirikira.
---

Verisiyo ya MPP (*Multi-Path Payments*) iteye imbere aho igice cose c’ukwishyura gifise ibanga ry’igice ryihariye, kigatuma igikorwa co gucuruza gishirwa mu buryo bw’atome, ni ukuvuga mu buryo bushitse canke butashizweho na gato.


MPPs ni ubuhinga bwo kwishura bukoreshwa kuri Lightning Network bushobora gutuma igikorwa gishobora gucagurwa mu bice bitobito vyinshi maze kigaca mu nzira zitandukanye. Mu yandi majambo, igice cose c’ukwishura gifata inzira itandukanye biciye ku rubuga. Ivyo bifasha guca mu nzira y’imipaka y’amahera ku muhora umwe mu gutanga amahera mu nzira nyinshi.


Mu MPP z’ishimikiro, igice cose c’ukwishyura gisangiye ibanga rimwe, rero Hash imwe mu HTLC. Ivyo bishobora gutuma iyo nzira ishobora gukurikirana, uwuyibona ari ku nzira nyinshi arashobora guhuza ayo mabanga yose asa. Vyongeye, kubera ko ibice vyose vyishingikiriza ku banga rimwe, si ivy’atome: ibice bimwebimwe vyoshobora kuroranirwa mu gihe ibindi bishobora kunanirwa, ivyo bikaba bituma umuntu atanga amahera adashitse.


AMP itorera umuti ivyo bibazo mu guha ibanga ry’igice ryihariye ku gice cose c’ukwishyura.  Ivyo bice vyose bimaze kwakirwa, biratuma uwuvyakira ashobora gusubira kwubaka ibanga ryose maze akasaba ko ariha. Ubwo buryo butuma guheba igice c’ivyo kwishura bidashoboka, kuko kugira amabanga yose y’igice bisabwa kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gufungura ivyo kwishura. Ivyo bituma **atomicity**: ukwishyura biheza bimwe bishitse canke bikananirwa vyose. AMP kandi iratuma umuntu agira ubuzima bwiwe bwite, kuko ata Hash ihuriyeko ihuza ibice mu nzira zitandukanye.


Ikindi ciza c’ama AMP ni uko uwurungika n’uwuyakira ari bo bonyene bakeneye gushigikira AMP; nodes zo hagati zifata izo nyishu nk'ama HTLC asanzwe kandi ziguma zitazi ko ari igice c'inyungu nini, y'ibice vyinshi.
