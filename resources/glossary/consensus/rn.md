---
term: UMWUMVIRO
---

Uburyo ama node yose yo mu rubuga rwa Bitcoin ashobora kwemeranya ku bijanye n'ingene Blockchain imeze. Uguhurizako gutuma abakoresha bose bafatanya n’amateka amwe y’ibikorwa vya Bitcoin, cane cane kugira ngo ntibakoreshe amahera kabiri. Uburyo bwo kwumvikana bwa Bitcoin rimwe na rimwe bwitwa "Umwumvikana wa Nakamoto". Ishingiye kuri Proof of Work kandi isobanura ko amanode yose y’uruja n’uruza yemera uruzitiro rufise igikorwa kinini cane cirundanijwe.


> ► *Mu kwagura, abantu bamwe bamwe bavuga ko amategeko atanditswe y'amasezerano ya Bitcoin ari "Uguhurizako".*
