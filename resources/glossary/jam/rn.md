---
term: JAM
---

JAM ni urubuga Interface rwa JoinMarket rwibanda ku kworoha gukoresha. JoinMarket ni ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa Chaumian CoinJoin, ariko ikoreshwa ryayo ry’umurongo w’amabwirizwa rishobora gutera ubwoba abakoresha batangura. JAM yaremewe cane cane kugira ngo ibintu vy’agaciro vya JoinMarket bishobore gushikirwa n’abatangura.
