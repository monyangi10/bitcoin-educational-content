---
term: Rust-lightning
definition: Ilibrary ya Lightning yakozwe muri Rust, ikora nk'ishingiro rya LDK.
---

Ico kibanza c’ibitabu c’umuravyo categuwe muri Rust n’umuryango wa Rust Bitcoin mu bufatanye na Square. Rust-Umuravyo itanga ugushirwa mu ngiro kw'Umuravyo. Ni co gikora nk'umushinge w'Igikoresho co Guteza imbere Umuravyo* (LDK).
