---
term: GUKURAMWO IBIBUMBANO VYA MBERE (IBD)
---

Yerekeza ku buryo urudodo rukura no kugenzura Blockchain mu gice ca Genesis, rugakorana n’izindi node ziri mu murongo wa Bitcoin. IBD itegerezwa gukorwa igihe hatanguzwa Full node nshasha. Mu ntango y’iyi nzira y’uguhuza, iyo node nta makuru ifise ku bijanye n’ibikorwa vya kera. Irakura rero igice cose mu zindi nzira ziri mu rubuga, ikagenzura ko ari ukuri, hanyuma ikayongera kuri verisiyo yayo yo mu karere ya Blockchain. Birabereye kumenya ko IBD ishobora kuba ndende kandi ikoresha amafaranga menshi kubera ubunini bugenda burakura bwa Blockchain n’umugwi wa UTXO. Uko bigenda vyihuta bivana n’ubushobozi bwa mudasobwa ifise iyo node, ubushobozi bwayo bwa RAM, umuvuduko w’igikoresho co kubika amakuru be n’uburebure bw’uruja n’uruza. Kugira ngo nkuhe iciyumviro, iyo ufise internet ikomeye, kandi iyo node ikaba iri kuri MacBook nshasha ifise RAM nyinshi, IBD izotwara amasaha makeyi gusa. Ku rundi ruhande, iyo ukoresheje mudasobwa ntoyi nka Raspberry Pi, IBD yoshobora gutwara indwi imwe canke irenga.


> ► *Mu gifaransa, muri rusangi vyemerwa ko umuntu yerekeza ku ndwara y’umutima (IBD). Impinduro rimwe na rimwe ikoreshwa ni "intango y'uguhuza", canke "ugushiramwo intango y'ibice".*
