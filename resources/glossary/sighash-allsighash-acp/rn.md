---
term: SIGHASH_ALL/SIGHASH_ACP

definition: Uburyo bwa SigHash butuma hongerwako ivyinjiye inyuma y'umukono wa mbere.
---

Ubwoko bw'Ibendera rya SigHash (`0x81`) rifatanijwe n'umuhinduzi w'ibara ry'umubiri (`SIGHASH_ACP`) rikoreshwa mu mikono y'ibikorwa vya Bitcoin. Iryo huriro rigaragaza ko umukono ukoreshwa ku bisohoka vyose kandi ku vyo kwinjiza gusa vy’ugucuruza. `SIGHASH_ALL | SIGHASH_ANYONECANPAY` iremesha abandi bari muri iyo nama kwongerako ibindi ku bijanye n'ugucuruza inyuma y'umukono wayo wa mbere. Ni ngirakamaro cane cane mu bikorwa vy’ubufatanye, nk’ugukorana n’abantu benshi, aho abatanga amahera batandukanye bashobora kwongerako ivyo bashiramwo mu gihe bazigama ubutungane bw’ivyo bashizeko vyatanzwe n’uwabanje gusinya.
