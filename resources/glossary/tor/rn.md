---
term: Tor (the onion router)
definition: Urusobe rw'ama server ruhisha amakuru y'aho unyuze (TCP) binyuze mu gupfunda amakuru incuro nyinshi, rukoreshwa na Bitcoin.
---

Urubuga rw’ibikoresho vy’ugutanga amakuru (nodes) bimenyesha inkomoko y’amahuza ya TCP kuri internet. Ikora mu gushiramwo amakuru mu bice vyinshi vy’ububiko. Buri nzira y’ugutanga amakuru ikuraho Layer kugira ngo yerekane Address y’inzira ikurikira, gushika iyo nzira ya nyuma ishitse. Urubuga rwa Tor ruratuma umuntu atamenyekana mu kubuza ko ama node yo hagati amenya aho ayo makuru akomoka n’aho aja, ivyo bikaba bituma bigorana cane ko uwuyibona akurikirana igikorwa c’uwukoresha. Igikoresho ca TOR gishobora gukoreshwa mu bijanye na Bitcoin kugira ngo umuntu yirinde gufatanya IP Address yiwe n’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin, gutyo umuntu yirinde gusohoka amakuru amwamwe y’ibanga.


> ► *“TOR” bisobanura “Umurongozi w’Igitunguru”. Ni ngombwa gutandukanya TOR urubuga, na TOR Browser, urubuga rushingiye kuri Firefox rwagenewe gukoresha urubuga rwa TOR.*
