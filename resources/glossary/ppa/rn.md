---
term: PPA
definition: Amasezerano y'igihe kirekire yo kugura umuyagankuba hagati y'inzu y'ubumina n'uwutanga inguvu.
---

Bihagarariye "Isezerano ryo kugura amashanyarazi". Ivyo ni Contract y’igihe kirekire hagati y’itongo rinini rya Bitcoin Mining n’uwutanga inguvu, ku bijanye no kugura amashanyarazi ataco akora ku giciro gitegekanijwe. Ubwo bwoko bwa Contract buratuma amatongo ya Mining ashobora kuronka ibiciro vy’inguvu bihoraho kandi bishobora guhangana n’ibindi mu myaka myinshi, bikagabanya ingaruka z’uguhinduka kw’isoko ry’amashanyarazi.
