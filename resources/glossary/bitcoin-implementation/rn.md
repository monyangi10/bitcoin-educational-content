---
term: Bitcoin GUSHIRA MU NGIRO
---

Yerekeza kuri porogarama ishitsa kandi ikurikije amategeko asobanuwe n’umurongo wa Bitcoin. Iyo abantu bavuze ngo “Bitcoin”, akenshi bavuga uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera y’ubuhinga bwa none ubwabwo, ni ukuvuga amategeko asobanura amategeko y’uguhurizako.

Igereranywa, mu bikorwa, n’ibihimba bikora urusobe rw’urunganwe. Iryo tegeko ubwaryo si kode yihariye ahubwo ni ihuriro ry’amategeko yemeranijwe cane ashirwa mu ngiro n’abakoresha biciye ku nzira zabo.

Mu buryo bunyuranye, ikintu gifise kode y’inkomoko nyayo ni abaguzi ba porogarama ya Bitcoin, bashira mu ngiro ayo mategeko. Ivyo ni ugushirwa mu ngiro kwigenga kw’umurongo wa Bitcoin ushobora gufatanya n’ibindi bice vy’urubuga rwa Bitcoin.


Mu gushirwa mu ngiro kwa Bitcoin, Bitcoin core ni yo ikoreshwa cane, ikaba iserukira hafi 99% vy’ibihimba vy’urubuga. Ivyiyumviro bike birahari, nka BTCsuite, Bcoin, na Bitcoin Knots. Naho hariho ubwo butandukanye, ubukuru bwa Bitcoin core burayiha ububasha hafi bwose ku kuntu amategeko ya Bitcoin akoreshwa. Ivyo bituma Bitcoin core akenshi ifatwa nk’iyiserukira umurongo wa Bitcoin ubwawo mu bikorwa.
