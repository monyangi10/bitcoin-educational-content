---
term: SIGNET

---

Verisiyo zidasanzwe z’urubuga rwa Bitcoin zagenewe gutegurwa no kugeragezwa. Ibimenyetso vyigana inyifato y’urubuga nyamukuru (mainnet) ariko bikaba bifise ubushobozi bwo kugenzura ibipimo bitandukanye. Gutyo biratuma umuntu ashobora kugerageza ibintu bishasha canke amahinduka ata ngorane yo guhungabanya urubuga nyamukuru kandi ata n’amahera yo kwishura. Ugereranije n’uruzitiro rw’igerageza, ibimenyetso bitanga ubugenzuzi butunganijwe cane ku bijanye n’uguhingura amabuye, akenshi burongorwa n’ikigo kimwe canke vyinshi vyizigiwe canke n’uburyo bwo kwumvikana busanzwe. Ivyo bituma habaho ibikorwa vy'igerageza bishobora gutegekanirwa, bitandukanye n'uruzitiro rw'igerageza rushobora gutegekanirwa mbere y'igihe rwa mining, mu buryo bumwe na mainnet.
