---
term: Ingorane
definition: Uburyo buhinduka bwerekana ugukomera kw'ugucukura, buhindurwa uko ibice 2016 bitowe.
---

Igiharuro gishobora guhindurwa kigaragaza uburemere bwa Proof of Work busabwa kugira ngo wongereko igice gishasha kuri Blockchain maze uronke impembo ijana. Iryo ngorane rigereranywa n’intumbero y’ingorane, agaciro k’ibice 256 gashinga urugero rwo hejuru Hash y’umutwe w’ibarabara itegerezwa gushikako kugira ngo ifatwe ko ari ngirakamaro. Intumbero ni uko Hash, ishikwako biciye mu gushitsa kabiri SHA256 (SHA256d), yoba nto canke ingana n’iyo ntumbero. Kugira ngo bashike kuri iyo Hash, abacukuzi bakoresha `Nonce` mu mutwe w'ibarabara. Ingorane irahindura buri mabuye y’umwaka wa 2016, canke hafi buri ndwi zibiri, kugira ngo igihe co kurema amabuye kibe nk’iminota 10.
