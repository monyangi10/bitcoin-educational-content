---
term: P2SH-P2WSH
---

P2SH-P2WSH bisobanura *Ishura Inyandiko Hash - Ishura Icabona Inyandiko Hash*. Ni urugero rw'inyandiko rusanzwe rukoreshwa mu gushinga ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha kuri UTXO, izwi kandi nka "Nested SegWit".


P2SH-P2WSH yashizweho n’ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa SegWit muri Myandagaro 2017. Iyi nyandiko idondora P2WSH izingiwe muri P2SH. Ifunga bitcoins ishingiye kuri Hash y’inyandiko. Itandukaniro na P2WSH ya kera ni uko inyandiko izingiwe muri `redeemscript` ya P2SH ya kera.


Iyi nyandiko yaremewe igihe SegWit yatanguzwa kugira ngo ishobore kwemerwa. Iremeza gukoresha iyo ngingo nshasha, mbere n'ibikorwa canke amasakoshi atarahuye na SegWit. Muri iki gihe rero, ntibigikenewe cane gukoresha ubwo bwoko bw'inyandiko za SegWit zizingirijwe, kuko amasakoshi menshi yashize mu ngiro SegWit y'akavukire. Aderesi za P2SH-P2WSH zandikwa hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwa `Base58Check` kandi zama zitangura na `3`, nk'ibindi vyose vya P2SH Address.
