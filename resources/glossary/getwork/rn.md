---
term: GUSHIRAHO AKAZI
---

GETWORK yari porotokole ya kera ya Mining ya Bitcoin yaremwe mu 2010 na m0mchil. Yatuma abacukuzi bashobora kuronka amakuru y’akazi avuye kuri Full node. Yari ishingiye ku bisabwa vya RPC vyatumye umuntu ashobora kuronka imitwe y'amabuye yo gukorako kugira ngo aronke Proof of Work ibereye. GETWORK yari yatunganijwe neza kuri GPU Mining. Ni yo yari porogarama ya mbere yuguruye yagenewe gutuma habaho uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’ibihimba vy’umubiri n’abacukuzi mu gihe abakinyi bakeyi bagumana iyo porogarama mu mwiherero. GETWORK yasubiriwe buhoro buhoro na Stratum, ikaba ikora neza cane cane ku ma ASIC, kandi idakoresha cane bandwidth.
