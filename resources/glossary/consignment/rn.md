---
term: Consignment
---

Nk’igice c’amasezerano ya RGB, amakuru y’imigwi ahindurwa hagati y’abafise *Client-side Validation*. Hariho ivyiciro bibiri nyamukuru vy’ibintu birungikwa:




- Contract Consignment: itangwa n'uwuyitanga, irimwo amakuru y'intango nka Schema, Genesis, Interface na Interface Implementation.
- Kwimurira Consignment: bitangwa n’uwuriha. Irimwo amateka yose y’ihinduka rya Leta rishika kuri Terminal Consignment (ni ukuvuga Leta ya nyuma yaronse n’uwuyitanga).


Ivyo bintu birungikwa ntivyandikwa ku mugaragaro muri Blockchain; birahanahana ata guca ku ruhande hagati y’ababijejwe biciye ku nzira y’itumanaho bahisemwo.
