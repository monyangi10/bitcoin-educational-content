---
term: Ordinals theory
definition: Uburyo bwo guha numero sats kugira ngo zishobore gukurikirwa kandi ntizishobore kugurana.
---

Itegeko ryo hanze kuri Bitcoin ritanga inomero z’urutonde kuri Sats (igice gitoyi kuruta ibindi vyose ca Bitcoin), bikaba bituma umuntu wese ashobora gukurikirana no guhererezwa biciye ku bikorwa vya Bitcoin. Dushingiye ku ciyumviro c’aba Ordinals, sat yose igira inomero mu buryo yacukuwemwo kandi igaca yimurirwa ku rugero rwa FIFO (*Uwambere-Mu-Uwambere-Asohotse*). Intumbero y'iyi nyigisho ni ugutuma Sats idashobora guhinduka hakurikijwe insobanuro yayo mu masezerano ya Ordinals, kugira ngo ifatanijwe n'amakuru yo hanze nk'amashusho (NFTs) yitwa "ivyanditswe".
