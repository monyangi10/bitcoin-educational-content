---
term: BIP0039
definition: Standard isobanura amajambo y'ubwenge 12 canke 24 yifashishwa mu kuzigama no kugarukana iwallet ya Bitcoin.
---

BIP39 izana uburyo bwo guhindura seed y’imburakimazi ya Wallet mu rutonde rw’amajambo yoroshe kwibuka, asomwa n’abantu azwi kw’izina ry’invugo ya Mnemonic.

Iyi mvugo, igizwe n'amajambo 12 canke 24, iremesha gusubiramwo imfunguruzo zose z'ibanga muri Wallet.

Aho gufata mu mutwe canke kubika igitabu gikomeye c’ubuhinga bwa none citwa seed, abakoresha barashobora gusubiza inyuma amahera yabo y’ama bitcoins bakoresheje urutonde rugufi gusa rw’amajambo.

BIP39 yarayoroheje cane uburongozi bwa Bitcoin Wallet.
