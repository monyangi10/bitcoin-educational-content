---
term: GUTANGA AMAFARANGA (AML)
---

Yerekeza ku rutonde rw’uburyo, amategeko n’ingingo ngenderwako bigamije gukingira gukoresha nabi amahera. Aya mategeko asaba ibigo vy’imari nk’ibibanza vya Bitcoin Exchange gukurikirana n’umwete ibikorwa vy’abaguzi bavyo, gukora igenzura ry’akaranga, kubungabunga amakuru, no kumenyesha abategetsi ibikorwa biteye amakenga.
