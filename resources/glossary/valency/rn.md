---
term: Valency
---

Mu masezerano ya RGB, Valency yerekana uburenganzira bwa bose budasaba ububiko butomoye bwa Leta, ariko bushobora gukoreshwa biciye kuri State Extension. Ubwo buryo bugira ubushobozi bwamenyeshejwe mbere mu nzira ya Contract, bushikira abakinyi bose canke abakinyi b’umwihariko, kandi bugatuma ukwongerwa kw’igihugu c’amasezerano gushobora gutangura iyo ivyangombwa vyasobanuwe bishitse. Valency rero itanga umuti ushobora guhinduka, ushobora guhindurwa wo kwitega amahinduka azoba muri kazoza kuri Contract.
