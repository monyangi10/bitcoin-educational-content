---
term: Valency
---

In the RGB protocol, a Valency designates a public right that does not require the explicit storage of a state, but can be subsequently activated via a State Extension. This mechanism constitutes a possibility previously declared in the contract logic, accessible to all or specific players, and enables a specific extension of the contractual state to be triggered when the defined conditions are met. Valency thus offers a flexible, scalable solution for anticipating future changes to the contract.
