---
term: BIP0152
---

BIP152, izwi kw’izina rya “Compact Block Relay,” ifise intumbero yo kugabanya uburebure bw’uruja n’uruza bukenewe kugira ngo amakuru arungikwe ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.

Yemejwe mu kwezi kwa 11 2016 na Bitcoin core verisiyo 0.13.0, iyi porotokole ishoboza guhanahana amakuru y’ibice, ishingiye ku ciyumviro c’uko ama node asanzwe afise amafaranga menshi y’ibice biherutse gucukurwa muri Mempool yabo.

Aho gutanga amakuru yose mu buryo bushitse, ivyo bikaba vyotuma habaho ugusubiranamo, BIP152 isaba ko borungika gusa ibimenyetso bigufi vy’amafaranga asanzwe azwi n’urunganwe, biherekejwe n’amafaranga makeyi yatoranijwe (cane cane Coinbase Transaction n’ayo node ishobora kuba itazi). Ico gice gishobora rero gusaba abo mu runganwe rwaco amafaranga yose abuze. Compact Block Relay rero igabanya igitigiri c’amakuru ahindurwa mu gihe c’ugukwiragiza amakuru, ivyo bikaba bigabanya uburemere bw’uruja n’uruza kandi bikaba bituma urusobe rwose rukora neza.
