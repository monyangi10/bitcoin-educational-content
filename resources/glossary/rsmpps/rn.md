---
term: RSMPPS
---

Inyuguti y'inyongera y'ikirundi risobanura "*Ivyishurwa vyinshi vyasangijwe vuba ku mugabane*". Ni uburyo bwo kubara impembo y’abacukuzi bo mu bidengeri vya Mining. RSMPPS isa na SMPPS, ariko ikaba ibanza guhabwa abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro baherutse gutanga intererano. Ubwo buryo bufise intumbero yo guhemba intererano ziriho ubu mu kwongera agaciro k’imigabane yashikirijwe mu bice vya Mining vyaherutse gukorwa, kugira ngo haboneke abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro baguma bakora kandi ari intahemuka.
