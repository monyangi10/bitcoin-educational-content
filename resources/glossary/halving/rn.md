---
term: Halving
---

Ijambo "Halving" ryerekeza ku kintu gitegekanijwe kigabanya igice c'impera ihabwa abacukuzi ku gice cose c'amabuye y'agaciro biciye mu nkunga y'amabuye y'agaciro. Ivyo bigabanywa vyerekeye canecane igice c’infashanyo y’amabuye igizwe n’ama bitcoins mashasha yaremwe avuye mu kirere gitoya. Halving yakozwe na Satoshi Nakamoto, umuremyi wa Bitcoin, nk’uburyo bwo kugenzura inflation no kumenya ko Supply y’ibiceri bifise aho igarukira.


Block reward ya mbere yari 50 bitcoins, kandi Halving iboneka buri mabuye 210.000 acukuwe, ivyo bikaba bifata nk’imyaka ine. Ivyo bice bibiri vyabaye:


- Ku bloc 210.000 ku wa 28 Ugushyingo 2012, kugira ngo infashanyo igabanuke ibe 25 BTC;
- Ku bloc 420.000 ku wa 9 Nyakanga 2016, kugira ngo infashanyo igabanuke ibe 12,5 BTC;
- Ku bloc 630.000 kw’igenekerezo rya 11 Rusama 2020, kugira ngo infashanyo igabanuke ibe 6,25 BTC;

**Ku bloc 840.000 ku wa 20 Ndamukiza 2024, kugira ngo infashanyo igabanuke ibe 3.125 BTC.**


Ivyiyumviro bizoguma bishika gushika Block reward ishitse kuri zero, aho rero Supply nkuru y’ama bitcoins nk’imiliyoni 21 izoba yashitse. Iyindi Bitcoin Halving itegerezwa kuba nko mu mpera z’umwaka w’2028, naho itariki nyayo yoshobora guhinduka gatoyi bivanye n’igihe ca Mining. Iciyo gihe, Block reward izogabanywa kuva ku 3.125 gushika ku 1.5625 bitcoins. Iyi izoba ari Halving ya gatanu mu mateka ya Bitcoin. Uko infashanyo z’amabuye zigabanuka, amafaranga y’ugucuruza araba isoko y’amahera y’agaciro ku bacukuzi, bikaba bituma bagira icipfuzo co kubandanya bagize uruhara muri Proof of Work.
