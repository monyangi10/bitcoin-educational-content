---
term: RPC (GUHAMAGARA UBURYO BWO KURE)
---

Itegeko ry’urudandazwa rituma porogarama ishobora gukora igikorwa ku yindi mudasobwa iri kure nk’aho yoba ikoreshwa aho hantu. By'umwihariko, mu bijanye na Bitcoin, ikoreshwa kugira ngo ibikorwa bishobore gukorana na bitcoind. Ishobora gukoreshwa mu gushitsa amabwirizwa ku nzira ya Bitcoin, nko kohereza amafaranga, gucunga amafaranga, canke kuronka amakuru kuri Blockchain. Umutekano w'iyi migenderanire ushikirizwa biciye mu kwemeza biciye mu dosiye `.cookie` canke ivyemezo, kugira ngo abaguzi bemerewe bonyene bashobore gukora RPCs ku nzira.


> ► *Mu congereza, rishobora guhindurwa ngo "Ihamagara ry'Ibikorwa vyo Kure".*
