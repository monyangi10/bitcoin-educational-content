---
term: GUI
---

Inyishu y'inyongera y'ikirundi risobanura "*Umukoresha w'ibishushanyo Interface*," canke "Umukoresha w'ibishushanyo Interface" mu gifaransa. Ni ubwoko bw'ukoresha Interface yemerera gukorana na porogaramu biciye mu gukorana n'amashusho Elements (amabuto, ibishushanyo, amashusho, amadirisho...) kandi ikunda gukoresha ibikoresho vyerekana (imbeba) ku mategeko y'inyandiko nk'uko biri kuri CLI.
