---
term: BIP0101
definition: Proposal to increase blocks to 8 MB integrated into Bitcoin XT in 2015. Never adopted, considered the start of the Blocksize War.
---

BIP101, introduced by Gavin Andresen in 2015, proposed increasing the maximum block size from 1 MB to 8 MB, and then doubling that limit every two years until reaching just over 8 GB by 2036.
This proposal was implemented in Bitcoin XT in August 2015, effectively creating a fork of Bitcoin. However, it was never widely adopted, and the proposal itself was ultimately abandoned.

Some consider the implementation of BIP101 in Bitcoin XT as the casus belli of the Blocksize War.
