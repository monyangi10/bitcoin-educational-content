---
term: OP_IFDUP (0X73)
definition: Opcode yigana ibiri k’inyuma ku mupfundikizo mu gihe gusa bitari zeru.
---

Isubiramwo ikintu co hejuru iyo kitari zero. Niba agaciro kari hejuru y'ikirundo ari ukuri (i.e., atari zero), ako gaciro karasubirwamwo ku kirundo; ahandi ho, ikirundo kiguma kitahindutse.
