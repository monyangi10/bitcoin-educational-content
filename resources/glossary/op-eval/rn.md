---
term: OP_EVAL

---

Opcode yashikirijwe na Gavin Andresen mu 2011. Ifata inyandiko iri hejuru y'ikirundo, ikayikora nk'aho yoba ari igice ca `scriptPubKey`, maze igashira igisubizo cayo ku kirundo. `OP_EVAL` yarahebwe kubera ivyiyumviro bijanye n'uburemere bw'iyi opcode, iyo opcode izosubirizwa na P2SH.
