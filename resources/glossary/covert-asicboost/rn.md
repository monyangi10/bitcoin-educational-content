---
term: ASICBOOST YIHISHYE
---

Ibanga ry’i AsicBoost. AsicBoost ni uburyo bwo gutuma Bitcoin Mining ikora neza. Mu nsiguro yayo ya Covert, abacukuzi bakoresha Merkle Tree aho gukoresha Nonce, gutyo bagabanura imibare ikenewe kuri buri SHA256 Hash mu kugumiza amakuru amwamwe atahindutse hagati y’ukugerageza gukora hashing. Udakunze verisiyo ya Overt ya AsicBoost, verisiyo ya Covert ihisha ikoreshwa rya AsicBoost mu gihe c’igikorwa ca Mining. Ariko kuva SegWit n’iyindi Merkle Tree yayo yashizweho, ubwo buryo ntibugira akamaro kanini, kuko umubare w’imibare isabwa kugira ngo ikoreshwe wacitse munini cane ugereranije n’uburyo bwa kera bwo gukoresha Mining.
