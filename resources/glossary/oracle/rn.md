---
term: Oracle

definition: Ahandi hantu hatanga amakuru yo mw'isi ya bose ku masezerano ya Bitcoin.
---

Inkomoko y'amakuru y'uwundi muntu itanga amakuru y'ukuri ashobora gusobanurwamwo kuri Bitcoin. Oracles zituma amasezerano y’ubwenge, nka DLCs, ashobora gushitsa amasezerano ashingiye ku bintu biva hanze. Muri rusangi, zitanga umukono wihariye uhuye n’ivyo ikintu kizoshikako. Iryo sinyatire rero rikoreshwa mu guheza no kwemeza igikorwa co gushitsa amahera yohereza ama bitcoins ku ruhande rwo kuyakira hakurikijwe ivyategekanijwe n’amasezerano y’ubwenge.
