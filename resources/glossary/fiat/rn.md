---
term: FIAT
---

Amafaranga, akenshi atangwa na Leta, agaciro kayo gashirwa mu ngiro n’ubutegetsi bwa Leta.


> ► *Ijambo "fiat" rimwe na rimwe rihindurwa ngo "fiduciaire", naho ijambo rya nyuma ridashiramwo umuce w'inguvu zibereye ijambo "fiat" ririmwo. Mu gifaransa, abantu benshi bemera gukoresha ijambo ry'icongereza "fiat" ataco rihinduye.*
