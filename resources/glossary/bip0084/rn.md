---
term: BIP0084
definition: Standard ya derivation ku ma adresse ya SegWit y'umwimerere (bc1q...), ikoresha index 84' mu nzira ya derivation.
---

Isobanura urugero rwo gukuraho aderesi za SegWit V0 (`bc1q...`) mu rwego rwo gutora no gukurikirana Wallet. Isobanura ikoreshwa ry'urutonde `84'` ku `intumbero` uburebure bwa HD Wallet ku bwoko bw'inyandiko P2WPHK.
