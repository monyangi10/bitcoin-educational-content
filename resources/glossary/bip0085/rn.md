---
term: BIP0085
definition: Uburyo bwemera kurema amajambo y'ubwenge menshi ku mawallet atandukanye bivuye kuri master seed imwe.
---

BIP85 itanga umuti wo guhuza ivy'amasakoshi menshi ya Bitcoin hakoreshejwe seed imwe.

Bishoboza gukura entropi kuva ku rufunguzo rw’umuzi ku generate amajambo menshi ya Mnemonic ku bikoresho bitandukanye, vyose ataco bihungabanya ku mutekano.

Intumbero ya BIP85 ni iyo kworohereza uburongozi no gucungera amafaranga menshi ya Bitcoin: aho gukingira amajambo menshi atandukanye ya Mnemonic, umuzi umwe seed urahagije kugira ngo uyakuremwo yose.
