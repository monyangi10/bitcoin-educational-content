---
term: INYANDIKO
---

Ururimi rwa porogarama rushingiye ku rutonde rukoreshwa mu gushinga ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha amahera, kandi gutyo, mu buryo butaziguye gukingira bitcoins. Script ni urutonde rw'amabwirizwa, rugizwe n'abakoresha n'amabwirizwa yo gukoresha ibirundo. Ivyo bigaragara biciye mu gukoresha ama opcodes atanga amabwirizwa yihariye ashirwa mu ngiro n’ibihimba kugira ngo hasuzumwe ko igikorwa gifise ukuri. Script ni ururimi rwuzuye rutari rwa Turing. Ishobora gushirwa mu rwego rw’ururimi rwo hagati (hafi y’urugero rwo hasi) rwahumekewe na Forth.
