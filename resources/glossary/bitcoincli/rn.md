---
term: bitcoin-cli
---

`bitcoin-cli`, mu buryo bugufi ni “Umurongo w'Itegeko rya Bitcoin Interface”, ni umurongo w'itegeko Interface wo gukorana n'inkuru ya Bitcoin core iriko irakora, cane cane daemon yayo, `bitcoind`. Ni porogaramu yigenga ituma abakoresha bashobora kohereza amabwirizwa yo kugenzura no kubaza uko `bitcoind` instance imeze. Uretse ubushobozi bwo gucunga urubuga, nk’ugukurikirana ibikorwa n’amabuye, `bitcoin-cli` iratanga kandi ibikorwa vya Wallet, bituma abakoresha bashobora gukora ibikorwa vya Bitcoin mu kohereza no kwakira amahera.
