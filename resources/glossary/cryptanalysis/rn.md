---
term: Cryptanalyse
definition: Iswirwa ry'ubuhinga bwa kime riraba ingene amayeri y'ukuhisha amakuru ashobora kugira intege nke.
---

Kwiga ubuhinga bw’imibare kugira ngo ugerageze guca ubuhinga bwo gukora amakuru y’ibanga. Ivyo birimwo n’imigenderanire yo kurondera amakosa canke intege nke mu gushirwa mu ngiro uburyo bwo gukora amakuru canke mu buryo bwo gukora amakuru ubwabwo.
