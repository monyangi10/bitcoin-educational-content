---
term: External chain
definition: Ishami rishira mu ngiro iremwa ry'ama-address yo kwakirirako amahera y'inyuma.
---

Mu gukuraho ama wallets deterministic n'ubukuru, uruzitiro rw'inyuma ni ishami ry'ugukuraho rikoreshwa kuri generate kwakira amaderesi agenewe kwakira amahera ava hanze, ni ukuvuga, avuye ku wundi Wallet. Inkuru yose, nk'uko isobanurwa mu burebure bwa 3, ifise iminyororo ibiri mu burebure bwa 4: urudodo rw'inyuma n'urudodo rw'imbere (na rwo nyene rwitwa "uguhinduka"). Uruhererekane rw'inyuma rukomoka ku rutonde rwa `/0/`. Uruzitiro rw'inyuma rukuramwo amaderesi agenewe kumenyeshwa abantu bose, ni ukuvuga amaderesi turonswa iyo dufyonda kuri buto ya "kwakira" muri porogarama yacu ya Wallet.
