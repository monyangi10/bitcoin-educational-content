Iryo shirahamwe rya Bitcoin rifise ingorane ikomeye: ryo gushobora gutera imbere. Naho igice nyamukuru (igice ca mbere) gitanga umutekano n’ugusenyura ubutegetsi bitagira uko bingana, gishobora gukora gusa umubare mutoyi w’ibikorwa ku segonda. Lightning Network yaje nk’umuti w’urugero rwa kabiri (urugero rwa 2) utanga umuhango, ushoboza kwishura vyihuta kandi bitazimvye. Ariko rero, Lightning irashiraho inzitizi zayo: uburongozi bw’imirongo, ugukenera amahera yinjira n’ubuhinga butoroshe bushobora gutuma abakoresha bashasha basubira inyuma.



Ivyo nivyo bishingiyeko **Ark**, porotokole nshasha y’urugero rwa 2 yagenewe gutanga ubumenyi bworoshe bw’abakoresha ata gutakaza ubusegaba. **ArkadeOS** (canke Arkade) ni ryo rya mbere rikomeye ry'iyi porotokole, ritanga uruvyaro rukurikira rwa Bitcoin.



Iyi nyigisho izoguyobora mw’isi ya Arkade. Tuzoca tubona ingene protocole ya Ark ikora, ingene yoshiramwo no gutunganya Arkade wallet, n’ingene yoyikoresha mu kohereza no kwakira ama bitcoins ubwo nyene, mu ibanga kandi ata ntambara isanzwe ya Lightning Network.



## Gutahura amasezerano y'Isandugu



Imbere yo kwisuka mu gukoresha Arkade, ni ngombwa gutahura ivyiyumviro nyamukuru vy’umurongo w’ubwato uyitwara. Ark si blockchain itandukanye, ariko ni uburyo bwo guhuza ibintu bufise ubwenge hejuru ya Bitcoin.



### Iciyumviro ca VTXO


Mu mutima w’Ubwato hari **VTXO** (UTXO y’Ivy’Imbuto). VTXO ni UTXO itarasohoka ku ruzitiro rwa Bitcoin: iriho hanze y’uruzitiro rwa mbere (off-chain) ariko ishigikiwe n’ibikorwa vyashizweko umukono mbere ku ruzitiro rwa blockchain.



Udakunze umubare uri ku giciro c’ubudandaji, VTXO ni iyawe vy’ukuri. Ufise ikimenyamenya c’ubuhinga bw’ibanga kigufasha, igihe cose, gusaba amafaranga y’ukuri ahuye n’ayo ku rubuga rwa blockchain, naho umukozi wa Ark yoba yazimiye. VTXOs zigufasha gutanga agaciro ubwo nyene hagati y'abakoresha utarindiriye kwemezwa.



### Uruhara rwa ASP (Umutanga Ibikorwa vy’Ubwato)


Itegeko ry’Ubwato rikora ku buryo bw’umukiriya-umukozi. Ico gikoresho citwa **ASP** (Umutanga Serivisi y’Ubwato). ASP irafise uruhara rw’umurongozi:




- Itanga amahera akenewe ku bijanye n’uruja n’uruza.
- Ihuza ibikorwa hagati y’abakoresha.
- Itunganya "imirongo" y'ugutura ku rubuga rwa blockchain.



Ni ngombwa cane kumenya ko ASP **ataco icungera**. Ntiyigera ifata imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga, eka mbere ntishobora kwiba amahera yawe. Uruhara rwayo ni urw’ubuhinga gusa n’urw’ivy’ubuhinga. Iyo ASP igenzura amafaranga yawe canke ikamanuka, urashobora kwama ugarura amahera yawe biciye mu buryo bwo gusohoka bushingiye ku ruhande rumwe.



### Ivyiyumviro n'ubuzima bwite


Amafaranga yo kuri Ark arangizwa mu bice vyitwa **Rounds**. Igihe cose (nk’akarorero, buri masegonda make), ASP ikoraniriza hamwe amafaranga yose ategerejwe maze akayashira ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin mu gikorwa kimwe gitunganijwe.


Ubu buryo buratanga ivyiza bibiri bihambaye:




- Scalability**: Igikorwa kimwe ca on-chain gishobora kwemeza ibihumbi vy’amahera off-chain yishuwe, bikagabanya cane igiciro ku bakoresha.
- Ibanga**: Igice cose gikora nk’igice ca **CoinJoin**. Amahera ava mu bari muri iyo nama bose avangwa mu kidengeri kimwe imbere y’uko asubira gutangwa mu buryo bw’ama VTXO mashasha. Ivyo bica bica ubucuti buri hagati y’uwurungitse n’uwuronka, bikaba bigoye cane, nimba bidashoboka, ko uwubibona wo hanze akurikirana amahera yishuwe.



## Iyerekanwa rya ArkadeOS



ArkadeOS ni porogaramu nyayo ituma umurongo wa Ark ushobora gukoreshwa na bose. Yateguwe na Ark Labs, ni urutonde rw’ibidukikije rwuzuye rugizwe n’ibikoresho (Wallet), umukozi (Operator) n’ibikoresho vy’abahinguzi.



Ku muntu akoresha, Arkade ifata uburyo bw’urubuga rwiza, rusanzwe wallet (PWA - Urubuga rw’Iterambere). Ihisha uburemere bw’ubuhinga bwa VTXOs n’ibizingiti inyuma y’ibarabara rimenyerewe. Na Arkade, urafise aderesi yo kwakira, ubuto bwo kohereza, n’amateka y’ibikorwa, nka wallet ya kera, ariko ufise ububasha bw’uguhita kwa Ark n’ibanga.

### Ihuzwa na ASP


Ku mburabuzi, igitabo kiratunganirizwa ubwaco kugira ngo gihure na Arkade Labs ASP yemewe. Ushobora kumenya server uhuyeko uja kuri **Ivyagezwe** > **Ivyerekeye** aho uzobona aderesi ya server (ubu `https://arkade.computer`).



Mu nsiguro y’ubu ya Arkade (Beta), ntibishoboka guhindura n’amaboko umukozi wa ASP. Iryo koraniro rihita rifatanya na Arkade Labs ASP yemewe. Mu gihe kizoza, abakoresha boshobora guhitamwo hagati ya ASP zitandukanye bivanye n’ivyo bakunda, ariko iyo nzira ntiraboneka.



### Gusubiza inyuma urufunguzo rwawe rw'ibanga


**Arkade ikoresha urufunguzo rw’ibanga mu buryo bwa Nostr (nsec) nk’uburyo bwo gusubiza inyuma no gusubizaho. Kugira ngo ushireho urufunguzo rwawe rw'ibanga :





- Genda kuri **Ivyagezwe** uvuye ku rubuga nyamukuru.
- Hitamwo **"Ububiko n'ubuzima bwite "**.
- Uzobona **urufunguzo rwawe rw'ibanga** rwerekanwa mu buryo bwa `nsec...`. Uyu murongo muremure w'inyuguti niwo buryo bwonyene bwo kugarura wallet yawe.
- Kanda **"KOPA NSEC KU RUBUGA "** kugira ngo ukope urufunguzo rwawe rw'ibanga.
- Urufunguzo urushire ahantu heza**: urushire ku mpapuro, urubike mu kibanza co gucungera ijambobanga ry’umutekano, canke ukoreshe ubundi buryo bwose bwo kuruzigama bugubereye.
- Arkade kandi itanga **"Gushoboza Nostr gusubiza inyuma "** uburyo. Ico gikoresho gikoresha umurongo wa Nostr (urubuga rwegerejwe) kugira ngo gisubire inyuma amakuru amwamwe ava kuri wallet yawe mu buryo bupfutse ku bikoresho vya Nostr. Ivyo bifasha gukorana neza hagati y’ibikoresho vyinshi kandi bikaba bitanga ugusubirana kworoshe kw’imimerere ya wallet yawe.



**Igihambaye**: Nostr backups ni **impumurizo** gusa. Ntizisubirira ububiko bw’urufunguzo rwawe rwa nsec. Nostr relays ntizitanga icemeza ko amakuru azobikwa ubudasiba. Urufunguzo rwawe rw’ibanga rwa nsec ruguma ari uburyo bwonyene bwo kugarura amahera yawe.

## Gukoresha Arkade



Umaze gushinga wallet yawe, uba witeguriye gutohoza ubushobozi bwa Arkade. Iryo koraniro ryakozwe kugira ngo rihuze ubwoko butandukanye bw’amahera y’ugutanga Bitcoin (Ku ruzitiro, Umuravyo, Ubwato) ata nkomanzi.



### Kwakira amafranga



Kugira ngo ushobore gutanga amahera y'ibikorwa vyawe, kanda **"Kwakira "**. Arkade itanga uburyo butatu bwo kwakira:





- Ark payment**: Nimba uwurungitse na we akoresha Arkade, usangire n’abandi aderesi yawe ya Ark kugira ngo ubone uko woherezwa mu kanya nk’ako gukubita, mu ibanga kandi hafi ku buntu.
- Ibiziga ku ruzitiro (Kwinjira mu ndege)**: Koresha aderesi ya Bitcoin (`bc1p...`) kugira ngo ubone ku wallet ya kera canke ku nzira y’uguhindura. Reka kwemezwe (~10 min) imbere y’uko amafaranga ahindurwamwo VTXOs.
- Guhindura umuravyo**: Gukora invoice y’umuravyo maze uyihe ukoresheje umuravyo wo hanze wallet. Amahera ashika ubwo nyene biciye ku guhinduranya amahera.



![receive amount](assets/fr/06.webp)



Igishushanyo c’inyandiko kigaragaza uburyo bwose bwo guhitamwo: kode ya QR, aderesi y’ubwato, aderesi ya Bitcoin (BIP21) n’inyandiko y’umuravyo. Ku bijanye n’ugutanga amahera y’umuravyo, guma ufunguye porogarama mu gihe c’ugucuruza.



![receive confirmation](assets/fr/07.webp)



### Kurungika amafranga



Kugira ngo wohereze amahera, kanda **"Ohereza "** hanyuma ushireko aderesi y'uwuzoyakira canke ushireko kode ya QR. Arkade ihita imenya ubwoko bw'amahera asabwa:





- Ark** kwishura: Kuri kuri aderesi ya Ark, ukwimurira ni ubwo nyene, ni ubwiwe kandi hafi y’ubuntu (0 SATS fee). Uwuronka iyo nkuru ntakeneye kuba ari kuri Internet.
- Ukwishura kw'umuravyo**: Scan invoice y'umuravyo (`lnbc...`) maze Arkade ihite ikora uguhindura. ASP irakwishura invoice kandi ikagukurako amahera yawe ya Arkade.
- Ukwishura ku ruzitiro**: Uja ku aderesi ya kera ya Bitcoin (`bc1q...` canke `bc1p...`), Arkade itangura "Igisubizo c'Ubufatanye" kizoshirwa mu rugendo rwa on-chain rukurikira.



Suzuma ido n'ido ku mugaragaro wa "Gushira umukono ku giciro", hanyuma wemeze ukoresheje **"KONDA GUSINYA "**.



![send payment](assets/fr/08.webp)



**Ikigereranyo c'ubu (Beta)**: VTXOs zaremwe amasaha atarenga 24 aheze ntizishobora gukoreshwa ku bisohoka vya on-chain. Niwahura n'ikosa, usabwe kurindira gushika VTXOs zawe "zikuze".



**Ibanga ry’isohoka rya on-chain**: Akarorero kari aha hepfo kerekana [Igikorwa c’isohoka ry’ubwato ku mempool.space] Turabona injiza igabanywa ku bisohoka 4 bitandukanye, nka CoinJoin. Ku muntu yihweza hanze, ntibishoboka kumenya umubare w’amahera ari uw’uwukoresha.



![transaction ark mempool](assets/fr/11.webp)



## Ibirango biteye imbere



### Ubuyobozi bw'iherezo rya VTXO


Ikintu kiranga ubuhinga bwa Ark protocol ni uko VTXOs zifise **ubuzima buke**. Uwo murongo w’igihe ni wo usanzwe uri mu gutegura iyo porotokole. Igihe co guhera gishobora gutunganirizwa na server ya ASP yose; ku ASP yemewe ya Arkade Labs, iki kiringo ni nk’**amayinga 4 (≈imisi 30)**.



**Iyi nzira ituma server ya Ark ishobora gucunga neza amafaranga no gusukura VTXOs ku bakoresha badakora. Inyuma y’aho igihe kirangiriye, umukozi wa Ark arashobora mu buryo bw’ubuhinga gusaba amahera asigaye mu giti ca VTXO.



**Kugira ngo VTXO zawe zigume zikora, zikeneye "gusubirwamwo" imbere y'uko zihera. Gusubiza ubuzima bushasha birimwo kwifatanya n'"urugendo" rushasha aho VTXO zawe zigiye guhera zihindurwa n'izindi VTXO nshasha zifise igihe gishasha c'uburenganzira bwo gukora (≈imisi 30 kuri Arkade Labs ASP).



Igikoresho ca Arkade kigenzura iyo nzira ubwaco: porogarama iguma ikurikirana uko VTXO zawe zimeze kandi ikazisubiramwo ubwazo imisi mikeyi imbere y’uko zihera. Igihe cose uzoba ufunguye porogarama yawe ubudasiba (nibura rimwe mu ndwi), VTXO zawe zizoguma zikora.



**Niwamara indwi zirenga 4 utafunguye portfolio yawe, ama VTXO yawe azohera. Ariko rero, ntutakaza amahera yawe: uguma ufise uburenganzira bwo kuyagarura biciye mu **gusohoka kw’uruhande rumwe** (raba igice gikurikira). Ubwo buryo burazimvye kandi buragenda buhoro, ariko buratuma amahera yawe aguma ashobora gusubirwamwo.



Ivy'ugufungura porogarama ubudasiba bituma Arkade iba **"Hot Wallet"** yagenewe gukoresha amahera ku musi ku musi, atari igisandugu co kuzigama igihe kirekire. Kugira ngo ubike ama bitcoins utayakoresheje igihe kirekire, ushiremwo igikoresho gikonje ca wallet.



**Suzuma uko VTXO zawe zimeze**: Ushobora gukurikirana uko VTXO zawe zimeze muri **Imiterere** > **Ivyiza**. Raba "Ivugurura rikurikira" kugira ngo ubone igihe ivugurura rikurikira rizobera, na "Ibiceri vy'ukuri" kugira ngo ubone urutonde rw'ido n'ido rw'ama VTXO yawe yose n'itariki azoherako.



![vtxo management](assets/fr/09.webp)



### Sortie Unilatérale (Isohoka ry'Uruhande rumwe)



Gusohoka ku ruhande rumwe ni **ingwati y’ishimikiro y’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru** y’amasezerano y’Isandugu ituma usubira kuronka amahera yawe, naho ASP yozimangana, igacengera amafaranga yawe canke ikanka gukorana. Mu buryo bw’ubuhinga, ama VTXO yawe ni **amasezerano ya Bitcoin yasinywe mbere** ari wewe ufise. Mu gihe c’ivyihutirwa vy’agahomerabunwa, urashobora gutangaza ivyo bikorwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin kugira ngo ubone amahera yawe ata n’umwe akurekuriye.



**Bigenda gute? Ivyo bigenda mu ntambwe zibiri. Ubwa mbere, **Unrolling**: utangaza mu buryo bukurikirana amafaranga yashizweko umukono imbere y'igihe agize VTXO zawe mu giti c'amafaranga. Hanyuma **Iherezo**: iyo timelocks ziheze (kenshi amasaha 24), utoragura ama bitcoins yawe kuri aderesi isanzwe.



**Ivyo Arkade iriko irakora ubu**: Muri verisiyo ya Beta, nta buto canke interface yoroshe y’ukoresha y’isohoka ry’uruhande rumwe irahari. Ivyo bikoresho ubu bisaba gukoresha Arkade SDK n’ubumenyi bw’ubuhinga bwo gukora porogarama za TypeScript.



**N'aho uburyo bwo gukora butashobora gushikwako umuntu akoresheje buto, garanti y'ubuhinga bwa cryptography irahari. VTXO zawe zirimwo amafaranga yashizweko umukono imbere y’igihe ariko ari ayawe mu buryo bubereye. Ni iyo garantie y’ubuhinga ituma Ark iba **protocole idashobora gucungera**: no mu bihe bibi cane, amahera yawe aguma ashobora gusubirwamwo mu buryo bw’ubuhinga. Igikoresho coroshe kizokwongerwako mu bindi bice vya Arkade.



## Inyungu n'aho bigarukira



Kugira ngo Arkade ishire mu kibanza kibereye, reka dufate incamake y’inkomezi zayo n’intege nke zayo ziriho ubu.



### Ibintu bihambaye




- Ubumenyi bw’Ukoresha (UX)**: Nta gucunga umurongo, ubushobozi bwinjira canke ububiko bw’imirongo bugoranye nk’uko biri kuri Lightning. Gusa n’uwushiremwo maze ukoreshe.
- Ubuzima bwite** : Ubwubatsi bwa CoinJoin busanzwe butanga urugero rwo hejuru cane rwo kutamenyekana kuruta ubucuruzi busanzwe bwa on-chain canke Lightning.
- Ugukorana neza**: Urihe kode iyo ari yo yose ya Bitcoin QR (Ku ruzitiro canke Umuravyo) ukoresheje interface imwe.



### Intambamyi




- Protocole y’abakiri bato**: Ubwato ni ubuhinga bushasha cane. Ibikoko bishobora kubaho. Ni vyiza ko udakoresha Isandugu kugira ngo ubike amahera yo gutakaza yoba ari ngombwa cane.
- ASP dependency**: Naho ataco ikora, iyo sisitemu yizigira ukuboneka kwa ASP kugira ngo ishobore guhinduka. Iyo ASP iri hanze y’umurongo, ntushobora gusubira gukorana ubwo nyene (ushobora gusohora amahera yawe ya on-chain gusa).
- Hot Wallet gusa** : Ivy’ugufungura porogaramu ubudasiba kugira ngo ushiremwo VTXOs ntibibereye kubika mu bibanza bikonje (Cold Storage).



## Igereranyo: Arkade n'umuravyo n'i Cashu



Kugira ngo dutahure neza aho Arkade ihagaze, reka tuyigereranye n’izindi nzira zibiri zihambaye zo gutera imbere.




| Ibipimo | Arkade (Ark) | Lightning Network | Cashu (E-cash) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Uburyo** | UTXO isangiwe ihagarariwe na seriveri (ASP) | Umuyoboro P2P w'inzira zo kwishura | Udutoke duhumye dusohorwa n'ibanki (Mint) |
| **Ububiko** | **Ububiko bwawe** (ufise imfunguruzo) | **Ububiko bwawe** (ufise imfunguruzo) | **Ububiko bwa Mint** (Mint ifise amahera) |
| **Ibanga** | **Riri hejuru** (CoinJoin karemano, amaso y'abantu ntabona) | **Riringaniye** (Inzira ya onion, ariko inzira ziragaragara) | **Riri hejuru cane** (Mint ubwayo ntibona) |
| **Ukwaguka** | Vyiza cane (Gukusanya on-chain binini) | Vyiza cane (Iyandika ry'amahera ritagira urugero off-chain) | Vyiza cane (Igikono ca seriveri coroshe) |
| **Ubukoreshi** | Vyoroheje (bimeze nk'agashakoshi on-chain) | Biragoye (gutubura inzira, amahera ahari) | Vyoroheje cane (nk'amahera y'intoke mu buhinga) |
| **Ingorane nkuru** | Ukuhaba kwa ASP & Guhera | Gutubura inzira & Kubika | Kwizera Mint (ishobora kwiba) |

**Arkade** ni co kintu ciza co gusenyera ku mugozi umwe: ukworoha n’ibanga rya Cashu, ariko n’ubusegaba (butagira ububiko) bwa Lightning.

## Iciyumviro gihambaye: Gukoresha muri Beta

## Iciyumviro



ArkadeOS igereranya iterambere rikomeye mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin. Mu gushitsa umurongo w’Isandugu, birerekana ko bishoboka guhuza ukworohereza gukoresha n’ingingo ngenderwako z’ishimikiro za Bitcoin: ntukizere, usuzume.



Naho ikiri mu ntango, Arkade itanga akarorero gashimishije k’ingene kazoza k’amahera y’i Bitcoin yoshobora gusa: ahita, ay’ibanga kandi ashobora gushikirwa na bose ata bisabwa vy’ubuhinga. Ni igikoresho ciza cane c’amahera ukoresha ku musi ku musi, kigashigikira umuti wawe wo kuzigama utekanye (Cold Wallet).



Turaguhimiriza ngo ugerageze Arkade n’amahera makeyi kugira ngo wivumbure iyo protocole nshasha. Ivyo bidukikije biriko biratera imbere cane, kandi Arkade ni yo iri imbere muri ivyo bishasha.
