**Hardware wallets** ni ibikoresho vy’umubiri vyakozwe gusa kugira ngo umuntu abike urufunguzo rw’ibanga rwa Bitcoin ata nkomanzi. Babika imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitari mu nzira, ivyo bisigura ko abasuma badashobora kuzishikira biciye kuri internet. Mu gihe **software wallets** zikoreshwa cane cane mu bikorwa vya misi yose, **hardware wallets** zikoreshwa kenshi mu kubika amafaranga menshi cane y’ama bitcoins mu gihe kirekire. Igihe ukora ibikorwa vya Bitcoin ukoresheje **hardware wallets**, wallet irashobora gusinya ibikorwa biri imbere mu gikoresho, rero urufunguzo rw’ibanga ntirwigera rugaragara mu bidukikije bihuye na internet.


Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwihweza kimwe mu bikoresho bikundwa cane wallets vyakozwe na Coinkite, Coldcard Mk4. Turaza kuraba ingene twoshiraho no gukoresha iyi hardware wallet kugira ngo dukore ibikorwa vya Bitcoin.


## Incamake y'ikarata ya Cold Mk4


Coldcard Mk4 ni igikoresho gikoreshwa na Bitcoin gusa gikozwe na Coinkite. Ico gikoresho gifise igicapo, igicapo c’imibare n’igipfukisho gikingira. Ikindi, iyo nkuru itanga uburyo bwinshi bwo gufatanya no gukorana, harimwo USB-C, gukoresha umuyaga hakoreshejwe ikarita ya MicroSD, NFC, n’uburyo bwa disiki y’ukuri. Mk4 kandi irimwo ibintu vy’umutekano biteye imbere nka BIP39 passphrase n’ama PIN y’ubuhendanyi, bikaba bituma abakoresha bashobora kugenzura no kurinda Bitcoin yabo.


## Gutegura mbere: PIN n'amajambo arwanya ubusuma


Kugira ngo utangure, Coldcard Mk4 ushobora kuyigura ataco uhinduye kuri [urubuga rwa Coinkite](https://iduka.coinkite.com/iduka). Abaguzi barashobora kandi guhitamwo kwishura bakoresheje amahera ya fiat canke Bitcoin. Ikindi, uzokenera kandi ikarita MicroSD (4 GB irahagije) n’isoko y’amashanyarazi ishobora gukoreshwa biciye ku nzira ya USB-C (ikarata Cold Mk4 ifise gusa icuma co kwinjiza amashanyarazi ca USB-C). Zirikana ko kuko Mk4 idafise bateri yubatswemwo, itegerezwa gufatanywa n’inkomoko y’amashanyarazi igihe cose iriko irakoreshwa.


Uzoronka Mk4 yawe mu musaho ushobora guhindurwa. Turagusavye urabe neza ko iyo saho itagira ico ikoze. Niwabona ikintu gishobora kuba ari ikibazo nk’ugusenyuka canke gutabuka ku musaho, urashobora kubimenyesha Coinkite mu kohereza ubutumwa kuri support@coinkite.com. Ikindi, urashobora kandi gusanga inomero y’imirongo 12 ku musaho ushobora guhindurwa, iyo tuzoyita inomero y’isaho ya Mk4. Iyi nomero y’isaho izokoreshwa mu nyuma kugira ngo hamenyekane ko ico gikoresho kitahinduwe mu gihe co kugirungika kandi ko kiva ataco kivuye kuri Coinkite. Igitigiri c’isaho kibikwa neza muri Coldcard’s secure element hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwa OTP (One-Time-Programmable), ivyo bikaba bisobanura ko kidashobora guhindurwa iyo kimaze gutegurwa. Iyo ufunguye ico gikoresho ku ncuro ya mbere, umubare ugaragazwa ku gicapo utegerezwa guhura n’uwo uri ku gafuko. Ivyo bituma Mk4 waronse ari igikoresho c’umwimerere kivuye mu ruganda kandi ko kitasubirijwe canke ngo gihindurwe. Naho iyo nzira yemeza gusa ubutungane bw’igikoresho igihe ca mbere gifungurwa, secure element irabandanya kurinda imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga, PIN, na passphrase, bikaba bigoye cane ko uwundi muntu yoshobora guhindura Bitcoin yawe. Ikindi, ingendo nziza, nk’ugukingira neza amakuru yawe ajanye na wallet, bizoba vyiza mu mutekano rusangi w’ikarata Cold ubwayo. Kugira ngo uronke ibindi bisobanuro, ushobora kuraba iyi [ngingo](https://blog.coinkite.com/gutahura-mk4-umutekano-akarorero/) isobanura neza umutekano wa Coldcard.


Ico gikoresho gifise amabuto 10 y’imibare, ubuto OK (`✓`), n’ubuto guhagarika (`✕`). Amabuto amwe amwe y'imibare arashobora kandi gukoreshwa mu kugendagenda: `5` kugira ngo ugende hejuru (`^`), `7` kugira ngo ugende ibubamfu (`<`), `8` kugira ngo ugende hasi `˅`, na `9` kugira ngo ugende iburyo (`>`).

1. Tegura urupapuro n'ikaramu.

2. Huza Mk4 n’umuyagankuba (umugozi wa USB-C) hanyuma ushiremwo ikarita ya MicroSD.

3. Igihe igikoresho kizoba carafunguwe ku ncuro ya mbere, igicapo kizokwerekana ubutumwa bwerekeye Amabwirizwa y’Ugurisha n’Ikoreshwa rya Coldcard. Numanuke, hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.

4. Hanyuma, umubare w’imirongo 12 uzokwerekanwa ku rubuga. Suzuma iyo nomero ugereranije n’iyo iri ku gafuko k’ibintu bishobora guhindurwa be n’iyindi kopi y’inomero y’umufuko yari mu gafuko k’ibintu bishobora guhindurwa kugira ngo umenye neza ko ico gikoresho kitahinduwe. Nimba imibare idahuye, bwira abafasha Coinkite ubwo nyene imbere y’uko ukomeza. Ahandiho, kanda `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.


![02](assets/en/02.webp)


5. Hitamwo `Hitamwo Kode ya PIN`.

6. Numanuke uko usoma amabwirizwa kugira ngo ugende ku ntambwe ikurikira.


![03](assets/en/03.webp)


7. Kuri Mk4, ureme kandi winjize PIN prefix (itegerezwa kuba ifise inyuguti 2 gushika kuri 6) hanyuma ukayindika, hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.

8. Andika amajambo abiri yerekanwa ku rubuga. Ayo ni yo majambo arwanya ubuhendanyi. Kanda `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.


![04](assets/en/04.webp)


9. Rema kandi winjize inyuma ya PIN (canke PIN isigaye, itegerezwa kuba ifise inyuguti 2 gushika kuri 6) hanyuma uyandike. Kanda `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.

10. Subira winjize PIN yawe. Kanda `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.


![05](assets/en/05.webp)


11. Suzuma nimba amajambo arwanya ubusuma ari amwe n’ayo wanditse ku ntambwe ya 8. Kanda `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.

12. Subira winjize inyuma ya PIN yawe (canke PIN isigaye). Kanda `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.


![06](assets/en/06.webp)


13. PIN ya Mk4 yawe n’amajambo arwanya ubuhendanyi ubu biraremwa neza kandi birabikwa n’ico gikoresho.


![07](assets/en/07.webp)


Zirikana ko Mk4 izokwama igusaba kwinjiza PIN yawe igihe cose uzoba ufunguye igikoresho cawe. Iyo udafise iyi PIN, ntushobora gushika ku ikarita yawe ya Coldcard Mk4. Rero urabe neza ko ukora ububiko buhagije bwa PIN n’amajambo arwanya ubuhendanyi.


## Gutegura Wallet yawe


Intambwe ikurikira ni ugushinga wallet yawe. Hari uburyo butatu wobigiramwo:


- Gukora wallet nshasha (itegeko)
- Gukora wallet nshasha n'imizingo y'amadayimoni
- Kuzana wallet


### Guhingura wallet nshasha (itegeko)


Kugira ngo ureme wallet nshasha, kora gusa intambwe zikurikira.


1. Hitamwo `Wallet nshasha` (canke `Amajambo y'Imbuto Nshasha`) > Hitamwo `Ijambo 12` canke `Ijambo 24 (risanzwe)` bivanye n'ivyo ukunda.


![08](assets/en/08.webp)


2. Ico gikoresho kizotanga amajambo 12 canke 24 nk’ayo seedphrase yawe ishingiye ku mahitamwo yawe. Nugende hasi uko wandika witonze ijambo ryose mu buryo bubereye. Hanyuma, ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.


https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

3. Ico gikoresho kizogusaba kugenzura seedphrase yawe mu kubaza the mu buryo butandukanye (nk’akarorero, `Ijambo 1 ni?`, hanyuma `Ijambo 5 ni?`, hanyuma `Ijambo 12 ni?`, n’ibindi) kandi hazoba amajambo atatu yo guhitamwo ku kibazo cose. Raba akajambo kavuye mu ntambwe ya 2 maze uhitemwo neza amajambo (ukanda `1`, `2` canke `3`, iryo ari ryo ryose rihuye n’ijambo ryiza) kugira ngo urangize irema rya wallet.


![09](assets/en/09.webp)


4. Mk4 izoca ibaza nimba ushaka gukoresha NFC/Tap canke atarivyo. Ubu, hitamwo `✕` kuri iyi nzira. Ivyo birashobora guhindurwa mu mirongo y’ibintu muri kazoza.

5. Ubwa nyuma, Mk4 izokora kandi iyo ushaka guhagarika USB Port (ishobora gukoreshwa mu gutanga amakuru atagira airgapped). Ubu, hitamwo `✓` kuri iyi nzira. Ivyo birashobora guhindurwa mu mirongo y’ibintu muri kazoza.

6. Ubu rero igicapo kizokwerekana urutonde nyamukuru rufise `Ready to Sign` hejuru. Ivyo bikaba ari ikimenyetso c’uko igikorwa co kurema wallet cararangiye.


![10](assets/en/10.webp)


### Guhingura wallet nshasha n'uruzitiro rw'amadayimoni


Canke, ushobora kandi guhitamwo gutanga seedphrase nshasha ifise entropie. Bikore nimba utizigira seedphrase ya Mk4 iherutse gusohoka.


Uburyo bwo kubikora kuri Coldcard Mk4 ni ubu:


1. Hitamwo `Wallet Nshasha` (canke `Amajambo Nshasha y'Imbuto`) > Hitamwo `Uruzitiro rw'Amajambo 12` canke `Uruzitiro rw'Amajambo 24` bivanye n'ivyo ukunda.

2. Uzosabwa kwinjiza ibivuye mu mirongo yawe y’amadayimoni. Buri ruzitiro rw’amadayimoni rwongerako ubuhinga bwo kurema wallet, bikaba vyerekana ko seedphrase yawe ivugwa mu buryo butekanye kandi butamenyekana. Igitigiri gitoyi c’imizingo ni 50 ku majambo 12 seedphrase na 99 ku majambo 24 seedphrase. Kanda `✓` umaze kwinjiza nibura agaciro k'uruzitiro rw'amadayimoni 99.


![11](assets/en/11.webp)


3. Ico gikoresho kizotanga amajambo 12 canke 24 nk’ayo seedphrase yawe ishingiye ku mahitamwo yawe. Nugende hasi uko wandika witonze ijambo ryose mu buryo bubereye. Hanyuma, ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.

4. Ico gikoresho kizogusaba kugenzura seedphrase yawe mu kubaza the mu buryo butandukanye (nk’akarorero, `Ijambo 1 ni?`, hanyuma `Ijambo 5 ni?`, hanyuma `Ijambo 12 ni?`, n’ibindi) kandi hazoba amajambo atatu yo guhitamwo ku kibazo cose. Raba akajambo kavuye mu ntambwe ya 3 maze uhitemwo neza amajambo (ukanda `1`, `2` canke `3`, iryo ari ryo ryose rihuye n’ijambo ryiza) kugira ngo urangize irema rya wallet.


![12](assets/en/12.webp)


5. Mk4 izoca ibaza nimba ushaka gukoresha NFC/Tap canke atarivyo. Ubu, hitamwo `✕` kuri iyi nzira. Ivyo birashobora guhindurwa mu mirongo y’ibintu muri kazoza.

6. Ubwa nyuma, Mk4 izokora kandi iyo ushaka guhagarika USB Port (ishobora gukoreshwa mu gutanga amakuru atagira airgapped). Ubu, hitamwo `✓` kuri iyi nzira. Ivyo birashobora guhindurwa mu mirongo y’ibintu muri kazoza.

7. Ubu igicapo kizokwerekana urutonde nyamukuru rufise `Ready to Sign` hejuru. Ivyo bikaba ari ikimenyetso c’uko igikorwa co kurema wallet carangiye.


![13](assets/en/13.webp)


### Kuzana wallet


Ihitamwo rya nyuma ni uko wozana wallet. Ivyo urashobora kubikora nimba ushaka gusubirana wallet ku seedphrase usanzwe ufise. Ushobora gukurikira izi ntambwe:


1. Hitamwo `Injiza Ibiriho`.

2. Hitamwo `Amajambo 24`, `Amajambo 18` canke `Amajambo 12`, bivanye n'igitigiri c'amajambo yo muri seedphrase yawe.


![14](assets/en/14.webp)


3. Coldcard Mk4 izoca ikubaza ijambo rimwe rimwe mu buryo bukurikirana. Ku jambo ryose, genda hasi canke hejuru gushika ubonye intango y’ijambo ryose ryo kwandika. Ico gikoresho kizogabanya ubushobozi gushika ubonye ijambo ryiza. Ivyo ubikore ku yandi majambo asigaye.

4. Ku jambo rya nyuma, Coldcard Mk4 izokwerekana gusa amajambo makeyi ashoboka. Nimba ata bihuye, ushobora kuba warashizemwo amajambo nabi. Ahandi ho, nuhitemwo ijambo rihuye n’iryo riri ku rupapuro rwawe rwa seedphrase.


![15](assets/en/15.webp)


5. Mk4 izoca ibaza nimba ushaka gukoresha NFC/Tap canke atarivyo. Ubu, hitamwo `✕` kuri iyi nzira. Ivyo birashobora guhindurwa mu mirongo y’ibintu muri kazoza.

6. Ubwa nyuma, Mk4 izokora kandi iyo ushaka guhagarika USB Port (ishobora gukoreshwa mu gutanga amakuru atagira airgapped). Ubu, hitamwo `✓` kuri iyi nzira. Ivyo birashobora guhindurwa mu mirongo y’ibintu muri kazoza.

7. Ubu igicapo kizokwerekana urutonde nyamukuru rufise `Ready to Sign` hejuru. Ivyo bikaba ari ikimenyetso c’uko igikorwa co kurema wallet kirangiye.


![16](assets/en/16.webp)


Zirikana ko seedphrase ariyo nzira yonyene yo gusubiza wallet yawe. Rema backup ya seedphrase yawe maze uyibike ahantu hatagira umutekano. **Si imfunguruzo zawe, si amafaranga yawe**, uwufise seedphrase yawe wese araronka amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins!


## Gutegura passphrase yawe


Kimwe mu vyiza vyo gukoresha muri Bitcoin ni ugukoresha passphrase. passphrase ikora nk’ijambo rya 13 canke rya 25 ryongeye kuri seedphrase. Igituma ritandukanye ni uko ushobora guhitamwo ijambo ryose ushaka, mu gihe seedphrase itoranywa mu rutonde rw’amajambo 2048 rwategekanijwe imbere y’igihe. Ku mburabuzi, umaze gushinga wallet yawe, uzotangura na wallet ifise passphrase idafise ikintu. Kugira ngo ushireho passphrase idafise ubusa, nukore gusa intambwe zikurikira:


1. Genda kuri `Ijambo ry'ibanga`.

2. Manuka usome insobanuro yerekeye passphrase, hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.

3. Hitamwo `Guhindura Invugo`.


![17](assets/en/17.webp)


4. Shiramwo passphrase yawe:


   - Kanda `1` (inyuguti), `2` (imibare) canke `3` (ibimenyetso) kugira uhitemwo ubwoko bw'inyuguti.
   - Kanda `4` kugira ngo uhindure hagati y'inyuguti nto n'inyuguti nini (ishobora gukoreshwa gusa igihe winjiza inyuguti).
   - Kugendagenda ukoresheje `^` canke `˅` kugira uhitemwo ikimenyetso ca passphrase yawe.
   - Kugendagenda ukoresheje `<` canke `>` kugira ngo ugende hagati y'inyuguti. Ushobora kandi gukoresha `>` kugira ngo wongereko imyanya.
   - Kanda `✕` kugira ngo usibe inyuguti.
   - Kanda `✓` iyo umaze guhindura passphrase.

5. Ikindi, izindi nzira zifise ibikorwa bikurikira:


   - `Ongera Ijambo` canke `Ongera Imibare` bishobora gukoreshwa mu kwongera inyuguti/imibare kuri passphrase uriko urahindura ubu.
   - Kanda `Clear ALL` kugira ngo usubiremwo passphrase uriko urahindura ubu.
   - Kanda `CANCEL` kugira ngo usubire ku rutonde rw'ibintu nyamukuru.

6. Andika passphrase yawe nk’igitabu c’inyuma.

7. Kanda `APPLY` kugira ngo ushikire wallet ukoresheje passphrase uherutse gushiramwo.

8. Mk4 izoca yerekana urutoke rw’urufunguzo rw’inyuguti 8. Ivyo bishobora gufatwa nk'"ID" ya wallet. Andika iki kimenyetso c'urutoke hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.


![18](assets/en/18.webp)


9. Ubu, wallet izokwerekana menu nyamukuru ya wallet iri kumwe na passphrase ufise input.

10. Ni vyiza kumenya ko wallet itazokubwira ko winjije passphrase idakwiriye, kuko passphrase yose ihuye na wallet yiwe bwite ifise akaranga kadasanzwe (urutoke rw’urufunguzo rwa mbere). Rero, ni akamenyero keza gusubira kwinjiza iyo passphrase nyene maze ukagenzura nimba itanga urutoke rwa wallet nyene, ukamenya neza ko wayinjije neza. Kugira ngo ubikore, nukore intambwe 11 gushika ku 14.

11. Ku rutonde nyamukuru, hitamwo `Subizaho Umukuru`, hanyuma ukande `✓`. Ubu warasubiye mu rutonde rwa wallet n'ubusa passphrase.


![19](assets/en/19.webp)


12. Subira kuri `Passphrase`, hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.

13. Subira winjize passphrase wanditse ku ntambwe ya 6, hanyuma ukande `APPLY`.

14. Suzuma urutoke rw’urufunguzo rwa master key rurerure rw’inyuguti 8 n’urwo wanditse ku ntambwe ya 8. Nimba izo ntoke zompi zidahuye, ushobora kuba waranditse inyuguti zidahuye. Ushobora guhitamwo passphrase nshasha aho gusubiramwo iyo nzira kuva ku ntambwe ya 1. Ariko iyo ibimenyetso vy’intoke vyose bihuye, bisigura ko winjije neza passphrase.

15. wallet iri kumwe na passphrase wahisemwo irateguwe gukoreshwa.


## Gurungika hanze Wallet mu Sparrow


Cokimwe n’ibindi bikoresho vy’ubuhinga wallet, Coldcard Mk4 ntishobora gukoreshwa yonyene. Ikeneye gufatanywa na porogarama wallet ikora nk’interface. Porogarama wallet iragufasha kubona amahera ufise, gukora amafaranga, no gucunga amaderesi, mu gihe Coldcard ishira umukono kuri ayo mafaranga ata nkomanzi ata n’umwe yigeze yerekana imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga.


Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokoresha Sparrow Wallet nk’ibarabara ry’ibarabara. Uburyo bwo gutanga wallet hanze ni ubu:


1. Urabe ko ufise ikarita ya MicroSD yinjijwe muri Mk4.

2. Kora intambwe zo "Gutegura passphrase yawe" kuri wallet ufise passphrase ushaka gutanga hanze. Niba ushaka kwohereza hanze wallet n'ubusa passphrase, urashobora gusimbuka iyi ntambwe.

3. Genda kuri `Ivyiza/Ibikoresho` > Hitamwo `Gusohora Wallet` > Hitamwo `JSON rusangi` > Numanuke uko usoma amabwirizwa, hanyuma ukande `✓`.


![20](assets/en/20.webp)


4. Mk4 ubu yakoze dosiye ifise uburyo bwa `.json` mu ikarita ya MicroSD.


![21](assets/en/21.webp)


5. Kura ikarita ya MicroSD kuri Coldcard maze uyishire mu gikoresho aho Sparrow Wallet ishizwe.

6. Gufungura Sparrow Wallet.

7. Fyonda kuri `Dosiye`


![22](assets/en/22.webp)


Ibikurikira, kanda kuri `Wallet nshasha`


![23](assets/en/23.webp)


Hanyuma, ushiremwo izina rya wallet


![24](assets/en/24.webp)


Inyuma y'ivyo, kanda kuri `Rema Wallet`


![25](assets/en/25.webp)


8. Hitamwo `Ubwoko bw'Inyandiko`.


![26](assets/en/26.webp)


9. Ku gice c'Ibubiko ry'Imfunguruzo, hitamwo `Hardware Wallet y'Ikirere`.


![27](assets/en/27.webp)


10. Rondera Coldcard hanyuma ukande `Dosiye yo kwinjiza...`.


![28](assets/en/28.webp)


11. Hitamwo dosiye yaremwe mu ntambwe ya 4 (iyo ifise uburyo `.json`).


![29](assets/en/29.webp)


12. Kuri Mk4, subira kuri menu nyamukuru maze ugende kuri `Ivyiza/Ibikoresho` > `Reba Identity`. Raba neza ko urutoke rwerekanwa ku gicapo ca Mk4 rujanye n’urwo kuri Sparrow Wallet (urutoke rwa Master ku gice ca Keystore)

13. Fyonda kuri buto ya `Apply` iri mu mfuruka iburyo hasi.


![30](assets/en/30.webp)


14. Ushobora kandi kwongerako ijambobanga rya wallet yoherejwe hanze. Iri jambobanga rikenewe igihe cose ufunguye porogarama ya Sparrow Wallet kugira ngo ushikire wallet. Niwaba wibagiwe ijambobanga muri kazoza, urashobora gusa gusubiramwo Intambwe 1-13 maze ugahitamwo ijambobanga rishasha.


![31](assets/en/31.webp)


15. Ubu wallet yarashoboye gushorwa hanze kandi irateguwe gukoreshwa.


![32](assets/en/32.webp)


## Kwakira bitcoin


Inyuma, tuzomenya ingene twokwakira Bitcoin dukoresheje Mk4. Ivyo bikorwa ni vyoroshe cane kuko ntukeneye gukoresha igikoresho ca Mk4 ubwaco. Igihe cose umaze kwohereza hanze wallet yawe muri Sparrow, ushobora kwakira Bitcoin biciye ku Sparrow Wallet. Kurikiza izi ntambwe kugira ngo uronke ama bitcoins na Mk4:


1. Gufungura Sparrow Wallet.

2. Hitamwo `Open Wallet` > Hitamwo dosiye ya wallet ushaka kwakira bitcoin > Shiramwo ijambobanga rijanye n'iyo wallet.


![33](assets/en/33.webp)


3. Ku rubuga rwa Sparrow, ukande ku rubuga rwa `Receive` ruri ibubamfu bw'urubuga.


![34](assets/en/34.webp)


4. Hejuru hazoboneka aderesi iri kumwe na kode ya QR. Ushobora gukopa no gushiramwo aderesi canke gucapura kode ya QR ukoresheje wallet uzokoresha kugira ngo wohereze bitcoin kuri Sparrow Wallet. Mu buryo bw’ubushake, ushobora kwandika ikimenyetso c’amahera ya bitcoin uronka.


![35](assets/en/35.webp)


5. Uhejeje kohereza ama bitcoins, ku rubuga rwa Sparrow, ukande ku rubuga rwa `Transactions` ruri ibubamfu bw’urubuga. Uzobona ikintu gishasha hejuru y’amateka y’ibikorwa, bihuye n’ibikorwa uherutse gukora.


![36](assets/en/36.webp)


6. Ushobora kandi guca ku rubuga rwa `UTXOs` ruri ibubamfu bw’ibarabara kugira ngo ubone bitcoin uherutse kwakira.


![37](assets/en/37.webp)


## Kurungika bitcoin


Mu buryo butandukanye n’ukwakira ama bitcoins, gukoresha ama bitcoins ajanye n’ikarita yawe ya Cold bisaba ko ukoresha ikarita ya Cold mu gusinya amafaranga. Uburyo bwo kohereza ama bitcoins na Mk4 ni ubu:


1. Shira ikarita ya MicroSD mu gikoresho aho Sparrow Wallet yawe ishizwe.

2. Gufungura Sparrow Wallet.

3. Hitamwo `Open Wallet` > Hitamwo dosiye ya wallet ushaka gukoresha kugira ngo wohereze bitcoins > Shiramwo ijambobanga rijanye n'iyo wallet.


![38](assets/en/38.webp)

## Kuvugurura porogaramu nshasha


### Gusuzuma verisiyo ya porogaramu yawe


Coldcard Mk4 ishobora kwama ivugururwa ikaja ku yindi nshasha. Kugira ngo umenye nimba Mk4 yawe yashizwe ku rugero rwa nyuma canke atarivyo, kora intambwe zikurikira:

1. Ushireko Mk4 yawe mu kuyifatanya n’inkomoko y’amashanyarazi.

2. Shira PIN yawe.

3. Genda kuri `Ivyiza/Ibikoresho` > Hitamwo `Kuvugurura Firmware` > Hitamwo `Kwerekana Verisiyo`.


![49](assets/en/49.webp)


Suzuma verisiyo yerekanwa ku rubuga rwa Mk4 n’iyo iri ku rubuga rwa [Coinkite](https://coldcard.com/downloads). Iyo verisiyo itandukanye, urashobora guhindura firmware ukayigira verisiyo nshasha.


![50](assets/en/50.webp)


### Kuvugurura porogaramu yawe


Niba ushaka kuvugurura porogaramu nshasha, kora intambwe zikurikira:


1. Shira ikarita ya MicroSD muri laptop/PC yawe.

2. Genda kuri [urubuga rwa Coinkite](https://coldcard.com/downloads) maze ushireko porogarama nshasha ku ikarita yawe ya MicroSD (Buto itukura iburyo bw’ishusho ya Mk4 iriko inomero ya verisiyo).


![51](assets/en/51.webp)


Ushobora kandi gukuraho izindi verisiyo ukanda kuri `All Files on Mk4` hanyuma ugaca urondera verisiyo ushaka gukuraho. Dosiye yavanwe izoba iri mu buryo bwa `.dfu`.


![52](assets/en/52.webp)


3. Kuraho ikarita ya MicroSD uyishire muri Mk4 yawe.

4. Ushireko Mk4 yawe mu kuyifatanya n’inkomoko y’amashanyarazi.

5. Shira PIN yawe.

6. Genda kuri `Ivyiza/Ibikoresho` > Hitamwo `Upgrade Firmware` > Hitamwo `Kuva kuri MicroSD` > Numanuke uko usoma amabwirizwa hanyuma ukande `✓`.


![53](assets/en/53.webp)


7. Hitamwo dosiye `.dfu` wakuye mu ntambwe ya 2.

8. Coldcard Mk4 izokwerekana ubutumwa buvuga ngo `Shiraho iyi porogaramu nshasha?`. Tera hasi uko usoma amabwirizwa hanyuma ukande `✓`.


![54](assets/en/54.webp)


9. Rindira ko Mk4 iheza gushiramwo firmware nshasha. Ntuce umuyagankuba mu gihe uriko urashiramwo.

10. Iyo irangije, Mk4 izosubira kwitangura. Ushobora kwinjiza PIN yawe maze ugakora intambwe zivuga ngo "Gusuzuma Verisiyo ya Firmware yawe" kugira ngo umenye nimba firmware yahinduwe canke atari yo.


## Ibirango biteye imbere


### Guhindura PIN yawe


Niba ushaka guhindura PIN yawe yo kwinjira, kora intambwe zikurikira:


1. Tegura ikaramu n'urupapuro.

2. Ushireko Mk4 yawe mu kuyifatanya n’inkomoko y’amashanyarazi.

3. Shira PIN yawe.

4. Genda kuri `Ivyagezwe` > Hitamwo `Ivyagezwe vyo kwinjira` > Hitamwo `Hindura PIN nyamukuru`

5. Nugende hasi uko usoma ubutumwa, hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.


![55](assets/en/55.webp)


6. Shira PIN yawe ya kera.

7. Shiramwo PIN yawe nshasha (itegerezwa kuba ifise inyuguti 2 gushika ku 6) hanyuma uyandike.

8. Mk4 ubu izokwerekana amajambo abiri mashasha arwanya ubuhendanyi, uyandike, hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ugende.

9. Shiramwo suffix yawe nshasha ya PIN (canke PIN isigaye, itegerezwa kuba ifise inyuguti 2 gushika kuri 6) hanyuma uyandike.


![56](assets/en/56.webp)


10. Subira winjize PIN yawe nshasha.

11. Suzuma nimba amajambo arwanya ubuhendanyi ahuye n'ayo wanditse mu ntambwe ya 8.

12. Subira winjize inyuma ya PIN yawe nshasha (canke PIN isigaye).


![57](assets/en/57.webp)


13. PIN yawe yarahinduwe neza.


### PINs z'ubuhendanyi - Wongereko ubuhendanyi bushasha


PIN y’ubuhendanyi ni kode ya PIN itandukanye n’iyo ukoresha mu gushinga Coldcard Mk4 yawe ku ncuro ya mbere. Iyo ufunguye Mk4 yawe, urashobora kwinjiza PIN(s) y’uruyeri aho kwinjiza PIN yawe nyamukuru kugira ngo ushireho ibikorwa bimwebimwe. Kugira ngo utunganye PIN y'uruyeri muri Mk4, ushobora gukora intambwe zikurikira:


1. Tegura ikaramu n'urupapuro.

2. Ushireko Mk4 yawe mu kuyifatanya n’inkomoko y’amashanyarazi.

3. Shiramwo PIN yawe.

4. Genda kuri `Ivyagezwe` > Hitamwo `Ivyagezwe vyo kwinjira` > Hitamwo `ama PINs y'ubuhendanyi` > Hitamwo `Ongera ubuhendanyi bushasha`.


![58](assets/en/58.webp)


5. Injiza PIN yawe y’uruyeri (itegerezwa kuba ifise inyuguti 2 gushika kuri 6) hanyuma uyandike.

6. Mk4 ubu izokwerekana amajambo abiri mashasha arwanya ubuhendanyi, uyandike, hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ugende.

7. Injiza suffix ya PIN yawe y’uruyeri (canke PIN isigaye, itegerezwa kuba ifise inyuguti 2 gushika kuri 6) hanyuma uyandike.


![59](assets/en/59.webp)


8. Ja hasi canke hejuru kugira uhitemwo igikorwa ushaka gufatanya na PIN y’uruyeri uherutse kurema. Urutonde rw'ibikorwa ni:


    - `Brick Self`, iyo utoye, ama chips ya Mk4 yawe azosambuka inyuma y'aho PIN yinjijwe, bitume Mk4 yawe idashobora gukoreshwa ubuziraherezo.
    - `Hunagura Imbuto`, ushobora guhitamwo hagati y'ibikorwa bikurikira:
      - `Guhanagura & Gusubira Gufungura`: seed irahanagurwa maze Coldcard izosubira gufungura inyuma y’aho PIN yinjijwe.
      - `Silent Wipe`: seed irahanagurwa mu gacerere, yamara Coldcard izokora nk'aho PIN yinjijwe nabi.
      - `Guhanagura -> Wallet`: seed irahanagurwa mu gacerere, kandi ikarita ya Cold izogutwara mu wallet y'agahato.
    - `Inguvu Wallet`, iyo uhisemwo, Mk4 yawe izojana ku nguvu wallet inyuma y'aho PIN yinjijwe.
    - `Ibara ry'injira`, ushobora guhitamwo hagati y'ibikorwa bikurikira:
      - `Wipe & Countdown`: seed ica ihanagurwa, hanyuma Mk4 itangure kwerekana igiharuro.
      - `Countdown & Brick`: Guharura biratangura maze Mk4 izokwigira itafari igihe kirangiye.
      - `Just Countdown`: Mk4 izotangura guharura kandi izosubira kwifungura igihe kirangiye.
    - `Look Blank`, iyo uhisemwo, inyuma y'aho PIN y'uruyeri yinjijwe, Coldcard ikora nk'aho seedphrase yahanaguwe, ariko mu vy'ukuri iracari mu ciyumviro.
    - `Just Reboot`, iyo itoranyijwe, Coldcard izosubira kwifungura inyuma y'aho PIN y'uruyeri yinjijwe.
    - `Delta Mode`, Iyi nzira iteye imbere igenewe abakoresha bazi utuntu n’utundi kandi igenewe gukingira ingorane zikomeye, nk’uguhatirwa n’umuntu afise ubumenyi bwo mu mutima. Iyo Delta Mode ikoreshejwe, COLDCARD isa n’ifungura wallet nyayo, igatuma uwuyitera ashobora gucapura no kwemeza ko isa n’iy’ukuri. Ariko rero, irabuza mu mpisho gusinyana amasezerano yose, rero nta bitcoin ishobora kwoherezwa. Irazibira kandi kuronka ijambo seed, kandi ukugerageza kwose kwo kuribona kuzorifuta burundu. Kugira ngo wallet y’ikinyoma isa n’iyikwemeza, Trick PIN ikoreshwa kuri Delta Mode itegerezwa gutangura n’imibare imwe na PIN nyayo (ni co gituma yerekana amajambo amwe arwanya ubuhendanyi) ariko igahera mu buryo butandukanye.
    - `Itegeko ryo Gufungura`, iyo ryatowe, Itegeko ryo Gukoresha Umuntu Umwe (SSSP) rizozimwa inyuma y'aho PIN y'uruyeri yinjijwe.
    - `Policy Unlock & Wipe`, iyo itoranyijwe, yigira nk'aho ipfutse SSSP ariko izohanagura seedphrase mu nzira.

9. Uhejeje guhitamwo igikorwa ushaka gufatanya na PIN y'uruyeri, wemeze ihitamwo ryawe ukanda `✓`. PIN yawe y'uruyeri yaratunganijwe neza.

10. Mu `Imitunganyirize` > `Imitunganyirize yo kwinjira` > `Imirongo y'ubuhendanyi`, ushobora kubona urutonde rw'ama PIN y'ubuhendanyi waremye n'ibikorwa bifatanye nayo. Ushobora guhitamwo gusubira gutunganya PINs z'uruyeri n'ibikorwa bifatanye navyo. Ushobora kandi kuyihisha canke kuyifuta uhisemwo PIN hanyuma ugahitamwo `Hide Trick` canke `Delete Trick`.


![60](assets/en/60.webp)


### PINs z'ubuhendanyi - Wongereko nimba ari bibi


Canke, ushobora kwongerako igikorwa ca `Add If Wrong` kizotangura inyuma y'aho PIN idakwiriye yinjijwe incuro zimwe zimwe. Ushobora gutunganya ibi ukoresheje intambwe zikurikira:


1. Ushireko Mk4 yawe mu kuyifatanya n’inkomoko y’amashanyarazi.

2. Shiramwo PIN yawe.

3. Genda kuri `Ivyagezwe` > Hitamwo `Ivyagezwe vy'Injira` > Hitamwo `Trick PINs` > Hitamwo `Ongera Niba Bitari vyo`.


![61](assets/en/61.webp)


4. Mk4 izokwerekana ubutumwa bujanye n’iyi setting. Numanuke uko usoma insobanuro, hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.

5. Shiramwo umubare w’ibigeragezo bitari vyo bisabwa kugira ngo igikorwa gitangurire. Iciyumviro: Igitigiri c'abagerageza ni `12`. Ivyo ni kubera ko Mk4 yakozwe ku buryo iyo PIN idakwiriye yinjijwe incuro `13`, igikoresho kizokwitera itafari, kigatuma kidashobora gukoreshwa ubuziraherezo. Uhejeje kwinjiza umubare, kanda `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze.

6. Nugende hejuru canke hasi kugira uhitemwo igikorwa. Ibikorwa biriho ni ibi bikurikira:


   - `Wipe, Stop`: seedphrase irafutwa maze igikoresho kigaragaza “Imbuto irafutwa, Hagarara.”
   - `Wipe & Reboot`: seedphrase irafutwa maze igikoresho kigasubira gukora ata butumwa na bumwe.
   - `Silent Wipe`: seedphrase irafutwa bucece kandi igikoresho kigira nk'aho cogerageza PIN mbi (nta butumwa bugaragara bwo guhanagura).
   - `Brick Self`: Ico gikoresho kirazimye ubudasiba kandi kigaragaza gusa “Bricked.”
   - `Igihe ca nyuma`: seedphrase irafutwa ariko ubona PIN imwe ya nyuma; winjize PIN idakwiriye kandi igikoresho kizoca gifata amatafari.
   - `Just Reboot`: Igikoresho kirasubira gukora gusa nta kindi gihinduka.

Hitamwo igikorwa ushaka gukoresha hanyuma ukande `✓` kugira ngo ukomeze


![62](assets/en/62.webp)


7. Uzogaruka mu bubiko bwa `Ivyagezwe > Ivyagezwe vyo kwinjira > Trick PINs`. Munsi ya `Trick PINs:`, uzosanga urutonde rw'ama pins y'ubuhendanyi hamwe n'igikorwa ca `WRONG PIN`. Ushobora kandi kuyihisha canke kuyifuta uhisemwo PIN hanyuma ugahitamwo `Hisha Ubuhendanyi` canke `Gukuraho Ubuhendanyi`.


![63](assets/en/63.webp)



## Iciyumviro


Iyi nyigisho yatanze ubuyobozi ku buryo bwo gushinga Mk4, ingene wokora amasezerano ya Bitcoin na Mk4 n’ingene wokoresha ibintu bimwe bimwe biteye imbere vya Mk4. Itanga uburyo butekanye kandi bushobora guhinduka bwo kubika no gucunga amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins. Igishushanyo caco gituma imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitazokwigera ziva muri ico gikoresho, mu gihe ibintu nka passphrases, PINs z’ubuhendanyi, n’ugucuruza mu kirere biha abakoresha ububasha bwose ku bijanye n’umutekano wabo. Ishobora gufatanywa na Sparrow Wallet kugira ngo ukoreshe neza mu kurema, gusinya, no gucunga amafaranga y’ibikorwa vya Bitcoin ataco bihinduye ku buzima bwite canke umutekano.


Niba wipfuza gutohoza ibindi bikoresho, ushobora kuraba inyandiko ku rubuga rwa Coinkite [hano](https://coldcard.com/docs/). Nizigiye ko uzosanga iyi nyigisho ari ngirakamaro igihe uriko urakoresha Coldcard Mk4 yawe. Murakoze kandi tuzobonana mu gihe kizoza!
