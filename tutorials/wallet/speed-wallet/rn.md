Speed ​​Wallet iraboneka nk’iporogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa ku mbuga za Android (Google Play Store) na iOS (Iduka rya Apple), ariko kandi nk’iyindi porogarama ya Chrome ishobora kugufasha gukora amafaranga ukoresheje mudasobwa yawe.

### Gukora ubucuruzi bwawe bwa mbere na Speed ​​Wallet



Niba usanzwe ufise ubumenyi ku bijanye n’amasakoshi ya Bitcoin, ntuzoba uvuye mu ntambwe na gato. Speed ​​Wallet iraguha Interface ishobora kugufasha gutangura.



Ku rupapuro rw'intango rwa **Wallet**, uzosanga :





- Igice c’amahera wipfuza gukoresha.





- Igiciro cawe kiri mu mafaranga wahisemwo.





- Ihitamwo ryo kurema no guhindura Lightning Address yawe kugira ngo uronke amahera ya Lightning.



![lightning-address](assets/fr/03.webp)



⚠️ **ICIBUKIRO**: Umuravyo Address ni izina ry'ibanga ry'imeli ry'umwihariko ryorosha gusoma URL yawe y'umuravyo. Raba neza ko utanga Lightning Address ibereye igihe wipfuza kwakira amahera ya Lightning.





- Kwakira amahera kuri Speed ​​Wallet:



Fyonda kuri buto ya **Receive**, hanyuma uhitemwo Layer wipfuza kwakirako n’amahera y’ugucuruza kugira ngo usangire Invoice yawe n’uwagurungikiye.



Ushobora kandi gukoresha gusa Lightning Address yawe igihe ushaka guha uwugurungikiye uburenganzira bwo gusobanura amahera y’ugucuruza.

- Wohereze amafaranga y'ibiceri n'**Umuvuduko Wallet**:



Kuva kuri buto ya **Kohereza**, ushobora kohereza amafaranga y'ibice ukoresheje umuravyo Address, umuravyo Invoice canke Bitcoin (Mainnet) Address y'uwakwakira.



Speed ​​Wallet izomenya, ishingiye ku buryo bwawe bwa Address, urubuga ikwiye gukorerako, biguhe ubumenyi bugenda neza.

### Gutahura ibindi vy'umuvuduko Wallet



Kimwe mu bintu vya mbere biranga Speed ​​Wallet ni uko igufasha kugura n’amahera ya Exchange utavuye muri porogarama. Muri ubwo buryo, urashobora kuronka bitcoins ku giciro c’iyindi mafaranga iri muri Wallet yawe. Nk’akarorero, urashobora kugura Bitcoin ukoresheje USDT usanzwe ufise muri Wallet yawe, n’amahera make ya Exchange.



Amahitamwo ya **Gugura** na **Guhindura** araguha Exchange bitcoins ku yandi mafaranga aboneka muri Speed.





- Gura Bitcoin n’ikarita yawe y’inguzanyo**: Speed ​​Wallet ituma vyoroha kuronka Bitcoin mu mafaranga ya fiat ukoresha buri musi. Irimwo igikoresho co kwishura kigufasha kwishura bitcoins ukoresheje ikarita yawe y’inguzanyo.



![credit-card-buy-btc](assets/fr/06.webp)





- Gura Bitcoin mu yandi mafaranga y’amahera**: Ushobora Exchange USDT yawe, USDC ku bitcoins muri Wallet yawe n’ibihushanye n’ivyo. Biciye muri iyo nzira, Speed ​​Wallet yorosha uburyo bwo kugura no kugurisha Bitcoin ataco yerekeza ku bikoresho vyo hanze vya Exchange. Ushobora rero gucuruza amahera makeyi nk’amasatoshi 20.000, nk’amadolari 20 ku giciro c’ubu, utasigaye mu modoka yawe yitwa Speed ​​Wallet.

## Umuvuduko Wallet wo kugurisha



Speed ​​Wallet ishimika cane ku gushiramwo no gukoresha Bitcoin n’ama stablecoins mu bucuruzi kw’isi yose.



Kuva kuri [Ubucuruzi bwihuta](https://www.tryspeed.com/), ufise umugwi w’amahera y’ubumwe kugira ngo wemere Bitcoin, ukoreshwa na Lightning Network ushobora gukoresha mw’iduka ryawe, kuri internet canke ku mubiri.



Mu vy’ukuri wibanda ku kwishura, uzosanga amahitamwo akurikira:





- Ukwishura kuri Internet**: Ukoresheje ubu buryo, urashobora kwemera Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura ibintu vyawe vy’ubuhinga bwa none, biciye ku mahuza yo kwishura, gutanga invoice no kwiyandikisha.





- Ivyishyurwa ku kibanza**: Kugira ngo utore ivyo wishyura mu iduka ryawe.





- Ivyishyurwa vuba**: Uburyo bushobora kugufasha gucungera ivyo kwishura, gukura amahera, amafaranga ukoresha n’ibipapuro vy’amahera y’abakozi bivuye kuri Speed ​​Business Interface.





- Ivyishyurwa ku mbuga**: Huza konti yawe ya Speed ​​Business n’ibikoresho vyo hanze ukoresha kugira ngo ukoreshe amahera no kwishura kuri izo mbuga.
