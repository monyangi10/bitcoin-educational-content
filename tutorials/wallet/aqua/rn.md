Aqua ni porogaramu yo kuri telefone ngendanwa ituma vyoroha gukora Hot Wallet ya Bitcoin na Liquid, kandi itanga ubushobozi bwo gukoresha Lightning ata ngorane zo gucunga urudodo, bivuye ku guhinduranya amakuru. Bituma kandi amafaranga y’USDT ashobora gucungirwa ku nzira zitandukanye.


Iyi porogarama ya Aqua yateguwe n’ishirahamwe JAN3 iyobowe na Samson Mow, mu ntango yari yateguwe cane cane ku bikenewe n’abakoresha bo muri Amerika Latini, naho nyene ibereye uwuyikoresha wese kw’isi yose. Birashimishije cane cane abatangura n’abakoresha Bitcoin ku musi ku musi ku bijanye n’amahera yabo.


Muri iyi nyigisho, turaza kumenya ingene twokoresha ibintu vyinshi vya Aqua. Ariko imbere y'uko tubikora, reka dufate umwanya wo gutahura ico Sidechain ari co kuri Bitcoin n'ingene Liquid ikora, kugira ngo dushobore gutahura neza agaciro ka Aqua.


![AQUA](assets/fr/01.webp)


## Sidechain ni iki?


Itegeko rya Bitcoin rifise imipaka y’ubuhinga ifasha mu kubungabunga ukwegereza urubuga no kumenya ko umutekano ukwiragizwa mu bakoresha bose. Ariko rero, ivyo bishobora rimwe na rimwe gutera ubwoba abakoresha, cane cane mu gihe c’uguhagarara kw’abantu kubera amafaranga menshi akoreshwa icarimwe. Impaka ku bijanye n’ubushobozi bwa Bitcoin zamaze igihe kirekire zica ibice abantu, cane cane mu gihe c’intambara ya Blocksize. Kuva muri iki gice, vyemewe cane mu muryango wa Bitcoin ko ubushobozi bwo gutera imbere butegerezwa gushikirizwa n’imiti ya off-chain, ku mirongo ya kabiri ya Layer. Ivyo bisubizo birimwo sidechains, zitarazwi cane kandi zikoreshwa bike ugereranije n’izindi nzira nka Lightning Network.


Sidechain ni Blockchain yigenga ikorana n'iyindi Bitcoin Blockchain. Ikoresha Bitcoin nk'igice ca konti, bivuye ku buryo bwitwa "*two-way peg*". Uwo murongo utuma bishoboka gufunga ama bitcoins ku ruzitiro rukuru kugira ngo asubire gutanga agaciro kayo kuri Sidechain, aho agendagenda mu buryo bw’ibimenyetso bishigikiwe n’ama bitcoins y’intango. Ivyo bimenyetso mu bisanzwe bigumya agaciro kamwe n’ibice vy’amahera bifungiwe ku ruzitiro rukuru, kandi iyo nzira ishobora gusubirwamwo kugira ngo umuntu asubire kuronka amahera kuri Bitcoin.


Intumbero y’ama sidechains ni ugutanga ibindi bikoresho canke ivyiza vy’ubuhinga, nk’ugucuruza vyihuta, amafaranga make canke gushigikira amasezerano y’ubwenge. Ivyo bishasha ntibishobora kwama bishirwa mu ngiro ataco bimaze kuri Bitcoin Blockchain ataco bihinduye ku kwegereza ubutegetsi canke ku mutekano wayo. Sidechains rero zituma bishoboka ko umuntu agerageza kandi akarondera inyishu nshasha mu gihe azigama ubutungane bwa Bitcoin. Ariko rero, ayo masezerano akenshi asaba ko haba ugusenyera ku mugozi umwe, cane cane mu bijanye no kwegereza ubutegetsi abaturage n’umutekano, bivanye n’akarorero k’ubutegetsi n’uburyo bwo kwumvikana bwatowe.


## Liquid ni iki?


Liquid ni umugwi wa Sidechain ushizweko Bitcoin, wateguwe na Blockstream kugira ngo utere imbere mu bijanye n’ugucuruza, ubuzima bwite n’imikorere. Ikoresha uburyo bwo gushingira intahe ibihugu bibiri bwashinzwe ku rwego rwo gufunga ama bitcoins ku ruzitiro rukuru no kurema Liquid-bitcoins (L-BTC) mu gusubiza, ibimenyetso bigendagenda kuri Liquid mu gihe biguma bishigikiwe n’ama bitcoins y’intango.


![AQUA](assets/fr/02.webp)


Liquid Network yizigiye ishirahamwe ry’abaje mu nama, rigizwe n’inzego zemewe zivuye mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin, zishingira intahe amabuye kandi zigacungera ugutera imbere kw’ibihugu bibiri. Uretse L-BTC, Liquid irashoboza kandi gutanga ibindi bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none, nk’amahera y’ubuhinga bwa none (USDT stablecoin) n’ayandi mahera y’ubuhinga bwa none.


![AQUA](assets/fr/03.webp)

## Rema Wallet yawe kuri Aqua


Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Rema Wallet*".


![AQUA](assets/fr/07.webp)


Kandi voilà, Wallet yawe iramaze kuremwa!


![AQUA](assets/fr/08.webp)


Ariko imbere ya vyose, kubera ko iyi ari Wallet yo kwikingira, ni ngombwa ko ukora ububiko bw’umubiri bwa Mnemonic yawe. **Iyi Mnemonic iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe yose y’ama bitcoins**. Umuntu wese afise iyo Mnemonic arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere n’aho atashobora gushika kuri telefone yawe.


Iragufasha kugarura uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ama bitcoins yawe iyo telefone yawe itakaye, yibwe canke ivunitse. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko umuntu ayibika neza ku gikoresho c’umubiri (atari ku bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none) akayibika ahantu hatagira uwuyibika. Ushobora kuvyandika ku mpapuro, canke kugira ngo urushirizeho umutekano, nimba ari Wallet nini, nogusaba ko woyicapura ku gifadika c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo uyikingire ingorane z’umuriro, umwuzure canke gusenyuka (ku Hot Wallet yagenewe gukingira umubare mutoyi w’inyuma, bibabco suffit of).


Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku rutonde rw’Ivyagezwe.


![AQUA](assets/fr/09.webp)


Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Raba Invugo ya seed*". Kora igitabu c’umubiri c’iri jambo ry’amajambo 12.


![AQUA](assets/fr/10.webp)


Muri iyo nzira nyene, urashobora kandi guhindura ururimi rw’ikoreshwa n’amahera ya fiat akoreshwa.


![AQUA](assets/fr/11.webp)


Imbere y’uko uronka ama bitcoins yawe ya mbere muri Wallet yawe, **Ndaguhanura cane gukora ikigeragezo co gusubirana ubusa**. Ushire ahabona amakuru amwamwe y’ishingiro, nka xpub yawe canke ubanza kwakira Address, hanyuma usibe Wallet yawe kuri app ya Aqua igihe ikiri ubusa. Hanyuma ugerageze gusubiza Wallet yawe kuri Aqua ukoresheje impapuro zawe zo gusubiza inyuma. Suzuma ko amakuru y’ibifungurwa vyasohowe inyuma yo gusubizaho ahuye n’ayo wanditse mu ntango. Nivyashika, urashobora kwizigira yuko amakete yawe y’impapuro ari ay’ukwizigirwa. Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwo gukora ikigeragezo co gukira, usabwe kuraba iyi yindi nyigisho:


https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/backup/recovery-test-5a75db51-a6a1-4338-a02a-164a8d91b895

Ntushobora kuyibona ku rubuga rwanje kuko ndiko nkoresha emulateur, ariko mu ma settings, uzosanga hariho uburyo bwo gufunga app ukoresheje ubuhinga bwo kwemeza ko ari umuntu. Ndagusavye cane gukoresha iki gikoresho c’umutekano kuko, ataco, umuntu wese afise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha telefone yawe ifunguye yoshobora kwiba amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri. Ushobora gukoresha Face ID kuri iOS canke urutoke rwawe kuri Android. Iyo ubwo buryo butashobotse mu gihe co kwemeza, urashobora gukoresha porogarama ukoresheje kode ya PIN ya telefone yawe.


## Kwakira amafaranga y'ibiceri kuri Aqua


None ko Wallet yawe yashizweho, urateguriye kwakira Sats yawe ya mbere! Fyonda gusa ku buto bwa "*Kwakira*" buri muri "*Wallet*".


![AQUA](assets/fr/12.webp)


Ushobora guhitamwo kwakira amafaranga y’ibiceri ku ruzitiro, kuri Liquid, canke biciye ku Muravyo.


![AQUA](assets/fr/13.webp)


Ku bikorwa vy'uruhererekane, Aqua izo generate Address aho ushobora kwakira Sats yawe.


![AQUA](assets/fr/14.webp)


Na wewe nyene, mu guhitamwo Liquid, Aqua izoguha Liquid Address.


![AQUA](assets/fr/15.webp)


Niba ushaka kwakira amahera biciye ku Lightning, uzobanza kuvuga amahera wipfuza.


![AQUA](assets/fr/16.webp)


Hanyuma ukande kuri "*generate Invoice*".


![AQUA](assets/fr/17.webp)


Aqua izokora Invoice kugira ngo ironke amahera ava ku Wallet y'umuravyo. Urashobora kubona ko, bitandukanye n’amahitamwo ya onchain na Liquid, amahera yaronse biciye ku Muravyo azohindurwa ubwo nyene abe L-BTC kuri Liquid hakoreshejwe igikoresho ca Boltz, kuko Aqua atari urudodo rwa Lightning. Iyi nzira ituma ushobora kwakira no kohereza amahera biciye ku Lightning, ariko utarigeze ubika ama bitcoins yawe kuri Lightning.


![AQUA](assets/fr/18.webp)


Ku bwanje, ngiye gutangura kohereza ama bitcoins biciye ku muravyo kuri Aqua. Igihe igikorwa co gucuruza kirangiye hakoreshejwe Invoice yatanzwe, turaronka ivyemezo.


![AQUA](assets/fr/19.webp)


Kugira ngo ukurikirane ingene iyo swap igenda, subira kuri paji y'intango ya Wallet yawe maze ukande kuri konti ya "*L2 Bitcoin*", irimwo urutonde rw'ibikorwa vya Lightning (biciye mu guhindura) na Liquid.


![AQUA](assets/fr/20.webp)


Aha ushobora kubona amafaranga yawe n’amahera yawe ya L-BTC.


![AQUA](assets/fr/21.webp)

## Wohereze amafaranga y'ibiceri na Aqua


None ko ufise ama bitcoins muri Aqua Wallet yawe, urashobora kuyarungika. Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Ohereza*".


![AQUA](assets/fr/31.webp)


Hitamwo umutungo ushaka kohereza canke uhitemwo urubuga rwo gukora igikorwa. Ku ruhande rwanje, ngiye kohereza ama bitcoins biciye ku Muravyo.


![AQUA](assets/fr/32.webp)


Inyuma y’aho, shiramwo amakuru akenewe kugira ngo wohereze amahera: ku ma bitcoins ya onchain canke Liquid, uzokenera kwinjiza Address yakira; ku Muravyo, bisaba Invoice. Ushobora gushiramwo ayo makuru mu kibanza catanzwe, canke ukoreshe ikimenyetso ca kode ya QR kugira ngo ufungure kamera yawe maze ushireko Address canke Invoice. Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya*".


![AQUA](assets/fr/33.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Bandanya*" kandi nimba amakuru yose asa n'ay'ukuri.


![AQUA](assets/fr/34.webp)


Aqua rero ikaguha incamake y’ivyo mwaguze. Raba neza ko amakuru yose ari ukuri, harimwo n’aho uja Address, amafaranga n’amahera. Kugira ngo wemeze ko ukoresheje, shira buto ya "*Slide to send*" iri musi y'ibarabara.


![AQUA](assets/fr/35.webp)


Uzoheza uronke icemezo c’uko ivyo bintu vyarungitswe.


![AQUA](assets/fr/36.webp)


Ubu rero urazi gukoresha app ya Aqua kugira ngo uronke no gukoresha amahera kuri Bitcoin, Lightning na Liquid, vyose biva kuri Interface imwe.
