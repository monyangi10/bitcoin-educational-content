Isi yose, ingamba, imiryango n’ubutunzi buzunguruka biriko biraduga hirya no hino ya Bitcoin kugira ngo bitorere umuti ingorane zidasanzwe zishikirwa n’abanyagihugu. Muri iyi nyigisho, turaza kuraba imwe muri izo nzira, Fedi Wallet, itanga atari Wallet gusa, ariko n’ibidukikije vyose bishobora guhindurwa ku kibano cawe.



## Gutangura na Fedi Wallet.



Fedi Wallet ni porogaramu yo kuri telefone ngendanwa iboneka kuri Android (Google Play Store) na iOS (Iduka rya Apple), ihuriza hamwe ibidukikije vyose bishobora guhindurwa kugira ngo bihure n’ivyo abantu bo mu kibano cawe bakeneye.



⚠️ Ni ngombwa gukura no gushiramwo Fedi Wallet ku rubuga rwemewe kugira ngo umenye neza ko iyo porogarama ari iyo kwizigirwa kandi ari iyo ukuri.



![download](assets/fr/01.webp)

### Gucuruza kuri Fedi



Bivanye n’imiterere y’ishirahamwe urimwo, ushobora kugira amategeko ku bijanye n’uburinganire n’ugukoresha amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri. Nkako, ishirahamwe ryose riri kuri Fedi rifise uburinganire n’amahera ukoresha ushobora gusanga kuri profile yawe.



Mu **Ishirahamwe**, genda hasi ku ishirahamwe, hanyuma ukande kuri **Ibisobanuro vy'Ishirahamwe** kugira umenye ibipimo vyashizweho.



![details](assets/fr/04.webp)





- Kwakira ama bitcoins kuri **Fedi**: Kuri paji y’intango, hitamwo ishirahamwe wipfuza gukoresha kugira ngo wakire ama bitcoins, hanyuma ukande kuri buto **Yakira** kugira ngo ureme Lightning Invoice ifise amahera uzokwakira.



![receive](assets/fr/05.webp)





- Wohereze ama bitcoins**: Kuri paji y’intango, fyonda ku buto **Kohereza** kugira ngo wohereze ama bitcoins ku Address ya Lightning, kugira ngo wishyure Invoice canke wishyure utari mu nzira.



Kimwe mu bintu bidasanzwe vy’iyi Fedi Wallet ni uko ishobora kwoherezwa ata n’imwe iri ku rubuga. Ntugikeneye Wi-Fi canke internet nziza, urashobora gukoresha ama bitcoins igihe cose, aho hose.



Mu kwishura ataco ukoresheje, urashobora gukoresha amafaranga y’ibiceri (bitcoins) mu gisata cawe, mu gihe utera imbere mu bijanye n’ubuzima bwite bwawe n’ubw’imigenderanire yawe y’ivy’amahera.



Fyonda kuri **Send offline** hanyuma ushireho umubare w'ama satoshis wipfuza kohereza.



![send-offline](assets/fr/06.webp)



Uwuzokwakira azokenera gucapura kode ya QR yashizweho kugira ngo asabe iyo satoshis.



![claim](assets/fr/07.webp)



Ukwishura hanze y’ingurukanabumenyi ahanini bikorwa hakoreshejwe [e-cash](https://planb.urubuga/ibikoresho/urutonde rw’amajambo/e-cash-david-chaum). Ivyo ugurisha bibikwa muri telefone yawe, kandi ukimara kwinjira kuri Internet, kwemeza ivyo ugurisha biraca bihita. Ushobora kandi kwemeza ko wishyuye n'amaboko ukanda kuri **Wemeza ko wishuye**.



![confirm](assets/fr/08.webp)



## Exchange kuri Fedi



Fedi ifise ubutumwa bwo guca bugufi mu **Chat**, bugufasha kuvugana, kwishura no gusangira ivy’amakuru n’abandi bakoresha Fedi, bo mu kibano cawe canke hanze yaco.



Kugira ngo utangure ikiyago n’ukoresha Fedi, shiramwo urupapuro rwiwe rwo kwinjira canke ushiremwo kode ya QR iri ku rubuga rwiwe.



![chat](assets/fr/09.webp)



Ushobora kohereza satoshis kuri uwo mukoresha utavuye mu kiyago cawe ukanda ku kimenyetso ca **Wallet** maze ugashinga igitigiri c'ama satoshis.



![pay-chat](assets/fr/10.webp)
