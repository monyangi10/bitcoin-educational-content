![cover](assets/cover.webp)


Iyi pasiporo ni Hardware Wallet ikoresha Bitcoin gusa, yakozwe n’ishirahamwe ry’abanyamerika ryitwa Foundation Devices, ryashinzwe muri Ndamukiza 2020 i Boston.


Pasiporo "*Batch 2*" yerekanwa muri iyi nyigisho ni yo ikurikira "*Igitabu c'Umushingwamanza*". Igaragara cane kubera ubuhinga bwayo bwiza cane, igicapo c’amabara gifise ubusobanuro bwinshi, n’ikibazo c’umubiri gikora neza. Ikora mu buryo bwa "*Air-Gap*", igatuma imfunguruzo zawe z'ibanga za Wallet ziguma zitandukanye rwose, n'uguhanahana amakuru bishoboka biciye ku ikarita ya MicroSD canke amakode ya QR. Ico gikoresho gifise bateri Nokia BL-5C ishobora gukurwako, igasubirwamwo umuriro, ikaba ifise ubushobozi bwa 1200 mAh. Iyi bateri idakoreshwa n’abantu ishobora gusubirwamwo bitagoranye, kuko iyo bateri BL-5C iboneka cane mu maduka.


💡 **Ivyashizwe ku rubuga:** Kuva muri Ntwarante 2025, izina ry'iyi Hardware Wallet ntirikiri "Pasiporo" canke "Pasiporo V2", ahubwo ni "Impasiporo Core".


Naho ku bijanye n’uguhuza, iyo Passport ifise icuma ca MicroSD, icuma ca USB-C co gucapura, n’inyuma ca kamera yo gucapura amakode ya QR.


Ku bijanye n’umutekano, iyo Passport irimwo secure element, kandi kode y’inkomoko y’ico gikoresho ni iyo gufungura yose. Itanga ibintu vyose vyitezwe ku Bitcoin Hardware Wallet nziza. Zirikana ko iyo Passport itarashigikira inyandiko ntoyi, ariko iki gice gitegekanijwe mu gihembwe ca kabiri c'umwaka w'2025.


Igiciro ca Passport ni amadolari 199, ikaba iri mu kibanza c’iyi modoka y’agaciro kanini yitwa Hardware Wallet, ikaba ihanganye n’iyindi modoka y’agaciro kanini yitwa Coldcard Q, Jade Plus, Tezor Safe 5 na Ledger.


![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)


Kugira ngo ushobore gucunga Wallet yawe itekanye kuri Passport, urafise uburyo bwinshi bwo kubikora. Iyi Hardware Wallet ihuye n’ibindi bikoresho vyinshi vyo gucunga Wallet biri kw’isoko, harimwo Sparrow wallet, Spectre Desktop, Nunchuk, Keeper, n’ibindi. Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwiga ingene twoyikoresha na Sparrow wallet.


Nimba uri umutanguzi, uburyo bworoshe ni ugukoresha Passport yawe n’iporogarama ya Envoy y’akavukire, yateguwe na Foundation. Kugira umenye ingene ukoresha Envoy na Passport yawe, raba iyi yindi nyigisho :


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/envoy-3ae5d6c7-623b-45b3-bb34-abcf9572b7cb

Iyo uronse Passport yawe, urabe neza ko isandugu n’igitabu ca Seal biri ku karito bitagiramwo ikintu gikomeye kugira ngo wemeze ko iyo paquet itafunguwe. Igihe kizoba carashizweho, hazokorwa kandi ivy’ugusuzuma ko ico gikoresho ari ic’ukuri be n’uko kitagiramwo ubutungane.

- Ikarata ya MicroSD ;

Ku gikoresho, uzosanga :




- Igikoresho co gukoresha (1) ;

## Gushinga kode ya PIN


Inyuma y’aho haza intambwe ya kode ya PIN. PIN code ni yo ifungura Pasiporo yawe. Ni co gituma ritanga uburinzi ku bijanye n’ugushika ku mubiri ata wemerewe. Kode ya PIN ntaco ikora mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwa none za Wallet yawe. Rero naho utoshobora kuronka kode ya PIN, kugira ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 canke 24 Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gusubira kuronka amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri.


![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)


Turagusavye guhitamwo kode ya PIN ishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo butari bwo. Kandi, urabe ko ubika iyi kode ahantu hatandukanye n’aho Passport yawe ibitswe (nk’akarorero mu mucungerezi w’ijambobanga).


Ushobora guhitamwo kode ya PIN iri hagati y’imibare 6 na 12. Ndabagira inama yo kubigira igihe kirekire uko bishoboka kwose.


Koresha urufunguzo kugira ngo winjize inomero zawe za PIN. Niwaheza, ukande kuri buto yemeza.


![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)


Wemeze PIN yawe ubugira kabiri.

## Rema Bitcoin Wallet nshasha


Ubu ni igihe co guhingura Bitcoin Wallet nshasha. Fyonda kuri buto yo kwemeza.


![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)


Kugira ngo ureme Wallet nshasha, kanda kuri "*Rema seed nshasha*".


![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)


Ushobora guhitamwo hagati y’amajambo 12 canke 24 y’ijambo Mnemonic. Umutekano utangwa n’izo nzira zompi ni usa, rero urashobora guhitamwo iyo yoroshe kubika, ni ukuvuga amajambo 12.


![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/30.webp)


Pasiporo yawe ubu izoba generate "*Kode y'Igihugu*". Iyi ni urutonde rw’imibare ishobora gukoreshwa mu gukuraho ububiko bwa Wallet yawe ibitswe kuri MicroSD. Ubu buryo bwo gucungera, bwihariye ku bikoresho vya Foundation, bugira ububiko bw’inyongera ku majambo yawe ya Mnemonic, ariko ntihuye n’izindi porogarama za Bitcoin.


Niwafata ingingo yo gukoresha iyi "*Backup Code*", urabe ko uyibika ahantu hatandukanye n'aho MicroSD yawe irimwo ububiko bwa Wallet yawe. Ariko rero, woshobora guhitamwo kudakoresha iyo nzira nimba wumva ko gufata neza amajambo yawe ya Mnemonic bihagije.


![Image](assets/fr/31.webp)


Injira "*Backup Code*" yawe kugira ngo wemeze ko wayibitse neza.


![Image](assets/fr/32.webp)


Niba MicroSD yinjijwe, ububiko bwa Wallet yawe bwabitswe ng’aho.


![Image](assets/fr/33.webp)


Passport yawe izokwerekana amajambo yawe y’amajambo 12 Mnemonic. Iyi Mnemonic iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe yose y’ama bitcoins ataco abujijwe. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere n’aho atashobora kubona Passport yawe.


Iryo jambo ry’amajambo 12 risubiza uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe y’ama bitcoins iyo Passport yawe yatakaye, yibwe canke ivunitse. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko umuntu ayizigama neza kandi akayibika ahantu hatagira umutekano.


Ushobora kuvyandika ku karto zitangwa mu gasandugu, canke kugira ngo wongere uyikingire, ndagusavye ko wobicapura ku gishingo c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo ubikingire umuriro, umwuzure canke ngo bisenyuke.

## Kuvumbura ibikubiyemo


Pasiporo yawe Interface ifise ibintu bitatu nyamukuru:




- "*Ishirahamwe*";
- "*Ibindi*";
- "*Imiterere*".

### *Konti*" urutonde

![Image](assets/fr/37.webp)


"*Ibikoresho vya konti*" bitanga amahitamwo nk'ugusuzuma Address, gusinya ubutumwa, canke kuraba amaderesi ari muri Wallet yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/38.webp)


Mu nzira ya "*Manage Account*", ushobora gufatanya Bitcoin Wallet yawe na porogaramu yo gucunga Wallet (ivyo tuzovuga mu ntambwe zikurikira z'iyi nyigisho), canke ubone kandi uhindure izina rya konti yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/39.webp)


### Ibindi" urutonde


Mu rutonde rwa "*Ibindi*", urashobora gukora konti nshasha muri Wallet yawe, ihuye n'iryo jambo nyene rya Mnemonic.


![Image](assets/fr/40.webp)


Ushobora kandi kwinjiza BIP39 passphrase (raba igice gikurikira) canke ukoreshe seed y'igihe gito.


![Image](assets/fr/41.webp)


### Amagenamiterere" urutonde


Mu "*Imiterere*", uzosangamwo Wallet yawe yose n'imiterere y'ibikoresho vyawe.


![Image](assets/fr/42.webp)


"*Igikoresho*" kiguha amahitamwo yo guhindura umuco w'igicapo, gushinga igihe co guteba imbere y'uko gifunga, guhindura kode ya PIN, canke guhindura izina ry'igikoresho.


![Image](assets/fr/43.webp)


"*Backup*" submenu iragufasha kwohereza hanze ububiko bwawe bwa Wallet bwashizwemwo amakuru, gusuzuma ukuri kw'ububiko bwa kera, canke gusubira kuraba "*Backup Code*" yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/44.webp)


"*Firmware*" ni iyo guhindura porogaramu ya Passport yawe. Turagusavye gukora ivyo bishasha ubudasiba kugira ngo wungukire ku bintu bishasha vyakosowe be n’ibintu bishasha.


![Image](assets/fr/45.webp)

![Image](assets/fr/46.webp)


Mu "*Advanced*", urashobora kuraba amajambo y'invugo yawe ya Mnemonic, gukora ibikorwa kuri MicroSD yinjijwe, gusubiramwo Passport yawe ku mirongo y'uruganda, canke gukora isuzuma ry'ukuri, nk'uko vyakozwe mbere.


![Image](assets/fr/47.webp)


Ushobora gukoresha "*Amajambo y'umutekano*", ikintu congeramwo Layer y'umutekano mu kugaragaza amajambo abiri yihariye igihe ufungura igikoresho umaze kwinjiza imibare ine ya mbere ya kode ya PIN. Aya majambo, kugira ngo azigame mu gihe co gutunganya, aratuma Pasiporo itasubirijwe canke ngo ihindurwe. Mu gihe hoba hariho ukutumvikana mu nyuma, turaguhanura ko utokoresha ico gikoresho. Ndaguhanura gukoresha iyo nzira kugira ngo wirinde ingorane nyinshi zo gusenyura umubiri w’igikoresho.


![Image](assets/fr/48.webp)


Ubwa nyuma, "*Ivyagutse*" bigufasha gukoresha ibikorwa vyihariye ku mikoreshereze imwe imwe y'igikoresho, nk'umurongo wa Whirlpool CoinJoin.

## Yongerako BIP39 passphrase


Imbere yo kubandanya, niwabishaka, urashobora kwongerako BIP39 passphrase. BIP39 passphrase ni ijambobanga ry’ubuhinga ushobora guhitamwo mu mwidegemvyo, kandi ryongerwa ku majambo yawe ya Mnemonic kugira ngo ukomeze umutekano wa Wallet. Iyo iyo nzira ikoreshejwe, gushika ku Bitcoin Wallet yawe bizosaba Mnemonic na passphrase. Iyo ata na kimwe muri ivyo bibiri, ntivyoshoboka ko umuntu asubirana Wallet.


Imbere yo gutunganya iyo nzira kuri Passport yawe, birakenewe cane ko usoma iyi ngingo kugira ngo utahure neza ingene passphrase ikora kandi wirinde amakosa yoshobora gutuma utakaza amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7

Kugira ngo uyikoreshe, genda kuri "*Ibindi*" hanyuma ukande kuri "*Injira passphrase*".


![Image](assets/fr/50.webp)


Injira passphrase yawe ukoresheje urupapuro rwa aA1, maze urabe ko uzigama incuro imwe canke nyinshi ku bikoresho vy’umubiri (impapuro canke ivyuma). Nk’akarorero, ndiko nkoresha passphrase igoyagoya cane, ariko ukwiye guhitamwo passphrase ikomeye, idasanzwe, irimwo ubwoko bwose bw’inyuguti kandi ireha bihagije (nk’ijambobanga rikomeye).


![Image](assets/fr/51.webp)


Urashobora kubona ko amajambo y'ibanga ya BIP39 ashobora gukoreshwa n'inyuguti nini n'amakosa yo kwandika. Iyo winjije passphrase itandukanye gato n'iyo watunganijwe mu ntango, Passport ntizotangaza ikosa, ariko izoronka uwundi mugwi w'imfunguruzo z'ibanga zizoba zitari izo muri Wallet yawe ya mbere.


Ni ngombwa rero, igihe uriko uratunganya, kwandika ahantu urutoke rw'urufunguzo rwa mbere uzohabwa mu ntambwe ikurikira. Nk'akarorero, n'urutoke rwanje rwa passphrase `Umugambi B`, urutoke rwanje rw'urufunguzo rwa mbere ni `745D526B`.


![Image](assets/fr/52.webp)


Igihe cose ufunguye Passport yawe, uzokenera gusubira muri iyi menu kugira ngo winjize passphrase yawe maze uyishire kuri Wallet yawe. Pasiporo ntizigama passphrase.


Igihe cose ufunguye, umaze kwandika passphrase, urabe kuri iki gicapo co kwemeza ko urutoke rwatanzwe ari rumwe n’urwo wanditse mu gihe co gutunganya. Niba ari uko biri, passphrase yawe ni yo kandi uriko uraronka Bitcoin Wallet ibereye. Niba atarivyo, uri kuri Wallet idakwiriye kandi ukeneye kwongera kugerageza, wiyubara kugira ngo ntukore amakosa yo kwinjiza.


Imbere y’uko uronka ama bitcoins yawe ya mbere kuri Wallet yawe, **Ndaguhanura cane gukora ikigeragezo co gusubirana ubusa**. Ushire ahabona amakuru amwe amwe y’ishingiro, nka xpub yawe canke ubanza kwakira Address, hanyuma usibe Wallet yawe kuri Passport igihe ikiri ubusa (`Ivyagezwe -> Ivyiza -> Gukuraho Passport`). Hanyuma ugerageze gusubizaho Wallet yawe ukoresheje impapuro zawe z'amajambo Mnemonic na passphrase iyo ari yo yose. Suzuma ko amakuru y’ibifungurwa vyasohowe inyuma yo gusubizaho ahuye n’ayo wanditse mu ntango. Nivyashika, urashobora kwizigira yuko amakete yawe y’impapuro ari ay’ukwizigirwa. Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwo gukora ikigeragezo co gukira, usabwe kuraba iyi yindi nyigisho :


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/recovery-test-5a75db51-a6a1-4338-a02a-164a8d91b895

## Kwakira ama bitcoins


Ubu ko Passport yawe yashizweho, witeguye kwakira Sats yawe ya mbere kuri Bitcoin Wallet yawe nshasha. Kugira ngo ubikore, kuri Sparrow, fyonda ku rutonde rwa "*Kwakira*".


![Image](assets/fr/70.webp)


Sparrow izokwerekana ikimenyetso ca mbere c'ukwakira Address muri Wallet yawe. Ushobora kwongerako ikimenyetso.


![Image](assets/fr/71.webp)


Imbere yo kuyikoresha, tuzosuzuma Address ku rubuga rwa Pasiporo kugira ngo twiyemeze neza ko ari iya Bitcoin Wallet yacu. Ku Sparrow, urashobora kwagura kode ya QR ya Address ukanda kuri yo nimba bikenewe. Mu "*Konti*" ya Pasiporo yawe, hitamwo "*Ibikoresho vya Konti*".


![Image](assets/fr/72.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Suzuma Address*", hanyuma ushireko kode ya QR yerekanwa kuri Sparrow wallet.


![Image](assets/fr/73.webp)


Raba neza ko Address yerekanwa kuri Pasiporo ihuye neza n'iyo yerekanwa kuri Sparrow, kandi ko "*Verified*" igaragara.


![Image](assets/fr/74.webp)


Ubu ushobora kuyikoresha kugira ngo uronke ama bitcoins. Igihe iyo nzira izoba itangajwe ku rubuga, izoboneka kuri Sparrow. Rindira gushika ubonye ivyemezo bihagije kugira ngo ubone ko igikorwa co gucuruza ari ikintu gitomoye.


![Image](assets/fr/75.webp)


## Wohereze ama bitcoins


Ubu ko ufise Sats nkeyi muri Wallet yawe, urashobora no kuzirungika. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku rutonde rwa "*UTXOs*".


![Image](assets/fr/76.webp)


Hitamwo UTXOs wipfuza gukoresha nk'inyungu z'iyi nzira, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ohereza Ivyatowe*".


![Image](assets/fr/77.webp)


Injira Address y'uwaronse, ikimenyetso kikwibutsa intumbero y'ugucuruza n'amahera wipfuza kohereza kuri iyo Address.


![Image](assets/fr/78.webp)


Guhindura igiciro c'amafaranga bivanye n'ingene isoko riri, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Create Transaction*".


![Image](assets/fr/79.webp)


Suzuma ko amaparametere yose y'ibikorwa ari ukuri, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Sozera ibikorwa kugira ngo usinye*".


![Image](assets/fr/80.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Erekana QR*" kugira ngo ubone igitabu ca PSBT (*Igitabo ca Partially Signed Bitcoin Transaction*). Sparrow yubatse iyo nzira, ariko iracari n’imikono yo gufungura ama bitcoins akoreshwa mu kwinjiza. Ivyo bimenyetso bishobora gukorwa gusa na Passport, ikaba ari yo yakira seed yawe itanga uburenganzira bwo gukoresha imfunguruzo z’ibanga zikenewe kugira ngo usinye iyo nzira.


![Image](assets/fr/81.webp)


Kuri Passport yawe, genda kuri "*Konti*" hanyuma ukande kuri "*Sinya ukoresheje QR Code*".


![Image](assets/fr/82.webp)


Scan igitabu ca PSBT (*Igitabu ca 126*) kigaragara kuri igitabu ca 127.


![Image](assets/fr/83.webp)


Wemeze ko Address yakiriye be n’amahera yoherejwe ari ukuri, hanyuma ukande kuri buto yo kwemeza.


![Image](assets/fr/84.webp)


Suzuma igitabu ca Exchange ca Address. Mu karorero kanje, nta n’imwe, nk’uko ugucuruza birimwo igisohoka kimwe.


![Image](assets/fr/85.webp)


Raba neza ko amahera ari yo wahisemwo.


![Image](assets/fr/86.webp)


Nimba amakuru yose ari ukuri, fyonda ku buto bwo kwemeza kugira ngo ushire umukono ku giciro.


![Image](assets/fr/87.webp)


Ku Sparrow wallet, ukande kuri "*Scan QR*" hanyuma ushireko kode ya QR yerekanwa kuri Pasiporo yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/88.webp)


Ivyo mwashizeko umukono ubu biteguye gutangazwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin kandi bishizwe mu gice ca Miner. Niba vyose bimeze neza, fyonda ku "*Itangazo ry'Itangazamakuru*".


![Image](assets/fr/89.webp)


Ivyo mwaguriye vyaratangajwe kandi birindiriye kwemezwa.


![Image](assets/fr/90.webp)


Urakoze cane, ubu urazi uko wotunganya no gukoresha Passport. Niwaba wabonye ko iyi nyigisho ari ngirakamaro, noshima cane iyo usiga urukumu rwa Green aha hepfo. Ntutinye gusangiza abandi iyi nkuru ku mbuga zanyu zo gusangirirako amakuru. Murakoze gusangiza abandi!
