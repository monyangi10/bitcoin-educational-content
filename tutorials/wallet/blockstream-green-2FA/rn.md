___


Software Wallet ni porogarama ishizwe kuri mudasobwa, kuri telefone ngendanwa canke ku kindi gikoresho gihuye na Internet, kigufasha gucunga no gukingira imfunguruzo zawe za Bitcoin Wallet. Udakunze ama wallets y'ibikoresho, atandukanya imfunguruzo z'ibanga, ama wallets "Hot" rero akora ahantu hashobora guterwa n'ibitero vyo kuri internet, bikongera ingorane z'ubusuma n'ubusuma.


Ivyuma vy’amahera vy’amaporogarama bikwiye gukoreshwa mu gucunga amafaranga akwiye, cane cane mu bikorwa vya misi yose. Bishobora kandi kuba uburyo bushimishije ku bantu bafise itunga ritoyi rya Bitcoin, abo gushiramwo amahera muri Hardware Wallet bishobora gusa n’ibidahuye. Ariko rero, kuba bama bakoresha Internet bituma badashobora kubika amahera yawe y’igihe kirekire canke amahera menshi. Ku bijanye n’ivyo vya nyuma, ni vyiza guhitamwo inyishu zitekanye cane, nk’amasakoshi y’ibikoresho.


Muri iyi nyigisho, nzokwereka ingene woshobora kwongereza umutekano wa Hot Wallet ukoresheje uburyo bwa "*2FA*" kuri Blockstream Green.

## Turabamenyesha Blockstream Green


Blockstream Green ni Software Wallet iboneka ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi no ku biro. Iyi Wallet yahora yitwa *Green Address*, yacitse umugambi wa Blockstream inyuma y'aho iguzwe mu 2016.


Green ni porogarama yoroshe cane gukoresha, ivyo bikaba bituma iryoshe ku batangura. Itanga ibiranga vyose vy’ingenzi vy’i Bitcoin Wallet nziza, harimwo RBF (*Replace-by-fee*), uburyo bwo gufatanya Tor, ubushobozi bwo gufatanya node yawe bwite, SPV (*Igenzura ry’Ukwishura ryoroshe*), Coin gushiramwo ibimenyetso no kuyigenzura.


Blockstream Green nayo ishigikira Liquid Network, Bitcoin Sidechain yateguwe na Blockstream kugira ngo yihute, Confidential Transactions hanze ya Blockchain nyamukuru. Muri iyi nyigisho, twibanda cane kuri Bitcoin, ariko kandi nakoze iyindi nyigisho yo kwiga gukoresha Liquid kuri Green :


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/blockstream-app-liquid-b3e4fb82-902e-4782-ad2b-a61ab05a543a

## 2/2 Multisig amahitamwo (2FA)


Ku Green, ushobora gukora "*igitabu kimwe*" Hot Wallet. Ariko kandi urafise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha "*2FA Multisig*", ivyo bikaba bituma Hot Wallet yawe igira umutekano mwinshi ataco igora cane mu bijanye n'uburongozi bwayo bwa misi yose.


Uzoshiraho rero 2/2 Multisig Wallet, bisobanura ko igikorwa cose kizosaba umukono w’imfunguruzo zibiri. Urufunguzo rwa mbere, ruva ku majambo yawe y’amajambo 12 canke 24 Mnemonic, rurakingirwa aho hantu hakoreshejwe kode ya PIN iri kuri telefone yawe. Ufise ububasha bwose kuri urwo rufunguzo. Urufunguzo rwa kabiri rufatwa n’abakozi ba Blockstream kandi gukoresha rwo gusinya bisaba kwemeza, ivyo bishobora gushikwako biciye ku kode yakiriwe kuri email, SMS, telefone, canke, nk’uko tuzobibona muri iyi nyigisho, biciye ku gikoresho co kwemeza (Authy, Google Authenticator, n’ibindi).


Kugira ngo ubone ubwigenge bwawe mu gihe Blockstream yoba yananiwe (nk’akarorero, mu gihe ishirahamwe rishobora gusenyuka canke abakozi bafise urufunguzo rwa kabiri bagasambura), uburyo bwo gufunga igihe burakoreshwa kuri Multisig yawe. Ubu buryo burahindura 2/2 Multisig ikaba 1/2 Multisig inyuma y’umwaka nk’umwe (canke neza na neza amabuye 51.840, ariko aka gaciro karashobora guhindurwa), hanyuma Wallet yawe izokenera gusa urufunguzo rwawe rwo mu karere kugira ngo ikoreshe ama bitcoins. Rero, iyo utakaje uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ama server ya Blockstream canke 2FA authentication, ubwirizwa gusa kurindira umwaka umwe kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha bitcoins zawe mu mwidegemvyo n’ikoreshwa ryawe, utavyifashemwo neza kuri Blockstream.


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/02.webp)


Ubu buryo bwongera cane umutekano wa Hot Wallet yawe, mu gihe igusiga ugenzura amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins kandi ukagufasha gukoresha neza iyo misi yose. Ariko rero, bisaba ko timelock iguma isubiramwo kugira ngo umutekano wa 2FA ugumeho. Igihe c’imisi 360, aho amahera yawe akinzwe na 2FA, gitangura ukimara kwakira ama bitcoins. Niba, imisi 360 inyuma y’iyi nkuru, utakoze igikorwa co gukoresha ayo mahera, ama bitcoins yawe azokingirwa gusa n’urufunguzo rwawe rwo mu karere, ata 2FA.


Ivyo bituma uburyo bwa 2FA bubereye cane ku gukoresha Wallet, aho amafaranga asanzwe asubiramwo igihe. Ku bijanye n’uguzigama kw’igihe kirekire Wallet, ivyo bishobora kuba ingorane, kuko uzokenera kwiyumvira gukora igikorwa co gukubura kuri wewe nyene buri mwaka imbere y’uko igihe co gufunga gihera.


Ikindi kibazo c’ubu buryo bwo gucungera umutekano ni uko uzobwirizwa gukoresha ibigereranyo vy’inyandiko ntoyi. Ivyo bisigura ko, uhereye ku bijanye n’ubuzima bwite, ibintu birushiriza kugorana: abantu bake cane ni bo bakoresha ubwoko bumwe bw’inyandiko nk’ubwawe, ivyo bikaba bituma umuntu wo hanze ashobora kumenya urutoke rwawe rwa Wallet. Ikindi, izo scripts zizotuma habaho amafaranga menshi yo gukoresha kubera ubunini bwazo.


Niba ushaka kudakoresha uburyo bwa 2FA kandi wipfuza gusa gushinga "*singlesig*" Wallet kuri Green, ndagutumiye kuraba iyi yindi nyigisho :


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/blockstream-app-liquid-b3e4fb82-902e-4782-ad2b-a61ab05a543a

## Rema Bitcoin Wallet kuri Blockstream Green


Ubu rero witeguye gukora Bitcoin Wallet. Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Tangira*".


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/12.webp)


Ushobora guhitamwo hagati yo gukora Software Wallet yo mu karere canke gucunga Cold Wallet biciye kuri Hardware Wallet. Kuri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwibanda ku kurema Hot Wallet, rero uzokenera guhitamwo "*Kuri iki gikoresho*".


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/13.webp)


Ushobora rero guhitamwo gusubizaho Bitcoin Wallet yariho canke ukarema indi nshasha. Kubera intumbero z'iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko turahingura Wallet nshasha. Ariko rero, nimba ukeneye gusubiramwo Bitcoin Wallet iriho kuva ku nsiguro yayo Mnemonic, nk’akarorero ukurikije ukubura kwa telefone yawe ya kera, uzokenera guhitamwo uburyo bwa kabiri.


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/14.webp)


Ushobora rero guhitamwo hagati y’amajambo 12 canke 24 y’amajambo Mnemonic. Iryo jambo rizotuma ushobora gusubira gukoresha Wallet yawe ukoresheje porogarama iyo ari yo yose ihuye iyo habaye ingorane kuri telefone yawe. Muri iki gihe, guhitamwo ijambo ry’amajambo 24 nta mutekano utanga nk’ijambo ry’amajambo 12. Ndagusavye rero guhitamwo ijambo ry’ikirundi ry’amajambo 12 Mnemonic.


Green izoca iguha ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic. Imbere yo kubandanya, urabe neza ko ata muntu ariko arakuraba. Fyonda kuri "*Kwerekana ijambo ry'ugusubirana*" kugira ngo uyigaragaze ku rubuga.


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/15.webp)


**Iyi Mnemonic iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe yose y’ama bitcoins**. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere n’aho atashobora gushika kuri telefone yawe (bishingiye ku gihe c’ugufunga canke 2FA iyo ufise 2/2 Wallet kuri Green).


Iragufasha gusubira gukoresha imfunguruzo zawe zo mu karere iyo telefone yawe yatakaye, yibwe canke ivunitse. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko uyigira backup neza **ku gikoresho c'umubiri (atari digitale)** ukayibika ahantu hatagira uwuyibika. Ushobora kuvyandika ku mpapuro, canke kugira ngo wongere umutekano, nimba ari Wallet nini, ndagusavye ko woyicapura ku gifadika c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo uyikingire ingorane z’umuriro, umwuzure canke gusenyuka (ku Hot super Wallet yagenewe gukingira umubare mutoyi w’inyuma, simple suffit is biup).


*Biragaragara ko udashobora kwigera usangira aya majambo kuri internet, nk’uko ndabigira muri iyi nyigisho. Ico kigereranyo ca Wallet kizokoreshwa kuri Testnet gusa kandi kizokurwaho ku mpera y’inyigisho.*


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/16.webp)


Umaze kwandika neza ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic ku gikoresho, kanda kuri "*Bandanya*". Green Wallet izoca igusaba kwemeza amajambo amwe amwe ari mu nteruro yawe ya Mnemonic kugira ngo umenye neza ko wayanditse neza. Uzuza amajambo abuze ahantu hatagira ikintu.


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/17.webp)


Hitamwo kode ya PIN y'igikoresho cawe, izokoreshwa mu gufungura Green Wallet yawe. Ivyo ni vyo bigukingira ku bijanye n’ugushika ku mubiri ata wemerewe. Iyi kode ya PIN ntaco ikora mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwa none za Wallet yawe. Rero, naho woba udashobora gukoresha iyo kode ya PIN, kugira amajambo yawe y’amajambo 12 canke 24 Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gusubira gukoresha imfunguruzo zo mu karere kawe.


Turagusavye guhitamwo kode ya PIN y’imirongo 6 ishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo butari bwo. Raba neza ko ubika iyi kode kugira ngo ntuyibagire, ahandi ho uzobwirizwa gukura Wallet yawe muri Mnemonic. Ushobora rero kwongerako uburyo bwo kubuza biometric kugira ngo ntubwirizwe kwinjira muri PIN igihe cose uyikoresheje. Muri rusangi, ubuhinga bwa biometrics buratekanye cane kuruta PIN ubwayo. Rero, ku buryo busanzwe, ndaguhanura ko utoshiraho iyi nzira yo gufungura.


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/18.webp)


Injira PIN yawe ubugira kabiri kugira ngo uyimenyeshe.


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/19.webp)


Rindira ko Wallet yawe iremwa, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Rema konti*".


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/20.webp)


Ushobora rero guhitamwo hagati y’umukono umwe Wallet canke Wallet irindwa n’ivyemezo bibiri (2FA). Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzohitamwo uburyo bwa kabiri.


![GREEN 2FA MULTISIG](assets/fr/21.webp)

Ni vyiza gukora backup y’urwo rufunguzo, naho woba ushobora gusubira kuronka uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ama bitcoins yawe inyuma y’aho igihe co gufunga kirangiriye, nk’uko vyasiguwe haruguru.

Ivyo biharuro bitangura igihe nyene ama bitcoins ashitse, kandi iyo timelock iheze, amahera yawe ashobora gukoreshwa gusa n’urufunguzo rwawe rwo mu karere, ata 2FA ikenewe. Ni wewe ubwawe ushobora kumenya igihe co gufunga igihe kigubereye.
