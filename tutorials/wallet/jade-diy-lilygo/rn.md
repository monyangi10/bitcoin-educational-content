## Iciyumviro c'intango (iminota 15)


### Ukwirinda ni iki kandi ni kubera iki bihambaye?


Bitcoin yaremewe gukuraho ivy’abantu ba gatatu bizigira, nk’amabanki n’amashirahamwe mu bijanye n’amahera yacu. Aho gukoresha ukwizigira, bitcoin ikoresha imibare, fizike, n’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru kugira ngo umuntu wese agire ububasha bwo kugira no kugenzura amahera yiwe ata n’umwe akeneye uruhusha.


Uko ivyo bikora ni uko bitcoin iriho ku gitabu c’amahera c’isi yose citwa blockchain aka the bitcoin timechain, kikaba ari igitabu ca bose kandi gitomoye gikoreshwa na mudasobwa, aho kuba igitabu c’amahera gihurikiye hamwe nk’ikonti ya banki.


Ikintu gihambaye co gutahura ni uko kugira ngo ushire bitcoin ahantu hamwe uyishire ahandi, utegerezwa gusinya iyo transaction ukoresheje ico bita urufunguzo rw’ibanga. Iyumvire nk’ugufungura ububiko bw’ibintu ukoresheje ijambo banga, hanyuma ukajana iyo bitcoin mu bubiko bw’ibintu bw’uwundi muntu. Bitcoin iraguha ububasha bwo gufata imfunguruzo z’ico kigega wewe nyene, aho kwizigira banki ngo ikurungikire amahera yawe.


Ububasha bukomeye buzana n’inshingano nyinshi, utakaze imfunguruzo zawe amahera yawe akabura ubuziraherezo. Muri ubwo buryo, urashobora kwiyumvira ko imfunguruzo z’aho hantu ari amahera ubwayo. Naho imfunguruzo zitari kimwe na bitcoin, ni uburyo bwo kwimurira amahera yawe kandi rero ni ngirakamaro cane mu kurinda. Ni co gituma tuvuga ngo "si imfunguruzo zawe, si ibiceri vyawe".


Ijambo ry’ukwizigama ryoshobora kumvikana nk’iriteye urujijo, ariko ivyo risobanura vyose ni ugufata imfunguruzo zawe bwite, no kugenzura bitcoin yawe bwite. Iyo udafise urwo rufunguzo, uba wizigiye uwundi muntu ngo azorugufashe. Nimba amafaranga yawe ari muri ETF canke ku giciro (Mt. Gox, FTX, Coinbase, Binance, n’ibindi), ntufise amafaranga, ufise uburenganzira bwo kugura amafaranga. Ivyo bizana ubwoko bwose bw’ingorane, nk’ama exchanges yoshobora gufatwa n’abantu bagatakaza bitcoin yawe canke amashirahamwe aguriza amahera yawe akaguha gusa agace k’amahera y’ububiko. Ikindi, abandi bantu bizigira boba bafise ububasha bwose ku mahera yawe kandi boshobora guhagarika canke guhagarika ukukura amahera.


![image](assets/fr/01.webp)


Mu kwirinda ukuraho ukwizigira mu nzira. Nta n’umwe ashobora guhagarika amahera yawe canke kwanka ko ukoresha, urashobora kohereza amahera hakurya y’imipaka, ku muntu uwo ari we wese, igihe cose, kandi ntukeneye konti ya banki, indangamuntu, canke kwemererwa n’umuntu uwo ari we wese. Nta n’umwe ashobora kuguhagarika, kugucengera, canke kukwiba, akagufungura ububasha bwose bwa bitcoin nk’amahera y’umwidegemvyo. Ni co gituma tuvuga, na bitcoin ushobora kuba banki yawe bwite.


Bitcoin yaremewe gutorera umuti ingorane yo gukoresha nabi ukwizigira n’amahera, uguhitamwo kuva muri sisitemu dufise ubu, ariko gusohoka bikora gusa iyo ufashe imfunguruzo. Ni co gituma ukwirinda ari ikintu gihambaye cane.


### Wallet ni iki?


Ijambo wallet ni ijambo ry’ikinyoma rero rishobora gutera urujijo. Ego ni ukuri ko bitcoin wallet, nka wallet y’umubiri, ibika agaciro. Ariko itandukaniro rikomeye ni uko ama wallets ya bitcoin mu vy’ukuri ata bitcoin iyo ari yo yose abika.


Bitcoin iriho gusa nk’inyandiko y’igitabu ku rubuga rwa bose, canke mu bibanza vy’ikigereranyo biri ku rubuga rwa interineti. Ibuka kwimurira bitcoin utegerezwa gukoresha imfunguruzo zawe kugira ufungure ububiko maze wimure ibiceri ahandi, imfunguruzo z’ibanga nivyo bikoreshwa spend bitcoin. Iyo ukoresheje wallet yawe, mu vy’ukuri uba uriko urakoresha gusa imfunguruzo zawe kugira ngo usinye iyo nzira. Uko ni ko werekana ikimenyamenya c’uko ari wewe ufise ayo mahera kandi ko ufise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ayo mahera.


Amasakoshi ya Bitcoin vy’ukuri abika gusa imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga, rero vyoba vyiza uyise imfunguruzo z’imfunguruzo.


### Hot n'amasakoshi ya Cold


wallet ishaje ni porogarama iri kuri telefone yawe canke kuri mudasobwa yawe. Ihuye na internet, rero yoroshe gukoresha kandi yihuta gusinya amafaranga, ariko ivyo bisigura kandi ko ishobora gushikirwa n’abasuma, porogarama mbi, n’ubuhendanyi. Ni co citwa "hot" kuko gifatanye na internet, gica gifatanywa n'umuriro kandi kigaca gifungurwa. Akarorero ni telefone wallet canke umucukumbuzi wallet.


Ku rundi ruhande wallet ikanye, canke hardware wallet, ni igikoresho kirema kandi kikabika urufunguzo rwawe ata murongo. Ivyo bikuraho ubushobozi bw’umuntu bwo gutera amahera yawe kandi biratekanye cane ku bijanye no kuzigama igihe kirekire, yamara rero ni igikoresho gikenewe kugira ngo umuntu ashire umukono ku giciro cose kandi gishobora kuba kidasanzwe.


### Hardware Wallet Igishushanyo c'Iterabwoba


Hardware wallets zirahari kugira ngo zitorere umuti ingorane y’ishimikiro: ni gute wosinya ku bikorwa vya Bitcoin utashize ahabona imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga kuri mudasobwa ihuye na internet ishobora guhungabanywa n’ibintu bibi canke abatera kure? Icogereranyo c’iterabwoba nyamukuru ciyumvira ko laptop canke telefone yawe ya misi yose bishobora kuba ari urwanko. Igikoresho ca wallet kiratuma haba ahantu h’ibanga aho imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitazokwigera ziva ku gikoresho, kandi gusinya ku bikorwa bibera muri secure element canke mu gikoresho gikoreshwa mu gucungera amakuru (microcontrôleur) kigasubiza gusa umukono kuri mudasobwa y’umushitsi, atari urufunguzo ubwarwo. Naho mudasobwa yawe yoba yasambutse burundu, uwugutera ntashobora kwiba Bitcoin yawe ata n’umwe afise uburenganzira bwo gushika ku gikoresho cawe no kuri PIN yawe.


Ariko rero, ama wallets y’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none arazana ingeramizi zayo bwite. Utegerezwa kwizigira ko umuhinguzi atashizeho inzugi z’inyuma, uruhererekane rw’ibikoresho ntirwahinduwe, kandi uguhingura umubare w’imburakimazi ni ukuri kw’imburakimazi. Abatera boshobora gukura imfunguruzo biciye ku bitero vyo ku ruhande canke gukoresha ubuhinga bwa chip, kandi umuntu afise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha igikoresho cawe mu gihe gito yoshobora guhindura igikoresho cawe. Kubaka ibikoresho vyawe bwite wallet biragufasha gutahura ivyo bintu bigufasha gutahura—uzofata ingingo ku bijanye n’ibintu bitekanye n’ibikoresho bikoreshwa muri rusangi, ingene wogenzura ibikorwa ku kigaragaza, n’ingene wokwikingira ibintu bishobora gutera ubwoba vyo kure n’ivyo ku mubiri. Intumbero si umutekano utunganye, ahubwo ni ugutahura ingeramizi uriko urakingira n’izisigaye.


### Ivyiyumviro nyamukuru



- Entropy na seed amajambo:** wallet yawe iratekanye gusa nk’uko ubusa buyivyara. Tuzovanga igikoresho gitanga umubare w’ibintu bitari vyo n’ubuhinga bwo gukoresha abantu nk’ugutera amadayimoni, duhindure iyo entropi mu majambo 12 canke 24 [ijambo BIP39] (https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0039.mediawiki), maze dusige n’icumba co gucungera amakuru.
- Isuku ry’amajambo y’imbuto:** Fata seed nk’imfunguruzo nyamukuru z’amahera uzigamye. Ntukigere wandika ayo majambo muri telefone canke kuri orodinateri—ibikoresho bifata amajwi, amafoto y’ivyo ukoresha be n’ivyo ufata mu gicu birashobora kuyasohoka ibihe bidahera. Gumana iyo mvugo ata murongo, uyibike ahantu wewe wenyene ushobora kuyironka, kandi wimenyereze kuyisoma n’ijwi rirenga imbere y’uko ugenda.
- Igikoresho gitekanye + umugenzuzi muto:** Wiyumvire secure element nk’ibarabara ry’imbere n’umugenzuzi muto nk’ubwonko. secure element irinda imfunguruzo z’ibanga zidashobora guhindurwa, mu gihe microcontrôleur ikoresha igicapo, amabuto be n’ubuhinga bwo gukoresha porogarama. Zirikana ko ama wallets y’ibikoresho turiko turakora uno musi atagira secure element. Ivyo ntibisigura ko ata mutekano ufise, ahubwo ni uko gusa ifise urugero rumwe rw’uburinzi rutoyi.
- Kwizigira porogaramu nkuru:** Porogaramu nkuru ni uburyo butaboneka bwo gukoresha wallet. Igihe cose ushobora gukuraho ibisohoka bifise amazina, usuzume hash yasohowe, kandi utahure ko inyubakwa zishobora gusubirwamwo zituma abantu benshi bakoranya kode imwe maze bagashika ku nkuru imwe nyene. Iyo umubare w’isuzuma udahuye, ntushirako umukono.

## Inzira yoroshe canke Inzira ya Hard .


Hari uburyo bubiri nyamukuru bwo gucapura ivyuma vyawe wallet: uburyo bworoshe n’uburyo bukomeye. Uburyo bworoshe bukoresha ibikoresho vyatunganijwe imbere y’igihe canke ibikoresho bikoreshwa ku rubuga bica bishira porogarama ku gikoresho cawe ataco ukoresheje. Ubu buryo ni bwiza ku batangura bashaka gutsinda ningoga canke bakunda kwirinda ingorane zo gukosora n’ugukorana n’umurongo w’amabwirizwa. Birorosha igikorwa kandi bikaguhagurutsa kandi bikagutera gukora vyihuta, bikagufasha gushikira abo bashasha mu bijanye n’iterambere ry’ibintu canke mu bikoresho vy’ubuhinga.


Ku rundi ruhande, inzira igoye ni ugukoresha n’amaboko ibikoresho vyo ku murongo w’amabwirizwa kugira ngo ushireko porogarama y’ivyuma. Ubwo buryo busaba kugenzura imikono ya porogarama n’ibiharuro vy’ibintu kugira ngo umenye neza ko ari ivy’ukuri n’ubutungane, bikaguha ugutahura kwimbitse kw’ingene porogarama ikorana n’ibikoresho. Naho bisaba utwigoro twinshi no kumenya amabwirizwa y’ibarabara, biratanga ubugenzuzi bwinshi, uguseruka, n’ukwizigira umutekano w’igikoresho cawe.


Uburyo bumwebumwe bwose burafise ivyo buvamwo: uburyo bworoshe buratanga urugero runaka rw’ukwizigira n’ugutahura kugira ngo bube bwihuta kandi bube bworoshe, mu gihe uburyo bugoye busaba umwanya mwinshi n’ubuhinga bwinshi ariko bukaguhemba mu guhindura uko umuntu abona ibintu no gutahura neza ubuhinga bushingiyeko. Abigisha bakwiye guhimiriza abanyeshure guhitamwo inzira ihuye neza n’urugero rw’uguhumurizwa kwabo be n’ugushaka kumenya ibintu, ivyo bikaba bituma bagira ukwizigira be n’ugutohoza.

## Gushinga Jade Wallet


Igihe porogaramu y'ubuhinga imaze guca neza, LilyGO T-Display yawe ubu ni porogaramu y'ubuhinga bwa Jade wallet ikora neza. Iki gice kizogufasha guca mu nzira yo gutegura, kuva ku guhingura ijambo ryawe rya seed gushika ku guhuza igikoresho na porogarama ya wallet nka Sparrow canke porogarama ya Blockstream Green.


### Gufungura mbere n'ugutegura igikoresho



- Inguvu ku gikoresho:** Iyo LilyGO ikihuye na laptop yawe biciye kuri USB-C, firmware ya Jade ikwiye kwifungura ubwayo. Uzobona ikimenyetso ca Jade kigaragara ku kigaragaza.



- Injira mu buryo bwo gutegura:** Igikoresho kizokwereka urutonde rw'intango. Koresha ubuto bubiri buri ku rubaho kugira ngo ugende:
 - Buto y'ibubamfu:** Kwimukira hejuru/inyuma
 - Buto y'iburyo:** Kwimukira hasi/imbere
 - Amabuto yose icarimwe:** Hitamwo/kwemeza



- Hitamwo "Setup":** Ja kuri Setup maze ukande kuri buto zompi kugira ngo wemeze. Ico gikoresho kizokuyobora mu nzira y’intango yo gutunganya.


### Guhingura Wallet yawe



- Hitamwo "Begin Setup":** Ico gikoresho kizogusaba gutangura igikorwa co kurema wallet. Wemeze ivyo wahisemwo.



- Hitamwo "Rema Wallet nshasha":** Uzoronka amahitamwo abiri:
 - Rema Wallet nshasha:** Itanga ijambo rishasha rya seed (hitamwo iri ku mahugurwa)
 - Gusubiza Wallet:** Gusubiza wallet iriho mu mvugo ya seed (ku bakoresha bateye imbere)



- Hitamwo "Rema Wallet Nshasha" maze wemeze.



- Generate entropy:** Ico gikoresho kizokoresha umubare waco w’imburakimazi kugira ngo gikoreshe entropi y’ibanga. Ivyo bifata amasegonda makeyi uko igikoresho gikorakoranya ibintu bitari vyo biva ku bintu vyinshi.


### Gufata amajwi y'ijambo ry'uruvyaro rwawe



- Andika amajambo yawe ya seed:** Ico gikoresho ubu kizokwerekana amajambo 12 ya BIP39 seed, ijambo rimwe ku rindi. Iyi ni yo ntambwe ihambaye kuruta izindi zose mu bijanye n’ivyo vyose.


**Ibikorwa bihambaye vyo gucungera umutekano:**


- Wandike neza ijambo ryose ku mpapuro (koresha amakarata y’amajambo seed yatanzwe nimba ariho)
- Suzuma kabiri ijambo ryose uko uriyandika
- Ntukigere ufotora amajambo seed ukoresheje telefone yawe
- Ntukigere wandika ayo majambo kuri mudasobwa canke kuri telefone iyo ari yo yose
- Gumana amajambo yawe ya seed mu bwiherero—ntusangire n’abandi igicapo cawe canke ngo werekane abandi



- Genzura amajambo yawe yo muri seed:** Uhejeje kwandika amajambo yose 12, ico gikoresho kizogusaba kwemeza amajambo menshi yo muri ayo majambo kugira ngo umenye neza ko wayanditse neza. Koresha amabuto kugira uhitemwo ijambo ryiza ry’ikibazo cose.


**Pro tip:** Imbere yo gutera imbere, wimenyereze gusoma ijambo ryawe rya seed ugasubira kwibwira n’ijwi rirenga (mu gacerere, kugira abandi ntibashobora kwumva). Ivyo birafasha gufata amakosa yose yo kwandika canke ukutumvikana.


### Uburyo bwo guhuza



- Hitamwo ubwoko bw'ihuriro:** Porogaramu ya Jade ishigikira uburyo bubiri bwo ihuriro:
 - USB:** Guhuza n'umugozi biciye ku nsinga ya USB-C (ni vyiza muri iri koraniro)
 - Bluetooth:** Guhuza ata nsinga n'ibikoresho vy'amaboko



- Hitamwo **USB** ubu, kuko ariyo nzira igororotse cane ku bikoresho vya wallet vyo ku biro kandi ntizana ibitero bitagira amashanyarazi.



- Izina ry'igikoresho:** Jade izokwerekana ikimenyetso kidasanzwe nka "Huza Jade A7D924". Ico kimenyetso kigufasha gutandukanya ama wallets menshi y’ibikoresho iyo uhejeje kwubaka menshi. Niwandike iki kimenyetso nimba ubishaka.


### Guhuza na porogaramu ya Wallet


Ubu ufise uburyo bubiri nyamukuru bwo gukorana n’ibikoresho vyawe bishasha wallet: porogarama ya telefone ngendanwa Blockstream Green (yo gukoresha uri ku rugendo) canke Sparrow Wallet (yo gukoresha ku biro bifise ibintu vyinshi vy’agaciro). Kuri iyi nama, tuzokwibanda kuri Sparrow Wallet, kuko itanga uburyo bwiza bwo kubona neza ivy’ubuhinga bw’ibikorwa vya Bitcoin.


#### Ihitamwo rya 1: Blockstream Green Porogaramu yo kuri telefone ngendanwa (Itanguriro ryihuse)


Niba ushaka kugerageza igikoresho cawe vyihuse ukoresheje igikoresho gigendanwa:



- Gukuraho porogaramu **Blockstream Green** mu bubiko bw'amaporogarama (iOS) canke muri Google Play (Android)
- Ugurure porogaramu maze uhitemwo "Huza Hardware Wallet"
- Hitamwo "Jade" ku rutonde rw'ibikoresho bishigikiwe
- Jade yawe uyishire muri telefone yawe ukoresheje umugozi wa USB-C uja kuri USB-C (canke USB-C uja kuri Lightning kuri iPhone 15+)
- Kurikiza ibisabwa biri ku rubuga kugira ngo uhuze kandi ureme wallet yawe ya mbere


**Iciyumviro ku vyerekeye Liquid:** Porogarama ya Blockstream Green ifasha Bitcoin na Liquid (uruzitiro rwo ku ruhande rwa Bitcoin). Niba uriko urakoresha ibiranga Liquid, ushobora gusaba "Export master blinding key"—ivyo bituma porogarama ibona amafaranga y'ibikorwa ku rubuga rwa Liquid, ariko akaba ari ibanga. Ku bw’iyi nama, urashobora gusimbuka ibiranga Liquid maze ukibanda ku bikorwa vya Bitcoin bimenyerewe.


#### Ihitamwo rya 2: Sparrow Wallet (Iremezwa ku mahugurwa)


Sparrow Wallet ni porogaramu ikomeye yo kuri mudasobwa iguha ububasha bwo kugenzura neza amafaranga yawe ya Bitcoin kandi igahuza neza n'ibikoresho vyawe vya Jade wallet.


**Gushiramwo:**



- Gukuraho Sparrow Wallet ku rubuga rwemewe: [Ikinyamakuru.com]
- Suzuma umukono wo gukuraho (raba inyandiko za Sparrow kugira ngo umenye vyinshi)
- Shiraho kandi utangure porogaramu


**Guhuza Jade yawe na Sparrow:**



- Muri Sparrow, genda kuri **Dosiye → Wallet nshasha**
- Ha wallet yawe izina (nk'akarorero, "Wallet yanje ya Jade")
- Fyonda **Ihuye Hardware Wallet**
- Sparrow ishobora kwibonera ubwayo igikoresho cawe ca Jade
- Niwaba usabwe, wemeze ko hariho ubufatanye ku kigaragaza Jade ukanda kuri buto zompi
- Hitamwo ubwoko bw'inyandiko wipfuza:
 - Native Segwit (P2WPKH):** Ni vyiza ku batangura—amahera make cane, ahuye cane n’amasakoshi y’ubu
 - Segwit yashizwemwo (P2SH-P2WPKH):** Kugira ngo bihure n'ibikorwa vya kera
 - Taproot (P2TR):** Iteye imbere cane, itanga ubuzima bwite bwiza n’amahera make, ariko isaba ubufasha bwa wallet buteye imbere
- Fyonda **Injiza ububiko bw'urufunguzo** kugira ngo urangize guhuza


**Gutunganya ihuriro rya Serveri ya Sparrow:**


Imbere y’uko ubona umubare wawe canke amafaranga yawe yo gutangaza, Sparrow ikeneye kwifatanya n’urudodo rwa Bitcoin kugira ngo ikure amakuru ya blockchain. Ufise uburyo bwinshi, buri bumwe bufise ivyo ushobora guhindura hagati y’ukuryoherwa, ubuzima bwite, n’ukwizigira:



- Electrum Server ya bose (Iyoroshe, idasanzwe):** Ihuza na server ya bose ikoreshwa n’uwundi muntu. Ivyihuta gushinga, ariko server ishobora kubona amaderesi yawe ya wallet kandi ishobora kuyahuza n’aderesi yawe ya IP. Ni vyiza kugerageza kuri testnet.
 - Muri Sparrow, genda kuri **Ibikoresho → Ivyo ukunda → Serveri**
 - Hitamwo **Serveri ya bose** hanyuma uhitemwo serveri muri list
 - Fyonda **Igerageza ry'ihuriro** kugira ngo ugenzure



- Bitcoin Core canke Knots Node (Ivy'ibanga cane, vyinshi bikora):** Gukoresha node yawe yuzuye ya Bitcoin. Iyi ni yo ngingo ngenderwako y’inzahabu y’ubuzima bwite n’ugusuzuma, kuko uriko urashingira intahe igikorwa cose wewe nyene kandi ntuzigire umukozi w’uwundi muntu. Ariko rero, bisaba gukuraho blockchain yose (~600GB) no kuguma ukora node yawe.
 - Shiraho kandi uhuze Bitcoin Core canke Amapfundo
 - Muri Sparrow, genda kuri **Ibikoresho → Ivyo ukunda → Serveri**
 - Hitamwo **Bitcoin Core canke Knots** hanyuma winjize amakuru y'ihuriro ry'urudodo rwawe



- Electrum Server y’ibanga (Uburinganire bwiza):** Wakira umukozi wawe bwite wa Electrum (nka Fulcrum canke Electrs) ahuye n’umurongo wawe wa Bitcoin Core canke Knots. Itanga ubuzima bwite bushitse ataco ukeneye gukoresha Sparrow ku mashini imwe n’urudodo rwawe.
 - Gushiraho umukozi wa Electrum yerekana ku nzira yawe ya Bitcoin Core canke Knots
 - Muri Sparrow, genda kuri **Ibikoresho → Ivyo ukunda → Serveri**
 - Hitamwo **Ibanga Electrum** hanyuma winjize URL ya server yawe


Kuri iyi nama, gukoresha **Public Electrum Server** ni vyiza cane ku bijanye n’ugucuruza kuri testnet. Mu bidukikije vy'ubuhinga bifise amahera nyayo, woshima gukoresha uruzitiro rwawe bwite canke gukoresha umukozi w'ibanga wizigirwa kugira ngo ubone ubuzima bwite bwinshi.

### Gusuzuma imiterere yawe


Igihe umaze gufatanya na Sparrow (canke uwundi murongo wa wallet):



- Suzuma amaderesi yawe:** Sparrow izokwerekana amaderesi y’ukwakira akomoka ku majambo yawe ya seed. Ushobora kugenzura aderesi iri ku gikoresho cawe ca Jade mu kuja ku rubuga rwa "Receive" muri Sparrow hanyuma ugakanda "Display Address"—iyo aderesi ikwiye kuboneka ku rubuga rwa mudasobwa yawe no ku rubuga rwa Jade.



- Gutanga aderesi yo kwakira:** Fyonda kuri **Kwakira** muri Sparrow maze ukope aderesi yawe ya mbere yo kwakira Bitcoin.



- Iteguye gukoresha:** Ivyuma vyawe wallet ubu biratunganijwe neza kandi biteguye kwakira no gusinya amafaranga Bitcoin. Bandanya mu gice gikurikira kugira ngo wimenyereze gusinya ku giciro ca testnet.
