Muri iyi nyigisho, nzogufasha gutegura Minibits Wallet kugira ukoreshe ecash. Ikoranabuhanga rikomeye ryo kwishura ryibanda ku buzima bwite rikorana na Bitcoin. Minibits ni ecash na Lightning Wallet ishobora gutuma umuntu ashobora gutanga amahera mu kanya nk’ako gukubita, ataco amaze, kandi ataco akora, bikaba bituma ari vyiza ku bikorwa vya misi yose aho ubuzima bwite buhambaye.


Imbere y’uko twisuka muri Minibits, reka dushireho ugutahura neza ico ecash ari co n’ico atari co. Abantu benshi bafatanya ecash n’ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin canke Blockchain, ariko ni ivyiyumviro bitandukanye.


Amafaranga NTIYARI Bitcoin. Ntiyisubirira Bitcoin Wallet yawe yo kwikingira ahubwo irayizuza. Ecash SI Blockchain kandi NTIBA kuri Ledger iyo ari yo yose ya bose. Ikintu gitangaje, ecash ATARI ubuhinga bushasha—mu vy’ukuri bwabanjirije urubuga rw’isi yose, n’ivyiyumviro vyateguwe mu myaka ya 1980 na 1990.


Ico ecash IS: incredibly private (amafaranga nta mateka asiga), peer-to-peer (ugutanga amafaranga ataco akora ata bahuza), kandi ikora nk’igikoresho co gutwara amakuru (niba uyifise, ni wewe uyigenzura). Inyungu nyamukuru ni uko ecash ISHOBORA gukoreshwa ata n’imwe iri ku rubuga—uwuyirungitse canke uwuyakira ashobora gukurwa kuri internet mu gihe c’ugucuruza. Ecash ISHOBORA gukorwa n'umuhari umwe canke n'ishirahamwe ry'ibigo vyizigirwa, kandi ni ubuhinga bwiza bwo kwuzuza Bitcoin, bukora ibikorwa bitobito, bikunda gukorwa mu gihe Bitcoin ikora nk'isoko Layer.


Urashobora kubona ko iyi Minibits igereranya `umuti wo kuzigama`, bisobanura ko wizigira umukoresha wa Mint kugira ngo acunge amafaranga yawe. Ivyo bizana ingorane zidasanzwe utegerezwa gutahura imbere y’uko ukomeza.


Umugambi ugaragaza iki ciyumviro gihambaye:


- Iyi Wallet ikwiye gukoreshwa mu bushakashatsi gusa.
- Wallet ni verisiyo ya beta ifise ibikorwa bidatunganye kandi ifise ibibazo bizwi n’ibitazwi.
- Ntuyikoreshe n’amahera menshi cane.
- Amahera abitswe muri Wallet asohorwa n’ishirahamwe ry’amahera .
- wizigira mint ko izoyishigikira na Bitcoin gushika ushize ivyo ufise ku yindi Bitcoin umuravyo Wallet.
- Itegeko rya Cashu Wallet ishira mu ngiro ntiryasubirwamwo cane canke ngo rigeragezwe.


Fata Minibits nk’ivyo Wallet ya misi yose, atari konti yo kuzigama, kandi ntukigere ubika agaciro kanini hano.


## 1️⃣ Gutegura Wallet yawe


Kugira ngo utangure, genda kuri [Urubuga rwa Minibits](https://www.minibits.cash/) aho uzosanga uburyo bwo gukuraho amakuru ku mbuga zose zikomeye. Abakoresha iOS barashobora gukura kuri [App Store](https://testflight.apple.com/join/defJQgTD), mu gihe abakoresha iOS y’Ubumwe bw’Uburayi bafise uburenganzira bwo gushiramwo kuri [Iduka ry’Umwidegemvyo](https://freedomstore.io/). Abakoresha Android barashobora kuronka iyo porogarama muri [Iduka rya Google Play] canke bagakura dosiye ya APK mu [Ibisohoka vya GitHub] (Ibisohoka vya GitHub]


Igihe ushizeho Minibits, uzobona amashusho y’intango asigura ivyiyumviro vy’ishimikiro—urashobora kubisoma canke ukabisimbuka nimba usanzwe umenyereye ubuhinga. Uhejeje izi ntambwe zambere, uzosabwa guhitamwo hagati ya:


- `Ndabibona, njana kuri Wallet` ku bashasha bakoresha canke
- `Gusubizaho Wallet yatakaye` nimba uriko urasubizaho uhereye ku vyo wabitse.


![image](assets/en/01.webp)

Uhejeje gutegura ivy’intango, uzoshika ku rubuga rw’Ingoro, rufise Elements zihambaye nyinshi zo kwibuka. Igishushanyo c'umwirondoro kiri mu mfuruka yo hejuru kigutwara kuri paji y'umwirondoro wawe aho ushobora gushika ku Minibits zawe Wallet Address maze ugahitamwo `batch receive` amahitamwo. Hagati y’ibarabara, uzobona Mints ushobora gukoresha, Minibits mint ikaba yatowe ku buryo busanzwe. Umurongo w’ibikorwa uri musi utanga uburyo bwo kohereza amahera canke amahera y’umuravyo, gucapura kode ya QR, no kwakira amahera. Ubwa nyuma, umurongo wo hasi wo kugenderamwo urimwo inzira ngufi zija ku rubuga rw’Imbere, Amateka y’Ibikorwa, Abahuye n’Imitunganyirize.

## 2️⃣ Gucungera amafaranga


Ku mburabuzi, Minibits mint irakora iyo utanguye porogarama. Ariko rero, kimwe mu bintu bikomeye vya ecash ni ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ama mint menshi kugira ngo haboneke ugusenyura ubutegetsi n’umutekano. Kugira ngo wongereko iyindi mint, genda kuri `Imiterere`, hanyuma uhitemwo `Gucungera mint`, hanyuma ukande kuri `Ongera mint`.


[Bitcoinmints.com](Bitcoinmints.com) itanga urutonde rw’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none biriho n’ibiharuro vy’abakoresha kugira ngo bigufashe guhitamwo uburyo bwiza. Gukoresha imiti myinshi biragabanya ingorane ufise. Iyo igice kimwe c’amahera gishikiwe n’ingorane, amahera yawe ku yindi mahinguriro aguma ashobora gushikwako.


![image](assets/en/04.webp)


## 3️⃣ Gukora backup


Gusubiza inyuma ni intambwe ihambaye cane mu nzira yose yo gutegura. Kugira ngo ushikire amahitamwo yo gucungera, genda kuri `Ivyagezwe`-> `Gucungera` Aha uzosanga amahitamwo abiri y'ingenzi:

1. `Ijambo ryawe rya seed` ririmwo `amajambo 12` rigufasha gusubirana amahera yawe mu gihe igikoresho coba caratakaye. Iyi nkuru ya seed ni urufunguruzo rwawe rw'amahera yose mu bice vyose vy'amahera wongereyeko. Bindike ku bintu bigaragara (impapuro canke ivyuma) hanyuma ubike neza ahantu henshi. Ntukigere ubika ijambo ryawe rya seed mu buryo bwa digitale aho ryoshobora guhungabanywa. Niwiyumvire gusura iyi [nyigisho](https://planb.network/ru/inyigisho/wallet/ububiko/ububiko-ububiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-bubiko-96f9-46e2a7954270) ku buryo bwiza bwo kurinda Wallet yawe.

2. `Wallet backup` irimwo urudodo rurerure rw'ububiko.


**Iciyumviro**: Uzoba ugikeneye ijambo ryawe rya seed igihe ukoresha iyi nkuru kugira ngo ugarure Wallet yawe.

## 4️⃣ Rema ibice bitobito Wallet Address


Inyuma y'aho, genda kuri `Abahuza` aha hepfo maze uhindure `Ibice vyawe vy'agaciro Wallet Address` ukanda kuri `Gihindura` -> `Gihindura Wallet Address`. Injira Address ukunda maze urabe ko iriho.


![image](assets/en/07.webp)


Uhejeje gushinga Address yawe, uzosabwa `Impanuro` ntoyi yo gushigikira umugambi. Naho ivyo ari ubusabe, ndagusavye cane ko wovyiyumvira nimba utegura gukoresha iyo serivisi ubudasiba. Imigambi y’inkomoko yuguruye nka Minibits yizigira infashanyo y’abanyagihugu kugira ngo ikomeze gutegura no gutunganya. N’intererano ntoyi irafasha kugira ngo uwo mugambi urambe.


![image](assets/en/08.webp)


## 5️⃣ Gutegura Nostr


Niba ushaka guha inama abantu ukurikira kuri Nostr, ushobora `Kwongerako urufunguzo rwawe rwa npub` uhisemwo `Abahuza` hanyuma `Public`. Ivyo bihuza Minibits Wallet yawe n’urubuga rwa Nostr, bikaba bishoboza gutanga amahera ata nkomanzi.


Ufise kandi uburenganzira bwo `Gukoresha urutonde rwawe bwite` mu kuja kuri `Ivyagezwe` hanyuma `Ubuzima bwite` kugira ngo ushiremwo Nostr Address yawe bwite n'urufunguzo. Ariko rero, menya neza ko gukora ivyo bizotuma Wallet yawe idashobora kuvugana na minibits.cash Nostr na LNURL Address servers, ivyo bikaba bizibira ibikorwa vya Address vy’umuravyo vyo kwakira zaps n’amahera.


![image](assets/en/09.webp)


## 6️⃣ Kwakira amafranga


Kugira ngo mu ntango uronke amahera, ukeneye kwongerako Wallet yawe biciye ku Invoice y’umuravyo. Iyi nzira iratomoye: kanda kuri `Topup` , winjize `Igiciro` ushaka kwongerako, wongereko `Iciyumviro` , hanyuma ukande kuri `Create Invoice`. Uzoca ukenera kwishura iyi Invoice ukoresheje iyindi Wallet y'umuravyo, ivyo bihindura amahera y'umuravyo ya Bitcoin mu bimenyetso vy'amahera mu Minibits Wallet yawe.


![image](assets/en/10.webp)


## 7️⃣ Wohereze amafranga


Ubu ko umaze gutanga amahera ya Wallet yawe, urashobora kohereza amahera mu buryo bubiri butandukanye.


### Wohereze amahera


Ihitamwo rya mbere ni ugutuma ecash ataco ukora. Kanda kuri `Send` , hanyuma uhitemwo `Send ecash`, winjize `Igiciro` , hanyuma ukande kuri `Create token.` Ivyo bizotuma generate kode ya QR ushobora gusangira n’uwuyironka canke umusabe akoresheje igikoresho ciwe. Uwuzoronka ivyo bimenyetso azobona ivyo bimenyetso biboneka muri Wallet yiwe hafi ubwo nyene, ata mahera ya Blockchain canke ugucererwa kwemeza.


![image](assets/en/11.webp)


### Ishura n'umuravyo


Uburyo bwa kabiri ni ubwo kwishura biciye ku Muravyo. Kanda kuri `Kohereza` , hanyuma uhitemwo `Ishura n'umuravyo`. Ushobora guhitamwo mu `bamenyeshamakuru` bawe ba Nostr(niba warahuje npub yawe), canke winjize/ushireko kode y'ukwishura ya Lightning Address, Invoice, canke LNURL ukoresheje uburyo bwo `Gushiramwo` canke `gupima`. Inyuma yo`Kwemeza`uwuronka, uzosabwa kwinjiza `Igiciro co kwishura`, wongereko inyandiko, hanyuma ukande kuri `Kwemeza` ukurikiwe na `Ishura ubu` kugira ngo urangize igikorwa ca Lightning.


![image](assets/en/12.webp)


## 8️⃣ Gukora uruja n'uruza rwa NWC


Ikindi kintu gikomeye ca Minibits ni ubushobozi bwo gukora amahuzu ya `Nostr Wallet Connect (NWC)`, atuma izindi porogaramu zisaba amahera kuri Wallet yawe ata mfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga zigaragaza.


Kugira ngo ushireho ivyo, genda kuri `Imiterere`, hanyuma uhitemwo `Nostr Wallet Ihuza`, hanyuma ukande kuri `Ongera ihuza`. Izina ry'ihuriro ryawe ikintu kidondora kugira ngo umenye porogaramu na konti y'ukoresha ijana. Gushinga umupaka w'umunsi kugira ngo ugenzure ingene ushobora gukoresha amahera biciye muri iri huriro, hanyuma ukande kuri `Bika` kugira ngo urangize gutegura.


Iyi nzira ni ngirakamaro cane cane ku bikorwa nka Nostr Clients aho ushaka gukoresha gutanga amahera ataco wemeye n’amaboko ku bikorwa vyose.
