Ahandi ho, wallet yoba yerekana ahantu hanini cane ho gutera, bikongera cane ingorane zo kwibwa amafaranga yawe. **Ni inama rero cane yo kudarungika amahera kuri wallet ivana n’ijambo ry’ugusubirana wewe ubwawe.** Naho woba ukurikije iyi nyigisho gushika kw’ikete, hariho ingorane y’uko iryo jambo ry’ugusubirana ryoshobora guhungabana. **Rero, iyi nyigisho ntikwiye gukoreshwa mu kurema wallet nyayo.** Gukoresha wallet y’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none muri iki gikorwa biratera ingorane nke cane, kuko bitera ijambo offline, kandi abahinga mu vy’ubuhinga bw’amabanga nyabo barihweje gukoresha amasoko y’ubuhinga bw’ubuhinga.


Iyi nyigisho ishobora gukurikirwa ku ntumbero z’igerageza gusa ku bijanye no kurema wallet y’ibinyoma, ata ntumbero yo kuyikoresha n’ama bitcoins nyayo. Ariko rero, ivyo bintu vyabaye biratanga ivyiza bibiri:



- Ica mbere, biratuma utahura neza uburyo buri ku musozo wa Bitcoin wallet yawe;
- Ica kabiri, biragufasha kumenya ingene wobikora. Si mvuze ko umusi umwe bizogira akamaro, ariko birashobora!


## Invugo y’ukwibuka ni iki?


Invugo yo gusubirana, na yo rimwe na rimwe yitwa "mnemonic," "invugo ya seed" canke "invugo y'ibanga," ni urutonde rusanzwe rugizwe n'amajambo 12 canke 24, ruvugwa mu buryo bw'ibinyoma buva ku isoko y'ubuhinga. Urutonde rw'ibintu vy'ibinyoma rwama rwuzuye n'umubare w'ibintu.


Ijambo ry'ukwibuka, hamwe n'ihitamwo passphrase, rikoreshwa mu gukuraho imfunguruzo zose zijanye n'i HD (Hierarchical Deterministic) wallet. Ivyo bisigura ko kuva muri iri jambo, bishoboka guhingura no gusubira kurema imfunguruzo zose z’ibanga n’iza bose za Bitcoin wallet, bigatuma umuntu ashobora kuronka amahera ajanye na yo.

![mnemonic](assets/notext/1.webp)

Intumbero y’iri rungane ni ugutanga uburyo bworoshe bwo gukoresha bwo gusubiza inyuma no gusubiza amafaranga y’ama bitcoins. Ni ngombwa cane ko iryo jambo ry’uruyeri riguma ahantu hatagira umutekano, kuko umuntu wese afise iryo jambo yoshobora kuronka amahera y’igitabu wallet gihuye. Iyo ikoreshejwe mu bijanye n’Ikigereranyo ca wallet, kandi ata passphrase y’ubuhinga, akenshi iba ari SPOF (Single Point Of Failure).

Kenshi, iri jambo riguhabwa ataco rihinduye igihe urema wallet yawe, na porogarama canke ibikoresho wallet ikoreshwa. Ariko rero, birashoboka kandi ko ushobora gukora iyo nteruro wewe nyene, hanyuma ukayishira ku nfashanyo yatowe kugira ngo ubone imfunguruzo za wallet. Ivyo nivyo tuzokwiga gukora muri iyi nyigisho.


## Gutegura ibikoresho bikenewe 


Ku bijanye no kurema ijambo ryawe ryo gukira n'ukuboko, uzokenera:



- Urupapuro rw’urupapuro;
- Ikaramu canke ikaramu, vyiza ni amabara atandukanye kugira ngo bishobore gutunganya ibintu;
- Ivyuma vyinshi, kugira ngo bigabanye ingorane zo gutera imbere zijanye n’urupfu rudafise uburinganire;
- [Urutonde rw’amajambo 2048 BIP39](https://github.com/PlanB-Irezo/Ivyigwa-vy’Ivyigwa/Ibirimwo/Ivyigwa/Ibikoresho/Ibikoresho/Ibikoresho/Ibikoresho/Ibikoresho/Ibikoresho/Ibikoresho/Ibikoresho/Ibikoresho.pdf) ryacapuwe.


Hanyuma, gukoresha orodinateri ifise igikoresho co gupima bizoba ari ngombwa kugira ngo umuntu abare umubare w’ibintu bigenzurwa. Ni co gituma nyene ngira impanuro yo kudakoresha amajambo y’ivy’ukwibuka n’amaboko. N’uko mbona, gufasha mudasobwa, mbere no mu gihe c’ukwiyubara kwavuzwe muri iyi nyigisho, birongereza cane ubugoyagoye bwa wallet.


Ku buryo bwo kugerageza bujanye n'"wallet y'ibinyoma", birashoboka gukoresha mudasobwa yawe isanzwe n'igikoresho cayo. Ariko rero, ku buryo bukomeye cane bugamije kugabanya ingorane zo gusenyura ijambo ryawe, ivyiza vyoba ari ugukoresha PC idakorana na internet (vyiza ni uko ata gice ca wifi canke RJ45 wired connection), ifise ibikoresho bikeyi (vyose bikwiye gukoreshwa n’intsinga, kugira ngo wirinde gukwiragiza Lix am all distribution), na [Tails](html), yatanguye ku buryo bushobora gukurwako.


![mnemonic](assets/notext/2.webp)


Mu gihe nyaco, vyoba vyiza cane ko ubona ko ahantu ukorera hari ibanga mu guhitamwo ahantu kure y’amaso y’abantu, ata bantu bagenda, kandi ata ma kamera (ama webcam, amatelefone...).

Ni vyiza gukoresha umubare munini w’ibice vy’umubiri kugira ngo ugabanye ingaruka z’urupfu rushobora kuba rudafise uburinganire ku bijanye n’uburemere bw’ibintu. Imbere y’uko zikoreshwa, birakenewe ko umuntu asuzuma izo nkoko: ivyo bishobora gushikako mu kuzigerageza mu gitereko c’amazi yuzuye umunyu, bigatuma iyo nkoko ishobora kureremba. Hanyuma ubandanye uzunguruka urupfu rumwe rumwe incuro nka mirongo ibiri mu mazi y’umunyu, wihweze ingene bivamwo. Nimba mu maso hamwe canke habiri habonetse mu buryo budahuye n’ubundi, nushiremwo ikigeragezo n’imizingo myinshi. Ivyavuyemwo bigabanywa mu buryo bumwe vyerekana ko iyo die ari iyo kwizigirwa. Ariko rero, nimba ubuso bumwe canke bubiri buhora buganza, izo nkoko zikwiye gushirwa ku ruhande, kuko zishobora gutuma entropie y’amajambo yawe y’ukwibuka ihungabana, bigatuma n’umutekano wa wallet yawe uhungabana.

Mu bihe vy’ukuri, umaze gukora ivyo bipimo, woba witeguriye gutanga entropie ikenewe. Ku bijanye n’igerageza ry’ibinyoma wallet ryakozwe nk’igice c’iyi nyigisho, woshobora gusiba ivyo biteguro.


## Ivyibutsa bike ku majambo yo gukira


Kugira ngo dutangure, tuzosubiramwo ivy'ishimikiro vyo kurema ijambo ry'ukwibuka hakurikijwe BIP39. Nk’uko vyasiguwe imbere y’aho, iyo mvugo ikomoka ku makuru y’ibinyoma y’ubunini bumwe, ayo makuru yongerako umubare w’ibintu kugira ngo amenye neza ko ari ay’ukuri.


Ubwinshi bw'ayo makuru y'intango, akenshi yitwa "entropie", bugenwa n'umubare w'amajambo ushaka kuronka mu mvugo yo gusubirana. Ivyiyumviro bikunze gukoreshwa cane ni amajambo y’amajambo 12 n’amajambo 24, akomoka ku nsiguro y’ibice 128 n’ibice 256. Aha niho hari imbonerahamwe yerekana ubunini butandukanye bw’uburemere bw’ibintu hakurikijwe BIP39:


| Phrase (words) | Entropy (bits) | Checksum (bits) | Entropy + Checksum (bits) |
| -------------- | -------------- | --------------- | ------------------------- |
| 12             | 128            | 4               | 132                       |
| 15             | 160            | 5               | 165                       |
| 18             | 192            | 6               | 198                       |
| 21             | 224            | 7               | 231                       |
| 24             | 256            | 8               | 264                       |

Entropie rero ni umubare w’imburakimazi uri hagati y’ibice 128 n’ibice 256. Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzofata akarorero k’invugo y’amajambo 12, aho entropie ari 128 bits, bisobanura ko tuzotanga urutonde rw’ibice 128 `0`s canke `1`s. Ivyo bigereranya umubare ugizwe n’imibare 128 iri mu rwego rwa 2 (binaire).

Hashingiwe kuri iyo entropie, umubare w’isuzuma uzosohoka. Igitigiri c’igenzura ni agaciro kaharuwe hakurikijwe amakuru, gakoreshwa mu kugenzura ubutungane n’ukuri kw’ayo makuru mu gihe yoherezwa canke abikwa. Checksum algorithms zigenewe kumenya amakosa canke ihinduka ry’amakuru ry’impanuka.

Ku bijanye n’ijambo ryacu ry’ukwibuka, igikorwa c’umubare w’isuzuma ni ukubona amakosa yose yo kwinjiza igihe winjiza iryo jambo muri porogarama ya wallet. Igitigiri c'igenzura kitari co kigaragaza ko hari ikosa mu mvugo. Ku rundi ruhande, igiharuro c’isuzuma gikwiriye kirerekana ko iyo mvugo ishobora kuba ari yo.


Kugira ngo umuntu aronke iyo checksum, entropie ica mu gikorwa ca SHA256 hash. Iyi nzira itanga urutonde rw'ibice 256 nk'igisohoka, muri ivyo ibice vya mbere `N` vyonyene ni vyo bizogumaho, `N` bivanye n'uburebure bwifuzwa bw'ijambo ry'ugusubirana (raba imbonerahamwe iri hejuru). Gutyo, ku nteruro y’amajambo 12, ibice 4 vya mbere vyo muri hash bizogumaho.

![mnemonic](assets/en/3.webp)

Ivyo bice 4 vya mbere, bikora umubare w’igenzura, bizoca vyongerwa ku ntungamubiri y’intango. Kuri iyi ntambwe, ijambo ry’ugukira rirashinzwe mu buryo nyabwo, ariko riracari mu buryo bw’ibice bibiri. Kugira ngo iyo nzira ibiri ihindurwe mu majambo bihuye n’ingingo ngenderwako ya BIP39, tuzobanza gucapura iyo nzira mu bice vy’ibice 11.

![mnemonic](assets/notext/4.webp)

Imwe muri izo mpapuro igereranya umubare uri muri binaire uzoca uhindurwa umubare w’icumi (ishingiro 10). Tuzokwongerako `1` ku mubare wose, kuko mu guharura, guharura gutangura kuri `0`, ariko urutonde rwa BIP39 ruharurwa gutangura kuri `1`.


![mnemonic](assets/notext/5.webp)


Ubwa nyuma, umubare uri mu gice c’icumi utubwira aho ijambo rihuye n’iryo jambo riri muri [urutonde rw’amajambo 2048 BIP39] Igisigaye ni uguhitamwo aya majambo kugira ngo dukore ijambo ryo gusubizaho ry'igitabu cacu ca wallet.


![mnemonic](assets/notext/6.webp)


None, reka tugende ku kwimenyereza! Tuzotanga amajambo 12 yo gukira. Ariko rero, iyo nzira iguma ari imwe ku bijanye n’invugo y’amajambo 24, kiretse ko yosaba ibice 256 vy’ubuhinga bw’ubuhinga n’ibice 8 vy’ubuhinga bwo gusuzuma, nk’uko vyerekanwa mu mbonerahamwe y’uburinganire iri mu ntango y’iki gice.


## Intambwe ya 1: Gutuma habaho Entropi


Tegura urupapuro rwawe, ikaramu yawe, n’ibice vyawe. Kugira ngo dutangure, tuzokenera gutanga ibice 128 mu buryo butandukanye, ni ukuvuga urutonde rw'ibice 128 `0`s na `1`s mu murongo. Kugira ngo ivyo tubishikeko, tuzokoresha amadayimoni.

![mnemonic](assets/notext/7.webp)


Amadayisi afise impande 6, zose zifise ubushobozi bumwe bwo gutera. Ariko rero, intumbero yacu ni ugutanga igisubizo c’ibintu bibiri, bisobanura ingaruka zibiri zishoboka. Rero, tuzotanga agaciro `0` ku ruzitiro rwose rugwa ku mubare w'ibiharuro, na `1` ku mubare w'ibiharuro vyose. Ivyo bizotuma dukora imizingo 128 kugira ngo dukore entropie yacu y’ibice 128. Iyo urupfu rwerekana `2`, `4`, canke `6`, tuzokwandika `0`; kuri `1`, `3`, canke `5`, bizoba `1`. Ico ciyumviro cose kizokwandikwa mu buryo bukurikirana, kuva ibubamfu gushika iburyo, kuva hejuru gushika hasi.


Kugira ngo tworohereze intambwe zikurikira, tuzoshira hamwe izo bice mu mapakete ane n’atatu, nk’uko vyerekanwa ku ishusho iri musi. Umurongo wose utegerezwa kuba ufise ibice 11: amapaki 2 y’ibice 4 n’imwe y’ibice 3.


![mnemonic](assets/notext/8.webp)


Nk’uko mubibona mu karorero kanje, ijambo rya cumi na kabiri ubu rigizwe n’ibice 7 gusa. Ivyo bizoheza bikarangizwa n’ibice 4 vy’umubare w’igenzura mu ntambwe ikurikira kugira ngo bibe ibice 11.

## Intambwe ya 2: Guharura umubare w'ibintu

## Intambwe ya 3: Guhindura amajambo mu nkuru y'icumi


Kugira ngo umurongo wose w’ibice bibiri duhindure umubare w’icumi, tuzokoresha uburyo bworohereza umuntu guharura n’amaboko. Ubu, ufise imirongo cumi n'ibiri ku mpapuro zawe, imwe yose igizwe n'imibare 11 `0` canke `1`. Kugira ngo ukomeze guhindura mu cumi, ushire kuri buri mubare wa mbere agaciro `1024` nimba ari `1`, ahandi ho `0`. Ku mubare wa kabiri, agaciro `512` kazohabwa iyo ari `1`, ahandi ho `0`, n'ibindi gushika ku mubare wa cumi n'umwe. Amakete yanditswe ni aya:



- Igice ca mbere: `1024`;
- Igice ca 2: `512`;
- Igice ca 3: `256`;
- Igice ca 4: `128`;
- Igice ca 5: `64`;
- Igice ca 6: `32`;
- Igice ca 7: `16`;
- igice ca 8: `8`;
- igice ca 9: `4`;
- Igice ca 10: `2`;
- Igice ca 11: `1`.


Ku murongo wose, tuzokwongerako agaciro gahuye n’imibare `1` kugira ngo turonke umubare w’icumi ungana n’umubare w’ibice bibiri. Reka dufate akarorero k'umurongo w'ibice bibiri ungana na:


## Intambwe ya 4: Gushaka Amajambo yo mu Rurimi rwa Mnemonic


Hamwe n’imibare y’icumi twaronse, ubu turashobora kurondera amajambo ahuye n’ayo muri urwo rutonde kugira ngo dukore ijambo ry’intangamarara. Ariko rero, igitigiri c'amajambo 2048 ari muri list ya BIP39 kiva kuri `1` gushika kuri `2048`. Ariko, ibisubizo vyacu vy'ibiharuro bibiri biva kuri `0` gushika kuri `2047`. Hariho rero uguhinduka kw’umugwi umwe gukeneye gukosorwa. Kugira ngo ukosore iyo mpinduka, wongereko gusa `1` ku mibare cumi n'ibiri y'icumi yari yaraharuwe mbere.


![mnemonic](assets/notext/24.webp)


Inyuma y’iryo hinduka, urafise urutonde rw’ijambo ryose riri muri urwo rutonde. Igisigaye ni ukumenya ijambo ryose ku mubare waryo. Biragaragara ko nk’uko biri ku zindi ntambwe zose, udategerezwa gukoresha mudasobwa yawe kugira ngo ukore iyo mpinduka. Ku bw’ivyo, nurabe neza ko waracapye urwo rutonde imbere y’igihe.


[**-> Sohora urutonde rwa BIP39 mu buryo bwa A4.**]


Nk’akarorero, nimba umubare ukomoka ku murongo wa mbere ari 1721, ijambo rihuye na ryo rizoba ari irya 1721 ku rutonde:


```plaintext
1721. strike
```


![mnemonic](assets/notext/25.webp)

Muri ubwo buryo, turabandanya dukurikirana n’amajambo 12 kugira ngo twubake ijambo ryacu ry’ukwibuka.

## Intambwe ya 5: Guhingura Bitcoin Wallet


Muri iki gihe, igisigaye ni ukuzana ijambo ryacu ry’ubuhinga bwo kwibuka muri porogarama ya Bitcoin wallet. Bivanye n’ivyo dukunda, ivyo bishobora gukorwa kuri porogarama yo ku biro kugira ngo uronke wallet ishushe, canke kuri hardware wallet kugira ngo uronke wallet ikanye.


![mnemonic](assets/notext/27.webp)


Mu gihe co kwinjiza ibintu hanze niho gusa ushobora kugenzura ko umubare wawe w’ibintu vy’agaciro ari ukuri. Iyo porogaramu yerekanye ubutumwa nk'ubu `Invalid Checksum`, bisigura ko hari ikosa ryinjiye mu nzira yawe yo kurema. Muri rusangi, iri kosa rikomoka ku kubara nabi mu gihe co guhindura no kwongerako, canke ku makosa yo kwandika igihe winjiza entropie yawe mu gice ca Tails. Bizoba ngombwa ko dusubira gutangura iyo nzira kuva mu ntango kugira ngo akosore ayo makosa.


![mnemonic](assets/notext/28.webp)

Uhejeje kurema wallet yawe, ntukibagire gusubiza inyuma ijambo ryawe ryo gusubirana ku kintu kigaragara, nk’impapuro canke ivyuma, maze usenye urupapuro rwakoreshejwe mu gihe c’uguhingura kwayo kugira ngo ntihagire amakuru ashobora gusohoka.


## Ikibazo kidasanzwe c'amahitamwo y'uruzitiro rw'amadayimoni ku makarita ya Cold


Ivyo bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none wallets biva mu muryango wa Coldcard bitanga [ikintu citwa _Dice Roll_](https://youtu.be/Rc29d9m92xg?si=OeFW2iCGRvxexhK7), kugira ngo ushireho ijambo ry’ugusubirana kwa wallet yawe ukoresheje amadayimoni. Ubu buryo ni bwiza cane kuko buguha ububasha butaziguye ku kurema entropie, ataco usaba gukoresha igikoresho co hanze co kubara checksum nk’uko biri mu nyigisho yacu.


Ariko rero, ibintu vy’ubusuma bwa bitcoin vyaravuzwe vuba cane kubera gukoresha nabi iyo nzira. Nkako, umubare muto cane w’ibipande vy’amadayimoni urashobora gutuma habaho entropie idahagije, mu vy’impwemu bikaba bishoboka gukoresha inguvu z’ububisha ku majambo y’ukwibuka no kwiba amafaranga ajanye na yo. Kugira ngo umuntu yirinde iyo ngorane, birakenewe ko akora n’imiburiburi ibice 99 vy’amadayimoni ku ikarita ya Cold, ivyo bikaba bituma habaho entropie ihagije.


Uburyo bwo gusobanura ibisubizo vyashikirijwe na Coldcard buratandukanye n’ubwo bwashikirijwe muri iyi nyigisho. Naho dusaba ko umuntu akoresha imizingo 128 kugira ngo ashike ku bice 128 vy’umutekano mu nyigisho, Coldcard isaba ko umuntu akoresha imizingo 99 kugira ngo ashike ku bice 256 vy’umutekano. Nkako, mu buryo bwacu, ingaruka zibiri gusa ni zo zishoboka kuri buri ruzitiro rw’amadayimoni: umubare (`0`) canke umubare (`1`). Rero, entropie iterwa n’uruzitiro rumwe rumwe ingana na `log2(2)`. Ku bijanye na Coldcard, ifata mu muzirikanyi amaso atandatu ashoboka y'ibice (kuva kuri `1` gushika kuri `6`), entropie ku ruzitiro ingana na `log2(6)`. Ni co gituma mu nyigisho yacu, dukeneye gukora imizingo myinshi kugira ngo turonke urugero rumwe rw’entropie.


```plaintext
Entropy = number of rolls * log2(number of possible outcomes on the dice)

Coldcard :

Entropy = 99 * log2(6)
Entropy = 255.91

Our tutorial :

Entropy = 128 * log2(2)
Entropy = 128
```
