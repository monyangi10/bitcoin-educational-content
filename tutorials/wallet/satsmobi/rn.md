## Sats.Mobi

SatsMobi ni Wallet ikoresha Telegram, ifise ibikorwa vyose vya Lightning Network (ivy’ububiko) Wallet, yongerako urutonde rw’ibintu biteye umunezero cane. Yavuye kuri Fork ya LightningTipBot ubu itagikoreshwa, iragwa ibiranga vyose mu gihe yongerako ibindi bishasha, gutyo ikaba igira ubuhinga bwa none. Cokimwe na LNTipBot, Sats.Mobi na yo nyene irakira ubuhinga bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bufunguye. Wallet ishobora gutunganirizwa no gucungirwa yigenga mu kuyikora nk’iyi [bubiko](https://github.com/massmux/SatsMobiBot).


Niba ushaka kuyikoresha mu buryo bworoshe, gutangura kuganira kuri Telegram bizokwerekana ko ari bot.

## Ukwakira Sats

Itegeko ryo gukora Invoice no kwakira Sats ni _/invoice_. Sats.Mobi ikorera gusa muri Satoshi, igice gitoyi kuruta ibindi vyose muri Bitcoin; rero kugira ngo ureme Invoice, birakenewe kwandika amahera muri Sats mu gice c’ubutumwa hanyuma ukayohereza mu chat n’iyo bot.

![image](assets/it/08.webp)


Mu karorero gakurikira, hahisemwo kuronka amahera angana na 210 Sats.


![cover](assets/it/09.webp)


Inyuma y’ibihe bikeyi umuntu arindiriye ko Invoice itegurwa, iraboneka nk’inyandiko be n’ikode ya QR. Ukwishura Invoice, Wallet yerekana amahera asigaye. Niba kubera imvo zimwe zimwe umubare wose utavuguruwe, wandike _/balance_ hanyuma ukande urufunguzo `enter`.

## Kurungika Sats


Naho Sats ari umutungo w’agaciro kanini cane, uwo umuntu adakwiye gutandukana nawo ataco akora, Sats.Mobi ituma iki gice gikwegera, gukora ibigeragezo bimwebimwe bigufi (ni ukuvuga, ibikorwa bibiri vy’igerageza) ntibizoba ikibazo.


### Kwishura Invoice


Uburyo bworoshe bwo kwishura Invoice ni ugukopa urudodo rw'ubutumwa `lnbc1xxxxx` ukarushira mu murongo w'ubutumwa umaze kwandika itegeko _/pay_. **Inyishu ibereye** isaba gusiga umwanya inyuma y'itegeko.


![image](assets/it/11.webp)


Wallet irungika ubutumwa busaba kwemezwa. Mu gufyonda kuri _Pay_, Invoice irahembwa.


![image](assets/it/12.webp)


Sats.Mobi ishobora kwizigira node ya Lightning ikora neza kandi ihuriye neza, gake cane amahera yishurwa adashobora kuko yama ishobora kuronka inzira ibereye.


### Kwishura neza ukoresheje telefone ngendanwa


Guca kuri Telegram, Sats.Mobi iraboneka no kuri telefone ngendanwa. Igikorwa kibereye kuruta ibindi vyose co kwishura ukoresheje telefone ngendanwa ni ugucapura kode ya QR, ariko iyi Wallet irayibura kubera uko yakozwe, kuko atari porogarama yigenga ahubwo iri ku rubuga rwo gusangirirako amakuru. Sats.Mobi rero irateguwe kugira ngo yorohereze ubumenyi bwo kuri telefone ngendanwa uko bishoboka kwose: irashobora vy’ukuri gusobanura ishusho, nk’ifoto yafashwe kuri kode ya QR ya Invoice ushaka kwishura.

### Sats.Mobi mu mirwi y'itelegaramu


![image](assets/it/27.webp)


Mu bintu vyatumye LNTipBot imenyekana kandi Sats.Mobi izana kuri Telegram, ni co gituma ubumenyi bushimisha kandi bukorana n’abagize umugwi.

Abafise iyo bot barashobora gutumira iyo bot ngo yinjire mu kiganiro c’umugwi hanyuma bagatora Sats.Mobi ngo abe umuyobozi. Kuva ico gihe, umunezero uratangura, kuko abanywanyi bashobora gutangura guhemba abandi bakoresha kubera intererano bagize mu mugwi.


- _/tip_ yongerako impanuro mu kwishura ubutumwa;
- _/send_ yohereza amafaranga yerekana LN Address canke igikoresho ca Telegaramu nk'uwuyakira;
- _/faucet_ (mu _/advanced_ menu) iremesha gukora urutonde rw'impanuro abagize umugwi bashobora gukusanya mu gufyonda kuri _/collect_;
- _/tipjar_ (mu _/advanced_ menu) irema ubundi bwoko bw'ugukwiragiza bushobora kwoherezwa ku bakoresha bo mu mugwi.


Imwe muri ayo mategeko irafise insiguro yayo, ikaba isigurwa mu rutonde rw’amabwirizwa nyamukuru.


Kandi nimba tutari nyen’umugwi? Nta kibazo: usabe gusa uwashinze uwo mugwi atumire Sats.Mobi, uwushiremwo nk'umuyobozi w'umugwi, vyose bibe vyateguwe!


## Ikibanza co kugurisha (POS)


Igihe Sats.Mobi itanguzwa ku ncuro ya mbere, iyo bot irarema kandi ikindi kintu ku muntu ayikoresha: **the POS**. "Igikoresho" gikoreshwa n'uwukoresha akoresheje itegeko _/pos_ canke akafyonda ku buto bujanye n'ivyo buva kuri console iri hasi iburyo. Nkako, POS ni porogarama y’urubuga, ifunguka nk’ikintu kigaragara ku kiganiro ca Telegram .


![image](assets/it/14.webp)


Interface yerekana igikoresho c’umuntu ku giti ciwe Telegram hejuru ibubamfu kandi ikoreshwa gusa nk’uko POS zose zikoreshwa: mu kwandika umubare ku rupapuro. Reka dufate ko ubu dushaka gutora amasenti 21 y’amayero ku gikorwa kimwe. Kumenya ko Sats.Mobi gusa icungera Sats, ntivyoroshe gukora uguhindura mu mutwe wawe. Ahubwo, POS yerekana euro nk’igice ca konti, yerekana ico gihe nyene ingana n’iyo muri Satoshi.


![image](assets/it/15.webp)

Ufyondeye kuri _/OK_ ubona Invoice ishobora kwerekwa umukiriya biciye ku kode ya QR, canke ishobora kwoherezwa nk’urudodo biciye ku butumwa buca buza, kugira ngo ishobore kwishurwa.

## Ibindi biranga


Hari n’ibindi bimenyetso biheza ishikanwa rya Sats.Mobi Wallet, nk’uko twabibonye, ​​ryagura iciyumviro ca Wallet birenze ibikorwa vyo kwakira no kohereza amahera:


- _/nostr_: gufatanya Wallet n'umukoresha wawe bwite wa Nostr kugira ngo uronke zaps;
- _/cashback_: yerekana kode ishobora gushikirizwa umucuruzi kugira ngo aronke amahera y'inyuma ku kintu aguze;
- _/buy_: itanguye uburyo burongowe muri bot, bushobora kugura Sats ku ma euro;
- _/activatecard_: gusaba gukoresha ikarita y’ideni ya NFC, ishobora gusubirwamwo amahera biciye ku Sats.Mobi Wallet kandi amatangazo ashobora gukoreshwa;
- _/link_: irema uruja n'uruza rwa Zeus yawe canke Blue Wallet, ishobora gukoreshwa nk'ibikoresho vyo gukoresha kure kuri iyi Wallet.


## Iciyumviro

Sats.Mobi ni Wallet iryoshe kandi iryoshe gukoresha, igarura ubumenyi yari afise na LNTipBot ikoresheje ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa none vya LNBits. Ariko rero, birahambaye kwibuka ko **ari igikorwa co kubungabunga**. Rero, ikwiye gukoreshwa mu gufata Sats nke cane, si Wallet nyamukuru y’amahera yawe ya Lightning Network. Hariho kandi n’umupaka w’ubushobozi bw’imbere, ungana n’ibihumbi 500 vya Sats, umupaka usabwa ko udashobora kuwurenga.


Niba uriko urarondera ama wallet Lightning Network atagira ububiko, ni vyiza cane ko uraba ibindi bikoresho.
