Umuragwa wemewe ata gatotsi ntazokwigera ashobora kuronka ayo mahera ata izo mfunguruzo afise, mu gihe umuntu w’umunyaruyeri afise ayo mabanga azoshobora kuyakoresha ata n’umwe afise uburenganzira bwo kuyakoresha.

## Imigambi yo kwiyandikisha Bitcoin Keeper



Bitcoin Keeper ikora ku buryo bwa freemium bufise ingero zitatu z’ukwiyandikisha zitanga ibikorwa bitera imbere. Kugira ngo ushikire imigambi, genda kuri **Ibindi**, hanyuma ukande kuri gahunda yawe y'ubu (imbere ni "Pleb") kugira ngo ufungure **Manage Subscription**.



![Plans d'abonnement](assets/fr/01.webp)



**Itegeko rya Pleb** (ry’ubuntu) ritanga uburyo bwo gushika ku bintu vy’ingenzi: guhingura amasakoshi y’urufunguzo rumwe n’urufunguzo rwinshi ataco amaze, guhuza n’amasakoshi yose akomeye y’ibikoresho (Coldcard, Trezor, Ledger, Jade, Tapsigner...), kugenzura ibiceri, gushirako amazina, no gufatanya na Electrum y’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Iyi gahunda irahagije ku gukoresha mu buryo busanzwe mbere no ku mirongo ya kera ya multi-sig.



**Itegeko rya Hodler** (€9.99/ukwezi, n’ukwezi kumwe ku buntu iyo wishyuye buri mwaka) ririmwo ibintu vyose vya Pleb kandi ryongerako amakuru yashizwe mu gicu (iCloud canke Google Drive) kugira ngo usubizeho amasafe yawe ku gikoresho ico ari co cose, Urufunguzo rwa Server kugira ngo wongereko amategeko y’ugukoresha amahera yihuta be n’amategeko y’ugukoresha amahera n’ibindi bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none. gushika ku mfunguruzo zawe.



**Itegeko ry’amaboko y’amadayimoni** (€29.99/ukwezi, n’ukwezi kumwe ku buntu iyo wishyuye buri mwaka) ni co gice cose co gutegura ivy’ubutaka. Harimwo umugambi wose wa Hodler kandi ugafungura Urufunguzo rw’Iragi (ugukora kwasubitswe), Urufunguzo rw’Ivyihutirwa (urufunguzo rw’Ivyihutirwa rwo gusubirana iyo utakaje), ibikoresho vyo Gutegura Iragi n’inyandiko, n’uguhamagara n’umugwi w’Abacungezi kugira ngo wemeze uko watunganije. Ivyo nivyo bihembo ku ba bitcoiners bipfuza gutanga iragi ryabo ku mayaruka menshi.



Iciyumviro gihambaye: amazu y’ububiko waremye azoguma ashobora gushikwako naho wosubira ku mugambi w’ubuntu. Ivyo utunganya bishingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako zifunguye (BSMS, Miniscript) kandi bikora bitandukanye n’ukwiyandikisha kwawe.



## Inyandiko z'iragi



Umaze gukoresha uburenganzira bwawe bwa Diamond Hands, genda mu gice ca **Inyandiko z’Iragi** uhereye ku rubuga rw’Ibindi. Bitcoin Keeper itanga inyandiko zitanu z’akarorero zo gutunganya umugambi wawe w’itongo, hamwe n’igice c’impanuro:



![Documents d'héritage](assets/fr/02.webp)





- Igishushanyo c'amajambo y'imbuto**: igishushanyo co kwandika neza amajambo yawe yo gukira mu buryo butunganijwe
- Abantu wizigirwa**: akarorero ko gutanga urutonde rw’amakuru y’abantu wizigirwa bari mu mugambi wawe (umunyamabanga, umuvoka, abazungu, abazigama urufunguruzo)
- Urufunguzo rw’Iyongera rwo Gusangira**: inyandiko idondora amakuru y’ubuhinga y’urufunguzo rumwe rumwe: kode ya PIN, inzira y’aho rwavuye, aho ruri, ubwoko bw’igikoresho, n’ayandi makuru yose ngirakamaro mu kumenya no gukoresha urufunguzo
- Amabwirizwa yo gusubiza amahera**: amabwirizwa y’intambwe ku yindi ku muragwa canke uwuronka inyungu kugira ngo asubize amahera
- Ikete ryandikiwe umuvugizi**: ikete ryuzujwe imbere y’igihe rishobora guhindurwa ry’umuvugizi wawe canke umunyamabanga wawe



Igice ca **Impanuro z’Iragi** kiratanga impanuro ngirakamaro ku bijanye no gucungera imfunguruzo z’abazungu no gutuma umugambi wawe w’iragi ugenda neza.



Izo nyandiko uzihindure kugira ngo zihure n’ivyo urimwo, kandi uzibike ahantu hatagira inkomanzi, zitandukanye n’imfunguruzo ubwazo.



## Kugena ububiko bw'igicu



Imbere yo kurema ububiko bwawe bwa kera, fungura ububiko bw’igicu kugira ngo ukinge amadosiye yawe y’imiterere. Kuva ku rubuga rw'Ibindi, kanda **Igicu c'Umuntu ku giti ciwe**.



![Configuration Cloud Backup](assets/fr/03.webp)



Hitamwo ijambobanga rikomeye kugira ngo ushiremwo amakuru yawe. Iri jambobanga ririnda gusa amadosiye y’imiterere ya wallet (si imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga). Wemeze ijambobanga hanyuma ukande **Wemeze**. Ivyo ubitse bizobikwa kuri iCloud yawe canke kuri Google Drive bivanye n’igikoresho cawe. Kanda **Backup Now** kugira ngo utangure ububiko bwawe bwa mbere.



## Injira imfunguruzo zawe



Ku karorero kacu, tuzokora safe 2-of-3 ifise imfunguruzo zibiri z’inyongera (Iragi n’Ivyihutirwa). Reka dutangure mu kwinjiza imfunguruzo zose zikenewe mu gice ca **Keys**.



![Import des clés hardware](assets/fr/04.webp)



Kanda **Ongera urufunguzo**, hanyuma uhitemwo **Ongera urufunguzo kuva ku gikoresho** kugira ngo uhuze igikoresho wallet. Bitcoin Keeper ifasha ibikoresho vyinshi: BitBox02, Ikarata y’ubukonje, Jade y’amazi, Ibuye ry’urufunguzo, Krux, Ledger, Impapuro z’uruzitiro z’umushinge, Portal mirongo ibiri n’ibiri, Igikoresho co gusinya imbuto, n’Imirongo y’ibisubizo vy’ibinyoma.



Mu mitunganyirize yacu, twinjiza :




- 2 **Ikarata y'ubukonje** imfunguruzo (MK4SP na MK4)
- 2 imfunguruzo **Tapsigner** (Ibarabara ry'umuyagankuba na Genesis)



Kugira ngo wongereko Coldcard, uyihitemwo muri list hanyuma ukurikize amabwirizwa ari ku rubuga kugira ngo urungike urufunguzo rwa bose biciye kuri kode ya QR, dosiye, USB canke NFC. Ku bindi bisobanuro ku buryo bwo gukoresha Coldcard canke Tapsigner, turagusavye urabe inyigisho zacu zihariye:

## Rema iragi wallet



Reka tugende ku kurema umubiri. Kuva ku **Ibikoko**, kanda **Ongerako Wallet**, uhitemwo **Bitcoin Wallet**, hanyuma **Rema Wallet**.



![Création du wallet](assets/fr/05.webp)



Hitamwo ubwoko bwa wallet. Ku mugambi wacu w'iragi, hitamwo **2 muri 3 z'urufunguzo rwinshi**. Mu nsi y’ibarabara, fungura **Amahitamwo y’umutekano** hanyuma ukande **Bandanya**.



![Options de sécurité avancées](assets/fr/06.webp)



Mu mahitamwo y'umutekano wongerewe, reba :




- Urufunguzo rw'Iragi**: urufunguzo rw'inyongera ruzokwongerwa ku rugero rw'abajejwe inyuma y'igihe cashinzwe
- Urufunguzo rw'Ivyihutirwa**: urufunguzo rufise ubugenzuzi bwose busubitswe kugira ngo umuntu asubize amafaranga iyo urufunguzo rutakaye



Kanda **Bika ivyo wahinduye**. Hanyuma uhitemwo imfunguruzo 3 zizogize wallet yawe mu zizanywe hanze (nk’imfunguruzo y’imbuto, Coldcard MK4SP, na Tapsigner Metro).



## Gushinga imisi idasanzwe y'iherezo



Igishushanyo gikurikira kigufasha gutunganya Urufunguzo rw’Ivyihutirwa n’Urufunguzo rw’Iragi. Aha niho usobanura ibicerezwa bigenzura ugukoresha izo mfunguruzo zidasanzwe.



![Configuration des délais](assets/fr/07.webp)



Ku **Urufunguzo rw’Ivyihutirwa**, hitamwo urufunguzo rw’ibikoresho ruzokora nk’ububiko bw’iherezo (aha Coldcard MK4) hanyuma uhitemwo igihe co gukora (mu karorero kacu: imyaka 2). Ubutandukanye n’Urufunguzo rw’Iragi, Urufunguzo rw’Ivyihutirwa ntiruyongera ku quorum: ruguha uburenganzira bwo **guca mu nzira ya multisig** burundu, kandi ruguha ububasha bwose ku mahera inyuma y’aho igihe categekanijwe kirangiriye. Ni umuti wawe wa nyuma: iyo imfunguruzo nyinshi zitakaye canke zisenyutse, urwo rufunguzo rumwe ruragufasha gusubirana vyose. Ritegerezwa rero kurindwa bimwe bikomeye cane.



Ku **Urufunguzo rw’Iragi**, hitamwo urufunguzo rwagenewe umuragwa (aha Coldcard MK4SP) hanyuma uhitemwo igihe co guteba (mu karorero kacu: umwaka 1). Inyuma y’umwaka umwe ata n’umwe yimukiye, urwo rufunguzo **ruzokwongerwa ku rugero rw’abashizeko umukono**. Mu majambo ngirakamaro, wallet yawe 2-of-3 izocika wallet 2-of-4 igihe ico kiringo kizoba caraheze, bizotuma umuragwa ashobora kugira uruhara mu gusinya iruhande y’imfunguruzo zisanzweho.



### None amafunguro y’igihe akora gute?



Bitcoin Keeper ikoresha **ibifungurwa vy'igihe vy'ukuri** (CLTV - GusuzumaIgihe c'Ifunguro), vyashobotse na Miniscript. Udakunze igihe c’ugufunga (CSV), gitangura igihe UTXO yose yakiriwe, igihe c’ugufunga gikorana n’**itariki y’iherezo ry’igihe** isobanurwa igihe wallet iremwa.



Mu majambo nyayo, iyo uremye wallet uno musi ufise urufunguzo rw'iragi rw'umwaka umwe, itariki yo gukoreshwa izoba "uyu musi + umwaka umwe". Amahera yose ashizwe muri iyi wallet, uko itariki yashizwe kwose, azoronka biciye ku Rufunguzo rw’Iragi kuri iyo tariki nyene.



Ivyiza vy’ibifungurwa vy’igihe vy’ukuri ni uko vyemerera igihe co gukora kirenze amezi 15 (umupaka w’ibifungurwa vy’igihe vy’ukuri vya CSV), ivyo bikaba bisigura igituma Bitcoin Keeper ishobora gutanga amahitamwo nk’imyaka 2.



### Uburyo bwo gusubiramwo



Kugira ngo ntukoreshe imfunguruzo zidasanzwe mu buzima bwawe, utegerezwa "gusubiramwo" wallet yawe. Kubera ko hariho igihe co gufunga, ivyo birimwo **gusubira kurema wallet n'itariki nshasha yo guhera** isunikiwe muri kazoza, hanyuma ugashira amahera yawe kuri iyi wallet nshasha.



Bitcoin Keeper yorosha iyo nzira n’igikorwa co gusubiramwo gihuriweko. Iryo koraniro rihita rikora ibintu bigoranye biri inyuma: ukurikiza gusa intambwe ziyobowe, ataco ukeneye gukora n’amaboko wallet nshasha canke ngo ushire amahera wewe nyene. Gutegura ico gikorwa ubudasiba, imbere cane y’uko igihe gitoyi kuruta ibindi vyose categekanijwe kirangira. Nk’akarorero, ufise Urufunguzo rw’Iragi rw’umwaka 1, ushobora gusubiramwo buri mezi 9-10 kugira ngo ugume ufise urugero rw’umutekano.



## Bika kandi wohereze hanze imiterere



wallet imaze kuremwa, iyo porogarama irakwibutsa kubika dosiye y’imiterere. **Iyi ntambwe ni ngirakamaro cane**: ata iyi dosiye, abazungu bawe ntibazoshobora gusubira gukora wallet multisig.



![Export de la configuration](assets/fr/08.webp)



Kanda **Dosiye yo Gusubizaho Wallet**. Amahitamwo menshi yo kwohereza hanze araboneka:




- PDF yoherezwa hanze**: itanga inyandiko yuzuye irimwo amakuru yose ya wallet
- Erekana QR**: yerekana kode ya QR kugira ngo ushiremwo imiterere ku kindi gikoresho
- Airdrop / Dosiye yoherezwa hanze**: yohereza hanze dosiye biciye mu mahitamwo yo gusangira
- NFC**: gusangira biciye kuri NFC n'igikoresho gihuye



Gukuza kopi: imwe kuri notari wawe, iyindi mu gisanduku ca banki, iyindi iri muri verisiyo ya digitale encrypté. wallet yawe nshasha ubu iboneka mu gice c'amasakoshi n'ibimenyetso "Urufunguzo rwinshi", "2 kuri 3", "Urufunguzo rw'iragi" na "Urufunguzo rw'ivyihutirwa".



## Rema igikoko ca Wallet



Canary Wallet ni ubuhinga bwo kugabisha imbere y’igihe. Iciyumviro: urufunguzo rwose rukoreshwa mu rufunguzo rwinshi rwa wallet rurashobora kandi gukoreshwa mu rufunguzo rumwe rwa wallet rutandukanye. Mu gushiramwo amahera make kuri iyi "canary" ya wallet, ukwinyiganza kwose kutarekuriwe kwerekana ko urufunguzo rwacitse intege.



![Canary Wallets](assets/fr/09.webp)



Hari uburyo bubiri bwo gutunganya indege ya Wallet Canary. Kuva ku **Ibindi**, kanda **Ibipapuro vy'i Canary** mu gice ca "Imfunguruzo n'Ibipapuro". Ico gicapo kirasigura ingingo ngenderwako: iyo umuntu yinjiye kuri rumwe mu mfunguruzo zawe maze akabona amahera mu rufunguzo rumwe rwa wallet rujanye n’urwo rufunguzo, azogerageza kurukuraho, ivyo bikaba bizogumenyesha.



![Configuration Canary depuis une clé](assets/fr/10.webp)



Ushobora kandi gutunganya Canary uhereye ku rufunguzo. Mu **Imfunguruzo**, hitamwo urufunguzo (nk'akarorero Tapsigner Genesis), ukande **Imiterere** (ivyuma), hanyuma **Canary Wallet**. Igikoko citwa wallet gifatanye n’ico gikoko kirafunguka, citeguriye kwakira amasatoshis amwamwe y’ugucungera.



Ushire amahera makeyi (ibihumbi bikeyi vy’amasatoshi) kuri buri ndege ya Canary Wallet. Iyo ayo mahera yimukiye ata masezerano yawe, ubwo nyene ukureho urufunguzo rwacitse mu bibanza vyawe vy’amahera vyinshi.



## Ibikorwa vyiza



**Gerageza configuration yawe** n'amahera makeyi imbere yo kuyishiramwo amahera menshi. Wohereze ibihumbi bikeyi vy’amasatoshi mu nzu y’ububiko, hanyuma ugerageze gukoresha amahera asohoka kugira ngo urabe ko wamenye neza uburyo bwo gusinya n’igikoresho cose. Kandi gerageza kwinjiza dosiye y’imiterere ku yindi telefone kugira ngo umenye neza ko iyo backup ikora.



**Gutanga imfunguruzo mu bwenge**. Ku ba Tapsigners, bashire mu ibahasha ry’ikidodo rifise PIN imenyeshejwe ukwaryo (nk’akarorero mw’ikete ry’Ibwirizwa ryo Gusubirana ribitswe ahandi). Ku bijanye n’amasakoshi y’ibikoresho vya kera, ushire igikoresho ku muntu agira gatatu wizigirwa be n’igikoresho ca seed ku mpapuro canke ku cuma uri kumwe nawe canke uwundi muntu. Raba urutoke rw’urufunguzo rumwe rumwe n’izina ryarwo muri dosiye y’imiterere kugira ngo ntihagire uwuvyivangako.



**Gutegura ibipimo vy’ibihe** (imyimenyerezo y’umuriro). Buri mwaka, nusuzume ko ushobora gusubira kwubaka iyo safe ukoresheje ama backups kuri telefone idafise ikintu. Igerageza Canary imburi mu kugenzura uburinganire. Kwigana ivyerekeye ukubura ("mbega noba ntakaje Coldcard?") kugira ngo wemeze ko imigwi y'urufunguzo isigaye ihagije.



**Ntimwibagire ivy'ugusubiramwo**. Niba warashizeho Urufunguzo rw’Iragi ryawe ku mwaka 1, n’usubire gusubirana buri mezi 9-10. Ico ni co kiguzi uriha ku bijanye n’ugutanga amakuru ata muntu agira ico akoze.



**Mugume mufise umugambi uri ku gihe**. Ihinduka ryose (gusubirira urufunguzo, guhindura abazungu, guhindura igihe co guhera) ritegerezwa kugaragara mu bitabo vyose vy’inyuma n’inyandiko. Gusubiramwo ama PDF inyuma y’uguhindura kwose no gusubira gutanga verisiyo nshasha.



## Imipaka n'ivyo kwitwararika



Naho ivyo bikoresho bifise ububasha, birahambaye kumenya aho bigarukira kugira ngo ushobore kubikoresha neza uko bishoboka kwose.



**Ugusobanuka** kw’isanduku y’ibintu vyinshi ifise timelocks bishobora kuba ingorane ubwavyo: gutunganya nabi, kudatahura neza n’abazungu, gutakaza ikintu gihambaye mu bihimba vyinshi. Bitcoin Keeper yorosha ubumenyi uko bishoboka kwose, ariko iguma ari igikorwa c’ubuhinga. Gukoresha uwo mugambi gusa nimba amahera azokingirwa ariko arawushingira intahe. Ku bijanye n’amahera makeyi, umugambi woroshe woshobora guhagije.



Ivyo **bishingiye ku gukoresha** birabereye kwiyumvira. Naho iyo kode ari inkomoko yuguruye kandi ishingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako zifunguye (Miniscript, BSMS), hariho ibikorwa bimwebimwe vyishingikiriza ku bidukikije vya Keeper. Bika kopi y’ico gikoresho (Android APK canke iOS IPA) maze wandike mu makete yawe urungikira abazungu ko ushobora gukoresha izindi nkweto zihuye na Miniscript (nka Liana) kugira ngo ubone amahera.



Abacuruzi bizigirwa** bazana ingorane z’umuntu. Bigenda gute iyo incuti afise umugambi mubi ikoresheje urufunguruzo bamushizeko imbere y’uko igihe categekanijwe kigera? Canke iyo uwo muvoka ashize nabi ivyangombwa vyawe? Hitamwo neza abo bantu, ubasigurire neza inshingano zabo, kandi ugire umugambi B. Canary Wallets, redundant backups n’imiterere ya multisig nyene biguma ari uburinzi bwawe bwiza kuri ivyo bibazo.



## Iciyumviro



Bitcoin Keeper, n’umugambi wayo wa Diamond Hands, itanga isandugu yuzuye y’ibikoresho vyo gutegura ivy’ubutaka: Ivyumba vy’ububiko vy’ibintu vyiza bifise imfunguruzo zifise igihe, inyandiko ziherekeza, Amasakoshi y’i Canary, n’ugushigikira umuntu ku giti ciwe.



Ni ikibazo c’ubuhinga gusa: ni ikibazo co guhingura ubuhinga bwo kwubaka itongo ryawe, gutanga imfunguruzo n’ubumenyi mu buryo bw’ubwenge, no kugerageza uburyo buhoraho. Itegeko ry’iragi rya Bitcoin ryateguwe neza rihindura satoshis zawe zikaba iragi nyaryo, rishobora kwimurirwa.
