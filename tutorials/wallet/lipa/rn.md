Bitcoin Lightning Wallet ni porogaramu yo kuri telefone ngendanwa ishobora gukoresha ubwo nyene, ku giciro gito kuri Lightning Network ya Bitcoin. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibikorwa vyo ku Blockchain (On-Chain) nyamukuru, amahera y’umuravyo ari hafi y’aho nyene kandi asaba amahera makeyi, bikaba bituma akwiriye canecane amahera makeyi, y’umusi ku musi.


Amasakoshi y'umuravyo, nk'amasakoshi yose agendagenda, afatwa nk'amasakoshi ya "Hot" kuko ahuye na Internet. Ni co gituma ahanini bigenewe gucunga amahera makeyi ukoresha ku musi ku musi. Ku mahera menshi, ni vyiza gukoresha uburyo bwo kubika butekanye nk’ibipapuro vy’ibikoresho.


Niba wifuza kumenya vyinshi ku vyerekeye Lightning Network no gutahura ingene ikora mu buryo bw’ubuhinga, ndagusavye gufata iri shure:


https://planb.academy/courses/34bd43ef-6683-4a5c-b239-7cb1e40a4aeb

Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko turaraba **Lipa**, Lightning Wallet yoroshe kandi ikora neza yakozwe mu Busuwisi.


## Turabamenyesha Lipa


Lipa ni Lightning Wallet idashobora gucungirwa itandukanye n'uko ikoreshwa mu buryo bworoshe kandi idashobora gukoreshwa na Interface. Yateguwe n’umugwi w’Abasuwisi, ishimika ku bijanye n’ibanga no ku buryo bworoshe gukoresha abatangura.


Ibirango vy'ingenzi birimwo:




- Umukoresha asobanutse Interface
- Ubuyobozi bwigenga bw'umurongo w'umuravyo
- Infashanyo y'amasezerano ya LNURL
- Ubushobozi bwo kugura bitcoins mu buryo butaziguye muri porogarama

Intambwe ya mbere ni ugukuraho porogarama ya Lipa. Kugeza ubu, iboneka kuri iOS gusa :




- [Ku bwa Pome](iporogarama.


Verisiyo ya Android ubu iriko irategurwa kandi izoboneka vuba.


![Installation de Lipa](assets/fr/01.webp)


Umaze gufungura iyo porogarama, uzoshika ku rubuga rw’intango, ruzoguha uburyo bubiri:




- Rema Wallet nshasha
- Kugarura Wallet iriho kuva ku bubiko


Umaze guhitamwo uburyo ushaka, iyo porogarama iragusaba gufungura amamenyesha. Iyi ntambwe ni ngirakamaro, kuko amatangazo ari ngombwa kuri :




- Kwakira imburi igihe amahera yashitse, mbere n’igihe ubusabe bufunze
- Kumenyeshwa intambwe zijanye n’ugugura Bitcoin biciye ku muti wabo wuzuye


Iryo koraniro rero rigaragaza ibikorwa vyaryo nyamukuru biciye ku rutonde rw’ibimenyetso vy’intango:




- Ikete ry’ukwishura ritagira umurongo**: Abakoresha barashobora kwakira amahera y’i Bitcoin n’igihe porogarama ifunze, bikaba vyemeza ko ari ukwizigirwa kandi ko bimeze neza.
- Amaderesi ya Lightning atagira ububiko**: Lipa ubu irashigikira aderesi za Lightning zitagira ububiko, igatuma ubuzima bwite n’umutekano bikomera mu guha abakoresha ububasha bwose ku bitcoins zabo.
- Gucungera amakuru y’isesengura**: Kubera ko uguseruka n’ibanga ari vyo bihambaye cane, abakoresha barashobora kubona ubwoko bw’amakuru yashizwe hamwe no guhitamwo ivyo bakunda gusangira.
- Wohereze biciye ku nimero ya telefone**: Nta maderesi akenewe - uhitemwo gusa umuntu wo guhamagara, winjize amahera, hanyuma wohereze ama bitcoins ku nimero yabo ya telefone.


Iryo koraniro kandi ryungukira ku gutera imbere gukomeza mu bijanye n’ugushikama, umutekano n’ukwizigirwa, kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukoresha neza.


## Gukoresha ingendo


Interface ya Lipa itunganijwe hirya no hino y’ibice 4 bihambaye bishobora gushikwako biciye ku murongo wo kugenderamwo uri musi y’igicapo:


![Navigation principale](assets/fr/02.webp)




- Inzu**: Igaragaza uburinganire bwawe n'amateka y'ibikorwa vyawe
- Scanner**: Igufasha gucapura amakode ya QR kugira ngo wishyure
- Ikarata**: Igaragaza ikarata y'ubucuruzi bwemeza Bitcoin mu karere kawe
- Amagenamiterere**: Ushobora gushika ku mategeko y'ikoreshwa, ububiko n'ivyo ukunda


Iyindi menyu ishobora gushikwako mu gukura hasi igicapo c'intango:


![Menu supplémentaire](assets/fr/03.webp)


Iki kimenyetso kigaragaza ibindi bikorwa nka :




- Gugura ama bitcoins
- On-Chain Bitcoin ibiziga
- Gukora inyemezabuguzi y'umuravyo kugira ngo wakire bitcoins
- Umuravyo Invoice kwishura


## Bika Wallet yawe


Kugira ngo ukore ububiko bwa Wallet yawe, genda kuri "Imiterere" maze uhitemwo "Ijambo ryo gusubizaho". Lipa akoresha ijambo ry’ugukira rikenewe cane kwandika neza ku kintu kigaragara (impapuro, ivyuma). Iri jambo ni ryo ryonyene ryo gusubiza amahera yawe iyo telefone yawe yazimiye canke yibwe. Kugira ngo wemeze ububiko bwawe, iyo porogarama izogusaba kwemeza amajambo 3 ataco amaze avuye mu nteruro yawe.


![Backup](assets/fr/04.webp)


Kugira ngo uronke ibindi bisobanuro ku buryo bwo gusubiza inyuma no gucunga neza ijambo ryawe ryo gusubirana, ndagusavye cane gukurikira iyi yindi nyigisho, cane cane nimba uri umutanguzi:


https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

## Kwakira ama bitcoins


Kugira ngo uronke ama bitcoins, ufise uburyo bubiri. Kugira ngo ushikire ayo mahitamwo, subira ku gicapo c’intango maze ukure hasi igicapo. Hanyuma ushobora :




- Hitamwo "Kwimurira BTC" kugira ngo ubone amafaranga On-Chain. Hanyuma rero, ushireko gusa scanner ya QR code n’iyindi Wallet yawe maze uheze gutanga.
- Hitamwo "Gusaba" kugira ngo uronke biciye kuri Lightning Network hanyuma winjize amahera wipfuza kwakira.


Muri ivyo bihe vyose, uzobwirizwa kwishura amahera angana na 0,4% y’amahera, canke hafi 2.500 Sats nimba iyo nzira itegerezwa gufungura inzira nshasha yo kwishura (ivyo bizoba ata kabuza ku kwishura kwa mbere).


![Recevoir des bitcoins on chain](assets/fr/05.webp)


![Recevoir des bitcoins lightning](assets/fr/06.webp)


## Wohereze ama bitcoins


Kugira ngo wohereze bitcoins, genda kuri screen y'intango, ukure hasi screen uhitemwo "Pay". Hanyuma gusa canke :




- winjize umuravyo LNURL Address
- scanner code QR y’umuravyo kugira ngo ushobore kwishura.


Ushobora kandi kuja ku rubuga rwa kabiri ruri musi y’ibarabara kugira ngo ushireko kode ya QR ataco uhinduye.


![Envoi de bitcoins](assets/fr/07.webp)


## Gugura ama bitcoins


Lipa itanga ubushobozi bwo kugura ama bitcoins ataco akora mu gikorwa ku giciro ca 1,5%. Kugira ngo ugure, genda ku rubuga rw’intango maze ukure hasi kugira ngo ubone ivyo ushobora gufungura. Hanyuma uhitemwo "Gura BTC". Ivyuma bitatu vy’intango bizokuyobora mu bijanye n’ugugura.


![Menu d'achat](assets/fr/08.webp)


Hanyuma rero, ushiremwo gusa amakuru yo muri banki y’iyo konti uzoba uriko urakoresha kugira ngo ugure. Hitamwo amafaranga yawe maze winjize e-mail yawe Address.


Inyuma y'aho ushiremwo, uzosanga inomero y'ishingiro izoshirwa mu vyo uzorungika, hamwe n'amakuru ya banki y'i Exchange.


![Sélection du montant](assets/fr/09.webp)


Ico ubwirizwa gukora n’ugukoresha banki yawe kugira ngo urungike amahera wipfuza, ushireho iyo nzira mu kugaragaza RIB yari yararonse mbere no kwerekana aho wakoresheje igihe wagurisha kugira ngo Lipa ishobore gufatanya iyo nzira ya banki na Lipa Wallet yawe.


![Confirmation d'achat](assets/fr/10.webp)
