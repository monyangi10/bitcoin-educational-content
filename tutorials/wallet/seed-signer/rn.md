## Ibikoresho:


**Ikimera ca Pi Zero (uburyo 1.3)**


Kugira ngo ubone umuti w’umuyaga wose, urabe ko ukoresha verisiyo 1.3 idafise ubushobozi bwa WiFi canke Bluetooth, ariko Raspberry Pi 2/3/4 canke Zero yose izokora.


Iciyumviro: Raspberry Pi ko izo zidakunda kuzana n’ibipimo bifatanye; izo nkoko zizokenera guterwako amabuye, canke ikintu citwa “GPIO Hammer” gishobora gukoreshwa.

Inyundo ya GPIO


Niba ubuhinga bwawe bwo gusodera butashitse ku rugero rwo hejuru, canke utaragira icuma co gusodera, rero urashobora gukoresha “GPIO Hammer” nk’ubundi buryo bwo gusodera.


**Inkofero LCD WaveShare 1,3 inches n'igicapo 240 × 240 pixels**


Inkofero y'Igishushanyo c'Imipfunda 1.3 "240×240 pxl


**Iciyumviro:** Hitamwo neza igicapo ca Waveshare; urabe ko ugura iyo modele ifise ubushobozi bwo gucapura 240×240 pixels.


**Igikoresho co gufata amasanamu gihuye na Pi Zero**


1080p n'igikoresho co gufata amasanamu c'amasanamu OV5647; izindi nzira zifise umurongo w’ibimenyetso OV5647 na zo nyene zikwiye gukora, ariko zishobora kutajanye n’uruzitiro rwa Orange Pill.


**Iciyumviro:** Uzokenera kugira umugozi w'urudodo rwa kamera uhuye cane cane na Raspberry Pi Zero.


**Ikarita ya MicroSD ifise ubushobozi nibura bwa 4 GB**


ibikoresho vyinshi: https://umusinyi w'imbuto.com/abasigura/

### Ikoraniro ry'amasanamu y'uburongozi yerekeye SeedSigner: inkomoko yuguruye, DIY Hardware Wallet/igikoresho co gusinya


![image](assets/1.webp)


SeedSigner ni Igikoresho co Gusinya Bitcoin ushobora kwubaka uhereye ku ntango. Birumvikana ko bigoye, ariko iyi series y’ibice 4 ikwiye kugufasha :) Ndagusavye urabe igice ca 1 n’ica 2, hanyuma ugafata ingingo nimba ushaka gukoresha desktop (raba igice ca 3) canke mobile device (raba igice ca 4).


Ivyo ukeneye kumenya vyose bizoba biri aha hepfo. Ayandi mahuza y’ingirakamaro ni urubuga rwa SeedSigner, Github yabo, Keybase yabo, ivyasohotse bishasha, n’ibisabwa ku bikoresho.
