Afirika iriko iratera imbere, irakora ibintu bishasha kandi irakora, yamara irabandanya guhura n’ingorane nyinshi, cane cane ugukoresha Internet no kuronka ibikorwa vy’ishimikiro vy’ivy’ubutunzi.


Nk'uko bivugwa n'ishirahamwe mpuzamakungu ry'itumanaho, mu mwaka w'2024, igitigiri c'abantu bakoresha Internet muri Afirika (igipimo c'umubare w'abantu bakoresha Internet n'abanyagihugu bose bo kuri uwo mugabane) cari 38%, ugereranyije n'ibice 68% kw'isi yose.



Ku bijanye no kuronka ibikorwa vy’ivy’ubutunzi, ibikorwa vyo kohereza amahera (amahera agendagenda) vyarateye imbere cane ku mugabane, bivuye ku ngorane zo kuronka ibikorwa vy’inguzanyo za banki.


Mu gihe c’ivyo bihe, Kgothatso Ngako, umuhinga mu vy’iterambere wo muri Afirika y’Epfo, yarashizeho umuti w’ihinduka rishobora gutuma iki gice c’abanyafrika kitagira amabanki, kitagira internet gishobora kwungukira kuri Bitcoin n’umugambi wiwe witwa **Machankura**.



## MACHANKURA NI IKI?



Machankura, ururimi rw'ikinyafirika y'epfo rusobanura "amahera", ni umugambi ufise iciyumviro co guhuza Afirika yo mu misozi n'ibidukikije vya Bitcoin.



Ni Wallet y’ububiko ishobora kugufasha kohereza no kwakira ama bitcoins kuri Lightning Network biciye ku buhinga bwa USSD. Machankura ikora ku matelefone yose, mbere n’ay’ishimikiro cane (telefone ya Symbian), ikoresheje urubuga rwa kera rwa GSM.


**8333.mobi** ikunda kuboneka musi y’ikimenyetso ni ugutera umutwe mu buryo buteye ubwoba. Nkako, **8333** ni port y'imbere ikoreshwa n'ibihimba vya Bitcoin kugira ngo bivugane. Machankura yerekana ko Afirika itari ku ruhande rw’iyi mpinduka, kandi ko ari igihugu c’ubuhinga bushasha muri ivyo.



Igihugu **.mobi** gikoreshwa mu gukoresha telefone ngendanwa zikoreshwa kuri telefone z’ishimikiro biciye kuri USSD. **8333.mobi** rero iraca irya n’ino ibiranga umuti wa Machankura.



Imbere yo kubandanya n’iyi nyigisho, reka turabe USSD, ubuhinga bushobora gutuma Machankura ikoreshwa ata Internet.



## USSD



Ubuhinga bwa USSD (*Ivyerekeye amakuru y’inyongera atagira imiterere*) bukoreshwa muri rusangi n’abakoresha telefone ngendanwa za GSM. Ni ikintu kigufasha kuvugana n’umuntu ari kure, mbere ata Internet, ukoresheje amakode yihariye kandi ugahitamwo uburyo bwo guhitamwo bivanye n’ubushobozi bushobora gutangwa n’uwo mugwi.



Mu nzira yo guhanahana amakuru kuri USSD, wiyumvire kwandika kode idasanzwe nka `*123#` kuri telefone yawe. Mu gushitsa iyi kode ya USSD, wohereje ubusabe bwihariye ku rubuga rwawe rwa GSM kugira ngo ushikire serivisi ihuye n’iyi kode.



Ushobora rero gukoresha USSD mu gukora ibikorwa nk’ugusaba amahera yawe yo kuri Internet, guhamagara amafaranga y’inguzanyo, n’ibindi.



Ni muri iyo nzira nyene yo guhanahana amakuru niho ubuhinga bwo gukoresha amabanki y’amatelefone ngendanwa n’ugutanga amafaranga y’amatelefone ngendanwa nka M-PESA, MTN Mobile Money, n’ibindi vyateye imbere ku mugabane.


Nk’uko twabivuze, iyo serivisi yateguwe cane cane kuri Afirika, ikora ku matelefone yose, ata n’ibintu bikomeye vy’ubuhinga canke gukoresha Internet bisabwa.



## Ubuhinga bushasha bwa Machankura



Ubutandukanye n’umuti usanzwe wa Wallet, Machankura yarashoboye gukoresha ubuhinga bwa USSD kugira ngo Address ingorane nyayo ihanganye n’abantu bo muri Afirika: ugukoresha Internet.



Machankura ni igikorwa cakozwe hanyuma kigahuzwa na kode ya GSM kugira ngo abakoresha bashobore kwakira no kohereza amafaranga y’ibiceri ata Internet. Ushobora gukoresha iyo serivisi mu bihugu bikurikira ukoresheje kode ya USSD ijana:

## Ibikorwa bitagira telefone ngendanwa (biciye muri USSD)



### Rema ububiko bwawe



Ku bijanye n'ihuriro ryawe rya mbere :




- Injira kode ya USSD ihuye n’igihugu cawe;
- Kanda buto yo guhamagara kugira ngo utangure.



Sisitemu izogusaba gukora kode ya PIN y'imibare 5




- Hitamwo kode ya PIN itekanye (imibare 5).
- Wemeze kode yawe ya PIN.
- Igitabu cawe ca Bitcoin kiraremwa ubwo nyene.



Iyi Bitcoin Wallet ifatanye n’inomero yawe ya telefone. PIN code yatowe neza izoshiramwo Wallet yawe kandi izokoreshwa no mu kwemeza amafaranga yose uzokoresha muri Machankura.


Igihe igitabu cawe kimaze kuremwa, uzokwinjira muri menyu nyamukuru ufise amahitamwo akurikira:



1. Wohereze ama bitcoins


2. Kwakira ama bitcoins


3. Ibisobanuro vya konti


4. Gugura ibintu/ibikorwa


5. Guhindura/gusubizaho kode ya PIN


6. Igisohoka



Kugira ngo ushikire uburyo bwose buri muri menyu nyamukuru, kanda gusa ku nomero y’urutonde bujanye n’ivyo.


Kugira ngo ushikire "amakuru ya konti", hitamwo uburyo **3** hanyuma ukande urufunguzo rwo guhamagara kugira ngo utangure.



### Umutekano n'ibikorwa vyiza



Machankura ni umuravyo Wallet, rero amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri acunzwe biciye ku nzira za Machankura. Konti yawe irindwa na kode ya PIN wahisemwo.





- Ntushireho kode ya PIN ijyanye n’itariki ihambaye mu buzima bwawe.
- Ntukigere usangira kode yawe ya PIN n’umuntu uwo ari we wese.
- Fata mu mutwe kode yawe ya PIN.
- Imisi yose nusuzume inomero za telefone imbere yo kohereza.
- Gumana uburinganire bubereye bwo gukoresha ku musi ku musi.



Wallet yawe ihuye n'inomero yawe ya telefone:




- Niwatakaza telefone yawe, bwira abafasha Machankura;
- Gumana urutonde rw’ibintu bihambaye ukoresha.



### Kwakira ama bitcoins





- Hitamwo umubare w'urutonde rw'ihitamwo "Kwakira Bitcoins" mu rutonde nyamukuru.
- Inomero yawe ya telefone ni yo Address yawe ya bose.
- Usangire numero yawe n’umuntu ashaka kukurungikira ama bitcoins.



#### Umuravyo usanzwe Address



Buri konti ya Machankura ihabwa umuravyo Address ishingiye ku nomero yawe ya Machankura .


`umubare w'amatora@8333.mobi`


Akarorero: umubare +2371234567890 uzoba `+2371234567890@8333.mobi`


Ariko rero, ushobora guhitamwo izina ry'ukoresha kugira ngo usubirize umubare (nk'akarorero `Satoshi@8333.mobi`).


Umuntu wese afise Lightning Address yawe arashobora kukurungikira ama bitcoins atazi inomero yawe ya telefone.



#### Gusubira gushiramwo ibitabo vyawe vya Machankura



Uretse kwakira amafaranga y’ibiceri biva ku yindi Wallet y’umuravyo, urashobora kwuzuza Wallet yawe ya Machankura **Azteco** na **1Voucher** iva ku **Itsinda ry’umuravyo**.



**Azteco** na **Flash Group** ni amashirahamwe abiri atanga **amavoucher** ya Bitcoin, ni ukuvuga amafaranga y’amavoucher yishuwe imbere y’igihe ushobora kugura kuri internet canke ku bagurisha kugira uronke Bitcoin utaciye ku rubuga rwa Exchange. Izo mpapuro zikora nk'**amakarata y'ingabirano**. Hariho **On-Chain** na **Imiravyo**.

Bitcoins zingana n’umubare w’ikete ryaguzwe zongerwa ku giciro cawe ca Machankura Wallet.



### Wohereze ama Bitcoins





- Genda kuri menyu nyamukuru.
- Hitamwo "Ohereza Bitcoin" mu kwinjiza inomero y'itegeko ryayo.
- Injira inomero ya telefone y’uwuronka, Lightning Address canke izina ry’ukoresha rya Machankura.
- Hanyuma wandike amahera uzorungika muri Sats.
- Wemeze ukoresheje kode yawe ya PIN.



Machankura aca yohereza izo bitcoins biciye kuri Lightning Network mu masegonda makeyi. Uwurungitse arashobora kandi kohereza ama bitcoins mu yandi mafaranga, harimwo n’ayo mu gihugu.



Iyo inomero y’uwuronka itarandikwa nk’uwuyikoresha, Machankura izobanza kwandikisha hanyuma ishire amahera kuri konti y’uwuronka.



Ubutumwa buraheza bugaca burungikwa ku nimero yari yarabanje kwandikishwa, uwukoresha akaronka amahera yiwe y’ibice vy’agaciro (bitcoins) amaze kwemeza ko yiyandikishije muri iyo serivisi.



Ushobora kuraba [ivyo biganiro vyo kohereza amasanamu] ya bitcoins na PIO TARAS (Umurongozi w’Igifaransa c’Afirika) biciye kuri Machankura kuri telefone y’ishimikiro.

## Koresha na telefone ngendanwa



Machankura yahisemwo WhatsApp ngo ibe urubuga rwayo rwo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa.
