Hardware Wallet ni igikoresho c'ubuhinga bwa none gikoreshwa mu gucunga no gucungera imfunguruzo z'ibanga za Bitcoin Wallet. Mu buryo butandukanye n’amasakoshi y’amaporogarama (canke amasakoshi ya Hot) ashirwa ku mashini zikoreshwa muri rusangi akenshi zikoreshwa kuri Internet, amasakoshi y’ibikoresho atuma umuntu ashobora kwitandukanya n’imfunguruzo z’ibanga, ivyo bikaba bigabanya ingorane zo guterwa n’abantu no kwiba.


Intumbero nyamukuru ya Hardware Wallet ni ugugabanya ubushobozi bw’ico gikoresho uko bishoboka kwose kugira ngo kigabanye ahantu gishobora gutera. Igitero gitoyi kandi bisigura ko hariho ibitero bikeyi bishobora gutera, ni ukuvuga, intege nke nkeyi muri sisitemu abatera boshobora gukoresha kugira ngo bashobore kuronka amafaranga y’ibiceri.


Ni vyiza gukoresha Hardware Wallet kugira ngo ukingire ama bitcoins yawe, cane cane iyo ufise amahera menshi, yaba mu gaciro nyako canke nk’igice c’itunga ryawe ryose.


Ivyuma vy’amasakoshi bikoreshwa hamwe n’ubuhinga bwo gucunga Wallet kuri mudasobwa canke kuri telefone ngendanwa. Iyi porogarama icungera uguhingura amafaranga, ariko umukono w’ibanga ukenewe kugira ngo ayo mafaranga yemezwe ukorwa gusa muri Hardware Wallet. Ivyo bisigura ko imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitigera zishirwa ahantu hashobora guhungabana.


Hardware wallets zitanga uburinzi bubiri ku muntu akoresha: ku ruhande rumwe, zikingira bitcoins zawe ibitero vyo kure mu kugumiza imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitari ku murongo, kandi ku rundi ruhande, muri rusangi zitanga uburinzi bwiza bw’umubiri ku kugerageza gukura imfunguruzo. Kandi ni kuri ivyo bipimo 2 vy’umutekano nyene umuntu ashobora gucira urubanza no gushiramwo ivyerekanwa bitandukanye biri kw’isoko.


Muri iyi nyigisho, ndasaba kuvumbura kimwe muri ivyo bisubizo: **Ledger Nano S Plus**.

## Intangamarara ya Ledger Nano S Plus


Ledger Nano S Plus ni Hardware Wallet yakozwe n’ishirahamwe ry’abafaransa Ledger, igurishwa kugiciro c’amayero 79.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/02.webp)


Nano S Plus ifise igice c'umubiri gifise icemezo ca CC EAL6+ ("*secure element*"), kiguha uburinzi buteye imbere ku bitero vy'umubiri ku bikoresho. Igikoresho n’amabuto bigenzurwa ata guca ku ruhande n’ico gice. Iciyumviro gikunze kunegurwa ni uko kode y’iyi chip atari open-source, ivyo bikaba bisaba ko umuntu yizigira ubutungane bw’ico gice. Naho biri ukwo, ico kintu kiragenzurwa n’abahinga bigenga.


Ku bijanye n’ikoreshwa, Ledger Nano S Plus ikora gusa biciye ku nzira y’umurongo wa USB-C.


Ledger iratandukanye n’iziyihiganwa kubera ko yama yihuta cane kwemera ibintu bishasha vya Bitcoin, nka Taproot canke Miniscript, nk’akarorero, ivyo bikaba bishimwa cane.

Maze kuyigerageza, mbona ko Ledger Nano S Plus ari Hardware Wallet nziza cane. Itanga umutekano wo hejuru ku giciro gisanzwe. Intambamyi yayo nyamukuru ugereranyije n’ibindi bikoresho biri mu giciro kimwe ni uko kode ya firmware atari open-source. Kandi, igicapo ca Nano S Plus ni gito cane ugereranije n’ibindi bizimvye, nka Ledger Flex canke Coldcard Q1. Naho biri ukwo, Interface yayo yarakozwe neza cane: naho ifise amabuto abiri n’igicapo gitoyi, iguma yoroshe gukoresha, harimwo n’ivyo gukoresha ibintu biteye imbere nka BIP39 passphrase. Ledger Nano S Plus nta bateri, nta n’ibarabara ry’umuyaga, kamera, canke n’ibarabara rya micro SD, ariko ivyo ni ibisanzwe kuri iki kigero c’igiciro.


Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, Ledger Nano S Plus ni uburyo bwiza bwo gukingira Bitcoin Wallet yawe, kandi irabereye abatangura n’abakoresha hagati. Ariko rero, muri iki kigero c’igiciro, jewe ubwanje ndakunda Trezor Safe 3, itanga nk’amahitamwo nk’ayo nyene. Inyungu ya Trezor, mu kubona kwanje, iri mu gucunga secure element yayo: ijambo rya Mnemonic n’imfunguruzo zicungirwa gusa na kode y’inkomoko yuguruye, yamara zica zironka inyungu mu kurindwa kw’iyi chip. Ikibi ca Trezor ni uko rimwe na rimwe ziteba cane mu gushiramwo ibintu bishasha bitandukanye na Ledger.

## Ibisabwa


Umaze kwakira Ledger Nano yawe, intambwe ya mbere ni ukugenzura uko ipfutse kugira ngo umenye neza ko itafunguwe. Iyo yononekaye, ivyo vyoshobora kwerekana ko Hardware Wallet yashizwe mu kaga kandi ko yoba atari iyo ukuri.


Uhejeje gufungura, ukwiye kubona ibintu bikurikira mu gasandugu:


- Ivyo bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none;
- Umugozi wa USB-C uja kuri USB-A;
- Igitabo c’abakoresha;
- Ikarata yo kwandikako ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic.


Kuri iyi nyigisho, uzokenera porogaramu 2: Ledger Live kugira ngo utangure Ledger, na Sparrow wallet kugira ngo ukoreshe Bitcoin Wallet yawe. Gukuraho [Ledger Live](Https://Ledger-live) na [Sparrow wallet](Sparrowwallet.com/gukuraho/) ku mbuga zabo zizwi.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/03.webp)

Ku bijanye n’izo porogarama zibiri, ndagusavye cane gusuzuma ko ari iz’ukuri (na GnuPG) n’ubutungane bwazo (biciye kuri Hash) imbere y’uko uzishira ku mashine yawe. Niba utazi neza uko wobikora, ushobora gukurikira iyi nyigisho:

https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/data/integrity-authenticity-21d0420a-be02-4663-94a3-8d487f23becc

Huza Nano yawe na mudasobwa yawe aho Ledger Live na Sparrow wallet zishizwe. Kugira ngo ugende kuri Ledger yawe, koresha buto y’ibubamfu kugira ngo ugende ibubamfu, buto y’iburyo na yo kugira ngo ugende iburyo. Kugira ngo uhitemwo canke wemeze ikintu, kanda kuri izo buto zompi icarimwe.

Iyi kode ya PIN nta ruhara ifise mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwa none za Wallet yawe. Gutyo, naho utoshobora kuronka iyo kode ya PIN, kugira ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 24 Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gusubira kuronka amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/07.webp)


Ni vyiza guhitamwo PIN y’imirongo 8, mu buryo bushoboka bwose. Kandi, urabe ko ubika iyo kode ahantu hatandukanye n’aho Ledger Nano S Plus yawe ibitswe (nk’akarorero, mu gikoresho co gucungera ijambobanga).


Koresha amabuto kugira ngo ugende hejuru y’imibare, hanyuma uhitemwo umubare wose ukanda kuri izo buto zompi icarimwe.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/08.webp)


Injira PIN yawe ubugira kabiri kugira ngo uyimenyeshe.

Nano yawe itanga amabwirizwa y’ingene wocungera ijambo ryawe ryo gukira.


**Iri jambo Mnemonic ritanga uburenganzira bwo gushika ku bitcoins zawe zose**. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere atashobora kuronka Ledger yawe. Iryo jambo ry’amajambo 24 rituma ushobora kugarukana uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri (bitcoins) mu gihe yoba yatakaje, yibwa canke yononekaye kuri Ledger Nano yawe. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko ubizigama witonze kandi ukabibika ahantu hatagira umutekano.


Ushobora kuvyandika ku mpapuro z’ikarata ziri kumwe n’igitabu cawe ca Ledger, canke kugira ngo urushirize kugira umutekano, ndagusavye ko wocapura ku gikoresho c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo ukingire ingorane z’umuriro, umwuzure canke ugusenyuka.


Ushobora gusoma aya mabwirizwa maze ugasimbuka amapaji ukanda kuri buto y’iburyo.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/10.webp)

Ledger izorema ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic ikoresheje umubare wayo w’imburakimazi. Raba neza ko ata muntu ariko arakwihweza mu gihe c’iyi operation. Andika amajambo atangwa na Ledger ku gikoresho c’umubiri uhisemwo. Bivanye n’ingene ukoresha umutekano, woshobora kwiyumvira gukora kopi nyinshi z’umubiri z’iryo jambo (ariko ikiruta vyose, nturigabanye). Ni ngombwa cane ko amajambo aguma afise inomero kandi akurikirana.

***Biragaragara ko udakwiye kwigera usangira aya majambo kuri internet, bitandukanye n’ivyo ndiko ndakora muri iyi nyigisho. Aka karorero Wallet kazokoreshwa gusa kuri Testnet kandi kazokurwaho inyuma y'inyigisho.***

![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/12.webp)

Koresha ubuto bw’ibubamfu n’ubw’iburyo kugira ngo ubone hagati y’ivyo ushobora guhitamwo, hanyuma uhitemwo ijambo ryiza ukanda kuri izo buto 2. Bandanya muri iyo nzira gushika kw’ijambo rya 24.

"*passphrase*" ishobora kugufasha gushinga BIP39 passphrase. passphrase ni ijambobanga ry'ubusa, rifatanijwe n'ijambo ryawe ryo gusubirana, ritanga Layer y'inyongera y'umutekano kuri Wallet yawe.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/23.webp)


Ubu, Wallet yawe ikomoka ku nvugo ya Mnemonic igizwe n’amajambo 24. Iryo jambo ryo gusubizaho ni ngirakamaro cane kuko rigufasha gusubizaho imfunguruzo zose za Wallet yawe iyo zitakaye. Ariko rero, rigize ikintu kimwe co kunanirwa (SPOF). Iyo bishizwe mu kaga, ama bitcoins yawe ari mu kaga. Aho niho passphrase yinjira, ni ijambobanga ry’ubusa, ushobora guhitamwo ataco ushaka, ryongera ku majambo ya Mnemonic kugira ngo wongere umutekano wa Wallet.


passphrase ntikwiye gutera urujijo n’ibara rya PIN. Irafise uruhara mu gukuraho imfunguruzo zawe z’ubuhinga bwa none. Ikorana n’ijambo Mnemonic, ihindura seed aho imfunguruzo zikomoka. Gutyo, naho umuntu yoronka amajambo yawe y’amajambo 24, ata passphrase, ntashobora kuronka amahera yawe. Gukoresha passphrase mu vy’ukuri bituma haba Wallet nshasha ifise imfunguruzo zitandukanye. Guhindura (mbere gatoyi) passphrase bizoba generate Wallet itandukanye.


passphrase ni igikoresho gikomeye cane co kwongereza umutekano w’amahera yawe ya bitcoins. Ariko rero, birahambaye cane ko utahura ingene ikora imbere y’uko uyishira mu ngiro, kugira ngo ntutakaze uburenganzira bwo gukoresha Wallet yawe. Ni co gituma ndaguhanura ngo urabe iyi yindi nyigisho yihariye nimba wipfuza gushinga passphrase kuri Ledger yawe:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-ledger-9ae6d9a2-7293-438a-8fe0-e59147ef2f49

## Ni gute worungika amafaranga y’ibiceri ukoresheje Ledger Nano?


None ko wararonse Sats yawe ya mbere muri Wallet yawe icungiwe na Nano S Plus, urashobora no kuyikoresha! Huza Ledger yawe na mudasobwa yawe, uyifungure, ufungure Sparrow wallet, hanyuma ugende ku rubuga rwa "*Send*" kugira ngo wubake igikorwa gishasha.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/51.webp)


Niba ushaka gukora "*Coin control*," bisobanura guhitamwo cane cane UTXOs uzokoresha mu gucuruza, genda kuri "*UTXOs*". Hitamwo UTXOs wipfuza gukoresha, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ohereza vyahisemwo*". Uzosubira ku rubuga rumwe rw'agace ka "*Send*", ariko n'ama UTXO yawe amaze gutorwa ku bijanye n'ugucuruza.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/52.webp)


Injira aho uja Address. Ushobora kandi kwinjiza amaderesi menshi ukanda kuri buto ya "*+ Add*".


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/53.webp)


Iyumvire "*Ikimenyetso*" kugira wibuke intumbero y'ayo mahera.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/54.webp)


Hitamwo amahera yoherejwe kuri iyi Address.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/55.webp)


Guhindura igipimo c’amahera y’ugucuruza bivanye n’isoko ririho ubu.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/56.webp)

Raba neza ko amasetingi yose y'ubucuruzi bwawe ari ukuri, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Rema ubucuruzi*".

![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/57.webp)


Niba vyose bigusa neza, fyonda ku "*Sozera Ibikorwa vyo Gusinya*".


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/58.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Ikimenyetso*".


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/59.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Ikimenyetso*" kiri iruhande y'ikinyabiziga cawe ca Ledger Nano S Plus.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/60.webp)


Suzuma ivy’ugukoresha amafaranga biri ku rubuga rwa Ledger yawe, harimwo n’ivyo uwuronka yakira Address, amahera yoherejwe be n’amahera y’amahera.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/61.webp)


Niba vyose bigusa neza, kanda kuri buto zibiri ziri kuri "*Sign transaction*" kugira ngo ushireko umukono.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/62.webp)


Igurisha ryawe ubu ryashizweko umukono. Suzuma kabiri ko vyose bigusa neza, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Broadcast Transaction*" kugira ngo ubimenyeshe ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/63.webp)


Ushobora kubisanga mu gice ca "*Ibikorwa*" ca Sparrow wallet.


![NANO S PLUS LEDGER](assets/notext/64.webp)


Urakoze cane, ubu uriko uramenya neza ikoreshwa ry’ishimikiro rya Ledger Nano S Plus iri kumwe na Sparrow wallet! Mu nyigisho izoza, tuzobona ingene twokoresha Ledger na Liana kugira ngo dukoreshe Miniscript.


Niwaba wabonye ko iyi nyigisho ari ngirakamaro, noshima iyo ushobora gusiga urukumu hejuru aha hepfo. Ntutinye gusangiza abandi iyi nkuru ku mbuga zanyu zo gusangirirako amakuru. Murakoze cane!
