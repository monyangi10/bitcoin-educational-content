Ariko kandi, iyo porogaramu irafise "Igikoresho gishizwemwo".



**Ni uru ruhande rw'iyi porogarama tuzoba turiko turatohoza muri iyi nyigisho.** Ivyo bituma umuntu wese agira umuravyo wiwe bwite kuri telefone ngendanwa, ataco akeneye server yihariye, nk'uko ACINQ itanga umuravyo wayo udasanzwe Wallet Phoenix.



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/phoenix-0f681345-abff-4bdc-819c-4ae800129cdf

*Nk’ukwibutsa, Lightning ni urubuga rukorana na Bitcoin, rushobora gutuma bitcoins zihindurwa ataco zikeneye gukora mu buryo butunganye ibikorwa vya On-Chain. Ico bivamwo ni ugucuruza hafi mu kanya nk’ako gukubita, ata nkenerwa yo kurindira iminota 10 kugira ngo ububiko bwemezwe. Ivyo birakenewe canecane igihe uriha umudandaza mw’isi y’umubiri. Ikindi, Lightning itanga urugero rudasanzwe rw'**ibanga** urubuga rwa Bitcoin rudafise mu buryo bw'imvukira*.



**Zeus "Integrated "** igenewe aba Bitcoin bashaka gutuma ubuzima bwite bwabo n'ubwigenge bwabo bugera kure.


Muri make, ni **bishoboka** ko ari Wallet mobile y’indoto z’aba cypherpunks. Naho yoba ikiri mu nzira y’ubuto bwayo (alpha version) kandi ikaba ifise ibikoko bikeyi, ibiranga ni legion, kandi nta nkeka ko izohimbara abafise uburindutsi kuruta abandi bose muri twebwe, bashaka ubugenzuzi bwinshi n’uguhitamwo.



Ku rundi ruhande, ntabwo mbona ko ubu bibereye abatanguje batamenyereye Bitcoin kandi bashaka gusa uburyo bworoshe bwo kohereza/kwakira satoshis. Naho ivyo bishobora guhinduka muri kazoza, nk'uko uburyo bwo kubungabunga biciye ku nzira ya Cashu (chaumian Ecash) buriko burashirwa mu ngiro ku batangura...

## Ihitamwo rya mbere - Kwakira ama bitcoins ya On-Chain & gufungura umurongo w'umuravyo



**Zeus Embedded** yakozwe cane cane nk'umuravyo ushizwemwo, ariko ishobora no gukoreshwa nk'umuravyo Wallet On-Chain.



Kugira ngo uronke ama bitcoins ya On-Chain, fyonda ku buto "On-Chain" hanyuma ukande kuri "Kwakira".


Ubwa nyuma, nushireko kode ya QR canke ukore kopi y’urupapuro rwa Bitcoin Address kugira ngo ushiremwo amahera.



![image](assets/fr/15.webp)





Amahera amaze kwemezwa no gushirwa muri Wallet yawe, urashobora kuyakoresha kugira ngo ufungure **umurongo w'umuravyo**. Umurongo wawe w’umuravyo ni irembo ryawe ry’i Lightning Network, rigufasha gukora Exchange bitcoins mu buryo bw’ibanga cane kandi bwihuta.





- Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku "KWIMURA AMAFARANGA On-Chain MU MURARO".



Ku rubuga rukurikira, usabwa gufungura umurongo mu bufatanye na **"Olympus by Zeus "**, LSP (Umutanga Serivisi z'Imiravyo) akundwa na Wallet.


Kuri iyi nyigisho, tuzohitamwo iyi nzira kubera ukworohereza, ariko birashoboka cane gufungura imirongo n'urudodo urwo ari rwo rwose ku rubuga.


Birashoboka mbere gufungura imirongo myinshi mu gucuruza kumwe uhisemwo "GUFUNGURA UMURONGO WONGERAKO". *Ariko ivyo tuzobiraba mu buryo "buteye imbere" bw'inyigisho **Zeus Embedded**.*





- Hanyuma uhitemwo amahera wipfuza gutanga kuri iyo nzira. Muri twebwe, amahera yacu yose ya On-Chain azokoreshwa, rero duca dukoresha buto ya "Koresha amahera yose ashoboka".





- Ubwa nyuma, uhitemwo buto ya "OPEN CHANNEL" iri musi y'ibarabara.



![image](assets/fr/16.webp)





Mu masegonda make, umurongo urashingwa kandi turiteguye gukora amafaranga yacu ya mbere y’umuravyo. Ku rubuga rw’imbere, turashobora kubona isaha ntoyi iruhande y’umubare wacu wa Wallet. Ivyo ni kubera ko tuzoguma dutegerezwa kurindira ivyemezo 3 vya On-Chain imbere y’uko iyo nzira ikora vy’ukuri.



![image](assets/fr/17.webp)





Inyuma y’ivyemezo 3, turabona ko ubu amahera yacu asigaye ashirwa mu giciro c’umuravyo.



![image](assets/fr/18.webp)



Akarongo gatoyi k’ido n’ido: iyo dufyondeye ku rutonde ruri musi y’ibarabara kugira ngo turabe uko imirongo yacu y’umuravyo imeze, turabona ko igice gitoyi c’amahera yacu ataco dushobora gukoresha: dushobora gukoresha gusa amasatoshi 208253 aho gukoresha 210370 dufise mu vy’ukuri. Ivyo ni ibisanzwe, kuko ari ivy’umwihariko ku bijanye n’umurongo w’umuravyo.



Ubwa nyuma, birakenewe kumenya ko umufatanyabikorwa wacu Olympus afise uburenganzira bwo gufunga iyo nzira ku bushake bwiwe, iyo idakoreshwa nk’akarorero. Kugira ngo umurongo wacu ukomeze, tuzobwirizwa kuriha LSP (Lightning Service Provider), nk’uko tuzobibona mu gice gikurikira, biciye mu buryo bwa 2 bwo gufungura umurongo.





## Wohereze bitcoins biciye ku Muravyo



None ko twashizeho umurongo wacu, reka turabe ingene twowukoresha mu kwishura umuravyo wa Invoice (Invoice).



Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku buto ya "Lightning", hanyuma ukande kuri "Send".



![image](assets/fr/19.webp)





Ku gicapo gikurikira, kopira Invoice yawe mu kibanza cagenewe, canke uyishire mu gicapo ukanda ku kimenyetso kiri hejuru iburyo. Ubwa nyuma, shira buto ya "Slide to Pay" iburyo kugira ngo wishyure.



![image](assets/fr/20.webp)






Rindira amasegonda makeyi Invoice irahembwa, kandi satoshis zawe zagendeye ku muvuduko w’umuco.



![image](assets/fr/21.webp)





Zeus rero araguha uburenganzira bwo kwongerako aka gatabo ko gutanga amahera warishe, canke ukabona inzira aba satoshis bawe bafashe imbere y’uko bashika aho baja (n’amahera asabwa n’ibihimba vyose vyo hagati). Ubu ni bwo bwoko bw'imikorere dukunda kuri Wallet.



![image](assets/fr/22.webp)



Zirikana ko bitandukanye na Wallet nka [Phoenix]([Plan ₿ Network - Phoenix]([Plan ₿ Network - Phoenix]), ni delegaus n'inzira y'aho hantu) umuntu agira gatatu (ACINQ mu bijanye n’i Phoenix). Rero ni wewe wenyene uzi uwuzokwakira iyo nkunga. Turatakaza ubushobozi buke (ugutanga amahera bifata igihe gito kugira ngo birangire, ariko turonka vyinshi mu bijanye n’ubuzima bwite).





Niwafyonda ku mwampi mutoyi uri musi y’ibarabara ry’imbere, urashobora kandi kubona amateka yacu y’ukwishyura. Aha turabona muri Green 212.121 Sats zaronswe ku On-Chain, hanyuma mu rangi itukura hakurikijwe 211.756 Sats zakoreshejwe mu gufungura umuhora wacu, hanyuma 121.212 satoshis zakoreshejwe mu kwishura umuravyo wacu wa Invoice.

## Ihitamwo rya 2 - Kwakira ama bitcoins ku Muravyo



Aho gufungura umurongo n’amaboko, nk’uko twabibonye, ​​birashoboka ko umuntu aronka amahera ataco akora biciye ku Lightning, mbere ata muhora uriho mbere, akoresheje Olympus, Zeus LSP.




- Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku buto ya "Lightning" iri ku rubuga rw'intango, hanyuma ukande kuri "Receive".
- Hanyuma uhitemwo amahera wipfuza kwakira mu gasandugu "Amount" hanyuma uhitemwo buto "CREATE Invoice" iri musi y'ibarabara.



![image](assets/fr/24.webp)





Igishushanyo gikurikira kirerekana Invoice izohembwa kugira ngo umuntu aronke ama satoshis. Tubwirwa ko LSP izofata 10.000 Sats iyo amahera yishuwe na Lightning. Tuzobona mu nyuma ingene ayo mahera yo gufungura imirongo afise ishingiro.



Uhe Invoice canke usabe uwundi muntu ayihe, uwo muhora uzoca ufunguka ubwo nyene, ariko ukuremwo Sats 10.000 nk’uko mwavyemeranije.



![image](assets/fr/25.webp)





Ubu turi ku mutwe w’imirongo 2 y’umuravyo, iyo mirongo ishobora gusuzumwa mu gufyonda kuri buto yerekanwa n’umwampi werurutse uri musi y’ibarabara ry’imbere.



Turashobora kubona ko, mu buryo butandukanye n’umurongo wuguruwe uvuye ku rugero rwacu rwa On-Chain, uwo wuguruwe ata guca ku ruhande biciye ku miravyo nta n’imburi ugaragaza.


Nk'uko warishe kugira ngo ushireho uyu muhora, uwutanga ibikorwa vy'umuravyo (LSP) yiyemeza kubungabunga uwo muhora amezi 3, kandi akaguha "amahera yinjira". Ku murongo wo hasi, urashobora kubona ko ubushobozi bwo kwakira ari 96383 satoshis. LSP rero yaraboshe umutungo kugira ngo ushobore kwakira amahera ataco ukoresheje umaze gufungura umuhora.



Rero ayo mahera 10.000 Satoshi yishuwe arapfuka: igiciro co gufungura uwo mwonga (ugucuruza Bitcoin On-Chain, ingwati yo kubungabunga uwo mwonga amezi 3, n’ugufunga umutungo).



![image](assets/fr/26.webp)
