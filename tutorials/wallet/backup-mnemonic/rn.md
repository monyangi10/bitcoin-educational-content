Iyo uremye Bitcoin Wallet nshasha, haba biciye kuri porogarama canke Hardware Wallet, uronka ijambo Mnemonic rigizwe n’amajambo 12 canke 24. Iri jambo rirahambaye cane, kuko ari ryo ry’inkomoko y’imfunguruzo zose z’ibanga zikingira Bitcoins zawe. Bitegerezwa rero kurindwa neza kugira ngo amahera yawe azosubirwamwo iyo umurongo wawe wa Wallet uvunitse, wibwe canke utakaye.


Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwihweza uburyo bwiza bwo kubika neza ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, kugira ngo ntutakaze uburenganzira bwo kuronka amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri.


## Ugukangurira ingorane


Mnemonic iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe yose y’ama bitcoins ataco abujijwe. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere atashobora gushika ku murongo wakira Wallet yawe.


Ivyo bisigura nk’akarorero, ko iyo ukoresheje Bitcoin Wallet kuri Ledger, umuntu wese afise uburenganzira bwo kuronka ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic arashobora kwiba amafaranga yawe yose, mbere atashobora kuronka Ledger ubwayo. Ni co gituma **utakwigera usangira ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic**, uko ibintu vyogenda kwose.


Iri jambo rero ni ryo makuru yihariye ashobora kugufasha kugarura uburenganzira bwo kuronka amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri (bitcoins) mu gihe yoba yatakaje, yibwa canke yononekaye ku binyamakuru vya Wallet. Reka dusubire gufata akarorero ka Ledger: iyo utakaje igikoresho, urashobora kugarura amahera yawe mu kwinjiza ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic kuri Ledger nshasha canke ku yindi Wallet yose ihuye, yaba ari porogarama canke ubuhinga.


Ni vyiza rero ko uzigama iri jambo n’ubwitonzi bwinshi kandi ukaribika ahantu hatagira umutekano, nk’uko tuzobidondora mu bice bikurikira.


**Ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic rero rishikirwa n’ingorane zibiri nyamukuru: ubusuma n’ugutakaza**


Ubusuma bushobora gushika mu buryo bubiri nyamukuru:




- Hari umuntu yaronse uburenganzira bwo gushika ku mubiri wawe, nk’akarorero mu gihe c’ubusuma canke biciye ku mugenzi;
- Wasangiye n’uwundi muntu urubanza rwawe ku bushake canke ata bushake.


Kugira ngo wirinde ubusuma bw’umubiri bw’ububiko bwawe bw’amajambo ya Mnemonic, birahambaye ko ubibika ahantu hatagira uwubibona. Tuzorabira kuri iyo ngingo mu buryo burambuye mu bice bikurikira.


Ku bijanye n’ukugerageza kwiba kure, wama wibuka ko utokwigera usangira ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, uko ibintu vyoba vyifashe kwose. Ivy’uguhenda abantu birasanzwe: ubutumwa bwo kuri interineti bw’ubuhendanyi, imbuga zikwigana izo mu ma wallets yemewe canke gusaba ataco bimaze biciye ku nzira zitandukanye zo guhanahana amakuru. Iyo umuntu akusavye passphrase yawe, ni ubuhendanyi, mbere no mu gihe c’ivyihutirwa! Ni ibisanzwe ko abasuma bigira nk’abakozi b’uwukora Hardware Wallet yawe, ariko menya neza ko ayo mashirahamwe atazokwigera agusaba passphrase yawe, uko ibintu vyogenda kwose. Rero, urabe maso cane ku bijanye n’amakuru yose uronka, yaba kuri email, kuri telefone, ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi, ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi canke mbere no kwibonera.


Igihe ukeneye kwinjiza ijambo ryawe muri Hardware Wallet canke muri porogarama kugira ngo usubire gukoresha Wallet yawe inyuma y’ingorane y’ugushigikira kwa mbere, fata umwanya wo kugenzura ukuri n’ubutungane bw’ibikoresho canke porogarama uriko urakoresha. Ntuhagarike umutima, kandi ugende mu buryo bubereye.


Vyongeye, nube maso igihe ukoresha amajambo yawe yo mu gitabu ca Mnemonic. Raba neza ko udakwihwezwa n’abandi bantu canke kamera.


Ku bijanye n’ingorane zo gutakaza, ivyo bishobora guterwa n’imvo nyamukuru zitatu: gutakaza ubuhinga bwo gusubiza inyuma, ugusenyuka kwabwo canke ikosa ryo gupima. Tuzokwihweza neza ingene twokwirinda canke tugabanura ivyo bibazo bitatu mu bice bikurikira.


## Infashanyo


Kugira ngo uzigame ijambo ryawe ryo gukira, urakeneye kurindika ku kintu kigaragara, nk’impapuro canke ivyuma. Ntukigere ukoresha ubuhinga bwa digitale: ntububike muri dosiye y’inyandiko, ntubufotore canke ngo ububike mu gikoresho co gucungera ijambobanga. Ubwo buryo buratuma igitero gishobora gutera cane cane ugereranije n’ibikoresho vy’umubiri. Itegeko rero riratomoye: ukoreshe impapuro, ikarito canke ivyuma kugira ngo uzigame ijambo ryawe.


Naho kwandika amajambo yawe ku mpapuro yoroshe ari akamenyero keza, guhitamwo ikintu co gufatako ivyuma, nk’icuma kitagira ibara, biratuma urushiriza kugira umutekano. Ubwo bwoko bw’igikoresho burakingira ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic ingorane z’umuriro, umwuzure canke ugusenyuka bishobora gutuma aho ubika.


Ku barondera uburyo bwo gushigikira ijambo ryabo ku cuma, [uburyo bwo kwikorera bwite bwa "*SAFU Ninja*"] Ico ukeneye gukora n’ugugura ibikoresho vyo gusukura ivyuma, umugozi n’umugozi mu maduka. Hanyuma ugacapura amajambo y’interuro yawe ku gikoresho cose co gusukura, ukamenya neza ko uyaha inomero, hanyuma ukayateranya ku gipfukisho gifise iyo nkoko. Ubwo buryo butazimvye burasanzwe butanga ubushobozi bwo kurwanya neza.

## Gusubiza inyuma


None ko wahisemwo uburyo bwo gukoresha umubiri wawe, ni igihe co kwandika ijambo ry’ugukira kwawe! Kugira ngo ntutakaze ama bitcoins yawe, ukurikize izi ngeso nziza.


Mbere na mbere, urabe neza ko udakwihwezwa, haba n’abandi bantu canke n’amakamera, uko wandika interuro yawe.


Hanyuma ufate umwanya wo kwandika ijambo rimwe rimwe mu buryo butomoye kandi busomwa. Ushobora gukenera gusubira gusoma igihano cawe mu myaka ikurikira, canke uwundi muntu yoshobora gukenera kubigira nk’igice c’iragi. Ni ngombwa rero kwandika n’ukuboko witonze.


Mu vyiyumviro, birashoboka kwandika gusa inyuguti 4 za mbere z'ijambo rimwe rimwe, kuko muri list y'amajambo 2048 ari muri BIP39, nta majambo abiri asangiye inyuguti 4 za mbere zimwe mu buryo bumwe. Ariko rero, nimba umurongo wawe ufise umwanya uhagije, ndagusavye ko wobika ijambo ryose ryose. Ivyo birashobora kuba ngirakamaro mu gihe ibinyamakuru bishobora gusenyuka bimwe bimwe. Nk'akarorero, iyo wanditse gusa `accu` ku jambo `accuse` maze urudome `u` rukabura, ushobora guhitamwo hagati ya `accuse`, `access`, `accident` canke `account`. Ku rundi ruhande, n’ijambo ryose, naho urudome rumwe rwoba rwabuze, biracari vyoroshe kumenya ijambo ryiza.


Ni ngombwa kandi kwandika amajambo yawe mu buryo bubereye. Niba ufise amajambo yawe 24 ariko ntuzi urutonde rwayo nyarwo, ntibizoshoboka ko usubirana Wallet yawe. Gutanga inomero ku majambo birahambaye nk’uko nyene: iyo umurongo w’amajambo wononekaye canke amajambo akagira ingorane, kuyaha inomero bizokuronsa ubushobozi bwo kuyasubiza mu rutonde rwiza bitagoranye.


![SEED](assets/fr/03.webp)


Ikindi, birahambaye gutahura ko, mu vyiyumviro, ijambo Mnemonic ryonyene ridahora rihagije kugira ngo wongere ubone amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri. Ukeneye kandi kumenya inzira z’ugukomoka zikoreshwa kugira ngo generate imfunguruzo. Niwakoresha SingleSig Wallet ifise inzira isanzwe, bizoba vyoroshe cane kugarura imfunguruzo zawe. Ariko rero, iyo umuntu afise inzira idasanzwe, ivyo vyoshobora kutashoboka, mbere no mu gihe umuntu afise ijambo Mnemonic. Kugira ngo wirinde iki kibazo, ndagusavye cane ko womenya, ku binyamakuru vyawe, inzira y’inkomoko ya konti uriko urakoresha. Aya makuru ushobora kuyasanga mu ma settings ya porogarama yawe ya Wallet. Nk'akarorero, ku Taproot Wallet, inzira y'ivyaduka izoba :


```txt
m / 86' / 0' / 0' /
```


![SEED](assets/fr/04.webp)


Niba ukoresha Multisig Wallet canke Wallet ifise inyandiko zikomeye harimwo n'imfunguruzo zo gusubizaho, nk'izo zitangwa na porogarama ya Liana, ni ngombwa ko ubika **Ibisobanuro vy'Inyandiko z'Isohoka** vyawe. Ivyo bisobanuro birimwo amakuru yose ukeneye, uretse amajambo yo gusubirana, kugira ngo wongere ubone amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins.


Ushobora kandi gutunganya ububiko bwawe n’amakuru y’inyongera ajanye n’ugushigikira kwawe kwa Wallet. Nk’akarorero, nubone kode ya PIN ikoreshwa mu gufungura Hardware Wallet yawe, canke amajambo arwanya ubuhendanyi nimba ukoresha COLDCARD.


![SEED](assets/fr/05.webp)


Ku rundi ruhande, nimba ukoresha igitabu ca passphrase, nurabe neza ko utacandika ku rurimi rumwe n’ijambo ryawe ry’igitabu ca Mnemonic. Intumbero ya passphrase ni ugukingira Wallet yawe iyo umuntu yivye. Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku bijanye n’ingene wokoresha passphrase, raba iyi nyigisho y’inyongera:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7

Umaze kubika ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic ku bimenyeshamakuru vy’umubiri, birakenewe cane ko ukora ikigeragezo co gusubira mu gihe Wallet yawe nshasha yaremwe ikiri ubusa. Ico kigeragezo gishimikiye ku kwandika amakuru y’akarorero, ugakuraho n’ibigirankana Wallet iri ubusa, hanyuma ukagerageza kuyigarura ukoresheje gusa ububiko bwawe bw’umubiri bw’ijambo Mnemonic. Ivyo bizokuronsa ubushobozi bwo kugenzura ko ububiko bwawe bushitse kandi ko ata makosa yo kwinjiza. Bituma kandi umenya neza ingene umuntu akira. Muri ubwo buryo, niwaba ukeneye gukira muri kazoza, uzoba witeguye neza kandi uzokwirinda umubabaro wo kugerageza ubwa mbere mu bintu vy’ukuri. Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwo gukora iki kibazo, raba iyi yindi nyigisho :


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/recovery-test-5a75db51-a6a1-4338-a02a-164a8d91b895

Ubwa nyuma, hariho ikibazo c’umubare w’ibisubizo. Iryo hitamwo rivana rwose n’ingene wewe ubwawe umeze. Guhagarika igitigiri c’amakopi, nk’akarorero mu kwandika ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic rimwe gusa ku gikoresho co gukoresha, biragabanya ingorane zo kwiba, mugabo bikongera ingorane zo gutakaza. Ku rundi ruhande, gukora kopi nyinshi biragabanya ingorane zo gutakaza, ariko bikongera ingorane zo kwiba. Ni wewe rero ubwirizwa kurondera uburinganire bubereye ivyo ukeneye, maze ukamenya igitigiri c’amakopi wumva ko ari yo akwiriye kuruta ayandi yose.


## Kubikwa


Ubu ko umaze gushigikira neza ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, ni igihe co guhitamwo ahantu heza ho kubika. Ivyo bizovana n’ingene ukoresha umutekano wawe. Uko biri kwose, nuhitemwo ahantu utaboneka, aho bishoboka ko umuntu atazotsitarako, yamara rero umuntu ashobora kuhabona kugira ngo asuzume rimwe na rimwe. Raba neza ko irindwa na Elements, na yo nyene, kugira ngo ntiyononekare igice co hasi.


Nanje noguhanura ko utobika igitabu cawe ca GW-55 ahantu udafise uburenganzira bwo kwigenga, nk’aho wobika amahera mu biro vy’umunyamabanga canke muri banki. Ivyo bishobora gusa n’ibitekanye, ariko bisobanura ko wizigira uwundi muntu kugira ngo abone ububiko bwawe, ivyo bikaba biteye kubiri n’ingingo ngenderwako z’ishimikiro za Bitcoin.


Kugira ngo umuntu agire umutekano mwinshi, ndagusavye gukoresha umufuko w’ipulasitike udashobora guhindurwa canke uburyo busa n’ubwo bwo gukingira. Ivyo bizotuma ushobora kumenya ko ata n’umwe yaronse uburenganzira bwo kuronka ijambo ryawe. Nk’akarorero, iyo ubitse ijambo ryawe i muhira maze ukakira abashitsi, vyoshobora kutashoboka kumenya nimba hari uwuribonye, ​​yarifashe mu mutwe canke yarifotoye. Igikapu kidashobora guhindurwa gituma ubwo bwoko bwo kugenzura bworoha: nimba ataco bukoze, urashobora kwemera udakeka ko ijambo ryawe ryagumye ari ibanga. Ivyo bikoresho vy’amaboko bitaboneka neza biraboneka kuri Internet canke mu maduka yihariye ya Bitcoin.


![SEED](assets/fr/06.webp)


Ubwa nyuma, igihe ijambo ryawe ribitswe mw’ibahasha ridashobora guhindurwa, ntukibagire kwandika ikimenyetso kidasanzwe c’iryo bahasha. Ivyo bizotuma ushobora kugenzura ko ari ivy’ukuri mu gihe uriko urasuzuma.


## Gucungera umwanya


Ubu ko ijambo ryawe ryabitswe neza, birahambaye ko ushiraho uburyo bwo kugenzura ubudasiba. Rimwe na rimwe, nusuzume ko ijambo ryawe rikiri aho ryabitswe, kandi ko ibahasha ryaryo ritaboneka ritarafungurwa.


Muri ivyo bipimo, urashobora kandi gufungura ibahasha kugira ngo usuzume uko igikoresho co hasi kiri. Raba neza ko ata co yononekaye kandi ko iyo nteruro iguma isomwa neza. Niwabona ibimenyetso vy'uko vyononekaye, ni vyiza ko ukora kopi nshasha y'igitabu cawe ca Hardware Wallet. Suzuma ko iyo kopi nshasha ikora, hanyuma usenye neza iyo kopi yononekaye kugira ngo wirinde ingorane iyo ari yo yose yo gusohoka.


Ubwa nyuma, uburongozi bw’amajambo Mnemonic na bwo buravyura ikibazo c’iragi. Iyi nkuru izokwihwezwa mu buryo burambuye mu nyigisho izoza.


## Kuja kure
