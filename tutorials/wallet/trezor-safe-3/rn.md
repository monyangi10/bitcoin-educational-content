Trezor Safe 3 ni Hardware Wallet yakozwe na SatoshiLabs kandi yaremwe muri 2023. Ni ikigereranyo gikomeye cane, gifise uburemere buke (14 grammes) cakozwe ku batangura n’abakoresha hagati. Ni yo yakurikiye Model One izwi cane, n’ibindi vyinshi yongeweko, mu gihe iguma ifise uburyo bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bufunguye buyitandukanya n’iyiyihiganwa nyamukuru, Ledger. Safe 3 igura €79. Ni co gituma iri mu gice ca Hardware Wallet co hagati, mu guhiganwa ata guca ku ruhande na Ledger Nano S Plus.



Safe 3 nta bateri igira kandi ikora gusa biciye ku nzira ya USB-C, ikoreshwa mu gutanga amashanyarazi no mu guhanahana amakuru. Ifise akagaragaza gatoyi k’ibara rimwe ry’inci 0,96 n’amabuto abiri agaragara.



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Safe 3 itanga ibintu vyose vy’ingenzi vyitezwe kuri Hardware Wallet nziza, harimwo n’ugushiramwo neza cane passphrase BIP39. Ariko rero, ntirashigikira Miniscript.



Iyi modele irabereye cane cane abatangura, kandi ishobora mbere kuba Hardware Wallet nosaba uwuyikoresha mushasha. Ni vyiza kandi ku bakoresha bo hagati. Ku rundi ruhande, bishobora kutahura n’ivyo vyose abakoresha bateye imbere barondera ibintu vyihariye, biboneka ku bikoresho nka Coldcard. Naho biri ukwo, nimba udakeneye izo nzira ziteye imbere, Trezor Safe 3 yoshobora kuba ari yo nzira nziza cane.



## Igishushanyo c'umutekano wa Trezor Safe 3



Trezor Safe 3 ubu ifise **secure element** yemejwe na EAL6+, ni iterambere rikomeye ku bikoresho vya kera nka Model One na Model T. Iyi ni chip ya OPTIGA Trust M V3, idabika seed ataco ihinduye, ariko ikora nk’igice c’ubuhinga bwa none kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kuyironka. secure element irafise ibanga rishobora gushikwako gusa iyo uwuyikoresha amaze kwinjiza neza PIN. Iryo banga rero rikoreshwa mu gukuraho seed, iyo seed ikaba ibikwa mu buryo bw’ibanga mu bubiko nyamukuru bw’ico gikoresho.



Ubu buhinga bwo gucungera umutekano buvanze buratanga uburinzi bwiza bwo ku mubiri, cane cane ku bitero vyo gukuraho canke gusesangura, ingorane Model One yari ishobora guhura na zo, cane cane mu gucunga PIN. Ivyo bibazo ubu birakurwaho biciye ku gukoresha secure element. Iyi nzira kandi iragumya ubuhinga bwa porogarama yuguruye: kode icungera uguhingura no gukoresha imfunguruzo z’ibanga iguma ishobora gushikirwa kandi igasuzumwa. Igikoresho ca OPTIGA gicungera gusa kode ya PIN, ikintu kiri hanze y’uburongozi bw’urufunguzo rwa Bitcoin Wallet. Irekura gusa ibanga rishobora gukoreshwa mu gukuraho seed. Kandi, igice ca OPTIGA Trust M V3 kironka inyungu ku ruhusha rwo ku buntu, ruha uburenganzira SatoshiLabs bwo gutangaza ku buntu ibintu bishobora gutuma umuntu agira ingorane.



Ico kigereranyo c’umutekano kigereranya, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, kimwe mu vyiza kuruta ibindi vyose vyo gusenyera ku mugozi umwe biri kw’isoko uno musi. Ihuza ivyiza vya secure element n’uburongozi bwa porogarama zifunguye. Mbere, abakoresha bategerezwa guhitamwo hagati y’umutekano w’umubiri wongereweko n’igice c’umubiri (chip) be n’uguseruka n’inkomoko yuguruye; na Trezor Safe 3, birashoboka ko umuntu ashobora kuvyungukirako vyose.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwereka ingene woshiraho no gukoresha Trezor Safe 3 yawe ata nkomanzi.

## Gushinga kode ya PIN



Inyuma y’aho haza intambwe ya kode ya PIN. Kode ya PIN irafungura Trezor yawe. Ni co gituma ritanga uburinzi ku bijanye n’ugushika ku mubiri ata wemerewe. Iyi kode ya PIN ntaco ikora mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwa none za Wallet yawe. Rero naho utagira uburenganzira bwo kuronka kode ya PIN, kugira ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo kuronka uburenganzira bwo kuronka amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri.



Kuri Trezor Suite, ukande kuri "*Bandanya ukoresheje PIN*", hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Shiraho PIN*".

Wemeze na Safe 3.

Ushobora gusobanura kode ya PIN iri hagati y’imirongo 8 na 50. Ndagusavye guhitamwo PIN code igihe kirekire gishoboka kugira ngo wongere umutekano.



Koresha ubuto bw’ibubamfu n’ubw’iburyo kugira uhitemwo umubare wose. Kugira ngo wemeze ihitamwo ryawe maze ugende ku mubare ukurikira, kanda kuri izo buto zompi icarimwe.

## Noshobora gute kohereza amafaranga y’ibiceri nkoresheje Trezor Safe 3?
