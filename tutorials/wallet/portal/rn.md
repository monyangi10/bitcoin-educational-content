![cover](assets/cover.webp)


Portal ni Bitcoin Hardware Wallet yakozwe na TwentyTwo Devices, ishirahamwe ry’abahinga mu guhingura amasakoshi y’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none vy’abakoresha amafaranga y’amahera. Yashinzwe na Alekos Filini, umuremyi w’umugambi w’ubupfumu Bitcoin ([kuva ubu witwa BDK]([https://github.com/bitcoindevkit)) kandi amaze gukorera Blockstream na BHB Network, ibikoresho vya TwentyTwo bifise intumbero yo kwibanda ku kwigenga kw’abakoresha, ku kworoha no ku mutekano.


Igitandukanya Portal n’izindi nkoko ziri ku isoko ni uko zikorana n’amatelefone ngendanwa. Ikora ata nsinga canke amabateri. Ikoresha ubuhinga bwa NFC kugira ngo yihe ubushobozi kandi ivugane n’itelefone yose ihuye na Wallet. Igishushanyo caco giteye umunezero cagenewe gukoreshwa mu buryo bubereye. Ico gice c’uruziga gishirwa inyuma ya telefone ngendanwa kugira ngo kigaragaze igicapo aho ushobora gusuzuma amakuru y’ivyo ukoresha imbere y’uko ubishirako umukono ukoresheje buto yihariye.


![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)


Urubuga rwose rufunguye, rushingiye kuri porogarama yanditswe mu rurimi rwa Rust kandi rukoresha ururimi rwa BDK (Bitcoin Dev Kit) mu gucunga urufunguzo n’ibikorwa. Igurishwa ku €89 [ku rubuga rwemewe](https://iduka.makumyabiri n’ibiri.xyz/ibicuruzwa/ibikoresho-vy’urubuga-Wallet).


Mu gihe c’ukwandika, Urubuga rurahuye n’ibikorwa vya Nunchuk na Bitcoin Keeper. Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko turayitunganya dukoresheje Nunchuk.


## Gufungura isanduku


Iyo waronse Urubuga rwawe, nusuzume ko akazu n’ikimenyetso kiwukingirako biri mu mimerere myiza. Imbere, uzosanga Urugi rwawe ruri mu gafuko kapfutse.


Raba neza ko Seal idakomeretse kugira ngo wemeze ko iyo saho itafunguwe. Igitigiri kidasanzwe kigaragazwa mu nyuguti nini ku gafuko gikwiye guhura n’ico canditswe mu mfuruka y’umwirabura munsi y’ubururu Seal, n’ico kiri ku rupapuro rw’agasanduku, be n’ico kizoboneka ku gicapo cawe igihe uzoba utanguye gukoresha.


![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)


## Gushirwaho kwa Nunchuk


Kugira ngo dushobore gucunga Wallet yakiriwe ku Rubuga, tuzokoresha porogarama ya Nunchuk. Kuvana porogaramu muri [Iduka rya Google Play](iduka.google.com/iduka/porogarama/ibisobanuro?id=io.nunchuk.android), [Iduka ry'iporogarama](iduka ry'iporogarama](iduka ry'iporogarama. `.apk`] (ibisohoka).


![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)


Niba uriko urakoresha Nunchuk ku ncuro ya mbere, iyo porogarama izogusaba gufungura konti. Kubera intumbero z'iyi nyigisho, si ngombwa ko urema imwe. Hitamwo "*Bandanya nk'umushyitsi*" kugira ngo ukomeze ata konti.


![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)


## Imiterere y'urugi


Ku rubuga rwa mbere rwa Nunchuk, fyonda ku kimenyetso ca "*NFC*" kiri hejuru y'urubuga.


![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)


Shira Portal yawe inyuma ya telefone yawe kugira ngo uyikoreshe.


![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)


Nunchuk azomenya Urugi rwawe. Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)


Kugira ngo ureme Wallet nshasha, hitamwo "*generate seed ku rubuga*" hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)


Ushobora guhitamwo hagati y’amajambo 12 canke 24 y’ijambo Mnemonic. Umutekano utangwa n’izo nzira zompi ni usa, rero urashobora guhitamwo iyo yoroshe kubika, ni ukuvuga amajambo 12.


![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)


Uzoca usabwa guhitamwo ijambobanga. Ijambobanga rifungura Urubuga rwawe. Ni co gituma ritanga uburinzi ku bijanye n’ugushika ku mubiri ata wemerewe. Iri jambobanga ntirigira uruhara mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ibanga za Wallet yawe. Rero, naho utagira uburenganzira bwo kuronka iri jambobanga, kugira ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 canke 24 Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo kuronka uburenganzira bwo kuronka amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins. Ni vyiza guhitamwo ijambobanga ry’ibanga ry’imburakimazi uko bishoboka kwose kandi rirerire bihagije. Raba neza ko ubika iri jambobanga ahantu hatandukanye n'aho Urubuga rwawe rubitswe (nk'akarorero mu mucungerezi w'ijambobanga).


![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)


Urubuga rwawe ruzokwerekana ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 Mnemonic. Iyi Mnemonic iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe yose y’ama bitcoins ataco abujijwe. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere atashobora gushika ku Rubuga rwawe.


Iryo jambo ry’amajambo 12 risubizaho uburenganzira bwo gushika ku bitcoins zawe iyo utakaje, wibwe canke uvunitse Urubuga rwawe. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko umuntu ayizigama neza kandi akayibika ahantu heza.


Ushobora kuyindika ku mpapuro, canke kugira ngo wongere uyikingire, ndagusavye kuyicapura ku gishingo c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo uyikingire umuriro, umwuzure canke gusenyuka.


Kugira ngo uronke ibindi bisobanuro ku buryo bwiza bwo kubika no gucunga ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, ndagusavye cane gukurikira iyi yindi nyigisho, cane cane nimba uri umutanguzi:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

_**Nivyo, ntushobora kwigera usangira aya majambo kuri internet, nk'uko ndiko ndabikora muri iyi nyigisho. Iyi nkuru Wallet izokoreshwa gusa kuri Testnet kandi izokurwaho ku mpera y'inyigisho.**_


Kanda cane kuri buto iri ku Rubuga rwawe kugira ngo ugende ku majambo akurikira. Raba neza ko ushize urutoke rwawe rwose kuri buto maze ugafata umukazo mu masegonda makeyi, kugira ngo iyo migenderanire imenyekane neza.


![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)


Urubuga rwawe ruzoca rwemeza ijambobanga winjije muri Nunchuk.


![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)


Ubu wararangije gutegura Urubuga rwawe no kurema ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic!

## Bitcoin Wallet imiterere


Ku Nunchuk, ukande kuri "*Continue*", ukiriho ufashe Portal yawe inyuma ya telefone yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)


Muri iyi nyigisho, ngiye gushinga Wallet y’ikimenyetso kimwe, rero ndiko ndahitamwo iyi nzira.


![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)


Koresha konti y’imbere, ni ukuvuga konti ya mbere muri Wallet (umubare 0). Nunchuk azoca agusaba kwemeza ijambobanga ryawe rya Portal kugira ngo uyifungure.


![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)


Ku Rubuga, wemeze ko xpub yawe yoherejwe hanze muri Nunchuk. Ivyo bigufasha gucunga Wallet ukoresheje telefone yawe y’amaboko ataco ushobora gukoresha bitcoins ata Portal. Kanda kuri buto kugira ngo wemeze.


Zirikana ko inzira y'ugukura yerekanwa mu kibazo cawe izoba itandukanye n'iyanje, kuko iyi nyigisho ikoreshwa kuri Testnet.


![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)


Izina rya Wallet yawe, nk'akarorero "*Urubuga*", hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)


Nunchuk aca aguha Descriptor yawe. Ni iciyumviro ciza gukora backup. Naho Descriptor itakwemerera gukoresha bitcoins, irakwemerera gukurikirana inzira z’ugukomoka kw’imfunguruzo zawe zivuye mu majambo yawe ya Mnemonic mu gihe Wallet yosubirana. Ubigumye ahantu hatagira umutekano, kuko naho ukuvuza kwavyo bishobora kutatera ingorane y’umutekano, biragereranya ikibazo c’ibanga.


Fyonda kuri "*Vyarangiye*".


![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)


Ubu uzokenera generate imfunguruzo za bose za Bitcoin Wallet yawe. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku buto "*Rema Wallet nshasha*".


![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)


Fyonda kandi kuri "*Rema Wallet nshasha*". Hanyuma uhitemwo "*Rema Wallet nshasha ukoresheje imfunguruzo zisanzweho*".


![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)


Hitamwo izina rya Wallet yawe hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)


Hitamwo Urubuga rwawe nk'igikoresho co gusinya kuri iyi nkuru nshasha, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)


Nimba vyose biguha umunezero, wemeze ivyaremwe.


![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)


Ushobora rero kubika dosiye yawe y'imiterere ya Wallet. Iyi dosiye irimwo imfunguruzo zawe za bose gusa, bisigura ko naho umuntu yoyironka, ntazoshobora kwiba ama bitcoins yawe. Ariko rero, bazoshobora gukurikirana ivyo ukoresha vyose. Iyi dosiye rero irashobora gusa gutera ingorane ubuzima bwite bwawe. Mu bihe bimwebimwe, bishobora kuba ari ngombwa cane kugira ngo ubone Wallet yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)


Kandi ivyo ni vyo vyose birimwo!


![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)


## Noshobora gute kwakira ama bitcoins nkoresheje Portal?


Kugira ngo uronke amafaranga, hitamwo Wallet yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)


Imbere yo gukoresha Address yashizweho, nuyisuzume ku rubuga rw’Urubuga. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda kuri "*Kwakira*".


![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)


Fyonda ku tudodo dutatu, hanyuma uhitemwo "*Suzuma Address biciye ku PORTAL*". Hanyuma winjize ijambobanga ryawe.


![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)


Shira Portal yawe inyuma ya telefone yawe, hanyuma wemeze ukanda kuri buto.


![Image](assets/fr/30.webp)


Raba neza ko Address yerekanwa ku Rubuga ihuye n’iyo kuri Nunchuk yawe, hanyuma wemeze mu gusubira gukanda buto. Nimba amaderesi asa, urashobora guha uwuyitanga iyi Address.


![Image](assets/fr/31.webp)


Igihe amafaranga y'uwutanga amaze gutangazwa, uzobona abonetse kuri Wallet yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/32.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Raba imfuruka*".


![Image](assets/fr/33.webp)


Hitamwo UTXO yawe nshasha.


![Image](assets/fr/34.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*+*" iruhande ya "*Tags*" kugira ngo wongereko ikimenyetso kuri UTXO yawe. Ivyo ni akamenyero keza, kuko bigufasha kwibuka aho amafaranga yawe akomoka kandi bikagufasha gukoresha neza ubuzima bwiwe bwite igihe uzoba uriko urakoresha amahera muri kazoza.


![Image](assets/fr/35.webp)


Hitamwo ikimenyetso kiriho canke ureme ikindi gishasha, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bika*". Ushobora kandi gukora "*amakoraniro*" kugira ngo utunganye ibice vyawe mu buryo butunganye.


![Image](assets/fr/36.webp)


## Norungika gute ama bitcoins nkoresheje Portal?


None ko ufise ama bitcoins muri Wallet yawe, urashobora no kuyarungika. Kugira ngo ubikore, nufyonde kuri Wallet uhisemwo.


![Image](assets/fr/37.webp)


Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Ohereza*".


![Image](assets/fr/38.webp)


Hitamwo amafaranga uzorungika, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/39.webp)


Wongereko "*note*" ku vyo uzokora muri kazoza kugira ngo bikwibutse intumbero yavyo.


![Image](assets/fr/40.webp)


Hanyuma ushire Address y’uwuronka mu kibanza catanzwe. Ushobora kandi gucapura igitabu Address gifise kode ya QR ukanda ku kimenyetso kiri hejuru iburyo bw’ibarabara. Hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Rema Ibikorwa*".


![Image](assets/fr/41.webp)


Suzuma amakuru y'ibikorwa vyawe, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Sign*" iri iruhande y'Urubuga rwawe, hanyuma winjize ijambobanga ryawe.


![Image](assets/fr/42.webp)


Shira Portal yawe inyuma ya telefone yawe. Suzuma ko urupapuro rwa Address rw’uwuronka n’amahera ari ukuri. Nimba ari ukwo biri, nufyonde kuri buto kugira ngo ukomeze.


![Image](assets/fr/43.webp)


Suzuma ko amahera y’ugucuruza ari yo, hanyuma wongere ukande kuri buto kugira ngo ushire umukono ku gicuruzwa cawe.


![Image](assets/fr/44.webp)


Ibicuruzwa vyawe vyashizweko umukono. Ushobora kumenya amakuru yayo ubwa nyuma kuri Nunchuk, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Broadcast transaction*" kugira ngo uyitangaze ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.


![Image](assets/fr/45.webp)


Ivyo mwaguriye ubu birindiriye kwemezwa.


![Image](assets/fr/46.webp)
