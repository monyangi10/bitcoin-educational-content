## Ivyo bikoresho vya BIP39 passphrase.


Niba utazi ico BIP39 passphrase ari co, canke nimba utagusobanukiye neza ingene ikora, ndagusavye cane ko wobanza kuraba iyi nyigisho, ivuga ivy’ishimikiro ry’ivyiyumviro bikenewe kugira ngo utahure ingorane zijanye n’ugukoresha passphrase :


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7

Zirikana ko umaze gushinga passphrase kuri Wallet yawe, Mnemonic yawe yonyene ntizohagije kugira ngo wongere ubone amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins. Uzokenera Mnemonic na passphrase. Ikindi, uzokenera kwinjira muri passphrase igihe cose ufunguye COLDCARD Q yawe. Ivyo bituma umutekano urushirizaho gushikira COLDCARD no kumenya PIN bidahagije ata passphrase.


Ku COLDCARDs, ufise uburyo bubiri bwo gucunga passphrase yawe:


1. **Classic entry:** Ushiramwo passphrase n’amaboko igihe cose ukoresheje Hardware Wallet yawe, nk’uko ubigira ku zindi nkweto z’ibikoresho. COLDCARD Q yorosha iki gikorwa n’ikibazo cayo cuzuye.


2. **Ushobora guhitamwo gushiramwo passphrase yawe ukayibika ku ikarita ya microSD. Muri ivyo, uzokenera kwinjiza microSD muri COLDCARD Q igihe cose uzoba uyikoresha. Zirikana ko iyi microSD izokora gusa kuri COLDCARD Q yawe kandi atari backup. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko nawe ubika kopi y’igitabu cawe ca passphrase ku kintu kigaragara, nk’impapuro canke ivyuma.**

## Gufungura amahitamwo


COLDCARDs na zo nyene zironka inyungu mu buryo bwinshi bwo gufungura igikoresho. Reka tumenye vyinshi ku bijanye n’izo nzira ziteye imbere.


### PINs z'ubuhendanyi


PIN y'ubuhendanyi ni kode ya PIN ya kabiri itandukanye n'iyo yasobanuwe mu gihe c'ugutunganya igikoresho ca mbere. Iyi kode ikoreshwa mu gutuma habaho ibikorwa vyihariye vyatunganijwe imbere y'igihe igihe nyene yinjijwe iyo COLDCARD ifunguwe. Ushobora gutunganya ama PIN menshi y'ubuhendanyi, imwe yose ihuye n'igikorwa gitandukanye. Ivyo bikoresho bigufasha guhindura COLDCARD yawe n’ingene ukoresha umutekano wawe bwite. Birakenewe cane cane iyo umuntu agoberewe ku mubiri, nk'igihe umuntu ariko arasahura (bikunze kwitwa mu muryango wa Bitcoin ko ari "*$5 wrench attack*").


Kugira ngo ukoreshe PIN y'ubuhendanyi maze uyifatanye n'igikorwa, genda kuri `Ivyagezwe > Ivyagezwe vyo kwinjira > PIN z'ubuhendanyi`.


![CCQ](assets/fr/11.webp)


Hitamwo "*Ongerako ubuhinga bushasha*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/12.webp)


Gushinga kode ya PIN kugira ngo ifatanye n’igikorwa maze wibuke kuyibika.


![CCQ](assets/fr/13.webp)


Hanyuma uhitemwo igikorwa kizokorwa ubwaco igihe cose winjije iyi PIN y’Uruyeri. Akira urutonde rw'ibikorwa biriho kuri PIN:




- "*Brick Self*: Iki gikorwa gisenya COLDCARD Q chips zompi iyo Trick PIN yinjijwe, bigatuma igikoresho kidashobora gukoreshwa na gato. Bizoba rero bidashoboka ko gisubira kugurishwa, gusubira kugikoresha canke mbere kugisubiza muri Coinkite. Ico gikoresho kizokwigera kidakoreshwa. ushobora gushika ku ma bitcoins yawe **Iyumvire**: ata backup y’umubiri y’ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic n’iryo passphrase, ama bitcoins yawe azozimangana ubuziraherezo.


![CCQ](assets/fr/14.webp)




- "*Wipe seed*": Iyi menu itanga ibikorwa vyinshi vyo gukuraho seed, ni ukuvuga gusubiramwo COLDCARD utayisenyuye. Udakunze uburyo bwa "*Brick Self*", bizoshoboka gusubira gutunganya igikoresho ukoresheje ububiko bw'ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic. Ariko rero, ata n’iyi backup, ama bitcoins yawe azobura. Akira amahitamwo ariho:
 - "*Wipe & Reboot* : Ikuraho seed igasubira gufungura COLDCARD ata makuru yerekanwa ku rubuga."
 - "*Silent Wipe*": Ahanagura seed mu gacerere, agafungura COLDCARD ku Wallet y'ikinyoma nk'aho ataco vyabaye.
 - "*Wipe -> Wallet*": Ikuraho seed mu buryo buteye ubwoba maze ifungure COLDCARD ku Wallet ya kabiri yatunganijwe mbere, yateguwe nk'umutego. Iyi Wallet ishobora kuba irimwo agace gatoyi k’amahera yawe ya Bitcoin uzigamye kugira ngo uhaze uwugutera.
 - "*Vuga Yahanaguwe, Hagarara*": Ifuta seed maze igaragaze ubutumwa `seed yahanaguwe, Hagarara` ku mugaragaro.


![CCQ](assets/fr/15.webp)




- "*Inguvu Wallet*": N'iki gikorwa, kode ya PIN y'ubuhendanyi ifungura Wallet iva kuri seed ikoresheje BIP85. Iyi Wallet ya kabiri irashobora gukoreshwa nk’umutego wo guhaza uwuyitera. COLDCARD ikora nk’aho yoba ari Wallet nyayo, ariko ata PIN nyamukuru (itandukanye n’iya Trick PIN), uwutera ntazokwigera ashobora gushika kuri Wallet nyayo. Iyi nzira igenewe gutuma abantu bemera ko Wallet ifatanye na Trick PIN ari yo yonyene iriho.


![CCQ](assets/fr/16.webp)




- "*Login Countdown*": Iyi menyu ihuriza hamwe ibikorwa bifise igiharuro imbere y'uko bishirwa mu ngiro. **Imbuzi**, zimwe murizo zishobora gusenyura igikoresho cawe canke zigatuma utakaza amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins. Aha niho hari ibikorwa bitobito biriho:
 - "*Wipe & Countdown* : Ikuraho seed mu bwenge bwa COLDCARD, hanyuma igatangura guharura isaha imwe. Utazigamye Mnemonic yawe canke passphrase, bitcoins zawe zizobura. Iyi option igenewe guhenda umuterabwoba ngo yiyumvire ko igikoresho ca e kizofungura ku, ivy’uruganda.
 - "*Countdown & Brick*": Itangura guharura isaha imwe, mu mpera zayo COLDCARD isenya ibice vyayo bibiri vy'umutekano, bigatuma idashobora gukoreshwa ubuziraherezo. Ata backup, ama bitcoins yawe azobura. Ico gikorwa gigenewe guhenda uwutera, yiyumvira ko arindiriye gufungurwa, mu gihe mu vy’ukuri ico gikoresho kizokwica.
 - "*Just Countdown* : Itera igiharuro c'isaha imwe yoroshe, hanyuma COLDCARD igasubira gutangura ata kindi gikorwa. seed ntiyimburwa kandi igikoresho kiguma ari co. Urabe ko udashobora kwivanga n'iki gikorwa n'ibara ry'ukuri ry'ibara ry'inyuma ry'ibara ry'inyuma ry'ibara ry'inyuma ry'ibara ry'inyuma, rivugwa mu bice bikurikira, Wallet.


![CCQ](assets/fr/17.webp)




- "*Look Blank*": Ico gikorwa gituma COLDCARD isa n'iyitagira ikintu, bikaba bitanga iciyumviro c'uko seed yasubiwemwo. Mu vy’ukuri, ntaco bishika kandi seed iguma ari yo nyene. Ivyo bishushanya COLDCARD itakoreshejwe canke yasubiwemwo.


![CCQ](assets/fr/18.webp)




- "*Just Reboot* : Iyo PIN ya Trick ikoreshejwe, COLDCARD irasubira gufungura gusa. Nta kindi gikorwa gikorwa."


![CCQ](assets/fr/19.webp)




- "*Delta Mode*": Iki gikorwa gikomeye, kigenewe abakoresha bazi utuntu n'utundi, gigenewe kurwanya ibitero vy'uguhatira bikomeye cane, vyaba bivuye kuri Leta canke incuti ifise amakuru y'agaciro. Iyo Delta Mode ikoreshejwe, COLDCARD itanga uburenganzira bwo gushika ku Wallet nyayo, igatuma uwutera ashobora kugendagenda no kugenzura ko ari Wallet nyayo. Ariko rero, imikono y’ugucuruza irabujijwe, gutyo bikaba bibuza ko umuntu yoherezwa Bitcoin. Ikindi, ukuronka ijambo Mnemonic birabujijwe kandi ukugerageza kwose kwo kurironka kuzotuma rikurwaho. Kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kwizera, PIN ya Trick ikoreshwa na Delta Mode itegerezwa gusangira intango imwe na PIN nyayo (kugira ngo yerekane amajambo amwe arwanya ubuhendanyi), ariko inyuma itegerezwa kuba itandukanye.


![CCQ](assets/fr/20.webp)


Umaze guhitamwo igikorwa, wemeze ivyo wahisemwo.


![CCQ](assets/fr/21.webp)


Ushobora rero kubona PINs zose zatunganijwe mu rutonde rwihariye.


![CCQ](assets/fr/22.webp)


Mu guhitamwo PIN y'ubuhendanyi iriho, ushobora kugenzura igikorwa gifitaniye isano. Ushobora kandi kuyihisha ukoresheje "*Hide Trick*", ugatuma itaboneka mu rutonde rwa PIN y'Uruyeri. Ushobora kuyifuta ukanda kuri "*Delete Trick*", canke uhindure kode ya PIN mu gihe ugumye igikorwa gifitaniye isano na "*Change PIN*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/23.webp)


Ihitamwo rya "*Add If Wrong*", riboneka mu rutonde rwa "*Trick PIN*", rigufasha gutunganya igikorwa kinaka kivyuka ubwaco inyuma y'umubare kanaka w'ibigeragezo bitari vyo vyo kwinjiza kode ya PIN nyamukuru. Umubare w'ibigeragezo vyemewe ushobora gushirwaho mu gihe co gutunganya.

Kugira ngo ukoreshe iyo nzira, genda kuri menyu `Ivyagezwe > Ivyagezwe vyo kwinjira > Imfunguruzo zo gucapura`.

### Kwinjira Guharura


Iryo hitamwo rigufasha gutegeka igiharuro gitunganye igihe cose ugerageje gufungura COLDCARD yawe. Bishobora gushirwa mu nzira yawe yo gucungera umutekano mu gutevya gushika ku gikoresho iyo habaye ubusuma, canke mu gutegeka gutevya imbere yo gusinya ku giciro, nk’akarorero kugira ngo ukingire iyo umuntu ahagaritse. Ariko rero, iyo nzira yo guharura irakora ku vyo ukoresha vyose, harimwo n’igihe ukoresha mu buryo bubereye COLDCARD yawe, ivyo na vyo bikaba bigutegeka kwihangana. Urabe maso ntuvange iyi nzira n'igikorwa ca "*Just Countdown*", gikora gusa iyo hakoreshejwe PIN y'Uruyeri yihariye.


Kugira ngo utunganye iyi nzira, genda kuri menyu `Ivyagezwe > Ivyagezwe vyo kwinjira > Guharura kwinjira`.

Igihe cose uzoba ufunguye, uzosabwa kwinjiza kode yawe ya PIN.

Iyi option ishobora kugufasha kwiyoberanya COLDCARD yawe nk’igiharuro igihe ufungura. Kugira ngo ukoreshe iki gikoresho, genda kuri meni `Ivyagezwe > Ivyagezwe vy'Injira > Injira y'Ibarabara`.

## Gusambura neza COLDCARD yawe


Niba uriko urategura guta COLDCARD Q yawe, nk’akarorero kubera ko ubu uriko ukoresha iyindi Hardware Wallet, birahambaye ko usenya neza ico gikoresho. Ivyo bituma ata makuru yerekeye Wallet yawe ashobora gusubirwamwo n’uwundi muntu.


Hariho ingero zitatu zo gusenyura amakuru, bivanye n’ivyo ukeneye. Imbere y’uko utangura, urabe neza ko Wallet yawe yinjijwe mu yindi Hardware Wallet, ko ufise uburenganzira bwo kuronka amahera yawe yose kandi, ikiruta vyose, ko ufise ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic n’iryo ari ryo ryose passphrase, ivyo vyose bikaba bikora. Ata backup ya Wallet, ugusambura kwa COLDCARD yawe kuzotuma utakaza amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri.


Urugero rwa mbere rwo gusambura ni ugukuraho seed gusa. Iryo hitamwo rikuraho ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic mu ciyumviro ca COLDCARD, mu gihe risiga igikoresho gikora. Ni vyiza cane nimba ushaka gukoresha COLDCARD Q kandi mu gihe kizoza. Kugira ngo usibe seed mu mutwe, genda kuri `Ivyiza/Ibikoresho > Ahantu h'akaga > Ibikorwa vya seed > Gusambura seed`.


![CCQ](assets/fr/38.webp)


Urugero rwa kabiri rw’ugusambura ni uguhagarika ubudasiba ibice bibiri vy’umutekano vya COLDCARD biciye kuri iyo porogarama. Ico gikorwa gituma ico gikoresho kidashobora gukoreshwa rwose. Ntuzoshobora kuyigurisha, kuyikoresha canke kuyisubiza muri Coinkite: izosambuka ubuziraherezo. Kugira ngo ukomeze, kurikiza intambwe zavuzwe mu gice ca mbere ku bijanye na PIN ya "*Brick Me*", hanyuma winjize iyo PIN n'ibigirankana igihe ufungura COLDCARD.


Urugero rwa gatatu ni ugusambura ku mubiri ibihimba vyawe vy’umutekano vya COLDCARD Q. Nk’uko vyari biri imbere, ivyo bizotuma ico gikoresho kidashobora gukoreshwa ata gusubira inyuma. Kugira ngo ubikore, ukoreshe igikoresho co gutera umwobo mu bipande bibiri biri hejuru iburyo bw'ico gikoresho (igihe umaze guhindukiza), hafi y'aho handitswe ngo "*SHOOT HERE*".


**Ivyitonderwa bihambaye** :




- Kugira ngo wirinde gukubitwa n’umuyagankuba, nukuremwo amabateri y’ico gikoresho hanyuma ugikure mu muyagankuba imbere y’uko ugikoresha.
- Rindira iminota mikeyi umaze kuzimya igikoresho imbere y’uko utangura gucukura.
- Ambara amaganzu n’amataratara y’umutekano kugira ngo ubone umutekano wawe.
