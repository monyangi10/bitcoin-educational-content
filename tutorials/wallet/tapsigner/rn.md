Hardware Wallet ni igikoresho c’ubuhinga bwa none gikoreshwa mu gucunga no gucungera imfunguruzo z’ibanga za Bitcoin Wallet. Mu buryo butandukanye n’amasakoshi y’amaporogarama (canke amasakoshi ya Hot) ashirwa ku mashini zikoreshwa muri rusangi akenshi zifatanye na Internet, amasakoshi y’ibikoresho atuma umuntu ashobora kwitandukanya n’imfunguruzo z’ibanga, ivyo bikaba bigabanya ingorane zo guterwa n’abantu no kwiba.


Intumbero nyamukuru ya Hardware Wallet ni ugugabanya ubushobozi bw’ico gikoresho kugira ngo kigabanye aho gishobora gutera. Igitero gitoyi kandi bisigura ko hariho ibitero bikeyi bishobora gutera, ni ukuvuga, utugingo ngengabuzima dukeyi muri sisitemu abatera bashobora gukoresha kugira ngo bashobore gushika ku bitcoins.


Ni vyiza gukoresha Hardware Wallet kugira ngo ukingire ama bitcoins yawe, cane cane iyo ufise amahera menshi, yaba mu gaciro nyako canke nk’igice c’itunga ryawe ryose.


Ivyuma vy’amasakoshi bikoreshwa hamwe n’ubuhinga bwo gucunga Wallet kuri mudasobwa canke kuri telefone ngendanwa. Iyi porogarama icungera uguhingura amafaranga, ariko umukono w’ibanga ukenewe kugira ngo ayo mafaranga yemezwe ukorwa gusa muri Hardware Wallet. Ivyo bisigura ko imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitigera zishirwa ahantu hashobora guhungabana.


Hardware wallets zitanga uburinzi bubiri ku muntu akoresha: ku ruhande rumwe, zikingira bitcoins zawe ibitero vyo kure mu kugumiza imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitari ku murongo, kandi ku rundi ruhande, muri rusangi zitanga uburinzi bwiza bw’umubiri ku kugerageza gukura imfunguruzo. Kandi ni kuri ivyo bipimo 2 vy’umutekano nyene umuntu ashobora gucira urubanza no gushiramwo ivyerekanwa bitandukanye biri kw’isoko.


Muri iyi nyigisho, ndasaba kuvumbura kimwe muri ivyo bisubizo: Tapsigner ya Coinkite.


## Intangamarara y'Igikoresho co Gushiramwo


Tapsigner ni Hardware Wallet yakozwe mu buryo bw’ikarita NFC n’ishirahamwe Coinkite, na ryo nyene rizwi mu gukora Coldcards.


![TAPSIGNER NUNCHUK](assets/notext/01.webp)


Tapsigner iremesha kubika umugwi ugizwe n’urufunguzo rw’ibanga rw’umukuru na chain code bihuye na BIP32, kugira ngo haboneke igiti c’urufunguzo rw’ibanga. Izo mfunguruzo zishobora gukoreshwa mu gusinya ku bikorwa mu gushiramwo Tapsigner kuri telefone canke ku gikoresho co gusoma ikarita ya NFC.

Iyi karata ya NFC igurishwa ku madolari 19.99, ariko ni ikintu gishoboka cane ugereranije n’izindi nkweto z’ibikoresho ziboneka ku isoko. Ariko rero, kubera uburyo bwayo, Tapsigner ntitanga uburyo bwinshi bwo guhitamwo nk’ibindi bikoresho. Biragaragara ko ata bateri, ata kamera, canke igikoresho co gusoma ikarita ya micro SD, kuko ari ikarita. Njye mbona, ikibazo cayo kinini ni ukubura igicapo kuri Hardware Wallet, ivyo bikaba bituma ishobora guterwa n’ubwoko bumwe bumwe bw’ibitero vyo kure. Nkako, ivyo bituma uwubikoresha ashirako umukono ata co abona kandi akizigira ivyo abona kuri orodinateri yiwe.


Naho Tapsigner ifise aho igarukira, irashobora gushimisha kubera igiciro cayo kigabanutse. Iyi Wallet ishobora gukoreshwa cane cane mu kwongerera umutekano Wallet ikoresha amahera yongerako Wallet izigama irindwa na Hardware Wallet ifise igikingira. Igereranya kandi umuti mwiza ku bafise amafaranga makeyi y’ama bitcoins kandi badashaka gushiramwo amayero ijana mu gikoresho gikomeye cane. Ikindi kandi, gukoresha Tapsigner mu mitunganyirize ya Multisig, canke bishoboka mu mitunganyirize ya Wallet ifise timelock muri kazoza, birashobora gutanga inyungu zishimishije.


## Ni gute umuntu yogura Tapsigner?


Ico gikoresho co gushirako umukono kiraboneka kugira ngo umuntu agure [ku rubuga rwemewe rwa Coinkite](https://ububiko.coinkite.com/ububiko/urutonde/ububiko). Kugira ngo uyigure mu iduka ry’umubiri, urashobora kandi kubona [urutonde rw’abagurisha bemerewe](https://coinkite.com/abagurisha) ku rubuga.


Uzokenera kandi telefone ihuye n’ivy’itumanaho rya NFC, canke igikoresho ca USB co gusoma amakarata ya NFC ku nkuru isanzwe ya 13.56 MHz.

Ico gikoresho gikoresha urugero rwa BIP32. Ivyo bisigura ko ugukomoka kw’imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga zikingira amafaranga yawe bitashingiye ku majambo ya Mnemonic nk’amasakoshi ya BIP39, ariko bishingiye ku rufunguzo rw’ibanga n’urufunguzo rwa chain code. Izo 2 Elements zica mu gikorwa ca HMAC kugira ngo zibone mu buryo butegekanijwe n'ubukuru n'ibindi bice vya Wallet yawe.


Urufunguzo rw'ibanga rwa mbere ruterwa n'i TRNG (*Urufunguzo rw'Imibare y'Imburakimazi y'Ukuri*) rwinjijwe muri Tapsigner yawe. Ku rundi ruhande, igitabu ca master chain code kigomba gutangwa kivuye hanze. Kuri iyi ntambwe, urafise amahitamwo: reka Nunchuk generate ubwawe mu gufyonda kuri "*Automatic*", canke generate wewe ubwawe uhisemwo "*Advanced*" ukayishira mu kibanza catanzwe.

## Ni gute wosubirana Wallet iyo Tapsigner yatakaye?


Niba waratakaje Tapsigner yawe, urashobora kuronka Wallet yawe ukoresheje kode yanditswe inyuma y’ikarata. Ni ngombwa rero kubika iyo kode itandukanye n’iya Tapsigner, kuko iyo ikarita itakaye, n’ugushika kuri iyo kode na vyo nyene birabura. Uzokenera kandi ububiko bwa Wallet.


Kugira ngo ukire, tuzokoresha porogarama ya Nunchuk, ariko uzirikane ko ivyo bisigura gucungera amahera yawe mu gihe gito mu Hot Wallet. Nimba Tapsigner yawe yariko iraronka amahera menshi, niwiyumvire gukurikira iyo nzira nyene yo gusubirana ukoresheje Coldcard nshasha.


Ugurure app ya Nunchuk ukande kuri buto "*+*" iri iruhande y'agace ka "*Keys*".


![TAPSIGNER NUNCHUK](assets/notext/49.webp)


Hitamwo "*Ongera urufunguzo rwa NFC*".


![TAPSIGNER NUNCHUK](assets/notext/50.webp)


Hitamwo uburyo "*Gusubiza urufunguzo rwa TAPSIGNER mu bubiko*".


![TAPSIGNER NUNCHUK](assets/notext/51.webp)


Uca urungikwa ku gikoresho cawe co gutohoza amadosiye. Rondera kandi uhitemwo dosiye y'ububiko bwa Wallet yawe. Mu bisanzwe, izina ry'iyi dosiye ritangura na `backup...`.


![TAPSIGNER NUNCHUK](assets/notext/52.webp)


Injira ijambobanga rifungura dosiye y'ububiko. Iryo jambobanga rihuye n’iryo mu ntango ryanditswe inyuma ya Tapsigner yawe.


![TAPSIGNER NUNCHUK](assets/notext/53.webp)

Hanyuma uhitemwo izina ry’ugukira kwawe Wallet.

![TAPSIGNER NUNCHUK](assets/notext/54.webp)


Ubu warasubiye kuronka uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ama bitcoins yawe. Wallet yawe ubu icungiwe nk'i Hot Wallet iboneka mu gice ca "*Imfunguruzo*" ca porogarama ya Nunchuk. Igikurikira, ukeneye gukora urutonde rushasha rw'imfunguruzo z'ubuhinga bwo gukingira mu gice ca "*Wallets*" mu gufatanya urwo rufunguzo na rwo. Kugira ngo ubikore, urashobora gusubira gukurikira intambwe ziri mu gice ca "*Uko wokora Wallet kuri Tapsigner?*" c'iyi nyigisho.


![TAPSIGNER NUNCHUK](assets/notext/55.webp)


Niba waratakaje Tapsigner yawe, ndaguhanura cane ngo uhite urungika ama bitcoins yawe ku yindi Wallet ufise, nziza cane irindwa na Hardware Wallet. Nkako, Tapsigner watakaje yoshobora kuba ari mu maboko atari yo. Ni ngombwa rero gukura ubusa muri Wallet uherutse gukira, ukareka kuyikoresha.


Urasangwa, ubu uriko urakoresha Tapsigner! Niwaba wabonye ko iyi nyigisho ari ngirakamaro, noshima iyo ushobora gusiga urukumu hejuru aha hepfo. Ntutinye gusangiza abandi iyi nkuru ku mbuga zanyu zo gusangirirako amakuru. Murakoze cane!
