Muri iyi nyigisho tuzokwihweza Cashu.me, Wallet ishingiye ku mucukumbuzi ku kwishura Bitcoin ku giti cawe ukoresheje Chaumian ecash. Imbere y’uko twisukamwo, reka tugire intangamarara ngufi ku vyerekeye ecash n’ingene ikora.


## Intangamarara y'amahera


Ibaze ko ufise amafaranga y’ubuhinga bwa none akora neza neza nk’amafaranga y’umubiri mu mufuko wawe - ay’ibanga, ahita, kandi akoreshwa n’abandi ata bahuza. Ivyo nivyo ecash ishoboza: uburyo bwo kwishura mu buryo bwa digitale bugarura ubuzima bwite bw’amahera y’umubiri mw’isi ya digitale. Udakunze onchain-Bitcoin, yandika ibikorwa vyose ku Ledger ya bose iboneka ku muntu wese, ecash irema ibimenyetso vy’ibanga bigereranya agaciro nyakuri ka Bitcoin mu gihe iguma ibanga ingeso zawe zo gukoresha amahera.


Wiyumvire ecash nk’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none bibitswe ku gikoresho cawe - iyo ubifashe, uba ubifise, nk’amahera y’umubiri. Ivyo bimenyetso bitangwa n’ibikorwa vyizigirwa vyitwa `Mints` bifise amafaranga y’ububiko bwa Bitcoin. Iyo ukoresheje ecash, ntuba uriko uratangaza amafaranga yawe ku rubuga rwose. Ahubwo, uriko urahana ibimenyetso vy’ibanga ataco uhinduye n’abandi, ugatuma haba uburyo bwo kwishura bumeze nk’uguha umuntu amahera kuruta kwishura mu buryo bwa kera.


Cashu ni uburyo bwo gukoresha amafaranga y'ubuntu kandi bufunguye bwa Chaumian bwakozwe kuri Bitcoin. Iryo koraniro ryubakiye ku bushakashatsi bwa David Chaum bwo mu myaka ya 1980, akoresheje `imikono y’impumyi` kugira ngo umuntu agire ubuzima bwiwe bwite. Iyo uronse ama ecash tokens, mint irayashirako umukono itazi aho azokoreshwa - ikintu gihambaye cane kibuza gukurikirana amafaranga. Igihambaye ni uko ecash itasubirira Bitcoin; irawuzuza mu gutorera umuti ibibazo bimwebimwe bihambaye biza n’ibisabwa vy’ubwubatsi bwa Bitcoin. Itanga ubuzima bwite amafaranga y’umubiri atanga (ivyo Ledger igaragara ya Bitcoin idafise) kandi ishoboza gukora amafaranga makeyi ata mahera ya Blockchain canke guteba kwemeza.


Ecash ifatanya neza na Lightning Network. Ukoresha Lightning kugira ngo ushire Bitcoin mu giciro c’amahera (uhindure agaciro ka Bitcoin yawe mu bimenyetso vy’amahera) no kugira ngo ukureho mu nyuma (uhindure ibimenyetso bisubire mu gice c’amahera gishobora gukoreshwa). Ivyo vyose hamwe, bikora ihuriro rikomeye: Bitcoin itanga umutekano Layer, Lightning ishoboza amafaranga yihuta n’ugukorana n’urubuga kandi ecash yongerako ubuzima bwite Layer butuma kwishura mu buryo bwa digitale bisubira kwumva ko ari ivy’ibanga vy’ukuri.


## Amahera.jewe


Cashu.me ni `Iporogarama y'urubuga (PWA)` ishira mu ngiro amasezerano ya Cashu - ishirwa mu ngiro ry'amahera ya Chaumian yagenewe Bitcoin. Nk'uko PWA, ikora mu buryo butaziguye mu mucukumbuzi wawe ataco usaba kuyishiramwo mu maduka y'amaporogarama, naho woba ushobora kuyishiramwo` ku gikoresho cawe kugira ngo ushobore kuyironka bitagoranye. Ubu buryo bushingiye ku rubuga buratuma habaho uguhuza kwagutse mu mirongo yose y’ibikorwa mu gihe umutekano ugumaho biciye ku mirongo ngenderwako y’ubuhinga bw’ibanga aho guhagarika uburenganzira bw’urubuga.


## 🎉 Ibirango vy'ingenzi


Reka twinjire mu biranga maze turabe ivyo Cashu.me ifise:



- Chaumian ecash ku Muravyo**: Ikoresha imikono y'impumyi kugira ngo amahinguriro ntashobore gukurikirana amafaranga y'abakoresha canke amateka y'ibikorwa
- Ukwirinda ibimenyetso**: Ugenzura ibimenyetso vya ecash mu karere ukoresheje ijambo ryawe seed
- seed amajambo y'ububiko**: amajambo 12 yo gusubizaho amajambo yo gusubizaho Wallet
- Ukwigenga kwa Mint**: Gukorana n'ama mint menshi yigenga—ntufunzwe mu mutanga umwe
- Ibikorwa vy'ubuntu, vy'ubuntu**: Mu gihe kimwe, ukwishyura biraheza mu masegonda make ata mafaranga
- Ubwubatsi buzigama ubuzima bwite**: Amahinguriro y'amahera ntashobora kubona uwukorana n'uwundi
- Offline ecash**: Wohereza/kwakira ibimenyetso biciye ku nzira yo gutanga amakuru yo mu karere, nka NFC, kode ya QR, Bluetooth, n'ibindi ata nzira ya interineti
- Vumbura amafaranga y'amahera biciye kuri Nostr**: Rondera no kugenzura amafaranga yizigirwa biciye ku masezerano ya Nostr
- Swap ecash hagati y'ama mints**: Ama mints yose avuga umuravyo ivyo, bisigura ko ushobora guhindura agaciro hagati yayo.
- Igenzura rya kure Wallet yawe ukoresheje Nostr Wallet Ihuza (NWC)**: Huza n'izindi porogarama nka Nostr Client maze utangure gukora zapping biciye kuri NWC


Ico nyene ni `ukwizigira`: mu gihe ugenzura ibimenyetso ubwavyo, utegerezwa kwizigira ama mints kugira ngo azigame amafaranga y’inyuma ya Bitcoin. Nk’uko inyandiko za Cashu zibivuga:


> ...i mint iriko irarongora ibikorwa remezo vya Lightning kandi iracungera ama satoshis y'abakoresha ecash y'i mints. Abakoresha bategerezwa kwizigira mint kugira ngo Redeem ecash yabo igihe bashaka guhindura Lightning.

— Ivyanditswe vya Cashu, [Ibibazo rusangi vyerekeye umutekano n’ubuzima bwite](https://docs.cashu.space/faq#ibibazo rusangi-vyerekeye umutekano n’ubuzima bwite)


Ivyo bituma ecash iba umuti wo kubungabunga Bitcoin ubwayo, naho woba ufise ububasha bwose ku bimenyetso.


## 1️⃣ Gutegura mbere


Tangira usura [Wallet.cashu.me](https://Wallet.cashu.me) mu mucukumbuzi wawe. Kubera ko Cashu.me ari `PWA`, ntukeneye kuyikura mu maduka y’ama app, ushobora gusa gufungura urubuga mu mucukumbuzi wawe. Kugira ngo ushobore kuyironka bitamugoye, urashobora kuyishira ku gicapo c’imbere c’igikoresho cawe.


Kugira ngo ushiremwo PWA, nukore kuri buto ya ⋮ mu mucukumbuzi wawe hanyuma uhitemwo `Ongera ku rubuga rw'imbere`. Iyo umaze kuyishiramwo, funga urupapuro rwa browser maze utangure Cashu.me uri ku rubuga rw’intango rw’igikoresho cawe. Ku mugaragaro w'akabazo, kanda `Ibikurikira` kugira ngo ukomeze.


Umutekano ni ikintu gihambaye cane. Bika neza amajambo yawe ya seed mu gikoresho co gucungera ijambobanga canke, mbere n’ikiruta vyose, uyandike ku mpapuro. Iyi mvugo y’amajambo 12 yo gusubiza ni yo nzira yawe yonyene yo gusubiza amahera iyo utakaje uburenganzira bwo gukoresha iki gikoresho. Kanda ku 👁️ icon kugira ngo uhishure ijambo ryawe rya seed, wandike neza amajambo yose 12 mu buryo bukurikiranye, hanyuma ushire akamenyetso ku gasandugu kavuga ko `Nayanditse`. Kanda `Ibikurikira` kugira ngo ukomeze, hanyuma ushire akamenyetso ku gasandugu kugira ngo wemeze ko wemeye `amabwirizwa` ari ku mugaragaro ukurikira.


![image](assets/en/01.webp)


Uhejeje gutegura, uzokenera kwifatanya na `Mint`. Kanda kuri `ADD MINT` ukurikiwe na `DISCOVER MINTS` kugira ngo ubone ama mints yashikirijwe n'umuryango wa Nostr. Kugira ngo ubone ibindi, urashobora gusubiramwo ibiharuro vy’amahera kuri [bitcoinmints.com](bitcoinmints.com).


Inyuma y'aho, ushireko `Fyonda kugira ngo ubone ama mints` kugira ngo ubone urutonde rwose. Hitamwo mint mu gukopa URL yayo, ukayishira mu kibanza ca URL, hanyuma ukayiha izina rimenyekana. Kuri aka karorero, tuzokoresha:


URL: `ibice.amafaranga/Bitcoin`

Izina: `Ibice bike`


![image](assets/en/02.webp)


Kanda kuri `ADD MINT` kugira ngo urangize igikorwa. Ku mugaragaro wo kwemeza, suzuma ko wizigira umukoresha w'iyi mint, hanyuma wongere ukande kuri `ADD MINT`. Igikoresho ca Minibits kizoboneka ubu ku rubuga rwawe rw’Ingoro. Wallet yawe imaze gushirwaho, uzokenera kuyitanga amahera imbere yo gukora amafaranga.


![image](assets/en/03.webp)


## 2️⃣ Gufasha Wallet yawe


Cashu.me itanga uburyo bubiri butandukanye bwo gufasha Wallet yawe. Iyo ukanda kuri `Receive` ku rubuga rw'imbere, uzobona uburyo bwo kwakira amahera biciye kuri `ECASH` canke biciye ku `LIGHTNING.` Reka dusuzume uburyo bwompi.


![image](assets/en/04.webp)


### Infashanyo biciye ku MURARO


Ihitamwo rya mbere ni ugufasha Wallet biciye ku Muravyo Invoice. `Hitamwo umubare` nimba wongeyeko umubare utandukanye maze usobanure `umubare (Sats)` ushaka kwakira. Hanyuma ukande kuri `CREATE Invoice.` Ubu rero ubona QR-Code yerekanwa ushobora gucapura n'iyindi Wallet y'umuravyo canke ushobora gusa `Copy` Invoice ukayishira mu yindi Wallet kugira ngo wishyure kandi ushiremwo amahera yawe Wallet.


![image](assets/en/05.webp)


### Kwakira amafaranga


Uburyo bwa ecash buratuma uronka ibimenyetso biva ku yindi ecash Wallet. Tangana n'ugukanda kuri buto ya `Kwakira`, hanyuma uhitemwo uburyo bwa `ECASH`. Uzoshobora `Gushiramwo` canke `Guca` canke ukoreshe `NFC` kugira ngo ushiremwo Cashu token ivuye ku yindi Wallet. Niwahitamwo gushiramwo, shiramwo urudodo rwa token wakopiye mu yindi Wallet, `Igiciro` na `Igiciro` bizokwerekanwa ubwavyo. Kanda kuri `RECEIVE` kugira ngo urangize gutanga, maze Sats izoboneka muri Wallet yawe. Urabona ko ubu amahera yawe asigaye agabanywa mu bibanza vyinshi vy’amahera. Nk'akarorero, woshobora kugira 1.000 Sats mu `Mint` yawe ya Minibits n'iyindi 1.000 Sats mu `Mint` ya Coinos. Ukwo gutandukanya hagati y’ibibanza bitandukanye vy’ubuhinga ni ikintu gihambaye mu vy’ubwubatsi bwa Cashu.


![image](assets/en/06.webp)


### Guhinduranya hagati y'ibikoresho


Niba utagizigira ikigo c'amahera wongeyeko, cashu.me itanga uburyo bwo `Guhindura` amahera kuva ku kigo kimwe uja ku kindi. Ja ku rubuga rw'amafaranga y'amafaranga maze ugende hasi gushika ubonye `Ivy'amafaranga menshi`. Hitamwo mint `FROM` na `TO` mu bimenyetso bimanuka maze winjize amafaranga wipfuza gutanga. Kanda `SWAP`kugira ngo ushire ibimenyetso hagati y'ibikoresho. Ivyo bizoshirwa mu ngiro biciye mu gukoresha Lightning, rero urakeneye gusiga umwanya w’amahera yo Lightning ashobora gutanga. Mu karorero kanje, 1 sat yari ihagije.


![image](assets/en/07.webp)


## 3️⃣ Kurungika amafranga


Kugira ngo wohereze Sats, Cashu.me itanga uburyo bubiri. Kurungika biciye kuri `ecash` canke biciye kuri `umuravyo`. Reka turabe izo nzira zompi.


### Kurungika biciye ku Muravyo


Kugira ngo wohereze biciye ku Muravyo, kurikiza izi ntambwe:


1. Kanda kuri `SEND` ku gicapo c'imbere maze uhitemwo `Umuraravyo`

2. Iyi porogaramu izogusaba kwinjiza `Umuravyo Invoice` canke `-Address`. Ushobora gushiramwo Invoice/Address ataco uhinduye, canke ukoreshe kode ya QR kugira ngo uyifate mu maso, hanyuma ukemeze ukoresheje `ENTER`

3. Hitamwo Mint ushaka kwishurako ukoresheje Dropdown field hanyuma ukande kuri `PAY` kugira ngo wemeze. **Iciyumviro**: hariho kandi uburyo bwo gukoresha `Multinut` munsi ya `Ivyagezwe` -> `Igerageza` bigufasha kwishura amafagitire ava mu bigo vyinshi icarimwe.

4. Uhejeje neza, uzobona ivyemezo vy’ukwishurwa n’amahera azokurwa ku giciro cawe.


![image](assets/en/08.webp)


### Ukohereza biciye kuri ecash


Kurungika ecash navyo nyene biragororotse.


1. Kanda kuri `SEND` hanyuma uhitemwo `ECASH`.

2. `Hitamwo mint` hanyuma winjize `Igiciro` ushaka kohereza muri Sats hanyuma ukande kuri `SEND` kugira ngo wemeze

3. Ivyo bituma haba `Animated QR Code` ushobora guhindura mu guhindura umuvuduko n'ubunini. Umuntu wese arashobora gucapura iyi QR Code kuri Redeem Sats ubwo nyene, canke ushobora gukanda COPY kugira ngo wohereze urudodo rwa token ku wundi muntu biciye ku yindi nzira nka Bluetooth, NFC, canke ubutumwa busanzwe.

4. Ndiko ndafungura uwundi Wallet. Nimushire mu gicapo maze muhitemwo `Kwakira ecash` mu yindi Wallet.


![image](assets/en/09.webp)


## 4️⃣ Ibindi biranga


Uretse ubushobozi bwo kohereza no kwakira, Cashu.me itanga ibindi bikoresho bikomeye vyongerera ubumenyi bwawe bwa Bitcoin mu bidukikije vya Nostr.


### Nostr Wallet Guhuza


Nostr Wallet Connect (`NWC`) ihindura ingene ukorana n'ibikorwa vya Nostr mu kurema ubufatanye butagira umurongo hagati ya Wallet yawe n'ibikorwa vy'imibano. Iyi porotokole iremesha porogaramu nka [Damus](https://damus.io/) canke [Primal](https://primal.net/home) gusaba amahera ataco ihinduye biciye ku nzira za Nostr ataco usaba kuva muri porogaramu.


Kugira ngo ushireho `NWC` muri Cashu.me:


1. Genda kuri `Ivyagezwe` hejuru ibubamfu ku rutonde rwa Hamburger

2. Genda ku gice ca `NOSTR Wallet CONNECT` hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya `Gushoboza`

3. Uzoca ushinga amahera yo gushinga amahera menshi abasaba bashobora gukoresha kuva kuri Wallet yawe.

4. Iyo umaze gutunganya, ushobora gukopa urudodo rw'ihuriro maze ukarushira mu mukiriya wa Nostr uwo ari we wese ashigikira `NWC`, bikaba bishoboza gukora zapping no tipping ubwo nyene.


![image](assets/en/10.webp)


### Umuravyo Address biciye kuri npub.cash


Cashu.me ifatanya [npub.cash](https://npub.cash/) kugira ngo iguhe umuco Address ukora neza n’umurongo wa Nostr. Aha ushobora kwiyandikisha no gusaba izina ryawe ry’ukoresha mu gutanga Nostr `nsec` yawe, igura 5.000 Sats kandi ishigikira umugambi npub.cash, canke ushobora gukoresha urufunguzo rwa bose rwa Nostr urwo ari rwo rwose (`npub`) ata kwiyandikisha.


Mbere, genda kuri `Ivyagezwe` hanyuma ukande kuri `Gushoboza` Umuravyo Address ukoresheje npub.cash. Ivyo bizotuma generate npub.cash Address ikoresha urudodo rwa `npub` ruvuye mu mvugo yawe Wallet seed ku buryo busanzwe.


Canke, genda kuri [uru rubuga](https://npub.cash/izina ry'ukoresha) kugira ngo usabe izina ry'ukoresha ukoresheje Nostr yawe bwite `nsec`, iguhe umuravyo Address wihariye nk'izina ry'ukoresha@npub.cash.


![image](assets/en/11.webp)


## 🎯 Insozero


Cashu.me itanga amahera y’ibanga ya Bitcoin akora nk’amahera y’umubiri — ubwo nyene kandi n’abandi ataco utangaza ku bijanye n’amateka yawe y’ugucuruza. Jewe ubwanje ndashima ubuhinga bwayo bwa PWA kuko bukora ataco buvuze mu maduka y’amaporogarama. Mu gufatanya umutekano wa Bitcoin, umuvuduko wa Lightning, n’ubuzima bwite bwa ecash, Wallet itanga ibikorwa vyo gukoresha bishobora kwongereza ukwemerwa kwa Bitcoin ku musi ku musi.


Naho ufise ububasha bwose ku bimenyetso vyawe vy’amahera biciye mu kwizigama, wibuke ko amahinguriro y’amahera akora nk’abandi bantu bizigira bafise amahera y’inyuma ya Bitcoin. Ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ama mint menshi no guhinduranya hagati yayo buratanga uguhinduranya mu gihe umuntu aguma afise ubuzima bwiwe bwite.


Kubera ibintu nka NWC na npub.cash aderesi, Cashu.me ni uburyo bukwegera Wallet ku bakiriya b’imibano baha agaciro ubuzima bwite n’ubusegaba ku bijanye n’amategeko manini y’ubuhinga.
