## Imenyekanisha


Muri iyi nyigisho ya videwo uzokwiga:


- Gukuraho no gucapura porogaramu ya Krux
- Gukora urufunguzo rushasha rw'ibanga no kururungika hanze biciye kuri kode ya QR
- Gufatanya n'ubururu Wallet kuri telefone ngendanwa kugira ngo ukoreshe rimwe
- Gushinga Multisig Wallet iri kumwe na Sparrow wallet.
- Gutohoza ibindi biranga igikoresho co gusinya ca Krux
