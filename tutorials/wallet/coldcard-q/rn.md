Hardware Wallet ni igikoresho c'ubuhinga bwa none gikoreshwa mu gucunga no gucungera imfunguruzo z'ibanga za Bitcoin Wallet. Mu buryo butandukanye n’amasakoshi y’amaporogarama (canke amasakoshi ya Hot) ashirwa ku mashini zikoreshwa muri rusangi akenshi zifatanye na Internet, amasakoshi y’ibikoresho aratuma imfunguruzo z’abantu zishobora gutandukanywa n’abandi, ivyo bikaba bigabanya ingorane zo gusuma n’ubusuma.


Intumbero nyamukuru ya Hardware Wallet ni ugugabanya ubushobozi bw’ico gikoresho uko bishoboka kwose kugira ngo kigabanye ahantu gishobora gutera. Igitero gitoyi bisigura kandi ko hariho ibitero bikeyi bishobora gutera, ni ukuvuga ko hariho ibice bikeyi vy’intege nke muri sisitemu abatera bashobora gukoresha kugira ngo bashobore gushika ku bitcoins.


Ni vyiza gukoresha Hardware Wallet kugira ngo ukingire amafaranga yawe, cane cane iyo ufise amafaranga menshi, haba mu gaciro nyako canke nk’igice c’itunga ryawe ryose.


Ivyuma vy’amasakoshi bikoreshwa hamwe n’ubuhinga bwo gucunga Wallet kuri mudasobwa canke kuri telefone ngendanwa. Iyi nyuma ni yo icungera uguhingura amafaranga, ariko umukono w’ibanga usabwa kugira ngo ayo mafaranga agire akamaro ukorerwa gusa muri Hardware Wallet. Ivyo bisigura ko imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitigera zishirwa ahantu hashobora guhungabana.


Ivyuma vy’amafaranga bitanga uburinzi bubiri ku muntu akoresha: ku ruhande rumwe, bikingira ama bitcoins yawe ibitero vyo kure mu kugumiza imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitari ku murongo, ku rundi ruhande, muri rusangi bitanga uburinzi bukomeye bw’umubiri ku kugerageza gukura imfunguruzo. Kandi ni kuri ivyo bipimo 2 vy’umutekano nyene dushobora gucira urubanza no gushikiriza mu migwi ibigereranyo bitandukanye biri kw’isoko.


Muri iyi nyigisho, ndashaka kubamenyesha umuti umwe nk'uwo: **COLDCARD Q**.

## Turabamenyesha COLDCARD Q


COLDCARD Q ni Hardware Wallet ikoresha Bitcoin gusa yakozwe n’ishirahamwe ryo muri Canada Coinkite, izwi cane kubera imodoka zayo za kera za MK. Q igereranya igicuruzwa cabo giteye imbere cane gushika ubu, kandi gishizwe ahantu nk'iherezo Bitcoin Hardware Wallet.


Ku bijanye n’ibikoresho, COLDCARD Q ifise ibintu vyose bisabwa kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukoresha neza:




- Igikoresho kinini citwa LCD kiratuma umuntu ashobora kugenda neza no gukoresha;
- Igikoresho c’ivyuma vyuzuye QWERTY;
- Igikoresho co gufata amasanamu co gucapura amakode ya QR;
- Ivyumba bibiri vy'amakarata ya microSD ;
- Ihitamwo ry’ububasha ryitandukanije ryose biciye ku mabateri atatu ya AAA (ntiyarimwo), canke biciye ku nsinga ya USB-C ;
- Two Secure Elements zivuye mu bigo bibiri bitandukanye kugira ngo umuntu agire umutekano wongereweko;
- Ubushobozi bwo kuvugana biciye kuri NFC.


Njye mbona ko COLDCARD Q ifise ingorane zibiri gusa. Ica mbere, kubera ibintu vyinshi ifise, irakomeye cane, ifise uburebure bwa santimetero hafi 13 n’ubwaguke bwa santimetero 8, ivyo bikaba bingana hafi n’uburebure bwa telefone ntoyi. Navyo nyene birakomeye cane kubera aho bateri iri. Niba uriko urarondera Hardware Wallet ntoyi, igendagenda cane, MK4 ikomeye cane yoshobora kuba ari yo nzira nziza. Intambamyi ya kabiri ni igiciro c’ico gikoresho, kikaba gifise igiciro ca **$239.99** ku rubuga rwemewe, ni ukuvuga amadolari 72 kurusha MK4, ivyo bikaba bituma Q ishobora guhangana n’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none nka Ledger Flex canke Foundation’s Passport.


![CCQ](assets/fr/001.webp)


Ku ruhande rwa porogarama, COLDCARD Q irafise ibikoresho vyiza nk’ibindi bikoresho vya Coinkite, ifise ibintu vyinshi vy’agaciro:




- Dice Roll ku generate ijambo ryawe ryo gukira ;
- Kode ya PIN ;
- Guharura gushika ku gufunga PIN ya nyuma ;
- BIP39 Igitabu-14 ;
- PIN yo gufunga ya nyuma ;
- Ibara ry'inyuma ry'ihuriro ;
- ImbutoXOR;
- BIP85...


Muri make, COLDCARD Q itanga ubumenyi bwiza kuruta MK4, kandi ishobora kuba nziza ku bakoresha bo hagati n’abateye imbere barondera uko bakoresha vyoroshe.


COLDCARD Q iraboneka ku kugurisha [ku rubuga rwemewe rwa Coinkite](https://iduka.coinkite.com/iduka/iduka ry'ubukonje). Ishobora kandi kugurwa n’umucuruzi.


## Gutegura inyigisho


Uhejeje kwakira COLDCARD yawe, intambwe ya mbere ni ugusuzuma igipapuro kugira ngo umenye neza ko kitafunguwe. Iyo iyo mpapuro yononekaye, ivyo vyoshobora kwerekana ko iyo Hardware Wallet yashizwe mu kaga kandi ko atari iyo ukuri.


![CCQ](assets/fr/002.webp)


Iyo ufunguye ako gasandugu, ushobora gusanga ibintu bikurikira:




- Ico COLDCARD Q kiri mu gafuko kapfutse;
- Ikarata yo kwandikako ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic.


![CCQ](assets/fr/003.webp)


Raba neza ko iyo saho itafunguwe canke ngo yononekare. Suzuma kandi ko umubare uri ku gafuko kawe uhuye n’umubare uri ku mpapuro ziri mu gafuko. Uwo mubare uwuzigame kugira ngo uzowukoreshe mu gihe kizoza.


![CCQ](assets/fr/004.webp)


Niba ushaka gukoresha COLDCARD yawe utayihuje na mudasobwa (air-gap), shiramwo amabateri atatu ya AAA inyuma y’ico gikoresho. Canke rero, urashobora kuyifatanya na mudasobwa yawe biciye ku nzira ya USB-C.


![CCQ](assets/fr/005.webp)


Kuri iyi nyigisho, uzokenera kandi Sparrow wallet kugira ngo ushobore gucunga Bitcoin Wallet yawe kuri mudasobwa yawe. Ushobora gukuraho [Sparrow wallet](https/gukuraho/) ku rubuga rwemewe. Ndaguhanura cane ko wosuzuma ukuri kwayo (na GnuPG) n’ubutungane bwayo (biciye kuri Hash) imbere yo gukomeza gushiramwo. Niba utazi uko wobikora, kurikiza iyi nyigisho:

## Guhitamwo kode ya PIN


Ubu ushobora gufungura COLDCARD yawe ukanda buto iri hejuru mu mfuruka y’ibubamfu.


![CCQ](assets/fr/006.webp)


Kanda kuri buto ya "*ENTER*" kugira ngo wemere amabwirizwa y'ikoreshwa.


![CCQ](assets/fr/007.webp)


COLDCARD Q yawe izoca yerekana umubare uri hejuru y’ibarabara. Raba neza ko uwo mubare uhuye n’uwo uri ku gafuko kapfutse be n’ari ku gipande c’ipulasitike kiri imbere muri ako gafuko. Ivyo bituma umuzigo wawe utafungurwa hagati y’igihe wapakiwe na Coinkite n’igihe wawufunguye. Kanda "*ENTER*" kugira ngo ukomeze.


![CCQ](assets/fr/008.webp)


Ja kuri "*Hitamwo PIN Code*" maze wemeze ukoresheje buto ya "*ENTER*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/009.webp)


Iyi PIN code ikoreshwa mu gufungura COLDCARD yawe. Ni uburinzi rero ku bijanye n’ugushika ku mubiri ata wemerewe. Iyi kode ya PIN ntaco ikora mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwa none za Wallet yawe. Rero, naho utoshobora gushika kuri iyi kode ya PIN, kugira ijambo ryawe Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gusubira gushika ku bitcoins zawe.


COLDCARD PIN codes zigizwe n’ibice bibiri: intango n’inyuma, kimwe cose gishobora kubamwo imibare iri hagati ya 2 na 6, vyose hamwe bikaba bifise imibare 4 gushika kuri 12. Iyo ufunguye COLDCARD yawe, uzobanza winjize imbere, hanyuma iyo device izokwereka amajambo 2. Hanyuma winjize inyuma. Aya majambo abiri azoguha muri iyi ntambwe yo gutunganya, kandi akwiye kubikwa neza, kuko uzoyakenera igihe cose ufunguye COLDCARD yawe. Nimba amajambo abiri yerekanwa mu gihe co gufungura ahuye n’ayo wabitse mu gihe co gutunganya, ivyo bizokwemeza ko igikoresho cawe kitagira ico gihinduye kuva caheruka gukoreshwa.


Fyonda kandi kuri "*Hitamwo PIN*"


![CCQ](assets/fr/010.webp)


Wemeze ko wasomye imburi.


![CCQ](assets/fr/011.webp)


Ubu uzohitamwo kode yawe ya PIN. Turagusaba ko ukoresha kode ya PIN ndende kandi idasanzwe. Raba neza ko ubika iyi kode ahantu hatandukanye n’aho COLDCARD yawe ibikwa. Ushobora gukoresha ikarata itangwa mu mpapuro zawe kugira ngo wandike iyo kode.


Injira mu ntango uhisemwo, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*ENTER*" kugira ngo wemeze igice ca mbere ca kode ya PIN.


![CCQ](assets/fr/012.webp)


Amajambo abiri arwanya ubuhendanyi azoca yerekanwa ku rubuga rwawe. Bizigame neza kugira ngo uzobikoreshe mu gihe kizoza. Ushobora gukoresha ikarata iri mu mpapuro zawe kugira ngo uvyandike.


![CCQ](assets/fr/013.webp)


Hanyuma winjize igice ca kabiri ca kode yawe ya PIN hanyuma ukande "*ENTER*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/014.webp)

## Guhingura Bitcoin Wallet


None ni igihe co generate Bitcoin nshasha Wallet! Kugira ngo ubikore, ukeneye guhingura ijambo Mnemonic. Kuri Coldcard, ufise uburyo butatu bwo gutanga iri jambo:




- Koresha gusa umubare w’imbere (TRNG);
- Koresha ihuriro rya TRNG n’ugutera amadayimoni kugira ngo wongereko entropie;
- Koresha imizingo y’amadayimoni gusa.


**Ku batangura n'abakoresha hagati, turabahimiriza gukoresha gusa umubare w'imbere wa COLDCARD**


Sinsaba ko ukoresha amadayimoni gusa, kuko gukora nabi bishobora gutuma umuntu atanga urubanza rudafise ubushobozi buhagije, bikaba bitera ingorane umutekano wa Wallet yawe.


Ariko rero, uburyo bwiza cane ni ubwa kabiri ata gukeka, bufatanya gukoresha TRNG n’inkomoko y’entropie yo hanze. Ubwo buryo buratuma habaho entropie ntoyi ingana n’iya TRNG yonyene, kandi bwongerako urugero rw’umutekano rwongerekanye mu gihe umuyagankuba wo mu mutima woshobora gusenyuka (ku bushake canke atarivyo). Mu guhitamwo iyo nzira, ihuriza hamwe TRNG n’ugutera amadayimoni, wungukira ku kugenzura cane uruvyaro rw’interuro, ata kwongera ingorane mu gihe woba ushitse nabi ku ruhande rwawe.


Fyonda kuri "*Amajambo mashasha ya seed*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/029.webp)


Ushobora guhitamwo uburebure bw’interuro yawe. Ndagusavye guhitamwo interuro y’amajambo 12, kuko idakomeye cane gucungera kandi itanga umutekano mutoyi wa Wallet kurusha interuro y’amajambo 24.


![CCQ](assets/fr/030.webp)


COLDCARD izoca yerekana ijambo ryawe ryo gusubirana ryakozwe na TRNG. Niba wipfuza kwongerako entropie y'inyuma, kanda urufunguzo "*4*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/031.webp)


Ivyo bizokujana kuri paji aho ushobora kwongerako entropie mu gutera ivyatsi. Kora ama throws menshi ashoboka (ni 50 nivyo bikenewe, ariko bike si ikintu kinini kuko usanga umaze kwungukira ku entropie ya TRNG), hanyuma wandike ibisubizo ukoresheje imfunguruzo "*1*" gushika kuri "*6*". Niwaheza, ukande "*ENTER*" kugira ngo wemeze.


![CCQ](assets/fr/032.webp)


Ijambo rishasha rya Mnemonic rizogaragara, rishingiye ku entropie uherutse gutanga n'iya TRNG.


**Imburi: Iyi Mnemonic itanga uburenganzira bushitse, butagira aho bugarukira ku ma bitcoins yawe yose**. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere ata n’umwe afise uburenganzira bwo gushika kuri COLDCARD yawe. Iryo jambo ry’amajambo 12 rigarura uburenganzira bwo gushika ku ma bitcoins yawe iyo COLDCARD yawe yatakaye, yibwe canke ivunitse. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko umuntu ayizigama neza kandi akayibika ahantu heza.


Ushobora kuvyandika ku karto zijana n’IKARITA yawe y’IKIRINGO, canke kugira ngo wongere uyikingire, ndagusavye ko woyicapura ku gifadika c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo uyikingire ingorane z’umuriro, umwuzure canke gusenyuka. Uko biri kwose, ntukigere ubizigama ku buryo bwa digitale, ahandi ho woshobora gutakaza amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins.


Andika amajambo atangwa ku rubuga ku gikoresho c’umubiri uhisemwo. Bivanye n’ingene ukoresha umutekano, woshobora kwiyumvira gukora kopi nyinshi z’umubiri z’ico kiganiro (ariko ikiruta vyose, ntukigabanye). Ni vyiza ko amajambo aguma afise inomero kandi akurikirana.


Biragaragara ko **ntushobora kwigera usangira aya majambo** kuri internet, bitandukanye n’ivyo muri iyi nyigisho. Iyi nkuru Wallet izokoreshwa gusa kuri Testnet kandi izokurwaho ku mpera y’inyigisho.


Uhejeje kwandika amajambo, ukande "*ENTER*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/033.webp)


Kugira ngo umenye neza ko wabitse neza amajambo yawe, iyo sisitemu izogusaba kwemeza amajambo amwamwe. Hitamwo umubare uhuye n’ijambo ryose riri ku rupapuro.


![CCQ](assets/fr/034.webp)


Wallet yawe ubu iraremwe! Mu mfuruka yo hejuru iburyo bw’ibarabara, urashobora kubona urutoke rwawe rw’urufunguzo rw’ibanga. Kanda "*INDA*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/035.webp)


Ubu rero urashobora gushika ku rutonde rwawe rwa COLDCARD.


![CCQ](assets/fr/036.webp)


## Gushinga Wallet nshasha kuri Sparrow


Hariho uburyo bwinshi bwo gushinga ubutumwa hagati ya porogarama ya Sparrow wallet na COLDCARD yawe. Igigororotse cane ni ugukoresha umugozi wa USB-C. Ariko rero, ku buryo busanzwe, COLDCARD yawe irafise ubuhinga bwo guhanahana amakuru n’imirongo ya NFC buzimye. Kugira ngo usubire kubikoresha, genda kuri "*Ivyagezwe*", hanyuma kuri "*Hardware On/Off*", hanyuma ukoreshe uburyo bwo guhanahana amakuru wipfuza.


![CCQ](assets/fr/037.webp)


Niba ushaka ko COLDCARD yawe iguma iri kure cane ya mudasobwa yawe, urashobora guhitamwo guhanahana amakuru mu buryo butaziguye "air-gap", ukoresheje amakode ya QR canke ikarita ya microSD. Ubu ni bwo buryo tuzokoresha muri iyi nyigisho.


Genda kuri "*Ivyiza/Ibikoresho*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/038.webp)


Hitamwo "*Sohora Wallet*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/039.webp)


Hanyuma uhitemwo "*Sparrow wallet*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/040.webp)


Kanda "*ENTER*" kugira ngo generate dosiye y'imiterere.


![CCQ](assets/fr/041.webp)


Hanyuma uhitemwo ingene worungika iyi dosiye kuri Sparrow. Muri aka karorero, nashize microSD mu gice "*A*", rero nzohitamwo buto ya "*1*". Ushobora kandi kwerekana ayo makuru nk'ibara rya QR ku gicapo cawe ca COLDCARD ukanda kuri buto ya "*QR*", hanyuma ugacapura iyo kode ya QR ukoresheje webcam ya mudasobwa yawe.


![CCQ](assets/fr/042.webp)


Tangaza Sparrow wallet maze usimbuke amapaji y’intango kugira ngo ushikire igicapo nyamukuru. Raba neza ko uhuye neza n'urudodo mu kugenzura umuhinduzi uri hasi iburyo bw'ibarabara.


![CCQ](assets/fr/043.webp)


Ni vyiza cane ko ukoresha urudodo rwawe rwa Bitcoin. Ku bw’iyi nyigisho, ndiko nkoresha urudodo rwa bose (umuhondo), nk’uko ndi kuri Testnet, ariko ku gukoresha mu guhingura, ni vyiza gukoresha Bitcoin core mu karere (Green) canke umukozi wa Electrum ku rudodo rwa kure (ubururu).


Hika kuri "*Dosiye*" maze uhitemwo "*Wallet nshasha*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/044.webp)


Izina rya Wallet yawe hanyuma ukande kuri "*Rema Wallet*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/045.webp)


Mu "*Ubwoko bw'inyandiko*", hitamwo ubwoko bw'inyandiko buzokingira amafaranga yawe.


![CCQ](assets/fr/046.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Hardware Wallet*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/047.webp)


Munsi y'agace ka "*Coldcard*", fyonda ku "*Scan...*" nimba utegura gucapura kode ya QR yerekanwa kuri COLDCARD yawe, canke "*Import File...*" kugira ngo ushiremwo dosiye muri microSD (iyi ni dosiye `.json`).


![CCQ](assets/fr/048.webp)


Inyuma yo kwinjiza, suzuma ko "*Ikimenyetso c'urutoke rwa Master*" kigaragara kuri Sparrow gihuye n'ico kigaragara kuri COLDCARD yawe. Wemeze iremwa ukanda kuri "*Shiraho*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/049.webp)


Gushinga ijambobanga rikomeye kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha Sparrow wallet yawe. Iri jambobanga rizokingira imfunguruzo zawe za bose, amaderesi, ama tags n’amateka y’ibikorwa vyawe kugira ngo ntihagire uwuyibona ata wemerewe.


Ni vyiza kubika iri jambobanga kugira ngo nturibagire (nk'akarorero mu mucungerezi w'ijambobanga).


![CCQ](assets/fr/050.webp)


Wallet yawe ubu iri kuri Sparrow wallet.


![CCQ](assets/fr/051.webp)


Imbere y’uko uronka ama bitcoins yawe ya mbere muri Wallet yawe, **Ndaguhanura cane gukora ikigeragezo co gusubirana ubusa**. Wandike amakuru y’ishingiro, nka xpub yawe, hanyuma usubiremwo COLDCARD Q yawe mu gihe Wallet ikiri ubusa. Hanyuma ugerageze gusubiza Wallet yawe muri COLDCARD ukoresheje ama backups yawe y’impapuro. Suzuma ko xpub yavutse inyuma yo gusubizaho ihuye n’iyo wanditse mu ntango. Nivyashika, urashobora kwizigira yuko amakete yawe y’impapuro ari ay’ukwizigirwa.

## Wohereze ama bitcoins


None ko wararonse Sats yawe ya mbere muri Wallet yawe icungiwe na COLDCARD, nawe urashobora kuyikoresha!


Nk'uko bisanzwe, tangura ufungure no gufungura COLDCARD Q yawe, hanyuma ufungure Sparrow wallet maze ugende ku rubuga rwa "*Send*" kugira ngo utegure igikorwa gishasha.


![CCQ](assets/fr/064.webp)


Niba wipfuza "Coin control", ni ukuvuga guhitamwo neza cane UTXOs uzokoresha mu gucuruza, genda kuri "*UTXOs*". Hitamwo UTXOs wipfuza gukoresha, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ohereza vyahisemwo*". Uzosubira ku rubuga rumwe mu gice ca "*Send*", ariko n'ama UTXO yawe amaze gutorwa ku bijanye n'ugucuruza.


![CCQ](assets/fr/065.webp)


Injira aho uja Address. Ushobora kandi kwinjiza amaderesi menshi ukanda kuri buto ya "*+ Add*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/066.webp)


Wandike "*Ikimenyetso*" kugira wibuke intumbero y'iyi nsiguro.


![CCQ](assets/fr/067.webp)


Hitamwo amahera uzorungika kuri iyi Address.


![CCQ](assets/fr/068.webp)


Guhindura igipimo c’amahera y’ugucuruza kwawe bivanye n’isoko ririho ubu.


![CCQ](assets/fr/069.webp)


Raba neza ko amaparametere yose y'ubucuruzi bwawe ari ukuri, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Rema ubucuruzi*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/070.webp)


Niba vyose bigushimisha, fyonda ku "*Sozera Ibikorwa vyo Gusinya*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/071.webp)


Umaze kwubaka igikorwa cawe muri Sparrow, ni igihe co kugishirako umukono ukoresheje COLDCARD yawe. Kugira ngo urungike PSBT (ibikorwa bitagira umukono) ku gikoresho cawe, urafise uburyo bwinshi bwo kubikora. Iyo gutanga amakuru biciye ku nzira y'amashanyarazi bishoboka, urashobora kohereza amakuru biciye ku nzira ya USB-C, nk'uko wobigira ku yindi Hardware Wallet iyo ari yo yose. Muri iki gihe, kuri Sparrow, wobwirizwa gukanda kuri buto ya "*Sign*" iri mu mfuruka iburyo hasi. Mu karorero kanje, iyi buto irabujijwe kubera ko COLDCARD idahuzwa n’umugozi.


![CCQ](assets/fr/072.webp)


Niba ushaka kuguma ufise "air-gap" connection ataco ukora hagati ya Hardware Wallet na mudasobwa yawe, urafise uburyo 2. Ica mbere, kandi gikomeye cane, ni ugukoresha ikarata microSD. Injira ikarita ya microSD muri mudasobwa yawe, wandike ivyo wakoze biciye ku buto "*Save Transaction*" kuri Sparrow, hanyuma ushire iyo karata ku port iri kuri COLDCARD yawe.


![CCQ](assets/fr/073.webp)


Hanyuma ugende ku rutonde rwa "*Witeguye Gusinya*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/074.webp)


Suzuma amakuru y’ivyo ukoresheje kuri COLDCARD yawe, harimwo n’ivyo wakiriye Address, amahera yoherejwe be n’amahera yo gukoresha.


![CCQ](assets/fr/075.webp)


Niba vyose bimeze neza, kanda kuri buto ya "*ENTER*" kugira ngo ushire umukono ku giciro.


![CCQ](assets/fr/076.webp)


Hanyuma usubize microSD muri mudasobwa yawe maze kuri Sparrow, ukande kuri "*Load Transaction*" kugira ngo ushiremwo iyo microSD yashizweko umukono. Uzoheza ushobore gukora isuzuma rya nyuma imbere y’uko uyishira ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.


![CCQ](assets/fr/077.webp)


Uburyo bwa kabiri bwo gusinya ukoresheje COLDCARD yawe muri Air-Gap, bworoshe cane kuruta uburyo bwa microSD, ni ugucapura PSBT ataco ukoresheje kamera y’ico gikoresho. Kuri Sparrow, hitamwo "*Kwerekana QR*".


![CCQ](assets/fr/078.webp)


Ku COLDCARD, hitamwo "*Scan kode ya QR yose*". Ushobora kandi gukoresha urufunguzo "*QR*" ruri kuri klavye.


![CCQ](assets/fr/079.webp)


Koresha kamera ya COLDCARD kugira ngo ushireko kode ya QR yerekanwa kuri Sparrow.


![CCQ](assets/fr/080.webp)


Ivyerekeye ugucuruza bizosubira kugaragara kugira ngo bigenzurwe. Kanda "*ENTER*" kugira ngo usinye nimba vyose bigushimisha.


![CCQ](assets/fr/081.webp)


COLDCARD yawe izoca yerekana ivyo washizeko umukono nk’ikode ya QR. Koresha webcam ya mudasobwa yawe kugira ngo ushireko iyi kode ya QR uhisemwo "*Scan QR*" kuri Sparrow.


![CCQ](assets/fr/082.webp)


Ivyo mwashizeko umukono ubu biraboneka kuri Sparrow. Suzuma ubwa nyuma ko vyose bimeze neza, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Broadcast Transaction*" kugira ngo ubimenyeshe ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.

Ushobora gukurikirana ibikorwa vyawe mu gice ca Sparrow wallet "*Ibikorwa*".
