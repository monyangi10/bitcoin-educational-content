## What is Blitz Wallet?


Blitz Wallet is a Bitcoin self-custody wallet whose source code is available (Open Source), which focuses on your sovereignty and a user experience that makes it easy to get to grips with.


[Blitz Wallet](https://blitz-Wallet.com/) is a mobile wallet available on Android (Play Store) and iOS (App Store).


⚠️**IMPORTANT**: Downloading a Bitcoin wallet on an official platform is important for verifying the authenticity of the application and, by extension, reinforcing the security of your funds.

## Getting started with Blitz


Trading with Blitz is more intuitive than with most other Bitcoin wallets.


In the Wallet menu, you have a minimalist Interface focused solely on the main actions:


### Receive bitcoins


To receive bitcoins on your Blitz wallet, click on the "Down Arrow" icon, insert the amount of satoshis you wish to receive and the wallet will create an invoice for you to share with your sender.


⚠️ **NOTES**: Satoshi (or "sat") represents the smallest unit of Bitcoin: 1 Bitcoin = 100,000,000 satoshis


One of the special features of Blitz Wallet is that it supports different networks and channels from the Bitcoin ecosystem:



- Lightning Network** : One of the Bitcoin overlays that lets you perform microtransactions instantly.



- Bitcoin Mainnet** : The main chain of the Bitcoin protocol, suitable for large-value transactions.



- Liquid Network**: A parallel chain to Bitcoin Mainnet developed by BlockStream that uses Liquid Bitcoins to carry out fast, confidential transactions.


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/blockstream-app-liquid-b3e4fb82-902e-4782-ad2b-a61ab05a543a

By default, all your transactions will be on Liquid Network, but Blitz lets you define the network on which you wish to receive satoshis by clicking on the **Choose format** button.


![receive-sats](assets/fr/03.webp)


### Create contacts with Blitz


Blitz Wallet makes it easy for you to send bitcoins from its wallet.


In the **Contacts** menu, you can register the Blitz usernames or Lightning URLs you interact with most.


This means you can send satoshis to these addresses easily, bypassing the scanning and manual address entry stage.


![add-contacts](assets/fr/04.webp)


### Send bitcoins


In addition to the classic methods of sending Bitcoins (QR code, manual entry), by using the contacts pre-registered in your wallet, you can send Satss to your recipient in just three clicks.


In the **Wallet** menu, click on the "Up Arrow" button, choose the method for sending bitcoins, then insert the amount to be sent and proceed with confirmation.


The minimum amount to send Bitcoin in Blitz Wallet is currently 1,000 satoshis.


![send-bitcoin](assets/fr/05.webp)

## Wallet Blitz behind the scenes: Going further


Behind the simplicity of Blitz Wallet's operation lies a wealth of power and customization.


As we pointed out earlier, all bitcoins you receive default to Liquid Network.


Blitz uses Liquid Network microexchanges to present your balance in Satoshi when you have a balance of less than 500,000 satoshis.


This approach is justified by the desire to facilitate the start-up experience and help new users to carry out transactions on Lightning Network with small amounts as simply as possible.


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/aqua-8e6d7dd3-8c03-45cc-90dd-fe3899a7d125

You can see the breakdown of your balance in the menu **Settings>Balance Info**.


![balance](assets/fr/09.webp)


Blitz Wallet does, however, give you the flexibility to activate Lightning mode, which will automatically open a payment channel for you once you've reached a balance of 500,000 satoshis.


To activate Lightning mode, go to **Settings**, then in the **Technical Settings** section, click on the **Node Info** option.


![enable-lightning](assets/fr/10.webp)


By activating Lightning mode, once the main condition has been met (balance of 500,000 satoshis or 0.005 Bitcoin), you will be able to carry out transactions on Lightning Network and will no longer have to go through BlockStream's Liquid Network.



- Accept Bitcoin in your shop** :


The integration of Bitcoin payments in shops is still in the experimental phase with Blitz Wallet. We recommend that you use it sparingly.


In the **Settings>Point-of-sale** menu, you can set the unique identifier associated with your store and the local fiat currency in which you wish to receive payments.
