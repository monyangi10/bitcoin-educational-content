Ubumenyi bw'abakoresha ni kimwe mu bintu bihambaye iyo bije ku bijanye no gutangura gukoresha Wallet. Muri iyi nyigisho, turabamenyesha Wallet yatumye ubumenyi bw’abayikoresha buba ikintu gihambaye: Blitz Wallet iraguha amasakoshi yoroshe kandi yuzuye ya Bitcoin ushobora gusanga.



## None igitero ca Blitz Wallet ni iki?



Blitz Wallet ni Bitcoin yigenzura Wallet ifise kode y’inkomoko iboneka (Open Source), yibanda ku busegaba bwawe n’ubumenyi bw’ukoresha butuma vyoroha kuyifata.



[Blitz Wallet] ni telefone ngendanwa Wallet iboneka kuri Android (Iduka ry’Ibikino) no kuri iOS (Iduka ry’Ibikoresho).



⚠️**IGIHARURWA**: Gukura Bitcoin Wallet ku rubuga rwemewe ni ikintu gihambaye mu kugenzura ukuri kw’ivyo usaba kandi, mu kwagura, gukomeza umutekano w’amahera yawe.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba twishingikirije kuri verisiyo ya Android ya Blitz Wallet, ariko uburyo bwose bushikirijwe aha hepfo burakora kimwe kuri iOS.



![installation](assets/fr/01.webp)



Kubera ko Blitz Wallet ari Wallet yifata yonyene ya Bitcoin, urashobora guhitamwo gukora Wallet nshasha canke ukazana amajambo yo gusubirana 12/24 uvuye muri Wallet usanzwe ufise.



Aha, turiko turatangura n'uguhingura Wallet nshasha. Raba aha hepfo ku vyiyumviro vyacu ku bijanye no gusubiza inyuma amajambo yawe yo gusubiza inyuma.

## Gutangura na Blitz



Gucuruza na Blitz birashoboka cane kuruta ibindi bikoresho vyinshi vya Bitcoin.



Mu rutonde rwa Wallet, ufise Interface y’agaciro gatoyi yibanze gusa ku bikorwa nyamukuru:



### Kwakira ama bitcoins



Kugira ngo uronke bitcoins kuri Blitz Wallet yawe, fyonda ku kimenyetso ca "Down Arrow", ushiremwo umubare w'ama satoshis wipfuza kwakira maze Wallet izogukorera Invoice yo gusangira n'uwagurungikiye.



⚠️ **IBIMENYETSO**: Satoshi (canke "sat") igereranya igice gitoyi kuruta ibindi vyose vya Bitcoin: 1 Bitcoin = 100.000.000 satoshis



Kimwe mu bintu bidasanzwe vya Blitz Wallet ni uko ishigikira imihora itandukanye n’imirongo iva mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin:





- Lightning Network**: Imwe mu nzira za Bitcoin zigufasha gukora ibikorwa bitobito ubwo nyene.





- Bitcoin Mainnet**: Uruhererekane nyamukuru rw’amasezerano ya Bitcoin, rwiza ku bikorwa vy’agaciro kanini.





- Liquid Network**: Uruzitiro rumwe na Bitcoin Mainnet rwateguwe na BlockStream rukoresha amafaranga y'ama Bitcoins kugira ngo rukore vyihuta, Confidential Transactions.



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/blockstream-app-liquid-b3e4fb82-902e-4782-ad2b-a61ab05a543a

Ku mbuga, amafaranga yawe yose azoba ari kuri Liquid Network, ariko Blitz iragufasha gusobanura urubuga wipfuza kwakirako satoshis ukanda kuri buto **Hitamwo uburyo**.



![receive-sats](assets/fr/03.webp)



### Rema ubucuti na Blitz



Blitz Wallet iragufasha kohereza amafaranga y’ibiceri uvuye kuri Wallet yayo.



Mu **Contacts**, ushobora kwandika amazina y'abakoresha ba Blitz canke URL za Lightning ukorana cane.



Ivyo bisigura ko ushobora kohereza satoshis kuri izo aderesi mu buryo bworoshe, uciye ku nzira yo gucapura no kwinjira mu gitabu ca Address.



![add-contacts](assets/fr/04.webp)



### Wohereze ama bitcoins



Uretse uburyo bwa kera bwo kohereza ama Bitcoins (QR code, kwinjira n’amaboko), ukoresheje ama contacts yanditswe imbere muri Wallet yawe, urashobora kohereza Satss ku muntu uzokwakira mu gukanda gatatu gusa.



Mu **Wallet** menu, ukande kuri buto ya "Up Arrow", uhitemwo uburyo bwo kohereza bitcoins, hanyuma winjize amahera uzorungika maze ugende n'ukwemeza.



Amahera makeyi yo kohereza Bitcoin muri Blitz Wallet ubu ni amasatoshi 1.000.



![send-bitcoin](assets/fr/05.webp)



## Iduka rya Blitz



Uretse ibikorwa vyo gutanga amafaranga vya Bitcoin, Blitz Wallet iraguha iduka aho ushobora gukoresha amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins kugira ngo wishyure ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa none.





- Ushobora gushika ku bikorwa vya AI**: Koresha ubuhinga bw’ubwenge bukora nk’ubu: Claude 3-5 sonnet, gpt-4o, gpt-4o-mini gemini-flash-1.5 maze wishyure ataco uhinduye mu bice vy’amahera.



![ia-credits](assets/fr/06.webp)





- Wohereze ubutumwa aho hose kw'isi**: Mu iduka rya Blitz, urashobora gukoresha ubuhinga bwa GSM bugufasha kohereza ubutumwa ata mazina yawe aho hose kw'isi, ukoresheje amafaranga ataco akora muri Bitcoin.



![sms-credit](assets/fr/07.webp)





- Surf mu ibanga ryose**: Urihe amafaranga y’ukwiyandikisha kuri WireGuard VPN (Irezo y’Ibanga) mu iduka rya Wallet Blitz n’amahera yawe y’ibiceri.

## Wallet Blitz inyuma y’ivyo: Kuja kure



Inyuma y’uko Blitz Wallet ikora mu buryo bworoshe, hariho ububasha bwinshi be n’uguhindura ibintu nk’uko umuntu abishaka.



Nk’uko twabibonye mbere, ama bitcoins yose uronka ahinduka Liquid Network.



Blitz ikoresha ama microexchanges ya Liquid Network kugira ngo yerekane umubare wawe muri Satoshi iyo ufise umubare uri munsi ya 500.000 satoshis.



Ubwo buryo burashingiye ku cipfuzo co kworohereza ubumenyi bwo gutangura no gufasha abakoresha bashasha gukora ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji kuri Lightning Network n’amahera makeyi mu buryo bworoshe uko bishoboka kwose.



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/aqua-8e6d7dd3-8c03-45cc-90dd-fe3899a7d125

Ushobora kubona ukuntu umubare wawe ugabanuka muri menyu **Ivyagezwe>Amakuru y'umubare**.



![balance](assets/fr/09.webp)



Blitz Wallet rero, iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha uburyo bwa Lightning, buzogufungurira ubwo nyene umurongo wo kwishura iyo umaze gushika ku giciro c’amahera 500.000 satoshis.



Kugira ngo ukoreshe uburyo bwa Lightning, genda kuri **Ivyagezwe**, hanyuma mu gice ca **Ivyagezwe vy’ubuhinga**, ukande ku **Amakuru y’Igihimba**.



![enable-lightning](assets/fr/10.webp)



Mu gukoresha uburyo bw’umuravyo, iyo ivyangombwa nyamukuru bishitse (uburinganire bwa satoshis 500.000 canke 0.005 Bitcoin), uzoshobora gukora amafaranga kuri Lightning Network kandi ntuzosubira guca kuri Liquid Network ya BlockStream.





- Wemere Bitcoin mu iduka ryawe**:



Ugushiramwo amafaranga Bitcoin mu maduka biracari mu rwego rw'igerageza na Blitz Wallet. Turagusavye ko woyikoresha mu buryo bubereye.



Mu **Ivyagezwe>Ikibanza co kugurishamwo**, urashobora gushinga ikimenyetso kidasanzwe gifitaniye isano n’iduka ryawe n’amafaranga y’aho hantu wipfuza kwakira amahera.



![pos](assets/fr/11.webp)
