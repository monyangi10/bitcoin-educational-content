Kumbure urazi neza [Rumble](https://rumble.com/), urubuga rwa videwo rwo kuri internet rwashinzwe mu 2013, rwagiye rugenda rushinga intahe nk’uburyo bwigenga bwo gusubirira Big Tech. Rumble izwi kubera ukwiyemeza kwayo mu kwidegemvya kw’amajambo n’intumbero yayo yo guha abaremyi ikibanza kitagiramwo ugucengera no kwizigira ibikorwa remezo binini binini bihurikiye hamwe, Rumble yubatse urutonde rw’ibidukikije rwuzuye. Uyu musi, Rumble iriko iratera intambwe nshasha mu vy’ubutunzi n’ugushiraho [**Rumble Wallet**](https://wallet.rumble.com/), ubuhinga bwa wallet bwakozwe mu bufatanye na Tether.


Icipfuzo kiratomoye: gufatanya umwidegemvyo wo kuvuga n’umwidegemvyo w’amahera. Naho Rumble itera imbere mu gukwiragiza ibintu bitagenzuwe kuri internet, Rumble Wallet ifise intumbero yo guha abaremyi n’abakoresha uburyo bwo kwishura bwihuta, bufise ubusegaba, butagira uwuzicungera. Mu vy’ukuri, ivyo bisigura ko uguma ufise ububasha bwose ku mfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga—kandi rero n’amahera yawe—utarizigiye banki, uwujejwe kwishura canke urubuga rw’uwundi muntu: ingorane ni nke, amahera make, kandi nta guteba gukura amahera.


## 1. Rumble Wallet ni iki?


Rumble Wallet ni wallet igendagenda ahanini yagenewe *ubutunzi bw’umuremyi*—ni ukuvuga, ku bantu basohora, bagatangaza, canke basangira ibirimwo kuri internet. Naho ama platforms ahurikiye hamwe ategeka uburyo bwayo bwo gukora amahera, Rumble Wallet izana uburyo butaziguye: abahinguzi barashobora gukoresha *Tipping Jar* maze bagaca baronka amahera y’ababumviriza. Ivyo bihembo bigenda ata n’umwe abihuza, biva ku mufana bikaja ku muremyi, bikaba vyemeza ko umuntu yigenga bimwe bishitse.


Uretse abahinguzi b’ibirimwo, Rumble Wallet na yo ni umuti mwiza ku muntu wese akoresha Bitcoin. Ubu irashigikira amafaranga atatu y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none aboneka ku nzira nyinshi: Bitcoin, USDT, na XAUT (*Tether Gold*). Iyi porogaramu kandi ifise ubuhinga bwo guhinduranya, bituma abakoresha bashobora guhinduranya hagati y’ivyo bintu bitandukanye batavuye muri Rumble Wallet.


Kubera ubufatanye na MoonPay, birashoboka kandi kugura no kugurisha Bitcoin n’izindi cryptocurrencies ataco ukoresheje app.

## 3. Gutanguza Rumble Wallet


Igihe utanguye porogarama, urashobora gusubizaho wallet yariho ukoresheje ijambo ryawe ry’ukwibuka canke ukinjira ukoresheje konti yawe ya Rumble. Muri iyi nkuru, tuzokora wallet nshasha, rero uhitemwo "*Bandanya na Rumble*".


![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)


Uzoca usabwa kwinjira ukoresheje konti yawe ya Rumble. Niba utaragira, kanda "*Sign up*". Ahandiho, ushiremwo gusa amakuru yawe yo kwinjira.


![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)


Uhejeje kwinjira, uzokenera gukora backup ya Bitcoin wallet yawe kugira ngo umenye neza ko ushobora gusubira kuyironka mu gihe telefone yawe yo mu bwoko bwa smartphone yoba yatakaye canke yibwe. Rumble Wallet itanga uburyo bubiri bwo gusubiza inyuma:


- Bika ijambo ryawe ry'ubwenge ry'ibanga ku gicu ca Rumble. Ushobora rero gusubirana wallet yawe gusa mu gusubira muri konti yawe ya Rumble.
- Koresha uburyo bwa kera: wandike n’amaboko ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 ry’ukwibuka.


Umaze guhitamwo, kanda "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)


Urabe maso: niwahitamwo uburyo bwo gukingira amakuru n’amaboko, ni wewe wenyene ufise inshingano yo gukingira iri jambo. Iratanga uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amahera yawe yose, kandi uwufise wese arashobora kuyaba—naho atashobora gukoresha telefone yawe canke kuri konti yawe ya Rumble. Niwaba wahisemwo ubu buryo, fyonda ku "*Hishura Ijambo ry'Imbuto*" kugira ngo ugaragaze ijambo ryawe. Raba neza ko ata n’umwe ariko arabibona muri iyo ntambwe.


![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)


Rumble Wallet rero yerekana ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 ry’ukwibuka ku rubuga. Iryo jambo rituma ushobora gusubira gukoresha amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri (bitcoins) iyo telefone yawe yoba yatakaye, yibwe canke yononekaye. Ni ngombwa rero ko uwushigikira witonze kandi ukawubika ahantu hatagira inkomanzi.


Ushobora kuyandika ku mpapuro, canke kugira ngo uyikingire cane, ukayicapura ku gipande c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo uyikingire umuriro, amazi canke ivyonona. Guhitamwo uburyo bwo kubika bivana n’ingene ukoresha umutekano: niwakoresha Rumble nk’ikoresha wallet n’amahera aringaniye, ububiko bw’impapuro buzoba buhagije.


Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku migenzo myiza yo gushigikira no gucunga amajambo y’ubwenge, ndagusavye cane kuraba iyi nyigisho, cane cane nimba uri mushasha muri Bitcoin:

Igihe ububiko bwawe buzoba burangiye, kanda "*Vyarangiye! Nabitse neza ijambo ryanje ry'imbuto*".

Ubu urashobora gukoresha Rumble Wallet.

## 4. Gusuzuma ububiko bwawe bwa wallet (ntibikenewe)


Niwaba wahisemwo uburyo bwo gusubiza inyuma n’amaboko ukoresheje ijambo seed (kandi atari biciye ku gicu ca Rumble), ndagusavye cane gukora ikigeragezo co gusubirana mu gihe wallet yawe ikiri ubusa. Ivyo bizotuma vy’ukuri ububiko bwawe bw’impapuro bugufasha gusubira kuronka uburenganzira bwo gukoresha Bitcoin wallet yawe mu gihe telefone yawe yoba yatakaye, yibwe canke yononekaye.


**Imburi: iyo nzira ikwiye gukorwa gusa n’ibara ry’ubusa rya wallet ritagira bitcoin canke USDT!**


Kugira ngo utangure, fyonda kuri buto ya "*Kwakira*" hanyuma uhitemwo, nk'akarorero, "*Bitcoin*".


![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)


Rumble Wallet izoca yerekana aderesi yawe ya mbere yakira Bitcoin, ivuye kuri seed yawe. Raba inyuguti za mbere n'iza nyuma z'iyi aderesi, zitangura na `bc1q...`.


![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)


Inyuma y’aho, n’ibigirankana ukureho iyo porogarama kuri telefone yawe, hanyuma wongere uyishiremwo kugira ngo ugerageze ingene yosubirana.


Igihe porogaramu itanguye, kanda "*Bandanya na Rumble*".


![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)


Injira, hanyuma uhitemwo "*Injiza ijambo seed n'amaboko*".


![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)


Injira neza amajambo yawe y'amajambo 12 nk'uko wayanditse, hanyuma ukande "*Wemeze amajambo y'imbuto*".


![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)


Iyo ubutumwa "*Ntitwabonye backup!*" bubonetse ku rubuga, bisigura ko backup yawe irimwo ikosa. Muri ivyo, subira utangure kuva ku ntambwe ya 3 y’iyi nyigisho kugira ngo ureme wallet nshasha maze wandike neza ijambo ryawe rishasha.


![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)


Niwaba warashoboye kugarura Rumble Wallet yawe, subira ku gice ca "*Kwakira*" uhitemwo "*Bitcoin*".


![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)


Nimba aderesi y’ukwakira yerekanwa isa n’iyo wabonye mu ntango y’iyi ntambwe, urashobora kwemera udakeka ko ububiko bwawe bw’amajambo 12 y’amajambo y’ukwibuka ari ukuri kandi bukora neza.


![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)


## 5. Kwakira no kohereza Bitcoin kuri Rumble Wallet


Ku Rumble Wallet, ushobora kwakira amafaranga y’ibiceri (bitcoins) haba on-chain canke biciye kuri Spark kugira ngo wishyure vyihuta kandi bifise amahera make. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku buto bwa "*Kwakira*".


![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)


Hitamwo wallet aho ushaka kwakira ayo mahera.


![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)


Hanyuma uhitemwo amafaranga y’ibanga wipfuza kwakira. Muri aka karorero, nzohitamwo Bitcoin.


![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)


Ushobora rero guhitamwo hagati yo kwakira on-chain canke biciye kuri Spark. Aha, nzohitamwo kwakira amahera yanje on-chain.


![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)


Hanyuma, kopira aderesi y’ukwakira maze uyirungikire uwuriko arayitanga, canke ureke gusa bakoreshe kode ya QR yerekanwa ku rubuga rwawe bakoresheje igitabu cabo bwite citwa wallet.


![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)


Kuva kuri paji y’intango ya Rumble Wallet, urashobora kubona amakonti yawe atandukanye: BTC, USDT, na XAUT.


![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)


Turashobora kubona hano ko naronse neza sats on-chain.


![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)


Kugira ngo wohereze bitcoins, uburyo bwo kubikora burasanzwe: ukande kuri buto ya "*Send*", haba kuri konti yawe ya Bitcoin canke uhereye kuri paji y'intango.


![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)


Hanyuma uhitemwo amafaranga y’ibanga uzorungika be n’urubuga rujanye n’ivyo. Ku bijanye nanje, ndiko ndarungika BTC on-chain. Injira amahera wipfuza gutanga.


![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)


Nimushire aderesi y'uwuronka canke mushireko QR code yiwe mu gufyonda ku kimenyetso gihuye, hanyuma mukande "*Confirm*".


![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)


Suzuma witonze ido n’ido ry’ugucuruza, hanyuma wemeze ko wohereje.


![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)


Ivyo mwaguriye vyaratangajwe. Ushobora gukurikirana ingene itera imbere n’ivyo yemejwe ku gikoresho co gutohoza amabuye ukoresheje TXID yerekanwa ku gicapo gikurikira.


![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)


## 6. Guhindura Bitcoin ku Rumble Wallet


Nk’uko ushobora kubibona hejuru kuri konti yawe ya Bitcoin, Rumble Wallet itanga vyinshi kuruta kwakira no kohereza bitcoin gusa: iraguha kandi uburenganzira bwo guhindura BTC yawe mu mafaranga ya fiat ataco uhinduye uva kuri wallet biciye mu gukorana kwayo na MoonPay. Ushobora kandi guhindura BTC yawe mu USDT canke XAUT, n’ibihushanye n’ivyo, vyose utavuye muri app.


### 6.1. Guhinduranya hagati y'amafaranga y'ibanga


Nk'akarorero, niwaba wipfuza guhindura BTC yawe mu USDT, genda kuri konti yawe ya Bitcoin ukande kuri buto ya "*Swap*". Iyi buto iraboneka kuri buri konti yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)


Injira amahera ushaka guhindura, uhitemwo amafaranga y’ibanga wipfuza kwakira, hanyuma uhitemwo urubuga ushaka kuyaronkako. Amaparametere yose amaze gutegurwa, kanda "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)


Suzuma neza ido n'ido ry'ivyo uhinduye, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Swap*" kugira ngo wemeze igikorwa.


![Image](assets/fr/30.webp)


Ubu swap yawe iriko iratunganirizwa.


![Image](assets/fr/31.webp)


### 6.2. Guhindura amafaranga y'i Fiat


Kuva kuri konti yawe ya Bitcoin, urashobora kandi kugura BTC n’amahera ya fiat, canke ibihushanye n’ivyo, ukagurisha satoshis zawe. Kugira ngo ugure, ushobora gufyonda gusa kuri buto ya "*Gura*".


![Image](assets/fr/32.webp)


Hitamwo amafaranga y'aho uba, winjize amafaranga wipfuza kugura, hanyuma ukande "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/33.webp)


Mu gihe uzokoresha ubwa mbere, uzokenera gufatanya konti yawe ya MoonPay. Igihe ugugura kwemejwe, amafaranga yawe azoca ahita ashirwa muri Rumble Wallet yawe.


Mu buryo nk’ubwo nyene, urashobora kugurisha ama bitcoins yawe ku ma euro, amadolari, canke ayandi mafaranga yose y’ubuhinga bwa none. Kugira ngo ubikore, ukande kuri buto ya "*Cashout*", hanyuma ugaragaze umubare n'amahera wipfuza.


![Image](assets/fr/34.webp)


## 7. Gutanga inama Umuremyi kuri Rumble


Nk’uko vyavuzwe mu ntangamarara, Rumble Wallet irahuye neza cane n’[urubuga rwa videwo rwa Rumble](https://rumble.com/). Ivyo bigufasha kohereza mu buryo bworoshe impanuro mu Bitcoin canke USDT ku baremyi ukunda ata n’umwe uciyemwo.


Kugira ngo ubikore, ukeneye gukoresha ***Tip Jars***. Kuva kuri paji y'intango ya Rumble Wallet, fyonda ku buto "*Gukoresha Ibibindi vy'Impanuro*".


![Image](assets/fr/35.webp)


Injira izina ryawe nimba ritaraheza kwuzuzwa kuri konti yawe ya Rumble.


![Image](assets/fr/36.webp)


Inyuma, hitamwo wallet aho wipfuza kwakira impanuro nk'umuremyi, hanyuma ukande "*Enable Tip Jars*".


![Image](assets/fr/37.webp)


Ku rupapuro rw’intango, uzobona urutonde rw’amakonti yose ya Rumble ukurikiza. Kugira ngo wohereze impanuro, kanda gusa "*Ohereza impanuro*".


![Image](assets/fr/38.webp)


Hitamwo inkomoko wallet ushaka koherezamwo impanuro yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/39.webp)


Hitamwo amafaranga y’ibanga wipfuza gukoresha.


![Image](assets/fr/40.webp)


Injira umubare, hanyuma wemeze ukanda "*Wemeze*".


![Image](assets/fr/41.webp)


Suzuma ido n'ido ry'ibikorwa vyawe maze ukande "*Wemeze ko utanga*".


![Image](assets/fr/42.webp)


Impanuro yawe yararungitswe neza!


![Image](assets/fr/43.webp)


Ushobora kandi guhemba abaremyi ukunda cane ku rubuga rwa Rumble, haba ku rubuga canke kuri porogarama ya telefone ngendanwa. Kugira ngo ubikore, genda kuri profile y’umuremyi canke munsi ya imwe mu mavidewo yabo hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Tip*".


![Image](assets/fr/44.webp)


Hanyuma, ushireko kode ya QR ukoresheje Rumble Wallet yawe kugira ngo wohereze intererano yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/45.webp)


Niba wifuza kohereza impanuro ukoresheje iyindi Bitcoin wallet, kanda "*Impanuro n'iyindi wallet*".


![Image](assets/fr/46.webp)


## 8. Kwakira impanuro zivuye kuri Rumble


Woba uri umuhinguzi w'ibintu kuri Rumble? Ubu ushobora kwemera impanuro zivuye ku bakunzi bawe muri BTC, USDT, na XAUT biciye kuri Rumble Wallet. Kugira ngo ubikore, ushobora gusa gukora wallet kuri Rumble Wallet ukoresheje konti yawe ya Rumble, nk’uko vyasiguwe mu ntambwe ya 3 y’iyi nyigisho. Konti yawe imaze gushirwaho, fyonda ku buto "*Shoboza Tip Jars*" — nivyo! Buto ya "*Tip*" izoca iboneka ku rubuga rwawe rwa Rumble no munsi y'amasanamu yawe.


Kuva kuri Rumble Wallet, fyonda ku konti yawe "*Tip Jar*" kugira ngo ubone impanuro waronse.


![Image](assets/fr/47.webp)


Uvuye muri Rumble Wallet, ushobora rero guhindura izo mpanuro n’amahera ya fiat biciye kuri MoonPay, ukazihindura mu yandi mafaranga y’amahera, ukazirungika mu yindi wallet yo hanze, canke gusa ukazibika neza hano — kuko ari wewe wenyene ugenzura iyi wallet.


Ubu urazi vyose ku vyerekeye Rumble Wallet!


Kugira wallet yikingira nka Rumble Wallet ni intambwe ihambaye, ariko ni intango gusa y’urugendo rwawe rwa Bitcoin. Kugira ngo ugende kure, uronke ubwigenge, ugire uruhara n’umwete mu rubuga, kandi ukomeze ubuzima bwite bwawe, ukwiye kandi gukoresha node yawe bwite ya Bitcoin. Ntuzi aho wotangura? Twarateguye inyigisho yuzuye kandi y’ubuntu ikuyobora intambwe ku yindi — kuva ku guhitamwo ibikoresho vyawe n’amaporogarama yawe gushika ku kubungabunga urudodo rwawe rwa mbere rwa Bitcoin.
