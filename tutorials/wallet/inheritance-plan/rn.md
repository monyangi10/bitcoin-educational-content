Ugutanga ama bitcoins bigereranya ingorane ikomeye y’ubuhinga benshi bafise ama bitcoins birengagiza. Mu buryo butandukanye n’itunga ry’amabanki ry’agaciro, aho ikigo c’ivy’imari gishobora kohereza ayo mahera abayifise uburenganzira, Bitcoin ikora ata bahuza. Abakunzi bawe ntibazokwigera bashobora kuronka amahera yawe ata makuru y’ubuhinga akenewe, ata kuraba ko yemewe n’amategeko.



Iyi nyigisho irakuyobora mu kurema umugambi w’iragi ry’ubuhinga. Uzomenya ingene uburyo bwa on-chain bwo gutanga amakuru bukora, ingene wondika ivyo utunganije, n’ingene wohitamwo inyishu zibereye kugira ngo iragi ryawe rya Bitcoin rigume rishikira abakunzi bawe.



## Igituma umugambi w'iragi ry'ubuhinga ari ngombwa



Bitcoin ishingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako y’ishimikiro y’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru: uwufise imfunguruzo z’ibanga wese ni we agenzura amahera. Ubwo busegaba buca buba ikintu gikomeye co guhungabana iyo uwubufise azimye ataco atanga amakuru akenewe.



Itegeko ry’iragi rya Bitcoin ritegerezwa gushitsa intumbero zibiri zisa n’izivuguruzanya: kwemera ko abakunzi bawe bashobora kuronka amahera yawe igihe kigeze, mu gihe ubuza uwundi muntu wese kuyaronka atarashika. Uwo murongo w’uburinganire ushingiye ku bushobozi bwa Bitcoin bwo gukora porogarama z’akavukire.



Ugusobanuka kw’ubuhinga kwongera ikindi kintu c’ingorane. Abazungu bawe bazobwirizwa gukoresha ivyiyumviro nk’amajambo yo gusubirana, ibisobanuro vy’ibikorwa, canke inzira z’ugukomoka. Iyo ata kwitegura guhagije, mbere n’umuragwa afise intumbero nziza arashobora gukora amakosa adashobora gusubirwamwo.



## Uko iragi rya on-chain rikora



Bitcoin ikoresha ururimi rwayo rwo kwandika kugira ngo ikoreshe amakuru y’ugukoresha amahera ataco akora mu bikorwa. on-chain inheritance ikoresha iyo porogarama kugira ngo ireme inzira zindi zo gusubirana zikora ubwazo.



### Insaha z'isaha



Timelocks ni uburyo nyamukuru bwo kuragwa Bitcoin. Bituma amahera ashobora gukingirwa gushika igihe gitegekanijwe kishitse.



**CLTV (CheckLockTimeVerify)**: Iyi nzira y'igihe igenzura ko igihe kinaka (itariki canke uburebure bw'ibarabara) cashitse imbere y'uko yemerera gukoresha amahera. Nk'akarorero, "aya mahera ashobora gukoreshwa gusa inyuma y'ibarabara 900000" canke "inyuma y'igenekerezo rya 1 Mukakaro 2026". Ivyiza vya CLTV ni uko ishobora guteba igihe kirekire (imyaka myinshi), ariko itariki irashirwaho kandi ikoreshwa mu buryo bumwe ku ma UTXO yose ari mu gisata. Kugira ngo ugume ugenzura amahera yawe, utegerezwa gukora buri gihe igitabu gishasha gifise itariki yo guherako amahera yawe yongereweko maze ukagirungikira amahera yawe.



**CSV (CheckSequenceVerify)**: Iyi nzira y'igihe igenzura ko umubare w'amabuye waciye kuva UTXO iremwa. Nk'akarorero, "ayo mahera ashobora gukoreshwa gusa amabarabara 52560 (~umwaka umwe) amaze kwakira". Ivyiza vya CSV ni uko UTXO yose ifise igiharuro cayo cigenga. Igihe cose ukoze igikorwa, ama UTXO mashasha yaremwe arasubiramwo igihe cayo. Ariko rero, igipimo c’ubuhinga c’amabuye 65535 (~amezi 15 n’ibindi) kiragabanya igihe gishoboka. Ubwo buryo burasanzwe cane mu gukoresha ku musi ku musi, kubera ko igikorwa cawe gisanzwe gica gisubiza inyuma igihe co guhera.



### Inzira nyinshi zo gukoresha



Igitabo c'iragi gifatanya inzira nyinshi zo gukoresha muri buri aderesi:





- Inzira nyamukuru** : Nyir’itongo arashobora gukoresha amahera yiwe igihe cose afise urufunguzo rwiwe nyamukuru, ata gihe kibujijwe.
- Inzira zo gusubirana**: Urufunguzo rumwe canke nyinshi zishobora gukoresha amahera gusa inyuma y’igihe cashinzwe.



Igihe cose igikorwa gikorwa na nyen'ikintu "kirasubiza ubuzima" UTXO, kigatuma habaho ibisohoka bishasha bifise igihe co gusubiramwo. Ubwo buryo buratuma igihe cose nyen’ivyo bikoresho aguma akora, inzira zo gukira ntizigera zikora.



### Ivyanditswe n’ivya Taproot



**Inyandiko ntoyi** ni ururimi rutunganijwe rwateguwe na Andrew Poelstra, Pieter Wuille na Sanket Kanjalkar rutuma vyoroha kwandika no gusuzuma inyandiko zikomeye za Bitcoin. Igufasha gukora ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha amahera bishobora gusomwa no kugenzurwa, bihambaye ku miterere y’iragi irimwo imfunguruzo nyinshi n’ibifungurwa vy’igihe.



**Taproot** (yatanguye gukora mu kwezi kwa 11 2021) iratuma iragi rya on-chain ritera imbere cane. Kubera imiterere yayo y’igiti (MAST), gusa uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera bukoreshwa ni bwo buhishurirwa kuri blockchain. Iyo nyen’itongo akoresheje amahera yiwe mu buryo busanzwe, ivy’iragi biguma bitaboneka. Iryo banga rigabanya kandi amahera yo gucuruza ku nzira zigoye.



## Akamaro gakomeye k'abadondora



Ku bitabo vy’iragi, ijambo ry’ugusubirana (seed) ntirihagije kugira ngo umuntu asubire kuronka amahera. **Igisobanuro** gica kiba ikintu gihambaye.



Insobanuro ni urudodo rudondora neza imiterere y'ibiharuro: imfunguruzo za bose zirimwo, ivyangombwa vyo gukoresha, inzira z'ugukura, n'ibifungurwa vy'igihe vyatunganijwe. Akira akarorero koroshe:



```
wsh(or_d(pk([fingerprint/path]xpub...),and_v(v:pkh([fingerprint/path]xpub...),older(52560))))
```



Iyi ndondoro ivuga: "canke urufunguzo rwa mbere rushobora gukoresha ubwo nyene, canke urufunguzo rwo gusubirana rushobora gukoresha inyuma y'amabuye 52560".



Reka dufungure aka karorero:




- `wsh()` : Inyandiko y'Icabona Hash, yerekana ubwoko bw'aderesi (P2WSH)
- canke_d()`: "canke" ivyangombwa n'ishami mburabuzi
- pk([urutoke/inzira]xpub...)`: Urufunguzo rwa bose rufise urutoke rwarwo n'inzira y'aho ruva
- na_v()`: "na" ivyangombwa bifatanya urufunguzo rwo gusubirana n'igihe co gufunga
- `ivya kera(52560)`: Igihe c'ugufunga c'amabuye 52560



**Nta nsobanuro, mbere n’amajambo yose yo gusubirana, abazungu bawe ntibazoshobora gusubira kwubaka igitabu.** Igitabu gisanzwe gishobora gusubirwamwo gusa kivuye kuri seed kuko gikurikira inzira z’ugusubirana zisanzwe (BIP44, BIP84). Ku rundi ruhande, igitabu c’iragi gikoresha inyandiko zisanzwe zidashobora gutegekwa. Igiharuro c'ibisobanuro (canke dosiye y'imiterere yoherejwe hanze na porogaramu yawe) itegerezwa kujana n'amajambo yo gusubiza mu mugambi wawe w'iragi.



## Inyandiko zigize umugambi w'iragi



Uretse uburyo bw’ubuhinga, umugambi mwiza w’iragi ushingiye ku nkingi zitatu z’inyandiko.



### Ikete ry'iragi .



Iri kete ry’umuntu ku giti ciwe ni ryo ry’injira mu mugambi wawe. Yandikiwe abazungu bawe, irasigura ivyerekeye iyo nkuru be n’ivyo umuntu akwiye kwirinda.



Ikete ryawe ritegerezwa kubamwo amategeko atomoye yerekeye umutekano:




- Ntukihute, fata umwanya wo kwiga imbere yo kwimurira amahera
- Ntukigere ubwira umuntu umwe amajambo yo gukira neza
- Ntukigere winjiza amajambo yo gusubiza muri porogaramu canke mudasobwa itagenzuwe
- Iyubare ubusuma n'abantu batanga imfashanyo batasavye
- Rondera impanuro n’imiburiburi ku bantu babiri wizigira imbere y’uko ufata ingingo iyo ari yo yose .



Iri kete ririmwo kandi amakuru y’aho umunyamabanga wawe wohamagara n’aho ivy’ubutaka bwawe biri. Ntibikwiye kwigera birimwo amajambo yo gusubiza canke amajambo banga.



### Ububiko bw'abantu wizewe



Nta muragwa akwiye guhura n’ugukira kwa bitcoin wenyene. Iyi nkuru iratanga urutonde rw’abantu bashobora gutanga imfashanyo mu vy’ubuhinga canke mu vy’amategeko.



Ku muntu wese wo guhamagara, ushireko inyandiko: izina ryose, ubucuti ufitaniye, uruhara muri uwo mugambi, urugero rw’ukwizigira, ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin, n’amakuru yose yo guhamagara. Itegeko ry’ishimikiro: abazungu bawe bakwiye kwama babaza n’imiburiburi abantu babiri batandukanye imbere yo gufata ingingo iyo ari yo yose ihambaye.



### Bitcoin ububiko bw'umutungo



Iki gice kigaragaza ikarita y’ama bitcoins yawe yose n’amakuru y’ubuhinga akenewe kugira ngo uyagarure.



Kuri buri nkuru, inyandiko :




- Ubwoko bw'ibikorwa**: ibikoresho, porogaramu, imiterere (ikimenyetso kimwe, ikimenyetso kinini, iragi)
- Aho igikoresho kiri**: aho igikoresho ca wallet kiri
- Descriptor/aho dosiye y'imiterere iri**: ni ngirakamaro ku bipimo biteye imbere
- Aho ijambo ry'ugusubirana riri**: ritandukanye n'iryo ridondora
- Amakode y'ukwinjira**: aho ama PIN n'amajambo y'ibanga bibikwa
- Guteba kwatunganijwe**: igihe inzira zo gusubirana zikora



## Inyishu z'ubuhinga ziraboneka



Ivyuma vyinshi vy’amaporogarama birashira mu ngiro uburyo bwo kuragwa on-chain. Imwe yose irafise ibiranga ubuhinga bwayo.



### Liana



Liana ni porogaramu yo ku biro (Linux, macOS, Windows) ikoresha Miniscript kugira ngo ikore ibitabo bifise inzira zo gusubirana zitegekanijwe. Uwo mugambi uteguwe na Wizardsardine, ukaba washinzwe na Antoine Poinsot (umuterankunga w’ishimikiro wa Bitcoin).



**Ubwubatsi bw'ubuhinga**: Liana irema ibitabo vya P2WSH (SegWit native) ku buryo busanzwe, n'ubufasha bwa Taproot buboneka bivanye n'uguhuza kw'ibikoresho vyawe vya wallet. Ubwubatsi bushingiye ku nzira nyamukuru n’inzira imwe canke nyinshi zo gusubirana. Insobanuro yavutse ishiramwo ivyangombwa vyose kandi itegerezwa kubikwa.



**Igihe gikoreshwa**: Liana ikoresha igihe gisanzwe (CSV), kigarukira ku bice 65535 (~amezi 15). Kugira ngo ugume ugenzura, utegerezwa gukora igikorwa co gusubiramwo imbere y’uko igihe co gufunga gihera.



**Ugushiramwo ibikoresho vya wallet**: BitBox02, Blockstream Jade, Coldcard, Ledger, Specter DIY, n’ibindi bikoresho birahuye mu gusinyana amasezerano.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/desktop/liana-306ef457-700c-4fdd-b07a-8fb7a8a29f04

### Bitcoin Keeper



Bitcoin Keeper ni porogaramu y'amatelefone ngendanwa (iOS, Android) ihuriza hamwe ibimenyetso vyinshi n'ibifungurwa vy'igihe biciye mu "Bibanza vy'ububiko". Uburyo bwo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa bufise ubuyobozi buhurikiyemwo butuma bushikira abakoresha batagira ubuhinga bwinshi.



**Ubwubatsi bw'ubuhinga**: Ivyumba vy'ububiko vy'ubuhinga bikoresha Miniscript kugira ngo bikore imiterere y'ibimenyetso vyinshi aho imfunguruzo z'inyongera zikora inyuma y'ugucerezwa kwasobanuwe. Urufunguzo rw’Iragi rwongera ku quorum iriho, mu gihe Urufunguzo rw’Ivyihutirwa rushobora guca ku nzira ya multisig burundu.



**Igihe gikoreshwa**: Bitcoin Keeper ikoresha igihe gikoreshwa (CLTV), kigatuma igihe co gukora kirenze amezi 15. Itariki yo gutangura gukora itegekanijwe ku kurema kwa wallet kandi ikoreshwa ku ma UTXO yose. Iyi porogaramu irimwo igikorwa co "guhindura" gicungera ubwaco: uwukoresha akurikiza gusa intambwe zirongowe, ataco akeneye gukora wallet nshasha.



**Ibindi**: Inyandiko z’iragi zihuriweko, Canary Wallets kugira ngo umenye ivy’ugusenyuka kw’ingenzi, no gusubiramwo ivyibutsa.

### Umurage



Heritage ni porogaramu yo kuri mudasobwa ikoresha inyandiko za Taproot kugira ngo ishiremwo ivyangombwa vy'iragi. Ikoreshwa rya Taproot ritanga ibanga ryongerekanye, kubera ko inzira zitakoreshwa ziguma zitaboneka kuri blockchain.



**Ubwubatsi bw’ubuhinga**: Aderesi yose y’Iragi ishiramwo inzira nyamukuru n’izindi nzira z’umuragwa wese, n’ibihe bigenda biratera imbere. Uburyo bw’ubukuru butuma bishoboka gusobanura umuntu ku giti ciwe ku mezi 6 n’abazungu b’umuryango ku mezi 12-15.



**Uburyo bwo gukoresha**: Verisiyo ihagaze yonyene ifise node yawe bwite (ku buntu) canke service icungiwe yongerako ivyibutsa n’amatangazo ku bazungu (0.05%/umwaka).

## Inzira y'ugukira kw'umuragwa



Gutahura ingene umuntu akira birafasha gutegura umugambi mwiza. Ehe intambwe z’ubuhinga umuragwa azokenera gukurikira.



### Ibisabwa kugira umuntu akire



Umuragwa akeneye :


1. **Igisobanuro canke dosiye y'imiterere** y'ibitabo vy'umwimerere (JSON canke uburyo bw'inyandiko, bivanye n'ivyo bikoresho)


2. **Ijambo ry’ugusubirana kwayo** (iryo rifatanye n’urufunguzo rw’iragi ryayo, akenshi amajambo 12 canke 24)


3. **Iporogarama zihuye** (Liana, Bitcoin Keeper, Iragi, canke Sparrow/Igishushanyo ku bisobanuro bisanzwe)


4. **Uguhuzwa n'umurongo wa Bitcoin** kugira ngo usuzume uko igihe kimeze no gutangaza ibikorwa



### Intambwe zo gukira



1. **Shira porogarama** ku gikoresho gitekanye maze utunganye uburyo bwo gukorana n’urubuga rwa Bitcoin (node ​​y’umuntu ku giti ciwe canke server ya Electrum)


2. **Imbere igisobanuro** kugira ngo wongere wubake imiterere y'ibiharuro. Porogaramu izokwisubiriza generate amaderesi yose yakoreshejwe


3. **Gusubiza urufunguzo rw'iragi** kuva ku mvugo yo gukira. Porogaramu izosuzuma ko urufunguzo ruhuye n'urufunguzo rumwe rwemerewe mu ndondoro


4. **Guhuza ibitabo** kugira ngo ubone ama UTXO yose n'ingene akoresha amahera


5. **Suzuma igihe co gufunga**: porogaramu izokwerekana kuri UTXO yose nimba inzira yo gusubirana ikora


6. **Rema igikorwa co gusubiza** kuri aderesi igenzurwa gusa n’umuragwa (vyiza ni wallet nshasha)


7. **Gusinya no gutangaza** ivy'ugucuruza ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin



Iyo igihe co gufunga kitarahera, umuragwa azobwirizwa kurindira. Porogarama izokwerekana itariki canke igice co gukira bishoboka. Muri ico kiringo co kurindira, amahera aguma ari mu mutekano ku rubuga rwa blockchain.



### Ingingo zo kuraba umuragwa



Uwuzoragwa ategerezwa kwitwararika cane :




- Suzuma ukuri kwa porogaramu yakuwe kuri interineti** (imibare, imikono)
- Ntukigere usangira ijambo ryawe ryo gukira** n'umuntu wese atanga imfashanyo
- Baza nibura abantu babiri wizigira** imbere yo gukora igikorwa co gukira
- Kwimurira amahera mu kigega coroshe** agenzura vyose amaze gukira .



## Ibikorwa vyiza



### Gutandukanya amakuru



Ntukigere ubika amakuru yose ahantu hamwe. Ico gisobanuro kigomba gutandukanywa n’amajambo yo gusubizaho, na yo agatandukanywa n’amakode ya PIN. Ukwo gutanga bigorana gushika ku muntu atera mu gihe aguma ashobora gusubirwamwo n’abazungu bawe babereye.



### Ibipimo vyo gukira



Imbere yo gushiramwo amahera menshi, gerageza uburyo bwose bwo gusubirana n’amahera makeyi. Genzura ko ushobora kugarura igitabo c’ibintu bivuye ku nsobanuro n’amajambo yo gusubizaho ku gikoresho kitagira ikintu. Niwandike intambwe z’abazungu bawe.



### Gucungera umwanya



Tegura gusubiramwo neza amasaha yawe imbere y’uko ahera. Ku bijanye n’igihe c’amezi 12, kora igikorwa co gucuruza buri mezi 9-10. Porogaramu akenshi itanga ivyibutsa canke ibikorwa vy’ugusubiramwo vyikora.



### Gutegura ivugurura



Ivyo Bitcoin yawe ikora biratera imbere. Ihinduka ryose rihambaye (igitabu gishasha, guhindura ibihe vy’iherezo, kwongerako umuragwa) ritegerezwa kugaragara mu nyandiko zawe. Gushinga urutonde rwo gusubiramwo buri mwaka.



## Guhitamwo uburyo ukoresha



Guhitamwo hagati y’imiti itandukanye bivana n’ubuhinga bwawe n’ivyo ukeneye vyihariye.



**Liana** irabereye abakoresha mudasobwa bakunda porogarama zifunguye zifise ububasha bwose biciye ku nzira yabo bwite. Itunganywa riguma rishoboka kubera interface iyobowe. Igihe co gufunga (CSV) kirosha gucungera, kuko igikorwa cawe gisanzwe gica gisubiza inyuma igihe co guhera. Igitigiri: igihe co guteba cane c’amasaha 100.000. amezi 15 (amabarabara 65535).



**Bitcoin Keeper** igenewe abakoresha telefone ngendanwa barondera interface yoroshe n’inyandiko ziherekejwe. Iryo koraniro ritanga ubwoko bubiri bw’urufunguzo rudasanzwe: Urufunguzo rw’Iragi (rwongera ku rugero rw’abaje mu nama) n’Urufunguzo rw’Ivyihutirwa (rururengera rwose). Igihe c’ugufunga (CLTV) kiremeza igihe co kurongora kirenze amezi 15, n’igikorwa co gusubiramwo gihuriweko corohereza umuntu gusubirana. Umugambi wa Diamond Hands ufungura ibintu vy’iragi biteye imbere.



**Iragi** rigenewe abakoresha b’ubuhinga bashima ibanga rya Taproot n’iragi ry’ubukuru n’ugucererwa gutera imbere. Igiti ca Taproot gihisha ivy’iragi mu gihe c’ibikorwa bisanzwe, kigahishura gusa ivy’iragi bikoreshwa mu gihe co gukira.



Ivyo bisubizo vyose bitatu birafise ikintu kimwe bihuriyeko: bisaba ko umuntu aguma asubirana kugira ngo inzira zo gukira ntizikore imbere y’igihe. Ico kigoyi ni igiciro n’ingwati y’iragi rya on-chain ritizigirwa. Tegura ivyibutsa bihoraho kandi ushire ivyo kwitaho igice c’urutonde rwawe rwo kurongora Bitcoin.



## Iciyumviro



Itegeko ry’iragi ry’ubuhinga rya Bitcoin rifatanya uburyo bwo gukora amakuru (ibihe vy’ugufunga, Miniscript, Taproot) n’inyandiko zikomeye. Ama wallets ateye imbere aragufasha gutanga ama bitcoins yawe ubwayo inyuma y’igihe udakora, ata muntu agira ico akoze.



Ibintu bihambaye vyo gushikiriza abazungu bawe ni: dosiye y’insobanuro canke y’imiterere, ijambo ryabo ryo gusubiza, amabwirizwa adondora neza yo gusubiza, n’amakuru y’abantu bafise ubushobozi bashobora kubafasha.



Tangana uhitemwo umuti w’ubuhinga ubereye ubuzima bwawe, uwugerageze n’agace gatoyi, hanyuma ushire mu nyandiko yose mu mugambi utunganijwe. Ugusobanuka kw’intango biremeza ko itunga ryawe rya Bitcoin rizoherezwa mu mwiherero wose.
