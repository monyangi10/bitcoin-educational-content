Inyuma y’aho abashinze Samourai Wallet bafashwe, bagafatwa n’ama server yabo ku wa 24 Ndamukiza, ubu ibikorwa bimwe bimwe vy’iyo porogarama ntibikora, kandi abakoresha badafise Dojo yabo ntibashobora gutangaza amafaranga.


Amaze gufasha abakoresha benshi mu gusubirana amafaranga yabo mu misi iheze, nizera ko nahuye n’ibibazo vyinshi bishobora kuvyuka mu gihe co gusubizaho Samourai Wallet. Rero, iyi nyigisho izotangura na raporo y’ivyabaye kugira ngo hamenyekane imikorere iguma ikora n’iyitagihari mu bidukikije vya Samourai Wallet be n’ubuhinga bwa none bwashikiwe n’ico kintu. Inyuma y’aho, tuzogenda intambwe ku yindi kugira ngo dusubire kuronka Samourai Wallet dukoresheje porogarama ya Sparrow wallet. Tuzosuzuma intambamyi zose zishobora gushika muri iyo nzira maze turabe inyishu zo kuzitorera umuti. Ubwa nyuma, mu gice ca nyuma, uzobona ingorane zishobora gutera ubuzima bwite bwawe hakurikijwe ugufatwa kwa server.


_Ndashimira cane [@Louferlou](https://twitter.com/Louferlou), yafashije abakoresha benshi mu gukira kwabo no kunsangiza ivyo yiboneye, kandi na we nyene yaratanze umusanzu mu gupima kugira ngo hamenyekane ivyo bikiriho._


## Mbega Samourai Wallet iracariko irakora?


Ego, **app ya Samourai Wallet iracariko irakora**, ariko mu bihe bimwe bimwe.


Ica mbere, ni ngombwa ko iyo app yari yarashizwe mbere kuri telefone yawe. Iduka rya Google Play ryakuyeho iyo porogarama, kandi APK yari yashizwe ku rubuga rwafashwe. Ni co gituma bigoye gushiramwo Samourai muri iki gihe. Ushobora gusanga APKs kuri internet, ariko ndaguhanura ko utozikura kuri internet kiretse uzi neza aho zikomoka.


Kubera ko urupapuro rwa Samourai Wallet rutagiboneka kuri Google Play Store, ntibishoboka ko umuntu ahagarika guhindura ibintu bishasha. Iyo app isubiye ku mbuga zo gukurako, vyoba vyiza **uzimye ama updates yikora** gushika habonetse amakuru menshi yerekeye iterambere ry’urubanza.


Nimba Samourai Wallet isanzwe ishizwe kuri telefone yawe, ukwiye kuguma ushobora gukoresha iyo porogarama. Kugira ngo ukoreshe ubuhinga bwa Wallet bwa Samourai, ni ngombwa ko uhuza Dojo. Mbere, abakoresha batagira Dojo y’umuntu ku giti ciwe bari bashingiye ku ma server ya Samourai kugira ngo bashobore kuronka amakuru ya Bitcoin Blockchain no gutangaza amafaranga. Kubera ko izo serveri zifashwe, iyo porogarama ntizosubira kuronka ayo makuru.

Niba mbere utagira Dojo ihuye ariko ubu ukaba ufise, urashobora kuyishiraho kugira ngo wongere ukoreshe app yawe ya Samourai. Ivyo birimwo gusuzuma ama backups yawe, gukuraho Wallet (Wallet, si porogarama), no kugarura Wallet mu gufatanya Dojo yawe na porogarama. Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku bijanye n'izo ntambwe, urashobora kuraba [iyi nyigisho, mu gice "_Gutegura igikapu cawe ca Samourai_" : CoinJoin - DOJO] (umugambi w’urubuga/inyigisho/ubuzima bwite/On-Chain/CoinJoin-dojo-c4b20263-5b30-4c74-ae59-dc8d0f8715c2).

Nimba app yawe ya Samourai yari isanzwe ifatanye na Dojo yawe bwite, rero igice ca Wallet kiragukorera neza cane. Ushobora kubona amahera yawe n’amahera yawe yo gutangaza. Naho ivyo vyose biriko biraba, mbona ko Samourai Wallet iguma ariyo porogarama nziza cane y’amatelefone ngendanwa ya Wallet muri iki gihe. Ku bwanje, ndategura kubandanya ndayikoresha.


Ikibazo nyamukuru woshobora guhura naco ni ukudashobora gushikira amakonti ya Whirlpool ukoresheje app. Kenshi, Samourai agerageza gushinga ubucuti na Whirlpool CLI yawe maze agatangura inzinguzingu za CoinJoin imbere y’uko aguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha izo konti. Ariko rero, kubera ko iyo nzira itagishoboka, iyo porogarama irabandanya irondera ibihe bidahera ata na rimwe ikuronsa uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amakonti ya Whirlpool. Muri ivyo, urashobora gusubirana izo konti ku yindi porogarama ya Wallet mu gihe uguma ufise gusa konti y’ibiziga kuri Samourai.


### Ni ibikoresho ibihe bikiriho kuri Samourai?


Ku rundi ruhande, ibikoresho bimwebimwe birashikirwa n’uguhagarara kwa server canke ntibiboneke rwose.


Ku bijanye n’ibikoresho vy’umuntu ku giti ciwe, vyose birakora mu buryo busanzwe, birashoboka ko ufise Dojo yawe bwite. Ibikorwa bisanzwe vya Stonewall (kandi atari Stonewall x2) birakora ata ngorane.


Ivyiyumviro vyavuzwe kuri Twitter vyerekanye ko ubuzima bwite butangwa n’ugucuruza kwa Stonewall ubu bushobora kugabanywa. Agaciro kongerewe k’isoko rya Stonewall riri mu kuba ridashobora gutandukanywa n’isoko rya Stonewall x2 mu bijanye n’imiterere. Iyo umusesanguzi ahuye n’ico kigereranyo kidasanzwe, ntashobora kumenya nimba ari Stonewall isanzwe ifise umukoresha umwe canke Stonewall x2 ikoresha abakoresha babiri. Ariko nk’uko tuzobibona mu ngingo zikurikira, gukora ibikorwa vya Stonewall x2 vyaciye bigorana cane kubera Soroban itaboneka. Bamwe rero biyumvira ko umuhinga mu vy’ugusesangura yoshobora ubu kwiyumvira yuko igikorwa cose co gukorana n’ico gice ari uruhome rw’amabuye rusanzwe. Ku bwanje, ivyo vyiyumviro ntabisangiye. Naho amafaranga y’ubudandaji ya Stonewall x2 ashobora kuba ari make (kandi nibwira ko yari asanzwe ariho imbere y’iki kintu), kuba agishoboka birashobora gutuma isesengura ryose rishingiye ku ciyumviro c’uko atari ryo ritagira akamaro.

Ku bijanye na Ricochet, sinari nshoboye kumenya nimba iyo serivisi ikiriko irakora, kubera ko ntagira Dojo kuri Testnet, kandi ndahitamwo kutagira ingorane zo gukoresha `100 000 Sats` ku Wallet yoshobora kugenzurwa n’ubutegetsi. Niba wararonse akaryo ko kugerageza iki gikoresho vuba, ndagutumiye ngo unyandikire kugira ngo dushobore guhindura iyi nkuru.


Niba ukeneye gukoresha Ricochet, menya neza ko ushobora kwama ukora iyo nzira n’amaboko ukoresheje porogarama iyo ari yo yose ya Wallet. Kugira ngo umenye ingene wokora neza n’amaboko ama hops atandukanye, ndagusavye kuraba iyi yindi ngingo: [**RICOCHET**]


Ico gikoresho ca JoinBot ntikigikora, kuko cari gishingiye ku ruhara rw’indege Wallet irongowe na Samourai.


Ku bijanye n'ubundi bwoko bw'ibikorwa vy'ubufatanye, akenshi vyitwa "cahoots", biraguma bishoboka, ariko bikoreshwa n'amaboko gusa. Imbere y’uko server ifunga, wari ufise uburyo bubiri bwo gukora ibikorwa vya Stonewall x2 canke Stowaway (PayJoin):



- Koresha urubuga rwa Soroban kugira ngo ukore Exchange PSBTs zihite kandi zibe kure;
- Canke ukore ivyo biganiro n’amaboko mu gucapura amakode menshi ya QR.


Inyuma y’ibipimo vyinshi, bisa n’uko Soroban atagikora. Kugira ngo ivyo bikorwa vy’ubufatanye bikorwe, Exchange y’amakuru rero itegerezwa gukorwa n’amaboko. Aha hari uburyo bubiri bwo gukora iyi Exchange:



- Nimba uri hafi y’uwo mukorana ku mubiri, urashobora gucapura amakode ya QR akurikirana;
- Niba uri kure y’uwo mukorana, urashobora Exchange PSBTs biciye ku nzira y’itumanaho yo hanze ku gikorwa. Ariko rero, urabe maso, kuko amakuru ari muri izo PSBT ari ay’agaciro ku bijanye n’ubuzima bwite. Ndabahimiriza gukoresha ubutumwa bufise amabanga kugira ngo Exchange ibe ibanga.

Naho Whirlpool, iyo protocole isa n’itagikora, mbere no ku bakoresha bafise Dojo yabo. Nariko ndakurikirana RoninDojo yanje muri iyi misi iheze kandi ngerageza gukora ibintu bimwe bimwe vy’ishimikiro, ariko Whirlpool CLI ntiyashoboye kwifatanya kuva server ihagarara.


Ariko rero, ndaguma mfise icizigiro c’uko iyo protocole ishobora gusubira gukora canke kumbure igasubira kwiyumvirwa mu buryo butandukanye mu ndwi ziza, bivanye n’ingene ibintu bizogenda. Iryo ruhusha ryoshobora kuba akaryo ko gutohoza uburyo bushasha canke ivyoshobora gutuma iyo nzira itera imbere.


### Ni ibikoresho ibihe vyo hanze bikiriho?


Ku bijanye n’ibindi bikoresho bijanye n’ibidukikije vy’Abasamourai, bimwe biracariho mu gihe ibindi bitaboneka.


Urubuga rwo gusesangura uruhererekane rw’ubuntu OXT.me, ikibabaje ni uko rutagihari muri iki gihe.


Igikoresho ca Whirlpool Stats ntikigishobora gukurwako, kuko cari cashizwe kuri GitLab ya Samourai. Naho woba waramaze gukuraho iki gikoresho ca Python mu karere kawe ku mashine yawe, canke iyo cashizwe ku nzira yawe ya RoninDojo, WST ntizokora ubu. Nkako, vyari bishingiye ku makuru yatanzwe na OXT.me kugira ngo bikore, kandi uru rubuga ntirugishobora gukoreshwa. Ubu, WST ntaco imaze cane kuko umurongo wa Whirlpool udakora.


Urubuga rwa KYCP.org ubu ntirugishobora gukoreshwa.


GitLab yari yakira kode y’igikoresho ca Python c’Ibarabara rya Boltzmann na yo nyene yarafashwe. Muri iki gihe rero, ntibigishoboka ko umuntu ashobora gukuraho ico gikoresho. Ariko iyo ufise RoninDojo, urashobora kubandanya ukoresha igiharuro ca Boltzmann nk’uko vyari kera.


Naho RoninDojo, iyo porogarama y’ivyuma (node-in-box) irabandanya gukora neza naho ata bikoresho bimwebimwe vyihariye nka Whirlpool CLI na WST bihari. Ishobora gukoreshwa n’izindi porogarama za Wallet kubera Fulcrum canke Electrs. Niba wipfuza kuronka amakuru menshi ku vyerekeye RoninDojo canke ufise ibibazo vyihariye, ndaguhimiriza kwinjira [mu mugwi wabo wa Telegram](https://t.me/RoninDojoNode).


Ariko rero, kode y’inkomoko ya RoninDojo ubu ntabwo igishobora gukoreshwa, kuko yari yashizwe kuri GitLab ya Samourai. Ntibishoboka rero ko umuntu ayishira ku Raspberry Pi n’amaboko muri iki gihe.


Ku bijanye n’iporogarama ya Watch-only wallet yitwa Sentinel, ibintu bisa n’ivyo kuri porogarama ya Samourai. Niba ufise Dojo yawe, urashobora kubandanya ukoresha Sentinel ata ngorane. Ariko rero, iyo udafise Dojo, ntuzosubira gushinga ubucuti. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ubw’i Samourai, urubuga rwa Sentinel ruracari ku rubuga rwa interineti. Ariko rero, urabe maso kuri uru rubuga be na APK itangwa ng’aho, kuko ntibisobanutse neza uwugenzura ivyo bikoresho ubu.


### Mbega Sparrow wallet yoba igira ico ikoze?


Sparrow wallet irabandanya ikora nk’uko bisanzwe, uretse ibikoresho vya Samourai bitagihari. Ubu, ntibigishoboka gukora coinjoins biciye kuri Sparrow. Na vyo nyene, ibikoresho vyo gukoresha amahera mu bufatanye ntibikiboneka, kuko Sparrow idatanga uburyo bwo gukoresha Exchange y’amaboko y’ama PSBT, bitandukanye na Samourai. Ku bindi bikorwa vyose, Sparrow ikora ata ngorane. Ushobora kandi gukoresha iyi porogarama kugira ngo ubone Samourai Wallet iyo bikenewe.


## Ni gute wogarura igisasu ca Samourai Wallet?


Nk’uko twabibonye mu bice vyabanje, iyo ufise Dojo yawe, ntibisabwa guhindura porogarama. **Samourai iguma ari ihitamwo ryiza cane rya Hot Wallet** ku vyo ukoresha ku musi. Ariko rero, nimba udafise Dojo canke nimba ushaka guhitamwo uwundi murongo, nzogusigurira ingene wovyifatamwo neza, ndagusobanurire ido n’ido intambamyi zose woshobora guhura na zo.


Uko biri kwose, birahambaye ko ufata umwanya wawe kandi ukamenya neza ko ata makosa ukora. Ibuka, nta kwihuta, nk’uko ufata imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga, kandi gufata ama server ya Samourai ntaco bihindura kuri ivyo mu buryo na bumwe. Uko vyogenda kwose, biragaragara ko badashobora gushika ku mfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga.


### Suzuma passphrase


Kugira ngo ubone Wallet yawe, utegerezwa kuba ufise passphrase yawe, naho woba uhisemwo kuyigarura biciye ku dosiye y’ububiko. Tangana n’ukugenzura ukuri kw’iyi passphrase. Ugurure porogarama yawe ya Samourai Wallet, ukande ku kimenyetso cawe ca Paynym kiri hejuru ibubamfu, hanyuma uhitemwo `Ivyagezwe`.


![samourai](assets/1.webp)


Inyuma, ukande kuri `Gutorera umuti ingorane` hanyuma ukande kuri `passphrase/ikigeragezo co gusubiza inyuma`.


![samourai](assets/2.webp)


Injira passphrase yawe hanyuma ukande `Ok`. Niba ari ukuri, Samurai azovyemeza. Ufise kandi uburenganzira bwo kugenzura dosiye y’ububiko nimba utegura kuyikoresha mu nyuma.


![samourai](assets/3.webp)


Iyi ntambwe ni iyo guhitamwo ariko ni ngirakamaro. Biremeza ko passphrase ari ukuri, bikaba bikuraho ikintu coshobora gutuma haba ingorane mu nyuma. Iyo Samourai yerekanye ko passphrase itari yo muri iki gihe, gukira ntibizoshoboka. Raba neza ko winjije neza passphrase hanyuma wongere uyisuzume.


### Ihitamwo rya 1: Gusubiza Wallet kuri Sparrow n'idosiye y'ububiko


Kuva kuri verisiyo 1.8.6 ya Sparrow wallet, birashoboka ko ushobora kwinjiza Samourai Wallet yawe ukoresheje dosiye y’inyandiko y’ububiko yitwa `samourai.txt`, iyo porogarama yawe ihita itanga. Iyi dosiye irimwo amakuru yose akenewe kugira ngo ushobore kugarura Wallet yawe kandi ipfutse na passphrase yawe kugira ngo uyikingire.


Niwahitamwo iyi nzira, uzokenera dosiye yawe `samourai.txt` igezweho na passphrase yawe. Kugira ngo generate iyi dosiye kuri Samourai Wallet, fyonda ku tudodo dutatu turi hejuru iburyo, hanyuma uhitemwo `Sohora Wallet ububiko`.


![samourai](assets/4.webp)

Ibikurikira, hitamwo `Sohoka hanze ku rutonde`. Inyuma y’ivyo, uzokenera gushiramwo iyo dosiye kuri PC yawe ata nkomanzi. Kubera ko iyo dosiye ipfutse, ariko passphrase yonyene ihagije kugira ngo uyifungure, birahambaye ko umuntu yiyubara mu gihe ariko arayirungika. Niwahitamwo gutanga amakuru ataco akora nk'inyandiko rusangi, uzokenera gukora dosiye `samourai.txt` kuri PC yawe maze uyishiremwo ibirimwo. Iyindi nzira yoba ari ugukura dosiye `samourai.txt` mu madosiye abitswe kuri telefone yawe.

Uhejeje kuronka dosiye kuri PC yawe, fungura Sparrow wallet, ukande kuri `Dosiye`, hanyuma uhitemwo `Import Wallet` kugira ngo utangure kwinjiza Wallet yawe.


![samourai](assets/5.webp)


Hanukira kuri `Igishushanyo ca Samourai`, ukande kuri `Dosiye yo kwinjiza`, hanyuma uhitemwo dosiye yawe ya `samourai.txt`.


![samourai](assets/6.webp)


Sparrow izoca igusaba ijambobanga ryo gufungura dosiye. Iri jambobanga ni passphrase yawe. Bishire mu kibanza gihuye hanyuma ukande kuri `Import`.


![samourai](assets/7.webp)


Niba muri iki gihe, Wallet yawe itaboneka, birashoboka ko wakoze ikosa igihe wakopa dosiye `samourai.txt` canke igihe winjiza passphrase. Ushobora kuraba igice co gutorera umuti ingorane kugira ngo ubone imfashanyo nyinshi.


![samourai](assets/8.webp)


Ku bwoko bw'inyandiko, nimba utaratunganya izindi nyandiko mu rurimi rwa Samourai, mu bisanzwe ushobora gukoresha SegWit V0 gusa (SegWit / P2WPKH y'imvukira). Gumana iyi nyandiko mburabuzi hanyuma ukande kuri `Import`.


![samourai](assets/9.webp)


Izina Wallet yawe, nk'akarorero, "Ugukira kwa Samourai", hanyuma ukande kuri `Rema Wallet`.


![samourai](assets/10.webp)


Sparrow izoca igusaba guhitamwo ijambobanga. Iri jambobanga ririnda gusa gushika kuri Wallet yawe kuri iyi PC kandi ntirijanye n’aho imfunguruzo za Wallet yawe zikomoka. Raba neza ko uhisemwo ijambobanga rikomeye, uriyandike kugira ngo uribuke, hanyuma ukande kuri `Set Password`.


![samourai](assets/11.webp)


Sparrow izoca ikuraho imfunguruzo za Wallet yawe maze irondere amafaranga ahuye.


![samourai](assets/12.webp)


Ubu rero, konti yawe yo gushiramwo amahera ni yo yonyene ishobora gukoreshwa. Iyo ukoresha Samourai kuri iyi konti gusa, wari ukwiye kubona amahera yawe yose. Ariko rero, nimba wariko urakoresha Whirlpool, uzokenera gukura amakonti ya `premix`, `postmix`, na `badbank`. Ku Sparrow, gusa ukande ku `Ivyagezwe`, hanyuma kuri `Ongera Konti...`.


![samourai](assets/13.webp)

Mu idirisha rifunguka, hitamwo `Whirlpool Accounts` mu bimenyetso biva hasi, hanyuma ukande kuri `OK`.

![samourai](assets/14.webp)


Uzoca ubona amakonti yawe atandukanye ya Whirlpool aboneka, maze Sparrow izoronka imfunguruzo zikenewe kugira ngo ikoreshe ama bitcoins ajanye.


![samourai](assets/15.webp)


Niba ukoresha porogarama itandukanye na Sparrow, nka Electrum, kugira ngo ugarure Samourai Wallet yawe, ng’ibi urutonde rwa konti ya Whirlpool kugira ngo ugarure n’amaboko:



- Ibiziga: `m/84'/0'/0'`
- Banki mbi: m/84'/0'/2147483644
- Imbere: `m/84'/0'/2147483645'`
- Ivangura: `m/84'/0'/2147483646'`


Ubu urashobora kuronka amafaranga yawe kuri Sparrow. Niba ukeneye gufashwa ukoresheje Sparrow wallet, urashobora kandi kuraba [inyigisho yacu yihariye](https://planb.network/inyigisho/wallet/desktop/sparrow-c674e2ac-d46f-4c82-92a7-7d1b0e262f5d).


Ndabagira inama kandi yo kwinjiza mu gihugu n’amaboko amabara mwari mufatanije n’ama UTXO yanyu kuri Samourai. Ivyo bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gukora neza ubugenzuzi bwa Coin kuri Sparrow mu nyuma.

Kugira ngo utangure, urabe neza ko ufise ijambo ryawe ryo gukira be n’igitabu cawe ca passphrase. Hanyuma, fungura porogaramu ya Sparrow wallet, ukande kuri `Dosiye`, hanyuma uhitemwo `Import Wallet` kugira ngo utangure kwinjiza Wallet yawe.

### Ni ingorane izihe abantu benshi bahura na zo?


Maze gufasha abantu benshi mu misi iheze, nizera ko nahuye n’ingorane nyinshi zishobora gutuma Wallet yawe idasubirana. Niba utashobora gushika ku Wallet yawe naho hari inyigisho za kera, ng’izi izindi mpanuro.

Igikurikira, ikosa ryo kwandika muri passphrase yawe mu gihe co gusubirana rizovamwo imfunguruzo zitari zo, ivyo bizotuma Wallet yawe idasubirana kuri Sparrow. **passphrase itegerezwa kuba ari ukuri ata gatotsi!**

2. **Ikosa rya passphrase:** Iyo Samourai yerekanye ko passphrase atari yo, nta co bimaze gukomeza kugerageza kuri Sparrow. Igihe cose passphrase ibereye itabonetse, ugusubirana kwa Wallet yawe ntibishoboka. Niba waratakaje ubuziraherezo passphrase yawe, uzigame neza igitabu cawe co gusaba Samourai. Ico ushobora gukora n’ukwizigira ko ama servers azosubira gukora kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha amafaranga ataco ukeneye gusubirana. **Ntugerageze gufatanya Dojo muri iki gihe**, kuko ivyo vyoba bisobanura gusubiramwo Wallet yawe kuri Samourai, bisaba gushika ku passphrase.


Mu yandi makosa yabonetse, menshi ajanye n'imiterere y'urubuga kuri Sparrow.

## Ni ingaruka izihe zizogira ku buzima bwite bw’amahera ukoresha?


### Nk'umukoresha wa Samourai ata Dojo


Iyo ukoresha Samourai Wallet utahuye na Dojo yawe bwite, xpubs zawe zategerezwa kumenyeshwa ama server ya Samourai kugira ngo iyo app ikore. Kubera ko izo servers zafashwe, birashoboka ko abategetsi ubu bashobora gukoresha izo xpubs.


Ivyo bintu biguma ari ivy’ukwiyumvira. Ntituzi nimba izo xpubs zanditswe, nimba hari ububiko bushobora gusenyuka, nimba abategetsi bazigaruye, kandi nimba bateguye kuzikoresha mu gusesangura uruhererekane. Ariko rero, mu gihe nk’ico, ni vyiza ko umuntu yiyumvira ikintu kibi kuruta ibindi vyose, aho abategetsi bafise ama xpubs y’abakoresha batahuje Dojo yabo bwite.

Kugira ngo ubone, xpub ni uruhererekane rw'inyuguti rurimwo vyose bikenewe Elements vyo gutanga imfunguruzo za bose z'abana (urufunguzo rwa bose + chain code). Ikoreshwa mu bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none kugira ngo generate yakire amaderesi no kwihweza ibikorwa vya konti ataco igaragaza ku mfunguruzo z’ibanga zijanye n’ivyo. Ivyo bituma, nk'akarorero, hakorwa "isaha gusa" Wallet. Ariko rero, gutangaza xpubs birashobora gutuma ubuzima bwite bw’uwukoresha buhungabana, kuko bituma abandi bantu bakurikirana amafaranga akoreshwa no kubona amafaranga asigaye kuri konti zijanye n’ivyo.

Umuntu wese azi xpubs zawe arashobora rero kubona ama aderesi yose y’ukwakira Wallet yawe, ayo yakoreshwa kera, n’ayo azosohoka muri kazoza.


Ku bakoresha batagira Dojo, gushobora gusohoka kw’ama xpubs yawe biragira ingaruka zibiri zikomeye:



- Ivyo coinjoins ushobora kuba warakoze bihindurwa bitagira akamaro bivuye ku bijanye n’ubuzima bwite ku muntu wese azi xpubs zawe, gutyo ibiceri vyawe biratakaza anonset yose;
- Uwo muntu arashobora kandi gukurikirana aderesi zose zakira Samourai Wallet yawe.


Ni ngombwa rero ko twihweza ikintu kibi kuruta ibindi vyose no gutandukana n’iyi Wallet, ishobora guhungabanywa mu bijanye n’ubuzima bwite. Kugira ngo ubikore, rema Wallet nshasha uhereye ku ntango ukoresheje uwundi murongo, nka Sparrow wallet. Uhejeje kugenzura ko ama backups yawe ari ay’ukuri, ushire amahera yawe yose mu gukora amafaranga. Naho iyo nzira itazoca uruja n’uruza rw’ibiceri vyawe, izotuma abategetsi batamenya ata gukeka amaderesi y’igitabu cawe gishasha ca Wallet.


Mu gihe c’iki gikorwa co kwimurira, ndabagira inama yo kwirinda gushiramwo amafaranga yawe. Nitwafata ko xpubs zawe zishobora guhungabanywa, ugushiramwo ntaco bizogira ku mbonerahamwe y’umuntu afise uburenganzira bwo kuronka izo xpubs, kuko ubuzima bwite bwawe busanzwe buhungabanywa na zo. Ariko ndaguhanura ko utoshiramwo cane ibiceri vyawe ahanini kugira ngo ukinge ubuzima bwite bwawe ku bandi bantu. Mu gihe kibi cane, abategetsi bonyene boshobora gushika ku xpubs zawe, ariko isi yose ntizizi. Gutyo, mu kubona kw’abandi, gushiramwo ibiceri vyawe vyoshobora kwonona cane ubuzima bwite bwawe kubera ubuhinga bwa Common Input Ownership Heuristic (CIOH).


**Iciyumviro:** kugira ngo ushireho ukurikirana, ushobora kandi kwiyumvira gukora coinjoins kuva kuri iyi Wallet nshasha.

**Imburi:** Gukura gusa Samourai Wallet yawe kuri Sparrow wallet ntibihagije. Ni ngombwa gukora Wallet nshasha yose ifise ijambo rishasha ryo gusubirana nimba ushaka kwirinda gukoresha xpubs zishobora kuba zasohotse. Iyo winjije seed yawe isanzwe muri Sparrow, uba uriko urahindura gusa porogarama yo gucunga Wallet, ariko Wallet iguma ari imwe.
