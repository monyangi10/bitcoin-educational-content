## BIP39 passphrase ni iki?


Ama wallet ya HD akenshi akomoka ku majambo Mnemonic agizwe n’amajambo 12 canke 24. Iri jambo rirahambaye cane kuko rituma umuntu ashobora kugarura imfunguruzo zose za Wallet mu gihe umurongo wayo w’umubiri (nk’akarorero Hardware Wallet) woba wazimiye. Ariko rero, ni ikintu kimwe co kunanirwa kuko iyo gishizwe mu kaga, uwugitera yoshobora kwiba ama bitcoins yose.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/01.webp)


Aho niho passphrase yinjira.Ni ijambobanga ry’uburenganzira ushobora guhitamwo mu mwidegemvyo, ryongerwa ku majambo Mnemonic mu nzira y’ugukuraho urufunguzo kugira ngo wongere umutekano wa Wallet.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/02.webp)


Urabe neza ko udashobora kwitiranya passphrase na PIN code ya Hardware Wallet yawe canke ijambobanga ukoresha kugira ngo ufungure uburenganzira bwo gushika kuri Wallet yawe kuri mudasobwa yawe. Udakunze izo Elements zose, passphrase irafise uruhara mu gukuraho imfunguruzo za Wallet yawe. **Ivyo bisigura ko ataco ufise, ntuzokwigera ushobora gusubirana ama bitcoins yawe.**


passphrase ikorana n’ijambo Mnemonic, ihindura seed aho imfunguruzo zikomoka. Gutyo, naho umuntu yoronka amajambo yawe y’amajambo 12 canke 24, ata passphrase, ntashobora kuronka amahera yawe. **Gukoresha passphrase mu vy’ukuri bituma haba Wallet nshasha ifise imfunguruzo zitandukanye. Guhindura (mbere gatoyi) passphrase bizoba generate Wallet itandukanye.**


## Ni kuki wokoresha passphrase?


passphrase ni ubusa kandi ishobora kuba ihuriro ryose ry’inyuguti zitowe n’uwukoresha. Gukoresha passphrase rero biratanga ivyiza vyinshi. Ica mbere, bigabanya ingorane zose zijanye n’ugusenyuka kw’ijambo Mnemonic mu gusaba ikintu ca kabiri kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kuronka ayo mahera (ubusuma, kuronka inzu yawe, n’ibindi).


Igikurikira, gishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo bw'ubuhinga bwo guhingura umutego Wallet, kugira ngo ushobore guhangana n'ingorane zo ku mubiri kugira ngo wibe amahera yawe nk'igitero c'urufunguzo rw'amadolari 5*. Muri iki gihe, iciyumviro ni ukugira Wallet ata passphrase irimwo gusa amafaranga makeyi, ahagije kugira ngo umuntu ashobora gutera, mu gihe afise Wallet yihishije. Iyi ya nyuma ikoresha iryo jambo nyene Mnemonic ariko ikaba icungiwe n'iyindi passphrase.


Ubwa nyuma, gukoresha passphrase biraryoshe iyo umuntu yipfuza kugenzura uburyo butari bwo bw’uruvyaro rwa HD Wallet rwa seed.


## Ni gute wohitamwo passphrase nziza?

Kugira ngo passphrase ikore neza, itegerezwa kuba ndende bihagije kandi ikaba idasanzwe. Nka kurya nyene ku bijanye n’ijambobanga rikomeye, ndabahimiriza guhitamwo passphrase ndende kandi idasanzwe uko bishoboka kwose, ifise inyuguti zitandukanye, imibare n’ibimenyetso kugira ngo igitero cose c’inkomezi z’agahomerabunwa ntigishoboka.


Dukurikije [itohoza ryakozwe na Trezor mu 2019], umuterabwoba afise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha seed yawe kandi akoresha GPU y’agaciro kanini yakodesheje kuri AWS (VVI10ar) yoshobora kugerageza miliyoni200. amajambo y’ibanga y’idolari 1. Nk’uko bigereranywa, n’ubushobozi bwa 2019, passphrase ikozwe mu nyuguti ntoya 12 zidasanzwe yoshobora gutwara mu mpande **amadolari miliyoni 77** kugira ngo imeneke.


Ariko rero, ndaguhanura ko utokwigarukira ku nyuguti 12. Ahubwo ushireho ingingo mfatirwako ziriho ubu z’amajambo y’ibanga akomeye: mu 2025, ushireho nibura inyuguti 13 zidasanzwe harimwo imibare, inyuguti ntoya n’inyuguti nini, n’ibimenyetso; canke inyuguti 14 nimba ukoresha inyuguti ntoya n’inyuguti nini gusa. Mu bisanzwe, ndasaba ko umuntu yoshira imbere, nk’akarorero mu guhitamwo igitabu passphrase c’inyuguti 20 gifise ibimenyetso, kugira ngo umuntu abone imbere y’igihe ivyozoshika no kumenya ingorane zishobora guterwa n’abantu zitarimburwa muri izo nyigisho.


Ni ngombwa kandi gukiza neza iyi passphrase, mu buryo bumwe n’ubw’ijambo Mnemonic. **Kuyitakaza bisigura gutakaza uburenganzira bwo kuronka ama bitcoins yawe**. Ndahanura cane ko utoyifata mu mutwe gusa, kuko ivyo bishobora kwongera ingorane zo gutakaza ata co bimaze. Iciyumviro ciza ni ukuvyandika ku gikoresho c’umubiri (impapuro canke icuma) gitandukanye n’ijambo Mnemonic. Ivyo bimenyetso bitegerezwa kubikwa ahantu hatandukanye n’aho ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic ribikwa kugira ngo vyose ntibishobora guhungabanywa icarimwe.
