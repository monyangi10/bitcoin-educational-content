## Nokwongerako gute passphrase ku SeedSigner?



Kwongera passphrase ku rutonde rwawe rucungiwe biciye ku SeedSigner bituma haba urutoke rushasha rwose, bikazana urutonde rw’imfunguruzo zitandukanye rwose. Ku bw’ivyo, iyo usanzwe ufise igitabu kirimwo satss, ntuzosubira kugishobora kugironka ukoresheje passphrase, kuko kizana igitabu gitandukanye rwose.



Kugira ngo ukoreshe passphrase kuri SeedSigner yawe, fungura igikoresho maze ushireko SeedQR yawe nk’uko bisanzwe. SeedSigner izoca yerekana urutoke rwa wallet yawe y'ubu, ihuye n'iyo **itagira passphrase**. wallet iri kumwe na passphrase izogira urutoke rutandukanye.



Fyonda kuri buto `BIP-39 Ijambobanga`.



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Hanyuma winjize passphrase uhisemwo mu kibanza catanzwe, ukoresheje klavye iri ku rubuga. Raba neza ko ukora backup imwe canke nyinshi z’umubiri (impapuro canke ivyuma): gutakaza iyi passphrase bizokuvamwo gutakaza ubudasiba uburenganzira bwo gushika ku bitcoins zawe. **Kugira ngo usubiremwo wallet, mnemonic na passphrase vyose ni ngombwa ** Iyo kimwe muri vyo kizimiye, bitcoins zawe zizobuzwa ataco zisubira gukora.



Uhejeje kwandika, wemeze ukanda buto ya `KEY3` iri hasi iburyo bwa SeedSigner.



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



*Muri aka karorero, nakoresheje passphrase `pba`. Ariko rero, ku bijanye nawe, nubone neza ko uhisemwo passphrase ikomeye. Kugira umenye ingene wosobanura passphrase nziza cane, urashobora kuraba iyi yindi ngingo:*



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7

SeedSigner rero yerekana urutoke rushasha rwa passphrase wallet yawe. Kora kopi nyinshi z'iki kimenyetso c'urutoke: birahambaye iyo ukoresheje wallet iri kumwe na passphrase, kuko bigufasha kugenzura, igihe cose winjiye muri passphrase, ko ata makosa yo kwandika wakoze kandi ko uriko uronka wallet ibereye.



Nk’akarorero, nimba mu gihe canje nshobora kwandika nabi passphrase `Pba` igihe ntangira SeedSigner aho kwandika `pba`, iyo mpinduka yoroshe kuva ku nyuguti ntoyi gushika ku nyuguti nini izotuma nkora igitabu gitandukanye rwose n’ico nshaka gushikako.



Ico gicapo c'urutoke nta ngorane gitera umutekano canke ibanga rya wallet yawe. Nta makuru yose, aya bose canke ay’ibanga, yerekeye imfunguruzo zawe itangaza. Rero, bitandukanye n’ivyo mnemonic na passphrase, urashobora kubika ikimenyetso c’urutoke ku gikoresho ca digitale. Ndagusavye ko wobika kopi ahantu henshi: ku mpapuro, mu gikoresho co gucungera ijambobanga, n’ibindi.



Umaze kubika urutoke rwawe, kanda `Vyarangiye`.



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Uca ushobora gushika ku bikorwa vyose vy'ibitabo vyawe, nk'uko biri kuri SeedSigner ya kera.



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Ubu ushobora kwinjiza ububiko bw'imfunguruzo muri Sparrow Wallet maze ugakoresha wallet yawe nk'uko bisanzwe. Igihe cose uzosubira gutangura, uzokenera gucapura SeedQR yawe no gusubira kwinjira muri passphrase yawe ukoresheje klavye, nk’uko twabigize hano.



Imbere y’uko ukoresha vy’ukuri wallet yawe na passphrase, ndagusavye cane gukora ikigeragezo co gukira c’ubusa. Ivyo bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo kwemeza ko ama backups yawe y’ijambo ry’ikirundi n’iry’i passphrase ari meza. Kugira ngo umenye ingene wokora iyo nsuzuma, raba inyigisho ikurikira:
