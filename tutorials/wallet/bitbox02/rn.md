BitBox02 (https://bitbox.swiss/) ni Wallet y’umubiri yakozwe n’Abasuwisi yagenewe canecane gukingira amafaranga yawe. Bimwe mu bintu nyamukuru biyigize ni ugushobora gusubiza ibintu mu buryo bworoshe hakoreshejwe ikarita ya microSD, ubuhinga bukeyi kandi buteye igomwe, n’ugushigikira neza Bitcoin.


![device](assets/1.webp)


Itanga umutekano w’ubuhinga bwa none wakozwe n’abahinga, ifise ubuhinga bwo gukora ibice bibiri birimwo n’igice gikingira. Inkomoko yayo yarasuzumwe neza n’abashakashatsi mu vy’umutekano kandi yose ni open-source. BitBox02 izana na BitBoxApp yoroshe ariko ikomeye, itanga uburongozi butekanye bw’ama Bitcoins yawe. Ishigikira Full node kuri Bitcoin kandi igatuma haba uguhanahana amakuru hagati ya porogarama n’igikoresho. BitBox02 yakozwe mu Busuwisi, yararonse izina ryiza mu bayikoresha.


![video](https://youtu.be/sB4b2PbYaj0)


Aha niho hari ibisobanuro vya Wallet:



- Uguhuza: USB-C
- Uguhuza: Windows 7 n'izikurikira, macOS 10.13 n'izikurikira, Linux, Android
- Injiza: Ubushobozi bwo gukora ku mubiri
- Igikoresho co gucungera amakuru: ATSAMD51J20A; 120 Mhz 32-ibice vy’umubiri-M4F; Umubare w'ukuri w'imburakimazi
- Igipande c’umutekano: ATECC608B; Umubare w'ukuri w'imburakimazi (NIST SP 800-90A/B/C)
- Iyerekanwa: 128 x 64 px OLED yera
- Ibikoresho: Polikarbonate
- Ingano: 54,5 x 25,4 x 9,6 mm harimwo n'umugozi wa USB-C
- Uburemere: Igikoresho 12g; n'ibikoresho vyo gupfunyika n'ibikoresho 160g


Ushobora gukuraho amakuru ku rubuga rwabo https://bitbox.swiss/bitbox02/


## Uko wokoresha BitBox02 Hardware Wallet.


### Gutegura BitBox02


BitBox02 ifise uruja n’uruza rwa USB-C rufatanye n’igipfukisho. Nimba mudasobwa yawe ikoresha port USB isanzwe, uzobwirizwa gukoresha adapteur izana n’ico gikoresho.


Uyihuze na mudasobwa yawe, iyo device izoca ifunguka (ntimube n’ubu).


Ifise ibimenyetso hejuru no musi, kandi izogusaba gukora hejuru canke hasi kugira ngo ushire igicapo mu buryo ushaka.

### NTIBIZIHUTA GUKO!!


Ni ibitangaje cane, ariko BitBox02 iriko iratubwira ko twiteguriye gukoresha ico gikoresho, ariko ntiyaduteye kwandika amajambo ya seed! Ivyo dufise vyonyene ni dosiye yabitswe ku ikarita ya microSD. Ivyo ntibihagije. Ivyo bikoresho vyo kubika ntibimara ibihe bidahera (kubera “ukubora kw’ibice”). Turakeneye impapuro zo gucungera, n’ibisubizo, bibitswe mu bibanza vy’amahera (nk’uko vyasiguwe mu gitabu rusangi c’ingene twokoresha ibikoresho-vy’amahera)


Kugira ngo ubone ijambo ryacu rya seed maze uriyandike, genda ku rubuga rwitwa “manage device” ruri ibubamfu, hanyuma ukande kuri “show recover words” .


![image](assets/16.webp)


Ushobora rero guca mu kwemeza, maze ico gikoresho kikakwereka amajambo. Bindike neza, kandi ntukigere ureka ngo umuntu abone amajambo.


![image](assets/17.webp)


Inyuma y’ivyo, urashobora gufyonda ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin ruri ibubamfu kugira ngo ubone amaderesi yawe yo kwakira.


![image](assets/18.webp)


Irerekana imwe imwe, ariko n’imiburiburi iragufasha guhitamwo Address uzokoresha mu 20 za mbere:


![image](assets/19.webp)


Ufyondeye kuri buto y’ubururu bizokwerekana Address yuzuye, kandi uzosabwa gusuzuma ko Address ihuye n’ivyo yerekanwa ku rubuga. Ivyo ni vyiza kugira ngo wemeze ko ata malware iri kuri mudasobwa yawe iriko iraguhenda ngo wohereze Bitcoin kuri Address y’umuterabwoba.


![image](assets/20.webp)


Kugira ngo wohereze Bitcoin kuri iyi Wallet, urashobora gukopa iyo Address ukayishira kuri paji yo gukurako amahera yo kuri Exchange aho amafaranga yawe ari. Ndagusavye ko wobanza kohereza amahera makeyi y’ikigeragezo, hanyuma wimenyereze kuyakoresha ugasubira kuri Exchange canke kuri Address ya kabiri iri muri Wallet yawe.


Ku mahera menshi, ndagusavye gukora passphrase (raba munsi). Wallet y’umwimerere (nta passphrase) ishobora gukoreshwa nk’uruyeri rwawe Wallet (izokenera kugira umubare ubereye muri yo kugira ngo ibe uruyeri rwizerwa).


### Huza n'urudodo


BitBox02 izokwifatanya n'urudodo. Reka turabe aho bifatanya. Fyonda ku rubuga rw’amasetingi ruri ibubamfu, hanyuma “uhuze Full node yawe bwite”.


![image](assets/21.webp)


Kandi aha turashobora kubona ko ihuye n’urudodo rwa shiftcrypto. Si bikomeye. Twarabahemukiye ama aderesi yacu yose ya Bitcoin, na IP yacu Address (si seed birashoboka; barashobora kubona ama aderesi/ama balance yacu, ariko ntibashobora kuyakoresha). Turashobora kwinjiza amakuru yacu bwite muri iyi paji (birenze urugero rw’iyi nkuru), canke dushobora gukoresha porogarama nziza nka Sparrow Bitcoin Wallet, Electrum Desktop Wallet, canke Spectre Desktop. Nzokwerekana Sparrow Bitcoin Wallet mu nyuma mu burongozi.


![image](assets/22.webp)


Wongereko passphrase


Ubu ko twashizeho igikoresho dukoresheje BitBox02 App (kandi tugahishura amaderesi yacu, bidashobora kwirindwa n’iyi Hardware Wallet), turashobora kwongerako passphrase ku nteruro yacu ya seed. Ivyo bizotuma dushobora gukora Wallet nshasha dukoresheje iyo seed nyene, kandi ShiftCrypto ntizokwigera ibona amaderesi yacu mashasha. Tuzoba turiko turahuza iyi Wallet na porogarama yacu gusa.
