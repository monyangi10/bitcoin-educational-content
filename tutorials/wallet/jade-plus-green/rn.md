Jade Plus ni Hardware Wallet ikoreshwa na Bitcoin gusa yakozwe na Blockstream. Ni yo yakurikiye Jade ya kera, ifise ivyiza vy’amaporogarama, amahitamwo menshi n’ubuhinga bushasha bwo gukoresha neza. Iyi verisiyo nshasha irafise igicapo ciza cane c’amasentimetero 1,9, gifise amabara menshi kuruta ayayibanjirije. Amabuto n’uguca mu menyu na vyo nyene vyaratunganijwe neza.


Jade Plus ishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo butari buke: biciye ku nzira y'umurongo wa USB-C, mu buryo bwa "*Air-Gap*" ufise ikarita ya micro SD (adapter irakenewe), biciye ku Bluetooth canke mbere biciye ku guhana amakode ya QR biciye ku kamera ihuriweko. Iyi Hardware Wallet ikoreshwa na bateri.


Iboneka kuva ku madolari 149.99 mu rwego rw'umwirabura rw'ishimikiro, kandi igiciro gishobora kuduga gushika ku madolari 20 ku rwego rwa "*Genesis Grey*" canke "*Lunar Silver*". Jade Plus rero ni ihitamwo riryoshe cane, rifise ubushobozi buteye imbere bugereranywa n’ubw’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none nka Coldcard Q canke Passport V2, ariko kugiciro gito cane, hafi y’ibikoresho vyo hagati.

## Igishushanyo c'umutekano wa Jade Plus


Jade Plus ikoresha uburyo bw'umutekano bushingiye kuri "secure element", ikoreshwa n'"umuvugizi w'impumyi". Mu majambo nyayo, ubu buryo burahuza PIN yatowe n’uwukoresha, ibanga ry’i Jade n’ibanga rifiswe na oracle (server ibungabungwa na Blockstream), kugira ngo haboneke urufunguzo rwa AES-256 rugabanywa ku bigo bibiri. Mu gihe co gutangura, ECDH Exchange iracungera ubutumwa n'umuvugizi, kandi igashiramwo amajambo yo gusubirana kuri Hardware Wallet. Mu majambo ngirakamaro, iyo ushaka gushika ku seed kugira ngo usinye amafaranga, ukeneye gushika ku :




- Ku gikoresho ca Jade Plus ubwaco;
- Ku PIN kugira ngo ufungure igikoresho ;
- Kandi ku banga ry’Imana.


Inyungu nini y’iyi nzira ni ukutagira ikintu na kimwe co kunanirwa ku rugero rw’ibikoresho, kuko iyo uwutera yigeze aronka uburenganzira bwo gushika ku Jade yawe, gukuraho imfunguruzo bisaba guhungabanya Jade n’ivyo bivugwa icarimwe. Iyi nzira kandi isigura ko Jade Plus ari inkomoko yuguruye yose, irinda ingorane zijanye n’ugukoresha Elements y’ukuri itekanye, nk’izo zikoreshwa kuri Ledger, nk’akarorero.


Ikibi c’iyi nzira ni uko gukoresha Jade Plus bivana n’ivyo bita oracle bicungiwe na Blockstream. Iyo iyo oracle idashobora gushikirwa, ntibizosubira gukoresha Hardware Wallet ataco ihinduye kuri PIN. Ariko ivyo ntibisigura ko ama bitcoins yawe yazimiye, kuko aracari ashobora gusubirwamwo ukoresheje ijambo ryawe ryo gusubizwamwo, ushobora kwinjira muri Jade Plus mu buryo bwa "*stateless*". Kugira ngo ushobore guca muri ivyo, urashobora kandi gutunganya no gucunga server yawe bwite ya oracle.

## Gushinga kode ya PIN


Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Continue*" kugira uhitemwo PIN code ya Jade yawe.


![JADE-PLUS-GREEN](assets/fr/24.webp)


Kode ya PIN irafungura Jade yawe. Ni uburinzi rero ku bijanye n’ugushika ku mubiri ata wemerewe. Iyi kode ya PIN ntaco ikora mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwa none za Wallet yawe. Rero, naho utagira uburenganzira bwo kuronka iyo kode ya PIN, kugira ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gusubira kuronka amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri. Turagusavye guhitamwo kode ya PIN ishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo butari bwo. Kandi urabe ko ubika iyi kode ahantu hatandukanye n’aho Jade yawe ibitswe (nk’akarorero mu mucungerezi w’ijambobanga).


Hitamwo kode ya PIN y’imirongo 6 kuri Jade yawe, ukoresheje ubuto bw’iburyo n’ubw’ibubamfu kugira ngo ugende mu mibare, n’ubuto bwo hagati kugira ngo wemeze ko umubare winjiye.


![JADE-PLUS-GREEN](assets/fr/25.webp)


Wemeze PIN yawe ubugira kabiri.


![JADE-PLUS-GREEN](assets/fr/26.webp)

Ihitamwo rya "*Rename*" rigufasha guhindura izina rya Wallet yawe, ivyo bikaba ari ngirakamaro cane iyo ucungera ama wallet menshi kuri porogaramu imwe. "*Unit*" ishobora kugufasha guhindura igice c'ishimikiro ca Wallet yawe. Nk’akarorero, urashobora guhitamwo kuyigaragaza mu satoshis aho kuyigaragaza mu bitcoins. Ubwa nyuma, urutonde rwa "*Parameters*" ruraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ayandi mahitamwo. Aha, nk’akarorero, uzosanga urufunguzo rwawe rwa bose rwagutse na Descriptor yarwo, ni ngirakamaro nimba uriko urategura gushinga Watch-only wallet uvuye kuri Jade yawe.


![JADE-PLUS-GREEN](assets/fr/32.webp)


Kugira ngo wongere wifatanye na Jade yawe umaze kuyizimya, kanda kuri buto yo gufungura/kuzimya iri musi y’ico gikoresho. Ku rupapuro rwa Green, hitamwo igikoresho cawe kuri paji y’intango:


![JADE-PLUS-GREEN](assets/fr/33.webp)


Hanyuma winjize PIN code kuri Jade yawe, uzosubira guhura.


![JADE-PLUS-GREEN](assets/fr/34.webp)


Jade yawe irafungurwa biciye ku "secure element" ya Blockstream (raba igice ca mbere c'iyi nyigisho). Ivyo bisaba ko umuntu akoresha Bluetooth n’iporogarama ya Green. Niwahura n’ingorane ku bijanye n’uguhuza Bluetooth mu gihe co gufungura, gerageza gutandukanya no gusubira gufatanya ivyo bikoresho bibiri. Iyo ingorane igumaho, urashobora gufungura Jade yawe uhisemwo uburyo bwa "*QR Scan*" hanyuma ukurikije amabwirizwa aboneka [ku rubuga rwa Blockstream](https://jadefw.blockstream.com/pinqr/index.html).


Imbere y’uko uronka ama bitcoins yawe ya mbere muri Wallet yawe, **Ndaguhanura cane gukora ikigeragezo co gusubirana ubusa**. Ushire ahabona amakuru amwamwe y’ishingiro, nka xpub yawe canke ubanza kwakira Address, hanyuma usibe Wallet yawe kuri app ya Green no kuri Jade Plus igihe ikiri ubusa (`Amahitamwo -> Igikoresho -> Gusubiramwo uruganda`). Hanyuma ugerageze gusubizaho Wallet yawe ukoresheje impapuro zawe z’inyuma z’ijambo Mnemonic. Suzuma ko amakuru y’ibifungurwa vyasohowe inyuma yo gusubizaho ahuye n’ayo wanditse mu ntango. Nivyashika, urashobora kwizigira yuko amakete yawe y’impapuro ari ay’ukwizigirwa. Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwo gukora ikigeragezo co gukira, usabwe kuraba iyi yindi nyigisho :


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/recovery-test-5a75db51-a6a1-4338-a02a-164a8d91b895
