Jade Plus ni Hardware Wallet ikoreshwa na Bitcoin gusa yakozwe na Blockstream. Ni yo yakurikiye Jade ya kera, ifise ivyiza vy’amaporogarama, amahitamwo menshi n’ubuhinga bushasha bwo gukoresha neza. Iyi verisiyo nshasha irafise igicapo ciza cane c’amasentimetero 1,9, gifise amabara menshi kuruta ayayibanjirije. Amabuto n’uguca mu menyu na vyo nyene vyaratunganijwe neza.


Jade Plus ishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo butari buke: biciye ku nzira y'umurongo wa USB-C, mu buryo bwa "*Air-Gap*" ufise ikarita ya micro SD (adapter irakenewe), biciye ku Bluetooth canke mbere biciye ku guhana amakode ya QR biciye ku kamera ihuriweko. Iyi Hardware Wallet ikoreshwa na bateri.


Iboneka kuva ku madolari 149.99 mu rwego rw'umwirabura rw'ishimikiro, kandi igiciro gishobora kuduga gushika ku madolari 20 ku rwego rwa "*Genesis Grey*" canke "*Lunar Silver*". Jade Plus rero ni ihitamwo riryoshe cane, rifise ubushobozi buteye imbere bugereranywa n’ubw’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none nka Coldcard Q canke Passport V2, ariko kugiciro gito cane, hafi y’ibikoresho vyo hagati.

## Igishushanyo c'umutekano wa Jade Plus


Jade Plus ikoresha uburyo bw'umutekano bushingiye kuri "secure element", ikoreshwa n'"umuvugizi w'impumyi". Mu majambo nyayo, ubu buryo burahuza PIN yatowe n’uwukoresha, ibanga ry’i Jade n’ibanga rifiswe na oracle (server ibungabungwa na Blockstream), kugira ngo haboneke urufunguzo rwa AES-256 rugabanywa ku bigo bibiri. Mu gihe co gutangura, ECDH Exchange iracungera ubutumwa n'umuvugizi, kandi igashiramwo amajambo yo gusubirana kuri Hardware Wallet. Mu majambo ngirakamaro, iyo ushaka gushika ku seed kugira ngo usinye amafaranga, ukeneye gushika ku :




- Ico gikoresho ca Jade Plus ubwaco;
- Ku PIN kugira ngo ufungure igikoresho ;
- Kandi ku banga ry’Imana.


Inyungu nini y’iyi nzira ni ukutagira ikintu na kimwe co kunanirwa ku rugero rw’ibikoresho, kuko iyo uwutera yigeze aronka uburenganzira bwo gushika ku Jade yawe, gukuraho imfunguruzo bisaba guhungabanya Jade n’ivyo bivugwa icarimwe. Iyi nzira kandi isigura ko Jade Plus ari inkomoko yuguruye yose, irinda ingorane zijanye n’ugukoresha Elements y’ukuri itekanye, nk’izo zikoreshwa kuri Ledger, nk’akarorero.


Ikibi c’iyi nzira ni uko gukoresha Jade Plus bivana n’ivyo bita oracle bicungiwe na Blockstream. Iyo iyo oracle idashobora gushikirwa, ntibizosubira gukoresha Hardware Wallet ataco ihinduye kuri PIN. Ariko ivyo ntibisigura ko ama bitcoins yawe yazimiye, kuko aracari ashobora gusubirwamwo ukoresheje ijambo ryawe ryo gusubizwamwo, ushobora kwinjira muri Jade Plus mu buryo bwa "*stateless*". Kugira ngo ushobore guca muri ivyo, urashobora kandi gutunganya no gucunga server yawe bwite ya oracle.


Iyindi nzira yo gucunga seed yawe ni ukutayindika kuri Jade Plus. Muri ivyo, Jade iba igikoresho co gusinya gusa. Mu gihe co gutangura, uretse kubika ijambo ry’ugusubirana nk’amajambo, uzoribika kandi nk’ikode ya QR ikozwe n’ukuboko. Uko niko, igihe cose ukoresheje Wallet yawe, urashobora kwinjiza seed ukoresheje kamera yawe ya Jade. Ivyo bishobora kuba uburyo bushimishije ku bakoresha bateye imbere, bivanye n’ingene ukoresha ubuhinga bwo gucungera umutekano, ariko urakeneye kwiyubara kugira ngo uzigame seed yawe kandi uyikingire, kuko naho yoba ari kode ya QR, yotuma umuntu wese yiba amahera yawe. Turaza kuraba iyo nzira muri iyi nyigisho, ariko si ngombwa.

## Yongerako BIP39 passphrase


BIP39 passphrase ni ijambobanga ry’ubuhinga ushobora guhitamwo mu mwidegemvyo, kandi ryongerwa ku majambo yawe ya Mnemonic kugira ngo ukomeze umutekano wa Wallet. Iyo iyo nzira ikoreshejwe, gushika ku Bitcoin Wallet yawe bizosaba Mnemonic na passphrase. Iyo ata kimwe muri ivyo bibiri, ntivyoshoboka ko umuntu asubirana Wallet.


Imbere yo gutunganya iyi nzira kuri Jade Plus yawe, birakenewe cane ko usoma iyi ngingo kugira ngo utahure neza ingene passphrase ikora kandi wirinde amakosa yoshobora gutuma utakaza amafaranga yawe :


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7

Jade yawe ikiri yuguruye (passphrase ishobora kwinjira gusa iyo igikoresho kitafunguwe), genda kuri menu ya "*Options*".


![Image](assets/fr/42.webp)


Hitamwo "*BIP39 passphrase*".


![Image](assets/fr/43.webp)


Mu nzira ya "*Frequency*", ushobora guhitamwo nimba Jade Plus izogusaba kwinjiza passphrase yawe igihe cose itanguye:




- "*Imugaye*" ibuza gukoresha passphrase;
- "*Next Login Only*" izogusaba gusubira muri iyi menu kugira ngo ukoreshe ubusabe bwa passphrase yawe mu gihe uzotangura. Iryo hitamwo rituma udashobora guhishura uko rikoreshwa;
- "*Always Ask*" bituma Jade akusaba passphrase yawe igihe cose itanguye, gutyo akagaragaza ko Wallet yawe irindwa na passphrase.


Hitamwo uburyo bushingiye ku nzira yawe yo gucungera umutekano. Ku bwanje, ntoranya "*Imisi yose usaba*" ku karorero.


![Image](assets/fr/44.webp)


Ushobora rero guhitamwo hagati y’uburyo bubiri bwo kwinjiza passphrase yawe:




- "*Igitabo*: Igikoresho co gukoresha kigufasha kwinjiza inyuguti (inyuguti nini n'into), imibare n'ibimenyetso, ikimenyetso ku kimenyetso. Ubu ni uburyo busanzwe ku bikoresho vyose vy'amasakoshi;
- "*WordList*": Uburyo bwihariye bwakozwe na Blockstream ku Jade, bwihuta kwinjira passphrase kandi bukongera entropie yayo. Mu gihe co kwinjiza, iyo sisitemu iratanga amajambo yo muri list ya BIP39, ivyo bikaba bituma gufungura vyoroha. Ubwo buryo buca buzana interuro mu gufatanya amajambo yatowe, atandukanijwe n’ibibanza (akarorero: `abandon ability able`).


Ku bwanje, ndaguhanura gukoresha uburyo bwa mbere, kuko ni co kigereranyo uzosanga ku bindi vyose bifasha Wallet.


![Image](assets/fr/45.webp)


Ushobora rero gusubira ku rubuga rw’imbere maze ugafungura Wallet yawe nk’uko bisanzwe, ukoresheje kode yawe ya PIN canke CompactSeedQR yawe (nk’uko bigaragara haruguru). Uzoca usabwa kwinjiza passphrase yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/46.webp)


Injira kuri klavye ya Jade, kandi urabe ko ukora backup imwe canke nyinshi ku bikoresho vy’umubiri (impapuro canke ivyuma). Nk’akarorero, ndiko nkoresha passphrase igoyagoya cane, ariko ukeneye guhitamwo passphrase ikomeye, idasanzwe irimwo ubwoko bwose bw’inyuguti kandi ikaba ndende bihagije (nk’ijambobanga rikomeye).


![Image](assets/fr/47.webp)


Nimba passphrase yawe ifise akamaro, yemeze.


![Image](assets/fr/48.webp)


Urashobora kubona ko amajambo y'ibanga ya BIP39 ashobora guhinduka amajambo y'inyuguti nini n'amakosa yo kwandika. Niwinjiza passphrase itandukanye gatoyi n'iyo watunganijwe mu ntango, Jade ntizotanga raporo y'ikosa ariko izoronka uwundi mugwi w'imfunguruzo z'ibanga zizoba zitari izo muri Wallet yawe ya mbere.


Ni ngombwa rero, igihe uriko uratunganya, gukora akamenyetso k'urutoke rwawe rw'urufunguzo rwa mbere, rushobora kuboneka mu mfuruka y'iburyo hasi y'ibarabara. Nk'akarorero, na passphrase yanje `PBN`, urutoke rwanje rw'urufunguzo ni `3AD1AE65`.


![Image](assets/fr/49.webp)


Igihe cose ufunguye Jade yawe ukoresheje passphrase yawe, urabe ko urutoke rwawe ari rumwe n’urwo winjije mu gihe co gutunganya. Niba ari uko biri, passphrase yawe ni ukuri kandi uriko uronka Bitcoin Wallet ibereye. Niba atarivyo, uri kuri Wallet idakwiriye kandi ukeneye kwongera kugerageza, wiyubara kugira ngo ntukore amakosa yo kwinjiza.


Imbere y’uko uronka ama bitcoins yawe ya mbere muri Wallet yawe, **Ndaguhanura cane gukora ikigeragezo co gusubirana ubusa**. Ushire ahabona amakuru amwe amwe y’ishingiro, nka xpub yawe canke ubanza kwakira Address, hanyuma usibe Wallet yawe kuri Jade Plus igihe ikiri ubusa (`Amahitamwo -> Igikoresho -> Gusubiramwo uruganda`). Hanyuma ugerageze gusubizaho Wallet yawe ukoresheje impapuro zawe z'amajambo Mnemonic na passphrase iyo ari yo yose. Suzuma ko amakuru y’ibifungurwa vyasohowe inyuma yo gusubizaho ahuye n’ayo wanditse mu ntango. Nivyashika, urashobora kwizigira yuko amakete yawe y’impapuro ari ay’ukwizigirwa. Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwo gukora ikigeragezo co gukira, raba iyi yindi nyigisho:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/recovery-test-5a75db51-a6a1-4338-a02a-164a8d91b895

Ufungure Jade Plus yawe, nimba uriko urakoresha imwe, winjize passphrase yawe. Hanyuma ugende kuri "*Amahitamwo*", uhitemwo "*Wallet*", hanyuma ukande kuri "*Sohora Xpub*".


![Image](assets/fr/55.webp)


Jade yawe izokwerekana Keystore yawe biciye ku ma code menshi ya QR. Bicapura ku mashine yawe ukoresheje Sparrow.


![Image](assets/fr/56.webp)


Ubu rero ushobora kubona xpub yawe n’urutoke rwawe rwa master key, rukwiye guhura n’urwo kuri Jade Plus yawe. Fyonda kuri "*Shiraho*".


![Image](assets/fr/57.webp)


Gushinga ijambobanga rikomeye kugira ngo ushobore gushika kuri Sparrow wallet yawe. Iri jambobanga rizokingira imfunguruzo zawe za bose, amaderesi, ibimenyetso n’amateka y’ibikorwa vyawe kugira ngo ntihagire uwushobora kuyironka ata wemerewe. Ni vyiza kubika iri jambobanga mu mucungerezi w'ijambobanga, kugira ngo ntuvyibagire.


![Image](assets/fr/58.webp)


Wallet yawe ubu iratunganijwe neza kuri Sparrow.


![Image](assets/fr/59.webp)


## Kwakira ama bitcoins


Ubu Jade Plus yawe yatunganijwe, witeguriye kwakira Sats yawe ya mbere kuri Bitcoin Wallet yawe nshasha. Kugira ngo ubikore, kuri Sparrow, fyonda ku rutonde rwa "*Receive*".


![Image](assets/fr/60.webp)


Sparrow izokwerekana Address ya mbere mu Wallet yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/61.webp)


Imbere yo kuyikoresha, reka tuyisuzume ku gicapo ca Jade Plus kugira ngo twiyemeze neza ko ari iya Bitcoin Wallet yacu. Ku Jade, fyonda ku "*Scan QR*", hanyuma ushireko kode ya QR ya Address yerekanwa kuri Sparrow.


![Image](assets/fr/62.webp)


Suzuma ko Address yerekanwa ku gicapo ca Jade yawe ihuye n'iyo yerekanwa kuri Sparrow wallet. Nivyashika, fyonda ku kimenyetso kugira ngo ukomeze.


![Image](assets/fr/63.webp)


Hardware Wallet yawe izoca yemeza ko iyo Address ari igice ca Wallet yawe kandi ko ifise urufunguzo rw’ibanga rujanye n’iyo Address.


![Image](assets/fr/64.webp)


Niba Address yemejwe na Jade yawe, ubu urashobora kuyikoresha kugira ngo uronke ama bitcoins. Igihe iyo nzira izoba itangajwe ku rubuga, izoboneka kuri Sparrow. Rindira gushika ubonye ivyemezo bihagije kugira ngo ubone ko iyo nzira ari iyo gucuruza.


![Image](assets/fr/65.webp)


## Wohereze ama bitcoins


Ubu ko ufise Sats nkeyi muri Wallet yawe, urashobora no kuzirungika. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku rutonde rwa "*UTXOs*".


![Image](assets/fr/66.webp)


Hitamwo UTXOs wipfuza gukoresha nk'inyungu z'iyi nzira, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ohereza Ivyatowe*".


![Image](assets/fr/67.webp)


Injira Address y'uwaronse, ikimenyetso kikwibutsa intumbero y'ugucuruza n'amahera wipfuza kohereza kuri iyo Address.


![Image](assets/fr/68.webp)


Guhindura igiciro c'amafaranga bivanye n'ingene isoko riri, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Create Transaction*".


![Image](assets/fr/69.webp)


Suzuma ko amaparametere yose y'ibikorwa ari ukuri, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Sozera ibikorwa kugira ngo usinye*".


![Image](assets/fr/70.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Erekana QR*" kugira ngo ubone igitabu ca PSBT (*Igitabu ca Partially Signed Bitcoin Transaction*). Sparrow yubatse iyo nzira, ariko iracari n’imikono yo gufungura ama bitcoins akoreshwa mu kwinjiza. Ivyo bimenyetso bishobora gukorwa gusa na Jade Plus, ishobora kwakira seed yawe itanga uburenganzira bwo gukoresha imfunguruzo z’ibanga zikenewe kugira ngo usinye iyo nzira.


![Image](assets/fr/71.webp)


Ku Jade Plus yawe, fyonda kuri "*Scan QR*" kugira ngo ushireko PSBT yerekanwa kuri Sparrow.


![Image](assets/fr/72.webp)


Wemeze ko Address yoherezwa n’amahera yoherejwe ari ukuri, hanyuma ukande ku mwampi kugira ngo wemeze.


![Image](assets/fr/73.webp)


Raba neza ko amahera ari yo wahisemwo, hanyuma ukande ku kimenyetso kiri hejuru ibubamfu c’igitabu ca Interface kugira ngo ushire umukono ku giciro.


![Image](assets/fr/74.webp)


Ku Sparrow wallet, ukande kuri "*Scan QR*" hanyuma ushireko kode ya QR yerekanwa kuri Jade yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/75.webp)


Ivyo mwashizeko umukono ubu biteguye gutangazwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin kandi bishizwe mu gice ca Miner. Niba vyose bimeze neza, fyonda ku "*Itangazo ry'Itangazamakuru*".


![Image](assets/fr/76.webp)


Ivyo mwaguriye vyaratangajwe kandi birindiriye kwemezwa.


![Image](assets/fr/77.webp)
