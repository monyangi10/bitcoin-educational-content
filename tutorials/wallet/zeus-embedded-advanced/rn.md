## Intangamarara ya ZEU Wallet.


ZEUS ni porogaramu yo gucunga Bitcoin Wallet n’ama node ifise ubushobozi bwose bwa Bitcoin lightning Wallet ituma kwishura Bitcoin vyoroha, iha abakoresha ububasha bwose bwo kugenzura amafaranga yabo, kandi igatuma abakoresha bateye imbere cane bashobora gucunga node zabo za Lightning bari mu kiganza cabo.


### None ZEUS ni uwa nde?


Ubu ZEUS ni iy’abantu bafise [Lightning Network Daemon (LND)](https://umuravyo.ubuhinga/) canke [umuravyo w’umuravyo w’ishimikiro (CLN)](https://blockstream.com/lightning/) node zo mu nzu / z’ubucuruzi kandi bakazicungera biciye kuri Zeus, bari kure.


Abacuruzi bakoresha [BTCPay](https://btcpayserver.org/) canke [LNBits](https://getalby.com/) canke [Alby](https://getalby.com/) (canke iyindi konti yose ya LNDhub) na bo nyene barashobora kwifatanya, gukoresha, no gucunga ama node / konti zabo bavuye kuri ZEUS.


[Guhera muri v0.8](https://blog.zeusln.com/zeus-v0-8-0-open-beta/), ZEUS izotangura gufasha abakoresha basanzwe bashaka gusa uburyo bworoshe bwo kwishura Bitcoin vyihuta kandi bihendutse bakoresheje igikoresho cabo c’amaboko mu kugira [L node](https://docs.zeusln.app/urutonde/node-ishizwemwo) n'[Umutanga Serivisi y'Umuravyo (LSP)](https://docs.zeusln.app/lsp/intangamarara).

## Inyigisho y'ingene wotangura gukoresha Zeus LN ku gikoresho cawe co kuri telefone ngendanwa


![Image](assets/en/01.webp)


Iyi nkuru ndayishikanira abo bose bashasha bakoresha Lightning Network (LN) bipfuza gutangura urugendo rushasha rw’ubusegaba bakoresheje urudodo rwo kwizigama Wallet ku bikoresho vyabo vy’amaboko.


Reka twiyumvire ko umaze guca muri ivyo vyose vy’amasakoshi y’ububiko bwa LN, ariko ntukiri witeguye gutangura gukoresha uruzitiro rwa LN, ushaka gusa guteranya Sats nyinshi kuri LN mu buryo bwo kwizigama maze ugatanga amahera yawe asanzwe kuri G2W.


Aha niho haje Zeus, atangura na [verisiyo v0.8.0 yatangajwe ku rubuga rwabo](https://blog.zeusln.com/zeus-v0-8-0/), ariko aratanga ubu urudodo rwa LND rwinjijwe muri porogarama. Kugeza ubu Zeus yari app yo gucunga node kure + amakonti ya LNDhub. Ariko ubu... iyo node iri muri telefone!


![Image](assets/en/02.webp)


### Ivyiyumviro vyihuse vy'ibiranga Zeus Node:



- Private LND node** - Ivyo bisigura ko iyi node ITAZOKORA inzira ya bose y'abandi kwishura biciye ku node yawe. Ico gice n’imirongo ntibimenyeshwa (ivy’ibanga, ntibiboneka ku gicapo ca LN ca bose). Kugira ngo uronke no kwishura bizokorwa biciye ku bagenzi bawe ba LSP bahuye. IBUKE: Zeus Embedded Node NTIZOKORA inzira ya bose!
- Serivisi ya LND igumaho** - umukoresha arashobora gukoresha iki gikoresho no kuguma akoresha serivisi ya LND ubudasiba nk'uko bigenda ku nzira yose ya LN. App ntibwirizwa kuba yuguruye, service igumaho izotuma ubutumwa bwose buguma kuri internet.
- Ivyunyuro vy’amabuye ya Neutrino** - gukorana n’amabuye bikorwa hakoreshejwe [ibiyunguruzo vy’amabuye n’umurongo wa Neutrino](https://bitcoinops.org/ru/ingingo/iyunguruzo-ry’amabuye/) (nta makuru yatanzwe yerekeye amahera y’abakoresha bacu ya On-Chain). Iciyumviro: ku bijanye n’amahuza ya internet agenda buhoro, iyi block sync ishingiye kuri neutrino, rimwe na rimwe yoshobora kunanirwa. Kugerageza guhindukira ukaja ku server ya neutrino iri hafi vyoshobora gufasha kugarura sync. Iyo ata n’iyi sync node yawe ya LND ntishobora gutangura!
- Imirongo yoroshe ya Taproot** - Iyo bafunze iyo mirongo, abakoresha bararonka amahera make kandi bagahabwa ubuzima bwite bwinshi kuko basa n’abakunda ibindi vyose Taproot bakoresha igihe basuzuma ikirenge cabo ca On-Chain.
- LSP** - Olympus ni urudodo rushasha rwa LSP rwa Zewu. Abakoresha barashobora gusubira kwakira Sats kuri LN ubwo nyene, batabanje gushinga imirongo ya LN. Gusa uzobwirizwa gukora LN Invoice no kwishura ku yindi LN Wallet yose, n’umurongo wa Zeus 0-conf. Soma ibindi ku vyerekeye Zewu LSP hano. LSP kandi itanga ubuzima bwite bwongerewe ku bakoresha bacu mu kubaha amafagitire azingiwe ahisha imfunguruzo za bose z’ibihimba vyabo ku bariha.
- Igitabo c'abantu** - ushobora kubika abantu n'amaboko canke ukabizana muri NOSTR, kugira ngo ushobore kohereza amahera aho uhora uja.
- Infashanyo yuzuye ya LNURL, LN Address kohereza no kwakira** - ubu urashobora gushinga LN Address yawe bwite ukoresheje @zeuspay.com. Iciyumviro: Ushobora kandi gukoresha Zeus for LN-auth ku mbuga aho ushobora kwinjira ukoresheje ivyemezo vya LN. Ni n’amaboko cane.
- Point of Sale** - Ubu abakoresha b’ubudandaji barashobora gushinga ibintu vyabo vy’ibicuruzwa maze bakabigurisha bavuye kuri Zeus, bafise PoS yunze ubumwe. Kugeza ubu harimwo ivya nkenerwa vy’ishimikiro ariko muri kazoza hazoba harimwo ibintu vyinshi.
- LND logs** - umukoresha ashobora gusoma mu gihe nyaco LND service logs maze akayikoresha mu gukosora ibibazo bishobora kubaho (ahanini ku mahuriro mabi)
- Ivyiyumviro vy'ubuhinga bwa none** - imirongo y'inzira ya LN ihita ikora ivy'ubuhinga bwa node kuri server ya Olympus. Ivyo bihimba vy’umubiri bikoreshwa n’urudodo rwawe Wallet seed (ata seed ntaco bimaze). Uwukoresha kandi arashobora kwohereza hanze n’amaboko SCB (ububiko bw’imirongo idahinduka) kugira ngo asubire mu buryo bw’ivyago.


### Uko wokwinjira mu bwato ukoresheje urudodo rwa Zewu LN (LND ishizwemwo)


Muri iyi nkuru nzovuga gusa ku bijanye n’uruzitiro rwa LND rwinjijwemwo, ntavuga ku bundi buryo bwo gukoresha iyi porogarama nziza cane (ugucungera uruzitiro rwo kure n’amakonti ya LNDhub). Ku bindi bwoko bw’amahuza, usabwe kuraba [Urupapuro rw’Inyandiko za Zewu](https://docs.zeusln.app/category/gutangura), ivyo birasiguwe neza cane kandi ntibikenewe kwandika uburongozi bwihariye.


#### INTAMBWE YA 1 - GUTEGURA


Kubera ko Zewu ari urudodo rwuzuye rwa LND nzogira impanuro zimwe zimwe z’intango:



- Ntukoreshe igikoresho ca kera, coshobora gutuma udakoresha iyi porogarama ikomeye. Cane cane mu gihe c’ugukorana (sync) app yoshobora gukoresha cane CPU na RAM. Kubura ivyo vyoshobora mbere gutuma umuntu adashobora gukoresha porogarama ya Zeus.
- Koresha nibura Android 11 nk’OS y’amatelefone ngendanwa kandi uyihindure uko bishoboka kwose. Ku bijanye na iOS nyene, gerageza gukoresha verisiyo ya OS iri hejuru cane.
- Uzokenera nibura 1GB y’umwanya kuri disk kugira ngo ubike amakuru. Mu gihe bishobora gukura cane, ariko hariho uburyo bwo gukoranya urutonde rw'amakuru ku rugero rwa MBs.
- NTA nkenerwa yo gukoresha Zeus na Tor canke Orbot. Ndabinginze ntimugoranye ibintu kuruta uko bikenewe. Tor muri iki gihe ntizoguha ubuzima bwite bwinshi ahubwo izotuma ibintu bikomera cane ku bijanye n’ugukorana kw’intango. Kandi urabe neza VPNs uriko urakoresha kandi usuzume latency y’ihuriro ryerekeye ku ma server ya neutrino. Zirikana, Neutrino block filter ntivuza canke ngo ikurikirane akaranga k’igikoresho cawe, ni amabuye gusa. Ivyo bikoresho vya LN navyo biri inyuma ya LSP ifise imihora y’ibanga rero amakuru make cane arasohoka, nta mpamvu yo guhagarika umutima ku bijanye n’ubuzima bwite.
- Ugire ukwihangana ku bijanye n’ugukorana kw’intango, bishobora gutwara iminota myinshi. Gerageza gukorana na internet y’umurongo wagutse ufise latency nziza. Iyo ukoresheje node yawe bwite ya Bitcoin, [ushobora gukoresha umurimo wa neutrino](https://docs.umuravyo.ubuhinga/ibikoresho-vy’uruja n’uruza rw’umuyagankuba/LND/gushoboza-uburyo-bw’umuyagankuba-mu-Bitcoin-core) maze ugahuza Zeus yawe n’umurongo wawe bwite, mbere ukoresheje maxipe LAN yo mu mutima.


Uhejeje gushinga ubwoko bw’ihuriro “Embedded node” app izotangura gukorana igihe gito. Rindira wihanganye kugira ngo urangize ico gice, hanyuma winjire kuri paji nyamukuru y’Ivyagezwe.


![Image](assets/en/03.webp)

**1 - Umutanga Serivisi y'Imiravyo (LSP)**


Aha niho hari ibikorwa bibiri vya LSP:



- _Just in time channels_ - iyo udafise umurongo ufunguye canke amahera yinjira, iyo service ikoreshejwe izogufungurira umurongo on-the-fly. Iyi nzira ishobora gukurwaho nimba udashaka gufungura imirongo myinshi y'ubwo bwoko.
- _Saba imirongo imbere y'igihe_ - ​​ushobora kugura imirongo yinjira muri Olympus LSP ataco uhinduye muri app ufise amahitamwo menshi n'amahera (y'injira n'isohoka).


LSP ifasha guhuza abakoresha na Lightning Network mu gufungura inzira zo kwishura ku nzira zabo. [Soma ibindi ku vyerekeye LSP hano](https://ubuhinga-bw’umuyaga/gushushanya-lsps-mu-butunzi-bw’umuravyo-832b45871992). ZEUS ifise LSP nshasha yinjijwe muri yo yitwa [OLYMPUS na ZEUS](https://Mempool.ikibanza/umuravyo/node/031b301307574bbe9b9ac7b79cbe1700e31e544513eae0b5d749748930) ni uwushobora gukoresha bose. n'urudodo rushasha rwinjijwe.


Muri iki gice, ku buryo busanzwe ni LSP ya Olympus (https://0conf.lnolymp.us), ariko vuba ushobora no gushinga iyindi LSP 0conf ishigikira iyi porotokole.


_Muzirikane:_

_iyo ufunguye umurongo na Olympus LSP ukoresheje ama factures ya LN azingiwe uronka kandi amafaranga yinjira 100k ! Iyi ni inzira nziza cane mu gihe woba ukeneye kwakira ubwo nyene Sats nyinshi._

_Akarorero: ushiramwo 400k Sats kugira ngo ufungure umurongo wa LSP, hanyuma LSP ikaba ifungura umurongo w'ubushobozi bwa 500k Sats yerekeza ku node yawe ya Zeus hanyuma ugasunika 400k Sats ushiramwo yerekeza ku ruhande rwawe._

_"Ivy'amahera yinjira" = “ikibanza” kinini mu muhora wawe co kwakira._


Mu gihe kizoza twizigiye ko twoshobora kugira izindi LSP nyinshi zishobora gushirwa muri Zewu maze tugakoresha mu buryo bundi buri imwe yose. Ni ikibazo c'igihe gusa gushika LSPs nshasha zizokwemera urugero rwuguruye rw'ubwo bwoko bw'imirongo ya 0conf.


Nimba udashaka gufungura imirongo mishasha “ku nzira”, woshobora guhagarika iyo nzira.


Muri iki gice nyene, urafise kandi uburenganzira bwo guhitamwo “gusaba imirongo yoroshe ya Taproot” igihe LSP izofungura umurongo uja ku nzira yawe ya Zeus. Iyi mirongo yoroshe ya Taproot itanga ubuzima bwite bwiza bwa On-Chain n’amahera make ku gufunga imirongo. Hari impamvu zibiri gusa zotuma udashaka kubikoresha:



- Ni bishasha, kandi hashobora kuba hakiriho ibikoko muri LND iyo ubikoresha.
- Mugenzi wawe ntaco abashigikiye. Mbere n’ibihimba vya LND bitegerezwa kubihitamwo mu buryo butomoye, ubu.


**2 - Ukwishura**


Iyi nzira izoguha uburyo bwo gushinga amafaranga wipfuza yo kwishura, hejuru ya LN canke onchain. Kandi ushireho uburyo bwo kwongera canke kugabanya igihe c’uguhera kw’amafagitire yawe.


Iyo bimwe mu vyo utanga vya LN bitashoboka, woshobora kwongera amahera kugira uronke inzira nziza. Kandi nimba uriko urakora onchain txs, urashobora gushinga amahera yihariye kugira ngo tx yawe ntishobore guhera mu Mempool igihe kirekire, mu gihe c’amahera menshi.


**3 - Amafagitire**


Muri iki gice hariho uburyo bumwe bumwe bwo gutanga amafagitire ya generate:



- Gushinga inyandiko isanzwe izogaragazwa muri Invoice wewe generate
- Igihe co guhera mu masegonda, mu gihe woba ushaka igihe kinaka, kirekire canke kigufi kugira ngo Invoice yawe irishwe
- Nimushiremwo impanuro z’inzira - mutange amakuru yo kuronka imihora itamenyekanishijwe, canke y’ibanga. Ivyo bituma amafaranga yishurwa arungikwa ku bice bitaboneka ku mugaragaro ku rubuga. Impanuro y'inzira itanga inzira y'igice hagati y'uruzitiro rw'ibanga rw'uwakira n'uruzitiro rwa bose. Ico ciyumviro c’inzira gica gishirwa muri Invoice ivugwa n’uwuyironka maze igahabwa uwuyitanga. Ndasaba ko yoshobora gukoreshwa ku buryo busanzwe, ahandi ho amahera yinjira yoshobora kunanirwa (nta nzira yabonetse).
- AMP Invoice - Ivyishyurwa vy’inzira nyinshi vy’atome ni ubwoko bushasha bw’ivyishyurwa vy’umuravyo bishirwa mu ngiro na LND bishobora gutuma umuntu ashobora kwakira Sats ata Invoice yihariye, akoresheje [Keysend] Ni mu vy’ukuri kode y’ukwishura idahinduka. [Soma ibindi hano](https://docs.umuravyo.ubuhinga/ibikoresho-vy’uruja n’uruza rw’umuravyo/LND/amp).
- Erekana umwanya w'ishusho y'imbere - koresha iyi nzira gusa mu bihe vyihariye iyo ushaka vy'ukuri gukoresha umwanya w'ishusho y'imbere. [Soma ibindi hano](https://Bitcoin.stackexchange.com/ibibazo/90797/ni gute-noshobora-generate-ishusho-imbere-y’urubuga-rw’umuravyo-Invoice-ikwiye-i).


Iyindi nzira muri iki gice ni ingene woshiraho ubwoko bw'uruzitiro Address ushaka gukoresha: SegWit iteye, SegWit, Taproot.


![Image](assets/en/04.webp)


Fyonda kuri buto y'uruziga rwo hejuru maze igicapo kizoboneka kugira uhitemwo ubwoko wipfuza bwa Address. Iyo umaze gushinga ivyo, igihe kizoza ukanda buto yakira kuri onchain, izoca generate ubwoko bwa Address bwatowe. Ushobora kubihindura igihe cose.


**4 - Amagenamiterere y'imirongo**


Muri iki gice ubanza gushinga ibiranga imirongo y'ugufungura nka:



- umubare w'ivyemezo
- Itangazo ry'umurongo (ku mburabuzi rizimye), ivyo bisigura ko bizoba ari umurongo utamenyeshejwe
- Imirongo yoroshe ya Taproot
- Erekana ubuto bwo kugura umurongo


**5 - Amagenamiterere y'ubuzima bwite**


Aha uzosanga amasetingi y'ishimikiro yo kwongerako ubuzima bwite ukoresheje app ya Zeus:



- Block explorer kugira ngo ufungure ibisobanuro vya tx (Mempool.ikibanza, amakuru canke amakuru y'umuntu ku giti ciwe)
- Soma urupapuro - gufungura/kuzima nimba ushaka ko Zeus asoma urupapuro rw'ibikoresho vyawe
- Uburyo bwo kwihisha - gufungura/gufunga niba ushaka guhisha amakuru yihariye y'agaciro muri porogaramu yawe ya Zeus. Ni uburyo bwiza iyo ukora ama demo canke amafoto.
- Iciyumviro c'amafaranga ya Mempool - fungura iyi nzira niba ushaka gukoresha ingero z'amafaranga zisabwa kuva kuri [Mempool.ikibanza](https://Mempool.ikibanza/)


**6 - Umutekano**


Iki gice gifise uburyo bubiri gusa bwo gukingira porogarama igihe ufungura: gushinga ijambobanga canke PIN.


Iyo umaze gushinga PIN kugira ngo ufungure app, urashobora kandi gushinga “PIN y’uguhatira”. Iyi PIN y’inyongera y’ibanga izokoreshwa GUSA mu gihe habaye ikintu c’uguhatirwa, iyo uteye ubwoba. Niwashiramwo iyi PIN, ivy’ugutunganya vyose bizoba WIPE OUT. Rero ni vyiza ko uguma uhindura amakuru yawe. Ivyiyumviro vy’ubuhinga bwa none bira ON ku buryo busanzwe, ariko ni vyiza kugira ivy’ubuhinga bwawe bwite, hanze y’igikoresho.


**7 - Amafaranga**


Gushoboza canke guhagarika uburyo bwo kwerekana uguhindura amafaranga y'amahera mu gukoresha porogaramu ya Zeus. Ubu ariko arashigikira amafaranga arenga 30 kw’isi yose.


**8 - Ururimi**


Ushobora guhindura hagati y’indimi nyinshi z’ubuhinduzi, zisubirwamwo n’umuryango wa Zewu n’abavuga ururimi kavukire.


**9 - Kwerekana**


Muri iki gice ushobora guhindura ikigaragaza cawe ca Zeus, ugahitamwo insanganyamatsiko z’amabara atandukanye, igicapo mburabuzi (urufunguzo canke uburinganire), kwerekana izina ryawe ry’urudodo, gukoresha amabuto manini y’urufunguzo, kwerekana ibindi bibanza vy’icumi.


**10 - Ikibanza co kugurisha**


Ivyo ni ikintu kidasanzwe co gushoboza / guhagarika ubuhinga bwa PoS bushizwe muri Zeus. Ushobora gukoresha PoS yigenga canke ihuye n’uburyo bwa Square PoS. Ubu iriko irashigikira ibikorwa vy’ishimikiro nka PoS, ariko bihagije kugira ngo umuntu atangure neza kandi yoshobora gufasha abo bacuruzi batobato (amabarabara/amaresitora, amaduka y’ibifungurwa) gutangura kwemera BTC mu buryo bw’akavukire.


Muri aya ma settings, uzosangamwo uburyo butandukanye bwo gutegura PoS yawe:



- Ubwoko bw'ukwishyura kwemeza: LN gusa, 0-conf, 1-conf
- Gushoboza / guhagarika impanuro ku bakozi bakoresha PoS
- Erekana / uhishe urufunguzo
- Ijanisha ry'ikori ryo gusaba ku tike
- Rema ibicuruzwa n'ivyiciro vy'ibicuruzwa
- Urutonde rworoshe rw'ivyo bagurisha vyose


Aha niho hari amasanamu yerekana ingene ukoresha Zeus PoS:


**B - Gusubiza inyuma Wallet**


Igikoresho gishizwe muri ZEUS gishingiye kuri LND kandi gikoresha [uburyo bwa seed bwa aezeed]. Ivyo bitandukanye n’ivyo ubona mu bipapuro vyinshi vya Bitcoin, naho nyene bishobora gusa n’ivyo. Aezeed irimwo amakuru y’inyongera harimwo n’itariki y’amavuko ya Wallet azofasha gusubira gupima mu gihe co gukira gushika neza.


Uburyo bw’urufunguzo rwa aezeed bukwiye guhura n’ibi bikoresho vy’amafaranga bigendagenda: Blixt, BlueWallet na Breez. Zirikana ko seed yonyene itazoba ihagije kugira ngo ubone amafaranga yawe yose nimba ufise imirongo y'ugufunga yuguruye canke irindiriye !


Menya vyinshi ku bijanye n’uburyo bwo gusubiza inyuma no gusubizaho kuri [urupapuro rwa Zeus Docs](https://docs.zeusln.app/ku-bakoresha/uruzitiro-rushizwemwo/gusubizaho-no-gusubizaho).


IMPANURO Z'UBUSHOBOZI: Iyo uzigamye seed yawe, ndagusavye ubike kandi node pubkey! Rimwe na rimwe ni vyiza ko uyigira hafi, hamwe na seed yawe na SCB (Static Channels Backup) mu gihe woba ukeneye kugenzura ko igarutse.

Rero uzirikane, iyo ukora amafagitire yo kwakira, urabe kandi mu mirongo yawe y’amahera maze ukureho amahera y’aho uba kuri ayo mahera, nimba ushaka gusunika ku rugero amahera akwiye kwakirwa.


### Impanuro yihuta ku bakoresha bashasha batangura na Zeus node:



- Fata neza imirongo yawe mishasha.


Nk’akarorero, nimba uzi ko uzoronka mu ndwi imwe reka tuvuge ko 1M Sats, ufungure umurongo wa 2M Sats maze uhindure hanze mu onchain Wallet canke mu yindi konti (y’igihe gito) y’ububiko bwa LN 50-60% vy’amahera yawe yo gusohoka. Imisi yose niwitegure n’amahera menshi. Igihe ukeneye amahera menshi yo gusubira mu mihora yawe ya Zeus, urashobora kuyasubiza mu makonti ya bubiko.


Niba uzi ko uzorungika reka tuvuge 500k Sats/week, rero ufungure umurongo wa 1M Sats. Muri ubwo buryo uzoba ugifise ububiko gushika usubiye kubuzuza.



- Nimba uri umucuruzi kandi uzokwama uronka amahera menshi kuruta ayo ukoresha ubudasiba, gura umurongo wihariye wo kwinjira. Ni inzira ihendutse kuruta izindi. Urishura amahera makeyi cane ukaronka umurongo "ubusa".



- Ntufungure imirongo mito mito itagira insiguro y'amashanyarazi 50-100-300-500k Sats. Uzozizuza mu misi mikeyi, naho wozikoresha gusa ku zaps. Gufungura binini n’ibindi bitandukanye, NTIMUZE umurongo umwe gusa.


Iyo umaze gufungura umurongo munini, urashobora kwama ukoresha ama swaps yo hanze yo mu mazi kugira ngo ushire Sats mu bikoresho vyawe vy’uruzitiro (harimwo no gusubira kuri Zeus onchain). Kuguma ufise uburinganire hagati y’amahera yinjira n’asohoka ni vyiza kandi urashobora “gusubira gukoresha” izo Sats kugira ngo ufungure imihora myinshi nimba ubishaka.


### Invoice yazingiwe


Nimba ushaka kwongerako ubuzima bwite igihe uzoronka, woshobora gukoresha uburyo bwa “wrapped Invoice.” Iciyumviro: kugira ngo ushobore kubikora, ukeneye umurongo ufise Olympus LSP. Inyemezabuguzi zizingirijwe zizo “nyegeza” aho uzoshika (urudodo rwawe rwa Zeus) kandi zigaragaze urudodo rwawe rwa LSP nk’aho uzoja ku wuriha.


Kugira ngo uronke Invoice izingiwe, genda ku rubuga rwa keypad nyamukuru, ushireko umubare maze ukande request. Izokwerekana kode ya QR isanzwe ya Invoice yawe. Ubu, fyonda ku buto “X” iri hejuru iburyo maze uzoja ku yindi mahitamwo ya Invoice.


![Image](assets/en/14.webp)


Ubu uzobwirizwa gukoresha iyo nzira iri hejuru “Enable LSP” hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya “Create Invoice”. Iryo hitamwo rizotuma haba Invoice izingiwe kandi wibuke, rizosaba amahera makeyi.


### Inyemezabuguzi zifise ivyiyumviro vy'inzira


Ivyo ni ikintu c’ingirakamaro cane nimba ushaka gucunga amafaranga menshi yinjira. Mu vy'ukuri ushobora kwerekana umurongo winjira ushaka kwakira Sats uvuye kuri Invoice.


Ivyo bishobora gukoreshwa no mu gusubiramwo uburinganire bw’uruziga, igihe ushaka gukura amahera mu muhora umwe wuzuye uja ku wundi ushitse.


Ni gute wokora Invoice ifise ivyiyumviro vy’inzira?



- Ku gicapo nyamukuru, genda iburyo igikapu ca LN hanyuma ukande kuri "Receive".
- Mu gutegura Invoice, ushitse ku gice co hasi maze ukoreshe buto "Injiza ivyiyumviro vy'inzira", hanyuma uhitemwo "Custom" tab. Bizogufungurira igicapo kirimwo imirongo yawe yose iriho. Hitamwo iyo ushaka kwakira.
- Uzuza ibindi vyose bisobanuro vya Invoice, umubare, inyandiko n'ibindi hanyuma ukande kuri "rema Invoice".
- Kwishura iyo Invoice bizozana Sats mu muhora werekanwa.


Niba ushaka kwishura iyo Invoice (circular rebalancing), iyo uyihevye ukoresheje node yawe nyene ya Zeus, mu gicapo co kwishura, uhitemwo umurongo wo gusohoka (umwe ufise amahera menshi) ushaka ko ukoreshwa nk’ugutuma kwishura.


### Ishura na Keysend


Keysend ni ikintu ca LN gifatwa nk’ikidahambaye cane kandi abakoresha bakwiye kugikoresha kenshi.


[Keysend](https://docs.lightning.engineering/ibikoresho-vy'urubuga-gW-214/LND/kohereza-ubutumwa-na-Keysend) iremesha abakoresha muri Lightning Network kohereza amahera ku bandi , ataco baciye ku rufunguzo rwabo rwa bose, igihe cose node yabo ifise umurongo wa bose kandi G31. Keysend ntisaba uwuyihembwa gutanga Invoice.


None ivyo wobikora gute na Zewu?


Gusa scanner canke copy nodeID y’aho uja (canke ukoreshe ama contacts ya Zeus kugira ngo ubike ama nodes yawe asanzwe y’aho uja nk’ama contacts) hanyuma uvuye ku rubuga rwa Zeus, ukande kuri “Send” buto. Muri iyo skrini rero ushiremwo nodeID canke uhitemwo mu bantu bawe.


Shira umubare wa Sats, ubutumwa nimba bikenewe (egome, urashobora kubukoresha kandi nk’ikiganiro c’ibanga kuri LN) hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya “Send”. Vyakozwe!


![Image](assets/en/15.webp)


Niba ufise umurongo ushitse n’uwo mugenzi wawe, NTA mahera azoba ariko aratanga.


Niba udafise umurongo utaziguye n’uwo mugenzi wawe, rero ukwishyura kwa Keysend kuzokwishyura amafaranga nk’ukwishyurwa gusanzwe kwa LN Invoice, guca ku nzira isanzwe nk’iyindi nshumbusho yose. Ivyo gusa, wibuke, nta n’akarongo kazogumaho nk’aka LN Invoice.
