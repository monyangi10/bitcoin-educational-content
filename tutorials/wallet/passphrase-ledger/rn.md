## None passphrase ikora gute ku Ledger?


Ku bikoresho vya Ledger, ufise uburyo bubiri butandukanye bwo gutunganya passphrase kuri Wallet yawe: uburyo "*PIN-tied*" n'uburyo "*temporary*".


Ukoresheje "*PIN-tied*", ufatanya passphrase na PIN ya kabiri kuri Ledger yawe. Ivyo bisigura ko uzoba ufise PIN 2: imwe yo gushika ku Wallet yawe isanzwe ata passphrase, iyindi yo gushika ku Wallet yawe ya kabiri irindwa na passphrase.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/03.webp)


Mu vy'ukuri, mbere n'aho iyi nzira ya passphrase iboshe kuri PIN ya kabiri, passphrase yawe iguma ari passphrase yawe. Ivyo bisigura ko iyo utakaje Ledger yawe maze wipfuza gusubirana ama bitcoins yawe ku kindi gikoresho canke porogarama, uzokenera cane amajambo yawe y’amajambo 24 n’**passphrase yawe yuzuye**. PIN ifatanye na passphrase ikoreshwa gusa kugira ngo uyironke kuri Ledger yawe y’ubu, ariko ntikora ku zindi Ledgers canke izindi porogarama. Ni ngombwa rero ko ukora backup yuzuye kuri passphrase yawe ku buryo bugaragara. **Kumenya PIN ya kabiri gusa ntibihagije kugira ngo wongere ubone Wallet yawe**; ni ikintu gusa gifasha kuri Ledger yawe.


Iyi nzira ya kabiri ya PIN iraryoshe cane cane mu guhangana n’ibitero vy’umubiri. Nk’akarorero, iyo uwugutera akuhatira gufungura igikoresho cawe kugira ngo yibe amahera yawe, urashobora gukoresha PIN ya mbere kugira ngo ushikire decoy Wallet irimwo amahera makeyi y’ama bitcoins, mu gihe amahera yawe nyamukuru aguma atekanye inyuma ya PIN ya kabiri.


Ikindi kandi, iyo nzira itanga ivyiza vyose vy’umutekano vya BIP39 passphrase ata ngorane yo kuyinjiramwo n’amaboko igihe cose ukoresheje igikoresho cawe co gusinya. Ivyo bituma ukoresha passphrase ndende kandi idasanzwe, gutyo ugakomeza uburinzi ku bitero vy’inkomezi z’agahomerabunwa, mu gihe wirinda ingorane zo kuyindika n’amaboko igihe cose ku buto buto buto bw’ico gikoresho.

Ihitamwo rya "passphrase y'igihe gito" ntiribika passphrase ku gikoresho. Igihe cose ushaka gushika ku Wallet yawe irindwa, uzokenera kwinjiza passphrase kuri Ledger. Ivyo bituma gukoresha bigorana cane ariko kandi bikongera gatoyi umutekano mu kutagira akamenyero ka passphrase ku gikoresho. Ukimara kuzimya ico gikoresho, kirasubira uko cari kimeze kandi gisaba ko umuntu yinjiza passphrase yuzuye kugira ngo ashobore gushika ku makonti yihishije. Iryo hitamwo "passphrase y'igihe gito" rero risa n'imikorere y'izindi nkoko z'ibikoresho.

Muri iyi nyigisho, nzokoresha Ledger Flex nk’akarorero. Ariko rero, nimba uriko urakoresha uwundi muderi wa Ledger, inzira iguma ari uko nyene. Ku bijanye na Ledger Stax, Interface ni co kimwe n’ica Ledger Flex. Naho ku bijanye n’ibikoresho vya Nano S, Nano S Plus, na Nano X, naho Interface itandukanye, uburyo bwo gukora n’amazina y’ibifungurwa biguma ari kimwe.


**Iciyumviro:** Niba waramaze kwakira ama bitcoins kuri Ledger yawe imbere yo gukoresha passphrase, uzokenera kuyarungika biciye ku nzira ya Bitcoin. passphrase itanga imfunguruzo nshasha, gutyo igatuma haba Wallet yigenga rwose kuri Wallet yawe ya mbere. Iyo wongeyeko passphrase, uzoba ufise Wallet nshasha izoba iri ubusa. Ariko rero, ivyo ntibikuraho Wallet yawe ya mbere ata passphrase. Urashobora kuyironka, biciye kuri Ledger yawe utashize muri passphrase canke biciye ku yindi porogarama ukoresheje ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 24.


Imbere yo gutangura iyi nyigisho, urabe neza ko umaze gutangura Ledger yawe kandi ugatanga ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic. Niba ivyo atari vyo kandi Ledger yawe ari nshasha, kurikiza inyigisho yihariye y’ikigereranyo cawe iboneka ku rubuga rwa PlanB. Iyi ntambwe imaze kurangira, urashobora gusubira muri iyi nyigisho.

## Ni gute woshiraho passphrase y’igihe gito n’iyi Ledger?


Ku rupapuro rw’intango rwa Ledger yawe, fyonda ku ruzitiro rw’ivyuma.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/04.webp)


Hitamwo "Ivyateye imbere", hanyuma "Shiraho passphrase".


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/05.webp)


Iyi ni intambwe aho ushobora guhitamwo hagati y'ihitamwo "linked to PIN" canke "temporary" twavuganye mu gice ca mbere. Aha, nzobasigurira ingene woshiraho passphrase y'igihe gito, rero ukande kuri "Shiraho passphrase y'igihe gito".


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/06.webp)

Uca usabwa kwinjiza passphrase yawe. Hitamwo passphrase ikomeye maze ubwo nyene ugende ku vyo gusubiza inyuma, ku kintu nk’impapuro canke ivyuma. Muri aka karorero, nahisemwo passphrase: `fH3&kL@9mP#2sD5qR!82`. Uhejeje kwinjira muri passphrase yawe, ukande kuri buto ya "*Bandanya*".

![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/07.webp)


Genzura ko passphrase yawe ihuye n'ivyo wanditse ku nkuru yawe y'umubiri, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Ego, ni ukuri*" kugira ngo wemeze.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/08.webp)


Kugira ngo uheze gukora passphrase yawe, shiramwo kode ya PIN ya Ledger yawe. Kuva ubu, igihe cose ushaka gushika kuri Wallet yawe ufise passphrase kuri Ledger, uzokenera gukurikira neza neza intambwe nk’izo zivugwa ngaha.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/09.webp)


Ubu ushobora kwinjiza imfunguruzo zawe za bose kuri Sparrow wallet kugira ngo ucunge Wallet yawe. Ku Sparrow, ivyo bizohura na Wallet itandukanye n'iya Wallet yawe ya mbere ata passphrase.


Gufungura Sparrow wallet. Raba neza ko porogaramu ihuye n'uruzitiro, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Dosiye*" maze uhitemwo "*New Wallet*".


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/10.webp)


Hitamwo izina rya Wallet yawe irindwa na passphrase. Kuri aka karorero, nahisemwo izina ririmwo ijambo "*passphrase*". Ariko rero, nimba ushaka ko ubukerebutsi bw’iyi Wallet buguma kuri orodinateri yawe, urashobora guhitamwo izina ridatera ubwoba cane.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/11.webp)


Hitamwo ubwoko bw'inyandiko ya Wallet yawe. Ndaguhanura guhitamwo "*Taproot*" canke "*SegWit*".


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/12.webp)


Huza Ledger yawe na mudasobwa yawe, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Hardware Wallet yahujwe*". Raba neza ko umaze kwandika passphrase yawe kuri Ledger yawe. Niba atarivyo, subira ku ntambwe za kera kugira ngo winjize passphrase yawe. Imbere yo gukomeza gupima, wibuke kandi gufungura porogaramu "*Bitcoin*" kuri Ledger yawe.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/13.webp)


Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Scan...*".


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/14.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Injiza ububiko bw'urufunguzo*" iruhande ya Ledger yawe.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/15.webp)


Wallet yawe irindwa na passphrase ubu yaremwe kuri Sparrow. Kugira ngo wemeze, fyonda ku buto ya "*Shiraho*".


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/16.webp)

Hitamwo ijambobanga rikomeye kugira ngo ushobore gushika kuri Sparrow wallet. Iri jambobanga rizotuma ushobora gushika ku makuru yawe ya Wallet kuri Sparrow, ivyo bikaba bifasha gukingira imfunguruzo zawe za bose, amaderesi, ibimenyetso, n’amateka y’ibikorwa vyawe kugira ngo umuntu ntashobora kuyaronka ata wemerewe.

Ndaguhanura ngo ubike iri jambo banga mu gikoresho co gucungera ijambo banga kugira ngo ntuvyibagire.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/17.webp)


Kandi aho urafise, Wallet yawe ubu iraremwe! Mu "*Ivyagezwe*", Sparrow izoguha "*Ikimenyetso c'urutoke rwawe*". Ivyo bigereranya urutoke rw'urufunguzo rwawe nyamukuru, rukoreshwa nk'ishimikiro ryo gukura Wallet yawe. Ndabahimiriza cane ko mwobika kopi y’iki gicapo c’urutoke. Mu karorero kanje, bihuye na: `281ee33a`.


![PASSPHRASE BIP39](assets/notext/18.webp)


Ibuka ivyo twavuze mu bice vyabanje: ikosa, mbere n’irito, mu kwinjira muri passphrase yawe rizotuma generate iba Wallet nshasha yose ifise imfunguruzo zitandukanye. Igihe cose ukeneye kumenya neza ko uriko uraronka Wallet ibereye ukoresheje passphrase ibereye, suzuma ko urutoke rw’urufunguzo rwawe rwa mbere rujanye n’urwo wanditse. Aya makuru, ubwayo, nta ngorane atera umutekano w’amahera yawe canke ubuzima bwite bwawe.


Imbere yo gukoresha Wallet yawe n’iyitwa passphrase, ndaguhanura cane gukora ikizame co gukira mu gihe c’umwuma. Andika amakuru y’ishingiro nka xpub yawe canke urutoke rw’urufunguzo rwawe rwa master, hanyuma usubiremwo Ledger yawe mu gihe Wallet ikiri ubusa. Inyuma y’aho, gerageza gusubiza Wallet yawe kuri Ledger ukoresheje impapuro zawe z’amajambo 24 na passphrase. Suzuma nimba amakuru yasohotse inyuma y’ugusubizaho ahuye n’ivyo wabonye mu ntango. Nimba ari ukwo biri, urashobora kwizigira ko amakete yawe y’impapuro ari ay’ukuri.
