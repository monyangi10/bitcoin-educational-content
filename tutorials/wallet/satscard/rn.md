Bitcoin ni ubuhinga bwo gutanga amahera bukoresheje ubuhinga bwa none butuma dushobora gukorana n’abandi. Ariko rero, kugira ngo umuntu yemere ko igikorwa co gucuruza kitahinduka, birakenewe ko arindirira ivyemezo vyinshi (kenshi na kenshi 6), kugira ngo umuntu yirinde ukugerageza gukoresha amahera incuro zibiri n’uwugirungitse. Ukwo gutevya kwemeza bishobora rimwe na rimwe kuba bigoye, cane cane iyo umuntu yipfuza ko ashobora gushika ku mpera y’ivyo akora. Mu buryo butandukanye n’amahera, aho ukugira amafaranga yimurirwa ubwo nyene, amafaranga y’ubudandaji ya Bitcoin asaba igihe co kurindira imbere y’uko afatwa ata gukeka ko adashobora gusubirwamwo.


Aho niho Satscard yinjira.Itanga uburyo bwo gutuma amafaranga y’ama bitcoins ashobora gutangazwa mu buryo bw’umubiri no mu kanya nk’ako gukubita, ataco akeneye gukora igikorwa ca On-Chain. Satscard ikora nk’ikarita y’umunyagihugu ishobora gutuma umuntu ashobora gutanga Bitcoin Ownership ata nkomanzi, gutyo agatanga ubumenyi bwegereye amahera asanzwe. Muri iyi nyigisho, nzobamenyesha uwo muti.


## Satscard ni iki?


Igitabo ca Satscard cakozwe na Coinkite ni co gikurikira igitabu ca Opendime. Ni ikarita ya NFC yemerera gutanga amafaranga y’ibiceri, nk’uko bimeze ku giciro canke Coin. Udakunze Hardware Wallet ya kera, Satscard ni ikarita y’uwuyitwara, bisobanura ko kugira ikarita ku mubiri bingana na Ownership y’ama bitcoins akingiwe n’imfunguruzo zibitswe kuri yo. Igiciro cayo kiri hagati y’amadolari 6,99 n’amadolari 17,99 bivanye n’ingene yahisemwo.


![SATSCARD](assets/notext/01.webp)


Ico gikoresho ca Satscard gifise ibibanza 10, bikaba bishobora kubika amafaranga y’ama bitcoins ashika incuro 10 ku ma aderesi 10 atandukanye. Buri nzira ikora yigenga kandi mu vy’impwemu ikwiye gukoreshwa rimwe gusa kugira ngo ikingire bitcoins muri yo. Kugira ngo ukoreshe ama bitcoins, ushobora gusa gufungura ikidodo c’aho hantu ukoresheje porogarama ihuye, nka Nunchuk, mu kwinjiza kode y’ukugenzura y’imirongo 6 yanditswe inyuma ya Satscard.


Iryo karata rituma urufunguzo rw’ibanga rukingira ama bitcoins kuri Blockchain rudashobora kugumwa n’uwahoze ari nyen’ikarata iyo bamaze gutandukana n’iryo karata. Uwuronka iyo mpapuro arashobora kandi kugenzura ko ikibanza gifise agaciro n’amahera yabitswemwo igihe ca Exchange.


Uwo murongo ni ngirakamaro cane cane mu kugura ibintu vy’umubiri ukoresheje amafaranga y’ibiceri, canke mu gutanga amafaranga y’ibiceri nk’ingabirano.


## Ni gute umuntu yogura Satscard?


Igitabu ca Satscard kiraboneka ku kiguzi [ku rubuga rwemewe rwa Coinkite](https://iduka.coinkite.com/iduka/urutonde/satscard). Kugira ngo uyigure mu iduka ry’umubiri, urashobora kandi kubona [urutonde rw’abagurisha bemerewe](https://coinkite.com/abagurisha) ku rubuga.

Uzokenera kandi telefone ihuye n’ivy’itumanaho rya NFC, canke igikoresho ca USB co gusoma amakarata ya NFC ku nkuru isanzwe ya 13.56 MHz.

## Ni gute woshiramwo umwanya kuri Satscard?


Uhejeje kwakira Satscard yawe, intambwe ya mbere ni ukugenzura ivyo ipfutse kugira ngo umenye neza ko itafunguwe. Iyo iyo paquete yononekaye, vyoshobora kwerekana ko iyo karata yashizwe mu kaga kandi ko yoshobora kuba atari iyo ukuri.


Kugira ngo ducunge Satscard, tuzokoresha porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa **Nunchuk Wallet**. Raba neza ko telefone yawe y’ubuhinga bwa none ihuye na NFC, hanyuma ushire Nunchuk kuri [Iduka rya Google Play], [Iduka ry’Iporogarama] (Iduka ry’Iporogarama] (Iduka ry’Iporogarama] biciye ku dosiye yayo [`.apk`] (ibisohoka).

## Ni gute wosuzuma ama bitcoins ku nzira?


Igihe amafaranga yemejwe, urashobora kumenya amahera asigaye ajanye n’aho ukoresha Satscard yawe mu kuyicapura ukoresheje Nunchuk. Gutyo, mu gihe c’ugucuruza, uwuronka ayo bitcoins arashobora guca asuzuma, biciye ku rubuga rwabo rwa Nunchuk, ko iyo karata irimwo vy’ukuri ayo bitcoins yari afise umwenda.


![SATSCARD](assets/notext/09.webp)

Iyo uwo mugenzi adafise app ya Nunchuk, barashobora kugenzura ko iyo Satscard ikora. Gusa n’ushobora gukoresha NFC kuri telefone yabo y’amaboko maze ugashira Satscard inyuma y’ico gikoresho. Ivyo bizoca bifungura urubuga rwa Satscard mu mucukumbuzi, aho umuntu ashobora kumenya ko iyo karata ikora be n’amahera ari mu bitcoins ajanye nayo.

## Ni gute wosubira gukoresha Satscard?


Udakunze imiti ikoreshwa rimwe nka Opendime, Satscard ifise igice c’umubiri kirimwo ibibanza 10 vyigenga, bishobora gutuma umuntu ashobora gukora ibikorwa bishika 10 akoresheje ikarita imwe. Igibanza ca mbere, catunganijwe imbere y’igihe mw’ihinguriro na Coinkite, gihuye n’ivyo kwakira Address yanditswe inyuma ya Satscard yawe.


![SATSCARD](assets/notext/21.webp)

Kugira ngo ukoreshe izindi nzira 9, uzokenera gukoresha generate urufunguzo rubiri na Address biciye ku rubuga rwa Nunchuk. Ku rupapuro rw'intango rwa porogarama, fyonda ku kimenyetso ca "*NFC*" kiri hejuru y'ibarabara.

![SATSCARD](assets/notext/22.webp)


Shira Satscard yawe inyuma ya telefone yawe.


![SATSCARD](assets/notext/23.webp)


Nunchuk yerekana ko ata slot ikora ku ikarita, ivyo bikaba ari ibisanzwe kuko iya mbere yaramaze gukoreshwa, iya kabiri ntiyari bwaboneke. Kugira ngo ubone ibibanza vyakoreshejwe mbere, kanda kuri "*Raba ibibanza bitakingiwe*". Ni vyiza cane ko udasubira gukoresha izo nzira, kuko ivyo vyotuma Address isubira gukoresha bishobora gutuma On-Chain yawe igira ingorane. Rero tuzoshiraho ikibanza gishasha mu gufyonda kuri buto ya "*Ego*".


![SATSCARD](assets/notext/24.webp)


Ubu uzokenera guhitamwo ingene generate shebuja wawe chain code.


Ivyo bibanza biri kuri Satscard bikurikira ingingo ngenderwako ya BIP32, bisobanura ko ugukura kw’imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwo gukingira ama bitcoins kutashingiye ku majambo Mnemonic nk’uko biri mu bipapuro vya BIP39, ariko bishingiye ku rufunguzo rw’ibanga n’urufunguzo rwa chain code. Ivyo bibiri Elements bikoreshwa nk’inyungu mu gikorwa ca HMAC-SHA512 ku generate urufunguzo rw’umwana. Buri nzira ifise urufunguzo rwayo rw’ingenzi n’urufunguzo rwayo rw’ingenzi chain code. Hariho urugero rumwe gusa rw’ugukura ku nzira imwe imwe.


Urufunguzo rwa kabiri rw’ibarabara rya mbere rwateguwe mbere na Coinkite. Ni co gituma ushobora kuyironka ataco uhinduye uciye kuri Nunchuk, kandi ni co gituma Address yakira yanditswe inyuma y’ikarata ya NFC. Ariko rero, ku bindi bibanza, ni wewe ushinzwe gutanga imfunguruzo.


Urufunguzo rw’ibanga rwa buri nzira ruterwa na Satscard, kandi amakode y’uruzitiro rwa mbere ategerezwa gutangwa avuye hanze. Ku chain code y'aho ukoresha mushasha, ufise uburyo bubiri: reka Nunchuk generate ihitemwo "*Automatic*", canke uyiremye wewe nyene uhisemwo "*Advanced*" maze ukayishira mu kibanza cagenewe. Kugira ngo chain code ikore neza, ikeneye kuba ivy’impfagusa uko bishoboka kwose.
