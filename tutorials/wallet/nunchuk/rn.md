## Igikoresho gikomeye cane Wallet.


Nunchuk yashitse mu mpera za 2020 afise filozofiya itomoye: guhindura umukono mwinshi ngo ube urugero. Yari rero yagenewe gukora ibikorwa biteye imbere cane, n’uguhitamwo kw’agaciro kwo kwubaka umugambi ataco uhinduye kuri Bitcoin core, porogarama y’ishingiro y’ibidukikije vya Bitcoin.



Inyuma y’imyaka irenga 4 itegurwa kandi ikoreshwa, irateguwe kugeragezwa ku rugero runini. Nimba uri umutanguzi kandi utamenyereye Nunchuk, iyi nkuru izogufasha gutera intambwe zawe za mbere no kuvumbura iyi porogarama, ibikorwa vyayo biteye imbere uzoshobora kumenya umaze guca ku ngaruka ya mbere. Inyigisho ubwayo igenewe abakoresha bo hagati bafise ubuhinga bukwiye bwo gukurikira intambwe zose, ariko ishobora kuba insiguro ku muntu wese kugira ngo amenye ingene yokwongerera ubuhinga. Tuzotangura n’ivyo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa, kandi ivyo vyerekana ni ngombwa, kuko Nunchuk afise porogarama yo gukoresha no kuri mudasobwa.

## Uruvyaro rw'ingenzi na Wallet


Nk’uko vyoroshe gutekereza ku filozofiya ya Nunchuk, iyo porogarama igenewe kuba igikoresho ngirakamaro co gucunga ama Wallets afise amasinyatire menshi. Kugira ngo Nunchuk akore ico gikorwa, aremera ko hakorwa Wallet mu kuyitandukanya n’imfunguruzo zikenewe kugira ngo umuntu atunganye imikono y’ivyuma.



Nkako, igikorwa ciza ca Nunchuk kirimwo uguhingura amasakoshi ashobora gukoreshwa gusa, bishingiye ku mfunguruzo zishobora kuba "Imbeho."



Mu bimenyetso vya kera ushobora kuba warabonye ko hariho urutonde ruri hasi rwitwa _Keys_. Niba umaze gukuraho Nunchuk, muri _Home_ na _Keys_ uzobona ubuto bunini bukutumira kwongerako urufunguzo, _Add Key_.



![image](assets/en/20.webp)


![image](assets/en/21.webp)



**Uku niko gusa Nunchuk ikora:** ubanza generate/import imfunguruzo hanyuma ugakora Wallet, ukayitunganya kugira uhitemwo imfunguruzo zizokwemerera gufungura amafaranga abitswe kuri yo.



Mbere no ku bijanye na Wallet singlesig, ubanza kurema urufunguzo hanyuma gusa Wallet. Kandi ivyo nyene nivyo tuzokora ubu, dutangure n’indirimbo ya Wallet singlesig kugira ngo tumenye urubura maze tuvumbure ibikorwa vya Nunchuk.



Fyonda _Kwongerako urufunguzo_



![image](assets/en/22.webp)



Nunchuk yerekana umubare w'ibikoresho vy'umukono bishigikiwe ariko, kugira ngo utangure, hitamwo _Porogaramu_.



![image](assets/en/23.webp)



Nunchuk azokora generate Mnemonic izobikwa kuri iyo mashini. Uca ukenera kwandika urutonde rw’amajambo yo gukoresha mu gusubiza inyuma, ugatuma haba ibidukikije vyiza kuruta ibindi vyose kandi ukamenya neza ko ufise umwanya wo kubikora neza kandi mu gacerere. Porogaramu yerekana Mnemonic rimwe gusa, waba uhisemwo kuyigaragaza ubu canke mu nyuma, rero uhitemwo _Create and backup now_.



![image](assets/en/24.webp)



Nunchuk aratanga amajambo 24 y’amajambo Mnemonic, ayo majambo akaboneka ubwo nyene ku rubuga rukurikira .



![image](assets/en/25.webp)



hanyuma abandanya gukora isuzuma ryihuse, bagusaba guhitamwo ijambo ryiza, mu mahitamwo 3, rihuye n’umubare uri mu rutonde rwa Mnemonic.


Iyo wanditse neza Mnemonic, buto _Continue_ iratangura gukora. Uyikande kugira ngo ugende.



![image](assets/en/26.webp)



Izina ry'urufunguzo rwawe hanyuma ukande _Bandanya_.



![image](assets/en/27.webp)



Iyo izo ntambwe zirangiye, uzobazwa nimba wokwongerako [passphrase](https://planb.network/ru/ibikoresho/urutonde rw’amajambo/passphrase-bip39) ku nteruro yawe ya Mnemonic. Niba udafise ubumenyi bukwiye bw'ingene ukoresha passphrase, gutunganya uko ikora, canke ingene ikora, ndagusavye uhitemwo _Sinkeneye ijambo ry'ibanga_.



![image](assets/en/28.webp)



Urufunguzo ruraremwa kandi rukwerekanwa muri menyu:




- Na _Key Spec_ urutoke rwa mbere rwerekanwa
- Ufise amasetingi, utudomo dutatu turi hejuru iburyo, aho ushobora gukuraho urufunguzo canke gusinya ubutumwa .
- Inyuma y’izina ry’urufunguzo uzosanga ikimenyetso c’urufunguzo, ukanda kurico ushobora guhindura izina ry’urufunguzo, nk’akarorero kugira ngo urufunguzo rwawe rugume ruri mu rutonde muri kazoza.
- Nk'itegeko rya nyuma, urashobora kugenzura ubuzima bw'urufunguzo: ukanda _Run health check_ ushobora gutuma app isuzuma nimba urufunguzo rwacitse intege.



Iyo uri mwiza, ukande _Done_



![image](assets/en/29.webp)



Mu _Keys_ menu uzobona urufunguzo rwawe rwa mbere ruboneka.



![image](assets/en/30.webp)



Mu kuja muri _Home_ menu, uburyo bwo kurema Wallet buraboneka. Fyonda _Rema igikapu gishasha_.



![image](assets/en/31.webp)



Nunchuk akwereka uburyo butari buke bushobora gukora, ahanini, n’ibikorwa iyo sosiyete itanga bitari vyo muri iyi nyigisho.



Muri iyi nkuru tuzokora _Hot Wallet na _Custom wallet_ mu gusobanura ido n'ido.


Reka dutangure na _Igikapu c'amahera_.



![image](assets/en/32.webp)



Mu buryo bworoshe, iyo porogarama izogusaba kwita izina ry’iyi Wallet nshasha maze uhitemwo inyandiko y’amaderesi. Ku nyigisho nahisemwo gusiga ivyashizweho, _Native segwit_. Niwaheza, uhitemwo _Bandanya_



![image](assets/en/33.webp)



Ivyo Wallet ikora birabandanya bigusaba gushinga urufunguzo amahera y’iyi Wallet azofungurwako. Nimba hariho imfunguruzo nyinshi, uzokwerekwa urutonde wohitamwo. Twebwe ubu twaremye imwe gusa, rero duhitamwo gushiramwo ikimenyetso kuri iyo. Mu mfuruka yo hasi iburyo urashobora kubona ingene Nunchuk azogusaba gushinga imikono myinshi ya Wallet yawe yo muri kazoza, wongere umubare w'imfunguruzo zikenewe_.



![image](assets/en/34.webp)



Kubera ko turiko turarema singlesig, dusiga `1` maze tukafyonda _Bandanya_.



Ubwa nyuma, haraboneka igicapo co kugenzura, aho ushobora kugenzura ibiranga Wallet:




- izina
- `1/1 Multisig`, ni ko Nunchuk yita Wallet y'umuntu umwe
- ubwoko bw'inyandiko, `Igihugu SegWit`
- Urufunguzo rwa `Imfunguruzo`, n'urutoke rwarwo n'inzira y'aho ruva



Niwaheza guhazwa, kanda _Rema igikapu_



![image](assets/en/35.webp)



Wallet yararemwe kandi urashobora gukuraho dosiye [.BSMS](https://github.com/Bitcoin/BIPs/blob/master/BIP-0129.mediawiki) nk’ivyo gusubiza inyuma. Kugira ngo usubire ku rutonde nyamukuru, kanda umwampi uri hejuru ibubamfu.



![image](assets/en/36.webp)



Uri mu _Ingoro_, aho werekanwa Wallet nshasha yaremwe itanga raporo y'uburinganire n'imiterere y'ihuriro. Mu gufyonda mu kibanza c'ubururu, urashobora kuronka ibikorwa nyamukuru vya Wallet.



![image](assets/en/37.webp)





- Igishushanyo c’ibarabara kiri hejuru iburyo kiragufasha gukora ubushakashatsi bw’ugucuruza;
- `View Wallet config` itanga uburenganzira bwo gushika ku rutonde rw'imiterere, aho ushobora guhindura izina rya Wallet no gukoresha amahitamwo ateye imbere, hejuru iburyo (ayo udashobora kuronka amafoto). Aha ushobora kwohereza hanze imiterere ya Wallet, ibimenyetso, gusubirira imfunguruzo, guhindura [umupaka w’ikinogo](https://planb.network/ru/ibikoresho/urutonde rw’amajambo/umupaka w’ikinogo) n’ibindi.

## Kugira ngo ugume wibuka gukomeza wigenga .


Nk’uko nyene hariho urutonde rw’irema, ni ukuvuga, ubanza gutanga imfunguruzo hanyuma ugatanga Wallet, uzokenera kuguma ufise urutonde rwo gukuraho ivyo bintu muri porogarama yawe.



Niba ufise ivyipfuzo vyo gukuraho imwe mu mfunguruzo, ukwiye kubanza kugira ivyiyumviro vyo gukuraho Wallet, canke Wallets, zikoresha imwe mu mfunguruzo z’umukono ku bikorwa: ubanza gukuraho Wallets hanyuma gusa imfunguruzo. Niwaba utakurikiye iyo nzira, uzosanga udashobora gukuraho urufunguzo.



Ubu ko uzi ingene wotangura gukoresha Nunchuk, urashobora kubandanya wiga iyi app maze ukamenya amabanga yayo. Muri iyi nyigisho twateye intambwe za mbere gusa, ariko hariho ibikoresho bikomeye cane n’ivyo ukeneye biteye imbere ivyo iyi Software Wallet ishobora kugufasha gushitsa.
