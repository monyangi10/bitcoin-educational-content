Misty Breez ni umuravyo wifata Wallet wakozwe na Breez ishingiye ku gikoresho cabo co gutegura porogarama n’urubuga rwa **Liquid** rwakozwe na BlockStream.


Izana n’uburyo bushasha bwo gukora ata n’urudodo rwa Lightning: **GAME CHANGER** ishobora gukoreshwa mu guhindura amakuru hagati y’imirongo ya Bitcoin.


Muri iyi nyigisho, turadondora ingene iyi Wallet ikora kandi tubahe n’insiguro yuzuye.



## None Misty Breez akora gute?



Misty Breez ni ugushirwa mu ngiro ata nzira y’umuravyo nk’inyuma. Yateguwe ishingiye kuri Breez SDK na Liquid.



Liquid ni Layer isa n’uruja n’uruza rwa Bitcoin, itanga iterambere rikomeye mu bijanye n’umuvuduko n’ibiciro vy’ugucuruza. Iyi Layer iremesha Misty Breez gutanga node ya Lightning ahubwo ikoresha ubuhinga bwa Exchange bw'abandi nka **Boltz** kugira ngo Liquid Network na Lightning Network bikore neza. Ntimwihute, tuzogaruka kuri ivyo.



Ubu rero, reka dutangure urugendo rwacu n'indege ya Misty Breez Wallet.



## Gutangura na Misty Breez



Porogarama ya Misty Breez iboneka ku mbuga zizwi zo gukurako amakuru nk’iduka rya Google Play (kuri Android) n’iduka rya Apple (kuri iOS). Ushobora kandi guhindurwa kuri app ibereye uhereye ku rubuga rwemewe rwa [Misty Breez](https://breez.technology/misty/).



⚠️ Urabe ko udashobora kwitiranya Misty Breez n'umuyaga Wallet.



⚠️ **IGIHARURWA**: Kubera umutekano w’ama bitcoins yawe, ni ngombwa ko ukura iyo porogarama ku mbuga zizwi kugira ngo umenye neza ko ari iyo ukuri.



![download-misty-breez](assets/fr/01.webp)



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzotangura dukoresheje igikoresho ca Android. Naho ari ukwo, intambwe yose be n’ibintu vyihariye bivugwa muri iki gice birakora kuri iOS.



Iyo ushizeho, Misty Breez iraguha uburenganzira bwo kurema Wallet nshasha canke gusubizaho Lightning Wallet ya kera ufise amajambo yo gusubizaho.


Muri iyi nyigisho, duhitamwo gukora Wallet nshasha.



⚠️Misty Breez ubu iri mu rwego rwo gutegura, rero turaguhanura gutangura n'amahera akwiye.

### Gukora amafaranga yawe yambere


Niba umaze kumenya Breez Wallet, ntuzosubirwamwo na gato na Interface ya Misty Breez.



Ku rutonde rwa Interface **Imibare**, fyonda ku **Kwakira** kugira ngo ukore amafagitire yo kwakira amafaranga yawe kuri Wallet yawe.



⚠️ Misty Breez izogusaba gukoresha amamenyesha y'ivyo bikoresho mu ma settings ya telefone yawe kugira ngo ubone uburenganzira bwo kuronka Lightning Address.



Na Misty Breeze, ushobora :




- Ushobora kwakira amafaranga y’ibiceri kuri Lightning Network kuva ku **amasatoshi 100** gushika ku **amasatoshi 25.000.000**.
- Kwakira amafaranga y’ibiceri ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin kuva ku **satoshis 25.000**.



![transactions](assets/fr/05.webp)



Aho niho ubupfumu bwa Misty Breez butangura.


Udakunze Breez Wallet, iguha node ya Lightning kandi ikagusaba kwishura amafaranga yo gufungura no gufunga imihora yo kwishura wewe nyene, Misty Breez ntaco igusaba gukora. Nk’uko twabibonye, ​​Misty Breez mbere ntakora ishingiye ku nzira y’umuravyo.



Reka twihweze neza ivyabaye inyuma y’ivyo bintu.



Mu vy'ukuri, ufise indege ya Liquid Wallet ifatanye n'indege yawe ya Misty Breez Wallet. Mu buryo bubereye, uzoba uriko urakoresha L-BTC (Liquid Bitcoin) ku biciro bihoraho bijanye n'ibikorwa vyo guhindura satoshis zo mu mazi zizogufasha gukorana na Lightning Network.



Iyo uronse amahera kuri Misty Breez Wallet yawe, uwugurungikiye akurungikira satoshis zizoca mu nzira yo guhindura nka Boltz (ubu ikoreshwa na Misty Breez), kugira ngo uhindure satoshis zoherejwe zibe L-BTC zizokwakirwa kuri Wallet yawe ya Misty Breez Wallet (ifatanye na Liquid).


Ehe ikigereranyo coroshe c’ingene ivyo bigenda inyuma y’ivyo bikorwa.



![lnswap-in](assets/fr/06.webp)



Fyonda kuri Interface mu **Balance**, fyonda ku **Kohereza** kugira ngo wishyure Invoice y'umuravyo.


Injiramwo umuravyo Invoice, umuravyo Address w’uwuguhaye canke gusa ushireko kode ya QR iri kuri Invoice kugira ngo ushobore kwishura.



![send-bitcoins](assets/fr/07.webp)



Inyuma y’ivyo, ushobora gutuma Liquid Wallet ifatanye na Misty Breez Wallet yawe ihindura ikingana na L-BTC mu satoshis biciye ku Boltz, hanyuma ugashira izo satoshis ku Lightning Wallet y’uwukwakira (iriho kuri Lightning Network).



![send-bitcoin-bts](assets/fr/08.webp)



Ico kintu kiri mu bikorwa remezo vya Misty Breez gituma abakoresha bashobora gukora amafaranga mbere n’igihe Misty Breez itari ku murongo.



Ku bazi cane, hariho kandi **Ivyo ukunda > Abahinguzi** biguha ibindi bisobanuro ku :




- Verisiyo y’Igikoresho co Gutegura Porogarama ya Breez.
- Urufunguzo rwa bose rw'umuyaga wawe Wallet.
- Uwugurisha, ikimenyetso kidasanzwe kiva ku rufunguzo rwa mbere rwa bose.
- Igitigiri cawe ca Wallet.
- Impanuro Liquid, yo kohereza amafaranga makeyi ya L-BTC.
- Impanuro ya Bitcoin, yo kohereza utugingo ngengabuzima dutoduto twa Bitcoin.



Ushobora kandi gukora ibikorwa bimwebimwe, nko gukorana na Liquid Network, gukingira imfunguruzo zawe, gusangira urutonde rw'ibikorwa vyawe no guhitamwo gusubira gupima Liquid Network.
