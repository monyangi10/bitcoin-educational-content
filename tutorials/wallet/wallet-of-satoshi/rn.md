## Gukuraho, Gushiraho no Gukoresha Wallet ya Satoshi.


Wallet ya Satoshi ni Lightning Network Wallet, ishobora kubikwa, kandi yoroshe cane gukoresha.

Kubera intumbero z’amashure [BTC105 - Kuronka Ubu], rikoreshwa ku mpapuro z’amahera za Redeem Lightning Network.


**Imisi yose wibuke**: _si imfunguruzo zawe, si ibiceri vyawe_


Ivyuma vy’ububiko ntivyemerera ababikoresha kugenzura neza amahera yabo. Mu bisanzwe ntivyiza, kiretse abatangura. WoS ikwiye gukoreshwa nk’iyi Wallet y’inzira canke yo gufata amahera yo mu mufuko, atari iyo kwirundanira amahera y’igihe kirekire.


---

Wallet ya Satoshi (WoS) ni igikoresho co kuzigama, ariko gifise izina rimwe. Turashobora guhindukira mu buryo bubereye ku gikoresho nka WoS, nk’akarorero, kugira ngo twongere ubushobozi bwacu bwo kwakira amahera. Turashinga WoS mu gihe gito "igikorwa c'umwanda" co kuducungera amafaranga y'imirongo. Igihe umubare kanaka ushitse, tuzoshira ubusa WoS On-Chain ku Wallet yacu idafise ububiko.

### Kwandika konti ya WoS


Muri iki gihe, Wallet iramaze gukora, ariko kugira ngo tugire umutekano mwinshi, turabandanya gushinga urubuga rwo kwinjiramwo: ruzokenerwa kugira ngo umuntu asubire kuronka amahera iyo igikoresho coba kidakora neza canke kigatakaza. Ku bw’ivyo, nuhitemwo urutonde ruri hejuru ibubamfu.


![image](assets/it/04.webp)


Idirisha ryose ry’ibifungurwa rifunguka, aho utegerezwa gushinga gusa amafaranga (Wallet ya Satoshi ku buryo busanzwe yerekana idolari ry’Amerika nk’amafaranga y’ishingiro) n’ibara ry’insanganyamatsiko (umuco/umwiza), bivanye n’ivyo ukunda. Ntukoreshe ayandi mabwirizwa.


Kubera ko WoS ari igikoresho co kubika, ntidushobora gusubiza inyuma Wallet n’ijambo Mnemonic, ariko turashobora gutuma WoS ishobora kugarura amahera yacu, mu gihe yoba yatakaje canke idakoreshwa igikoresho co mu ntoke, mu gufyonda kuri _Login/Register_

Idirisha rizoboneka ridusaba kwinjiza imeyili Address. Ishobora kuba **ikete rya Proton** (recommended), ariko ritegerezwa kuba rikora, kuko rizotuma dushobora gusubirana amahera ari muri Wallet iyo telefone ngendanwa yoba yatakaye/yibwe canke yononekaye.


![image](assets/it/08.webp)


Wallet ya Satoshi yarungitse ubutumwa ku gasandugu k’imeli kagaragajwe.


![image](assets/it/09.webp)

#### Gusubiza konti ya WoS


None, turabona ingene twoshira WoS ku gikoresho gishasha; ivyo bizoba ngirakamaro kandi mu gihe umuntu yivye, atakaje canke adashobora gukoresha telefone ngendanwa Wallet yari yarashizweko mbere. Iyo umaze gusubira gushiramwo, utegerezwa gusubira gukora uburyo bwo kwandika konti bwaherutse gusigurwa, ufise uburyo bumwe: ku mpera y’ugusaba kwinjira ukoresheje imeyili yashizweho mbere, WoS izoboneka gutya:


![image](assets/it/33.webp)


Ubutumwa buratuburira ko email yoherejwe irimwo uburyo bwo gusubira gukoresha konti. Utegerezwa gufungura ubutumwa bwawe bwa email.


**IGIHARURWA**: fungura email ukoresheje PC canke, uko biri kwose, ukoresheje igikoresho gitandukanye n’ico ugiye gusubiramwo konti ya WoS. Mu gasandugu k'ubutumwa, turaronka ubutumwa butwereka kode ya QR yo gushiramwo .


![image](assets/it/34.webp)


Iyo kode ya QR imaze gushirwa mu kazu, kuri paji nyamukuru ya WoS hazoboneka konti yasubijwe, irimwo amahera asigaye n’amateka.
