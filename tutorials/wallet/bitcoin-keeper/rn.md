Uburongozi butekanye bw’ama bitcoins bugereranya ingorane ikomeye ku muntu wese afise azi neza ivyerekeye ubusegaba bw’ivy’ubutunzi. Hagati y’ukworohereza kwa wallet igendagenda n’ubukomezi bw’umuti wa multi-sig, itandukaniro ry’ubuhinga rishobora gusa n’iriteye ubwoba ku bakoresha benshi. Bitcoin Keeper iri ahantu nyene kuri iyo nzira, itanga uburyo butera imbere bwo gucungera umutekano bujana n’abakoresha uko bagenda baratera imbere.



Bitcoin Keeper ni porogaramu y’amatelefone ngendanwa, yihariye kuri Bitcoin, yateguwe n’umugwi wa BitHyve. Intumbero yayo ni ugutuma uburongozi bw’ibiharuro buteye imbere bushikira abantu, cane cane imiterere y’imikono myinshi, mu gihe igumya urubuga rworoshe ku batangura. Iryo koraniro ryemera insiguro ivuga ngo "Umutekano uno musi, Tegura ejo", ryerekana filozofiya yaryo yo gushigikira igihe kirekire.



Udakunze ama wallet rusangi acungera amafaranga menshi, Bitcoin Keeper iguma yibanda cane kuri Bitcoin. Ubu buryo bwo gukoresha bitcoin gusa buragabanya igitero gishobora guterwa kandi bugatuma umuntu ashobora gukoresha neza cane. Iyi porogaramu kandi iratandukanye kubera ubufatanye bwayo bw’akavukire bw’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none vy’ubuhinga bwa none vy’ubuhinga bwa none n’ubuhinga bwayo bwo gucunga UTXO buteye imbere.



## Bitcoin Keeper ni iki?



### Filozofiya n'intumbero



Bitcoin Keeper yari yagenewe gushitsa ivya nkenerwa vyihariye vy’aba bitcoiners bipfuza kuguma bafise ububasha bwose ku mfunguruzo zabo z’ibanga. Uwo mugambi ushiramwo bimwe bishitse ingingo ngenderwako z’ishimikiro za Bitcoin: kode y’inkomoko yuguruye kandi ishobora gusuzumwa, kwubahiriza ubuzima bwite, n’ubusegaba bw’abakoresha. Nta kwiyandikisha canke amakuru y’ibanga bisabwa kugira ngo umuntu akoreshe iyo porogarama, kandi irashobora mbere gukora ata murongo kugira ngo umuntu akoreshe ibikorwa vyo gusinya.



Intumbero nyamukuru ni ugutanga igikoresho gishobora guhinduka, gishobora gukoreshwa muri kazoza co kubika BTC mu myaka myinshi, mbere n’ibisekuru vyinshi, bivuye ku mikorere y’iragi. Iryo koraniro rituma abakoresha bashobora gutangura gusa n’itelefone ngendanwa yitwa wallet, hanyuma buhoro buhoro bagatera imbere baja ku gutorera umuti ibibazo vy’imikono myinshi bitekanye.



### Ubwubatsi bw'ikoreshwa



Bitcoin Keeper itegura uburongozi bw’amahera bushingiye ku vyiyumviro bibiri bitandukanye. **Hot Wallet** ni wallet yoroshe y’urufunguzo rumwe, ibitswe kuri telefone, igenewe gukoresha amahera ku musi ku musi n’amahera makeyi. Vaults** ni amasandugu afise imikono myinshi (Multi-Key) asaba imfunguruzo nyinshi kugira ngo umuntu yemerere gukoresha amahera, yagenewe kubika igihe kirekire ata nkomanzi.



### Ibirango nyamukuru



Bitcoin Keeper ifasha hafi amasakoshi yose y'ibikoresho biri kw'isoko: Ikarata y'ubukonje, Trezor, Ledger, Ibuye ry'urufunguzo, BitBox02, Jade, Uwushirako umukono ku mbuto, Pasiporo, na Tapsigner ya Coinkite. Ugushiramwo bica mu buryo butandukanye bivanye n’igikoresho: gucapura kode ya QR, gufatanya na NFC, canke kwinjiza dosiye.



Iryo koraniro kandi ritanga uburongozi buteye imbere bwa UTXO bufise ibimenyetso vy’ibikorwa, ubugenzuzi bw’ibiceri kugira ngo uhitemwo n’amaboko ivyo winjiza igihe wohereje, n’ubufasha bw’uburyo bwa PSBT ku bikorwa vyashizweko umukono bimwe bimwe.



## Gushiramwo n'imiterere y'intango



Bitcoin Keeper iraboneka ku buntu kuri Android biciye ku iduka rya Google Play no kuri iOS biciye ku iduka rya App. Uwo mumenyeshamakuru yanditswe ni BitHyve. Imbere yo gushiramwo, urabe neza ko igikoresho cawe kitagiramwo porogarama mbi, ko kiri ku gihe, kandi ko kitagira imizi canke ngo gipfuke.



Igihe utangura, iyo porogarama iragusaba gukora kode ya PIN y’umutekano. Iyi kode irinda ukuronka wallet yawe kandi igashiramwo amakuru y’agaciro mu karere. Hitamwo kode ikomeye maze uyifate mu mutwe. Ushobora rero gukoresha ubuhinga bwo kwemeza ko umuntu ari we (urutoke canke Face ID) kugira ngo ufungure ningoga.



![Installation et configuration du PIN](assets/fr/01.webp)



Iryo koraniro rero ritanga amasanamu menshi y’intango asigura inkingi zayo zitatu: wallet irema kugira ngo yohereze kandi yakire bitcoins, uburongozi bw’urufunguzo n’ibikoresho wallet bihuye, n’ugutegura iragi ryo gutanga bitcoins. Kanda "Tangura", hanyuma uhitemwo "Tangira nshasha" kugira ngo ureme uburyo bushasha.



![Écrans d'introduction](assets/fr/02.webp)



## Kuvumbura ikigaragaza



Igikoresho ca Bitcoin Keeper gitunganijwe hirya no hino y'ibice bine vy'ingenzi bishobora gushikwako uhereye ku murongo wo hasi wo kugenderamwo:



![Les quatre onglets de l'application](assets/fr/03.webp)



Igipande ca **Wallets** kigaragaza ama wallet yawe n'amahera asigaye. Aho niho ushika ku ma wallets yawe kugira ngo wohereze no kwakira ama bitcoins. Ivyo bimenyetso "Hot Wallet" na "Urufunguzo rumwe" canke "Urufunguzo rwinshi" bigufasha kumenya ningoga ubwoko bwa wallet yose.



**Imfunguruzo** tab ishira hamwe uburongozi bw'imfunguruzo zawe z'umukono. Aha uzosanga Urufunguzo rwa Mobile rwakozwe na porogarama, hamwe n’urufunguzo rwose rwavuye mu bikoresho vy’amasakoshi. Aha niho kandi wongerako ibikoresho bishasha vyo gusinya.



Igipande ca **Concierge** kiratanga ubufasha: ushire ibibazo ku mugwi w’abafasha kandi wihuze n’abajanama ba Bitcoin kugira ngo bagufashe ku giti cabo.



**Ibindi** (Ibindi Amahitamwo) tab itanga uburenganzira bwo gushika ku mirongo nk’ihuriro rya server y’umuntu ku giti ciwe, ububiko bw’urufunguzo, inyandiko z’iragi, ivyo ukunda kwerekana n’uburongozi bwa wallet.



## Ukwihuzwa na server yawe bwite



Kugira ngo ukomeze ibanga ryawe, Bitcoin Keeper iragufasha gufatanya porogarama na server yawe bwite ya Electrum, aho gukoresha server za bose zisanzwe.



![Configuration du serveur Electrum](assets/fr/04.webp)



Kuva ku rubuga Ibindi, genda hasi kugira uronke amasetingi ya server. Kanda "Ongera Server" kugira ngo utunganye uruja n'uruza rushasha. Ushobora guhitamwo hagati ya "Server ya bose" (serveri za bose zatunganijwe mbere) na "Electrum y'ibanga" (serveri yawe bwite).



Ku serveri yihariye, shiramwo URL (nk'akarorero umbrel.local ku nzira ya Umbrel) n'inomero y'icuma (kenshi 50001). Gukoresha SSL nimba umukozi wawe ayishigikiye. Ushobora kandi gucapura kode ya QR y’imiterere. Umaze kwinjiza amaparametere, kanda "Huza na Serveri".



Niba utaragira urudodo rwawe rwa Bitcoin, raba inyigisho yacu ku bijanye na Umbrel, uburyo bworoshe kandi bushoboka bwo guhindura urudodo rwawe:



https://planb.academy/tutorials/node/bitcoin/umbrel-8b0e3b5b-d3cf-4a1e-8bb8-1ad2db4dd848

## Kwakira ama bitcoins



Mu gice kivuga ngo Wallets, nuhitemwo wallet wipfuza gukuramwo amahera ukayikanda. Igishushanyo ca wallet kirerekana uburinganire n’amabuto atatu y’ibikorwa: Wohereze Bitcoin, Kwakira Bitcoin, na Raba Ibiceri Vyose.



![Réception de bitcoins](assets/fr/05.webp)



Kanda "Kwakira Bitcoin". Bitcoin Keeper itanga aderesi nshasha yo kwakira abantu mu buryo bwa Bech32 (itangura na bc1...), hamwe n’ikode yayo ya QR. Ushobora kwongerako ikimenyetso kuri iyo aderesi kugira ngo umenye aho ayo mahera akomoka. Sangira aderesi n’uwarungitse mu kugaragaza kode ya QR canke mu gukopa aderesi y’inyandiko.



Iryo koraniro rihita ritanga aderesi nshasha ku bijanye n’ukwakira umuntu wese, ivyo bikaba bituma uguma ufise ubuzima bwite. Koresha "Get New Address" kugira uronke aderesi y'ubusa iyo bikenewe.



## Ubuyobozi bwa UTXO



Bitcoin Keeper itanga ukuboneka kwose kwa UTXO (Ibisohoka mu bikorwa bitakoreshejwe) bigize umubare wawe. Kuva ku gicapo ca wallet, kanda "Reba ibiceri vyose" kugira ngo ushikire umuyobozi w'imfuruka.



![Gestion des UTXO](assets/fr/06.webp)



Igishushanyo ca "Manage Coins" kigaragaza urutonde rwa UTXO yose ku giti cayo n'umubare wayo n'ikimenyetso cayo. Ivyo bigufasha gukurikirana inkomoko y’ibiceri vyawe no kubitunganya. Ushobora guhitamwo UTXOs zihariye biciye ku "Hitamwo kohereza" kugira ngo wohereze n'ubugenzuzi bw'ibiceri, gutyo wirinde kuvanga ibiceri biva mu bihugu bitandukanye.



## Wohereze ama bitcoins



Kugira ngo wohereze, hitamwo inkomoko y'inyandiko hanyuma ukande "Ohereza Bitcoin". Injira aderesi y’aho uja (uyishizeko canke uyishizeko biciye ku kode ya QR) hanyuma wongereko ikimenyetso kugira ngo umenye uwo uyironka.



![Envoi de bitcoins](assets/fr/07.webp)



Igishushanyo gikurikira kigufasha kwinjiza amahera uzorungika. Igikoresho kigaragaza amafaranga yawe ariho n’uguhindura amafaranga y’amahera. Hitamwo ivy’imbere: Bike (ubutunzi, ~60 iminota), Biringaniye, canke Hejuru (ivy’imbere). Ivyiyumviro vy’amahera bigereranywa mu sats/vbyte vyerekanywe mu gihe nyaco. Kanda "Ohereza" kugira ngo ukomeze.



![Confirmation et envoi](assets/fr/08.webp)



Igishushanyo c’incamake kigaragaza ibintu vyose: Inkomoko ya wallet, aderesi y’aho ija, ivy’imbere mu gucuruza, amahera y’urubuga, amahera yoherejwe n’umubare wose. Turagusavye usuzume neza aya makuru, kuko amafaranga Bitcoin adashobora gusubirwamwo. Kanda "Kwemeza & Wohereze" kugira ngo wohereze amafaranga.



Ivyemezo vy'uko "Wohereje vyaroraniwe" biraboneka hamwe n'incamake yuzuye. Iryo soko riboneka mu mateka ya "Isoko rya vuba" n'ikimenyetso caryo.



## Bika imfunguruzo zawe



Gusubiza inyuma Urufunguzo rwawe rwo Gusubirana ni intambwe ihambaye cane. Kuva ku bindi, genda ku gice ca "Gusubiza n'ugusubiza" hanyuma ukande kuri "Urufunguzo rwo gusubiza".



![Sauvegarde de la Recovery Key](assets/fr/09.webp)



Igishushanyo kigaragaza uko amakuru yawe ameze. Kugira ngo ugenzure ububiko bwawe, porogaramu iragusaba kwemeza ijambo ry’umwihariko mu nteruro yawe (nk’akarorero ijambo rya 7). Ukwo kugenzura kuratuma wanditse neza ijambo ryawe ryo gukira.



Kuva ku "Imiterere y'Urufunguzo rwo Gusubizaho", ushobora kubona ijambo ryawe ryose biciye ku "Raba Urufunguzo rwo Gusubizaho" maze ukabona Urutoke rw'Umushizeko Umukono rw'urufunguzo rwawe. Gumana amajambo yawe y’amajambo 12 ku mpapuro, ahantu heza, kure y’ubukonje n’umuriro. Ntukigere ubika ku gikoresho gihuye.



## Yongerako urufunguzo rwo hanze (ibikoresho vya wallet)



Kimwe mu bintu bihambaye vya Bitcoin Keeper ni ugushiramwo ama wallets y’ibikoresho. Kuva ku rutonde rw'imfunguruzo, kanda "Ongera urufunguzo" kugira ngo wongereko igikoresho gishasha co gusinya.



![Ajout d'une clé hardware](assets/fr/10.webp)



Hitamwo "Ongera urufunguzo kuva ku gikoresho" kugira ngo uhuze igikoresho wallet. Iryo koraniro rifasha ibikoresho vyinshi: BitBox02, Coldcard, Blockstream Jade, Keystone, Krux, Ledger, Pasiporo y’umushinge, Portal, Seedsigner, n’Ibisubizo vy’Ibitangaza.



### Tapsigner imiterere



Tapsigner ni ikarita ya NFC iva kuri Coinkite cane cane ibereye gukoreshwa kuri telefone ngendanwa. Niba ushaka kumenya vyinshi, turafise inyigisho yihariye :



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/hardware/tapsigner-ab2bcdf9-9509-4908-9a4a-2f2be1e7d5d2

Kugira ngo wongereko Tapsigner, uhitemwo mu rutonde rw’ibikoresho vy’amasakoshi.



![Configuration du Tapsigner](assets/fr/11.webp)



Ubanze winjize kode ya PIN y’imirongo 6-32 icapwe inyuma y’ikarata yawe (ni yo isanzwe ku makarita mashasha), canke PIN yawe nimba isanzwe itunganijwe. Kanda "Proceed", hanyuma uzane Tapsigner yawe hafi y'inyuma ya telefone yawe igihe "Ready to scan" izogaragara. Imenyekanisha rya NFC rihita rizana urufunguzo rwa bose. Ushobora rero kwongerako insobanuro (nk'akarorero "Ikarata ya Métro") kugira ngo umenye urufunguzo.



## Rema ububiko bwa multisig



Umaze gushinga imfunguruzo zawe, urashobora gukora wallet y’imikono myinshi ihuriza hamwe ibikoresho vyinshi. Kuva ku rubuga rwa Wallets, kanda kuri "Ongera Wallet".



![Création d'un nouveau wallet](assets/fr/12.webp)



Ufise uburyo butatu: "Rema Wallet" ku bijanye n'ibitabo bishasha, "Injiza Wallet" kugira ngo ugarure wallet yariho, canke "Gukorana Wallet" ku bijanye n'ububiko busangiye. Hitamwo "Rema Wallet" hanyuma "Bitcoin Wallet".



![Sélection du type de wallet](assets/fr/13.webp)



Igishushanyo gikurikira gitanga imiterere itandukanye: "Urufunguzo rumwe", "2 kuri 3 urufunguzo rwinshi", canke "3 kuri 5 urufunguzo rwinshi". Kugira ngo ubone multi-sig, kanda "Hitamwo ivyagezwe". Nk'akarorero, hitamwo "1 kuri 2": umukono umwe usabwa ku mfunguruzo zibiri zishoboka.



Hanyuma uhitemwo imfunguruzo zizogira Vault yawe. Mu karorero kacu, dufatanya "Urufunguzo rwa Telefone ngendanwa" (urufunguzo rwa porogarama ya telefone) na "TAPSIGNER" (Ikarata ya Metro). Iyi ntunganyo itanga redundancy: iyo rumwe mu rufunguzo rudashobora gushikwako, urashobora kwama ukoresha amahera yawe n’urundi.



![Finalisation du wallet multisig](assets/fr/14.webp)



Izina rya wallet yawe (nk'akarorero "IgeragezaB"), wongereko insobanuro y'ubusa, hanyuma usuzume imfunguruzo zatowe. Kanda "Rema Wallet yawe". Ubutumwa bwo kwemeza "Wallet Yaremwe Neza" buraboneka, bukwibutsa kubika dosiye yo gusubizaho wallet.



Multisig yawe nshasha wallet ubu iboneka mu gice ca Wallets gifise ikimenyetso "Urufunguzo rwinshi" n'ikimenyetso "1 kuri 2".



### Bika dosiye y'imiterere



**Bitandukanye na wallet yoroshe, aho ijambo ryo gusubirana rihagije kugira ngo umuntu asubire kuronka, wallet multisig na yo isaba dosiye y’imiterere idondora imiterere y’igisandugu (ni imfunguruzo izihe zigira uruhara, ni imikono ingahe isabwa). Ata iyi dosiye, mbere n'amajambo yose yo gusubizaho, ntuzoshobora gusubira kwubaka wallet yawe.



![Export du fichier de configuration](assets/fr/15.webp)



Kugira ngo urungike iyo dosiye hanze, hitamwo wallet multisig yawe mu gice ca Wallets, hanyuma ukande ku kimenyetso c’Ivyagezwe (gear) kiri hejuru iburyo. Mu "Imiterere ya Wallet", kanda kuri "Dosiye y'imiterere ya Wallet". Amahitamwo menshi yo kwohereza hanze araboneka:





- Gusohora PDF**: itanga inyandiko ya PDF irimwo amakuru yose ya wallet
- Erekana QR**: yerekana kode ya QR ishobora gukoreshwa mu kwinjiza imiterere ku kindi gikoresho
- Airdrop / Gusohora dosiye**: yohereza dosiye hanze biciye ku mahitamwo yo gusangira kuri telefone yawe
- NFC**: gusangira biciye kuri NFC n'igikoresho gihuye



Gumana iyi dosiye y'imiterere itandukanye n'amajambo yawe yo gusubizaho, vyiza ku buryo bushizwemwo canke bucapwe. Iyo utakaje telefone yawe, iyi dosiye ifatanijwe n’amajambo yo gusubizaho urufunguzo rwose rugira uruhara izogufasha gusubira kwubaka wallet multisig yawe kuri Bitcoin Keeper canke iyindi porogarama yose ihuye.



## Ibikorwa vyiza



### Ishirahamwe ry'ikigega



Tunganya amafaranga yawe y’ama bitcoins akurikije uko akoresha: urufunguzo rumwe rwa wallet rushushe ku mafaranga ukoresha ubu afise amahera make, n’urufunguzo rumwe canke nyinshi Vaults Multi-Key ku kuzigama igihe kirekire. Gushiramwo ikimenyetso ca UTXO mu buryo butunganye bizokugirira akamaro mu kumenya aho amahera yawe ava, ivyo bikaba ari ngirakamaro cane mu gucunga ibanga no kwirinda kuvanga ibiceri vy’inkomoko zitandukanye.



Gumana telefone yawe mu mutekano: fungura urufunguzo rwa biometric, ukore ivy’uguhindura sisitemu ubudasiba, kandi ugume uri maso ku bijanye n’ibikoresho vyashizwemwo. Kandi Bitcoin Keeper igumye iri ku gihe n’ibipande vy’umutekano.



### Gusubiza inyuma umutekano



Bika n’imiburiburi kopi zibiri z’ijambo ryose ryo gusubizaho ku mpapuro, uzibike ahantu hatandukanye hakurikijwe ivy’ubutaka. Ku mahera menshi, nuzirikane ivyuma bicapwe, bishobora guhangana n’ivyago. Ntukigere ubika ayo majambo ku gikoresho gifatanye na Internet, kandi ntukigere uyafotora.



Ku bibanza vya multi-sig, ubike kandi dosiye y’imiterere (Dosiye yo gusubizaho Wallet), irimwo imfunguruzo za bose zigira uruhara n’imiterere y’ibibanza. Iyi dosiye, ifatanijwe n’amajambo y’ingenzi yo gusubizaho, ituma wallet ishobora gusubira kwubakwa kuri porogarama iyo ari yo yose ihuye nka Sparrow canke Spectre.



## Inyungu n'aho bigarukira



### Ibintu bihambaye





- Bitcoin-gusa gukoresha bigabanya ubugoyagoye n'ingorane
- Ubufatanye bw'ibibanza vy'ububiko bw'ibintu vyinshi n'ubuyobozi bw'intambwe ku yindi
- Igikoresho cagutse wallet gifasha (Tapsigner, ikarita ikonje, Ledger, Jade, n'ibindi)
- Uburongozi buteye imbere bwa UTXO n'ubugenzuzi bw'ibiceri
- Ishobora gufatanywa na server ya Electrum y'umuntu ku giti ciwe
- Gufungura, kode y'inkomoko igenzurwa



### Intambamyi zo kwiyumvira





- Interface ahanini mu congereza
- Ibintu bimwe bimwe vy'agaciro (Igicu c'ububiko, Server ifashwa) bisaba kuvugururwa
- Multisig gutunganya bisaba amahugurwa y'intango



## Iciyumviro



Bitcoin Keeper igaragara nk’umuti wo gucungera amafaranga yawe. Uburyo bwayo butera imbere, kuva ku wallet yoroshe ishushe gushika ku Vaults zifise imikono myinshi, bisigura ko umutekano ushobora guhindurwa uko ivya nkenerwa bihinduka. Ubushobozi bwo gushiramwo mu buryo bworoshe ama wallets y’ibikoresho nka Tapsigner burafungura inzira y’imiterere ikomeye ataco igoranye cane.



Ivyerekeye bitcoin gusa, kode y’inkomoko yuguruye n’ukwubaha ubuzima bwite bituma ari uguhitamwo guhuye n’agaciro nyamukuru k’ibidukikije vya Bitcoin.



Iyi nyigisho ivuga ibiranga Bitcoin Keeper mu buryo bwayo bw’ubuntu. Iyi porogaramu kandi itanga ibikorwa vy’agaciro (Gufata amakuru mu gicu, Gufata amakuru mu gicu, Amafaranga y’amafaranga y’i Canary) bizoba ari ivy’inyigisho yihariye. Mu nsiguro izoza, tuzoba turiko turatohoza kandi igice co Gutegura Iragi, kigufasha gutegura ugurungika amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri ku bakunzi bawe, biciye ku bibanza vy’ububiko vy’amahera vy’ubuhinga bwa none (Enhanced Vaults) n’inyandiko zijana n’ivyo bikoresho vyinjijwe muri iyo porogarama.
