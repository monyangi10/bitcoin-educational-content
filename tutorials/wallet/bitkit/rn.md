Bitkit (https://www.bitkit.to) ni Wallet yoroshe, ariko ikomeye yo kwikingira. Uhe umuntu wese, aho ari ho hose, igihe cose.


Bitkit ni Wallet igendagenda yizigama iguha ububasha bwo gufata Ownership y’ukuri ya Bitcoin yawe kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha ku mategeko yawe. Ishizwemwo ibintu vyiza cane be n’umubumbe mwiza, Bitkit ifungura amahera yo kwishura umuntu wese, igihe cose, aho ari ho hose. Ivyo vyose mu gihe ari open-source yuzuye ku muntu wese yobigenzura.

## Ubuyobozi


Bitkit ni vy’ukuri vy’ukuri yoroshe gukoresha.


Nk’uko Bitcoin Wallet yuzuye, Bitkit irimwo ibikorwa vyose witega:


Ivyishyurwa vuba: Nta gusubira gukorana hagati y’amasakoshi y’amahera ya On-Chain n’aya Lightning. Bitkit irabivanga vyose ata nkomanzi.


Gucungera amafaranga: Ushobora gukoresha amahera ata ngorane hagati ya konti yawe y’amahera uzigamye n’ayo ukoresha kugira ngo wama ufise ubushobozi buhagije bwo kwishura ubwo nyene.


Invugo yo gusubiza: Gusubiza amafaranga yawe uzigamye kuri Wallet iyo ari yo yose ishigikira BIP 39.


Ivyiyumviro vy’Ukwisunga: Amakuru atari ay’agaciro ava muri Wallet yawe arasubirwamwo kugira ngo ushobore kwama usubizaho amahera wakoresheje.


Amateka y’Ibikorwa vy’Imigenderanire: Shira abo muvugana na bo kandi ushireko amazina y’ibikorwa vyawe kugira ngo bigume bitunganijwe.


![image](assets/en/01.webp)


Bitkit nayo irafise ubushobozi budasanzwe buyitandukanya:


Abashobora guhamagara: Sezera ku gusaba amaderesi canke amafagitire. Wongereko gusa abagenzi ku rutonde rw’abantu ushobora guhamagara maze ukabahemba.


Ibikoresho vy’ubuzima: Wongereko umunezero n’ubuhinga ku mugaragaro wawe w’inzu wa Wallet ukoresheje ibikoresho bikora ku mutima.


Social Profile: Fata ububasha ku rubuga rwawe rwa bose n’amahuza, kugira ngo abo muvugana bashobore kugushikira no kuguhemba igihe cose.


Konti zitagira ijambobanga: Injira ku mbuga zishigikira Slashtags canke Lightning.


QuickPay: Shiraho umupaka w’amahera y’umuravyo ari munsi y’amadolari 50, kandi Invoice yose iri munsi y’aho irihwa ubwo nyene—nta n’ugucapura, nta n’ugucererwa. Ibereye ku kwiruka ikawa canke gucapura amafaranga.


Iduka: Urihe Netflix, Airbnb, ibifungurwa, amakuru yo kuri telefone ngendanwa, n’ibindi, ukoresheje Bitcoin, imbere muri Bitkit nyene.


Nta mabanki. Nta gushikirana.

## Inyigisho y’abatanguzi kuri BitKit: Inyigisho y’intambwe ku yindi


### 1. Shiraho BitKit


Gufungura ububiko bw’amaporogarama (iOS) canke gukina kuri Google (Android).


Rondera “BitKit” maze wemeze ko ubona ikimenyetso c’ibara ry’umuhondo.


Kanda kuri Get canke Install, hanyuma Ufungure iyo umaze gukuraho.


Impanuro: Niwabona porogarama z’ibinyoma, nusuzume kabiri uwazisohoye. BitKit isohowe n’ishirahamwe ry’amaporogarama y’amajambo amwe.



### 2. Gutangura kwa mbere n'amasezerano


Tangaza BitKit, wemere Amabwirizwa y’Ikoreshwa, hanyuma ukande kuri Bandanya.


Kanda ku Tangira kugira ngo utangure urugendo rwo kwinjira mu ndege.


Nimwirengagize Gusubiza Wallet ubu; tuzokora Wallet nshasha.


### 3. Gusubiza inyuma Wallet seed yawe


BitKit ni self custodial, rero amajambo cumi n’abiri seed ni uburyo bwonyene bwo gukira.


Kanda ku Back Up Now iyo bisabwe.


Wandike ayo majambo cumi n’abiri ku mpapuro maze uyabike ahantu hatagira umurongo.


Wemeze amajambo igihe ubajijwe, hanyuma ukande kuri Save.


Imburi: Utakaze seed, utakaze ibiceri vyawe. Nta buto yo gusubiramwo.


### 4. Kuzigama n'ugukoresha


BitKit ihita irema konti zibiri:


Ivyo uzigamye: Bitcoin ishingiro Layer


Ukwimurira ibintu binini, bigenda buhoro, bitekanye


Ivyo ukoresha: Lightning Network


Ukwishura guto, guhita buri musi


Hindura hagati yavyo ukoresheje urupapuro ruri hejuru y’ibarabara ry’intango.


### 5. Kwakira Bitcoin on chain (Ivyo uzigamye)


Hitamwo Ivyo Uzigama.


Kanda kuri generate Bitcoin Address nshasha.


Usangire n’uwugurungikiye kode ya QR canke ukope Address.


Amahera azoboneka inyuma y’ukwemezwa kumwe. Confetti azohimbaza ukuza kwayo.


### 6. Kwakira Ibirenze Umuravyo (Gukoresha)


Hitamwo Gukoresha hanyuma ukande kuri Kwakira.


Injira umubare (nk’akarorero, 10 000 Sats) hanyuma ukande kuri Bandanya.


BitKit izogereranya amahera yo gufungura umurongo (ibisabwa vy’amahera).


Kanda ku Bandanya kuri generate a Umuravyo Invoice.


Reka uwugurungikiye akore scanner canke ashireko Invoice. Amahera arahera mu masegonda.



### 7. Wohereze amahera y'umuravyo


Kanda ku kimenyetso ca Scan, scanner umuravyo Invoice, canke uyishireko.


Injira amahera nimba bisabwa, hanyuma ukande kuri Continue.


Suzuma amahera wongereko ikimenyetso c’ubuhinga bwo kubika ibitabu.


Swipe to Pay, winjize PIN yawe, uraheza.


Ivyerekeye iyo nzira, harimwo n’amahera n’ama Timestamp, biraboneka mu gitabu c’Ibikorwa.


### 8. Rema urutonde rwawe n'abo ushobora guhura na bo


Kanda ku avatar iri hejuru ibubamfu.


Injira izina ry'iyerekanwa maze uhitemwo ifoto y'umwirondoro.


BitKit yerekana kode yawe bwite ya QR. Abagenzi barashobora kuyicapura kugira ngo bazokwongereko.


Wemere canke wanke igisabwa co kwemera ko abaguhamagara baguhemba.


Igihe umuntu yongeweko, ushobora gufyonda kw’izina ryiwe muri Contacts kugira ngo wohereze canke usabe Sats.


### 9. Gutahura ibikoresho

Kuva ku rubuga rw’intango, genda ku gice c’Ibikoresho. Kanda ku kimenyetso c'ivyuma kugira ngo wongereko canke uhindure:


Bitcoin Price Ticker – uhitemwo amafaranga ashika ku ma fiat ane maze ushireho igihe (umusi, indwi, ukwezi, umwaka).


Amakuru – imitwe y’amakuru iva ku masoko akomeye ya Bitcoin.


Bitcoin Ivy’ukuri – ibintu bitagira akamaro ku batangura.


Igiharuro – guhindura hagati ya Sats na fiat ubwo nyene.


Weather Widget – yerekana amafaranga y’urubuga “ikirere,” yerekana ibihe vyiza vyo gukoresha On-Chain (izuba risobanura ko hariho umudugararo muto).



### 10. Gurira na Bitcoin


BitKit ifatanya Bitrefill ku makarita y’ingabirano, kuzuza telefone, n’ibindi.


Kanda ku Duka mu nzira yo hasi.


Hitamwo Amakarata y’Ingabirano maze uhitemwo umucuruzi (nk’akarorero, Bitrefill).


Hitamwo umubare (ushobora gushika kuri 1 USD), wongereko email yo kugushikana, hanyuma ushiremwo.


Gukubita kugira wishyure. Kode y’ikarata y’ingabirano ishika kuri email mu masegonda makeyi.



### 11. Kwishura vyihuse


Quick Pay yemeza ubwayo amafagitire ya Lightning ari munsi y’umupaka (50 USD).


Genda kuri Amategeko → Muri rusangi → Kwishura vyihuta.


Tegura urubibi rwawe maze ushoboze umuhinduzi.


Ugugura utuntu dutoduto nk’ikawa ntitugikeneye gucapura.




### 12. Umutekano n’Ibanga


PIN Code – ushobora gukoresha munsi y’Ivyagezwe → Umutekano n’Ibanga maze ushireho kode y’imirongo itandatu.


Biometrics – guhindura kuri Face ID canke gufungura urutoke.


Auto-Lock – guhitamwo igihe co guhera (nk’amasegonda 90 ataco ukora).


Gusaba PIN kugira ngo wishyure – amahoro yo mu mutima y’inyongera.



### 13. Gucungera imirongo y'umuravyo


Umurongo ni iki?


Umuyoboro ni umuyoboro w’imigwi ibiri ufise amahera, ugabanywemwo:


Ivyo gusohoka – Bitcoin yawe, yahora yohereza.


Inbound – umugenzi Bitcoin, asabwa kwakira.


Kwimurira mu kuzigama gushika mu gukoresha


Gufungura Ukuzigama, ukande kuri Kwimurira mu Gukoresha.


Injira amahera (nk’akarorero, 25 000 ₿ ) hanyuma ukande kuri Continue.


Wemeze amahera maze urindire ko igikorwa ca On-Chain cemezwa.


Iyo imaze gufungurwa, uburinganire bw’amahera n’ubushobozi bwo kwakira birahindurwa.


Reba canke Funga umurongo


Amategeko → Ateye imbere → Amahuza y'umuravyo


Kanda ku nzira kugira ngo ubone uko ibintu vyifashe, amahera asigaye, n’amahera.


Tera kuri Close Connection nimba wipfuza kuyifunga maze usubize amahera mu Bubiko.


Iciyumviro: Kubera imvo z'amategeko, amafaranga yose yo muri BitKit agera ku ma euro amajana icenda na mirongo icenda y'agaciro ka Bitcoin.


### 14. Ibiranga biteye imbere


Guhitamwo Coin – guhitamwo ibintu vyihariye vya UTXO ku bijanye n’ubuzima bwite canke kugenzura amafaranga (Ivyagezwe → Ivyateguwe → Guhitamwo Coin).


![image](assets/en/03.webp)


Ivyihuta vy’Ibikorwa – hitamwo Buhoro, Bisanzwe, canke Vyihuta On-Chain amafaranga intego.


Amafaranga yo mu karere – hindura kuva ku madolari y’Amerika ukaja ku giciro gikomeye cose kiri munsi y’Ivyagezwe → Rusangiye.


## Urakoze cane


Warashizeho BitKit, uracungera seed yawe, uramenya neza kwishura Lightning, mbere uragura n’ikarata yawe ya mbere y’ingabirano. BitKit’s design ituma ibikoresho bikomeye vya Bitcoin bimeze neza, yamara wama ufise imfunguruzo. Gukuraho BitKit uno musi maze winjire muri Bitcoin Ownership y'ukuri. Ibibazo canke inyishu? Shikira umugwi wa BitKit ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi canke biciye ku nzira y’ugufasha iri muri porogarama.


**Iciyumviro ku bijanye no kugura canke kugurisha Bitcoin**: Bitkit ntishigikira kugura no kugurisha Bitcoin. Kugira ngo ugure canke ugurishe, ukoreshe amabarabara nka Bitfinex, hanyuma wohereze kuri Bitkit canke uyikuye kuri Bitkit.
