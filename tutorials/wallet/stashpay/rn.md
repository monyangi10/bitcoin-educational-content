Ubumenyi bw’abakoresha ni ikintu nyamukuru mu kwemera inyishu za Bitcoin kw’isi yose. Gutanga ubumenyi bubereye, bworoshe kandi butagira umutwaro mu vy’ubuhinga ni co kintu ca mbere ku bikoresho vyinshi vy’amasakoshi n’ibikoresho vya Exchange. Muri ivyo, StashPay igaragara kubera uburyo bwayo bwo gukora ibintu bike, mu gihe nyene yerekana ububasha bwa Lightning Network.



Muri iyi nyigisho, turaza kuraba iyi portfolio kugira tumenye ingene ikora n’ingene ari nziza ku bucuruzi buto buto canke aba solopreneurs.



## Gutangura na StashPay



StashPay ni Lightning yizigamira Wallet yemewe cane cane kubera ubumenyi bwayo buke, bushingiye ku bakoresha.  Iyi Wallet, ntukeneye ubumenyi bwo mu vy’ubuhinga bwo kwakira no kohereza ama satoshis yawe ya mbere.



StashPay ni umugambi w’inkomoko yuguruye wateguwe muri React Native kandi ugamije gutorera umuti ingorane y’amahera menshi y’ugucuruza mbere n’ugucuruza ku ruhererekane nyamukuru rwa porotokole ya Bitcoin. Iraboneka nk’iporogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa ku mbuga za Android na iOS biciye ku mahuza yo gukuraho amakuru ari kuri [urubuga](https://stashpay.me/).



![introduce](assets/fr/01.webp)



Ni vyiza gukura porogarama ya Android ku rubuga, kuko itaboneka kuri Google Play Store.


Igihe uzoba umaze gukuraho iyo porogarama, usabwe gutanga uruhusha rukenewe kugira ngo ushobore gushiramwo iyo porogarama kuri telefone yawe ya Android.



Iyo porogaramu imaze gushirwamwo, StashPay izogukorera Bitcoin Wallet y’intango igihe uzoyifungura ubwa mbere. Imbere y'uko ukora igikorwa cose, turagusavye gukora ububiko bw'iyi Wallet. Aha hepfo, uzosanga amabwirizwa yacu yose yo kumenya neza ko amajambo yawe yo gusubirana ashigikiwe neza.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

Ushobora gushika ku mirongo ya StashPay ukanda ku kimenyetso ca "Imirongo", hanyuma ukande ku **Rema ububiko**. Hanyuma wemerere kwerekana amajambo yo gusubizaho. Ntukope amajambo yawe yo gusubirana mu gicapo ca telefone yawe, kuko yoshobora gukoreshwa n’izindi porogarama z’ubuhendanyi zishizwe kuri telefone yawe ngendanwa.



![backup](assets/fr/02.webp)



Ushobora kandi kugarura Bitcoin Wallet usanzwe ukoresha ukanda ku **Recover Wallet** maze ugashiramwo amajambo yawe 12 canke 24 yo gugarura.



### Ushobora kwakira amasatoshi yawe ya mbere kuri StashPay



Ku rubuga rw’intango, fyonda ku buto **Receive** hanyuma ushireho umubare uruta umubare werekanwa mu mutuku. Mu gihe cacu, ntidushobora kwakira amadolari ari munsi ya 0.11 na StashPay Wallet.



![receive](assets/fr/03.webp)



Umaze gusobanura umubare, urashobora gufyonda kuri buto **Create Invoice**, hanyuma ugaca ukora scanner canke ukopa Invoice kugira ngo uyirungikire uwugurungikiye satoshis.



![receive_sats](assets/fr/04.webp)



Ushobora kubona amateka y'ibikorwa vyawe ukanda ku kimenyetso ca "isaha" kiri kuri paji y'intango.



![network_fee](assets/fr/05.webp)



Uzoba warabonye ko kugira uronke satoshis uzobwirizwa kwishura amahera y’urubuga. Ivyo bihembo bizokurwa ku satoshis ugiye kuronka. Ivyo ni kubera ko StashPay ari Wallet ishingiye ku gikoresho co guteza imbere Breez. Kugira ngo uronke ama satoshis n’ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’Igikoresho ata n’urudodo rw’umuravyo, Breez izosaba umukiriya (StashPay mu gihe cacu) `0.25% + 40 satoshis`. Ibindi ubimenye mu nyigisho yacu ya Misty Breez.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/mobile/misty-breez-738ced2a-0764-4d7f-a150-ec0ce84a9d25

### Wohereze amafaranga make na StashPay



Kurungika bitcoins na StashPay ni vyiza cane kubera Interface. Ku rubuga rw’intango, fyonda ku buto **Ohereza**. Scan code ya QR canke ushireko Address wipfuza koherezako satoshis. StashPay izoca imenya ubwo nyene uruhererekane rw’amasezerano ya Bitcoin wipfuza koherezako amafaranga y’ibiceri.



![send](assets/fr/06.webp)



Nk’uko StashPay ari Wallet ishingiye ku Kit y’Iterambere ya Breez, ivyungukira ku nyungu iryoshe cane: kohereza amafaranga y’ibiceri ku ruzitiro nyamukuru ku giciro gito. Breez ikoresha serivisi ya Boltz mu gukora ibikorwa hagati y’iminyororo itandukanye y’amasezerano ya Bitcoin, bikaba bishoboza abakiriya bashira mu ngiro Development Kit kwungukira kuri iyo serivisi ataco baciyeko mu gukoresha kwabo.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/exchange/centralized/boltz-34ad778e-6dc7-41c2-8219-e11e3361a43d

Ariko rero, Breez SDK itegekanya umubare mutoyi ushobora koherezako bitcoins ku Address ku ruzitiro rukuru.



![onchain](assets/fr/07.webp)



Ushobora kandi kohereza ama bitcoins ukoresheje umuravyo Address w'uwukwakira. Suzuma amakuru y'ivyo wakoresheje, hanyuma wemeze ukanda kuri buto **Ohereza**.



![confirm](assets/fr/08.webp)



## Ibindi vyatunganijwe



Mu mitunganyirize ya StashPay, urashobora guhindura imiterere kugira ngo ukoreshe Wallet.



StashPay iguha Exchange satoshis zishingiye ku mafaranga y’aho uba uhisemwo. Fyonda ku **Amafaranga**, hanyuma urondere amafaranga yawe ku rutonde rw’amafaranga +113 atangwa na StashPay.



![currencies](assets/fr/09.webp)



Mu **Kwakira amahitamwo**, uzosangamwo amasetingi yose yo kwakira bitcoins na StashPay. Nk’akarorero, mu guhitamwo **Hitamwo Umuravyo canke Onchain**, ushoboze Wallet yawe kwakira ama bitcoins avuye ku ruzitiro nyamukuru.



![receive-onchain](assets/fr/10.webp)



Ihitamwo rya **Scan OnChain addresses** rigufasha gusubiramwo amafaranga yawe ya Wallet mu kugenzura ama UTXO yose (ama bitcoins utarakoresha) ahuye n’amaderesi yawe atandukanye.



![rescan](assets/fr/11.webp)



**Export log** menu itanga urutonde rw'ibikorwa vyose vy'ibikorwa remezo vya Breez na Boltz bijanye n'ibikorwa vyawe n'uguhinduranya atome hagati y'iminyororo itandukanye y'amasezerano ya Bitcoin.



![export](assets/fr/12.webp)
