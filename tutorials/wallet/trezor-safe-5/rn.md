Trezor Safe 5 ni Hardware Wallet y’uruvyaro rwa nyuma yakozwe na SatoshiLabs kandi yatangujwe mu 2024. Ishizwe ahantu nk’iyi Safe 3 y’agaciro kanini, yibanze ku bijanye n’ubuhinga bwo gukora neza n’ugukomera. Ironka inyungu mu bijanye n’umutekano nk’iyo yari imbere yayo, Safe 3, ugereranyije n’iyitwa Model One na Model T.



Igiciro ca Safe 5 ni €169, iri mu rwego rw’ibikoresho vy’agaciro kanini Hardware Wallet, ihanganye n’ibikoresho nka Coldcard, Ledger Nano X na Flex, Jade Plus, Passport na Bitbox.



Safe 5 itandukanye n’iyindi n’ibara ry’inci 1,54, irindwa na *Gorilla Glass 3*, ishobora guhangana n’ugukubitwa n’ibikomere. Ifise kandi moteri *Trezor Touch* ikora ku mubiri itanga utunyiganyiga dutoduto iyo umuntu ayikozeko. Cokimwe na Safe 3, ifise secure element kandi ikora biciye ku nzira ya USB-C, yongeweko n’ibarabara rya Micro SD.



Itandukaniro rikomeye hagati ya Safe 3 na Safe 5 riri mu buryo igikoresho gikoreshwa, uretse ivyerekeye umutekano. Itera imbere cane mu gukoresha neza, ikaba ikora neza kandi ikaba igaragara neza. Ku bijanye n’umutekano, birangana.



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Safe 5 ifise ibintu vyose vy'ingenzi wokwitega kuri Hardware Wallet nziza, harimwo n'ugushiramwo neza cane passphrase BIP39. Ariko rero, ntirashigikira Miniscript.



Iyi modele irabereye cane cane abatangura n’abakoresha bari hagati. Ku rundi ruhande, bishobora kutahura n’ivyo vyose abakoresha bateye imbere barondera ibintu vyihariye biboneka ku bikoresho nka Coldcard. Naho biri ukwo, nimba udakeneye izo nzira ziteye imbere, Trezor Safe 5 yoshobora kuba ari yo nzira nziza cane.



## Igishushanyo c'umutekano wa Trezor Safe 5



Cokimwe na Safe 3, Trezor Safe 5 ifise **secure element** yemejwe na EAL6+, ni ugutera imbere gukomeye ku bigereranyo vya kera nka Model One na Model T. Iyi ni chip ya OPTIGA Trust M V3, itabika seed kugira ngo igere ku buryo butaziguye, ariko ikora nk’igice c’ubuhinga bwa cryptographic. secure element irafise ibanga rishobora gushikirwa gusa iyo uwuyikoresha amaze kwinjiza neza PIN. Iryo banga rero rikoreshwa mu gukuraho seed, iyo seed ikaba ibikwa mu buryo bw’ibanga mu bubiko nyamukuru bw’ico gikoresho.



Ubu buhinga bwo gucungera umutekano buvanze buratanga uburinzi bwiza bwo ku mubiri, cane cane ku bitero vyo gukuraho canke gusesangura, ingorane Model One yari ishobora guhura na zo, cane cane mu gucunga PIN. Ivyo bibazo ubu birakurwaho biciye ku gukoresha secure element. Iyi nzira kandi iragumya ubuhinga bwa porogarama yuguruye: kode icungera uguhingura no gukoresha imfunguruzo z’ibanga iguma ishobora gushikirwa kandi igasuzumwa. Igikoresho ca OPTIGA gicungera gusa kode ya PIN, ikintu kiri hanze y’uburongozi bw’urufunguzo rwa Bitcoin Wallet. Ni ugusohora ibanga rishobora gukoreshwa mu gusobanura seed gusa. Kandi, igice ca OPTIGA Trust M V3 kironka inyungu ku ruhusha rwo ku buntu, ruha uburenganzira SatoshiLabs bwo gutangaza ku buntu ibintu bishobora gutuma umuntu agira ingorane (NDA-Free).



Ico kigereranyo c’umutekano kigereranya, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, kimwe mu vyiza kuruta ibindi vyose vyo gusenyera ku mugozi umwe biri kw’isoko uno musi. Ihuza ivyiza vya secure element n’uburongozi bwa porogarama zifunguye. Mbere, abakoresha bategerezwa guhitamwo hagati y’umutekano wo ku mubiri wongereweko n’igice c’umubiri (chip) be n’uguserukira abantu mu buryo buboneka (open-source); na Trezor Safe, birashoboka ko umuntu ashobora kuvyungukirako vyose.



Muri iyi nyigisho, uzomenya ingene wotunganya no gukoresha Trezor Safe 5 yawe ata nkomanzi.



## Gufungura isanduku y'i Trezor 5



Iyo uronse Safe 5 yawe, urabe neza ko iyo sandugu na Seal bitagiramwo ikintu kugira ngo wemeze ko iyo paquete itafunguwe. Igenzura ry’ubuhinga bwa none ry’uko ico gikoresho ari ic’ukuri be n’uko kidatunganye na co nyene kizokorwa igihe kizoba carashizweho mu nyuma.



Ibiri mu gasandugu birimwo :




- Igitero c’Igiti 5;
- Igikapu kirimwo amakarata yo kwandikako amajambo yawe yo muri Mnemonic, amakete n’amabwirizwa;
- Umugozi wa USB-C ku USB-C.



Iyo ufunguye, Trezor Safe 5 yawe ikwiye kurindwa n’ipulasitike y’uburinzi kandi icuma ca USB-C gikwiye kurindwa n’igikoresho co gukingira Seal. Raba neza ko biri ng’aho.



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Kugendera ku gikoresho birasanzwe:




- Kora ku gice co hasi c’ibarabara kugira ngo ugende imbere;
- Nimuterere hasi kugira ngo musubire inyuma ;
- Kanda kandi ufate igicapo kugira ngo wemeze igikorwa.

## Gushinga kode ya PIN

Iyi kode ya PIN ntaco ikora mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwa none za Wallet yawe. Rero naho utagira uburenganzira bwo kuronka kode ya PIN, kugira ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo kuronka uburenganzira bwo kuronka amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri.

Ubu, ushobora gufyonda gusa ku kimenyetso ca Green kiri hejuru iburyo kugira ngo wemeze ko wipfuza gukoresha passphrase ataco irimwo (ni ukuvuga ata passphrase). ![Image](assets/fr/34.webp)



Ubu urashobora kubona ido n’ido ry’urupapuro rwawe rwa Wallet, harimwo n’urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse rwa konti yawe ya mbere. Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Apply*" kugira ngo uheze gukora Wallet.
