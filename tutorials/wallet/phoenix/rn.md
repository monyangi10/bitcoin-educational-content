Phoenix ni Lightning Wallet n’uruzitiro rwikingira rwakozwe na ACINQ, ishirahamwe ry’Abafaransa ryitaho gutorera umuti ibibazo vy’amaporogarama ashingiye ku Lightning. Udakunze ama wallets ya Lightning nk’aya Wallet ya Satoshi, aho bitcoins zifatwa n’uwundi muntu, Phoenix ishoboza abakoresha kuguma bafise ububasha bwose ku mfunguruzo zabo z’ibanga.


Phoenix ikora nk’urudodo rw’umuravyo rw’ukuri rwinjijwe muri telefone yawe, igaca ifungura umurongo ufise urudodo rw’umuravyo rwa ACINQ. Iryo koraniro rishingiye kuri Lightning-KMP, ubuhinga bwo gukoresha Lightning Network muri Kotlin, bugenewe neza amasakoshi y’amatelefone ngendanwa. Udakunze ibindi bisubizo vy’imiravyo, Phoenix yorosha cane uburongozi. Uwukoresha ntakeneye gukorana n'ugufungura no gufunga umurongo, gukoresha urudodo rwa Bitcoin, canke gucunga amafaranga kuri Lightning Network. Phoenix yitwararika ivyo bikorwa vyose vy’ubuhinga mu nyuma.


Iyi porogaramu ihuriza hamwe ukworoha gukoresha n’ugukoresha neza amasakoshi y’umuravyo agendagenda n’umutekano n’ubusegaba bw’urudodo rw’umuravyo nyakuri rw’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Phoenix ituma bishoboka gukoresha Lightning Network mu buryo butekanye, bubereye kandi bwigenga, mu gihe umuntu anezererwa n’ubumenyi bw’abakoresha bugenda neza kandi busanzwe.


Mu gusubiza, amafaranga amwamwe arakoreshwa:




- Kurungika biciye ku Muravyo bitwara 0,4% vy’amahera wongeyeko 4 Sats ;
- Iyo amafaranga akenewe kugira ngo umuntu aronke biciye ku Lightning, 1% y’amahera arafatwa;
- Buri nzira igura 1000 Sats kugira ngo ifungurwe.


Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, Phoenix iserukira umuti mwiza cane wo hagati hagati y’ibipapuro vy’umuravyo vy’ububiko n’uburongozi bw’amaboko bw’uruzitiro rw’umuravyo. Iyi porogaramu irabereye abatangura n’abateye imbere bakunda kudakorana n’ibintu vy’ido n’ido vyo gucunga LND canke Core Lightning yabo. Reka tumenye ingene twoyikoresha!

## Bika amajambo Mnemonic


Imbere y’uko dutangura, turakeneye kubika ijambo ryacu ry’amajambo 12 Mnemonic. Iyi mvugo itanga uburenganzira bwo gushika ku bitcoins zawe zose, ataco zibujijwe. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere n’aho atashobora gukoresha telefone yawe.


Iryo jambo ry’amajambo 12 risubizaho uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe y’ama bitcoins iyo telefone yawe itakaye, yibwe canke ivunitse. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko umuntu ayizigama neza kandi akayibika ahantu heza.


Ushobora kuyandika ku mpapuro canke, kugira ngo urushirizeho gutekana, ukayicapura ku cuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo uyikingire umuriro, umwuzure canke ngo isenyuke. Guhitamwo uburyo bwo gukoresha Mnemonic yawe bizovana n’ingene ukoresha umutekano, ariko nimba uriko urakoresha Phoenix nk’umurongo w’amahera Wallet ukoresha urimwo amahera aringaniye, impapuro zikwiye kuba zihagije.


Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwiza bwo kubika no gucunga ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, ndagusavye cane gukurikira iyi yindi nyigisho, cane cane nimba uri umutanguzi:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

## Gutegura ubusabe


Imbere yo gukora amafaranga yawe ya mbere, urashobora guhindura amasetingi mu gufyonda ku kimenyetso c’uruziga rw’amano kiri hasi ibubamfu bwa Interface.


![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)


Mu "*Display*", ushobora guhitamwo insanganyamatsiko y'ikoreshwa, igiciro gikoreshwa kuri Bitcoin, n'amahera yawe yo mu karere.


![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)


Mu "*Amahitamwo yo kwishura*", uzosangamwo amasetingi atandukanye ateye imbere y'ukwishura kwa Lightning. Ushobora kuguma ufise ivyo washizeho.


![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)


Mu "*Uburongozi bw'imirongo*", shiraho amafaranga menshi cane witeguriye kwishura igihe ufungura umurongo wa Lightning.


![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)


Mu nzira ya "*Access control*", ndagusavye cane ko ukoresha uburyo bwo kwemeza ko umuntu ari uwundi kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha porogarama iri kuri telefone yawe. Ivyo bizotuma umuntu wese afise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha telefone yawe ifunguye adashobora gushika kuri Phoenix no kwiba amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri.


![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)


Mu "*Electrum server*", iyo ufise server ya Electrs, urashobora kuyifatanya kugira ngo utangaze amafaranga yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)


Kugira ngo wongere ibanga ry'amahuza yawe, fungura amahuza biciye kuri Tor mu rutonde rwa "*Tor*". Naho gukoresha Tor bishobora gutuma utanga amahera makeyi, kandi bisaba ko porogarama ya Phoenix yuguruye imbere igihe uyironka, birongereza cane ubuzima bwite bwawe.


![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)


## Kwakira amafaranga y'ibiceri On-Chain


Ku gukoresha kwa mbere, urafise uburenganzira bwo gushiramwo amahera ya On-Chain muri Phoenix Wallet yawe. Ushobora kandi gutanga iyo deposit ya mbere uhereye kuri Lightning (raba igice gikurikira), ariko muri ivyo vyose, amahera y’inyongera azosaba kugira ngo ufungure umurongo wawe wa mbere.


Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Kwakira*".


![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)


Gukuraho kode ya QR ibubamfu kugira ngo ubone Bitcoin yakira Address. Wohereze amahera wipfuza gushiramwo muri Phoenix kuri iyo Address.


![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)


Amahera waronse On-Chain azobanza kugaragara nk’atarindiriye munsi y’amahera yawe ya Wallet. Bizotwara ivyemezo 3 imbere y’uko amafaranga aboneka kugira ngo akoreshwe.


![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)


Amahera amaze gushika, Phoenix iragufungurira umurongo wa Lightning. Ubu ushobora kohereza no kwakira ama bitcoins biciye kuri Lightning Network.


![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)


## Kwakira ama bitcoins biciye ku muravyo


Kugira ngo uronke Sats biciye kuri Lightning Network, ukande kuri buto ya "*Receive*".


![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)


Phoenix itanga umuravyo Invoice. Ushobora kuyicapura canke ukayirungikira uwo yipfuza kugurungikira Sats.


![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)


Ufyonda kuri buto ya "*Edit*", urashobora kwongerako insobanuro izoboneka ku wuguha kuri Invoice, kandi ugasobanura umubare wihariye uwuguha ategerezwa kohereza.


![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)


Amafagitire ya kera twavuze haruguru ashobora gukoreshwa rimwe gusa. Ku bijanye n’uburyo bwo kwishura bushobora gusubirwamwo, urashobora gukoresha kode yawe ya QR ishobora gusubirwamwo, ari yo nzira ya BOLT12.


![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)


Igihe igiciro ca Invoice canke BOLT12 kizoba cararangijwe, igiciro kizoboneka kuri Lightning Wallet yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)


## Wohereze bitcoins biciye ku Muravyo


Ubu ko ufise Sats kuri Phoenix, witeguriye kwishura biciye kuri Lightning Network. Tangana ukanda kuri buto ya "*Send*".


![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)


Hariho uburyo bwinshi bwo guhitamwo. Ukanda kuri "*Scan QR code*", urashobora gucapura Invoice y'umuravyo, igiciro ca BOLT12, canke mbere Address yakira kugira ngo wishyure On-Chain.


![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)


Ushobora kandi kwinjiza aya makuru n'amaboko biciye ku klavye iri mu kibanza kiri hejuru ya Interface, canke winjize Address y'umuravyo (BOLT12 canke LNURL). Ushobora kandi gushiramwo amakuru ukoresheje buto ya "*Paste*".


![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)


Ku karorero, narasuzumye Invoice ku 10.000 Sats. Kugira ngo wishyure, fyonda gusa kuri "*Pay*".


![Image](assets/fr/30.webp)


Ivyo gucuruza birarangiye.


![Image](assets/fr/31.webp)
