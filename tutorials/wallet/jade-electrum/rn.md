_Iyi nsiguro yakuwe mu cigwa c'amahugurwa ya Bitcoin



Inyigisho ikoreshwa na Jade Classic, ariko ibikorwa birakora no ku bafise Jade Plus.



Uhejeje gutangura Jade, ushobora gutangura kuyikoresha kandi--kugira ngo ubikore--uhitemwo ikigaragaza wallet.



Jade ni igikoresho gishobora gukoreshwa na wallet nyinshi, canke amaporogarama y’abagenzi nk’uko Blockstream ibivuga ku rubuga rwayo.



Muri iyi nyigisho uzobona intambwe zo gukoresha Electrum Wallet, biciye ku nzira ya USB.



## Kwimurira urufunguzo rwa bose



Fata maze ushireko Jade yawe yatangujwe. Ukimara kuyifungura isa n’iyi:




![img](assets/en/32.webp)



Niwahitamwo _Unlock Jade_, uzoronka menu aho utegerezwa guhitamwo ingene uzofatanya igikoresho cawe na app y'umugenzi.



Na Electrum ushobora gufatanya Jade gusa biciye kuri USB, rero uhitemwo ubu buryo.



Gutanguza Electrum, izofungura gutanga nk'uburyo bwo gufungura wallet ya nyuma ikoreshwa.



Niba ari ubwa mbere uhuza Jade na Electrum, hitamwo _Rema Igikapu Gishasha_ hanyuma _Heza_.



![img](assets/en/34.webp)



Izina wallet.



![img](assets/en/35.webp)



Hitamwo Itegeko Wallet.

Igihe uhisemwo ububiko bw'urufunguzo, ni ngombwa guhitamwo _Koresha igikoresho c'ubuhinga_.



![img](assets/en/37.webp)



Electrum itangura gupima igikoresho c’ubuhinga.



![img](assets/en/38.webp)



Mu gufatanya USB na mudasobwa (isanzwe ifatanye ku ruhande rwa USB C na Jade), ibikoresho vya wallet birakubonekera mu buryo bwo gufunga. Jade ifungura mu gushiramwo PIN y’imirongo itandatu mu gihe co gutegura.




![img](assets/en/39.webp)



Igikoresho c’ivyuma kifunguwe, Electrum kimenya Jade. Bandanya ukanda _Ibikurikira_.



![img](assets/en/40.webp)



Kuri iyi nkuru Electrum iragusaba gushinga inyandiko y'amategeko: uhitemwo _Native Segwit_.



![img](assets/en/41.webp)



Icigwa co guhindura urufunguzo rwa bose kuva kuri wallet kuva kuri Jade gushika ku kwerekana Electrum kiratangura.



Iyo urufunguzo rwa bose rwoherezwa hanze rurangiye, igikorwa kiraheza.



Isaha-gusa irateguwe kandi Electrum iramenyesha ko irangiriye n’ikigaragaza gikurikira.



![img](assets/en/42.webp)



wallet mu vy'ukuri yararemwe kandi urashobora gutangura kuyitohoza: ushobora kubona _amaderesi_, amakuru _y'ipoke_, kandi-ikiruta vyose-ushobora kubona mu mfuruka yo hasi iburyo ikimenyetso c'uko ari igikoresho ca Blockstream. Akadomo k’icatsi kibisi kari iruhande y’ikimenyetso ca Blockstream kerekana ko igikoresho gifunguye kandi ko gifatanye neza n’urubuga rwo mu karere.



![img](assets/en/43.webp)



## Kwakira no gukoresha amafaranga



Kuva ku _Kwakira_ urutonde rwa Electrum, generate `scriptPubKey` (aderesi) yo kwakira amahera. Igihe cose utangure n’amahera makeyi maze ukore ikibazo co kwakira+gukoresha.



![img](assets/en/44.webp)



Umaze kwakira satss, ushobora kumenya aho bashitse mu _History_ menu.



![img](assets/en/45.webp)



![img](assets/en/46.webp)



Iyo amafaranga yemejwe, urashobora gukoresha iyi UTXO maze ugaheza ikibazo.



Ivyo bihembo birimwo gukoresha Jade kugira ngo asinye.



Genda kuri _Send_ menu ya Electrum, ushireko scriptPubKey, hanyuma uyisuzume neza.



![img](assets/en/47.webp)



Niwaheza, ukande _Pay_.



Idirisha ry’ugucuruza rifunguka, aho bihambaye gushinga amafaranga y’ugucuruza akwiriye. Niwaheza gukora ivyo vyose ukande kuri _Preview_ mu mfuruka yo hasi iburyo.



![img](assets/en/48.webp)



Idirisha ry'ibikorwa ryerekana ibintu bimwe bimwe bihambaye, imbere ya vyose ikibanza: `Unsigned`.



Kuri iyi ntambwe ushobora kandi kubona itegeko _Sign_, utegerezwa gukanda kugira ngo ushireko umukono na Jade.



![img](assets/en/49.webp)



Ubu rero hatangura igice c’uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’ikigaragaza wallet n’igikoresho c’ivyuma, aho Electrum ikumenyesha gukurikiza amabwirizwa ari ku gikoresho c’ivyuma, ifunguye kandi yiteguriye gusinya.



![img](assets/en/50.webp)



**Ubwa mbere, ariko, woba ushobora kugenzura ivyo uriko urashirako umukono: amaparametere yose y'isoko uherutse gushinga, na yo nyene araboneka kuri Jade** kandi urashobora kuyagenzura yose.



![img](assets/en/51.webp)



Kugira ngo ukomeze urabe ko wama ushira urudome ku mwampi `→` ujana ku ntambwe zikurikira kandi ntuzokwigere ushira ku `X` kiretse ushaka guheza igikorwa utagirangije.



Igice co kugenzura kirangirana n’iyerekanwa ry’amahera. Muri iki gihe kwemeza ni nk’ugushirako umukono wawe.



![img](assets/en/52.webp)



Mu mwanya mutoyi Jade akora igikorwa, iyo kirangiye kigasubira ku rutonde rw’i muhira.



![img](assets/en/53.webp)



Mu gihe uri kuri Electrum ushobora kubona uko ibikorwa vy'ubudandaji vyifashe, vyahindutse kuva kuri `Unsigned` gushika kuri `Signed` kandi ubu birashoboka, kuri wewe, kubikwiragiza ukanda _Broadcast_.



![img](assets/en/54.webp)



wallet, rero igeragejwe, irashobora gukoreshwa mu kwakira UTXO igenewe kubikwa neza.



![img](assets/en/55.webp)



Iyi nkuru ni akarorero k’ingene wokoresha Jade yawe, ihuye biciye kuri USB, ku isaha ya wallet gusa. Electrum ni akarorero ka kera, ariko ushobora guhitamwo izindi porogarama za wallet. Jade yohereza urufunguzo rwa bose mu bindi bikoresho vyinshi: rondera ibikorwa bisa n’ivyo wasomye muri iyi nyigisho, kugira ngo bikuyobore kandi uronke ingene wobikoresha app companio ukunda cane.
