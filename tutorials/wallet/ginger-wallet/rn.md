Ginger Wallet ni inkomoko yuguruye, idashobora kubikwa Bitcoin yibanze ku mabanga n’ubuzima bwite. Yatanguye ubuzima nk’uko fork ivuye kuri Wasabi Wallet (inyuma ya verisiyo 2.0.7.2 - uruhusha rwa MIT).



Ginger Wallet igumya ubuhinga bwo kwubaka bwa Wasabi mu gihe yongerako ibintu bikeyi vyihariye. Dushingiye ku nyandiko za Ginger Wallet, Wasabi ishimika ku **ukwigenga no kugenzura**, mu gihe Ginger yibanda ku **kworohereza gukoresha, umutekano n’ubumenyi bworoshe bushobora gukoreshwa n’umuryango, bigatuma umuntu ashobora kuyironka mu buryo bworoshe.



Ginger Wallet ni porogaramu ya wallet ikoreshwa kuri mudasobwa gusa (nta porogaramu yo kuri telefone ngendanwa).



## None Coinjoin ni iki?



**coinjoin** ni uburyo budasanzwe bwo gukorana Bitcoin buhuriza hamwe abantu benshi mu gukorana hamwe. Ubwo buryo buravanga ivyo abakoresha batandukanye binjira mu gikorwa kimwe, bikaba bigoye cane - nimba atari kenshi bidashoboka, iyo bikozwe neza - gukurikirana amahera. Ivyo bituma bica bigorana cane ko umuntu wo hanze ashobora kumenya ata gukeka aho ama bitcoins ari muri ivyo akomoka n’aho aja, bitandukanye n’ivyo mu bikorwa bisanzwe vy’ubudandaji bwa Bitcoin.



Kuri wewe nk’ukoresha, coinjoin ifasha kuzigama ibanga ryawe. Nk’akarorero, iyo uronse intererano y’ama sats 10.000 kuri aderesi ya Bitcoin, uwugurungikiye arashobora gukurikirana ayo mahera, rimwe na rimwe, akabona ko ufise amahera menshi cane, canke akabona ibikorwa vyawe. Mu gukora coinjoin inyuma y’iyi mpano y’ama sats 10.000, uba uciye uburenganzira bwo gukurikirana: uwugurungikiye ntazosubira kuronka amakuru yose yerekeye wewe muri iyo nkunga.



Ico gikoresho ca Chaumian coinjoin gitanga umutekano mwinshi cane, kuko amahera aguma ari munsi y’ububasha bw’uwukoresha wenyene ibihe vyose. Mbere n’abakoresha ama server ahuza ntibashobora guhindura amafaranga y’abaje mu nama uko vyogenda kwose. Nta n’umwe mu bakoresha canke abahuzabikorwa akwiye kwizigirana: umwe wese aguma afise ububasha ku mfunguruzo ziwe z’ibanga, kandi aguma ari we wenyene yemerewe kwemeza amafaranga. Nta wundi muntu rero ashobora kwigarurira amafaranga yawe mu gihe c’ugukorana, canke ngo ashingeho isano hagati y’ivyo winjiza n’ivyo usohora.

## Gukoresha ama bitcoins



Biroroshe kugura no kugurisha Bitcoin ukoresheje Ginger Wallet. Mu ntambwe nkeyi gusa, urashobora gukoresha amahera yawe ya bitcoins.



### Gugura ama bitcoins



![video](https://youtu.be/lEqTBzm5MEA)



Abakoresha Ginger Wallet barashobora kugura amafaranga y’ibiceri.





- Kanda kuri buto ya **Gura**. Iryo buto riguma riboneka naho wallet yoba ari ubusa.



![screen](assets/fr/32.webp)





- Hitamwo igihugu cawe, canke mbere intara yawe (mu turere tumwetumwe, nka Canada), imbere y’uko utangura kugura bitcoin. Nkako, iyo ukanda kuri **Gura** ku ncuro ya mbere, uzokenera kandi kugaragaza akarere kawe.



![screen](assets/fr/33.webp)



Kanda **Bandanya** kugira ngo utere imbere mu nzira yo kugura.





- Hanyuma winjize umubare w’ama bitcoins wipfuza kugura mu kibanza cagenewe. Ushobora kandi guhitamwo amahera yo gukoresha.



![screen](assets/fr/34.webp)



Ifaranga ryose rifise igipimo gitoyi n’igipimo kinini co kugura. Nk’akarorero, mu madolari y’Amerika, igipimo co hejuru ni amadolari 30.000.



Niba umaze kugura, urashobora kubona amateka yawe y’ugugura ukanda kuri buto **Amategeko ya kera**. Urutonde rw’ibikorwa vya kera n’aho vyifashe bizokwerekanwa.





- Hitamwo ivyo ushobora guhabwa bigubereye.



Muri iki gihe, uzobona urutonde rw’ibintu vyose biriho. Ku vyo utanga vyose, ufise :




 - izina ry’umuguzi (1) ;
 - umubare w’ama bitcoins ungana n’amahera yinjijwe mbere, uburyo bwo kwishura n’amahera yo kugura (2) ;
 - buto ya **Kwemera** (3).



![screen](assets/fr/35.webp)



Amahera yerekanwa mu vyo utanga ntabwo ari ikindi giciro. Biramaze gushirwa mu giciro cose c’ivyo umuntu atanga.



Imfuruka yo hejuru iburyo bw'ibarabara, yanditsweko **Vyose**, iragufasha kuyungurura amashimwe hakurikijwe uburyo bwo kwishura. Uburyo bwo kwishura wahisemwo buzoshirwaho ku buryo busanzwe, ariko bushobora guhindurwa igihe cose.



![screen](assets/fr/36.webp)



Niwabona ikintu kibereye, ukande kuri buto **Emera** kugira ngo ukomeze ugugura. Uzoca urungikwa kuri paji y’ugurisha, aho ushobora guheza igikorwa co gucuruza.



### Kugurisha ama bitcoins



Abakoresha Wallet barashobora kugurisha Bitcoin. Buto ya **Kugurisha** iboneka gusa iyo hari amahera ari muri portfolio.





- Fyonda kuri **Kugurisha**.



![screen](assets/fr/37.webp)





- Nk’uko biri ku bijanye n’uguhitamwo **Gugura**, iyo ukoresheje igikorwa co kugurisha ku ncuro ya mbere, utegerezwa guhitamwo igihugu cawe imbere yo gukomeza kugurisha amafaranga y’ibiceri.





- Igikurikira, ukeneye kwinjiza umubare w’ama Bitcoins wipfuza kugurisha. Ushobora kwinjiza ayo mahera muri BTC canke mu mafaranga y’amahera nk’idolari ry’Amerika (USD).





- Ivyo umaze kubikora, uzobona urutonde rw’ibintu bishobora guhabwa. Hitamwo igiciro co kugurisha kigubereye, hanyuma ukande **Emera** kugira ngo ukomeze.





- Ubu ukeneye gusozera igikorwa:
 - Uhejeje kwemera ivyo baguhaye, uzoca urungikwa kuri paji y’uwuguha;
 - Kurikiza amabwirizwa ari kuri paji y'umuguzi ;
 - Hari igihe uzoronka aderesi y’uwuzokwakira n’amahera nyayo uzorungika;
 - Hanyuma usubire kuri Ginger Wallet kugira ngo ukomeze iyo nzira;
 - Usubiye muri Ginger Wallet, akazu k'ikiganiro kazoboneka, kagufashe kubandanya ukanda **Ohereza**.



Ivyo bizofungura **Send** screen irimwo aderesi y'uwuzokwakira n'amahera yuzuye imbere y'igihe. Ushobora kandi gukoresha buto ya **Kohereza** iri ku rubuga rw'intango. Naho ushobora kohereza amafaranga n’amaboko, turagusavye kuyaheza biciye mu gasandugu k’ikiganiro kugira ngo ukore neza.



## Gukora igice c'amahera kuri Ginger Wallet



![Vidéo](https://youtu.be/AJe67RDfB1A)



Gukingira ibanga ry'amahera yawe n'**Coinjoin**, yinjijwe ataco ihinduye muri Ginger Wallet. wallet ikoresha **WabiSabi**, uburyo bwo gukorana n’abandi bwa Chaumian bwagenewe gutuma habaho uburyo bwo gukorana n’abandi mu buryo bushoboka kandi bukora neza.



Ni wewe uhitamwo ubuhinga bwo gukora coinjoin (automatique canke manuel) bugubereye cane.



Ginger Coinjoin irateguye gukoreshwa ukimara kuyikurako (nta ntambwe z’inyongera zikenewe). Ubwo nyene, Ginger Coinjoin ikora inyuma kugira ngo ikingire ubuzima bwite bwawe igihe cose ukora. Mu bikorwa, umukinyi wa Coinjoin azoboneka igihe cose uzoba ufise umubare ushobora gutamenyekana.



Naho ku bijanye no gutangura coinjoin n’amaboko, ni igikorwa co gukanda rimwe. Tangira urugendo maze urindire ko igikorwa ca coinjoin cubakwa kandi cemezwa. Uzobona igiharuro c'ukutamenyekana mu kigaragaza.



Ivyiyumviro vyinshi birashobora gukorwa gushika umuntu ashitse ku rugero rwifuzwa rwo kutamenyekana. Ushobora kandi gukuraho ibice bimwebimwe mu vyo uvanga.



Ku mburabuzi, Ginger ikoresha umuhuzabikorwa wayo bwite afise amaparametere yose yatunganijwe mbere n’amahera yizewe. Coinjoins z’ibimenyetso bifise agaciro karenga 0.03 BTC zitera 0.3% y’amahera y’umuhuzabikorwa yongerako amahera ya mining. Ivyinjira bifise 0.03 BTC canke hasi, hamwe n’ivyo guhindura, birasonerwa amahera y’umuhuzabikorwa, mbere n’inyuma y’ugucuruza rimwe. Rero, ukwishyura gukorwa n’amahera ya Coinjoin bituma uwurungika n’uwuronka bompi bashobora gusubiramwo ibiceri vyabo ata mahera y’umuhuzabikorwa.



Ginger akunda coinjoins n’abantu benshi kuruta ama rounds mato, yihuta. Coinjoins nini zitanga ukutamenyekana kwinshi, ibiciro bito n’ubushobozi bwinshi bw’ahantu h’amabuye.




## Umutekano n'ibikorwa vyiza



Icipfuzo co kwegereza ubutegetsi abaturage no kubungabunga ubuzima bwite bisaba kwemera uburyo bwiza bwinshi:




- Imisi yose ushire seedphrase yawe ahantu hatagira umurongo;
- Niwatakaza mudasobwa yawe canke wikeka ko hari uwuyironka ata wemerewe, uce ukora wallet nshasha ubwo nyene. Kwimurira amahera yawe muri iyi portfolio nshasha hanyuma usibe iya kera;
- Koresha aderesi itandukanye ku bijanye n’ukwakira abantu kugira ngo wirinde gusubira gukoresha aderesi;
- Igihe cose ushobora gukuraho ibisabwa vyawe vy’ibitabo gusa kuri konti yemewe ya GitHub canke urubuga rwemewe.



Ubu uramenyereye gukoresha porogarama ya Ginger Wallet kugira ngo wohereze, wakire no gukoresha amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri.
