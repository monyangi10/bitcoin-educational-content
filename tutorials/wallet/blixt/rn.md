## Blixt - Ubwa kabiri wo guhamagara


Ok rero ubu dufise umurongo wa LN ufise amafaranga OUTBOUND gusa. Ivyo bisigura ko dushobora GUTUMA gusa, ntidushobora KWAKIRA Sats kuri LN.


![blixt](assets/en/09.webp)


Kubera iki? Woba warasomye uburongozi bwerekanwa mu ntango? Oya? Subirayo ubisome. Ni vyiza cane gutahura ingene imirongo ya LN ikora.


![blixt](assets/en/10.webp)


Nk’uko mushobora kubibona muri aka karorero, umurongo ufunguka n’amahera ya mbere, ntugire amahera menshi cane INBOUND (“Ashobora kwakira”) ariko afise amahera menshi OUTBOUND (“Ashobora kohereza”).


None ni amahitamwo ayahe ufise, nimba ushaka kwakira Sats nyinshi kuruta LN?



- Gukoresha Sats zimwe zimwe zivuye ku muhora uriho. Ego, LN ni urubuga rwo kwishura rwa Bitcoin, rukoreshwa cane cane mu gukoresha Sats yawe vyihuta, bihendutse, vy’umwihariko kandi vyoroshe. LN SI uburyo bwo gutera, kubera ivyo ufise Wallet.



- Guhindura Sats, usubire mu Wallet yawe, ukoresheje ubuhinga bwo guhindura ubwato bwo munsi y’amazi. Muri ubwo buryo nturiko urakoresha Sats yawe, ahubwo uriko urayisubiza Wallet yawe bwite. Aha ushobora kubona mu buryo burambuye uburyo bumwe bumwe, muri [Urupapuro rw'Inyigisho za Blixt](https://blixtwallet.github.io/inyigisho).



- Gufungura umurongo wa INBOUND uva ku mutanga LSP wese. Aha niho hari video yerekana ingene wokoresha LNBig LSP mu gufungura umurongo winjira. Ivyo bisigura ko uzoriha amahera makeyi ku muhora w’UBUSA (ku ruhande rwawe) maze uzoshobora kwakira Sats nyinshi muri uwo muhora. Nimba uri umucuruzi uronka vyinshi kuruta ivyo ukoresha, iyo ni inzira nziza. Kandi nimba uriko ugura Sats ku LN, ukoresheje Robosats canke uwundi LN Exchange.



- Gufungura umurongo wa Dunder, n'urudodo rwa Blixt canke uwundi mutanga LSP wa Dunder. Umurongo wa Dunder ni uburyo bworoshe bwo kuronka amahera INBOUND, ariko muri ico gihe nyene ushiramwo Sats muri uwo murongo. Ni vyiza kandi kuko izofungura umurongo ukoresheje [UTXO] (UTXO) itavuye kuri Blixt Wallet yawe. Ivyo birongereza ubuzima bwite. Ni vyiza kandi kuko, iyo udafise Sats mu onchain Wallet, kugira ngo ufungure umurongo usanzwe wa LN, ariko ukaba ufise mu wundi LN Wallet, ushobora gusa kwishura kuri uwo wundi Wallet biciye kuri LN ugufungura n’ugushiramwo (ku ruhande rwawe) uwo muhora wa Dunder. [Ibindi bisobanuro vy'ingene Dunder ikora n'ingene wokoresha server yawe bwite hano](https://github.com/hsjoberg/dunder-lsp).


![blixt](assets/en/11.webp)


Dore intambwe zo gufungura umurongo wa Dunder:



- Genda kuri Settings, mu gice ca “Ivyageragejwe” ushireho akazu ka “Gushoboza Dunder LSP”.
- Ivyo umaze kubikora, subira hejuru ku gice ca “Lightning Network” uzobona ko habonetse uburyo bwo guhitamwo “Set Dunder LSP Server”. Aho, ku buryo busanzwe hariho “https://dunder.blixtwallet.com” ariko ushobora kuyihindura n’uwundi mutanga LSP wa Dunder Address. [Aha ni urutonde rw’abanyamuryango b’i Blixt] n’ibihimba bishobora gutanga imirongo ya Dudner LSP ya Blixt yawe.
- Ubu ushobora kuja kuri screen main ugafyonda kuri buto ya “Receive”. Hanyuma ukurikize ubu buryo [busobanuwe muri iyi nsiguro](https://blixtwallet.github.io/uburongozi#uburongozi-lsp).


OK, rero umurongo wa Dunder umaze kwemezwa (bizofata iminota mike) uzoheza ufise imirongo 2 ya LN: imwe yuguruwe mu ntango n’umudereva w’indege (umurongo A) n’iyindi ifise amahera menshi yinjira, yuguruwe na Dunder (umurongo B).

### UBUHITANIRO B - Gusubizaho Wallet


Mu bihe vya kera naranditse ubuyobozi bwihariye ku [ingene wosubizaho urudodo rwa Umbrel rwasenyutse] (https://darth-Coin.github.io/nodes/shtf-restore-LND-node-ru.html), aho navuze kandi uburyo bwo gukoresha uback the GW channel nk’uko. dosiye ivuye ku Mutaka.


Nanditse kandi ubuyobozi bw’ingene wosubizaho urudodo rwawe rwa Blixt canke wimurira Blixt yawe ku kindi gikoresho, [hano](https://blixtwallet.github.io/faq#blixt-restore).


![blixt](assets/en/13.webp)


Ariko reka dusigure mu ntambwe zoroshe iyo nzira. Nk’uko ushobora kubibona kw’ishusho iri hejuru, hari ibintu 2 ukwiye gukora kugira ngo usubizeho node yawe ya kera ya Blixt/LND:



- top box niho utegerezwa kwuzuza amajambo yose 24 yo muri seed yawe (node ​​ya kera / yapfuye)
- hasi hariho amahitamwo abiri y'ubuto bwo kwinjiza / gushiramwo dosiye ya channel.backup, mbere yabitswe ku nzira yawe ya kera ya Blixt/LND. Bishobora kuva muri dosiye yo mu karere (urayishira mu gikoresho cawe mbere) canke bishobora kuba bivuye kuri Google drive / iCloud ahantu kure. Blixt ifise ubu buryo bwo kubika amakuru y’imirongo yawe mu buryo butaziguye mu muduga wa Google / iCloud. Raba ibindi bisobanuro muri [Urupapuro rw'Ibiranga Blixt](https://ibirango.github.io/ibiranga#amahitamwo-ya-blixt).


Naho biri ukwo, nimba mbere utagira imirongo ya LN yuguruye, nta nkenerwa yo gushiramwo dosiye channels.backup. Niwinjize gusa amajambo 24 seed hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya restore.


Ntukibagire gukoresha Tor, kuva ku rutonde rw’utudomo 3 rwo hejuru, nk’uko twabisiguye mu gice c’Ihitamwo A. Uko ni ko vyari igihe wari ufise GUSA abagenzi ba Tor kandi udashobora guhamagara kuri clearnet (domain/IP). Ahandi ho si ngombwa.


Ikindi kintu ngirakamaro ni ugushinga urudodo rwihariye rwa Bitcoin ruvuye muri iyo menyu yo hejuru. Ku mburabuzi, ikoranya amabuye avuye kuri node.blixtwallet.com (uburyo bwa neutrino) ariko ushobora gushinga uwundi murongo wose wa Bitcoin utanga ubufatanye bwa neutrino.


Rero umaze kwuzuza izo nzira, ugatera buto ya restore, Blixt izotangura mbere gukorana n’ama blocks biciye kuri Neutrino nk’uko twabisiguye mu kigabane ca First Contact. Rero wihangane maze urabe ingene ivyo bisubirwamwo mu gicapo nyamukuru, mu gufyonda ku kimenyetso co gukorana.

Nk’uko mubibona muri aka karorero, vyerekana ko ama blocs ya Bitcoin akoranye 100% (A) kandi ko igikorwa co gusubirana kiri mu nzira (B). Ivyo bisigura ko imirongo ya LN mwari mufise mbere, izopfungwa maze amahera asubire muri Blixt onchain Wallet yawe.


Ivyo bifata umwanya! Rero ndagusavye, wihangane kandi ugerageze Blixt yawe kuguma ikora kandi iri kuri internet. Ivyo gukora sync vya mbere vyari gutwara iminota 6-8, imirongo yo gufunga na yo ikaba ishobora gutwara iminota 10-15. Ni vyiza rero ko iyo device ishaje neza.

Igihe umurongo wose upfutse, woshobora kubona tx ifunga mu onchain Wallet (raba Magic Drawer - Onchain), hanyuma ugafungura urutonde rw’ibifungurwa vya tx.

Kandi [hano muri iyi nkuru yashizwe kuri SN](https://stacker.news/items/679242/r/DarthCoin) narasiguye ingene wocungera amafaranga y’amanode y’abantu ku giti cabo.
