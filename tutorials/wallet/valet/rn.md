## Imenyekanisha


Valet ni imodoka yoroshe, yikingira, Bitcoin na Lightning wallet itanga uburyo bworoshe kandi bubereye bwo kwinjira mu ndege ku batangura. Yari igenewe canecane gufasha imiryango ya Bitcoin n’ubutunzi buzunguruka, cane cane mu turere twa kure.


Ni fork ya **Simple Bitcoin Wallet (SBW)**, ifise ubuhinga buteye imbere bw’umurongo w’umuravyo witwa **Imirongo ya Fiat**, bugenewe gutuma umuntu wese yemera kwishurwa n’umuravyo ata ngorane zo guhinduka.


Ubu Valet iboneka gusa ku bikoresho vya Android, kandi ishobora gukurwa no gushirwa mu maduka menshi y’amaporogarama yuguruye. Ariko rero, Valet **nti** yakira kuri **Google Play Store** kubera ubuzima bwite bw’abahinguzi n’ivy’uburenganzira bwa KYC.

## Kwakira no kohereza Bitcoins kuri Valet


Valet ni indege yigenzura wallet ifise ubushobozi bwo gutera on-chain na Lightning Bitcoin. Ivyo bisigura ko ushobora kwakira no kohereza Bitcoin uvuye muri Valet biciye ku **On-chain** canke biciye ku **Lightning Network**.


Ariko kugira ngo ushobore kwakira canke kohereza Bitcoin biciye ku Muravyo, ukeneye gushinga umurongo w’Umuravyo ukoresheje on-chain Bitcoins zawe nk’i Liquidity. Canke ushobora kugura amahera y’umurongo wa Lightning ku baguzi.

👉 Fyonda ku ikarita y'umutuku yanditsweko **Bitcoin**. Uzosubira ku rubuga rwerekana aderesi ya Bitcoin.

👉 Ushobora kandi gukanda kuri buto ya **Edit** kugira ngo ushireho umubare w'ama Bitcoin akwiye kwoherezwa kuri iyo aderesi.


**Iciyumviro:** Nk'invoice, igikorwa co guhindura kiraza mu bihe aho ushobora gushaka kwakira umubare wihariye wa Bitcoins ku aderesi ku ntumbero; ariko ivyo ntibisigura ko iyo aderesi idashobora kwakira amahera menshi canke make.

## Gushinga umurongo wa Lightning Network


Nk’uko twabibonye haruguru, Valet ni indege yigenzura Bitcoin n’umuravyo wallet; rero, kugira ngo ushobore kohereza no kwakira Bitcoin biciye ku rubuga rwa Lightning, uzobwirizwa kubanza gushinga umurongo wa Lightning, ukurikije izi ntambwe:


👉 Ku rubuga rw'intango, ukande kuri **Ikarata y'ibara ry'umuyugubwe** yanditsweko **Imiravyo**. Uzojanwa kuri paji irimwo amahitamwo akurikira:



- Gupima urudodo QR
- Gugura kuri LNBIG.COM
- Gurira kuri BITREFILL.COM
- Gusaba LN gusubiramwo igishushanyo.


Iyo uhisemwo **Gurira kuri lnbig.com** canke **Gurira kuri bitrefill.com**, uzoca urungikwa ku mbuga z’izo sosiyete, aho ushobora kugura amahera yinjira mu gihugu afise ubushobozi bwinshi. Nimwirengagize uburyo bwa nyuma **Gusaba LN graph sync** ubu.


Rero ihitamwo ryacu ryo kuja hano ni **Gucapura Node QR**. Muri iki gihe, utegerezwa kuba warafashe ingingo kandi ukaronka **QR code** y’urudodo ushaka gufungurirako umurongo. Ushobora gufungura imirongo ku nzira yose ya bose uhisemwo. Raba [1ML](https://1ml.com/) canke [Amboss](https://amboss.space/), uhitemwo urudodo urwo ari rwo rwose rwa bose uhisemwo, hanyuma ushireko kode ya QR ijana n’urudodo wahisemwo.


![channel_opening_options](assets/en/016.webp)


👉 Uzoca usubira kuri page ikurikira, aho ubu utegerezwa gutanga amahera kuri channel yawe. Na none, gukoresha urubuga rwa Lightning rwo kwizigama bisaba ko ukoresha Bitcoins zawe kugira ngo utere amahera umurongo. Ivyo bisigura ko utegerezwa kuba ufise ama Bitcoin muri on-chain wallet yawe uzokoresha mu gufasha umurongo wa Lightning. Turagusavye urabe iyi nkuru yanditswe na [Hacken](https://hacken.io/discover/urubuga-rw’umuravyo/), usome vyinshi ku bijanye n’urubuga rw’umuravyo.


![fund_channel](assets/en/017.webp)


👉 Shiramwo **umubare** w'ama Bitcoin ushaka gufasha umurongo, canke ukande kuri **Max** kugira ukoreshe amahera yawe yose ya on-chain Bitcoin. Ushobora guhindura **amahera**, canke ugasiga amafaranga y'imbere, hanyuma ukande **Ok**.


**Iciyumviro:** Amahera ukoresha mu gufasha umurongo azoba ari ubushobozi bw’umurongo wawe mushasha (i.e., umubare wose wa Sats ushobora gukoreshwa no kuva kuri uwo murongo)


Ni vyiza kandi ko ukoresha amafaranga arenga **100.000 Sats** nk’amahera y’infashanyo igihe ufungura umurongo. Ivyo ni kubera ko ama node menshi ya Lightning asaba ubushobozi bukeyi bwa 100.000 Sats kugira ngo afungure umuhora. Rero, kugira ngo wirinde kugerageza no gukosa, ukoreshe gusa amahera arenga iyo nzira.


![enter_funding_amount](assets/en/018.webp)


![funding_approval](assets/en/019.webp)


👉 Muri iki gihe, iyo usuzumye kuri page yawe y'intango ya wallet, uzobona ko amahera yawe y'infashanyo ubu yavuye ku **ikarita ya Bitcoin** aja ku **ikarita ya Lightning**. Ku mateka yawe y’ibikorwa, uzobona ibikorwa vy’amahera biriko birakorwa.


![channel_funding_processing](assets/en/020.webp)


👉 Niwafyonda ku ikarita ya Lightning, uzobona amakuru yerekana ko umurongo wawe wa Lightning uriko urafunguka. Uzobona kandi **ugutanga amafaranga y'umurongo** ku rutonde rwawe rw'amafaranga. Rindira ko amafaranga yawe yemezwa kuri blockchain, maze umurongo wawe wa Lightning uzoba witeguye.


![channel_opening](assets/en/021.webp)


👉 Igihe nyene amafaranga azoba yemejwe, ukande kuri **Lightning card** iri kuri page yawe y'intango, uzobona amakuru yerekeye umurongo wawe wa Lightning nk'uko bikurikira:



- RANDOM SET Y'IMIBARE ITANDUKANWA N'UTUDO:** Izo ni zo IP z'ama node. (IPV4 na IPV6, uko bikurikirana)
- UBUSHOBOZI:** Ubu ni umubare wose wa Sats ushobora kwoherezwa no kwakirwa biciye kuri iyi nzira
- CAN SEND:** Iyi ni yo Sats ushobora kohereza hanze muri iki gihe. Uzobona ko ari hafi igiharuro kimwe n'ico **Ubushobozi**. Ivyo ni kubera ko ata n’amahera yohereje biciye ku nzira.
- CAN RECEIVE:** Uyu ni umubare wa Sats ushobora kwakira kuri iyi channel muri iki gihe. (Bizoba ari bike canke ataco bimaze muri iki gihe kuko kugira ngo ushobore kwakira, utegerezwa kubanza kohereza Sats kugira ngo ureme Liquidity yinjira)
- REFUNDABLE:** Ivyo vyerekana amahera asubizwa kuri aderesi yawe ya on-chain iyo ufunze umurongo wawe. Ivyo navyo vyitwa **Ikigereranyo c'aho uba c'umurongo wawe**. Urabona ko ari hasi gatoyi gusa y’ubushobozi bw’umurongo, kandi ivyo ni kubera ko iyo ufunze umurongo, utegerezwa kwishura amahera kugira ngo utangaze igikorwa co gufunga kuri blockchain, nk’uko wabigize igihe wariko urafasha umurongo. Rero iyo sisitemu yarakuyeko amahera makeyi cane uzotanga.)
- AGACIRO MU RUGERO:** Iyo umuntu yohereje sats ku muhora wawe, canke iyo ugerageje kohereza umuntu sats, kandi kubera imvo iyo ari yo yose, iyo transaction iracererwa, akenshi yerekanwa muri iki kibanza.


![channel_info](assets/en/022.webp)


## Kurungika Sats Biciye ku Murongo Wawe


Kurungika Sats biciye kuri Lightning Network ni igikorwa kigororotse.


👉 Hasi kuri page y'intango, ukande kuri **Send**, hanyuma **ushire** invoice ya Lightning (utegerezwa kuba warayikopiye) mu kibanza catanzwe, canke ukande kuri **Scan** kugira ngo ukore scanner QR code y'invoice ya Lightning.


![click_send_or_scan](assets/en/023.webp)


Invoice nyinshi za Lightning ziza n’amahera yashizwemwo imbere y’igihe azokwishurwa. Ariko mu bihe bikeyi, bishobora kuba ari invoice yuguruye aho utegerezwa kwuzuza amahera.


👉 Shiramwo amafaranga muri **Dollars** canke **Sats**, canke ukande kuri **Max**, kugira ukoreshe amafaranga yose asigaye ku muhora wawe wa Lightning, hanyuma ukande kuri **Ok**. Inzira nziza ikimara kuboneka, amahera yawe azorungikwa kandi azoheza mu masegonda makeyi. Gumana ijisho kuri paji y’intango kugira ngo urabe nimba amahera yarangiye. Izoronka ikimenyetso c’icatsi kibisi iyo imaze guheza.

## Ukwakira Sats Biciye ku Murongo Wawe


Kwakira amahera ku muhora wawe wa Lightning bivana cane cane n’uko ufise Liquidity yinjira canke atarivyo. Uhejeje gufungura umurongo, ntuzoshobora kwakira amahera gushika nibura wohereje Sats kugira ngo **ureme amahera yinjira** ku rundi ruhande rw’ihuriro ry’umurongo. Kugira ngo wemeze nimba ushobora kwakira Sats n’ingero ya Sats ushobora kwakira, reba ahantu **Ushobora Kwakira** mu makuru y’umurongo wawe. Ivyo bizokwerekana ingene Sats uronka ku gihe cose.


None, reka twiyumvire ko wohereje Sats uvuye ku muhora wawe, ubu ufise amahera yinjira, kandi ushobora kwakira Sats.


Kugira ngo uronke ku muhora wa Lightning, uzobwirizwa gukora invoice. Udakunze Bitcoin on-chain, ikoresha amaderesi, urubuga rwa Lightning rukoresha amafagitire. Hari inzira zibiri zo gutanga invoice kuri Valet.

## Imirongo y'abashitsi n'iya Fiat


Cokimwe n'izindi ndege nyinshi za Bitcoin, Valet ni indege yoroshe, yoroshe Bitcoin n'umuravyo wallet. Ariko ifise ikintu kidasanzwe c’umuravyo kiyitandukanya n’izindi ndege nyinshi za Bitcoin wallet. Ico kintu citwa **Imirongo ya Hosted na Fiat**.


Imirongo ya Fiat ni ubwoko bw’ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa Lightning gutuma umuntu ashobora kwinjira mu ndege no gukoresha neza urubuga rwa Lightning. Ni umuti wo kubika ushobora gutuma umuntu atamenyekana, nk’uko bigenda ku murongo usanzwe wa Lightning. Ikoranabuhanga ry’imirongo ya Fiat na ryo nyene rituma habaho uguhingura ibintu biva kuri Bitcoin ku rubuga rwa Lightning. Akarorero ni uko n’imirongo ya Fiat, abacuruzi bashobora kwemera amahera y’agaciro kadahinduka ya Bitcoin ataco bitwararika ku bijanye n’uguhinduka kwa Bitcoin.


Ubu rero, ugushirwa mu ngiro kw'imirongo ya Fiat kuri Valet biratuma habaho amafaranga y'i Fiat ashigikiwe na Sats. Ikoresha ubucuti bw'Umushitsi n'Umukiriya aho Umushitsi ari urudodo urwo ari rwo rwose rw'Umuravyo rutanga iyo serivisi, kandi umukiriya ni umukoresha wa Valet. Ni umuti wo kubungabunga kuko Sats zose ziri ku ruhande rw’Umushitsi; ariko, gukora invoice, tor routing, no gukora preimage biracariho ku ruhande rw'umukiriya nk'uko biri mu muhora usanzwe wa Lightning.


Gufungura umurongo wa Fiat bifata inzira imwe n’ugufungura umurongo wa Lightning. Itandukaniro rikomeye ryonyene ni uko muri iki gihe, umukiriya (ukoresha Valet) ntabwirizwa gukora ikintu na kimwe c’amahera on-chain kugira ngo areme ubushobozi bw’umuyoboro. Uwakira abashitsi niwe ashiraho ubushobozi bw’umurongo kandi akarungika amahera yose yishurwa ku mukiriya.


👉 Kugira ngo ufungure umurongo wa Fiat, fyonda ku **ikarata y'umuravyo** y'ibara ry'umuyugubwe iri kuri page yawe y'intango ya wallet. Hitamwo **Scan Node QR** (Aha, utegerezwa kuba waramenye Host ushaka gufungurirako umurongo, maze ukaronka QR y'iyo node. Akarorero k'umurongo wa Host ushobora gufungurirako umurongo wa Fiat ni node ya SATM na Standard Sats.)


**Iciyumviro:** Ni vyiza kumenya ko umuntu wese ashobora kuba Umushitsi. Ivyo usaba vyose ni ugukoresha urudodo rwa Lightning rufise **Igikoresho co gukoresha umurongo wa Fiat** n'igikoresho co gukingira**.
