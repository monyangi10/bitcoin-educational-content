## Cold Wallet.



Muri iyi nyigisho nzobasigurira ingene wokora igikoresho cawe ca mbere co gusinya ku kirere, kidakorana na Internet, mbere udafise Hardware Wallet yihariye. Ico ukeneye ni ukugira mudasobwa zibiri:




- igikoresho ca kera co kubuzwa ibihe bidahera kwifatanya na Internet;
- mudasobwa yawe ukoresha ku musi ku musi.



Iyi ntunganyo iremesha umutekano mwinshi kuruta `Hot Wallet` ya kera: mudasobwa ya kera--itagira uruja n’uruza rw’urubuga--ni yo icungera imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga, zitigera zigaragara kuri Internet, ariko zibikwa hanze y’urubuga ("airgap" canke "Cold").



Ahubwo uzoshiraho ikigaragaza Wallet ("watch-only") kuri mudasobwa yawe ya misi yose, ikaba ifatanye n'urubuga kandi ushobora gukoresha, nk'akarorero, gusuzuma amafaranga asigaranye no gutegura amafaranga y'inyungu.



## Wallet Ikirere: Ni igiki n’ingene



Mu gukora intambwe ziri muri iyi nkuru, tuzoshiraho Electrum zibiri za Software Wallet kuri mudasobwa zibiri zitandukanye maze mu nyuma dukore Wallets zibiri zifise ububiko bw’imfunguruzo butandukanye: Wallet airgap izokoresha uburongozi bwose bwa Wallet HD, mu gihe ikigaragaza Wallet kizokorwa n’urufunguzo rwa bose rwa mbere.



Izo Wallet zibiri zizoba, mu buryo bwose, zitandukanye cane n’izindi. Ikintu kimwe gusa bazohurizako, nk’uko tuzobibona, ni amaderesi:




- gW-13 kuri mudasobwa ya airgap ishobora gusinya gusa ariko, ivuye ku rubuga, ntizi umubare n’amaderesi akoreshwa;
- Wallet iri kuri mudasobwa y’umusi ku musi izoshobora gusa gutegura no gukwiragiza amafaranga, itazoshobora gutanga amafaranga, iyo ata mfunguruzo z’ibanga zihari.

## Irema Wallet ry'Ivyiyumviro



Ubu ko Wallet yawe ifise imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitari ku murongo, urakeneye gushinga ikigaragaza Wallet, canke `watch-only`, kizotuma ubona amahera asigaye, hamwe no gutegura amafaranga y’inyungu kugira ngo ukomeze kwirundanira Sats ata nkomanzi.



Kuva kuri Wallet iri ku gikoresho kitari ku murongo, hitamwo _Wallet_ -> _Amakuru_



![image](assets/en/08.webp)



Idirisha ririmwo amakuru yawe yose ya Wallet rizoboneka, aho ushobora gusuzuma `inzira y'inkomoko` n'`ikimenyetso c'urutoke rwa mbere`, nk'akarorero kugira ngo ubishire ikimenyetso iruhande y'amajambo ari mu nteruro ya Mnemonic (ni vyiza cane).



![image](assets/en/09.webp)



Ibuka ko uriko urakura aya makuru kuri mudasobwa idahuye, rero uzobwirizwa gukopa/gushiramwo `zpub` muri dosiye y'inyandiko maze ukayibika kuri usb stick.



Ubu ushobora kuja kuri mudasobwa ihuye na Internet, kugira ngo utangure Electrum maze ureme Wallet nshasha.



Mu _Dosiye_, hitamwo _Nshasha/Subizaho_.

![image](assets/en/14.webp)



Nimushire hano `zpub` yakopiwe muri Wallet itari mu murongo kandi mwazanye kuri iyi mudasobwa biciye ku nzira ya usb.



![image](assets/en/15.webp)



Sozera ushizeho ijambobanga rikomeye ry'iyi Wallet na ryo nyene, rishobora kuba ritandukanye n'iryo watoye kuri Cold ihuye.



Uzobona ikigaragaza Wallet kigaragara, kirimwo imburi. Ubutumwa burakwibutsa ko iyi ari Wallet yerekanwa gusa kandi ko udashobora, ukoresheje yo, gukoresha amahera ajanye na yo.



**Iciyumviro neza**: **uzokenera rero kwama ufise imfunguruzo z'ibanga zo gukuraho ama UTXO y'iyi Wallet**. Iyo ufise uburyo bwiza bwo gucungera, ntibizokugora kuguma ufise Bitcoins zawe zose.



![image](assets/en/16.webp)



Iyi ngabisho izoboneka igihe cose ufunguye iyi Wallet. Fyonda _Ok_ maze tuje ku ntambwe yo kugenzura.



## Igenzura ry’Ivya Wallet Bibiri



Nk’uko twavyize mu ntango y’iki gitabu, airgap ya Wallet n’ikigaragaza cayo Wallet ni amasakoshi abiri afise ubushobozi butandukanye ariko **asangiye amaderesi amwe**.



Turavye izo Wallets zibiri iruhande n'uruhande, mu maso turabona ko mu gice c'ikirere ca Wallet hari ikimenyetso ca "seed", mu gihe mu gice c'isaha gusa ataco kirimwo. Mbere n’ivyo bizogufasha kwibuka ko ikigaragaza Wallet Wallet kidafise imfunguruzo z’ibanga.



![image](assets/en/17.webp)



Kugira ngo ukore isuzuma rya mbere ry'ukuri, ariko, hitamwo muri izo Wallet zompi `Adresses`: kuko zisangira amaderesi amwe, urutonde rw'amaderesi rukwiye kuba rumwe kuri zompi.



![image](assets/en/18.webp)



⚠️ **KWITWARARIKA**: **nta nzira yo hagati ishobora kubaho; amaderesi ategerezwa kuba ari imwe. Mu gihe vyoba bitandukanye, birakenewe gukuraho ibikorwa vyose vyakozwe gushika ubu ugatangura bushasha**.



Ubu rero urashobora gukomeza gukora ivyigwa bibiri bitandukanye. Ubwa mbere, gerageza gukuraho izo Wallet zibiri maze uzigarure kuva mu ntango, imwe yose kuri mudasobwa ibereye. Mu gihe wobandanya gukora ivyo bigenzura, uburyo bwo kwerekana Wallet burasa n’ubwo bwavuzwe haruguru.



Ku bijanye n’igice c’umuyaga ca Wallet, ariko, ku rubuga rwa `keystore` uzobwirizwa guhitamwo _Ndasanzwe mfise akabuto_ maze winjize amajambo mu kuyakopa mu mpapuro zawe z’ububiko.



Inyuma y'aho ikigeragezo ca "no-load" kirangiriye, urashobora kugerageza gukora transaction y'amahera makeyi maze ukayakoresha ubwo nyene.



## Kwakira no gukoresha amafaranga



Kugira ngo utangure gukoresha airgap yawe ya Electrum, urashobora gukora igikorwa co gutanga amahera makeyi, hanyuma ukayakoresha mu gukoresha Address yawe bwite. Ushobora rero kwimenyereza ingene bigenda, ugasuzuma ko ari wewe ugenzura neza ayo mahera.



**Iciyumviro**: Singusavye gushiramwo amahera menshi kuri Wallet imbere y’uko wizigira ko ushobora gukora ibikorwa vyose neza.



Intambwe zisiguwe aha hepfo zirashobora, iyo uzibonye ubwa mbere, gusa n’izigoye. Ntureke ngo ivyo bigucike intege: iyo umaze kubaha ikigeragezo cawe ca mbere, uzosanga bafata iminota mikeyi gusa kugira ngo baheza.



Kugira ngo uronke amahera, utegerezwa gukoresha ata kabuza ikigaragaza Wallet kiri kuri orodinateri yawe ihuriye kuri Internet. Mu `Receive` menu kanda kuri _Rema ubusabe_ kugira ngo Electrum generate ibe Address ya mbere iboneka maze uyikoreshe kugira ngo utwoherereze Satss nkeyi.



![image](assets/en/19.webp)



![image](assets/en/20.webp)



Igihe iyo transaction imaze gukwiragizwa urashobora kubona ko-nk’uko ari ibisanzwe- iboneka gusa ku kigaragaza Wallet atari ku Wallet airgap.



![image](assets/en/21.webp)



Inyuma y’aho amafaranga yawe amaze kwemezwa, urashobora gutegura amahera uzokoresha maze gutyo ukagerageza uburyo bwo gusinya uhereye kuri Wallet iri hanze y’urubuga. Hanyuma utegure ibikorwa ku masaha gusa hanyuma ukande _Preview_ kugira ngo ubisuzume



![image](assets/en/22.webp)



Uronka idirisha ry'ibikorwa vy'ubudandaji aho ushobora kubona ko:




- igikorwa ntikishizweko umukono (`Ikibanza: Ntikishizweko umukono);
- Amabwirizwa ya `Sign` na `Broadcast` arabujijwe.



Ico wokora n’ugusohora hanze iyo transaction uko iri, ukayijana ku Wallet airgap ukayishirako umukono.



Injira muri mudasobwa yawe umuduga wa USB, hanyuma, mu bimenyetso biri hasi ibubamfu, uhitemwo _Share_.



![image](assets/en/23.webp)



Inyuma y'ivyo uhitemwo _Bika muri dosiye_.



![image](assets/en/24.webp)



Bika ivyo ugurisha ku nkoni ya usb.



Uzobona ko Electrum iha dosiye izina rifise imibare ya mbere ya transaction ID, kandi urugero rwa dosiye ni `.PSBT`, bisobanura `Partially Signed Bitcoin Transaction`.



Kura ibinyamakuru bifise dosiye `.PSBT` hanyuma ubihuze na mudasobwa itari ku murongo.



Kuva mu gice c'umuyaga ca Wallet, ubu uhitemwo _Ibikoresho_, hanyuma _Ushiremwo ibikorwa_ hanyuma ukurikize Kuva kuri dosiye_.



![image](assets/en/25.webp)



Na umucungerezi wa dosiye, hitamwo `.PSBT` aho iri.



![image](assets/en/29.webp)



Porogarama ya orodinateri iri hanze y’urubuga izoca ifungura idirisha ry’ugucuruza ry’ibintu biteye imbere, risa cane n’ingene wabibonye ku kigaragaza Wallet. Igihugu ni `Unsigned`, ariko itandukaniro ni uko itegeko `Sign` hano rikora. Kandi ivyo ni vyo nyene uzobwirizwa gushitsa.



![image](assets/en/26.webp)



![image](assets/en/27.webp)



Ubu ko iyo nzira yashizweko umukono, wibuke yuko Wallet yawe iri ku mashini idakoreshwa n’ingurukanabumenyi. Rero-naho wobona itegeko rya `Broadcast` rikora-Wallet yawe ntizoshobora gukwiragiza iyo nzira ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.



Ico ukeneye gukora ubu ni ugusubiramwo igikorwa co gusohora iyo transaction yashizweko umukono ku usb stick, kugira ngo ushobore kuyizana kuri mudasobwa ihuriye kuri internet maze uyikwiragize.



Kuva ku rutonde rwo hasi ibubamfu, uhitemwo _Sangira_ hanyuma _Bika muri dosiye_.



![image](assets/en/28.webp)



Ubu dosiye ifise ukwaguka gutandukanye: **aho kuba `.PSBT` ubu igikorwa gifise ukwaguka `.txn`. Kuva ubu niko Electrum izoguha umenye amafaranga yashizweko umukono n'atashizweko umukono**.



![image](assets/en/30.webp)



Ku bijanye n’ugukwiragiza kwa nyuma kw’isoko, ukure inkoni ya usb muri mudasobwa itari ku murongo, uyishire muri mudasobwa ihuye na Internet.



Kuva ku isaha-gusa, subiramwo uburyo bwo kwinjiza, ni ukuvuga, kuva ku _Ibikoresho_ uhitemwo _Gushiramwo ibikorwa_ hanyuma _Kuva kuri dosiye_.



![image](assets/en/31.webp)



Electrum izogufungurira idirisha ry'ugucuruza, ritandukanye cane n'iryo ryerekanwe mbere kuri iyi Wallet, kuko ubu ryashizweko umukono (`Ikibanza: Ryashizweko umukono`) kandi itegeko rya `Broadcast` rishobora gushikwako.

## Insozero



Ibigeragezo vyawe ubu biraheze. Niwaba wakurikije iyo nzira maze ukaronka ibisubizo bimwe, wararemye Wallet Cold ukoresheje Electrum, kuri mudasobwa zibiri zitandukanye, ushobora gukoresha mu buryo bwa airgap kugira ubike ama Bitcoins yawe.



Ibintu vyonyene uzobwirizwa kwitwararika cane ni bibiri:


1) **ntiwigere ukoresha airgap ya Wallet ku ma aderesi yakira generate**. Kubera ko itari ku murongo, izokwama iguha Address ya mbere, ihuye n’iyo uherutse gukoresha mu gukora igikorwa co kugerageza;



![image](assets/en/33.webp)



_Nk'uko mubibona ku ishusho iri hejuru, Wallet iri hanze y'umurongo ntazi amateka yayo bwite ya Address. Ni impumyi rwose muri ivyo. **Igikorwa conyene gishobora kugukorera ni ukubika imfunguruzo zawe zitari mu nzira no gusinya amafaranga yawe**_.



2) Koresha USB flash drive yihariye iyo ntumbero gusa, **ntukoreshe uburyo ukoresha kenshi**. Ibikoresho vya misi yose birashobora guterwa n’ingurukanabumenyi, kandi utabishaka, woshobora gutera mbere na orodinateri uguma uyikuye ku rubuga. Inkoni ya USB ukoresha gusa kubera iyo ntumbero irafise amahirwe make cane yo gukorana na PC yawe kuri internet, cane cane iyo uri umuhodler udategerezwa gukoresha amahera, gutyo ugabanura amahirwe yo kwakira hanyuma ukarungika virusi, malware, n’ibindi.
