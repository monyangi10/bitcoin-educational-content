## Watch-only wallet ni iki?


Watch-only wallet, canke Wallet isomwa gusa, ni ubwoko bwa porogarama yagenewe gutuma uwuyikoresha ashobora kwihweza ibikorwa bijanye n’urufunguzo rwa bose rwa Bitcoin rumwe canke nyinshi, atashobora gushika ku rufunguzo rw’ibanga rujanye n’urwo.


Ubwo bwoko bw’ikoreshwa buzigama gusa amakuru akenewe kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kugenzura Bitcoin Wallet, harimwo no kuraba umubare wayo n’amateka y’ibikorwa vyayo, ariko ntashobora gushika ku mfunguruzo z’ibanga. Ku bw’ivyo, ntibishoboka ko umuntu akoresha amafaranga y’ama bitcoins afise muri Wallet ku bijanye n’ikoreshwa ry’isaha gusa.

![watch-only](assets/en/1.webp)

Isaha-gusa muri rusangi ikoreshwa hamwe na Hardware Wallet. Ivyo bituma imfunguruzo z’ibanga za Wallet zishobora kubikwa "Cold," ku gikoresho kitahuye na internet, gifise ahantu hatoyi ho gutera, bikaba bitandukanya imfunguruzo z’ibanga n’ibidukikije bishobora gutera ingorane. Porogaramu y'isaha gusa, ku rundi ruhande, ibika gusa urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse (`xpub`, `zpub`, n'ibindi) rwa Bitcoin Wallet. Urufunguzo rw’umuvyeyi ntirwemerera kuvumbura urufunguzo rw’ibanga rujanye n’urwo rufunguzo kandi, kubera ivyo, ntirwemerera gukoresha amafaranga y’ibiceri. Ariko rero, biremeza gukuraho imfunguruzo za bose z’abana n’amaderesi yo kwakira. Kubera ubumenyi bw’amaderesi ya Wallet akinzwe na Hardware Wallet, porogarama y’isaha gusa irashobora gukurikirana ivyo bikorwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, igaha uwuyikoresha ubushobozi bwo kugenzura umubare wiwe n’amaderesi mashasha y’ukwakira generate, ataco akeneye gufatanya Hardware Wallet yiwe igihe cose.


## Ni iyihe Watch-only wallet yo gukoresha?


Ubu, ubuhinga bwo kuraba gusa ni [Sentinel](https://sentinel.watch/), bwateguwe n'imigwi yo muri Samourai Wallet. Irimwo ibintu vyose bihambaye kugira ngo umuntu agire Watch-only wallet nziza:



- Gushigikira imfunguruzo zagutse, imfunguruzo za bose, n’amaderesi;
- Ubushobozi bwo gutunganya amakonti menshi canke amasakoshi mu bice vy’amahera;
- Guhingura amaderesi yo kwakira bitcoins kuri Hardware Wallet y’umuntu ataco asaba ko ayikoresha ataco akora;
- Ubushobozi bwo kwubaka no gutangaza amafaranga ata murongo;
- Ihitamwo ryo kwifatanya n’urudodo rwiwe bwite rwa Bitcoin;
- Inyungu ya Tor kugira ngo umuntu agire ubuzima bwite.

Intambamyi zidasanzwe za Sentinel ziri mu kuba iyo porogarama iboneka kuri Android gusa kandi idashobora gukoresha ama wallets afise amasinyatire menshi. Rero, nimba ufise igikoresho ca Android kandi Wallet yawe ari umukono umwe wa kera, ndagusavye Sentinel.

Ku bashaka gukurikirana Wallet ifise amasinya menshi, Blue Wallet ni yo porogarama yonyene nzi itanga uburyo bwo kuraba gusa kuri ubwo bwoko bw’amasakoshi, kandi iraboneka kuri Android na iOS.


Ku bakoresha iOS barondera ubundi buryo bwo gukoresha Sentinel, [Green Wallet] canke [Blue Wallet] (Blue Wallet] (Blue Wallet) bishobora kuba amahitamwo, naho nyene uburyo bwabo bwo gukoresha isaha gusa butasobanukiwe.

## Ni gute twokoresha igitabu Watch-only wallet?


### Gushiramwo no Gutegura


Tangana n’ugushiramwo porogarama ya Sentinel. Ivyo ushobora kubigira ukoresheje Google Play Store canke ukoresheje [APK iboneka ku rubuga rwemewe](https://sentinel.raba/gukura/).


![watch-only](assets/notext/3.webp)


Iyo ubanza gufungura porogarama, uhabwa amahitamwo hagati ya:



- `Ihuze na Dojo`;
- `Huza na server ya Samourai`.


Dojo, yateguwe n’umugwi wa Samourai, ni verisiyo yuzuye y’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin rushobora gushirwaho rwonyene canke rwongerwako mu gukanda rimwe ku mirongo y’uruzitiro nk’iyi [Umbrel](https://umbrel.com/) na [RoninDojo](https://ronindojo.io/).


[**-> Tora ingene woshiramwo RoninDojo v2 kuri Raspberry Pi.**]


Niba ufise Dojo yawe, urashobora kuyihuza kuri iyi ntambwe. Niwabigira, uzokwungukira ku rugero rwo hejuru rw’ubuzima bwite igihe usuzuma amakuru yawe y’ugucuruza ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.


![watch-only](assets/notext/4.webp)


Ahandi ho, birashoboka guhitamwo umukozi wa Samourai. Ushobora kandi guhitamwo nimba uzokwifatanya na Tor canke utarivyo.


![watch-only](assets/notext/5.webp)


Uzoca ushika kuri paji nyamukuru y’ikinyamakuru Sentinel.


![watch-only](assets/notext/6.webp)


Kugira ngo utangure, urashobora gushinga iyo porogarama. Fyonda ku tudodo dutatu turi hejuru iburyo, hanyuma ukande kuri `Ivyagezwe`.


![watch-only](assets/notext/7.webp)

Mu guhitamwo `Kode ya PIN y'ukoresha`, urashobora gushinga ijambobanga kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha Watch-only wallet yawe. Ufise kandi ubushobozi bwo guhindura amafaranga y'ishingiro yo guhindura amafaranga yawe mu mafaranga y'amafaranga, canke mbere no guhisha agaciro k'amafaranga mu gukoresha uburyo bwa `Hide fiat values`. Kugira ngo umutekano urushirize, urashobora gukoresha `Disable Screenshots`, ivyo bikaba bibuza amafoto yose y’iporogarama yawe ya Sentinel, gutyo bikabuza gutanga amakuru yose ku gicapo co hanze.

![watch-only](assets/notext/8.webp)


Muri iyi menyu y’imiterere, urafise kandi uburenganzira bwo gukora ububiko bwa Sentinel yawe.


### Gukoresha igitabu Watch-only wallet


Kuva kuri paji y'intango, kanda buto y'ubururu `NSHASHA` kugira ngo wongereko urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse rwo gukurikirana. Ushobora rero gucapura kode ya QR y'urufunguzo rwawe, canke gushiramwo urufunguzo (`xpub`, `zpub`...) uhisemwo `Gushiramwo Pubkey`.


![watch-only](assets/notext/9.webp)


Muri rusangi, `xpub` ya Wallet yawe irashikira biciye ku buhinga bwo gucunga Wallet ukoresha. Nk'akarorero, iyo ucungera Hardware Wallet yawe ukoresheje Sparrow, aya makuru araboneka mu gice ca `Ivyagezwe`, munsi y'igice ca `Keystore`.


![watch-only](assets/notext/10.webp)


Amaze kwinjira urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse muri Sentinel, iyo porogarama iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukora ihuriro rishasha. Ikoraniro rigereranya urutonde rw'imfunguruzo za bose zagutse zitunganijwe hamwe. Iyi nzira iguha ubushobozi bwo kutadondora gusa `xpubs` zawe zose, ariko no kuzishira mu migwi mu buryo bubereye. Nk’akarorero, nimba ufise Samourai Wallet ifise amakonti menshi (ugushiramwo amahera, gushiramwo amahera, gushiramwo amahera...), urashobora gukoranya ayo makonti yose munsi y’ikoraniro rya `Samourai`. Ku ma wallets acungiwe umuryango wawe, ushobora gukora ihuriro ryitwa `Umuryango`.


Hitamwo `Rema ikoraniro rishasha`. Hanyuma winjize izina ry'urufunguzo rwagutse uherutse gushiramwo. Nk'akarorero, iyo nkora scanner kuri konti y'amahera yo mu modoka yanje ya Samourai Wallet, noyita urufunguzo `Ibiziga`. Fyonda kuri `SAVE` kugira ngo uheze.


![watch-only](assets/notext/11.webp)


Hanyuma, ushire izina kuri iri koraniro maze ukande ku kimenyetso co kwemeza kiri hejuru iburyo bw’ibarabara kugira ngo ubike iryo koraniro. Ivyo mwakoranije ubu biraboneka ku rubuga rw’intango rwa Sentinel.


![watch-only](assets/notext/12.webp)


Niba wifuza kwongerako urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse, wongere ukande kuri `RUSHASHA` maze winjize urufunguzo rwawe.


![watch-only](assets/notext/13.webp)

Uzoca usabwa guhitamwo ihuriro wipfuza gushiramwo uru rufunguzo, canke guhingura urufunguzo rushasha. Nk'akarorero, ku bijanye nanje, narashizeho ihuriro ry'ibintu vy'umwihariko ku Ledger Wallet yanje.

![watch-only](assets/notext/14.webp)


Kugira ngo ubone imfunguruzo zagutse z’ikoraniro mu buryo burambuye, ushobora kuyikanda gusa. Ushobora rero guca mu bice bitandukanye kugira ngo ubone amateka y’ibikorwa.


![watch-only](assets/notext/15.webp)


Kuva mu gukoranya, mu gufyonda ku tudodo dutatu dutoduto turi hejuru iburyo, hanyuma kuri `Reba Ivyiza Bitakoreshejwe`, ushobora kuronka urutonde rw'ibisohoka bitakoreshejwe na Wallet ikurikiranywe.


![watch-only](assets/notext/16.webp)


### Kurungika no kwakira ama Bitcoins avuye kuri Sentinel


Nk'uko biri ku Watch-only wallet nziza iyo ari yo yose, Sentinel iraguha uburenganzira bwo kwakira amaderesi kugira ngo wakire bitcoins kuri Wallet ikurikiranywe. Ariko kandi, Sentinel iratanga ikindi kintu giteye imbere: uguhingura no gutangaza indege yitwa Partially Signed Bitcoin Transaction (PSBT). Gutyo, Wallet ifise imfunguruzo z’ibanga irashobora gusinya iyo nzira, iyo imaze gusinywa, ishobora gutangazwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin na Sentinel. Reka turabe ingene ivyo vyose twobikora.


**Ukwitonda, ntivyiza kwakira bitcoins ku Address yakira itagenzuwe na Wallet ubwayo.** Nimba Wallet ifise imfunguruzo z’ibanga, nka Hardware Wallet, itaremeje neza ko Address imwe imwe ifise ubucuti n’ivyo, Nkako, ata vyo kwemeza, nta cizigiro co kuba Address ari iya Wallet yawe vy’ukuri. Ku bw’ivyo, ubuhinga bwo kwakira amahera bwa Watch-only wallet bukwiye gukoreshwa n’ubwenge, umuntu azirikana ko amahera yoherejwe yoshobora gutakazwa.


Kugira ngo uronke ama bitcoins biciye kuri Sentinel, hitamwo ihuriro ry’inyungu, hanyuma ukande ku rubuga rujanye n’urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse wipfuza kohereza amahera.


![watch-only](assets/notext/17.webp)


Ubwa nyuma, fyonda ku kimenyetso c’umwampi kiri musi ibubamfu bw’ibarabara. Sentinel rero ikaguha ubusa bwakira Address. Ushobora kuyikopa, canke ukayicapura ukoresheje kode ya QR.


![watch-only](assets/notext/18.webp)


Kugira ngo generate PSBT iva kuri Sentinel, maze gutyo utangure gukoresha amahera, genda ku rufunguzo rwagutse rwa Wallet wipfuza gukoresha amahera. Reka dufate nk’akarorero, konti yanje yo gushiramwo amahera kuri Samourai Wallet yanje. Hanyuma ukande ku kimenyetso c’umwampi kiri musi iburyo bw’ibarabara.


![watch-only](assets/notext/19.webp)


Injiramwo amaparametere yose ajanye n'ugucuruza kwawe:



- Injira Address y’uwuronka (ukanda ku kimenyetso ca QR code, urafise uburenganzira bwo gucapura iyo Address);
- Sobanura amahera uzorungika kuri iyi Address;
- Menya amafaranga y’ugucuruza.


Uhejeje kwuzuza ibikenewe vyose ku bijanye n'ugucuruza kwawe, kanda kuri buto ya `COMPOSE UNSIGNED TRANSACTION`.


![watch-only](assets/notext/20.webp)


Uzoca ubona PSBT, igereranya igikorwa ca Bitcoin cubatswe ariko kitashizweko umukono, kuko Sentinel idashobora gushika ku mfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga. Ufise uburenganzira bwo gukopa iyo nzira, ukayirungika hanze nk’idosiye `.PSBT`, canke ukayicapura biciye ku kode ya QR igendagenda.


![watch-only](assets/notext/21.webp)


Hanyuma, genda kuri Wallet yawe ifise imfunguruzo z’ibanga kugira ngo usinye iyo nzira (Samourai, Hardware Wallet...).


![watch-only](assets/notext/22.webp)


Iyo iyo nzira imaze gusinywa, urashobora gusubira kuri Sentinel kugira ngo uyitangaze. Kugira ngo ubikore, mu rutonde rw'imbere, fyonda ku tudodo dutoduto dutatu turi hejuru iburyo, hanyuma kuri `Broadcast transaction`.


![watch-only](assets/notext/23.webp)


Ufise uburenganzira bwo kwinjiza PSBT yawe yashizweko umukono mu buryo butatu butandukanye:



- Mu kuyimanika ataco uhinduye ku rubaho rwawe;
- Mu kuyizana muri dosiye `.PSBT`;
- Mu kuyicapura biciye ku kode ya QR.


![watch-only](assets/notext/24.webp)


Igihe amafaranga yashizweko umukono yinjijwe mu gicapo c’umweru, urashobora gufyonda kuri buto ya Green `BROADCAST TRANSACTION` kugira ngo uyatangaze ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin. Sentinel izoguha txid yayo.


![watch-only](assets/notext/25.webp)
