*Ishusho: [Trezor.io]*



Trezor Model One ni Hardware Wallet ya mbere yasohotse, yatangujwe mu 2014 na SatoshiLabs. Inyuma y’imyaka irenga cumi iriho, iguma ari ihitamwo riryoshe cane cane ku bakoresha barondera Hardware Wallet ishobora gushikwako mu buryo bw’ubuhinga no mu bijanye n’ingengo y’imari. Nkako, igiciro cayo ni €49 ku rubuga rwemewe rwa Trezor. Ni kimwe mu bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none vyonyene biri muri iki kigero c’igiciro. Iri hagati y’ibikoresho vy’urugero rwo kwinjira ku €20, nka Tapsigner, akenshi idafise igicapo, n’ibikoresho vyo hagati ku €80, nka Ledger Nano S Plus canke Trezor Safe 3.



Model One ifise ikigaragaza OLED c’ibara rimwe c’amasentimetero 0,96 n’amabuto abiri agaragara. Ikora ata bateri, ikoresha gusa ubuhinga bwa micro-USB kugira ngo ibone ubushobozi n’amakuru Exchange.



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Intambamyi nyamukuru ya Model One ni ukubura secure element, ivyo bikaba bituma ishobora guterwa n’ibitero bitandukanye vy’umubiri, bimwe muri vyo bikaba vyoroshe gukora. Ivyo bitero bishobora kubamwo ugusuzuma imirongo y’inyongera kugira ngo umuntu amenye PIN y’ico gikoresho, canke ubuhinga buteye imbere cane bwo gukuraho seed yashizwemwo amakuru kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kuyikoresha mu nyuma. Zirikana ko ivyo bitero bisaba ko umuntu ashobora gushika ku gikoresho. Ariko rero, iyo ngorane irashobora kugabanywa cane hakoreshejwe igikoresho gikomeye citwa passphrase BIP39. Niwahitamwo iyi Hardware Wallet, ndaguhanura cane gutunganya passphrase.



Icogereranyo ca mbere kiratanga ivyiza bibiri bihambaye:




- Ishingiye ku nyubakwa y’inkomoko yuguruye yose. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibikoresho vya vuba bifise secure element, ibihimba vyose vy’ibikoresho n’amaporogarama vya Model One birashobora gusuzumwa;
- Ifise igikingira. Ku bumenyi mfise, iyi ni yo Hardware Wallet yonyene iri kw’isoko muri iki kigero c’igiciro ifise ikigaragaza. Ivyo ni ikintu gihambaye cane, kuko bituma amakuru yashizweko umukono be n’amaderesi y’aho umuntu yakira amakuru ashobora kugenzurwa, gutyo bikaba bibuza ibitero vyinshi vy’ubuhinga bwa none.



Trezor Model One rero ishobora guserukira ihitamwo ryiza ku batangura n’abakoresha hagati ku ngengo y’imari ntoyi. Ariko rero, birahambaye ko uguma uzi aho bigarukira mu bijanye no kwikingira umubiri, kubera ko ata secure element iriho. Niba ingengo y’imari yawe ari nto, iyi ni inzira nziza, ariko nimba ushobora guhitamwo ikindi gisumba ibindi, nka Trezor Safe 3 ku €79, ni vyiza, kuko harimwo secure element.



## Gufungura isandugu y'Icitegererezo ca mbere



Iyo uronse Model One yawe, nurabe neza ko isandugu n’igitabu ca Seal bitagira ikibi kugira ngo wemeze ko iyo paquete itafunguwe. Igenzura ry’ubuhinga bwa none ry’uko ico gikoresho ari ic’ukuri be n’uko kidakora neza na co nyene kizokorwa igihe kizoba carashizweho mu nyuma.



Ibiri mu gasandugu birimwo :




- Igishushanyo ca Trezor ca mbere;
- Cardstock yo kwandikako ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, ama stickers n’amabwirizwa;
- USB-A ku nzira ya USB.



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Gukoresha igikoresho biroroshe cane:




- Fyonda iburyo kugira ngo wemeze maze ugende ku ntambwe zikurikira;
- Koresha buto y’ibubamfu kugira ngo usubire inyuma.

## Guhingura Bitcoin Wallet



Kuri Trezor Suite, ukande kuri buto ya "*Rema Wallet nshasha*".



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Wemere amabwirizwa y’ikoreshwa ari kuri Hardware Wallet.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Muri Trezor Suite, kanda kuri "*Bandanya ukora ububiko*".



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



Porogarama itanga amabwirizwa yerekeye ingene wocungera ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic.



Iyi Mnemonic iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe yose y’ama bitcoins ataco abujijwe. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere ata n’umwe afise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha Trezor Model One yawe.



Iryo jambo ry'amajambo 24 rigarura uburenganzira bwo kuronka amafaranga yawe mu gihe woba utakaje, wibwe canke uvunitse Hardware Wallet yawe. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko umuntu ayizigama neza kandi akayibika ahantu heza.



Ushobora kuvyandika ku karto zitangwa mu gasandugu, canke kugira ngo wongere uyikingire, ndagusavye ko wobicapura ku gishingo c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo ubikingire umuriro, umwuzure canke ngo bisenyuke.



Wemeze amabwirizwa, hanyuma ukande kuri buto "*Rema Wallet backup*".



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Model One izokora ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic ikoresheje umubare wayo w’imburakimazi. Raba neza ko utariko urabonwa mu gihe c’iyi operation. Andika amajambo atangwa ku rubuga ku gikoresho c’umubiri uhisemwo. Bivanye n’ingene ukoresha umutekano, woshobora kwiyumvira gukora kopi nyinshi z’umubiri z’iryo jambo (ariko ikiruta vyose, nturigabanye). Ni vyiza ko amajambo aguma afise inomero kandi akurikirana.



**Biragaragara ko udashobora kwigera usangira aya majambo kuri internet, nk’uko ndabigira muri iyi nyigisho. Aka karorero Wallet kazokoreshwa gusa kuri Testnet kandi kazokurwaho ku mpera y'inyigisho**



Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwiza bwo kubika no gucunga ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, ndagusavye cane gukurikira iyi yindi nyigisho, cane cane nimba uri umutanguzi:

## Gushinga kode ya PIN



Inyuma y’aho haza intambwe ya kode ya PIN. Kode ya PIN irafungura Trezor yawe. Ni co gituma ritanga uburinzi ku bijanye n’ugushika ku mubiri ata wemerewe. Iyi kode ya PIN ntaco ikora mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwa none za Wallet yawe. Rero naho utagira uburenganzira bwo kuronka kode ya PIN, kugira ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo kuronka uburenganzira bwo kuronka amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri.



Kuri Trezor Suite, ukande kuri "*Bandanya ukoresheje PIN*", hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Shiraho PIN*".



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Wemeze ku citegererezo ca mbere.



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Turagusavye guhitamwo kode ya PIN ishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo butari bwo. Raba neza ko ubika iyi kode ahantu hatandukanye n'aho Trezor yawe ibitswe (nk'akarorero mu mucungerezi w'ijambobanga). Ushobora gusobanura kode ya PIN iri hagati y’imirongo 8 na 50. Ndagusavye guhitamwo kode ya PIN igihe kirekire gishoboka kugira ngo wongere umutekano.



Kode ya PIN itegerezwa kwinjira muri Trezor Suite kuri mudasobwa yawe mu gufyonda ku tudodo duhuye n’imibare, bivanye n’imiterere ya klavye yerekanwa kuri Trezor Model One.



Ubu buryo bwo kwinjira muri PIN burakenewe igihe cose ufunguye Trezor Model One yawe, haba biciye kuri Trezor Suite canke Sparrow wallet.



![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)



Uhejeje, ukande kuri buto ya "*Enter PIN*".



![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)



Wandike PIN yawe kandi kugira ngo wemeze.



![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)



Kuri Trezor Suite, ukande kuri buto ya "*Guheza gutegura*".



![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)



Ivyo gutegura Model One yawe ubu birarangiye. Niba ubishaka, urashobora guhindura izina n’urupapuro rw’intango rwa Hardware Wallet yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)



Ntituzosubira gukenera porogarama ya Trezor Suite, kiretse gukora ivy’uguhindura porogarama zidasanzwe kuri Hardware Wallet yawe, canke nimba ushaka gukora ikigeragezo co gusubirana. Ubu tugiye gukoresha Sparrow mu gucunga Wallet, kuko iyi porogarama ibereye neza gukoresha Bitcoin gusa.



## Gushinga Wallet kuri Sparrow wallet



Tangana n’ugukura no gushiramwo Sparrow wallet [ku rubuga rwemewe](https://sparrowwallet.com/) kuri mudasobwa yawe, nimba utarabikora.

https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/desktop/sparrow-c674e2ac-d46f-4c82-92a7-7d1b0e262f5d

![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)

## Ni gute worungika ama bitcoins ukoresheje Trezor Model One?



None ko wararonse Satss yawe ya mbere mu modoka yawe Wallet icungiwe na Model One, nawe urashobora kuzikoresha! Huza Trezor yawe na mudasobwa yawe, ufungure Sparrow wallet, hanyuma ugende kuri "*Send*" kugira ngo wubake igikorwa gishasha.



![Image](assets/fr/36.webp)



Niba wipfuza *Coin Control*, ni ukuvuga guhitamwo neza cane UTXOs uzokoresha mu gucuruza, genda kuri "*UTXOs*". Hitamwo UTXOs wipfuza gukoresha, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ohereza vyahisemwo*". Uzosubira ku rubuga rumwe mu gice ca "*Send*", ariko n'ama UTXO yawe amaze gutorwa ku bijanye n'ugucuruza.



![Image](assets/fr/37.webp)



Injira aho uja Address. Ushobora kandi kwinjiza amaderesi menshi ukanda kuri buto ya "*+ Add*".



![Image](assets/fr/38.webp)

![Image](assets/fr/39.webp)



Hitamwo amahera uzorungika kuri iyi Address.



![Image](assets/fr/40.webp)



Guhindura igipimo c’amahera y’ugucuruza kwawe bivanye n’isoko ririho ubu. Nk’akarorero, urashobora gukoresha [Mempool.ikibanza](https://Mempool.ikibanza/) kugira uhitemwo igiciro c’amahera kibereye.



Raba neza ko amaparametere yose y'ubucuruzi bwawe ari ukuri, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Rema ubucuruzi*".



![Image](assets/fr/41.webp)
