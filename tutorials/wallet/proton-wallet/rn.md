Proton ni ishirahamwe ryo mu Busuwisi ryitaho ivy’ubuzima bwite bw’abantu, ryashinzwe mu 2014 n’abahinga bo muri CERN. Izwi kubera inyishu zayo zifunguye, Proton itanga urutonde rw’ibikorwa harimwo ubutumwa bwa Proton, Proton VPN na Proton Drive.


Proton iherutse gutangaza Proton Wallet, ubuhinga bufunguye, bwigenzura Bitcoin Wallet buboneka nk’iporogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa canke umukiriya w’urubuga, ihuye na konti yawe ya Proton. Imikorere ya Wallet ni iya kera cane muri iki gihe, n'ibikoresho vy'ingenzi vyitezwe kuri Bitcoin Wallet nziza, nka RBF (*Replace-by-fee*), gushiramwo ibimenyetso, canke ubushobozi bwo kwongerako BIP39 passphrase.


Ikintu kidasanzwe muri iyi Wallet ni ubushobozi bwo kohereza ama bitcoins hakoreshejwe email y’uwuyakira Address, iyo Proton ihita itanga Bitcoin Address idafise ikintu ihuye na Wallet y’uwuyikoresha. Proton itegura kwongerako ibintu bishasha muri kazoza, harimwo Lightning na coinjoins (bishoboka ko ikoresha Whirlpool, nk’uko bivugwa n’ibikorwa biri ku bubiko bwabo bwa GitHub).


## Rema konti ya Proton


Kugira ngo ukoreshe Proton Wallet, ukeneye konti ya Proton. Ushobora gukora imwe ku buntu ukurikije intambwe za mbere z'iyi nyigisho yihariye gukora ubusandugu bw'ubutumwa bwa Proton (igice ca "*Gukora konti ya Proton*" gusa). Konti yawe imaze gutegurwa, urashobora kubandanya n’ibindi bice vy’iyi nyigisho.

## Rema Bitcoin Wallet


Hitamwo amafaranga y'imbere y'igihe kuri konti yawe, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Rema Wallet nshasha*".


![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)


Bitcoin Wallet yawe ubu yararemwe. Ushobora theoretically gutangura kuyikoresha ubwo nyene, ariko birahambaye cane ko ubanza kubika Mnemonic yawe. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku "*Secure your Wallet*" iri hejuru iburyo bwa Interface.


![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)


Niba utarabikora kuri Proton, uzosabwa gushinga backup ya konti yawe maze ukayicungera ukoresheje uburyo bwa 2FA. Izo ngingo z’umutekano, naho zikoreshwa kuri konti yawe yose ya Proton, zirarushiriza kugira akamaro iyo Bitcoin Wallet yawe yinjijwe muri yo. Ndabahimiriza cane ko mwobishira mu ngiro.


![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)


Kugira ngo ubike ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, kanda kuri "*Backup iri jambo rya seed rya Wallet*".


![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)


Injira ijambobanga ryawe rya Proton.


![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)


Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Raba ijambo rya Wallet seed*" kugira ngo ugaragaze ijambo rya Wallet rya Wallet yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)


Proton Wallet yerekana ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 Mnemonic. **Iyi Mnemonic iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe yose y’ama bitcoins**. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere atashobora gushika kuri konti yawe ya Proton. Iryo jambo ry’amajambo 12 rishobora gukoreshwa mu kugarukana uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri (bitcoins) iyo utakaje uburenganzira bwo gukoresha konti yawe. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko umuntu ayizigama neza kandi akayibika ahantu hatagira umutekano.


Ushobora kuvyandika ku mpapuro, canke kugira ngo wongere umutekano, ndagusavye kubicapura ku gishingo c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo ubikingire umuriro, umwuzure canke gusenyuka.


![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)


Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwiza bwo kubika no gucunga ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, ndagusavye cane gukurikira iyi yindi nyigisho, cane cane nimba uri umutanguzi:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

_**Ego cane, ntukwiye kwigera ufata ifoto y'aya majambo, bitandukanye n'ivyo nkora muri iyi nyigisho.**_


Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Done*" umaze kubika ijambo ryawe.


![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)


## Vumbura igitabu Interface


Interface ya Proton Wallet irakora cane. Ibubamfu, uzosanga ama wallet yawe atandukanye n’amakonti ajanye na yo. Konti "*Ibanze*" ni konti yawe nyamukuru. Kubera ibanga, amafaranga y'ama bitcoins yaronswe biciye kuri email ya Proton Address ashirwa kuri konti itandukanye, yitwa "*Bitcoin biciye kuri email*".


![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)


Kugira ngo wongereko konti nshasha, kanda kuri "*Ongerako konti*".


![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)


Kugira ngo ureme Wallet nshasha, fyonda ku kimenyetso "*+*" kiri iruhande ya "*Wallets*".


![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)


Aha niho ushobora kwongerako BIP39 passphrase ku Wallet nshasha.


![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)


Kugira ngo urushirize kumenya neza passphrase, ndagusavye iyi nyigisho:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7

## Kwakira ama bitcoins


Kugira ngo uronke ama bitcoins muri Wallet yawe, hitamwo konti wipfuza iri ibubamfu bwa Interface, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Receive*".


![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)


Proton Wallet ica ihita itanga Address nshasha, ataco irimwo.


![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)


Iyo amafaranga arangiye, uzoyasanga mu gice ca "*Ibikorwa*". Fyonda kuri "*+Add*" kugira ngo ushire ikimenyetso ku gicuruzwa.


![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)


## Wohereze ama bitcoins


None ko ufise ama bitcoins muri Wallet yawe, urashobora kuyarungika. Hitamwo konti uhisemwo ku ruhande rw'ibubamfu rwa Interface, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Send*".


![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)


Injira Bitcoin Address y'uwuronka. Ushobora gucapura kode ya QR ukanda kuri ako kimenyetso gatoyi. Kugira ngo wohereze kuri e-mail Address, uyishiremwo muri iki kibanza. Umaze kwinjira muri Bitcoin Address, ukande ku mwampi mutoyi hanyuma ukande kuri "*Wemeze*".


![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)


Injira amafaranga azorungikwa, haba mu mafaranga y'amahera canke mu bitcoins, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Subiramwo*".


![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)


Hitamwo amafaranga y’ugucuruza ashingiye ku kuntu isoko riri ubu.


![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)


Wongereko ikimenyetso ku vyo ugurisha, hanyuma usuzume kabiri ido n’ido ryose. Niba vyose bimeze neza, ukande kuri "*Wemeze kandi wohereze*" kugira ngo usinye kandi wohereze amafaranga.


![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)


Ubu amafaranga yawe azoboneka arindiriye kwemezwa mu gice ca "*Amafaranga*" ca konti yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)


## Injira muri porogaramu


Uretse umukiriya w’urubuga, Proton Wallet na yo nyene irashobora gushikira biciye ku rubuga rwo kuri telefone ngendanwa. Mu guhuza Wallet na konti yawe ya Proton, urashobora guhuza Wallet yawe hagati y’umukiriya w’urubuga n’iporogarama ya telefone ngendanwa.


Gukuraho Proton Wallet mu bubiko bwawe bw'ibikoresho:




- [Ku iduka ry’amaporogarama](porotoni-Wallet-itekanye-btc/id6479609548);
- [Ku bubiko bwa Google](ububiko/porogarama/ibisobanuro?id=me.proton.Wallet.android).


![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)


Uhejeje gushiramwo, ukande kuri "*Log in*" hanyuma winjize email yawe Address n'ijambobanga rya Proton.


![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)


Uzoheza ushobore gukoresha Bitcoin Wallet yawe, ifise ibintu nk’ivyo biri ku mukiriya w’urubuga.


![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)
