# Huza Ledger na Sparrow Bitcoin Wallet (uburongozi bwo kwandika)


Raba neza ko ubanza guca mu kindi gice “Gukoresha Amasakoshi y’Ivyuma vya Bitcoin”. Nzoca mu ntambwe zimwe zimwe maze nshire umutima cane ku vyo Ledger ivuga hano.


## Gutegura igikoresho


Ledger izana n’umugozi wayo wa USB. Raba neza ko ukoresha ivyo atari umugozi wose wa kera gusa. Hariho intsinga za USB zikoreshwa n’amashanyarazi gusa. Iyi irungika amakuru n’ububasha. Iyo nkoresheje igikoresho gifise umugozi wa USB wo gushaja telefone uryamye hirya no hino, igikoresho carananiwe gukorana.


Uyihuze na mudasobwa yawe, iyo telefone izoca ikora.


![image](assets/1.webp)


Nimugende mu mahitamwo. Uzobona


1. Gushiraho nk'igikoresho gishasha

2. Kugarura kuva ku majambo yo gukira


Mu bisanzwe, ni ukubaza nimba ushaka ko igikoresho kigukorera seed canke nimba ufise iyo ushaka gukoresha. Ni vyiza kwikorera seed yawe, ariko kubikora ata nkomanzi ni ikintu giteye imbere cane, kandi kiri hanze y’urugero rw’iki kiganiro. Hitamwo “Gushiraho nk’igikoresho gishasha”


Uzoca usabwa guhitamwo PIN. Ivyo si igice ca Bitcoin seed yawe kandi ni ivy’iki gikoresho gusa. Irafunga igikoresho.


Izoca ikubwira amajambo 24 ukeneye guca mu nzira maze ukayandika.


Igitangaje, iyo ushitse ku mpera, havuga ngo “kanda ibubamfu kugira ngo ugenzure amajambo yawe”. Ivyo ntibidondora ingene wemeza ngo ubandanye, bisigura gusa ko ushobora gusubirayo ugasubira kuraba amajambo. Kanda iburyo aho, hanyuma wemeze ukanda ibubamfu n’iburyo icarimwe.


Igikurikira ni ikintu kibabaza cane. Ivanga amajambo 24 maze ubwirizwa kwemeza rimwe rimwe, 1 gushika kuri 24, mu guca mu majambo yose y’uguhitamwo kwose. Iyo umaze guheza, biragufasha kwemeza ukoresheje gukanda buto zibiri maze ugakomeza.

## Kuvanaho Ledger Ubuzima


Ushobora gukura Ledger Live kuri urubuga rwabo, ariko ni vyiza ko uyironka kuri GitHub, aho kode y’inkomoko ibikwa.


Google “GitHub y’ubuzima” canke ukande ![iki](ihuza

Hanukira gushika ubonye umutwe, “Ivyo gukuraho”...

Hasi, uzobona uruja n’uruza: Amabwirizwa yo kugenzura Hash n’imikono y’amapaki y’ugushiraho araboneka kuri iyi paji. Fyonda kuri iyo nzira.


![image](assets/5.webp)


Hejuru, hariho amahitamwo y’amahuza y’iporogarama ukeneye, bivanye n’uburyo ukoresha. Fyonda kuri iyo ibereye kugira ngo uyikureko.


Igikurikira, turashaka kugenzura Hash y’ivyo twaronse, kugira ngo tugire umutekano mwinshi. Ledger isohora Hash ya buri dosiye iriho. Ubu tuzogira Hash ivyo gukuraho no kugereranya ivyo bivako. Bikenewe ko bisa kugira ngo umenye neza ko dosiye itahinduwe.


Gufungura urubuga kuri Mac canke CMD kuri Windows; wandike muri yo amabwirizwa akurikira hanyuma ukande enter:


```bash
cd Downloads
```


```bash
shasum -a 512 ledger-live-desktop-2.32.2-mac.dmg # <--- For Mac
certutil -hashfile ledger-live-desktop-2.32.2-win.exe SHA512 # <--- For Windows
```


Nizere ko bigaragara ko amabwirizwa atangura inyuma y’imyampi. Raba neza, nimba iyi ngingo yacitse igihe, uhindura izina rya dosiye mu mategeko ngo ribe ry’izina ry’idosiye wavuyeko. Ukeneye gutera urufunguzo <Enter> inyuma y'itegeko ryose. Amabwirizwa nk'uko abonetse hano yoshobora kutaja ku murongo umwe mu mucukumbuzi wawe w'urubuga. Iciyumviro, vyose vyanditswe ku murongo umwe.


Raba igisohoka ca Hash maze urabe ko gisa n’ico casohowe kuri GitHub.


Ivyiza ni uko ushaka kuronka ivyiza vyinshi kandi ukamenya neza ko ama hashes asohoka atari ay’ibinyoma. Ivyo tubikora dukoresheje imikono ya gpg, ariko biri hanze y’urugero rw’iyi ngingo. Niba ushaka kwiga ivyo (kandi ndagusavye amaherezo ubimenye), rero urabe muri iyi ngingo.

## Wongereko passphrase


Ubu ko dufise App ya Bitcoin, turashobora kwongerako passphrase ku nsiguro yacu ya seed. Ivyo ntitwari dushobora kubikora mbere igihe seed yaremwa ubwa mbere kuko mu ntango, ntitwari dufise Bitcoin App, kandi twari dukeneye kwifatanya na Ledger Live kugira ngo tuyironke.


Genda kuri “settings” iri muri iyo device, hanyuma ugende ku “security”. Hanyuma uhitemwo passphrase. Uzobona “Ikintu giteye imbere”. Fyonda kuri buto y’iburyo, uzobona “read manuel...” hanyuma umaze gukanda kuri buto y’iburyo, uzobona “back”. Ariko aho si ho iherezo. Mu buryo bw’umutima, wovyiyumvira ariko wongere ukande kuri buto y’iburyo. Uzobona “gushinga passphrase”.


Ushobora gufata ingingo yo “gufatanya na PIN” canke “Gushiraho by’igihe gito”. Ndabagira inama yo “gufatanya na PIN”. Uko ni ko ushobora gushika ku ma wallet atandukanye bivanye na PIN winjiza igihe ubanza gukoresha igikoresho. Iyo “ushizeho by’igihe gito”, uzobwirizwa kwinjira muri passphrase igihe cose ushaka gushika kuri iyo Wallet, ariko yama iva kuri PIN y’imbere.


Injira muri passphrase maze uyimenyeshe.


Izogusaba “PIN y’ubu”. Iyi si PIN uriko urafatanya na passphrase nshasha. Ni PIN winjije igihe wafungura igikoresho muri iki kiganiro.


Ubu ushobora gusohoka ukaja muri menyu nyamukuru mu guhitamwo uburyo bwo gusubira inyuma incuro nke.


## Turaba Wallet


Mu ngingo za kera, narasiguye ingene wokura no kugenzura Sparrow wallet, n’ingene woyifatanya n’urudodo rwawe bwite, canke urudodo rwa bose. Ukwiye gukurikiza aya mabwirizwa:



- Gushiramwo Bitcoin core ( urubuga rwacu/)



- Gushiramwo Sparrow Bitcoin Wallet (ku rubuga rwa Sparrow/)



- Huza Sparrow Bitcoin Wallet na Bitcoin core


Iyindi nzira yo gukoresha Sparrow Bitcoin Wallet ni Electrum Desktop Wallet, ariko nzobandanya gusigura Sparrow Bitcoin Wallet uko mbona ko ari yo nziza ku bantu benshi. Abakoresha bateye imbere bashobora gukunda gukoresha Electrum nk’iyindi nzira.


Ubu tuzoyishiramwo maze duhuze Ledger, na Wallet irimwo passphrase. Iyi Wallet ntiyigeze igaragara kuri Ledger Live kuko yaremwe IHEZE ko dufatanya igikoresho na Ledger Live. Raba neza ko utazosubira kuyifatanya na Ledger Live kugira ngo ntushire ahabona Wallet yawe nshasha.

## Kwakira


Kugira ngo uronke ama Bitcoin, genda ku rubuga rwitwa Addresses ruri ibubamfu maze uhitemwo imwe muri izo uzoronka. Fyonda gusa iburyo kuri Address ushaka, hanyuma uhitemwo “kopi Address”. Hanyuma ugende kuri Exchange yawe aho amahera ariko ararungikwa, uyamanikeyo. Canke woshobora guha Address umukiriya ashobora kuyikoresha kugira ngo akuhe amahera.


Iyo ukoresheje Wallet ku ncuro ya mbere, ukwiye kuronka amahera make cane, wimenyereze kuyakoresha mu yindi Address, haba muri Wallet canke ugasubira muri Exchange, kugira ngo werekane ko Wallet ikora nk’uko vyitezwe.


Ivyo umaze kubikora, utegerezwa gushigikira amajambo wanditse. Kopi imwe gusa ntihagije. Nimugire n’imiburiburi kopi zibiri z’impapuro (ivyuma ni vyo vyiza kuruta), maze muzibike ahantu habiri hatandukanye, hakingiwe neza. Ivyo bigabanya ingorane z’ivyago vy’ivyaduka bishobora gusenyura HWW yawe n’impapuro zawe mu gihe kimwe. Raba “Gukoresha Amasakoshi y’Ivyuma vya Bitcoin” kugira ngo ubone ikiganiro cuzuye ku bijanye n’ivyo.


## Kurungika


![image](assets/22.webp)


Igihe uriko urishura, urakeneye gushiramwo Address uriko urishura mu kibanza kivuga ngo “Pay to”. Ntushobora gusiga Label ataco irimwo, ni ivy’amadosiye yawe gusa, ariko Sparrow ntivyemera – winjize ikintu gusa (ni wewe wenyene uzokibona). Injira amahera kandi urashobora no guhindura n’amaboko amahera ushaka.


Wallet ntishobora gusinya iyo nzira kiretse HWW ihuye. Uwo ni wo murimo wa HWW – kwakira amafaranga, akayashirako umukono, akayasubiza, akayashirako umukono. Raba neza ko iyo usinye ku gikoresho, usuzuma n’amaso ko Address uriko urishura ari imwe ku gikoresho no ku rubuga rwa mudasobwa, n’uko Invoice uronka (nk’akarorero woba wararonse imeyili yo kwishura Address kanaka).


Kandi uzirikane ko niwahitamwo gukoresha Coin nini kuruta amahera y’ukwishura, rero amahera asigaye azosubirwamwo kuri imwe mu ma aderesi y’uguhindura amasakoshi yawe. Abantu bamwe ntibamenye ivyo, bararondera amafaranga yabo ku Blockchain ya bose, biyumvira ko hari Bitcoin yoherejwe ku Address y’abatera, ariko mu vy’ukuri, yari ihinduka ryabo bwite Address.


## Porogaramu nshasha


Kugira ngo uhindure porogarama, ukeneye kwifatanya na Ledger Live. Niba ushaka kubikora, ukwiye kubanza guhanagura igikoresho, kandi ukamenya neza ko ufise amajambo yawe yo gusubiza inyuma be na passphrase kugira ngo ugarure igikoresho. Impamvu nkunda guhanagura igikoresho mbere ni uko utegerezwa gufatanya igikoresho cawe na Ledger Live kugira ngo uhindure firmware, kandi nkunda kudashira ahabona Wallet yawe nshasha (iyo ifise passphrase) kuri Ledger Live, ever. Ntaco nizigira ko Ledger itariko irakura amakuru yanje y’urufunguzo rwa bose ku gikoresho iyo nkoresheje Ledger Live. Bavuga ko batabikora, ariko jewe ubwanje sinshobora kubigenzura kiretse nsomye kode, kandi ntahura n’ibikoresho vyo mu mutima.
