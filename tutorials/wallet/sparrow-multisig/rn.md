Wallet y'imikono myinshi (kenshi yitwa "*Multisig*") ni umubumbe wa Bitcoin Wallet usaba imikono myinshi y'ibanga, iva ku mfunguruzo zitandukanye, kugira ngo yemererwe gukoresha amahera. Udakunze Wallet isanzwe ("*singlesig*"), aho urufunguzo rumwe rw'ibanga ruhagije kugira ngo umuntu afungure UTXO, Multisig ishingiye ku citegererezo ca **m-of-n**: ku rufunguzo _n_ rujanye n'urufunguzo rwa Wallet, _m_ ategerezwa gusinya ku giciro cose.



Ubwo buryo buratuma ubugenzuzi bwa Wallet bushobora gusangira hagati y’ibigo canke ibikoresho vyinshi. Nk’akarorero, mu ntumbero ya 2-ku-3, imigwi itatu y’imfunguruzo yigenga irasohoka, ariko ibiri gusa ni vyo bikenewe kugira ngo umuntu arekure amahera. Ubwo buhinga buragabanya cane ingorane zijanye n’ugusenyuka canke gutakaza urufunguzo: umusuma afise urufunguzo rumwe gusa ntashobora gukuraho Wallet, kandi uwuyikoresha atakaje urufunguzo rumwe arashobora kuronka amahera yiwe n’izindi zibiri zisigaye.



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Ariko rero, uwo mutekano mwinshi uzanana n’ibintu vyinshi bigoranye. Gushinga Multisig Wallet bisaba gucungera amajambo menshi ya Mnemonic (imwe ku nkuru y'umukono) n'imfunguruzo za bose zagutse ("*xpub*"). Nkako, nimba uriko urakoresha Multisig 2-of-3 Wallet, kugira ngo ubone Wallet utegerezwa kuba ufise amajambo yose atatu ya Mnemonic, canke n’imiburiburi abiri mu majambo atatu. Ariko iyo ufise amajambo abiri gusa muri ayo atatu, urakeneye kandi gushika ku *xpubs* zitatu, iyo utazifise ntibizoshoboka ko uronka imfunguruzo za bose zikenewe kugira ngo ushikire ama bitcoins zikingira.



Mu ncamake, kugira ngo ubone Multisig Wallet, utegerezwa :




- Canke ushikire amajambo yose ya Mnemonic ajanye n’ikintu cose c’umukono;
- Canke afise umubare mutoyi w’amajambo Mnemonic asabwa n’uruzitiro kugira ngo ashobore gusinya, kandi afise n’uburenganzira bwo gushika ku xpubs z’ibintu vyose kugira ngo ashobore kuronka imfunguruzo za bose zikenewe.



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Ubwo burongozi bw’ibiharuro vya Multisig Wallet bufashwa n’ibisobanuro vy’inyandiko z’isohoka*, bikoranya amakuru yose ya bose asabwa kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kuronka ayo mahera. Ariko rero, iyo nzira ntirashirwa mu ngiro muri porogarama zose zo gucunga Wallet.



Multisig irabereye cane cane abakoresha amafaranga y’ama bitcoins barondera umutekano mwiza canke gucunga amafaranga hamwe: amashirahamwe, amashirahamwe, imiryango, canke abakoresha ku giti cabo bafise amafaranga menshi cane. Ishobora gukoreshwa mu gushinga imigambi y’ubutegetsi yegerejwe, nk’akarorero, mu gutanga ububasha bwo gusinya mu barongozi benshi canke mu bagize umugwi.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwiga ingene twokora no gukoresha Wallet y'amasinya menshi n'**Sparrow wallet**. Niba wifuza gukora Wallet y'imikono myinshi ifise amabara y'igihe, ndagusavye gukoresha Liana aho:



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/desktop/liana-306ef457-700c-4fdd-b07a-8fb7a8a29f04

## Ibisabwa



Ku bw’iyi nyigisho, ngiye kukwereka ingene wokora Multisig ukoresheje [porogarama yo gucunga Wallet](https://sparrowwallet.com/download/). Niba utarashiramwo iyi porogaramu, ndagusavye uyikore ubu. Niba ukeneye gufashwa, turafise kandi inyigisho ido n'ido ku bijanye no gutunganya Sparrow wallet :



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/desktop/sparrow-c674e2ac-d46f-4c82-92a7-7d1b0e262f5d)

Kugira ngo ushireho Wallet ifise amasinyatire menshi, uzokenera ama wallets atandukanye y’ibikoresho. Ku Multisig 2-de-3, nk'akarorero, woshobora gukoresha :




- Igishushanyo ca Trezor ca mbere;
- Ledger Guhinduranya;
- Ikarata y’ubukonje MK3.



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Ni vyiza gukoresha imigwi itandukanye ya Hardware Wallet mu gutegura Multisig yawe. Ivyo bituma iyo ikigereranyo kinaka gihuye n'ingorane ikomeye, ntibizogira ico bikoze ku mutekano wose wa Multisig yawe. Ikindi kandi, biratuma wungukira ku vyiza vyihariye vy’igikoresho kimwekimwe cose. Akarorero, mu mitunganyirize yanje :





- Trezor Model One ni ifunguye rwose, ivyo bikaba bituma umuntu ashobora kugenzura uruvyaro rwa seed. Ariko rero, kubera ko idafise igisasu ca secure element, iguma iterwa n’ibitero vy’umubiri;





- Ku rundi ruhande, Ledger Flex, ivyungukira ku bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bitagenzurwa, ariko birimwo secure element itanga uburinzi bwiza cane ku mubiri;





- Coldcard ifise secure element kandi kode yayo irashakishwa. Ni amahitamwo aryoshe ku bijanye n’imiterere yacu, kuko itanga ibintu vyo kugenzura bitaboneka ku bindi bigereranyo.



Imbere yo gutunganya Multisig Wallet yawe, urabe neza ko Hardware Wallet yose itunganijwe neza (Mnemonic guhingura no kubika, insobanuro ya PIN). Kugira ngo ubone amabwirizwa arambuye, ushobora kuraba inyigisho zacu ku Hardware Wallet yose, nk'akarorero :



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/hardware/trezor-model-one-5c250c49-ce3b-4c63-bd05-4600d7c11a02

https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/hardware/ledger-flex-3728773e-74d4-4177-b39f-bd923700c76a

https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/hardware/coldcard-q-73e86d1a-6fe6-4d8b-bb15-8690298020e3

Nk'uko tuzobibona mu nyuma muri iyi nyigisho, birashoboka kandi ko ushiramwo mu mitunganyirize yawe Multisig ikintu kidafitaniye isano na Hardware Wallet, ariko imfunguruzo zayo z'ibanga zibitswe kuri PC yawe. Ubwo buryo buragaragara ko butagira umutekano mwinshi nk’ugukoresha gusa amasakoshi y’ibikoresho, ariko burashobora kuba ngirakamaro mu bihe bimwebimwe. Nk'akarorero, ku Multisig 2-de-3, woshobora guhitamwo amasakoshi abiri y'ibikoresho n'imwe ya Software Wallet.

## Gushigikira Multisig Wallet



Ubu tugiye kubika *Output Script Descriptor* yacu kuri Coldcard (ivyo bireba gusa abakoresha bafise Coldcard muri Multisig yabo), kandi ikiruta vyose, tugiye kubika ububiko bwayo ku buryo bwigenga.



*Descriptor* irimwo xpubs zose ziri muri Multisig Wallet yawe, hamwe n'inzira z'ugukomoka zikoreshwa mu generate imfunguruzo. Ibuka ivyo twabonye mu Gice ca 1: kugira ngo ugarure Multisig Wallet, utegerezwa kuba ufise **yose** amajambo ya Mnemonic, canke umubare mutoyi gusa usabwa kugira ngo ushikire urugero rw’umukono. Ariko rero, mu gihe ca nyuma, ni ngombwa kandi ko haba **the xpubs** z’abasinye babuze. *Descriptor* irimwo ama xpubs yose ya Multisig yawe.



Nimba ivyo bidatomoye, wibuke iki gusa: kugira ngo ubone Multisig, ukeneye umubare mutoyi w'amajambo Mnemonic kuri Hardware Wallet yose ukoresha, bivanye n'urugero (mu gihe canje: amajambo 2), hamwe n' *Descriptor*.



Iyi *Descriptor* nta mfunguruzo z’ibanga zirimwo, ni izo rusangi gusa. Ivyo bisigura ko itatanga uburenganzira bwo kuronka ayo mahera. Si rero ngombwa nk’amajambo ya Mnemonic, atanga uburenganzira bwo gushika ku bitcoins zawe. Ico kibazo kiri muri *Descriptor* gifitaniye isano n’ibanga gusa: iyo habaye ugusenyuka, uwundi muntu yoshobora kwihweza ivyo ukora vyose, ariko ntashobora gukoresha amahera yawe.



Ndagusavye cane gukora kopi nyinshi z'iyi *Descriptor*, ukazibika ku gikoresho cose co gusinya kiri kuri Multisig yawe. Nk’akarorero, ku bijanye nanje, ndacapura *Descriptor* ku mpapuro nkabika kopi imwe n’i Coldcard, iyindi n’i Trezor, iyindi n’i Ledger. Iyi *Descriptor* ndayibika kandi nk’idosiye ya PDF ku nkoni zitatu za USB, imwe yose ikaba ibitswe n’imwe mu nkoko z’ibikoresho. Muri ubwo buryo, ndaronka amahirwe menshi yo kutazokwigera ntakaza iyi *Descriptor*, kandi ndazi neza ko nzogira kopi zibiri (imwe y’umubiri n’iyindi y’ubuhinga bwa none) ku gikoresho cose.



Iyo Multisig Wallet yawe imaze kuremwa, Sparrow iraguha iyo *Descriptor*. Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Save PDF...*" kugira ngo uyibike nk'inyandiko canke nk'ikode ya QR.



![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)



Ushobora rero gucapura iyo PDF ukayikopa ku bikoresho vyawe vya USB.



![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)



Tuzokwandika kandi iyi *Descriptor* muri Coldcard (niba ukoresha imwe mu ntunganyo yawe). Ivyo bizotuma Coldcard ishobora kugenzura ko igikorwa cose cashizweko umukono mu nyuma gihuye n’ica Wallet c’umwimerere: xpubs zigororotse, uburyo bwa Address bugororotse, inzira y’ugukomoka igororotse... Ata iyi *Descriptor* yashizwe hanze, Coldcard ntishobora kwemeza ko amaderesi ya Exchange atashizweko umukono na Wallet.



Ivyo ni vyo bituma Coldcard iryoshe cane muri Multisig: itanga ibindi bipimo ku bitero bimwebimwe bikomeye, ivyo izindi wallets z’ibikoresho zitemera (igihe cose, birashoboka, ko uyikoresha kugira ngo usinye).



Muri Sparrow, genda kuri "*Imiterere*", hanyuma ukande kuri "*Sohora...*".



![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)



Inyuma y'ihitamwo rya "*Coldcard Multisig*", ukande kuri "*Dosiye yo hanze...*" hanyuma ubike dosiye y'inyandiko ku ikarita ya Micro SD.



![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)



Hanyuma ushire iyo karata muri Coldcard. Genda kuri "*Ivyagezwe*", hanyuma "*Ibipapuro vya Multisig*", hanyuma uhitemwo "*Izana bivuye kuri SD*".



![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)



Hitamwo dosiye ibereye maze wemeze ivyo ushizemwo.



![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)



Fyonda kw'izina rya Multisig yawe nshasha.



![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)



Suzuma amaparametere y’imiterere ya Multisig, hanyuma wemeze ko wiyandikishije.



![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)



Multisig yawe ubu irabitswe neza muri Coldcard yawe. Niba ufise ama Coldcards menshi muri Multisig imwe, subiramwo iyo nzira kuri imwe yose.



Uretse kubika *Descriptor*, ntimwibagire kwitwararika cane kubika amajambo ya Mnemonic kuri buri gikoresho canyu co gusinya. Niba uriko uratangura, ndagusavye cane kuraba iyi yindi nyigisho kugira ngo umenye ingene wozibika no kuzicungera neza:



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

Imbere yo kwakira ama bitcoins yawe ya mbere kuri Multisig yawe, **Ndaguhanura cane gukora ikigeragezo co gusubirana ubusa**. Niwibuke amakuru amwamwe y’ishingiro, nk’uwa mbere yakira Address, hanyuma usubiremwo amasakoshi yawe y’ibikoresho mu gihe Wallet ikiri ubusa. Inyuma y'aho, gerageza gusubizaho Multisig Wallet yawe ku bikoresho vy'ibikoresho ukoresheje amafoto yawe y'impapuro z'amajambo Mnemonic, hanyuma kuri Sparrow ukoresheje *Descriptor*. Suzuma ko Address ya mbere yasohotse inyuma yo gusubizaho ihuye n’iyo wanditse mu ntango. Nivyashika, urashobora kwizigira yuko amakete yawe yo mu mpapuro ari ay’ukwizigirwa.



Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwo gukora ikibazo co gukira, ndagusavye urabe iyi yindi nyigisho:



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/recovery-test-5a75db51-a6a1-4338-a02a-164a8d91b895

## Kwakira amafaranga kuri Multisig yawe



Wallet yawe ubu iriteguye kwakira ama bitcoins. Muri Sparrow, fyonda ku rubuga rwa "*Kwakira*".



![Image](assets/fr/30.webp)



Imbere yo gukoresha Address ivugwa na Sparrow wallet, fata umwanya wo kuyisuzuma ataco uhinduye ku gicapo c’amasakoshi yawe y’ibikoresho. Ivyo bizotuma Address itahindurwa, kandi ko ibikoresho vyawe bifise imfunguruzo z’ibanga zikenewe kugira ngo ukoreshe amahera ajanye na yo. Ivyo bifasha kugukingira ibitero vyinshi.



Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku "*Erekana Address*" kugira ngo ugaragaze Address kuri Trezor yawe canke Ledger, iyo ihujwe n'umugozi.



![Image](assets/fr/31.webp)



Na Coldcard, uku kugenzura gushobora gukorwa ata gukorana na Sparrow. Gufungura gusa "*Address Explorer*", hanyuma uhitemwo Multisig yawe iri musi cane.



![Image](assets/fr/32.webp)



Uzoca ubona amaderesi y'ukwakira abantu yashizweho na Multisig.



![Image](assets/fr/33.webp)



Suzuma ko Address yerekanwa kuri buri Hardware Wallet ihuye neza n'iyiri muri Sparrow wallet. Ni vyiza gukora ivyo imbere gato y’uko usangira Address n’uwuyitanga, kugira ngo umenye neza ubutungane bwayo.



Ushobora rero gushiramwo "*Label*" kuri iyi Address, kugira ngo werekane inkomoko y'ama bitcoins yaronse. Ubu ni uburyo bwiza bwo gutunganya uburongozi bw’ama UTXO yanyu.



![Image](assets/fr/34.webp)



Ivyo bimaze kugenzurwa, urashobora gukoresha Address kugira ngo uronke ama bitcoins.



![Image](assets/fr/35.webp)



## Kurungika amafaranga y'ibiceri na Multisig yawe



None ko wararonse Satss zawe za mbere kuri Multisig Wallet yawe, nawe urashobora kuzikoresha! Muri Sparrow, genda kuri "*Send*" kugira ngo wubake igikorwa gishasha.



![Image](assets/fr/36.webp)



Niba wipfuza gukoresha *Coin Control*, ni ukuvuga guhitamwo n'amaboko UTXOs wipfuza gukoresha, genda kuri "*UTXOs*". Hitamwo UTXOs wipfuza gukoresha, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ohereze Ivyatoranijwe*". Uzoca urungikwa ku rubuga rwa "*Send*", UTXOs zikaba zaramaze kwuzuzwa.



![Image](assets/fr/37.webp)



Injira aho uja Address. Aderesi nyinshi zishobora kwongerwako ukanda kuri "*+ Wongereko*".



![Image](assets/fr/38.webp)



Wongereko "*Label*" kugira ngo udondore intumbero y'ayo mahera, kugira ngo vyorohe gukurikirana amafaranga yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/39.webp)



Injira amahera azorungikwa kuri Address yatowe.



![Image](assets/fr/40.webp)



Tunganya igipimo c'amafaranga bivanye n'ingene urubuga ruri. Nk’akarorero, raba [Mempool.ikibanza](https://Mempool.ikibanza/) kugira uhitemwo urugero rw’ibara ryiza.



Uhejeje kugenzura amaparametere yose y'ibikorwa, kanda kuri "*Rema ibikorwa*".



![Image](assets/fr/41.webp)



Niba unezerewe na vyose, fyonda ku "*Sozera Ibikorwa vyo Gusinya*".



![Image](assets/fr/42.webp)



Hasi ku rubuga, uzobona ko Sparrow irindiriye amasinyatire 2. Ivyo ni ibisanzwe: Wallet ikoreshwa ngaha ni Multisig 2-de-3.



![Image](assets/fr/43.webp)



Ntangura gusinya nkoresheje Coldcard yanje. Kugira ngo mbikore, nshiramwo ikarita ya Micro SD muri mudasobwa, hanyuma nkafyonda kuri "*Save Transaction*".



![Image](assets/fr/44.webp)



Hari uburyo 3 bwo gutanga amafaranga ngo asinywe kuri Hardware Wallet yawe, hanyuma ukayakura kuri Sparrow. Ica mbere ni ugukoresha ikarita Micro SD, nk’uko tuzobigira hano ku ikarita Coldcard. Ica kabiri ni biciye ku nzira y’uguhuza, iyo tuzokoresha ku musinya wa kabiri (Ledger na Trezor). Ubwa nyuma, birashoboka gukoresha kode ya QR, ku bikoresho bifise amafoto nka Coldcard Q, Jade Plus canke Passport V2.



Iyo PSBT (*Partially Signed Bitcoin Transaction*) imaze kubikwa kuri Micro SD, ndayishira muri Coldcard MK3, hanyuma ngahitamwo "*Iteguye gusinya*".



![Image](assets/fr/45.webp)



Ku gicapo cawe ca Hardware Wallet, nusuzume neza ibijanye n’ivyo ukoresha: Address y’uwuronka, amahera yoherejwe be n’amahera yoherezwa. Igihe amafaranga yemejwe, yemeze kugira ngo ukomeze gusinya.



![Image](assets/fr/46.webp)



Hanyuma usubize Micro SD kuri mudasobwa yawe, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Gushiramwo Ibikorwa*" muri Sparrow. Hitamwo PSBT yashizweko umukono na Coldcard mu madosiye yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/47.webp)



Ushobora kubona ko umukono wa Coldcard wongereweko. Ubu ngiye gukoresha igikoresho ca kabiri, muri iki gihe ni Ledger, kugira ngo nkore umukono wa kabiri usabwa. Ndayihuza, ndayifungura, hanyuma nfyonda kuri "*Sign*" kuri Sparrow.



![Image](assets/fr/48.webp)



Fyonda kuri "*Sign*" iruhande y'izina rya Hardware Wallet yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/49.webp)



Igihe ca mbere ukoresha Ledger yawe n’iyi Multisig, Sparrow izogusaba kugenzura imfunguruzo za bose zagutse (xpubs) z’abashizeko umukono. Nk’uko biri ku Coldcard, iyo ntambwe irakubuza gusinya ataco ubona mu nyuma. Kugira ngo wemeze ayo makuru, gereranya xpub yerekanwa ku gicapo ca Ledger n’izo zitangwa n’izindi nkweto zawe z’ibikoresho.



![Image](assets/fr/50.webp)



Suzuma urupapuro rwa Address rw’uwuronka, amahera yoherejwe be n’amahera yo gukoresha, hanyuma ushireko umukono kuri iyo nkuru.



![Image](assets/fr/51.webp)



Kanda ku gicapo kugira ngo usinye.



![Image](assets/fr/52.webp)



Sparrow ubu ifise imikono ibiri ikenewe kugira ngo irekure amahera ya Multisig Wallet. Suzuma igikorwa ca nyuma, vyose nivyagenda neza, ukande kuri "*Broadcast Transaction*" kugira ngo uyitangaze ku rubuga.



![Image](assets/fr/53.webp)
