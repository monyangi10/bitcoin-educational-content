## 1. Intangamarara



Uburyo bwa **Ninja SAFU** ni umuti w’**DIY (Do It Yourself)** ushobora kugufasha gukora **ububiko buramba, butekanye kandi buteye ubwoba** bw’ijambo ryawe **seed** (ijambo 12 canke 24 Mnemonic risobanurwa n’itegeko rya **BIP-39). Iri jambo ni nkenerwa mu kugarura Bitcoin Wallet canke uwundi Wallet wose uhuye.



Aho kwandika amajambo yawe ku mpapuro - uburyo bworoshe ariko bushobora guhungabana - uzoyacapura ku **ibikoresho vyo gusukura vy'icuma kitagira ibara**, bikoranijwe ku **Bolt**. Ico bivamwo ni ikintu gikomeye, kidashobora guterwa n’umuriro, kidashobora gusenyuka, kidashobora guterwa n’amazi be n’ugukubitwa n’inkuba. Ubutandukanye n’impapuro, zishobora gusenyuka n’imbeya, ubukonje canke igihe, ivyuma bitagira ibara birashobora kuzigama igihe kirekire, mbere no mu bihe bikomeye cane (bishika ku 1300°C canke amatoni 20 y’umuvuduko).



Uburyo bwa Ninja SAFU buratanga ivyiza vyinshi:





- Ibanga**: Nturiko uragura igicuruzwa kimenyekanye ko kigenewe gukoreshwa mu gucungera amafaranga y’ibanga. Ivyo bihimba ni ibisanzwe (ibikoresho vyo gusukura, amabouti, isandugu y’ivyuma), biboneka mu maduka y’ibikoresho, ivyo bikaba bigabanya ingorane zo gutera umuntu iyo amakuru asohotse avuye ku mucuruzi yihariye.





- Ugushobora kuronka**: Uyu muti ugura hagati ya **15 na 140 EUR**, bivanye n’ibikoresho usanzwe ufise.





- Ukwizigirwa**: Ubu buryo bwageragejwe kuva mu 2020, bwageragejwe kandi burageragezwa n’abahinga mu vy’umutekano nka [Jameson Lopp](https://jlopp.github.io/metal-Bitcoin-ububiko-bw’ibisubirwamwo/ibisubirwamwo/safu-ninja/), bakabugerageza cane.



Iyi ntambwe ku yindi izokwereka ingene wokwikorera Ninja SAFU backup, kugira ngo ukingire neza bitcoins zawe ku gutakaza canke gusenyuka. Niba wifuza kumenya vyinshi ku bijanye no gukingira no kurinda ijambo seed, turagusavye urabe mu migereka.




## 2. Ibikoresho



Kugira ngo ukore backup ya Ninja SAFU, uzokenera ibi bice bikurikira, vyose biboneka mu maduka y’ibikoresho canke kuri internet.



### 2.1 Ibikoresho bisabwa





- Ivyuma bimesa (M8 ni vyiza)**:
- Ibikoresho**: Ivyuma bitagira ibara (nk'akarorero 304 canke V4A kugira ngo bishobore guhangana n'ubumara)
- Ingano**: M8 (umurongo w’imbere 8 mm, umurongo w’inyuma ~24 mm). Ama washers ya M6 ni mato cane kandi aragoye gucapura.
- Igitigiri**: 12 canke 24 vy’amawashi ku nteruro isanzwe ya seed, wongereko amawashi y’ubuhinga (raba igice ca 3.4) n’icumi canke nk’ivyo ku bigeragezo canke amakosa.





- Icuma kitagira ibara Bolt n'umugozi (M8)**:
- Ivyerekeye**: Bolt uburebure bwa cm 2,5 gushika kuri 5, bivanye n’umubare n’uburemere bw’ibikoresho vyo gusukura, umurongo 8mm. Igipfunsi c’amababa kiratuma umuntu ashobora gufungura ata gikoresho, mugabo n’igipfukisho coroshe kirashobora gukoreshwa.



![image](assets/fr/03.webp)





- Inyuguti n'umubare (3 mm canke 6 mm)**:
- Ivyerekeye**: Inyuguti zifise uburebure bwa milimetero 6 zituma umuntu ashobora gusoma neza kandi zishobora gukundwa iyo igice c’inyuguti cakozwe nabi. Hitamwo ikintu gikomeye co gukoresha incuro nyinshi.



![image](assets/fr/04.webp)





- Inyundo canke inyundo y'urusenga**:
    - Inyundo y'urusenga irakundwa kubera inguvu zihagije kandi zitomoye zo gutera





- Anvil canke ahantu hakomeye**:
 - Igipande gikomeye, Hard (nk’akarorero, igiti c’ikiro 1 canke ibuye ry’amabuye ry’amasentimetero 10) kugira ngo nshobore gufata ibintu bikubita.



Niba udashaka gushiramwo amahera mu mugwi w’ibipfunsi, urashobora kandi gucapura ama washers yawe ukoresheje ikaramu yo gucapura. Uwo muti akenshi uratuma umuntu agira ubutunzi bwinshi, ariko usaba kwitwararika cane kugira ngo umuntu aronke ingaruka zishimishije.



### 2.2 Ibikoresho vy'ubusabe





- Igikoresho co gutera ikidodo**: Gifata igikoresho co gumesa kandi kikayobora igikoresho co gutera ikidodo, kigatuma umuntu ashobora gutera ikidodo neza, ataco akora, akamenya neza aho amadome ari hagati



![image](assets/fr/05.webp)





- Ibikoresho vyo gukingira**: Igikapu gikingira canke umugozi wo gukingira



![image](assets/fr/06.webp)





- Igikoresho gipfutse neza**: Co kubikamwo ibikoresho vyo gumesa



![image](assets/fr/07.webp)


### 2.3 Umutekano





- Amaganzu** n’**Ibirahuri vy’umutekano** nivyo vyiza.
- Pipe wrench** iyo uzoshiramwo igipfunsi, kugira ngo ufate igipfunsi n’igipfukisho c’imiyoboro aho gufata n’intoke.



### 2.4 Ubwinshi n'igiciro gigereranywa





- Igitigiri c’amajambo 24 yo gusubiza inyuma**: amawasha 24 (atari make), 1 Bolt, 1 y’amababa, 1 y’ibipfunsi, 1 inyundo/massette, 1 anvil/igishigikira.





- Igiciro cose**:
 - Amashashi n'amabuye: ~ 15 EUR
 - Ivyuma vy'ibipfunsi: ~ 45 EUR
 - Ikibazo co kwikingira: ~ 55 EUR
 - N'ibikoresho vyose: ~ 140 EUR





- Raba umugereka ku bijanye n’ibikoresho vy’akarorero.




## 3. Amabwirizwa y'intambwe ku yindi



1. **Kwitegura:**




 - Ikibanza c'ibanga, nta kamera (harimwo n'amatelefone ngendanwa)
 - Uburebure bukomeye, bushobora gufata umukubita
 - Amaganzu n'amataratara y'umutekano
 - Sukura amavuta yose n'umwanda wo mu bikoresho vyo gumesa
 - Imyimenyerezo ku bikoresho vyo kugerageza


2. **Turira amajambo Mnemonic** :




    - Ucape ijambo ryose ku ruhande rumwe. Ntugarukire ku nyuguti 4 za mbere, mu gihe iya 4 yoba yononekaye.
    - Kubita cane n’inyondo, ufate igipfunsi n’urufunguzo rw’imiyoboro


3. **Igitigiri c'abamesa** :




    - Ku ruhande rumwe, ushireko ijambo umubare w’aho umuntu ari, rikaba ari ngirakamaro nimba amawashi arekuye.


4. **Kwandika amakuru** (ntibikenewe kandi birakenewe) :




    - Gucapura ijambo ridakenewe ku rundi ruhande rw'umupira
    - Gucapura ikimenyetso ca Wallet ku gikoresho co gusukura c'inyongera
    - Gucapura inzira y’inkomoko ya konti uriko urakoresha ku gikoresho co gusukura c’inyongera. Ushobora gusanga ayo makuru mu mitunganyirize ya porogarama yawe y’ibitabo. Nk'akarorero, ku bijanye n'ibiharuro vya Taproot, inzira y'ivya kera izoba: `m / 86' / 0' / 0' /`
    - Turira muri PIN code kugira ngo ufungure Hardware Wallet yawe, canke amajambo arwanya ubusuma nimba uriko urakoresha COLDCARD.


5. **NTUTURIRE passphrase :**




 - Nimba ukoresha urupapuro rwa passphrase, nurabe neza ko utazorundika ku karata imwe n’iyo ukoresha urupapuro rwawe rwa Mnemonic. passphrase yakozwe kugira ngo ikingire Wallet yawe iyo Mnemonic yibwe. Ibindi bisobanuro biri mu mugereka.


6. **Suzuma ko bisomwa** :




    - Raba neza ko ijambo ryose n’umubare wose bitomoye kandi bisomwa.


7. **Koranya abamesa** :




    - Nimushire urudodo ku Bolt mu buryo bw’urutonde rw’imibare.
    - Ivy’uguhitamwo: wongereko ibikoresho vyo gusukura ataco bimaze ku mpera.
    - Nimushireko umugozi w’amababa kugira ngo bateri ifate neza.
    - Fata neza kugira ngo wongere uburinzi ku mazi, umuriro n’imibabaro y’ubuhinga.


8. **Igerageza ry'ububiko** :




    - Kuva ku bubiko bwawe bushasha, gerageza gusubiza ibitabo vyawe
- Gukingira ububiko** (ntibikenewe kandi biraremeshwa):
 - Mu gukingira imirongo, canke mu mifuko ipfutse.
 - Nimba ukoresha umufuko, nushireko inomero yawo yihariye, kugira ngo ushobore kumenya nimba ari umufuko ubereye atari umutego usubirira uwa mbere.




## 4. Ububiko



### 4.1 Guhitamwo ahantu heza



Bika amakuru yawe mu kibanza **kitaboneka**, aho utaboneka kandi ushobora gushikirwa kugira ngo ushobore kuvyisuzuma rimwe na rimwe. Hitamwo **ububiko butagira umuriro n'amazi**, muhira canke ahantu **ugenzura wewe nyene**.



Irinde ahantu wizigira uwundi muntu (safe ya banki, notaire): uzotakaza uburenganzira bwo kwigenga bwo kuronka amahera yawe, ivyo bikaba biteye kubiri n’ingingo ngenderwako z’ubusegaba za Bitcoin.



Ntukigere uhishura ko ukoresha uburyo nka Ninja SAFU. Ubukerebutsi ni Layer y’umutekano mu buryo bwayo bwite.



### 4.2 Gusubiramwo



Niba bisabwe, nushireho **amakopi menshi** maze uyabike mu **bibanza bitandukanye**.


Naho ibikoresho vyatowe ku gikoresho cawe vyoba bidashobora guterwa n’amazi n’umuriro, ntuzoshobora kubironka iyo bihambwe munsi ya m3 y’ibisigazwa vy’ibintu mu nzu yawe, kandi bizoba bigoye cane, nimba bidashoboka, kubigarura.




## 5. Gukurikirana no gucungera



Naho yoba ibitswe neza, ububiko bwawe burakeneye **gusuzumwa ubudasiba**:





- Kubera ko ataco ubona, usuzume ivy’inyuma **rimwe canke kabiri mu mwaka**.
- Mu gihe **ibicapo** vyoba vyasenyutse, subira ureme igice gishasha co gusubiza inyuma, **ukigerageze**, hanyuma **usenye kopi ya kera** witonze.
- Niba ububiko buri mu gafuko kapfutse :
 - Suzuma aho winjiye
 - Suzuma ubutungane bwayo
 - Ufungure ibahasha ubudasiba kugira ngo usuzume ukuntu ivyo bicapo vyifashe, kandi nimba vyose bimeze neza, ushire ivyo bishushanyo mu mufuko mushasha .
