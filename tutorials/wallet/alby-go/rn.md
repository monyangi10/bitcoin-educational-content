## 🔌 Uhuze Wallet.


Alby Go ifatanya n’umurongo wawe ukoreshwa n’umuravyo canke Wallet ikoresheje ibanga rya NWC (Nostr Wallet Connect). Ushobora guhuza igikapu kimwe canke vyinshi kugira ngo uhindure hagati bitagoranye.

Intambwe:

1. Gufungura Ivyagezwe → Amasakoshi → Huza Wallet.

2. Scan kode ya QR canke ushireko ibanga ry'ihuriro rya NWC (nk'akarorero nostr+walletconnect://...)

3. Ha urubuga rwawe izina ry'akamenyero


Iyo umaze guhuzwa, Wallet yawe canke node yawe irateguye kohereza no kwakira amahera y’umuravyo ya Bitcoin biciye kuri Alby Go.


✅ Impanuro: Ushobora gufatanya ama wallet menshi ugahindura hagati yayo igihe cose.




## ⚡Ohereze Bitcoin.


Kugira ngo wohereze Sats kuri Lightning Network:

1. Kanda kuri buto nini y’umuhondo **SEND**.

2. Hitamwo kimwe muri ibi bikurikira:


 - Gucapura umuravyo Invoice kode ya QR
 - Gushiramwo umuravyo Invoice uvuye ku rubaho rw'ibitabo
 - Injira n'amaboko umuravyo Address


Ushobora kandi guhitamwo uwuzokwakira mu Gitabu cawe ca Address, aho ushobora kubika amaderesi y’umuravyo kugira ngo ushobore gusubira kuyakoresha bitagoranye.




## 💸 Kwakira Bitcoin

Kugira ngo uronke Sats na Alby Go:

1. Kanda kuri buto nini y’umuhondo **RECEIVE**.

2. Hitamwo kimwe muri ibi bikurikira:


 - Sangira umuravyo wawe Address nk'uko yerekanwa
 - Hitamwo "Igiciro" ku generate umuravyo Invoice n'igiciro gisanzwe
 - Fyonda "Redeem" kugira ngo ubone LNURL ukureho amakode ya QR


Ivyo bimenyetso vyose bibiri, QR codes be n’imirongo ya Invoice, biraboneka kugira ngo uwubirungitse bishobore kuvyifatamwo neza.
