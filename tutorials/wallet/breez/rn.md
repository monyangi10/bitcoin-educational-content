Ivyuma vy’amahera vy’ukwifata biriko biraba uburyo bwiza bwo gufata amahera yawe y’amahera (bitcoins) mu gihe wungukira ku bubasha n’ivyiza vy’ivyo bikoresho vy’umuravyo vya Bitcoin. Breez, kubera uburyo bwayo, cane cane iragaragara muri iki gice c’amasakoshi.



## None umuyaga Wallet ni iki?


Breez ni Wallet yizigama yakozwe n’ishirahamwe Breez iguha ububasha ku bitcoins zawe kandi, muri ico gihe nyene, itanga ibintu bishasha vyose mu gikorwa kimwe.


Ushobora gukuraho Breez Wallet kuri Android na iOS ku mbuga zizwi zo gukuraho. Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko turafata uburyo bwo gukoresha porogarama iri ku rubuga rwa Android. Ivyo vyose bivugwa aha hepfo birakora no kuri iOS.



⚠️ **IGIHARURWA**: Ni ngombwa ko ushobora gukura iyo porogarama ku rubuga rwemewe nka Google Play Store canke Apple Store kugira ngo umenye neza ko iyo porogarama ari iyo ukuri n’umutekano w’itunga ryawe ryo muri kazoza.



Aha, kuri Android, niho hari porogarama ya **Breez** (ntimuyivange na Misty Breez, ikindi gikoresho kiva muri sosiyete ya Breez).

## Gufata neza Wallet.

### Bika imfunguruzo zawe


Ikintu ca mbere wokora iyo urema Bitcoin/Lightning Wallet ni ukuzigama imfunguruzo zayo.


Mu Menu, genda hasi kuri **Ivyo ukunda** hanyuma kuri **Umutekano**.


Breez iragufasha kubika amajambo yawe 12 yo gusubirana kuri Google Drive canke kuri server y’umuntu ku giti ciwe iri kure ushobora guhindura.


Hanyuma ukoreshe **Chiffer backup**: ivyo bizokwerekana amajambo y’ingenzi ari muri Wallet yawe ushobora kubika n’amaboko.


![sécurité](assets/fr/06.webp)


Hanyuma ukurikize amabwirizwa kugira ngo wemeze ububiko bwawe maze uhuze konti yawe y’ububiko bwa kure na Breez Wallet.



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

⚠️ **IGIHARURWA**: Kugira ngo wongereko Layer y’umutekano ku Breez Wallet yawe, urashobora gusobanura PIN code maze ukayishiraho kugira ngo yemeze ko gushika kuri Wallet vyemewe.



### Gukora amafaranga yawe ya mbere na Breez


Breez ishira imbere ubuhinga bwo gutahura mu gukoresha kwayo. Kwakira ama bitcoins yawe ya mbere n’iyi Wallet ntivyoba vyoroshe. Ku rupapuro rw’intango, ukande kuri **Receive**, hanyuma uhitemwo uburyo wipfuza kwakira ama bitcoins yawe.


Breez iraguha uburyo butatu:




- Kwakira n’umuravyo canke **ID** Invoice: generate Invoice maze uhebwe.
- Kwakira biciye ku Bitcoin Address**: Kwakira amafaranga y’ibiceri n’ibikorwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.
- Gura Bitcoin**: Breez ishiramwo uburyo bwo kuronka Bitcoin ataco ukoresheje mu mafaranga y’amahera.



![receive-bitcoin](assets/fr/07.webp)



Injiramwo insobanuro y’igitabu cawe ca Invoice, hanyuma ushiremwo amahera wipfuza kuronka.



⚠️ Ku bijanye n'ugucuruza kwa mbere kuri Breez, uzobwirizwa kwishura amafaranga yo gufungura umurongo no kuyibungabunga **2500 satoshis**. Udakunze ama wallet menshi ya Lightning, Breez iguha ibikorwa remezo vyose vy’uruzitiro rwa Lightning, bikaguha umwidegemvyo wo gucunga amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri. Uzobwirizwa gufungura imihora yawe yo kwishura, n’umwidegemvyo wo kuvugana n’uruzitiro rwa Lightning uhereye muri porogarama.



*Utizere, uzobwirizwa kwishura aya mafaranga rimwe gusa, igihe uzoba utanguye Wallet yawe*


![receive-invoice](assets/fr/08.webp)


Invoice yawe imaze gusohoka, urashobora kuyisangira, canke ukayigira scanner kugira ngo wishyure facture maze uronke ama bitcoins yawe.



Kurungika bitcoins kuri Breez ni ikintu gisanzwe nk’uko nyene uyakira.


Breez iraguha uburyo butatu bwo kohereza ama bitcoins.




- Gushiramwo Invoice canke ID y'ukoresha**: Wishura umuravyo Invoice.
- Connect to pay**: Rema ikiganiro maze utumire uwukwakira ngo yinjire mu kiganiro kugira ngo umurungikire ama bitcoins.
- Wohereze kuri BTC Address: Gukoresha ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.



![send-bitcoins](assets/fr/09.webp)



Hanyuma winjize amakuru y’Uwuzoronka inyungu canke uyikoreshe scanner kugira ngo utangure kwishura Invoice maze uyishire mu ngiro.



![validate-send](assets/fr/10.webp)



### Ikintu kidasanzwe kiri muri iyi Wallet.


Uretse kuba Wallet ishobora gukoreshwa mu kubika amafaranga, Breez ni ubuhinga bushasha bwo kubika ibidukikije.


Uzosanga ibikorwa vy'ingirakamaro mu buryo butaziguye muri porogarama.





- Umviriza ama podcast**: Breez ni umukinyi wa podcast 2.0 ashobora kugufasha gushigikira abahinguzi ukunda n’intererano za Bitcoin.


Mu vyo ubona, uhitemwo **Podcasts**, hanyuma urondere, uvumbure kandi wumvirize abahinguzi b’ibintu ukunda.


![podcasts](assets/fr/11.webp)


Shigikira igikorwa c’abahinguzi b’ibintu ukunda mu gutanga intererano.


![boost](assets/fr/12.webp)




- A Point of Sale**: Breez irahuye neza n’ubucuruzi bwawe, ikagufasha gukoresha ahantu h’ugurisha muri porogarama. Ushobora gucunga ibikoresho vyo mu iduka ryawe, ukaronka amahera y’abaguzi bawe be n’amafagitire acapwa generate ku kintu cose uguze. Ikindi, urashobora gusanga amafaranga yawe yo mu karere kawe mu mafaranga menshi cane ashigikiwe na Breez.



Ushobora guhindura amafaranga yawe mu **Ivyo ukunda > Amafaranga ya Fiat**.


![custom-fiat](assets/fr/13.webp)


Mu **Point of Sale (POS)**, urashobora gutunganya ibintu ugurisha mw’iduka ryawe.


![products](assets/fr/14.webp)


Igihe urutonde rwawe rurangiye, urashobora gutanga Invoice ku bakiriya bawe ku bijanye n’ivyo bikoresho, maze ukemera Bitcoin mu bucuruzi bwawe.


![print-receipe](assets/fr/15.webp)




- Ushobora gushika ku bikorwa vy'abandi**: Breez ishiramwo ibikorwa vy'abandi bigufasha gukora vyinshi utavuye muri Wallet. Ivyo birimwo Bitrefill, Amasoko ya LN, Wavlake, Fold, Fixed Float, Ishirahamwe rya Bitcoin, Azteco, Boltz, Geyser, Lightsats, SMS Sats, LN.PIZZA, LNCAL.


![apps](assets/fr/16.webp)


### Inkomezi za Breez


Breez ituma ubwigenge bwawe buba inkomezi zayo. Ibikorwa remezo vya Breez biguha urudodo rukora woshobora gukorana narwo uhereye muri porogaramu (**Abahinguzi** amahitamwo). Ufise kandi ubwigenge bwo guhindura imiterere y'ishimikiro, haba kuri :




- Ihuzwa na Bitcoin/Umuravyo node: Imenyu **Ivyo ukunda > Urubuga**.


![reseau](assets/fr/17.webp)




- Guhindura amafaranga y'ugucuruza: Menu **Ivyo ukunda > Amafaranga y'umuravyo**.


![fees](assets/fr/18.webp)




- Gucungera imihora yo kwishura: Ibikubiyemo **Ivyo ukunda > Gufunga imihora**.


![channels](assets/fr/19.webp)



⚠️ **IGIHARURWA**: Turagusavye ko woba ufise ubumenyi bukeyi ku bijanye n’imiterere ya Lightning imbere y’uko ugira ivyo uhinduye. Ivyo uzokoresha muri kazoza bizoshikirwa n’ivyo uzohindura kandi amafaranga yawe yoshobora gutakazwa.



Ku bazi cane, ushobora gukorana n'urudodo uhereye ku **Ivyo ukunda>Abahinguzi**.


Aha uzosanga imirongo y'amabwirizwa ya Lightning ushobora gukora mu kwongerako ivyiyumviro bisabwa.
