Muri iyi nyigisho, turasigura intambwe ku yindi ingene wokoresha porogarama ya Liana kuri mudasobwa. Mu bindi, uzomenya ingene woshiraho umugambi wo gukurikirana, kwakira no kohereza amafaranga bitcoins mu bihe bisanzwe, no kugarura amahera muri Wallet iriho inyuma y’igihe kinaka.


Muri Mukakaro 2025, amasakoshi y’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none ahuye na Liana yari: BitBox02, Blockstream Jade, Blockstream Jade Plus, COLDCARD MK4, COLDCARD Q, Ledger Nano S, Ledger Nano S Plus, Ledger Nano X, Ledger Flex, Spec.


Niba wipfuza gusubirana amahera kuri Liana Wallet iriho, soma ikiganiro kiri aha hepfo maze ugende uhereye ku gice ca "Gusubirana amafaranga y'ibiceri".


## Kuzana porogaramu ya Liana


Liana ni porogarama yuguruye yagenewe kurema no gucunga amasakoshi ateye imbere, cane cane nk’igice c’uburyo bwo kuragwa bwikoresha canke uburyo bukomeye bwo gucungera. Uwo mugambi wateguwe kuva mu mwaka w’2022 n’ishirahamwe ryitwa Wizardsardine, ryashinzwe na Kévin Loaec na Antoine Poinsot. Ku rubuga rwemewe, Liana yerekanwa nk'"Wallet yoroshe yo kwivura, ifise ubushobozi bwo gukira no kuragwa". Iyi porogaramu ikoreshwa kuri mudasobwa -- Linux, MacOS, Windows -- kandi kode yayo (ifunguye) iraboneka [kuri GitHub].


Liana yubakiye ku bushobozi bwa Bitcoin kugira ngo ireme Wallet iteye imbere. Cane cane, rikoresha uburyo bwo gufunga igihe (*timelocks*), butuma amahera akoreshwa rimwe gusa igihe kanaka kirangiye, kandi bufise uruhara mu kugarura amafaranga y’ama Bitcoins. Liana Wallet rero igizwe n’inzira nyinshi zo gukoresha amahera:




- Inzira nyamukuru yo gukoresha amahera, yama iriho;
- Nibura inzira imwe yo gukira, ishobora gushikwako haciye igihe kinaka.


Igishushanyo kiri aha hepfo kirerekana ingene Wallet ikora ifise inzira zibiri zo gukoresha amahera:


![Schéma explicatif](assets/fr/01.webp)


Iyi nzira ishobora kugufasha gushinga imiterere itandukanye, harimwo :




- Itegeko ry’ugukurikirana (canke ry’iragi), rituma abazungu bashobora gusubirana amahera iyo uwuriko arakoresha apfuye. Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi kuri iyo nkuru, turagusavye gusoma [igice ca 4](https://planb.academy/courses/f3e3843d-1a1d-450c-96d6-d7232158b81f/233c88d3-2e8e-5eba-ac08026-
- Igikoresho co gusubiza inyuma gikomeye gifise igihe co gusubirana, giha uwugikoresha ubushobozi bwo gukoresha Wallet yiwe ataco ategerezwa kubika ijambo ry’ibanga rihuye n’iryo jambo kandi akagira ingorane yo kwibwa, mu gihe c’ubusuma nk’akarorero.
- Urusenga rw'umutekano ku bantu batangura na Bitcoin: bazocungera Wallet yabo, kandi "uwubacungera" (incuti yabo, nk'akarorero) azoba afise uburenganzira bwo gusubirana amahera yabo inyuma y'igihe kinaka.
- Itegeko ry’imikono myinshi (*Multisig*) rifise ibisabwa bigabanywa uko igihe kigenda kirarenga, kugira ngo umuntu ashobore guhangana n’ukubura kw’umwe canke benshi mu bari muri iyo nama, nk’abafatanyabikorwa b’ishirahamwe.


Inkomezi nini za Liana ni uko izana uburyo bumwe bwo gutanga icemezo co gusubirana amafaranga iyo urufunguzo nyamukuru rwatakaye, rukoreshwa mu gukoresha amafaranga y’ubu. Ivyo bigereranya ubuhinga bushasha bukomeye bwo kubika amahera mu buryo busukuye, ivyo bikaba vyuzuye ingorane, cane cane iyo utazi neza ivyo. Liana rero yoshobora guhimiriza mbere n’abakoresha bakunda gutera ingorane cane guhagarika gukoresha umucungezi (nk’urubuga rwa Exchange) kugira ngo bafate amahera yabo maze basubire kuronka Ownership y’amahera yabo, bihuye n’inyifato runtu ya Bitcoin ya Cypherpunk.


Ego ni ko, Liana irafise ingorane zayo. Ica mbere ni uko utegerezwa guhindura Wallet yawe ubudasiba mu gukora amafaranga kuri Bitcoin Blockchain. Ivyo bishobora kuba umubabaro (bivanye n’ingene ukoresha iyo porogarama) kandi bikagutwara amahera menshi (bivanye n’urugero rw’amahera utanga muri ico gihe), ariko ni igiciro uriha kugira ngo ubone umutekano wongerekanye.


Iciyumviro ca kabiri kibi coshobora kuba ari ibanga. Iyo ushizemwo uwundi muntu mu gutegura, aramenya amaderesi yawe yose, rero arashobora gukurikirana igikorwa cawe. Ariko rero, ivyo ntibizoba ingorane iyo uhisemwo gusubira inyuma bikomeye, canke umugambi wo gukurikirana aho umuragwa wawe ata bumenyi afise ubwo nyene ku bijanye n’ibintu vy’ido n’ido vya Wallet.

![Rapide explication](assets/fr/05.webp)


Umaze gusoma insobanuro, uzoshobora gushinga imfunguruzo za Liana Wallet yawe. Iyi ni intambwe ihambaye cane, kuko ari yo igena ibiranga konti yawe.


![Configurer clés](assets/fr/06.webp)


Mbere na mbere, mu rutonde rwa "Advanced Settings", urashobora gufata ingingo ku bwoko bwa "Descriptor", ni ukuvuga uburyo Contract izokwandikwa ku ruzitiro. Ushobora guhitamwo hagati y’ubwoko bubiri: P2WSH (SegWit) canke Taproot. Muri ivyo bihe vyose bibiri, insobanuro y’ivyo gukoresha amahera izoba imwe. Naho P2WSH ituma Contract yoroha gutahura, Taproot iraruta izindi kuko ihisha ibintu bitakoreshejwe kandi ikazigama amahera mu gihe co kuyirondera.


Iryo hitamwo ni ryo guhitamwo: iyo ufise amakenga, reka ihitamwo ry’imbere (P2WSH mu gihe ca verisiyo 0.9, ariko ivyo birashobora guhinduka).


![Choisir le type de descripteur](assets/fr/07.webp)


Inyuma, ushireho urufunguzo rwawe rwa mbere (*urufunguzo rwa mbere*). Uwo mufunguzo (canke ahubwo, uru rutonde rw’imfunguruzo) ruzokoreshwa mu gukoresha amahera muri iki gihe, ataco bimaze igihe. Ukanda kuri "*Set*", ushobora guhitamwo *igikoresho co gusinya* gihuye. Mu gihe cacu, twahisemwo Ledger Nano S yongeyeko Hardware Wallet.


Kwemerera gusangira urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse ruvuye ku gikoresho. Uhe urufunguzo izina rifise insiguro (muri iki gihe, "Nano S+"). Zirikana ko porogarama zose zishizwe kuri iyo telefone zizobandanya zikora neza.


![Configurer clé principale](assets/fr/08.webp)


Inyuma y'aho, ushireho igihe co kwishura, ni ukuvuga igihe amahera ashobora gukoreshwa n'urufunguzo rw'iragi*. Ukwo gucererwa gusobanurwa mu buryo bw’ibice, igice cose kikaba gitandukanijwe n’iminota 10. Bishobora kuva ku minota 10 (ibarabara 1) gushika ku mezi nka 15 (ibarabara 65.535). Ico kigero co hejuru ni ikigereranyo c’umurongo wa Bitcoin, kuko igihe co gufunga gishizwe ku bice 16.


Uretse ibintu bidasanzwe, uhitemwo igihe kirekire kuruta ibindi vyose: amezi 15 canke amabarabara 65.535. Ivyo bizokuzigamira amahera. Ariko rero, turagusavye ko wokora uburyo bwo guhindura (buvugwa mu gice ca "Guhindura Wallet") rimwe mu mwaka, wama mu gihe kimwe c'umwaka, kugira ngo "mugire umugenzo" uwo mugenzo kandi ntimwibagire.


Aha, twashizeho igihe co gukira c’isaha imwe (amabarabara 6) kugira ngo dukore ibipimo vyacu.


![Configurer temps de verrouillage](assets/fr/09.webp)


Ubwa nyuma, nushireho urufunguruzo rw’itongo ryawe. Uwo mufunguzo (canke ahubwo, umugwi w’imfunguruzo) uzokoreshwa mu gusubiza amahera mu gihe woba wazimiye. Fyonda kuri "*Set*", uhitemwo igikoresho co gusinya maze wemeze gusangira urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse kuri rwo.


Kuri iyi nyigisho, twahisemwo Jade. Urufunguzo ruhaze izina ry'ugukangura (aha "Jade"). Nk’uko vyari vyifashe ku gikoresho ca mbere, amakonte asanzwe azobandanya gukora.


![Configurer clé de succession](assets/fr/10.webp)


Ivyo bikorwa vyose bimaze kurangira, urabe ko vyose biri ku rutonde hanyuma ukande kuri "*Continue*" kugira ngo wemeze amahitamwo yawe.


![Confirmer clés](assets/fr/11.webp)

Igihe imiterere y'urudodo irangiye, igicapo nyamukuru gikwiye gufunguka, kigaragaza Liana Wallet yawe nshasha.


Fata akaryo ko kubika igikoresho co gusubiza mu buryo ahantu hatagira inkomanzi. Ikwiye kubikwa ahantu heza, kugira ngo abakuzungura bashobore kuyironka iyo upfuye.


Kugira ngo uronke umutekano wongerekanye, urashobora gushiramwo ibihimba bikoreshwa mu gusubiza mu gafuko gapfutse (*tamper-evident bag*) hanyuma ukandika inomero yayo y’urutonde ahantu kanaka. Ivyo bituma ata n’umwe ayironka, kandi ko igikoresho cawe kiguma gikora.


Mu karorero kacu, twakoranije Elements ikurikira:




- Blockstream Jade nk’igikoresho co gusinya ku butaka;
- Umugozi wa USB wo gufatanya no gusubiramwo umuriro;
- Impapuro z’inyuma z’interuro iyo igikoresho coba kidakora neza canke coba cononekaye (menya ko igikoresho gishobora kuba ari icuma, kandi rero kigakingirwa na Elements, nk’uko bigenda ku bikoresho vy’ivyuma vyitwa Cryptosteel, nk’akarorero);
- Urufunguzo rwa USB rurimwo Wallet Descriptor ;
- Igipapuro c’inyuma ca Descriptor, iyo urufunguzo rwa USB rudakora neza canke rwononekaye (iryo funguro ntiryafotowe hano);
- Ikete ry’ugukurikirana ridondora intambwe zizoterwa kugira ngo ayo mahera asubire.

## Ukwakira amafranga


Igishushanyo nyamukuru ca Liana kigaragaza amafaranga yawe n'amahera ukoresha (ivya kera n'ivyo ubu) bihuye na Wallet yawe. Muri twebwe, umubare ni zero, ivyo bikaba ari ibisanzwe.


![Écran principal](assets/fr/20.webp)


Kugira ngo uronke amahera, genda kuri "*Kwakira*" hanyuma ukande kuri "*generate Address*". Address nshasha ikwiye kugaragara ku gicapo cawe. Ni ndende kuruta iyo mu masakoshi asanzwe: ni Address ifatanye n’iyindi Contract yigenga (P2WSH canke Taproot).


![Générer nouvelle adresse](assets/fr/21.webp)


Ukeneye kugenzura iyi Address kuri Hardware Wallet yawe ukanda kuri "*Suzuma ku gikoresho ca hardware*".


![Vérifier adresse portefeuille matériel](assets/fr/22.webp)


Amahera amaze gutumwa, iyo nzira y’ugucuruza iraboneka ku rubuga nyamukuru (imbere nk’iyitaremejwe, hanyuma nk’iyiremejwe). Aha, twarungitse amasatoshi 50.000 (arenga gato amadolari 50 igihe co kwimurirwa) ku bw’iki kigeragezo. Ntaco bimaze gutangaza ko amahera yimuriwe mu rubanza rwawe azobwirizwa kuba ari urutonde rw’ubunini rurengeye ako gaciro, kubera amafaranga y’ugucuruza.


![Vérifier solde](assets/fr/23.webp)


Ushobora kumenya uko amafaranga yawe azohera mu kuja ku rubuga rwa "*Ibiceri*". Iyi tab ikwereka ibiceri bitandukanye (UTXO) biri muri Wallet yawe. Aha, turashobora kubona ko iyo satoshis 50.000 Coin yaremwe n’ugucuruza kwacu ihera kuri uwo musi nyene (igihe c’isaha imwe).


![Obtenir informations pièce](assets/fr/24.webp)

## Amafaranga y'ubu


Amafaranga akoreshwa ubu ni vyo bisanzwe mu gukoresha Liana. Kurungika bitcoins n'urufunguzo rwa mbere bikora nk'uko biri mu bikoresho vyose vya kera vya Bitcoin nka Electrum canke Sparrow.


Kugira ngo ushireho amahera, genda kuri "*Send*" maze winjize amakuru y'ingenzi: BTC Address y'uwuronka, amahera azorungikwa n'igipimo c'amahera wipfuza. Insobanuro (yabitswe aho hantu) na yo nyene irashobora kwongerwako kugira ngo bigufashe. Mu karorero kacu, twarungitse amasatoshi 10.000 ku Bob imwe, ku giciro c’amahera 4 sat/ov, canke amadolari 0,67 igihe twariko turagurisha.


Liana nayo itanga "ubugenzuzi bwa Coin": ugaragaza Coin (UTXO) wipfuza gukoresha. Aha, twahisemwo satoshis 50.000 Coin zari zaremwe mbere.


![Envoyer fonds clé principale](assets/fr/25.webp)


Hanyuma ushire umukono ku gicuruzwa ukoresheje igikoresho cawe co gusinya gihuye n'urufunguzo rwa mbere ukanda kuri "*Sinya*". Uzokenera kugenzura no kwemeza amafaranga yawe kuri Hardware Wallet yawe. Aha, twakoresheje Nano S Plus kugira ngo dusinye iyo nzira.


![Signer transaction clé principale](assets/fr/26.webp)


Ubwa nyuma, tangaza amakuru ku rubuga ukanda "*Broadcast*". Zirikana ko kohereza amahera bizosubiramwo igihe co gusubirana ibiceri vyakoreshejwe.


![Diffuser transaction clé principale](assets/fr/27.webp)


Ivyo bizoboneka ku rubuga nyamukuru kandi amahera yawe azohindurwa.

## Ivugurura ry'ibikorwa


Nk’uko vyasiguwe haruguru, Liana Wallet isaba ko uhora uhindura amahera yawe mu gukora ibikorwa vyo gukoresha kuri Blockchain. Iyo unaniwe kubigira, amahera yawe arashobora gusubirwamwo n’umuragwa wawe (canke n’igikoresho cawe ca kabiri mu gihe woba ufise ububiko bwongereweko). Ivyo bintu ntibiteye akaga cane, ariko biratsinda intumbero yo gushinga ubu buryo: kuguma ugenzura amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri ataco ukoresheje uwundi muntu wizigirwa, mu gihe wungukira ku rusenga rwo kwikingira.


Imburi izogaragara imbere y’uko amahera yawe (canke igice cayo) ahera kandi ashobora gukoreshwa n’urufunguzo rwo gusubirana. Bizokwerekana ko "inzira yawe yo gukira" (*inzira yo gukira*) izoboneka vuba. Kubera ko igihe cacu co gukira ari gito (isaha imwe), ubu butumwa buragaragazwa ata guca ku ruhande ku bijanye natwe.


![Avertissement chemin récupération](assets/fr/29.webp)


Igihe c’iherezo kigeze, hazoboneka ubuto bugusaba guhindura amahera yerekeye.


![Actualiser pièces depuis l'écran principal](assets/fr/30.webp)


Kugira ngo uhindure ibiceri vyawe, genda kuri "*Ibiceri*" hanyuma ukande kuri "*Refresh Coin*" mu gasandugu ka Coin kahuye. Nimba ufise ibiceri vyinshi, ukwiye kubisubiramwo kimwe kimwe, kandi mu bihe bitari vyinshi, kubera imvo z’ibanga. Kugira ngo amahera yawe agume ari make, urashobora gukoranya amahera yawe mu kohereza Wallet yose ku yindi Address nshasha iriko iraronka, ariko ivyo bizogira ico bikoze ku ibanga ryawe.


![Actualiser pièce](assets/fr/31.webp)


Erekana igipimo c’amahera wipfuza ku bijanye n’ugucuruza. Kubera ko ivyo ari ukwimurirwa kuri wewe nyene, urashobora gushinga amahera make cane cane iyo ubikoze imisi myinshi imbere y’uko igihe kirangira.


![Transfert à soi-même](assets/fr/32.webp)


Ivyo bikoresho (vyanditsweko "*ukwimurira*") bizoboneka gusa mu gice ca "*Ivyo bikoresho*".


![Transactions après auto-transfert](assets/fr/33.webp)


Iyo yemejwe, Coin yawe iratekanye! Ushobora kuruhuka gushika kw’itariki y’iherezo ikurikira.


## Bitcoin gukira


Igihe usubirana amahera muri Liana Wallet, woshobora guhura na kimwe muri ivyo bintu bibiri. Ushobora kuronka uburenganzira bwo gukoresha mudasobwa iyo porogarama ishizweko, muri ivyo ivyo ubwirizwa gukora vyose ni ukuyifungura (ivyo bizoshika mu gihe c’umuderi w’ububiko bwa enhanced). Ariko rero, ushobora kuba udashobora gukoresha iyi mudasobwa, rero tuzotangura kuva ku ntango hano. Zirikana ko uburyo bwo gukira ari bumwe muri ivyo bihe vyose bibiri.

## Ivyiza: Isesengura rya Descriptor


Descriptor ni urudodo rw’inyuguti umuntu ashobora gusoma, rukaba rudondora neza urutonde rw’amaderesi. Ihuza amakuru menshi ahambaye yo gukuraho ibihimba (UTXO) vy’iyi Wallet iteye imbere. Uburyo Descriptor yanditswe bushingiye ku rurimi rw’inyandiko rwateguwe na Andrew Poelstra, Pieter Wuille na Sanket Kanjalkar mu 2019.


Kugira ngo dutahure neza igituma uru rudodo rw'inyuguti ruhambaye, reka dusuzume Descriptor mu karorero kacu, ni ukuvuga :


```plaintext
wsh(or_d(pk([3689a8e7/48'/0'/0'/2']xpub6FKYNH4XbbdADV98yTVxgZZrtB4eE2tiUPreEv5iJAS3U1CvXGAtQGFXSHyFYdYNn9wNa9KU1pwfYoxQhwq4sPXGihD725VncdSy66v9WQa/<0;1>/*),and_v(v:pkh([42e629dd/48'/0'/0'/2']xpub6DpQGv9LkwAQXvghWASvsfA7t1BVj7bGDQ939v32iB6aUJsMRB6inckim26gRp74NBdS2zuyfHNXDZ9dTuNXkFFiz6QvwEeVvBuC2cnRWQd/<0;1>/*),older(6))))#8alrve5h
```


Amakuru akurikira arashobora gukurwa muri iyi Descriptor:




- `wsh` (mu ncamake *inyandiko y'icabona Hash*): Ubu ni bwo bwoko bw'umusaruro w'ibikorwa vyaremwe. Iyo duhitamwo gukoresha Taproot, ikimenyetso cari kuba `tr`.
- `canke_d`: Iyi ni ikoreshwa ry'ubwenge ryerekana ko *kimwe muri ibi bibiri* bikurikira bitegerezwa gushitswako kugira ngo amafaranga yemerwe (`_d` yerekana insiguro yihariye).
- `pk` (mu nsiguro ngufi *urufunguzo rwa bose*): Uyu mukoresha asuzuma umukono watanzwe ku rufunguzo rwa bose rukurikira, maze agatanga inyishu nk'iy'i Boolean (UKURI canke IKINYOMA).
- `[3689a8e7/48'/0'/0'/2']`: Iki kintu kirimwo *urutoke* rw'urufunguzo rwa mbere rwa Hardware Wallet (muri iki gihe ni Nano S Plus), n'inzira y'ugukomoka ku rufunguzo rw'ibanga rwagutse rwahuye (aho izindi mfunguzo z'ibanga zose zikomoka).
- `xpub6FKY ... WQa`: Uru ni urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse rufatanye na Hardware Wallet nyamukuru (aha ni Nano S Plus)
- `/<0;1>/*`: Izi ni inzira z'ugukuraho zo kuronka imfunguruzo n'amaderesi vyoroshe: `0` ku kwakira, `1` ku bikorwa vyo mu mutima (*guhindura*), n'"ikimenyetso" (`*`) cemerera gukuraho urutonde rw'amaderesi menshi mu buryo bushobora guhindurwa bwa porogaramu ya class manage-18.
- and_v`: Uyu ni umukoresha w'ubwenge yerekana ko *ivyo bibiri* bikurikira bitegerezwa gushitswako kugira ngo amafaranga yemerwe (`_v` yerekana insiguro yihariye).
- `v:pkh` (mu ncamake *kugenzura: urufunguzo rwa bose Hash*): Uyu mukoresha agenzura umukono watanzwe n'urufunguzo rwa bose ku rufunguzo rwa bose Hash (*Hash*) rukurikira. Ivyo ni vyo nyene bikoreshwa mu gusuzuma inyandiko za P2PKH na P2WPKH.
- `[42e629dd/48'/0'/0'/2']`: Ico ni co kintu kimwe nk'ico kiri hejuru (gifise inzira n'inzira y'ugukomoka), kiretse ko inzira y'urufunguzo rwa mbere rw'ugusubizaho ibikoresho Wallet (muri iki gihe ni Jade) yerekanwa.
- `xpub6DpQ ... WQd`: Uru ni urufunguzo rwa bose rwagutse rufitaniye isano n'ugusubizaho ibikoresho Wallet (aha ni Jade).
- `older(6)` : Uyu mukoresha asuzuma ko igisubizo c'ibikorwa cakozwe kigomba kuba gifise imyaka irenga amabuye 6 kugira ngo gikoreshwe.


Igikoresho ca nyuma c'amakuru (`8alrve5h`) ni umubare w'igenzura wa Descriptor, kandi gihuye n'ikimenyetso ca Wallet.


Ivyanditswe vyakozwe n’iyi Wallet bizofata uburyo bukurikira:


```plaintext
<primary_key> CHECKSIG IFDUP NOTIF DUP HASH160 <recovery_key_hash> EQUALVERIFY CHECKSIGVERIFY <locktime> CHECKSEQUENCEVERIFY ENDIF
```
