## Kubera iki umuyagankuba?


Iyi ni ubuyobozi burambuye ku buryo bwo gukoresha Electrum Bitcoin Wallet, n’imiti y’imitego yayo yose n’ibintu biteye ubwoba -ikintu nateguye inyuma y’imyaka myinshi nkoresha, no kwigisha abanyeshure ivyerekeye umutekano/ubuzima bwite bwa Bitcoin. Electrum si Bitcoin Wallet nziza ku muntu ashaka ko vyose biguma vyoroshe uko bishoboka kwose, kandi agahitamwo kuguma ku rugero rw’intango. Ahubwo, ni ivy’umuntu ari, canke yipfuza kuba, umukoresha w’“ububasha”.


Ku ba Bitcoiner bashasha, ni vyiza cane gusa iyo bari munsi y’ubugenzuzi bw’umukoresha azi utuntu n’utundi kugira ngo ababereke inzira. Iyo biga kuyikoresha yonyene, vyoba vyiza igihe cose bafashe umwanya wabo bakayikoresha mu kibanza co kugerageza harimwo umubare mutoyi gusa wa Sats mu ntango. Iyi nkuru irashigikira ico gikorwa, ariko kandi ni igikoresho ciza ku wundi wese.


**Imburi:** iyi nkuru ni ndende cane, rero ntugerageze gukora intambwe zose zadondowe mu musi umwe. Ni vyiza kuguma ufise iyo ndongozi kandi ugatera imbere uko igihe kigenda kirarenga.

## Gukuraho umuyagankuba


Ivyiza ni uko ukoresha mudasobwa yihariye ya Bitcoin ku bikorwa vyawe vya Bitcoin (Inyigisho yanje kuri iyi https://armantheparman.com/mint/) _(IRI KURI NYO ku gice c’ubuzima bwite)_. Ni vyiza kwimenyereza n’amahera makeyi kuri mudasobwa “yanduye” igihe uriko uriga ubwa mbere (ni nde azi ingene mudasobwa yawe isanzwe yirundanirije ibintu bibi vyihishije mu myaka – ntushaka kubishira ahabona amasakoshi yawe ya Bitcoin).


Ushobora kuronka Electrum kuri https://electrum.org/.


Fyonda kuri tab Download iri hejuru.


Fyonda ku nzira yo gukuraho ibintu ihuye na mudasobwa yawe. Mudasobwa yose ya Linux canke Mac irashobora gukoresha uruja n’uruza rwa Python (uruziga rutukura). Mudasobwa ya Linux ifise igice ca Intel canke AMD irashobora gukoresha Appimage (uruziga rwa Green; ivyo ni nk’idosiye ishobora gukoreshwa muri Windows). Igikoresho ca Raspberry Pi gifise umurongo muto wa ARM kandi gishobora gukoresha gusa verisiyo ya Python (uruziga rutukura), atari Appimage, naho Pi’s ikoresha Linux. Uruziga rw’ubururu ni rwa Windows, uruziga rw’Umwirabura ni rwa Mac.

## Gusuzuma umuyagankuba


Intumbero yo “kugenzura” ivyo umuntu yaronse ni ukumenya neza ko ata n’agace na kamwe k’amakuru kahinduwe. Bikubuza gukoresha verisiyo mbi y’iyo porogarama “yaciwe”. Ni vyiza gusimbuka ivyo igihe cose ukoresheje kopi yaronse gusa mu kwimenyereza, ni ukuvuga ntukoreshe amasakoshi afise amahera menshi. Hanyuma, umaze kwitegurira gukoresha Electrum ku mahera yawe nyayo, ukwiye gukuraho kopi yawe maze ugatangura bushasha, ubu rero ugasuzuma ivyo waronse.


Kugira ngo ivyo tubishikeko, dukoresha ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru y’urufunguzo rwa bose/bw’ibanga – gpg, vyanditse uburongozi ku bijanye n’aha (https://armantheparman.com/gpg/). Igikoresho ca gpg kizanana n’ibikoresho vyose vya Linux. Ku bijanye na Mac na Windows, raba urubuga rwa gpg kugira ngo ubone amabwirizwa yo gukuraho.


Uretse gukuraho porogarama ya Electrum, kugira ngo ugire umutekano, urakeneye kandi SIGNATURE digitale y’iyo porogarama. Iyi ni urudodo rw’inyandiko (mu vy’ukuri ni umubare ushizwemwo inyandiko) umuhinguzi yakoze n’urufunguzo rwiwe rwa gpg rw’ibanga. Dukoresheje porogarama ya gpg, turashobora rero “kugerageza” SIGNATURE ku rufunguzo rwiwe rwa PUBLIC (rwaremwe ruvuye ku rufunguzo rw’ibanga rw’umuhinguzi) umuntu wese ashobora kuronka, ugereranyije na FILE yo gukuraho.


Mu yandi majambo, n’ivyo bitatu vyinjizwa (umukono, urufunguzo rwa bose, na dosiye y’amakuru), turonka igisohoka c’ukuri canke c’ikinyoma kugira ngo twemeze ko iyo dosiye itahinduwe.


Kugira ngo ubone umukono, fyonda ku nzira ihuye n’iyo dosiye wakuye kuri interineti (raba imyampi y’amabara):


![image](assets/2.webp)


Ufyonda kuri iyo nzira birashobora guca bikura iyo dosiye muri dosiye yawe y’ivyo ushobora gukura, canke bishobora gufunguka mu mucukumbuzi. Iyo ifunguye mu mucukumbuzi, ukeneye kubika iyo dosiye. Ushobora gukanda iburyo ugahitamwo “save as”. Bivanye n’uko ukoresha ubuhinga canke umucukumbuzi, woshobora gukenera gukanda iburyo ku kibanza cera, atari ku nyandiko.


Aha hepfo ni uko icanditswe cavuye kuri interineti kimeze. Ushobora kubona hariho imikono myinshi – iyo ni imikono y’abantu batandukanye. Ushobora kugenzura kimwe cose canke ico ari co cose. Nzoba ndiko ndakwereka ingene wogenzura gusa ivy’abahinguzi.


![image](assets/3.webp)


Igikurikira, ukeneye kuronka urufunguzo rwa bose rwa ThomasV – ni we muhinguzi mukuru. Ivyo ushobora kubironka kuri we, kuri konti yiwe ya Keybase, Github, canke uwundi muntu, kuri keyserver, canke kuri rubuga rwa Electrum.


Kuyironka ku rubuga rwa Electrum ni yo nzira idatekanye cane, kuko nimba uru rubuga ari urw’ububisha (ico kintu nyene turiko turasuzuma) ni kuki turiko turaronka urufunguzo rwa bose muri rwo (urufunguzo rwa bose rwoshobora kuba ari urw’ikinyoma)?


Kugira ngo bibe vyoroshe ubu, nzokwereka ingene wobikura ku rubuga uko biri kwose, ariko ivyo ubizirikane. Aha niho hari kopi (https://github.com/electrum/blob/master/pubkeys/ThomasV.asc ) iri kuri GitHub ushobora kuyigereranya nayo.


Hanukira kuri paji gatoyi kugira uronke urufunguzo rwa ThomasV rwa bose (uruziga rutukura ruri musi). Uyifyondeko maze uyikureko, canke nimba yuguruye umwandiko umwe mu mucukumbuzi, ukande iburyo kugira ngo uyibike.


![image](assets/4.webp)


Ubu ufise amadosiye mashasha 3, kumbure yose ari muri dosiye y’ivyo ushobora gukuraho. Ntaco bimaze aho biri, ariko biratuma inzira yoroha iyo ubishize vyose muri dosiye imwe.


Izo dosiye zitatu:


1. Igikoresho ca Electrum

2. Dosiye y’umukono (kenshi ni izina rya dosiye imwe n’iyo Electrum ishobora gukuraho n’iyindi “.asc”

3. Urufunguzo rwa bose rwa ThomasV.


Gufungura urubuga muri Mac canke Linux, canke umurongo w’itegeko (CMD) muri Windows.


Ja ku bubiko bw’ivyo ushobora gukuraho (canke aho ushize izo dosiye zitatu hose). Nimba ata ciyumviro ufise c’ico ivyo bisobanura, wigira kuri iyi videwo ngufi y’i Linux/Mac (AO0jzD1hpXc) n’iyi y’i Windows (9zMWXD-xoxc). Ibuka ko kuri mudasobwa za Linux, amazina y’ububiko akoreshwa mu nyuguti nini.


Mu nzira, wandike ibi kugira ngo winjize urufunguzo rwa bose rwa ThomasV muri “urufunguzo” rwa mudasobwa yawe (urufunguzo ni iciyumviro kitaboneka – mu vy’ukuri ni dosiye gusa kuri mudasobwa yawe):


```bash
gpg --import ThomasV.asc
```


Raba neza ko izina rya dosiye rihuye n’ivyo wavuyeko. Kandi, urabona ko ari akarongo kabiri atari akarongo kamwe. Kandi, menya ko hariho umwanya imbere n’inyuma ya “–import”. Hanyuma ukande <injira>.


Dosiye igomba kwinjizwa. Niwabona ikosa, suzuma ko uri mu bubiko aho dosiye iri vy'ukuri. Kugira ngo umenye ububiko urimwo (kuri Mac canke Linux), wandike pwd. Kugira ngo ubone amadosiye ari mu bubiko urimwo (kuri Mac canke Linux), wandike ls. Ushobora kubona dosiye y’inyandiko “ThomasV.asc” iri ku rutonde, bishoboka ko iri mu zindi dosiye.


Hanyuma tugakoresha itegeko ryo kugenzura umukono.


```bash
gpg –verify Electrum-4.1.5.tar.gz.asc Electrum-4.1.5.tar.gz
```


Iyumvire ko hariho “Elements” 4 hano, imwe yose itandukanijwe n’umwanya. Nashizeko icanditswe mu buryo butari bwo kugira ngo ndagufashe kubona. Ivyo Elements bine ni:


1. porogaramu ya gpg

2. uburyo bwo kugenzura

3. izina rya dosiye y'umukono

4. izina rya dosiye ya porogaramu


Ikintu gishimishije, rimwe na rimwe urashobora gusiga ikintu ca 4 maze mudasobwa igaca iteba ico ushaka kuvuga. Sinzi neza, ariko nizera ko bikora gusa iyo amazina ya dosiye atandukanye gusa n’“asc” iri ku mpera.


Ntukope gusa amazina ya dosiye yerekanye hano – urabe ko ahuye n’izina rya dosiye ry’ivyo ufise kuri system yawe.


Hita <enter> kugira ngo ukoreshe itegeko. Ukwiye kubona “umukono mwiza wa ThomasV” kugira ngo werekane ko uroraniwe. Hazoba amakosa kuko tutagira imfunguruzo za bose z’imikono y’abandi bantu ziri muri dosiye y’imikono (ubu buryo bwo gufatanya imikono muri dosiye imwe bushobora guhinduka mu bindi bihe). Kandi, hariho imburi iri musi dushobora kwirengagiza (ivyo biriko biratugabisha ko tutabwiye mu buryo butomoye mudasobwa twizigira urufunguzo rwa bose rwa ThomasV).


Ubu dufise kopi ya Electrum yagenzuwe kandi ikaba ari nziza gukoreshwa.


## Gukoresha umuyagankuba


### Gukoresha umuyagankuba nimba ukoresha Python


Niwaba warakuyeho verisiyo ya Python, uku niko yoyikora. Uzobona kuri paji yo gukuraho ibi:


![image](assets/5.webp)


Ku Linux, Ni vyiza ko ubanza guhindura sisitemu yawe:


```bash
sudo apt-get update
sudo apt-get upgrade
```


Kopa umwandiko w'umuhondo ushizwe ahabona, uwushire mu gitereko, hanyuma ukande <enter>. Uzosabwa ijambobanga ryawe, bishoboka ko ari ivyemezo vyo kubandanya, hanyuma igashiramwo izo dosiye (“dependencies”).


Uzokenera kandi gukura dosiye zip mu bubiko uhisemwo. Ushobora kubikora ukoresheje umukoresha w’igishushanyo Interface, canke uhereye ku murongo w’itegeko (itegeko ry’ibara ry’agahama) – wibuke amazina ya dosiye yawe ashobora gutandukanywa. (Iyumvire ko igihe twasuzuma ivyo twakuye mu gice c’imbere, ni dosiye ya zip twagenzura, atari ububiko bwakuwemwo.)


Hariho uburyo bwo “gushiramwo” ukoresheje porogarama ya PIP, ariko ivyo ntibikenewe, kandi vyongerako intambwe z’inyongera n’ugushiramwo amadosiye. Gusa n’ukoreshe porogarama ukoresheje terminal kugira ngo urenge ivyo vyose.


Intambwe (Linux) ni:


1. genda ku bubiko aho amadosiye akurwa

2. ubwoko: ./gukoresha_umuyagankuba


Ku Mac, intambwe ni zimwe ariko ushobora gukenera kubanza gushiramwo Python3, hanyuma ukoreshe iri tegeko kugira ngo ukore:


```bash
python3 ./run_electrum
```


Igihe Electrum iriko irakora, idirisha ry’ibarabara rizoguma ryuguruye. Iyo uyifunze, izoheza porogarama ya Electrum. Gusa n’uwugabanye mu gihe ukoresha Electrum.


### Gukoresha umuyagankuba n'ishusho


Ivyo biroroshe gato, ariko ntivyoroshe nk’idosiye ya Windows. Bivanye na verisiyo ya Linux ukoresha, ku buryo busanzwe, amadosiye ya Appimage ashobora kuba afise ibirango vyashizweho kugira ngo gushirwa mu ngiro ntikwemererwe na sisitemu. Ivyo tubwirizwa kubihindura. Niba Appimages yawe ikora, urashobora gusimbuka iyi ntambwe. Kugendera aho dosiye iri, ukoresheje terminal, hanyuma ukoreshe iri tegeko:


```bash
sudo chmod ug+x Electrum-4.1.5-x86_64.AppImage
```


“sudo” itanga uburenganzira bw’abakoresha bakuru; “chmod” ni itegeko ryo guhindura uburyo, guhindura uwushobora gusoma, kwandika canke gukora; “ug+x” bisigura ko turiko turahindura umukoresha n’umugwi kugira ngo twemere gushirwa mu ngiro.


Guhindura izina rya dosiye kugira ngo rijane na verisiyo yawe.


Hanyuma, Electrum izokora mu gukanda kabiri ku kimenyetso ca Appimage.


### Gukoresha umuyagankuba na Mac


Fyonda kabiri gusa kuri dosiye yakuweko (ni “drive”). Idirisha rizokwuguruka. Kurura ikimenyetso ca Electrum kiri mw’idirisha ku biro vyawe, canke aho ushaka hose kubika porogarama. Ushobora rero “gukuraho” iyo drive, hanyuma ugafuta iyo drive (dosiye yashizwe kuri interineti).


Kugira ngo ukoreshe iyo porogarama, ushobora kuyikanda kabiri gusa. Ushobora kuronka amakosa amwe amwe y’“nanny” yihariye kuri Mac akeneye gukurwaho.


### Gukoresha umuyagankuba n'amadirisho


Naho nanka Windows kuruta ibindi vyose, ubu ni bwo buryo bworoshe. Fyonda kabiri kuri dosiye ishobora gukorwa kugira ngo ukore.


## Tangana n’ikinyoma Wallet.


Iyo ubanza gushiramwo Electrum, idirisha rizofunguka gutya:


![image](assets/6.webp)


Mu nyuma tuzohitamwo server yawe n’amaboko, ariko ubu, reka ivy’imbere maze wihuze.


Igikurikira, nushireho Wallet y’ikinyoma – ntukigere ushiramwo amahera muri iyi Wallet. Intumbero y'iyi Wallet ni ugutera imbere biciye muri porogarama no kumenya neza ko vyose bikora neza imbere y'uko ushiramwo Wallet yawe nyayo. Turagerageza kwirinda guheba ubuzima bwite mu mpanuka dukoresheje Wallet nyayo. Niba uriko urimenyereza gusa, Wallet urema ishobora gufatwa nk’ikinyoma Wallet uko biri kwose.


Ushobora gusiga iryo zina ngo “default_wallet” canke ukarihindura uko ushaka kwose, hanyuma ukande ku bikurikira. Mu nyuma, nimba ufise ama wallet menshi, urashobora kuyaronka ukayafungura kuri iyi ntambwe ubanza gukanda “Hitamwo...”


![image](assets/7.webp)


Hitamwo “Wallet isanzwe” na <Ibikurikira>:


![image](assets/8.webp)


Hanyuma, uhitemwo “Ndasanzwe mfise seed”. Sinshaka ko mugira akamenyero ko gukora Electrum seed, kuko ikoresha protocole yayo idahuye n’izindi wallets – ni co gituma tutakanda “new seed”.


![image](assets/9.webp)


Genda kuri https://iancoleman.io/bip39/ maze ureme igishushanyo c'ikinyoma seed. Ubwa mbere, hindura umubare w’ijambo ube 12 (ari vyo bimenyerewe), hanyuma ukande kuri “generate” maze ukope amajambo ari muri ako gasandugu uyashire ku rubaho rwawe.


![image](assets/10.webp)


Hanyuma ushire ayo majambo muri Electrum. Aha niho akarorero:


![image](assets/11.webp)


Electrum izoba iriko irondera amajambo ahuye n’umurongo wayo bwite. Ivyo tubwirizwa kubirenga. Fyonda amahitamwo, hanyuma uhitemwo BIP39 seed:


![image](assets/12.webp)


seed rero iraheza ikagira akamaro. (Imbere y’uko Electrum ikora ivyo, yari yiteze Electrum seed rero iyo seed yabonwa ko ataco imaze). Imbere y’uko ukanda kuri next, urabe inyandiko ivuga ngo “Checksum OK”. Ni ngombwa (ku Wallet nyayo ushobora gukoresha mu nyuma) ko ubona ivyo imbere y’uko ukomeza, kuko vyemeza ko seed washizemwo ari ukuri.


**Inzira yihuta kugira ngo ubone imburi ihambaye:** intumbero y’ibara ry’isuzuma ni ukumenya neza ko winjije seed yawe ata makosa yo kwandika. Igiharuro c’isuzuma ni igice ca nyuma ca seed (ijambo rya 12 riheza rikaba ijambo ry’isuzuma) mu mibare rigenwa n’igice ca mbere ca seed (amajambo 11). Iyo wanditse ikintu kibi mu ntango, ijambo checksum ntirizohura n’imibare, kandi porogarama ya Wallet izogumenyesha n’imburi. Ivyo ntibisigura ko seed idashobora gukoreshwa mu guhingura Bitcoin Wallet ikora. Ibaze ko urema Wallet ifise ikosa ryo kwandika, ugashiramwo Wallet Bitcoin, hanyuma umusi umwe ushobora gukenera kugarura Wallet, ariko iyo ubikoze, ntusubira kurema ikosa ryo kwandika – uzogarura Wallet idakwiriye! Birateye akaga cane ko Electrum izogureka ugakora Wallet nimba umubare wawe w’isuzuma udafise akamaro, rero urabe maso, ni inshingano yawe kumenya neza. Ayandi ma wallet ntazoguha uburenganzira bwo gukomeza, ivyo bikaba bitekanye cane. Ivyo ni bimwe muvyo mvuga iyo mvuze ngo Electrum ni nziza gukoresha, umaze kwiga kuyikoresha neza (Electrum devs bakwiye kubikosora).


Urabona ko iyo ushaka kwongerako passphrase, amahirwe yo guhitamwo iyo ari muri iri dirisha ry’amahitamwo, hejuru cane.


Uhejeje gukanda OK, uzosubira aho wanditse ijambo seed. Niwaba wahisemwo uburyo bwa passphrase, NTUZOBURINZA n’amajambo ya seed (ikibazo c’ivyo kirakurikira).


Niba utasavye passphrase, uzobona iyi nkuru ikurikira – amahitamwo menshi y’ubwoko bw’inyandiko yawe ya Wallet n’inzira y’aho ikomoka ushobora kwiga hano (https://armantheparman.com/public-and-private-keys/), ariko ureke gusa ivyo vyategekanijwe maze ugende.


![image](assets/13.webp)


**Kugira ngo ubone amakuru menshi** Uburyo bwa mbere buragufasha guhitamwo hagati ya:



- iragi (amaderesi atangura na “1”),
- Pay-to-Script-Hash (amaderesi atangura na “3”), .
- bech32/SegWit y’akavukire (amaderesi atangura na “bc1q”).


Mu gihe c’ukwandika, Electrum ntirashigikira Taproot (amaderesi atangura na “bc1p”). Ihitamwo rya kabiri muri iri dirisha rigufasha guhindura inzira y'inkomoko. Ndagusavye ko utokwigera uhindura ivyo, cane cane imbere y’uko utahura ico bisobanura. Abantu bazoshimika ku kamaro ko kwandika inzira y’ugukomoka kugira ngo ushobore gusubirana Wallet yawe iyo bikenewe, ariko niwayisiga nk’ivyo usanzwe, birashoboka ko uzoba uriko uragenda neza, rero ntuhagarike umutima – ariko n’ubu ni akamenyero keza kwandika inzira y’ugukomoka.


Inyuma y’aho, uzohabwa uburenganzira bwo kwongerako PASSWORD. Ivyo ntivyokwitiranywa na “passphrase”. Ijambobanga rifunga dosiye iri kuri mudasobwa yawe. passphrase ni kimwe mu bigize urufunguzo rw’ibanga. Kubera ko iyi ari Wallet y’ikinyoma, urashobora gusiga ijambobanga ataco rivuze maze ugakomeza.


![image](assets/14.webp)


Uzobona pop-up yerekeye amatangazo ya verisiyo nshasha (ndagusavye guhitamwo oya). Wallet izoca iba generate ubwayo kandi ibe yiteguriye gukoreshwa (ariko wibuke, iyi Wallet igenewe gukurwaho, ni Wallet y’ikinyoma gusa).


![image](assets/15.webp)

### Gushoboza Serveri imwe


Ku mburabuzi, Electrum ifatanya n'urudodo rw'imburakimazi. Ihuza kandi n’izindi nzira nyinshi z’inyuma. Sinzi neza amakuru ahindurwa n’izo node zibiri, ariko ntidushaka ko bishika, kubera ubuzima bwite. Naho woba ugaragaza urudodo, nk. node yawe bwite, izo node nyinshi zizohuzwa, kandi sinzi neza amakuru ariko arasangira. Utitaye kuri ivyo, biroroshe kubikingira. Imbere y’uko ndakwereka ingene woshobora gusobanura node yawe bwite, tuzohatira Electrum gukorana na server imwe gusa ku gihe.


**Iciyumviro:** hari uburyo bwo kubikora mu kugaragaza “oneserver” uhereye ku murongo w’amabwirizwa, ariko ntabwo nsaba ko ubigira. Nzokwereka ubundi buryo mbona ko bworoshe mu gihe kirekire, kandi bushobora kutagureka ngo wihuze n’izindi node mu mpanuka.


Impamvu dukoresha Wallet y’ikinyoma ni uko iyo tuzoba twashizeko Wallet yacu nyayo, na Bitcoin yacu nyayo, twoba twarahuye n’uruzitiro rw’imburakimazi ubu (naho twoba twahisemwo “gushiraho server n’amaboko” mu ntango, iracariko irahuzwa n’izo zindi nzira zibiri kubera imvo zimwe zimwe, hey you private G was-3 were). vyobaye ari akaga.


Ntidushobora kandi gukora intambwe nzokwereka aha hepfo tutabanje gushiramwo ubwoko bumwe bwa Wallet. (Tugiye guhindura dosiye y’imiterere yuzuye gusa kandi iteguye guhindurwa iyo Wallet imaze gushirwako).


None rero funga Electrum **(IGIHARURWA: iyo utabikoze, amahinduka akurikira ukora azokurwaho)**.

"oneserver": false,

## Gukora imyimenyerezo Wallet


Amaze gukuraho Wallet y’ikinyoma, wongere utangure ukore indi nshasha, mu buryo nk’ubwo nyene, ubu gusa, wandike amajambo ya seed maze uyazigame neza.


Ni iciyumviro ciza kwiga ingene Electrum ikorana n’iyi nzira Wallet, ata Hardware Wallet igoye (ikenewe ku mutekano mwinshi). Gusa shiramwo Bitcoin ntoyi muri iyi Wallet – Wiyumvire ko uzotakaza ayo mahera. Umaze kumenya neza, rero wige gukoresha Electrum ufise Hardware Wallet.


Mu Wallet nshasha waremye, uzobona urutonde rw’amaderesi. Izo Green zitwa “amaderesi y’ukwakira”. Ni igicuruzwa c’ibintu 3:



- Invugo seed
- passphrase
- Inzira y'inkomoko


Wallet yawe nshasha ifise urutonde rw’amaderesi yo kwakira ashobora kuremwa mu mibare no mu buryo bwo gusubirwamwo na Software Wallet iyo ari yo yose ifise seed, passphrase n’inzira y’ugukomoka. Hariho amamiliyaridi 4,3 y’abantu! Ibirenze ivyo uzokenera. Electrum ikwereka gusa 20 za mbere, hanyuma ikakwereka n’izindi uko ukoresha izo mbere.


Ibindi bisobanuro ku mfunguruzo z’ibanga za Bitcoin urashobora kubisanga muri iyi nkuru.


![image](assets/23.webp)


Ivyo bitandukanye cane n’ibindi bikoresho bimwe bimwe bitanga gusa Address 1 ku gihe.


Kubera ko winjije ijambo seed mu kurema iyi Wallet, Electrum irafise urufunguzo rw’ibanga rwa buri aderesi, kandi gukoresha amahera muri izo aderesi birashoboka.


Zirikana kandi ko hariho amaderesi y’umuhondo, yitwa “amaderesi yo guhindura” – Ayo ni ayandi maderesi gusa ava mw’ishami ry’imibare ritandukanye (ayandi miliyari 4,3 muri ayo arahari). Bikoreshwa na Wallet kugira ngo birungike ubwavyo amahera arenga muri Wallet nk’ihinduka. Nk’akarorero, iyo ufashe 1,5 Bitcoin ugakoresha 0,5 ku mudandaza, iyo 1,0 isigaye irakeneye kuja ahantu. Wallet yawe izoyikoresha ku mpinduka y’umuhondo ikurikira y’ubusa Address – ahandi ho, ija kuri Miner! ### Gufatanya biciye ku nzira ya USB.


Ubu buryo bukwiye kuba bworoshe, ariko kuri mudasobwa za Linux, biragoye cane. Ikintu citwa “amategeko ya Udev” gikeneye guhindurwa. Ehe ingene (uburongozi burambuye https://armantheparman.com/gpg-ingingo/ ), no muri make:


Ni vyiza kumenya neza ko iyo sisitemu iri ku gihe. None:


```bash
sudo apt-get install libusb-1.0-0-dev libudev-dev
```


none...


```bash
python3 -m pip install ckcc-protocol
```


Niwabona ikosa urabe neza ko pip yashizwemwo. Ushobora kugenzura na (pip –verisiyo), kandi ushobora kuyishiramwo na (sudo apt gushiramwo pythron3-pip)


Rema canke uhindure ibiriho, dosiye, /n'ibindi/udev/amategeko.d/


Nk'ibi:


```bash
sudo nano /etc/udev/rules.d
```


Umuhinduzi w'ivyanditswe azofunguka. Kopa umwandiko uvuye hano uwushire muri dosiye rules.d, ubike maze usohoke.


![image](assets/53.webp)


Hanyuma ukoreshe aya mabwirizwa rimwe rikurikira irindi:


```bash
sudo groupadd plugdev
sudo usermod -aG plugdev $(whoami)
sudo udevadm control –reload-rules && sudo udevadm trigger
```


Niwabona ubutumwa “group plugdev” busanzwe buriho, ni vyiza, genda. Inyuma y’itegeko rya kabiri, nta n’inyishu uzoronka, genda gusa ku itegeko rya gatatu.


Ushobora gukenera guca iyo ColdCard hanyuma ugasubira kuyifatanya na mudasobwa.


Nimba inyuma y’ivyo vyose utashobora gufatanya ColdCard, nogerageza guhindura firmware (uburongozi vuba, ariko ubu, ushobora gusanga amabwirizwa ku rubuga rw’umuhinguzi).


Ibikurikira, ureme Wallet nshasha:



- Itegeko Wallet
- Koresha igikoresho
- Izoca isuzuma kandi ibone ColdCard yawe. Bandanya.
- Hitamwo insobanuro y'inyandiko n'inzira y'inkomoko
- Fata ingingo nimba dosiye Wallet ikwiye gushirwa mu nzira (ni vyiza)

Uhejeje gukora transaction no kuyiheza, ukeneye gukanda buto export iri mu mfuruka ibubamfu hasi. Uzobona “gubika muri dosiye” ivyo bikaba bitari vyo, atari vyo twipfuza. Mu vy’ukuri ubwirizwa kubanza kuja ku nzira ya nyuma cane ivuga ngo “for hardware wallets”, hanyuma, muri iyo nzira, urondere iyindi “save to file” maze uhitemwo iyo. Igicapo kizovuga ngo witeguye gusinya. Fyonda ku kimenyetso, usuzume ivyo ugurisha, hanyuma ubandanye wemeza ukoresheje ikimenyetso. Uhejeje, ukureho iyo karata, hanyuma uyisubize muri orodinateri.

## Guhindura Electrum n'ububiko bwa “.electrum” bwihishije


Electrum iba kuri mudasobwa yawe ahantu habiri. Hariho porogarama ubwayo, kandi hariho dosiye y’imiterere yihishije. Iyo dosiye iba ahantu hatandukanye bivanye n'uko ukoresha:


Amadirisho:


```bash
C:/Users/your_user_name_goes_here/AppData/Roaming/Electrum
```


Mac:


```bash
/Users/your_user_name_goes_here/.electrum
```


Linux:


```bash
/home/your_user_name_goes_here/.electrum
```


Zirikana ko `.` imbere ya `electrum` muri Linux na Mac – ivyo vyerekana ko umuyobozi yihishije. Kandi, menya ko ubu bubiko buremwa gusa (ubwo nyene) iyo ukoresheje Electrum ku ncuro ya mbere. Ububiko burimwo dosiye y'imiterere ya electrum hamwe n'ububiko bufise ama wallets yose wabitse.


Iyo usubije porogarama ya Electrum kuri mudasobwa yawe, ububiko bwihishije buragumaho, kiretse iyo na yo nyene uyisubiyemwo n’umwete.


Kugira ngo ushire hejuru electrum, uca mu nzira nk’iyo nadondoye mu ntango kugira ngo uyikureho kandi uyigenzure. Uzoca ugira kopi zibiri za porogarama kuri mudasobwa yawe, kandi ushobora gukoresha imwe muri zo – porogarama yose izoshika kuri dosiye imwe nyene y’umuyagankuba yihishije kubera imiterere yayo n’ugushika kuri Wallet. Ibintu vyose twabitse, nk’igice c’ishimikiro, urudodo rwo kwifatanyako, ibindi ukunda, n’ugushika ku ma wallet, bizogumaho kuko ivyo vyose bibitswe muri iyo dosiye.
