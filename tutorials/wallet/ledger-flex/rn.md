Hardware Wallet ni igikoresho c'ubuhinga bwa none gikoreshwa mu gucunga no gucungera imfunguruzo z'ibanga za Bitcoin Wallet. Mu buryo butandukanye n’amasakoshi y’amaporogarama (canke amasakoshi ya Hot) ashirwa ku mashini zikoreshwa muri rusangi akenshi zifatanye na Internet, amasakoshi y’amaporogarama atuma umuntu ashobora kwitandukanya n’imfunguruzo z’ibanga, ivyo bikaba bigabanya ingorane zo guterwa n’abantu no kwiba.


Intumbero nyamukuru ya Hardware Wallet ni ugugabanya ubushobozi bw’ico gikoresho kugira ngo kigabanye aho gishobora gutera. Igitero gitoyi bisigura kandi ko hariho ibitero bikeyi bishobora gutera, ni ukuvuga, uturongo dukeyi tw’intege nke muri sisitemu abatera bashobora gukoresha kugira ngo bashobore gushika ku bitcoins.


Ni vyiza gukoresha Hardware Wallet kugira ngo ukingire ama bitcoins yawe, cane cane iyo ufise amahera menshi, yaba mu gaciro nyako canke nk’igice c’itunga ryawe ryose.


Ivyuma vy’amasakoshi bikoreshwa hamwe n’ubuhinga bwo gucunga Wallet kuri mudasobwa canke kuri telefone ngendanwa. Iyi porogarama icungera uguhingura amafaranga, ariko umukono w’ibanga ukenewe kugira ngo ayo mafaranga yemezwe ukorwa gusa muri Hardware Wallet. Ivyo bisigura ko imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitigera zishirwa ahantu hashobora guhungabana.


Hardware wallets zitanga uburinzi bubiri ku muntu akoresha: ku ruhande rumwe, zikingira bitcoins zawe ibitero vyo kure mu kugumiza imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitari ku murongo, kandi ku rundi ruhande, muri rusangi zitanga uburinzi bwiza bw’umubiri ku kugerageza gukura imfunguruzo. Kandi ni kuri ivyo bipimo 2 vy’umutekano nyene umuntu ashobora gucira urubanza no gushiramwo ivyerekanwa bitandukanye biri kw’isoko.


Muri iyi nyigisho, ndasaba kuvumbura kimwe muri ivyo bisubizo: **Ledger Flex**.

## Intangamarara y'Igikoresho ca Ledger


Ledger Flex ni Hardware Wallet yakozwe n’ishirahamwe ry’abafaransa Ledger, igurishwa kugiciro c’amayero 249.


![LEDGER FLEX](assets/notext/02.webp)


Ifise igikoresho kinini gikora ku mubiri citwa E Ink, ubuhinga bwo kwerekana ibintu vy’umwirabura n’umweru. Ubwo ni bwo buhinga nyene buboneka mu bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none. Igikoresho ca E Ink kiratuma umuntu abona neza kandi ashobora gusoma neza, mbere no mu muco w’izuba ukayangana, kandi gikoresha inguvu nke cane, canke nta n’inguvu zikoreshwa iyo igikoresho gihagaze. Ikora ikoresheje utugingo ngengabuzima dutoduto turimwo ibintu vy’ibara ryirabura n’iry’umweru. Iyo hakoreshejwe umuyagankuba, ivyo bihimba vyirabura canke vyera biragenda ku ruhande rw’ico gicapo, gutyo bikaba bituma habaho inyandiko canke amashusho.

Ledger Flex ifise igice ca "secure element" cemewe na CC EAL6+, kiguha uburinzi buteye imbere ku bitero vy'umubiri ku bikoresho. Ico gicapo gigenzurwa ata guca ku ruhande n’ico gice. Iciyumviro gisanzwe co kunegurwa ni uko kode y’iyi chip atari open-source, bisaba urugero runaka rw’ukwizigira ubutungane bw’ico gice. Ariko rero, ico kintu gisuzumwa n’abahinga bigenga.


Ku bijanye n’ikoreshwa, Ledger Flex itanga uburyo bwinshi bwo gukorana: Bluetooth, USB-C, na NFC. Igikoresho kinini kiratuma umuntu ashobora kugenzura neza amakuru y’ivyo mwakoze. Ledger nayo iratandukanye n’iziyihiganwa n’uko yihuta kwemera ibintu bishasha vya Bitcoin, nka Miniscript, nk’akarorero.


Maze kuyigerageza, ndatangazwa n’uko ico kintu ari ciza. Ubumenyi bw’uwukoresha ni bwiza cane, kandi igikoresho kirakoreshwa neza. Ni Hardware Wallet nziza cane. Ariko rero, irafise ingorane 2 zikomeye mu vyiyumviro vyanje: ukudashobora kugenzura kode ya chip, kandi, birumvikana, igiciro cayo, kikaba ari kinini cane kuruta abayihiganwa. Kugereranya, imodoka iteye imbere cane ivuye muri Foundation igurishwa ku madolari 199, iya Coinkite ku madolari 219,99, mu gihe Trezor ya nyuma, na yo nyene ifise igikoresho kinini gikora ku mubiri, igurishwa ku madolari 169€.

![LEDGER FLEX](assets/notext/13.webp)


Flex yawe izotanga amabwirizwa y’ingene wocungera ijambo ryawe ryo gukira.


**Iri jambo Mnemonic ritanga uburenganzira bwo gushika ku bitcoins zawe zose**. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere atashobora kuronka Ledger yawe. Iryo jambo ry’amajambo 24 rituma umuntu ashobora kugarukana uburenganzira bwo kuronka amafaranga yawe mu gihe yoba yatakaje, yibwe, canke yononekaye kuri Ledger Flex yawe. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko ubizigama witonze kandi ukabibika ahantu hatagira umutekano.


Ushobora kubindika ku mpapuro z’ikarata ziri kumwe na Ledger yawe, canke kugira ngo urushirize kugira umutekano, ndagusavye ko wobicapura ku gikoresho c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo ukingire ingorane z’umuriro, umwuzure canke ugusenyuka.


Ushobora guca muri ayo mabwirizwa no gusimbuka amapaji ukoresheje igicapo.


![LEDGER FLEX](assets/notext/14.webp)

Ledger izokora ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic ikoresheje umubare wayo w’imburakimazi. Raba neza ko ata muntu ariko arakwihweza mu gihe c’iyi operation. Andika amajambo atangwa na Ledger ku gikoresho c’umubiri uhisemwo. Bivanye n’ingene ukoresha umutekano, woshobora kwiyumvira gukora kopi nyinshi z’umubiri z’iryo jambo (ariko ikiruta vyose, nturigabanye). Ni ngombwa ko amajambo aguma afise inomero kandi akurikirana.

***Biragaragara ko udakwiye kwigera usangira aya majambo kuri internet, bitandukanye n’ivyo ndiko ndakora muri iyi nyigisho. Aka karorero Wallet kazokoreshwa gusa kuri Testnet kandi kazokurwaho ku mpera y'inyigisho.***


![LEDGER FLEX](assets/notext/15.webp)


Kugira ngo ugende mu mugwi w'amajambo ukurikira, fyonda ku buto "*Ikurikira*". Amajambo yose amaze kwandikwa, ukande kuri buto ya "*Done*" kugira ngo ugende ku ntambwe ikurikira.


![LEDGER FLEX](assets/notext/16.webp)


Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Start confirmation*", hanyuma uhitemwo amajambo yo mu nteruro yawe ya Mnemonic mu rutonde rwayo kugira ngo wemeze ko wayanditse neza. Bandanya muri iyo nzira gushika kw’ijambo rya 24.


![LEDGER FLEX](assets/notext/17.webp)


Nimba ijambo uriko uremeza rihuye neza n’iryo Flex yaguhaye mu ntambwe iheze, urashobora gukomeza. Niba atarivyo, ivyo vyerekana ko ububiko bwawe bw’umubiri bw’ijambo Mnemonic butari bwo kandi ukeneye gusubira gutangura iyo nzira.


![LEDGER FLEX](assets/notext/18.webp)


Kandi aho urafise, seed yawe yararemwe neza kuri Ledger Flex yawe. Imbere y’uko tubandanya guhingura Bitcoin Wallet nshasha ivuye kuri iyi seed, reka dusuzume hamwe imiterere y’igikoresho.

![LEDGER FLEX](assets/notext/28.webp)


passphrase ni ijambobanga ry'ubusa, rifatanijwe n'ijambo ry'ugusubirana, ritanga Layer y'inyongera y'umutekano kuri Wallet yawe.


Ubu, Wallet yawe ikomoka ku nteruro ya Mnemonic igizwe n’amajambo 24. Iryo jambo ryo gusubizaho ni ngirakamaro cane, kuko rigufasha gusubizaho imfunguruzo zose za Wallet yawe iyo zitakaye. Ariko rero, rigize ikintu kimwe co kunanirwa (SPOF). Iyo bishizwe mu kaga, ama bitcoins aba ari mu kaga. Aho niho passphrase yinjira, ni ijambobanga ry’ubuhinga, ushobora guhitamwo ataco ushaka, ryongera ku majambo Mnemonic kugira ngo ukomeze umutekano wa Wallet.


passphrase ntikwiye gutera urujijo n’ibara rya PIN. Irafise uruhara mu gukuraho imfunguruzo zawe z’ubuhinga bwa none. Ikorana n’ijambo Mnemonic, ihindura seed aho imfunguruzo zikomoka. Gutyo, naho umuntu yoronka amajambo yawe y’amajambo 24, ata passphrase, ntashobora kuronka amahera yawe. Gukoresha passphrase mu vy’ukuri bituma haba Wallet nshasha ifise imfunguruzo zitandukanye. Guhindura (mbere gatoyi) passphrase bizoba generate Wallet itandukanye.


passphrase ni igikoresho gikomeye cane co kwongereza umutekano w’amahera yawe ya bitcoins. Ariko rero, birahambaye cane ko utahura ingene ikora imbere y’uko uyishira mu ngiro, kugira ngo ntutakaze uburenganzira bwo gukoresha Wallet yawe. Nzobasigurira ingene mukoresha passphrase mu yindi nyigisho yihariye.


![LEDGER FLEX](assets/notext/29.webp)
