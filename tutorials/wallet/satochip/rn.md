Hardware Wallet ni igikoresho c'ubuhinga bwa none gikoreshwa mu gucunga no gucungera imfunguruzo z'ibanga za Bitcoin Wallet. Mu buryo butandukanye n’amasakoshi y’amaporogarama (canke amasakoshi ya Hot) ashirwa ku mashini zikoreshwa muri rusangi akenshi zifatanye na Internet, amasakoshi y’ibikoresho atuma umuntu ashobora kwitandukanya n’imfunguruzo z’ibanga, ivyo bikaba bigabanya ingorane zo guterwa n’abantu no kwiba.


Intumbero nyamukuru ya Hardware Wallet ni ugugabanya ubushobozi bw’ico gikoresho kugira ngo kigabanye aho gishobora gutera. Igitero gitoyi kandi bisigura ko hariho ibitero bikeyi bishobora gutera, ni ukuvuga, intege nke nkeyi muri sisitemu abatera bashobora gukoresha kugira ngo bashobore gushika ku bitcoins.


Ni vyiza gukoresha Hardware Wallet kugira ngo ukingire ama bitcoins yawe, cane cane iyo ufise amahera menshi, yaba mu gaciro nyako canke nk’igice c’itunga ryawe ryose.


Ivyuma vy’amasakoshi bikoreshwa hamwe n’ubuhinga bwo gucunga Wallet kuri mudasobwa canke kuri telefone ngendanwa. Iyi porogarama icungera uguhingura amafaranga, ariko umukono w’ibanga ukenewe kugira ngo ayo mafaranga yemezwe ukorwa gusa muri Hardware Wallet. Ivyo bisigura ko imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitigera zishirwa ahantu hashobora guhungabana.


Hardware wallets zitanga uburinzi bubiri ku muntu akoresha: ku ruhande rumwe, zikingira bitcoins zawe ibitero vyo kure mu kugumiza imfunguruzo z’ibanga zitari ku murongo, kandi ku rundi ruhande, muri rusangi zitanga uburinzi bwiza bw’umubiri ku kugerageza gukura imfunguruzo. Kandi ni kuri ivyo bipimo 2 vy’umutekano nyene umuntu ashobora gucira urubanza no gushiramwo ivyerekanwa bitandukanye biri kw’isoko.


Muri iyi nyigisho, ndasaba kuvumbura kimwe muri ivyo bisubizo: Satochip.


## Intangamarara ya Satochip


Satochip ni Hardware Wallet mu buryo bw’ikarita ifise igice c’umubiri cemejwe na *EAL6+*, kikaba ari ikigereranyo c’umutekano gikomeye cane (*NXP JCOP*). Ikoreshwa n’ishirahamwe ry’Ababirigi.


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/01.webp)


Iyi smart card igurishwa ku €25, ikaba ari nziza cane ugereranije n’izindi hardware wallets ziri kw’isoko. Ico gikoresho ni secure element gishobora gutuma umuntu ashobora guhangana neza cane n’ibitero vy’umubiri. Ikindi kandi, kode yayo ni iyo gufungura (*AGPLv3*).

Ariko rero, kubera uburyo bwayo, Satochip ntitanga uburyo bwinshi nk’ibindi bikoresho. Biragaragara ko ata bateri, ata kamera, canke igikoresho co gusoma ikarita ya micro SD, kuko ari ikarita. Intambamyi yayo nini, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, ni ukubura igicapo kuri Hardware Wallet, ivyo bikaba bituma ishobora guterwa n’ubwoko bumwe bumwe bw’ibitero vyo kure. Nkako, ivyo bituma uwubikoresha ashirako umukono ata co abona kandi akizigira ivyo abona kuri orodinateri yiwe.


Naho Satochip ifise aho igarukira, iracari iryoshe kubera igiciro cayo kigabanutse. Iyi Wallet ishobora gukoreshwa cane cane mu kwongerera umutekano Wallet ikoresha amahera yongerako Wallet izigama irindwa na Hardware Wallet ifise igikingira. Ni umuti mwiza kandi ku bafise amafaranga makeyi y’ama bitcoins kandi badashaka gushiramwo amayero ijana mu gikoresho gikomeye cane. Ikindi kandi, gukoresha Satochips mu mibumbe ya Multisig, canke bishobora kuba mu mibumbe ya Wallet ifise timelock muri kazoza, birashobora gutanga inyungu zishimishije.


Iryo shirahamwe rya Satochip na ryo nyene ritanga ibindi bikoresho 2. Hariho Satodime, ariyo ikarita y’umunyagihugu yagenewe kubika amafaranga y’ama bitcoins atari mu nzira, ariko ntiyemerera gukoresha amafaranga. Ni ubwoko bw’impapuro Wallet zitekanye cane, zishobora gukoreshwa, nk’akarorero, mu gutanga ingabirano. Ubwa nyuma, hariho Umucungezi w’Imbuto, ari we mucungerezi w’amajambo Mnemonic. Ishobora gukoreshwa mu gukiza seed yacu ata nkomanzi ataco ivuze ku mpapuro.


## Ni gute umuntu yogura Satochip?


Ico gikoresho citwa Satochip kiragurishwa [ku rubuga rwemewe](https://satochip.io/igicuruzwa/satochip/). Kugira ngo uyigure mu iduka ry’umubiri, urashobora kandi kubona [urutonde rw’abagurisha bemerewe](https://satochip.io/abagurisha/) ku rubuga rwa Satochip.


Kugira ngo ukoreshe porogarama yawe yo gucunga Wallet, Satochip itanga uburyo bubiri: biciye ku guhanahana amakuru biciye kuri NFC canke biciye ku gikoresho co gusoma ikarita y’ubwenge. Ku bijanye n’uburyo bwa NFC, urabe neza ko imashini yawe ihuye n’ubu buhinga canke uronke igisomyi ca NFC co hanze. Satochip ikora ku nzira isanzwe y’amajwi 13,56 MHz. Ahandi ho, urashobora no kugura igikoresho co gusoma amakarata y’ubwenge. Ushobora kuyironka ku rubuga rwa Satochip canke ahandi.

## Ni gute woshiraho Satochip ifise Sparrow?


Umaze kwakira Satochip yawe, intambwe ya mbere ni ugusuzuma ivyo ipfutse kugira ngo umenye neza ko itafunguwe. Ivyo Satochip ipfutse bikwiye kubamwo ikimenyetso co gukingira. Iyo iyo sticker ibuze canke yononekaye, vyoshobora kwerekana ko iyo smart card yashizwe mu kaga kandi ko yoshobora kuba atari iyo ukuri.

![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/03.webp)

Uzosanga Satochip imbere.


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/04.webp)


Kugira ngo ushobore gucunga Wallet, muri iyi nyigisho, ndagusavye gukoresha Sparrow. Niba utararonka iyo porogarama, [genda ku rubuga rwemewe kugira ngo uyikureko](https://sparrowwallet.com/download/). Ushobora kandi kuraba inyigisho yacu ku Sparrow wallet (izoza vuba).


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/05.webp)


Injira Satochip yawe mu gikoresho co gusoma amakarata y’ubwenge canke uyishire ku gikoresho co gusoma NFC, hanyuma uhuze ico gikoresho na mudasobwa yawe Sparrow yuguruyeko.


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/06.webp)


Ugurure Sparrow wallet maze urabe neza ko uhuye neza n’urudodo rwa Bitcoin. Kugira ngo ubikore, reba akamenyetso kari hasi iburyo: gakwiye kuba umuhondo nimba uhuye n’uruzitiro rwa bose, Green ku bijanye n’uguhuza na Bitcoin core, canke ubururu ku bijanye na Electrum.

Inyuma y’aho, ukeneye gushinga PIN code kugira ngo ufungure Satochip yawe. Hitamwo ijambobanga rikomeye, riri hagati y’inyuguti 4 n’inyuguti 16. Kora ububiko bw'iri jambobanga.


Menya, iri jambobanga si passphrase. Ivyo bisigura ko naho utagira iri jambobanga, ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic rizotuma usubira kwinjiza Wallet yawe muri porogarama nimba bikenewe. Ijambobanga rikoreshwa gusa kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gushika kuri Satochip ubwayo. Ni nk’uko PIN code iboneka ku bindi bikoresho vy’amasakoshi.


Ijambobanga rihejeje kwinjira, wongere ukande kuri buto "*Import Keystore*".


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/14.webp)


Usubire kwibuka ijambobanga, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Initialize*".


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/15.webp)


Uca ushika ku idirisha ryo gutanga ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic. Fyonda kuri buto ya "*generate Nshasha*".


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/16.webp)

Kora kopi imwe canke nyinshi z’umubiri z’ijambo ryawe ryo gukira mu kuyandika ku mpapuro canke ku gikoresho c’icuma. Menya, iri jambo ritanga uburenganzira bwo gushika ku bitcoins zawe ata yindi nzira yo kwikingira. Rero, iyo umuntu abivumbuye, yoshobora guca yiba ama bitcoins yawe, mbere atashobora gushika kuri Satochip yawe canke kuri PIN code yayo. Ni ngombwa rero gucungera ivyo bimenyetso. Ikindi kandi, iri jambo rituma ushobora gusubira kuronka amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins iyo utakaje, Satochip yononekaye, canke iyo wibagiwe kode yawe ya PIN.

Iryo jambobanga rizotuma umuntu agira umutekano wo gukoresha Sparrow wallet, ivyo bikaba bifasha kurinda imfunguruzo zawe za bose, amaderesi yawe be n’amateka yawe y’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji ku muntu wese yoshobora kuyironka ata wemerewe.

## Ni gute umuntu yoronka ama bitcoins akoresheje Satochip?


Igihe umaze gushika muri Wallet yawe, ukande ku rubuga rwa "*Receive*".


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/24.webp)


Sparrow wallet itanga Address ku Wallet yawe. Kenshi, ku zindi nkweto z'ibikoresho, ni vyiza gukanda kuri "*Display Address*" kugira ngo ugenzure Address ataco uhinduye ku gicapo c'igikoresho. Ikibabaje, iyo nzira ntiboneka kuri Satochip, ariko urabe ko uyikoresha ku bindi bikoresho vyawe.


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/25.webp)


Ushobora kwongerako "*Label*" kugira ngo udondore inkomoko y'ama bitcoins azokingirwa n'iyi Address. Ivyo ni ibikorwa vyiza bigufasha gucunga neza ama UTXO yawe.


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/26.webp)


Kubindi bisobanuro ku bijanye no gushirako amazina, ndagusavye kandi kuraba iyi yindi nyigisho:


https://planb.network/tutorials/privacy/on-chain/utxo-labelling-d997f80f-8a96-45b5-8a4e-a3e1b7788c52

Ushobora rero gukoresha iyi Address kugira ngo ubone ama bitcoins.


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/27.webp)

## Ni gute worungika ama Bitcoins ukoresheje Satochip?

None ko mwaronse Sats zanyu za mbere muri Wallet zanyu zitekanye zifise Satochip, namwe murashobora kuzikoresha! Huza Satochip yawe na mudasobwa yawe, ufungure Sparrow wallet, hanyuma ugende kuri "*Send*" kugira ngo wubake igikorwa gishasha.


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/28.webp)


Niba ushaka gukora ubugenzuzi bwa Coin, ni ukuvuga, guhitamwo cane cane UTXOs uzokoresha mu gucuruza, genda ku rubuga rwa "*UTXOs*". Hitamwo UTXOs wipfuza gukoresha, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ohereza vyahisemwo*". Uzosubira ku rubuga rumwe rw'agace ka "*Send*", ariko n'ama UTXO yawe amaze gutorwa ku bijanye n'ugucuruza.


![SATOCHIP](assets/notext/29.webp)
