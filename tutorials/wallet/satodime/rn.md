Iyi nkuru iragutwara mu gushiramwo, gutunganya no gukoresha porogarama ya Satodime yo kuri telefone ngendanwa. Uzomenya ingene wofata ikarita yawe, ukora amasafe, ukongerako amahera, ufungura ikidodo no kugarura imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga. Ivyiyumviro vy’imigereka biratanga ibikoresho, ingendo nziza n’insobanuro z’ubuhinga.



## Imenyekanisha



**Satodime**, yateguwe na **[Satochip]**, ni ikarita y’umunyamideri itekanye yo kubika Bitcoin mu buryo buboneka kandi bworoshe. Ikora nk’igikoresho ca wallet cizigama, aho ufise ububasha bwose ku mfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga ataco wizigiye uwundi muntu. Open-source kandi yemejwe na EAL6+, ifasha amasafe ashika kuri atatu yigenga.



### Inyuma y'igicuruzwa



Satodime, **carte au porteur, ni iy’uwuyifise ku mubiri**, ataco akeneye kwiyandikisha canke kumenya uwo ari we wese. Ihuye n’ivyo bikenewe kugira ngo umuntu wese afise iyo karata ashobore kuyikoresha canke akayirungika mu kuyicapura biciye ku rubuga rwa telefone ngendanwa kugira ngo ayifate canke afungure ama safe. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ikete ry’impapuro, rikoresha igice gikingira kugira ngo rikingire imfunguruzo z’ibanga, izo mfunguruzo zikaba zigaragara gusa umuntu amaze gufungura, ivyo bikaba bituma iyo karata isa n’amahera aho uwuyifise amenyekana biciye ku vyo umuntu afise. Ni vyiza cane mu gutanga bitcoins nk’ingabirano, bifasha mu gutanga bitcoins mu buryo butekanye kuva ku kuboko ku kuboko, mu gihe porogarama ya telefone ngendanwa ikoresha NFC kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukorana n’amatelefone ngendanwa.





- Umutekano**: Imfunguruzo z’ibanga zivugwa kandi zibitswe mu gice c’umubiri kidashobora guhindurwa; ikibanza kiboneka (kidozwe, kitadozwe, ubusa).
- Ibirango**: Gugura bitcoins uciye kuri app (umufatanyabikorwa wa Paybis); gucunga Mainnet na Testnet.
- Inkomoko yuguruye**: Kode iri munsi y'uruhusha rwa AGPLv3, ishobora kugenzurwa kuri GitHub.



### Ugukomeza gutera imbere



Ivyo bikoresho biratera imbere ubudasiba. Raba urubuga rwa Satochip canke amaduka kugira ngo ubone amakuru mashasha. Nk’akarorero, blockchains nshasha canke ibikorwa vyo kugura birashobora kwongerwako. Suzuma [GitHub ya Satochip] kugira ngo ubone iterambere riguma rigenda.



## 1. Ibisabwa



Imbere yo gutangura **Satodime**, urabe neza ko ufise ibi bikurikira:





- Telefone ngendanwa ihuye**: Igikoresho ca Android canke iOS gifise NFC.
- Ikarata ya Satodime**: Nshasha canke itatanguje.
- Ihuriro rya Internet**: Kugira ngo ubone porogarama.
- Ubumenyi bw’ishimikiro**: Gutahura imfunguruzo z’ibanga/za bose n’ingaruka zo gutakaza (zidashobora gusubirwamwo).
- Secure medium**: Ahantu heza ho kwandika imfunguruzo z’ibanga iyo zimaze gufungurwa (impapuro, ivyuma; ntuzokwigera zikoreshwa mu buryo bwa digitale).

## 3. Itunganywa ry'intango



### 3.1 Gutanguza porogaramu no gupima



Ugurure app hanyuma ukurikire umupfumu. Shira ikarita ya Satodime ku gisomwa ca NFC co muri telefone yawe (kenshi na kenshi kiri inyuma). Uyifate hasi mu gihe cose uriko urakora kugira ngo ubone ko ihuriye neza.





- Niba NFC idakora, reba ama settings ya telefone yawe.
- Igifungurwa c'umukate cemeza ko umuntu yaroraniwe: "Gusoma neza".



![image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Nk’itegeko rusangi, **ibikorwa vyose bikurikira bizosaba kwemezwa mu gucapura ikarita ya Satodime**



### 3.2 Gufata ikarita (*Ownership*)



Ku bijanye n’ugukoresha ubwa mbere, nube nyen’ikarata kugira ngo uyikingire:





- Fyonda kuri "*Fata Ownership*" muri app.
- Wemeze igikorwa: ibi bitanga urufunguzo rwa nyen'urufunguzo rwihariye.
- Subira ushireko ikarita kugira ngo ushire mu ngiro ivyo wahinduye.
- Imburi**: Iyi ntambwe ntishobora gusubirwamwo. Turagusavye urabe [ingingo ivuga ku *ubutunzi*](https://satochip.io/ubutunzi-bwa-satodime-bwasobanuwe/).



![image](assets/fr/03.webp)




## 4. Rema ububiko



Satodime ishigikira ama safe ashika kuri 3. Rema imwe yo kubika bitcoin:





- Hitamwo isanduku y'ubusa (nk'akarorero Isanduku 01).
- Hitamwo uruzitiro (Bitcoin).
- Fyonda kuri "*Rema & Seal*".
- Scan ikarita kuri generate hanyuma ushire ikidodo ku rufunguzo rw’ibanga (ntirumenyekana gushika rufunguwe).
- Urakoze**: Ubu safe yawe irakingiwe kandi yiteguriye kwakira amahera.



![image](assets/fr/04.webp)



![image](assets/fr/05.webp)



## 5. Wongereko amafranga



Igihe umaze gufunga, ushiremwo ama bitcoins:





- Hitamwo igisandugu.
- Fyonda kuri "*Ongera amafaranga*".
- Kopa aderesi ya bose canke ushireko kode ya QR.
- Wohereze amahera avuye mu yindi wallet.
- Suzuma umubare usigaye inyuma yo kwemezwa ku blockchain.
- Uburyo bwo kugura: Fyonda kuri "*Gugura*" kugira ngo ugure ukoresheje Paybis (Visa, Mastercard, n'ibindi). Amafaranga akoreshwa.



![image](assets/fr/06.webp)



## 6. Gufungura isandugu



Kugira ngo ushikire urufunguzo rw'ibanga no kwimurira amahera ahandi hantu, fungura ikidodo:





- Hitamwo igisandugu gikingiwe n’ikidodo.
- Fyonda kuri "Gufungura".
- Wemeze imburi: iki gikorwa ntigishobora gusubirwamwo.
- Scan ikarata kugira ngo uyifungure.
- Igikapu gishizwe kuri "*Ikidodo*"; urufunguzo rw'ibanga ubu rushobora kurabwa/gusohoka hanze.
- Imburi**: Iyo umaze gufungura, urufunguzo rw’ibanga rurashikira. Iyo umuntu afashe telefone yawe, arashobora gushika kuri urwo rufunguzo, gutyo akagarura amahera ari mu safe yawe (irreversible).



![image](assets/fr/07.webp)



## 7. Kugarura urufunguzo rw'ibanga



Amaze gufungura, urungike urufunguzo hanze kugira ngo ukoreshe mu yindi wallet :





- Raba neza ko uri ahantu hatagira umutekano.
- Fyonda kuri "*Kwerekana urufunguzo rw'ibanga*".
- Hitamwo uburyo: Iragi, SegWit, WIF, n'ibindi.
- Kopa urufunguzo canke ushireko kode ya QR.
- Umutekano**: Ntukigere usangira urufunguzo rwawe rw’ibanga. Bibike ata murongo.
- Uyishire muri porogarama ya wallet ihuye n’ugucungera amafaranga (Sparrow Wallet canke Electrum, nk’akarorero).



![image](assets/fr/08.webp)





## 8. Gusubiramwo igikapu



Gusubiramwo umutekano bikuraho urufunguzo rw'ibanga rujanye n'ivyo. Mu yandi majambo, nimba utaronse kopi y’urufunguzo rwawe rw’ibanga, canke ngo urushire mu yindi wallet, gusubiramwo iyo safe bizoteza gutakaza amahera ari muri yo ataco asubiramwo.



![image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Gusubiramwo igikapu bituma aho hantu haba ubusa kandi hakaba hateguwe igikapu gishasha.



## 9. Kwimurira ububasha



Kugira ngo - nk'akarorero - ushireho amafaranga y'ibiceri biciye kuri Satodime, utegerezwa :




- Fata uburenganzira bw’ikarata,
- Rema igisandugu ca Bitcoin,
- Kwimurira satss ng'aho,
- Ukwimurira ikarita: umuntu akurikira gucapura ikarita ni we aba nyirayo,
- Ha ikarita ya Satodime umuntu uhisemwo, maze umutumire ngo akureho iyo porogarama hanyuma akore scanner iyo karata kugira ngo ayifate - gutyo akaronka ama bitcoins 'yabitswe' kuri yo.



![image](assets/fr/10.webp)




## UMUGEREKA



### A1. Ibikorwa vyiza



Kugira ngo ukoreshe **Satodime** neza :





- Gukingira ikarita**: Uyifate nk’amahera; loss = amahera yatakaye nimba atari nyen’ayo.
- Key backup**: Inyuma yo gufungura, wandike imfunguruzo z’ibanga ku kintu gifise umutekano. Ntukigere ugira digitale.
- Suzuma uko amasafe ameze**: Imisi yose ushireko scanner kugira ngo wemeze ko ikarita ari iyayo be n’uko ama safe yashizweko ikidodo canke ataco yashizweko ikidodo.
- Ibanga**: Koresha amaderesi mashasha; kwirinda guhinduranya amafaranga hagati y’abantu.
- Ivyashizwe ku rubuga**: Gumana app ku gihe biciye mu maduka.
- Gusubirana**: Iyo ikarita yazimiye ariko ikaba ari iyayo, amahera aba ari kuri blockchain; koresha urufunguzo rw'ibanga iyo rufunguwe.
