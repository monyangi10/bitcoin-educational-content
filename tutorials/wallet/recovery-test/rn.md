Igihe urema Bitcoin Wallet, usabwa kwandika amajambo Mnemonic, akenshi akaba ari amajambo 12 canke 24. Iyi mvugo iragufasha gusubira kuronka amafaranga yawe mu gihe woba watakaje, wononekaye canke wibwe igikoresho cakira Wallet yawe. Imbere y’uko utangura gukoresha Bitcoin Wallet yawe nshasha, birahambaye cane ko usuzuma ko iri jambo Mnemonic rifise ukuri. Uburyo bwiza bwo kubigira ni ugukora ikizame co gukira mu gihe c’umwuma.


Ico kigeragezo kirimwo kwigana ugusubirana kwa Wallet imbere yo gushiramwo amafaranga yose y’ibiceri. Igihe cose Wallet iri ubusa, twigana ibintu aho igikoresho cakira imfunguruzo zacu kizimira, kandi ivyo dusigaje vyose ni ijambo ryacu rya Mnemonic kugira ngo tugerageze gusubirana ama bitcoins yacu.


![RECOVERY TEST](assets/notext/01.webp)


## Intumbero ni iyihe?


Uwo murongo wo kugerageza uratuma ushobora kugenzura ko ububiko bw’umubiri bw’ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, yaba ku mpapuro canke ku cuma, bukora. Iyo utashoboye mu gihe c’iki kigeragezo co gusubirana, bigaragaza ko hari ikosa mu gucungera amajambo, gutyo ugashira ama bitcoins yawe mu kaga. Ku rundi ruhande, iyo ikigeragezo kigenze neza, cemeza ko ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic rikora neza, kandi ushobora rero gukingira bitcoins n’amahoro yo mu mutima ukoresheje iyi Wallet.


Gukora ikizame co gukira mu gihe c’umwuma birafise akamaro kabiri. Si uko gusa bigufasha kugenzura ukuri kw’ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, ariko kandi biraguha akaryo ko kwimenyereza ingene Wallet isubirana. Uko ni ko uzobona ingorane zishobora gushika imbere y’uko ikintu nyaco kigushikira. Umusi mu vy’ukuri ukeneye gusubirana Wallet yawe, uzoba utagira umubabaro mwinshi, kuko uzoba umaze kumenya ingene bigenda, bigabanye ingorane zo gukora amakosa. Ni co gituma bihambaye ko umuntu adasuzugura iyo ntambwe yo kugerageza kandi agafata umwanya ukenewe kugira ngo ayikore neza.


## Ikigeragezo co gukira ni iki?


Inzira y'ikibazo ni yoroshe cane:


- Amaze kurema Bitcoin Wallet yawe nshasha, kandi imbere yo gushiramwo satoshis yawe ya mbere, wandike amakuru y’icabona nk’ica xpub, uwa mbere yakira Address, canke mbere urutoke rw’urufunguzo rwa master;
- Hanyuma, ushiremwo n’ibigirankana Wallet ikiri ubusa, nk’akarorero, mu gusubiza Hardware Wallet yawe ku mirongo y’uruganda;
- Inyuma y’aho, wigane ukuntu Wallet yawe isubirana ukoresheje gusa impapuro z’inyuma z’ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic be n’iry’i passphrase yawe nimba ukoresha imwe;
- Ubwa nyuma, nusuzume nimba amakuru y’icabona ahuye n’ayo muri Wallet yasubiwemwo. Iyo amakuru ahuye, urashobora kwizigira ko ububiko bwawe bw’umubiri ari ubwo kwizigirwa, hanyuma ushobora kohereza amafaranga yawe ya mbere kuri iyi Wallet.

Urabe maso, mu gihe c'ikigeragezo co gukira, **utegerezwa gukoresha igikoresho kimwe cagenewe Wallet yawe ya nyuma**, kugira ngo ntuzongere igitero ca Wallet yawe. Nk’akarorero, iyo uremye Wallet kuri Trezor Safe 5, urabe ko ukora ikigeragezo co gusubirana kuri iyo Trezor Safe 5 nyene. Ni ngombwa ko udashiramwo ijambo ryawe ryo gusubirana mu yindi porogarama iyo ari yo yose, kuko ivyo vyotuma umutekano utangwa na Hardware Wallet yawe uhungabana, naho emp-19 yoba ikiri.


## Ni gute wokora ikibazo co gukira?


Muri iyi nyigisho, nzobasigurira ingene wokora ikigeragezo co gukira ku Bitcoin Software Wallet, ukoresheje Sparrow wallet (ku Hot Wallet). Ariko rero, iyo nzira iguma ari imwe ku bundi bwoko bwose bw’igikoresho. Na none, **niba ukoresha Hardware Wallet, ntukore ikigeragezo co gukira kuri Sparrow wallet** (raba igice c’imbere).


Naciye nkora Hot Wallet nshasha kuri Sparrow wallet. Muri iki gihe, nta bitcoins n’imwe nkirungika kuri yo. Ni ubusa.


![RECOVERY TEST](assets/notext/02.webp)


Naranditse neza amajambo yanje y’amajambo 12 yitwa Mnemonic ku mpapuro. Kandi ko nshaka kwongereza umutekano w’iyi Wallet, nashizeho kandi BIP39 passphrase nazigamye ku rundi rupapuro:


```txt
1. shield
2. brass
3. sentence
4. cube
5. marble
6. glad
7. satoshi
8. door
9. project
10. panic
11. prepare
12. general
```


```text
Passphrase: YfaicGzXH9t5C#g&47Kzbc$JL
```


***Biragaragara ko udakwiye kwigera usangira ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic n’ijambo ryawe rya passphrase kuri internet, bitandukanye n’ivyo ndiko ndakora muri iyi nyigisho. Aka karorero Wallet ntikazokoreshwa kandi kazokurwaho ku mpera y'inyigisho.***


Ubu nzokwandika ku murongo amakuru y'icabona avuye mu gitabu canje ca Wallet. Ushobora guhitamwo amakuru atandukanye, nk’uwa mbere yakira Address, xpub, canke urutoke rw’urufunguzo rwa mbere. Ku bwanje, ndabahimiriza guhitamwo uwubanza kwakira Address. Ivyo bigufasha kugenzura ko ushoboye kuronka inzira ya mbere yuzuye ijana kuri iyi Address.


Ku Sparrow, fyonda ku rubuga rwa "*Aderesi*".


![RECOVERY TEST](assets/notext/03.webp)


Hanyuma, wandike ku mpapuro igihe ca mbere waronse Address y’igitabu cawe ca Wallet. Mu karorero kanje, Address ni:


```txt
tb1qxv56mma5x5r7uhdkn0ldvcx6m0gj6f3kre0gwd
```


Uhejeje kubona amakuru, genda kuri "*Dosiye*", hanyuma uhitemwo "*Subira Wallet*". Ndabibutsa kandi ko Bitcoin Wallet yanyu itegerezwa kuba iri ubusa imbere y’uko mubandanya muri iki gikorwa.

Nimba Wallet yawe ari ubusa, wemeze ko Wallet yawe yasubiwemwo.

Ubu ukeneye gusubiramwo igikorwa co kurema Wallet, ariko ukoresheje ibipapuro vyacu vy’ububiko. Fyonda kuri "*Dosiye*" hanyuma ukande kuri "*Wallet nshasha*".

Hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Software Wallet nshasha canke yashizwe hanze*".

Injira ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic muri porogarama. Iyo ubutumwa "*Invalid Checksum*" bubonetse, ivyo vyerekana ko ububiko bw'ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic butari bwo. Uzoca ubwirizwa gutangura kurema Wallet yawe uhereye ku ntango, kuko ikigeragezo cawe co gukira cananiwe.

Ubwa nyuma, nusuzume ko igitabu ca mbere caronse Address gihuye n’ico wari warabonye nk’icabona ku rutonde rwawe rwo gusoma igisirikare.

Iyo amaderesi y'ukwakira ahuye, ikigeragezo cawe co gukira kiraba ciza, kandi urashobora gukoresha Bitcoin Wallet yawe nshasha. Niba bidahuye, ivyo bishobora kwerekana ikosa mu guhitamwo ubwoko bw'inyandiko, bituma inzira y'inkomoko idakwiriye, canke ingorane mu gucungera amajambo yawe Mnemonic canke passphrase yawe. Muri ivyo vyose, ndabahimiriza cane gutangura kuva ku ntango no kurema Bitcoin Wallet nshasha kuva mu ntango kugira ngo ntihagire ingorane iyo ari yo yose. Ico gihe, nushire umutima ku kwandika amajambo yo muri Mnemonic ata makosa.

Urakoze cane, ubu uriko urihutisha gukora ikibazo co gukira! Ndabagira inama yo gushikiriza muri rusangi iyo nzira yo kurema ama wallets yanyu yose ya Bitcoin. Niwaba wabonye ko iyi nyigisho ari ngirakamaro, noshima iyo ushobora gusiga urukumu hejuru aha hepfo. Ntutinye gusangiza abandi iyi nkuru ku mbuga zanyu zo guhanahana amakuru. Murakoze cane!
