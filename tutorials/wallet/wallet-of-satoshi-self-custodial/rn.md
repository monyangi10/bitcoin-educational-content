***Si imfunguruzo zawe, si ibiceri vyawe* ni ikintu kirenze imvugo, ni ingingo ngenderwako y’ishimikiro ya Bitcoin, bisigura ko iyo udacungera **imfunguruzo z’ibanga** zifungura ama bitcoins yawe, ntuba uyifise vy’ukuri.



Abakoresha benshi muri rusangi batangura na **wallet** y’ububiko, hanyuma bakimukira ku **wallet** y’ububiko bwite, aho bo ubwabo bagenzura imfunguruzo zabo z’ibanga.


Muri iyi nyigisho, ntituzoba turiko turakumenyesha wallet nshasha yo kwizigama. Ahubwo, tuzobamenyesha ikintu gishasha ca ***Wallet of Satoshi*** wallets: **uburyo bwo kwizigama**.



Intumbero y’iyi nzira nshasha ni ugutuma abakoresha bashobora kuguma bagenzura imfunguruzo zabo z’ibanga mu gihe bungukirwa n’ukworohereza n’ubumenyi bw’abakoresha bugenda neza.



Imbere y’uko tugera ku mutima w’ikibazo, reka dufate umwanya wo gusuzuma uburyo budasanzwe bwo kwirinda butangwa na Wallet of Satoshi (WoS).



## Ikintu kidasanzwe c'uburyo bwo kwirinda



Ukworoha n’uguhinduka kw’uburyo bwo kwizigama bwa WoS birakuraho ubugoyagoye bwo gufungura imirongo ya Lightning, gutanga amanode... Ariko ivyo bishoboka gute?



Uburyo bwo kwizigama bwa Wallet of Satoshi bukoreshwa na **Spark**. Iyi ni umuti wa Layer 2 wa Bitcoin, waremwe na Lightspark, ikoresheje ubuhinga bwa **statechains**.



Ivyo bituma udakora amafaranga yawe kuri Lightning Network. Imigenderanire hagati y'urubuga rwa **LN** na **Spark** ibera biciye ku **uguhinduranya atome**.



Nk’akarorero, Bob yipfuza kwishura invoice ya Lightning ikoresheje WoS. Arungika ama satoshi yiwe, ariko mu nyuma, ayo arungikwa ku **Umutanga Sevisi ya Spark (SSP)** kuri Spark, na we agakora ivyo kwishura ku rubuga rwa Lightning.



Ku rundi ruhande, Alice yipfuza kuronka amahera ataco akora mu gisata cayo ca WoS. Muri ivyo, **SSP** irakira sats biciye kuri LN maze igaca itanga amafaranga y’ibikorwa vya Alice.



Rero, birahambaye kumenya ko kugira ngo wungukire ku kworoha n’uguhinduka kwa WoS, ukeneye kwizigira abakozi ba Spark. Ariko rero, ku bijanye n’umutekano, iyo SSP ibaye mbi canke itaboneka, urafise uburyo bwo **gusohoka ku ruhande rumwe**, uburyo bwo gucungera umutekano bugufasha gusubirana amahera yawe kuri Bitcoin on-chain (naho ivyo bishobora kuba bigenda buhoro kandi bizimvye) , biguha icemezo c’uko ata bumenyi bwo kwirinda bugereranywa n’ubw’ibanga.



Ufashe ivyo vyose mu muzirikanyi, urashobora rero gufata ingingo y’ingene sats ushaka kubika mu bubiko bwawe bwa WoS.



Nimba uri mushasha muri Wallet of Satoshi, birasanzwe ko uzokenera gukuraho porogarama ya wallet yo kuri telefone ngendanwa. Ariko rero, nimba umaze kuyikoresha kandi ushaka kumenya ingene ukoresha **uburyo bwo kwizigama**, usabwe guca mu gice ca **Uburyo bwo kwizigama** co muri iyi nyigisho.

## Ukwirinda uburyo bwo gutunganya



Fyonda ku rutonde rw’ibifungurwa vy’umubiri (ikimenyetso c’imirongo 3) kiri hejuru iburyo bw’ibarabara ry’imbere.



![screen](assets/fr/06.webp)



Hanyuma, mu rutonde ruzoboneka, ukande ku rutonde ruto ruvuga ngo **Gufungura Ukwirinda Wallet**.



![screen](assets/fr/07.webp)



WoS ica ikubwira ko *uburyo bwo kwirinda buzana n’ijambo ryo gukira. Kandi, ni wewe wenyene ushinzwe umutekano w’amahera yawe*.



![screen](assets/fr/08.webp)



Suzuma buto ya "**Ndatahura**" (1), hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya **Gufungura Ukwirinda Wallet** (2), igaragara mu muhondo ukeye.



![screen](assets/fr/09.webp)



### Gukora ibitabo vy'ukwizigama



Uhejeje gukanda kuri buto **Gufungura Wallet yo kwirinda**, ukande kuri buto **Rema Wallet nshasha**.



![screen](assets/fr/10.webp)



WoS izoca igukorera igitabu co kwibungabunga, kandi muri iyo porogarama nyene. Uzoshobora guhindura hagati y'uburyo bwo **kubungabunga** (buboneka mu turere tumwetumwe) n'uburyo bwo **kwibungabunga** igihe ushaka kandi igihe cose.



![screen](assets/fr/11.webp)



Igihe umaze kuremwa, uzosubirwamwo ku nzira nyamukuru yo kwibungabunga ya WoS. Uzobona ko ata tandukaniro riri hagati y’insiguro rusangi n’imirongo y’ibitabo vy’ububiko bwa WoS n’ivyo mu bitabo vy’ububiko bwa WoS.



### Kubika amajambo yawe y'ukwibuka



Kanda ku **Igipfukisho + Igipfukisho ca Wallet** kiri hejuru y’igicapo hagati y’izina rya Wallet of Satoshi n’ivyo kurya vy’inyama z’ingurube.



![screen](assets/fr/12.webp)



WoS itanga uburyo bubiri butandukanye bwo kubika amajambo yawe 12 (invugo y’ubwenge): **gusubiza inyuma biciye kuri Google Drive** na **gusubiza inyuma n’amaboko**.



Naho dusaba ko wobikora n’amaboko, ari vyo bitekanye kuruta ibindi vyose, tuzokwereka kandi ingene wobikora biciye kuri Google Drive.



#### Gusubiza inyuma biciye kuri Google Drive



Fyonda kuri buto ya **Igikoresho ca Google**.



![screen](assets/fr/13.webp)



Niwahitamwo gukora ububiko bwa Google Drive, hari ingorane nyinshi z'uko konti yawe ya Google yoshobora guhungabanywa. Umuntu w'umunyaruyeri yoshobora kuronka dosiye irimwo amajambo yawe 12, kandi yoshobora rero kuronka wallet yawe.



Kwongera ijambobanga ryo gupfuka dosiye irimwo amajambo yawe 12 ni uburyo bwiza bwo kwongerako umutekano.



Rero fungura buto ya **Encrypt n'ijambobanga** mu mahitamwo ateye imbere.



![screen](assets/fr/14.webp)



Ku rubuga rushasha ruzoboneka, shiraho ijambobanga rikomeye, hanyuma wongere uryemeze.



![screen](assets/fr/15.webp)



Ni vyiza kwibuka ko iyo umaze guhitamwo ijambobanga, udategerezwa kuribagira canke ngo utakaze uburyo wariyanditseko. Iyo wibagiwe canke ukayitakaza, ntuzosubira kuronka amajambo yawe 12, rero n’amahera yawe.



Uhejeje guhitamwo ijambobanga ryawe, hitamwo konti ya Google kugira ngo ukoreshe ububiko, hanyuma ushireho uburenganzira bwo gukoresha busabwa na WoS.



![screen](assets/fr/16.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/17.webp)



Rindira amasegonda make. Bingo! Ububiko bwawe bwarangiye neza.



![screen](assets/fr/18.webp)



Uhora ufise uburenganzira bwo gukora ububiko bw’inyongera mu guhitamwo uburyo bwa kabiri bwo gukora ububiko: ububiko bw’amaboko.

## Gusubizaho ibitabo vy'ukwibungabunga



Igihe ushaka gusubirana canke gusubizaho WoS self-custody wallet yawe ukurikije uguhindura telefone canke kubera iyindi mvo iyo ari yo yose, ng’izi intambwe ushobora gukurikira.



Kugira ngo ufungure igitabu ca WoS, fyonda ku rutonde rw’ibifungurwa vy’ibitoke biri hejuru iburyo bw’ikiganiro nyamukuru.


Mu rutonde ruzoboneka, fyonda ku **Gufungura Ukwirinda Wallet**.


Ku rubuga rushasha ruzoboneka, kanda kuri buto ya **Gusubizaho Wallet iriho**.



![screen](assets/fr/24.webp)



Hitamwo uburyo bwo gusubizaho maze ugende ku ntambwe ikurikira.



![screen](assets/fr/25.webp)



### Kugarura biciye kuri Google Drive



1. Kanda kuri buto ya **Gusubizaho kuri Google Drive**.


2. Hitamwo konti ya Google maze ureke WoS igarure amakuru y’ugusubirana yabitswe kuri Google Drive yawe.


3. Hanyuma winjize ijambo banga ryawe ry’ububiko (niba wari wararisobanuye mbere, birashoboka) muri dosiye irimwo amajambo yawe 12.


4. Rindira umwanya muto kugira ngo ivyo gusubizaho bitangure gukora, uzosubira kuronka igitabu cawe.



### Gusubizaho n'amaboko



1. Kanda kuri buto ya **Gusubizaho n'amaboko**.


2. Hanyuma winjize amajambo 12 y’invugo yawe y’ukwibuka, wandike ijambo ryose imbere y’umubare waryo w’intango.


3. Rindira umwanya muto kugira ngo ivyo gusubizaho bitangure gukora, uzosubira kuronka igitabu cawe.




### Kwimurira bitcoins hagati y'ububiko bwa WoS n'ububiko bwa WoS



Iyo ufise ama bitcoins (sats) mu bubiko bwawe bwa WoS wallet maze ugakora ububiko bwa WoS bwite wallet, ntuzotakaza amahera yawe. Ivyiza kuruta, urashobora kubirungika mu gitabu cawe co kwibungabunga n’ibihushanye n’ivyo.



Kugira ngo ubikore :


Ushobora gukopa aderesi yawe y’ukwizigama y’umuravyo WoS canke invoice waremye.



![screen](assets/fr/26.webp)



Ubu rero fungura wallet yawe y’ububiko maze ukande kuri buto ya **Envoyer**.



Hanyuma ushireko aderesi canke invoice. Kanda **Envoyer** ubugira kabiri.



Subira mu gitabu cawe co kwibungabunga uzobona ko vy’ukuri wari wararonse ayo mahera.
