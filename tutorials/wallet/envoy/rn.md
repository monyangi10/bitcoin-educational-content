Envoy ni porogaramu y’uburongozi ya Bitcoin Wallet yateguwe n’ishirahamwe. Ikoreshwa mu buryo budasanzwe na Pasiporo Hardware Wallet.


Pasiporo "*Batch 2*" yerekanwa muri iyi nyigisho n'iporogarama ya Envoy ni yo ikurikira "*Igitabo c'Umushingwamanza*". Igaragara cane kubera ubuhinga bwayo bwiza cane, igicapo c’amabara gifise ubusobanuro bwinshi, n’ikibazo c’umubiri gikora neza. Ikora mu buryo bwa "*Air-Gap*", igatuma imfunguruzo zawe z'ibanga za Wallet ziguma zitandukanye rwose, n'uguhanahana amakuru bishoboka biciye ku ikarita ya MicroSD canke amakode ya QR. Ico gikoresho gifise bateri Nokia BL-5C ishobora gukurwako, igasubirwamwo umuriro, ikaba ifise ubushobozi bwa 1200 mAh. Iyi bateri idakoreshwa n’abantu ishobora gusubirwamwo bitagoranye, kuko iyo bateri BL-5C iboneka cane mu maduka.


Naho ku bijanye n’uguhuza, iyo Passport ifise icuma ca MicroSD, icuma ca USB-C co gucapura, n’icamera y’inyuma yo gucapura amakode ya QR.


Ku bijanye n’umutekano, iyo Passport irimwo secure element, kandi kode y’inkomoko y’ico gikoresho ni iyo gufungura yose. Itanga ibintu vyose vyitezwe ku Bitcoin Hardware Wallet nziza. Zirikana ko iyo Passport itarashigikira inyandiko ntoyi, ariko iki gice gitegekanijwe mu gihembwe ca kabiri c'umwaka w'2025.


Igiciro ca Passport ni amadolari 199, ikaba iri mu kibanza c’iyi modoka y’agaciro kanini yitwa Hardware Wallet, ikaba ihanganye n’iyindi modoka y’agaciro kanini yitwa Coldcard Q, Jade Plus, Tezor Safe 5 na Ledger.


![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)


Kugira ngo ushobore gucunga Wallet yawe itekanye kuri Passport, urafise uburyo bwinshi bwo kubikora. Iyi Hardware Wallet ihuye n’ibindi bikoresho vyinshi vyo gucunga Wallet biri kw’isoko, harimwo Sparrow wallet, Spectre Desktop, Nunchuk, Keeper, n’ibindi.


Muri iyi nyigisho, igenewe abatangura n’abakoresha hagati, turaza kumenya ingene wokoresha porogarama ya Envoy na Passport yawe. Ni uburyo bworoshe bwo kuronka vyinshi muri Hardware Wallet yawe.


Niba uri umukoresha ateye imbere kandi ushaka gutohoza ibintu bikomeye, ndagusavye urabe iyi yindi nyigisho aho dutunganya Passport na Sparrow wallet :


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/hardware/passport-74e53858-3fa2-43f9-b866-573297546236

## Gufungura pasiporo

- Pasiporo;
- Igipande c’ikarata co kwandikako ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic;
- Umugozi wa USB-C wo gucapura ;
- Ikarata ya MicroSD ;
- Ivyuma bibiri vy’uguhindura MicroSD ku Muravyo canke USB-C ;
- Ivyuma bifatanya.


Ku gikoresho, uzosanga :




- Igikoresho co gukoresha (1) ;
- Igikoresho ca USB-C (2);
- Buto yo gukuraho (3);
- Buto yo gusubirayo (4) ;
- Buto yo kwemeza (5);
- Igipande c’inzira (6);
- Buto yo gufungura/gufunga (7);
- Ikimenyetso c’aho umuntu ari (8);
- Igikoresho ca MicroSD (9) ;
- Buto yo guhindura uburyo aA1 (10) ;
- Igikoresho co gufata amasanamu inyuma.

## Gushinga kode ya PIN


Inyuma y’aho haza intambwe ya kode ya PIN. PIN code ni yo ifungura Pasiporo yawe. Ni co gituma ritanga uburinzi ku bijanye n’ugushika ku mubiri ata wemerewe. Kode ya PIN ntaco ikora mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga bwa none za Wallet yawe. Rero naho utagira uburenganzira bwo kuronka kode ya PIN, kugira ijambo ryawe ry’amajambo 12 canke 24 Mnemonic bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gusubira kuronka amahera yawe ya bitcoins.


![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)


Turagusavye guhitamwo kode ya PIN ishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo butari bwo. Kandi, urabe ko ubika iyi kode ahantu hatandukanye n’aho Passport yawe ibitswe (nk’akarorero mu mucungerezi w’ijambobanga).


Ushobora guhitamwo kode ya PIN iri hagati y’imibare 6 na 12. Ndabagira inama yo kubigira igihe kirekire uko bishoboka kwose.


Koresha urufunguzo kugira ngo winjize inomero zawe za PIN. Niwaheza, ukande kuri buto yemeza.


![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)


Wemeze PIN yawe ubugira kabiri.


![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)


Kode yawe ya PIN yaranditswe.


![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)

## Rema Bitcoin nshasha Wallet


Ubu ni igihe co guhingura Bitcoin Wallet nshasha. Fyonda kuri buto yo kwemeza.


![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)


Kugira ngo ureme Wallet nshasha, kanda kuri "*Rema seed nshasha*".


![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)


Ushobora guhitamwo hagati y’amajambo 12 canke 24 y’ijambo Mnemonic. Umutekano utangwa n’izo nzira zompi ni usa, rero urashobora guhitamwo iyo yoroshe kubika, ni ukuvuga amajambo 12.


![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/30.webp)


Pasiporo yawe ubu izoba generate "*Kode y'Igihugu*". Iyi ni urutonde rw’imibare ishobora gukoreshwa mu gukuraho ububiko bwa Wallet yawe ibitswe kuri MicroSD. Ubu buryo bwo gucungera, bwihariye ku bikoresho vya Foundation, bugira ububiko bw’inyongera ku majambo yawe ya Mnemonic, ariko ntihuye n’izindi porogarama za Bitcoin.


Niwafata ingingo yo gukoresha iyi "*Backup Code*", urabe ko uyibika ahantu hatandukanye n'aho MicroSD yawe irimwo ububiko bwa Wallet yawe. Ariko rero, woshobora guhitamwo kudakoresha iyo nzira nimba wumva ko gufata neza amajambo yawe ya Mnemonic bihagije.


![Image](assets/fr/31.webp)


Injira "*Backup Code*" yawe kugira ngo wemeze ko wayibitse neza.


![Image](assets/fr/32.webp)


Niba MicroSD yinjijwe, ububiko bwa Wallet yawe bwabitswe ng’aho.


![Image](assets/fr/33.webp)


Passport yawe izokwerekana amajambo yawe y’amajambo 12 Mnemonic. Iyi Mnemonic iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe yose y’ama bitcoins ataco abujijwe. Umuntu wese afise iri jambo arashobora kwiba amahera yawe, mbere n’aho atashobora kubona Passport yawe.


Iryo jambo ry’amajambo 12 risubiza uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe y’ama bitcoins iyo Passport yawe yatakaye, yibwe canke ivunitse. Ni ngombwa rero cane ko umuntu ayizigama neza kandi akayibika ahantu hatagira umutekano.


Ushobora kuyandika ku karto itangwa mu gasandugu, canke kugira ngo wongere uyikingire, ndagusavye ko woyicapura ku gishingo c’icuma kitagira ibara kugira ngo uyikingire umuriro, umwuzure canke ngo isenyuke.


Fyonda kuri buto yo kwemeza kugira ngo ubone ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic.


![Image](assets/fr/34.webp)


Kugira ngo uronke ibindi bisobanuro ku buryo bwiza bwo kubika no gucunga ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic, ndagusavye cane gukurikira iyi yindi nyigisho, cane cane nimba uri umutanguzi:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

n’ukuri, ntushobora kwigera usangira aya majambo kuri internet, nk’uko ndiko ndabikora muri iyi nyigisho. Ico kigereranyo ca Wallet kizokoreshwa gusa kuri Testnet kandi kizokurwaho ku mpera y’inyigisho.


Kora igishushanyo c’umubiri c’iri rungane rivuga.


![Image](assets/fr/35.webp)


Passport yawe yaratunganijwe neza. Fyonda kuri buto yemeza kugira ngo ukomeze.

## Kubona ibikubiyemo vya pasiporo


Pasiporo yawe Interface ifise ibintu bitatu nyamukuru:




- "*Ishirahamwe*";
- "*Ibindi*";
- "*Imiterere*".


Kugira ngo ushobore guca hagati y’ivyo bimenyetso, koresha umwampi wo mu bubamfu n’uw’iburyo uri ku rupapuro rw’inzira.


### *Konti*" urutonde


Mu "*Konti*", uzosanga ibiranga Bitcoin Wallet yawe. Ushobora gusinya ku giciro biciye ku kamera canke biciye ku nzira ya MicroSD.


![Image](assets/fr/37.webp)


"*Ibikoresho vya konti*" bitanga amahitamwo nk'ugusuzuma Address, gusinya ubutumwa, canke kuraba amaderesi ari muri Wallet yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/38.webp)


Mu nzira ya "*Manage Account*", ushobora gufatanya Bitcoin Wallet yawe na porogaramu yo gucunga Wallet (ivyo tuzovuga mu ntambwe zikurikira z'iyi nyigisho), canke ubone kandi uhindure izina rya konti yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/39.webp)


### Ibindi" urutonde


Mu rutonde rwa "*Ibindi*", urashobora gukora konti nshasha muri Wallet yawe, ihuye n'iryo jambo nyene rya Mnemonic.


![Image](assets/fr/40.webp)


Ushobora kandi kwinjiza BIP39 passphrase canke ukoreshe seed y'igihe gito.


![Image](assets/fr/41.webp)


### Amagenamiterere" urutonde


Mu "*Imiterere*", uzosangamwo Wallet yawe yose n'imiterere y'ibikoresho vyawe.


![Image](assets/fr/42.webp)


"*Igikoresho*" kiguha amahitamwo yo guhindura umuco w'igicapo, gushinga igihe co guteba imbere y'uko gifunga, guhindura kode ya PIN, canke guhindura izina ry'igikoresho.


![Image](assets/fr/43.webp)


"*Backup*" submenu iragufasha kwohereza hanze ububiko bwawe bwa Wallet bushizwemwo amakuru, gusuzuma ukuri kw'ububiko bwa kera, canke gusubira kuraba "*Backup Code*" yawe.


![Image](assets/fr/44.webp)


"*Firmware*" ni iyo guhindura porogaramu ya Passport yawe. Turagusavye gukora ivyo bishasha ubudasiba kugira ngo wungukire ku bintu bishasha vyakosowe be n’ibintu bishasha.


![Image](assets/fr/45.webp)


"*Bitcoin*" ishobora kugufasha guhindura igice kigaragara (BTC canke satoshis), gucunga Multisig Wallet ishoboka, canke guhindukira ukaja ku buryo bwa "*Testnet*".


![Image](assets/fr/46.webp)


Mu "*Advanced*", urashobora kuraba amajambo y'invugo yawe ya Mnemonic, gukora ibikorwa kuri MicroSD yinjijwe, gusubiramwo Passport yawe ku mirongo y'uruganda, canke gukora isuzuma ry'ukuri, nk'uko vyakozwe mbere.


![Image](assets/fr/47.webp)


Ushobora gukoresha "*Amajambo y'umutekano*", ikintu congeramwo Layer y'umutekano mu kugaragaza amajambo abiri yihariye igihe ufungura igikoresho umaze kwinjiza imibare ine ya mbere ya kode ya PIN. Aya majambo, kugira ngo azigame mu gihe co gutunganya, aratuma Pasiporo itasubirijwe canke ngo ihindurwe. Mu gihe hoba hariho ukutumvikana mu nyuma, turaguhanura ko utokoresha ico gikoresho. Ndaguhanura gukoresha iyo nzira kugira ngo wirinde ingorane nyinshi zo gusenyura umubiri w’igikoresho.


![Image](assets/fr/48.webp)


Ubwa nyuma, "*Ivyagutse*" bigufasha gukoresha ibikorwa vyihariye ku mikoreshereze imwe imwe y'igikoresho, nk'umurongo wa Whirlpool CoinJoin.


![Image](assets/fr/49.webp)

## Wohereze ama bitcoins


Ubu ko ufise Sats nkeyi muri Wallet yawe, urashobora no kuzirungika. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku buto bwa "*Send*".


![Image](assets/fr/78.webp)


Injira Address y’uwuriko araronka, mu kuyishiramwo ataco uhinduye, canke mu gucapura kode ya QR ukoresheje kamera ya telefone yawe ngendanwa.


![Image](assets/fr/79.webp)


Menya amafaranga wipfuza kohereza, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Confirm*".


![Image](assets/fr/80.webp)


Hitamwo amahera y’ugucuruza hakurikijwe uko isoko riri ubu, hanyuma usuzume amakuru y’ugucuruza. Niba vyose bimeze neza, fyonda ku "*Sinya na Passport*".


![Image](assets/fr/81.webp)


Wongereko ikimenyetso ku vyo ugurisha kugira ngo ukomeze kwandika neza intumbero yavyo.


![Image](assets/fr/82.webp)

Ivyo bimenyetso bishobora gukorwa gusa na Passport, iyo Passport ikaba ari yo itanga seed yawe itanga uburenganzira bwo gukoresha imfunguruzo z’ibanga zikenewe kugira ngo usinye iyo nzira.

![Image](assets/fr/84.webp)

![Image](assets/fr/85.webp)

![Image](assets/fr/86.webp)

![Image](assets/fr/87.webp)


Raba neza ko amahera ari yo wahisemwo.


![Image](assets/fr/88.webp)

![Image](assets/fr/90.webp)
