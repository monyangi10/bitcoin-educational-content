## Muun Wallet - Inyobora yuzuye


Igitabo c’abakoresha (kirimwo amafoto) y’ikoreshwa rya Muun; n'ikoreshwa ryiza ry'ivyuma Bitcoin Wallet rishobora kugufasha gukoresha Lightning Network.


![image](assets/0.5.webp)


### Gukuraho Muun maze ureme Wallet yawe.


Ubwa mbere, urakeneye gukuraho iyo porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa, ikaba iri kuri iOS no kuri Android. Imisi yose nurabe ko ukura verisiyo ibereye. Rimwe na rimwe hariho ibishushanyo vy’ubuhendanyi ku isoko. Ndagusavye urondere urubuga rwemewe rwa Muun Wallet, ari rwo https://muun.com/, hanyuma ukoreshe urubuga rwa sisitemu wahisemwo (iOS/Android), uhereye ng’aho urashobora kumenya neza ko uriko ukoresha porogarama yemewe.


![image](assets/1.webp)


Iyo ufunguye iyo porogarama, uzoba ufise uburenganzira bwo gukora Wallet nshasha canke gusubirana iyo yariho. Reka dutangure dukore Wallet nshasha. Nzokwerekana intambwe zo gukira Wallet inyuma. Kanda "Rema Wallet nshasha".


![image](assets/2.webp)


Muun Wallet rero iragusaba gukora inomero y’irangamuntu (PIN) y’imirongo ine. Ufise PIN, wongera umutekano wa Wallet yawe mu gihe, nk’akarorero, umukinyi w’umunyaruyeri yokwiba telefone yawe, bigatuma wiba amafaranga yawe y’amahera.


![image](assets/3.webp)


Aho rero, iyo porogarama yatumye habaho Wallet nshasha cane ubu izoba ari yo page y’intango. Ubu urakeneye gucungera amakuru yerekeye ugusubirana kwa Wallet imbere y’uko uyirungikira amahera yose, kuko ivyo vyoba ari umugenzo uteye akaga.


![image](assets/4.webp)


### Ububiko bw'urufunguzo


Kanda akazu kavuga ngo "Back up your Wallet", uzoca urungikwa ku rubuga rwa "Umutekano". Ivyo Muun akora mu gusubiza inyuma bigabanwa neza mu ntambwe zitatu. Si ngombwa ko umuntu aheza izo ntambwe zitatu zose, mugabo iyo azihuriye hamwe biratuma umuntu yiyubara cane.


![image](assets/5.webp)


Ihitamwo rya mbere rituma ushobora gufatanya Wallet yawe n’imeli Address, uretse no kuyirinda n’ijambobanga. Iyi nzira ni iyo guhitamwo kandi ushobora gusimbuka ata ngorane. Niba ushaka kuyikoresha, kanda "1: Backup Wallet yawe" hanyuma "Start" ku gicapo gikurikira maze winjize imeyili Address. Igishushanyo gikurikira kizokubwira ko ukeneye kwemeza iyo email mu kwinjira mu gasandugu kawe k’ubutumwa maze ukanda kuri link iri muri email ya Muun.


![image](assets/6.webp)


Iyo email imaze kugenzurwa, uzosabwa gukora ijambobanga. Hanyuma, uzokenera gushiramwo akamenyetso mu masandugu abiri yerekana ko utahura ko gusubirana kwa Wallet, nimba bikenewe, bizosaba gukoresha imeyili n’ijambobanga uherutse guhitamwo. Ivyo bitandukanye na porogarama za kera zigufasha gusubiramwo ijambobanga ryawe mu gihe woba watakaje canke wibagiwe, rero nurabe neza ko vyose wabibonye.


![image](assets/7.webp)

![image](assets/8.webp)


Igipande ca "Umutekano" ubu kigaragaza ko ufise ububiko bw'ishimikiro. Ubu ushobora gusubira ku rubuga rwa "Wallet" maze ugakoresha porogarama kugira ngo urangize ibikorwa (ivyo bikorwa bizokwerekanwa mu nyuma muri iyi nsiguro), uzi ko Wallet ubu ishobora gusubirwamwo. Ariko rero, ndagusavye gukoresha uburyo bwo gucungera umutekano #2 gushika kuri generate kode y’inyongera, mu gihe ijambobanga ryinjijwe mu buryo #1 ryoba ryahungabanijwe canke nimba ushaka kudakoresha uburyo bwo gusubiza imeyili.


![image](assets/9.webp)


Ihitamwo rya Muun "ubundi buryo bwo gusubiza inyuma" risa n'ijambo Mnemonic rikoreshwa n'ibikoresho vyinshi vya Wallet benshi mu bakoresha Bitcoin bamenyereye. Kanda "Start" kugira ngo ugaragaze kode yawe yo gusubirana maze uyandike ku mpapuro (porogarama iracengera ifoto iri kuri paji yerekana iyo kode). Iyo umaze kuyibona, uyigereranye na kode yerekanwa ku rubuga kuko uzokenera kuyisubiramwo muri porogarama kugira ngo wemeze ko ari ukuri.


Na none, Muun aragusaba kwemeza ko utahura ikibazo, ni ukuvuga ko uzokenera iyo kode y’inyuguti 32 mu gihe woba watakaje ijambobanga ryawe wari warashizeho mbere.


Ivyo bikoresho vya Wallet ubu biracungiwe cane hakurikijwe ingingo mfatirwako zo muri iki gihe tuzi. Ariko rero, porogarama ya Muun ifise uburyo bwa gatatu bwo gucungera umutekano bwitwa "Emergency Kit". Gukora Emergency Kit bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo kugarura Wallet yawe utakeneye guca muri Muun. Mu yandi majambo, mu gukoresha porogarama ya Bitcoin Wallet itandukanye n’iya Muun.


![image](assets/10.webp)


Uhejeje gukanda "Create an Emergency Kit", uzosigurirwa ko iyo kit iza mu buryo bw'inyandiko ya PDF irimwo amakuru & amabwirizwa yerekeye uguhererekanya amahera yawe wigenga. Ico gikoresho gishobora kubikwa ku gicu ataco wiganyiye kuko gisaba ko "Recovery Code" yawe ikoreshwa, iyo kit itari mu nyandiko. Gukuraho igicapo kugira ngo ugere kuri paji y'irema ry'ibikoresho.


![image](assets/11.webp)

### Gusubirana n'imeli Address


Niba warakoresheje uburyo bwo gusubiza inyuma bwa Muun #1, shiramwo email Address yatowe ico gihe. Kubera ko iyo nzira ari ubusabe, urashobora kandi guhitamwo gukomeza aho gukoresha kode yo gusubirana, iyo na yo ikaba ari yo nzira #2 itangwa na Muun. Reka tubanze tugende ku mahitamwo ya email.


![image](assets/15.webp)


Iyo email yawe Address yinjijwe, Muun izokumenyesha ko email yawe yohererejwe kandi ko ukeneye kuyironka kugira ngo wemerere gusubirana Wallet. Suzuma ubutumwa bwawe (harimwo n'igice c'ubutumwa butari bwo) hanyuma ukoreshe urubuga rwatanzwe muri email ya Muun. Uzosubira ku rubuga aho igicapo kizogusaba kwinjiza ijambobanga ryawe rijanye n'imeli yanditswe Address.


![image](assets/16.webp)


Intambwe ya nyuma ni uguhingura inomero y’umuntu, hanyuma uzosubira mu cibare umenyereye ku rupapuro rw’intango rwa Wallet, ugaragaze umubare usigaye ujana na yo.


![image](assets/17.webp)


### Gukoresha "Itegeko ryo Gusubirana".


Igihe usubira gukoresha Wallet iriho, ushobora guhitamwo gukoresha kode yo gusubirana ("Kode yo gusubirana" nk'uko yashizweho na Muun) wari warabonye mbere nimba wahisemwo uburyo bwo gusubiza inyuma #2.


Ivyo bigenda bisa n’ivyo twavuze mu buryo burambuye mu gice ca mbere; gusubirana biciye kuri imeyili. Hitamwo gusa gukoresha "Recover With Recovery Code" maze ukayishira mu bibanza bikwiye vyerekanywe ku rubuga. Nimba Wallet yawe na yo nyene ishigikiwe n’imeli yongeyeko kode yo gusubiza, Muun izogusaba gusuzuma isandugu yawe y’ubutumwa kugira ngo wemerere igikorwa co gusubiza, ivyo ushobora kubiheza umaze gusubira ku nzira y’ugusaba umaze gufyonda ku nzira yatanzwe. Na none, utegerezwa guhingura inomero y’irangamuntu. Aho niho uzoja, uzosubira kuronka Wallet yawe.

Ushobora gushika ku nyandiko ya PDF wabitse igihe wakora ikintu. Iyi nyandiko irimwo amabwirizwa akenewe kugira ngo ubone Wallet yawe. Zirikana ko iyo nzira isaba gukoresha mudasobwa y’ibiro canke y’iminwe kuko ukeneye gukuraho inyandiko yakozwe n’umugwi w’abahinguzi ba Muun. Iryo huriro riri muri iyo email, ariko nzorisangira hano uko biri kwose: https://github.com/muun/recovery


Ico gikoresho c’ivyihutirwa gifise kode yo kugenzura, iyo umaze gukoresha kugira ngo wemeze ko ico gikoresho cakozwe, hamwe n’imfunguruzo zibiri. Imfunguruzo zizoba zikenewe igihe uzoba ukoresha inyandiko yo gusubizaho Muun. Rero, nurabe neza ko ubifise hafi mu gihe c’ugubagwa.

## Kwakira amafaranga


### Bitcoin Urupapuro


Ubu tuzovuga ku gice ca "Kwakira" ca Muun Wallet n'ibikorwa vyayo bitandukanye. Paje y'intango y'ivyo usaba ni "Wallet". Igitigiri cawe kigaragara hagati, kandi urashobora kugikorako kugira ngo uhindure hagati yo guhisha amahera no kuyagaragaza. Tuzoca ku mirongo yose y’ivyo bikoresho mu nyuma muri iki kiganiro. Ubu rero, reka dufyonde kuri "Receive" kugira ngo tubone iyo nzira.


![image](assets/22.webp)


Kuri iyi paji, ushobora guhitamwo kwakira amafaranga kuri nzira zompi Bitcoin na Lightning. Address nshasha (n’ikode ya QR ijana) ihuye n’urubuga wipfuza izokwerekanwa. Ku mburabuzi, Bitcoin Address iragaragara iyo ushitse ku rubuga rwa "Kwakira". Niwafyonda kuri kode ya QR, iyo Address izokopirwa ku gicapo c’ibikoresho vyawe. Ushobora gusangira Address mu buryo bworoshe n'izindi porogarama ukoresheje buto ya "Share", kandi ushobora no gukopa Address ukoresheje buto ya "Copy". Gukanda ku kimenyetso c’ijisho kiri ku mpera y’igitabu ca Address bizokwerekana igitabu cose c’igitabu ca Address, ivyo bikaba bizotuma ushobora kugigereranya n’ico wakopeye ku gicapo igihe uriko urasangira.


![image](assets/23.webp)


Aya makuru arimwo ivyo ukeneye vyose kugira ngo ubone amafaranga yo gukoresha ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin. Ikindi, Muun iraguha uburyo bwo guhindura uko ushaka munsi y'urutonde rwa "Address settings". Ubwa mbere, urashobora kwongerako amahera ku ndondoro ya Address. Ubwa kabiri, urashobora guhitamwo gukoresha SegWit Address (uburyo busanzwe) canke Address ya kera (iragi).


![image](assets/24.webp)


Mu gukanda "Add +", urashobora kwongerako amahera yihariye kuri Address, bikaba vyoroha ku muntu arungitse. Iyi nzira ni ubusabe. Zirikana ko iyo umuntu amaze kwinjiza amahera, buto ya "Kopa" iri ku rupapuro rw'imbere izokwongerako amakuru kuri Address yakopiwe ("Bitcoin:" nk'intango, ikurikirwa n'amahera nk'inyuma). Kugira ngo ntubwirizwe guhindura iyo nkuru, nufyonde kuri kode ya QR kugira ngo ukope Address. Amakuru y’ingero azoguma afatanye na yo. Ikindi, iyo porogarama iragufasha guhitamwo kwinjiza amahera mu mafaranga uhisemwo, ivyo bikaba bikorosha guhindura amahera mu BTC.


![image](assets/25.webp)


Ku bijanye no guhitamwo ubwoko bwa Address, SegWit canke Legacy, ndabahimiriza gusiga SegWit mu kibanza cayo. Ubwo bwoko bwa Address (butangura na "bc1") buragabanya ubunini bw'amakuru y'ibikorwa vy'ubudandaji gutyo bugabanura amafaranga y'ibikorwa ajanye n'ibikorwa. Ariko rero, birashoboka ko ushobora gukenera gukoresha uburyo bwa "Legacy" (Address butangura kuri "3") mu gihe Wallet canke porogarama idahuye n'amaderesi ya SegWit. Ni ngombwa rero kumenya gutandukanya ubwo bwoko bubiri.


![image](assets/26.webp)


## Umuravyo Tab


Kugira ngo uronke amahera ukoresheje Lightning Network, urakeneye gufyonda ku rubuga rufise izina nyene ruri hejuru y’ibarabara. Ubu hazogaragara kode ya QR irimwo Lightning Address, iyo ushobora kuyikopa no kuyisangiza abandi mu buryo bumwe n’ubw’amaderesi ya Bitcoin twavuze imbere y’aho muri iyi nsiguro. Ndabibutsa ko Lightning Network ishobora kugufasha kwinovora umuvuduko wo gukorana hafi mu kanya nk’ako gukubita ushizwmwo n’amahera yo gukorana ari agace k’ayo ari ku ruhererekane rwa Bitcoin.


![image](assets/27.webp)


Amahitamwo yo guhindura ibintu ashobora kuboneka munsi y'urutonde rwa "Invoice settings". Aha, ushobora guhindura umubare ujanye na Address ukanda kuri "Ongera +". Nshingiye ku bumenyi mfise ku bijanye na Lightning Network, nizera ko ari vyiza kwinjiza amahera igihe urema igikorwa kuko ama wallet menshi adasubiza neza amafagitire ataco avuga. Uzobona kandi ko muri iyi menyu hariho igihe co guhera. Muri iyo porogarama, igihe gishirwaho iminota 60, hanyuma Address ntizoba ikora. Zirikana ko Muun itanga Lightning Address nshasha igihe cose uhinduye umubare canke igihe uvuyeyo ugasubira kuri paji.


![image](assets/28.webp)


## Gukoresha umukozi wa LNURL


Muun Wallet itanga ubushobozi bwo gukoresha LNURL kugira ngo wakire amafaranga. Iryo koraniro, rikoreshwa mu gufyonda ku kimenyetso co gucapura kiri hejuru iburyo kuri iyo paji, rifise ivyiza vyinshi, harimwo no kwirinda gusangira Invoice kugira ngo umuntu aronke amahera. Ahubwo, urakeneye gucapura kode ya QR kugira ngo ubone amakuru yerekeye ivyo kwishura, hanyuma ugashobora kwemeza ko wemeza ingene ivyo bihembo vyifashe.


![image](assets/29.webp)


Muun azobanza kukwereka urupapuro rw’insobanuro (raba ifoto iri hejuru) hanyuma akusabe gukoresha kamera y’igikoresho cawe, intambwe ikenewe kugira ngo ukoreshe iyo porogarama. Urashobora kubona ko aderesi za LNURL ubu zidashigikirwa n'amasakoshi yose ya Lightning. Abo babishigikiye muri rusangi batanga ubushobozi bwo gukoresha LNURL kugira ngo baronke amafaranga ntibayarungike.


![image](assets/30.webp)


## Kurungika amafaranga


### Biciye ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin


None ko twabonye ingene twokwakira bitcoins na Muun, reka twihweze ingene twozirungika. Gusubira kuri paji y'intango munsi y'agace ka "Wallet", uzokenera gukanda "Kohereza". Ubu hazoboneka urupapuro rworoshe aho uzoba ufise uburenganzira bwo gukopa Bitcoin canke Lightning Address mu kibanza cagenewe canke ugakanda ikimenyetso ca kode ya QR kiri iburyo bw’ico kibanza kugira ngo ukoreshe kamera maze ushireko Address mu buryo bwa kode ya QR.


![image](assets/31.webp)

![image](assets/32.webp)


Iyo ushitse kuri paji ya "Kohereza", nimba usanzwe ufise Address ikopiwe ku gikoresho cawe, Muun azomenya uburyo bwa Address (Bitcoin canke Lightning) maze akubwire ko woyikoresha mu gutangaza amakuru biciye ku butumwa bufise akadiri.


![image](assets/33.webp)

![image](assets/34.webp)


Igihe utegura amafaranga y’i Bitcoin, urakeneye kwandika amahera uzorungika. Raba neza ko iyo Address ija yerekanwa hejuru y'ibarabara ihuye n'iyo Address yari yarakopewe mbere. Munsi y'amahera uzorungika, Muun yerekana umubare w'amahera yawe ya Wallet kandi akaguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amahera yawe yose, ikintu c'ingirakamaro cane nimba ushaka gukuraho ubusa bwose muri Wallet yawe maze wirinde gusiga "Dust" (satoshis nkeyi).


![image](assets/35.webp)


Muun amaze kwemeza amahera azorungika, aragusaba kuri paji ikurikira ngo wandike akajambo. Ivyo bikora nk’inyongezo y’ukwemeza, kandi urafise umwidegemvyo wo kwandika ivyo ushaka vyose, bifitaniye isano canke bitabereye.


![image](assets/36.webp)


Gusubiramwo kwa nyuma kw’ibintu vy’ido n’ido vy’ugucuruza ni ngombwa imbere y’uko amaherezo bimenyeshwa. Wemeze Address n'amahera winjije, hanyuma uhindure amafaranga y'ugucuruza nimba bikenewe ukanda ikimenyetso c'ikaramu y'ubururu iburyo bwa "Amafaranga y'urubuga". Gutahura ivy’ishimikiro vy’ingene ikidengeri c’amafaranga ca Bitcoin (Mempool) gikora ni inyigisho nziza yoshobora kugukiza amasatoshi menshi mu gihe c’igihe!


![image](assets/37.webp)


Porogarama ya Muun ishira mu ngiro ubuhinga buharura amahera akenewe kugira ngo umuntu yemeze mu minota 30 canke n’ibiri munsi yayo. Ivyo nivyo bizokwerekanwa iyo ugerageje guhindura amafaranga y’ugucuruza. Buto ya "Enter Fee Manually" iragufasha guhindura ivyo bintu wewe nyene, ikintu gishobora kuba ngirakamaro cane iyo ukeneye kwemezwa vyihuta canke, ibihushanye n'ivyo, iyo ufise urugero runini rwo gukora.


![image](assets/38.webp)

Mu guhitamwo kwinjiza amafaranga y’ugucuruza wewe nyene, uzoja kuri paji nshasha yerekana amafaranga uzokwinjiza muri sat/vbyte (satoshis kuri byte y’ukuri). Muun mbere yerekana ingereranyo y’igihe co kwemeza gifitaniye isano n’amahera wahisemwo, hamwe n’igiciro mu BTC n’amahera ya fiat wahisemwo.

![image](assets/39.webp)


Subira kuri paji y'insiguro y'ibikorwa maze ukande "Ohereza". Voilà, amafaranga yawe aratangazwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin! Uzosubirwamwo kuri paji y’intango ya Wallet aho uzobona ivyo bikuwe ku giciro cawe. Mu nsi y’ibarabara, hari umwampi ushobora gukanda kugira ngo usuzume amateka yawe y’amahera. Ivyo uherutse gukora bizokwongereza ku gihe ca mbere co gusohoka kwavyo.


![image](assets/40.webp)


Kanda inyandiko kugira ngo ubone ido n’ido ry’ibintu vy’agaciro. Ivyo ugurisha bizokwemezwa igihe Miner yongerako igice gishasha kirimwo ku ruzitiro. Muun asangira transaction ID ahantu hasi ku rubuga, bikagufasha kumenya uko amafaranga yawe ameze kuri Block explorer.


![image](assets/41.webp)


## Biciye kuri Lightning Network


None rero reka dukoreshe Bolt 11 Invoice (umuravyo Invoice w’agaciro/uw’imbere) kugira ngo dukore igikorwa co gucuruza. Kopa canke scanner umuravyo Address kuri paji "Yohereza". Uzorungikwa kuri paji nshasha ikwereka ido n'ido ry'ivyo Invoice iriho ubu. Amafaranga azokoreshwa azokwerekanwa (harimwo n’amahera y’urubuga), hamwe n’agapapuro canke insobanuro yanditswe kuri Invoice, be n’igihe co guhera kiri musi. Zirikana ko amafaranga y’ibikorwa adashobora guhindurwa ku bikorwa vya Lightning. Bigenwa n’inzira canke inzira bazobwirizwa gufata kugira ngo bashike ku muntu bazobironka.


![image](assets/42.webp)


(Ehe imburi yerekanwa ku rubuga iyo ukoresheje Invoice ataco ivuga, bisobanura ko ata mahera yuzuye imbere y’igihe. Hari ama wallet ashigikira ubwo bwoko bwa Invoice kandi akaguha uburenganzira bwo guhindura amahera wewe nyene. Ivyo si ko biri kuri Muun.)


![image](assets/43.webp)


Gukanda ku kimenyetso c’ijisho bikwereka ido n’ido ry’urudodo rw’umuravyo uriko urakora muri iki gikorwa. Ufise mbere uburenganzira bwo kuraba urubuga kugira ngo uronke ibindi bisobanuro. Uyu ni akarorero keza k’ubuhinga bwo gufata ibintu mu buryo butari bwo (abstraction technique) Muun yashitseko.


![image](assets/44.webp)

## Amagenamiterere y'ikoreshwa


Urupapuro rwa gatatu kuri paji y'intango, "Ivyagezwe," ni ho ushobora gusanga ivyagezwe vy'ikoreshwa. Iyi page ni ngufi bitangaje cane cane ugereranije n’izindi wallets zizwi cane zigendanwa. Njye mbona ko ivyo si ikibazo; ahubwo mbona ko ari akarusho mu bijanye n’ukworohereza.


![image](assets/47.webp)


Mu rwego rusangi, urashobora guhitamwo igice ca konti n’amahera ukunda, hamwe n’insanganyamatsiko y’ukuntu porogarama igaragara (umwiza canke umuco), ivyo bikaba bizomenyekana mu ntango bishingiye kuri sisitemu y’igikoresho cawe.


Ku bijanye n’igice ca konti y’ivyo usaba, hitamwo hagati ya Bitcoin (BTC) canke Satoshi (SAT). Kugira ngo mumenye gusa, Satoshi ni agace gatoyi kuruta ayandi yose k’igice ca Bitcoin, ari co kibanza ca munani c’icumi (1 SAT = 0.00000001 BTC). Gushiramwo Wallet mu Satoshis akenshi birakundwa igihe ahanini ukoresha Lightning Network n’amahera makeyi.


Muun itanga amahitamwo menshi y’amafaranga, bigufasha kuronka uguhindura BTC gukenewe ku vyo ukeneye mu bijanye n’ugucuruza n’/canke vy’umuntu ku giti ciwe.


Niwabona ko bikenewe guhindura ijambobanga ryawe ryo gusubizaho Wallet, urashobora kubigira kuri paji y’imiterere. Raba neza ko ufise ijambobanga ryawe ry’ubu canke kode yo gusubizaho n’uko ushobora gukoresha imeyili yawe.


![image](assets/48.webp)


Injira ijambobanga ryawe ry’ubu canke uhitemwo kwinjiza kode yawe yo gusubirana kugira ngo utangure gusubiramwo. Muun azorungika ubutumwa kuri Address yari yaranditswe mbere.


![image](assets/49.webp)

![image](assets/50.webp)


Igice c'imiterere iteye imbere kirimwo ibintu bibiri: Bitcoin Network na Lightning Network. Mu nzira ya Bitcoin, duhabwa amahitamwo yo gutuma Taproot ishobora kwakira amaderesi (bc1p, ubwoko bushasha bwa Address) ku buryo busanzwe.


![image](assets/51.webp)


Imbere muri Lightning Network, uzosanga:



- Porotokole yo kwakira: Hitamwo urubuga rwawe rwo kwakira rwerekanwa ku mugaragaro wo kwakira. Igikorwa kigerageza, Unified, na co nyene kiraboneka. Iyi ni kode ya QR ihuriza hamwe amaderesi ya Bitcoin n’aya Lightning. Ariko rero, ubu ni porogarama nkeyi za Bitcoin zishigikira iyo nzira.

-


- Imirongo ya Turbo: Iyi nzira iragufasha gukoresha canke guhagarika igikorwa c’imirongo ya Turbo. Ku mburabuzi, birashoboka.


![image](assets/52.webp)


Kugira ngo dutahure ivyo bita imirongo ya Turbo, dutegerezwa ubwa mbere kumenya ko amafaranga y’umuravyo akorwa biciye ku mihora iva ku muntu umwe aja ku wundi, kandi ko iyo nzira itegerezwa gutangura guterwa inkunga n’amafaranga akoreshwa kuri Bitcoin Blockchain.


Imirongo ya Turbo iragufasha gutangura gukoresha kuri Lightning Network mbere n’imbere y’uko igikorwa cose ca On-Chain cemezwa. Guhagarika iyo nzira bizosigura ko uzobwirizwa kurindira igihe kirekire cane kugira ngo ukoreshe amafaranga yawe kuri Lightning Network, muri Exchange kugira ngo ubone umutekano mwinshi w’amahera yawe, kuko ahandi ho utegerezwa kwizigira ko Muun atazokora nabi (ugukoresha kabiri kwa bose) mu gihe urindiriye ko amafaranga yawe yemezwa kuri GW10.


Hasi kuri paji y'imiterere hariho uburyo bwo guhitamwo "Sohoka". Ushobora gukoresha iyi nzira niba ushaka ko porogaramu igabanya Wallet yemewe ubu. Ivyo bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gukora Wallet nshasha canke kwinjiza/gusubiza iyariho.
