passphrase BIP39 ni ijambobanga ry’ubusa, rifatanijwe n’ijambo Mnemonic, ritanga Layer y’inyongera y’umutekano ku bikoresho vy’ibanga vy’ubuhinga n’ivy’ubuhinga. Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzobona hamwe ingene woshiraho passphrase kuri Bitcoin Wallet yawe itekanye kuri Trezor (Safe 3, Safe 5 na Model One).



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Before starting this tutorial, if you're not familiar with the passphrase concept, how it works and its implications for your Bitcoin Wallet, I strongly recommend that you consult this other theoretical article where I explain everything (this is very important, as using a passphrase without fully understanding how it works can put your bitcoins at risk) :



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7

passphrase kuri Trezor ikoreshwa mu buryo bwa kera nimba wahisemwo urugero rwa BIP39 mu gihe co gutunganya (ico ndagusaba nimba udakeneye *Multi-share Backup*). Ikintu kidasanzwe kuri Trezor ni uko ushobora kwinjira muri passphrase ukoresheje Hardware Wallet, canke ukoresheje klavye ya mudasobwa yawe ukoresheje porogarama ya Trezor Suite. Ubu buryo bwa kabiri buratekanye cane, kuko iyo mudasobwa ifise ahantu hanini cane ho gutera kuruta Hardware Wallet. Ariko rero, kwandika ururimi rwa passphrase rutoroshe vyoshobora kwihuta ku klavye isanzwe kuruta ku rurimi rwa Hardware Wallet, ivyo bikaba vyoshobora gutuma umuntu akoresha amajambo y’ibanga akomeye. Ni vyiza rero kwama ukoresha passphrase, naho yoba itegerezwa kwandikwa, kuruta kutayikoresha na gato. Ariko rero, birahambaye ko tuguma tuzi ko ivyo bishobora gutuma umuntu agira ingorane nyinshi z’ibitero vy’imibare.



Ivyo bihitamwo ntibiboneka kuri porogarama zose zo gucunga Wallet zihuye na Trezor. Nk'akarorero, ku citegererezo ca mbere, passphrase ishobora kwinjira biciye ku klavye iri kuri Sparrow wallet. Ku bikoresho vya Model T, Safe 3 na Safe 5, utegerezwa gukoresha Trezor Suite canke winjiza passphrase uhereye kuri Hardware Wallet, kuko uburyo bwo kwinjira biciye kuri Sparrow bwazimijwe na HWI mu myaka mikeyi iheze.



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Muri Trezor Suite, ufise uburyo bubiri butandukanye bwo gucunga ivyifuzo vya passphrase. Ushobora gukoresha uburyo bwa "*passphrase*" mu gice ca "*Igikoresho*". Iyo bishoboka, Trezor Suite n’izindi porogarama zose zo gucunga Wallet zizogusaba mu buryo butunganye kwinjiza passphrase yawe igihe cose utanguye. Niba ushaka uburyo bwo gukoresha passphrase, ushobora kuguma ukoresha "*Standard*". Muri ivyo, uzokenera gushika ku rutonde rwa Hardware Wallet yawe ruri mu mfuruka yo hejuru ibubamfu, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*+ passphrase*" igihe cose uzoyitangura.



Imbere yo gutangura iyi nyigisho, urabe neza ko umaze gutanguza Trezor yawe kandi ugatanga ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic. Niba utarabikora, kandi Trezor yawe ari nshasha, kurikiza inyigisho zijanye n'akarorero ziboneka kuri Plan ₿ Network. Uhejeje iyi ntambwe, urashobora gusubira muri iyi nyigisho.

## Kwongera passphrase ku Safe 3 canke Safe 5



Uhejeje gukora Wallet yawe, ukabika Mnemonic yawe, ugashiraho PIN, uzoja ku rutonde rw’inzu rwa Trezor Suite. Mu mfuruka yo hejuru ibubamfu, idirisha rikwiye kuboneka rigutumira gukoresha passphrase BIP39.



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Niba iri dirisha ritabonetse, uzokenera gukoresha n'amaboko uburyo bwa "*passphrase*" mu gice c'imiterere ya "*Igikoresho*".



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Iri dirisha rigusaba kwinjiza passphrase yawe. Hitamwo passphrase ikomeye maze uce ukora ivy’umubiri, ku kintu nk’impapuro canke ivyuma. Muri aka karorero, nahisemwo passphrase: `fH3&kL@9mP#2sD5qR!82`. Uyu ni akarorero; ariko rero, ndagusavye uhitemwo passphrase ireha gatoyi. Hagati y’inyuguti 30 na 40 vyoba vyiza (nk’ijambobanga ryiza).



n’ukuri, ntukwiye kwigera usangira passphrase yawe kuri Internet, nk’uko ndabigira muri iyi nyigisho. Aka karorero Wallet kazokoreshwa gusa kuri Testnet kandi kazokurwaho ku mpera y’inyigisho.



Kugira ngo uronke impanuro zitomoye ku bijanye no guhitamwo passphrase yawe, ndagutumiye kandi ngo wongere urabe iyi yindi ngingo:



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7

Niba ushaka kwinjiza passphrase yawe uciye kuri klavye ya mudasobwa yawe, yinjize mu kibanza catanzwe, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Access passphrase Wallet*".



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Hardware Wallet yawe izoca yerekana passphrase yawe. Raba neza ko bihuye n’ivyo ufise (impapuro canke ivyuma) imbere y’uko ukanda ku rubuga kugira ngo ukomeze.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Ivyo bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gukoresha Wallet yawe irindwa na passphrase.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Niba ushaka kwongereza umutekano mu kwinjiza passphrase yawe gusa kuri Trezor yawe, iyo usabwe, kanda kuri "*Injira passphrase kuri Trezor*".



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



Igikoresho ca T9 kizoboneka kuri Trezor yawe, kiguhe uburenganzira bwo kwinjira muri passphrase yawe. Uhejeje kwandika, fyonda ku kimenyetso ca Green kugira ngo ukoreshe passphrase kuri Wallet yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Uzoca ushobora gukoresha passphrase yawe itekanye Wallet.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Kugira ngo ukoreshe Sparrow wallet, uburyo bwo kubikora burasa n’ubwo, ariko ku bikoresho T, Safe 3 na Safe 5, passphrase itegerezwa kwinjira kuri Hardware Wallet atari biciye kuri klavye ya mudasobwa.



Igihe cose Sparrow wallet isaba ko ushobora gukoresha Trezor yawe, kandi passphrase itarakoreshwa kuva watangura, uzokenera kuyinjira ukoresheje klavye ya T9.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



## Kwongera passphrase ku Model One



Ku kigereranyo ca mbere, gukoresha passphrase BIP39 ni hafi y’ikintu gihambaye cane. Kubera ko ico gikoresho kitarimwo secure element, biroroshe cane gukura amakuru y’agaciro. Ntishobora rero guhangana n’uguterwa ku mubiri. Ariko rero, nk’uko passphrase idaguma ku gikoresho kimaze kuzimwa, gukoresha passphrase ikomeye (idashobora gutera) birashobora kukurinda ibitero vyinshi bizwi vy’umubiri kuri iki kigereranyo.



Ku kigereranyo ca mbere, ntibishoboka kwinjira muri passphrase ku Hardware Wallet. Uzokenera kuyishiramwo uciye kuri klavye ya mudasobwa yawe.



Umaze gukora Wallet yawe, ukabika Mnemonic yawe, ugashiraho PIN, uzoja ku rutonde rw’inzu rwa Trezor Suite. Mu mfuruka yo hejuru ibubamfu, idirisha rigutumira gukoresha passphrase BIP39 rikwiye kuboneka.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Niba iri dirisha ritabonetse, ukeneye gukoresha uburyo bwa "*passphrase*" mu gice ca "*Igikoresho*" c'imiterere.



![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)



Iri dirisha rigusaba kwinjiza passphrase yawe. Hitamwo passphrase ikomeye maze uce ukora igice c’umubiri, ku kintu nk’impapuro canke ivyuma. Muri aka karorero, nahisemwo passphrase: `fH3&kL@9mP#2sD5qR!82`. Ico ni akarorero gusa; ariko rero, ndagusavye uhitemwo passphrase ireha gatoyi. Hagati y’inyuguti 30 na 40 vyoba vyiza (nk’ijambobanga ryiza).



Kugira ngo ubone impanuro zitomoye ku bijanye no guhitamwo passphrase yawe, ndagutumiye kandi ngo wongere urabe iyi yindi ngingo:



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7

Injira passphrase yawe mu kibanza catanzwe, hanyuma ukande kuri buto "*Shika kuri passphrase Wallet*".



![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)



Hardware Wallet yawe izokwerekana passphrase yawe. Raba neza ko bihuye n’ivyo ufise (impapuro canke ivyuma), hanyuma ukande kuri buto y’iburyo kugira ngo ukomeze.



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Ivyo bizokujana kuri Wallet yawe irindwa na passphrase.



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Kugira ngo ukoreshe Sparrow wallet inyuma y’aho, uburyo bwo kubikora buraguma ari ubwo nyene. Igihe cose Sparrow isaba ko umuntu ashobora gukoresha Hardware Wallet yawe, kandi passphrase itarashirwamwo kuva igikoresho caheruka gutangura, uzokenera kuyishiramwo.



![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)



Urakoze cane, ubu uriko urakoresha neza passphrase BIP39 ku bikoresho vya Trezor. Niba wifuza gutera intambwe imbere mu mutekano wawe wa Wallet, raba iyi nyigisho ku buryo bwo gucungera amakuru bwa Trezor *Multi-share* (*Ibanga ryo gusangira ibanga rya Shamir*):
