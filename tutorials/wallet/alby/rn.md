## 🐝 Ukwagura umucukumbuzi wa Alby

Igikoresho ca Alby Browser Extension ni igikoresho gisanzwe kandi gikoreshwa neza gishiramwo Bitcoin Lightning Network mu bumenyi bwawe bwo ku rubuga. Ituma umuravyo wawe Wallet ukorana n’imbuga mu buryo bwa porogarama.


## 🌟 Ibirango vy'ingenzi

### 💸 Ukwishura n'ingengo y'imari


- Wohereze kandi wakire amafaranga kuri Lightning, Bitcoin On-Chain na Liquid mu buryo butaziguye mu mucukumbuzi wawe
- Gushinga ingengo y'imari y'imbuga
- Gushoboza kwishura ubwavyo no gutangaza Bitcoin.


### 🔄 Ubuyobozi bwa Wallet


- Huza uturongo dutandukanye tw’umuravyo n’ibipapuro (nk’akarorero, [Ikigo ca Alby](https://albyhub.com/), LND, CLN, ibipapuro vy’ububiko).
- Gucungera no guhindura hagati y'amasakoshi ahuye mu buryo bworoshe


### 🧾 Ibikorwa


- Raba amateka y'ibikorwa, harimwo n'amakuru y'imbuga.


### 🐦‍⬛


- generate urufunguzo rwihariye rwa Nostr canke wongereko urufunguzo rwawe ruriho
- Injira mu mutekano muri porogaramu za Nostr udasangira urufunguzo rwawe rw'ibanga
- Sinya neza ubutumwa bwa Nostr hakurikijwe uruhusha rwawe bwite


### 🌐 Gushigikira amasezerano

Infashanyo yuzuye [WebLN](https://www.webln.guide/) ku kwishura muri porogaramu n'infashanyo yuzuye ya LNURL (-kwishura, -gukura, -kwemeza, n'ibindi)


## 🌎 Ubuhuza bwa mucukumbuzi

Ukwaguka kw'umucukumbuzi wa Alby kuraboneka kuri:


- Mucukumbuzi zose zishingiye kuri Chromium (nk'akarorero, Chrome, Intwari, Urubibi, Opera)
- Firefox (Ibiro & *Telefone ngendanwa*)


**Ubuzima bwite n’umutekano**: Igikoresho ca Alby Browser Extension ni igikoresho co kwikingira. Imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga zirashizwe mu buryo butekanye kandi zibitswe ku gikoresho cawe. Uguma ugenzura itunga ryawe n’amakuru yawe.


Kugira ngo ubone amabwirizwa atari yo kandi yoroshe gukurikiza, raba [Ivyigwa vya Alby](https://ibirongo.getalby.com/ibirongo-vy'abakoresha/ivyagutse-vy'umucukumbuzi)
