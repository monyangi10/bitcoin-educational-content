Gutuma kwishura bigenda vyoroha cane hakoreshejwe bitcoin ni ingorane ihanganye n’amashirahamwe menshi muri uwo muce. Alby iratandukanye n’izindi n’iyindi Alby wallet y’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none. Iyi extension igamije gushinga urutonde rw’amazi rushobora kumenya amaderesi kandi rukagufasha kwishura ata ngorane. Muri iyi nyigisho, turabona ivyungura Alby maze tukagerageza ingene vyorosha kwishura bivuye ku mucukumbuzi.




![video](https://youtu.be/nd5fX2vHuDw)




## Ukwagura Alby



Igikoresho ca Alby ni igikoresho gifasha umucukumbuzi wawe w’urubuga gukorana mu buryo bworoshe kandi ata nkomanzi n’urubuga rwa Bitcoin be n’uruzitiro rwayo rwa Lightning Network. Irangwa n’ibintu bitatu:




- Lightning Network wallet: Huza node yawe ya Alby canke konti yawe kugira ngo wohereze kandi wakire bitcoin vyihuse kandi bihendutse biciye ku nzira ya Lightning Network.
- Kwishura biciye ku rubuga: Bikuraho ivy’ugucapura amakode ya QR canke guhindura hagati y’ibikoresho vyo kwishura bitcoin ku mbuga zishigikira Lightning. Bituma amafaranga agenda neza mu gukanda rimwe, canke ata kwemeza nimba warashizeho ingengo y’imari.
- Umuyobozi wa Nostr: Uwo mugwi ucungera imfunguruzo zawe za Nostr, bigatuma vyoroha gufatanya no gukorana n’ibikorwa vya Nostr bikora nk’umusinyi w’umutekano ataco ushize ahabona ku rubuga rwawe rwose.

## Huza n'ukwaguka



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko turakoresha igice ca Alby mu mucukumbuzi wa Firefox munsi y’uburyo bwo gukoresha Ubuntu. Ariko rero, iraboneka no kuri Windows no ku bikoresho nka Chrome.



Ushobora kwongerako ivyagutse vya Alby ku mucukumbuzi wawe mu gusura ububiko bw'ivyagutse [Firefox] (https://addons.mozilla.org/fr/firefox/addon/alby/) canke ububiko bw'ivyagutse [Chrome] (Ibisobanuro/ibisobanuro/alby-bitcoin-wallet-ku-l/iokeahhehimjnefflcihljlcjccdbe).



![firefox](assets/fr/01.webp)



![chrome](assets/fr/02.webp)



i️ Ni vyiza ko usuzuma ko umwanditsi w’iyo extension ari we vy’ukuri konti ya Alby yemewe, kugira ngo wirinde uburyo bwose bwo kwiba canke kwiba amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins.



Yongerako iyo extension ku mucukumbuzi wawe ukanda kuri buto iri iburyo.


Tanga uruhusha rukenewe kugira ngo ushiremwo no gukoresha iyo nzira, hanyuma ushire iyo nzira ku mwanyabikoresho kugira ngo uyironke bitagoranye.

## Gutanga amahera yawe yambere



Iyo umaze kwinjira, urashobora gufyonda ku nkuru ya Alby iri mu mwanya w’ibikoresho kugira ngo ubone igitabu cawe.



![buzzin](assets/fr/06.webp)



Umaze gukora konti yawe ya Alby, uzokenera kuyifatanya na wallet kugira ngo ukoreshe satoshis. Kugira ngo uhuze bitcoin wallet na konti yawe ya Alby, turagusavye gukoresha node ya Alby Hub, ushobora kuyishira kuri mudasobwa yawe canke ukayandikisha ku mugambi utangwa na Alby.



![hubplan](assets/fr/13.webp)




Muri iyi nyigisho, konti yacu ya Alby ishigikirwa n’ugushiraho ivy’aho hantu ku mashine yacu.


Kugira ngo wubake node yawe ya Alby, turagusavye inyigisho yacu ya Alby Hub.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/node/lightning-network/alby-hub-62e6356c-6a6d-4134-8f22-c3b6afb9882a

Iyi node iragufasha gukora ibitabo vy’umuravyo vyicungera no gucunga neza imirongo yawe y’umuravyo kugira ngo wohereze no kwakira satoshis.



![channels](assets/fr/14.webp)



Gufungura imihora y’ukwakira isobanura umubare wose w’amasatoshi ushobora kwakira.



![receivechanal](assets/fr/15.webp)



Gufungura imihora yo kohereza mu guhagarika satoshis kuri aderesi y’uruzitiro rwa bitcoin. Ama satoshis wabujije asobanura ama satoshis yose ushobora gukoresha.



![spend](assets/fr/16.webp)



Ubu urashobora kohereza no kwakira ama satoshis biciye ku nzira ya Alby.



![exchange](assets/fr/08.webp)



Kuva ng’aho, iyo extension ya Alby irashobora kumenya amaderesi ya Lightning n’amafagitire ari kuri paji z’urubuga usura, kandi izogusaba ko wovyishura mu bitcoin canke Lightning uhereye ku extension yawe.



![suggest](assets/fr/09.webp)



![pay](assets/fr/10.webp)




## Gucungera urufunguzo rwo gusubirana n'urufunguzo rwa mbere



Urufunguzo rwa mbere rutangwa n’ukwaguka kwa Alby rukora nk’igipfukisho co gukingira kigufasha kuvugana n’urubuga nyamukuru rwa Bitcoin (Onchain), urutonde rwa Nostr kandi kigufasha gukoresha uruja n’uruza rwa Lightning n’ibikorwa vya Nostr.



![masterKey](assets/fr/11.webp)



Urufunguzo nyamukuru rufata uburyo bw’amajambo 12 asa n’ijambo ryawe ryo gukira. Turagusavye rero ko wobika ukoresheje uburyo butekanye kugira ngo ushobore kuyironka igihe cose.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270


![masterKey](assets/fr/12.webp)



Ubu ushobora kumenya amahera ataco akora ku bitcoin na Lightning n’ivyo kwagura Alby. Niba warashimishijwe n’iyi nyigisho, turagusavye inyigisho yacu ya Alby Hub kugira ngo ushireho urudodo rwawe rwa Alby no kugenzura imice yose y’amasakoshi yawe ya Alby ukoresheje interface iryoshe kandi ikomeye.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/node/lightning-network/alby-hub-62e6356c-6a6d-4134-8f22-c3b6afb9882a
