## 🐝 Alby Browser Extension
The Alby Browser Extension is a lightweight and user-friendly browser extension that integrates the Bitcoin Lightning Network into your web experience. It allows your lightning wallet to programmatically interact with websites.

## 🌟 Key Features
### 💸 Payments & Budgets
- Send and receive payments over Lightning, Bitcoin on-chain and Liquid directly in your browser
- Set custom spending bugets for websites
- Enable auto-payments and streaming Bitcoin

### 🔄 Wallet Management
- Connect different Lightning nodes and wallets (e.g., [Alby Hub](https://albyhub.com/), LND, CLN, custodial wallets).
- Manage and switch between between connected wallets easily

### 🧾 Transactions
- View detailed transaction history, including metadata from websites.

### 🐦‍⬛ Nostr
- Generate a Nostr private key or add your existing key
- Securely log into Nostr apps without sharing your private key
- Seamlessly sign Nostr messages according to your own permissions

### 🌐 Protocol Support
Full [WebLN](https://www.webln.guide/) support for programmatic payments and comprehensive LNURL support (-pay, -withdraw, -auth, etc.)

## 🌎 Browser Compatibility
The Alby Browser Extension is available for:
- All Chromium-based browsers (e.g., Chrome, Brave, Edge, Opera)
- Firefox (Desktop & *Mobile*)

**Privacy and Security**: The Alby Browser Extension is a self-custodial application. Your private keys are securely encrypted and stored on your device. You retain control over your assets and data.

For the most accurate and easy-to-follow instructions, check out [Alby's guides](https://guides.getalby.com/user-guide/browser-extension)
