Bitcoin Knots ni ubundi buryo bwo gushirwa mu ngiro kw'amasezerano ya Bitcoin, akomoka kuri Bitcoin core. Yahinguwe kandi ibungabungwa na Luke Dashjr, itanga ibintu bimwebimwe vy’inyongera n’amategeko ahindurwa kuva kuri Mempool, mu gihe iguma ihuye n’izindi nzira ziri ku rubuga. Bitcoin Knots ihuriza hamwe Bitcoin Wallet, ariko kandi ishobora gukoreshwa nk’urudodo rworoshe rwa Bitcoin iruhande y’izindi porogarama za Wallet.


## Ni kuki ukoresha Knots aho gukoresha Core?


Ubu, Core ni yo ishira mu ngiro cane umurongo wa Bitcoin ku rubuga. Itegeko rya Bitcoin ni amategeko gusa. Bisaba porogarama kugira ngo umuntu abikoreshe. Imashini ikoresha porogarama ishira mu ngiro umurongo wa Bitcoin yitwa node, kandi izo node zose hamwe zigize urusobe rwa Bitcoin.


Mu mateka yose ya Bitcoin, abakiriya benshi bavuye muri porogarama ya mbere yateguwe na Satoshi Nakamoto baraserutse. Uyu musi (Ntwarante 2025), Bitcoin core ni yo ihambaye cane, hafi 98% vy’ibihimba biri ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin bikoresha uwo mukiriya.


Ariko rero, hariho n’izindi porogarama zishobora gukoreshwa. Ivyo si ibihimba bihuye na Altcoin nka Bitcoin Cash, ariko ni abandi bakiriya bahuye n’urubuga nyarwo rwa Bitcoin. Muri izo, Bitcoin Knots ni yo izwi cane. Ubu iserukira hafi 1,4% vy’uruja n’uruza. Abandi bakiriya b’ubundi buryo baracari bake cane.


![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)


Hari impamvu zibiri nyamukuru zo gukoresha uwundi mukiriya nka Knots aho gukoresha Core:




- Ivy’ubuhinga**: Abo bakiriya kenshi batanga uburyo butandukanye kuri Core, cane cane mu bijanye n’uburongozi bwa Mempool, mu kumenya amafaranga yemerwa kandi atangazwa n’uruzitiro rwawe.
- Policy**: Abantu bamwe bamwe barakunda gukoresha ubundi bakiriya nka Knots kubera imvo zitari izo mu vy’ubuhinga, cane cane kugira ngo bashigikire ubundi buryo bwo gukoresha Core gutyo bagabanye ububasha bwayo bwo gukoresha. Iyo Core izokwigera ihungabana, vyoba vyiza atari gusa kugira abaguzi bakomeye kandi batunganijwe neza, ariko kandi kumenya uko yobakoresha. Abandi bakoresha Knots mu ntumbero zo kwiyamiriza, kuko batakaje icizigiro mu bategura Core canke ntibemera uburongozi bw’abaguzi benshi.

## Gutanguza IBD


Igihe ca mbere utanguye Bitcoin Knots, uzoshobora guhitamwo ububiko bwo mu karere aho amakuru yawe y'inzira (harimwo Blockchain, UTXO set na parameters) azobikwa.


![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)


Ushobora kandi guhitamwo guca amakuru ya Blockchain kugira ngo ugume gusa amabuye aheruka. Iryo hitamwo rituma urudodo rwawe rushobora gusuzuma buri bubiko bwose mu gihe c’ububiko bwashinzwe, gutyo buhoro buhoro bugakuraho ububiko bwa kera cane. Niba ufise umwanya uhagije kuri disiki (ubu uri hafi 650 GB, ariko uwo mubare uriko urakura), reka iyo nzira idasuzumwe. Niba umwanya wawe wo kuri disiki ari muto, fungura ugukata maze ugaragaze ubushobozi bwo hejuru bwemerewe.


Iyumvire: Nimba urudodo rwawe ari pruned kandi ukarukoresha mu guhuza Wallet yagarutse, ntuzoshobora kugarura amafaranga y'imbere y'ibarabara rya kera ryabitswe mu karere.


![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)


Ubundi buryo buboneka ni "*Wiyumvire ko ari ukuri*". Ivyihuta guhuza ivy'intango mu gusimbuka ukugenzura umukono ku bikorwa vy'ubudandaji biri mu bice imbere y'igice kinaka.


Intumbero ya "*Assume Valid*" ni ukwihutisha uguhuza kwa mbere kw'uruzitiro ata kugabanya cane umutekano, mu kwiyumvira ko ivyo bikorwa vyamaze kwemezwa cane n'urubuga imbere y'igihe. Ico nyene gihambaye ni uko node yawe itazomenya ubusuma bwose bwa Bitcoin bwa kera, ariko izoguma yemeza ukuri kw’umubare wose w’ama bitcoins yasohotse. Node yawe izogenzura imikono yose y'ibikorwa inyuma y'ibarabara ryatanzwe. Ubwo buryo bushingiye ku ciyumviro c’uko igikorwa kimaze igihe kirekire cemewe n’urubuga ata ngorane ari co gishobora kuba gifise akamaro.


Nk'akarorero, aha, "*Twiyumvire ko ari ngirakamaro*" itegurwa ku bubiko no. 855 000 `000000000000000000000000000000000000000000000000000000d38aa4486cd7033fffc2c4df556d0b9138`, yasohowe ku wa 1 Myandagaro 2024, nta nkuru yanje yose izotangura gusa. uruzitiro.


![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)


Hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*OK*" kugira ngo utangure *Intial Block Download*. Uzokenera kwihangana mu gihe c’uguhuza amanode y’intango. Niba wipfuza gusubira gukorana n’ibindi bikoresho mu nyuma, funga gusa iyo porogarama maze uzime mudasobwa yawe. Gukorana bizosubira ata ngorane igihe uzofungura porogarama.


![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)


## Gutegura urudodo rwawe rwa Bitcoin


Fyonda kuri "*Ivyagezwe*", hanyuma uhitemwo "*Amahitamwo*".


![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)


Mu "*Main*" tab, ushobora gushika ku mirongo nyamukuru y'uruzitiro:




- "*Start...*" ihita itanguza node iyo mudasobwa yawe itanguye kugira ngo itangure gukorana ubwo nyene;
- "*Prune...*" itunganya umupaka w'ububiko nimba wahisemwo gukata Blockchain ;
- "*Ububiko bw'amakuru...*" bushinga umubare munini wa RAM wemerewe ku nzira yawe;
- Ubwa nyuma, fungura "*Shoboza server ya RPC*" nimba wipfuza gufatanya node yawe ya Bitcoin Knots n'iyindi porogarama ya Wallet, nka Sparrow wallet canke Liana nk'akarorero.


![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)


Mu gice ca "*Wallet*", uzosanga amasetingi ya Wallet yunze ushobora kurema mu nyuma kuri Knots. Ndabagira inama yo gukoresha RBF na Coin. Ushobora kandi gusobanura ubwoko bw'inyandiko izokoreshwa.


![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)


Urupapuro rwa "*Urusobe*" rurimwo amaparametere y'urusobe ushobora guhindura bivanye n'ivyo ukeneye.


![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)


Igipande ca "*Mempool*" kigufasha gutunganya *Memory Pool*, ni ukuvuga uburongozi bw'ibikorwa bitaremejwe bibitswe mu bubiko, n'ubunini burengeye ubw'ibindi bikoresho (300 MB ku buryo busanzwe).


![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)


"Ukuyungurura Spam" ni ikintu ca Bitcoin Knots. Aha uzosanga umubare w’ibintu bigufasha guhitamwo amafaranga uzokwemera canke wanka gutangaza. Intumbero ihambaye ni uguhagarika ikoreshwa rimwe rimwe ry’inyuma rya Bitcoin, cane cane ama meta-protocols, kugira ngo urwanye iyo ngeso mu gihe wirinda gukoresha cane node yawe. Ni amahitamwo ya politike, bivanye n'ivyo ubona kuri Bitcoin.


Uzosanga kandi amaparametere ya kera nk'insobanuro y'umurongo wa "*Dust*".


Ariko rero, ivyo bipimo biragira ico bikoze gusa ku mategeko y’ugushinga urugero. Node yawe izobandanya kwemera amafaranga ataremejwe gusa iyo ashizwe mu gice, kugira ngo igumye ihuye n’ibindi bice vy’urubuga rwa Bitcoin. Ivyo bihindura gusa uburyo node yawe ikora no gutanga amafaranga ataremejwe ku bagenzi bayo. Mu bikorwa, nk’uko Knots iri mu bake, ni amategeko yashizweho ku buryo busanzwe kuri Bitcoin core asobanura ugushiraho urugero ku rubuga.


![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)


"*Mining*" tab iragufasha gutunganya uruhara rwawe rushoboka muri Mining, niba wipfuza gukoresha iyi nzira.


![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)


Ubwa nyuma, urupapuro rwa "*Display*" rwerekeye ibipimo bijanye n'ibishushanyo vya Interface, harimwo n'ururimi rwa porogarama.


![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)


## Gukora Bitcoin Wallet


Igihe uguhuza kwa mbere kwarangiye, urudodo rwawe rwa Bitcoin Knots rurakora neza. Ubu ufise uburenganzira bwo gufatanya iyi node n’iyindi porogarama ya Wallet, canke ukoreshe Hot Wallet yubatswemwo ataco uhinduye. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku buto "*Rema Wallet nshasha*".


![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)


Wallet yawe uyihe izina. Ushobora kandi kuyikingira na passphrase BIP39 ukanda kuri "*Gushiramwo Wallet*". Niwamara kwitegura, ukande kuri buto ya "*Create*".


![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)


passphrase BIP39 ni ijambobanga ry'ubuhinga ushobora guhitamwo mu mwidegemvyo, ushizeko ijambobanga ryawe rya Mnemonic, kugira ngo wongere umutekano wa Wallet yawe. Imbere yo gutunganya iki gikoresho, turaguhanura cane gusoma ingingo ikurikira, isigura mu buryo burambuye ingene passphrase ikora mu vyiyumviro, n’ingene wokwirinda amakosa yoshobora gutuma utakaza amafaranga yawe ubudasiba:


https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/backup/passphrase-a26a0220-806c-44b4-af14-bafdeb1adce7
Niba warakoresheje uburyo bwa passphrase, nuhitemwo uburyo bukomeye maze ububike witonze ku kinyamakuru kimwe canke vyinshi bifise umutekano.


![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)


Bitcoin Wallet yawe ubu iraremwe.


![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)


## Gusubiza inyuma Bitcoin Wallet yawe


Mbere n’imbere y’uko uronka ama bitcoins yawe ya mbere, ni ngombwa ko ukora backup ya Bitcoin Wallet yawe kugira ngo ushobore kugarura amahera yawe iyo utakaje canke mudasobwa igakora nabi. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku rubuga rwa "*Dosiye*" hanyuma ukande kuri "*Gufata Wallet*".


![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)


Iyi operation itanga dosiye imwe ishobora gukoreshwa mu kugarura ama bitcoins yawe yose. Rero urabe maso cane, kandi ubike ku kintu co hanze gitekanye.


## Kwakira ama bitcoins


Kugira ngo ubone ama bitcoins ataco ushize kuri Knots Wallet yawe, fyonda ku buto bwa "*Kwakira*".


![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)

Ushobora kandi gusobanura imbere y’igihe amahera nyayo uzoronka kuri iyi Address, canke ukongerako ubutumwa ku wuzoyitanga. Umaze gushinga amaparametere, kanda kuri "*Saba kwishurwa*".


![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)

## Guhuza urudodo rwawe n'iyindi porogaramu


Bitcoin Knots' Interface yunze ubumwe yo gucunga Bitcoin yawe Wallet si ngombwa ko ari yo ishobora gutahurwa cane, kandi ibikorwa vyayo biguma ari bike cane. Ariko rero, urashobora gufatanya node yawe ya Bitcoin Knots na porogarama yihariye yo gucunga Wallet kugira ngo ushobore kuronka amakuru ya Blockchain Bitcoin mu buryo bworoshe no gutangaza amafaranga yawe.


Uburyo bwo kubikora buzovana n’ivyo ukoresha, ariko hariho ibintu bibiri nyamukuru: Bitcoin Knots yoba ishizwe kuri mudasobwa imwe na porogarama yawe ya Wallet, canke ikoreshwa n’imashini itandukanye.


### Na Bitcoin Knots yo mu karere :


Niba Bitcoin Knots ishizwe kuri mudasobwa yawe, rondera dosiye `Bitcoin.conf` muri dosiye za porogarama. Niba iyi dosiye itahari, urashobora kuyirema. Gufungura n'umuhinduzi w'inyandiko hanyuma winjize umurongo ukurikira:


```ini
server=1
```


Hanyuma ubike ivyo wahinduye.


Ushobora kandi kubikora biciye ku gishushanyo ca Bitcoin-QT ca Interface mu kuja kuri "*Ivyagezwe*" > "*Amahitamwo...*" hanyuma ugakoresha "*Gushoboza server ya RPC*".


Ntukibagire gusubira gufungura porogarama umaze guhindura ivyo.


![Image](assets/fr/34.webp)


Hanyuma uje kuri porogaramu yawe yo gucunga Wallet (nk'akarorero Sparrow wallet canke Liana) maze winjize inzira ija muri dosiye yawe y'amakuru, akenshi iri muri dosiye imwe na `Bitcoin.conf`, bivanye n'uko ukoresha:


|**macOS**|~/Library/Application Support/Bitcoin|
|---|---|
|**Windows**|%APPDATA%\Bitcoin|
|**Linux**|~/.Bitcoin|

![Image](assets/fr/35.webp)


Siga ibindi bipimo nk'ivya kera, URL `127.0.0.1` n'icuma `8332`, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Igerageza ry'Ihuza*".


![Image](assets/fr/36.webp)


### Na Bitcoin Knots iri kure :


Niba Bitcoin Knots ishizwe ku yindi mashini ifatanye n’urubuga rumwe, banza urondere dosiye `Bitcoin.conf` mu madosiye ya porogarama. Niba iyi dosiye itarabaho, urashobora kuyirema. Gufungura iyi dosiye n'umuhinduzi w'inyandiko wongereko umurongo ukurikira:


```ini
server=1
```


Uhejeje guhindura dosiye, urabe ko uyibika muri dosiye ibereye sisitemu yawe:


|**macOS**|~/Library/Application Support/Bitcoin|
|---|---|
|**Windows**|%APPDATA%\Bitcoin|
|**Linux**|~/.Bitcoin|

Ivyo bishobora kandi gukorwa biciye ku bishushanyo vya Bitcoin-QT vya Interface. Genda kuri "*Ivyagezwe*", hanyuma "*Amahitamwo...*", hanyuma ukoreshe amahitamwo ya "*Gushoboza server ya RPC*" mu guca ku gasandugu kahuye. Niba dosiye `Bitcoin.conf` itahari, ushobora kuyihingura uhereye kuri iyi Interface ukanda kuri "*Gufungura dosiye y'imiterere*".


![Image](assets/fr/37.webp)


Rondera IP Address y’imashini yakira Bitcoin Knots ku rubuga rwawe rwo mu karere. Kugira ngo ubikore, urashobora gukoresha igikoresho nk’iki [Igikoresho co gupima IP y’ishavu](https://angryip.org/). Reka twiyumvire, kubera impaka, ko IP Address y'urudodo rwawe ari `192.168.1.18`.


Mu dosiye `Bitcoin.conf`, wongeremwo imirongo ikurikira, ushireho `rpcbind=192.168.1.18` kugira ngo ihure na IP Address y'urudodo rwawe.


```ini
[main]
rpcbind=127.0.0.1
rpcbind=192.168.1.18
rpcallowip=127.0.0.1
rpcallowip=192.168.1.0/24
```


![Image](assets/fr/38.webp)


Wongereko kandi izina ry'ukoresha n'ijambobanga ry'amahuza yo kure kuri dosiye `Bitcoin.conf`. Raba neza ko usubiriza `loic` izina ryawe ry'ukoresha na `ijambobanga ryanje` n'ijambobanga rikomeye:


```ini
rpcuser=loic
rpcpassword=my_password
```


![Image](assets/fr/39.webp)


Amaze guhindura no kubika dosiye, subiramwo Bitcoin Knots.


Ubu ushobora kuja kuri porogaramu yawe yo gucunga Wallet (nk'akarorero Sparrow wallet canke Liana). Ku Sparrow, genda kuri "*Ukoresha / Guca*". Injira izina ry'ukoresha n'ijambobanga watunganije muri dosiye `Bitcoin.conf`. Siga ibindi bipimo nk'ivya kera, ni ukuvuga URL `127.0.0.1` n'icuma `8332`. Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Igerageza ry'Ihuza*".


![Image](assets/fr/40.webp)


Iryo sano rirashingwa.
