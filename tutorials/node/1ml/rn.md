## Imenyekanisha



Lightning Network ni umuti wo kwishura wihuta kandi utazimvye wubatswe hejuru ya Bitcoin, ushoboza gukoresha amafaranga ako kanya kandi ata nkomanzi. Kwihweza iyo nzira ni ngombwa kugira ngo umuntu atahure ingene ikora, topologie yayo n’ingene nodes ziyigize zimeze. Igikoresho ca Lightning explorer, nka 1ML, gikoreshwa mu kubona amakuru ya bose y’urubuga, harimwo n’ibihimba bikora, imirongo yuguruye n’ubushobozi buriho, bitanga inyishu y’agaciro ku bakoresha, abategura n’abakoresha ibihimba.



## Ushike kuri 1ML maze utahure urupapuro rw'intango



Kugira ngo ushikire 1ML, fungura gusa urubuga rwawe maze wandike [https://1ml.com](https://1ml.com). Ivyo bigushikana kuri paji y’intango, ikora nk’ibarabara ry’isi yose rya Lightning Network.



![capture](assets/fr/03.webp)



Iyi paji yerekana imibare myinshi ihambaye hejuru y'ibarabara, harimwo :





- **Igitigiri cose c'ibice bikora** ku rubuga, ni ukuvuga mudasobwa zikora mu kurungika no kwakira amahera y'umuravyo.
- **Igitigiri c'imirongo yuguruye**, ihuye n'amasano hagati y'izo nzira zishoboza gutanga amafaranga.
- **ubushobozi bwose bw’urubuga**, bugaragazwa mu bitcoin (BTC), bugaragaza umubare w’ubushobozi bw’imihora yose ya bose.



Ivyo biharuro birahindurwa ubudasiba kugira ngo vyerekane uko urubuga rumeze ubu. Bitanga iciyumviro c’ubunini bwayo, ukuntu ikura n’ukuntu ikomeye.



![capture](assets/fr/04.webp)



Hasi gusa, iyo paji itanga urutonde rufise urutonde:





- **Ivyiyumviro bihuye cane**, bifise imirongo yuguruye cane ku yindi mirongo.
- **Ubushobozi burengeye ubw'ibindi** ku nzira, vyerekana ingingo zishobora gutanga amafaranga menshi.
- Imirongo ihambaye cane mu bijanye n’ubushobozi.



Amayunguruzo arashobora kandi gukoreshwa mu gutunganya izo lists hakurikijwe aho umuntu ari canke ibindi bipimo.



Iyi paji ni intango nziza yo gutohoza Lightning Network no gutahura ubuhinga bwayo rusangi.

## Gutohoza uturongo tw'umuravyo



Kugira ngo utore urudodo kuri 1ML, tangura ukoresheje umurongo w'ubushakashatsi uri hejuru kuri paji. Ushobora kwinjiza **Node ID**, ni ukuvuga ikimenyetso kidasanzwe c'urudodo, canke **izina ry'urudodo**, ari ryo zina ryoroshe kwibuka.



Igihe ubushakashatsi burangiye, fyonda ku nzira ihuye kugira ngo ubone paji yayo ido n’ido.



![capture](assets/fr/07.webp)



Kuri iyi paji, hari amakuru menshi ahambaye yerekanwa:





- Node ID**: iki kimenyetso kidasanzwe ni urudodo rurerure rw'inyuguti rutuma node imenyekana neza mu rubuga rwose.



![capture](assets/fr/08.webp)





- Alias**: iri ni ryo zina ryatowe na nyen'urudodo kugira ngo ariserukire ku mugaragaro.



![capture](assets/fr/09.webp)





- **Umubare w’imirongo** werekana ingene iyo node ifise amahuzu yuguruye n’izindi node, mu gihe **ubushobozi bwose** bugereranya umubare w’ama bitcoins aboneka muri iyo mirongo. Igikoresho gifise imirongo myinshi n’ubushobozi bwinshi muri rusangi gihuye neza kandi gishobora gutanga amahera menshi vyihuse ku rubuga rwose.



![capture](assets/fr/10.webp)





- **Igihe co gukora**, canke ukuboneka, bipima igihe urudodo rwamaze rukora kandi rushobora gushikira kuri internet, bikaba vyerekana ko rwizigirwa. **Imyaka** y'urudodo, ku rundi ruhande, yerekana igihe rumaze ku rubuga, yerekana ukuntu ruhagaze n'ubumenyi rufise muri Lightning Network.



![capture](assets/fr/11.webp)



Aya makuru aragufasha gutahura akamaro n'ukwizigirwa kw'urudodo muri Lightning Network. Nk’akarorero, node ifise umubare munini w’imirongo, ubushobozi bwinshi n’igihe kinini co gukora akenshi ni umukinyi mukuru mu rubuga.



## Gutohoza imihora y'umuravyo



### Umurongo w’umuravyo ni iki?



Umurongo w’umuravyo ni uruja n’uruza hagati y’ibice bibiri vy’uruja n’uruza. Bituma izo nzira zibiri zishobora guhanahana amahera ubwo nyene, ataco zitwaye zitaciye mu ruhererekane rwa Bitcoin ku bijanye n’ugucuruza kwose. Imirongo ni umushinge utuma Lightning Network yihuta kandi ishobora gukoreshwa.



### Soma amakuru y'umurongo kuri 1ML



Kuri 1ML, umurongo wose ufise urupapuro rwawo canke urupapuro rw'insobanuro rurimwo amakuru ahambaye:



Kuva kuri paji y'uruzitiro, ushobora gushika ku rutonde rw'imirongo yarwo. Mu gufyonda ku muhora, 1ML yerekana urupapuro rwihariye rufise amakuru menshi y’ingenzi.



![capture](assets/fr/12.webp)



![capture](assets/fr/13.webp)



Amakuru nyamukuru aboneka ni aya akurikira:





- Ivyuma vy'abafatanyabikorwa**: umurongo wose uhuza ivyuma bibiri. 1ML yerekana ibimenyetso vyose n'amazina yabo y'ibanga.



![capture](assets/fr/14.webp)





- Ubushobozi bw’umurongo**: uwu ni umubare wose w’ama bitcoins yugarijwe muri uyu murongo. Ubwo bushobozi bugereranya urugero rwo hejuru rw’amahera ashobora guca muri uwo muhora.



![capture](assets/fr/15.webp)





- Imyaka y'umurongo**: yerekana igihe umurongo umaze ufunguye. Umurongo wa kera akenshi ni ikimenyetso c’ubucuti buhamye hagati y’ibihimba bibiri.



![capture](assets/fr/16.webp)



### Imipaka y'ukuboneka kw'umurongo



Ni ngombwa gutahura ko 1ML yerekana gusa **igice** ca Lightning Network. Ico gikoresho kigaragaza gusa **imirongo ya bose**, ni ukuvuga iyo yatangajwe ku rubuga. Imihora y’abantu ku giti cabo, akenshi ikoreshwa kubera imvo z’ibanga canke ingamba, ntiboneka. Ikindi kandi, 1ML ntiyerekana neza ukuntu amahera agabanywa ku ruhande rumwe rumwe rw’umuhora, canke amahera yishurwa, canke amahera ashobora gukoreshwa mu vy’ukuri mu gihe kanaka. Amakuru yerekanwa rero arashobora gukoreshwa mu gusuzuma **imiterere rusangi y’urubuga**, ariko si igikorwa nyaco c’ivy’ubutunzi canke ivy’ubutunzi vy’ido n’ido.



## Gutahura Lightning Network ukurikije aho iri



### Amanode hakurikijwe igihugu n'akarere



1ML ishobora kugufasha gutohoza Lightning Network hakurikijwe **ugusenyuka kw'ibibanza**. Kuva kuri paji y'intango canke biciye ku bice vy'umwihariko, birashoboka kwerekana amanode hakurikijwe igihugu canke akarere. Ivyo bishingiye ku makuru y'aho hantu yatangajwe n'abakoresha amanode.


Mu mwanya wo kugenderamwo, uzobona **Ikibanza**. Kuri paji, uturongo dushizwe mu mirwi hakurikijwe umugabane, igihugu n'igisagara.



![capture](assets/fr/17.webp)



Mu guhitamwo igihugu, 1ML yerekana urutonde rw'ibice bifitaniye isano, hamwe n'imibare ihuriweko nk'umubare w'ibice n'ubushobozi bwose buboneka bw'ako karere.



#### Ivyo ivyo bivuga ku bijanye n’ugutora abana bo mu karere .





- Ukwemera ikoranabuhanga**: Umubare munini w'ibice mu karere vyerekana ko abakoresha Bitcoin, amashirahamwe canke ibikorwa bariko barakoresha Lightning Network. Ivyo vyerekana ko umuntu akuze mu vy’ubuhinga n’umutima ukunze wo kwungukira ku nyungu za Lightning (ugucuruza vyihuta, amafaranga make).
- Ubutunzi bw’ibidukikije** : Kuba hariho ama node menshi birashobora kandi kwerekana ubutunzi bwo mu karere bukikuje Bitcoin: abacuruzi bemera Lightning, amashirahamwe atangura gutegura ibikoresho, ibintu bibera mu kibano, n’ibindi.
- Umutekano n’ukwihangana**: Ugukwiragizwa mu bibanza bitandukanye bituma uruja n’uruza rw’urubuga rushobora kwihangana mu gihe habaye uguhagarara kw’amashanyarazi canke amategeko yo mu karere. Uko izo node zirushiriza gushwiragizwa, ni ko urushiriza gukwiragizwa kandi bigorana gucengera urubuga.
- Amategeko n’amabwirizwa**: Ibihugu bimwebimwe birashobora kubona vyihuta kwemerwa bivuye ku nzira nziza y’amategeko canke umuryango ugira uruhara. Ku rundi ruhande, mu bice aho amategeko akomeye canke ari urwanko, igitigiri c’ibihimba bizoba ari gito.



#### Imipaka y'amakuru y'ubutaka



Ariko rero, zirikana ko ikibanza c’ibice vy’umuravyo gifise aho kigarukira be n’aho kigarukira:





- Aho IP iri**: 1ML muri rusangi ikoresha aderesi IP ya bose y'ibice kugira ngo igereranye aho biri. Ariko rero, ubu buryo burashobora guhindurwa n’ugukoresha VPNs, ama server yo mu gicu (AWS, Google Cloud), canke kwakira abashitsi mu bihugu bitandukanye n’aho nyen’uruzitiro aba.
- Virtual nodes**: Hari abakoresha bakira nodes zabo ku ma server yo kure kubera imvo z’ukwizigirwa n’ukuboneka, bishobora gutanga inyiyumvo y’ikinyoma y’aho umuntu ari.
- User mobility**: Uwukoresha node ashobora guhindura aho ari, kwimurira ibikorwa remezo vyiwe, canke gufungura node nyinshi mu turere dutandukanye, bigatuma gusoma amakuru bigorana.
- Kutaboneka kw’ibice vy’ibanga**: Hari ibice bimwe bimwe bitatangaza aderesi IP yavyo canke ngo bikoreshe uburyo bwo guhisha aho biri, bikaba bituma geolocation idashoboka.



## 1ML beton ikoreshwa



### Gutahura ubuhinga bw'urubuga



1ML ishobora kugufasha kubona mu ciyumviro **imiterere rusangi ya Lightning Network**. Mu kwihweza amasano hagati y’ibice, umubare w’imirongo n’ubushobozi muri rusangi, birashoboka gutahura ingene urubuga rutunganijwe, ibice bifise uruhara runini n’ingene amafaranga ashobora guca.



Ukwo gutahura ni ngombwa nimba dushaka gutahura ingene Lightning Network ikora, atari ugukoresha gusa mu bitabo.



### Kumenya uturongo duhambaye



Kubera urutonde rutangwa na 1ML (ibihimba vyinshi bihuye, ubushobozi bwinshi, imyaka), birashoboka kumenya ibihimba bifise ikibanza gihambaye mu rubuga. Izo node akenshi zikora nk’amarembo akomeye y’amahera y’umuravyo.



![capture](assets/fr/18.webp)



### Suzuma ukuntu urudodo ruboneka



Ku mukoresha w'urudodo, 1ML iraguha uburenganzira bwo kugenzura nimba urudodo rwawe **rwamamajwe ku mugaragaro** kuri Lightning Network. Iyo node ibonetse kuri 1ML, ivyo bisigura ko iboneka kandi ishobora gushikirwa n’izindi node zo gufungura imihora ya bose.


Ivyo birashobora kuba ngirakamaro mu gusuzuma ingorane zo kuboneka canke zo guhuza.



### Uravye Lightning Network itera imbere uko igihe kigenda kirarenga



Mu kugereranya imibare y’isi yose mu bihe bitandukanye, 1ML iradushoboza kwihweza iterambere rya Lightning Network: ukwiyongera canke ukugabanuka kw’umubare w’ibihimba, uguhinduka kw’ubushobozi bwose canke uguhinduka kw’ugukwiragira kw’ibibanza.


Ivyo vyiyumviro biratanga iciyumviro gishimishije ku bijanye n’iterambere, ukukura n’imigenderanire ya Lightning Network.



## 1ML ibikorwa vyiza n'imipaka



### Amakuru ya bose ≠ ukuri kwuzuye



1ML ishingiye gusa ku makuru **yatangajwe ku mugaragaro** kuri Lightning Network. Ivyo bisigura ko ico gikoresho kigaragaza gusa igice c’ukuri. Imirongo myinshi ishobora kuba ari iyo kwiherera, ama node amwe amwe yoshobora kutamenyeshwa, kandi amahera nyayo aboneka mu mirongo ntaboneka. Ni ngombwa rero gukoresha 1ML nk’igikoresho co gusesangura isi yose, atari nk’uguserukira urubuga rwose.



### Ubuzima bwite n'umuravyo



Lightning Network yakozwe yibanda cane ku **ubuzima bwite bwo kwishura**. Ibikorwa vy’umuntu ku giti ciwe ntibiboneka kuri 1ML, kandi amafaranga nyayo y’imirongo ntaboneka kuri bose. Ivyo bigabanya si ikosa ry’umugenzuzi, ahubwo ni ikintu nyamukuru kiri muri Lightning Network, gigenewe kurinda ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha.



### Ntusimbukire ku nsozero



Node ifise ubushobozi bwinshi canke imirongo myinshi si ngombwa ko iba "yizigirwa" canke "ikora neza" mu bihe vyose. Na vyo nyene, ugugabanuka kw’igihe gito kw’ubushobozi bwose bw’urubuga ntibisigura ko ari ingorane y’imiterere. Imibare ikwiye kwama isobanurwamwo umuntu abona inyuma kandi igashirwa mu vyo ivuga.



### Uwuzuzanya n'ibindi bikoresho



1ML ni intango nziza cane yo gutohoza Lightning Network, ariko ikoreshwa neza hamwe n'ibindi bikoresho nk'ibikoresho vya Lightning, interfaces zo gucunga node n'ibindi bikoresho vyo gutohoza. Ubwo buryo buratuma umuntu abona neza kandi afise utunenge twinshi ku bijanye n’uruja n’uruza.



## 1ML guhuza amahitamwo (imikorere iteye imbere)



1ML itanga **uburyo bwo kwinjira / gukora konti**, buboneka ku rubuga, ariko ivyo **ntibikenewe** kugira ngo ubone amakuru ya Lightning Network. Ivyo vyose twavuga gushika ubu muri iyi nyigisho biraboneka **ata konti**.



Iryo huriro rigenewe cane cane **Abakoresha b'imirongo y'umuravyo**. Cane cane, bituma urudodo rushobora gufatanywa na konti ya 1ML kugira ngo rushobore gucunga amakuru amwamwe ya bose, nk’ugushikiriza urudodo, amahuzu yo guhamagara n’ibindi bimenyetso. Ico gikoresho gikoreshwa mu gutuma urudodo ruri mu gikoresho co gutohoza rugaragara neza kandi rukamenyekana.



Ni vyiza kumenya ko 1ML **atari igikorwa co kubungabunga**. Gukora konti ntibitanga uburenganzira bwo kuronka amahera, imihora canke kwishura kw’urudodo. Bikoreshwa gusa mu gukorana n’umugenzuzi akoresheje uburyo bwo gutangaza no gutanga amakuru.



Mu bijanye no kwiga canke kuvumbura Lightning Network, iyo nzira rero irashobora gufatwa nk’**iyidasanzwe** kandi ikaba igenewe gukoreshwa mu buryo buteye imbere cane.



## Iciyumviro



1ML ni igikoresho c’agaciro co kwihweza no gutahura Lightning Network bivuye ku makuru yayo ya bose. Bigufasha gutohoza imiterere y’urubuga, gusuzuma uturongo n’imirongo, no gukurikirana iterambere ryose ry’ukwemera Lightning Network mu gihe.
