Gukoresha Bitcoin yawe na Lightning Full node ntibigikeneye kuba igikorwa kigoye gikorwa n’abahinga mu vy’ubuhinga gusa. Mu migenzo, gushinga no gucunga ama node vyasaba ubumenyi bwimbitse bw’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru, gukorana n’abandi be n’ugutegura porogarama. Nakamochi arabihindura mu gutuma ama node ashobora gushikira umuntu wese, ataravye ubuhinga afise.


Nakamochi, umuntu wese arashobora gushinga no gukoresha node ari i muhira, bikaba bimufasha kugira ubuzima bwiwe bwite n’ukwigenga mu vy’amahera. Gukoresha Full node ntibigukingira gusa amafaranga yawe ariko kandi biratuma urubuga rwa Bitcoin rukomera. Igikoresho ca Bitcoin kyegerejwe kandi gikomeye cizigira ugukwiragira kw’ibice vyinshi kugira ngo kibe gifise umutekano n’ukwigenga.

## Nakamochi ni iki kandi ikora gute?


Nakamochi ni Full node ikoreshwa na Bitcoin gusa ifasha mu gukoresha imirongo ya Bitcoin n’iya Lightning. Harimwo Bitcoin na Lightning Wallet zihuriweko, zituma abakoresha bashobora gukoresha uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin rutekanye kandi rwigenga mu gihe bungukirwa n’umuvuduko wa Lightning Network n’ibiciro bito vy’ugucuruza.


Igikoresho cawe ca Nakamochi gicungirwa biciye ku rubuga rwa telefone ngendanwa, [BitBanana (Android)](iOS) na [Zeus (iOS)](iOS), bikagufasha kugicungera neza uri aho hose. Izo porogaramu zikora nk’igenzura ry’inyuma ry’uruzitiro rwawe, zigufasha kwishura ataco ukoresheje Bitcoin canke Lightning, gucunga amafaranga, gufungura canke gufunga imihora, kugenzura amafaranga, no kugenzura ingene uruzitiro rwawe rukora, vyose bitagoranye.

## Ivyerekeye Lightning Network


https://planb.network/courses/34bd43ef-6683-4a5c-b239-7cb1e40a4aeb

Bitcoin Lightning Network ihindura cane ibikorwa vya Bitcoin mu kubigira vyihuta, bihendutse kandi bikora neza. Ni vyiza ku gukoresha buri musi, bishoboza kwishura hafi y’aho nyene n’amahera make, vyiza ku bikorwa bitobito nk’ugugura ikawa canke gukorana n’ibintu bitobito bigurwa kenshi.

Mu gukoresha off-chain, Lightning yagenewe gutera imbere, gufasha ibihumbi vy’ibikorwa ku segonda ata kuremerera Bitcoin Blockchain nyamukuru. Ivyo bituma iba umukinyi w’ingenzi mu gutera imbere kwa Bitcoin mu buryo bwo kwishura bukora, kw’isi yose.

Ubuzima bwite ni ikindi ciza, kuko amafaranga akoreshwa kuri Lightning akoreshwa mu nzira z’ukwishyura z’abantu ku giti cabo aho kwandikwa kuri Blockchain. Ivyo bituma haba uburyo bwo gukorana mu buryo bwite mu gihe umuntu aguma afise umutekano ukomeye wa Bitcoin.



#### Inzira zo kwishura zasiguwe


Lightning Network ikora biciye mu nzira zo kwishura, ari zo nzira zihuza abantu babiri zituma habaho amafaranga menshi ataco zikorana na Blockchain. Iyo umurongo wuguruye, uburinganire hagati y’ababiri burahindurwa kuri iki gisubizo ca kabiri-Layer Lightning ku bijanye n’ugucuruza kwose, bikaba bituma umuntu yishura vyihuta kandi ataco atwara. Gufungura n'ugufunga kw'umurongo vyonyene nivyo vyandikwa kuri On-Chain, bikagabanya umudugararo kuri Bitcoin Blockchain. Iyi nzira ituma habaho ugukwiragira n'ubuzima bwite, kuko amafaranga y'umuntu ku giti ciwe aguma atandikwa kuri Ledger ya bose.


**Akarorero:** Alice na Bob bafungura umurongo mu kuwushinga Bitcoin. Alice yohereza Bitcoins kuri Bob, kandi amafaranga yabo ya off-chain arahindurwa ubwo nyene ata nkuru ya On-Chain. Iyo Alice rero iriha Charlie, kandi Alice ata nzira ishika kuri Charlie, iyo nzira ishobora guca ku nzira ya Bob kugira ngo igere kuri Charlie. Gukoresha inzira biciye mu bice vy’ubuhuza bituma umuntu ashobora kwishura mbere ata n’umwe akora neza, ivyo bikaba bituma iyo nzira ikora neza cane.



## Gufungura imirongo no gukora amafaranga muri Lightning Network


Igihe Nakamochi yawe imaze gushirwaho kandi ihuye n’iporogarama yo gucunga node, urashobora gutangura gukoresha Lightning Network mu gufungura imihora no gukora amafaranga.


### Gufungura imirongo kuri Zewu (iOS):


1. Genda kuri **“Imirongo”** (menyu yo hasi).

2. Fyonda kuri **“+”** kugira ngo ufungure umurongo mushasha.

3. Scan canke winjize pubkey ya node ushaka gufatanya nayo.

4. Injira umubare ufungiwe (hitamwo n’urunganwe rwawe canke ukoreshe umubare mutoyi udahinduka ku nzira zizwi cane).

5. Fyonda kuri **“Gufungura umurongo”**.


![image](assets/en/06.webp)

_Igishushanyo ca ZEUS_


Ushaka kumenya vyinshi: [Imirongo | Inyandiko za Zewu](inyandiko.zeusln.app/)


### Gufungura imirongo kuri BitBanana (Android):


1. Fungurira urutonde rw’ibifungurwa vy’ibitoke (ibubamfu).

2. Genda kuri **“Imirongo”**.

3. Fyonda kuri **“+”** kugira ngo wongere/ufungure umurongo mushasha.

4. Scan canke ushireko urufunguzo rwa pub.

5. Injira umubare ufungiwe (hitamwo n’urunganwe rwawe canke ukoreshe umubare mutoyi udahinduka ku node zizwi cane).


![image](assets/en/07.webp)

_Igishushanyo c'ibitoke_


Ushaka kumenya vyinshi: [Ibitoke](https://ibitoke.com)


Iyo umurongo wawe umaze gufungurwa, amahera ashobora guca kuri wo ku bandi bari muri iyo nzira. Ivyiyumviro bihindura off-chain, bikaba bituma amafaranga akoreshwa hafi y’aho nyene kandi akagira amahera makeyi.

Niba utagikeneye umurongo, urashobora kuwufunga. Ico gikorwa kiratuma habaho uburinganire bwa nyuma hagati yawe n'urunganwe rwawe maze kikavyandika On-Chain. Ivyiza ni uko bompi bemeranya kandi bari kuri internet kugira ngo “bafunge mu buryo bw’ubufatanye” (amahera yihuta kandi make n’“ugufunga ku nguvu” n’uwo mugenzi ataco yishuye/atari kuri interineti).

Muri rusangi, turahimiriza gusiga imihora yuguruye kugira ngo ugabanye ibiciro no kwongera ukwizigirwa n’ubushobozi bw’urubuga. Mu kuguma ufunguye imirongo, ugabanya amafaranga y’ugucuruza On-Chain, ukirinda igihe co guhagarara kubera gusubira gukorana n’imirongo, kandi ugakomeza ubushobozi bwo gukoresha inzira buhamye kugira ngo ushobore kwishura ata nkomanzi. Ubwo buryo buratuma habaho urubuga rukomeye kandi rukomeye mu gihe rwongera ubumenyi bw’abakoresha muri rusangi no kugabanya amafaranga y’ibikorwa.
