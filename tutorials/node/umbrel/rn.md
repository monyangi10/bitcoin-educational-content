## Imenyekanisha



### Node ya Bitcoin ni iki?



Node ya Bitcoin ni mudasobwa igira uruhara mu rubuga rwa Bitcoin ikoresheje porogarama ya Bitcoin core canke uwundi mukiriya. Uruhara rwayo ni ngirakamaro ku mikorere n’umutekano w’urubuga:





- Ububiko bwa Blockchain**: Bubungabunga kopi yuzuye kandi igezweho y’igitabu ca Blockchain Bitcoin.
- Igenzura ry'ibikorwa**: ryemeza ibikorwa vyose n'ububiko hakurikijwe amategeko y'amasezerano
- Gukwiragiza amakuru**: Gusangira amafaranga mashasha n'amabuye n'izindi nzira
- Ukwubaka amasezerano**: Gufasha mu gushitsa amategeko y'urubuga



Gukoresha node yawe bwite ya Bitcoin ni intambwe ihambaye cane mu bijanye n’ubusegaba bw’ivy’ubutunzi, itanga inyungu nyinshi z’ingenzi:





- Ibanga**: Gusangira amafaranga yawe utamenyesheje abandi
- Uguhangana n’ugucengera**: Nta n’umwe ashobora kukubuza gukoresha Bitcoin.
- Igenzura ryigenga**: Ntibikenewe kwizigira ama node y'abandi kugira ngo ugenzure amafaranga yawe
- Ukwubaka amasezerano**: Gutanga umusanzu mu gushitsa amategeko y'urubuga rwa Bitcoin
- Infashanyo y'urubuga**: Ba umunyamuryango w'umunyamwete mu gukwiragiza urubuga no kwegereza ubutegetsi



### Umutaka: Umuti woroshe wo gukoresha urudodo rwa Bitcoin



Umbrel ni ubuhinga bwo gukoresha bufise inkomoko yuguruye bworosha gushiramwo no gucunga node ya Bitcoin. Ihindura kandi mudasobwa yawe ikaba server yo mu nzu y'umuntu ku giti ciwe n'iy'umuntu ku giti ciwe, bikaba vyoroshe kwakira :





- Igipande cuzuye ca Bitcoin
- Bitcoin ibikorwa vy'ingenzi (amashanyarazi, Mempool.ikirere)
- Ibindi bikorwa vy'umuntu ku giti ciwe (ububiko bwo mu gicu, gukurikirana, VPN, n'ibindi)



N’umukoresha wayo w’agaciro kandi woroshe Interface Interface, Umbrel ituma ibikorwa vy’ukwikorera bishikira bose, mu gihe igumya ububasha bwose ku makuru yawe.

### Inzu y'umutaka: Umuti w'urufunguzo



Umbrel Home ni server yo mu nzu yatunganijwe imbere y’igihe, yateguwe cane cane kugira ngo ukoreshe neza. Uyu muti w'ibikoresho vyose muri kimwe urimwo:



**Ibirango vy'ibikoresho**




- Prosesseur ikora neza cane yatunganijwe kugira ngo yiyakire
- Ububiko bwa SSD bwihuta cane bwashizweho mbere
- Uburyo bwo gukonjesha butekanye
- Igishushanyo gikomeye, ciza cane
- Ivyuma vya USB na Ethernet vyinjijwemwo



**Ivyiza vyihariye**




- Gushiramwo-n'ugukina: gushiramwo maze ugende
- Infashanyo nziza n'imfashanyo yihariye
- Ivyemezo vy'ivugurura ry'ubwo nyene
- Umupfumu w'ukwimuka yunze ubumwe
- Garanti yuzuye y'ibikoresho
- Infashanyo yuzuye ku mikorere yose



**Igiciro**: €399 (igiciro gishobora guhinduka bivanye n'ibihe)

## Gukurikirana ubucuruzi na Mempool.space



Mempool.space ni igikoresho co gutohoza Blockchain gifise inkomoko yuguruye gitanga amashusho y’igihe nyaco y’urubuga rwa Bitcoin. Bigufasha gukurikirana amafaranga yawe no gutahura ingene amafaranga akwiragizwa ku rubuga.



### Gutahura Mempool n'ivyemezo



"Mempool" (memory pool) ni nk'icumba co kurindiriramwo c'ukuri aho amafaranga yose ya Bitcoin ataremejwe abikwa imbere y'uko ashirwa mu gice. Ehe ingene amafaranga akoreshwa:



1. **Itangaza**: Iyo wohereje amafaranga, abanza gutangazwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin .


2. **Kurindira muri Mempool**: Kurindira gutorwa n’umu Miner bivanye n’ibiciro


3. **Ivyemezo vya mbere**: Umwana muto abishira mu gice (Ivyemezo vya mbere)


4. **Ibindi vyemezo**: Buri bubiko bushasha bucukurwa inyuma y’iryo ririmwo amafaranga yawe bwongerako ivyemezo



Umubare w'ivyemezo vyemewe uvana n'umubare :




- Ku mahera make: 1-2 vyemezo bishobora guhagije
- Ku mafaranga menshi: Ivyemezo 6 muri rusangi bifatwa ko bitekanye cane



### Gutahura Interface kuva kuri Mempool.ikirere



1. **Urupapuro rw'intango** ruguha icegeranyo c'urubuga rwa Bitcoin:




   - Amabuye aherutse gucukurwa
   - Ibiciro vy'ibiciro vy'ibintu bitandukanye bibanza
   - Ivyo Mempool iriko irakora ubu



![Page d'accueil de Mempool.space avec visualisation des blocs et estimations de frais](assets/fr/23.webp)



2. **Rondera amafaranga**: Kugira ngo ukurikirane amafaranga yihariye, shira ikimenyetso cayo (txid) mu kabati k’ugushaka kari hejuru kuri paji.



![Recherche d'une transaction dans Mempool.space](assets/fr/24.webp)



### Gusuzuma ido n'ido ry'ibikorwa



Igihe amafaranga yawe amaze kuboneka, Mempool.space iraguha isesengura ryuzuye:



1. **Amakuru y'ingenzi** :




   - Uko bimeze (vyemejwe canke bitarivyo)
   - Amafaranga yishuwe (mu Sats/vB)
   - Igihe co kwemeza kigereranywa



![Détails des frais et statut de la transaction](assets/fr/25.webp)



2. **Imiterere y'ubucuruzi** :




   - Igaragara ry'ibishirwamwo n'ibisohoka
   - Urutonde rw'amaderesi arimwo
   - Amafaranga yimuriwe



3. **Amakuru y'ubuhinga** :




   - Uburemere bw'ugucuruza
   - Ingano y'ibintu
   - Uburyo na verisiyo vyakoreshejwe
   - Ayandi makuru yihariye



![Informations techniques et structure des entrées/sorties](assets/fr/26.webp)



### Inyungu zo gukoresha Mempool.space ku Mutaka



1. **Ibanga**: Ivyo usaba bica mu nzira yawe bwite


2. **Ukwikukira**: Nta nkenerwa yo kwizigira uwundi muntu


3. **Ukwizigirwa**: Ushobora gushika ku makuru n’igihe ama browser ya bose adakora



Ukoresheje iyi porogarama, urashobora gukurikirana neza amafaranga yawe, ugatahura ingene amafaranga agira ico akoze ku muvuduko wo kwemeza, kandi ugatahura neza ingene urubuga rwa Bitcoin rukora.

- Ukwiherera ariko buhoro
