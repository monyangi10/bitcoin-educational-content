## Nostr ni iki?


Nostr ni umurongo ufise ububasha bwo gusubirira Twitter, Telegram, n’izindi mbuga zo gusangirirako amakuru. Ni umurongo woroshe wuguruye ushobora kurema urubuga rw’imigenderanire rushobora guhangana n’isi yose rimwe rizima.


## None bigenda gute?


Nostr ishingiye ku bice bitatu: ababiri b’ingenzi, abaguzi, n’abasubiza inyuma.


Buri mukoresha afise akaranga kamwe canke kenshi, kandi akaranga kose kagenwa n’urufunguzo rw’ibanga.


Kugira ngo ushikire urubuga, ukeneye gukoresha porogarama y’abaguzi no kwifatanya n’ibikoresho vy’ugutanga amakuru kugira ngo wakire no gutanga ibirimwo.


![Key system](assets/2.webp)


## 1. Imfunguruzo z'ibanga


Udakunze Facebook canke Twitter, aho abakoresha bategerezwa gutanga email Address n’amakuru menshi cane ku kigo c’abikorera ku giti cabo, Nostr ikora ata buyobozi bukuru. Abakoresha generate urufunguzo rw’ibanga, urufunguzo rw’ibanga (ruzwi kandi nk’urufunguzo rw’ibanga), n’urufunguzo rwa bose.


Urufunguzo rw’ibanga, nsec, ruzwi n’uwurukoresha gusa, rukoreshwa mu kwemeza no gutangaza ibirimwo.


Urufunguzo rwa bose, npub, ni ikimenyetso kidasanzwe aho ibirimwo vyose vyasohowe n'ukoresha bifatanywa. Urufunguzo rwawe rwa bose ni nk’izina ry’ukoresha rituma abandi bakoresha bakuronka no kwiyandikisha ku rubuga rwawe rwa Nostr.


## 2. Abaguzi


Abaguzi ni porogarama zishobora gutuma umuntu akorana na Nostr. Abaguzi nyamukuru ni:



- iOS: umuvyeyi
- Android: ibuye ry'agaciro
- Urubuga: iris.ku; gusonza.imibano; inyenyeri.ninja


Abaguzi bemerera abakoresha generate urufunguzo rushasha (rungana no gukora konti) canke kwemeza urufunguzo ruriho.


## 3. Ivyiyumviro


Relays ni ama servers yoroshe ushobora guheba igihe cose iyo udakunda ivyo bagushikanako. Ushobora kandi gukoresha relay yawe iyo ubishaka.


💡 **Impanuro:** Ama relays yishurwa muri rusangi arakora neza cane mu kuyungurura spam n’ibintu bidakenewe.


## Ubuyobozi


Ubu urazi bihagije kuri Nostr kugira utangure no kurema akaranga kawe ka mbere kuri iyi protocole.


Kubera intumbero z’iyi nkuru, tuzokoresha iris.to (https://iris.to/) nk’uko uyu mukiriya w’urubuga akora ku rubuga urwo ari rwo rwose.


## Intambwe ya 1: Gutanga imfunguruzo


ris izogukorera urutonde rw'imfunguruzo ataco ubwirizwa gukora kirenze kwinjiza izina (ry'ukuri canke ry'ibinyoma) ku rubuga rwawe. Hanyuma ukande kuri GO uraheza!


![Main menu](assets/3.webp)


⚠️ **Attention!** Uzokenera kuguma ukurikirana imfunguruzo zawe nimba ushaka gusubira kuronka profile yawe iyo session yawe imaze gufunga. Nzokwereka ingene ivyo wobikora ku mpera nyene y’iki gitabu.


## Intambwe ya 2: Gutangaza ibirimwo


Gusohora ibirimwo, biroroshe kandi birashoboka nk’ukwandika amajambo makeyi mu kibanza co gusohora.


![Publication](assets/4.webp)


Ni ho rero! Mwasohoye inyandiko yanyu ya mbere ku bijanye na Nostr.


![Post](assets/5.webp)


## Intambwe ya 3: Rondera umugenzi


Nsanga kuri Nostr ntuzosubire kuba wenyene. Nzosubira gukora subscribe ku muntu wese azoba subscribe kuri feed yanje. Kugira ngo ubikore, winjize gusa urufunguzo rwanje rwa bose


mu murongo w'ubushakashatsi.


![My profile](assets/6.webp)


Fyonda kuri "follow" mu misi mikeyi cane, nanje nzoba subscribe ku feed yawe. Tuzoba abagenzi. Nanje nzonezerwa no gusoma ubutumwa bwawe nimba ushaka kunyandikira.


Ubwa nyuma, urabe ko kandi wiyandikishije ku rubuga rwa Agora256 kugira ngo uronke akamenyetso igihe cose dusohoye ikintu gishasha: npub1ag0rawstycy7nanuc6sz4v287rneen2yapcq3fd06972f8ncrhzqx


## Intambwe ya 4: Guhindura umwirondoro wawe


Uracari n’igikorwa co gukora kugira ngo uhindure urutonde rwawe. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku gicapo c'ibara ry'umubiri kigukoreye mu mfuruka yo hejuru iburyo bw'ibarabara hanyuma ukande kuri "edit profile".


![Profile](assets/7.webp)


Ico ubwirizwa gukora ubu ni ukubwira iris aho wosanga ishusho yawe n’ibendera ryawe ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi. Ndagusavye kwakira ibirimwo vyawe bwite: ukinge ivyo ari ivyawe.


![Another option](assets/8.webp)


Niba ushaka, urashobora kandi gushiramwo amashusho, azokubikwa na iris kuri nostr.build, igikorwa co kwakira ibintu vy’amashusho ku buntu ca Nostr.


Nk'uko ushobora kubibona, urashobora kandi gutunganya umukiriya wawe kugira ngo ashobore kwakira no kohereza Sats. Uko ni ko, ushobora guhemba abanditsi b’ibintu wakunze canke, mbere n’ikiruta, ukarundanya Sats ku bintu vyiza uzosohora.


## Intambwe ya 5: Gusubiza inyuma urufunguzo rubiri


Iyi ntambwe ni ngirakamaro cane nimba ushaka kuguma ubona urutonde rwawe umaze gusohoka mu mukiriya canke igihe cawe kirangiye.

Mbere, fyonda ku kimenyetso ca "imiterere" kigereranywa n'ivyuma

![Setting](assets/9.webp)


Hanyuma, ukope npub yawe, npub hex, nsec, na nsec hex imwe imwe muri dosiye y’inyandiko uzozigama. Ndabagira inama yo gupfuka iyi dosiye nimba muzi uko muyikora.


![Key](assets/10.webp)


⚠️ **Iyumvire imburi iris iguha:** naho ushobora gusangira urufunguzo rwawe rwa bose ata bwoba, ni inkuru itandukanye ku rufunguzo rwawe rw’ibanga. Uwufise iyo nyuma azoshobora gushika kuri konti yawe.


## Iciyumviro


Ni ho ugenda, mbuni ntoyi, warateye intambwe zawe za mbere kuri Nostr. Ubu, uzokenera kwiga kwiruka nk’umuravyo. Tuzosohora vuba uburongozi buzokwereka ingene wocungera imfunguruzo zawe n’ingene woshiramwo imiravyo mu bumenyi bwawe bwa Nostr ukoresheje getalby.
