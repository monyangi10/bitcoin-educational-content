## Imenyekanisha



[LN+ (Lightning Network Plus)](https://umuravyo.plus/) ni urubuga rw’abanyagihugu rwagenewe kworohereza ubufatanye hagati y’abakoresha ama node ya Lightning Network. Intumbero yayo nyamukuru ni ugutuma uruja n’uruza rw’urubuga rwa Lightning rushobora gukorana neza, rukagira amahera menshi kandi rukagira ubushobozi bwo gukorana n’abandi, ata n’umwe akeneye abahuza bahurikiye hamwe.



Iyi nyigisho izoshimika ku **"Swaps "** service, ariyo ikoreshwa cane kandi itunganya ibintu vya LN+ muri iki gihe. Ibindi bikorwa bitangwa n’iyo nzira na vyo nyene bizokwerekanwa.



## LN+ incamake



### Lightning Network Plus ni iki?



Lightning Network Plus (LN+) ni urubuga rw’abanyagihugu rw’abakoresha ama node ya Lightning bipfuza gukorana ataco baciyeko kandi ku bushake. Igamije kworohereza uguhingura imirongo y’umuravyo ngirakamaro, iri ku rugero kandi iramba, mu gihe umuntu yirinda gukenera inyishu zihuriweko canke ibibanza bitegekanijwe.



LN+ ishingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako y’ishimikiro: ubufatanye hagati y’urunganwe, bushingiye ku guseruka, gusubizanya no kumenyekana.



### Ibikorwa vya LN+ mu kanya nk'ako gukubita urugohe



LN+ itanga ibikorwa vyinshi vyagenewe gutuma urubuga rwa Lightning rutera imbere, harimwo :





- Swaps**: gufungura imihora hagati y’abakoresha (service principal).
- Rings**: imirongo y’uruziga yuguruye hagati y’abaje mu nama benshi.
- Ivyiyumviro bishingiye ku kwizigira**: ibihinduka bishingiye cane ku kwizigira n’amateka y’abaje mu nama.
- Ibirango vy’imibano**: imirongo, ibitekerezo n’uburyo bwo kumenyekana.



Mu bice bisigaye vy'iyi nyigisho, tuzokwibanda cane ku **Swaps** service, ari yo iri mu mutima w'ikoreshwa rya LN+ ubu.



## LN+ "Guhindura" serivisi



### Insobanuro y'uguhindura LN+



LN+ **swap** ni amasezerano y’ubushake hagati y’abakozi babiri b’imirongo y’umuravyo kugira ngo bafungure imirongo ya Lightning y’ubushobozi bungana (canke bemeranije imbere y’igihe). Udakunze ugufungura umurongo w’uruhande rusanzwe, uguhinduranya gushingiye ku **ugusubizanya gutomoye**.



Mu bikorwa :





- Ufungura umurongo ku node y’uwo mukundana.
- Uwo mufatanije arafungura umurongo ku nzira yawe.
- Izo mpande zompi ziriko ziraboha umubare nk’uwo w’ama bitcoins ya on-chain.



### Ni igiki gifise intumbero y’uguhinduranya abakoresha ama node?



Iryo shirahamwe ryitwa Swaps rivuga ku bibazo vyinshi vy’ingenzi bihura n’abakoresha Lightning:





- Uguhuza neza**: guhingura imihora y’ingirakamaro y’inzira zibiri igihe nyene ifunguwe.
- Gucungera neza amafaranga**: umuhari wose ufise ubushobozi bwo kwinjira n’ugusohoka.
- Kugabanya ingorane z’imihora idakenewe**: uwo bafatanya araremeshwa kuguma yuguruye iyo mihora.
- Ukwegereza ubutegetsi abaturage cane**: amasano ataco akora hagati y’abakoresha, ata nzira zitegekanijwe.



### Ni ku mirongo iyihe y’imirongo swaps ifise akamaro?



Guhinduranya ni ngirakamaro cane kuri :





- Ivyuma bishasha vyipfuza gutera imbere ningoga mu bijanye n’uguca mu nzira.
- Abakozi b’ubuhuza barondera kwongerera uburemere bw’igishushanyo c’imirongo yabo.
- Ivyuma vyerekeye inzira bishaka gutuma topologie yavyo igenda neza.



## Huza urudodo rwawe rw'umuravyo na LN+



### Ibisabwa mu vy'ubuhinga



Imbere y'uko utangura, uzokenera :





- Igikoresho c’Umuravyo gikora (LND, Umuravyo w’Ishingiro canke Eclair).
- Ugushika ku nzira y'uburongozi bw'uruzitiro rwawe.
- Ubushobozi buhagije bwa on-chain bwo gufungura imihora.

## Kugira uruhara mu guhinduranya biriho



### Ugushika ku guhindura amashimwe



Kugira ngo ushiremwo uruhara mu gufungura umurongo wawe wa mbere, genda kuri `Swaps` iri hejuru y'urubuga. Aha uzosanga ama swaps yose ariho ubu, bivanye n'ibiranga node yawe.



![capture](assets/fr/10.webp)



### Ibisabwa vyo kwemererwa



Kugira wifatanye n'isoko ry'uguhindura risanzweho, uhitemwo gusa itangazo rihuye maze wiyandikishe. Ariko rero, LN+ iremeza umuhinguzi w'ihinduka gusobanura **ivyangombwa bimwebimwe vyo kwemererwa**, nka :





- umubare mutoyi w'imirongo isanzwe yuguruye ;
- ubushobozi bukeyi bwose bw'urudodo ;
- ubwoko bumwe bumwe bw’uguhuza bwemewe.



### Ivyiyumviro biherutse



Nk'inyishu, cane cane mu ntango z'ikoreshwa, birashoboka ko **ibihembo bike canke atavyo biboneka** ku nzira yawe. Ivyo ni ibintu bisanzwe ku nzira nshasha canke zitarahuzwa.



Ku bijanye nanje, naho hariho imihora mikeyi yuguruye, nta n’imwe mu vyo batanga yari ihuye n’ivyo bisabwa.



## Rema ivyo utanga



### Ni ryari ukwiye kwiremera swap yawe?



Iyo ata n’imwe iriho, kwihingurira swap yawe ni co kintu ciza kuruta ibindi vyose. Bigufasha kandi kuguma ugenzura amaparametere y’ivyo guhindura.



### Guhindura imiterere



Fyonda kuri **Tangira Liquidity Swap**, hanyuma utunganye amaparametere y'ivyo utanga:





- guhitamwo umubare wose w’abazoja muri iyo nama (3, 4 canke 5);
- kwerekana ubushobozi bw’imihora izofungurwa;
- gusobanura igihe c’ukwiyemeza abaje mu nama bemera kudafunga imihora yabo;
- gusobanura amategeko yose ashobora gukoreshwa ku bari muri iyo nama (umubare mutoyi w’imirongo, ubushobozi bukeyi bwose, ubwoko bw’ihuriro ryemewe).



![capture](assets/fr/11.webp)



### Gusohora n'ivyo abaje mu nama biteze



Ivyo vyose bimaze kwinjira, fyonda ku **Start Liquidity Swap** kugira ngo utangaze ivyo utanga. None ivyo ubwirizwa gukora n’ugutegerezwa ko abandi ba operateur bakwiyandikisha.



![capture](assets/fr/12.webp)



## Gusozera guhindura

Ubu ko ivyicaro vyose vy’uguhinduranya vyafashwe, umwe wese mu bari muri iyo nama arashobora kubona, akoresheje interface yiwe ya LN+, ni uruhe rudodo akeneye gufungurirako umurongo wa Lightning.



![capture](assets/fr/13.webp)



Ku ruhande rwawe, fungura umurongo ukoresheje Node ID yatanzwe na LN+ kandi wubahe umubare werekanwa. Ivyo bishobora gukorwa biciye kuri ThunderHub, uwundi muyobozi w’uruzitiro rwa Lightning canke biciye ku nzira y’ishimikiro y’uruzitiro rwawe.



![capture](assets/fr/14.webp)



Iyo umaze gufungura, uwo muhora uraboneka mu gice c’imirongo irindiriye.



![capture](assets/fr/15.webp)

Hanyuma usubire kuri LN+ kugira ngo wemeze ko watanguye gufungura umurongo, ukanda kuri buto `Ugufungura umurongo kwatanguye`.



![capture](assets/fr/16.webp)



### Iherezo ry'uguhanahana



Iyo abaje bose bamaze gufungura imihora bari biyemeje, iyo swap ifatwa ko yuzuye.



## Izina ryiza n'ingeso nziza zo kuvugana n'abandi



### Uburyo bwo kumenyekana bwa LN+



LN+ ishiramwo uburyo bwo kumenyekana bushingiye ku vyiyumviro vyasizwe n’abaje mu nama ku mpera y’uguhinduranya. Ivyo vyiyumviro ni ivy’abantu bose kandi biragira ico bikoze ku bushobozi bw’umukoresha bwo kwifatanya canke guhingura amahinduka yo muri kazoza.



![capture](assets/fr/17.webp)



### Ivyiza vy'ibikorwa



Kugira ngo umuntu azigame izina ryiza kandi ashobore gukora neza, birakenewe ko :





- kwubahiriza igihe co gufungura imihora ;
- kuvugana n’abandi ningoga iyo habaye ingorane canke iyo umuntu acerewe;
- gukoresha igice c’ibitekerezo kugira ngo usangire ivyiyumviro n’abandi bari muri iyo nama;
- kudafunga umuhora imbere y’uko igihe c’ukwiyemeza gihera.




![capture](assets/fr/18.webp)



### Kubera iki izina ryiza ari ryo shingiro rya LN+



LN+ ishingiye ku citegererezo c’ubufatanye bw’ubushake, ata ngorane zikomeye z’ubuhinga. Kumenyekana rero ni co kintu nyamukuru gituma abaje mu nama bashobora kwizigirwa kandi bakizigirwa.



## Ibindi bikorwa vya LN+



Uretse Swaps, LN+ itanga izindi serivisi zigenewe gutuma habaho uguhuza n’uguhuza hagati y’abakoresha ama node ya Lightning. Impeta** zituma abaje mu nama benshi bashobora gukora uruzitiro rw’ibifungurwa vy’imirongo, gutyo bikaba bikomeza ugusubira inyuma kw’inzira z’inzira n’uburemere bw’igishushanyo c’umuravyo. Ivyiyumviro bishingiye ku kwizigira** bishingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako zisa n’iz’ivyigwa vya kera, ariko bitanga uburenganzira bwo guhindura ibintu ku bari muri iyo nama bamaze kuronka izina ryiza kuri iyo nzira.



LN+ kandi ishiramwo ibintu vy’imibano nk’imirongo y’imirongo ya bose, ibivugwa vyo guhinduranya n’uburyo bwo kumenyekana. Ivyo bikoresho si ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga ubwavyo, ariko birafise uruhara runini mu gutuma urubuga rugenda neza, bikaba vyorohereza uguhanahana amakuru, uguhuza n’ukwizigirana hagati y’abakoresha.



## Iciyumviro



Serivisi ya LN+ ya Swaps ni igikoresho ciza co gutuma habaho uguhuza, ugutanga amahera n’ugusenyura ubutegetsi mu rubuga rwa Lightning. Mu guteza imbere ugufungura imirongo y’ubuhinga, LN+ irashoboza abakoresha ama node gukomeza ubuhinga bwabo, mu gihe nyene itera imbere ubufatanye bufise inshingano kandi bushingiye ku nzego zitandukanye.
