Naho Bitcoin ibonwa canecane nk’uburyo bwo kuzigama muri iki gihe, iguma ari ifaranga ry’agaciro kari mu bushobozi bwaryo bwo guca mu kiganza kuva ku kuboko kuja ku kindi. Nimba abacuruzi bari hafi yawe bemeye Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura, urafise uburenganzira bwo kuyikoresha ataco uhinduye. Ikibabaje ni uko uwo mugenzo utarakwiragira, kandi benshi mu bacuruzi ntibarakira BTC mu buryo bw’akavukire.


Umuti mwiza usigaye ni "orange pill" abacuruzi bo mu karere kugira ngo bemere Bitcoin, mu kubasigurira ivyiza vy'iyi nzira. Ushobora gukoresha inyigisho ziboneka mu gice ca ["*abacuruzi*"](https://planb.network/inyigisho/umucuruzi) co ku rubuga rwacu nk'infashanyo. Iryo tegeko rishobora gukora neza ku bacuruzi batobato bigenga, ariko rigaragara ko rigoye cane ku minyororo minini y’igihugu, kuko muri rusangi abo bahuriye ku kibanza atari bo bafata ingingo ku bijanye n’uburyo bwo kwishura.


Mu gihe urindiriye ko abantu benshi bavyemera n’izo nzira, urashobora gukoresha amahera yawe ukoresheje uburyo bwo kwishura: amakarata y’ingabirano. Kandi ivyo nivyo nyene Bitrefill itanga kugira ngo ikuronse.


## Bitrefill ni iki?


Bitrefill ni urubuga rugufasha kugura amakarita y’ingabirano ukoresheje amafaranga y’ibiceri. Iciyumviro ni ugukoresha ayo makarita y’ingabirano kugira ngo ugure ku mucuruzi uhisemwo, gutyo ukagufasha kuronka ibintu n’ibikorwa ukoresheje amahera y’ibiceri, naho uwo mudandaza yoba atavyemera ataco akora. Nk’umunya Bitcoin, ivyo bigufasha gukingira ingorane y’ukwemerwa kwa BTC n’abadandaza ba kera n’iminyororo minini. Ikindi kandi, biragufasha gusesa amafaranga yawe y’ama bitcoins ataco uciye ku rubuga rwa Exchange rusaba ko umuntu asuzuma akaranga kawe (KYC).


Bitrefill itanga amahitamwo menshi cane kuva ku gucuruza gushika ku vyo kurya vyihuta, harimwo n’ibibanza vyo gukurikirana amakuru, imikino yo kuri internet, imbuga z’ubudandaji, mbere n’ugushiramwo amahera kuri telefone.

![BITREFILL](assets/notext/01.webp)

Bitrefill ni ikintu coroshe cane gukoresha. Kugira ngo ufungure konti, ukeneye gusa gutanga imeyili Address. Rero, ntukeneye gutanga akaranga kawe. Hanyuma, uhitamwo gusa ubwoko bw’ikarata y’ingabirano, ugahitamwo amahera yayo, hanyuma ugatanga amahera mu bitcoins. Igihe ukwishyura kwemejwe (hafi ubwo nyene ukoresheje Lightning), kode y’ikarita y’ingabirano iraguhabwa. Ivyo bishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo bw’umubiri mu maduka canke kuri Internet ku rubuga rw’umucuruzi.
