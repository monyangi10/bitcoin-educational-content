Kuva yatangura gukoreshwa mu 2009, ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin bwo gutanga amahera mu buryo bw’ubuhinga bwa none bwarateye imbere cane, bugatanga ubuzima ku mirongo y’inyishu ubu butuma buba uburyo dushobora gukoresha ubwo nyene mu bikorwa vyacu vya misi yose, cane cane biciye kuri Lightning Network.



Ariko rero, hari ingorane ikomeye yasigaye hagati y’ibice vy’amasezerano ya Bitcoin: ugukorana kw’amazi. Kugira ngo ubushobozi bwose bwa Bitcoin bukoreshwe, vyari ngombwa ko haronswa umuti wotuma haba ugucuruza hagati y’ibice bitandukanye vy’amasezerano. Ivyo vyatumye mu 2019 habaho ikiraro ca Boltz, ikiraro gihuza ibice vyinshi vya Bitcoin.



## Boltz ni iki?



[Boltz](https://boltz.Exchange) ni urubuga rudashobora gukoreshwa n’abantu bose yipfuza gukorana hagati y’ibice bitandukanye vy’amasezerano ya Bitcoin:




- on chain**: Uruhererekane rwa Bitcoin aho amafaranga yemezwa buri minota 10 ku rugero rwa mwayeni, amafaranga y’amafaranga akenshi aba ari menshi, ivyo bikaba bidashobora gushitsa ivyo abakoresha bakeneye ;
- Lightning Network**: Igipfukisho ca Bitcoin co kwishura ubwo nyene ku mahera make, kigatuma Bitcoin ikoreshwa mu kwishura ku musi ku musi;
- Liquid Network**: igipfukisho ca Bitcoin cakozwe na Blockstream, gishoboza kwihuta, Confidential Transactions n’ugukoresha ibindi bikoresho vy’ivy’ubutunzi bishingiye kuri Bitcoin;
- RootStock**: Umuti wo gutegura amasezerano y’ubwenge ashingiye ku masezerano ya Bitcoin.



![layers](assets/fr/01.webp)



Ugukorana hagati y’izo nzira zitandukanye ni ikintu gihambaye cane, kuko biha abakoresha uburenganzira bwo guhindura ibintu bakeneye kugira ngo bashobore kwungukira ku kintu cose ibidukikije vya Bitcoin bishobora gutanga.



Boltz akoresha ubuhinga bwo guhindura atome. Iryo koraniro rituma bitcoins zishobora guhindurwa hagati y’ibice 2 (e.g. BTC onchain muri Exchange ku BTC kuri Lightning) ataco zihinduye hagati y’abantu babiri, ata kwizigirana kandi ata n’umwe akeneye guhuza. Ivyo biganiro vyitwa "atome" kuko bishobora gutanga ibisubizo bibiri gusa:




- Canke Exchange iraroranirwa kandi abo babiri bari muri iyo nama barahinduye neza BTC yabo ;
- Canke Exchange irananirwa, abo bose bari muri iyo nama bakagenda bafise BTC yabo y’intango.



Muri ubwo buryo, uguma ufise uburenganzira bwo kwizigama ubudasiba ku ma bitcoins yawe, kandi Exchange ntishingiye ku kwizigira uwo mugenziwe: canke Exchange iraroranirwa canke irananirwa, ariko nta n’umwe muri bo ashobora kwiba amahera y’uwundi.



Exchange y’atome ikorana n’amasezerano y’ubwenge [HTLC] (*Contract*). Muri ubwo bwoko bwa Contract, amafaranga "arakingirwa" mu murongo w'inzira zibiri kandi hakaba hariho igihe co guhagarika, ku buryo iyo igikorwa kitarangiye mu kiringo kinaka, amafaranga asigaye asubira ku wubitse. Ubwo ni bwo buryo bukoreshwa n’urubuga rwa Boltz.



## Ivyo mwaganiriye ubwa mbere na Boltz



Boltz ni urubuga rwo ku rubuga rudafise amakuru y’ibanga rudasaba. Boltz ifise Interface y’agaciro gatoyi, igenda neza, ishobora kugufasha gutangura gucuruza mu munota umwe gusa.



![boltz](assets/fr/02.webp)



Iyo umaze ku rubuga, urashobora gukora ubuhinga bwo guhinduranya atome hagati y’ibice bitandukanye vy’ibidukikije vya Bitcoin.



![home](assets/fr/03.webp)



Uzobona umubare mutoyi n’umunini w’ama satoshis (igice gitoyi cane ca Bitcoin) ushobora gucuruza uciye kuri Boltz, harimwo amafaranga y’urubuga n’ijanisha ry’ijanisha Boltz isaba hagati ya 0.1% na 0.5%.



![fees](assets/fr/04.webp)



Hanyuma uhitemwo Layer wipfuza gukoresha Exchange y’atome, hanyuma uhitemwo Layer wipfuza kwakirako ama bitcoins.



![couches](assets/fr/05.webp)



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba twibanda ku atome Exchange kuva kuri Layer gushika kuri Lightning Network.



Ushobora gutunganya igice c'ishimikiro c'ivyo uhindura uhisemwo hagati y'amahitamwo :




- BTC ;
- Sats.
