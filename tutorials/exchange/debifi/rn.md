## Imenyekanisha


Muri iyi nyigisho ya video y’intambwe ku yindi, uzobona ingene ukoresha Debifi kugira ngo ufungure agaciro ka BTC yawe biciye ku ngurane ishigikiwe na Bitcoin. Uzomenya ingene wokora konti, ukayikingira na 2FA, ugaca mu bihembo, mbere ugakoresha Coldcard Q mu gutegura Multisig kugira ngo umenye neza ko Bitcoin yawe idashobora gusubira gukoreshwa.


Waba uriko uratanga amahera y’ubudandaji, uriko urahangana n’ibintu vyihutirwa, canke ushaka gusa amahera y’igihe gito ataco utera—iyi nkuru iraguha ibikoresho vyo kubikora neza.
