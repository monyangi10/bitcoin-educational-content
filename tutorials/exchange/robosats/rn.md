## Imbere y'uko utangura


### Ibintu ukwiye kumenya


| Jargon       | Definition                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Robot        | Your automatically generated private trade identity. Do not re-use the same robot more than once as this can degrade your privacy.                                                             |
| Token        | A string of random characters used to generate your unique robot.                                                                                                                              |
| Maker        | A user who creates an offer to buy or sell Bitcoin.                                                                                                                                            |
| Taker        | A user who takes another user up on their offer to buy or sell Bitcoin.                                                                                                                        |
| Bond         | An amount of Bitcoin locked up by both peers as a pledge to play fair and complete their part of the trade. Bonds are typically 3% of the total trade amount and are powered by Hodl Invoices. |
| Trade Escrow | Used by the seller as a method of holding the trade amount of Bitcoin, again using Hodl Invoices.                                                                                              |
| Fees         | RoboSats charges 0.2% of the trade amount, which is split between both maker and taker. The taker pays 0.175% and the maker pays 0.025%.                                                       |

## Ibintu ukeneye kugira


### Umuravyo Wallet.


RoboSats ni umuyagankuba, rero uzokenera umuravyo Wallet kugira ngo ushobore gutanga amahera y’ingwati kandi uronke Sats yaguzwe nk’umuguzi. Ukwiye kwitwararika igihe uhitamwo Wallet yawe, kubera ubuhinga bukoreshwa kugira ngo RoboSats zikore, si zose zihuye.


Nimba uri umunyarugamba, Zewu ni we mahitamwo meza kuruta ayandi yose. Niba udafise node yawe bwite, ndagusavye cane Phoenix, telefone ngendanwa y’umusaraba Wallet ifise imiterere yoroshe n’ugushika ku Lightning. Phoenix ni yo yakoreshejwe mu gukora iyo nkuru.


### Hariho Bitcoin.


Abaguzi n’abagurisha barakeneye gutanga amahera y’ingwati imbere y’uko ubudandaji ubwo ari bwo bwose bushobora kubaho. Ivyo akenshi ni amahera make cane (~3% y’amahera y’ubudandaji), ariko ni ikintu gikenewe naho biri ukwo.


Ukoresheje RoboSats kugira ugure Sats yawe ya mbere? Kubera iki utoronka umugenzi ngo akugurize amahera makeyi asabwa kugira ngo utangure!? Niba uriko uraguruka wenyene, ng’izi izindi nzira nziza zo kuronka noKYC Sats kugira ngo utangure.


### Ukwigera kuri RoboSats


Biragaragara ko uzokenera gushika ku RoboSats! Hari uburyo bune nyamukuru wobigiramwo:


1. Biciye kuri Tor Mucukumbuzi (Ni vyiza!)

2. Biciye ku rubuga rusanzwe (Ntivyiza!)

3. Biciye kuri APK ya Android

4. Umukiriya wawe bwite


Nimba uri mushasha mu mucukumbuzi wa Tor, menya vyinshi maze uyikureko [hano](https://www.torproject.org/kuyikureko/).


Ikete ryihuse ku bakoresha iOS barondera gushika kuri RoboSats biciye kuri Tor bavuye kuri telefone zabo. ‘Umucukumbuzi w’igitunguru’ si Umucukumbuzi wa Tor. Ahubwo ukoreshe Orbot + Safari na Orbot + Inka y’Inda.

### Gukora amategeko


Uretse no gufata ivyo uwundi muntu aguhaye, urashobora kwiremera ivyawe maze ugategerezwa ko uwundi Robot aza kuri wewe.



- Gufungura urupapuro rwo Rema.
- Sigura nimba itegeko ryawe ari ugugura canke kugurisha Bitcoin.
- Injira umubare n’amahera ushaka kugura/kugurisha.
- Injira uburyo bwo kwishura ushaka gukoresha.
- Injira ‘Igiciro c’Isoko’ % wifuza kwemera. Zirikana ko iki gishobora kuba igiharuro kibi co gutanga igiciro ku giciro gitoya vs kuruta igiciro c’isoko kiriho ubu.
- Fyonda Rema Urutonde.
- Ishura Umuravyo Invoice kugira ngo ufunge Bond yawe y’Umuremyi.
- Itegeko ryawe ubu ririko rikora. Icara wicare urindire umuntu azovyemera.

### On-Chain Ivyishyurwa


RoboSats ni umuravyo, ariko abaguzi barafise uburenganzira bwo kwakira Sats yabo ku On-Chain Bitcoin Address. Abaguzi barashobora guhitamwo iyo nzira bamaze gufunga imbogo zabo. Amaze guhitamwo On-Chain, uwuguze azobona icegeranyo c’amahera azotanga. Amafaranga y’inyongera y’iyi serivisi ni:



- Amafaranga y’uguhinduranya akoranirizwa hamwe na RoboSats - Aya mafaranga arahinduka kandi aragenda bivanye n’ingene urubuga rwa Bitcoin rukora cane.
- Amahera ya Mining ku bijanye n’ugutanga amahera - Ivyo bishobora gutunganirizwa n’umuguzi.


![image](assets/16.webp)


### P2P Guhindura


RoboSats iremesha abakoresha guhindura Sats mu canke hanze y'umuravyo Wallet yabo. Fyonda gusa kuri buto yo guhindura iri hejuru kuri paji y’amashimwe kugira ngo ubone amashimwe y’ubu.


Nk’umuguzi w’isoko ry’‘Swap In’, wohereza On-Chain Bitcoin ku mugenzi wawe maze ugasubira kuronka Sats, ukuyemwo amafaranga yatangajwe n’/canke amafaranga y’imbere, kuri Lightning Wallet yawe. Nk’umuguzi w’isoko ry’‘Guhindura’, wohereza Sats biciye ku Muravyo maze ukaronka Bitcoin, ukuyemwo amafaranga yose n’/canke amafaranga y’imbere, kuri On-Chain Address yawe. Abakoresha Samourai canke Sparrow wallet na bo nyene barashobora gukoresha ubuhinga bwa Stowaway kugira ngo barangize guhindura.


Ivyiyumviro vy’uguhinduranya RoboSats birashobora kandi gushiramwo ubundi buryo bwo guhindura Bitcoin burimwo RBTC, LBTC na WBTC. Ukwiye kwitwararika cane nimba uriko urakorana n’ivyo bimenyetso kuko vyose biza n’ibintu bitandukanye. Bitcoin yashizweko amabuye si Bitcoin!

## IBIBAZO bibazwa


### Noshobora guhenda?


Nk’umuguzi, nimba wohereje fiat isabwa ku ruhande rwawe rw’ubudandaji, ariko uwugurisha akananirwa kukurekura Sats rero urashobora gufungura impaka. Nimba muri iyo nzira y’amatati ushobora kwemeza abakemurampaka ba RoboSats ko ari wewe wohereje iyo fiat, abagurisha barabitse amahera kandi ingwate yabo y’ubudandaji izogusohoka.

None nosubiramwo gute ubudandaji?


Ushobora gukuraho ubucuruzi umaze gushiramwo ingwati yawe ukanda kuri buto ya Collaborative Cancel iri muri menyu y’ubucuruzi. Nimba umugenzi wawe wo mu bucuruzi anezerewe no gusubiramwo, nta mahera uzokwishura. Ariko iyo uwo mukorana mu bucuruzi ashaka kurangiza ubucuruzi nawe ukaja imbere ugasubiramwo uko biri kwose, uzotakaza ingwati yawe y’ubucuruzi.


### RoboSats yoba ikorana n’uburyo bwo kwishura ‘X’?


Nta n’imwe ibujijwe ku buryo bwo kwishura muri RoboSats. Nimba utabona ivyiza mu buryo wipfuza, nushireho ivyiza vyawe ubikoresheje!


![image](assets/19.webp)


### None RoboSats ingira iki kuri jewe iyo ndayikoresheje?


Kubera ko ukoresha RoboSats biciye kuri Tor canke kuri app ya Android, ntaco bimaze! Ibindi ubimenye hano.



- Tor irinda ubuzima bwite bw'urubuga rwawe.
- PGP encryption ituma ubutumwa bwawe bw’ubudandaji buguma ari ubw’ibanga.
- Nta konti bisigura Robot imwe ku bucuruzi. Ivyo bisigura ko RoboSats idashobora guhuza ubudandaji bwinshi n’ikintu kimwe.


Ariko rero, hariho ibintu bimwebimwe vyo kwirinda! Umuravyo ni uwudasanzwe nk’uwurungika, ariko si nk’uwukira. Iyo wakiriye ku nzira yawe bwite ya Lightning, ID yawe y'inzira irasangizwa mu mafagitire yawe. Iyi node ID iha umuntu wese afise ubumenyi kuri yo intango yo kugerageza no guhuza igikorwa cawe ca On-Chain. Ivyo ni ukuri kandi iyo uwukoresha ahisemwo kwakira ubudandaji bwiwe biciye ku kwishura On-Chain.


Kugira ngo ivyo bigabanywe, abakoresha barashobora guhitamwo gukoresha umuti nka Proxy Wallet ku Muravyo canke CoinJoin ku On-Chain.


### Ihuriro


Ubu nyene hariho umuhuzabikorwa umwe wa RoboSats akoreshwa n’umugwi w’abahinguzi ba RoboSats. Mu Bitcoin, uburyo bwose bwo gushiramwo ibintu vyose hamwe buratuma intwaro canke abagenzuzi bashobora kutabona neza igikorwa kinaka.


Kubera ko RoboSats ari umugambi w’inkomoko yuguruye, umuntu wese yoshobora gufata kode agatangura gukoresha umuhuzabikorwa wiwe bwite. Naho ivyo bishobora gutuma ingorane zidashika kure y’intumbero imwe, biraca ibice isoko ry’amahera ryari risanzwe rigabanutse.


Ishirahamwe rya RoboSats rirabitahura kandi ryatanguye gukora ku citegererezo c’ubumwe. Nk’umukoresha w’iherezo, ivyo ntibikwiye guhindura uruja n’uruza rw’ubudandaji rwerekanwa haruguru cane, ariko hazoba hariho ivyerekanwa canke amashusho y’inyongera kugira ngo wongere canke ukureho abahuzabikorwa batandukanye bavyuka.
