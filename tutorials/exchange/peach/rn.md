## Imenyekanisha


Uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’abantu (P2P) ata KYC ni ngombwa kugira ngo abakoresha bazigame ubuzima bwite n’ukwigenga mu vy’amahera. Bituma umuntu ashobora gukorana n’abandi ataco akeneye kugenzura akaranga kabo, ivyo bikaba ari ngirakamaro ku bantu baha agaciro ubuzima bwite. Kugira ngo utahure neza ivyiyumviro vy’inyigisho, raba inyigisho ya BTC204:


https://planb.network/courses/65c138b0-4161-4958-bbe3-c12916bc959c

### 1. Peach ni iki?


Peach ni urubuga rwa P2P Exchange rufasha abarukoresha kugura no kugurisha amafaranga y’ibiceri ata KYC. Itanga Interface ishobora gukoreshwa neza be n’ibintu vy’umutekano biteye imbere. Ugereranije n’ibindi bisubizo nka Bisq, HodlHodl, na Robosat, Peach iratandukanye n’izindi kubera ko yoroshe gukoresha kandi n’amahera make.


### 2. Ubuzima bwite n'Ikwegeranya Amakuru


**Ni amakuru ayahe Peach akusanya?**


Peach yihatira kubika amakuru makeyi cane yerekeye abayikoresha. Aha niho hari incamake y'amakuru abitswe kuri server zayo:




- Hash y'ikimenyetso c'ibisabwa vyawe kidasanzwe (AdID)
- Hash y'amakuru yawe y'ukwishyura
- Ibiganiro vyawe vyashizwemwo
- Amakuru y’ibikorwa kugira ngo abakoresha batazwi ntibarenge urugero rw’ubudandaji (ubwoko bw’uburyo bwo kwishura bukoreshwa, amafaranga y’ugugura n’ugurisha)
- Aderesi zikoreshwa mu kohereza no kwakira kuri konti y'ububiko
- Amakuru y'ikoreshwa (Firebase n'Isesengura rya Google), wemeye gusa


Nk’ukwibutsa, Hash ni amakuru yahinduwe atamenyekana, asa n’ugushiramwo amakuru. Amakuru nyene azokwama atanga Hash imwe, ivyo bikaba bizotuma umuntu ashobora kumenya amakuru yisubiriza atazi amakuru y’intango.


*Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi kuvyerekeye hashing, ushobora gukurikira iyi nyigisho:*


https://planb.network/courses/46b0ced2-9028-4a61-8fbc-3b005ee8d70f

**Ninde ashobora kubona amakuru y'uko ndiha?**




- Uwo mufatanije nawe ni we wenyene ashobora kubona amakuru y'ukwishyura kwawe
- Amakuru arungikwa biciye ku maserukira ya Peach ariko arashirwa mu buryo bushitse kuva ku mpera gushika ku mpera
- Iyo habaye impaka, amakuru yawe y’ukwishyura n’amateka y’ibiyago vyawe azoboneka ku muhuza wa Peach yashinzwe .

- Amagenamiterere**: Igikoresho ca konti yawe:
 - Gucungera uburyo bwo kwishura
 - Gutunganya ububiko bwawe
 - Guhindura ivyo ukunda
 - Ugushika ku mfashanyo no gushigikirwa

### 4. Uko wokingira Wallet yawe


**Gutahura konti yawe ya Peach**


Konti ya Peach si konti y’injira n’ijambobanga ry’agaciro. Ni dosiye ibitswe aho hantu kuri telefone yawe, bisobanura ko Peach idakeneye kubika amakuru yawe canke kumenya akaranga kawe: ni wewe ubigenzura. Iyi dosiye irimwo amakuru yawe yose, kuva ku mfunguruzo zawe za Bitcoin Wallet gushika ku makuru y’ukwishura.


Ubwo buryo buratuma umuntu agira ubuzima bwiwe bwite, ariko kandi buratuma umuntu agira inshingano nyinshi. Gutakaza telefone yawe ata backup bisigura gutakaza uburenganzira bwo gukoresha konti yawe ya Peach n’amahera yawe. Ni ngombwa rero ko ukora backup y’iyi dosiye ukayikingira n’ijambobanga rikomeye.


**Rema ububiko bwawe**


![Accéder aux sauvegardes](assets/fr/13.webp)




- Kugera ku mategeko kuva ku rubuga ruri hasi iburyo bw'ibarabara ry'intango
- Hitamwo "ububiko" mu mirongo y'imiterere


![Processus de sauvegarde](assets/fr/06.webp)


Hari ubwoko bubiri bw'ububiko:


**Bika dosiye ya konti (ishusho 14)**




- Fyonda kuri "Rema ububiko bushasha"
- Rema ijambobanga rikomeye ryo gupfuka dosiye yawe y'ububiko
- Bika iyi dosiye ahantu heza


Ububiko bwa dosiye bugarura konti yawe yose ya Peach, harimwo :




- Wallet yawe
- Uburyo bwo kwishura
- Amateka y'ibiganiro
- Amakuru y'ukwishyura
- Amateka y'ubucuruzi n'ibisobanuro vy'abafatanyabikorwa


**Kuzigama ijambo ryo gusubirana (ishusho 15)**




- Kurikiza amabwirizwa kugira ngo ugaragaze amajambo yawe yo gukira
- Wandike neza amajambo mu buryo bubereye .
- Bika iyi nkuru ahantu hatagira umutekano, bitandukanye n'ivyo muri dosiye ya konti


Ijambo ryo gukira rikira gusa :




- Ukwinjira kuri konti yawe
- Amafaranga yawe ya Bitcoin


Uzotakaza :




- Amateka y'ibiyago
- Amakuru y'ukwishyura
- Amakuru y'abafatanyabikorwa mu mateka y'ibikorwa


Kugira ngo ubone umutekano mwiza, turagusavye gukora ubwo bwoko bwompi bwo gucungera.


## Gugura no kugurisha ama Bitcoins


### 1. Uko wogura ama Bitcoins


![Création et vue des offres](assets/fr/07.webp)




- Ku rubuga rw'intango, fyonda ku buto "Gura" (ishusho 16)
- Gutunganya ivyo ugura bivanye n'ivyo ukunda (ishusho 17)

![Sélection et confirmation d'achat](assets/fr/08.webp)




- Hitamwo ivyo ushobora gutanga bigubereye (ishusho 19)
- Kwishura mu buryo mwemeranije
- Wemeze ko wishuye mu busabe maze usuzume ivyo wakoze (ishusho 20)


![Réception des bitcoins](assets/fr/09.webp)




- Gukurikirana uko amafaranga yawe ameze
- Suzuma kwemeza ko wakiriye bitcoins
- Amahera azoboneka mu gitabu cawe ca Peach Wallet.


### 2. Uko wogurisha ama Bitcoins .


![Création d'un ordre de vente](assets/fr/10.webp)




- Gutunganya ivyo ugurisha (ishusho 24)
- Gutanga amafaranga y’ugucuruza mu kohereza ama bitcoins kuri Address yatanzwe (ishusho 25)
- Rindira kwemeza ko ugurisha (ishusho 26)
- Ivyo utanga ubu biraboneka ku baguzi (ishusho 27)


![Attente du paiement](assets/fr/11.webp)




- Gukurikirana uko ivyo utanga bimeze
- Rindira kwemezwa n'umuguzi
- Suzuma ibisobanuro vy'ugucuruza


![Finalisation de la vente](assets/fr/12.webp)




- Suzuma uko kwishura
- Kwemeza ko wakiriye amahera
- Gusuzuma ubucuruzi
- Bitcoins zihita zirekurwa ku muguzi .


**Impanuro zo gukora neza**




- Niwishure ningoga ubutumwa buvuye ku mugenzi wawe
- Suzuma neza ibisobanuro vy'ukwishyura
- Ntukekeranye gukoresha ubuhuza iyo ufise ingorane .


**Iciyumviro co gukingira**: Ntukigere wemeza ko wakiriye amahera gushika umenye ko yakiriwe kuri konti yawe.


## Ivyiza n'ibibi

- Nta KYC isabwa**: Izigama ubuzima bwite bw'abakoresha.
