## Imenyekanisha



Uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’abantu (P2P) ata KYC ni ngombwa kugira ngo abakoresha bazigame ibanga n’ukwigenga mu vy’amahera. Bituma umuntu ashobora gukorana n’abandi ataco akeneye kugenzura akaranga kabo, ivyo bikaba ari ngirakamaro ku bantu baha agaciro ubuzima bwite. Kugira ngo utahure neza ivyiyumviro vy’inyigisho, raba inyigisho ya BTC204:



https://planb.academy/courses/65c138b0-4161-4958-bbe3-c12916bc959c

### 1. Peach ni iki?



Peach ni urubuga rwo guhinduranya amafaranga rwa P2P rushobora gutuma abakoresha bagura no kugurisha amafaranga y’ama bitcoins ata KYC. Itanga interface isanzwe n’ibintu vy’umutekano biteye imbere. Ugereranije n’ibindi bisubizo nka Bisq, HodlHodl, na Robosat, Peach iratandukanye n’izindi kubera ko yoroshe gukoresha.


Uburyo bwo gushiramwo amahera multisignature (2-2) buratanga umutekano w’amahera mu gihe c’ugucuruza. Peach ishigikira uburyo butandukanye bwo kwishura, kandi ifise uburyo bwo kumenyekana kugira ngo iyobore abacuruzi mu bikorwa vyabo. Nk’uko bisanzwe ku bibanza vya P2P, birahambaye rero ko umuntu aguma afise izina ryiza kugira ngo akomeze kwizigirwa n’abandi bacuruzi.



### 2. Ubuzima bwite n'amakuru yashizwe hamwe



**Ni amakuru ayahe Peach ikusanya?



Peach iraharanira kubika amakuru makeyi cane yerekeye abayikoresha. Aha niho hari incamake y'amakuru abitswe kuri server zacu:





- hash y'ikimenyetso c'ibisabwa vyawe (AdID)
- hash y'amakuru yawe y'ukwishyura
- Ibiganiro vyawe vyashizwemwo
- Amakuru y’ibikorwa kugira ngo abakoresha batazwi ntibarenge urugero rw’ubudandaji (ubwoko bw’uburyo bwo kwishura bukoreshwa, amafaranga y’ugugura n’ugurisha)
- Addresses zakoreshwa mu kohereza no kwakira kuri konti y'ububiko
- Amakuru y'ikoreshwa (Firebase n'Isesengura rya Google), gusa n'ukwemera kwawe



Nk’ukwibutsa, hash ni amakuru yahinduwe atamenyekana, asa n’ugushiramwo amakuru. Amakuru nyene azokwama atanga hash imwe, ivyo bikaba bizotuma umuntu ashobora kumenya amakuru yisubiriza atazi amakuru y’intango.



*Kugira ngo ubone insobanuro irambuye y'ivya hashing, fata iyi nyigisho:*



https://planb.academy/courses/46b0ced2-9028-4a61-8fbc-3b005ee8d70f

**Ninde ashobora kubona amakuru y'uko ndiha?





- Uwo mufatanije nawe ni we wenyene ashobora kubona amakuru y'ukwishyura kwawe
- Amakuru arungikwa biciye ku maserukira ya Peach, ariko arashirwa mu buryo bushitse kuva ku mpera gushika ku mpera
- Iyo habaye amatati, amakuru yerekeye ukwishyura kwawe n’amateka y’ibiyago vyawe azoboneka ku muhuza washinzwe Peach

### 4. Uko wokingira ibitabo vyawe



**Gutahura konti yawe ya Peach



Konti ya Peach nta zina ry’ukoresha n’ijambobanga igira. Ni dosiye ibitswe aho hantu kuri telefone yawe, bisobanura ko Peach idakeneye kubika amakuru yawe canke kumenya akaranga kawe: ni wewe uyigenzura. Iyi dosiye irimwo amakuru yawe yose: harimwo amajambo 12 yo gusubiza bitcoin, imfunguruzo za PGP, amakuru y’ukwishura n’ibindi. Ni ngombwa rero kubika iyi dosiye ukayikingira n'ijambobanga __robust__.



Ubwo buryo buratanga urugero rw’ibanga, kandi bugasiga inshingano yo gucunga amakuru n’ugucungera amakuru mu minwe y’uwukoresha. Gutakaza telefone yawe ata backup bisigura gutakaza uburenganzira bwo gukoresha konti yawe ya Peach n’amahera yawe.



**Rema ububiko bwawe**






- Kugera ku mategeko kuva ku rubuga ruri hasi iburyo bw'ibarabara ry'intango

## Gugura no kugurisha ama bitcoins



### 1.a Uko wogura bitcoins: Fata offer yo kugurisha



Ico umuguzi akwiye gukora ca mbere ni ukuraba ivyo agurisha bimaze guterwa inkunga n’amahera ya bitcoin.



![Vue des offres de vente et filtres](assets/fr/07.webp)





- Ku rubuga rw'intango, fyonda ku buto "Gura" (ishusho 16)
- Ushobora rero guca mu rutonde rw’ama bitcoins yashizwe muri escrow system kandi yiteguye kugurishwa (ishusho 17). Ushobora kubona umubare, igiciro (mu % ugereranije n’isoko rya KYC), uburyo bwo kwishura n’amafaranga yemewe.
- Koresha amayunguruzo kugira ngo utore kandi ushire mu rutonde ivyo utanga (ishusho 18).
- Iciyumviro: ubuto buri musi y’urupapuro rw’amayunguruzo buragufasha kwakira itangazo igihe igiciro gihuye n’amayunguruzo yawe casohowe; na buto ya "reset", ishobora gukuraho gusa amayunguruzo yose (ishusho 18).



![Sélection et confirmation d'achat](assets/fr/08.webp)





- Raba ivyo ushobora gutanga bigubereye maze wohereze ubusabe bwo guhindura (ishusho 19)
- Ushobora gusaba guhinduranya ibintu vyinshi, kandi ikintu ciza ca mbere kizogukuraho ibindi wasavye.
- Nimwishure mu buryo mwemeranije.


**Icibutso:** inkomoko y'amahera itegerezwa guhura n'iyo mwavuze igihe mwongerako uburyo bwo kwishura.




- Wemeze ko wishyuye mu gisata c'ugusaba igihe nyene bizoba vyarangiye**.
- Rindira ko uwugurisha aronka amahera maze uyamenyeshe ko ari uko (ishusho 20)
- Kandi mu nyuma, usuzume ubumenyi ufise n’uwo agurisha (ishusho 21)



![Réception des bitcoins](assets/fr/09.webp)





- Gukurikirana uko amafaranga yawe ameze
- Suzuma kwemeza ko wakiriye bitcoins
- Amahera azoboneka mu gitabu cawe ca Peach (ishusho 22 na 23)



### 1.b Uko wogura ama bitcoins: Gukora igiciro



Niwaba udashobora kuronka igiciro ciza co kugurisha, urashobora guhingura igiciro co kugura. Kubera ko ivyo bitagira bitcoin n’imwe ikora muri iki gihe, uzogira amahirwe make yo kuronka uwo muzohinduranya, cane cane iyo urutonde rwawe n’izina ryawe ari bibi canke bitabaho. Kugira ngo ivyo ubitorere umuti, birahambaye, igihe urema igiciro, *ukora igiciro c’agaciro kanini* kugira ngo utume abagurisha bahitamwo igiciro cawe. Reka tubandanye:



![Creation d'ordre d'achat](assets/fr/10.webp)





- Ku rubuga rw'intango, fyonda ku buto "Rema igiciro co kugura" (ishusho 24)
- Wongereko uburyo bwo kwishura, nimba utarabikora, hanyuma winjize ivyo ukunda (umubare, igiciro n’ibindi) (ishusho 25).


Ihitamwo "instant" rituma wemera igisabwa c'ubudandaji ubwaco.




 - Fyonda kandi kuri "rema igiciro" kugira ngo ukomeze
- Iyo imaze kuremwa, ni igihe c’abagurisha kuza kugusaba guhindura. Ushobora gufunga no gusohoka muri app ata ngorane.
- Ushobora guhindura igiciro c’imbere iyo utaronse ivyo usaba. Ibuka: igiciro kinini kizotuma abagurisha baza kurondera ivyo utanga (ishusho 26).
- Uzosanga ivyo utanga mu gice ca "Gura", na co ubwaco kiri mw'idirisha rya "Exchange" (igishushanyo 27)



![Reception d'une demande de vente, messagerie](assets/fr/11.webp)





- Iyo ubonye igisabwa co kugura (igishushanyo 28) (kandi nimba utarazimya ubudandaji bwihuse mu gishushanyo 25), wemere ubudandaji umaze kugenzura izina ry’uwugurisha. Niba ubucuruzi buca bushoboka, simbukira ku ishusho 29.
- Uwugurisha ategerezwa rero gushiramwo iyo bitcoin mu buryo bwo gucungera, ("gutanga amahera mu safe"). (ishusho 29)
- Hanyuma wishyure uwugurisha aho aja yerekanwa ku gishushanyo ca 30, biciye ku nzira yawe bwite ya banki. Ntukwegere urudome rwa "Narishe" gushika ubikoze!
- Ushobora kuvugana n’uwugurisha biciye ku nzira y’ubutumwa (P2P encrypté). Iyo habaye ingorane, urashobora gufungura impaka ukanda ku kimenyetso kiri hejuru iburyo (ishusho 31). Umuhuza wa Peach azoca yinjira mu kiganiro.



![Offre de vente completée](assets/fr/12.webp)





- Uwugurisha amaze kwakira amahera, azoyatanga raporo maze ubuhinga bwo gushiramwo amahera buzorekura iyo bitcoin, iyo bitcoin izoba iri mu nzira ija kuri wallet yawe (ku buryo busanzwe biciye ku GroupHug, ubuhinga bwo gukora amatsinda y’amahera bwa Peach, bukora rimwe ku musi), 
- Gupima ubumenyi ufise n'umugurisha



Ni vyo!



**Iciyumviro ku baguzi bashasha:** abagurisha bashingira ubucuruzi bwabo ku ruhara rw’abaguzi, kandi bakunda kwirinda amasoko y’abaguzi ata bucuruzi burangiye. Biroroshe, mu ntango, kwubaka izina mu gufata amashimwe asanzwe agurishwa.




### 2.a Uko wogurisha bitcoins: Gukora ugurisha



Uburyo bwihuta kandi bworoshe bwo kugurisha kuri Peach ni **gukora igiciro co kugurisha**.



![Création d'un ordre de vente](assets/fr/13.webp)





- Kuva kuri paji y'intango, fyonda ku "Rema igiciro co kugurisha" (ishusho 32)
- Gutegura ivyo utanga, urabe ko winjiza uburyo bwo kwishura n'ibigenderwako bikwiye


ushobora kandi :




  - Rema amashikanwa menshi asa
  - activate "instant exchange" kugira ngo umuguzi wa mbere azoza ashobore gufata amasezerano (ataco wemeje) maze abandanye kwishura.
  - Hitamwo aderesi yo gusubiza amahera
  - amafaranga y'igikapu kuva kuri wallet Peach yawe
- Gutanga amafaranga y’ugucuruza mu kohereza ama bitcoins kuri aderesi yatanzwe (ishusho 34)
- Rindira kwemezwa kw’ivyo mwaguze. Iyo umaze kubikora, ivyo utanga bizoboneka kw’isoko.



![Attente du paiement](assets/fr/14.webp)





- Rindira umuguzi ngo afate ivyo utanga. Niwiyumvire kwongera igiciro (%) nimba ushaka kwihutisha ibintu (ishusho 36)
- Umaze kwakira ubusabe bwo guhindura, nusuzume ukuntu uwuguze azwi. Uce urubanza wewe nyene nimba iyo profile igubereye, hanyuma ukande kuri "accept" nimba ari yo. (37)
- None rero ni umuguzi azotanga amahera avuye muri banki yiwe aja kuri yawe. Azoca akurungikira amahera. Ntukekeranye guhamagara uwuguze mu chat.
- umaze kumenya ko amafaranga yashitse muri banki yawe*, urekure ayo mahera ukanda kuri buto ya "naronse amahera" (ishusho 38). Ntukigere wemeza ko wakiriye amahera imbere y’uko usuzuma ko yakiriwe kuri konti yawe.
- Gusuzuma ubucuruzi
- Bitcoins zihita zirekurwa ku muguzi,



Ni ho rero!



**Ikete ry'umutekano n'impanuro zo gukora neza:**




 - Ihweze ido n’ido ry’umuguzi, maze usuzume ko inkomoko y’amahera ihuye n’iyo idondora kuri Peach Nimba inkomoko y’amahera idahuye n’iyo yatangajwe, genda kuri Chat ufungure argument (ishusho 39), hanyuma wohereze ayo mahera aho yakomotse.
 - Kurikiza amabwirizwa ari mu nkoko y’umuhondo.
 - Niwishure ningoga ubutumwa buvuye ku mugenzi wawe
 - urabe maso ku nyifato y’umuguzi, canecane igihe uriko urakorana n’umuntu afise ubumenyi buke
 - Ntukekeranye gukoresha ubuhuza iyo ufise ingorane 



### 2.b Uko wogurisha ama bitcoins: fata igiciro



Birashoboka kandi kuraba no guhitamwo ivyo ugura. Uzokenera kwiyubara cane, kuko ni ho abahendanyi benshi bashobora kuboneka.



![Prendre une offre d'achat](assets/fr/15.webp)





- Kuva kuri paji y'intango, ukande kuri "Ugurisha" (ishusho 40)
- Koresha amayunguruzo kugira ngo ubone kandi uhitemwo ivyo utanga bikwegera cane (ishusho 41)



![vérification de la réputation](assets/fr/16.webp)





- imbere yo gusaba ubudandaji, turagusavye gusuzuma ko urutonde rw’umuguzi rubereye. Ushobora gufyonda ku kintu umuntu atanga, hanyuma ukafyonda ku ID y’uwukoresha kugira ngo ubone urutonde rwiwe. Nk'akarorero, ivyo utanga biri ku ishusho ya 42 vyoshobora gufatwa nk'ibifise "akaga" (umukoresha mushasha, umubare munini cane). "Risk" wirukira mu gufata iyo offer ni iyo gutakaza umwanya gusa, igihe cose utakoze ikosa ryo kurekura ama bitcoins utaronse ayo mahera. Ushobora kuguma ushiramwo ama bitcoins mu safe.


Ku rundi ruhande, iyo iri ku ishusho ya 43, iva ku mudandaza azi utuntu n’utundi (ishusho ya 44), ata mpari zihari muri kahise kayo. Ni rero igiciro kidafise ingorane nyinshi.



![Match avec vendeur](assets/fr/17.webp)





- Igihe iyo mpano imaze gusaba, iyo umuguzi yemeye ivyo wasavye, iyo porogarama izogutwara ku gishushanyo ca 34, aho ushobora kubandanya ubudandaji nk’uko bivugwa aha hepfo.
