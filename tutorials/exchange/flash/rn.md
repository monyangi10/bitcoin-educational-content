Gutuma kwemera Bitcoin bishoboka mu bihugu biri mu nzira y’amajambere vyacitse uruganda urubyiruko n’abarongozi bariko baragira ibintu bishasha cane. Ivyo bigaragara cane ku mugabane wa Afirika aho havutse imiryango y’uruziga, iba rimwe na rimwe ifise ubutunzi buke cane kandi ibona ko Bitcoin ari uburyo bwo kugarukana ubusegaba bwayo mu gihe hariho uburyo bw’ivy’ubutunzi bubonwa ko bugenda burahungabana.



Ivyo bishizwe mu muzirikanyi, urubuga rwishizeho intumbero yo gutuma abanyabubasha bo mu karere kabo bashobora kuronka amafaranga makeyi nk’100 XOF, angana n’ama eurocents 15, gutyo bica bimenagura ivyiyumviro benshi bari bafise ku bijanye n’igiciro kinini ca Bitcoin.



Muri iyi nyigisho, turaza kuraba Flash, umuti wo muri Benin ushobora kugufasha kuronka ama bitcoins ukoresheje amafaranga makeyi cane nk’amafaranga 100 ya CFA.



## Flash ni iki?



Flash ni urubuga rwa Exchange rwateguwe na [BlockSolut](https://bitcoinflash.xyz) rukora mu bihugu vyinshi vyo muri Afirika y’Uburengero. Intumbero nyamukuru ya Flash ni ugufasha mu kuronka, Exchange no gukoresha Bitcoin mu buzima bwa misi yose.


Flash yizigira Lightning Network, igipfukisho ca Bitcoin, kugira ngo ikore ibikorwa vya Bitcoin ata nkomanzi kandi ata ngorane, yerekane umuryango wayo ububasha bwa Bitcoin mu buzima bwa misi yose.



![welcome](assets/fr/01.webp)



Hariho ibikorwa bibiri nyamukuru bitangwa na platform:




- FlashX**: Exchange amafaranga y’ibiceri n’amahera y’amatelefone ngendanwa.
- FlashPayment**: Koresha Bitcoin ku bikorwa vya misi yose.



Ivyo bikoresho bibiri, Flash ifise intumbero yo gutuma ikoreshwa rya Bitcoin rigira demokarasi, guhindura iciyumviro c’ababona Bitcoin nk’ubuhungiro gusa, no kwerekana ko Bitcoin ari uburyo bwa Exchange bujanye n’ukuri kwo muri Afirika.



Bitcoin ifasha kugabanya ubusumbasumbane mu vy’ubutunzi no mu karere mu gutanga uburyo bwo gukoresha uburyo bw’ivy’ubutunzi bwo kw’isi yose, ata kuraba aho abantu bakomoka canke aho baba.



Reka dufate urugendo rwo gutembera maze tubone igikorwa cose gitangwa n’urubuga.



Flash ni urubuga rufise urupapuro rumwe (SPA) aho uzosanga ibikorwa vyose dutanga.

## Ukwishura vuba



Uretse kugura no kugurisha bitcoins, Flash itanga ubuhinga bwayo bwo gukoresha Bitcoin. Biciye kuri FlashPayment, ushobora gukoresha amafaranga yawe kugira ngo ugure ku nzira z’amatelefone ngendanwa ziriho:





- Ivyiyumviro vya GSM**;
- Amapaki ya Internet**;
- Guhamagara **amapaki**;
- Ihuriro ry’uguhamagara n’ugukoresha Internet.



Mu gice co gushika ku bikorwa vya misi yose, hitamwo **Igice co kugura**.


Hanyuma uhitemwo igihugu, urubuga rwa telefone ngendanwa n’ubwoko bwa servisi ya digitale wipfuza kwishura.



![flashpayment](assets/fr/09.webp)



Uzuza amakuru yawe maze ubandanye kwishura Invoice yasohotse.



![selection](assets/fr/10.webp)



Uretse ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa none, Flash iraguha urutonde rw’ubucuruzi bwemera Bitcoin kandi ikagufasha kwishura ibintu n’ibikorwa ukoresheje Bitcoin.


Ushobora rero kwishura ibikoresho canke ukaja gufungura n’abagenzi n’umuryango, nk’akarorero, hanyuma ukishura vyose ukoresheje Lightning Wallet yawe.



Mu gice co kuronka ibikorwa vya misi yose, hitamwo **Ibindi** maze ubone amaduka aho ushobora gukoresha Bitcoin nk'uburyo bwo gukoresha Exchange.
