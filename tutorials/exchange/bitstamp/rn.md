Bitstamp (https://www.bitstamp.net/) ni umucuruzi wa Bitcoin wo kugura no kugurisha Bitcoin, hariho ibitabo vyinshi n’amahitamwo menshi. Ni vyiza ku masosiyete canke ku baguzi baremereye. Ni KYC rero ivyo mubimenye. batangura kandi kwishura kubera gufata Bitcoin yawe imbere muri Wallet yabo.
