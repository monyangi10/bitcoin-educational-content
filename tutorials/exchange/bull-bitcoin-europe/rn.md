Bull Bitcoin ni urubuga rwa Exchange rukoreshwa na Bitcoin gusa rwashinzwe mu 2013 muri Canada na Francis Pouliot, umuntu w’agaciro mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin. Filozofiya ya Bull Bitcoin ni uguha abakoresha ibikoresho vyo kwigenga mu vy’amahera no kwidegemvya. Igikoresho ca Exchange ntikigira ububiko, ivyo bisigura ko wama ufise ubusegaba ku ma Bitcoins yawe.


Mu ntango iyo porogarama yaboneka muri Canada gusa, ubu iraboneka mu Buraya no mu bindi bihugu vyinshi, ikaba ifise igikoresho gishasha citwa Interface. Iyi verisiyo nshasha ya Bull Bitcoin iragufasha kugura no kugurisha ama bitcoins mu buryo bworoshe kandi bwihuta, bivuye ku gukoresha Lightning n’ugutanga amafaranga SEPA ubwo nyene. Ishiramwo kandi amahera y’amafaranga mu ma bitcoins. Mu gihe kizoza, iyo nzira izoshiramwo kandi amahitamwo ya DCA, ugugura kwihuta, n’amategeko y’imipaka.


Bull Bitcoin yiyemeje gutanga ubuhinga buboneye ata mahera yihishije. Zirikana ko, nk’ibindi bihugu vyinshi, gukoresha Bull Bitcoin bisaba ko wuzuza uburyo bwa KYC (Menya Umukiriya Wawe).

## Gutegura konti yawe


None rero reka dukingire konti yawe. Fyonda kuri "*Konti yanje*".


![BULL](assets/fr/05.webp)

![BULL](assets/fr/06.webp)


Hitamwo "*Ongerako ivyemezo vy'ibintu bibiri*" kugira ngo wongereko Layer y'umutekano kuri konti yawe.


![BULL](assets/fr/07.webp)


Scan kode ya QR ukoresheje porogaramu yawe yo kwemeza, winjize kode y'imirongo 6 hanyuma ukande kuri "*Activate 2FA*".


![BULL](assets/fr/08.webp)


Niba utamenyereye gukoresha porogaramu zo kwemeza 2FA, ndagusavye urabe iyi nyigisho ku porogaramu ya Authy:


https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/authentication/authy-a76ab26b-71b0-473c-aa7c-c49153705eb7

Injira ijambobanga ryawe kugira ngo wemeze.


![BULL](assets/fr/09.webp)


Itunganywa ry'ukwemeza kabiri ubu ryararangiye.


![BULL](assets/fr/10.webp)


Ndagusavye ubike amakode yawe yo gusubirana. Kugira ngo ubikore generate, kanda kuri "*Gucungera amakode y'ububiko bwa 2FA*".


![BULL](assets/fr/11.webp)


Hitamwo "*generate amakode mashasha yo gusubizaho ububiko*" maze ubike ayo makode ahantu hatagira inkomanzi.


![BULL](assets/fr/12.webp)

## KYC


Kugira ngo ubone ibikorwa vyose vya Bull Bitcoin, ari vyo bikoresho bitegekanijwe, ukeneye gukora ivy’ukugenzura akaranga (KYC). Fyonda kuri "*Bandanya ugenzura*".


![BULL](assets/fr/14.webp)


Hitamwo "*Konti y'umuntu ku giti ciwe*".


![BULL](assets/fr/15.webp)


Uzuza amakuru yawe bwite maze ukurikize intambwe z’uburyo bwo kugenzura. Uzobazwa :




- Izina ryawe rya mbere n'iry'umuryango ;
- Inomero ya telefone yawe ;
- Ikete ryawe ry’iposita Address ;
- Itariki yawe y’amavuko, umwuga wawe n’imigambi yawe ku rubuga (gushiramwo amahera, kugurisha, kwishura, n’ibindi);
- ID n’ifoto y’umuntu;


![BULL](assets/fr/16.webp)


Ivyo bigenzurwa bikwiye kwemezwa vuba. Iyo umaze kwemezwa, uzoshobora kuronka ibintu vyose bitangwa n’iyo nzira.


## Gushiramwo amahera ya fiat


Imbere y'uko utangura kugura no kugurisha Bitcoin kuri Bull, birahambaye ko utahura ko urubuga rutigera rubika Sats yawe. Ivyo bisigura ko kugira ngo ugure Bitcoin, utegerezwa guca urungika amahera yawe mu Wallet yicungera. Na wewe nyene, kugira ngo ugurishe, ukeneye kohereza ama bitcoins yawe uvuye ku Wallet yicungera kuri Bull. Bizoheza bihindurwe amafaranga y’amahera (fiat) iyo bishikirijwe. Ntibishoboka ko umuntu abika ama bitcoins ataco akora ku rubuga. Ariko rero, urashobora kuguma ufise amahera menshi mu ma euro.


Kugira ngo utangure, ushobora gushaka gushiramwo ama euros muri Bull kugira ngo ugure Sats yawe ya mbere. Fyonda kuri "*Amahera yo gushiramwo*".


![BULL](assets/fr/17.webp)


Hitamwo "*Iherezo rya SEPA*".


![BULL](assets/fr/18.webp)


Amakuru y’ibankire y’iyi platforme azokwerekanwa. Ukore ubutumwa bwa SEPA ubwo nyene ku muntu azoronka inyungu yerekanwa, ukoresheje konti ya banki iri mw’izina ryawe (ni yo nyene ikoreshwa kuri KYC ku rubuga). Raba neza ko ushiramwo kode yo kwimurira yerekanwa kuri iyi paji mu ndondoro y’ukwimurira.


![BULL](assets/fr/19.webp)


Nitwamara kwakira amafaranga yawe, amafaranga yawe mu ma euro azosubirwamwo.


![BULL](assets/fr/20.webp)


## Gugura ama bitcoins


Ubu ko umaze gushiramwo ama euro kuri konti yawe, ni igihe co kugura Sats yawe ya mbere. Fyonda kuri "Gura Bitcoin".


![BULL](assets/fr/21.webp)


Niwinjize amahera wipfuza kugura. Uzokenera kandi guhitamwo ingene ushaka gukura Sats yawe. Ku mahera make, ndabagira inama yo gukoresha Lightning, ariko no gukura amahera On-Chain canke Liquid biraboneka. Mu gihe ca nyuma, uzokenera gutanga urupapuro rwa Address uzorungikirako ayo mahera. Umaze kwuzuza amakuru yawe, ukande kuri "*Bandanya*".


![BULL](assets/fr/22.webp)


Niba utamenyereye gukoresha ama wallet Bitcoin, Lightning canke Liquid, ndagusavye gufata inyigisho yacu yihariye:


https://planb.network/courses/f3e3843d-1a1d-450c-96d6-d7232158b81f

Ushobora kandi gutohoza [inyigisho zacu zitomoye ku bikoresho vyiza kuruta ibindi vyose vya Bitcoin](https://planb.network/inyigisho/wallet).


Hanyuma wemeze ivyo uguze ukanda kuri "*Wemeze uguze*".


![BULL](assets/fr/23.webp)


Ku ruhande rwanje, maze guhitamwo gukuraho biciye ku Lightning, mfise uburyo butatu bwo gusubirana Sats yanje:




- LNURL mu gucapura kode ya QR ;
- Mu gutanga Umuravyo wanje Address ;
- Mu gutanga Invoice y’amahera y’ugugura.


Uburyo bwa LNURL ni bwo bworoshe kuruta ubundi. Iboneka mu bikoresho vyinshi vy'umuravyo, nka Phoenix, aho uhitamwo "*Kwakira*" hanyuma "*Scan*".


![BULL](assets/fr/24.webp)


Ama bitcoins yawe ubu aratekanye muri Wallet yawe.


![BULL](assets/fr/25.webp)


## Kugurisha ama bitcoins


Kugira ngo ugurishe bitcoins, uburyo bwo kubikora burasanzwe cane. Fyonda kuri "*Gurisha Bitcoin*".


![BULL](assets/fr/26.webp)


Injira amafaranga wipfuza kugurisha maze uhitemwo uburyo bwo kwimurira Sats kuri Bull Bitcoin. Ku bijanye n’amahera make, ndabagira inama yo gukoresha umuravyo. Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya*".


![BULL](assets/fr/27.webp)


Hitamwo konti wipfuza ko amayero avuye mu kugurisha ashirwako. Ushobora guhitamwo konti yawe kuri platform canke konti ya banki yo hanze. Fyonda kuri "*Bandanya*".


![BULL](assets/fr/28.webp)


Amabwirizwa y’ugukora iyo nzira araheza akagaragazwa. Mu karorero kanje k'umuravyo, nzoba ndiko ndiha Invoice.


![BULL](assets/fr/29.webp)


Igihe ukwishyura kwemejwe, amahera yawe azosubirwamwo.


![BULL](assets/fr/30.webp)
