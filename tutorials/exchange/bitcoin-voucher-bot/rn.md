## Imenyekanisha


BitcoinVoucherBot ni igikoresho umuntu ashobora gukoresha mu kugura amafaranga y’ama Bitcoins mu ma Exchange ku ma euro.


### KYC Umuco


Igikorwa co guhindura amayero ku Bitcoin ni intambwe ya mbere kandi y’ishimikiro yo gutangura kwiga iyo nyigisho, ariko biboneka ko ari co kandi kigoye kandi gikomeye kuruta ibindi vyose. Hariho uburyo bwinshi bwo guhitamwo: gutanga Bitcoin biciye mu guhanahana amakuru hagati, amateraniro afise insiguro ya Bitcoin, abagenzi, abagenzi, n’ibindi. Twifatanya n’umuryango w’aba Bitcoiner kandi **turahimiriza cane gukoresha ama Exchanges ahuriweko** kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kwitwararika cane ubuzima bwiwe bwite.


Naho iryo hitamwo rishobora kuba ritari ryiza, birahambaye gutahura ko Amashirahamwe y’Ibicuruzwa ashira mu ngiro itegeko ry’Ukumenya Umukiriya Wawe (KYC), gutyo agatanga akaranga, hamwe n’aho umuntu ari, ku Satoshi yose aguriwe kuri yo. "Ugufasha" birafise ingaruka zikomeye cane.


### None twobikora gute?


Aha niho haje igikorwa, igikorwa ca Telegram gikora nk’umuyoboro hagati y’amahera durungika muri SEPA n’ivyo tugura muri Sats.


### Ibisabwa


Kugira ngo utangure gukoresha BitcoinVoucherBot, nta nkenerwa yo gutanga amakuru y’ibanga y’agaciro ku bakozi ba Bot. **Nta ruhusha rukenewe**.


Ico gikenewe ni konti ya Telegram isanzwe ikora hamwe na konti ya banki. **Iciyumviro**: Konti yuguruwe na Poste Italiane (ku bakiriya b’Abataliyano) canke, muri rusangi, yerekeza ku ikarita ishobora gusubirwamwo amahera ntibereye.


Mu kiganiro ca Telegram dutegura itegeko, n’amahera yo muri banki tukariha, hanyuma biciye kuri bot tukaronka igipapuro c’ugugura gitanzwe n’ishirahamwe ry’uwundi muntu ritazi ico tuguze.

Ico gikorwa gituma Bot yishura, iyo na yo ikaba yerekana urutonde rw’amabwirizwa nyamukuru ifise. ### Guhembwa


Inyuma y’ibihe bikeyi, bikenewe kugira ngo amakuru akorerwe, VoucherBot irasubiza n’ubutumwa burimwo amakuru yose akenewe kugira ngo umuntu arangize itegeko. Bivanye n’ivyo banki yawe isaba, amakuru akenewe ni:




- `IBAN`, ari vyo bihambaye ku bijanye n`ivyo gushiramwo, hamwe n`ivyo Address y`uwuyironka;
- `amahera yatowe` mbere biciye mu gihe co guca, kigomba gushikwako kugira ngo VoucherBot ishobore kwemera itegeko igihe amahera yashitse;
- `Impamvu yo kwishura`, ariyo mpamvu yo kwishura. **Bitegerezwa gukoporwa no gushirwako ataco bikuyeko canke ngo bishiremwo mu kibanza kibereye c’ivyo wimuriye. Ico ari co cose "." canke "-" iri mu mpamvu yo kwishura, ishobora gusubirizwa na `umwanya w'umweru'**.
- `OrderID` yihariye yo gukoresha igihe usaba imfashanyo iyo ari yo yose.


Ushobora rero gukomeza kwishura, uciye kuri app yawe canke kuri banki. Iyo amahera yemejwe na banki, birahambaye ko wibuka gukanda _Notify payment_ mu kiganiro na VoucherBot. Ico gikorwa coroshe kirakumenyesha ko hari amahera ari mu nzira.

### Uko woshiramwo amahera mu mpapuro z'uruzitiro


Nk’uko twabibonye mu gutegura amategeko, VoucherBot iremeza ko Sats igurwa ataco ihinduye ku ruzitiro, n’uguhitamwo igitabu c’izina ry’ivyo bikoresho.


**Iciyumviro**: Gutegura amategeko no kwishura ntibihinduka, bihora ari bimwe. Ico gihinduka ni ingene igipapuro c’amahera c’onchain gikoreshwa.


Amaze kurangiza gusaba, gutanga amahera, gukanda _Notify payment_ no kurindira igihe c’ubuhinga c’amabanki co gutanga amahera, VoucherBot izokwishura mu kohereza iyo voucher ataco ihinduye mu chat.


Iyi mpapuro na zo nyene ziri mu buryo bwa kode ya QR, ariko ibara ry’ingenzi ni umuhondo w’umukara kandi-ikiruta vyose-mu ndondoro birasigurwa neza ko ari mpapuro z’uruzitiro, uzitanga amahera ataco uhinduye kuri Wallet onchain yawe kandi, kugira ngo utangure uburyo bwo gutanga amahera, utegerezwa gufyonda kuri _Redeem on Telegram_. Ikete ry’uruzitiro na ryo nyene ririmwo amakuru asanzwe abonetse ku bijanye n’iryo ry’umuravyo:




- amafaranga ari muri Sats, angana n’ayo yoherejwe biciye ku nzira y’ugutanga amakuru, hatarimwo amafaranga y’ibikorwa n’amafaranga y’uruja n’uruza;
- kode y’ikete;
- ID y’ishingiro y’igitabu c’inyandiko;
- itariki iyo mpapuro z’urugendo zitegerezwa gucungurwa canke amahera azobura, ni ukuvuga imisi 25 inyuma y’aho zisohokeye.
