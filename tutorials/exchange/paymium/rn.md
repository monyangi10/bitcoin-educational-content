Paymium ni urubuga rwa mbere rukora Bitcoin Exchange kw’isi yose. Paymium, ifise abaguzi barenga 230.000, ni umunyamuryango washinze ishirahamwe rya Labchain, rihuzwa na Caisse des Dépôts. Iyo sosiyete ifise icicaro i Paris, ishigikirwa n’amashirahamwe y’ishoramari yitwa Newfund na Kima Ventures. Kuva mu mwaka w’2021, Ikigo c’Ubufaransa kijejwe amasoko y’ivy’imari cemera uburyo bw’ibikorwa vya Paymium mu kuyiha ikibanza c’Umutanga ibikorwa vy’Itunga ry’Ivyuma (inomero y’iyandikwa E2021-011).
