Ku isi yose, hariho inyishu ziriko ziraboneka kugira ngo ikoreshwa rya Bitcoin ribe ryiza kandi ribe ryiza. Muri iki kiciro, Vexl iratandukanye cane n’uburyo ikoresha n’ivyo ibona: gusubiza Bitcoin mu maboko y’abantu.



Vexl ni urubuga rw’imigenderanire rw’abantu benshi ruhuza abaguzi n’abagurisha Bitcoin kw’isi yose. Ntibisaba kugenzura akaranga kawe kandi biratuma ubuzima bwite bwawe bugenda neza mu kutamenyesha amakuru yawe yose y’agaciro.



## Intambwe za mbere na Vexl



Gutangura gukoresha Vexl biragoye cane: sura [urubuga rwemewe](https://vexl.it) hanyuma ushireko porogarama y’itelefone ngendanwa kuri telefone yawe ya iOS canke Android.



⚠️ Gukuraho porogarama ya telefone ngendanwa ku rubuga rwemewe birabuza gukora kopi y’ubuhendanyi ku rubuga kandi bikaba bituma amakuru yawe aguma ari meza.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokora cane cane ku rubuga rwa Android, ariko iyo nzira yose idondora aha hepfo irakora no kuri iOS.



![app](assets/fr/01.webp)



Umaze gushiramwo iyo porogarama, nushireho konti yawe mu kugenzura inomero yawe ya telefone. Vexl isaba amakuru makeyi cane kugira ngo ntumenyekane kandi ukomeze ibanga ryawe.



![verify](assets/fr/02.webp)



Uhejeje kugenzura konti yawe, tangura kumenya abagize urubuga bari mu bantu uvugana na bo. Injira mu bantu bawe maze utangure gukoresha Vexl.



![contacts](assets/fr/03.webp)



Vexl ikora nk’isoko ry’urunganwe, iguha uburyo bwinshi:





- Gura Bitcoin n’amahera.
- Gugurisha Bitcoin ku mafaranga.
- Gura igicuruzwa gifise Bitcoin.
- Gurisha igicuruzwa muri Exchange ku bitcoins.
- Raba ivyo Bitcoin igurisha vyose mu karere kawe.
- Raba ivyo vyose Bitcoin ushobora kugura mu karere iwanyu.
- Kurondera ikintu kidasanzwe.



![options](assets/fr/04.webp)



Ku rupapuro rwa mbere rwa Vexl, ukimara kwiyandikisha, uzobona ivyiyumviro vy’ingene wokora ibikorwa vyihariye be n’ivyo ushobora guhindura kugira ngo ubumenyi bwawe bwo gukoresha bube bwiza.



Ufyondeye ku kimenyetso ca **Chat**, uzosanga amateka y'ibiganiro wagiranye n'abantu mwavuganye n'abantu mwavuganye na bo.

### Gutanga amategeko



Vexl iguha ubuhinga bwo guhindura ibidukikije kugira ngo ushobore guhindura ivyo ushaka gukora.



![initiate](assets/fr/07.webp)



Hitamwo ubwoko bw’urutonde rw’urutonde ushaka, hanyuma ugaragaze nimba uri mu kibanza kirekire canke kigufi.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko twibanda ku guhinduranya Bitcoin.



![rate](assets/fr/08.webp)



Hitamwo amafaranga wipfuza gucuruzako, hanyuma uhitemwo amafaranga angana n’ayo wipfuza kugura canke kugurisha. Ushobora kugura no kugurisha ku banywanyi benshi b’urubuga gushika ushitse ku giciro ushaka. Ushobora kandi gushinga itariki Exchange yawe izoherako.



![préférences](assets/fr/09.webp)



Bivanye n’uko bigufasha, urashobora gukora Exchange yawe mu buryo bw’umubiri mu karere uzosanga, canke ukaguma kuri Vexl, ugakorana n’umuntu wo guhamagara maze ugakora Exchange kuri internet. Ku bijanye n’uguhinduranya Bitcoin, Vexl ishigikira vyose Lightning Network, vyiza ku bijanye n’ugutanga amahera make, vyihuta, n’uguhinduranya uruhererekane rukuru iyo hariho amafaranga menshi y’ama bitcoins.

Nuvuge kandi ururimi wipfuza kuyaga, kandi ushiremwo n’imiburiburi imvo imwe yumvikana yotuma abandi bagize urubuga bafata nka nkama ivyo utanga.
