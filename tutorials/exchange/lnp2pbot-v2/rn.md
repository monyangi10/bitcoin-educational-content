## Imenyekanisha


Uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’abantu (P2P) ata KYC ni ngombwa kugira ngo abakoresha bazigame ubuzima bwite bwabo n’ukwigenga kwabo mu vy’amahera. Bituma umuntu ashobora gukorana n’abandi ataco akeneye kugenzura akaranga kabo, ivyo bikaba ari ngirakamaro ku bantu baha agaciro ubuzima bwite. Kugira ngo utahure neza ivyiyumviro vy’inyigisho, raba inyigisho ya BTC204:


https://planb.network/courses/65c138b0-4161-4958-bbe3-c12916bc959c

Gugura no kugurisha Bitcoin peer-to-peer (P2P) ni bumwe mu buryo bwihariye bwo kuronka canke gutanga amafaranga y’ibiceri. LNP2PBot ni Telegram bot ifunguye ifasha mu guhinduranya P2P kuri Lightning Network, igatuma habaho uguhinduranya vyihuta, bihenda, ata KYC.


### Ni kuki ukoresha Lnp2pbot?




- Nta KYC isabwa
- Ivyiyumviro vyihuta kuri Lightning Network.
- Ibiciro bito
- Interface yoroshe biciye kuri Telegram, ni porogarama izwi cane yo gutanga ubutumwa umuntu ashobora kuyironka aho ari ho hose kw’isi
- Uburyo bwo kumenyekana buhuriweko
- Ivyiyumviro vy'ubucuruzi vyihuta
- Infashanyo y'amafaranga menshi
- Umuryango ukora kandi uriko urakura


### Ibisabwa


Kugira ngo ukoreshe Lnp2pbot, uzokenera :


1. Lightning Network Wallet (Umuyaga, Fonikisi canke Blixt ni vyo vyiza)


2. Porogaramu ya telegaramu yashizweho (https://telegaramu.org/)


3. Konti ya Telegram ifise izina ry'ukoresha

## Gugura no kugurisha ama Bitcoins


Hari uburyo bubiri nyamukuru bwo Exchange ku Lnp2pbot:


1. Gusura no kwishura ku bihembo biriho kw'isoko


2. Rema ivyo ushaka kugura canke kugurisha .


Muri iyi nkuru, turaza kuraba mu buryo burambuye ingene :




- Gura bitcoins ku giciro kiriho
- Gurisha bitcoins mu kwihingurira ivyo utanga


### Uko wogura ama Bitcoins


**1. Rondera kandi uhitemwo ivyo utanga**


![Sélection d'une offre de vente](assets/fr/01.webp)


Raba ivyo utanga muri [@p2plightning](https://t.me/p2plightning) hanyuma ukande kuri buto "Gura satoshis" iri munsi y'itangazo ushaka.


**2. Kwemeza ivyo utanga n'umubare**


![Validation de l'offre](assets/fr/02.webp)


1. Subira ku kiganiro ca bot


2. Wemeze amahitamwo yawe y'ivyo utanga


3. Shiramwo amafaranga wipfuza kugura


4. Bot izogusaba gutanga umuravyo Invoice ku giciro kiri mu satoshis .


**3. Baza uwugurisha**


![Mise en relation](assets/fr/03.webp)


Iyo Invoice imaze gutumwa, iyo bot iragushira mu nzira n’uwugurisha.


**4. Guhanahana amakuru n'uwugurisha**


![Chat privé](assets/fr/04.webp)


Fyonda kw’izina ry’akataraboneka ry’umugurisha kugira ngo ufungure umurongo w’ivy’ibanga aho ushobora gutanga amakuru y’ukwishyura Exchange fiat.


**5. Kwemeza ko wishyuye**


![Confirmation du paiement](assets/fr/05.webp)


Amaze kwishura, koresha itegeko `/fiatsent` mu kiganiro ca bot. Igihe igikorwa co gucuruza kizoba cararangiye, uzoshobora gupima uwugurisha maze igikorwa co gucuruza kizopfungwa.


### Uko wogurisha ama Bitcoins


**1. Rema igiciro co kugurisha**


![Création d'une offre de vente](assets/fr/06.webp)


Kugira ngo ureme igiciro c'ugucuruza, koresha itegeko :


`/gurisha`


Bot izoca ikuyobora intambwe ku yindi:


1. Hitamwo ifaranga ryawe


2. Erekana umubare w’amasatoshi yo kugurisha .


3. Ku bijanye n’igiciro, ufise uburyo bubiri:




   - Gushinga igiciro gihoraho c'ingero ya satoshis
   - Koresha igiciro c'isoko ufise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha igiciro c'imbere (ciza canke kibi)


💡 **Impanuro**: Ico kiguzi kigufasha guhindura igiciro cawe kijanye n'igiciro co kw'isoko. Nk’akarorero, igiciro c’imbere ca -1% bisigura ko uriko uragurisha 1% hasi y’igiciro c’isoko.


**2. Kwemeza ko urutonde rwashizweho**


![Confirmation de l'ordre de vente](assets/fr/07.webp)


Igihe urutonde rwashizweho, uzobona icemezo gifise uburenganzira bwo gukuraho urutonde ukoresheje itegeko `/cancel`.


**3. Gucungera ugurisha**


![Prise de l'ordre par un acheteur](assets/fr/08.webp)




- Iyo umuguzi yishuye ivyo umuguze, uzoronka itangazo ririho kode ya QR n’ikete Invoice ryo kwishura.
- Suzuma urutonde rw’umuguzi n’izina ryiwe.


![Mise en relation avec l'acheteur](assets/fr/09.webp)




- Fyonda kw'izina ry'akataraboneka ry'umuguzi kugira ngo ufungure umurongo w'ibiganiro vy'ibanga.
- Menyesha uwuguze amakuru yerekeye ukwishyura kwa fiat.
- Rindira kwemezwa kw’amahera ya fiat avuye ku muguzi.
- Suzuma ko amahera yashitse kuri konti yawe.


![Confirmation de la fin de l'ordre](assets/fr/10.webp)




- Wemeze ibikorwa na `/release` hanyuma wuzuze itegeko. Uzoronka akaryo ko gupima uwuguze.

## Kugereranya n'ibindi bisubizo


Lnp2pbot ifise ivyiza n’ibibi vyinshi ku bindi bisubizo vya P2P Exchange nka Peach, Bisq, HodlHodl, na Robosat:


### Inyungu za Lnp2pbot




- Nta KYC isabwa**: Bitandukanye n’ibindi bikoresho, Lnp2pbot ntibisaba kugenzura akaranga, gutyo bikazigama ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha.
- Ivyimburwa vyihuta**: Kubera Lightning Network, ivyimburwa birashika hafi mu kanya nk’ako gukubita.
- Amafaranga make**: Ibiciro vy’ugucuruza ni bito kuruta ivyo gucuruza mu buryo busanzwe.
- Ushobora gukoreshwa kuri telefone ngendanwa**: LNP2PBot ishobora gukoreshwa kuri Telegram, bikaba vyoroshe gukoresha kuri telefone ngendanwa.
- Biroroshe gukoresha**: Interface ya Lnp2pbot ishobora gukoreshwa neza, mbere n’abakoresha batazi cane.


### Inyungu za Lnp2pbot




- Ugushingira kuri Telegram**: Gukoresha Lnp2pbot bisaba konti ya Telegram, ishobora kuba idakwiriye abakoresha bose.
- Amafaranga make**: Ugereranije n’amashirahamwe akomeye nka Bisq, amafaranga ashobora kuba make cane.


Mu kugereranya, inyishu nka Bisq zitanga amahera menshi n’ikoreshwa ry’ibiro Interface, ariko zishobora gusaba amahera menshi n’igihe kirekire co gukora. HodlHodl na Robosat, muri ico gihe, na bo nyene baratanga ubudandaji ata KYC, ariko bafise imiterere y’amahera itandukanye n’imirongo y’ubudandaji.
