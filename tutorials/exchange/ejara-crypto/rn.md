### Imiterere ya konti



Mbere na mbere, birahambaye gukora ama configurations makeyi kugira ngo ushobore gushika ku bikorwa bitandukanye vy’iyi porogarama. Ivyo bizobera ku rwego rubiri.



**Urwego rwa 1**



Fyonda kuri buto ya **Konti**, hanyuma ukande kuri **Imbonerahamwe**. Muri iyi menu, urashobora guhindura ururimi rw’ikoreshwa ryawe, guhindura ijambobanga ryawe no gukoresha ivyemezo vy’ibintu bibiri (ivyo dusaba cane, kuko ari ikindi gice c’umutekano).



![screen](assets/fr/17.webp)



Turagusavye kandi gukoresha ubuto **Saba PIN**, **Saba ko porogarama ifungurwa** na **Saba gukurwaho** mu **Imiterere ya PIN** kugira ngo urushirize gukomeza umutekano w’iporogarama yawe.


Nkako, kubera ivyo bikoresho, kode yawe ya PIN izokenerwa ku bijanye n’ukugerageza kwose gufungura porogarama yawe no ku bijanye n’ugusaba kwose kwo gukuraho ivy’ubuhinga bwa none canke amahera. Ivyo bica bibuza uwundi muntu uwo ari we wese gukoresha porogarama yawe ataco uzi.



![screen](assets/fr/18.webp)



**Urwego rwa 2**



Uwo murongo w’imiterere ni uwuhambaye cane.



Mbere, **gusubiza inyuma imfunguruzo zawe**. Mu nyigisho yacu, tuzokora backup y’amaboko. Ni vyiza cane kubisubiza inyuma neza no kubibika ahantu hatagira umutekano.



![screen](assets/fr/19.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/20.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/21.webp)



Igikurikira, ukore **KYC**. Zirikana ko hariho ingero zine za KYC kuri Ejara Crypto. Uko urushiriza guheza ingero, niko urushiriza gukora amafaranga menshi y’amadolari.



![screen](assets/fr/22.webp)



Kubera ko wakoze configuration y’intango igihe wakora konti yawe, urugero rwa mbere rwaramaze gushika rero rurasuzumwa mu rangi y’icatsi kibisi. ✔️



![screen](assets/fr/23.webp)

Kugira ngo ubone biciye kuri LN kuri Ejara Crypto, subira ku rubuga rwa **Wallet** hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya **Kwakira**. Kanda **Lightning Network** urutonde rw’itunga (1) hanyuma uhitemwo **Lightning Network** itunga (2).



![screen](assets/fr/34.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/35.webp)

![screen](assets/fr/36.webp)

![screen](assets/fr/37.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/38.webp)

![screen](assets/fr/39.webp)

![screen](assets/fr/40.webp)

## Gugura ama bitcoins



Uretse kwakira no kohereza amafaranga y’ibiceri kuri Ejara Crypto, urashobora kandi kugura no kugurisha amafaranga y’ibiceri on-chain na satoshis.



Waba uriko ugura on-chain **bitcoins** canke **satoshis**, uburyo bwo kugura buraguma ari bumwe.



Kuva ku **Igitabo**, ukande kuri buto **Gura**.



![screen](assets/fr/43.webp)



Hanyuma uhitemwo :





- gW-13 umutungo nimba wipfuza kuronka ama bitcoins ya on-chain;
- na Lightning Network kugira ngo baronke ama satoshis.



Muri iki gihe, uzoronka ubutumwa bukusaba guhindura uko wishura, kuko ari bwo bwa mbere ugerageza kugura.



Niba wibuka, narakubwiye ivy’iyindi nzira yo gutunganya uburyo bwo kwishura. Kanda **Bandanya** kugira ngo ukomeze n'iyi ntunganyo.



![screen](assets/fr/44.webp)



Kanda **Change** kugira usobanure wallet aho ama bitcoins yawe azogurishwa.



![screen](assets/fr/45.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/46.webp)



Fyonda kuri **Ongera uburyo** kugira uhitemwo uburyo bwo kwishura. Mu gihe cacu, tuzohitamwo kwishura kuri telefone ngendanwa. Urashobora kubona ko amafaranga y’ibikorwa vy’iyi nzira ashobora gushika kuri 2% y’amahera y’ugugura.



![screen](assets/fr/47.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/48.webp)



Ikindi, iri hitamwo risaba ko twongerako uwukoresha telefone ngendanwa.



![screen](assets/fr/49.webp)



Kanda kuri **Hindura** kugira ngo uhindure amafaranga yawe ya fiat.



![screen](assets/fr/50.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/51.webp)



Bingo! Ubu nyene warashoboye gutunganya neza uburyo bwo kwishura.



Uzoca urungikwa kuri iyi interface kugira ngo ukomeze igikorwa co kugura. Aha niho usobanura amahera y’ugugura. Igiciro gitoyi co kugura ni amafranga 5.000 y’ama CFA ku bijanye no kugura ama satoshis, n’amafaranga 100.000 y’ama CFA ku bijanye no kugura ama bitcoins ya on-chain. Hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya **Bandanya**.



![screen](assets/fr/52.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/53.webp)



Suzuma amakuru y'ivyo ugurisha hanyuma ukande kuri buto **Wemeze ugugura**.



![screen](assets/fr/54.webp)

![screen](assets/fr/55.webp)



Intambwe ya nyuma ni ukwinjiza ijambo banga rya konti yawe y’amahera yo kuri telefone ngendanwa kugira ngo uyikureko amahera uguze n’amahera y’ibikorwa vyo gusaba.


Nkako, ivyo bihembo vy’ibikorwa bigizwe n’amahera ava ku bandi bantu, cane cane abakoresha amatelefone ngendanwa, amahera y’uruja n’uruza (blockchain) n’amahera asabwa na Ejara ubwayo kugira ngo iyo nzira ikomeze gukoreshwa.
