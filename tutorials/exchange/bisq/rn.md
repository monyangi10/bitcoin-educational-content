## Ivyo gucuruza n’ugugura ibitari KYC (na Bitcoin Ibibazo n’Inyishu)


Nk’uko kugura kuri Bisq vyoroshe, hariho ibintu bimwe bimwe umuntu akwiye kumenya.


1. Ukeneye BTC imbere yo gucuruza kugira ngo ukore umutekano. Si ikibazo nimba umaze igihe ariko bishobora kuba ikibazo nimba ushaka gukoresha Bisq nk’ugugura kwawe kwa mbere. Ico ushobora guhitamwo muri iki gihe ni ukuronka Bitcoin ku mugenzi canke ku munyamuryango.


2. KYC free Bitcoin akenshi ikwegera amahera menshi hejuru y’igiciro c’ibintu bishobora gutuma abaguzi bamwebamwe basubira inyuma. Nk’uko nabivuze mbere, ivyo ni ivyerekeye uguhinduranya kandi jewe ubwanje noshima kwishura 5-8% hejuru y’igiciro c’aho nyene kubera ubutunzi bwo kubungabunga amakuru yanje bwite.


Niba utari mu kwihuta kugura rero urashobora kandi gushinga ‘Buy Offer’ aho ushobora kumenyesha urubuga rwa Bisq ko witeguriye kugura umubare w’ama Bitcoin ku giciro kinaka kijanye n’agaciro k’isoko. Iyo umucuruzi aje agakunda ivyo utanga barashobora kugutwara kuri vyo.


3. Amasoko yegerejwe rimwe na rimwe arashobora gushikirwa n’ubukene bw’amahera iyo agereranijwe n’amashirahamwe manini manini yegerejwe. Nimba uriko urarondera gugura amahera menshi kenshi, woshobora kugorwa.


4. Naho bitarigeze bimbaho, rimwe na rimwe ubucuruzi bw’urunganwe ntibugenda nk’uko vyari biteguwe kandi abakoresha bazokenera guca mu nzira yo gutorera umuti amatati. Igishimishije ni uko kubera uburyo inzego z’inzego za Bisq zubatswe, izo ngero ni nke cane.


Nk’uko bigenda ku bintu vyinshi bijanye na Bitcoin, impaka yose ya KYC/Non-KYC irafise utunenge kandi yuzuyemwo ivyiza. Nca ndabibona, fiat onramps zisanzwe nka Coinbase na CashApp zituma ubuzima bworoha cane kugura, cane cane ku bashasha, ariko ndagusavye ufate isegonda kugira ngo wiyumvire ivyo ushobora guhindura maze urabe ubundi buryo imbere yo gutanga amakuru yawe bwite bitagoranye.
