Kuza kwa Lightning Network kwabaye intango y’uruja n’uruza rwa Bitcoin, rutanga ubushobozi bwo guca bugufi kandi butazimvye kuruta uruja n’uruza rw’uruja n’uruza rwa Bitcoin (Mainnet).


Ubu, kuronka Bitcoin ni ingorane y’ubuhinga bushasha mu bihugu bitaratera imbere no/canke biri mu nzira y’amajambere, kugira ngo bisubize inyuma ukunanirwa kumwe kw’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubutunzi bwo mu karere.


Muri iyi nyigisho, turabona **Banxaas**, urubuga rwo guhinduranya amafaranga rutuma abanya Senegal begeranya amahinguriro ya Bitcoin.


## Gutangura na Banxaas



Banxaas rikomoka ku rurimi rw’ikinyasenegali (Wolof) bisobanura ishami. Mu vy’ingengabitekerezo, turashobora kubona Banxaas nk’ishami rihuza abanya Senegal n’ugukoresha Bitcoin. Ishami ushobora kwizigira kugira ngo ukinge amahera uzigamye inflation n’ugucengera. Yateguwe n’ishirahamwe ry’ubuhinga ryo muri Senegal (Yité Technologies), [Banxaas](https://banxaas.com) itanga ubuhinga bwa Exchange hagati ya Bitcoin n’ifaranga ya CFA (XOF) n’ibindi, bivuye ku bubasha bwa Lightning Network. Banxaas izana n’uburyo budasanzwe bwo gukoresha ama platforms ya Exchange mu karere k’uburengero bwa Afirika.




- Banxaas ni urubuga rudafise ububiko: ntukeneye gukora konti canke kugira Wallet kuri urwo rubuga. Uguma ugenzura amahera yawe, kandi ukutamenyekana kwawe kurakomera.

### Uguseruka ni co kintu nyamukuru c'ivyo bikorwa



Guseruka ni inkingi ihambaye kuri Banxaas, kugira ngo igabanye ugucengera no gutuma abakoresha umuti bashobora guca urubanza ku kwizigirwa kwawo.
