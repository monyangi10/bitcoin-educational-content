Ukwimurira abantu mu bindi bihugu muri Afirika bigereranya umutwe nyawo ku buntu Exchange y’ibicuruzwa n’ibikorwa. Mu guhangana n’ico kibazo, Bitcoin ni ubuhinga bushasha, iyo bushizwe mu rwego rwiza, bushobora gutorera umuti ingorane nyinshi zihanze imiryango ya Afirika, kuva ku busegaba bw’ivy’ubutunzi gushika ku Exchange y’agaciro ku buntu. Uyu musi, turiko turagenda kumenya BitSpenda, umugambi wa Bitcoin Dua, umuryango w’abanya Ghana uriko urakura ufise intumbero yo gutuma demokarasi ikoreshwa ku musi ku musi muri Bitcoin no kworohereza ubucuruzi bujabuka imipaka.



## Igitabo ca Genesis.

BitSpenda ni urubuga rw’amafaranga Exchange rugufasha guhindura hagati ya Bitcoin na Mobile Money ataco ugenzura, ata makuru y’ibanga kandi ata mahera. Biza gutorera umuti ingorane yihariye ikunda kuboneka muri Ghana ariko no mu bihugu vyinshi vyo muri Afirika: ukwishyura amahera ajabuka imipaka. BitSpenda, urashobora gukora ubucuruzi bwihuta, butazwi ushobora gukoresha mu bucuruzi bwawe bwa misi yose. Intumbero ihambaye ya BitSpenda ni ugutuma habaho ubucuruzi bugenda neza kugira ngo Bitcoin yemerwe kw’isi yose mu mibano y’Afrika.



## Gutangura na BitSpenda



BitSpenda ni urubuga rushingiye ku rubuga aho ushobora gukoresha Exchange Bitcoin na Mobile Money mu gihe uzigama ibanga ryawe. Nkako, BitSpenda ni umuhinduzi asaba ko ata konti, ata makuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe, kandi ata kugenzura akaranga: ni wewe gusa n’ivyo ukora.



![hero](assets/fr/01.webp)



Gukorana na BitSpenda ni ikintu gisanzwe, bisaba intambwe nke. Ku rubuga rwemewe rwa BitSpenda (https://bitspenda.app), fyonda ku buto "Tangura", uzoca urungikwa kuri Interface Exchange.



BitSpenda ubu ikora mu bihugu bitatu:




- Ghana n’ama Bitcoin ahindura ku nimero z’amahera y’amatelefone ngendanwa.
- Nijeriya biciye ku kohereza amahera kuri banki.
- Kenya biciye mu mafaranga y’amatelefone ngendanwa ya M-pesa.



![countries](assets/fr/02.webp)



Iyo ukoresheje ayo mahitamwo, biroroshe ko ukoresha Bitcoin uciye kuri Lightning Network kugira ngo wohereze amahera muri ivyo bihugu no kugura ibintu ku musi ku musi ata mafaranga yo mu gihugu.

### M-Pesa muri Kenya



BitSpenda ishingiye ku buhinga bubiri bw’ivy’ubutunzi n’ubuhinga: Bitcoin n’amahera y’amatelefone ngendanwa. Irya mbere rituma abakoresha basubira kuronka ubusegaba bwabo bw’ivy’ubutunzi mu gukora amafaranga y’uruyeri ata n’umwe abahuza n’ikigo gikuru, aho hose kw’isi kandi ubwo nyene biciye kuri Lightning Network Layer. Naho ica kabiri, kivuga ku ngorane y’urugero ruto cane rw’ukwinjira kw’amabanki mu mibano ya Afirika.



BitSpenda ikoresha amahera muri Kenya biciye muri M-Pesa, ubuhinga buzwi cane bwo gukoresha amahera y’amatelefone ngendanwa mu gihugu.


Kubera ingamba nka BitSpenda, uno musi urashobora gukoresha Bitcoin kugira wishyure mu iduka, urye mu restora, wishyure inzu n’amafaranga yawe.



Hitamwo Kenya nk’igihugu uzojako, hanyuma wuzuze inomero ya M-pesa y’uwuguhaye n’amahera uwo muntu akwiye kuronka.

## Gumenyekanisha Bitcoin muri Afirika



Biciye muri BitSpenda, [Bitcoin Dua] igamije gushinga urutonde rwiza rwo kwemera Bitcoin muri Afirika. BitSpenda igaragara cane cane kuri :




- Ibanga**: Ntukeneye gukora konti kuri platform, ntukeneye kugenzura akaranga kawe, kandi ntukeneye gutanga amakuru y’ibanga.
- Umwidegemvyo**: Ushobora gukoresha BitSpenda uvuye mu gihugu ico ari co cose kugira ngo ukore amafaranga muri Ghana, Nijeriya na Kenya.
- Ivyihuta**: Kwizigira amahera y’umuravyo vyatumye BitSpenda igabanya igihe co gukora no gutuma amafaranga akoreshwa mu kanya nk’ako gukubita.
- Uguseruka**: Ibikorwa vyose wakoze kuri BitSpenda bifitaniye isano n’ibimenyetso bidasanzwe bigufasha gukurikirana no gukurikirana ingene ivyo bikorwa bigenda. Ushobora kandi gukoresha ivyo bimenyetso kugira ngo ushikire [ubufasha bw'ibikorwa kuri X](https://x.com/bitspenda).



Ubu ushobora gukoresha Bitcoin ku musi ku musi mu bihugu bikoreshwa na BitSpenda.



Ibindi bikorwa vyinshi n’imiti nka BitSpenda biriko biraseruka mu mibano y’Afrika: turabatumiye kugira ngo muvumbure Banxaas, umuti w’abanyasenegal wo gukoresha Bitcoin ku musi ku musi.
