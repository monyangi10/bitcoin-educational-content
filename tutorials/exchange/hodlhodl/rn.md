## Tangira urugendo rwawe kuri HODL HODL.



![image](assets/5.webp)


HodlHodl ni amafaranga y’urunganwe (P2P) Exchange, ashigikira gusa amafaranga imwe y’ubuhinga bwa none ariko atanga ubushobozi bwo gucuruza mu mafaranga ayo ari yo yose, ashiramwo amafaranga yose y’ubuhinga bwa none n’amafaranga y’ubuhinga bwa none. Ikintu gihambaye kuri iyi platform ya P2P ni uko idasaba amafaranga yo gushiramwo canke yo gukura, ahubwo isaba amafaranga y’ubudandaji kuva kuri 0.5% gushika kuri 0.6%, bivanye n’ingene umukoresha agenzurwa. Abakoresha barafise akamaro k’uburyo bwinshi bwo kwishura, ivyo bikaba bituma HodlHodl iba imwe mu nzira zitandukanye cane za P2P. Bimwe mu buryo bwo kwishura bukoreshwa cane ni SWIFT, SEPA, gukoresha amahera muri banki, gukoresha ikarita y’inguzanyo, PayPal, Venmo, MoneyGram, na Western Union. Ariko rero, uburyo bwo kwishura buriho buvana n’uko uwugurisha yemera.


Kimwe mu bintu bihambaye vya HodlHodl ni imipaka y’ubudandaji ihinduka, ishobora gutegurwa buhoro buhoro n’ukwiyongera kw’ubudandaji n’ubudandaji bugenda neza. Igiciro gitoyi c’ubudandaji bwa BTC gishirwa kuri 0.001 BTC, kandi inguzanyo ntoyi cane ntikwiye kuba munsi ya 50 USD mu gaciro ka crypto. Iryo koraniro ntirikeneye uburyo ubwo ari bwo bwose bwo kumenya umukiriya wawe (KYC) canke uburyo bwo kurwanya ugukoresha nabi amahera (AML) mu gukurikiza ingingo ngenderwako zaryo zo kubungabunga amazina y’abakoresha. Ariko rero, abakoresha bahisemwo kugenzura barashobora kuronka inyungu nk'ugugabanya amakomisiyo, kwongerekana kw'imipaka y'ibikorwa, n'ikibanza kigaragara c'uko "Bagenzuwe". HodlHodl irafise uruhara runini kw’isi yose, ishobora kwakira abakoresha bo mu bihugu hafi vyose uretse bikeyi nka Koreya ya Ruguru, Iraki, Siriya, na Sudani.


### Gukora konti


Kugira ngo ukore konti yawe bwite, ushobora gufyonda gusa kuri 'Register', iri mu mfuruka iburyo kuri paji y'intango y'urubuga. Nk’uko vyitezwe, uzosabwa kwinjiza imeyili Address, uhitemwo izina ry’ukoresha, kandi ushireho ijambobanga ryo kwishura. Ikindi, iyo nzira iragusaba gusobanura isaha yawe yo mu karere kuko ivyo bifasha gutuma amasaha yawe y’ibikorwa agenda neza kandi bikongera uburyo bwo gukorana.

### Guhindura umwirondoro wawe


Iyo umaze kwandika neza konti yawe, ikintu gikurikira coba ari ugutanga urutonde rwawe amakuru amwamwe akenewe.


Guhindura umwirondoro wawe bikwiye kubamwo:



- Amakuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe – Ivyo birimwo izina ry’ukoresha wahisemwo, imeyili, n’inkuru ngufi y’ubuzima bwawe.
- Ivyemezo vy’Ibintu Bibiri (2FA) – Gukoresha 2FA bituma umutekano w’urutonde rwawe urushiriza kuba mwiza, ivyo bikaba ari ibintu vyama ari ikintu c’ubwenge.
- API Access – Ivyo si ngombwa kandi biguma bidakora ku buryo busanzwe.
- Ivyerekeye P2P vya kera – Niba warakoranye n’izindi nzira za P2P mbere, guhuza aya makuru birashobora kwongera icizigiro cawe mu bandi bacuruzi.


### Iyandikishe BTC yawe Address.


Kugira ngo utangure gucuruza BTC, uzokenera gutanga BTC Address yawe mbere. Ivyo bishobora kwinjira mu rutonde rwa "Ivyagezwe vy'Ubucuruzi". Uhejeje gushiramwo Address yawe, urashobora gukomeza "Gura BTC" canke "Gurisha BTC" kugira ngo utangure urugendo rwawe rwo gucuruza.

Igikoresho co kurondera kiratwereka ivyo dushobora kugura ku buryo busanzwe.


Niba dushaka kuguriza dutegerezwa guhitamwo buto ihuye.


Twinjiza amahera dushaka kugurana n’amahera y’ibanga (cryptocurrency) turiko turarondera.


Igitigiri ca LTV kivuga ijana kw’ijana ry’amahera y’ibanga tuzoronka twisunze agaciro kari muri BTC dushiramwo nk’ingwati. Uko LTV igenda irakura, niko n’amahera dugura aba menshi ugereranije n’agaciro muri BTC dushiramwo nk’ingwati, ariko ubushobozi bw’uko ingwati yacu (BTC) izosenyuka na bwo burarushiriza kuba bwinshi iyo igiciro cayo kigabanutse. Nitwahitamwo LTV iri hasi, amahera twiguriza ugereranije n’agaciro kari muri BTC dushiramwo nk’ingwati na yo nyene azoba ari make, kandi nta n’amahirwe make y’uko ingwati yacu (BTC) yoshobora gusenyuka iyo igiciro cayo kigabanutse.


Ubwa nyuma duhitamwo igihe c’inguzanyo twiteguriye gufata. Ni ukuvuga ko uwuduha inguzanyo azomara igihe kingana iki aduha amahera twasavye, kandi hazoshirwaho itariki ya nyuma yo kuyagarura. Inguzanyo ishobora gusubirwamwo yose ku musi igihe c’inguzanyo kizohera canke biciye mu kwishura igice mu kiringo cose ca Contract.


Nitwafyonda kuri buto “Rondera ivyo dutanga”, iyo nzira izodusubiza ivyo dutanga vyose bihuye n’ivyo turondera, maze tuzoshobora guhitamwo ivyo dukunda kuruta ibindi vyose.
