## Intangamarara ya Relai


Relai ni porogaramu yoroshe kandi ishoboka yo gufasha abantu n’ubudandaji gushiramwo amahera muri Bitcoin ata ngorane. Waba uri mushasha muri Bitcoin canke umushoramari azi utuntu n’utundi, Interface ya Relai ikoreshwa neza n’ibintu vyiza bituma vyoroha gutangura no kubungabunga umugambi wo kuzigama Bitcoin. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibibanza vya kera, Relai ntibisaba gutahura cane Bitcoin canke uburyo butoroshe bwo gukoresha amahera—ushobora gukuraho gusa iyo porogarama maze ugatangura kuzigama.


Relai igaragara kubera ko yoroshe, ishobora gushikirwa, kandi yibanda ku kuzigama Bitcoin mu kiringo kirekire. Waba ushaka gushinga umugambi wo kuzigama usubiramwo canke ugura ibintu rimwe gusa, Relai iragufashije. Reka turabe ingene woshobora gutangura n’iki gikoresho ciza cane.

## Gutangura na Relai


### Intambwe ya 1: Gukuraho porogarama ya Relai



- Aho woyisanga:** Iyi porogarama iboneka ku maduka y’amaporogarama (iOS) no ku maduka y’amaporogarama (Android). Rondera “Relai” maze uyikure ku buntu.
- Gutegura vyihuse:** Iyo umaze gushiramwo, fungura app hanyuma ukurikize uburyo bwo gutegura burongowe.


### Intambwe ya 2: Rema konti yawe



- KYC itegekanijwe:** Relai ubu isaba ko umenya umukiriya wawe (KYC). Gutegura inyandiko y’indangamuntu n’ikimenyamenya ca Address.
- Uburyo bwo kugenzura:** Shirako ivyangombwa vyawe, ufate selfie kugira ngo wemeze akaranga kawe, hanyuma urindire kwemezwa (mu bisanzwe mu masaha 24).
- Kubera iki KYC?:** Ituma amategeko agenga ivy’ubutunzi yubahirizwa kandi igatuma abakoresha bose bagira umutekano.


### Intambwe ya 3: Gukingira Wallet yawe.



- Ukwirinda:** Relai itanga Wallet idashobora kwirinda, bisobanura ko ari wewe ugenzura imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga.
- Backup Your Keys:** Andika amajambo yawe yo gusubizaho amajambo 12 ku mpapuro (atari mu buryo bwa digitale) maze uyabike neza. Ivyo ni ngirakamaro kugira ngo ushobore kuronka amahera yawe iyo telefone yawe yazimiye canke yibwe.

## Uko wokora umugambi wo kuzigama Bitcoin


Gahunda yo kuzigama ya Relai’s Bitcoin iragufasha gukoresha ubuhinga bwo gushiramwo amahera, bikaba vyoroshe kwubaka ubutunzi uko igihe kigenda kirarenga. Ehe ingene woshiraho imwe:


### Intambwe ya 1: Sigura Intumbero Zawe



- Fata ingingo y’ingene Bitcoin ushaka kwirundanira no mu kiringo ikihe.
- Tangana muto nimba uri mushasha muri Bitcoin; urashobora kwama wongera intererano zawe mu nyuma.


### Intambwe ya 2: Gushinga Ishoramari risubiramwo



- Genda ku gice kivuga ngo “Itegeko ryo kuzigama” muri porogarama.
- Hitamwo incuro wipfuza gushiramwo amahera (nk’akarorero, buri ndwi, buri ndwi zibiri, buri kwezi).
- Sobanura amahera ushaka gushiramwo. Relai yemera kwishura guhera ku €10.


### Intambwe ya 3: Huza uburyo bwo kwishura



- Koresha konti yawe ya banki canke ikarita y’inguzanyo ukunda kugira ngo ushireho amahera asubiramwo.
- Raba neza ko banki yawe ishigikira ugutanga amahera kwa SEPA kugira ngo amafaranga akore neza.


### Intambwe ya 4: Gukurikirana ingene utera imbere



- Suzuma Wallet yawe ubudasiba muri app kugira ubone ingene amafaranga yawe ariko arakura.
- Guhindura umugambi wawe igihe cose kugira ngo uhure n’imigambi yawe y’ivy’amahera.
