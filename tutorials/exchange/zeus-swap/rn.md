![cover](assets/cover.webp)



Ivyuma vy’ibidukikije vya Bitcoin biratanga ububiri: urubuga nyamukuru (On-Chain) rutanga umutekano mwinshi, mu gihe Lightning Network ishobora gutuma umuntu ashobora gukora amafaranga ako kanya. Ubwo buhinga bw’ubwubatsi bw’ama Layer abiri buratuma haba ingorane ngirakamaro: amahera yoshobora gute guhererekanwa neza hagati y’izo nzira zibiri ata bahuza bari hagati?



Ikibazo ni nyaco: uronka amahera ya Lightning ariko ushaka kuyagumya mu bubiko bwa Cold, canke ibihushanye n’ivyo, ufise ama bitcoins ya On-Chain ariko ukeneye amahera ya Lightning. Inyishu za kera zijanye n’ugufungura/gufunga n’amaboko imirongo ya Lightning (izimvye kandi y’ubuhinga) canke ama platforms ahuriweko asaba KYC.



Zeus Swap itorera umuti ico kibazo ikoresheje ubuhinga bwikora, butagira uwubucungera Exchange. Yateguwe na Zeus LSP, iragufasha guhindura amafaranga y’ama bitcoins ya On-Chain mu ma Lightning satoshis mu buryo bubiri, utashize amahera yawe ku muntu agufasha. Ivyo bikoresha amasezerano y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa atomike (HTLC) yemeza ko Exchange izoheza canke izosubira inyuma.



Ico gishasha kiri mu buryo bworoshe: gukanda gatoyi gusa kugira ngo ubone Exchange izigama ubusegaba bwawe bw’ivy’amahera, ata kwiyandikisha canke KYC bisabwa.



## None Zewu Swap ni iki?



Zeus Swap ni ubuhinga bwo guhindura amafaranga Exchange bwateguwe na Zeus LSP bushobora guhindura atome hagati y'urubuga rwa Bitcoin na Lightning Network. Ni ibikorwa remezo vy’ubuhinga bikoresha ubuhinga bwo guhindura ibintu mu mazi yo mu mazi n’ubuhinga bwo guhindura ibintu mu mazi kugira ngo bishobore guhindura inzira zibiri hagati ya On-Chain BTC na Lightning satoshis, mu gihe bizigama kamere y’ibikorwa bitagiramwo ububiko.



### Ubwubatsi bw'ubuhinga



Zeus Swap ikoresha ubuhinga bwa Boltz bwo guhindura atome Bitcoin/Lightning. Iryo tegeko rikoresha Hash Amasezerano y’Igihe (HTLC): amasezerano y’ugufunga amahera afise ivyangombwa bibiri vyo kurekura (uguhishura ibanga ry’ibanga canke igihe co guhera).



Ku bijanye n’uguhinduranya ubwato bwo mu mazi (On-Chain → Umuravyo), uwukoresha yohereza amafaranga kuri Address irimwo Hash y’umuravyo Invoice. Zeus LSP ifungura ayo mahera gusa mu kwishura Invoice ihuye, igahishura ishusho y’imbere ifungura ubwo nyene ama bitcoins. Ubwo buryo buratuma haba atome.



Ku bijanye n’uguhindura (Umuravyo → On-Chain), uwukoresha ariha Umuravyo Invoice uva kuri Zeus LSP, yerekana ishusho y’imbere ishoboza gusohora igikorwa ca Bitcoin categuwe gushika aho Address ija.



Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo Lightning Network ikora, raba inyigisho yacu yihariye :



https://planb.academy/courses/34bd43ef-6683-4a5c-b239-7cb1e40a4aeb

### Ubuhinga bwo gukora ubucuruzi



Zeus LSP ikora nk’umuhinguzi w’isoko, ibungabunga amahera ya On-Chain na Lightning kugira ngo yubahirize amahinduka. Ku bijanye n’uguhinduranya, Zeus ikoresha amahera ahinduka (mu bisanzwe 0.1% gushika kuri 0.5% bivanye n’inzira n’imimerere) yongerako amahera ya Bitcoin ya Mining, yerekanwa mu buryo buboneka imbere y’uko yemezwa.



Nk’Umutanga Serivisi y’Imiravyo, Zeus iratuma ibiciro bigenda neza kubera ubuhinga bwayo bwo gufungura imirongo y’amazi ku vyo umuntu asaba, gutanga inzira nziza no gutorera umuti ibibazo vy’amahera.



### Ubufatanye



Zeus Wallet ihuriza hamwe iyo serivisi, igatuma umuntu ashobora guhindura ataco avuye kuri Interface Bitcoin/Umuravyo. Ivyo bikuraho uguhuzagurika kwo gukopa no gushiramwo hagati y’ibikoresho.



Urubuga rwigenga rwa Interface ruraguma rushobora gushikirwa n’amasakoshi yose, rugatuma umuntu ashobora gukoresha neza.



## Ibirango nyamukuru



### Guhinduranya inzira zibiri



Zeus Swap itanga ubwoko bubiri bwa Exchange:



**Imirongo y’amazi yo mu mazi (On-Chain → Lightning)**: ushiremwo amazi y’umuravyo avuye mu bikoresho vyawe vya Bitcoin, bifise akamaro mu kugaburira Wallet canke Lightning node igendagenda ata gufungura imirongo n’amaboko.



**Impinduka zihinduka (Umuravyo → On-Chain)**: guhindura satoshis z’Umuravyo mu bitcoins za On-Chain kugira ngo zibikwe igihe kirekire, wirinde gufunga imirongo bizimvye.



### Ibigaragara vy'ukoresha



**Interface web** (swaps.zeuslsp.com): ubumenyi bworoshe ata kwiyandikisha, uburyo burongowe n’iyerekanwa ry’igihe nyaco ry’amahera n’aho umuntu ari.



**Zeus Wallet integration**: guhinduranya ataco bimaze biva ku gikorwa, gucunga neza amafagitire n’amaderesi, gukuraho amakosa yo gufata.



### Umutekano n'ugukira



Buri guhindura bitanga Contract yihariye ifise ibipimo bidahinduka: Hash Umuravyo, igihe co guhera, gusubiza Address. Iyo bishitse, gusubirana kwihuta biciye ku Address vyatanzwe, bitandukanye na Zeus LSP.



**Zeus Swaps Rescue Key**: mu gihe c’uguhindura On-Chain → Lightning, Zeus ahita atanga urufunguzo rwo gusubizaho rwose rusubirira amadosiye ya kera y’ugusubiza amahera y’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Urufunguzo rudasanzwe rukora ku gikoresho cose no ku bihinduka vyose vyakozwe na rwo. Ni ngombwa cane gukuraho no kubika urufunguzo ahantu hatagira inkomanzi kugira ngo ushobore gusubirana amahera yawe iyo swap idashoboka.



### Gutuma urubuga rugenda neza



Zeus Swap ihita ihindura igihe co guhera n’amahera ya Mining bivanye n’ingene urubuga rumeze. Abakoresha Zeus bungukira ku mahitamwo ateye imbere: guhitamwo LSP, gucerezwa ku buryo bubereye, guhuza n’ibindi bikorwa (Boltz).

## Inyungu n'aho bigarukira



### Inyungu





- Ukworohereza**: Guhindura mu gukanda bike n'ugukoresha umurongo w'amaboko
- Ntaco bimaze**: nta KYC, nta konti, amahera ntiyigeze ashikirizwa uwundi muntu
- Uguseruka**: amafaranga yerekanwa neza imbere y’uko yemezwa (0.1% gushika kuri 0.5% + minage bivanye n’ibipimo vy’abakoresha - suzuma amafaranga ariho ubu ku guhinduranya kwose)
- Ugushiramwo telefone ngendanwa**: ubumenyi bw'imvukira muri Zeus Wallet



### Ivyo bigarukira





- Igihe co guhera**: 24-48h n'ibindi, kunanirwa iyo Bitcoin itaremejwe ku gihe
- Igitigiri c'amafaranga**: 25.000 Sats, Zeus LSP amafaranga ahinduka hakurikijwe ivyangombwa
- Ivyiyumviro vya On-Chain**: Inyandiko za HTLC zishobora kumenyekana biciye ku gusesangura kwa Blockchain
- Kwemeza birakenewe**: iminota 10 kugira ngo Bitcoin yemezwe

## Kugereranya n'ibindi bikorwa vyo guhindura



### Zewu yahinduye na Boltz Exchange



Zeus Swap ikoresha ubuhinga bwa Boltz, ariko iragira ivyo ihinduye bihambaye:



**Ivyiza vyo guhindura Zewu** :




- Interface yunze ubumwe**: ubufatanye bw'imvukira muri Zewu Wallet n'ubuhinga bw'urubuga rwa Interface Boltz
- WebSocket API**: ivugurura ry'igihe nyaco n'itora ry'amaboko
- Ubuyobozi bwikora**: gutanga amafaranga bwikora n'ubuyobozi bwa Address
- Gufasha telefone ngendanwa**: telefone ngendanwa n'ugutuma mudasobwa igenda neza gusa
- Inyandiko za Swagger**: yuzuye REST API ku bategura



**Boltz iguma ari ngirakamaro** ku kwigenga kwose no gukoresha n'imiterere yose ya Bitcoin/Lightning.



Zeus Swap ihindura ubuhinga bwa Boltz bwagaragaye bukaba ubumenyi busanzwe bw’abakoresha, bugereranywa n’itandukaniro hagati y’umurongo w’ubuhinga butagiramwo ivyiza n’ubuhinga bukoreshwa neza.



### Zewu guhinduranya na Phoenix/Umuyaga (uguhinduranya guhuriweko)



Phoenix na Breez zishiramwo ibikorwa vy’uguhinduranya biboneka bihisha ubuhinga butoroshe ku muntu akoresha. Phoenix ikoresha uburyo bwo guhinduranya/gusohoka aho uwukoresha adatandukanya neza hagati y'ibice vya Bitcoin: "arungika ku Bitcoin Address" maze porogarama igakora ivyo guhindura mu nyuma.



Ubu buryo bworoshe cane burabereye cane abatangura, ariko buragabanya ugutahura n’ukugenzura ibikorwa. Zeus Swap ifata ubuhinga bwo kwigisha cane: abakoresha baratahura ko bariko barahinduranya hagati y’ibice bibiri bitandukanye, buhoro buhoro bagatera imbere mu gutahura ibidukikije vy’ibidukikije bibiri-Layer Bitcoin.

## Iciyumviro



Zeus Swap igereranya ubuhinga bushasha mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin, itorera umuti neza ingorane y'ugukorana hagati y'urubuga nyamukuru na Lightning Network. Mu gufatanya ubukomezi bw’ubuhinga bwa none bw’uguhinduranya atome n’ubumenyi bw’abakoresha bushikira, iyo serivisi ituma uburongozi bwa Bitcoin dual-Layer bugira demokarasi ataco buhinduye ku ngingo ngenderwako z’ubusegaba bw’ivy’ubutunzi.



Ubwubatsi bwa Zeus Swap butagiramwo ububiko, burazwe n’ubuhinga bwa Boltz bwagaragaye, buratuma amahera yawe aguma ari munsi y’ububasha bwawe wenyene mu gihe cose c’uguhindura. Ubu buryo burawubaha impwemu ya Bitcoin mu gihe butanga uburyo bwo gukoresha busabwa kugira ngo abantu benshi bashobore kwemerwa. Gutanga ibiciro mu mucyo no kutagira uburyo bwo gutanga amakuru (KYC) birakomeza iyo nzira y’agaciro yihariye.



Ku muntu akoresha Bitcoin muri iki gihe, Zeus Swap ni igikoresho c’ingene yotuma amafaranga agabanywa neza bivanye n’ivyo akeneye: On-Chain ububiko butekanye bwo kuzigama igihe kirekire, Ukuboneka kwa Lightning ku gukoresha amahera ku musi ku musi n’ugukora ibikorwa bitobito. Ukwo guhinduranya guhindura uburongozi bwa Bitcoin kuva ku nzitizi y’ubuhinga bukaja ku ntumbero y’uguhiganwa.



Iterambere ry’imbere rya Zeus Swap, rishigikiwe n’umugwi wa Zeus LSP ufise ubumenyi n’umuryango wa Boltz open-source, risezerana ugukomeza gutera imbere mu bijanye n’ibiciro, igihe co gukora n’ubumenyi bw’abakoresha. Iyi serivisi iri mu nzira yagutse yo gukura kw’ibikorwa remezo vya Bitcoin, aho ubuhinga buteye imbere buca bugaragara ku muntu akoresha.
