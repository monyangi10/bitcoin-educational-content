LN Markets ni urubuga rwa mbere rw’ubudandaji rwa Bitcoin rwo mu vy’ukuri rwa Lightning, ruguha uburenganzira bwo gucuruza ibintu biva kuri Bitcoin biva kuri wallet Lightning yawe, ata KYC, gutanga amafaranga ako kanya n’ugucungera bike. Yatangujwe mu 2020, irakuraho amakimbirane y’ubucuruzi bwa kera: nta kugenzura akaranga, nta gushiramwo amafaranga abujijwe, nta kurindira ivyemezo vy’ubuhinga bwa blockchain. wallet Lightning yawe iba konti yawe y’ubudandaji.



## LN Markets ni iki?



LN Markets itanga **Futures** (amasezerano ahoraho afise ubushobozi bushika kuri 100×) na **Options** (Call/Put afise ingorane zigarukira ku giciro c’amahera yishuwe). Iryo koraniro rikora nk’uruzitiro rw’ugukoranya amafaranga rwo gukoranya ibibanza vyinshi vy’ubudandaji kugira ngo hakorwe neza zero-slippage. Amahera yawe afungwa gusa mu kiringo nyaco c’ibikorwa vyawe, atari imisi canke amayinga nk’uko bigenda ku konti y’ububiko isanzwe.



### Ibicuruzwa vy'ubudandaji biraboneka



**Ivya kazoza (amasezerano y'ibihe bidahera)**



Amasezerano y’ibihe bidahera ni amasezerano y’imbere ata musi azoherako, bigufasha guca irya n’ino igiciro ca Bitcoin ukoresheje ubuhinga bwo gukoresha. LN Markets itanga uburyo bubiri bwo gucunga amafaranga:



**Isolated mode**: Ikibanza cose gifise uruhande rwaco rwihariye. Amahera yonyene agenewe ico kibanza kidasanzwe ni yo ari mu kaga. Iyo iyo position ishitse kugiciro co gusesa, **iki kibanza conyene ni co gisesa**, ibindi bibanza vyawe n’amahera asigaye ntibigira ico bikozeko. Ni vyiza kugabanya cane ingorane ku bucuruzi.



**Uburyo bw'umusaraba (Urubibi rw'umusaraba)** : Urubibi rusangizwa hagati y'ibibanza vyawe vyose vyuguruye. Ivyo ufise vyose kuri konti yawe bikora nk’ingwati y’ibibanza vyawe vyose. Iyo ikibanza kigenda nabi, iyo sisitemu ikoresha amahera yawe yose ufise kugira ngo wirinde gusenyuka. **Risk**: iyo balance yawe yose iheze, positions zawe zose zishobora gusenyuka icarimwe. Iremezwa gusa ku bacuruzi bazi utuntu n’utundi barondera gukoresha neza umutungo.



**Uburongozi bw'imyanya** :





- Igitigiri kirekire**: utera imbere ku kwiyongera kwa BTC/USD. Iyo igiciro kiduze, ni wewe utsinda; iyo iguye, uratakaza. **Akarorero**: Bitcoin ku madolari 100.000, ufungura Long ufise sats 10.000 n’ubushobozi bwo gukoresha 10×. Iyo Bitcoin iduze ikagera ku madolari 105.000 (+5%), ikibanza cawe kiraronka 50% (5% × 10), ni ukuvuga ~5.000 sats inyungu. Iyo Bitcoin igabanutse ku madolari 95.000 (-5%), utakaza 50%, ni ukuvuga gutakaza ~5.000 sats.





- Igitigiri kigufi**: utera intambwe ku BTC/USD igwa. Iyo igiciro kigabanutse, ni wewe utsinda; iyo iduze, uratakaza. **Akarorero**: Bitcoin ku madolari 100.000, ufungura short ufise 10.000 sats n’10× leverage. Iyo Bitcoin igabanutse ku madolari 95.000 (-5%), utsinda 50%, ni ukuvuga ~5.000 sats. Iyo Bitcoin idutse ikagera ku madolari 105.000 (+5%), uratakaza 50%.



Leverage (gushika kuri 100×) ikomeza inyungu n’ibihombo bihuye. Uburyo bwa **igipimo c’amahera** (amafaranga y’igihe cose amasaha 8 yose) buraringaniza ivyicaro birebire n’ibigufi. Ushobora gutwara ibibanza bishika 100 vy’imbere icarimwe.



**Amahitamwo**



Ihitamwo ni nk’**itike ya loteri ifise itariki y’iherezo**. Urishura amahera y’imbere kugira ngo ubeshe ku nzira y’igiciro ca Bitcoin. **Ivyiza bikomeye**: ntushobora kwigera utakaza ibirenze amahera y’imbere y’igihe warishe, nta gusesagura bishoboka.





- Ihitamwo ryo guhamagara (gutera imbere)**: Utera imbere ko Bitcoin izoduga hejuru y’igitero imbere y’uko ihera. Utsinda iyo igiciro kiduze, igihombo kigarukira ku giciro c’imbere iyo igiciro kigabanutse.





- Gushiramwo Ihitamwo (bet bearish)**: Utera ko Bitcoin izogwa munsi y’igitero. Utsinda iyo igiciro kigabanutse, igihombo kigarukira ku giciro c’imbere iyo igiciro kiduze.





- Straddle (bet y’uguhinduka)**: Ugura Call AND a Put icarimwe. Utsinda iyo Bitcoin ikoze urugendo runini mu nzira iyo ariyo yose, utakaza izo premiums zompi iyo igiciro kiguma kidahinduka.



Igitigiri: Ivyicaro 50 vy’igihe kimwe. Ni vyiza gutangura ubucuruzi bushingiye ku nguvu ata bwoba bwo gusenyuka.



**sats ↔ sUSD**: Ubwo nyene uhindure satoshis zawe mu madolari y’ubuhinga (sUSD) kugira ngo wikingire uguhinduka, canke ibihushanye n’ivyo kugira ngo wongere ubone Bitcoin.

### Amahitamwo y'ubudandaji: uburenganzira bwo kugura



Amahitamwo atanga **akaga gatoyi** ku giciro c’imbere, ata gusesagura gushoboka. A Call iguha uburenganzira (si inshingano) bwo kugura Bitcoin kugiciro c’iterabwoba imbere y’uko igihe kirangira. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ivy’imbere, ntushobora kwigera utakaza ibirenze amahera y’imbere yashizwemwo.



Kuva ku Dashboard, ukande kuri "Amahitamwo", hanyuma uhitemwo "Call" tab.



![Interface Options Call](assets/fr/19.webp)



Gutunganya ubucuruzi bwawe ukoresheje ibi bikurikira:




- Igitigiri**: $100 (ubunini bw'amasezerano yawe)
- Igihe co guhera** : 2025-11-15 (itariki yo guhera)
- Igitero**: $96,000 (igiciro c'intumbero)



Ibindi bibanza biharurwa ubwavyo:




- Igiciro**: 1.045 sats (amahera y’imbere, ishoramari ryawe)
- Ivyiza**: $96,923 (igiciro co gusubirana umugabane wawe)
- Delta**: 40 (Igiciro ca Bitcoin)



**Uzoharura gute intsinzi yawe?**



Inyungu yawe ivana n’igiciro ca Bitcoin igihe kizohera. Igishushanyo: **(Igiciro ca BTC - Igitero) × Ingano ya Contract - Igiciro ca mbere carishe**.



**Ingero zitomoye** :





- Bitcoin ku madolari 96.000** (igiciro c'ubu) : Agaciro k'imbere = $0. **Ugutakaza: -1.045 sats** (utakaza igiciro)





- Bitcoin ku madolari 97.000**: Agaciro k'imbere = (97.000 - 96.000) × 0,00105 BTC = amadolari 1,05. Yahinduwe ikaba sats ≈ 2.175 sats. **Inyungu: 2,175 - 1,045 = +1,130 sats** (+108% inyungu)





- Bitcoin ku madolari 98.000**: agaciro k'imbere = amadolari 2.000 ≈ 3.224 sats. **Inyungu: +2.179 sats** (+208% inyungu)





- Bitcoin ku madolari 100.000**: agaciro k'imbere = amadolari 4.000 ≈ 5.263 sats. **Inyungu: +4.218 sats** (+403% inyungu)





- Bitcoin munsi y’amadolari 96.000**: Ihitamwo rihera ata co rimaze. **Igihombo gitoyi: -1.045 sats**, nta gusesagura gushoboka



Fyonda kuri "Gura Hamagara". Agasanduku k'ikiganiro k'ukwemeza karaboneka:

**Iciyumviro ku mahitamwo yo gushiramwo** : Ico gikorwa ni kimwe n'ico guhamagara, ariko mu buryo buhindutse. Na Put, urabesha kugiciro ca Bitcoin kija **manuka**. Iyo Bitcoin iguye munsi y’igitero cawe, uratsinda; iyo igumye hejuru, utakaza gusa amahera y’imbere y’igihe warishe. Ibara ry’inyungu rikurikira iyo nzira nyene: **(Igitero - Igiciro ca BTC) × Ubunini bwa Contract - Premium yishuwe**.

## Ivyago n'imigenzo myiza



**Ivyago nyamukuru** :




- Igitigiri cose c'amafaranga**: amafaranga menshi ashobora gukuraho 100% y'amafaranga mu minota mikeyi
- Igerageza ry'ibikorwa**: igice ca alpha, ukudakeka kw'ubuhinga
- Ico kibazo c'umugenzi**: urubuga ruguma ari umugenzi umwe



**Ibikorwa vyiza** :



1. **Ugucunga umutungo**: ushireho ubuhinga bwo gucunga ingorane zijanye n’ivyo ukora. Nk’akarorero: ushire 5% vy’itunga ryawe ryose ku bucuruzi bushingiye ku nkunga, hanyuma ushire mu kaga 1% y’uwo mutungo ku bucuruzi (nk’akarorero: umutungo 1 wa BTC → 5M sats ku bucuruzi → 50k sats max risk ku kibanza)



2. **Systematic stop-loss**: gutegura guhagarika gutakaza kugira ngo ugabanye ibihombo vyawe ku bucuruzi. Nk’akarorero, n’itegeko ry’ingorane rya 1%, mbere n’ubudandaji 10 bukurikiranye butakaza bugereranya gusa 10% vy’umutungo kawe wo gucuruza .



3. **Tangira bike**: banza ugerageze n’ibihumbi bikeyi vy’ama satss kugira ngo utahure uburyo bikora imbere yo gukoresha ubuhinga bwawe bwo gucunga umutungo



4. **Uhora ukura inyungu zawe**: ushire inyungu zawe kuri wallet yawe nyamukuru, usige gusa umutungo w’ubudandaji ukora ku rubuga



5. **Urabe neza ko ukoresha amafaranga**: wirinde gukoresha amafaranga >20× kiretse uzi neza ingorane zo gusenyuka



**Ibiciro**: nta mafaranga yo gushiramwo/gukura amahera y’umuravyo, ikwiragizwa ~0.1% ku bucuruzi (kugwa ku 0.06% bivanye n’ubunini).

## Iciyumviro



LN Markets ifise iterambere rikomeye ry’ubudandaji bwa Bitcoin mu gushiramwo Lightning Network kugira ngo isubire gucungera abakoresha. Ku ba bitcoiners b’abanyabwenge barondera guca irya n’ino ataco bahinduye ku busegaba bwabo, ni uburyo bwihariye bwo guhindura amafaranga y’ubuhinga bwa kera.



Iryo koraniro rirabandanya gutera imbere n’ubudandaji bw’umurongo w’amadolari y’Amerika (USDT) n’ubudandaji butagiramwo ububiko biciye ku masezerano y’ivya kera (DLC) ariko arategurwa. Mu gukoresha uburyo bwiza bwo gucunga ingorane (amahera make, guhagarika gutakaza, gukura amahera ubudasiba), LN Markets iba igikoresho gikomeye co gutohoza uburyo Bitcoin ikoresha mu buryo bubereye.



Tangana n’ibintu bitobito, ugerageze n’ibihumbi bikeyi vy’ama sats, hanyuma buhoro buhoro ugende ugendere kuri iyi frontier nshasha ya Lightning Network. Ubucuruzi bwiza bw'ubusegaba ⚡️!
