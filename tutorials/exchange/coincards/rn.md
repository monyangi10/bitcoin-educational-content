> Bitcoin: Uburyo bwo gutanga amahera y'ubuhinga bwa none

Ivyo nivyo Bitcoin ivuga, gukoreshwa n’abandi ata n’umwe asavye uruhusha. Ubu, abacuruzi bake cane ni bo bemera Bitcoin muri Exchange ku bicuruzwa vyabo n’ibikorwa vyabo (turacari kare cane), ivyo bikaba bituma bigorana kuri twebwe abakeneye gukoresha amasatoshi yacu. **Coincards** iri hano kugira ngo igerageze gutorera umuti iki kibazo, ikurekurire gukoresha amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins kugira **ugure amakarita y'ingabirano**



Kuri iyo nkuru, insiguro ngufi ku ruhande kugira ngo nshimike ku kamaro, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, ko gukoresha Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura aho gukoresha gusa nk’ububiko bw’agaciro. Nkako, uko iyo mirongo iriko irandikwa, reflex y’aba Bitcoiners benshi cane, iyo bafise akaryo ko gukoresha Sats yabo y’agaciro, ni ukwirinda kubigira, ahubwo bagakoresha euro / fiat shitcoin babategetse.



Iyo ubibonye, ​​ivyo vyoshobora gusa n’ibibereye ata gatotsi, mugabo biratuma wibaza ukuntu ivyo bihuye n’ubwenge. Bitcoin yagenewe gukoreshwa ata ruhusha. None ntibigaragara ko uko ubucuruzi bwemeza Bitcoin bugenda buragwira, niko ugira amahitamwo menshi nk’umunya Bitcoin afise ubusegaba? Mu yandi majambo, uko bikorohera gukoresha Sats yawe utasavye uruhusha, niko Bitcoins zawe bwite zigira agaciro. Rero muri iyo nsiguro, urakeneye guhimiriza abantu kwemera, ugashigikira ubucuruzi bwemera Bitcoin kugira ngo ubuhe ubushobozi bwo guhiganwa buzotuma abandi bakurikiza akarorero kabo.



Rero no mu bijanye no kuzigama agaciro k'amahera umuntu azigamye, kugira uruhara mu kwemera Bitcoin kugira ngo yorohe uko bishoboka kwose kuyakoresha, nibura birahambaye nka "HODL".



Niba ubwirizwa guca muri Exchange kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha Bitcoins zawe, rero zishobora kuba zifise agaciro ka 0. Niba ushobora kuzikoresha aho hose, zifise agaciro kanini cane.



Mu ncamake, "gukoresha" & "gusubirira" bisa n'uko ariyo nzira y'imbere. Nimugire uruhara mu gutegura ivy’ugutora abana, mugatanga ama satoshis ahantu henshi hashoboka, hanyuma mugasubire kugura Satss yakoreshejwe vuba bishoboka.

## None nogura gute ikarita y’ingabirano ya BTC kuri Coincards?
