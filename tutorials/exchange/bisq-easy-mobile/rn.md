Igenewe abaguzi bashasha ba Bitcoin barondera uburyo butagiramwo KYC bipfuza gukoreshwa neza n’abagurisha bazi utuntu n’utundi kandi bafise ubumenyi bamenyereye urubuga rwa Bisq.


Ubu, Bisq Easy ni yo nzira yonyene y'ubudandaji ya Bisq 2. Hariho n’ibindi bitegekanijwe mu gihe kizoza. Menya vyinshi ku vyerekeye Bisq 2 muri iyi nyigisho:

## 1️⃣ Gutangura


Kugira ngo utangure, fungura Bisq Easy Mobile kuri [urupapuro rwo gukuraho](https://bisq.urubuga/ivyo gukuraho/). Ni vyiza ko ugenzura ivyo waronse. Amabwirizwa yo kugenzura araboneka kandi kuri [paji yo gukuraho](https://bisq.network/ugukuraho/). Inyuma yo gushiramwo, ukeneye kwemera `Amasezerano y'Ukoresha`. Inyuma, rema urutonde rwa bose rugizwe n'izina ry'ibanga` n'ishusho (igereranywa n'ikimenyetso ca bot`). Na Bisq Easy, urashobora kandi gukora imirongo y'abakoresha benshi mu mukiriya umwe. Inyuma y'ugutangura gatoyi, uzoshika ku `Igishushanyo c'Ingoro`. Iyo porogarama irashira ahabona ibintu vy’inyigisho ata guca ku ruhande kuri paji nyamukuru. Kanda kuri `Igitabu c'ubudandaji gifungura` kugira ngo umenye amakuru mashasha.


![image](assets/en/01.webp)


Igitabo c'ubudandaji gisigura vyose bihambaye mu ntambwe zoroshe:



- Uko wocuruza kuri Bisq Biroroshe
- Ni gute ubucuruzi bugenda
- Ni igiki nkeneye kumenya ku mategeko y’ubudandaji.


Ikindi gice gihambaye ni **"Ni gute bitekanye gucuruza kuri Bisq Easy?"**


![image](assets/en/08.webp)


Suzuma akazu kanditsweko ngo `Nasomye kandi ndatahura` hanyuma ukande kuri `Heza`.

## 2️⃣ Gukora backup y'amakuru yawe


Imbere y'uko dutangura, reka twitwararike ibikorwa bimwe bimwe vyo mu nzu maze dukore `backup` ya dosiye yawe yo kubika amakuru. Genda kuri `Ibindi` > `Gusubizaho no Gusubizaho` kugira ngo ubike urutonde rwawe n'amateka y'ubudandaji. Iyo utakaje igikoresho cawe ataco ufise, izina ryawe n’ubudandaji bubandanya ntibishobora gusubirana. Tanga `Ijambobanga` kugira ngo ushiremwo amakuru yawe.

## 3️⃣ Amashikanwa


Kuva ku `Home screen`, hari uburyo bubiri bwo kuja ku vyo utanga. Kanda ku `Gutohoza ivyo utanga` hagati mu gicapo canke kanda kuri `Ivyo utanga` mu bimenyetso biri hasi. Aho, uhitemwo `amafaranga` ushaka gucuruza.


![image](assets/en/03.webp)


Udakunze [Bisq 1](https://planb.academy/ru/inyigisho/uguhanahana/urunganwe-ku-bagenzi/bisq-fe244bfa-dcc4-4522-8ec7-92223373ed04), isaba umutekano, Bisq Easy yizigira cane ku bijanye n’ugusubiramwo. Naho iyo nzira ituma abaguzi bashobora kugura Bitcoin ku ncuro ya mbere ata n’umwe abayifise, ishira icizigiro kinini ku bushobozi bw’uwugurisha bwo gutanga Bitcoin amaze kwakira amahera y’amahera. Rero, uburyo bw’umutekano bworoshe bwa Bisq buratunganijwe gusa ku mahera make y’ubudandaji kandi ubudandaji bugarukira ku madolari 600 y’Amerika angana n’ugucuruza kugira ngo bigabanye ingorane. Mu gice ca `Igitabo c'Ishikanwa`, hitamwo amayunguruzo y'uburyo bwo kwishura n'uguheba mu Muravyo canke Bitcoin (on-chain).


![image](assets/en/04.webp)


Amaze gukoresha `amayunguruzo`, hitamwo igiciro kibereye kivuye ku mugenzi w’ubudandaji w’icubahiro. Uburyo bwo kwishura n'ubwoko bwo kwishura (`Lightning` canke `on-chain`) umuguzi yatoye mbere y'igihe bizokwerekanwa. Raba neza ko ivyo bihuye n’ivyo ukunda imbere y’uko ukomeza. Duhitamwo uburyo bw’Umuravyo ⚡ hano.


![image](assets/en/05.webp)


Suzuma kandi wemeze ibisobanuro vy'ubudandaji ukanda kuri `Wemeze gufata ivyo utanga`. Hanyuma haza igicapo kigaragaza ko warafashe neza iyo mpano. Kanda Erekana ubucuruzi muri `Ubucuruzi Buguruye`. Mu gice c'ubudandaji bufunguye, shira `Ifagitire yawe y'umuravyo` hanyuma ukande kuri `Ohereza ku mugurisha` kugira ngo uyisangire. Ubu rero Rindira amakuru ya konti y’ukwishyura y’umugurisha. Abagurisha boshobora gufata umwanya wo kwishura. Subira usuzume kenshi kugira ngo ubone ivyahinduwe mw'idirisha ry'ibiganiro.


![image](assets/en/06.webp)


Wohereze indamukanyo ngufi mu kiganiro. Uwugurisha azosangira amakuru y'ukwishyura iyo baje kuri internet


![image](assets/en/09.webp)


Umaze kwakira amakuru akenewe y’ukwishura ku mugurisha, genda uheza kwishura. Inyuma y’aho, ushireko buto `Wemeza ko warishe`, hanyuma wihanganye urindirire kwemezwa ko warishe. ️ ⌛️


Ubwa nyuma, igihe uwugurisha yemeje ko yakiriye amahera, utegerezwa kandi kwemeza ko yakiriye Bitcoin. Ivyo biheza ugugura hakoreshejwe Bisq mu buryo bworoshe. Urakoze cane! Ubu ushobora gukora kuri buto ya `funga ubucuruzi`.

## 4️⃣ Gutorera umuti amatati kuri Bisq Biroroshe


Iyo hari ikintu kigenda nabi mu bucuruzi bwawe, abaguzi n’abagurisha bose barashobora gusaba infashanyo y’ubuhuza.


**Ivyo Abahuza Bashobora Gukora:**

• Gufasha mu gutuma ubudandaji buheza neza 

• Suzuma amahera y’amahera y’amahera, amahera y’amahera y’amahera, n’amahera y’amahera y’amahera Bitcoin

• Gukuraho ubudandaji iyo bikenewe 

• Kumenyesha abagenzuzi ukurenga ku mategeko kugira ngo abakoresha bashobora kubuzwa


**Inkurikizi ku bagurisha ivy'ubuhendanyi:**

Bivanye n’ubwoko bw’izina ryabo:



- BSQ Bond Izina**: DAO ishobora gufata BSQ yabo y'imbohe
- Igitunguru Address Izina**: Aderesi yabo y'igitunguru Bisq 1 ishobora kubuzwa


**Iciyumviro gihambaye:** Kubera ko izina ryose riboshe ku rubuga rwawe rw’ukoresha, gukumirwa birazibira izina ryawe burundu.


## 5️⃣ Ukore offer yawe


Aho gufata amashimwe asanzweho, urashobora kwihingurira amashimwe yawe yo kugura maze ukareka abagurisha bakaza aho uri. Iyi ni yo nzira nziza iyo utabonye amashimwe afise amafaranga y’imbere canke uburyo bwo kwishura bwiza mw’isoko ushaka gucururizamwo, naho uzokenera kurindira ko uwugurisha yemera.


Kuva ku `Ibitanga`, ukore ku kimenyetso c'icatsi kibisi `+` kiri mu mfuruka iburyo hasi. Hanyuma uhitemwo `Gura Bitcoin` uhitemwo amafaranga yawe ya fiat.


Gushinga amategeko y'ubucuruzi bwawe:



- Igiciro gihoraho canke Igiciro c'urutonde**: Hitamwo ingene ushaka gukoresha.
- Uburyo bwo kwishura**: Hitamwo mu mahitamwo ariho
- Igihugu**: Hitamwo Umuravyo ⚡ canke ₿ on-chain


Suzuma amakuru yawe maze ukande kuri `Rema igiciro`. Ivyo utanga ubu biraboneka mu `Igitabo c'Ivyo utanga`.


![image](assets/en/07.webp)


*Iciyumviro: Nk’umuguzi kuri Bisq Easy, ntukeneye izina ryiza—ivyo ni vyo vyiza nyamukuru. Abagurisha barikorera igisabwa co kumenyekana n’ingorane, ni co gituma basaba amahera y’imbere. Ivyo utanga bikwiye gusa kuba bifise igiciro gikwegera bihagije kugira ngo abagurisha b’icubahiro bavyiyumvireko.*


Igihe kizoba carasohotse, rindira mu gice ca `Igitabo c'Ishikanwa`. Iyo umuguzi afashe ivyo utanga, uzoronka itangazo. Gufungura ubucuruzi muri `Open Trades`, aho uwugurisha na wewe mushobora guhana amakuru y'ukwishyura kwanyu. Wohereze aderesi yawe ya Bitcoin canke invoice ya Lightning ku wugurisha. Amaze kohereza fiat, wemeze ko wishuye. Uwugurisha amaze kwemeza ko yakiriye, bazorekura Bitcoins kugira ngo barangize ubudandaji.


## 🎯 Insozero


Bisq Easy ishoboza kugura Bitcoin ata n’ingwati, igatorera umuti ingorane ya kera y’inkoko n’amagi ku baguzi bashasha. Ivyo ushobora guhindura biratomoye: wizigira izina ry’umuguzi aho kwizigira amahera yugarijwe kugira ngo ubone umutekano. Ubwo buryo bushingiye ku kwizigira burasigura igiciro gisanzwe c’amahera 5-15%, kikaba ari co gisubiza abagurisha b’icubahiro ku vyo bashoye mu kwubaka ukwizigira no gutanga infashanyo. Naho iyo sisitemu igabanya ubudandaji ku mahera makeyi kugira ngo hagire ibihombo bishobora guhomba, wama wifatanya n’abagurisha bafise amanota akomeye y’izina. Ku bashasha bashaka kwemera aya majambo, Bisq Easy itanga inzira yoroshe yo kwinjira muri Bitcoin.
