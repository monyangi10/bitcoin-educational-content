Ikoranabuhanga rya Bitcoin rigereranya ubuhinga bushasha imiryango myinshi yo muri Afirika iriko irizigira kugira ngo yubake kandi ikomeze ubusegaba bwayo, no kugira ngo ubudandaji bwayo bube bwiza mu gukuraho intambamyi z’ubutunzi n’iz’akarere.



Ivyo bikorwa vya Tando, super-app y’abanya Kenya bizotangura mu mwaka w’2023 mu **Inama y’Afrika Bitcoin** bishingiye ku nsiguro ya **Femi Longe**: *Abanyafirika barakeneye guhingura inyishu za Bitcoin ku Banyafirika*. Mu mwaka w’2024, Tando irarungikwa muri Kenya kandi irabandanya gufasha abantu gukoresha Bitcoin ku musi ku musi biciye kuri Lightning Network.



## Gutangura na Tando



Tando ni porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa iboneka ku mbuga za Android na iOS, ishobora kugufasha gukoresha Bitcoin aho hose muri Kenya. Muri iyi nyigisho tuzokwibanda ku rubuga rwa Android. Ariko rero, inzira zivugwa mu buryo burambuye aha hepfo zirakora no ku rubuga rwa iOS.



![download](assets/fr/01.webp)



Tando ni umurongo wa Exchange ushingiye ku buhinga bubiri:





- Lightning Network: Bitcoin Layer yo kwishura ubwo nyene, hafi ku buntu.
- M-Pesa: Amahera y’amatelefone ngendanwa azwi cane muri Kenya.



Nta makuru y’ibanga isaba ku bayikoresha, kandi ntibisaba ko umuntu asuzumwa akaranga kawe. Ukoresheje Tando, urashobora kurinda ubuzima bwite bwawe mu gihe uriko urakora amafaranga.



Muri Kenya, benshi mu bacuruzi, amaduka manini, abatanga ibikorwa n’ibikorwa vy’amahera bishobora gukorwa hakoreshejwe M-Pesa. Mu gukuraho ikinogo kiri hagati ya Lightning Network na M-Pesa, Tando iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha bitcoins aho hose M-Pesa yemerwa kandi uwuyironka akaronka amashilingi ya Kenya (KES).



Na Tando, ushobora gukoresha Bitcoin kugira ngo :




- Kwishura ibicuruzwa n'ibikorwa
- Wohereze amafranga
- Wishura amafaranga yawe



![menu](assets/fr/02.webp)



Tando ifise Interface, ishobora kugufasha gukoresha Lightning Wallet iyo ari yo yose kugira ngo wishyure mu mashilingi ya Kenya (KES). Ivyo bigereranya akaryo ko abanyamahanga baza muri Kenya ata mahera bafise, bakabaho mu buryo busanzwe bakoresheje Lightning Wallet yabo. Tando kandi itanga Bitcoin uburyo bubereye bwo kuyikoresha, iremesha ko yemerwa mu mibano yo muri Kenya.



## Gukoresha Tando



Tando iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins kugira ugure ikintu cose M-Pesa ishobora kugura muri Kenya. Ikindi, amafaranga y’ugucuruza ni hafi y’ubusa ugereranije n’ugucuruza kwa M-Pesa. Iyo ukoresheje Tando, uriha amahera angana na KES ukoresheje Lightning Wallet yawe kandi, ukoresheje ibikorwa remezo vyabo, Tando izohindura amahera yawe ya Lightning mu mashilingi ya Kenya (KES) hanyuma yohereze ayo mahera ku nimero ya M-Pesa utanga.

- Scan kugira wishyure**:



Scan to pay ni imwe mu nzira zo kwishura zihita zikoreshwa muri iyo porogarama. Scan code ya M-Pesa iboneka ku mucuruzi wawe, hanyuma winjize amahera uzokwishura maze ubandanye kwishura Lightning Invoice izogukorera.



![scan](assets/fr/03.webp)





- Kwohereza amafranga muri Kenya**:



Uburyo bwo kohereza amahera bwa Tando buratuma wohereza amahera muri Kenya uri aho hose kw'isi. Ushobora rero gukora amafaranga ajabuka imipaka no hagati y’imigabane, ata mahera menshi cane, muri Kenya ukoresheje Lightning Wallet yawe.



Hitamwo **Kohereza Amahera**, hanyuma winjize inomero y'uwuzokwakira. Injira amahera uzorungika (hagati ya 15 na 50.000 KES), hanyuma ureme Lightning Invoice ijana n’ivyo kwishura.



![send-money](assets/fr/04.webp)



Ishura fagitire ukoresheje Lightning Wallet yawe maze Tando izoyihindura mu mashilingi ya Kenya (KES) maze ayirungikire uwo azoyironka.



![pay](assets/fr/05.webp)





- Ishura amafaranga yawe**:



Injira umubare wa Invoice wipfuza kwishura, hanyuma ubandanye kwishura Lightning Invoice ijana.



![bills](assets/fr/06.webp)





- Gugura ibicuruzwa**:



Gurira ataco ukoresheje Tando. Injira inomero y’umucuruzi wawe n’amahera ari mu mashilingi ya Kenya (KES) y’ivyo uguze vyose, hanyuma generate Invoice ya Lightning kugira ngo wishyure.



![buy](assets/fr/07.webp)



Mu rutonde rw’ivyo bikoresho, uzosanga kandi amakuru yose ukeneye ku bijanye na Tando, kugira ngo ushobore gutahura neza ivyerekanwa vyabo n’igiciro cabo.
