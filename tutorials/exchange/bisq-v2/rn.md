## Imenyekanisha


Uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’abantu (P2P) ata KYC ni ngombwa kugira ngo abakoresha bazigame ubuzima bwite n’ukwigenga mu vy’amahera. Bituma umuntu ashobora gukorana n’abandi ataco akeneye kugenzura akaranga kabo, ivyo bikaba ari ngirakamaro ku bantu baha agaciro ubuzima bwite. Kugira ngo utahure neza ivyiyumviro vy’inyigisho, raba inyigisho ya BTC204:


https://planb.academy/courses/65c138b0-4161-4958-bbe3-c12916bc959c

### Bisq 2 ni iki?


Bisq 2 ni verisiyo nshasha ya Bisq Exchange izwi cane, yatangujwe mu 2024. Bitandukanye n’iyayibanjirije, Bisq 2 yateguwe kugira ngo ifashe amasezerano menshi ya Exchange, itanga abakoresha uburyo bwinshi bwo guhindura ibintu.


**Ibiranga bishasha:**




- Gushigikira imihora myinshi y'ubuzima bwite (Tor, I2P)
- Ibirango vyinshi kugira ngo umuntu agire ubuzima bwite
- REST API y'ubucuruzi
- Gushigikira ubwoko bwinshi bwa Wallet
- Uburyo bw'uruhara n'ugushiramwo amahera ku nguvu muri BSQ


Iyi nkuru yibanze cane kuri "Bisq Easy", ariyo protocole yonyene iriho ubu. Bisq Easy yakozwe cane cane ku bashasha bakoresha Bitcoin. Iyi porotokole ituma abakoresha bashobora kugura no kugurisha Bitcoins ku mafaranga ya fiat ku rubuga rw’abantu bose. Ivy’ugucuruza bigarukira ku madolari angana na 600 USD (n’agaciro gatoyi k’amadolari 6), kandi umutekano wa Exchange wizigiye izina ryiza ry’abagurisha BTC. Bisq Easy nta mafaranga y’ubudandaji canke ibisabwa vyo gushiramwo umutekano. Bisq Easy itegerezwa gusubirira Bisq 1 ku mafaranga ari munsi ya 600 USD (canke angana na yo).


**Ibiranga cane:**




- Porogaramu y'ibiro vy'umusaraba
- Amategeko menshi ya Exchange araboneka
- Urubuga rwa P2P rwegerejwe
- Kwibanda ku buzima bwite (nta KYC, gukoresha Tor)
- Udashobora kuzigama (uguma ugenzura amahera yawe)
- Inkomoko yuguruye (AGPL)


### Ubutandukane na Bisq 1


**Ku baguzi:**




- Nta giciro c'umutekano gikenewe
- Nta mafaranga yo gucuruza
- Nta mafaranga ya Mining
- Umutekano ushingiye ku ruhara rw'umucuruzi
- Imipaka y'ubucuruzi iri hasi (ingana n'amadolari 600)


**Ku bagurisha:**




- Nta giciro c'umutekano gikenewe
- Kugira izina ryiza
- Bishoboka ko guturira BSQ canke gukora amabondi ya BSQ
- Ibiciro vy'ugurisha bishobora kuba vyinshi (10-15% hejuru y'isoko)


**Iciyumviro gihambaye:** Igihe umurongo wa Bisq Multisig umaze gushirwa mu ngiro muri Bisq 2, verisiyo ya kera ya Bisq irashobora gukurwaho. Ariko rero, Bisq 1 izobandanya ikoreshwa nk’igikoresho co gucunga Bisq CAD no ku bijanye n’uguhinduranya BSQ-BTC.


### Exchange uburyo




- Uwaremye iyo mpano asigura amajambo yo muri Exchange .
- Abacuruzi bamaze kwumvikana ku masezerano (uburyo bwo kwishura n’igiciro), Exchange iratangura .
- Uwugurisha arungikira uwumugura amakuru yiwe yo muri banki, uwo muguzi na we akarungikira uwumugurisha igitabu ciwe citwa Bitcoin Address .
- Uwuguze ariha amafranga kandi akamenyesha uwugurisha
- Iyo amahera amaze gushikwako, uwugurisha yohereza ayo ma bitcoins ku Address y’umuguzi.
- Exchange iraheza iyo umuguzi aronse ama bitcoins .


### Amategeko ahambaye




- Imbere yo guhindura amakuru y’ukwishurwa, Exchange irashobora gukurwaho ata mvo n’imwe .
- Inyuma y’aho amakuru arambuye, ukudashitsa ivyo umuntu ategerezwa gushitsa vyoshobora gutuma yirukanwa .
- Ku bijanye n'ugurungika amahera muri banki, **ntiwigere ukoresha amajambo "Bisq" canke "Bitcoin "** mu mpamvu yo kwishura (ivyo bishobora gutuma amahera ahagarikwa canke mbere bigatuma konti ya banki y'uwuyakira canke y'uwuyitanga ihagarara)
- Abacuruzi bategerezwa kwinjira nibura rimwe ku musi kugira ngo bakurikire inzira .
- Iyo habaye ingorane, abacuruzi barashobora gusaba umuhuza .

### Ni gute umuntu yogurisha ama Bitcoins?


Uburyo bwo kugurisha kuri Bisq 2 bukurikira uburyo busa n’ubwo kugura, bufise intambwe nyamukuru zimwe ariko mu buryo buhindutse. Ushobora kwiremera ivyo ugurisha, canke ugasubiza ivyo ugura vyariho. Aha niho hari amahitamwo atandukanye n'intambwe ziri muri iyo nzira:


**Ihitamwo rya mbere: Rema igiciro co kugurisha**




- Hitamwo "Bisq Easy" hanyuma "Igitabo c'Ishikanwa".
- Fyonda kuri "Rema igiciro" uhitemwo "Gurisha Bitcoin".
- Gutunganya ivyo utanga :
 - Hitamwo amafaranga (EUR, USD, n'ibindi)
 - Hitamwo uburyo bwo kwishura bwemewe
 - Gushinga umubare (hagati ya 6 na 600 USD bingana)
 - Gushinga igiciro cawe (gihoraho canke % y'isoko)
- Suzuma ido n'ido maze utangaze ivyo utanga


**Ihitamwo rya 2: Fata ikintu kiriho**




- Gushakisha amashikanwa mu gice ca "Igitabo c'amashikanwa"
- Kuyungurura hakurikijwe amafaranga n'uburyo bwo kwishura
- Hitamwo igiciro kigubereye
- Suzuma ido n'ido maze wemere ivyo baguhaye


**Uburyo bwo kugurisha:**


Igihe Exchange izoba yatanguye :




   - Wohereze amakuru yawe ya banki ku muguzi
   - Rindira Bitcoin Address y'umuguzi
   - Suzuma ko Address ikora


Inyuma y'itangazo ry'ukwishyura :




   - Suzuma ko amahera yashitse kuri konti yawe
   - Wemeze ko umubare n'ibisobanuro bihuye
   - Wohereze ama bitcoins kuri Address yatanzwe
   - Shira ikimenyetso ku gucuruza nk'uko vyarangiye


Iherezo :




   - Rindira kwemezwa n'umuguzi
   - Siga inyishu ku bijanye n'ugucuruza
   - Yubaka izina ryawe mu bijanye n'ugucuruza muri kazoza


**Iciyumviro gihambaye:** Nk’umucuruzi, urakeneye kuba maso cane cane ku bijanye n’ingorane zo gusubizwa amahera. Guhitamwo uburyo bwo kwishura butekanye, kandi wama usuzuma ko kwishura kwakiriwe imbere yo kohereza ama bitcoins.


## Ivyiza n'umutekano


### Impanuro zo gukingira


**Ku baguzi:**




- Tangana n'amahera makeyi
- Suzuma izina ryiza ry'abagurisha (amanota makeyi ni 30.000)
- Koresha uburyo bwo kwishura gusa
- Suzuma ko uwugurisha ariko arakora imbere yo kohereza amahera
- Koresha gusa amakuru ya banki yatanzwe mu kiganiro ca Exchange
- Ntukigere uvugana hanze y'urubuga rwa Bisq 2
- Bika ikimenyamenya c'ukwishyura
- Ntukigere wohereza amakuru y'agaciro


**Ku bagurisha:**




- Urabe maso ku makonti mashasha
- Irinde uburyo bwo kwishura bushobora gutuma umuntu asubizwa amahera (PayPal, Venmo)
- Suzuma ko amakuru ya konti ahuye n'uwuguze
- Guhagarika ubutumwa kugira ngo uganire Bisq 2
- Gufungura ubuhuza mu gihe haba amakenga


### Ukwubaka izina (ku bagurisha)


Kugira ngo ushire izina ryawe ryiza kuri Bisq nk’umuguzi, kora ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji bihoraho kandi ugume uvugana n’abaguzi mu buryo bw’umwuga. Gusubiza ningoga ivyo umuguzi asaba kugira ngo ubone ko ufise ubumenyi bwiza. Ushobora kandi gukora bond ya BSQ kugira ngo werekane Commitment yawe ku rubuga. Ivyo bikorwa bizotuma abantu bavyizigira kandi bizokwegera abaguzi benshi.


### Gutorera umuti amatati




- Baza mugenzi wawe biciye ku kiganiro
- Niba ari ngombwa, fungura impaka
- Tanga ibimenyamenya vyose vyasabwe
- Kurikiza amabwirizwa y'umuhuza


### Amategeko y'ubuzima bwite :




- Nta kwiyandikisha canke kugenzura akaranga kari bisabwa
- Amahuza yose aca ku rubuga rwa Tor (kandi vuba I2P)
- Nta serveri nkuru yo kubika amakuru
- Ivyerekeye ibikorwa vy'ubudandaji bishobora gusomwa gusa n'ababifisemwo uruhara


### Uburinzi bwo gucengera :




- Urubuga rwa P2P rwakwiragijwe rwose
- Gukoresha urubuga rwa Tor (kandi vuba I2P) kugira ngo urwanye ugucengera
- Umugambi w'inkomoko yuguruye urongowe na DAO, ata kigo gishingiye ku mategeko

## Amasezerano ya kazoza


Naho Bisq Easy ariyo protocole yonyene iriho ubu, hari izindi protocoles nyinshi ziriko zirategurwa kuri Bisq 2 :




- Bisq Lightning**: Itegeko rya Exchange rishingiye ku buryo bwo gukingira hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwo guharura bw'imigwi myinshi kuri Lightning Network.
- Bisq MuSig**: Kwimura porotokole nyamukuru kuva kuri Bisq 1 gushika kuri Bisq 2, hakoreshejwe Multisig 2-ku-2 ifise amafaranga y’umutekano.
- BSQ Swaps**: Guhinduranya atome hagati ya BSQ na BTC.
- Ivy’uguhinduranya amafaranga y’amafaranga**: Amafaranga y’amafaranga y’amafaranga y’amafaranga 44 y’umutungo uri ku giciro c’amafaranga y’amafaranga (USDT, BTC-L) biciye mu guhindura amafaranga y’amafaranga y’amafaranga.
- Ivy’uguhinduranya amafaranga**: Ivy’uguhinduranya atome hagati ya Bitcoin na Monero.
- Liquid MuSig**: Verisiyo y’amasezerano ya Multisig ikoresha L-BTC kugira ngo igiciro gito kandi umuntu agire ubuzima bwiwe bwite.
- Guhinduranya ubwato bwo mu mazi**: Guhinduranya hagati ya Bitcoin kuri Lightning Network na Bitcoin On-Chain.
- Ivy’uguhindura amafaranga**: Ivy’uguhindura amafaranga y’atome hagati ya Bitcoin n’amafaranga y’amadolari y’Amerika.
- Amahitamwo ya Multisig**: Guhingura amahitamwo yo gushiramwo no guhamagara P2P n’uguhagarika BTC mu gucuruza On-Chain Multisig.
- Multisig Amasezerano Yuguruye**: Bishoboza gukora amasezerano y’ivyangombwa akoresheje uburyo bwa 2-ku-3 bwa Multisig bufise ubukemurampaka.


Izo porotokole ubu ziriko zirategurwa kandi zizogenda zishirwa muri Bisq 2, zihe abakoresha uburenganzira bwo guhindura ibintu bivanye n’ivyo bakeneye.
