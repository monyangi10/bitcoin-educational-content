### Intangamarara ya Bittr


Bittr ni igikoresho kigororotse ku muntu wese yipfuza gukura amafaranga yiwe azigamye muri Bitcoin - ata app igoye, ata gusuzuma akaranga, canke ukuzigama kw’uwundi muntu. Igenewe abantu bakunda kworoha, ubuzima bwite, no kugenzura neza amahera yabo.


Wohereze gusa amahera yo kuri banki asanzwe avuye kuri konti yawe, maze uzoronka Bitcoin uyishire muri Wallet yawe bwite. Waba ushaka kuzigama buri ndwi, buri kwezi canke igihe cose bigubereye, Bittr yateguwe kugira ngo ijyane n’ivyo ukora ataco ikubuza.


Reka tugendere mu kuntu vyoroshe gutangura guteranya Sats na Bittr.


## Gutangura na Bittr



- Ku rubuga canke kuri telefone ngendanwa genda kuri [getbittr.com](https://getbittr.com/gure-Bitcoin?inkomoko_utm=umugambi&utm_uburinganire=inyigisho&utm_isekeza=intambwe1) hanyuma ukande kuri “Gura Bitcoin”



 - Nta Konti:** Ntubwirizwa gukora konti kugira ngo ugure Bitcoin.
 - Nta nzira ya KYC:** Ntubwirizwa guca muri KYC (gushika kuri 999CHF ku misi 30)
 - Gutangura direct:** Uca utangura ubwo nyene kandi ushobora kwakira Sats yawe mu minota mikeyi


![image1](assets/en/01.webp)



- Injira IBAN uzoba uriko urarungika



 - SEPA gusa:** Bittr ikora gusa mu Buraya


![image2](assets/en/02.webp)


### Guhitamwo Wallet yawe.


Bittr ikorana n’ubwoko bwose bwa Wallet harimwo n’amasakoshi ya app, amasakoshi ya software canke amasakoshi y’ibikoresho.


Tuzotangura na BlueWallet ku batangura, hanyuma mu nyigisho tuzoyishiraho na BitBox.
