### Introduction to Bittr

Bittr is a straightforward tool for anyone who wants to grow their savings in Bitcoin - without a complicated app, identity checks, or third-party custody. It’s designed for people who prefer simplicity, privacy, and full control over their money.

Just send a regular bank transfer from your account, and you’ll receive Bitcoin directly into your own wallet. Whether you want to save weekly, monthly, or whenever it suits you, Bittr is designed to fit into your routine without getting in the way.

Let’s walk through how easy it is to start stacking sats with Bittr.

## Getting started with Bittr

1) On web or mobile go to [getbittr.com](https://getbittr.com/buy-bitcoin?utm_source=planb&utm_medium=tutorial&utm_campaign=step1) and click on “Buy Bitcoin”

- **No Account:** You don’t have to create an account to buy Bitcoin
- **No KYC process:** You don’t have to go through a KYC (up to 999CHF per 30 days)
- **Direct start:** You start right away and can receive your sats within minutes

![image1](assets/en/01.webp)

2) Enter the IBAN from which you’ll be sending from

- **SEPA only:** Bittr only works within Europe

![image2](assets/en/02.webp)

### Choosing your Wallet

Bittr works with any type of wallet including app wallets, software wallets or hardware wallets.

We'll begin with BlueWallet for beginners, and later in the tutorial we’re setting it up with the BitBox.

## Buy Bitcoin directly into BlueWallet

We recommend you do the setup in a quiet and private place. It should not take you longer than 5 minutes.

1) Select “bluewallet” on the website

2) Download the BlueWallet App here: [App Store](https://itunes.apple.com/app/bluewallet-bitcoin-wallet/id1376878040), [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.bluewallet.bluewallet).

![image3](assets/en/03.webp)

3) Click on “Add a wallet” (If you already have a wallet go to the next step).

![image4](assets/en/04.webp)

4) Select your wallet and go the message signing

![image5](assets/en/05.webp)

5) Complete the message signing and paste your signature on the website

![image6](assets/en/06.webp)

**Note: You can also click on "Share" in BlueWallet, copy the whole link, and paste it into the field on the Bittr website.**

6) Set up your bank transfer with your personal payment description

![image7](assets/en/07.webp)

## Buy Bitcoin directly into the BitBox

1) Select “BitBox”

![image8](assets/en/08.webp)

2) Click to open the BitBox App on your computer

![image9](assets/en/09.webp)

3) Unlock your BitBox and follow the steps and complete the message signing

![image10](assets/en/10.webp)

4) Set up your bank transfer with your personal payment description

![image11](assets/en/11.webp)

Wuhuu, that’s it! 🤩 As soon as money arrives in Bittr’s bank account, Bittr will buy your Bitcoin, send the bitcoin directly to your Wallet, and email you the transaction details (including the exchange rate at the time of conversion).
