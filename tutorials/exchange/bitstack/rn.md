Bitstack ni urubuga rw’ishoramari rw’Abafaransa Bitcoin, rugenzurwa na PSAN na AMF. Itanga app iboneka kuri Android na iOS kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kugura no kugurisha Bitcoin mu buryo bworoshe.


Igitandukanya Bitstack ni uburyo bwayo bwo guzunguruka, buhuza konti ya banki y’uwukoresha kugira ngo ihindure itandukaniro hagati y’amahera yashizwe mu gihe c’ugukoresha amahera ku musi n’iyi euro ikurikira, ataco ihinduye mu bitcoins. Ubwo buryo buratuma umuntu ashobora gukoresha *Dollar Cost Averaging* (DCA), bikaba bituma umuntu ashobora kwirundanira buhoro buhoro amasatoshi ata kubabara. Uretse iki kintu c’intango, iyo nzira iratanga kandi ubuhinga bwa kera bwa DCA bufise amafaranga yikora, hamwe n’ugugura no kugurisha aho hantu.


![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)


Amafaranga y’ugucuruza ya Bitstack arahindagurika bivanye n’ingero y’ugucuruza mu misi 30 iheze:




- Munsi y'amayero 250: 1,49
- 250 € - 5 000 € : 0,99 %
- 5 000 € - 10 000 € : 0,89 %
- 10 000 € - 50 000 € : 0,79 %
- 50 000 € - 250 000 € : 0,69 %
- 250 000 € - 500 000 € : 0,59 %
- Amayero arenga 500.000: 0,49


Ibindi bikorwa vyose bikorerwa kuri iyo nzira ni ubuntu, harimwo n’ugukura amahera Bitcoin (n’amahera makeyi ashobora gukurwa ku 100.000 Sats). Rero, bitandukanye n’izindi nzira za Exchange, gukura, tuvuge, 0.01 BTC muri porogarama kugira ngo uyishire mu kwizigama ntivyoba generate igikorwa ico ari co cose canke amahera ya Mining, bikaguha uburenganzira bwo kwakira 0.01 BTC ku kwakira Address.

![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)


Tangana n'igice ca "*Umutekano*", aho ushobora gukoresha ubuhinga bwo kwemeza ko umuntu ari uwundi kugira ngo wirinde kwinjiza ijambobanga ryawe n'amaboko igihe cose winjiye.


![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)

![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)

## Gugura Bitcoin


Nk’uko vyavuzwe mu ntangamarara, Bitstack itanga uburyo 3 bwo gushiramwo amahera muri Bitcoin:




- Guca ugura ubwo nyene amahera yihariye;
- Ivyerekeye Gugura Bisubirwamwo (RPC) ;
- Ikoreshwa ry’uguzunguruka kwihuta ku mahera akoreshwa.


### Gugura n'amahera


Kugira ngo ugure rimwe gusa ama bitcoins kugiciro c'isoko, kanda gusa kuri "*Gura*".


![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)


Injira amafaranga wipfuza, uhitemwo uburyo bwo kwishura, hanyuma ukande "*Bandanya*". Suzuma ivyo waguze imbere yo kwemeza ukanda kuri "*Gura*".


![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)


Ivyo mwaguriye ubu vyaremejwe.


![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)


### Gushinga DCA


**Dollar Cost Averaging** (DCA) ni ubuhinga bukwegera abantu bwo kwirundanira Bitcoin mu gihe ugabanya ingorane zijanye n’uguhinduka kwayo. Mu kugura ubudasiba amahera atari make, utaravye ku giciro, urashobora gutuma amahera ugura agenda neza kandi ukirinda gufata ingingo udatevye. DCA ifasha kugabanya inyifato mbi nk’ubwoba bwo gutakaza akaryo canke ubwoba iyo igiciro cogabanuka, gutyo bikagufasha gucunga neza amahera yawe ya Bitcoin mu buryo butekanye kandi bufise indero.


Kugira ngo ushireho ugugura gusubiramwo (DCA) kuri Bitstack, genda ku gice ca "*Ukuzigama*" kuri paji y'intango maze uhitemwo "*Isubiramwo*". Hanyuma ukande "*Bandanya*".


![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)


Sigura umubare w’ugugura usubiramwo n’uburyo bwo kwishura.


![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)


Hitamwo itariki n’incuro DCA yawe izotangura, ufise amahitamwo kuva ku masaha gushika ku kwezi. Wemeze ukanda kuri "*Ingingo*".


![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)


DCA yawe ubu irakora. Ushobora kuyibona canke kuyihindura uri kuri paji y'intango uhisemwo "*Recurring*" kandi.


![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)


### Gushoboza gukikuza amafranga


Nk’uko vyasiguwe mbere, Bitstack itanga ubuhinga bwo gukikuza bwite bw’umwimerere ku bijanye n’amahera. Ivyo bigufasha gushiramwo ivyo ugura ku musi ku musi ku ma euro ari hafi (canke n’ibindi, bivanye n’aho wahisemwo) maze ugashiramwo itandukaniro muri Sats. Nk’akarorero, ugugura €1.10 bizovamwo ishoramari ry’amahera €0.90 muri BTC na Bitstack, iyo na yo ikaba imenya kandi igakoranya ivyo bikorwa buri ndwi.


Ivyo bigufasha gushiramwo amahera n’ubwoko bwa DCA bushingiye ku mahera yawe bwite, mu buryo hafi butaboneka, kugira ngo buhoro buhoro wubake amahera uzigamye mu bitcoins.


Kugira ngo uyikoreshe, utegerezwa kubanza gukorana na konti ya banki, nk’uko vyavuzwe mu ntango y’iki gice. Hanyuma, kuri porogarama ya Bitstack, ukande kuri "*Arrondi*" mu gice ca "*Ukuzigama*".


![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)


Hitamwo umugwizatunga wo gukikuza. Nk’akarorero, n’umugwizatunga x2, uguze €1.10 bizokuzigama €1.80 mu bitcoins: `0.9 * 2 = 1.8`.


![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)


Hitamwo konti yawe ya banki hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya*". Hanyuma uhitemwo uburyo bwo kwishura ivyo uguze.


![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)


Ihitamwo ryawe ryo gukikuza ryikora ubu rirakora. Ushobora kuyibona canke kuyihindura mu gice ca "*Kuzunguruka*".


![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)


## Gukura muri Bitcoin


Umaze kugura satoshis zawe za mbere kuri Bitstack, urashobora kuzikura kuri platform ukazigumya mu kwicungera. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku kimenyetso "*Ohereza*", hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ohereza Bitcoin*".


![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)


Hitamwo amafaranga wipfuza gukuraho hanyuma ukande kuri "*Send*". Hanyuma uhitemwo "*Bitcoin Address*".


![Image](assets/fr/30.webp)


Injira mu kwakira ubusa Address kuva kuri Wallet yawe. Urabe maso, kuko iyo ukoze ikosa, iyo nzira y’ugucuruza ntizosubira inyuma.


![Image](assets/fr/31.webp)


Menya ivy’ugucuruza imbere y’ibindi. Kugira ngo ukureho amahera ataco utanga, hitamwo "*Standard*" imbere. Suzuma amakuru yawe yo gukura maze wemeze.


![Image](assets/fr/32.webp)


Niba utazi ingene Wallet yo kwibungabunga ikora, turatanga inyigisho ku mirongo myinshi y'ibisubizo iriho [ku Plan ₿ Network mu gice ca "*Wallet*"](https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet).
