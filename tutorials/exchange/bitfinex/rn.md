Bitfinex yashinzwe mu mwaka w’2012, ni imwe mu nzira za mbere za Bitcoin na Altcoin Exchange. Mu ntango yari yibanze ku masoko ya P2P Bitcoin, iyo nzira yaciye yihuta kwagura ibikorwa vyayo kugira ngo ishiremwo ubudandaji bw’amahera, amahera ya P2P, ubudandaji bw’ibintu biva ku bindi, n’isoko rya OTC ("*over-the-counter*") ry’ubudandaji bw’amahera menshi.


Ubu, Bitfinex ni urubuga rwuzuye, rwemerera kugura amafaranga yoroshe no gukoresha ibintu vy’ubudandaji biteye imbere bifise ibikoresho vyo gucunga ingorane. Iraboneka ku rubuga, kandi ku bijanye n’ugucuruza vyoroshe, hariho kandi porogarama yoroshe gukoresha yo kuri telefone ngendanwa.


Bitfinex irashigikira cane iterambere rya Bitcoin, ishiramwo ubuhinga nka Lightning Network no guteza imbere inyishu z’urunganwe kugira ngo umuntu agire umwidegemvyo mu vy’amahera.

## 2 - Gusuzuma konti


Bitfinex ikoresha uburyo bwo kugenzura (KYC) bujanye n’amategeko ariho ubu. Mu buryo bwa "Basic", ntibishoboka gushiramwo amahera, gukura canke gukorana, ivyo bikaba bituma biba ngombwa ko umuntu aronka nibura urugero rwo kugenzura "Basic Plus", canke mbere "Intermediate" canke "Full" iyo bikenewe:




- Basic** ni uburyo bwo kwerekana gusa, bugufasha kuraba urubuga ataco ukora;
- Basic Plus** itanga uburyo bwo gushika ku bikorwa vy’ingenzi, harimwo n’ugucuruza kw’isoko ry’ibintu n’uburyo bwo gushiramwo no gukura amahera bwatowe;
- Intermediate** ifungura ibikorwa biteye imbere nk’ubudandaji bw’amahera, inguzanyo, P2P n’ubudandaji bwa OTC, kandi itanga igihe co gushiramwo amahera kigabanutse;
- Full** iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha amafaranga yo mu mabanki mpuzamakungu n’ibintu vyose biri kuri iyo platform.


Ku rwego rwose, hakenewe izindi nyandiko kugira ngo umuntu asuzume. Ku bakoresha benshi, *Basic Plus* canke *Intermediate* muri rusangi birahagije.


Konti yawe imaze kuremwa, idirisha rizoza rizogusaba ko wosuzuma konti yawe. Fyonda kuri "*Suzuma*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/07.webp)


Niba iri dirisha ritabonetse, genda kuri profile yawe hejuru iburyo bwa Interface, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Verification*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/08.webp)


Munsi ya "*Ubwoko bwa konti*", hitamwo "*Umuntu ku giti ciwe*". Mu gihe canje, ndiko ndasubiramwo "*Basic Plus*", rero mpitamwo "*Basic Plus*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/09.webp)


Uzuza intambwe mu gutanga amakuru n’inyandiko bisabwa. Igihe ubusabe bwawe buzoba bushitse kandi bushikirijwe, Bitfinex izogenzura konti yawe kugira ngo ifungure ibintu bifitaniye isano.


## 3 - Intangamarara yihuta ku Bitfinex Interface.


Uhejeje kwinjira, uzobona umurongo wo kugenderamwo hejuru ya Interface ufise: "*Ubucuruzi*", "*Ibikomoka*", "*Iterambere*", "*OTC*", "*P2P*", "*Wallet*", n'ibindi Bitfinex itanga ibikorwa vyinshi, harimwo :




- Ubucuruzi**: "*classic*" isoko aho ushobora gushiramwo amategeko yo kugura no kugurisha amafaranga y'amahera (harimwo Bitcoin) ;
- OTC**: Over-The-Counter Serivisi yo gucuruza ibitabu vyinshi ataco uhinduye n’uwundi mukinyi, hanze y’ibitabu vy’amategeko ya bose;
- Infashanyo**: Akarere kagenewe gutanga inguzanyo n’amahera y’inyuma;
- Ivyimburwa**: Igice c’ivy’ivy’imbere (ivy’imbere, n’ibindi), bigenewe abacuruzi bazi utuntu n’utundi;
- P2P**: Iguha uburenganzira bwo kugura canke kugurisha ama cryptos ku bandi bakoresha ku buryo bw’urunganwe.


Ku bijanye n'ugukoresha mu buryo busanzwe (kugura/kugurisha bitcoins n'ugushiramwo/gukuraho), uzokoresha cane cane urupapuro rwa "*Gucuruza*", hamwe n'ibice "*Wallet*", "*Ibiziga*" na "*Gukura*".

![BITFINEX](assets/fr/20.webp)

![BITFINEX](assets/fr/21.webp)

Hitamwo "*BTC*". Hitamwo konti ya Bitfinex izokurwako amahera yawe ("*Exchange*" ku bintu vy'ishimikiro). Injira umubare n’aho bishika Address vy’ama bitcoins. Raba neza ko usuzuma igitabu co gukuraho Address kugira ngo ntihagire amakosa agira.

Bitfinex kandi itanga uburyo bwo gushiramwo no gukura amahera biciye ku Lightning Network, bikaba bituma umuntu ashobora gukoresha amahera yihuta kandi atari make.

## 6 - Gugura no kugurisha amafaranga y'ibiceri kuri Bitfinex .


Bitfinex itanga uburyo butandukanye bwo gucuruza. Kugira ngo ukoreshe neza, hitamwo isoko ry'imbere, rizwi kandi nka "*Ubucuruzi*" canke "*Exchange*". Aha, urashobora gushiramwo amategeko yo kugura canke kugurisha ku giciro c’isoko, canke ugashinga igiciro c’iherezo.


Mu rutonde rwo hejuru, kanda kuri "*Ugucuruza*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/25.webp)


Hitamwo "*BTC/EUR*" pair nimba ushaka kugura canke kugurisha BTC mu Exchange ku ma euro, nk'akarorero.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/26.webp)


Interface yerekana urutonde rw’ibiciro hagati, igitabu c’amategeko kiri hasi, n’igice co kwandika amategeko kiri ibubamfu. Mu gice co kwinjiza amategeko, ushobora guhitamwo hagati y'itegeko rya "*Isoko*" (rishirwa mu ngiro ubwo nyene kugiciro ciza kuruta ibindi vyose kiriho) canke amategeko ya "*Igipimo*" (urasobanura igiciro). Erekana igitigiri ca BTC uzogura canke ugurisha, canke uhitemwo ijana kw’ijana ry’amahera usigaje. Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Gura*" kugira ugure canke "*Gurisha*" kugira ugurishe.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/27.webp)


Ushobora kubona amateka y'amategeko yawe yashitse mu gice co hasi ca Interface.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/28.webp)


Ushobora kandi gushinga ugusubiramwo ugugura ama bitcoins kuva kuri porogarama kugira ngo ushiremwo igiciro cawe co kugura mu gihe (*DCA*).
