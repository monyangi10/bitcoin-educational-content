Turacariko twumva ivya BitcoinVoucherBot, bot ya Telegram yaremewe kugura Bitcoin ata [KYC] Ikibabaje, kuva mu kwezi kwa 11/2025, BitcoinVoucherBot mu buryo bwayo buhurikiye hamwe ntigishobora gukoreshwa nk’igikorwa ata KYC.



Muri iyi nkuru turaza kuraba ingene iyo nzira nshasha ikora ituma abakoresha bagura no kugurisha Bitcoin ataco baciye ku isoko rishasha rya P2P (Peer-To-Peer), rero nta KYC. Kugira ngo barwanye amategeko mashasha agenda atera ubwoba umwidegemvyo n’ubuzima bwite bw’ubuhinga bwa none, abayiteguye bararemye iyo nzira, iha abakoresha ubushobozi bwo kugura no kugurisha Bitcoin ataco bazi biciye kuri P2P (Peer-To-Peer). Reka turabe hamwe ingene ubu buryo bushasha bwo guhanahana amakuru bukora.



Kugira ngo ukoreshe iyo serivisi uzobwirizwa gukoresha Lightning Network. Rero urabe ko ufise wallet ishigikira iyo porotokole kandi iguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha "LNURL" canke "Lightning Address" kugira ngo wakire no kohereza amahera.

## Rema itegeko ryo kugura Bitcoin



Injira mw'Isoko: uhereye ku murongo mukuru wo kugenderamwo, ukande ku kimenyetso c'igare "Isoko"("Isoko") kugira ngo ufungure igitabu c'amategeko. hanyuma utangure urutonde rushasha: ukande kuri buto ya "Itegeko rishasha" ("Itegeko rishasha") kugira ngo utangure igikorwa.



![image](assets/it/13.webp)





- Gushiraho ibisobanuro vy'urutonde:
- Hitamwo uburyo "Gura Bitcoin"("Gura Bitcoin").
- Injira umubare wa sats ushaka.
- Sigura igiciro c’agaciro (hagati ya -20% na +20% kuva ku gaciro k’isoko).
- Hitamwo uburyo bwo kwishura (SEPA y’ubu nyene, n’ibindi).
- Igaragaza amafaranga akundwa.
- Kwemeza Itegeko: fyonda ku "Rema Itegeko"("Emeza Itegeko") kugira ngo ugende ku ntambwe yo gutanga amakuru.



![image](assets/it/14.webp)



Ivyimburwa bisabwa: ivyimburwa bingana na 10% vy’amahera yose, hamwe n’amahera y’ibikorwa, birakenewe kugira ngo umuntu akore itegeko.




- Ukwishura amafaranga: iyo itegeko ryakozwe, iyo sisitemu ihita itanga invoice ya Lightning. Ivyo bihembo ni ivy’akanya gatoyi gusa kandi birasubirwamwo iyo umuntu arangije gusaba.
- Ibirango nyamukuru:
- Ivy’umutekano: 10% vy’agaciro k’itegeko.
- Amafaranga y’ibikorwa: igiciro co gukoresha urubuga.
- Igihe: Ufise iminota 5 yo kwishura, ahandi ho iyo nzira irahera.



![image](assets/it/15.webp)



Inyuma y’ukwishura neza, iyo nkuru izoboneka mu gitabu kandi izoboneka ku bakoresha bose, bashobora kuyihitamwo no kuyemera. Kugira ngo ukore itegeko ryo kugurisha ivyo ukeneye gukora n'ugufyonda kuri "Gurisha Bitcoin" ("Gurisha Bitcoin"), winjize umubare w'ama satoshi wipfuza kugurisha, ugashinga urugero, uhitemwo uburyo bwo kwishura n'amahera, hanyuma ugakomeza n'amahera 10% nk'amahera y'umutekano. Iyo umaze guheza, umukino wawe uzoboneka kuri list.

1. Abagurisha barashobora kubona urutonde rw’amategeko yose ariho mu gitabu.


2. Suzuma ido n’ido: itegeko ryose ryerekana amakuru nk’aya:




  - Igitigiri c’ama Bitcoin,
  - Igiciro kugiciro,
  - Uburyo bwo kwishura,
  - Izina ry'ukoresha.

3. Fyonda ku rutonde kugira ngo ufungure urupapuro rwuzuye rurimwo amakuru yose.


4. Kanda kuri "Gurisha Bitcoin"("Gurisha Bitcoin") kugira ngo wemere iciyumviro.

## Ibiziga bisabwa n'ugurisha



Iyo itegeko ryemewe, iyo sisitemu iratanga invoice y’ukwishura. Ivyo bihembo birimwo:



- Igitigiri cose c’itegeko,



- komisiyo y’igikorwa.



Ukwishura amafaranga y’ibizigamirwa bitegerezwa gukorwa mu kiringo categekanijwe, ahandi ho iyo nzira ntizokwemezwa.



![image](assets/it/19.webp)



## Kurungika amabwirizwa yo kwishura



Inyuma y’aho umuntu ashize amahera, iyo nzira izoboneka ku rubuga rw’ivyo agurisha, uwo na we ategerezwa gutanga amakuru yose ku muguzi kugira ngo arangize kwishura mu mafaranga y’amahera.



1. Uwugurisha yerekana igikorwa gikora mu gice cabo.


2. Fyonda kuri "Shereza amabwirizwa yo kwishura."


3. Niwinjize amakuru yose akenewe ku bijanye n’ukwishurwa kwa fiat (IBAN, uwuzokwakira, aderesi, impamvu y’ukwishurwa, n’ibindi).


4. Fyonda kuri "Ohereza ubutumwa"("Ohereza ubutumwa") kugira ngo urungike amakuru ku muguzi.



![image](assets/it/20.webp)



## Uburyo bwo kwishura



Uwuguze aronka, mu gihe c’ikiganiro co kuri platform, ubutumwa burimwo amakuru yose akenewe kugira ngo ashobore kwishura mu mafaranga y’amahera:




- Ivyerekeye banki: IBAN irimwo izina n’aho uwugurisha afise konti yiwe.
- Igitigiri nyaco: igitigiri nyaco c’amahera azokwimurirwa.
- Intandaro/insobanuro: umwandiko uzoshirwa mu gucuruza.
- Igihe: igihe co kurangiza kwishura.



Ukwo kwimurira amahera bibera hanze y’urutonde rwa P2P kandi bitegerezwa gukorwa biciye mu kigo c’umuntu ciwe co muri banki.



⚠️ **Iciyumviro gihambaye:** kwemeza kuri platform bikwiye gukorwa gusa umaze gutunganya vy’ukuri ugutanga canke kwishura fiat biciye muri banki yawe.



![image](assets/it/21.webp)



## Kwemeza kwishura fiat



Iyo ntambwe ni ngirakamaro ku muguzi kandi ikwiye gukorwa gusa amaze kugenzura ko vy’ukuri amahera yoherejwe.



1. Kwakira amakuru: uwuguze yararonse amabwirizwa yo kwishura avuye ku mugurisha.


2. Gushitsa amahera: fiat transfer itunganijwe ivuye kuri konti y’umuntu muri banki.


3. Gusuzuma: kugenzura ko igikorwa cakozwe neza.


4. Wemeze ku rubuga: ukande kuri "Wemeze ko wishyuye fiat"("Wemeze ko wishyuye fiat") kuri paji y'ubudandaji.


Buto ya "Confirm Payment fiat" iboneka mu gice c'ibikorwa kandi ikwiye gukoreshwa gusa inyuma yo kugenzura ko vy'ukuri amahera yoherejwe.



![image](assets/it/22.webp)



Intambwe ya nyuma muri iyo nzira ni uko uwugurisha yemeza ko yakiriye amahera ya fiat, hanyuma satss zigaca zirekurwa ku muguzi.



![image](assets/it/23.webp)



## Iciyumviro



Mu cizigiro c’uko iyi nyigisho izogufasha gukoresha uburyo bushasha bwo kugurisha ama Bitcoin canke mbere ukayagura gusa, haba ku bubiko bwawe bwite bw’agaciro canke gutangura kuzana amahera y’umusi ku musi mu buzima. Naho ari ukwo, riguma ari urugi rwo gutohoza kugira ngo umuntu ashobore guhangana n’ibigiye kuba mw’isi yacu y’ubuhinga bwa none.



Uruzitiro rurongowe n’imibiri idugenzura ruriko rurakomera, kugira ngo ruvyare Internet igenda igenzurwa cane. Mu kugura P2P, uzoguma utanga amazina y’ivyo uguze, nta n’akarongo usigaye kandi nta n’ingaruka z’ukurikirana zivuye ku bandi bantu.
