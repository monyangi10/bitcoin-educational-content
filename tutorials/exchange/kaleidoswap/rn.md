KaleidoSwap ni porogaramu y'ibiro ifunguye ishobora gukingira ikinogo kiri hagati ya RGB Protocol na Lightning Network. Ikora nk’urubuga rwuzuye rwo gucunga ama Node y’imiravyo ya RGB, gukorana n’abatanga imiravyo ya RGB (LSPs) biciye ku nsiguro ya LSPS1, no gukorana n’uguhinduranya atome y’itunga rya RGB.


Nk’umuti udafise ububiko, KaleidoSwap iha ububasha abakoresha kugira ngo bagume bagenzura neza imfunguruzo zabo n’amakuru yabo. Mu gukoresha uburyo bwo kwemeza RGB ku ruhande rw'abaguzi, bishoboza amasezerano y'ubwenge yigenga kandi ashobora guhindurwa hejuru ya Bitcoin. Iyi nyigisho yinjira mu bintu biteye imbere vya KaleidoSwap, ikakuyobora mu bigoranye vy'uburongozi bwa UTXO "Ibara", uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera ku mitungo yihariye, n'uburyo bwo gucuruza bw'abaguzi, kugira ngo umenye neza ko ushobora gukoresha neza iyi nzira ikomeye y'uguhinduranya amafaranga.


## Gushiramwo


KaleidoSwap itanga ama binaire yubatswe mbere ku mirongo y’ibikorwa ikomeye, ariko ku bakoresha bateye imbere, kwubaka uhereye ku ntango bituma ukoresha kode nshasha n’imiterere yawe yihariye.


### Gukuraho ibice bibiri


Ushobora gukuraho igisohoka gishasha ca sisitemu yawe y’ibikorwa ku [urubuga rwemewe](https://kaleidoswap.com/) canke [urupapuro rw’ibisohoka rwa GitHub](https://github.com/kaleidoswap/desktop-app/releases).


### Uburyo bwo gushiramwo


1. **Windows**: Gukuraho `.exe` maze uyikoreshe.

2. **macOS**: Kura dosiye `.dmg`, uyifungure, hanyuma ukure KaleidoSwap mu dosiye yawe y’Ibikoresho.

3. **Linux**: Gukuraho dosiye `.AppImage` canke `.deb` maze uyishiremwo/uyikoreshe.



## Amahitamwo y'imiterere y'urudodo


Iyo utanguye KaleidoSwap, uzoshikirizwa **Igishushanyo c'Ihuza**. Iyi ni intambwe ya mbere mu gutunganya ibidukikije vyawe.


![Node Selection Screen](assets/en/01.webp)


Utegerezwa guhitamwo ingene wofatanya na RGB Lightning Network. Iryo hitamwo rigira ico rihinduye ku rugero rwo kugenzura n’ubuzima bwite bwawe.


### Ihitamwo rya 1: Igikoresho co mu karere (Iremezwa kugira ngo umuntu yizigame)


**Kugira ngo ushobore kugenzura neza no kugira ubuzima bwite**, ukoreshe node ku mashine yawe, raba ivyiza biri aha hepfo:


- Ukwirinda**: Ni wewe ufise imfunguruzo. Nta wundi muntu ashobora guhagarika amahera yawe canke gucengera amafaranga yawe.
- Ubuzima bwite**: Amakuru yawe aguma ku gikoresho cawe.
- Ukwikukira**: Nta kwizigira abatanga ibikorwa bo hanze.


Niwahitamwo **Local Node**, uzoja ku rubuga rwo gutegura aho ushobora gukora wallet nshasha canke gusubizaho iyariho.


![Local Node Setup Screen](assets/en/02.webp)


### Ihitamwo rya 2: Urudodo rwa kure


Ihuze na RGB Lightning Node iri kure (yitunganije kuri VPS canke umutangabikorwa).


- Ivyiza**: Nta gukoresha ibikoresho vyo mu karere, biraboneka 24/7.
- Ivy’uguhindura**: Bisaba kwizigira umushitsi canke gucunga umukozi wo kure.


![Remote Node Setup Screen](assets/en/03.webp)


**KaleidoSwap yagenewe guha ubushobozi bwo kwizigama.** Turagusavye cane gukoresha uruzitiro rwawe bwite—haba mu karere (Ihitamwo 1) canke mu kwikorera uruzitiro rwo kure—kugira ngo ukoreshe neza ubushobozi bwo guhangana n’ugucengera bwa Bitcoin na RGB.


## Gukora Wallet


KaleidoSwap icungera ivy’ubutunzi vya Bitcoin n’ivya RGB. Inzira yo kurema wallet itanguza ububiko bw'imfunguruzo bucungera amahera yawe ya on-chain n'intara zawe z'umurongo wa Lightning.


Akira ingene bigenda mu buryo burambuye:

1. Fungurira KaleidoSwap uhitemwo **Node yo mu karere**.

2. Fyonda kuri **Rema Wallet Nshasha**.

3. **Gutegura konti**: Injira **Izina rya konti** hanyuma uhitemwo **Irezo** (nk’akarorero, Bitcoin Mainnet, Testnet, Ikimenyetso).

4. **Itunganywa ry'imbere** (Ihitamwo): Niba uri umukoresha w'ububasha, ushobora gutunganya iherezo rya RPC, Indexer URLs, canke amategeko ya Proxy munsi ya "Itunganywa ry'imbere".

5. Kanda kuri **Bandanya**.

6. **Ijambobanga**: Shiraho ijambobanga rikomeye ryo gupfuka dosiye yawe ya wallet mu karere.

7. **Ijambo ryo Gusubirana**: Andika ijambo ryawe rya seed maze uribike neza.


    - Critical**: Iri jambo rikenewe kugira ngo ubone amahera yawe ya on-chain n’akaranga ka node.
    - Imburi**: **Imirongo y'umuravyo ntishobora gusubirwamwo bimwe bishitse kuri seed yonyene**. Utegerezwa kandi kubungabunga amakuru y’imirongo idahinduka (SCB) kugira ngo ubone amahera yugarijwe mu mirongo.

## Gufasha Wallet yawe


Kugira ngo ukoreshe itunga rya RGB, ukeneye gutanga amahera ya wallet yawe. KaleidoSwap ishigikira gushiramwo ivy'ubutunzi vya Bitcoin na RGB biciye mu bikorwa vya on-chain canke Lightning Network.


### Gushiramwo Bitcoin


1. Fyonda **Deposit** mu rutonde rw'Ibikorwa vyihuta.

2. Hitamwo **BTC** mu rutonde rw’itunga.


![Select BTC Asset](assets/en/06.webp)


3. Hitamwo uburyo bwo gushiramwo amahera: **Ku ruzitiro** canke **Umuravyo**.


![BTC Deposit Options](assets/en/07.webp)



- Ku ruzitiro**: Scan kode ya QR canke ukope aderesi kugira ngo wohereze Bitcoin uvuye ku yindi wallet.
- Umuravyo**: Gutanga invoice y’amahera wipfuza.


![BTC On-chain Deposit](assets/en/08.webp)


### Gushiramwo Itunga rya RGB n'amabara UTXOs


Kugira ngo uronke umutungo wa RGB (nk'uwa USDT), ukeneye UTXO zihariye zihari kugira ngo zibe "ibara" (zihawe umutungo).


1. Kanda **Ibiziga** hanyuma uhitemwo umutungo wa RGB (nk’akarorero, USDT). **Igihambaye**: Niba ari **ubwa mbere** urudodo rwawe ruriko ruraronka iki kintu, **siga umwanya w'Indangamuntu y'Ikintu ubusa**. Kwinjiza ID y'umutungo utazwi bizotuma node igarura ikosa kuko itaramenya.

2. Hitamwo **Ku-ruzitiro** canke **Umuravyo**.


![USDT Deposit Options](assets/en/09.webp)


#### Uburyo bwo kwakira ku ruzitiro: Icabona n'Impumyi


Iyo uronse itunga rya RGB on-chain, ufise uburyo bubiri bwo kwirinda:



- Ukwakira mu mpumyi (Iremezwa kubera ubuzima bwite)**: Utanga "blinded" UTXO ku wurungitse. Uriko usaba uwugurungikiye ko yohereza itunga kuri UTXO iriho ufise, ariko ugapfukiranya ikimenyetso nyaco ca UTXO. Ivyo bituma umuntu agira ubuzima bwiwe bwite.
- Icabona Yakira**: Utanga aderesi Bitcoin isanzwe. Uriko usaba uwugurungikiye ngo akuremere *UTXO nshasha* mu kurungikira ivyo bintu kuri iyo aderesi. Ivyo bituma itunga rya RGB rifatanywa ataco rihinduye na UTXO nshasha yaremwe n’ugucuruza.


#### Ibiziga vy'umuravyo


Ku bijanye n’amahera y’umuravyo, gusa generate invoice. Ico kintu ca RGB kizogushikako biciye ku nzira zawe zifunguye.


![USDT Lightning Invoice](assets/en/10.webp)



## Gufungura imirongo n'itunga rya RGB


Kugira ngo ushire umutungo wa RGB hejuru ya Lightning Network, ukeneye umurongo ufise amahera ahagije n’ugutanga umutungo. Uburyo bworoshe bwo gutangura ni **Ugugura Channel** kuri LSP (Umutanga Serivisi z’Imiravyo).


### Gugura umurongo kuri Kaleido LSP


1. Genda ku rubuga rwa **Imirongo**. Uzobona amahitamwo yo "Gufungura Umurongo" (mu gitabu) canke "Gugura Umurongo" (LSP).

2. Fyonda **Gura umurongo**.


![Channels Dashboard](assets/en/11.webp)


3. **Huza na LSP**: Iyi porogaramu izohuzwa na LSP ya Kaleido. Uyu mutanga amafaranga atanga amahera yinjira kandi ashigikira inzira y’itunga rya RGB.


![Connect to LSP](assets/en/12.webp)


4. **Gutunganya umurongo**:


    - Ubushobozi**: Hitamwo ubushobozi bwose bwa Bitcoin bw’umurongo.
    - RGB Gutanga**: Hitamwo umutungo wa RGB (nk’akarorero, USDT) ushaka ko ushobora kwakira canke kohereza. LSP izobona ko umurongo utunganijwe kugira ngo ushobore gushigikira iki kintu.


![Configure Channel](assets/en/13.webp)



    - Ugutanga RGB**: Hitamwo umutungo wa RGB (nk’akarorero, USDT) ushaka ko ushobora kwakira canke kohereza. LSP izokora vyose kugira ngo umurongo utunganywe kugira ngo ushigikire iki kintu.


![RGB Allocation](assets/en/14.webp)


5. **Ukwishura**: Utegerezwa kwishura LSP amahera yo gufungura umuyoboro no gutanga amahera. Ushobora kwishura ukoresheje **Umuravyo** canke **Ku ruzitiro** Bitcoin. Ushobora kwishura ukoresheje KaleidoSwap wallet yawe yo mu mutima canke wallet yo hanze.


![Complete Payment](assets/en/15.webp)


6. Igihe ukwishyura kwemejwe, LSP izotangura igikorwa co gufungura umuyoboro. Uzobona **Itegeko ryarangiye**.


![Order Completed](assets/en/16.webp)


7. Inyuma yo kwemezwa kuri blockchain, umurongo wawe uzoba uriko urakora kandi witeguriye gutanga amakuru ya RGB.



## Ubucuruzi: Uwufata-Uwukora Icitegererezo


Moteri y’ubudandaji ya KaleidoSwap ikoresha **umuderi wa Taker-Maker**. Ushobora guhindura ivy’ubutunzi n’amaboko n’uwo mugenziwe canke ukoreshe Umuhinguzi w’Isoko (LSP).


### Guhinduranya n'Umuhinguzi w'Isoko (LSP)


Ubu ni bwo buryo busanzwe bwo gucuruza. Ukora nk’**Uwufata**, ushitsa amategeko arwanya amahera ariho atangwa na LSP (**Umuhinguzi**).


1. Ja kuri **Ubucuruzi** uhitemwo **Umuhinguzi w'Isoko**.

2. **Injira Amafaranga**: Injiza amafaranga Bitcoin ushaka kohereza (canke umutungo ushaka kwakira). Ico kigaragaza kizokwerekana igiciro c’amahera n’amahera.


![Market Maker Swap](assets/en/17.webp)


3. **Confirm Swap**: Suzuma ido n’ido, harimwo n’igiciro c’amahera n’amahera nyayo uzoronka. Fyonda **Wemeze guhindura**.


![Confirm Swap](assets/en/18.webp)


4. **Gukora**: Ivyo guhindura bikorwa mu buryo bw'atome kuri Lightning Network. Uzobona igicapo c'imimerere kigaragaza ko uguhindura kurindiriwe.


![Swap Pending](assets/en/19.webp)


5. **Ukuroranirwa**: Iyo ama HTLC amaze gusohoka, swap iraheza, kandi ivy’ubutunzi biri mu muhora wawe.


![Swap Success](assets/en/20.webp)



## Umuhinguzi API


Ku bategura bubaka hejuru ya KaleidoSwap, porogaramu yerekana API ishigikira:



- RGB LSPS1**: Ku bikorwa vy’ugutanga amahera vy’ubuhinga bwa none.
- Swap API**: Ku bijanye n’ubudandaji bushingiye kuri porogarama n’ugukora isoko.
- WebSocket**: Ku bijanye n'isoko ry'amakuru y'igihe nyaco.


Raba [Inyandiko za API](https://docs.kaleidoswap.com/api/intangamarara) kugira ngo ubone iherezo ryuzuye n’ibisobanuro.
