## Imenyekanisha


BitcoinVN.io ni yo ya mbere kandi yizigirwa cane muri Vietnam mu guhindura Bitcoin kuva mu 2014. Turaha ubushobozi abakoresha bwo guhindura itunga ry’ubuhinga bwa none mu buryo butaziguye mu ma Dong ya Vietnam (VND) biciye ku bikorwa bitatu vy’ingenzi:


- (b)** Guhinduranya kuri interineti
- (e)** Ama ATM ya Bitcoin
- (d)** Ivyo kugura ku giciro (OTC)


Ukwiyemeza kwacu mu bijanye n’umutekano wo ku rwego rwo hejuru no kwubahiriza amategeko bituma abanyagihugu n’abanyamahanga batwizigira ku bijanye no kurungika amahera, gushiramwo amafaranga, no kwishura ku musi ku musi ku buryo bwa digitale.

![https://bitcoinvn.io/](https://bitcoinvn.io/)


Urubuga rwacu rwo guhanahana amakuru ku rubuga rwa interineti rwateguwe kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukoresha neza, bigufashe gukora ivyo guhinduranya ubwo nyene ata ngorane. Turashigikira indimi nyinshi, harimwo **(a)** Icongereza, Ikinyavietnam, n’Ikirusiya. Ku bijanye n’uguhinduranya utuntu dutoduto, nta konti isabwa; ariko, urashobora **(c)** kwiyandikisha kugira ngo wongere cane imipaka y’amahera ukoresha ku musi. Ubu kuri iyo nzira irimwo urutonde rw’ibintu birenga 99 vyo kuri interineti, ivyo ushobora guhindura mu buryo bworoshe ukabigira Bitcoin (BTC)—ibintu bikomeye kuruta ibindi vyose—canke ukabihindura ata guca ku ruhande mu giciro c’amahera y’i Vietnam (VND), hakaba hariho uburyo butandukanye bwo kwishura.


## Ukwiyamiriza


Iyi nkuru ni iyo kwigisha gusa ku bijanye n’ibikoresho vyacu kandi **si impanuro z’amahera**. Turabinginze mumenye ko amafaranga y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none ahinduka cane kandi **atari amafaranga yemewe muri Vietnam**. Turabahimiriza kwubahiriza amategeko yose yo mu karere no kwiyumvira cane kurondera impanuro z’abahinga mu vy’amahera.


## Uko wogura BTC ku VND


![BitcoinVN.io](assets/en/02.webp)


### Intambwe ya 1: Hitamwo amafaranga yo kohereza


Mu gice ca "Urungika" – ikibanza kinini gifise ikimenyetso c'umutuku ca VND, hitamwo uburyo bwo kwishura ukunda kugira ngo wishyure BTC uhereye ku rutonde ruja hasi. Muri iki gihe, VND Bank Transfer (kwishura VND biciye mu mabanki yo mu karere) iratorwa. Turashigikira **amabanki 67 yo mu karere** yo muri Vietnam.


### Intambwe ya 2: Hitamwo kwakira amafaranga


Mu gice ca "Uraronka" - ikibanza kinini gifise ikimenyetso ca Bitcoin, hitamwo BTC (Bitcoin blockchain/mainchain) — akenshi ni co gisanzwe canke gisanzwe kiboneka mu rutonde ruja hasi.


### Intambwe ya 3: Injira umubare wa VND no gusubiramwo ibisobanuro vy'amasezerano


Injira n’amaboko amahera wipfuza guhindura maze ukoreshe [ihuriro ry’imipaka y’umusi](https://bitcoinvn.io/info) canke ubuto Min/Max kugira ngo umenye ubushobozi bwawe nimba udafise konti. Ubuhinga buca buharura ubwo nyene umubare wa nyuma wa BTC uzoronka umaze gufata mu muzirikanyi [amahera yo guhindura](https://support.bitcoinvn.io/help/en-us/4-orders/12-fees) n’igiciro c’amahera yo guhindura muri ico gihe nyene. Suzuma umubare wa BTC wa nyuma kugira ngo urabe nimba unezerewe n’ivyo ukora.


#### Imfashanyo


Ibuka ko **ibiciro vy’ugucuruza bihinduka**, rero wama usuzuma igiciro ca nyuma imbere yo kwemeza.


**Konti ntibisabwa n'** amafaranga y'umusi ashobora gushika ku VND 250.000 (~ USD $8). Ibindi bisobanuro biraboneka muri [amategeko yo kwimurira amahera muri banki](https://support.bitcoinvn.io/help/ru-us/5-uburyo- bwo-gutanga-amahera/17-vnd-yo kwimurira-amahera muri banki).


**Urabe maso**: amafaranga y’urubuga muri rusangi arahenda iyo uhisemwo Lightning Network ugereranyije n’uburyo bwo gukoresha onchain.


### Intambwe ya 4: Injira Bitcoin Address yawe.


Kopa maze ushireko aderesi yawe ya BTC wallet, canke ushireko kode ya QR iri mu kibanza c’aderesi (igaragazwa n’umutuku ku gicapo). Bisuzume kabiri kuko niho BTC yawe izorungikwa.


### Intambwe ya 5: Genda ku kwishura uko uhisemwo


Fyonda kuri buto nini y’umuhondo Swap kugira ngo ugende kuri paji yo kwishura iriko amakuru ya banki ya BitcoinVN aherekejwe na kode ya QR (ku maporogarama y’ivy’amahera yo mu karere nka Momo na VNPAY), n’amahera nyayo ya VND. Kwimurira amahera nyayo kuri konti yawe ya banki ya Vietnam/app mu gihe categekanijwe ahandi ho, amasezerano azohera kandi ubwirizwa gusubira gutangura kuva ku ntambwe ya 3.


### Intambwe ya 6: Suzuma uko wishuye no kwakira umutungo


Uhejeje kwishura, shiraho ifoto/inyandiko y’uko wishuye kugira ngo ugenzure (kenshi iminota 1–10 ku misi y’akazi). Iyo yemejwe, BTC ica irungikwa kuri wallet yawe. Uzoronka amakuru mashasha kuri email kandi ushobora gukurikirana inomero y’itegeko.


## Uko wogurisha BTC ku VND


![BitcoinVN.io](assets/en/03.webp)


### Intambwe ya 1, 2, na 3: Hitamwo Kohereza, Kwakira Amafaranga, winjize amafaranga yo guhindura


Kimwe n’uburyo bwo kugura bwavuzwe haruguru, fyonda ku gice ca `Urungika` kugira uhitemwo umutungo ushaka guhindura biciye ku nzira iyihe y’ubuhinga bwa none nk’akarorero. BTC (bitcoin on-chain), canke BTC biciye ku rubuga rw’umuravyo (umuti wa off-chain) uva ku vyo bimanuka.


Hanyuma mu gice ca `Urakira`, uhitemwo umutungo ushaka kwakira n’ingene ushaka kwakira; nk’akarorero biciye ku gutanga amahera muri banki ya VND (kwakira biciye ku banki yawe).


Injiza umubare wa BTC ushaka guhindura. Iyi app ica iharura ubwo nyene VND uzoronka. Suzuma amafaranga y’urubuga, amafaranga y’iherezo yo kwakira n’ikete ry’uguheba (bishobora guteba hanze y’amasaha y’akazi).


### Intambwe ya 4: Suzuma kandi winjize amakuru y'ukwishyura kwawe


Hitamwo imwe mu mabanki 67 yo mu karere ashigikiwe mu rutonde ruja hasi hanyuma wuzuze izina ryawe ryose ry’uwufise Konti, inomero ya Konti, wongereko akajambo nimba ubishaka.


### Intambwe ya 5: Wohereze umutungo & wakire amahera yawe


Fyonda kuri buto ya Swap kugira ngo werekane aderesi y’amahera ya BTC ya BitcoinVN na kode ya QR. Wohereze neza neza amahera ya BTC yatanzwe avuye kuri wallet yawe kuri aderesi yerekanwe mu gucapura kode ya QR canke mu gukopa aderesi ya Bitcoin.


Reka umwanya wo kwemeza (amasegonda ku Muravyo, iminota on-chain). VND izokwimurirwa kuri konti yawe ya banki—kenshi mu masaha 1–2 mu masaha y’akazi. Imeli n’inomero y’itegeko bizotangwa kugira ngo umuntu akurikirane.
