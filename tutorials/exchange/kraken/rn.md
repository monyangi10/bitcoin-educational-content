Kraken ni imwe mu nzira za kera cane kw’isi Bitcoin n’amahera y’amahera Exchange. Iyo sosiyete yashinzwe kera cane mu mwaka w’2011 na Jesse Powell n’abo bakorana. Powell amaze kuba umujanama w’ishirahamwe ry’isoko ry’ico gihe ryitwa MtGox, kandi yari yiteze ko rizogwa, yatanguje Kraken mu 2013 afise intumbero nyamukuru yo gusubirira MtGox, iyo yari azi ko izopfa, no kudasubiramwo amakosa nk’ayo. Ivyo bituma iba imwe mu nzira za kera kandi zizwi cane za Exchange kw’isi yose.



Ubu, Kraken ni urubuga rwuzuye, kuva ku kugura Bitcoin yoroshe gushika ku bikoresho vy’ubudandaji biteye imbere n’ibikoresho vyo gucunga ingorane. Iraboneka ku rubuga, kandi ku bijanye n’ugucuruza vyoroshe, hariho kandi porogarama yoroshe gukoresha yo kuri telefone ngendanwa.



Uretse ivyo vyose, ivyo nk’aba Bitcoiners batadushimisha cane, birabereye kumenya ko Kraken yari imwe mu 1st exchanges zashigikiye Lightning Network ku rubuga rwayo, yemerera **ku buntu** gukuraho satoshis zaronswe, gutyo iremesha kwizigama mu bakoresha bayo.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwereka ingene wokoresha Kraken nk’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Niba ufise ishirahamwe kandi ushaka guhitamwo verisiyo y'ubucuruzi, usabwe kuraba iyi yindi nyigisho:



https://planb.network/tutorials/business/others/kraken-pro-07b1c16c-d517-4bf7-9a78-b42dc0f21785

## 2 - Gukingira igiharuro cawe ca Kraken



Ubu winjiye muri konti yawe. Uzoca usabwa guhitamwo hagati y’ubwoko 2 bw’ubumenyi. Imwe yoroshe iri ibubamfu, yitwa "Kraken", ifise Interface idateye akaga, ishobora kugufasha kugura no kugurisha gusa ama bitcoins. Iyindi, iburyo, yitwa "Kraken Pro", itanga uburyo bwinshi cane, ifise Interface ikora cane kandi idasomwa cane yagenewe imirongo y'"abacuruzi".



Birabereye kumenya ko amahera yo kugura atandukanye kuri ivyo vyose bibiri, nk’uko tuzobibona mu nyuma.



Reka dutangure duhitamwo ubumenyi bworoshe mu gufyonda kuri "Genda kuri Kraken" mu mfuruka ibubamfu hasi.



![KRAKEN](assets/fr/12.webp)



![KRAKEN](assets/fr/13.webp)



Interface vy’ukuri ni nziza cane kandi irafise amabara. Imbere mbere y’uko tuja gukingira konti yacu mu kwongerako uburyo bwo kwemeza ko ari 2, reka dufyonde hasi ibubamfu bw’urubuga rwacu kugira ngo duhindukire tuje mu buryo bw’ijoro maze twirinde kubabaza amaso yacu cane n’ivyo vyera vyose.



![KRAKEN](assets/fr/14.webp)



Amaso yacu ubu aratekanye, reka dusubire gufyonda kuri profile yacu kugira ngo tuzane menu yo kwongerako 2FA (2nd authentification factor). Hanyuma uhitemwo "Umutekano".



![KRAKEN](assets/fr/15.webp)



Uca uhabwa uburyo bubiri bwa 2FA, canke "passkey", ishobora kugufasha kwemeza biciye ku buhinga bwa telefone yawe, canke "Authenticator App".





- Niwahitamwo "passkey", ukurikize amabwirizwa ari ku rubuga. Mu ncamake, uzosabwa guhitamwo igikoresho kizokenerwa kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha konti yawe. Yaba PC uriko urakoresha, telefone ngendanwa, canke urufunguzo rw’ibikoresho vy’ikimenyetso c’ubwoko bwa YubiKey.





- Niwahitamwo gukoresha porogaramu yo kwemeza kuri telefone yawe, nka Google Authenticator canke Authy nk'akarorero, ushobora kubona inyigisho kuri iki gikoresho hano :



https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/authentication/authy-a76ab26b-71b0-473c-aa7c-c49153705eb7

Tumaze guhitamwo uburyo bubereye wewe, amaherezo turiteguye gushiramwo amahera yo kugura amasatoshi yacu ya mbere.

![KRAKEN](assets/fr/20.webp)


Iyi nkuru, birashoboka, uhitemwo "Bitcoin".



![KRAKEN](assets/fr/29.webp)



Hanyuma uhitemwo "Lightning" canke "Bitcoin" maze wirengagize ibindi - ni amafaranga gusa ataco ahuriyeko na Bitcoin.



Nk’ukwibutsa, Lightning Network iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukora Exchange Bitcoin ubwo nyene kandi ufise ubuzima bwite bwinshi, ugereranyije n’urubuga rwa Bitcoin, ruzosaba iminota mirongo myinshi imbere y’uko Exchange ifatwa nk’iya nyuma.



Niba ukunda Lightning Network, turafise kandi inyigisho yuzuye kugira ngo ushobore gutahura ingene ikora:



https://planb.network/courses/34bd43ef-6683-4a5c-b239-7cb1e40a4aeb

*Ku karorero kacu, tuzoshiramwo amahera angana n'amadolari 21 y'ama bitcoins, ayo tuzokoresha mu kwerekana igice co kugura/kugurisha haciye igihe gitoyi*

Reka dukore ikigeragezo tugerageze kugura ama € 10 ya Bitcoin kubera ama € 21 twashize kuri konti yacu ya Kraken mu gice ca **4 - Gushiramwo no gukura ama euros (fiat)**.

### 6.4 - Gugura / Gurisha biciye kuri Kraken Pro



Kraken pro, atari yo nkuru y’iyi nyigisho, naho biri ukwo, itegerezwa kuganirwako kubera imvo imwe ihambaye. Iyi ni Interface yagenewe abakoresha bateye imbere aho gukoresha abazigama Bitcoin boroshe.



Ni bike cane kuruta urubuga rwa kera rwa Kraken, kandi rutanga ubundi buryo bwinshi bugenewe cane cane abacuruzi.



Ariko rero, birabereye kumenya ko amafaranga asabwa ari hasi y'ayo **1%** yishurwa na Interface **Kraken *simple***. Mu kugereranya, **Kraken Pro** itanga amafaranga adondowe aha hepfo.



Mu gusigura muri make, ku kugura/kugurisha Bitcoin iri munsi ya 10.000 USD ku kwezi (ivyo bizoshika kuri benshi muri twebwe), nk'"Umufata" twungukira ku mahera 0.4%, ni ukuvuga incuro zirenga 2 zitari munsi ya **Kraken *simple***.



Nk'"Umuhinguzi", ni vyiza cane, kuko wungukira ku mafaranga 0.25% - incuro 4 zihendutse kuruta biciye kuri Kraken ***simple***.



(Kugira ngo umenye, uri "Uwufata" iyo ugura/ugurisha kugiciro c'isoko, kandi uri "Umuhinguzi" iyo uremye itegeko ryo kugura/ugurisha kugiciro kitari igiciro c'isoko).



![KRAKEN](assets/fr/51.webp)



Kugira ngo ushikire **Kraken Pro** , kanda mu mfuruka yo hejuru iburyo bw'ibarabara ku tudodo 9 dutoduto dushizwe mu nzira y'inzira maze uhitemwo "Kraken Pro".



![KRAKEN](assets/fr/52.webp)

Uburyo bwo kugurisha na Kraken Pro ni co kimwe nyene, kandi ntibuzokwihwezwa hano. Ni ikibazo gusa co gukwegera umukoresha ku ntandukaniro y’ibiciro hagati y’ivyo bisubizo 2.
