> Bitcoin: Uburyo bwo gutanga amahera y’ubuhinga bwa none

Ivyo nivyo Bitcoin ivuga, gukoreshwa n’abandi ata n’umwe asavye uruhusha. Ubu, abacuruzi bake cane ni bo bemera Bitcoin muri Exchange ku bicuruzwa vyabo n’ibikorwa vyabo (turacari kare cane), ivyo bikaba bituma bigorana kuri twebwe abakeneye gukoresha amasatoshi yacu. **Ishirahamwe Bitcoin** riri hano kugira rigufashe gutorera umuti iki kibazo, rigufashe gukoresha amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins kugira **ugure amakarita y'ingabirano**



Kuri iyo nkuru, insiguro ngufi ku ruhande kugira ngo nshimike ku kamaro, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, ko gukoresha Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura aho gukoresha gusa nk’ububiko bw’agaciro. Nkako, uko iyo mirongo iriko irandikwa, reflex y’aba Bitcoiners benshi cane, iyo bafise akaryo ko gukoresha Sats yabo y’agaciro, ni ukwirinda kubigira, ahubwo bagakoresha euro / fiat shitcoin babategetse.



Iyo ubibonye, ​​ivyo vyoshobora gusa n’ibibereye ata gatotsi, mugabo biratuma wibaza ukuntu ivyo bihuye n’ubwenge. Bitcoin yagenewe gukoreshwa ata ruhusha. None ntibigaragara ko uko ubucuruzi bwemera Bitcoin bugenda buragwira, niko ugira amahitamwo menshi nk’umunya Bitcoin afise ubusegaba? Mu yandi majambo, uko bikorohera gukoresha Sats yawe utasavye uruhusha, niko Bitcoins zawe bwite zigira agaciro. Rero muri iyo nsiguro, urakeneye guhimiriza abantu kwemera, ugashigikira ubucuruzi bwemera Bitcoin kugira ngo bubahe ubushobozi bwo guhiganwa buzotuma abandi bakurikiza akarorero kabo.



Rero no mu bijanye no kuzigama agaciro k'amahera umuntu azigamye, kugira uruhara mu kwemera Bitcoin kugira ngo yorohe uko bishoboka kwose kuyakoresha, nibura birahambaye nka "HODL".



Niba ubwirizwa guca muri Exchange kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha Bitcoins zawe, rero zishobora kuba zifise agaciro ka 0. Niba ushobora kuzikoresha aho hose, zifise agaciro kanini cane.



Muri make, "gukoresha" & "gusubirira" bisa n'uko ariyo nzira y'imbere. Nimugire uruhara mu gutegura ivy’ugutora abana, mugatanga amasatoshi mu bibanza vyinshi bishoboka, hanyuma mugasubire kugura Sats yakoreshejwe vuba bishoboka.

## Noshobora gute gukora konti ya Sosiyete Bitcoin?

Kubera intumbero z’iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko turahitamwo iyo ya nyuma, kuko bitandukanye n’iporogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa, isaba ko ukora konti ukoresheje e-mail Address n’ijambobanga, verisiyo y’urubuga iratanga uburyo bwinshi, nk’uko tuzobibona aha hepfo.

Niba uri mw'ishure rya kera kandi ushaka kumenya ingene wokora mu buryo bworoshe ubusandugu bw'ubutumwa butekanye no gucunga amajambo y'ibanga yawe, ndagusavye urabe izi zindi nyigisho 2:

## Ni gute wogura ikarita y’ingabirano ya BTC kuri Sosiyete The Bitcoin?



Tangana uhitemwo urutonde rw'ibicuruzwa ukunda. Muri twebwe, kuko tugiye kuja mu biruhuko kandi dukeneye gutora amahoteli, tuzohitamwo "Ingendo".



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/12.webp)



Hanyuma "Ihoteri.com".



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/13.webp)


Ubu uri kuri paji y’ugushikiriza ikarita y’ingabirano wahisemwo. Ntukekeranye kuraba ido n’ido ryoshobora kuba rihambaye igihe ukoresha ikarata iri ku rubuga rw’umudandaza. Niwinjize amahera wipfuza gushiramwo kw’ikarata y’ingabirano. Muri aka karorero, tuzohitamwo amadolari 10, ariko ushobora gushika ku madolari 500 kuri iyi karata y’ingabirano. Igiciro gitoyi n’ikinini c’ikarata y’ingabirano kiratandukanye ku maduka ku rindi.



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/14.webp)



Impembo ishobora gusubizwa amahera muri Bitcoin yerekanwa hejuru y’insobanuro (aha 4%).



Mu gushiramwo akamenyetso kuri buto ya "Apply Reward Balance", urashobora guhitamwo gukoresha amahera y'inyuma y'ivyo waguze kera kugira ngo ushobore gutanga amahera y'iyi.



Ubwa nyuma, buto ya "Send as Gift" iragufasha kohereza ikarita y'ingabirano ku muntu afise amahirwe yo kuyironka biciye kuri e-mail, ivyo ni vyo tuzokora.



Ubwa nyuma, fyonda ku "generate Invoice".



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/15.webp)



Ishyure Invoice hanyuma ugaragaze On-Chain canke Umuravyo.



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/16.webp)



Iyo Invoice imaze kwishurwa, uzobwirwa ko iyo karata y’ingabirano iherutse gutumwa kuri e-mail ku muntu yatowe mu ntambwe ibanza. Reka turabe uko bigenda.



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/17.webp)



Twararonse vy’ukuri email yacu irimwo ikarita y’ingabirano. Mu gukopa uruja n’uruza rwatanzwe maze tukarufungura mu mucukumbuzi, tuzoca tuja kuri paji y’urubuga izotuma turonka amakuru akenewe kugira ngo dukoreshe iyo karata ku rubuga rw’uwo mudandaza.



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/18.webp)



Fyonda kuri "Reveal card details" kugira uronke ama code akenewe kugira ngo ukoreshe ikarita igihe uriha ku rubuga rw'umucuruzi.



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/19.webp)



Nimba udashaka kohereza ikarita y'ingabirano kuri e-mail, umaze kwishura Invoice, ushobora gufyonda gusa kuri "View Card" kugira ngo ubone urubuga nk'uko biri haruguru.



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/20.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, urashobora kubona amakarata yose y'ingabirano yaguzwe biciye kuri iyi konti igihe cose biciye ku rubuga "Amakarata yanje" ruri hejuru y'ibarabara. Ikarata imaze gukoreshwa, ntukibagire kuyibika mu bubiko kugira ngo uzoyikoreshe mu gihe kizoza. Ivyo bizokuronsa ubushobozi bwo kumenya neza hagati y’amakarata yawe agikora, n’ayo asanzwe ari ubusa.



![TheBitcoinCompany](assets/fr/21.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, nimba wipfuza gukoresha **kuri telefone ngendanwa** uburyo bwo kwemeza LNURL Auth twabonye muri iyi nyigisho, uzokenera kwinjira ku rubuga rwa The Bitcoin Company biciye ku mucukumbuzi wawe wa telefone ngendanwa, hanyuma wongereko inzira ngufi ku rubuga rwawe rw’inzu. Porogaramu yemewe yemerera gusa kwemeza umuntu biciye kuri email/ijambobanga.



Kandi aho urafise, ubu urazi gukoresha ama bitcoins yawe ata KYC ku maduka akomeye kubera amahera ukoresha buri musi. Si uburyo bushimishije cane, kandi buracari "ukwikorera". Ariko gushika abantu benshi bamenye ko ari mu nyungu zabo kwemera amahera y'ubuhinga bwa none mu bucuruzi bwabo, biracari ngirakamaro cane...



Niba wifuza gufasha gukwiragiza Bitcoin no guhimiriza umucuruzi yigenga kwemera ubu buryo bwo kwishura, ndagusavye urabe inyigisho yacu yuzuye ku vyerekeye Swiss Bitcoin Pay.
