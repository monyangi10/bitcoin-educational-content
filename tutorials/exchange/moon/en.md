Moon lets you use cryptocurrencies like bitcoin to purchase KYC-free virtual Visa cards and gift cards, which can be used at millions of online merchants, just like a traditional bank card. It thus offers a simple way to spend your sats wherever Visa is accepted, without going through an exchange platform.


This approach illustrates Moon's primary ambition: to make bitcoin accessible to the general public by facilitating everyday payments throughout the world, while waiting for more merchants to accept this means of payment natively. It's a sort of bridge between Bitcoin and the traditional banking world. In this tutorial, we invite you to discover this innovative service, its concept and the main features it offers.


## Get your virtual card


Online payments represent a revolution brought about by the evolution of the Internet, amplifying exchanges between all countries, regardless of their geographical position. In this context, two giants in particular stand out for their international coverage: **Visa** and Mastercard**. The aim of these companies is to provide payment cards, associated with bank accounts, to enable users to pay and make purchases.


Moon brings a major innovation: the ability to use bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies to fund these virtual bank cards. The service leverages Visa's infrastructure to enable you to obtain reloadable bitcoin cards (Lightning), without opening a bank account or providing any identity information, up to the specified usage limits.


With Moon, you have a real bridge between your bitcoins and the Visa card payment system, which is accepted on most merchant sites. You can use your bitcoins to :


- Shopping on e-commerce sites;
- Pay your subscriptions online ;
- And many other uses besides.


*Based on user experience, the Moon X Visa card works on almost all sites. Only a handful of merchants seem not to support it, but this is exceptional.*


Go to Moon's [official platform](https://paywithmoon.com), then click on the **"Register "** button, pass the human verification (CAPTCHA), then register by entering your email address and password.


![register](assets/fr/01.webp)


Please note: this password is the only protection you need to access the information on your future virtual card. Make sure you choose a strong, unique password.


![register2](assets/fr/02.webp)


Once your account has been created, on the **"Dashboard "**, you'll notice the following sections:


- The monthly spending limit granted, before having to do a KYC ;
- Moon credit balance ;
- Transaction history ;
- The addition of new cards.


![dashboard](assets/fr/03.webp)


To obtain a new map, click on the **"New map "** button. Moon offers several types of card:


- The Moon 1X** Visa card, a personal virtual card that can only be used in the United States and cannot be reloaded. This card lets you spend up to $1,000, with no transaction fees;
- The Moon X** Visa card, a personal virtual card available in over 147 countries, with a monthly spending limit of $4,000. It is valid for three years and charges a 1% transaction fee, with a minimum of $1 per transaction.


![visas](assets/fr/04.webp)



- Moon also offers a **gift card** service, enabling you to pay directly for specific products or subscriptions, such as Google Play Store, PlayStation Store, and many others.


![gifts](assets/fr/05.webp)


In this tutorial, we'll be using a ***Visa Moon X*** card. However, the acquisition process remains the same for all Moon virtual and gift cards.


![getCard](assets/fr/06.webp)


Click on the **Buy now** button, then accept the Visa card terms of use and eligibility.


![agree](assets/fr/07.webp)


Congratulations, you've just acquired your first virtual card.


![mooncard](assets/fr/08.webp)


You can recharge this virtual card using Moon credits.


![recharge](assets/fr/09.webp)


As you may have noticed, obtaining this virtual card does not require any identity information: all you need is an e-mail address and a password. KYC is only required if you exceed the monthly spending limits.


## Reload your card


The Moon card is topped up with Moon credits, which you can buy by paying with :


- Bitcoin (Lightning),
- USDT (TRC-20, on the Tron network),
- USDC (on the Polygon network).


Once your Moon virtual card has been activated, click on the **"Buy Moon Credits "** button, or on the **"Moon Credits "** balance displayed in your dashboard, to recharge your account in just a few moments.


![mooncredit](assets/fr/10.webp)


A minimum amount of 5 USD is set per reload, with no upper reload limit on your Moon X card. Insert the amount you wish to top up, then pay the Lightning invoice generated.


![invoice](assets/fr/11.webp)


Your card will be automatically credited as soon as payment of the Lightning invoice is confirmed.


![recharged](assets/fr/12.webp)


With this virtual card, you can pay for products and services online just as you would with any conventional Visa card. ## Organizing your cards


Moon is a service that allows you to have several cards on the same account. You can have a Moon Visa card and gift cards with different balances, or pay for gift cards from your Moon Visa X card.


![buygift](assets/fr/16.webp)


When you check the **Apply credit** box, Moon charges your gift card directly from your Visa card.


You now have a virtual card processor based on Visa's infrastructure, which respects your confidentiality and protects your data.
