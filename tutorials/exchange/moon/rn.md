## Uronke ikarita yawe



Ukwishura kuri Internet bigereranya ihinduka ryazanywe n’iterambere rya Internet, rituma habaho uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’ibihugu vyose, ata kuraba aho biherereye. Muri ivyo, amashirahamwe abiri akomeye cane cane aragaragara kubera ubuhinga bwayo bwo gutanga amakuru ku rwego mpuzamakungu: **Visa** na Mastercard**. Intumbero y’ayo mashirahamwe ni ugutanga amakarata yo kwishura, ajanye n’amakonte ya banki, kugira ngo abakoresha bashobore kwishura no kugura.



Ukwezi kuzana ikintu gishasha gikomeye: ubushobozi bwo gukoresha bitcoin n’ayandi mafaranga y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gutanga amahera kuri ayo makarita ya banki y’ubuhinga bwa none. Iyi serivisi ikoresha ibikorwa remezo vya Visa kugira ngo ushobore kuronka amakarita ya bitcoin (Lightning) ashobora gusubirwamwo, utafunguye konti ya banki canke ngo utanga amakuru y’akaranga, gushika ku rugero rwo gukoresha rwategekanijwe.



Ufise Ukwezi, urafise ikiraro nyaco hagati y’amahera yawe ya bitcoins n’uburyo bwo kwishura n’ikarita ya Visa, ivyo bikaba vyemewe ku mbuga nyinshi z’ubudandaji. Ushobora gukoresha amafaranga yawe kugira ngo :




- Gugura ku mbuga z’ubudandaji zo kuri interineti;
- Ishyure amafaranga yawe kuri internet ;
- N’ibindi vyinshi bikoreshwa uretse ivyo.



*Bishingiye ku bumenyi bw’abakoresha, ikarita ya Moon X Visa ikora hafi ku mbuga zose. Abacuruzi bakeyi gusa ni bo basa n’abatabishigikiye, mugabo ivyo ni ibidasanzwe.*
