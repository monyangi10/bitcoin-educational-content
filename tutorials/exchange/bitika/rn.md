Guhingura ubutunzi no gushiramwo akarere n’abanyagihugu bishingiye ku guhanahana ubutunzi. Ivyo biganiro biratuma amahera agendagenda kandi bikavyura ugukoresha. Uko igihe cagenda kirarenga, uguhanahana ubutunzi kwafashe uburyo bwinshi butandukanye, kuva ku guhinduranya gushika ku bukungu bw’ubuhinga bwa none n’ubutunzi bw’inganda.



Afirika iriko irarushiriza kuba ahantu ubutunzi bushingiye ku buhinga bwa none butera, cane cane biciye mu :




- ubuhinga bwo kohereza amahera ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi (ubuhinga bwa mbere bw’ubwo bwoko, M-Pesa, bwatangujwe muri Kenya n’ishirahamwe ry’amatelefone ryitwa Safaricom);
- n’itunga ry’ubuhinga bwa none (ahanini Bitcoin kubera kamere yayo yegerejwe, idacengera, n’ibindi).



*umuhinga mu vy'amaporogarama *FIDEL OTIENO** yaciye akora umuti - **Bitika** - uhuza Bitcoin n'amahera y'amatelefone ngendanwa kugira abantu bashobore kuronka ubufasha bw'ivy'amahera mu buryo bworoshe.



Umuti wa **Bitika** ni urubuga rwa Kenya rwagenewe kudategeka ibikoresho bishasha ku bikorwa vya misi yose, ahubwo kugira ngo ruherekeze abanyagihugu bo muri ako karere baja kuri Bitcoin biciye kuri M-Pesa, ivyo bamaze imyaka bamenyereye.



Abanyakenya rero barashobora kugura satoshis mu ntambwe zitatu zoroshe, ata KYC canke ama platforms akomeye yo guhinduranya.



## Gutangura na Bitika



Kugira ngo ukoreshe umuti, genda ku rubuga [Bitika](https://bitika.xyz/). Yaba kuri mudasobwa yawe canke kuri telefone yawe, interface iguma ari imwe.

4. amasakoshi y’umuravyo, nimba udasanzwe ufise.



## Intambwe zitatu zo kugura .



![screen|500](assets/fr/05.webp)



Kugira ngo ugure :


1. shiramwo amafaranga uguze (mu **KES**) n’aderesi ya Lightning ya wallet yawe;


2. winjize kandi wemeze inomero yawe ya M-Pesa;


3. shiramwo PIN code ya konti yawe y’amahera ya M-Pesa kugira ngo wemeze ko waguze.



wallet yawe hafi y’aho nyene irashirwako ama satoshis yawe.



![screen|250](assets/fr/06.webp)



Urashobora kubona ko amafaranga uguze kuri **Bitika** adashobora kuba **munsi ya** **50 KES** canke **kurenga** **10.000 KES** (amashilingi y’i Kenya). Iryo koraniro ryaramaze gushikirwa n’ubukene bw’ama satoshis (*atari mu bubiko*) kubera abantu benshi bayashaka. Ivyo vyerekana igishika abanyagihugu bo ng’aho bafise ku bijanye n’umuti.



Ibuka gusuzuma neza ibintu vyose kandi ushire akamenyetso ku gasandugu imbere y’uko utangura kwishura. Kandi, hariho amahera y’ibikorwa.



![screen|250](assets/fr/07.webp)



Ikindi, **OTP verification** yongeweko mu mezi make aheze kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gucungera amafaranga kubera abahendanyi bigira nk’ama platforms bagakwegera abantu ngo bohereze amafaranga biciye kuri M-Pesa. Kode y’igihe kimwe rero irarungikwa kuri telefone yawe biciye kuri SMS, ikaba ikora igihe gito. Uca uyishira mu kibanza catanzwe. Iyo umaze kwemezwa, uzosubira kuri paji y’ukwishyura.
