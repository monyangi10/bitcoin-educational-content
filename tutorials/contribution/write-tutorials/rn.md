Intumbero ya Plan ₿ Academy ni ugutuma ibikoresho vy’inyigisho bihambaye ku bijanye na Bitcoin biboneka mu ndimi nyinshi zishoboka. Ibintu vyose bisohoka kuri uru rubuga ni ivy’ugufungura kandi bishirwa kuri GitHub, bikaba biha umuntu wese akaryo ko kugira uruhara mu gutunganya urubuga. Intererano zishobora kuba mu buryo bwinshi: gukosora no gukosora ivyanditswe vyariho, guhindura mu zindi ndimi, guhindura amakuru canke mbere guhingura inyigisho nshasha zitaraboneka ku rubuga rwacu.


Muri iyi nyigisho, turaza kuraba ingene twokwongerako ibintu bishasha, nk’inyigisho canke inyigisho, ku rubuga rwa Plan ₿ Academy.


## Ni itandukaniro irihe riri hagati y'inyigisho n'inyigisho?


Ivyigwa bibiri nyamukuru vyo ku rubuga rwacu ni amashure n’inyigisho. Amashure atanga ibikoresho vy’inyigisho vy’ivyiyumviro, mu gihe inyigisho zitanga ibintu vy’ingirakamaro vyerekana ingene umuntu yokoresha igikoresho kinaka (nk’akarorero, Hardware Wallet canke porogarama) canke ubuhinga bumwe bumwe (nk’ugukingira ijambo Mnemonic canke gusuzuma ukuri kw’iporogarama).


Ivyigwa birareha kandi biratunganijwe neza kuruta inyigisho. Bakwiye gutohoza ingingo yihariye ijanye na Bitcoin canke ibidukikije vyayo mu buryo bwimbitse, mu buryo bubereye no mu buryo burambuye.


[Tora amashure ya Plan ₿ Academy](https://planb.urubuga/amashure)

Ivyigwa muri rusangi ni bigufi. Bigenewe kuba ubuyobozi bw’intambwe ku yindi ku buryo bwo gukora igikorwa kinaka gifitaniye isano na Bitcoin, hakaba hariho amashusho nk’infashanyo.


[Tora inyigisho za Plan ₿ Academy](https://planb.urubuga/inyigisho)

## Notanga gute inyigisho nshasha?


Kugira ngo wongere inyigisho nshasha kuri Plan ₿ Academy, ushobora kubigira kuri GitHub mu gutanga Igisabwa co Gukura. Mu nyigisho iri musi n’inyigisho ziherekejwe, ndagutwara intambwe ku yindi muri iyo nzira kandi ndakumenyesha amabwirizwa ukwiye gukurikiza kugira ngo umenye neza ko inyigisho yawe ihuye n’urubuga, mu gihe uguma ufise amateka asobanutse y’intererano.


## 1 - Hitamwo insanganyamatsiko y'inyigisho yawe


Turarondera cane cane inyigisho ku bikoresho bijanye na Bitcoin canke ibidukikije vyayo. Ibi birimwo bishobora guca mu mice itandatu nyamukuru:




- Igitabo ;
- Node ;
- Mining ;
- Umudandaza;
- Exchange ;
- Ibanga.


Uretse izo nsiguro zijanye cane na Bitcoin, Plan ₿ Academy iriko irondera kandi intererano ku nsiguro zishimika ku busegaba bw’umuntu ku giti ciwe, nk’izi:




- Ibikoresho vy'inkomoko yuguruye ;
- ICO ;
- Ivy'ugushiramwo amakuru ;
- Inguvu ;
- Ivy'imibare;
- Ubutunzi;
- DIY;
- Gutera ubuzima...


Nk’akarorero, ubu dufise inyigisho ku mirizo, Nostr na GrapheneOS. Ivyo bikoresho ntibifitaniye isano n’ivya Bitcoin, ariko ni uburyo bwoshobora kudufasha nk’igice c’ugutera imbere mu bijanye n’ubusegaba mw’isi y’ubuhinga bwa none. Ivyo birimwo bishobora gushirwa mu gice c'igice ca "Ibindi".


Ushobora guhingura inyigisho uhereye ku ntango, canke ugafata inyigisho yasohotse mbere ku rubuga rwawe (igihe cose ari wewe ufise uburenganzira bwo kuyikoresha) ukayisangiza abandi kuri Plan ₿ Academy na yo nyene, ukongerako uruja n’uruza rw’ingingo y’intango.


Uko wohitamwo kwose, ndagusavye wibuke ko ibirimwo vyose vyasohowe kuri Plan ₿ Academy bifise uruhusha rwo ku buntu [CC-BY-SA](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/). Uru ruhusha rwemerera umuntu wese gukopa no guhindura ibirimwo, ariko gusa iyo inkomoko y’intango ivuzwe.
