Intumbero ya PlanB ni ugutanga ibikoresho vy’inyigisho vyo ku rwego rwo hejuru ku Bitcoin, mu ndimi nyinshi zishoboka. Ibintu vyose bisohoka kuri uru rubuga ni open-source kandi bishirwa kuri GitHub, ivyo bikaba bituma umuntu wese agira uruhara mu gutunganya urubuga.


Woba ushaka kwongerako "umugambi" mushasha wa Bitcoin ku rubuga rwa Plan ₿ Academy maze ugatanga visibilité ku sosiyete yawe canke kuri software yawe, ariko ntuzi ingene? Iyi nyigisho ni iyawe!

![project](assets/01.webp)


- Ubwa mbere, ukeneye kugira konti ya GitHub. Niba utazi uko wokora konti, twakoze inyigisho ido n’ido kugira ngo ikuyobore.

- Niba utigeze utanga umusanzu ku birimwo Plan ₿ Academy mbere, uzokenera gukora Fork yawe y’ububiko bw’intango. Forking a repository bisigura gukora kopi y'ubwo bubiko kuri konti yawe ya GitHub, ivyo bikaba bigufasha gukora ku mugambi ataco uhinduye ku bubiko bw'intango. Fyonda kuri buto ya `Fork ubu bubiko`:

![project](assets/05.webp)


- Rema dosiye y'ishirahamwe ryawe. Kugira ngo ubikore, mu gasandugu ka `Izina rya dosiye yawe...`, wandike izina ry'ishirahamwe ryawe mu nyuguti ntoyi ukoresheje uturongo aho kwandika uturongo. Nk'akarorero, nimba ishirahamwe ryawe ryitwa "Bitcoin Baguette", ushobora kwandika `Bitcoin-baguette`:

![project](assets/06.webp)


- Kugira ngo wemeze ko dosiye yaremwe, wongereko gusa akarongo inyuma y'izina ryawe mu gasandugu kamwe, nk'akarorero: `Bitcoin-baguette/`. Kwongerako akarongo bihita bikora ububiko aho kuba dosiye:

![project](assets/07.webp)


- Muri iyi dosiye, uzokora dosiye ya mbere ya YAML yitwa `project.yml`:

![project](assets/08.webp)


- Uzuza iyi dosiye amakuru yerekeye ishirahamwe ryawe ukoresheje iyi nkuru:


```yaml
name:

address_line_1:
address_line_2:
address_line_3:

language:
-

links:
website:
twitter:
Github:
youtube:
nostr:

tags:
-
-

category:
```


Ehe ivyo wuzuza kuri buri rufunguzo:


- `izina`: Izina ry'ishirahamwe ryawe (ritegekanijwe);
- `Address` : Aho ubucuruzi bwawe buri (ntibikenewe);
- `ururimi` : Ibihugu ubucuruzi bwawe bukoreramwo canke indimi zishigikiwe (ntibikenewe);
- `amahuza` : Amahuza atandukanye yemewe y'ubucuruzi bwawe (ntibikenewe);
- `tags` : amajambo 2 akwiriye ubucuruzi bwawe (ni ngombwa);
- `category` : Urutonde rudondora neza igice ubucuruzi bwawe bukoreramwo mu mahitamwo akurikira:
 - 'Wallet`,
 - `ibikorwa remezo`,
 - 'Exchange`,
 - `uburezi`,
 - `igikorwa`,
 - `imiryango`,
 - `inama`,
 - `ubuzima bwite`,
 - `ishoramari`,
 - `urudodo`,
 - 'Mining`,
 - `amakuru`,
 - `umuhinguzi`.


Akarorero, dosiye yawe ya YAML yoshobora gusa n'iyi:


```yaml
name: Bitcoin Baguette

address_line_1: Paris, France
address_line_2:
address_line_3:

language:
- fr
- en

links:
website: https://bitcoin-baguette.com
twitter: https://twitter.com/bitcoinbaguette
Github:
youtube:
nostr:

tags:
- bitcoin
- education

category: education
```

- Ku rufunguzo rwa `abaterankunga`, ushobora kwongerako ikimenyetso c'umuterankunga wawe ku rubuga rwa PlanB nimba ufise. Niba utabikora, reka iki kibanza kiri ubusa.
- Ku rufunguzo rwa `insobanuro`, ukeneye gusa kwongerako paragarafu ngufi idondora ishirahamwe ryawe canke porogaramu yawe. Insobanuro igomba kuba mu rurimi rumwe n'izina rya dosiye. Ntukeneye guhindura iyi ndondoro mu ndimi zose zishigikiwe ku rubuga, kuko amashirahamwe ya PlanB azobikora akoresheje akarorero kabo. Nk'akarorero, ng'ibi uko dosiye yawe yoshobora gusa:

```yaml
description: |
Founded in 2017, Bitcoin Baguette is a Paris-based association dedicated to organizing Bitcoin meetups and technical workshops. We bring together enthusiasts, experts, and curious minds to explore and discuss the intricacies of Bitcoin technology. Our events provide a platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and fostering a deeper understanding of Bitcoin's inner workings. Join us at Bitcoin Baguette to be a part of Paris's Bitcoin community and stay updated with the latest advancements in the field.

contributors:
-
```
