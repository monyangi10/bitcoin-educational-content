## Rikosheti ni iki?



Ricochet ni ubuhinga bugizwe no gukora ibikorwa vyinshi vy’ibinyoma ku muntu kugira ngo yigane ukwimurira ubutunzi bwa bitcoin. Ico gikoresho gitandukanye n’ibindi bikorwa vya Ashigaru (vyarazwe na Samurai Wallet) kuko kitatanga ubutamenyekana bw’imbere, ahubwo ni uburyo bwo kutamenyekana bw’inyuma. Nkako, Ricochet irapfuka ibintu vyihariye bishobora gutuma igice ca Bitcoin kidakora neza.



Nk’akarorero, iyo ukoze coinjoin, igice cawe kiva mu mix kizomenyekana ko ari co. Ibikoresho vyo gusesangura uruzitiro birashobora kumenya uburyo bw’ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa coinjoin no guha ikimenyetso ibice biva muri vyo. Mu vy’ukuri, coinjoin igamije guca amasano y’amateka y’igiceri, ariko ukuntu gica mu coinjoins kiraguma kigaragara. Mu buryo bw’ugugereranya, ico kintu gisa n’ugushiramwo amakuru y’inyandiko: naho igisomwa c’intango kidashobora gushikwako mu nyandiko itomoye, biroroshe kumenya ko gushiramwo amakuru y’ibanga vyakoreshejwe.



Ikimenyetso "coinjoined" gishobora kugira ico gikoze ku bushobozi bwa UTXO. Ibigo bigenzurwa, nk’ibibanza vyo guhinduramwo amahera, birashobora kwanka kwemera UTXO ihuriweko, canke mbere bikasaba nyen’iyo mpapuro insobanuro, bikaba bishobora gutuma konti yawe ihagarara canke amahera yawe agahagarara. Mu bihe bimwebimwe, iyo nzira yoshobora mbere kumenyesha abategetsi ba Leta inyifato yawe.



Aho ni ho uburyo bwa Ricochet buza.Kugira ngo Ricochet izimangane ikimenyetso gisizwe n’amahera, ikora ibikorwa bine bikurikirana aho uwuyikoresha yirungikira amahera yiwe ku ma aderesi atandukanye. Inyuma y’uru rukurikirane rw’ibikorwa, igikoresho ca Ricochet kiraheza kigatuma ama bitcoins aja aho aja, nk’aho yoba ari ahantu ho guhinduranya. Intumbero ni ugutuma haba urugendo hagati y’ugucuruza kw’intango kw’amahera n’igikorwa ca nyuma co gukoresha amahera. Muri ubwo buryo, ibikoresho vyo gusesangura blockchain bizokwiyumvira ko kumbure habayeho ukwimurira ubutunzi inyuma y’aho coinjoin ikoreye, kandi ko rero ata nkenerwa yo gufata ingingo ku wuyitanga.



![image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Umuntu ahanganye n’uburyo bwa Ricochet, yoshobora kwiyumvira yuko porogarama yo gusesangura uruzitiro yoshobora gutuma isuzuma ryayo rirushiriza kurenga incuro zine. Ariko rero, izo nzira zihura n’ingorane yo gutuma urugero rwo kumenya ibintu rugenda neza. Bakeneye gushinga umubare w’imbere w’ibipfunsi hanyuma bakemera ko hariho uguhinduka kw’ubunywanyi, kandi ko ihuriro ry’ivy’imbere ry’imbere rikwiye kwirengagizwa. Ariko rero, kumenya iyo nzira ni akaga: ukwaguka kwose kw’umubare w’ibisimbuka vyerekanwa kwongerekana cane kw’ivyiza vy’ibinyoma, ni ukuvuga abantu bashizweko ikimenyetso mu makosa nk’abagira uruhara mu coinjoin, mu gihe mu vy’ukuri igikorwa cakozwe n’uwundi muntu. Ivyo bishobora gutuma ayo mashirahamwe agira ingorane nyinshi, kuko ivyiza bitari vyo bituma abantu badashira akanyota, ivyo bikaba bishobora gutuma abakiriya bashikiwe n’ingorane baja mu mahiganwa. Mu gihe kirekire, igipimo co kumenya ibintu gifise icipfuzo kirenze urugero gituma urubuga rutakaza abakiriya benshi kuruta abaruhanganye, ivyo bikaba vyoshobora gutuma urubuga rudashobora kubaho. Biragoye rero ko izo nzira zongerera umubare w’ibisubizo vyerekanwa, kandi 4 akenshi ni umubare uhagije wo guhangana n’isesengura ryazo.



Gutyo, **ikibazo gisanzwe co gukoresha Ricochet kivyuka iyo bikenewe guhisha uruhara rwa kera mu coinjoin ku UTXO ufise.** Mu vyiza, ni vyiza kwirinda gutanga bitcoins zaciye mu coinjoin ku bigo bitegekanijwe. Naho biri ukwo, mu gihe umuntu yoba asigara ata yindi nzira, cane cane mu gihe bikenewe vyihutirwa gusesa amafaranga y’ama bitcoins mu mafaranga ya Leta, Ricochet itanga umuti mwiza.



## None Rikosheti ikora gute kuri Ashigaru?



Ricochet ni uburyo bwo kwirungikira amafaranga, bwavumbuwe n’abahinguzi ba Samurai Wallet. Birashoboka rero neza cane kwigana Ricochet n’amaboko, ata gikoresho kidasanzwe gikenewe. Ariko rero, ku bipfuza gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none mu gihe bariko bararonka igisubizo ciza cane, igikoresho ca Ricochet kiboneka biciye ku gikorwa ca Ashigaru (ari co Samourai fork) kigereranya umuti mwiza.



Kubera ko Ashigaru isaba amahera y’ibikorwa vyayo, Ricochet igura `100.000 sats` nk’amahera y’ibikorwa, yongerako amahera y’ibikorwa vya mining. Ikoreshwa ryayo rero ni ngirakamaro ku bijanye no kwimurira amahera menshi.



Igikoresho ca Ashigaru gitanga uburyo bubiri bwa Ricochet:





- Reinforced Ricochet, canke "ugutanga ibicuruzwa bigenda bihinduka", bitanga akamaro ko gukwiragiza amafaranga y'ibikorwa vya Ashigaru ku bikorwa bitanu bikurikirana. Iryo hitamwo kandi rituma igikorwa cose gitangazwa ku gihe gitandukanye kandi kigashirwa mu kibanza gitandukanye, kigaca kigana neza uko bishoboka kwose inyifato y’uguhindura uwuyitunga. Naho bugenda buhoro, ubwo buryo buraryoshe ku bantu batari mu vyihuta, kuko butuma Ricochet ikora neza cane mu gukomeza ubushobozi bwayo bwo guhangana n’isesengura ry’uruzitiro;





- Ricochet ya kera, ikaba igenewe gukora igikorwa mu buryo bwihuta, igatangaza amakuru yose mu gihe gitoyi. Ubwo buryo rero buratanga ibanga ritoyi kandi ntibushobora guhangana n’ugusesangura kuruta uburyo bukomeye. Ishobora gukoreshwa gusa mu vyoherezwa vyihutirwa.



## Ni gute wokora Rikosheti kuri Ashigaru?



Gukora ricochet kuri Ashigaru biroroshe cane: gusa ukoreshe uburyo buhuye igihe urema igikorwa co kohereza. Kugira ngo utangure, fyonda ku buto `+`, hanyuma ukande kuri `Send`, hanyuma uhitemwo konti ushaka koherezako amahera.



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Uzuza amakuru y’ugucuruza: amahera azorungikwa n’aderesi ya nyuma y’uwuzoyakira inyuma y’aho Ricochet isimbukiye. Hanyuma usuzume uburyo bwa `Ricochet`.



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Ushobora rero guhitamwo hagati y’uburyo bubiri bwa Ricochet bwavuzwe mu gice c’ivyiyumviro: canke Ricochet ya kera, aho amafaranga yose akoreshwa ashirwa mu gice kimwe, canke ugutanga ibicuruzwa bigenda bihinduka, ivyo bikaba bituma Ricochet igira uburyo bwiza ku giciro c’uguteba igihe kirekire.



Umaze guhitamwo, kanda umwampi uri musi y’ibarabara kugira ngo wemeze.



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Ku gicapo gikurikira, uzobona incamake yuzuye y'ivyo wakoze. Aha niho kandi ushobora guhindura igiciro c’amahera y’ibikorwa vyawe vya Ricochet bivanye n’ingene isoko rimeze. Nimba vyose bigushimisha, nkwega umwampi w’icatsi kibisi kugira ngo usinye kandi ugabanye amafaranga y’i Ricochet.



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Rindira mu gihe ibisimbuka bitandukanye bigenda vyihuta.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Ivyo mwakoze vyaratangajwe neza.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)
