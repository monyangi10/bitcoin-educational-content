> *Muce uruja n'uruza ibiceri vyanyu bisiga inyuma*

Iki ni ikibazo nkunda kubazwa. **Igihe ukora coinjoins na Whirlpool, ni remix zingahe ukwiye gukora kugira ngo ubone ibisubizo bishimishije?**


Intumbero ya CoinJoin ni ugutanga ukwihakana gushobora kwemerwa mu kuvanga Coin yawe n’umugwi w’ibiceri bitatandukanijwe. Intumbero y’ico gikorwa ni uguca amahuzu y’ugukurikirana, kuva muri kahise gushika muri iki gihe, kuva muri iki gihe gushika muri kahise. Mu yandi majambo, umusesanguzi azi igikorwa cawe ca mbere mu kwinjira kw’ingendo za CoinJoin ntashobora kumenya neza UTXO yawe mu gusohoka kw’ingendo za remix (isesengura kuva ku nzira zo kwinjira gushika ku nzira zo gusohoka).

![past-present links diagram](assets/en/1.webp)


Ku rundi ruhande, umusesanguzi azi UTXO yawe ku gusohoka kw’ingendo za CoinJoin akwiye kutabasha kumenya igikorwa c’intango ku kwinjira kw’ingendo (isesengura kuva ku nzira zo gusohoka gushika ku nzira zo kwinjira).

![present-past links diagram](assets/en/2.webp)

Ariko rero, igitigiri c’indirimbo zisubirwamwo si co kintu co kwizigirwa co gusuzuma ingorane umusesanguzi yohura na zo mu gushinga amasano hagati ya kahise n’ubu, canke ibihushanye n’ivyo. Ikimenyetso gihambaye kuruta coba ari ubunini bw’imigwi Coin yawe yihishijemwo. Ivyo bimenyetso vyitwa "anonsets". Ku bijanye na Whirlpool, hari ubwoko bubiri bw’ibintu bitamenyekana.


Ica mbere, turashobora kumenya ubunini bw’umugwi aho UTXO yawe yihishije ku nzira y’isohoka ry’ingendo za CoinJoin, ni ukuvuga umubare w’ibiceri bitatandukanijwe biri muri uwo mugwi.

![probable UTXOs at exit](assets/en/3.webp)

Ico kigereranyo, citwa "prospective anonset" mu gifaransa, "forward anonset" mu congereza, canke "ibipimo vy'imbere", kidufasha gusuzuma ubushobozi bwa Coin yawe bwo guhangana n'isesengura rikurikirana inzira yayo kuva mu kwinjira gushika mu gusohoka kw'ingendo za CoinJoin. Iyi metric igereranya urugero UTXO yawe irindwako ku kugerageza gusubira kwubaka amateka yayo kuva aho yinjira gushika aho isohokera mu nzira ya CoinJoin. Nk'akarorero, iyo igikorwa cawe coba gifise uruhara mu nzira ya mbere ya CoinJoin kandi hakaba hariho izindi nzira zibiri z'inyongera, igihe co gucuruza ca Coin yawe coba `13`:

![forward anonset](assets/en/4.webp)

Ica kabiri, ikindi kigereranyo gishobora kubarwa kugira ngo umuntu asuzume ukuntu igice cawe gishobora guhangana n’isesengura kuva ubu gushika muri kahise. Mu kumenya UTXO yawe ku mpera y’ingendo, iki kimenyetso kigaragaza umubare w’ibikorwa vya Tx0 bishobora kuba vyari bigize inyungu yawe mu nzira za CoinJoin (isesengura kuva ku mpera gushika mu ntango y’ingendo). Iki kigereranyo gipima ingene bigoye umusesanguzi gukurikirana inkomoko y'igice cawe kimaze guca mu bice vy'amahera.![Isoko zishobora kuba ziri ku nzira](assets/en/5.webp)

Izina ry'iki kimenyetso ni "ugusubira inyuma" canke "ibipimo vy'inyuma". Mu gifaransa, ndakunda kuvyita "ugusubira inyuma kw'intango". Mu kigereranyo kiri musi, ivyo bihuye n’ibibumbano vyose vy’umukara Tx0:

![backward anonset](assets/en/6.webp)

Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku buryo bwo kubara ivyo bimenyetso, ndagusavye gusoma [urudodo rwanje rwa Twitter](20) kuri iyi nkuru. Turategura kandi ingingo irushirije kuba nziza ku bijanye n’Ihuriro rya PlanB.


Ndazi ko inyishu yatanzwe ishobora gusa n’iyidashimishije nk’uko mwari mwizigiye umubare wihariye w’ama remix, kandi ndabayobora ku nyandiko. Impamvu y’ivyo ni uko umubare w’ama remix ari ikimenyetso kitari co co gusuzuma ukutamenyekana kwaronswe mu nzinguzingu za CoinJoin. Ntibishoboka rero gusobanura umubare udahinduka w’ibisubizo nk’urugero rw’umutekano rwuzuye kandi rw’isi yose.


Ni ukuri ko igihe cose wongereyeko remix y’indirimbo yawe, yongera ama sets yayo y’ukutamenyekana. Ariko rero, birahambaye gutahura ko ahanini ari ama remixes akorwa n’urunganwe rwawe ari yo atuma ukura kw’ivyo uzoba uriko urakora. Hamwe n’akarorero ka Whirlpool, ugucuruza kwawe kurashobora gushika ku rwego runini rw’ugutahura kw’ivyizigiro n’ingendo zibiri canke zitatu gusa za CoinJoin, biciye ku gikorwa c’abo mu runganwe rwawe bari mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji vya kera.


Ku rundi ruhande, iyo anonset y’inyuma si ikintu kiduhagaritse umutima. Nkako, kuva kuri CoinJoin yawe ya mbere, wungukira ku ntunganyo y’ibikorwa vy’imbere vy’ipool, ubwo nyene ugaha igice cawe anonset y’inyuma cane, n’ukwiyongera kw’agace mu nzira yose ikurikira.

Ni ngombwa kandi gutahura ko iremwa ry’uguhakana ryumvikana ritagira aho rigarukira. Bishingiye ku bushobozi bwo gukurikirana Coin yawe. Ivyo bishoboka biragabanuka uko ubunini bw’imigwi ibihisha bugenda buragwira. Ukwiye rero guhindura intumbero zawe mu bijanye n’ibintu bitaboneka bivanye n’ivyo wewe ubwawe witega. Niwibaze imvo zituma ukoresha coinjoins n’ingero z’ukutamenyekana zikenewe kugira ngo ushikire izo ntumbero. Nk’akarorero, nimba gukoresha coinjoins bigamije gusa kuzigama ubuzima bwite bwa Wallet yawe igihe wohereje Sats nkeyi ku mwana wawe w’Imana ku musi wiwe w’amavuko, ingero zihambaye cane zo kutamenyekana ntizikenewe. Umwana wawe w’Imana kumbure ntashobora gukora isesengura ry’uruhererekane ry’ibintu vyimbitse, kandi naho vyoba ari vyo, ingaruka ku buzima bwawe ntizoba ari mbi cane. Ariko rero, nimba uri intumbero y’intwaro y’agahomerabunwa aho amakuru makeyi gusa yoshobora gutuma ufungwa, ivyo ukora bizokenera kuyoborwa n’ingingo ngenderwako zikomeye cane.


Kugira ngo umenye ivyo bimenyetso bizwi cane, ushobora gukoresha igikoresho ca Python citwa **WST** (Igikoresho ca Whirlpool Stats).


Ariko rero, si ngombwa ko wama ubara anonsets z’ibiceri vyose vyawe vy’amahera. Igishushanyo ca Whirlpool ubwaco kiramaze kuguha ivyemezo. Nk’uko twabibonye, ​​ukuntu umuntu abona ibintu inyuma ni gake cane. Kuva ku mix yawe ya mbere, uramaze kuronka amanota menshi cane y’ugusubira inyuma. Naho ku bijanye n’ugutahura, ukeneye gusa kugumiza Coin yawe muri konti y’inyuma y’uguvanga igihe kirekire bihagije. Nk’akarorero, ng’ibi ibiharuro vy’ibiceri vyanje `100.000 Sats` maze kumara amezi abiri mu kidengeri ca CoinJoin:

![WST anonsets](assets/en/7.webp)

Igaragaza amanota y'inyuma `34,593` n'amanota y'imbere `45,202`. Mu majambo nyayo, ivyo bisigura ibintu bibiri:


- Iyo umusesanguzi azi Coin yanje ku mpera y’ingendo akagerageza gukurikirana inkomoko yayo, bazohura n’amasoko ashobora kuba `34.593`, imwe yose ikaba ifise amahirwe angana yo kuba ayanje.
- Iyo umusesanguzi azi Coin yanje mu ntango z’ingendo akagerageza kumenya ukuntu ihuye mu mpera, azohura n’ibishoboka `45.202` UTXO, imwe yose ikaba ifise ubushobozi bumwe bwo kuba iyanje.

Ni co gituma mbona ko gukoresha Whirlpool ari ngirakamaro cane mu nzira ya `HODL -> Vanga -> Gukoresha -> Gusubirira`. Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, uburyo bubereye kuruta ubundi bwose ni ugutuma igice kinini c’amahera umuntu azigamye mu bitcoins mu Cold Wallet, mu gihe aguma afise umubare kanaka w’ibiceri mu CoinJoin kuri Samourai kugira ngo ashobore kwishura amahera akoresha ku musi. Igihe ama bitcoins ava mu coinjoins amaze gukoreshwa, asubirizwa n’ayandi mashasha kugira ngo asubire ku rugero rwasobanuwe rw’ama coinjoins. Ubu buryo buratuma twibohora ku bijanye n’amaganya y’ibintu bitari vyo vy’ama UTXO yacu, mu gihe dutuma igihe gikenewe kugira ngo coinjoins zibe nziza kibe gito cane.
