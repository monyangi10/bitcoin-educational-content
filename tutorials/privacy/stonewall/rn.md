## None ugucuruza kw’Ibuye ni iki?

Stonewall ni uburyo bwihariye bw’ugucuruza Bitcoin bugamije kwongerera ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha mu gihe c’ugucuruza mu kwigana CoinJoin hagati y’abantu babiri, ata mu vy’ukuri ari umwe. Nkako, iyo nzira y’ugucuruza si iyo gukorana n’abandi. Uwukoresha arashobora kuvyubaka wenyene, akoresheje gusa UTXO ziwe nk’ibintu vyinjizwa. Rero, urashobora gukora igikorwa ca Stonewall ku musi mukuru uwo ari wo wose utakeneye guhuza n’uwundi muntu akoresha.


Ibikorwa vy’ugucuruza kwa Stonewall ni ibi: nk’inyungu, uwurungika akoresha UTXO 2 ari izo. Nk’umusaruro, igikorwa gitanga umusaruro 4, harimwo 2 uzoba ungana neza na neza. Abandi 2 bazoba ari uguhinduka. Mu bisohoka 2 vy’amahera amwe, kimwe gusa ni co kizoja mu vy’ukuri ku wuzoronka amahera.


Hariho gusa uruhara 2 mu gucuruza Stonewall:


- Uwurungitse, ni we ariha vy’ukuri;
- Uwuronka, yoshobora kuba atazi kamere yihariye y’ivyo bicuruzwa kandi akaba yiteze gusa ko uwumurungikiye amwishura.


Reka dufate akarorero kugira ngo dutahure iyo nzira y’ugucuruza. Alice ari ku mfuruka y'imikate kugira ngo agure umukate wiwe, ukaba ugura `4.000 Sats`. Ashaka kwishura mu bitcoins mu gihe agumana urugero runaka rw’ubuzima bwite mu kwishura kwiwe. Ni co gituma afata ingingo yo gukora igikorwa co gukoresha Stonewall kugira ngo ashobore kwishura.

![transaction stonewall bakery](assets/en/1.webp)

Dusuzumye iyo nzira, turabona ko uwo mutetsi vy’ukuri yaronse `4.000 Sats` nk’amahera yo kwishura iyo baguette. Alice yakoresheje UTXO 2 nk’ibikoresho: imwe ya `10.000 Sats` n’iyindi ya `15.000 Sats`. Nk'umusaruro, yaronse UTXO 3: imwe y'amashanyarazi 4.000 Sats`, iyindi y'amashanyarazi `6.000 Sats`, n'iyindi y'amashanyarazi 11.000 Sats`. Alice ifise amafaranga `-4.000 Sats` muri iyo nzira, ahuye n’igiciro c’iyo baguette.


Muri aka karorero, narakuyeho n’ibigirankana amahera ya Mining kugira ngo nshobore gutahura. Mu vy’ukuri, amahera y’ugucuruza aratangwa n’uwurungitse.


## Ni itandukaniro irihe riri hagati y’Ibuye n’Ibuye x2?

Ibikorwa vya Stonewall bikora nk'ivyo StonewallX2 ikora, itandukaniro ryonyene ririho ni uko iryo rya nyuma risaba gukorana, bitandukanye n'ibikorwa vya kera vya Stonewall, ni co gituma ryitwa "x2". Nkako, igikorwa ca Stonewall gishobora gukorwa ata gufashanya kw’inyuma bisaba: uwugirungitse arashobora kugikora ata wundi muntu amufashije. Ariko rero, ku bijanye n'ugucuruza kwa Stonewall x2, uwundi muntu, yitwa "umufatanyabikorwa," arifatanya n'ivyo. Uwukorana atanga amafaranga yiwe bwite nk’inyungu, iruhande y’ay’uwurungitse, kandi akaronka amahera yose nk’inyungu (akuweko amafaranga Mining).


Reka dusubire ku karorero kacu na Alice ku mfuruka y’imikate. Iyo ashaka gukora igikorwa ca Stonewall x2, Alice yari gutegerezwa gukorana na Bob (umuntu agira gatatu) igihe yariko ararema igikorwa. Umwe wese muri bo yari gutanga inyishu UTXO. Bob yari rero kuronka umubare wose w’ivyo yinjije nk’ivyo asohora. Uwo mutetsi yari guhabwa amahera y’umukate wiwe nk’uko vyari vyifashe mu gihe c’ugucuruza Stonewall, mu gihe Alice yari gusubizwa amahera yiwe ya mbere, akuweko igiciro c’umukate.

![transaction stonewall x2](assets/en/2.webp)


Umuntu aravye hanze, uburyo bwo gucuruza bwari kuguma ari ubwo nyene.

![Stonewall or Stonewall x2 ?](assets/en/3.webp)


Mu ncamake, amasezerano ya Stonewall na Stonewall x2 asangiye imiterere imwe. Itandukaniro riri hagati y’ivyo bibiri riri mu kamere kavyo ko gukorana. Ivyo gucuruza Stonewall bitegurwa ku muntu ku giti ciwe, ata n’umwe akeneye gukorana. Mu buryo bunyuranye, igikorwa ca Stonewall x2 gishingiye ku gukorana hagati y’abantu babiri kugira ngo gishirwe mu ngiro.

## Ni igiki gifise intumbero y’ugucuruza Stonewall?

Igishushanyo ca Stonewall congera umubare munini w’uburemere bw’ibintu ku bijanye n’ugucuruza kandi kigapfukiranya isesengura ry’uruzitiro. Umuntu aravye hanze, mwene ukwo gucuruza gushobora gusobanurwamwo ko ari CoinJoin ntoyi hagati y’abantu babiri. Ariko mu vy’ukuri, nk’uko nyene vyagenze ku bijanye n’ugucuruza Stonewall x2, ni ukwishura. Ubwo buryo rero buratuma habaho ukudakeka mu gusesangura uruhererekane, kandi bushobora mbere gutuma habaho uburongozi bw’ibinyoma.


Reka dusubire kuraba akarorero ka Alice ku mfuruka y’imikate. Ivyo gucuruza kuri Blockchain vyoboneka gutya:

![Stonewall or Stonewall x2 ?](assets/en/4.webp)

Umuntu yihweza ibintu hanze yizigiye ubuhinga bwo gusesangura uruhererekane rwoshobora gushika ku ciyumviro c'uko "*abantu babiri bakoze CoinJoin ntoyi, umwe wese afise UTXO imwe nk'inyungu n'ibindi bibiri UTXOs nk'inyungu*".

![Stonewall or Stonewall x2 ?](assets/en/5.webp)

Iyi nsobanuro ntabwo ari yo kuko nk’uko mubizi, UTXO yarungikiwe umutetsi, izo UTXO 2 ziri mu vyo kwinjiza ziva kuri Alice, kandi yararonse ibisohoka 3 vy’uguhindura.


![transaction stonewall baker](assets/en/1.webp)

Naho umuntu wo hanze yoshobora kumenya ingene ivyo bicuruzwa vya Stonewall vyagenda, ntibazogira amakuru yose. Ntibazoshobora kumenya uwuhe muri izo UTXO zibiri z’amahera amwe ahuye n’ivyo kwishura. Ikindi kandi, ntibazoshobora kumenya nimba izo UTXO zibiri ziri mu vyo bashizemwo zikomoka ku bantu babiri batandukanye canke nimba ari iz’umuntu umwe yazihuje. Ico ciyumviro ca nyuma kiva ku kuba amafaranga y’i Stonewall x2, twavuganye haruguru, akurikira neza na neza uburyo bumwe n’ubw’amafaranga y’i Stonewall. Uvuye hanze kandi ata makuru y’inyongera ku bijanye n’aho ibintu bibera, ntibishoboka gutandukanya igikorwa ca Stonewall n’igikorwa ca Stonewall x2. Ariko rero, ivyo vya mbere si ivyo gukorana n’abandi, mu gihe ivyo vya nyuma ari vyo. Ivyo birongereza mbere n’amakenga menshi ku bijanye n’ayo mahera akoreshwa.
