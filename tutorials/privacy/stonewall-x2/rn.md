## Igikoresho ca Stonewall x2 ni iki?


Stonewall x2 ni uburyo bwihariye bwo gukoresha Bitcoin bugamije kwongerera ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha mu gihe c’ugukoresha amahera, mu gukorana n’uwundi muntu atagira uruhara muri ayo mafaranga. Ubwo buryo bwigana mini-CoinJoin hagati y’abantu babiri bari muri iyo nama, mu gihe bariko bariha uwundi muntu. Ivy’ugucuruza vya Stonewall x2 biraboneka kuri porogarama ya Samourai Wallet no kuri porogarama ya Sparrow wallet. Ivyo vyose birakorana.


Ivyo ikora birasanzwe: dukoresha UTXO dufise kugira ngo twishure kandi turonde imfashanyo y’uwundi muntu na we nyene atanga n’agapapuro ka UTXO kabo bwite. Iryo soko rituma habaho ibintu bine: bibiri muri vyo bifise amahera angana, kimwe kigenewe Address y’uwuronka amahera, ikindi kigenewe Address y’uwukorana. UTXO ya gatatu isubizwa ku yindi Address y’umufatanyabikorwa, bikaba bituma bashobora kuronka amahera y’intango (igikorwa kitagira aho kigarukira kuri bo, amafaranga modulo Mining), UTXO ya nyuma igasubizwa ku Address yacu, ivyo bikaba ari vyo bihinduka bivuye ku kwishura.


Gutyo, uruhara rutatu rutandukanye rurasobanurwa mu bikorwa vya Stonewall x2:


- Uwurungitse, ni we ariha vy’ukuri;
- Uwo mufatanyabikorwa, atanga bitcoins kugira ngo atere imbere mu bijanye n’ukutamenyekana muri rusangi kw’ibikorwa, mu gihe asubiza amahera yabo yose ku mpera (igikorwa kitagira aho kigarukira kuri bo, amafaranga modulo Mining);
- Uwuronka, yoshobora kuba atazi kamere yihariye y’ivyo bicuruzwa kandi akaba yiteze gusa ko uwumurungikiye amwishura.


Reka dufate akarorero kugira ngo tuvyiyumvire neza. Alice ari ku mfuruka y'imikate kugira ngo agure umukate wiwe, ukaba ugura `4.000 Sats`. Ashaka kwishura mu bitcoins mu gihe agumana urugero runaka rw’ubuzima bwite ku bijanye n’ukwishura kwiwe. Aca rero ahamagara umugenzi wiwe Bob, azomufasha muri ivyo.

![schema stonewall x2](assets/en/1.webp)

Dusuzumye iyo nzira, turashobora kubona ko uwo mutetsi vy’ukuri yaronse `4.000 Sats` nk’amahera yo kwishura iyo baguette. Alice yakoresheje `10.000 Sats` nk’ivyo yinjiza, ironka `6.000 Sats` nk’ivyo isohora, ivyo bikaba vyatumye habaho `-4.000 Sats`, ivyo bikaba bihuye n’igiciro c’inyama z’ingurube. Naho Bob, yatanze `15.000 Sats` nk’inyungu, aronka n’ibisohoka bibiri: kimwe ca `4.000 Sats` ikindi ca `11.000 Sats`, bituma habaho uburinganire bwa `0`.

Muri aka karorero, narasuzuguye n’ibigirankana amahera ya Mining kugira ngo nshobore gutahura. Mu vy’ukuri, amahera y’ugucuruza asangizwa kimwe hagati y’uwurungika amahera n’uwukorana.

## Ni gute twoshinga isano hagati ya Paynyms kugira ngo bashobore gukorana biciye kuri Soroban?


Nk’uko biri ku bindi bikorwa vy’ubufatanye kuri Samourai (*Cahoots*), gukora Stonewall x2 birimwo Exchange y’ibikorwa vy’ubufatanye vyashizweko umukono hagati y’uwurungitse n’uwukorana. Iyi Exchange ishobora gukorwa n’amaboko, mu gihe woba uri kumwe n’uwo mukorana, canke ubwo nyene biciye ku nzira y’uguhanahana amakuru ya Soroban.


Niwahitamwo uburyo bwa kabiri, uzokenera gushinga isano hagati ya Paynyms imbere y'uko ushobora gukora Stonewall x2. Kugira ngo ubikore, Paynym yawe itegerezwa "gukurikira" Paynym y'uwo mukorana, n'ibihushanye n'ivyo.


**Ukwinjira ku Paynym y'umufatanyabikorwa:**


Kugira ngo utangure, birakenewe ko uronka kode y’ukwishura ya Paynym y’uwo mukorana. Mu gikorwa ca Samourai Wallet, uwo mukorana ategerezwa gukanda ku kimenyetso ca Paynym yabo (akarobo gatoyi) kari hejuru ibubamfu bw’ibarabara, hanyuma agakanda kw’izina ryabo ry’ibanga rya Paynym, atangura na `+...`. Nk'akarorero, icanje ni `+uburyo butagira izina0aF`.


![samourai paynym](assets/notext/6.webp)

Niba uwo mukorana ariko arakoresha Sparrow wallet, akwiye gufyonda ku 'Ibikoresho', hanyuma kuri 'Erekana PayNym'.![izina ry'amahera Sparrow](itunga/inyandiko/7.webp)

**Ukurikije izina ry'umufasha wawe rivuye kuri Samourai Wallet:**


Niba ukoresha Samourai Wallet, fungura porogarama maze winjire ku rutonde rwa 'PayNyms' mu buryo nk'ubwo nyene. Nimba ari ubwa mbere ukoresha PayNym yawe, uzokenera kuronka ikimenyetso cayo.


![request paynym samourai](assets/notext/8.webp)


Hanyuma ukande ku '+' y'ubururu iri hasi iburyo bw'ibarabara.

![add collaborator paynym](assets/notext/9.webp)

Ushobora rero gushiramwo kode y'ukwishyura y'uwo mukorana uhisemwo 'PASTE PAYMENT CODE', canke ugafungura kamera kugira ngo ushireko kode yabo ya QR ukanda 'SCAN QR CODE'.

![paste paynym identifier](assets/notext/10.webp)


Fyonda kuri buto ya 'KURIKIRA'.

![follow paynym](assets/notext/11.webp)

Wemeze ukanda 'EGO'.

![confirm follow paynym](assets/notext/12.webp)

Iyo porogaramu izoca iguha ubuto bwa 'CONNECT'. Si ngombwa gukanda kuri iyi buto kugira ngo ubone inyigisho yacu. Iyi ntambwe ikenewe gusa iyo utegura kwishura iyindi PayNym nk’igice ca BIP47, ivyo bikaba bidafitaniye isano n’inyigisho yacu.

![connect paynym](assets/notext/13.webp)

Igihe PayNym yawe iriko irakurikira PayNym y’uwo mukorana, subiramwo iyo nzira mu buryo bunyuranye kugira ngo uwo mukorana nawe ashobore kugukurikira. Ushobora rero gukora igikorwa ca Stonewall x2.


**Ukurikije PayNym y'umufasha wawe kuva kuri Sparrow wallet:**


Niba ukoresha Sparrow wallet, fungura Wallet yawe maze winjire ku rutonde rwa 'Show PayNym'. Niba uriko urakoresha PayNym yawe ku ncuro ya mbere, uzokenera kuronka ikimenyetso ukanda kuri 'Retrieve PayNym'.

![request paynym sparrow](assets/notext/14.webp)

Hanyuma winjize ikimenyetso ca PayNym c'umufatanyabikorwa wawe (yaba izina ryiwe ry'akataraboneka '+...' canke kode yiwe yo kwishura 'PM...') mu gasandugu 'Rondera uwo muvugana', hanyuma ukande kuri buto 'Ongera uwo muvugana'.

![add contact paynym](assets/notext/15.webp)

Iyo porogarama izoca iguha ubuto 'Link Contact'. Si ngombwa gukanda kuri iyi buto kugira ngo ubone inyigisho yacu. Iyi ntambwe ikenewe gusa iyo utegura kwishura kuri PayNym yerekanwa nk’igice ca BIP47, idafitaniye isano n’inyigisho yacu.


Igihe PayNym yawe iriko irakurikira PayNym y’uwo mukorana, subiramwo iyo nzira mu buryo bunyuranye kugira ngo uwo mukorana nawe ashobore kugukurikira. Ushobora rero gukora igikorwa ca Stonewall x2.
