## None Rikosheti ni iki?


Ricochet ni ubuhinga bujanye n’ugukora amafaranga menshi y’ibinyoma kugira ngo umuntu yigane ukwimurira Bitcoin Ownership. Ico gikoresho gitandukanye n’ibindi bikorwa vy’ubudandaji vy’Abasamorai kuko kitatanga ubutamenyekana bw’imbere, ahubwo ni uburyo bwo kutamenyekana bw’inyuma. Ricochet ifasha gupfuka ibintu vyihariye bishobora gutuma Bitcoin Coin idakora neza.


Nk’akarorero, iyo ukoze CoinJoin, umusaruro wawe wa Coin uva mu mvange uzomenyekana ko ari ukwo. Ibikoresho vyo gusesangura uruzitiro birashobora kumenya uburyo bw’ibikorwa vya CoinJoin no gushiramwo amazina y’ibiceri biva muri vyo. Nkako, CoinJoin ifise intumbero yo guca amahuzu y’akahise y’iyi Coin, ariko ukuntu ica mu bihimba vy’umubiri biguma bigaragara. Kugira ngo tugereranye, ico kintu gisa n’ugushiramwo amakuru mu nyandiko: naho tutashobora gushika ku nyandiko y’intango, birashoboka ko umuntu amenya bitagoranye ko hakoreshejwe uburyo bwo gushiramwo amakuru.


Mu vy'ukuri, iki kimenyetso ca "CoinJoin igisohoka Coin" gishobora kugira ico gikoze ku bushobozi bwa UTXO. Ibigo bigenzurwa, nk’ibibanza vya Exchange, birashobora kwanka kwemera UTXO yaciyemwo CoinJoin, canke mbere bisaba insobanuro kuri nyen’iyo, bishobora gutuma konti yabo ihagarara canke amahera agahagarara. Mu bihe bimwebimwe, iyo nzira yoshobora mbere kumenyesha abategetsi ba Leta inyifato yawe.


Aho ni ho uburyo bwa Ricochet buza. Kugira ngo ikirenge gisizwe na CoinJoin gipfuke, Ricochet ikora ibikorwa bine bikurikirana aho uwuyikoresha yirungikira amahera yiwe ku ma aderesi atandukanye. Inyuma y’uru rukurikirane rw’ibikorwa, igikoresho ca Ricochet kiraheza kigatuma ama bitcoins aja aho aja, nk’urubuga rwa Exchange. Intumbero ni ugutuma haba urugendo hagati y’isoko ry’intango rya CoinJoin n’igikorwa ca nyuma co gukoresha amahera. Muri ubwo buryo, ibikoresho vyo gusesangura uruhererekane bizokwiyumvira ko bishoboka ko habayeho ukwimurirwa kwa Ownership inyuma ya CoinJoin, rero ntibikenewe ko umuntu agira ico akoze ku muntu yarungitse.


![ricochet diagram](assets/en/1.webp)


Mu gihe c’uburyo bwa Ricochet, umuntu yoshobora kwiyumvira ko porogarama yo gusesangura uruzitiro yotuma isuzuma ryabo rirushiriza gukomera birenze amabounces ane. Ariko rero, izo nzira zihura n’ingorane yo gutuma urugero rwo kumenya ibintu rugenda neza. Bategerezwa gushinga urugero rw’umubare w’ibitoke hanyuma bakemera ko hariho ihinduka ry’umutungo rishobora kuba ryabaye kandi ko isano riri hagati ya CoinJoin ya kera rikwiye kwirengagizwa. Ariko rero, kumenya iyo nzira birateye akaga: igihe cose umubare w’ibihimba vy’umubiri vyerekanwa wongerekana cane, ni ukuvuga abantu bashizweko ikimenyetso c’uko ari abaje mu gikorwa ca CoinJoin, mu gihe igikorwa cakozwe n’uwundi muntu. Ivyo bishobora gutuma ayo mashirahamwe agira ingorane nyinshi, kuko ivyiza bitari vyo bituma abantu badashira akanyota, ivyo bikaba bishobora gutuma abakiriya bashikiwe n’ingorane baja mu mahiganwa. Mu gihe kirekire, uruzitiro rufise icipfuzo kinini cane rutuma urubuga rutakaza abakiriya benshi kuruta abaruhanganye, ivyo bikaba vyoshobora gutuma urubuga rudashobora kubaho. Biragoye rero ko izo nzira zongerera umubare w’ibisubizo vyerekanwa, kandi 4 akenshi ni umubare uhagije wo guhangana n’isesengura ryazo.


Gutyo, **ikibazo gisanzwe co gukoresha Ricochet kivyuka iyo bikenewe guhisha uruhara rwa kera mu CoinJoin ku UTXO ari iyawe**. Mu vyiza, ni vyiza kwirinda gutanga ama bitcoins yaciye mu CoinJoin ku bigo bigenzurwa. Ariko rero, mu gihe ata yindi nzira iriho, cane cane mu gihe vyihutirwa guhindura amafaranga y’ama bitcoins mu mafaranga y’amafaranga, Ricochet itanga umuti mwiza.


## None Ricochet ikora gute ku ndege ya Samourai Wallet?

Ricochet ni uburyo gusa aho umuntu yirungikira ama bitcoins. Birashoboka rero rwose kwigana Ricochet n’amaboko ata gikoresho kidasanzwe akoresheje. Ariko rero, ku bipfuza gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none mu gihe bungukira ku ciyumviro ciza cane, igikoresho ca Ricochet kiboneka biciye ku gikorwa ca Samourai Wallet ni umuti mwiza.


Kubera ko iyo nsiguro irihwa kuri Samourai, Ricochet igira amahera angana na `100.000 Sats` nk’amahera y’ibikorwa, yongerako amahera y’ama Mining. Gutyo, ikoreshwa ryayo riraremeshwa cane ku bijanye no kwimurira amahera menshi.


Igikoresho ca Samourai gitanga uburyo bubiri bwo gukoresha Ricochet:


- Ico gikoresho citwa Reinforced Ricochet, canke “ugutanga ibicuruzwa bigenda bihinduka,” kiratanga akamaro ko gukwiragiza amafaranga y’ibikorwa vy’i Samourai ku bikorwa bitanu bikurikirana. Iryo hitamwo kandi rituma igikorwa cose gitangazwa ku gihe gitandukanye kandi kigafatwa mu gice gitandukanye, ivyo bikaba vyigana cane inyifato y’ihinduka rya Ownership. Naho bugenda buhoro, ubwo buryo burahimbaye ku bantu batari mu vyihuta, kuko butuma Ricochet ikora neza cane mu gutuma ishobora guhangana n’ugusesangura uruzitiro.
- Ricochet ya kera, ikaba igenewe gukora igikorwa vyihuse mu gutangaza ibikorwa vyose mu kiringo gitoyi. Ubwo buryo rero buratanga ubuzima bwite buke kandi buratuma umuntu adashobora gusesangura ugereranije n’uburyo bukomeye. Ishobora gukundwa gusa ku bijanye n’uguhindura abantu vyihutirwa.
