Naho Sparrow, Payjoins biciye kuri BIP78 iracariko irakora. Ariko birashoboka ko ivyo bikoresho bizosubira gukoreshwa mu ndwi ziza. Hagati aho, urashobora gusoma iki kiganiro kugira ngo utahure ingene Stowaway ikora mu vy’inyigisho.*

Itandukaniro rikomeye riri mu guhitamwo ubwoko bw'ubucuruzi bwa Stowaway: aho guhitamwo `Kuri Internet`, kanda kuri `Mu muntu / mu Gitabu`. Hanyuma, uzokenera gukoresha amaboko Exchange PSBTs kugira ngo wubake igikorwa ca Stowaway. Nimba uri hafi y’uwo mukorana, urashobora gucapura amakode ya QR akurikirana. Niba uri kure, amadosiye ya JSON arashobora guhindurwa biciye ku nzira y’itumanaho itekanye. Ibindi vyigwa ntibihindutse._

_Iyi nyigisho itangwa ku ntumbero y'inyigisho n'amakuru gusa. Ntidushigikira canke ngo turemeshe gukoresha ivyo bikoresho mu ntumbero z’ubugizi bwa nabi. Ni inshingano y'ukoresha wese kwubahiriza amategeko ari mu bubasha bwiwe._

> *Ghatira abatasi ba Blockchain gusubira kwiyumvira ivyo biyumvira ko bazi vyose.*

PayJoin ni uburyo bwihariye bwo gukoresha Bitcoin butera imbere ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha mu gihe c’ugukoresha amahera mu gukorana n’uwuronka amahera. Hariho uburyo bwinshi bwo gushirwa mu ngiro bworohereza gutegura no gukoresha PayJoin. Muri izo nzira, izwi cane ni Stowaway, yateguwe n'imigwi yo muri Samourai Wallet. Iyi nyigisho irasigura ingene wokora igikorwa ca Stowaway PayJoin ukoresheje porogarama ya Samourai Wallet.


## None Stowaway ikora gute?


Nk'uko twabibonye, ​​Samourai Wallet itanga igikoresho ca PayJoin citwa "Stowaway." Ushobora kuyironka biciye kuri porogarama ya Sparrow wallet iri kuri PC canke kuri porogarama ya Samourai Wallet iri kuri Android. Kugira ngo umuntu akore PayJoin, uwuyironka, na we nyene akora nk’umufasha, ategerezwa gukoresha porogarama zihuye na Stowaway, ni ukuvuga Sparrow canke Samourai. Izo porogarama zibiri zirakorana, zituma habaho ugucuruza kwa Stowaway hagati ya Sparrow wallet na Samourai Wallet, n’ibihushanye n’ivyo.


Stowaway yizigiye urutonde rw'ibikorwa Samourai yita "Cahoots." Cahoot ni ugukorana hagati y'abakoresha benshi, bisaba amakuru ya off-chain Exchange. Kugeza ubu, Samourai itanga ibikoresho bibiri vya Cahoots: Stowaway (Ivyuma bifatanya) na StonewallX2 (ivyo tuzobitohoza mu kiganiro kizoza).


Ibikorwa vya Cahoots birimwo uguhanahana ibikorwa vyashizweko umukono hagati y’abakoresha. Ivyo bishobora kuba bimara igihe kirekire kandi bikaba bigoye canecane iyo bikozwe kure. Ariko rero, birashobora gukorwa n’amaboko n’uwundi muntu, ivyo bikaba bishobora kuba vyiza iyo abakorana bari hafi y’umubiri. Mu vy’ukuri, ivyo bisaba ko umuntu ahinduranya n’amaboko amakode atanu ya QR kugira ngo akurikirane.


Iyo bikozwe kure, iyo nzira iraba igoye cane. Ku Address iki kibazo, Samourai yarateguye uburyo bwo guhanahana amakuru bushingiye kuri Tor, bwitwa "Soroban." Na Soroban, amahinduka akenewe ku PayJoin akora inyuma ya Interface ikoreshwa neza. Ubu ni bwo buryo bwa kabiri tuzokwiga muri iki kiganiro.


Ivyo biganiro vy’ubuhinga bwa none bisaba ko habaho ubucuti n’ukwemeza hagati y’abaje muri Cahoots. Ivy’itumanaho vya Soroban rero bishingiye ku mazina y’abakoresha. Niba utazi neza amazina y’amahera, ndagutumiye ngo urabe iyi nkuru kugira ngo umenye vyinshi: [BIP47 - PAYNYM]



Mu kuvuga mu buryo bworoshe, Paynym ni ikimenyetso kidasanzwe gihuye na Wallet yawe gishobora gukora ibikorwa bitandukanye, harimwo n’ubutumwa bushizwe mu mfuruka. Iryo zina rya Paynym rigaragazwa mu buryo bw’ikimenyetso n’ikigereranyo kigereranya ururobo. Aha niho akarorero kanje kuri Testnet: ![izina ry'amahera Wallet](itunga/ru/1.webp)


**Mu ncamake:**


- _Payjoin_ = Uburyo bwihariye bw'ibikorwa vy'ubufatanye;
- _Stowaway_ = Ishirwa mu ngiro rya PayJoin riboneka ku Samourai na Sparrow wallet;
- _Cahoots_ = Izina ry’aba Samourai ku bwoko bwose bw’ibikorwa vyabo vy’ubufatanye, harimwo na PayJoin Stowaway;
- _Soroban_ = Itegeko ry'itumanaho ry'ibanga ryashinzwe kuri Tor, ryemerera gukorana n'abandi bakoresha mu bijanye n'ugucuruza kwa Cahoots;
- _Paynym_ = Ikimenyetso kidasanzwe ca Wallet cemerera guhanahana amakuru n'uwundi muntu kuri Soroban, kugira ngo akore igikorwa ca Cahoots.


[**-> Menya vyinshi ku bijanye n’ibikorwa vya PayJoin n’akamaro kavyo**]


## Ni gute twoshinga isano hagati ya Paynyms?


Kugira ngo ukore igikorwa ca Cahoots co kure, cane cane PayJoin (Stowaway) biciye kuri Samourai, birakenewe ko "Ukurikira" umukoresha ushaka gukorana na we, ukoresheje Paynym yabo. Ku bijanye na Stowaway, ivyo bisigura gukurikira umuntu ushaka kohereza ama bitcoins.


**Ehe uburyo bwo gushinga iyo nzira:**


Kugira ngo utangure, ukeneye kuronka kode y’ukwishura y’izina ry’uwuronka ry’urupapuro rwa PayJoin. Mu gikorwa ca Samourai Wallet, uwuronka ategerezwa gufyonda ku kimenyetso c’izina ryiwe ry’ibanga (akarobo gatoyi) kari hejuru ibubamfu bw’ibarabara, hanyuma agafyonda ku izina ryiwe ry’akataraboneka rya Paynym, atangura na `+...`. Nk'akarorero, icanje ni `+uburyo butagira izina0aF`. Nimba uwo mukorana akoresha Sparrow wallet, ndagutumiye ngo urabe inyigisho yacu yihariye ukanda hano.


![connexion paynym samourai](assets/notext/2.webp)


Uwo mukorana azoca arungikwa kuri paji yabo ya Paynym. Bavuye ng’aho, barashobora kugusangiza amakuru yabo ya Paynym canke bakagusangiza kode yabo ya QR kugira ngo uyikoreshe. Kugira ngo babikore, bategerezwa gufyonda ku kimenyetso gitoyi citwa "share" kiri hejuru iburyo bw'ibarabara ryabo.

![partager paynym samourai](assets/en/1.webp)


Ku ruhande rwawe, fungura porogarama yawe ya Samourai Wallet maze winjire ku rutonde rwa "PayNyms" mu buryo bumwe. Nimba ari ubwa mbere ukoresha Paynym yawe, uzokenera kuronka ikimenyetso.


![demander un paynym](assets/notext/3.webp)


Hanyuma ukande ku kimenyetso c'ubururu "+" kiri hasi iburyo bw'ibarabara.

![ajouter paynym collaborateur](assets/notext/4.webp)

Ushobora rero gushiramwo kode y'ukwishyura y'umufasha wawe uhisemwo `COLLER LE CODE PAIEMENT`, canke ugafungura kamera kugira ngo ushireko kode yabo ya QR ukanda `SCANNEZ LE CODE QR`.![shiramwo ikimenyetso c'izina ry'ukwishyura](itunga/inyandiko/5.webp)


Fyonda kuri buto ya `SUIVRE`.

![follow paynym](assets/notext/6.webp)

Wemeze ukanda `EGO`.

![confirm follow paynym](assets/notext/7.webp)

Porogaramu izoca iguha ubuto bwa `SE CONNECTER`. Si ngombwa gukanda kuri iyi buto kugira ngo ubone inyigisho yacu. Iyi ntambwe ikenewe gusa iyo utegura kwishura uwundi Paynym nk’igice ca BIP47, ivyo bikaba bidafitaniye isano n’inyigisho yacu.

![connect paynym](assets/notext/8.webp)

Igihe Paynym y’uwuronka ikurikiwe na Paynym yawe, subiramwo iyo nzira mu buryo bunyuranye kugira ngo uwuronka na we akurikire. Ushobora rero gukora PayJoin.
