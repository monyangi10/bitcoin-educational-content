## Gutangura na Bitfeed



Bitfeed ni urubuga rwibanda ku ngingo zitatu nyamukuru:





- Blockchain inama**,
- Gushakisha ubucuruzi**,
- Gushushanya mempool**.



### Kugisha inama ubuhinga bwa blockchain



Kuri paji y'intango ya Bitfeed, uzosanga ahanini :





- Umurongo w’ugushaka: Iki gice ni co kibanza co kwinjiramwo ibibazo vy’ubuhinga bwa blockchain. Aha ushobora kurondera igice kinaka ku mubare waco canke hash. Ushobora kandi kurondera amafaranga yihariye n’amaderesi ya Bitcoin kugira ngo usuzume amakuru amwamwe y’amafaranga ari ku rubuga.



![searchBar](assets/fr/01.webp)



Mu mfuruka yo hejuru ibubamfu, urashobora kubona igiciro ca bitcoin kiriho ubu, kigereranywa n’amadolari y’Amerika (USD).



![price](assets/fr/02.webp)



Ku ruhande rw’iburyo hariho urutonde rw’ibintu vyo ku rubuga. Muri iyi menu ushobora guhindura interface y’urubuga nk’uko uvyipfuza, kwongerako canke gukuraho ibintu no guhindura amayunguruzo yo kuraba. Nk’akarorero, raba ubunini bw’igice kimwekimwe cose ku gaciro canke ku buremere mu bice vy’ukuri (vBytes).



![menu](assets/fr/03.webp)



Hagati muri iyo paji hariho igice ca nyuma cacukuwe, kikaba kigaragazwa n’ibikorwa vyose biri muri ico gice. Iki gice kiratanga amakuru ku kidodo c’igihe, umubare wose w’ama bitcoins ari muri iyo block, ubunini bw’i block mu bytes, umubare w’ibikorwa n’igipimo c’igiciro c’ibikorwa ku byte y’ukuri iri muri iyo block.



![block](assets/fr/04.webp)



Ushobora gusubira mu mateka y’umurongo mu kurondera igice kinaka mu kabati k’ugushaka, maze ukagibona ukurikije ivyo ushaka.



Nk'akarorero, turashaka kubona ibikorwa biri mu gice `879488`.



![bloc](assets/fr/05.webp)



Igikoresho ca mbere c’iyi block giserukira igikorwa ca **coinbase** gishoboza umucukuzi w’iyi block aronka impembo ya mining, ishobora gukoreshwa gusa inyuma y’aho ama block 100 acukuwe, igizwe n’amahera y’igurisha arimwo n’infashanyo y’i block. Iryo soko ni ryo rituma ama bitcoins mashasha asohoka kuri system.



![coinbase](assets/fr/06.webp)



https://planb.academy/courses/obtenir-ses-premiers-bitcoins-f3e3843d-1a1d-450c-96d6-d7232158b81f

Ku mburabuzi, amafaranga yo mu bubiko aserukirwa hakurikijwe ibigenderwako bibiri:




- Ingano, ishobora guhinduka hagati y'agaciro n'uburemere (vBytes) ;
- Ibara rishobora guhinduka hagati y’imyaka n’igipimo c’amahera y’ugucuruza kuri byte y’ukuri.



![options](assets/fr/07.webp)



Turashobora rero gushika ku ciyumviro c’uko amafaranga yose ari muri block imwe afise umubare umwe w’ivyemezo biri muri blockchain. Ivyo bice binini cane bigereranya amafaranga arimwo amafaranga menshi cane.



Iyi nsobanuro yemezwa kandi n'uburyo bwo guhitamwo **"Info "**, butumenyesha ubuhinduzi bw'ibara n'ubunini bw'ibikorwa vy'ibarabara.



![infos](assets/fr/08.webp)



Ushobora kandi kubona ibikorwa vy'ibarabara biciye ku bytes z'ukuri n'igipimo c'amahera. Ivyo bishobora gutandukanywa n’ivyo mbere, kuko agaciro ka bitcoin kari mu gucuruza katasobanura ubunini bwayo.



![visualisation](assets/fr/09.webp)



### Kuraba ibikorwa



Ushobora kurondera igikorwa ukoresheje ikimenyetso caco biciye ku murongo w'ugushaka. Ushobora kandi gufyonda ku gice c’umubiri kugira ngo ubone amakuru yerekeye iyo nzira.



Mu gihe cacu, reka dufate igikorwa gifata umwanya munini mu gice `879488`.



![biggest](assets/fr/10.webp)



Uzobona ko iyo nzira ifise `42,989`, ivyo bikaba bigereranya itandukaniro hagati y'ibarabara rya nyuma ririko ricukurwa n'ibarabara twahisemwo. Ivyo vyemezo birafasha gukomeza umutekano w’urubuga rwa Bitcoin, kuko kugira ngo umuntu ahindure iyo nzira mu buryo bubi, abatera bokenera kugira ububasha bungana n’ubwo bwo gusubira kwandika uruhererekane rwose rw’amabuye.



Ubwinshi bw’iyi nzira ni `57.088 vBytes`, ahanini bivuye ku mubare munini w’ama UTXO yakoreshejwe mu kwubaka iyo nzira (842 entries). Igitangaje ni uko igipimo c’amahera akoreshwa kiguma ari gito cane (4 sats/vByte gusa) ugereranyije n’igipimo cose c’amafaranga 5,82 sats/vByte.



Igicuruzwa gifata umwanya munini rero si co gicuruzwa gifise igiciro kinini c’igicuruzwa.



![transaction](assets/fr/11.webp)



Niwakurikiza urugero ruri mu rutonde rwa **Info**, igikorwa gifise igiciro kinini c'igicuruzwa ni ic'umutuku. Iyi ni yo nzira [bfd81fdd02055cé809419b4fae094576bc4384a1d044441d04441d04444c3ba052eya0999a35](https://bito 809419b4fae094576bc4384a1d0444c723b3ba052e99a35) n’igipimo c’igiciro c’ugucuruza `147,49 sats/vBytes`.

### Mempool gushushanya



Mempool ni ahantu h’ukuri aho amafaranga y’ubudandaji ya Bitcoin arindiriye gushirwa mu gice kimwe akoranywa. Bitfeed iremeza ko umuntu ashobora kuganira n’abacukuzi benshi ba Bitcoin, itanga uburyo bwinshi bwo gukurikirana ibikorwa.



![mempool](assets/fr/13.webp)



Muri iki gice, urashobora gukurikirana amafaranga yose afise akamaro n’ataremejwe ku ruhererekane nyamukuru rw’urubuga rwa Bitcoin.



![unconfirmed](assets/fr/14.webp)



Ubu ufise ubuyobozi bwo gukoresha urubuga rwa Bitfeed kugira ngo usesengure amabuye n’ibikorwa kugira ngo ubone mu ciyumviro amakuru ari ku ruhererekane nyamukuru rw’urubuga rwa Bitcoin, mu gihe wungukira ku nzira yoroshe gukoresha. Niba warashimishijwe n’iyi nyigisho, turagusavye gutera intambwe ikurikira: kuvumbura Lightning Network biciye ku mugambi wa Amboss.
