> *Ghatira abatasi ba blockchain gusubira kwiyumvira ivyo biyumvira ko bazi vyose.*

Payjoin ni uburyo bwo gukoresha Bitcoin bugenewe kwongereza ibanga ry’abakoresha mu gukorana n’uwuronka amahera. Hariho uburyo bwinshi bwo gushirwa mu ngiro kugira ngo bishobore gushirwa mu ngiro kwayo no gutuma inzira igenda neza. Ico kizwi cane muri ivyo ni Stowaway, mu ntango cakozwe n’umugwi wa Samurai Wallet, ubu kikaba cashizwe mu gice caco ca fork Ashigaru.



## None Stowaway ikora gute?



Nk’uko twabibonye, ​​Ashigaru ashiramwo igikoresho ca PayJoin citwa `Stowaway`. Ivyo biraboneka muri porogarama ya Ashigaru iri kuri Android. Kugira ngo Payjoin ikorwe, uwuyironka (na we nyene afata uruhara rw’umufatanyabikorwa) ategerezwa kuba ariko arakoresha porogarama ihuye na Stowaway, ni ukuvuga Ashigaru gusa muri iki gihe.



Stowaway ishingiye ku rwego rw'ibikorwa Samurai yita "Cahoots". Cahoot ni ugukorana hagati y’abakoresha benshi, birimwo uguhana amakuru hanze y’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin. Ashigaru ubu atanga ibikoresho bibiri vya Cahoots: Stowaway (Ivyuma vy'amahera) na StonewallX2.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/privacy/on-chain/ashigaru-stonewall-x2-05120280-f6f9-4e14-9fb8-c9e603f73e5b

Ibikorwa vya Cahoots bisaba uguhana ibikorwa vyasinywe igice hagati y’abakoresha. Ivyo bishobora kuba bimara igihe kirekire kandi biraruhije, canecane iyo bikozwe kure. Ariko rero, ivyo biracari bishoboka ko umuntu abikora n’amaboko, nimba abakorana bari ahantu hamwe. Mu majambo nyayo, ivyo birimwo gucapura amakode atanu ya QR akurikirana, akagahanahana hagati y’abo babiri bari muri iyo nama.



Iyo umuntu ari kure, ubwo buryo buraba ubugoye cane. Kugira ngo ivyo bishoboke, Samourai yarateguye ubuhinga bwo guhanahana amakuru bushingiye kuri Tor bwitwa "*Soroban*". Kubera Soroban, amahinduka asabwa kugira ngo umuntu akore Payjoin arakora kandi abera mu nyuma.



Ivyo bimenyetso vy’ibanga bisaba ko haba uguhuza n’ukwemeza hagati y’abaje muri Cahoot. Ni co gituma Soroban yizigira Paynyms z’abakoresha. Niba utaramenya neza ingene Paynyms ikora, raba iyi nyigisho yihariye kugira ngo umenye vyinshi:



https://planb.academy/tutorials/privacy/on-chain/paynym-bip47-a492a70b-50eb-4f95-a766-bae2c5535093

Mu majambo make, Paynym ni ikimenyetso kidasanzwe gifitaniye isano na wallet yawe, kigufasha gukoresha ibikorwa bitandukanye, harimwo n’uguhanahana amakuru mu buryo bupfutse. Rifata uburyo bw’ikimenyetso kijana n’ikigereranyo. Nk’akarorero, ng’iyi iyo nkoresha kuri Testnet:



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



**Mu ncamake:**





- payjoin" = Uburyo bwihariye bwo gukorana n'abandi;





- `Stowaway` = Ishirwa mu ngiro ry'ugufatanya n'amahera riboneka kuri Ashigaru ;





- `Cahoots` = Izina ryahawe na Samourai ku bwoko bwose bw'ibikorwa vyabo vy'ubufatanye, cane cane Payjoin `Stowaway`, ryafashwe uno musi kuri Ashigaru ;





- soroban = Itegeko ry'itumanaho ry'ibanga ryashinzwe kuri Tor ryemerera gukorana n'abandi bakoresha mu gucuruza `Cahoots`;





- paynym" = Ikimenyetso kidasanzwe ca wallet gikoreshwa mu gushinga ubutumwa n'uwundi mukozi kuri "Soroban", kugira ngo akore igikorwa ca "Chahoots".



Kugira ngo ubone mu buryo bwimbitse ingene Payjoins ikora n’akamaro kayo mu buzima bwite bwa onchain, ndagusavye iyi yindi nyigisho:



https://planb.academy/tutorials/privacy/on-chain/payjoin-848b6a23-deb2-4c5f-a27e-93e2f842140f

## None nogira gute umushahara kuri Ashigaru?



Kugira ngo ukore igikorwa ca Stowaway, fyonda ku ishusho ya Paynym yawe iri hejuru mu mfuruka y’ibubamfu y’ibarabara, hanyuma ufungure `Collaborate` menu. Umuntu ariko aragira uruhara mu gucuruza nawe ategerezwa gukora nk’ukwo nyene, kiretse iyo uriko urahana amakode ya QR ubwawe.



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Ufise uburyo bubiri: uhitemwo `Gutangura` nimba ari wewe wohereje amahera, canke `Ugira uruhara` nimba uri uwuronka amahera y'iyi payjoin.



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Nimba ari wewe uyironka, uburyo bwo kubikora burasanzwe cane. Kugira ngo ukoreshe kure biciye ku rubuga rwa Soroban, fyonda ku `Participate`, uhitemwo konti wipfuza gukoresha, hanyuma ukande kuri `LISTEN FOR CAHOOTS REQUESTS` kugira ngo urindirire igisabwa coherejwe n’uwutanga.



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Ku rundi ruhande, kugira ngo ukoreshe umuntu ku giti ciwe biciye ku gucapura kode ya QR, genda kuri paji y’intango y’igitabu cawe ca wallet, ukande ku kimenyetso ca kode ya QR kiri hejuru y’ibarabara, hanyuma ucapure kode ya QR yatanzwe n’uwuriha atanguye gutanga.



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Niba uri mu ruhara rw'ukwishura, ni ukuvuga uwutanguza ibikorwa, genda kuri `Gukorana`, hanyuma uhitemwo `Gutanguza`.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Ku bwoko bw’ibikorwa, kuko twipfuza gukora Payjoin Stowaway, hitamwo iyi nzira.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Ushobora rero guhitamwo hagati yo gukorana kuri internet (*Cahoots* biciye kuri *Soroban*) canke gukorana amaso mu yandi, n’uguhanahana amakode ya QR.



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



### Cahoots kuri interineti



Niba wahisemwo uburyo bwa `Online`, rero uhitemwo uwuzokwakira mu mazina y'amahera uriko urakurikira.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Fyonda kuri `Set up transaction`, hanyuma uhitemwo konti wipfuza gukoresha amahera.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Ku rupapuro rukurikira, wandike amakuru y’ivyo ukoresha: amahera azorungikirwa uwuzoyakira be n’igipimo co kwishura. Ntaco bimaze kwinjiza aderesi y'ukwakira, kuko uwuzokwakira azoyirungika we nyene mu gihe c'uguhanahana PSBT.



Hanyuma ukande kuri `Subiramwo imiterere y'ibikorwa`.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



Suzuma neza amakuru, urabe neza ko uwo mukorana ariko yumviriza ivyo *Cahoots* asaba, hanyuma ukande kuri buto y'icatsi kibisi `BEGIN TRANSACTION` kugira ngo utangure guhana PSBTs biciye kuri Soroban.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Rindira gushika abo bompi bashize umukono kuri iyo nzira, hanyuma uyimenyeshe ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin.



![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)



### Ibiganiro vy'amaso mu yandi



Niba wipfuza gukora iyo nzira y’uguhinduranya ubwawe, hitamwo ubwoko bw’uguhinduranya `STONEWALL X2`, hanyuma uhitemwo uburyo bwo guhinduranya `Umuntu / Ukoresheje igitabu`.



![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)



Fyonda kuri `Set up transaction`, hanyuma uhitemwo konti wipfuza gukoresha amahera.



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Ku rupapuro rukurikira, wandike amakuru y’ivyo ukoresha: amahera azorungikirwa uwuzoyakira be n’igipimo co kwishura. Ntaco bimaze kwinjiza aderesi y'ukwakira, kuko uwuzokwakira azoyirungika we nyene mu gihe c'uguhanahana PSBT.



Hanyuma ukande kuri `Subiramwo imiterere y'ibikorwa`.



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Suzuma ido n’ido, hanyuma ukande kuri buto y’icatsi kibisi `BEGIN TRANSACTION` kugira ngo utangure guhana PSBTs biciye ku gucapura kode ya QR.



![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)



Uguhanahana bikorwa mu guhinduranya scanner n’uwo mufatanije: ukande kuri `SHOW QR CODE` kugira ngo werekane QR code yawe ku mufatanyabikorwa wawe, na we azoyicapura. Azoca afyonda kuri `SHOW QR CODE` kugira ngo yerekane ivyiwe, nawe uzobicapura ukoresheje `LAUNCH QR Scanner`. Subiramwo iyo nzira gushika intambwe zitanu zose zo guhanahana amakuru zirangiye.



![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)



Ivyo vyose bimaze guhindurwa, nusuzume amakuru y’ivyo ugurisha, hanyuma ubirekure mu gukurura umwampi w’icatsi kibisi uri musi y’igicapo.



![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)

Nitwasuzuma iyo nzira, turabona UTXO yanje ya `164.211 sats` ku ruhande rw’injiza, hamwe n’iyi UTXO ya `190.002 sats` y’uwuronka amahera nyayo. Ku ruhande rw’isohoka, ndonka UTXO y’uguhindura `63.995 sats`, mu gihe uwuyironka aronka UTXO y’`290.002 sats`. Tugereranije ivyo twinjije n’ivyo usohotse, turashobora kubona ko uwuronka vy’ukuri yaronse `100.000 sats`, ivyo bikaba bihuye n’amahera narihevye vy’ukuri, kandi ko natakaje `100.000 sats`, ivyo navyo nongeyeko amahera ya mining.



Biragaragara ko nshobora gusobanura iyo mibumbe kuko ni jewe ubwanje nubatse iyo transaction. Ariko ku muntu yihweza hanze, muri rusangi ntibishoboka ko umuntu amenya UTXO ziri mu mugwi uwuhe, haba mu vyo yinjiza canke mu vyo asohoka.
