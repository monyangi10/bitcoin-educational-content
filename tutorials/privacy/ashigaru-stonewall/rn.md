## None igikorwa ca Stonewall ni iki?



Stonewall ni uburyo bwihariye bwo gukoresha Bitcoin bugenewe kwongerera ibanga ry’abakoresha igihe bakoresha amahera mu kwigana coinjoin hagati y’abantu babiri, ataco mu vy’ukuri ari umwe. Nkako, iyo nzira y’ugucuruza si iyo gukorana. Uwukoresha arashobora kuyubaka wenyene, akoresheje gusa ama UTXO afise nk’inyungu. Ushobora rero gukora igikorwa ca Stonewall ku musi mukuru uwo ari wo wose, utabwirizwa gukorana n’uwundi muntu.



Igikoresho ca Stonewall gikora gutya: nk’inyungu mu gikorwa, uwutanga akoresha 2 UTXO ari vyo vyiwe. Ku ruhande rw’ibisohoka, iyo nzira y’ugucuruza itanga ibisohoka 4, 2 muri vyo bikaba bingana neza na neza. Ibindi 2 bizoba ari amafaranga y’amahanga. Mu bisohoka 2 vy’amahera amwe, kimwe gusa ni co kizoja mu vy’ukuri ku wuzohembwa.



Rero hariho gusa uruhara 2 mu gucuruza Stonewall:




- Uwutanga, ni we ariha vy’ukuri ;
- Uwuronka, yoshobora kuba atazi kamere yihariye y’ivyo bicuruzwa kandi akaba yiteze gusa ko uwumurungikiye amwishura.



Reka dufate akarorero kugira ngo dutahure iyo nzira y’ugucuruza. Alice ari ku mutekamutwe kugira ngo agure umukate wiwe, ukaba ugura `4.000 sats`. Ashaka kwishura mu bitcoins, mu gihe agumana uburyo bumwe bumwe bw’ibanga ku bijanye n’ukwishura kwiwe. Ni co gituma afata ingingo yo kwubaka ikibanza co gukoreramwo Stonewall kugira ngo ashobore kwishura.



![image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Dusuzumye iyo nzira, turashobora kubona ko uwo mutetsi vy’ukuri yaronse `4.000 sats` yo kwishura iyo baguette. Alice yakoresheje 2 UTXO nk’ibikoresho: imwe ya `10.000 sats` n’iyindi ya `15.000 sats`. Ku ruhande rw’umusaruro, ryasubiye kuronka 3 UTXO: imwe ya `4.000 sats`, imwe ya `6.000 sats` n’iyindi ya `11.000 sats`. Alice rero ifise amafaranga `- 4.000 sats` kuri iyo nzira, ivyo bikaba bihuye neza n’igiciro c’iyo baguette.



Muri aka karorero, narasuzuguye n’ibigirankana amafaranga y’i mining kugira ngo vyorohe gutahura. Mu vy’ukuri, amahera y’ugucuruza yishurwa yose n’uwutanga.



## Ni itandukaniro irihe riri hagati y'Ibuye n'Ibuye x2?



Ibikorwa vya Stonewall bikora nk'ivyo StonewallX2 ikora, kiretse ko iryo rya nyuma risaba gukorana, bitandukanye n'ibikorwa vya kera vya Stonewall, ni co gituma izina "x2". Ivyo ni kubera ko igikorwa ca Stonewall gikorwa ata n’umwe akeneye gukorana n’abandi: uwugirungitse arashobora kugikora ata wundi muntu amufashije. Mu buryo bunyuranye, ku bijanye n'ugucuruza kwa Stonewall x2, uwundi muntu, azwi kw'izina rya "umufatanyabikorwa", arinjira muri iyo nzira. Aratanga amafaranga yiwe bwite y’ama bitcoins mu gucuruza, iruhande y’ay’uwarungitse, maze agafata amahera yose ku mpera (modulo the mining costs).



Reka dusubire ku karorero kacu na Alice ku mfuruka y’imikate. Iyo ashaka gukora igikorwa ca Stonewall x2, Alice yari gutegerezwa gukorana na Bob (umuntu agira gatatu) igihe co gushinga igikorwa. Umwe wese muri bo yari kuzana UTXO. Bob rero yari gusubizwa intererano yayo yose. Uwo mutetsi yari guhabwa amahera y’umukate wiwe w’umukate nk’uko vyari vyifashe mu gihe c’ugucuruza Stonewall, mu gihe Alice yoba yarasubiye kuronka amahera yasigaye mu ntango, hakuweko igiciro c’umukate w’umukate.



![image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Umuntu wo hanze abona ibintu, iyo nzira yo gucuruza yari kuguma ari uko nyene.



![image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Mu guca irya n’ino, amasezerano ya Stonewall na Stonewall x2 asangiye imiterere imwe. Itandukaniro hagati y’ivyo bibiri riri mu kamere kavyo k’ugukorana canke k’ukudakorana. Ivyo gucuruza Stonewall bitegurwa ku muntu ku giti ciwe, ata n’umwe akeneye gukorana. Ku rundi ruhande, igikorwa ca Stonewall x2 gishingiye ku gukorana hagati y’abantu babiri kugira ngo bashobore kugishiraho.

## None ugucuruza kwa Stonewall bifise akamaro akahe?



Igishushanyo ca Stonewall congeramwo entropie nyinshi cane ku bijanye n’ugucuruza, kigatuma imirongo y’isesengura ry’uruzitiro igaragara neza. Iyo umuntu abonye hanze, mwene ukwo gucuruza gushobora gusobanurwamwo ko ari agace gatoyi k’amahera hagati y’abantu babiri. Ariko mu vy’ukuri, nk’ugucuruza kwa Stonewall x2, ni ukwishura. Ubwo buryo rero buratuma habaho ukudakeka mu gusesangura uruhererekane, canke mbere bukajana ku nzira z’ibinyoma.



Reka dufate akarorero ka Alice ku nzu y’umukate. Ivyo gucuruza ku rubuga rwa blockchain vyosa n’ibi:



![image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Umuntu wo hanze yizigiye ubuhinga bwo gusesangura uruhererekane yoshobora gushika ku ciyumviro kitari co c'uko "**abantu babiri bakoze igice gitoyi, umwe wese afise UTXO imwe nk'inyungu na UTXO zibiri nk'isohoka**".



![image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Iyi nsobanuro ntabwo ari yo, kuko nk’uko mubizi, UTXO imwe yarungitswe ku mutetsi, izo UTXO 2 ziza zavuye kuri Alices, na we aca asubirana ibisohoka 3 vy’igiciro c’amahera.



![image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Naho uwubibona wo hanze yoshobora kumenya paterne y’ivyo bicuruzwa vya Stonewall, ntazogira amakuru yose. Ntazoshobora kumenya uwuhe muri izo UTXO zibiri z’amahera amwe ahuye n’ivyo yishuye. Ikindi, ntazoshobora kumenya nimba izo UTXO zibiri zinjiye zivuye mu bantu babiri batandukanye, canke ko ari iz’umuntu umwe yazihuje. Ico ciyumviro ca nyuma kiva ku kuba amafaranga y’i Stonewall x2, twavuze haruguru, akurikirana neza na neza uburyo bumwe n’ubw’amafaranga y’i Stonewall. Uravye hanze, kandi ata makuru y’inyongera, ntibishoboka kumenya itandukaniro hagati y’ugucuruza kwa Stonewall n’ugucuruza kwa Stonewall x2. Ivya mbere si ibikorwa vy’ubufatanye, mu gihe ivya nyuma ari vyo. Ivyo birongereza mbere n’ugukekeranya ku bijanye n’amahera azokoreshwa.



![image](assets/fr/03.webp)



## None nokora gute igikorwa c’uruhome rw’amabuye kuri Ashigaru?



Mu ntango vyateguwe n’umugwi wa Samourai Wallet, amafaranga y’i Stonewall yatwawe n’ikoreshwa rya Ashigaru, fork ya wallet y’intango yaremwe inyuma y’ugufatwa kw’abahinguzi ba Samourai. Uzokenera gushiramwo Ashigaru maze ureme wallet:



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/mobile/ashigaru-9f903b55-2e55-4b06-9627-80f8e178158f

Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibikorwa vya Stowaway canke Stonewall x2 (*cahoots*), ibikorwa vya Stonewall ntibisaba gukoresha amazina y’amahera. Bishobora gukorwa ataco bimaze, ata kwitegura canke gukorana n’uwundi muntu abikoresha.



Nkako, ntukeneye vy’ukuri inyigisho kugira ngo ukore amafaranga y’i Stonewall, kuko Ashigaru ayizana ubwayo igihe cose ukoresheje, igihe nyene wallet yawe iba irimwo ama UTXO ahagije.



Fyonda kuri buto `+` iri musi iburyo bw'ibarabara, hanyuma uhitemwo `Kohereza`.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Hitamwo konti wipfuza gukoresha amahera.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Hanyuma ushiremwo amakuru y’ivyo ukoresha: aderesi y’uwuzokwakira be n’amahera uzorungika, hanyuma ukande umwampi kugira ngo wemeze.



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



Aha, urashobora, birumvikana, guhindura amahera y’ugucuruza y’imbere y’igihe akurikije uko isoko rimeze. Ariko rero, ikintu gishimishije cane kuri iyi paji ni ubwoko bw’ibikorwa. Uzobona ko Ashigaru yahisemwo `STONEWALL`. Fyonda kuri buto ya `PREVIEW` kugira ngo umenye vyinshi.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Ushobora kubona ko iyo nzira y’ugucuruza ari iyo mu bwoko bwa Stonewall vy’ukuri: igizwe n’ibintu 2 vy’injiza vy’agaciro kamwe, ibintu 2 vy’injiza vy’amahera amwe, hamwe n’ibintu vy’uguhinduranya kandi, mu gihe canje, ikintu c’inyongera co guhazwa n’amahera y’ukwishura.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Niba udashaka gukoresha Stonewall, ariko ushaka kwishura mu buryo busanzwe, fyonda ku kimenyetso c'ikaramu iri hejuru iburyo bw'ibarabara, hanyuma uhitemwo `Simple` aho guhitamwo `STONEWALL`.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



Umaze gusuzuma ido n’ido ryose, kurura umwampi w’icatsi kibisi uri musi y’ibarabara kugira ngo usinye kandi urekure iyo nzira.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)
