## None igikorwa ca Stonewall ni iki?



Stonewall ni uburyo bwihariye bwo gukoresha Bitcoin bugenewe kwongerera ibanga ry’abakoresha igihe bakoresha amahera mu kwigana coinjoin hagati y’abantu babiri, ataco mu vy’ukuri ari umwe. Nkako, iyo nzira y’ugucuruza si iyo gukorana n’abandi. Uwukoresha arashobora kuvyubakira, akoresheje gusa ama UTXO yiwe nk’inyungu. Ushobora rero gukora igikorwa ca Stonewall ku musi mukuru uwo ari wo wose, utabwirizwa gukorana n’uwundi mukozi.



Igikoresho ca Stonewall gikora gutya: nk’inyungu mu gikorwa, uwutanga akoresha 2 UTXO ari vyo vyiwe. Ku ruhande rw’ibisohoka, iyo nzira y’ugucuruza itanga ibisohoka 4, 2 muri vyo bikaba bingana neza na neza. Ibindi 2 bizoba ari amafaranga y’amahanga. Mu bisohoka 2 vy’amahera amwe, kimwe gusa ni co kizoja mu vy’ukuri ku wuzohembwa.



Rero hariho gusa uruhara 2 mu gucuruza Stonewall:




- Uwutanga, ni we ariha vy’ukuri ;
- Uwuronka, yoshobora kuba atazi kamere yihariye y’ivyo bicuruzwa kandi akaba yiteze gusa ko uwumurungikiye ariha.



Reka dufate akarorero kugira ngo dutahure iyo nzira y’ugucuruza. Alice ari ku mutekamutwe kugira ngo agure umukate wiwe, ukaba ugura `4.000 sats`. Ashaka kwishura mu bitcoins, mu gihe agumana uburyo bumwe bumwe bw’ibanga ku bijanye n’ukwishura kwiwe. Ni co gituma afata ingingo yo kwubaka ikibanza co gukoreramwo Stonewall kugira ngo ashobore kwishura.



![image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Dusuzumye iyo nzira, turashobora kubona ko uwo mutetsi vy’ukuri yaronse `4.000 sats` yo kwishura iyo baguette. Alice yakoresheje UTXO 2 nk’ibikoresho: imwe ya `10.000 sats` n’iyindi ya `15.000 sats`. Ku bijanye n'umusaruro, ryasubiye kuronka 3 UTXO: imwe ya `4.000 sats`, imwe ya `6.000 sats` n'iyindi ya `11.000 sats`. Alice rero ifise amafaranga `- 4.000 sats` kuri iyo nzira, ivyo bikaba bihuye neza n’igiciro c’iyo baguette.



Muri aka karorero, narasuzuguye n’ibigirankana amafaranga y’i mining kugira ngo vyorohe gutahura. Mu vy’ukuri, amahera y’ugucuruza yishurwa yose n’uwutanga.



## Ni itandukaniro irihe riri hagati y'Ibuye n'Ibuye x2?



Ibikorwa vya Stonewall bikora nk'ivyo StonewallX2 ikora, kiretse ko iryo rya nyuma risaba gukorana, bitandukanye n'ibikorwa vya kera vya Stonewall, ni co gituma izina "x2". Ivyo ni kubera ko igikorwa ca Stonewall gikorwa ata n’umwe akeneye gukorana n’abandi: uwugirungitse arashobora kugikora ata wundi muntu amufashije. Mu buryo bunyuranye, ku bijanye n'ugucuruza kwa Stonewall x2, uwundi muntu, azwi kw'izina rya "umufatanyabikorwa", arinjira muri iyo nzira. Aratanga amahera yiwe bwite y’ibice vy’umubiri (bitcoins) mu gucuruza, iruhande y’ay’uwarungitse, maze agafata amahera yose ku mpera (akuyeko amahera ya mining).



Reka dusubire ku karorero kacu na Alice ku mfuruka y’imikate. Iyo ashaka gukora igikorwa ca Stonewall x2, Alice yari gukorana na Bob (umuntu agira gatatu) igihe co gushinga igikorwa. Umwe wese muri bo yari kuzana UTXO. Bob yari rero kuronka amahera yose y’intererano yiwe iyo asohotse. Uwo mutetsi yari guhabwa amahera y’umukate wiwe w’umukate nk’uko vyari vyifashe mu gihe c’ugucuruza Stonewall, mu gihe Alice yoba yarasubiye kuronka amahera yari yasigaye mu ntango, hakaba hakuwemwo igiciro c’umukate w’umukate.



![image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Umuntu wo hanze abona ibintu, iyo nzira yo gucuruza yari kuguma ari uko nyene.



![image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Mu guca irya n’ino, amasezerano ya Stonewall na Stonewall x2 asangiye imiterere imwe. Itandukaniro hagati y’ivyo bibiri riri mu kamere kavyo k’ugukorana canke k’ukudakorana. Iryo soko rya Stonewall ritegurwa ku muntu ku giti ciwe, ata n’umwe akeneye gukorana. Ku rundi ruhande, igikorwa ca Stonewall x2 gishingiye ku gukorana hagati y’abantu babiri kugira ngo bashobore kugishiraho.

## None ugucuruza kwa Stonewall bifise akamaro akahe?



Igishushanyo ca Stonewall congeramwo entropie nyinshi cane ku bijanye n’ugucuruza, kigatuma imirongo y’isesengura ry’uruzitiro igaragara neza. Iyo umuntu abonye hanze, mwene ukwo gucuruza gushobora gusobanurwamwo ko ari agace gatoyi k’amahera hagati y’abantu babiri. Ariko mu vy’ukuri, nk’ugucuruza kwa Stonewall x2, ni ukwishura. Ubwo buryo rero buratuma habaho ukudakeka mu gusesangura uruhererekane, canke mbere bukajana ku nzira z’ibinyoma.



Reka dufate akarorero ka Alice ku nzu y’umukate. Ivyo gucuruza ku rubuga rwa blockchain vyosa n’ibi:



![image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Umuntu wo hanze yizigiye ubuhinga bwo gusesangura uruhererekane yoshobora gushika ku ciyumviro kitari co c'uko "*abantu babiri bakoze igice gitoyi c'amahera, umwe wese afise UTXO imwe nk'inyungu na UTXO zibiri umwe wese nk'isohoka*".



![image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Iyi nsobanuro ntabwo ari yo, kuko nk’uko mubizi, UTXO imwe yarungitswe ku mutetsi, izo UTXO 2 ziza zavuye kuri Alices, na we aca asubirana ibisohoka 3 vy’uguhinduranya.



![image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Naho uwubibona wo hanze yoshobora kumenya paterne y’ivyo bicuruzwa vya Stonewall, ntazogira amakuru yose. Ntazoshobora kumenya uwuhe muri izo UTXO zibiri z’amahera amwe ahuye n’ivyo yishuye. Ikindi, ntazoshobora kumenya nimba izo nkuru zibiri za UTXO zikomoka ku bantu babiri batandukanye, canke nimba ari iz’umuntu umwe yazihuje. Ico ciyumviro ca nyuma kiva ku kuba amafaranga y’i Stonewall x2, twavuze haruguru, akurikirana neza na neza uburyo bumwe n’ubw’amafaranga y’i Stonewall. Uravye hanze kandi ata makuru y’inyongera, ntibishoboka kumenya itandukaniro hagati y’ugucuruza kwa Stonewall n’ugucuruza kwa Stonewall x2. Ivya mbere si ibikorwa vy’ubufatanye, mu gihe ivya nyuma ari vyo. Ivyo birongereza mbere n’ugukekeranya ku bijanye n’amahera azokoreshwa.



![image](assets/fr/03.webp)



## None nokora gute igikorwa ca Stonewall kuri Sparrow?



Mu ntango vyateguwe n'umugwi wa Samurai Wallet, amafaranga y'i Stonewall yatwawe n'ikoreshwa rya Ashigaru, fork ivuye mu gitabu c'intango cakozwe inyuma y'ugufatwa kw'abateguzi ba Samurai, no kuri Sparrow Wallet.



Uzokenera gushiramwo Sparrow maze ureme :



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/desktop/sparrow-c674e2ac-d46f-4c82-92a7-7d1b0e262f5d

Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibikorwa vya Stowaway canke Stonewall x2 (*cahoots*), ibikorwa vya Stonewall ntibisaba gukoresha amazina y’amahera. Bishobora gukorwa ataco bimaze, ata kwitegura kudasanzwe canke gukorana n’uwundi muntu abikoresha.



Kugira ngo ukore igikorwa ca Stonewall kuri Sparrow, uburyo bwo kubikora burasanzwe cane: tangura ukoresheje igikorwa nk'uko bisanzwe, biciye ku rutonde rwa `Send`, canke biciye ku rutonde rwa `UTXOs` nimba wipfuza gukora *Coin Control*.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Hanyuma winjize amakuru y’ivyo ugurisha: aderesi y’uwuzoronka, ikimenyetso, amahera azorungikwa be n’amahera canke igipimo c’amahera azokwishurwa, bivanye n’ingene isoko rimeze.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Imbere yo kwemeza, aha niho ushobora guhitamwo umubumbe wa Stonewall. Hasi y'ikigaragaza, subiriza `Ubushobozi` na `Ubuzima bwite`. Iyo iyo nzira itabonetse, ivyo bisigura ko igitabu cawe kidafise umubare uhagije w’ama UTXO yo kwubaka ubwo bwoko bw’ugucuruza.



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



Uhejeje guhitamwo `Ubuzima bwite`, uzobona ko imiterere y'ugucuruza ihinduwe burundu: ihinduka ugucuruza kwa Stonewall, ifungura UTXO zawe nyinshi nk'inyungu kandi igatanga ibisohoka bibiri vy'amahera asa, kimwe muri vyo gihuye n'ukwishura nyakuri kw'ihinduka rya G`0,7-20. ibisohoka.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Niba vyose bimeze neza, kanda kuri `Rema Ibikorwa`.



Ushobora rero kugenzura amakuru y'ibikorwa vyawe ubwa nyuma, hanyuma ukande kuri `Gusozera ibikorwa kugira ngo usinye`.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Hanyuma ushire umukono ku bijanye n’ugucuruza hakurikijwe uburyo bwihariye ku bijanye n’igitabu cawe, hanyuma ukande kuri `Broadcast Transaction` kugira ngo uyitangaze ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, urindiriye kwemezwa.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)
