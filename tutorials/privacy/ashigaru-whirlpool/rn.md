## Uko amahuriro ya Whirlpool akora



Muri iyi nyigisho, sinzosubira ku ciyumviro ca coinjoin, akamaro kayo canke imikorere y’ivyiyumviro ya Whirlpool, kuko izo nsiguro ziramaze gusigurwa mu buryo burambuye mu gice ca 5 c’amahugurwa ya BTC 204 ku vyerekeye Plan ₿ Academy. Niba utaramenya neza uko Whirlpool ikora canke ingingo ngenderwako y'ivyo gukorana, ndagusavye cane ko wobanza kuraba iki gice ca 5 imbere yo kubandanya :



https://planb.academy/courses/65c138b0-4161-4958-bbe3-c12916bc959c

Ariko rero, ng’ibi ibintu bikeyi vyihuta be n’imibare yoshobora kugufasha.



Whirlpool ihuye n'ibitabo bikoresha amakonti 4 atandukanye kugira ngo bishobore gushitsa ivya nkenerwa mu nzira ya coinjoin:




- Konti ya **Ibiziga**, igaragazwa n'urutonde `0'` ;
- Konti ya **Banki mbi** (canke *isoko rya doxxic*), igaragazwa n’urutonde `2.147.483.644'` ;
- Konti ya **Imbere**, igaragazwa n'urutonde `2 147 483 645'` ;
- Konti ya **Postmix**, igaragazwa n'urutonde `2 147 483 646'`.



Ku musi wa Ashigaru, mu kwezi kwa 11 2025, amashengero abiri y’ibidengeri araboneka (uru rutonde ruzoshobora guhinduka mu mezi ari imbere: rero wibuke gusuzuma agaciro uko usoma):




- 0,25 BTC`, n’amahera yo kwinjira `0,0125 BTC`;
- 0,025 BTC, n’amahera yo kwinjira ni 0,00125 BTC.



Buri nzira y’uguvanga ishobora gushiramwo hagati ya UTXO 5 na 10 mu kwinjiza no gusohora.

## Kwakira ama bitcoins



Uhejeje gukora igitabo cawe, uzotangura na konti imwe, igaragazwa n'urutonde `0'`. Iyi ni konti ya `Ibiziga`. Ni kuri iyi konti uzorungika ama bitcoins agenewe ama coinjoins. Ushobora kubironka biciye ku rubuga rwa Ashigaru (raba igice ca 5 c’inyigisho yihariye), canke biciye kuri Terminal ya Ashigaru (na yo nyene iradondaguwe mu gice ca 5 c’inyigisho yihariye).



Igihe konti yawe yo gushiramwo amahera izoba irimwo n’imiburiburi amahera akenewe kugira ngo winjire mu gice gitoyi kuruta ibindi vyose (ushizemwo amafaranga y’ibikorwa n’amahera makeyi asabwa kugira ngo ushobore kwishura amafaranga y’i mining) uzoba witeguriye gutangura amafaranga yawe ya mbere.

## Gukora Tx0.



Amahera amaze gushika kuri konti yawe y’ibiziga kandi amafaranga yemejwe, urashobora gutangura igikorwa co gukora coinjoin. Kugira ngo ubikore, ku kibanza ca Ashigaru, fungura `Wallets`, hanyuma uhitemwo wallet yawe. Iyo wallet yawe ipfungiwe, iyo porogarama izogusaba ijambobanga ryawe na passphrase.



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Hanyuma uhitemwo konti ya `Ibiziga`.



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Genda ku rutonde rwa `UTXOs`.



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Aha uzobona urutonde rw'ama UTXO yose ari kuri konti yawe y'amahera. Hitamwo izo wipfuza kohereza mu nzinguzingu za coinjoin.



Kugira ngo habeho ibanga ryinshi kandi kugira ngo umuntu yirinde *Ivyiyumviro rusangi vy’Ivyiyumviro (CIOH)*, birakenewe ko umuntu akoresha UTXO imwe gusa ku vyiyumviro vyose biri muri Whirlpool (insobanuro y’ido n’ido y’iryo hame iraboneka muri BTC 204).



Kanda urufunguzo `ENTER` kuri klavye yawe kugira uhitemwo UTXO: akamenyetso `(*)` kazoboneka iruhande rwayo kugira ngo werekane ko yatowe.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya `Vanga ivyo wahisemwo`.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Niba ufise UTXO nini bihagije kugira ngo ushobore kuja muri kimwe muri ivyo bidengeri bibiri biriho, urashobora guhitamwo ikidengeri uzojako ukoresheje imyampi. Kuri iyi paji, uzobona ibisobanuro vya Tx0 yawe :




- umubare w’ama UTXO azokwinjira mu kidengeri;
- amafaranga atandukanye akoreshwa (amafaranga y’ibikorwa n’amafaranga ya mining) ;
- umubare w’ihinduka ry’ *doxxic*.



Suzuma neza amakuru, hanyuma ukande kuri `Broadcast` kugira ngo utangaze Tx0.



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



Ashigaru azoca yerekana TXID ya Tx0 yawe, yemeze ko iyo transaction yatangajwe ku rubuga.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



## Gukora amafaranga



Tx0 imaze gutangazwa, subira kuri paji y’intango ya konti yawe y’amahera, hanyuma ukande kuri `Accounts` uhitemwo konti ya `Premix`.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Mu `UTXOs`, uzobona ibice bitandukanye bingana, biteguye kwinjira mu nzira z'ugukorana. Tx0 ikimara kwemezwa, Ashigaru Terminal izoca itanguza ubwo nyene urugendo rwabo rwa mbere rwo kuvanga.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



Igihe Tx0 imaze kwemezwa, igikorwa ca mbere co coinjoin kizoremwa kandi kimenyekane ubwaco na Ashigaru Terminal. Mu kuja kuri `Konti > Postmix > UTXOs`, urashobora kubona UTXOs zawe zingana, urindiriye kwemezwa kw'ingendo zazo za mbere.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Ubu ushobora gusiga Ashigaru Terminal ikora inyuma: izobandanya ivanga no gusubiramwo indirimbo zawe ubwayo.



## Guheza amafaranga



Ubu ushobora kureka ibiceri vyawe bikavyisubirako. Icogereranyo ca Whirlpool bisigura ko ata bindi bihembo vy’ugusubiramwo: nta mahera y’ibikorwa, nta mahera ya mining. Rero kureka ibiceri vyawe bikagira uruhara mu nzinguzingu nyinshi zo kuvanga bishobora gusa kugirira akamaro ibanga ryawe.



Kugira ngo utahure neza ubu buryo n’ingene bibereye kurindira inzinguzingu zingahe, ndagusavye gusoma iyi ngingo:



https://planb.academy/tutorials/privacy/on-chain/remix-whirlpool-2b887bd9-8a6a-4dca-8aa9-a1c33682b0aa

Kugira ngo ubone umubare w'ama remix yakozwe na buri kimwe mu bice vyawe, fungura `UTXOs` muri konti ya `Postmix`.



![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)



Kugira ngo ukoreshe amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri bivanze, genda kuri porogarama ya Ashigaru, ikoresha wallet imwe na porogarama yawe ya Ashigaru Terminal. wallet yerekanwa ku gufungura ihuye na konti yawe ya `Deposit`. Kugira ngo ushikire konti ya `Postmix`, irimwo UTXO zawe zivanzwe, fyonda ku kimenyetso ca Whirlpool kiri hejuru iburyo.



![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)



Muri iyi konti, uzobona amafaranga yawe yose ubu ariko aravangwa. Kugira ngo ubikoreshe, kanda ikimenyetso `+` kiri hasi iburyo bw'ibarabara, hanyuma uhitemwo `Kohereza`.



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Uzuza amakuru y’ivyo ukoresha: aderesi y’uwuzokwakira, amahera uzorungika, kandi niwabishaka, uhitemwo uburyo bumwe bumwe bwo gukoresha kugira ngo urushirizeho kwongereza ibanga ryawe (raba inyigisho zijanye n’ivyo).



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Suzuma neza amakuru y’ivyo ugurisha, hanyuma ukure umwampi uri musi y’ibarabara kugira ngo wemeze ko ivyo warungitse.

## Gukoresha Doxxic guhindura



Ibuka: Icogereranyo ca Whirlpool gishingiye ku kuringaniza ibice vyawe kuri Tx0, imbere y’uko vyinjira mu bidengeri. Ubu buryo ni bwo buca mu gukurikirana ama UTXO. Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, iyi ni yo modele ya coinjoin ikora neza cane, ariko irafise ingorane imwe: itanga *ihinduka* ridaca mu nzira ya coinjoin.



Iryo hinduka rihuye na UTXO yaremewe Tx0 yose. Iri ukwayo muri konti yihariye yitwa `Doxxic Change` canke `Bad Bank`, bivanye n’ivyo ukoresha, kugira ngo wirinde kuyikoresha n’izindi UTXO zawe. Ivyo birahambaye cane, kuko izo UTXO ntizavanzwe: amahuzu yazo yo gukurikirana aguma ari meza, kandi arashobora guhungabanya ibanga ryawe mu gushinga isano hagati yawe n’igikorwa cawe co coinjoin. Rero uzifate neza kandi **ntiwigere uzikoresha n’izindi UTXO**, zaba zivanzwe canke zitavanzwe. Gufatanya UTXO y’ubumara na UTXO ivanze bizokuraho inyungu zose z’ubuzima bwite waronse mu gukorana n’abandi.



Kugeza ubu, Ashigaru ntatanga uburenganzira bwo gushika kuri iyi konti ya `Doxxic Change` (nibura, ntayibonye). Ivyo bishobora kwongerwako mu gihe kizoza. Hagati aho, uburyo bwonyene bwo kuronka ayo mahera ni ukuzana seed yawe muri Sparrow Wallet. Ivyo bizoca bimenya ko iyo ari wallet ikoreshwa na Whirlpool maze biguhe uburenganzira bwo gukoresha konti zose zine, harimwo na konti ya `Doxxic Change`. Ushobora rero gukoresha izo UTXO nk'ama bitcoins asanzwe ava kuri Sparrow.



Aha niho hari ingamba nyinshi zishoboka zo gucunga amafaranga yawe y’amahanga UTXOs kuva ku coinjoins ataco uhinduye ku buzima bwite bwawe:





- Kubivanga mu bidengeri bitobito:** Nimba UTXO yawe y’ubumara ari nini cane ku buryo ishobora kwifatanya n’ibidengeri bitobito, muri rusangi iyo ni yo nzira nziza. Ariko rero, urabe maso, ntukigere ufatanya UTXO nyinshi zitera ubumara kugira ngo ivyo ubishikeko, kuko ivyo bizotuma habaho ihuriro hagati y'ibintu vyawe bitandukanye muri Whirlpool.





- Bishireko ikimenyetso c’uko bidashobora gukoreshwa:** Ubundi buryo bw’ubwenge ni ukubigumya gusa uko biri mu nkuru yavyo itandukanye maze ukabisiga ataco bikozeko. Ivyo bizotuma udashobora kubikoresha mu mpanuka. Iyo agaciro ka bitcoin kagwiriye, ama pool mashasha ahuye n’ubunini bwayo yoshobora gufungurwa.





- Gutanga intererano:** Ushobora guhitamwo guhindura izo UTXO zitera ubumara zibe intererano ku bategura Bitcoin, imigambi y’inkomoko yuguruye canke amashirahamwe yemera BTC. Ivyo bituma ushobora kubijugunya mu buryo ngirakamaro mu gihe ushigikira ibidukikije.





- Gura amakarita y’ingabirano canke amakarita ya Visa yishuwe mbere:** Ivyuma nka [Bitrefill](https://www.bitrefill.com/) bigufasha guhindura amafaranga yawe y’ibiceri (bitcoins) ukayagira amakarita y’ingabirano canke amakarita ya Visa ashobora gusubirwamwo ashobora gukoreshwa mu maduka. Ivyo bishobora kuba uburyo bworoshe kandi buteye igomwe bwo gukoresha ama UTXO yawe y’ubumara.





- Bihindure Monero:** Samourai Wallet yahora itanga ubuhinga bwo guhindura atome BTC/XMR ubu butagikora. Niba hariho service isa n'iyo (nta n'imwe nzi jewe ubwanje), ni umuti mwiza wo gutandukanya izo UTXO, kuzihindura Monero, hanyuma ukazisubiza muri Bitcoin. Ariko rero, ubwo buryo bwari buzimvye kandi bwavana n’amahera yariho.





- Kubirungika muri Lightning Network:** Kwimurira izo UTXO muri Lightning Network kugira ngo zironke inyungu mu kugabanya amafaranga y’ugucuruza ni uburyo bushobora gushimisha. Ariko rero, ubu buryo burashobora guhishura amakuru amwamwe bivanye n’ingene ukoresha Lightning, kandi rero ukwiye kubukoresha witonze.



## Nomenya gute ibijanye n’uburyo bw’ingendo zacu za coinjoin?



Kugira ngo coinjoin ibe ngirakamaro vy’ukuri, itegerezwa kwerekana urugero rwo hejuru rw’uguhuza hagati y’amahera yinjizwa n’ayo asohoka. Ukwo guhuza kwongerera umubare w’insobanuro zishoboka ku muntu yihweza hanze, ivyo na vyo bikaba bituma habaho ukudakeka ku bijanye n’ugucuruza. Kugira ngo dupime ukwo kudakeka, dukoresha iciyumviro c’entropie gikoreshwa ku bijanye n’ugucuruza. Icogereranyo ca Whirlpool cemewe ko ari kimwe mu bikora neza cane muri ivyo, kuko gitanga ubumwe bwiza cane hagati y’abaje mu nama. Kugira ngo ubone neza iyo ngingo ngenderwako, ndagusavye ko woraba igice ca nyuma c’Igice ca 5 c’amahugurwa ya BTC 204.



https://planb.academy/courses/65c138b0-4161-4958-bbe3-c12916bc959c

Ivyo inzinguzingu nyinshi z’amahera zikora bipimwa n’ubunini bw’ibice vy’amahera bihishijwemwo. Ivyo bice bisigura ivyo bita *anonsets*. Hari ubwoko bubiri: ubwa mbere bupima ibanga mu gihe c’isesengura ry’inyuma (kuva muri iki gihe gushika muri kahise), ubwa kabiri bupima ukurwanya isesengura ry’imbere (kuva muri kahise gushika muri kahise). Kugira ngo ubone insobanuro yuzuye y’ivyo bimenyetso bibiri, usabwe gusoma inyigisho ikurikira (canke, kandi, inyigisho y’amahugurwa ya BTC 204):



https://planb.academy/tutorials/privacy/on-chain/remix-whirlpool-2b887bd9-8a6a-4dca-8aa9-a1c33682b0aa

## Ni gute wocungera postmix?



Amaze gukora inzinguzingu nyinshi za coinjoin, ubuhinga bwiza ni ugutuma UTXO zawe ziguma muri konti ya `Postmix`, ukazireka zigakora remix ubuziraherezo gushika ukeneye kuzikoresha vy’ukuri.



Abakoresha bamwebamwe barakunda kwimurira amafaranga yabo y’ibice bivanze kuri wallet icungiwe n’ibikoresho vya wallet. Iryo hitamwo rirashoboka, ariko risaba uburindutsi bumwe bumwe kugira ngo ibanga ryaronswe n’ama coinjoins ntiribe ryinshi.



Gufatanya UTXOs ni ryo kosa rikunda gushika. Ni vyiza ko utigera ufatanya UTXO zivanzwe n’izitavanze mu gucuruza kumwe, ahandi ho woshobora gutuma *Common Input Ownership Heuristic (CIOH)* itera. Ivyo bisigura gucunga neza ama UTXO yawe, cane cane biciye mu gushirako amazina asobanutse kandi atagiramwo amahinyu. Muri rusangi, gufatanya ama UTXO ni umugenzo mubi akenshi utuma umuntu atakaza ibanga iyo atacungiwe neza.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/privacy/on-chain/utxo-labelling-d997f80f-8a96-45b5-8a4e-a3e1b7788c52

Ushobora kandi kwiyubara ku bijanye no gushiramwo ama UTXO avunganye. Ivyiyumviro bifise aho bigarukira birashobora kwiyumvirirwa nimba UTXOs zifise amakuru akomeye, ariko nta kabuza zigabanya urugero rw’ibanga ryawe. Irinde gukoranya ibintu vyinshi canke vyihuta, bikorwa imbere y’umubare uhagije w’ibintu bisubiramwo, kuko bishobora gutuma habaho amasano hagati y’ibintu vyawe vy’imbere n’inyuma y’ivyo ukora. Mu gihe woba ufise amakenga, ni vyiza ko udafatanya ama UTXO yawe y’inyuma y’ivyo ukora maze ukayarungika imwe imwe ku bikoresho vyawe vya wallet, ugatanga aderesi nshasha y’ukwakira igihe cose. Ntimwibagire kwandika UTXO yose yimuriwe.



Turahanura cane ko utokwimurira ama UTXO yawe y’inyuma mu bipimo ukoresheje inyandiko ntoyi. Nk'akarorero, iyo mwagize uruhara muri Whirlpool muvuye mu gitabu ca multi-sig muri `P2WSH`, hazoba bake muri mwebwe basangira ubwo bwoko bw'inyandiko. Mu kohereza ama UTXO yawe ya postmix kuri ubwo bwoko nyene bw’inyandiko, uragabanya cane ukutamenyekana kwawe. Uretse ubwoko bw’inyandiko, ibindi bimenyetso vy’intoke vyihariye vya wallet birashobora gutuma ibanga ryawe rihungabana, rero ikintu ciza wokora ni ukubikoresha ukoresheje porogarama ya Ashigaru.



Ubwa nyuma, nk’uko bigenda ku bikorwa vyose vya Bitcoin, ntukigere usubira gukoresha aderesi y’ukwakira. Ivyo kwishura vyose bitegerezwa kwoherezwa kuri aderesi nshasha, yihariye, ata co ivuga.



Uburyo bworoshe kandi butekanye ni ukureka UTXO zawe zivanzwe ziruhukira muri konti yabo ya `Postmix`, zigasubiramwo mu buryo busanzwe, kandi zikazikoresha gusa iyo zikenewe zivuye kuri Ashigaru.



Ivyo bikoresho vya Ashigaru na Sparrow birimwo ubundi buryo bwo kwirinda amakosa asanzwe ajanye n’ugusesangura blockchain, bikagufasha kuzigama ibanga ry’amahera ukoresha.
