Gufatanya ububasha bwa Lightning Network n’ubumenyi bwiza bwo gukoresha kugira ngo wemere Bitcoin mu bucuruzi bwawe: iyo ni yo ntumbero **Wallet ya Satoshi** yishizeho mu gushiramwo ikibanza co kugurisha mu bikorwa vyayo. Muri iyi nyigisho, turaza kumenya ingene woshobora gushinga ikibanza co kugurisha cemera Bitcoin mu bucuruzi bwawe.


## Ibitabo



Mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin, Wallet ya Satoshi ni Wallet iremezwa kugira ngo utangure kwishura umuravyo. Naho utari mu bubasha bwose bw’amahera yawe (ukwizigama), Wallet ya Satoshi itanga ubumenyi bwiza bwo gutangura na Bitcoin n’amahera make ukoresheje Lightning Network Layer. Nimba ari vyo vya mbere ubona kuri iyi Wallet, turagusavye inyigisho yacu yo Gutangura.



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/wallet-of-satoshi-39149d86-e42b-4e8f-ae9f-7e061e7784f7

Kugira ngo Bitcoin ishobore kwemerwa mu mibano yose kw’isi, Wallet ya Satoshi na yo nyene yarashizeho Ikibanza co Gugurishamwo kugira ngo iremeshe abayikoresha kuronka uburyo bugaragara bwo gukoresha Bitcoin mu mibano yabo: kugura no kugurisha ibintu n’ibikorwa bakoresheje Bitcoin.



## Ikibanza co kugurisha



Ivyerekeye Point-of-Sale bitangwa na Wallet ya Satoshi biratandukanye n’ibindi bikoresho ahanini kubera ko vyoroshe kandi bigenda neza. Uretse ko ata kindi usaba atari porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa iri kuri Google Play Store no kuri iOS, urashobora gukoresha Wallet kugira ngo:





- Gukora ibikorwa vy’umuntu ku giti ciwe.
- Gucungera urutonde rwawe.
- Invoice abakiriya bawe.
- Bika amakuru atandukanye y’itunga ryawe n’amahera wishyura n’ubucuruzi bwawe.



⚠️ **IGIHARURWA**: Ni ngombwa ko ushobora gukura iyo porogarama ku rubuga rwemewe canke uciye ku rubuga rwemewe kugira ngo ushobore kumenya neza ubutungane bw’amakuru yawe n’umutekano w’amahera yawe.



Muri iyo porogarama, genda muri menyu, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya **Point de Vente** kugira ngo uje aho ugurisha.



❗Kugira ngo ugere aho ugurisha bisaba ko winjiye ukoresheje email yawe.



![pos](assets/fr/01.webp)



Mu gice ca **Keyboard**, urashobora guca ugura Invoice mu kwinjiza umubare w'igicuruzwa no kwongerako akajambo kugira ngo ushobore gukurikirana neza.



![pos_keyboard](assets/fr/02.webp)



Kugira ngo ushobore gutunganya neza aho ugurisha, urashobora gutunganya ibintu ufise mw’iduka ryawe mu gutanga izina n’ibiciro ubigurishako.



![items](assets/fr/03.webp)



Ushobora guhitamwo ibintu vyinshi kugira ngo generate a Lightning Invoice kugira ngo umukiriya wawe ashobore kwishura. Mu gufatanya amahitamwo ya Keyboard na Item, urashobora gukora Invoice idafise umurongo ku bicuruzwa biri mu rutonde rwawe no hanze yarwo.



![charge](assets/fr/04.webp)



Fyonda kuri buto ya **Charge** kugira ngo generate a Lightning Invoice ifatanye n'ivyo umukiriya wawe aguze. Umukiriya wawe azoshobora kwishura amafaranga yiwe ubwo nyene akoresheje ikarita yiwe ya Lightning Wallet canke akoresheje ikarita yiwe Bolt akoresheje NFC.



![charge](assets/fr/05.webp)



Mu **History** menu, uzosanga urutonde rw'amahera y'amahera kuri buri Invoice washizeho mu kibanza cawe co kugurisha. Iyi nkuru itandukanye n’inkuru y’ivya kera ya Wallet ya konti yawe ya Satoshi, ivuga amahera yose warishe n’ayo waronse hanze y’aho ugurisha.



![history](assets/fr/06.webp)



## Kunoza imiterere y'aho ugurisha



Kugira ubumenyi butagira umurongo n’aho ugurisha bisigura kandi kuronka imiterere ibereye ibidukikije vyawe.



Mu Wallet ya Satoshi portfolio menu, hitamwo amafaranga yawe yo mu karere mu rutonde rw’amafaranga ashigikiwe, kugira ngo utunganye ibicuruzwa vyawe mu bice vy’amafaranga umenyereye.




![currencies](assets/fr/07.webp)



Kugira ngo ukomeze gukurikirana neza amahera wishyura, mu mirongo ya Wallet canke Satoshi, urashobora gukura amateka yawe muri dosiye ya CSV maze ugakora isesengura ryose wipfuza ushingiye kuri ayo makuru.



![history](assets/fr/08.webp)



E-mail izorungikwa kuri e-mail ya konti yawe Address irimwo uruja n’uruza rw’igihe gito ushobora gukuramwo dosiye ya CSV.



![download](assets/fr/09.webp)



## Ibindi n'aho ugurisha



Wallet ya Satoshi ntitanga gusa inyishu za Bitcoin ku bucuruzi buto buto. Kuva kuri Wallet ya Satoshi POS application, urashobora gutuma abakozi bawe bashobora gutanga amahera y’amahera ya Bitcoin ku bibanza vyawe bitandukanye vy’ugurisha.



Gukuraho iyo porogarama, iri kuri iOS no kuri Google Play Store, ku bikoresho vyawe bitandukanye canke ku bikoresho vy’abakozi bawe.



![download-pos](assets/fr/10.webp)



Huza Lightning Address yawe na Wallet yawe ya Satoshi Wallet maze ushireho igice c’amahera c’akarere k’aho ugurisha.



![connect](assets/fr/11.webp)



Abakozi bawe rero barashobora kwakira amahera ya Bitcoin mw’iduka ryawe. Igiharuro ca Wallet ca Satoshi Wallet kiguma ari co ushobora gukoreshamwo amafaranga y’ibiceri (bitcoins) no gusubira gutunganya ivy’iduka ryawe.



![items](assets/fr/12.webp)



Buri muntu wese akoresha porogarama ya Wallet of Satoshi POS afise amateka y’ukwishura yihariye y’amafagitire ya Lightning yashizeho muri iyo porogarama.



![pos-history](assets/fr/13.webp)
