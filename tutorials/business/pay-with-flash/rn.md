Ukwemera Bitcoin kwabaye kwinshi kw'isi yose, bivuye ku bushobozi butangwa na Lightning Network. Ubu hariho inyishu nyinshi kugira ngo ushobore kuba mu bagize uwo muvuduko wo kwemera, no gushiramwo Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura kugira ngo uhe igikorwa cawe, ubucuruzi bwawe, uburyo bwo gushikira kw’isi yose kugira ngo ubone kwishurwa ubwo nyene uvuye aho hose kw’isi. Muri ubwo buryo, Flash, umuti wo kwizigama, uragaragara kubera uko ikora n’ubumenyi bw’abayikoresha.



## Gutangura na Flash



Flash ni urubuga rugufasha, nk’umucuruzi canke rwiyemezamirimo, gutora amahera ashingiye ku nzira ya Lightning overlay y’umurongo wa Bitcoin. Iryo koraniro rirasanzwe, kandi kuva mu ntango riragutwara mu ntambwe zijanye n’ugushinga ishirahamwe ryawe.



### Gutegura ishirahamwe ryawe



Nukoreshe uburyo bworoshe bwa Flash kugira ngo ushireho ishirahamwe ryawe mu ntambwe zitatu gusa.




- Uzuza ido n'ido ry'ishirahamwe ryawe:


Iryo koraniro rya Flash ryemera ubucuruzi buva ku migabane yose, harimwo Afirika (Nigeriya, Ghana), Uburayi, Amerika na Aziya (Vietnam, Ubushinwa).



![organizations](assets/fr/01.webp)





- Nta kimenyetso na kimwe gikenewe ku rubuga. Niwinjize gusa amazina yawe ya mbere n’ay’umuryango wawe, hanyuma ushiremwo igihugu wipfuza gukoreramwo, hanyuma ubandanye.



![infos](assets/fr/02.webp)

Umaze gufatanya Bitcoin Wallet yawe, nuvuge ibintu bigushimisha kandi wipfuza gukoresha kuri iyo nzira. Muri ivyo harimwo .




- Uburongozi bw’amafaranga: ni vyiza cane mu gucunga amafaranga y’abagufasha mu kuronka amahera atari make mu kiringo kinaka.
- Amahuza y’ukwishura aja kuri paji za generate aho abakiriya bawe bashobora kwishura amafagitire yawe.
- Intererano zo gushigikira imigambi yanyu.
- e-commerce, ishobora kugufasha gutunganya no kugurisha ibintu vy’umubiri n’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none kuri Internet.

### Rema ahantu ho kugurisha



Woba usanzwe ufise iduka ry’umubiri mu karere iwanyu kandi wipfuza kwemera amahera ya Bitcoin? Ihitamwo rya Flash **Ikibanza co kugurisha** ni umuti mwiza kuri wewe.



Iyo uremye ikibanza cawe co kugurisha, uzohabwa urubuga rwo kuri interineti ari rwo Interface uzokoresha mu gukora ibikorwa mu kibanza co kugurisha.



![pos-link](assets/fr/08.webp)




Ku rupapuro rwawe rw'ibarabara, fyonda ku **Tangura** mu gice ca **Ikibanza co kugurisha**. Uzosubirwamwo kuri Interface kugira ngo utunganye aho ugurisha. Uzuza amakuru ahuye n’aho ugurisha maze uyahuze n’igitabu wari warahuje mbere, hanyuma ushiremwo amakuru yawe.



![pdv](assets/fr/09.webp)




Imbere y’uko uja kuri iyi Interface kugira ngo utangure gutoragura amahera muri Bitcoin, turagusavye gukora ibintu biboneka mu iduka ryawe kugira ngo ushobore gukurikirana neza, kugira ngo ugire urutonde kuri Flash.



Ku rubuga, fyonda ku **Ibindi** icon, hanyuma ukande ku **Igicuruzwa gishasha**.



![products](assets/fr/10.webp)



Uzuza amakuru y’igicuruzwa cawe maze uyahuze n’aho ugurisha.



![link_product](assets/fr/11.webp)



Ushobora gushika ku rutonde rwawe rw'aho ugurisha ukanda kw'izina ry'aho ugurisha mu gice ca **Ibikorwa bikora**.



![active_service](assets/fr/12.webp)



Kopa urubuga rwa Interface rw’aho ugurisha maze uyironke. Mu gice ca **Ibicuruzwa**, uzosangamwo urutonde rw'ibicuruzwa vyose washize hamwe n'iri soko. Ushobora rero gufata amafagitire ya generate Bitcoin (Imiravyo) kugira ngo ushire amahera abakiriya bawe.



![pos1](assets/fr/13.webp)



### Amahuza yo kwishura



Flash iragufasha kwemera kwishura aho hose kw’isi biciye kuri Bitcoin atari gusa biciye ku kibanza co kugurishako, ariko kandi biciye ku nzira yo kwishura. Ushobora generate amahuza y’ukwishyura nk’umuntu yikorera, umuhinga mu bijanye n’imigambi canke uko ikibanza cawe coba cose. Aya mahuza yoroshe gusangira n’uwuguhaye kandi aragufasha kwakira ama bitcoins ataco uhinduye mu gitabu cawe co kwizigama Wallet, ata n’umwe akufasha.



Ku rubuga rwa Flash, ukande kuri **Ihuza ryo kwishura** hanyuma wuzuze urupapuro kuri generate uruja n’uruza rwawe rwo kwishura.

### Intererano



Mu **Checkouts**, urashobora gukora ama plugins y’intererano ushobora gushira ku rubuga rwawe canke ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi, kugira abantu bashobore gushigikira umugambi wawe.



Fyonda ku **Igikoresho gishasha c'intererano**, hanyuma utunganye ido n'ido ry'ivyo



![donation](assets/fr/17.webp)



Umaze gukora plugin yawe y’intererano, uzoronka code ya javascript ushobora gushiramwo ku rubuga rwawe kugira ngo utangure kwakira intererano.




![dons](assets/fr/18.webp)

### Ubufatanye



Flash si ikintu gipfutse gusa: ushimira **Integrations** menu, urashobora guhuza ubucuruzi bwawe n'ibikoresho usanzwe ukoresha. Hashingiwe ku nyandiko, ushobora gushiramwo ishirahamwe ryawe rya Flash na :




- Ubucuruzi bwa Woo
- Gucuruza
- Wix
- Vimeo OTT (izoza vuba)

### Rema ububiko bwawe



Woba uri umucuruzi mushasha, canke nta rubuga ufise woshiramwo Flash? Iryo koraniro riguha uburyo bwo kwubaka urubuga bugufasha gukora amaduka ya Flash no kugurisha ibintu vyawe mu gihe wemera kwishura muri Bitcoin.



Mu menu, genda kuri Interface ku **Iduka**, hanyuma ukande kuri **Iduka rishasha**.



Gutunganya amakuru y’iduka ryawe mu kwinjiza izina ryihariye riboneka kuri wewe gusa, izina rusangi ry’abashitsi bawe, insobanuro y’iduka ryawe n’ibishushanyo vy’akaranga k’iduka ryawe (ikimenyetso, igipfukisho). Hitamwo amafaranga wipfuza gushiramwo ibintu ku rubuga rwawe. Ibiciro vy’ivyo bikoresho bizohindurwa bibe Bitcoin hakoreshejwe igipimo co guhindura isoko kiriho ubu, hanyuma uhuze Bitcoin Wallet wipfuza kwakirako amahera.



![store](assets/fr/22.webp)
