Naho benshi bakiriko baragabanya Bitcoin ngo ibe igikoresho co gushiramwo amahera no gutekereza ku bintu, uwuyiremye Satoshi Nakamoto yarayibonye ahanini nk’uburyo bwo kwishura bushingiye ku buhinga bwa none. Ariko rero, ni Hard kumenya aho ushobora gukoresha ama bitcoins yawe. Ariko ivyo vyari imbere y’Ikarita ya BTC.



Ishingiye kuri OSM (OpenStreetMap), ubwayo igikoresho co gukora ikarita y’abanyamuryango, BTC Map itanga uburyo bworoshe bwo gutanga urutonde rw’ibigo vyemera kwishura muri BTC, Lightning canke On-Chain. Urutonde rw’amakuru rwo kw’isi yose, rukiri mu ntango ariko rusanzwe ari ngirakamaro, rwuzuye n’abakoresha amafaranga y’amahera.

## Ikarata y'umucuruzi



![image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Ikarata y’abacuruzi iratanga urutonde rw’ibibanza vyo kw’isi yose vyemera kwishura muri BTC. Ushobora kubona ubwoko butandukanye bw’ibishushanyo, ari vyo bimenyetso vya mbere vyerekana kamere y’ishirahamwe (ipinte y’ibarabara, idiyama y’iduka ry’amabuye y’agaciro...). Nzofata imwe mu buryo bw’impfagusa.



Niwafyonda kuri uwu muhinga mu vy'ubuhinga bwo gushushanya ahantu ho muri Texas, ikintu co kwinjiza kiraboneka:



![image](assets/fr/05.webp)





- Izina riri mu bururu ni iry'ishirahamwe .
- Address n’ibihe vyo gufungura biratangwa aha hepfo, nimba biri kuri OSM .
- "Navigate" ishobora gushinga inzira hagati y'aho uri n'aho ukorera
- "Guhindura" bizokurekurira gutanga ivyiyumviro vyo guhindura dosiye ya OSM (bisaba konti ya OSM ku buntu), ni ukuvuga izina, amakuru yo guhamagara, ingengabihe n'ibindi
- "Sangira" bigutwara ku ndondoro y'urubuga
- "Ibindi" bigaragaza amahitamwo akurikira:



![image](assets/fr/06.webp)





- "Hamagara" ni inzira ngufi kuri telefone yo guhamagara ikigo
- "Urubuga" rugutwara ku rubuga rwa sosiyete
- "Kwerekana ibimenyetso" yerekana ibintu vyinjijwe kuri OSM ataco bitera amahuza canke ibikorwa
- "Ibibazo vy'Ikimenyetso" na "Ikarita y'Imigani" ubu ni amahuza yavunitse
- "Reba OSM" igusaba gufungura ikibanza c'uruganda ku gikorwa ca OSM



Amaherezo,





- "Ivyaherutse gusuzumwa" yerekana itariki dosiye yaherutse kuremwako canke yavuguruweko
- "Boost" iraguha uburenganzira, ku giciro ca Sats, guha urubuga rwawe akamaro ko kugaragara mu gihe gito
- mu nyuma, "Suzuma Aho" ikujana ku rupapuro rw'Ikarita ya BTC kugira ngo usabe guhindura urutonde. Reka turabe ingene bisa:



![image](assets/fr/07.webp)





- 1: Shira akamenyetso muri aka gasandugu nimba ushaka gusa kwemeza ko amakuru ari muri dosiye ari ku gihe (muri iki gihe, genda ku ntambwe ya 4)
- 2: Erekana aha amakuru, nimba ariho, atari yo, n’ugukosora usaba .
- 3: Sigura hano ingene waronse amakuru (sura, hamagara...)
- 4: Bandanya kugenzura captcha (bishingiye ku nyuguti nini)
- 5: Fyonda kuri "Submit Report" kugira ngo wohereze iciyumviro cawe



## Ikarata y'abanyagihugu



![image](assets/fr/08.webp)



"Ikarata y'imiryango" iragufasha kumenya imiryango itandukanye ya Bitcoin kw'isi yose, ukayigabanya ukayigaragaza hakurikijwe aho iri.



![image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Uzoca ubona ubwo nyene uturere tw’ibara ry’umuhondo. Nk’uko mubibona, izo ni imiryango itandukanye ya Bitcoin iri ku rutonde rw’ikarita ya BTC. Ufyondeye ibubamfu kuri kimwe muri vyo, uzobona agace gatoyi kerekana amahuzu yose yatanzwe, nk’urubuga rwa interineti be n’amakonte yo ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi. Ni nde azi, ushobora kuba uri hagati y’umuryango wa Bitcoin ukora cane, ukaba ukiri kure y’ugukanda gatoyi gusa ngo uyishiremwo nimba wumva ubishaka!



## Ibindi bice vy'urubuga



**Apps**: iyi page irakwibutsa ibinyamakuru aho ikarita ya BTC iboneka.



**Stats** iguha imibare myinshi ku bijanye n'ivyo usaba:




- Dashboard: imibare ku rutonde rw'amakuru, nk'umubare w'ibibanza biri ku rutonde canke umubare w'igenzura riherutse gukorwa
- Tagger Leaderboard: imbonerahamwe y'abakoresha bashizwe ku rutonde hakurikijwe ubwinshi bw'intererano (bafatanye n'abo!)
- Uburongozi bw'abanyagihugu: urutonde rw'abanyagihugu
- Igihugu



**Uturere (uturere)** tuguha amakuru atari amakarata:




- Imiryango: itanga urutonde rw’imiryango itandukanye yanditswe, urupapuro rwo kwandika uwundi mushasha, n’imibare mikeyi, vyose bishizwe hamwe hakurikijwe umugabane .
- Ibihugu: imibare mikeyi ihuriweko hakurikijwe ibihugu ikwereka umubare w'ibigo, abaje mu nama, amadosiye azohindurwa...



**Kubungabunga (kubungabunga)**




- Yongerako ahantu: wongere ahantu hashobora kwishurwa Bitcoin
- Genzura ahantu: guhindura/gukosora amakuru ku kibanza cari ku rutonde
- Yongera Umuryango: wongere umuryango (hariho ikosa ryo kwandika muri url, kugira ngo ushikire urupapuro, genda kuri Areas -> Umuryango -> Yongera Umuryango)
- Amatike yuguruye: aha niho ibisabwa vyo kwongerwako, kugenzura, ahantu n’imibano bishika, kugira ngo bikorerwe n’abakozi b’intahemuka
- Ibikorwa vyo gushiramwo amazina: bikwereka ibikorwa vyakozwe n'abaje mu mugambi (ukoresha wese nkawe canke jewe ashobora kuba umunyamuryango), nk'ahantu herutse kwongerwako, havuguruwe canke mbere hasubiwemwo...
- Ibibazo vyo gushiramwo: aha niho abakoresha bashira urutonde rw'amakosa yose yo gushiramwo



**Wiki**: Iyi nzira ikujana kuri paji ya GitHub y'umugambi.



**Dushigikire**: Iyi page irakubwira ingene wotanga intererano (muri Sats) canke ukaba umuterankunga w'umugambi.

## "Kwongerako ahantu



[Iyi paji](https://btcmap.org/add-location/) ikwereka ingene wokwongerako urutonde rw’ibintu vyubahiriza Bitcoin wewe nyene, ukoresheje Ikarita y’Ibarabara ryuguruye. Niba uriko uragira ingorane, hariho urupapuro wowuzuza, kugira ngo uwundi muntu akuremerere urutonde (ivyo bishobora gutwara amayinga menshi). Reka turabe:



![image](assets/fr/11.webp)





- 1: Izina ry'ishirahamwe
- 2: Address y’umubiri (ni ngombwa, ukeneye Address y’ibarabara)
- 3: Shira ikimenyetso nyaco ku ikarita
- 4: Ico giterwa kija mu rugero uruhe?
- 5: Ni ubuhe buryo bwo kwishura bwa Bitcoin buboneka (BTC, Umuravyo, ubudakorana)?



![image](assets/fr/12.webp)





- 6: URL y'urubuga (ntibikenewe)
- 7: Inomero ya telefone (ntibikenewe)
- 8: Amasaha y'ugufungura (ntibikenewe)
- 9: Konti ya X (twitter) y’ishure, hanyuma iyawe (ntibikenewe)
- 10: Ido n’ido ryose ubona ko rikwiye
- 11: Uruhara rwawe
  - "Ndi nyen'ubucuruzi": Ndi umuyobozi w'ubucuruzi
  - "Nagendeye nk'umukiriya": Nagendeye iki kigo nk'umukiriya
  - "ubundi buryo
- 12: E-mail yawe Address mu gihe iyo nzira yoba isaba ibindi bisobanuro
- 13: Gusuzuma Captcha (gutahura inyuguti nini)
- 14: Fyonda kuri "Submit Aho" kugira ngo wohereze urutonde rwawe
