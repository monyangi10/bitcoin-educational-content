## Imenyekanisha


Intumbero y’iyi nyigisho ni ugutuma nyen’ubucuruzi buto, vyiza cane ari iduka, umucuruzi wo mw’ibarabara, canke ikindi gikorwa cose c’ubudandaji, yemera Bitcoin nk’amahera yo kwishura no kubana nayo mu buryo bworoshe. Ivyo biganiro ni nyen’ubudandaji, ni we arongora igikorwa c’ubudandaji yigenga kandi yizigira abakozi bamwebamwe.


### Ingero zizwi cane


Mu bice bimwe bimwe vyo kw'isi, hariho imiryango ishobora gusobanurwamwo "uruziga", aho Bitcoin yemerwa kandi ikoreshwa cane n'abanyagihugu mu kwishura ibikorwa ku bikorwa vy'ubudandaji. Mbere n’abadandaza, bamaze guhembwa mu Bitcoin n’abaguzi babo, baragiranye amasezerano n’abagurisha ibintu vyabo kugira ngo barihe ibicuruzwa mu Bitcoin.


## Kwemera Bitcoin.


Intambwe ikurikira ni ukwemera Bitcoin ataco uhinduye mu bucuruzi bwawe; uburyo bworoshe bwo kubikora ni ugushiramwo no gutunganya Lightning Network (LN) Wallet kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kwishura ubwo nyene, ivyo bikaba bituma abakiriya bashobora kwishura amahera y’ugucuruza agabanutse igihe kimwe.


Kugira ngo vyorohe, tuzokoresha Wallet ya Satoshi nk’akarorero. Kurikiza izi ntambwe kugira ngo uyishiremwo kandi uyitunganye:


https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/mobile/wallet-of-satoshi-39149d86-e42b-4e8f-ae9f-7e061e7784f7

Uhejeje gukurikiza iyo nsiguro, uzoba witeguriye kwemera Bitcoin nk'uburyo bwo kwishura: ufungure gusa porogarama ya telefone ngendanwa maze ukande kuri buto ya "Receive", hanyuma winjize amahera (akenshi mu mafaranga y'aho uba) kuri generate Invoice yishurwa n'uwuyikoresha.


**Impanuro**: kwongerako akajambo kuri Invoice imbere yo kuyirema bituma umuntu ashobora gufatanya vyoroshe amahera n’ikintu kinaka (nk’ugucuruza ikiro 1 c’amapome ku 900 Sats). Ivyiyumviro vyinjijwe n’amaboko ntibiboneka ku mugenzi, rero bizokoreshwa gusa mu mutima.


### Itunganywa ry'imbere


Igikoresho giteye imbere gatoyi ni ugukoresha Wallet y’ikintu ca Satoshi co kugurisha, ushobora kuronka ukoresheje porogarama ukanda kuri menyu ya hamburger iri hejuru iburyo:


![image](assets/en/01.webp)


Ubu ni bumwe mu buryo bubiri bwo gushika ku kibanza c’Ikibanza c’Ugurisha. Ubundi buryo ni ubwo gukoresha [Wallet ya Satoshi PoS app] yihariye.


Inyungu zo gukoresha ikintu co kugurisha ni zitatu:


- igikorwa co kwakira abantu conyene, gibereye gusiga abakozi ngo baje gutora amahera y’abakiriya ataco bashoboye gukoresha ayo mahera;
- yemerera gukora urutonde rw'ibiciro n'ibicuruzwa vy'iduka ryawe, kugira ngo ushobore gukora invoice za Lightning uhisemwo ibicuruzwa vyaguzwe;
- gukuraho raporo z'ugurisha mu buryo bwa CSV.


Ushaka kumenya vyinshi, raba inyigisho yihariye kuri Wallet ya Satoshi - Ikibanza co kugurisha:


https://planb.network/tutorials/business/point-of-sale/wallet-of-satoshi-pos-efc9f266-cb21-49a8-94a8-5fe15a82eb07

## Amabwirizwa


Amategeko agenga Bitcoin aratandukanye cane ku gihugu kimwe gushika ku kindi. Ibihugu bimwe bimwe birafise amabwirizwa asobanutse kandi akomeye, mu gihe ibindi bifise amabwirizwa asobanutse kandi adasobanutse. Ibihugu vyinshi bisaba gutangaza ko ufise Bitcoin (nk’umuntu ku giti ciwe canke nk’ubudandaji) kugira ngo ukurikirane imisoro canke ngo ukoreshe imisoro.


## Umuti ku bacuruzi


Utitaye ku mategeko yo mu gihugu cawe, igikorwa coroshe ku muntu afise ubucuruzi buto ni uguhindura ama Bitcoins yaronse mu mafaranga y’igihugu ubwo nyene amaze kuyaronka. Muri ubwo buryo, urashobora kwirinda ingorane mu kwemera, gutangaza no kubara Bitcoin mu gikorwa cawe c’ubudandaji.


**Iciyumviro**: Mu bwoko bumwe bumwe bw’ubucuruzi, hashobora kuba ata nyandiko z’impapuro zijanye n’ugucuruza hagati y’umukiriya n’ugucuruza, rero kuri nyen’ubucuruzi, bizokworoha mbere kandi bizoca vyihuta!


Reka turabe ivyiza n’ibibi bimwe bimwe vy’uwo muti:


### Inyungu


Ubwo buryo bworoshe buratuma wirinda ingorane nyinshi: burakuraho ivy’uko hakenewe uburyo bwa KYC (Know Your Customer) kuri izo Bitcoins zaronse nk’ukwishura, ivyo bikaba ari umugenzo wonona cane ubuzima bwite n’umutekano w’umuntu ku giti ciwe; kandi iguma ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwo guharura amafaranga bitahindutse, kuko ata nkenerwa yo kwandika izo Bitcoins.


Ntuzokenera gushiramwo umwanya n’amahera vy’inyongera n’umujanama wawe mu vy’imisoro kugira ngo ukurikirane ivyo bihembo, kuko bizoba bingana n’ibindi vyose uzoronka.


### Inyungu


Uko umubare w’amahera agurishwa mu Bitcoin wiyongera, kwinjiza amahera mu gitereko c’amahera bishobora kuba bidashoboka, nk’akarorero, amahera ariho yoshobora kutaronka amahera yose ya Bitcoin. Ariko rero, ivyo ntibishoboka kuri benshi mu bafise ubucuruzi batangura kwemera Bitcoin. Ikindi kibazo gishobora gushika mu bihugu bimwebimwe vyo mu Buraya ni uko ubwoko bw’ubudandaji bwawe bushobora kukubuza kwemera amahera nk’uburyo bwo kwishura ibintu n’ibikorwa utanga ku bakiriya. Mu bihe nk’ivyo, umuti ushikirijwe ntuzosubira kuba uhagije.


## Raporo ya Bitcoin


Nk’uko twabibonye, ​​biroroshe kwemera Bitcoin ata buryo bushasha bwo guharura amafaranga bushobora gutuma umuntu agira ingorane; ariko rero, vyoba vyiza ukomeje gukoresha uburyo bwo guharura amafaranga buhuye kugira ngo ukomeze gukurikirana ingene Bitcoin yawe igenda. Ntuhagarike umutima, bishobora kuba vyoroshe uko ushaka, igihe cose uzoba utahura ingene ama Bitcoins yinjira n’asohoka muri Wallet yawe.


**Iciyumviro**: kwuzuza ahantu `Iciyumviro` igihe urema LN Invoice ku mukiriya bizokworoha cane guhindura ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin. Ni ko biri no ku gutanga LN ku baguzi. Mu vyiza, igikorwa cose gifitaniye isano n’agapapuro ko kwishura.


## Iciyumviro


Kwemera Bitcoin ni intambwe ya mbere gusa. Ku bikorwa vy’ubudandaji, ni ngombwa ko ukurikirana ibikorwa vyawe ataco utakaje ku buzima bwite n’umwidegemvyo w’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Kandi nk’uko twabibonye, ​​umuti woroshe utorera umuti ingorane nyinshi kandi ugahaza abafise uruhara mu gucuruza bose - bompi abacuruzi n’abaguzi.
