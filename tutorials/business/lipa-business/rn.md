Abacuruzi ubu bararushiriza gukunda kwemera amahera ya Bitcoin biciye ku Lightning Network. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ukwishura kw’ikarata y’inguzanyo, ivyo bikaba birimwo amahera menshi be n’igihe kirekire co kwishura, amafaranga y’umuravyo ashobora gukorwa hafi mu kanya nk’ako gukubita, ntashobora kwishurwa, kandi arazigama ibanga ry’abaguzi.



Kugira ngo ubucuruzi bushobore kwemera Bitcoin mu buryo bworoshe, umuti wo kwishura utegerezwa kuguma woroshe n’igikoresho co kwandika amafaranga Interface gisanzwe, gutanga ibikorwa vy’abakoresha benshi kandi mu vyiza gutanga uguhindura amafaranga y’aho hantu kugira ngo bigabanye uguhinduka.



**Lipa for Business** ni inyishu itunganye kuri ivyo bikenewe. Ni umuti w’Ubusuwisi wateguwe na Lightning Payment Services AG, wagenewe gutuma abacuruzi bemera amahera ya Bitcoin Lightning mu buryo bworoshe kandi bubereye, mu gihe baguma ataco bazigama.



*Iciyumviro: Amafoto akoreshwa muri iyi nyigisho yakuwe ku rubuga rwemewe rwa Lipa for Business (lipa.swiss/en/for-business) kandi akoreshwa mu kwigisha*



## Kumenyesha Lipa ku bucuruzi



Lipa for Business ni porogaramu yo kuri telefone ngendanwa ikora nk’igikoresho co gufata amahera ca Bitcoin na Lightning. Itanga Interface itunganijwe neza kugira ngo ushire hamwe amahera muri Sats kandi ikaba ishiramwo ibintu vy’umwuga: gushika ku bakozi, gutanga amakuru hanze, urubuga rwo gukoresha, vyose ata na rimwe wigeze ufata amahera yawe.



### Ibirango nyamukuru



**Ivyishyurwa vy’umuravyo ubwo nyene**: Inyemezabuguzi z’umuravyo za Bitcoin zisohoka mu masegonda make, bikaba bituma amafaranga akorwa mu kanya nk’ako gukubita ata bahuza bo muri banki. Amafaranga ni make kandi araboneka ugereranije n’imiti y’imigenzo.



**Interface POS irashoboka**: Iyi porogaramu itanga POS ya Interface igaragara, yagenewe gukoreshwa mu buryo bworoshe mu maduka. Injira amahera mu mafaranga yo mu gihugu, maze iyo porogarama ica igaragaza ayo mahera mu BTC be n’ikode ya QR yo kwishura. Ihuye n’amasakoshi yose ya Lightning ari kw’isoko.



**Uburongozi bw’abakozi benshi**: Abakozi bose barashobora gukoresha iyo porogarama kugira ngo bashobore gukura amahera, ata n’umwe afise uburenganzira bwo kuyaronka. Nyir’ivyo bikoresho aguma afise ububasha bwose, mu gihe uguhuza ibintu mu gicu bituma ata n’imwe ishobora gutakazwa. Buri mukozi wese aronka uburenganzira bwo kwinjira atandukanye biciye ku butumire kuri QR code.



**Guhindura ubwavyo mu CHF**: Ku bacuruzi b’Abasuwisi, Lipa itanga uguhindura ubwo nyene ivyo bagurisha mu mafranga y’Ubusuwisi kuri konti ya banki. Iyi nzira ni iyo guhitamwo: ushobora kuguma utanga amahera mu Bitcoin (ku buntu) canke ukayahindura mu CHF/EUR ku giciro ca 0,98%.



**Urubuga rw'urubuga**: Urubuga rw'ubuyobozi rwa Interface rushobora gushikwako biciye kuri dashboard.lipa.swiss, rugufasha kubona ibikorwa vyose, gucungera hakurikijwe igihe canke umukozi, no kwohereza hanze amakuru y'ivy'ubuhinga bw'ibarabara mu buryo bwa CSV. Ico gikoresho gishobora kandi gukoreshwa ku mafagitire y'urubuga rwa generate afise amakode ya QR avuye kuri Interface.

Iyo ifomu imaze gushikirizwa, Lipa irarema ikibanza cawe c’ubudandaji. Igenzura ry’amaboko rigufi rishobora gukorwa (uburyo bworoshe bwa KYC) imbere y’uko ritangura gukora. Ubusanzwe, iyo nzira ikora mu masaha 24, ariko ibihe birashobora guhinduka.



**Igihambaye**: Nta nzira ya konti ya banki isabwa kugira ngo utangure gutanga amahera muri Bitcoin. Amakuru ya banki asabwa gusa iyo uhisemwo guhindura ubwo nyene mu CHF.



## Gushiraho na Interface



**Iporogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa**: Iboneka ku matelefone ngendanwa n’amatablette afise Android/iOS. Interface yagenewe gukoreshwa aho igurishwa, ifise Elements yoroshe gusoma kandi imigenderanire igarukira ku vyo bikenewe. Buto ya "Cash a payment" itanga uburyo bwo kwinjira ku gicapo c'amahera.



**Ibisabwa mu vy’ubuhinga**: Bisaba ko umuntu afise Internet ihamye (3G minimum) kugira ngo ashobore kwishura Lightning mu gihe nyaco.



**Urubuga rw'urubuga**: Urubuga rw'ubuntu rushobora gushikwako biciye ku rubuga.lipa.swiss. Ihuriro ry’imeli ry’umutekano (ihuriro ry’ubupfumu ata jambobanga). Interface yerekana amafaranga yawe yose n’amakuru yuzuye: itariki, umubare wa BTC/fiat, igipimo ca Exchange, umukozi, n’ibindi CSV yoherezwa hanze kugira ngo ukoreshe ubuhinga bwo guharura.

## Kwemera kwishurwa



Uburyo bwo gutoragura amahera busa n’ubwo mu gihe c’ugucuruza:



![Processus de paiement Lipa](assets/fr/01.webp)





- Injiza amafaranga**: Ku rubuga rwo kwishura, shiramwo amafaranga mu mafaranga y’aho hantu (CHF canke EUR). Akarorero: ku kafe ku 4.50 CHF, winjize 4.50
- Invoice generation** : Porogarama ica ihindura ubwo nyene ayo mahera mu satoshis ku rugero ruriho ubu, maze igatanga Lightning Invoice mu buryo bwa kode ya QR .
- Ukwishura kw'umukiriya** : Umukiriya aracapura kode ya QR akoresheje Wallet Lightning yiwe maze akavyemeza ko yishuye .
- Kwemeza** : Ukwishura kwemezwa mu masegonda make, n'iyerekanwa ry'amashusho ry'ukuroranirwa



## Ibikoresho vy'umwuga



**Amateka n’imibare**: Ukwishura kwose kwandikwa n’ibintu vyose. Igikoresho gitanga inyishu rusangi n’ibiyunguruzo hakurikijwe igihe canke umukozi, bigereranywa n’uburyo bwa kera bwo kwandika amahera.



**Ivyoherezwa hanze mu vy’ubuhinga bw’ibarabara**: Ivyoherezwa hanze mu buryo bwa CSV/Excel n’amakuru yose akenewe (igipimo ca Exchange, transaction ID) kugira ngo ushire muri porogarama yawe y’ibarabara.



**Uburongozi bw'abakozi**: Wongere/ukureho abakoresha bemerewe biciye ku rubuga. Buri mukozi aronka uburenganzira bwo gushika aho akora n’ubushobozi bwo gukurikirana amafaranga. Gukuraho birashoboka igihe cose.



**Backup**: Konti y’umucuruzi atagira ububiko ifise amajambo 24 yo gusubiza ibintu kugira ngo abikwe neza. Nyir’iyi Wallet nyamukuru ni we wenyene akwiye gucungera iyo backup - abakozi ntaco bashobora kuyironka.



## Guhindura CHF kwikora



**Ukuboneka**: Ivyo bikoresho biboneka ku bacuruzi bo mu Busuwisi umuntu yishuye mu CHF (EUR bivanye n’ukuboneka).



**Itunganywa**: Guhitamwo hagati yo kwakira muri Bitcoin (ku buntu) canke guhindura fiat biciye ku mufatanyabikorwa w’ivy’amahera. Niba uriko urahindura CHF, shiramwo IBAN ku bijanye n’ugutanga amahera.



**Amahera**: 0.98% ya komisiyo ku mahera yahinduwe (ugereranije na 0% ku mahera yishurwa afise muri BTC). Nta yindi mahera canke kwiyandikisha vyihishijwe.



**Uko bigenda** : Amahera ubona aca agurishwa kugiciro c’isoko, akajanwa kuri konti yawe ya banki. Kwimurira amahera hakurikijwe igihe c’iherezo ca banki yawe.



**Uguhinduranya**: Ihitamwo rishobora gusubirwamwo igihe cose mu mirongo. Iguha uburenganzira bwo kugerageza mu buryo bwa "fiat conversion", hanyuma ugahindukira ukaja kuri 100% BTC umaze kwiyumvamwo.



## Umutekano n'ibanga



**Ibitagira ububiko**: Uguma ufise ububasha buhoraho ku mahera uronka. Urufunguzo rw'ibanga/rw'abantu bose ruvugwa mu gihe c'ugutunganya (ni co gituma ijambo 24 rivugwa). Lipa ntibika imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga.



**Umutekano w’abakozi**: Abakozi bashobora gukora amafagitire gusa, ntibakoreshe amahera. Iyo habaye ingorane y’ibarabara, amahera yawe aguma ari meza kandi urashobora gukuraho uburenganzira bwo kuyaronka.



**Ibanga ry’abaguzi**: amafaranga y’uruyeri Lightning ata makuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe afatanye. Utandukanye n’ukwishura ku makarita biciye ku nzira zihurikiye hamwe.



**Ivyemezo**: Dashboard ishobora gushikwako biciye ku nzira y'ubupfumu. Porogaramu yo kuri telefone ngendanwa irindwa na PIN canke biometrics.



## Ibibazo vy'ikoreshwa ryiza

- Abacuruzi b'abanyamahanga**: amakamyo y'ibifungurwa, amasoko, ibirori bifise telefone ngendanwa gusa

## Iciyumviro



Lipa for Business ishizwe ahantu nk’umuti wuzuye wo kwemera Bitcoin mu maduka. Nta bikorwa remezo bizimvye bisabwa (telefone yoroshe ni yo yonyene ukeneye), ibiciro ni bito kandi bihoraho, kandi kwinjira mu migenderanire iriho biroroshe.



Kubera ko idashobora kubikwa, ikaba ishobora gukingira ubuzima bwite, ifatanijwe n’ibikoresho vy’uburongozi vy’umwuga, bituma iba ihitamwo ryiza ku bacuruzi bipfuza gukoresha Bitcoin mu gihe bagumana uburyo bworoshe n’umutekano.
