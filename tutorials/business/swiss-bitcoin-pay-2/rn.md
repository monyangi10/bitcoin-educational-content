## Ni kuki wemera Bitcoin mu bucuruzi bwawe?


Kwemera Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura biratanga inyungu nyinshi ku bucuruzi bwawe. Ica mbere, Bitcoin ikora nk’amahera, ariko ata ngorane z’amahera y’umubiri. Cokimwe n’amahera, ama Bitcoins yaronswa nk’ukwishurwa ntakeneye guca mu nzira y’amabanki ya kera. Ni ugucuruza ataco uhinduye kuva ku mukiriya wawe gushika kuri wewe nyene, kiretse ukoresheje umuhuza w’ukwishyura.


Ikindi kandi, ukwishyura kwa Bitcoin gutanga iherezo ry’aho nyene na Lightning kandi bifata iminota mikeyi gusa mu gihe c’ukwishyura kuri onchain. Iyo bimaze kwemezwa, ntibishoboka gukuraho amahera ya Bitcoin. Iyo uhisemwo kwizigama, ugenzura ataco uhinduye Bitcoins zawe, ivyo bikaba bituma ubucuruzi bwawe bugira ubwigenge bw’amahera.


Na Bitcoin, uzobona kandi ko uzozigama amahera menshi ku makomisiyo n’amahera yo muri banki. Gukoresha Bitcoin bikuraho ivy’ugugura canke gukodesha ikibanza co kwishurako; mudasobwa canke telefone yoroshe birahagije. Vyongeye, mu kwemera kwishura Bitcoin ataco uhinduye, urinda amakomisiyo akenshi asabwa ku bijanye n’ugucuruza. Naho woca mu muhuza kugira ngo ukoreshe amahera, amahera ajanye n’amahera ya Bitcoin muri rusangi ni make cane kuruta ay’amabanki asanzwe.

Bitcoin isangira ivyiza vyinshi n’amahera, ariko itanga mbere n’ivyiza vyinshi nk’umutungo. Nk’akarorero, umubare wose wa BTC ushirwaho n’uguhurizako ku miriyoni 21, bikaba bituma iba umutungo udasanzwe kandi ushobora guhangana n’ugutera imbere kw’amahera, bitandukanye n’amahera ya Leta. Mu kwemera Bitcoin mu bucuruzi bwawe, uraronka akaryo ko kuzigama agaciro k’itunga ryawe mu kiringo kirekire no guhindura itunga ry’ishirahamwe ryawe.


Ku musi ku musi, ukwishura kwa Bitcoin kuratanga uburyo bworoshe n’uguhinduranya kuruta amahera. Inyungu ikomeye ni uko udakeneye gutanga ihinduka ku bijanye n’ukwishura Bitcoin, ivyo bikaba bikuraho ivy’ugucungera igitabu c’amahera. Vyongeye, kwemera ama bitcoins biragabanya ingorane zo gushikirwa n’ubusuma, ubusuma n’ubusuma. Vyongeye, ni amafaranga adashobora kwigana, atandukanye n’amafaranga y’amafaranga.


Niba ufise abaguzi bo kw’isi yose, Bitcoin irafise akamaro ko kuba amafaranga y’isi yose, gutyo ugakuraho ivy’uko abaguzi bawe badakeneye amafaranga y’i Exchange.


Ku bucuruzi bwo kuri internet, Bitcoin na yo ni uburyo bwiza bwo kwemera kwishura kure ata nkomanzi, ataco ukeneye guca mu buryo bwa kera bwo gukoresha amabanki. Iryo koreshwa ryari rimwe mu ntumbero za mbere za Satoshi Nakamoto, uwahinguye Bitcoin, uwayidondora nk'"*Uburyo bw'amahera y'ubuhinga bwa none*".


Ubwa nyuma, kwinjiza Bitcoin mu bucuruzi bwawe navyo birashobora kuba igice c’ingene womenyekanisha. Kwemera BTC birashobora gutuma ishirahamwe ryawe rigira ubuhinga bushasha, rishobora guhindura, kandi rihuye n’iterambere ry’ubuhinga, mu gihe rigufungurira isoko rishasha. Dukurikije [itohoza ryakozwe na KPMG](https://kpmg.com/fr/fr/home/media-releases), 12% vy’Abafaransa barafise amafaranga y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none, kandi benshi muri bo bafise amafaranga y’ubuhinga bwa none, kandi benshi muri bo bafise amafaranga y’ubuhinga bwa none. Mu kwemera ubu buryo bwo kwishura, urashobora gukwegera abo bakiriya bipfuza gukoresha Sats yabo, iyo bataba ari bo boba batazokwigera baza mu bucuruzi bwawe. Ni uburyo bwiza kandi bwo kwimenyekanisha ku ruvyaro rwa Z.


Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, kwemera Bitcoin bikwiye gufatwa nk’akaryo gatoyi gashobora kugira ingaruka nziza cane ku bucuruzi bwawe. Iyo ivyo bintu vyabaye bigaragaye ko bitashoboye, amahera azokoreshwa azoguma ari make cane. Igiciro nyamukuru kiri mu gihe gikenewe kugira ngo ushireho umuti wo kwemera BTC, ariko nk’uko uzobibona muri iyi nyigisho, ubu biroroshe cane kandi biranyaruka gushikako.


## Gushikiriza umushahara w'Ubusuwisi Bitcoin

Swiss Bitcoin Pay ni umuti utuma abacuruzi bemera amahera ya Bitcoin mu buryo bugororotse. Ishiramwo porogarama ya PoS (*Point of Sale*) n’umukoresha ashobora gukoreshwa neza Interface, yoroshe ku bakozi bawe gukoresha ata bumenyi bwimbitse bw’ubuhinga busaba. Udakunze igikoresho ca kera ca Bitcoin Wallet, igikoresho ca Swiss Bitcoin Pay gigenewe gusa kwakira amahera ya Bitcoin, ivyo bikaba bigufasha kwemera abakozi ico gikoresho ata ngorane. Ufise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha porogarama nyinshi za PoS zihuye na konti imwe y’Ubusuwisi Bitcoin Pay, bikaba vyoroshe gukoresha haba kuri tablette, ku gitabu c’abakozi bawe, canke kuri telefone ngendanwa z’abakozi bawe. Porogarama y’Ubusuwisi yitwa Bitcoin Pay irashobora gushirwa ku bikoresho vya Android na iOS, kandi iraboneka no ku rubuga rwa mudasobwa.


Swiss Bitcoin Pay itanga uburyo bubiri bwo gucunga amahera: amahera ashobora gukurwa ataco akora muri Bitcoin akaja mu Address yihariye canke agahindurwa mu mafaranga y’amahera (fiat) agashirwa kuri konti ya banki. Ivyo bikorwa bikorana n’ivyuma kandi bibera ku musi ku musi amasaha 24 yose. Gutyo, ntubwirizwa gucungera n’amaboko amahera y’i Bitcoin canke ayo biciye kuri Lightning Network; Swiss Bitcoin Pay irakwitaho vyose. Buri musi, uzoronka amahera asigaye mu gitabu cawe bwite ca Wallet canke muri konti yawe ya banki, bivanye n’ivyo ukunda. Naho atari umuti 100% udafise ububiko nk’uwo utangwa na BTCPay Server, nk’akarorero, Swiss Bitcoin Pay igereranya ugusenyera ku mugozi umwe gushimishije hagati y’ugufasha n’umutekano, kuko umuhuza afata gusa amafaranga yawe amasaha 24 imbere y’uko akurungikira ayo mahera. Ikindi kandi, bitandukanye n’ibindi bisubizo vyo kubungabunga, Swiss Bitcoin Pay ntibisaba ivyemezo vy’akaranga (KYC) ku bucuruzi bwawe.


Amafaranga ajanye no gukoresha Swiss Bitcoin Pay arahiganwa cane: umwaka wa mbere, komisiyo y’ibice 0,21% irakoreshwa ku bikorwa vy’ubudandaji. Hanyuma, amafaranga ni 1% ku mahera abikwa muri Bitcoin na 1,5% ku mahera yahinduwe mu mafaranga y’amahera. Ariko rero, birabereye kumenya ko mu gusubiza, bakwishura 100% vy’amahera y’ugucuruza Bitcoin ku mahera yose ukura.


Swiss Bitcoin Pay rero yigaragaza nk’ubundi buryo bushimishije hagati y’imiti y’ububiko bushitse n’uburyo bugoranye bwo kwicungera nka BTCPay Server. Ni ugusenyera ku mugozi umwe neza hagati y’ukworoha, umutekano, n’ukwigenga mu vy’amahera ku mudandaza.
