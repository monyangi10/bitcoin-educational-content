## Ikibazo ni ikihe


Nimba vyose vyagenda neza gushika ubu, none ingorane ni iyihe? Ehe rero: ushobora kubona ko abakiriya batagura ama bitcoins mw’iduka ryawe! Birashoboka cane ko abantu benshi batazi mbere ico Bitcoin ari co, abandi bamwebamwe boshobora kuba bayimenyereye ariko ntibarigeze bayikoresha mu kugura ibintu ku musi ku musi, kandi ni igice gitoyi gusa c’abanyagihugu bayizi neza, yamara n’ubu ntibayikoresha mu vyo bakoresha.


Kubera iki? Biroroshe: abantu bakunda gukoresha amafaranga babona ko ari yo agoyagoya! Iyo mpamvu nyene ituma wipfuza Bitcoin nyinshi kugira ngo ukingire ugucika intege kw’agaciro k’ayandi mafaranga yose, iremesha abandi gukoresha ayandi mafaranga yose imbere yo kugurisha ama bitcoins yabo.


Aha, muri iyi nyigisho, tuzogerageza kwungukira kuri abo bantu bakeyi maze tubafashe gukoresha amafaranga yabo ya bitcoins ku iduka ryawe, mu gihe wewe uyaronka.


## Ivyiyumviro


Reka turabe uburyo bumwe bumwe bwoshobora gutuma abakiriya bariha bakoresheje Bitcoin. Tuzovuga bitatu: **ingingo ngenderwako y'igabanya ry'ibiciro**, **ingingo ngenderwako y'igiciro kinini igihe cose**, na **ingingo ngenderwako y'ugutanga ibindi**.


### Amategeko y'Igabanya ry'Igiciro


Uwo mudandaza atanga igiciro kidahinduka kingana n’ijanisha ry’agaciro kose k’ibintu vyose canke ivyo bihisemwo. Iyi politike ishobora gushirwa mu ngiro mu buryo bubiri nyamukuru:


#### Agaciro k'izina k'ibicuruzwa


Ivyo ni ugugabanya igiciro ku gaciro k’ibintu, ari vyo bigabanya igiciro vyoroshe kandi bitaziguye. Ico giciro kizokoreshwa gusa iyo umuntu yishuye muri Bitcoin. Reka tuvuge ko igiciro gikoreshwa ari 10%. Iyo ipantaro igura amadolari 50 yishuwe n’ikarita y’ideni, igiciro kiri muri Bitcoin coba kingana na ```50 - (50 × 0,1)```, ivyo bikaba bingana n’amadolari 45 y’Amerika.


#### Kugabanya umubare ufise impera


Muri ivyo, iciyumviro ni ugutandukanya n’igiciro umusoro canke igiciro co hanze gisanzwe kiri mu giciro ca nyuma ariko kidakora canke kidakwiye gukoreshwa ku kwishura Bitcoin. Umukiriya akenshi arazi neza ingaruka z’ico giciro c’iyi nkori canke amahera kandi azotahura ingingo ngenderwako y’ugugabanya igiciro.


Ubwo bwoko bw’igabanya ry’ibiciro burakora neza cane ku maduka agaragara, cane cane ku bicuruzwa bifise imisoro myinshi; mu `umusoro munini` dushaka kuvuga igice kiri hejuru ya 10% congerewe ku giciro c’izina c’ikintu. Mu bihe aho igiciro kiri hasi kandi igicuruzwa kikaba coroshe kandi kidahenda, biragoye ko abakiriya babona itandukaniro, bikaba bituma badashobora guhitamwo Bitcoin.


##### Akarorero


Ibihugu vyose vyo mu Buraya bifise umusoro ku vyo umuntu akoresha, witwa mu congereza "Umusoro ku gaciro k'ivyongerwako", ubu hakaba [VAT](https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umusoro ku gaciro_kongereweko). Uwo musoro wavumbuwe mu Bufaransa mu 1954, kandi mu 1970 wari umaze gutangura gukwiragira mu Muryango w’Uburaya wo mu ntango; mu 2020 ryari ryarakwiragiye mu bice vyinshi vyo kw’isi.


Ku karorero, reka twiyumvire ko turi mu Buraya, mu gihugu aho igipimo c’IVA ari 20% ku rwego urwo ari rwo rwose rw’ibicuruzwa. Turi mw’iduka ry’inkweto, kandi reka tuvuge ko inkweto z’urukoba zigura amadolari 120 iyo zirishwe n’ikarata yo gukurako amahera.


Uwo mudandaza yoshobora gusaba igiciro kingana n’igipimo c’IVA ku vyo kwishura Bitcoin, rero iyo nketo yoba igura ```120 / 1,20``` bingana n’amadolari 100. Aka karorero karerekana imwe mu misoro yoroshe kandi yihuta; ariko rero, urashobora gukoresha uwundi musoro canke amahera yose azwi mu karere kawe kugira ngo iyo nkunga ibe ngirakamaro.


### Ibihe vyose vy'igiciro kinini


Muri iyo ngingo, umucuruzi yemera kwishura Bitcoin kandi agaha umukiriya igiciro kinini kuruta ibindi vyose c’ama Exchange hagati ya Bitcoin n’amahera kanaka, akenshi USD canke EUR. Ni ngombwa kumenya ko ata mvo n’imwe yotuma udakoresha igipimo ca Exchange c’amahera yo mu karere kawe, naho yoba itandukanye n’izo zibiri twavuze.


Iyi politike ikoreshwa muri rusangi ku bijanye n’ugucuruza kuri Internet. Intambamyi ni uko benshi mu bacuruzi badafise imbuga zabo zo kugurishako ibintu ku rubuga; uzokenera kwizigira urubuga rw’ubudandaji rwo kuri interineti rushigikira iyo ngingo ngenderwako.


### Ibindi bikorwa


Muri iyo ngingo, aho gukoresha igiciro c’igabanuka, umucuruzi aratanga inyungu y’inyongera iyo umukiriya arishe akoresheje Bitcoin. Igiciro kiguma ari co nyene, ariko agaciro umuntu abona karaduga. Iyi nzira ni ngirakamaro mu bihe aho:



- Ntushaka guhindura urutonde rw’ibiciro vyawe.
- Ushaka kwirinda ingorane z’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubuhinga.
- Ushaka gutuma iyo mpanuro iba iyo mu mutima kuruta iyo mu mibare.


Ingero zimwe zimwe ni:



- Iduka ry’ibifungurwa ryongerako ikintu c’ubuntu (nk’akarorero, igikombe canke ifunguro ry’ingurube).

**Ikigo c'ubwiza gitanga ubundi buhinga bwo ku buntu.**


- Ingoro y’amasinema itanga igikoresho, ivyo kunywa canke gusubiramwo ivyicaro.
- Iduka ry’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none ryongerako ikintu co gukoresha ku kintu nyamukuru.


Ingingo ngenderwako ni yoroshe: ku bici- ro bingana, abakiriya bariha na Bitcoin bararonka vyinshi. Akenshi, uwo mukiriya yari gushobora kugura ico kintu c’inyongera mu nyuma, mugabo inyungu y’ubuntu yoshobora kuba ari ikintu kidasanzwe kitaboneka mu buryo bundi. Ubwo buryo burashobora kuba bwiza cane mu kugumiza abakoresha Bitcoin bariho no gutanga ubutumwa bwiza ata kugabanya amafaranga nk’uko vyogenda ku yindi mice y’ibiciro.


## Kumenyesha abakiriya


Ego cane, gushinga amategeko ntibihagije; utegerezwa kandi kumenyesha abakiriya ivyo bintu. Nimba abakiriya bawe batazi ko bashobora kwishura na Bitcoin canke ntibabona akamaro gatomoye mu kubigira, ntibazohitamwo iyo nzira. Intumbero yawe ni ukumenyesha abakiriya ko igihe co gukoresha Bitcoin ubu kiri mw’iduka ryawe.


Guhanahana amakuru neza bisigura kuboneka: amakuru akwiye gusobanuka, yoroshe, kandi akaboneka aho hose afise insiguro – ku kibanza co kugurisha, ku rubuga, ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi, ku nzira zitaziguye zo guhanahana amakuru n’abaguzi. Abakozi bategerezwa kandi kwitegurira gusigura, mw’ijwi ry’ubugenzi, ko kwishura ukoresheje Bitcoin bishoboka kandi ko ari vyiza.


Itegeko ryose ryemejwe (ugugabanya igiciro, gutanga, canke akarusho k’igiciro ca Exchange) rikwiye gushikirizwa nk’akaryo ku mukiriya, atari nk’ibintu vy’ubuhinga. Ubutumwa butegerezwa guca buza: "Ishura na Bitcoin maze ubone ibindi."


Uburyo bwo kubishira mu ngiro burasigazwa n’umucuruzi, ariko muri make, ng’ubu uburyo bumwebumwe buboneka:



- Ibimenyetso vyo mu maduka (amadirisho, igikoresho co kwandika amafaranga, amashelufu)

*Ibimenyetso canke ibimenyetso ku madirisha*


- Amatangazo yo ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi, amakuru, amatangazo yo ku rubuga
- Ibiganiro bitaziguye n'abaguzi iyo bishoboka

**Ikwamamaza rivuye ku ba influencers ba Bitcoin bavuga ururimi rumwe n'abaguzi bawe**

## Iciyumviro


Amategeko twabonye – ugugabanya igiciro, igiciro kinini c’igihe cose, ibindi bihembo – ni bikeyi gusa mu ngabire zishoboka, zose zitanga agaciro nyakuri ku bahisemwo kwishura na Bitcoin. Gutera intege abantu ngo bayikoreshe bisigura gutanga umusanzu mu kwubaka ubutunzi bufise umwidegemvyo, buboneka kandi bukomeye.


Ubwa nyuma, nugumye ugira ikiyago gifunguye n’abaguzi bawe: wumvirize inyishu, wihweze ibikora n’ibidakora, kandi uhindure amategeko yawe uko igihe kigenda kirarenga. Ukoresheje uburyo bwiza, uzokwegera abakiriya benshi bashaka kwishura na Bitcoin. Amahigwe masa!
