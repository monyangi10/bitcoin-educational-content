## Imenyekanisha


Iyi nyigisho irasigura ingene wokoresha [Divly](https://divly.com/) kugira ngo utegure raporo y’ikori rya Bitcoin (BTC). Gutegura raporo y’imisoro birimwo kwegeranya amafaranga yose yerekeye BTC mu mwaka w’itunganywa, kubara inyungu canke inyungu zishobora gukorwako imisoro, no kwohereza hanze raporo ishobora gushikirizwa ubuyobozi bw’imisoro bwo mu karere iwanyu.


Mu bihugu aho Bitcoin itegekanijwe n’amategeko y’imisoro, utegerezwa gutanga raporo y’inyungu canke ibihombo biva ku gutanga BTC n’inyungu waronse muri BTC. Gukoresha porogarama yo gutanga raporo y’imisoro biragufasha gutunganya ayo makuru no gutanga raporo y’imisoro neza.


## Gutangura


Gutangura:



- Rema konti ya Divly.
- Tegura **igihugu ubamwo mu gutanga umusoro** na **amafaranga yo mu karere**.
- Wemeze ko aya mahinduka yerekana ububasha aho utanga raporo y'imisoro.


Divly ikoresha iyi ntunganyo kugira ngo ikoreshe amategeko y'imisoro akwiye n'uguhindura amafaranga igihe ukora raporo yawe.

## Intambwe ya 1 - Injira mu bikorwa vyose vya Bitcoin bivuye mu bikorwa vyawe vya wallet n'ivyo uhindura


Ibikorwa vyose vya Bitcoin vy’umwaka w’ikori bitegerezwa gushirwa hanze imbere y’uko habaho imibare y’ikori.


Divly ishigikira uburyo bwinshi bwo kwinjiza:



- API canke amahuza ya OAuth** ku bijanye n'uguhanahana canke ibikorwa biyishigikira
- Dosiye ya CSV ishirwa kuri wallets canke guhanahana
- Kwinjira n'amaboko** ku bikorwa vyose bitavuzwe n'uburyo bwikoresha


Raba neza ko uzana **ivyo BTC yose yinjira n’isohoka** bihuye n’igihe c’ikori uriko uratanga raporo.


![img](assets/en/02.webp)


## Intambwe ya 2 - Gusubiramwo amafaranga yinjijwe hanze


**Wemeze umubare wawe w'amahera:**


Ahantu heza wotangura ni ukugenzura ko crypto yose ufise ihuye n’umubare werekanwa muri Divly. Divly ibara ivyo ufise mu gucapura amafaranga yose washizemwo.


Kugira ngo ubikore, tangura uja kuri paji y’Iciyumviro rusangi. Suzuma ko crypto yose iri ku rutonde ari umubare ufise vy’ukuri. Divly ntiyerekana amafaranga yawe ya fiat mu Nsiguro, rero ndagusavye uyarengagize muri iki gikorwa.


Ushobora kuyungurura kuri wallet niwaba ubonye ibibazo. Ivyo bigufasha gutahura ico wallet ishobora kuba itari mu nzira.


![img](assets/en/03.webp)


Inyuma yo kwinjiza:



- Genda mu gice ca **Ibikorwa**.
- Suzuma ko igikorwa cose kigaragara kirimwo amatariki n’amahera bikwiye.
- Gutorera umuti igiciro kibuze canke imburi zishingiye ku giciro aho bikenewe.


**Igihambaye:** Urabe ko winjiza **YOSE** mu bikorwa vyawe vy'ubuhinga bwa none muri Divly imbere y'uko utera intambwe ikurikira. Harimwo n’ama wallet akanye! Ahandi ho hariho ingorane y’uko imisoro yawe itazoba igororotse.


![img](assets/en/04.webp)


## Intambwe ya 3 - Gushira mu migwi amafaranga abitswe n'ayo gukurwa


Ubwoko butandukanye bw’ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa none burashobora kugira ingaruka zitandukanye ku misoro. Ivyo birimwo ibikorwa nk’ugutanga ingabirano z’amahera y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none, ivy’ubutunzi vyatakaye, ivyiza vya mining, forks, airdrops, n’ibindi bisa n’ivyo. Ni ngombwa ko amafaranga yose akoreshwa ashirwa mu migwi neza.


Mu bihe vyinshi, Divly ihita itanga ibimenyetso bikwiye. Ariko rero, iyo amakuru yerekeye ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji ariho adahagije, ugushira mu migwi ubwavyo vyoshobora kutashoboka. Muri ivyo bihe, ni inshingano y’uwukoresha gutanga n’amaboko ikimenyetso kibereye. Kugira ngo utahure insobanuro n’ingene imisoro ifatwa ku kimenyetso kimwekimwe cose, turagusavye urabe ingingo y’imfashanyo ijana n’ivyo.


Kugira ngo ushireko amazina y’ibikorwa, genda kuri paji y’Ibikorwa. Hitamwo igikorwa kimwe canke vyinshi maze uhitemwo ikimenyetso kibereye mu bimenyetso bimanuka biri hejuru kuri paji.


![img](assets/en/05.webp)


## Intambwe ya 4 - Gukora raporo y'imisoro


Igihe amafaranga amaze kwinjizwa mu gihugu no gushirwa mu migwi:



- Genda mu gice ca **Raporo y’Ikori**.
- Hitamwo **umwaka w'ikori** ubereye.
- Suzuma incamake y’inyungu, ibihombo, n’ivyiciro vy’inyungu vyaharuwe.


Incamake ihuriza hamwe ibintu bishobora gukorwako umusoro bishingiye ku makuru yazanywe n’imigwi.


Raporo y’imisoro ya Divly iragufasha kwemeza ko amafaranga yose afashwe imbere yo kuyarungika hanze.

Inyuma y'isubiramwo:

- Bika dosiye yoherejwe hanze canke uyicape kugira ngo uyishikirize ubuyobozi bwawe bw’imisoro.
