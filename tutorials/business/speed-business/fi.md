## Getting started with Speed Business



[Speed Business](https://www.tryspeed.com/) is a platform developed by [Speed Wallet](https://www.speed.app/) that enables any merchant to integrate instant, low-cost Bitcoin and stablecoin payments.



Speed has a wide range of features to cover the financial aspects of your business. You'll find:





- Online payment configuration**: Receive payments from your customers wherever they are, thanks to your website.





- On-site payments**: Ideal for stores and businesses that collect cash in-store.





- Withdrawals**: Withdraw your assets smoothly and use your bitcoins to pay back your customers and salaries.





- Connection with other platforms**: Do you use external tools to manage your payments? Speed offers you the possibility of connecting them to its platform, for an all-in-one ecosystem that reflects your business.



Create your account on [Speed](https://app.tryspeed.com/register/) and we'll start setting up payments for your business.

For the purposes of this tutorial, we'll be working with a standard integration using the extension provided by Speed.

## Receiving payments



Speed Wallet gives you the option of using multiple methods to receive online or on-site payments in your business.



In the **Receive Payments > Payments** menu, you'll find the history of payments received and their status (paid, expired, unpaid, cancelled).



![payments](assets/fr/11.webp)





- Payment links are located in the **Checkout Links** option and allow you to set up unique payment pages for your products.



![checkout-link](assets/fr/12.webp)



Depending on your needs, you can configure and customize the payment page to receive payments of a specific amount.



![configure-checkout](assets/fr/13.webp)



![finalize-checkout](assets/fr/14.webp)



You can find the list of payment links you have set up in your account in the **Payment Links** menu.





- Invoices: Speed lets you generate quotations and invoices for your customers.



![invoices](assets/fr/16.webp)



Select a customer you've already registered, or easily create your own.



By setting the currency, you will have access to the list of products configured in that currency.



You can send this Invoice in PDF format, by e-mail, or generate a QR code link to scan (ideal for stores that collect on site) so that your customer can pay the Invoice.



![configure-invoice](assets/fr/17.webp)






- The **Payment Addresses** menu lets you set up a Lightning Address to which you can receive multiple payments of varying amounts.



![addresses](assets/fr/19.webp)



⚠️ You can add and use domain names other than Speed's. However, for your first experience, we recommend that you use the standard configuration to benefit from all the expertise of Speed business technical support.





- The **One QR**: Ideal for on-site payments, create a QR code associated with your business to enable your customers to pay for your products.



![one-qr](assets/fr/20.webp)



## Make payments from Speed



Speed business doesn't just collect payments for your business, it's a portfolio that lets you manage the entire financial side of your business without a hitch.



In the **Send Payments** menu, you'll find all the money transfer options speed has to offer.





- Instant payments**: With the Instant Send option, securely send bitcoins instantly from your merchant account.





- generate withdrawal links** to enable your partners and suppliers to access their payment at a later date without requiring your online presence.



In the **Withdrawal Links** option, create a new withdrawal link, then configure it by defining the currency, the amount and a password to secure your recipient's transaction.



![withdrawal-links](assets/fr/21.webp)



⚠️Les withdrawal links can only be used once, we strongly recommend that you set a unique password for each link, otherwise anyone in possession of the link can withdraw the amount set on the withdrawal link.





- Payouts**: In the Payouts menu, initiate withdrawals from your Speed Business balance to your personal Wallet.



![payouts](assets/fr/22.webp)





- Discounts**: Encourage your regular customers by setting up rebate options to earn bonuses.



![cashbacks](assets/fr/23.webp)

## Customize your business



In the **Settings** menu, you can configure your merchant profile and business information.



In the **Business Settings** section:





- Edit your merchant account details such as your name, location and time zone.





- Check the permissions activated (receive payment, send Bitcoin, Exchange, transfer, withdraw) on your account in the **Account Status** menu.





- Define your withdrawal wallets in the **Payout Wallets** menu and configure them in the **Payout Scheduling** menu.





- Define the graphic guidelines for your business and customize payment pages, emails, QR codes and invoices to your liking in the **Branding** menu.





- Configure payment methods in accepted currencies in the **Payment Method** menu.



![payment-method](assets/fr/30.webp)



⚠️ The tolerance corresponds to the percentage reduction you accept on the Invoice amount for a payment to be considered valid. If your customer has to pay USD 100 and your tolerance is 1%, any payment of USD 99 will validate the USD 100 Invoice.
