## Gutangura n'ubucuruzi bwihuta



[Ubucuruzi bwihuta](https://www.tryspeed.com/) ni urubuga rwateguwe na [Speed ​​Wallet](https://www.speed.app/) rushoboza umucuruzi wese gushiramwo amahera y’ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin n’amahera y’amahera y’amahera.



Speed ​​ifise ibintu vyinshi vyo gupfuka ivy’amahera y’ubucuruzi bwawe. Uzosanga:





- Uburyo bwo kwishura kuri internet**: Ushobora kwakira amahera y’abaguzi bawe aho bari hose, ushimira urubuga rwawe.





- Ukwishura ku kibanza**: Ni vyiza ku maduka n’ubucuruzi bukorakoranya amahera mu maduka.





- Ivyo gukura**: Kura itunga ryawe neza kandi ukoreshe ama bitcoins yawe kugira ngo wishure abakiriya bawe n’imishahara yawe.





- Guhuza n’izindi nzira**: Woba ukoresha ibikoresho vyo hanze kugira ngo ushobore gucunga ivyo wishura? Speed ​​iraguha ubushobozi bwo kubifatanya n’urubuga rwayo, ku bijanye n’ibidukikije vyose muri kimwe vyerekana ubucuruzi bwawe.



Rema konti yawe kuri [Speed](https://app.tryspeed.com/yandikishe/) maze dutangure gushinga amahera y'ubucuruzi bwawe.

## Gutanga amahera ukoresheje Speed



Ubucuruzi bwihuta ntibukoranya gusa amahera y’ubucuruzi bwawe, ni Wallet igufasha gutwara uruhande rwose rw’ivy’ubutunzi rw’ubucuruzi bwawe ata ngorane.



Mu **Send Payments**, uzosangamwo uburyo bwose bwo kohereza amahera umuvuduko ushobora gutanga.





- Ivyishyurwa ubwo nyene**: Ukoresheje uburyo bwo kohereza ubwo nyene, wohereze bitcoins ubwo nyene ukoresheje konti yawe y’umucuruzi.





- generate **amahuzu yo gukura amahera** kugira ngo abafatanyabikorwa bawe n’abaguha ibikoresho bashobore kuronka amahera yabo mu gihe kizoza ataco usaba ko uba uri kuri internet.



Mu **Withdrawal Links**, rema ihuriro rishasha ryo gukura, hanyuma uritunganye mu gusobanura amafaranga, umubare n’ijambobanga kugira ngo ukingire amafaranga y’uwuguhaye.



![withdrawal-links](assets/fr/21.webp)



⚠️Les withdrawal links zishobora gukoreshwa rimwe gusa, turagusavye cane gushinga password yihariye kuri buri link, ahandi ho umuntu wese afise iyo link ashobora gukura amahera yashizweho kuri link yo gukuraho.





- Ivyishyurwa**: Mu rutonde rw'Ivyishyurwa, tangura gukura amahera mu bucuruzi bwawe bwihuta ku Wallet yawe bwite.



![payouts](assets/fr/22.webp)





- Ivyimburwa**: Remesha abakiriya bawe bahoraho mu gushinga uburyo bwo gusubiza inyuma kugira ngo uronke ivyiza.

## Guhindura ubucuruzi bwawe



Mu **Ivyagezwe**, ushobora guhindura urutonde rw'umucuruzi wawe n'amakuru y'ubucuruzi.



Mu gice ca **Imiterere y'ubucuruzi**:





- Guhindura amakuru ya konti yawe y'umucuruzi nk'izina ryawe, aho uri n'isaha yawe.





- Suzuma uruhusha rwakoreshejwe (kwakira amahera, kohereza Bitcoin, Exchange, kwimurira, gukura) kuri konti yawe mu **Ikibanza ca Konti**.





- Sigura amasakoshi yawe yo gukuramwo amahera mu **Amasakoshi yo kwishura** hanyuma uyatunganye mu **Itegeko ryo kwishura**.





- Sigura amabwirizwa y’ibishushanyo y’ubucuruzi bwawe maze uhindure amapaji y’ukwishura, ama email, amakode ya QR n’amafagitire nk’uko uvyipfuza mu **Branding** menu.





- Gutunganya uburyo bwo kwishura mu mafaranga yemewe mu **Uburyo bwo kwishura**.



![payment-method](assets/fr/30.webp)



⚠️ Ukwihangana guhuye n’ijanisha ry’ugugabanya wemera ku giciro ca Invoice kugira ngo amahera yishuwe abonwe ko ari ay’ukuri. Niba umukiriya wawe ategerezwa kwishura amadolari 100 kandi ukwihangana kwawe ari 1%, ukwishura kwose kw’amadolari 99 kuzokwemeza amadolari 100 Invoice.
