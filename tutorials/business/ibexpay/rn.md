Gutuma Bitcoin ikoreshwa na bose bisigura kandi kwerekana akamaro kayo n’ingene ihuye neza n’ubucuruzi bwawe.  Ni ubuhe buryo bwiza bwo kwerekana ububasha bwa Bitcoin kuruta kukureka ugatoragura amahera kw’isi yose?  Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko turaraba IbexPay, urubuga rworoshe rugufasha gushiramwo bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura haba mu iduka ryawe ry’umubiri canke ku rubuga rwawe rw’ubudandaji.



## Gutangura na IbexPay



IbexPay ni urubuga rwateguwe na IBEX, umutsimvyi mu bikorwa vy’ibikorwa remezo vya Lightning. Kugira uronke konti, genda kuri IbexPay [urubuga rwemewe](https://www.ibexpay.io/) hanyuma ukande kuri buto "**Tangira**".



![start](assets/fr/01.webp)



Uzuza amakuru atandukanye yerekeye ubucuruzi bwawe, hanyuma uhitemwo igihugu washinzemwo iduka: iyo ntambwe irahambaye ku mafaranga azokoreshwa mu gutora amahera.



![register](assets/fr/02.webp)



Ikibabaje ni uko IbexPay itaboneka mu bihugu vyinshi vyo muri Afirika, ariko hariho uburyo bwinshi bwo kuyikoresha mu Buraya, muri Aziya no muri Amerika.



Iyo wemeje ko wiyandikishije, uzorungikirwa ubutumwa kuri e-mail kugira ngo ushobore gushiramwo ijambobanga rya konti yawe. Urasabwe gushinga ijambobanga rikomeye kugira ngo ukinge amakuru yawe y'ubucuruzi y'agaciro.



![passe](assets/fr/03.webp)



## Gutunganya konti yawe



IbexPay ni urubuga rutoyi, rugenda ruhinduka rugufasha gushinga no gutangura gutora amahera biciye kuri Bitcoin (onchain na Lightning) vyihuta cane.



Umaze kwinjira, dashboard iraguha inyishu y’amakuru y’amahera y’ubucuruzi bwawe.



Uhereye kuri iyi dashboard, ushobora :





- Raba amateka y'ibikorwa ku musi, ukwezi, umwaka canke igihe gisanzwe;
- Amateka yo gusohora hanze mu buryo bwa Excel (CSV) ku ntumbero z’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ibarabara;
- Raba umubare wose w’amasatoshi azorungikwa kuri aderesi yawe bwite;
- Suzuma amafaranga y’aho hantu angana n’ayo ari kuri konti yawe.




![dashboard](assets/fr/04.webp)




Mu **Settings**, ushireho amakuru yose akenewe ku bucuruzi bwawe: amaderesi yawe bwite, inyandiko z’ishirahamwe, n’ibindi.



Muri iki gice, urashobora gutunganya aderesi yawe yo kwakira bitcoin onchain n’aderesi yawe ya Lightning. Aya makuru abiri ni ngirakamaro kuko amasaha yose 24, IbexPay izorungika amafaranga yose ukoresha ku musi ataco uhinduye kuri aderesi yawe ya bitcoin onchain.



![addresses](assets/fr/05.webp)



Aya makuru azosuzumwa n’umugwi wa IbexPay. Ivyo bigenzurwa bimaze kurangira, urafise kandi uburenganzira bwo gusobanura ijana kw’ijana ry’amahera ya bitcoin wipfuza gutanga amahera ku giciro cose. Nk’akarorero, ku kwishura ama euro 20 biciye ku Lightning, iyo ijana ryawe rya Bitcoin rishizwe kuri 30%, IbexPay izorungika ayo mahera mu ma euro 6 angana n’ama bitcoins, hanyuma ama euro 14 aboneka mu giciro cawe ca FIAT.



![percent](assets/fr/06.webp)



IbexPay izokenera kandi amakuru yawe yo muri banki kugira ngo ushobore kwishura amahera yawe mu mafaranga y’igihugu. Tegura amakuru ya konti yawe ya banki kugira ngo ubone amahera yawe.



![bank](assets/fr/07.webp)



Niba ufise amaduka menshi, urashobora gukora konti kuri buri maduka yawe maze ugakurikirana amafaranga ukoresha utandukanye n’urubuga rumwe rwa IbexPay.



![accounts](assets/fr/08.webp)



Ushobora kandi gushinga umuyobozi atandukanye kuri buri konti yawe (amaduka). Kugira ngo ubikore, mu **Abarongozi**, rema umuyobozi w’iduka winjiza izina ry’umucungerezi w’iduka n’aderesi ya e-mail. Uwo mucungerezi azoca abona ubwo nyene ubutumire kugira ngo arongore iduka yashinzwe.



![manager](assets/fr/09.webp)



Mu **Konti**, ushobora guhuza umucungerezi n'iduka ukanda kuri buto **Gushinga umucungerezi**.



![assign](assets/fr/10.webp)



Hanyuma uhitemwo iduka (konti) n’umucungerezi yashinzwe, hanyuma ukande kuri buto y’ihuriro.



![link](assets/fr/11.webp)



## Bitcoin ibibanza vyo kwishura



Mu **BTPs** (*Ibikoresho vyo kwishura Bitcoin*), urashobora gukora urupapuro rw'aho ugurisha kugira ngo ukoreshe aho usohora amahera mw'iduka ryawe. Fyonda kuri buto **Ongera BTP**, hanyuma wuzuze :




- Izina ry'iherezo ;
- iduka rifitaniye isano n’iyi terminal;
- amafaranga yo mu karere azokoreshwa.



Igihe terminal yawe yo kwishura imaze kuremwa, fyonda ku nzira kugira ngo igusubire ku nzira yo kwishura.



![btps](assets/fr/12.webp)



![interface](assets/fr/13.webp)



Ubu ushobora gutangura gukora amafagitire ya Lightning abakiriya bawe n’abafatanyabikorwa bawe bashobora kwishura. Ufyonda ku kimenyetso ca IbexPay kiri ku rubuga, uronka kode ya QR ushobora gucapura kuri telefone yawe kugira uronke POS ya IbexPay ku gikoresho ico ari co cose.

## Kwinjira mu maduka yo kuri internet



IbexPay ntigarukira gusa ku bibanza vy’amaduka yawe. Mu **Integrations**, uzosangamwo ama platforms yose y’ubudandaji n’imiti yo kwishura ijana na IbexPay. Cane cane, ushobora guhuza konti yawe ya IbexPay biciye kuri :





- API ku rubuga rwawe rw’ubudandaji rwo kuri interineti ;
- WooUbucuruzi ;
- Gugura;
- Zapiriti;
- TiloIshura;
- na paji z’intererano za IbexPay.



![integrations](assets/fr/18.webp)
