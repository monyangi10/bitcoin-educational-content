Kraken ni kimwe mu bibanza bizwi cane kw’isi vy’amahera y’amahera Exchange. Itanga ibikorwa vyinshi bihuye n’amashirahamwe yipfuza kwinjiza Bitcoin mu gucunga amafaranga yabo.


Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzovuga ingene umuntu yokora konti y’ubudandaji ya Kraken, kugura no kugurisha amafaranga y’ibiceri, gucunga amafaranga y’ubucuruzi kubera intumbero z’ubuhinga bwo guharura amafaranga, no gushiramwo no gukura amayero n’ama bitcoins. Intumbero ni ukuguha ubumenyi bw’ishimikiro, waba uriko uriyumvira gushiramwo Bitcoin mu bijanye n’amahera yawe canke wemera Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura.


Niba ukunda ivyigwa vyo kwinjiza Bitcoin mu bucuruzi bwawe, ndagusavye kandi kumenya inyigisho yacu yuzuye y’ivyigwa ku bijanye n’ico ciyumviro:


https://planb.academy/courses/a804c4b6-9ff5-4a29-a530-7d2f5d04bb7a

## 1 - Gukora konti y'ishirahamwe rya Kraken


Intambwe ya mbere yo gukoresha Kraken mu gucunga amafaranga y’ishirahamwe ryawe ni, birumvikana, gukora konti. Aha, ntituzokora konti y’abantu ku giti cabo, ahubwo ni konti y’ishirahamwe, idatuma gusa umuntu yubahirizwa amategeko, ariko kandi itanga ibintu bidasanzwe nk’ivyoherezwa hanze.


Genda ku rubuga rwemewe [kraken.com](https://www.kraken.com/) ukande kuri buto ya "*Sign Up*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/01.webp)


Ku rupapuro rw'iyandikisha, hitamwo "*Ubucuruzi*" mu mahitamwo ya konti kugira ngo ugaragaze uguhingura konti y'ubucuruzi.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/02.webp)


Injira muri e-mail yawe y’umwuga Address maze uhitemwo ijambobanga ry’umutekano. Aya makuru amaze kwinjira, ukande kuri "*Create an account*" kugira ngo urangize kwiyandikisha.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/03.webp)


Uhejeje gutanga amakuru yawe, Kraken izokwoherereza e-mail yo gukora.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/04.webp)


Ugurure iyi e-mail ukande kuri "*Activate Account*" canke winjize code y'imirongo 6 yatanzwe kugira ngo ukoreshe konti yawe.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/05.webp)


## 2 - Kugenzura konti y'ishirahamwe


Uhejeje gukora konti yawe, uzokenera kurangiza uburyo bwo kugenzura KYC (*Know Your Customer*) ku konti z’amashirahamwe. Iyi ntambwe ituma Kraken ishobora kugenzura ko ishirahamwe ryawe ryemewe n’uko ryubahiriza amategeko agenga ivy’amahera.


Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Bandanya*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/06.webp)


Urasabwa kwinjiza amakuru asabwa yerekeye ishirahamwe ryawe.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/07.webp)


Uzokenera kandi gushiramwo inyandiko nyinshi zijanye n’ishirahamwe ryawe.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/08.webp)


Uhejeje guheza ibice vyose no kohereza ivyangombwa bikenewe, ukande kuri "*Submit*" kugira ngo wohereze ubusabe bwawe bwo kugenzura. Ivyo usaba bishobora gutwara indwi zibiri kugira ngo bikorwe. Kugira ngo ivyo bikorwa vyihute, nurabe ko utanga ivyangombwa vyose bisabwa mu ntango.


## 3 - Umutekano wa konti


Konti yawe imaze kugenzurwa, uzokwinjira kuri platform aho uzohabwa interfaces zibiri:




- Kraken**: Interface yoroshe itanga ibikorwa vy’ingenzi.
- Kraken Pro**: Interface iteye imbere ifise ibindi bikoresho, cane cane ivyo gucuruza.


Nimba intumbero yawe ari ugugura no kugurisha Bitcoin gusa, hitamwo Interface yoroshe. Uzokwama ufise uburenganzira bwo guhindukira ukaja kuri Interface iteye imbere mu nyuma nimba bikenewe.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/09.webp)


Mbere na mbere, birahambaye cane ko ucungera konti yawe kugira ngo ntihagire uwuyironka ata wemerewe. Uretse ijambobanga rikomeye ryashizweho igihe umuntu yiyandikisha, tuzoba turiko turashira mu ngiro uburyo bwo kwemeza umuntu kabiri. Ivyo bisigura ko igihe cose winjiye muri konti yawe ya Kraken, ntuzokenera ijambobanga ryawe gusa, ariko kandi uzokenera n’ikindi kintu co kwemeza, gishobora kuba urufunguzo rw’umubiri canke kode ivugwa n’ikoreshwa ry’ukwemeza.


Genda ku kimenyetso ca konti yawe ukande kuri "*Umutekano*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/10.webp)


Mu mahitamwo ya 2FA, ushobora guhitamwo gukoresha urufunguzo rw’umutekano w’umubiri nk’ikintu ca kabiri.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/11.webp)


Ushaka kumenya vyinshi ku buryo bwo gukoresha ubwo bwoko bw'igikoresho, raba inyigisho yacu yihariye:


https://planb.academy/tutorials/computer-security/authentication/security-key-61438267-74db-4f1a-87e4-97c8e673533e

Ubundi buryo ni ugukoresha porogaramu yo kwemeza ko umuntu ari uwuriho nka Google Authenticator canke Authy. Izo porogaramu generate ni kode y’imirongo 6 uzokenera kwinjira igihe cose winjiye.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/12.webp)


Kugira ngo ukoreshe ubwo buryo, scanner kode ya QR ukoresheje porogarama uhisemwo kuri telefone yawe, hanyuma winjize kode y’imirongo 6 yasohotse.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/13.webp)


Kubindi bisobanuro ku bikorwa vyo kwemeza, ushobora kandi kuraba iyi nyigisho:


https://planb.academy/tutorials/computer-security/authentication/authy-a76ab26b-71b0-473c-aa7c-c49153705eb7

Munsi, urashobora kandi gushika ku mirongo iteye imbere, harimwo ubushobozi bwo gushinga 2FA zitandukanye. Kraken itanga intumwa z’umutekano ziteye imbere biciye ku 2FA. Ivyo bigufasha gukora ingero zitandukanye za 2FA ku bikorwa bitandukanye, nk’ukwinjira, kwemeza amategeko no gukura amahera. Ivyo bituma nk’akarorero, umuntu ashobora guha umucungezi w’amakonte uburenganzira bwo gukora ibikorwa bimwebimwe ataco amurekuriye gukura amahera. Akira amahitamwo:




- **Urufunguzo rwa mbere** rukora nk’uburyo bwo gusubirana, bwo gukoreshwa iyo udashobora gushika ku buryo bwawe busanzwe bwa 2FA bwo kwinjira;
- **2FA yo gutanga amahera** isaba ko habaho ivyemezo vy’inyongera ku bikorwa vyose vyo gukura amahera canke igihe urema ikigega Address, gutyo ugakomeza umutekano w’amahera yawe;
- **2FA yo gucuruza** itegeka 2FA ku gikorwa cose gikorwa kuri konti.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/14.webp)


Aya mahitamwo ateye imbere aragufasha gusobanura ingero zitandukanye z’uburenganzira mw’ishirahamwe ryawe.


## 4 - Gushiramwo no gukura amafaranga y'amafranga


Konti yawe imaze kugenzurwa no gukingirwa, urashobora gushiramwo amahera mu ma euro (canke mu mafaranga y’aho uba). Ushobora kandi gukura amahera bivanye n’ivyo ukeneye mu bucuruzi bwawe.


Kugira ngo ushiremwo ama euro, genda ku gice ca "*Transfert*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/15.webp)


Hitamwo "*Euro*" mu gasandugu "*Asset*" maze wemeze kugira uronke amakuru akenewe kugira ngo ushobore kwishura kuri konti yawe ya banki y'ubucuruzi. Ushobora kandi guhitamwo kwishura ukoresheje ikarita y’inguzanyo.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/16.webp)


Kugira ngo ukure ama euro kuri konti yawe ya Kraken, ukurikize iyo nzira nyene ariko ukoreshe urupapuro rwa "*Withdraw*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/17.webp)


## 5 - Gushiramwo no gukura ama bitcoins


Kugira ngo ushire ama bitcoins muri konti yawe ya Kraken, subira mu gice ca "*Transfer*". Mu gasandugu ka "*Asset*", hitamwo "*BTC*" hanyuma wemeze ko uzoronka Address utegerezwa koherezako ama bitcoins avuye mu Wallet yawe.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/18.webp)


Kugira ngo ukure ama bitcoins kuri konti yawe ya Kraken uyashire muri Wallet, fyonda ku rubuga rwa "*Kura*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/19.webp)


Injira Wallet Address yawe, hanyuma wemeze igikorwa ukoresheje 2FA yawe.


## 6 - Gugura no kugurisha amafaranga y'ibiceri


Kraken itanga uburyo butandukanye bwo gucuruza, buri bumwe bugenewe gushitsa ivyo umuntu akeneye. Ku bijanye n’uburongozi bw’umwuga, buteye imbere, Kraken Pro ni ngirakamaro. Ku bijanye n’ugucuruza vyoroshe, Interface ya Kraken izohagije. Kugira ngo uhindure Interface, fyonda ku tudodo dutoduto turi hejuru iburyo bw’ibarabara:


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/20.webp)


Ku Interface y'imbere, kugira ngo ugure Bitcoin, tangura ukanda kuri "*Gura*" kuri paji y'intango.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/21.webp)


Injira amahera wipfuza kugura maze wemeze igikorwa. Niba 2FA itunganijwe kuri iki gikorwa, shiramwo kode y'imirongo 6 itangwa n'ikoreshwa ryawe ry'ukwemeza.


Kugira ngo ugurishe Bitcoin, kanda kuri "*Gurisha*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/22.webp)


Injira amahera azogurishwa maze wemeze ivy’ugucuruza.


Ushobora kandi Exchange bitcoins zawe ku zindi cryptocurrencies, nk’stablecoins, kugira ngo ugabanye ukuntu ushikirwa n’uguhinduka kwa Bitcoin. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku "*Guhindura*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/23.webp)


Hitamwo umubare, uhitemwo amafaranga y’ibanga wipfuza kuronka, hanyuma wemeze ko ukoresha.


Ku bijanye n'ivyo bikorwa, ushobora guhitamwo hagati y'ubwoko butandukanye bw'amategeko: *Isoko*, *Igipimo*, canke *Guhagarika-Guhomba*:




- Isoko**: Riremera kugura ubwo nyene kugiciro c’isoko kiriho ubu;
- Limit**: Iguha uburenganzira bwo kugaragaza igiciro co kugura, kandi itegeko rizoshirwa mu ngiro gusa iyo igiciro c'isoko kigeze kuri uwo mubare;
- Stop-Loss**: Igurisha ubwo nyene iyo igiciro kigeze ku rugero runaka.


## 7 - Uburongozi bw'ibikorwa n'ibara ry'amafaranga


Nk’ubucuruzi, birashoboka ko uzokenera kwohereza hanze amakuru y’ibikorwa vyawe vya Kraken kugira ngo ukoreshe ubuhinga bwo guharura amafaranga. Kraken itanga ibikoresho vyo kworohereza kwohereza hanze amateka y'ibikorwa kandi itanga ivyiyumviro vy'ido n'ido vya Wallet yawe.


Kugira ngo ubone mu buryo bworoshe amateka y'ibikorwa vyawe, genda kuri "*Ibikorwa*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/24.webp)


Kugira ngo ubone ibindi bisobanuro vy'ivyoherezwa hanze kubera intumbero z'ibarabara, genda ku mirongo iri munsi y'agace ka "*Inyandiko*".


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/25.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Rema dosiye yo kohereza hanze*" hanyuma uhitemwo ubwoko bw'ivyo kohereza hanze ushaka. Ku karorero, ngiye gutanga hanze amateka y'ibikorwa.


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/26.webp)


Ushobora rero gusobanura ibintu vyose bikenewe ku bijanye n'ivyo urimwo:




- Amakuru azoshirwa mu vyoherezwa hanze ;
- Amatariki yo kwihweza ;
- Exchange zibiri zibiri kugira ngo zishiremwo;
- Uburyo bwo kohereza hanze dosiye (PDF canke CSV).


![KRAKEN](assets/fr/27.webp)


## 8 - Gukoresha ibibazo vy'ishirahamwe


Bivanye n’intumbero n’imiterere y’ishirahamwe ryawe, ikoreshwa rya Kraken rishobora gutandukanywa.


### Gugura ama bitcoins ku mafaranga


**Intego:** Guhindura amafaranga y'ishirahamwe mu gushiramwo amafaranga muri Bitcoin.


**Intambwe zo gukurikira:**




- Gushiramwo amayero kuri Kraken kuri konti ya banki y’iyo sosiyete;
- Koresha ayo ma euro kugira ngo ugure Bitcoin;
- Gumana bitcoins ku rubuga canke uzikuremwo kugira ngo uzikingire mu kwizigama ;
- Amateka y’ibikorwa vyo gushora hanze nk’uko bisabwa.


### Kwemera Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura


**Intego:** Guha ishirahamwe ryawe ubushobozi bwo kwemera Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura ibintu canke ibikorwa vyawe.


**Intambwe zo gukurikira:**




- Gushiramwo uburyo bwo kwishura Bitcoin. Ku bucuruzi buto buto, porogarama yoroshe ya Wallet irashobora guhagije, canke ushobora guhitamwo inyishu zidasanzwe nka Swiss Bitcoin Pay canke BTCPay Server kugira ngo ushiremwo Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura ku rubuga rwawe canke aho ugurisha;
- Kwimurira amahera yaronse mu bitcoins kuri konti yawe ya Kraken;
- Bivanye n’ingene ukoresha amahera, ugurishe ama bitcoins waronse ku ma euro, uyazigame kugira ngo ushobore gushimwa muri kazoza, canke uhitemwo ivyo vyose hamwe;
- Gumana bitcoins ku rubuga canke uzikuremwo kugira ngo uzigumye. Ushobora kandi gukura ama euro kuri konti yawe ya banki;
- Amateka y’ibikorwa vyo gushora hanze nk’uko bisabwa.
