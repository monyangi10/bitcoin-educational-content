# Uburyo bwo kubara amafaranga


LNbits yuzuye ibikoresho vyinshi vyo kugenzura no gukoresha amahera yawe yinjira n’asohoka, guhuza webstore yawe canke mbere n’ibikoresho nka Hardware Wallet canke ATM wiyubakiye. Ubwoko bw'abakoresha burimwo:


- Abafise Wallet bashaka gukoresha LNbits nka Interface mu gucunga amafaranga yabo hamwe n’ibindi bikoresho vyayo.
- Abacuruzi bo kuri internet no hanze yayo canke abatanga ibikorwa bashaka kwemera kwishura Bitcoin onchain na Lightning Network.
- Abahinguzi bashaka kwubaka amaporogarama ya Lightning Network.
- Abakoresha amanode bashaka gushiramwo amanode yabo na sisitemu ya LNbits ku ntumbero z’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ibarabara.
- Abo bose barafise ivyo bakeneye bitandukanye. Twubaka LNbits mu buryo bw’ibice kugira ngo umukoresha wese ashobore gukoresha ibintu vyacu mu buryo bubereye wewe.


# Umuyobozi wa Wallet


LNbits ni uburyo bwo guharura amafaranga ku buntu kandi bufunguye - si umuyobozi w'ibihimba. Uburongozi bw’imirongo ni igice c’umurongo w’umuravyo uhuye na LNbits nk’inkomoko y’amahera nka LND canke c-lightning. Abakoresha bakuru canke abakoresha b’ubuyobozi muri sisitemu ya LNbits ni bo bajejwe gucunga uburyo bwo kuronka no gutunganya ibintu vyose vy’ubuhinga bw’ibarabara n’ivy’imbere.


LNbits ikora nka Interface hagati y’uwukoresha n’uruzitiro rwa Lightning, itanga uburyo bworoshe, bukoreshwa neza bwo gucunga no gukorana n’urubuga rwo kwishura.


Iyumvire LNbits nk’“inzego z’ubuhinga bwa wordpress” ku nzira yawe. Urubuga rworoshe gucunga, rushingiye ku vyo ushobora gufatanya ku bikorwa vyinshi.


Iyumvire LNbits nk’ubuhinga bwawe bwite bwo gucunga amafaranga y’ibanki. Node yawe itanga imihora yo kwishura biciyemwo kandi LNbits iraguha node kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha imiravyo irenga imwe Wallet node yawe izana. Ivyo bikoresho kugira ngo ntibibe ngombwa ko biba ivyawe. Reka tuvuge ko wewe, nk’umunyarugamba w’inzira ya LN, usanzwe ufise amahera ahagije n’amahera ahagije kandi ubu ushaka gutanga ubufasha bumwe bumwe bwa banki ya Bitcoin ku ncuti zawe, umuryango wawe, iduka ryawe bwite canke abandi bacuruzi basanzwe.


Uzobaha uburyo bworoshe bwo gufungura “konti ya banki” ku node yawe utagira uburenganzira bwo gushika ku bindi bipapuro vy’amahera biri ku node yawe no ku mafaranga yose y’node yawe, ariko igice cabo gusa. Node yawe (banki) ikora gusa nk’umurongozi w’ivy’ugutwara abantu ku bijanye n’amahera yabo (in/out).


ICIBUKIRO: amahera yose “abakiriya” bawe bashira muri konti zabo za banki za LNbits ku node yawe, azoca aja mu mihora yawe ya node LN. Ivyo bisigura ko mu vy’ukuri ari wewe nyen’ayo mahera vy’ukuri. Muzogira inshingano nini ku mahera yabo. Ntube umubisha ngo uhunge n’amahera, ntube umubisha ngo usabe amafaranga menshi. Turashaka gusambana n’aba fiat banksters, ntidusambanye (abakoresha Bitcoin).

Ishobora gukoreshwa itandukanye n’uruzitiro rwa BTC/LN (PC yo mu karere canke VPS yo kure) canke hamwe ku mashini imwe n’uruzitiro canke ikaba isanzwe ishizwe mu mashini ya porogarama y’uruzitiro rw’umugwi.

## Inkomoko z'amahera


LNbits si porogaramu yo gucunga amanode ariko ni uburyo bwo gucungera amafaranga bwibanda kuri LN hejuru y’inkomoko y’amahera ya LND canke CLN. Inyuma y’ugushiraho ubwa mbere ushobora gusura LNbits zawe kuri http://localhost:5000/.


Kugira ngo uhindure inkomoko y'amahera, genda kuri URL yawe y'umukoresha maze uhitemwo inkomoko y'amahera muri "Gucungera Serveri" canke uhindure dosiye .env uhindure `LNBITS_BACKEND_WALLET_CLASS` ku nkomoko ukeneye iyo ushizeho `adminUI=TRUE` muri `.env`.


Uzosanga dosiye .env muri dosiye yawe lnbits/ canke lnbits/apps/data mu kwagura itegeko ryo gutanga urutonde rw'amadosiye mu bubiko bwawe na `ls -a`.


Ushobora kandi gukenera gushiramwo izindi mpapuro canke gukora izindi ntambwe zo gutegura, ugahitamwo aho amahera wipfuza ava. Inyuma yo gusubira gutangura, setup yawe nshasha izokora.


Ni ayahe masoko y’amahera nokoresha ku LNbits?


LNbits ishobora gukora hejuru y’amasoko menshi y’amahera y’umuravyo. Ubu hariho ugushigikira CoreLightning, LND, LNbits, LNPay, OpenNode, n’ibindi vyongerwako ubudasiba.

Ni vyiza guhitamwo isoko ifise amahera meza n’urunganwe rwiza rufitaniye isano. Iyo ukoresheje LNbits ku bikorwa vya Leta, amahera y’abakoresha bawe ashobora gusa gutemba neza mu nzira zompi.

## Inyigisho z'inyongera / gutorera umuti ingorane


Aha hariho amabwirizwa y’inyongera mu gihe woba uyakeneye. Fyonda ku mwampi kugira ngo wagure insobanuro.


### Uwo kwica 🚨


Hariho ibikoko vyinshi biteye akaga muri iyi misi iheze atari mu kirere cose gusa ariko no muri LNbits ku buryo twahisemwo kugira ico tubikozeko. Ubu ushobora guhitamwo kwinjira mu ngabisho no/canke gufata ingingo itaziguye, igihe ubugoyagoye canke ikibazo coshobora gutuma amahera atakaza kigasubira kubaho.


![killswitchn](assets/lnbits-killswitch.webp)


Iyo uhinduye void-Wallet ubwoko bwose bw'abakoresha ku nkuru buzobona ibendera ry'umuhondo aho wosanga "LNbits iri muri Beta" itangazo iruhande y'insanganyamatsiko/ururimi gushika iburyo - kandi ni ikimenyetso kigaragara cane, ko hari ikintu cabaye. Raba server-tab yawe nshasha yerekanwa muri Green mu gice c'ibubamfu c'idirisha.


Bigenda gute ? Igihe killswitch ikoreshejwe, ububiko bw’ibanga bwa github buboneka gusa ku mugwi w’ishimikiro wa LNbits buzosuzumwa mu kiringo c’iminota X (ishobora gusobanurwamwo). Iyo ikibazo gishobora guhungabana gisohotse muri ubu bubiko, gikora nk'ikimenyetso gitera killswitch ku bikoresho vyose vyashizweho kandi kigahindura lnbits yawe kugira ngo ukoreshe ubusa Wallet. Niba ibicu vyavuyeho kandi warashizeho ivugurura ry’umutekano ushobora gushiramwo inkomoko y’amahera yawe ku node yawe, Wallet canke ico uriko urakoresha cose kandi biciye mu gice ca Manage Server. Iyi wiki ifise igice kivuga ku guhindura inkomoko y'amahera iyo utazi ico wotunganya.



### Itandukaniro hagati y'umuyobozi n'umukoresha


UI y'Umuyobozi wa LNbits ishobora kugufasha guhindura imiterere ya LNbits biciye ku nzira y'imbere ya LNbits. Izimye ku buryo busanzwe kandi igihe ca mbere ushizeho umuhinduzi w'ibidukikije `LNBITS_ADMIN_UI=ukuri` muri dosiye `.env`, amagenamiterere aratangura kandi azokoreshwa. Kuva ng'aho, amasetingi ashingiye ku rutonde rw'amakuru aho gukoresha ayo muri dosiye .env.


### Ukoresha akomeye


Na Admin UI twashizeho super user afise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha server kugira ngo ashobore guhindura ama settings ashobora gutuma server ihungabana canke akayigira idashobora kwishura biciye ku frontend na api, nk’uguhindura inkomoko y’amahera. Ukoresha super abikwa gusa imbere mu mbonerahamwe y'imiterere y'urutonde rw'amakuru. Inyuma y'aho ama settings "asubiwemwo" agasubira gutangura, haca haremwa umukoresha mushasha. Twongeyeko kandi umusharizi w’inzira za API kugira ngo tugenzure ko hariho umukoresha w’agaciro. ID yayo ntiyigera yoherezwa kuri api na frontend kandi ironka gusa bool (yes/oya) iyo uri super user canke atarivyo.


Uwukoresha cane ni we wenyene yemerewe gukora brrrr satoshis ku ma wallet atandukanye biciye ku gice ca "Top Up".


Ushobora kandi gushiramwo umukoresha mukuru biciye kuri webhook ku yindi serivisi iyo iremwe. Ibindi bisobanuro hano


Mu frontend uzosanga kandi ubushobozi bwo guhindura ishusho y'iduka yerekanwa kuri paji ya "create Wallet" mu gufungura igice ca Manage Server ugahitamwo Insanganyamatsiko -> Ikimenyetso c'umuntu ku giti ciwe.


### Abakoresha


Impinduka y'ibidukikije: `LNBITS_ADMIN_USERS`, urutonde rw'ibimenyetso vy'abakoresha bitandukanijwe n'agacamuzingi. Abakoresha b'ubuyobozi barashobora guhindura amategeko muri admin ui - uretse amategeko y'inkomoko y'amahera, kuko ivyo vyosaba ko server isubira gutangura kandi bishobora gutuma server idashobora gukoreshwa. Kandi barafise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ivyungura vyose vyabo muri `LNBITS_ADMIN_EXTENSIONS`.


### Abakoresha bemerewe


Impinduka y'ibidukikije: `LNBITS_BEMEREWE_ABAKORESHA`, urutonde rw'indangamuntu z'abakoresha zitandukanijwe n'agacamuzingi. Mu gusobanura abo bakoresha LNbits ntizosubira gukoreshwa na bose. Abakoresha n'abarongozi bonyene ni bo bashobora gushika ku rubuga rwa LNbits.

#### SQLite ku kwimuka kwa nyuma


Niba usanzwe ufise LNbits zishizweho kandi zikora ku rutonde rw'amakuru rwa SQLite turagusavye cane kwimukira kuri postgres nimba uriko urategura gukoresha LNbits ku rugero.


Hariho script irimwo ishobora gukora migration bitagoranye. Ukeneye kuba ufise Postgres imaze gushirwamwo kandi hakwiye kubaho ijambobanga ry’ukoresha (raba ubuyobozi bwo gushiramwo Postgres haruguru). Ikindi, instance yawe ya LNbits ikeneye gukora rimwe kuri postgres kugira ngo ushire mu ngiro urutonde rw'amakuru Schema imbere y'uko ukwimuka gushobora gukora:


```
# STOP LNbits

# add the database connection string to .env 'nano .env' LNBITS_DATABASE_URL=
# postgres://<user>:<password>@<host>/<database> - alter line bellow with your user, password and db name
LNBITS_DATABASE_URL="postgres://postgres:postgres@localhost/lnbits"
# save and exit

# START LNbits
# STOP LNbits
poetry run python tools/conv.py
# or
make migration
```

Nizere ko ubu vyose bikora kandi bikaja kwimuka... Subira utangure LNbits maze ugenzure nimba vyose bikora neza.

#### Ikosa ry'urubuga

Naronse "ikosa rya https", ikosa ry'urubuga" canke ibindi igihe nariko ndapima QR</summary>


Inkuru mbi, iri ni ikosa ry’inzira rishobora kuba rifise imvo nyinshi cane. Ubanze usuzume LNURL ya QR ́s ukoresheje [Igikoresho co Gusobanura Umuravyo](https://umuravyodecoder.com/) nimba ushobora kubona ikintu kidasanzwe muri co. Reka tugerageze ingorane nkeyi zishoboka n ́umuti wazo.


LNbits iriko irakora biciye kuri Tor gusa, ntushobora kuyifungura ku rubuga rwa bose nka lnbits.yourdomain.com



- Kubera ko ushaka ko setup yawe iguma gutyo, fungura LNbits yawe Wallet ukoresheje .onion URI hanyuma wongere uyireme. Muri ubwo buryo QR ivugwa kugira ngo ishobore gushikwako biciye kuri iyi .onion URI rero biciye kuri tor gusa. Ntukore generate iyo QR ivuye kuri .local URI, kuko ntizoshobora gushikirwa kuri internet - gusa ivuye mu home-LAN yawe.
- Ugurure porogarama yawe ya LN Wallet wakoresheje mu gucapura iyo QR kandi ubu ukoresheje tor (raba amasetingi ya porogarama ya Wallet). Niba app idatanga tor, ushobora gukoresha Orbot (Android) aho gukoresha. Raba igice co gushiramwo kugira ngo ubone amabwirizwa arambuye y'ingene wofungura LNbits yawe ku clearnet/https.



#### Kubuza abandi gukora ama wallet kuri LNbits zanje


Iyo ukoresheje LNbits zawe muri clearnet mu bisanzwe umuntu wese arashobora generate a Wallet kuri yo. Kubera ko amafaranga y’urudodo rwawe aboshe kuri izo wallets woshobora gushaka kubibuza. Hari uburyo bubiri bwo kubigira:


Gutegura abakoresha bemerewe n'ivyagutse muri dosiye `.env` ([raba akarorero ka env hano](https://github.com/lnbits/lnbits/blob/main/.env.akarorero)). Ivyo bikora gusa iyo ukoresheje `adminUI=FALSE` muri .env, ahandi ho ukeneye kubikora mu gice ca Gucungera Serveri -> Abakoresha -> Abakoresha Bemerewe. Abandi bose ntibazokwemererwa inyuma.

## Wallet-URL yasubiwemwo


### Wallet ku nkuru y'iyerekanwa.lnbits


Igihe cose ubike kopi ya Wallet-URL yawe, Export2phone-QR canke LNDhub kugira ngo ukoreshe amasakoshi yawe bwite ahantu hatagira inkomanzi. LNbits NTIZISHOBORA kugufasha kuzigarura iyo zitakaye.


### Wallet ku nzira yawe y'amahera

Igihe cose ubike kopi ya Wallet-URL yawe, Export2phone-QR canke LNDhub kugira ngo ukoreshe amasakoshi yawe bwite ahantu hatagira inkomanzi. Ushobora kubona abakoresha bose ba LNbits na Wallet-IDs mu nzira yawe y'umuyobozi w'abakoresha ba LNbits canke mu rutonde rwawe rwa sqlite. Kugira ngo uhindure canke usome urutonde rw’amakuru rwa LNbits, genda muri dosiye ya LNbits /data maze urondere dosiye yitwa sqlite.db. Ushobora kuyifungura no kuyihindura ukoresheje excel canke ukoresheje SQL-Editor yihariye nka [umucukumbuzi wa SQLite](https://mucukumbuzi wa SQLite.org/).


Kandi ushobora gutera ama wallets biciye kuri CLI maze ukabona Wallet yose iri mu rutonde rwawe.


```
cd ~/app-data/lnbits/data
sqlite3 database.sqlite3
.dump wallets
```


Igisohoka kizosa n'iki


```
INSERT INTO wallets VALUES('f8a43fc363ea428db5c53b3559935f1f','NAME OF WALLET','1280ff5910a9c485a782a2376f338b6c','33b95b099ce848e3b484124373f681e5','2cca208ae6d94d438227b9487ff216f9');
```

kandi ushaka gushiramwo ibi bipimo muri url nk'iyi


```
https://your.lnbits.com/wallet?usr=1280ff5910a9c485a782a2376f338b6c&wal=f8a43fc363ea428db5c53b3559935f1f
```


Aho usubiriza f8a43fc363ea428db5c53b3559935f1f n'agaciro kaza imbere y'izina (mu karorero kacu f8a43fc363ea428db5c53b3559935f1f) na 1280ff591035a ni umukoresha wawe kandi akwiye kuba agaciro karekanwa inyuma y'izina. Kugira ngo ureke sqlite3 ushiremwo


```
.quit
```

##### Biciye ku nzira y’uguhindura hanze.


Abandi bakoresha badashobora gukoresha LNb yawe bashobora gukoresha ubuhinga bwo guhindura nk'[Boltz](https://boltz.Exchange/), [FixedFloat](https://fixedfloat.com/), [Ivyuma vy'amadayimoni](zitps://swap.diamondhands.technology/) canke [Zhg). Uburyo bwo kubikora burasanzwe: umukoresha yohereza onchain btc ku nzira y’uguhinduranya maze agatanga LNURL / LN Invoice iva kuri LNbits nk’aho uguhinduranya kuja.


##### Gukoresha umurongo wa Onchain na Boltz LNbits.


Zirikana ko iyi ari Wallet itandukanye, atari LN btc iyo igereranywa na LNbits nk'"Wallet yawe" ku nzira yawe y'amahera ya LN. Iyi Wallet ishobora gukoreshwa no guhindura LN btc ku (nk'akarorero, igikapu cawe) ukoresheje LNbits Boltz canke Deezy. Iyo ufise webshop ihuye na LNbits zawe ku bijanye n’ugutanga LN, birafasha cane gukura Sats yose kuva kuri LN ikaja mu onchain. Ivyo bituma haba umwanya mwinshi mu mihora yawe ya LN kugira ngo ushobore kwakira Sats nshasha.


Uburyo bwo gukora ku batagira Bitcoin Hardware Wallet:



- Koresha Electrum canke Sparrow wallet kugira ngo ureme Wallet nshasha y’uruzitiro maze ubike seed y’inyuma ahantu hatagira inkomanzi.
- Genda kuri Wallet amakuru maze ukope xpub.
- Genda kuri LNbits - Ukwaguka kwa Onchain maze ureme Watch-only wallet nshasha n'iyo xpub.
- Genda kuri LNbits - Ukwaguka kwa Tipjar maze ureme Tipjar nshasha. Hitamwo kandi uburyo bwo gukoresha uruzitiro uretse LN Wallet.
- Ivyo bishobora gukorwa - Genda kuri LNbits - SatsPay maze ureme igiciro gishasha ca onchain btc. Ushobora guhitamwo hagati ya onchain na LN canke vyose bibiri. Izoheza ireme Invoice ishobora gusangira.
- Ivyo ushobora guhitamwo - Niwakoresha LNbits zawe zihujwe na paji ya Wordpress + Woocommerce, umaze gukora/guhuza Watch-only wallet n’iduka ryawe rya LN btc Wallet, umukiriya azogira uburyo bwompi bwo kwishura ku rubuga rumwe.

## Ibikoresho n'inyubako na LNbits


LNbits ifise ubwoko bwose bw'ibikoresho vyo gukora porogarama no gufatanya n'ibikoresho vyinshi bitandukanye ku bikorwa vyinshi.


Igihe uri mushasha mu kwubaka tangura n’ibi [ibiganiro vya MakerBits](amasanamu) bivuye kuri Ben Arc ku bijanye no kwubaka ibikoresho bishingiye kuri LNbits.


### BIHAMBAYE:


- LNbits ikora ishingiye ku nzira ya LNURL ibisabwa bifise akamaro mu buryo bubiri: nk'ihuriro rya https:// clearnet (nta vyemezo vy'ukwisinya vyemewe) canke nk'ihuriro ry'igitunguru http:// v2/v3. Kugira ngo ushireho ibikorwa vya LNbits nk’amakode ya QR ya LNURLp/w canke amakarita ya NFC, ashobora gukoreshwa mu mashamba, uzokenera gufungura LNbits kuri clearnet (https).
- Koresha gusa DATA-Cables kugira ngo ukoreshe esp32 yawe. Si intsinga zose zishigikira amakuru uretse no guha ubushobozi esp. Ntuzoba uwa mbere iyo umugozi waje na esp ari uwukoresha ubushobozi gusa
- Raba neza ko udakoresha USB-Hub iri kumwe n’ibindi bikoresho bifatanye. Ivyo bishobora gutuma habaho ingaruka zidasanzwe ziri Hard zo gukosora (nk'akarorero kudatangura canke guhagarika).
- Kugira ngo ushobore gushitsa imigambi ya esp ukoresheje MacOS uzokenera umushoferi w’ikiraro ca UART. Niba ufise ingorane n’umushoferi kuri Mac canke Linux, ushobora kuronka izo hano canke, nimba hariho TTGO Display iri muri iyi. Niba uri kuri windows kandi ufise ingorane zo gufatanya urabe ko ushobora gukuraho version ya kera 11.1.0 kuko iyo nshasha ntikora! Ushobora kandi kubona umurongo w'uruhererekane hano kugira ngo usuzume ubufatanye bwawe - ushireho baudrate 115200.
- Naho ari uburyo bwiza gukoresha Platform.io (nk'akarorero, ivyifashe bishirwaho ubwavyo) turahimiriza gukoresha Arduino ku muntu wese mushasha mu kwubaka.
- TT-Go Display S3: Ibara ry’uruzitiro rwa filime y’ukurinda igicapo rikubwira neza neza umugenzuzi (ST7735_redtab, ST7735_blacktag, ST7735_greetab, greentab128, ..) yakoreshejwe mu kuyikora. Bigumye kugira ngo bishobore gukosora iyo wikoreye porogarama kandi igicapo ntikigaragaza neza ibishushanyo, nk’akarorero. amabara atari yo, amashusho y'indorerwamo, canke amapixels atari yo ku nkengera.
