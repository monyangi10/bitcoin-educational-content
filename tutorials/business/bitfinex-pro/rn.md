Bitfinex yashinzwe mu mwaka w’2012, ni imwe mu nzira za mbere za Bitcoin na Altcoin Exchange. Mu ntango yari yibanze ku masoko ya P2P Bitcoin, iyo nzira yaciye yihuta kwagura ibikorwa vyayo kugira ngo ishiremwo ubudandaji bw’amahera, amahera ya P2P, ubudandaji bw’ibintu biva ku bindi, n’isoko rya OTC ("*over-the-counter*") ry’ubudandaji bw’amahera menshi.


Ubu, Bitfinex ni urubuga rwuzuye, rushoboza kugura bitcoins mu buryo bworoshe no gukoresha ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji biteye imbere (ubudandaji bwa kera, ibiva ku bindi, OTC, inguzanyo, n’ibindi) n’ibikoresho vyo gucungera ingorane. Iraboneka ku rubuga, kandi ku bijanye n’ugucuruza vyoroshe, hariho kandi porogarama yoroshe gukoresha yo kuri telefone ngendanwa.


Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzovuga ingene umuntu yokora konti y’ubudandaji kuri Bitfinex, kugura no kugurisha ama bitcoins, gucunga amafaranga y’ubudandaji kubera intumbero z’ubuhinga bwo guharura amafaranga, no gushiramwo no gukura ama euros n’ama bitcoins. Intumbero ni ukuguha ubumenyi bw’ishimikiro, waba utegura kwinjiza Bitcoin mu nzira y’amahera yawe canke wemera Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura.


Niba ukunda ivyerekeye gushiramwo Bitcoin mu bucuruzi bwawe, ndagusavye kandi kumenya inyigisho yacu yuzuye y’ivyigwa ku bijanye n’ico ciyumviro:


https://planb.network/courses/a804c4b6-9ff5-4a29-a530-7d2f5d04bb7a

## 2 - Kugenzura konti y'ishirahamwe


Bitfinex ikoresha uburyo bwo kugenzura (KYC) bujanye n’amategeko ariho ubu. Mu buryo bwa "Basic", ntibishoboka gushiramwo amafaranga, bikaba ari ngombwa ko umuntu aronka n'imiburiburi urugero rwo kugenzura "Intermediate", canke mbere "Full" iyo bikenewe.


Iyo konti yawe imaze gushirwaho, idirisha rishobora gusohoka rikwiye kukubwira ko wosuzuma konti yawe. Fyonda kuri "*Suzuma*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/07.webp)


Niba iri dirisha ritabonetse, genda kuri profile yawe hejuru iburyo bwa Interface, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Verification*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/08.webp)


Munsi ya "*Ubwoko bwa konti*", hitamwo "*Ishirahamwe*". Mu gihe canje, ndiko ndasubiramwo "*Igihugu*", rero mpitamwo "*Igihugu*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/09.webp)


Uzuza intambwe mu gutanga:




- Amakuru yerekeye ishirahamwe (izina ry’ishirahamwe, amakuru yo guhamagara, urwego rw’ubudandaji, n’ibindi) ;
- Ivyangombwa vy’amategeko (ingingo z’ishirahamwe, igice ca Kbis, urutonde rw’abarongozi n’abafise imigabane);
- Amakuru ya KYC ku muntu wese afise inyungu canke umuyobozi (ivyangombwa vy’akaranga, ikimenyamenya ca Address, n’ibindi).


Igihe ubusabe bwawe bumaze kwuzuzwa no gushikirizwa, bishobora gutwara imisi myinshi kugira ngo urubuga ruvyemeze kugira ngo rusuzumwe ubucuruzi. Muri ico kiringo, amafaranga azobikwa azobuzwa by’igihe gito.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/10.webp)


## 3 - Intangamarara yihuta ku Bitfinex Interface.


Uhejeje kwinjira, uzobona umurongo wo kugenderamwo hejuru ya Interface ufise: "*Ubucuruzi*", "*Ibikomoka*", "*Iterambere*", "*OTC*", "*P2P*", "*Wallet*", n'ibindi.




- Ubucuruzi**: "*classic*" isoko aho ushobora gushiramwo amategeko yo kugura no kugurisha amafaranga y'amahera (harimwo Bitcoin);
- OTC**: Over-The-Counter Serivisi yo gucuruza ibitabu vyinshi ataco uhinduye n’uwundi mukinyi, hanze y’ibitabu vy’amategeko ya bose;
- Infashanyo**: Akarere kagenewe gutanga inguzanyo n’amahera y’inyuma;
- Ivyimburwa**: Igice c’ivy’ivy’imbere (ivy’imbere, n’ibindi), bigenewe abacuruzi bazi utuntu n’utundi;
- P2P**: Iguha uburenganzira bwo kugura canke kugurisha ama cryptos ku bandi bakoresha ku buryo bw’urunganwe.


Ku gukoresha bisanzwe (kugura/kugurisha bitcoins, gushiramwo/gukura n'ugucungera amahera), uzokoresha cane cane urupapuro rwa "*Gucuruza*", hamwe n'ibice "*Wallet*", "*Gushiramwo*" na "*Gukura*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/11.webp)


Ikindi ciza c’umurongo w’Ishirahamwe ni ubushobozi bwo gukora amakonti mato mato. Ivyo bisigura ko ushobora guha abakoresha benshi uburenganzira bwo gukoresha izo konti ntoya, imwe yose ikaba ifise uburenganzira bwihariye (gusoma gusa, gucuruza, gushiramwo amahera gusa, n’ibindi).


## 4 - Gushiramwo no gukura amayero (fiat)


Kugira ngo ushire ama euro kuri konti yawe ya Bitfinex Corporate, genda ku rutonde rwa "*Gushiramwo*" ruri mu rutonde rwa "*Wallet*" ruri hejuru kuri Interface.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/12.webp)


Hitamwo "*Ugurungika amahera muri banki*" canke "*Ikarata y'inguzanyo/y'inguzanyo*" kugira ngo ushire amahera mu ma euro (canke ayandi mafaranga yose).


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/13.webp)


Hitamwo amafaranga y'amahera azorungikwa, nk'akarorero. amayero. Niba ukoresha gusa ibikorwa vy'ishimikiro "*Ubucuruzi*", kanda kuri "*Exchange*". Werekane kandi amahera wipfuza gushiramwo n’igihugu ca banki yawe y’ubudandaji.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/14.webp)


Gukura amahera kuri konti yawe ya banki y’ubudandaji uyishire kuri konti ya banki yerekanwa na Bitfinex.


Kugira ngo ukure amahera, uburyo bwo kubikora ni nk'ubwo: genda kuri "*Withdraw*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/15.webp)


Fyonda kuri "*Ivyoherezwa muri banki*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/16.webp)


Hitamwo amafaranga y'amafiat wipfuza gukura, konti uzokurwa kuri Bitfinex ("*Exchange*" nimba ukoresha gusa ibiranga ivy'ishimikiro) n'amahera uzokura.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/17.webp)


Bitfinex ishobora gusaba kwemeza konti yawe ya banki imbere yo kwemera ko yoherezwa, kubera imvo zo kwubahiriza amategeko.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/18.webp)


Iyo nzira imaze gutangura, Bitfinex izorungika ayo mahera kuri konti yawe ya banki.

![BITFINEX](assets/fr/20.webp)

![BITFINEX](assets/fr/21.webp)

## 6 - Gugura no kugurisha amafaranga y'ibiceri kuri Bitfinex .


Bitfinex itanga uburyo butandukanye bwo gucuruza. Kugira ngo ukoreshe neza, hitamwo isoko ry'imbere, rizwi kandi nka "*Ubucuruzi*" canke "*Exchange*". Aha, urashobora gushiramwo amategeko yo kugura canke kugurisha ku giciro c’isoko, canke ugashinga igiciro c’iherezo.


Mu rutonde rwo hejuru, kanda kuri "*Ugucuruza*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/25.webp)


Hitamwo "*BTC/EUR*" pair nimba ushaka kugura canke kugurisha BTC mu Exchange ku ma euro, nk'akarorero.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/26.webp)


Interface yerekana urutonde rw’ibiciro hagati, igitabu c’amategeko kiri hasi, n’igice co kwandika amategeko kiri ibubamfu. Mu gice co kwinjiza amategeko, ushobora guhitamwo hagati y'itegeko rya "*Isoko*" (rishirwa mu ngiro ubwo nyene kugiciro ciza kuruta ibindi vyose kiriho) canke amategeko ya "*Igipimo*" (urasobanura igiciro). Erekana igitigiri ca BTC uzogura canke ugurisha, canke uhitemwo ijana kw’ijana ry’amahera usigaje. Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Gura*" kugira ugure canke "*Gurisha*" kugira ugurishe.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/27.webp)


Ushobora kubona amateka y'amategeko yawe yashitse mu gice co hasi ca Interface.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/28.webp)


## 7 - Amateka y'ibikorwa vyoherezwa hanze n'ibara ry'amafaranga


Kubera intumbero z’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubuhinga, uzokenera gutanga amakuru y’ivyo ukoresheje (ivyo uguze, ivyo ugurisha, ivyo ubitse, ivyo ukuye). Bitfinex itanga igikoresho co gutanga raporo yuzuye. Fyonda ku kimenyetso c'umwirondoro wawe kiri hejuru iburyo bwa Interface, hanyuma ku rutonde rwa "*Reports*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/29.webp)


Ibubamfu, ushobora guhitamwo ubwoko bw'amakuru azorungikwa hanze. Nk'akarorero, guhitamwo "*Ubucuruzi*" bizokuronsa uburenganzira bwo gushika ku bucuruzi bwawe bwose.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/30.webp)


Sigura igihe wipfuza mu gasandugu "*Itariki*" kandi, nimba bikenewe, ushire ubushakashatsi bwawe ku babiri babiri canke benshi biciye ku mwanya "*Ikimenyetso*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/31.webp)


Kugira ngo urungike aya makuru hanze, fyonda ku buto "*Export*".


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/32.webp)


Suzuma amaparametere, hanyuma wemeze ivyoherejwe hanze.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/33.webp)


Raporo izorungikwa kuri Address ifatanye na konti yawe ya Bitfinex, nk’idosiye ya CSV.


![BITFINEX](assets/fr/34.webp)


Iyo dosiye imaze gusohoka hanze, urashobora kuyishira muri porogarama yawe y’ivy’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubuhinga canke ukayirungikira umucungamari wawe.


## 9 - Gukoresha ibibazo vy'ishirahamwe


Bivanye n’intumbero n’imiterere y’ishirahamwe ryawe, ikoreshwa rya Bitfinex rishobora guhinduka.


### Gugura ama bitcoins ku mafaranga


**Intego:** Guhindura amafaranga y'ishirahamwe mu gushiramwo amafaranga muri Bitcoin.


**Intambwe zo gukurikira:**




- Gushiramwo amayero kuri Bitfinex kuri konti ya banki y’ishirahamwe;
- Koresha ayo ma euro kugira ngo ugure Bitcoin;
- Gumana bitcoins ku rubuga canke uzikuremwo kugira ngo uzikingire mu kwizigama ;
- Amateka y’ibikorwa vyo gushora hanze nk’uko bisabwa.


### Kwemera Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura


**Intego:** Guha ishirahamwe ryawe ubushobozi bwo kwemera Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura ibintu canke ibikorwa vyawe.


**Intambwe zo gukurikira:**




- Gushiramwo uburyo bwo kwishura Bitcoin. Ku bucuruzi buto buto, porogarama yoroshe ya Wallet irashobora guhagije, canke ushobora guhitamwo inyishu zidasanzwe nka Swiss Bitcoin Pay canke BTCPay Server kugira ngo ushiremwo Bitcoin nk’uburyo bwo kwishura ku rubuga rwawe canke aho ugurisha;
- Kwimurira amahera waronse mu bitcoins kuri konti yawe ya Bitfinex;
- Bivanye n’ingene ukoresha amahera, ugurishe ama bitcoins waronse ku ma euro, uyazigame kugira ngo ushobore gushimwa muri kazoza, canke uhitemwo ivyo vyose hamwe;
- Gumana bitcoins ku rubuga canke uzikuremwo kugira ngo uzigumye. Ushobora kandi gukura ama euro kuri konti yawe ya banki;
- Amateka y’ibikorwa vyo gushora hanze nk’uko bisabwa.
