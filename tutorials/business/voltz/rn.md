Iciyumviro c’urubuga rwa Voltz cavuye mu mugwi w’abakoresha ama bitcoin bashaka gutanga ubuhinga bwabo bwite bwa bitcoin wallet. Ico vyavuyemwo ni ibikorwa remezo vyuzuye vyo kwemera bitcoin ku masoko. Muri iyi nyigisho, turagutwara mu rugendo rwa Voltz n’ubushobozi bwo gushiramwo bitcoin iyo nzira ishobora gutanga.



## Gutangura na Voltz



Uretse kuba Lightning wallet y’ububiko ishobora kugufasha kohereza, kwakira no kwishura ku musi ku musi, Voltz ni urubuga rwuzuye rutanga ivyungura vyinshi vyo gushiramwo bitcoin nk’aho ugurisha canke isoko mu bucuruzi bwawe.



Genda ku rubuga rwa [Voltz](https://www.voltz.com/ru) maze ukore konti ukanda kuri buto ya "Gerageza ubu".



![voltz](assets/fr/01.webp)



![login](assets/fr/02.webp)



⚠️ Ni vyiza gushinga ijambobanga rikomeye ry’inyuguti n’imibare kugira ngo wongere amahirwe yawe yo guhangana n’ibitero vy’ubukazi, kandi ugenzure ko vy’ukuri uri ku rubuga rwemewe rwa Voltz kugira ngo wuzuze amakuru yawe yo kwinjira kugira ngo wirinde ubuhendanyi.



Igikoresho ca Voltz gisa cane n’ico ku rubuga rwa LnBits.

https://planb.academy/tutorials/business/others/lnbits-server-6a406046-3cef-4a64-a82b-8d8f0f82a192

Urubuga ruragufasha gucunga neza ibitabo vyinshi. Ku mburabuzi, iyo wiyandikishije, uhita ugira igitabu c’umuravyo. Ariko rero, urashobora kwongerako ibindi bitabo.

### Ugutwara



Voltz ntigarukira ku rubuga gusa: mu gice ca **Ukwinjira kuri telefone ngendanwa**, ushobora gufatanya Voltz wallet yawe ikora n'ibikorwa nka Zeus canke Blue Wallet.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/mobile/zeus-embedded-c67fa8bb-9ff5-430d-beee-80919cac96b9

https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/mobile/blue-wallet-2f4093da-6d03-4f26-8378-b9351d0dbc90

Kugira ngo ubikore, ukeneye gushiramwo no gukoresha **LndHub** ku rubuga.



![lndhub](assets/fr/04.webp)



Hitamwo igitabu cawe ca Voltz gikora, bivanye n’uburenganzira wipfuza gutanga, ushireko kode ya QR ibereye.




- Icode QR y’inyemezabuguzi ishobora gusa gusoma amateka y’ibikorwa vyawe n’inyemezabuguzi nshasha za generate.
- Ico gikoresho ca admin QR kigufasha kubona amateka, amafagitire ya generate no kwishura amafagitire ya Lightning.



![qrs](assets/fr/05.webp)



Muri iyi nyigisho, dufatanya Voltz wallet yacu n’ikoreshwa rya Blue Wallet.



![connect](assets/fr/06.webp)



Igihe igitabu cacu kizoba carahujwe, ibikorwa vyose bizokorwa bizohuzwa hagati ya Blue Wallet na Voltz. Nk’akarorero, iyo generate invoice kuri Blue Wallet, uba ufise kandi amateka kuri Voltz.



![sync](assets/fr/07.webp)



Mu gice ca **Itunganywa ry'ibitabo**, uzosangamwo ibintu bikeyi nk'uguhindura izina ry'ibitabo n'amafaranga wipfuza kwakira amahera yawe.



![config](assets/fr/08.webp)



### Ukwagura



Kimwe mu bintu bidasanzwe vy’urubuga rwa Voltz ni uko rushobora gukoreshwa mu buryo butandukanye, bikaba bigufasha gukoresha ama extensions ukeneye. Urutonde rw'ivyagutse rushobora kuboneka muri **Ivyagutse**.



![extensions](assets/fr/09.webp)



Muri ivyo bikoresho harimwo TPoS, ni ikibanza co kugurisha ushobora gukoresha kugira ngo ubike urutonde rw’ibintu no gutora amahera y’abaguzi bawe.



![tpos](assets/fr/10.webp)



Kuva ku kibanza co kugurisha, ushobora :




- Uronke urubuga ushobora gusangira n’abaguzi bawe n’abafatanyabikorwa bawe;
- Gucungera urutonde rw’ibintu;
- Gutanga amafagitire y’umuravyo;
- Ivyishyurwa vy’amahera biciye ku makarita ya Bolt.



Iryo koraniro riboneka nk’iry’ubuntu be n’iry’amahera kugira ngo ubone ibintu vyinshi biteye imbere. Kugira ngo ukore terminal ya POS, fyonda ku buto **Open** buri musi y'ikimenyetso c'ivyo bikoresho, hanyuma ukande kuri **New POS**.



![new_tpos](assets/fr/11.webp)



Sigura izina ry’aho ugurisha, hanyuma uhuze Voltz wallet yawe n’ibarabara ryawe kugira ngo ushiremwo amahera. Ushobora gukoresha amashimwe mu guca ku gasandugu kavuga ngo **Gukoresha amashimwe**. Kandi ushireho uburyo bwo gucapura invoice nimba wipfuza gutanga amafaranga y’inyemezabuguzi ku bakiriya bawe (ivyo bikoresho biracariko birategurwa, rero vyoshobora kuba bitari vyo).



Igikoresho co kugurisha kirimwo kandi ugutunganya umusoro igihe ushaka gukoresha umusoro w’igihugu cawe ku bicuruzwa vyawe.



![tpos](assets/fr/12.webp)



Igihe POS terminal yawe imaze kuremwa, urashobora kwongerako ibintu n’ibikorwa vyatunganijwe imbere y’igihe kugira ngo ushobore kwishura neza no gucungera amafaranga kuri wewe n’abaguzi bawe.



![product](assets/fr/13.webp)



Rema ibintu vyawe mu gusobanura izina ryavyo, ukongerako ishusho no gushinga igiciro co kugurisha.  Ushobora gushiramwo ibintu vyawe mu migwi kugira ngo bishobore gukurikirana vyoroshe. Ushobora gukoresha imisoro ataco uhinduye ku kintu kinaka. Iyo igicuruzwa ata musoro gikoreshwa, umusoro watunganijwe ku rwego rwo kurema ikibanza co kugurisha uzokoreshwa ubwo nyene.



![products](assets/fr/14.webp)



Ushobora kwinjiza urutonde rw'ibicuruzwa vyawe mu buryo bwa JSON ukanda kuri buto **Import/Export**.



![exports](assets/fr/15.webp)



Ushobora gushika aho ushobora gusohora amahera uciye ku nzira ukanda ku kimenyetso ca **New Tab**, canke usangire urubuga rwawe rwa POS biciye ku kode ya QR n’abaguzi bawe ukanda ku kimenyetso ca **QR code**.



![lien](assets/fr/16.webp)



![qr](assets/fr/17.webp)



Abaguzi bawe barashobora kuraba urutonde rwawe maze bagatanga amahera yabo bakoresheje iyi interface.



![pos](assets/fr/18.webp)



![chose](assets/fr/19.webp)



![pay](assets/fr/20.webp)



![checkout](assets/fr/21.webp)



Mu mugwi w’ivyongeweko biriho, uzosanga kandi ibikoresho nk’ivyongeweko **Invoice** na **Payment Link**, bigufasha gutanga amafagitire ya generate afise ibintu vyihariye kugira ngo ushire hamwe amahera yihariye utaciye ku nzira ya POS.



## Gukurikirana ivyo wishyura



Mu **Ivyishyurwa**, Voltz iraguha icegeranyo c’ivyo wishyura ku bitabo vyawe bitandukanye.


Ushobora gukurikirana ivyo wishyuye ukoresheje :




- Umwanya.
- Ico kimenyetso: nk’akarorero **TPOS** ku bijanye n’ugutanga amahera aho ugurisha na **lnhub** biciye ku nzira y’uguhuza telefone ngendanwa mu bikoresho vya Zeus na Blue Wallet.
- Igitabu c’ivy’ugukorakoranya.
- Igitigiri cose c’amahera yinjira n’asohoka.
- Igihe gisobanuwe neza.



![payments](assets/fr/22.webp)



## Ibindi bihitamwo



Voltz nayo ni ibikorwa remezo ushobora kwubakirako inyishu zawe. Mu gice ca **Ivyagutse**, uzosangamwo ubuyobozi bwo gutegura ivyagutse vyawe bwite mu bidukikije vya Voltz na LnBits.



![build](assets/fr/23.webp)



Mu gihe woba ushaka gutegura inyishu hanze y'ibidukikije ariko ugakoresha ibikorwa remezo vyavyo, mu gice ca **URL of node**, uzosanga imfunguruzo za API n'amakuru y'inyandiko n'akarorero k'ico ushobora gukora n'aya makuru.



Ushobora gukora amafagitire ya Lightning ukoresheje amaporogarama yawe ukoresheje Voltz wallet yawe, kwishura amafagitire ya Lightning, gusobanura no kugenzura amafagitire.



![keys](assets/fr/24.webp)
