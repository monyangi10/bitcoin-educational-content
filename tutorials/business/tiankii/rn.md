Mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin, kwemera kwishura mu gihe nyaco biracari ingorane ikomeye ku bucuruzi no ku bantu ku giti cabo. Inyishu z’imigenzo akenshi zisaba ubumenyi buteye imbere mu vy’ubuhinga, ibikorwa remezo bikomeye vyo kubungabunga, canke zisaba amahera afise abahuza. Ivyo biriko birabuza abantu benshi kwemera Bitcoin nk’ifaranga ry’imisi yose, naho nyene hari umuhango w’iterambere ry’ubuhinga bwa Lightning Network.



Tiankii, isosiyete yo muri Salvador yavutse mu 2021, yishura ku ngorane mu gutanga ibikorwa remezo vya Bitcoin bishobora gushikirwa n’abantu bose, bifise ibice. Aho guhatira kwemera ibidukikije vyugarijwe, Tiankii itanga urutonde rw’ibikoresho bihuye bishobora gutuma umuntu wese ashobora kwinjiza Bitcoin Lightning mu bucuruzi bwiwe ataco atakaje ku kugenzura amahera yiwe.



## None Tiankii ni iki?



### Inkomoko na filozofiya



Tiankii - ijambo ry'ikinyahuatl risobanura "isoko ry'imbere ry'ikirere ry'abantu bose" - ni ryo shirahamwe rya mbere ry'i El Salvador ryitanguye 100% Bitcoin. Yashinzwe na Darvin Otero inyuma y’aho Bitcoin yemerewe kuba amafaranga y’igihugu, iyo sosiyete ikaba igamije guhindura Bitcoin ikava mu bubiko bw’agaciro ikaja mu mafaranga ashobora gukoreshwa mu gugura ibintu vya misi yose. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibibanza vyo kubika amafaranga, Tiankii ikoresha uburyo butagira ububiko aho abakoresha baguma bagenzura amafaranga yabo, ibikorwa remezo bikaba bikora gusa nk’umuhuza w’ubuhinga.



### Ubwubatsi bw'ubuhinga



Tiankii ishinzwe nk'uwutanga ibikorwa remezo vya Bitcoin-nk'Ibikorwa bishingiye kuri Lightning Network. Ubwo buhinga bwo mu rwego rwa kabiri buratuma umuntu ashobora gukora amafaranga hafi mu kanya nk’ako gukubita ataco atwara, ivyo bikaba bituma umuntu ashobora kwishura amahera makeyi be n’ugugura ibintu ku musi ku musi.



Ubwubatsi bushingiye ku nkingi zitatu:



**Lightning-first**: Gukunda mu buryo butegekanijwe urubuga rwa Lightning kubera umuvuduko warwo n’ibiciro bito, mu gihe uguma ushigikira on-chain ku mahera menshi.



**Ivyiyumviro vyuguruye**: Gukoresha LNURL kugira ngo ukoreshe ibisabwa vyo kwishura, Lightning Address ku IDs za email zisomwa, na Bolt Card ku kwishura NFC ikorana.



**wallet-agnostic modularity**: Ubushobozi bwo gufatanya ibice bitandukanye vy’umuravyo (LNbits, Blink, BlueWallet) canke urudodo rwawe bwite, utanga ubushobozi bwo guhindura bivanye n’urugero rw’ubuhinga n’ukwigenga bisabwa.



## Ibikoresho vy'ibidukikije vya Tiankii



### Bitcoin POS - Igikoresho co kwishura mu iduka



Iryo koraniro rihindura telefone ngendanwa canke tablette ngo ibe ikibanza c’umuravyo. Uwo mudandaza yinjiza ayo mahera mu mafaranga yo mu gihugu, maze iyo porogarama igaca itanga kode ya Lightning QR canke ikemera ikarata Bolt. Ibicuruzwa bica bishirwako ata komisiyo, n’uguhindura kwihuta mu mafaranga arenga 150, uburongozi bw’amahera n’amateka y’ugurisha.



### Urupapuro rw'abacuruzi - Ubuyobozi bumwe bw'ubucuruzi



Interface urubuga rwo guhuza wallet Lightning yayo, gukurikirana ibikorwa mu gihe nyaco, gutanga amafagitire na raporo z’ivy’ubuhinga bwa generate. Iryo koraniro rihuriza hamwe imihora yose: ukwishura mu maduka (POS), ugurisha kuri interineti (ubudandaji bwo kuri interineti), canke ubufatanye bwa API. Ivyishyurwa bihurira ku wallet yatowe.



### Ikarata Bitcoin idakorana n’umuntu (Ikarata Bolt)



Ikarata ya NFC Bolt iserukira ubuhinga bushasha bwa Tiankii mu gutuma Bitcoin igira demokarasi ku bantu bose. Ikora nk’ikarita ya banki idakorana n’umuntu, iratuma ushobora kwishura ivyo uguze Bitcoin Lightning ukoresheje gusa gukora kuri terminal ihuye.



Mu buryo butandukanye n’imiti y’ububiko bwa kera, iri karata rikurikiza ingingo ngenderwako yuguruye yemeza ko umuntu ashobora gukorana neza. Abakoresha barashobora kuyihuza na wallet yabo bwite biciye ku rubuga rwa IBI, gutyo bakaguma bafise imfunguruzo zabo z’ibanga kandi bakagenzura neza amahera ajanye na yo. Kwishura bifata isegonda gusa, ntaco bimaze gukura telefone yawe canke ngo ube ufise internet ikora igihe uriko urishura.



Uwo muti ni uwushiramwo cane cane abantu batamenyereye amatelefone ngendanwa, utanga irembo ry’ubutunzi bwa Bitcoin ryoroshe gushikako.



### IBI Porogaramu - Interface umuntu ku giti ciwe Bitcoin



Igikoresho ca IBI (Umuntu ku giti ciwe Bitcoin Interface) cagenewe abantu bipfuza gukoresha Bitcoin Lightning ku musi ku musi. Inyungu yayo nyamukuru iri mu guhingura Address Lightning igenewe umuntu, ikimenyetso c’ukwishyura gisomwa mu buryo bwa email (akarorero: alice@ibi.me).



Ico kimenyetso kirosha cane kwakira amahera: nta nkenerwa yo generate invoice nshasha ku giciro cose, uwurungitse ashobora gusa kwinjiza aderesi ya Lightning. Iryo koraniro kandi rituma ushobora gucunga ikarita yawe ya Bolt (gukoresha, guhagarika, gukoresha amahera), gufatanya ama wallet atandukanye ya Lightning, no kwishura ukoresheje gucapura amakode ya QR.



### Ubucuruzi bwo kuri interineti



Ivyiteguye gukoreshwa mu gukorana n’ubudandaji bwa WooCommerce, Shopify na Cloudbeds. Ishiramwo mu minota kugira ngo wongereko Bitcoin Umuravyo kugira ngo ushiremwo. Inyungu: nta komisiyo (vs. 2-3% y’amakarata y’inguzanyo), kwishura ubwo nyene, gushika kw’isi yose, gukuraho amahera yo kwishura. Ivyishyurwa bishika ataco bimaze kuri wallet y'umucuruzi.



### Bitcoin API n'ibikoresho vy'abahinguzi



Tiankii SDK ituma bishoboka gushiramwo Bitcoin Lightning mu bikorwa biriho ataco igumye. API ikora ibikorwa vyo gukora invoice, kugenzura ukwishyura no kohereza ubutumwa bwinshi biciye ku bikorwa remezo bikomeye vyakiriwe kuri Google Cloud. Command Center iraheza ishikanwa ku mashirahamwe afise Address Lightning ku bijanye n’ivy’ubuhinga, ukwishura vyinshi, n’ugucungera hamwe kw’ibikoresho vya NFC n’amakarata.



## Gushiramwo n'intambwe zambere



### Ibisabwa vy'ingenzi



Gukoresha Tiankii ntibisaba ibintu bikomeye vy’ubuhinga. Ivyo bikoresho bikoreshwa biciye ku mucukumbuzi w’urubuga kuri telefone ngendanwa, kuri tablette canke kuri mudasobwa. Nta gushiramwo porogarama y’akavukire bisabwa: ivyo ukeneye vyose ni ugukoresha Internet n’umucukumbuzi uherutse kugira ngo ushikire IBI canke Dashboard y’Umucuruzi.



**Ku bakoresha b’ibanga (IBI App)**: Nta n’umuravyo wa wallet usabwa imbere. Iyo uremye konti yawe, Tiankii ihita itunganya umuravyo ukora Address biciye ku [Breez SDK Liquid](https://sdk-doc-liquid. Ushobora guca ukira no kohereza amahera ata n’imwe mu nzira z’ubuhinga. Uwo muti worosha cane kuronka ku batangura, mu gihe baguma bariko barizigama.



**Ku bacuruzi (Dashboard y’abacuruzi)** : Gufatanya umuravyo wa wallet usanzweho ni ngombwa kugira ngo ukoreshe ama terminals ya Point of Sale no kwakira amahera. Tiankii ishigikira inyishu nyinshi: amasakoshi y’amatelefone ngendanwa (Blink, Strike), inyishu zikoreshwa n’abantu (LNbits, LND/CLN node), canke amasakoshi y’urubuga (Alby). Ivyiyumviro vy’ido n’ido vy’uguhuza biraboneka mu gice c’Ibikoresho c’iyi nyigisho.

### Ku bacuruzi: Dashboard y'abacuruzi na POS



**Gukora konti y'ubucuruzi



Genda kuri accounts.ibi.me kugira ngo ukore konti yawe. Hitamwo "Ikoreshwa ry'ubucuruzi" kugira ngo ushikire ibikoresho vy'ubucuruzi. Ubwo bwoko bwa konti burafungura uburyo bwo gushika ku rubuga rw’abacuruzi no ku bibanza vy’ugucururizamwo.



Uhejeje guhitamwo "Ikoreshwa ry'ubucuruzi", uzoca urungikwa ku rubuga rw'abacuruzi (pay.tiankii.com). Ivyo bigutwara ku rubuga rwo gucunga ubucuruzi rufise ibikoresho vyo gukurikirana inyungu, amafaranga n’ivyo kwishura. Kugira ngo utangure kwemera amahera, utegerezwa kubanza gufatanya wallet Lightning yawe ukanda ku nzira iri hejuru kuri iyo paji (raba umwampi uri ku ishusho).

**wallet Umuravyo** guhuza

Urashobora kubona ko hariho uburyo bumwe bumwe (Bitcoin Onchain na Lightning Network) bukiriko burategurwa kandi bugaragara nk’ubwoya ku rubuga.

![Paiement encaissé](assets/fr/19.webp)



**Gukurikirana no gucunga**



Ivyo ukora vyose vyandikwa mu rutonde rw’abacuruzi bawe. Ufise ukurikirana kwuzuye kw’amahera yinjiye hakurikijwe igihe, umubare wose w’ugurisha, n’amateka y’ido n’ido y’ivy’ubuhinga bwawe.



Ikigaragaza c'Ivyagezwe gitanga amabara menshi y'imiterere. "Ivyagezwe rusangi" bigufasha gucunga urutonde rw'umucuruzi wawe n'amatangazo. Urupapuro rwa "Abakoresha" ruragufasha kwongerako no gucunga ukwinjira ku Rutonde rw'Abacuruzi rw'umugwi wawe (ugucungera abakoresha benshi bivanye n'umugambi wawe). "Ikibanza c'Iterambere" gitanga uburenganzira bwo gukoresha imfunguruzo za API ku bijanye n'ugushiramwo ibintu biteye imbere, mu gihe "Ukwiyandikisha" bigufasha gucunga ukwiyandikisha kwawe.



![Paramètres du compte marchand](assets/fr/20.webp)



**Freemium n'imigambi y'ubucuruzi



Tiankii itanga amapaki abiri y’Igikoresho c’Umucuruzi. Ico gikorwa ca **Freemium** (c’ubuntu) kirabereye ku bigo bitangura bifise aho bigarukira: ahantu hamwe ho kugurisha, uwukoresha umwe, umubare w’amahera y’ukwezi ugera ku madolari 1.000, kandi nta n’umwe ashobora gushika ku bihuza ubudandaji bwo kuri interineti. Gahunda ya **Business** (0.5% y’amahera ku giciro) itanga ama terminal atagira aho agarukira, abakoresha batagira aho bugarukira, umubare utagira aho ugarukira, ushobora gukoresha ibikoresho vya WooCommerce/Shopify/Cloudbeds, n’amatangazo yihariye ya WhatsApp.

## Inyungu n'intambamyi



### Ibintu bihambaye



**Ukwizigama**: Ugumya imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga kandi ukagenzura neza amahera yawe. Nta ngorane yo guhagarika konti canke gusenyuka kw’urubuga rw’uwundi muntu.



**Ukworohereza**: Lightning Address nk’aderesi ya email, ukwishyura kwa NFC n’ugucapura kworoshe, interface yoroshe ata buhinga bw’ubuhinga busabwa.



**Ibidukikije vyuzuye**: Ibikoresho vy’imirongo yose (abantu ku giti cabo, abagurisha, abategura) bifise ubufatanye bw’ibice bihuye n’ivyo ukeneye.



**Ukwubahiriza amategeko y’umwuga**: Ukwakira igicu gitekanye, kwubahiriza PCI-DSS, kwubahiriza amategeko y’i Salvador.



### Ivyo bigarukira



**Ibibazo vy’umuravyo**: Bisaba gufatanya wallet ubudasiba, gucunga amafaranga y’ibitabo vyinshi, ingorane zo kunanirwa iyo uwuyakira atari ku muhora. Tiankii iragabanya ivyo bintu n’ama LSP yunze ubumwe.



**SaaS dependency**: Iyo Tiankii itaboneka, gukora invoice ntibishoboka mu gihe gito (amahera yawe aguma ari mu mutekano).



**Ibanga**: Tiankii ashobora kwihweza amakuru y’ibikorwa (amahera, amatariki). Ivy’ibanga bike kuruta umuti wishingira nk’uwa BTCPay Server.



## Iciyumviro



Tiankii yerekana ingene ibikorwa remezo vyubatswe neza bishobora gukuraho intambamyi z’ubuhinga zibuza abantu benshi kwemera Bitcoin nk’ifaranga ry’imisi yose. Mu gufatanya ububasha bwa Lightning Network n’uburyo butagiramwo ububiko n’ibikoresho bishobora gushikwako, ibidukikije biratanga inzira iri ku rugero hagati y’ubusegaba bw’ivy’ubutunzi n’ukworohereza gukoresha.



Ku bacuruzi, Tiankii igereranya akaryo ko kugabanya cane igiciro c’ugucuruza mu gihe bashobora kuronka abakiriya bashasha. Ku baguzi, amakarita ya Lightning Address na NFC ahindura Bitcoin akayigira amahera akora, ata n’ibintu bikomeye vy’ubuhinga.



Naho kwemera cane Bitcoin biguma ari ingorane isaba inyigisho n’igihe, ibikorwa remezo nka Tiankii vyerekana ko ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bimaze kubaho. Intumbero yo kworohereza abantu kwishura Bitcoin ntabwo ari iyerekwa ryo kure, ahubwo ni ukuri gushikira umuntu wese yipfuza gufata iyo nzira.
