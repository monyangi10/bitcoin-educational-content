## Imenyekanisha



Mu isi aho ukwishyura biriko birarushiriza kuba ivy’ubuhinga bwa none, Bitcoin iriko irishingira intahe buhoro buhoro nk’iyindi nzira. Yamara ku bantu benshi bashasha, gucunga wallet canke kwemera amahera ya Bitcoin birashobora gusa n’ibigoye.



Aho niho Coinos yinjira, urubuga rwakozwe na Adam Soltys, umuhinga mu bijanye n’urubuga yaremye verisiyo ya mbere yo gufasha abacuruzi bo mu karere kiwe kwemera kwishura Bitcoin.



Coinos ni urubuga rworoshe, rusanzwe rwateguwe kugira ngo rworohe gukoresha. Iragufasha kohereza, kwakira no kwemera amahera mu rurimi rwa Bitcoin, Liquid canke Lightning, uhereye ku mucukumbuzi wawe, ataco ushizeko mu buryo bw’ubuhinga. Coinos ishobora gushikirwa na bose, ihuriza hamwe ivyiza vya Bitcoin n’uburyo bworoshe bwo gukoresha urubuga (*Progressive Web App*, canke PWA), rwiza cane ku bantu ku giti cabo, abacuruzi n’abashaka kumenya vyinshi.

## Intambwe za mbere na Coinos



Nta bumenyi bw'ubuhinga canke bwimbitse ukeneye imbere yo gufata **Coinos** mu minwe. Ariko rero, gutahura ivy’ishimikiro ku bijanye n’ibikorwa vya Bitcoin, on chain, Lightning na Liquid ni ikintu co kwongerako.

### Imiterere y'ibikorwa



Fyonda kuri **buto y'imbere y'ivyo ukunda** hanyuma kuri **Ivyo ukunda**.



![screen|220](assets/fr/18.webp)



Mu **Ivyo ukunda**, hariho ibice 4 bigufasha gutunganya ibikorwa vyawe:





- Ishirahamwe**



Muri iyi submenu, urashobora guhindura izina ryawe, gushinga ijambobanga rishasha, guhindura avatar yawe (ifoto y’ibanga), kwongerako ifoto y’ibendera mbere n’insobanuro.



![screen|220](assets/fr/19.webp)



![screen|220](assets/fr/20.webp)





- Ikibanza co kugurisha** (POS)



Aha, urashobora guhitamwo ururimi rwawe n’amahera yo mu karere kawe, ugashiramwo aderesi yawe ya e-mail, ugakoresha amatangazo bivanye n’ivyo ukunda, kandi, nimba uri umucuruzi, ugahuza wallet yawe na Square kugira ngo ushobore kwishura neza.



![screen|220](assets/fr/21.webp)



![screen|220](assets/fr/22.webp)





- Nostr**



Niba ufise konti muri Nostr, urashobora kuyihuza na konti yawe ya Coinos.



![screen|220](assets/fr/23.webp)





- Ibiranga umutekano**



Ushobora gukoresha kode y’umutekano, ugashobora gukoresha ivyemezo bibiri, mbere ukongerako n’iyindi konti muri iyi nkuru.



![screen|220](assets/fr/24.webp)



Ntukibagire gukanda kuri **Save settings** igihe cose ukoze ihinduka kugira ngo ubike ivyo wahinduye.



![screen|220](assets/fr/25.webp)

## Kwakira ama bitcoins



Kugira ngo uronke bitcoins kuri Coinos, fyonda ku **Kwakira**, canke ku **kwakira interface** icon. Uhejeje ku rubuga rwo kwakira, ubaze uwugurungikiye **ni uruhe rubuga worungikirako ama bitcoins**, kugira umenye **uko worungikirako aderesi iyihe**.



### Ukwakira abashitsi ku muhora mukuru



Fyonda kuri **Bitcoin**.



![screen|220](assets/fr/27.webp)



Hanyuma haca haboneka kode ya QR n’aderesi yo gushikanako.



![screen|220](assets/fr/28.webp)

![screen|220](assets/fr/29.webp)



![screen|220](assets/fr/30.webp)

![screen|220](assets/fr/31.webp)

### Lightning Network kwakira abashitsi



Ku mburabuzi, ikigaragara c'ukwakira kifunguka n'ihitamwo ry'umuravyo. Uca ugira kode yawe ya QR n’aderesi yawe ya Lightning, ivyo ushobora guhindura uko ushaka ukoresheje umubare wihariye be n’inyandiko.



![screen|220](assets/fr/37.webp)



![screen|220](assets/fr/38.webp)



**Kode yawe ya QR** na **Aderesi y'umuravyo** vyawe vy'umuntu ku giti ciwe ubu birateguwe. Birungike uwugurungikiye maze ubone satoshis zawe ubwo nyene.



![screen|220](assets/fr/39.webp)



Kugira ngo uronke amahera biciye kuri **Lightning**, ushobora gukoresha akamenyetso gatoyi kari ku rubuga nyamukuru rwa wallet. Ivyo bituma ushobora kwerekana aderesi yawe ya Lightning: ushobora rero kuyikopa canke ugahindukira ukaja kuri kode yayo ya QR kugira ngo uyisangire bitagoranye.



![screen|220](assets/fr/40.webp)



Igihe cose winjije amahera, utitaye ku rubuga ukoresheje, Coinos ica yerekana ubwo nyene amahera angana na yo mu satoshis.



## Wohereze ama bitcoins



Niba wipfuza kohereza ama bitcoins ukoresheje Coinos wallet yawe, fyonda ku **icon yo kohereza**. Igihe ugeze kuri interface ihuye, ufise amahitamwo menshi:




- Scan kode ya QR yatanzwe n’uwuyironka;
- Nimushire aderesi yakopiwe mu gicapo canyu ;
- Mu gice ca **Contacts**, uhitemwo ataco uhinduye izina ry’umuntu umaze gukorana na we;
- Injira aderesi ya Bitcoin (on-chain, Liquid canke Bolt LN), inyemezabuguzi y’umuravyo, canke izina ry’ukoresha rya Coinos ry’uwukwakira mu kibanza catanzwe.



![screen|220](assets/fr/41.webp)



Uhejeje kwandika canke gushiramwo aderesi, ukande **Ibikurikira**, winjize amahera y’ugucuruza, hanyuma wongere ukande **Ibikurikira**.

Coinos itanga ibindi bikoresho vyinshi, harimwo :





- La **Ikarata y'abacuruzi** (Igifaransa gusa)



Coinos wallet irimwo ikarata yerekana abacuruzi bakoresha iyo nzira. Ivyumba vyose biri ku rutonde vyemera kwishura Bitcoin, cane cane biciye ku Coinos wallet zavyo. Kugira ngo ugaragaze iyi karita, fyonda ku **ivyo bimenyetso**, hanyuma uhitemwo **Ikarita**. Uzoca ubona urutonde rw’ubudandaji n’aho buri kw’isi yose.

## Umutekano n'ibikorwa vyiza



Umutekano ni ikibazo nyamukuru igihe ukoresha amafaranga y’ama bitcoins. Naho Coinos ituma ubumenyi bworoha kandi butagira umurongo, birahambaye ko ukingira ama bitcoins yawe.





- Koresha ijambobanga rikomeye: Imisi yose uhitemwo ijambobanga ry’umwihariko, rirerire kandi rigoye gutekereza ku bintu.
- Gukoresha ivyemezo bibiri (2FA).
- Gumana ijambobanga ryawe ry’ibanga: ntukigere usangira ijambobanga ryawe n’umuntu uwo ari we wese. Coinos ntazokwigera akusaba, haba ku butumwa canke kuri e-mail.
- Ntubike amahera menshi ku nkuru yakira: Coinos irabereye ku kwishura ku musi, ariko iguma ari umuti wo kubika. Bika ivyo ukeneye gusa kugira ngo ukore ibikorwa vya misi yose. Ku mahera menshi, hitamwo wallet idashobora kubikwa.
