Muri iyi nyigisho, turaza kuraba ingene twoshiraho Canaan Avalon Mini 3, kugira ngo ukoreshe Miner mu rugo.



Kugeza ubu, imashini za ASIC (*Igikoresho c’Ikoreshwa ry’Igikoresho*) zagenewe gukora igikorwa kinaka - muri iki gihe, uguharura Hash (SHA-256) ku Miner ya Bitcoin - ntizari zibereye cane gukoreshwa mu rugo. Urusaku rutera umubabaro, ubushuhe buva muri ivyo be n’uko bikenewe guhindura amashanyarazi yawe kugira ngo ashobore gushigikira ububasha buhambaye bw’ivyo bikoresho vyatumye benshi muri twebwe tutagira uruhara muri ivyo.



Uyu musi, Canaan, umwe mu bakora imashini za ASIC, yafashe ingingo yo guhangana n’isoko ry’abantu ku giti cabo bashaka Miner muhira, mu gutanga ibikoresho bitandukanye bimeze nk’ibicereje kandi vyoroshe cane gushiramwo (plug & play).



Ivyo bikoresho bigurishwa nk’igikoresho co gushushanya co gufasha mu gihe c’i **Avalon Nano 3S (140W)**, canke nk’igikoresho co gushushanya gifise ubushobozi bwa **800W** mu gihe c’i **Avalon Mini 3**.



https://planb.network/tutorials/mining/hardware/canaan-avalon-nano-3f6ac96e-ea8a-4dee-9b9b-13875824c9a6

Turagusavye uzirikane ko itandukaniro ry’igiciro n’ibikoresho vya kera vy’ugushushanya vy’ububasha bungana, mu bihe vyinshi, ritaguha uburenganzira bwo kuronka inyungu y’amahera. Ivyo bita satoshis biva ku gikorwa ca Mining ntibizokwigera bisubiza inyuma iyo ntandukaniro y’igiciro, kiretse iyo ufise amashanyarazi y’ubuntu (arengeye) canke atari menshi cane.



Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, ivyo bikoresho bikwiye gufatwa cane nk'uburyo bworoshe bwo gushika ku Miner muhira ku bipfuza kubigira kubera imvo z'umuntu ku giti ciwe: *kuronka Satss zitari za KYC / gukina "loteri" mu gukora solominate / kugira uruhara mu kwegereza Hashrate n'ibindi. Ariko si nk’uburyo bwo kuzigama amahera mu bihe vyinshi n’imiburiburi (ibihugu vy’uburengero).

## Guhuza na Mining pool



**Iki gice ni ikintu gisanzwe ku bikoresho vya Nano 3s na Mini 3, kuko uburyo bwo kubikora busa cane **



Twaba dushaka "solominate" canke "pool" ya Miner, tuzokenera kwifatanya na Mining pool. Nkako, igikoresho cacu nta kindi ariko ni igikoresho co gukora Hash ataco azi ku bijanye n’uruja n’uruza rwa Bitcoin. Kuyifatanya n’ikidengeri biyiha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha urubuga rwa Bitcoin, kandi bikayiha uburenganzira bwo kwakira ibigereranyo vy’amabuye yo gukorako.



### Gukoresha porogarama kugira ngo uhuze na Mining pool



Ku rubuga rwa Avalon Family, hitamwo igikoresho nk’uko vyerekanwa aha hepfo. Uzoca usabwa guhindura ijambobanga ry’umuyobozi w’iyo mashini. Fyonda kuri "OK" niba ushaka kubikora, canke kuri cancel kugira ngo usige ijambobanga ry'imbere "admin".


![image](assets/fr/16.webp)


Hanyuma uhitemwo "Ivyagezwe", hanyuma "Pool Config" hanyuma winjize amaparametere y'ibidengeri 3 vyasabwe.


Pools #2 na #3 zizokora nk’ivy’inyuma iyo kimwe muri vyo kizonanirwa, kugira ngo Miner yawe ntikore ubusa. Ku mburabuzi, Hashrate izokwerekezwa ku kidengeri #1.



Mu gihe cacu duhitamwo:




- 1 - Ikidengeri ca bose,
- 2 - CkIkidengeri,
- 3 - Ikiyaga.



![image](assets/fr/17.webp)



Kubindi bisobanuro ku buryo bwo kwifatanya na Mining pool, urashobora kuraba aya nyigisho :

### Gukoresha umucukumbuzi kugira ngo uhuze na Mining pool



Ushobora kandi kwifatanya na Mining pool kandi, muri rusangi, ushobora gushika ku nzira y’uburongozi bwa Interface y’igikoresho cawe biciye ku mucukumbuzi ukunda.



Kugira ngo ubikore, wandike mu murongo w’ubushakashatsi wa mucukumbuzi IP Address y’igikoresho kigaragara ku rubuga ruri musi, mu karorero kacu **192.168.144.6**

![image](assets/fr/20.webp)



Ugurure iyo porogarama, hanyuma ukande ku bimenyetso 3 biri hejuru iburyo, hanyuma ukande kuri scan. Scan kode ya QR ya browser, hanyuma winjize ijambobanga ry’umuyobozi wa porogarama hanyuma ukande OK.



![image](assets/fr/21.webp)



Aha uri kuri paji y’urubuga igufasha gukorana na Avalon yawe. Ni nk'urubaho rwo kwerekana ibipimo vy'imashini, kuruta uburyo bwo kuyitunganya.

## Gucungera igikoresho cawe ukoresheje porogaramu y'umuryango wa Avalon



Ubu twahuje Miner yo mu nzu yacu n’urubuga rwacu rwo mu karere, maze twerekana Hashrate yacu ku bidengeri vy’amabuye y’agaciro. None rero reka tubone ibintu nyamukuru vy’imashini yacu biciye ku rubuga rwa Avalon Family.



Mu rutonde nyamukuru rw’iporogarama y’umuryango wa Avalon, fyonda ku kimenyetso gihuye na Avalon Mini 3. Uzoja ku rutonde rwo gucunga uburyo bwo gukoresha.



3 amahitamwo araboneka: "Heater" uburyo, "Mining" uburyo canke "Ijoro".





- Mu buryo bwa "Heater" ufise ubushobozi 2 "Eco" canke "Super".


Urugero rwa "Eco" rujanye n'ububasha bwo gushushanya 500W ku Hashrate bugera hafi 25 Th/s na 40 dB ku rugero rw'ijwi.


Urugero rwa "Super" rujanye n'ububasha bw'isohoka bwa 650 W ku nko. 30T/s na 45 dB. Ubu buryo buratuma ushobora gushinga ubushuhe bwo hejuru y’aho igikoresho kizohagarika gukora.



![image](assets/fr/36.webp)




- Mu buryo bwa "Mining", iyo nkuru ikora ku muvuduko munini cane, ata mahitamwo yo gushinga ubushuhe bugenewe (uretse umupaka w'ubushuhe burenze urugero, birashoboka). Intumbero ni ugukoresha neza ubushobozi bwa Miner. Aha, ubushobozi bwo gusohoka bwegera 800 W ku 37.5 Th/s n’urugero rw’urusaku rwa 50-55 dB.



![image](assets/fr/37.webp)


Ubwa nyuma, mu buryo bwa "Ijoro", Mini 3 yawe ikora ku muvuduko mutoyi cane ata rusaku rwinshi. 400 W, 20 Th/s n’ibilometero nka. 33 dB.



Aha na ho, urashobora gushinga ubushuhe bwo hejuru aho igikoresho kija mu buryo butakora maze kigahagarika Miner. Iyo ubushuhe bugabanutse, iyo nkuru izosubira gukora kandi isubire gushusha na Miner. Muri ubwo buryo, amatara yerekana arazimwa ku buryo busanzwe, ariko urashobora guhitamwo kuyazimya iyo bisabwe kugira ngo umurikire icumba mu mwiza, nk’itara ry’ijoro (raba ifoto iri musi).



![image](assets/fr/38.webp)



![image](assets/fr/39.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, urashobora gukina n'amatara yawe ya Avalon biciye ku rutonde rwa "Display". Ushobora guhitamwo guca mu makuru yerekeye ibikorwa, ugahitamwo kwerekana isaha, canke mbere ishusho y’ibintu (ifise amapixel).



![image](assets/fr/40.webp)



![image](assets/fr/41.webp)



Nk'uko biri kuri Avalon Nano 3S, urutonde rwa "Imiterere" ruragufasha guhindura ijambobanga ry'umuyobozi, imiterere y'ikiyaga, gusuzuma inzitizi y'akayunguruzo (iri munsi y'igikoresho), guhamagara abafasha, canke kuraba inyandiko z'igikoresho.



![image](assets/fr/42.webp)



Na none, akayunguruzo kari hasi y’ico gikoresho gashobora gusukurwa n’igikoresho co gusukura, uko umuntu akora ubudasiba ni ko bigenda neza.



Twarashitse ku mpera y’iyi nyigisho, yatwigishije ingene twohuza Avalon Mini 3 yacu n’urubuga rwacu rwo mu karere, ingene twokwerekana Hashrate yacu ku bidengeri vya Mining, n’ingene twoca mu mahitamwo n’imiterere dukoresheje porogarama ya Avalon Family.
