## Attakaï - inzu Mining yashobotse kandi irashikira abantu!


Ico gikorwa ca "Attakaï" kiratohoza Bitcoin Mining hakoreshejwe ubushuhe buva muri ivyo. Iyo nkuru itanga inyishu zo gutuma abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bashobora gukoreshwa nk’ama radiateur mu ngo, bikaba bituma umuntu agira umunezero mwinshi kandi akazigama inguvu. Bitcoin ihita ihindura ingorane ya Mining maze igahemba abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro kubera igikorwa bakora. Ariko rero, ukwibanda kwa Hashrate birashobora gutera ingorane ku kutagira aho bahengamiye kw’urubuga. "Attakaï" itanga ubuyobozi ngirakamaro bwo gusubiramwo abacukuzi b'amabuye y'agaciro mu vy'ubutunzi, bikaba bituma abaje mu nama bagabanya amafaranga y'amashanyarazi kandi bagahembwa Sats ata KYC.


## Imenyekanisha


"Attakaï," bisobanura "ubushuhe bwiza" mu kiyapani, ni izina ry'umugambi ugamije kuvumbura Bitcoin Mining biciye mu gusubira gukoresha ubushuhe bwatangujwe na @ajelexBTC na @BlobOnChain na Découvre Bitcoin. Iyi nkuru yo gusubiramwo ASIC izokora nk’ishimikiro ryo kumenya vyinshi ku bijanye na Mining, ingene ikora, amateka yayo aheruka, n’ubutunzi bushingiyeko.


### Ni kuki wosubira gukoresha ubushuhe buva ku ASIC?


Ni ngombwa gutahura isano riri hagati y’inguvu n’ubushuhe buva mu nzira y’umuyagankuba.


Ku bijanye n’ishoramari ry’inguvu z’umuyagankuba 1 kW, radiator y’umuyagankuba itanga ubushuhe 1 kW, ntabwo ari bwinshi canke buke. Amaradiyo mashasha ntakora neza kuruta amaradiyo ya kera. Inyungu zavyo ziri mu bushobozi bwazo bwo gutanga ubushuhe ubudasiba kandi mu buryo buringaniye mu cumba, bikaba bituma umuntu agira umunezero mwinshi ugereranije n’ibikoresho vya kera vy’ugushushanya bihinduka hagati y’ububasha bwinshi bwo gushushanya n’ukutagira ubushuhe, gutyo bikaba bituma ubushuhe buhinduka ubudasiba be n’ukudatekana.


Ordinateri, canke mu buryo bwagutse, urubaho rw’ubuhinga bwa none, ntirukoresha inguvu kugira ngo rukore ibiharuro; rikeneye gusa inguvu kugira ngo rice mu bihimba vyaryo kugira ngo rikore. Inguvu zikoreshwa ziterwa n’ubushobozi bw’umuyagankuba bw’ibihimba, ivyo bikaba bituma habaho uguhomba, gutyo bikazana ubushuhe, ivyo bikaba bizwi kw’izina rya Joule effect.

Amashirahamwe amwe amwe yaraje n’iciyumviro co gukoranya ivyipfuzo vy’ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none n’ivy’ugushusha biciye mu bikoresho vy’amashanyarazi (radiator/servers). Iciyumviro ni ugusangira ama server y’ishirahamwe mu bice bitobito vyoshobora gushirwa mu ngo canke mu biro. Ariko rero, ico ciyumviro kirahura n’ingorane zitari nke. Ivy’ugukenera amaserver ntibijanye n’ugukenera gushusha, kandi amashirahamwe ntashobora gukoresha ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none bw’amaserver yabo mu buryo bubereye. Hariho kandi n’imipaka y’uburebure bw’uruja n’uruza abantu bashobora kugira. Ivyo vyose bibuza iyo sosiyete gutuma ivyo bikoresho bizimvye bitanga inyungu canke gutanga umurongo ushikamye wo kuri Internet ata bibanza vy’amakuru bishobora gufata igihe ubushuhe butakenewe.


> Ubushuhe buva kuri mudasobwa yawe ntibupfa ubusa iyo ukeneye gushusha inzu yawe. Iyo ukoresheje ubushuhe bw’umuyagankuba aho uba, rero ubushuhe buva kuri mudasobwa yawe ntibupfa ubusa. Bigura gutyo nyene iyo generate ubu bushuhe ukoresheje mudasobwa yawe. Niba ufise uburyo bwo gushusha buzimvye kuruta umuyagankuba, rero imyanda iri gusa mu ntandukaniro y’ibiciro. Nimba ari mu ci ukoresha ivyuma bikoresha umuyaga, rero ni kabiri.
> Bitcoin Mining ikwiye kuba aho bihendutse. Kumbure hazoba aho ikirere kiri Cold kandi aho ubushuhe bukoreshwa n’amashanyarazi, aho Mining yobaye ubuntu.

_Nk'uko Satoshi Nakamoto yabivuze ku wa 8 Myandagaro 2010._


Bitcoin na Proof of Work yayo ziratandukanye kuko zihita zihindura ingorane za Mining zishingiye ku rugero rw’ubuhinga bwo guharura bukorwa n’urubuga rwose, urwo rugero rwitwa Hashrate kandi rugaragazwa mu ma hashes ku segonda. Ubu bigereranywa ko ari Exahashes 280 ku segonda, canke hashes miliyaridi 280 ku segonda. Iyi Hashrate igereranya igikorwa rero n’ingero y’inguvu zikoreshwa. Uko Hashrate igenda irakura niko ingorane zigenda zirushiriza, n’ibihushanye n’ivyo. Gutyo, Bitcoin Miner irashobora gukora canke guhagarara igihe cose ataco ihinduye ku rubuga, bitandukanye n’ivyo bita radiator/servers vyokenera kuguma bishikamye kugira ngo bishobore gutanga igikorwa cavyo. Miner irahembwa kubera igikorwa gikorwa ugereranije n’igikorwa c’abandi, naho iyo nkuru yoba ari ntoyi gute.


Mu ncamake, radiator y’umuyagankuba n’umuyagankuba Bitcoin Miner vyose bitanga ubushuhe bwa kW 1 ku kW 1 y’umuyagankuba ukoreshwa. Ariko rero, Miner na yo nyene iraronka ama bitcoins nk’impera. Utitaye ku giciro c’umuyagankuba, igiciro ca Bitcoin, canke uguhiganwa kw’ibikorwa vya Mining ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, birarushiriza kuba vyiza mu vy’ubutunzi gushushanya ukoresheje Miner aho gukoresha radiator y’umuyagankuba.



### Agaciro kongerewe kuri Bitcoin


Ntituzoja mu ndondoro y’ibikorwa vya Mining hano (ibikoresho biri ku mashure yisumbuye nimba bikenewe). Igihambaye gutahura ni ingene Mining igira ico ikoze mu kwegereza ubutegetsi Bitcoin. Ivyuma vyinshi vyariho vyarahurijwe hamwe mu buryo bw’ubuhinga kugira ngo ivyo Nakamoto yemeranyako bibeho. Ukwo kwumvikana guhemba mu vy’ubutunzi abakozi b’inzirabugunge kubera uruhara bagize mu bikorwa vy’urubuga rwa Bitcoin, mu gihe rica intege abakozi b’inzirabugunge. Ivyo ni bimwe mu bintu nyamukuru bituma iyo nzira ibaho mu buryo buramba.


Abakinyi b’inzirabugunge, abacukura hakurikijwe amategeko, bose bariko barahiganwa kugira ngo baronke umugabane munini ushoboka w’impera yo guhingura amabuye mashasha. Ivyo bitera intege ubutunzi mu bisanzwe bijana mu buryo bwo gushiramwo ibintu vyose hamwe uko amashirahamwe ahitamwo kwihariza muri ico gikorwa gitanga inyungu mu kugabanya ibiciro vyayo biciye mu bukungu bw’urugero. Abo bakozi b’inganda barafise ikibanza ciza co kugura no gucungera imashini, hamwe no guhanahana ibiciro vy’amashanyarazi menshi.


> Mu ntango, benshi mu bakoresha bokoresha ama node y’urubuga, ariko uko urubuga rwagenda rurakura rukarenga urugero runaka, rwari gusigazwa cane n’abahinga bafise amatongo y’abakozi b’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwihariye. Itongo ry’abakozi ryokenera gusa gukoresha node imwe ku rubuga maze LAN isigaye igafatanya n’iyo node imwe.

_Nk'uko Satoshi Nakamoto yabivuze ku wa 2 Ugushyingo 2008_


Hari ibigo bifise igice kinini c’ijana kw’ijana c’umubare wose w’ama Hashrate mu matongo manini manini y’ama Mining. Turashobora kwihweza umupfunda wa Cold uherutse kuba muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika aho igice kinini ca Hashrate cakuwe hanze y’umurongo kugira ngo ihindure ingufu zijane mu miryango ikeneye amashanyarazi bidasanzwe. Mu misi myinshi, abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bararemeshwa mu vy’ubutunzi kugira ngo bazimye amatongo yabo, gutyo rero turashobora kubona ico kirere kidasanzwe ku nzira y’umurongo wa Bitcoin Hashrate.


Ico kibazo coshobora kuba ingorane kandi kigatera ingorane nyinshi ku kutagira aho twehamiye kw’urubuga. Umukinyi afise ibice birenga 51% vya Hashrate yoshobora gucengera amafaranga y’ibikorwa vyoroshe iyo abishaka. Ni co gituma bihambaye ko Hashrate igabanwa mu bantu benshi aho gukwiragiza inzego zihurikiye hamwe zishobora gufatwa mu buryo bworoshe na Leta, nk’akarorero.


**Iyo abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bakwiragiye mu miryango ibihumbi, canke mbere amamiliyoni kw’isi yose, biragora cane Leta kubagenzura.**


‘Ku ruganda, Miner ntibereye gukoreshwa nk’iradiyeri mu nzu, kubera ingorane zibiri nyamukuru: urusaku rwinshi n’ukudashobora guhindura. Ariko rero, izo ngorane zirashobora gutorwa umuti bitagoranye biciye ku guhindura vyoroshe ibikoresho n’ubuhinga, bikaba bituma habaho Miner icereje cane ishobora gutunganirizwa no kwikoresha nk’ibikoresho vy’umuyagankuba vyo muri iki gihe.


**Attakaï ni umugambi w'inyigisho wigisha ingene wosubiramwo Antminer S9 mu buryo butazimvye.**


Iryo ni akaryo keza cane ko kwiga mu kwimenyereza. Uretse kugabanya amafaranga y’umuyagankuba, urahezagirwa kubera uruhara rwawe n’urupapuro rwa KYC Sats ku buntu.


## Igice ca 1: Inyobora yo kugura ASIC yakoreshejwe


Muri iki gice, tuzovuga ku buryo bwiza bwo kugura Bitmain Antminer S9 ikoreshwa, imashini iyi nyigisho yo gusubiramwo radiator izoshingirako. Iyi nkuru irakora no ku bindi bikoresho vya ASIC kuko ari nkuru rusangi yo kugura ibikoresho vya Mining vyakoreshejwe.


Antminer S9 ni igikoresho gitangwa na Bitmain kuva muri Gitugutu 2016. Gikoresha amashanyarazi 1323W kandi gitanga 13.5 TH/s. Naho ibonwa ko ari iya kera, iguma ari uburyo bwiza cane bwo gutangura Mining. Kubera ko yakozwe mu bwinshi, biroroshe kuronka ibihimba vy’inyuma vyinshi mu turere twinshi two kw’isi. Muri rusangi ishobora kuronswa n’abandi ku mbuga nka Ebay canke LeBonCoin, kuko abagurisha b’abahinga batagiyitanga kubera ko idashobora guhangana n’ibindi bikoresho bishasha. Ni nto cane kuruta ASICs nka Antminer S19, zashizweho kuva muri Ntwarante 2020, ariko ivyo bituma iba hardware ikoreshwa ishoboka kandi ibereye cane ivyo tuzohindura.


Antminer S9 iza mu mice myinshi (i, j) izana utuhinduko dutoduto ku bikoresho vy’uruvyaro rwa mbere. Ntitwemera ko ivyo bikwiye kugira ico bikoze ku ngingo yawe yo kugura, kandi iyi nsiguro izokora kuri ivyo bihinduka vyose.


Igiciro ca ASICs kirahindagurika bivanye n’ibintu vyinshi nk’igiciro ca Bitcoin, ingorane z’uruja n’uruza, ubushobozi bw’imashini, n’igiciro c’amashanyarazi. Ni co gituma bigoye gutanga ingereranyo y’ukuri y’ugugura imashini ikoreshwa. Muri Ruhuhuma 2023, igiciro citezwe mu Bufaransa muri rusangi kiri hagati y’amayero 100 n’amayero 200, ariko ivyo bici- ro birashobora guhinduka ningoga.

## Igice ca 3 - INYIGISHO: Ni gute wohindura Miner ngo ibe Heater?


Nimba uri umuhinga mu vyo gukora ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa none kandi uriko urarondera guhindura Miner ngo ibe igikoresho co gushushanya, iyi nyigisho ni iyawe. Turashaka kubaburira yuko guhindura igikoresho c’ubuhinga bwa none bishobora gutuma haba ingorane z’amashanyarazi n’umuriro. Ni ngombwa gufata ingingo zose zikenewe kugira wirinde ivyonona canke gukomeretswa.

Inyuma y’uruganda, Miner ntishobora gukoreshwa vy’ukuri nk’iradiyeri mu nzu kubera ko igira urusaku rwinshi kandi ntishobora guhindurwa. Ariko rero, birashoboka ko umuntu ahindura ibintu vyoroshe kugira ngo ivyo bibazo bitorerwe umuti.


**Iciyumviro:** Ni ngombwa ko uba warashizeho Braiins OS+ kuri Miner yawe canke uwundi murongo wose ushobora kugabanya ubushobozi bw’imashini yawe. Ico gipimo ni ikintu gihambaye cane kuko, kugira ngo tugabanye urusaku, tuzoshiraho **ibikoresho bifise ubushobozi buke bishobora gukuraho ubushuhe buke**.

### Gutera isaha n'isaha nke


Overclocking na autotuning vyose birimwo guhindura incuro ku makarita ya hashing kugira ngo ASIC ikore neza. Itandukaniro hagati y’ivyo bibiri riri mu kuntu ivyo bimenyetso vy’incuro bikomeye.


**Overclocking** ni uguhindura vyoroshe birimwo kwongera incuro ku makarita ya hashing kugira ngo wongere igipimo ca Hash c’imashini. Ku rundi ruhande, gukoresha isaha iri munsi y’isaha, birimwo kugabanya incuro z’isaha y’uruzitiro rwinshi ruri munsi y’incuro zayo z’izina. Mu kugabanya incuro isaha y’iyi ASIC biciye mu gukoresha isaha y’inyuma, ubushuhe buva ku bikoresho na bwo nyene buragabanywa. Ivyo bituma umuvuduko w’ibikoresho bikoreshwa mu gukonjesha ASIC ugabanuka, kuko bidategerezwa gukora nka Hard kugira ngo bigumye bifise ubushuhe bukwiye. Mu kugabanya umuvuduko w’umuyaga, urusaku ruterwa na ASIC na rwo nyene ruragabanywa. Ivyo bishobora kuba ngirakamaro cane cane ku bakoresha bakoresha ASICs muhira kandi bariko barondera kugabanya urusaku ruterwa n’ibikoresho vya Mining.


Ni vyiza kumenya ko gutera imbere bishobora gutuma ASIC igabanuka, ni ngombwa rero kurondera uburinganire bwiza hagati y’ibikorwa n’urusaku.


Braiins OS+ ifasha gukoresha igihe kirenze, gukoresha igihe gito c’ama ASIC, no guhindura igihe c’ama ASIC. Ituma abakoresha bashobora guhindura incuro isaha y’ibikoresho vyabo mu buryo bubereye kugira ngo bashobore gukora neza canke bazigame inguvu bivanye n’ivyo bakunda.


### Ukwitunganya


Imbere y’umwaka wa 2018, abacukuzi bari bafise uburyo bubiri bwo kuronka inyungu mu gikorwa cabo: kuronka amashanyarazi ku giciro gisanzwe no kugura ibikoresho bikora neza kuruta. Ariko mu mwaka w’2018, harabonetse iterambere rishasha mu bijanye n’ubuhinga bwa none n’ubuhinga bwa none bwa Mining, bwitwa AsicBoost. Ubwo buryo buratuma abacukuzi bagabanya amahera bakoresha hafi 13% mu guhindura porogarama zikoreshwa ku bikoresho vyabo.

Ubu, hariho iterambere rishasha mu bijanye n’ubuhinga bwa none n’ubuhinga bwa none Mining ryitwa autotuning, ritanga akamaro kanini kuruta AsicBoost. ASICs zigizwe n’utugingo ngengabuzima dutoduto twinshi twa mudasobwa dukora hashing. Ivyo bice bikozwe muri silikoni, kikaba ari co kintu nyene gikoreshwa cane mu bikoresho bikoreshwa n’umuyagankuba be n’ibindi bikoresho bikoreshwa n’umuyagankuba. Ico dutahura aha ni uko ibice vyose vya silikoni bitasa - kimwe cose gishobora guhinduka gatoyi mu bijanye n’umuyagankuba waco. Abahinguzi b’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none barabizi kandi baratangaza ivyerekeye ubushobozi bw’amamashini yabo Mining bishingiye ku rugero rwo hasi rw’ivyo bashobora kwihanganira. Mu yandi majambo, abahinguzi barazi incuro ikora neza ku bice vy’umubiri biri hagati kandi bakoresha iyo nkuru mu buryo bumwe ku bice vyose biri mu mashini.


Ivyo bishira umupaka wo hejuru ku rugero rwa Hash imashini ishobora kugira. Autotuning ni uburyo aho ubuhinga busuzuma incuro nziza zo gukora hashing y’ibice ku bice, aho gufata imashini yose nk’ikintu kimwe. Ivyo bisigura ko igice c’agaciro kanini gishobora gukora ama hashes menshi ku segonda kizoronka incuro nyinshi, igice c’agaciro gatoyi gishobora gukora ama hashes make cane kizoronka incuro nke cane. Chip-level autotuning ni uburyo bwo gutuma ASIC ikora neza biciye ku maporogarama n’amaporogarama akoreshwa kuri yo.


Iherezo ry’ivyo ni igipimo kinini c’umuyagankuba Hash ku watt, ivyo bikaba bisobanura ko abacukuzi bashobora kuronka inyungu nyinshi. Impamvu imashini zidakwiragizwa n'ubwo bwoko bwa porogaramu ni uko uguhinduka kw'imashini kudakwiriye, kuko abaguzi bashaka kumenya neza ivyo bariko bararonka, kandi rero ni iciyumviro kibi ku bahinguzi kugurisha igicuruzwa kidafise ubushobozi buhoraho kandi bushobora gutegekanirwa kuva ku mashini imwe gushika ku yindi. Ikindi, autotuning ku rugero rwa chip bisaba uburyo bwinshi bwo guteza imbere, kuko biragoye gushirwa mu ngiro. Abahinguzi baramaze gukoresha amahera menshi mu guhingura ama firmware yabo. Hariho ubuhinga bwo gutorera umuti ibibazo bishobora gutuma umuntu ashobora guhindura ibintu, nka Braiins OS+. Uretse no gutuma ASIC ikora neza gushika ku bice 20%.
