Muri iyi nyigisho, turaza kuraba ingene twoshiraho Canaan Avalon Nano 3S, kugira ngo ukoreshe Miner mu rugo.



Kugeza ubu, imashini za ASIC (*Igikoresho c’Ikoreshwa ry’Igikoresho*) zagenewe gukora igikorwa kinaka - muri iki gihe, uguharura Hash (SHA-256) ku Miner ya Bitcoin - ntizari zibereye cane gukoreshwa mu rugo. Urusaku rutera umubabaro, ubushuhe buva muri ivyo be n’uko bikenewe guhindura amashanyarazi yawe kugira ngo ashobore gushigikira ububasha buhambaye bw’ivyo bikoresho vyatumye benshi muri twebwe tutagira uruhara muri ivyo.



Uyu musi, Canaan, imwe mu mashirahamwe akomeye akora imashini za ASIC, yafashe ingingo yo guhangana n’isoko ry’abantu ku giti cabo bashaka Miner muhira, mu gutanga ibikoresho bitandukanye bimeze nk’ibicereje kandi vyoroshe cane gushiramwo (plug & play).



Ivyo bikoresho bigurishwa nk’igikoresho co gushushanya co gufasha mu gihe c’i **Avalon Nano 3S (140W)**, canke nk’igikoresho co gushushanya gifise ubushobozi bwa **800W** mu gihe c’i **Avalon Mini 3**.



https://planb.network/tutorials/mining/hardware/canaan-avalon-mini-f2185435-10a3-4d7b-b88f-f1a489babab7

Turagusavye uzirikane ko itandukaniro ry’igiciro n’ibikoresho vya kera vy’ugushushanya vy’ububasha bungana, mu bihe vyinshi, ritaguha uburenganzira bwo kuronka inyungu y’amahera. Ivyo bita satoshis biva ku gikorwa ca Mining ntibizokwigera bisubiza inyuma iyo ntandukaniro y’igiciro, kiretse iyo ufise amashanyarazi y’ubuntu (arengeye) canke atari menshi cane.



Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, ivyo bikoresho bikwiye gufatwa cane nk'uburyo bworoshe bwo gushika ku Miner muhira ku bipfuza kubigira kubera imvo z'umuntu ku giti ciwe: *kuronka Satss zitari za KYC / gukina "loteri" mu gukora solominate / kugira uruhara mu kwegereza Hashrate n'ibindi. Ariko si nk’uburyo bwo kuzigama amahera mu bihe vyinshi n’imiburiburi (ibihugu vy’uburengero).

## Guhuza na Mining pool



**Iki gice ni ikintu gisanzwe kuri Nano 3s na Mini 3, kuko uburyo bwo gukora burasa cane**.



Twaba dushaka "solominate" canke "pool" ya Miner, tuzobwirizwa kwifatanya na Mining pool. Nkako, igikoresho cacu nta kindi ariko ni igikoresho co gukora Hash ataco azi ku bijanye n’uruja n’uruza rwa Bitcoin. Kuyifatanya n’ikidengeri biyiha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha urubuga rwa Bitcoin, kandi bikayiha uburenganzira bwo kwakira ibigereranyo vy’amabuye yo gukorako.



### Gukoresha porogarama kugira ngo uhuze na Mining pool



Ku rubuga rwa Avalon Family, hitamwo igikoresho nk’uko vyerekanwa aha hepfo. Uzoca usabwa guhindura ijambobanga ry’umuyobozi w’iyo mashini. Fyonda kuri "OK" niba ushaka kubikora, canke kuri cancel kugira ngo usige ijambobanga ry'imbere "admin".


![image](assets/fr/16.webp)


Hanyuma uhitemwo "Ivyagezwe", hanyuma "Pool Config" hanyuma winjize amaparametere y'ibidengeri 3 vyasabwe.


Pools #2 na #3 zizokora nk’ivy’inyuma iyo kimwe muri vyo kizonanirwa, kugira ngo Miner yawe ntikore ubusa. Ku mburabuzi, Hashrate izokwerekezwa ku kidengeri #1.



Mu gihe cacu duhitamwo:




- 1 - Ikidengeri ca bose,
- 2 - CkIkidengeri,
- 3 - Ikiyaga.



![image](assets/fr/17.webp)



Ku bindi bisobanuro ku buryo bwo kwifatanya na Mining pool, urashobora kuraba aya nyigisho :



https://planb.network/tutorials/mining/pool/public-pool-42b9e1b5-722d-471d-b1e3-9ca758065be1

https://planb.network/tutorials/mining/pool/ocean-pool-30c9e2c9-2364-44a1-bae0-2afbdb8b1c9c

Mu guca irya n’ino, turakeneye .





- ikidengeri Address, akenshi **igice+tcp://xxxxxxxx:icuma**.





- izina ry'"umukozi" rigizwe na *Bitcoin yawe Address* n'*ikinyoma* uhisemwo ku gikoresho cawe, ivyo 2 bitandukanijwe n'akadomo*, nk'akarorero:**bc1qxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx.MonAvalonNano3S**





- ijambobanga, risanzwe ari "**x**".



Amakuru y'ikidengeri amaze kwinjira, kanda kuri "Bika".



![image](assets/fr/18.webp)


Subira ufungure igikoresho nk’uko vyategetswe, hanyuma urindire iminota mikeyi gushika Hashrate yawe yerekanwe ku kidengeri ukunda (#1).



### Gukoresha umucukumbuzi kugira ngo uhuze na Mining pool



Ushobora kandi kwifatanya na Mining pool kandi, muri rusangi, ushobora gushika ku nzira y’uburongozi bwa Interface y’igikoresho cawe biciye ku mucukumbuzi ukunda.



Kugira ngo ubikore, wandike mu murongo w’ubushakashatsi wa mucukumbuzi IP Address y’igikoresho kigaragara ku rubuga ruri musi, mu karorero kacu **192.168.144.6**



![image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Iyi paji ikurikira izoboneka, igusabe gufungura porogarama ya Avalon Family maze ugacapura kode ya QR yerekanwa n’iyo porogarama.



![image](assets/fr/20.webp)



Ugurure iyo porogarama, hanyuma ukande ku bimenyetso 3 biri hejuru iburyo, hanyuma ukande kuri scan. Scan code QR ya browser, hanyuma winjize ijambobanga ry’umuyobozi wa porogarama hanyuma ukande OK.



![image](assets/fr/21.webp)



Aha uri kuri paji y’urubuga igufasha gukorana na Avalon yawe. Ni nk'urubaho rwo kwerekana ibipimo vy'imashini, kuruta uburyo bwo kuyitunganya.



Ariko amasetingi ya pool arashobora gushikwako muri ubu buryo, ukanda kuri **"Pool Config "** mu mfuruka iburyo hasi.



![image](assets/fr/22.webp)



Mu buryo bumwe nk'uko biri kuri porogarama ya telefone ngendanwa, urashobora kwinjiza amaparametere yawe y'ikidengeri hano.



![image](assets/fr/23.webp)



## Gucungera igikoresho cawe ukoresheje porogaramu y'umuryango wa Avalon



Ubu twahuje Miner yo mu nzu yacu n’urubuga rwacu rwo mu karere, maze twerekana Hashrate yacu ku bidengeri vy’amabuye y’agaciro. None rero reka tubone ibintu nyamukuru vy’imashini yacu biciye ku rubuga rwa Avalon Family.



Mu gikorwa c’umuryango wa Avalon, fyonda ku kimenyetso gihuye na Avalon Nano 3S.


rero uboneke n'ibikubiyemo 3: "Uburyo bwo gukora", "Igenzura ry'umuco", na "Ivyagezwe". Mbere, fyonda ku "Uburyo bwo gukora". Uzoca uhabwa uburyo 3 bw’ububasha bw’imashini yawe.



**Low**: ikuzanira hafi 3 Th/s ya Hashrate ku 70W y'inguvu zikoreshwa


**Ikigereranyo**: kikuzanira nk’ibiharuro. 4.5 Th/s ya Hashrate ku 100W y'inguvu zikoreshwa


**High**: izoguha nk'ama Th/s 6 ya Hashrate ku gukoresha 140W



![image](assets/fr/31.webp)


Reka dusubire inyuma maze tubone "Igenzura ry'umuco" menu. Ivyo ni ivyo kwisiga gusa. Hariho uburyo bwinshi bwo guhitamwo ibara ritandukanye, uburemere, ubushuhe, kuzimya amatara y’igikoresho mw’ijoro n’ibindi... Biroroshe kumenya wewe nyene.



![image](assets/fr/32.webp)


![image](assets/fr/33.webp)



![image](assets/fr/34.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, urutonde rwa nyuma dufise ni urutonde rwa "Settings" twamaze kubona rwo gufatanya n'ibidengeri vyacu vya Mining. Aha urashobora gucunga ibidengeri vyawe, guhindura ijambobanga ry’umuyobozi w’igikoresho, no kwihweza ibipimo bitandukanye nk’itariki garanti izoherako, isuku ry’akayunguruzo, canke ingene wohamagara abafasha mu gihe co kunanirwa.



![image](assets/fr/35.webp)


Kugira ngo iyo ncunguru ikomeze gusukurwa neza uko bishoboka kwose, turahimiriza gukoresha igikoresho co gusukura no gusukura ubudasiba aho umuyaga winjira n’aho usohokera kugira ngo ntuzibire.
