### Itandukaniro hagati ya Bitcoin core na Bitcoin Ipfundo

Bitcoin core ni porogaramu ishoboza nk’ibice 99% vy’ibice vya Bitcoin kw’isi yose gukora. Bitcoin ni protocole, bisobanura ko, nka Internet, aho hariho ama browser menshi, hashobora kubaho porogarama zitandukanye zikorana kuri TimeChain imwe. Ariko rero, kubera uguhagarika umutima ku bijanye n'uguhuza no kugabanya ingorane z'ibikoko vyoshobora gusiga ibimenyetso bidashobora gukurwaho kuri TimeChain, hafi abahinguzi bose ba Bitcoin bakora kuri Bitcoin core. Bitcoin Knots ni Fork ya Bitcoin core, bisobanura ko isangira igice kinini c’amakode yabo, igabanura cane ingorane z’ibikoko. Iyi Fork yaremwe na Luke Dashjr, yashaka gukoresha amategeko abuza cane kuruta Bitcoin core ata Hard Fork yaremye. Ubu, Bitcoin core na Bitcoin Knots biraba kumwe kubera amasezerano ya Nakamoto.
