## mining pool ni iki?



Cryptocurrency mining irimwo gutanga ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukingira urubuga rwa blockchain no kwemeza amafaranga akoreshwa. Mu gusubiza, miners zironka impembo mu buryo bw’amahera y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none.



Ku bijanye na **solo mining**, miner akora wenyene kandi aronka impembo gusa iyo ashoboye kuronka block. Ubwo buryo ntibushoboka ku bakoresha benshi muri iki gihe, kubera uguhiganwa n’ububasha bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none bisabwa.



**mining pool** ni umuti rusangi: miner nyinshi zikoranya ubushobozi bwazo bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none (hashrate). Iyo habonetse ububiko, impembo irasangira hagati y’abayigize, bivanye n’intererano yabo.



## F2pool incamake



**f2pool** ni imwe mu ndege za kera kandi nini cane za mining pool kw'isi yose. Yashinzwe mu 2013, ikoreshwa n’abantu ku giti cabo miner, amatongo y’inganda n’abakozi b’umwuga bakwiragiye mu bihugu vyinshi.



**f2pool** iremeza miner gukoresha amafaranga menshi, ikoresheje ubwoko butandukanye bw’ibikoresho (ASIC, GPU), mu gihe itanga :





- ido n'ido ry'ikurikirana,
- kwishura ubudasiba,
- amahitamwo ateye imbere nk’aya mining mining,
- ibikoresho vy’uburongozi ku batangura n’abazi miners.



## F2pool igaragaza ido n'ido



### Amateka n'ukwizerwa



**f2pool** ni mining pool yashinzwe mu 2013, mu gihe ibidukikije vya Bitcoin vyari bikiri mu nzira yo gutunganya. Yaciye yihuta kwishingira intahe nk’umukinyi akomeye mu bijanye n’ubuhinga bwa mining kubera ugushikama kwayo, ukwizigirwa kw’ibikorwa remezo vyayo no kwemerwa n’abahinga benshi ba miner.



Kubera ko f2pool imaze imyaka irenga cumi ikora, ni kimwe mu bidengeri vy’amateka vy’iyo nganda. Ihora iri mu bibanza bihambaye kw’isi mu bijanye n’ububasha bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none (hashrate), cane cane kuri Bitcoin n’izindi nzira zikomeye.



Ukwo kubaho igihe kirekire ni ikimenyetso gihambaye c’ukwizigirwa mu bidukikije aho ibikorwa vyinshi biboneka hanyuma bikazimangana ningoga.

### Ubuhinga bwa mining bushigikiwe



Ifaranga ryose ry’ibanga ryishimikije ubuhinga bumwe bumwe bwa mining. f2pool ishigikira ubuhinga bwinshi, harimwo :





- SHA-256 (Bitcoin n'ibiva kuri vyo)
- Inyandiko (Igiceri)

Guhitamwo algorithme ni vyo bigena ubwoko bw’ibikoresho bizokoreshwa, hamwe n’inguvu zikoreshwa be n’inyungu zishobora gushikako.



### Ubwoko bw'ibikoresho bihuye



**f2pool** yagenewe gukorana n'ubwoko butandukanye bw'ibikoresho vya mining:





- ASIC**: ibikoresho vyihariye bifise ubushobozi bwinshi ku bijanye n'ubuhinga bumwe (nk'akarorero SHA-256, Scrypt). Iyi ni yo nzira nziza cane ku mining Bitcoin na Litecoin.
- GPU**: amakarata y’ibishushanyo ashobora gukoreshwa mu buryo bwinshi, ahuye n’ubuhinga bumwe bumwe. Akenshi zikoreshwa mu guhindura amafaranga y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none.



Ico kigega nta n’amategeko yihariye gishiraho ku bimenyetso canke ku bigereranyo vy’ibikoresho, igihe cose ivy’ugutunganya bihuye n’ingingo z’ubuhinga zikenewe.



## Uko mining pool ikora



### Solo n'amazi mining



Solo mining** irimwo miner imwe igerageza gutorera umuti ikibazo ku rubuga rwa blockchain. Iyo habonetse igice, impembo yose isubira kuri miner. Mu bikorwa, iyo nzira isaba ubushobozi bwinshi cane bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none kandi ijana n’ibihe vy’impera bitari vyo cane, rimwe na rimwe bikamara amezi canke imyaka myinshi.



Pool mining**, ku rundi ruhande, ishingiye ku gukoranya ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa miner nyinshi. Umwe wese mu bari muri iyo nama aratanga hashrate yiwe, kandi iyo pool ibonye block, impembo irasangira hagati y’abanywanyi hakurikijwe amategeko yategekanijwe imbere y’igihe.



Ku bantu benshi n'abato miners, pool mining itanga **ugushikama kw'amahera** kurusha solo mining.



### Ivyiyumviro vya hashrate n'imigabane



**hashrate** igereranya ubushobozi bwo guharura butangwa na miner canke ikidengeri. Muri rusangi igaragazwa mu H/s (hashes ku segonda), canke mu bipimo vy’agaciro kanini nka MH/s, GH/s, TH/s canke PH/s.



Mu mining pool, miners ntizikora ataco zihinduye ku nzira yuzuye y’ibarabara. Batanga **imigabane**, ni ukuvuga ibimenyamenya vy’igice c’akazi. Ivyo bice bituma iyo pool ishobora gupima neza intererano ya miner yose.



Uko miner itanga imigabane igira akamaro, ni ko n’intererano yiwe mu kidengeri iba nini, kandi ni ko n’umugabane wiwe w’impera uba munini.



### Uko ikidengeri kironka ibuye



mining pool itanga ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa none ku ma miner yose ahuye. Iyo umwe mu bari muri iyo nama aronse umuti ubereye uhuye n’ingorane y’urubuga, iyo nzira irashikirizwa blockchain.



Naho miner imwe gusa yoba ari yo ibona mu vy’ukuri iyo block, iyo mpera ihabwa **pool yose uko ingana**, hanyuma igasubira guhabwa abayigize bose hakurikijwe intererano yabo nk’uko ipimwa n’imigabane.



### Gutanga ibihembo



Ugusangira impembo mu kidengeri bishingiye ku **uburyo bwo kwishura** busobanurwa n’uwujejwe ikidengeri. Ubwo buryo buragira ingaruka zitaziguye ku bijanye n’uguhora umuntu aronka amahera no gufata ingorane.



Kuri f2pool, uburyo bwo kwishura busanzwe burimwo :





- PPS (Pay Per Share)**: umugabane wose ufise akamaro urihwa ubwo nyene, ataco bimaze ko pool ironka ububiko canke atarivyo.
- PPS+**: PPS ihinduka ryinjiza mu gutanga amafaranga y’ugucuruza hamwe n’impera y’ububiko.



Ivyo bigereranyo bishimika ku **gushobora gutegekanya inyungu**, ivyo bikaba bisigura ukuntu bikundwa n’aba miner b’abahinga.



### Uruhara rw'amafaranga y'amazi



Kugira ngo f2pool ishobore kwishura ibikorwa remezo, ivyo gusanasana n’ivyo guteza imbere, ikoresha **amahera y’ibarabara**. Ivyo bica bikurwako ubwavyo ku ngororano imbere y’uko umuntu yishura.



Igitigiri c’amahera gihinduka bivanye n’amahera y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none be n’uburyo bwo kwishura bukoreshwa. Ni vyiza ko ivyo ubizirikana igihe usuzuma inyungu yose y’ubucuruzi bwa mining.



### Kubera iki uhisemwo ikidengeri nka f2pool



Guhitamwo mining pool yemewe nka f2pool bigufasha kwungukira kuri :





- ibikorwa remezo bikomeye kandi bikora neza cane,
- gukurikirana neza intererano n'inyungu,
- kwishura ubudasiba, bishobora kumenyekana,
- igabanuka rikomeye ry’ugutandukana kw’amahera umuntu aronka.

## Inyungu za f2pool



Inyungu nyamukuru za f2pool ni :





- ibikorwa remezo bikomeye kandi bishikamye,
- amafaranga akomeye n’ukwishura ubudasiba,
- ikigaragara gitomoye co kugenzura ibikorwa,
- gushigikira mining ihuriweko ku nzira zimwe zimwe,
- n’ibindi bikoresho vyinshi vya mining n’ibindi bikoresho biri kw’isoko.



Ivyo bisigura igituma f2pool ikoreshwa cane n’indege za miner z’ingero zose.



## Imipaka n'ingingo zo kwitwararika



Cokimwe n'ikidengeri cose gihurikiye hamwe, f2pool nayo irafise aho igarukira:





- kwisunga uwundi muntu akoresha,
- amafaranga y'amazi aratandukanye bivanye n'amahera y'ibanga,
- interface y’ubutunzi yoshobora gusa n’iyigoye ku batangura.



Ivyo bintu si intambamyi zikomeye, ariko bikwiye kwitwararikwa igihe uhitamwo mining pool.



## Iciyumviro
