Igikarabiro ca Braiins, mbere citwa igikarabiro ca Slush, ni co ca mbere Bitcoin Mining pool. Yashinzwe mu kwezi kwa cumi na kumwe 2010, yacukuye igice ca mbere ku wa 16 Kigarama 2010, igice ca 97834.


Kuva muri Rusama 2024, Braiins Pool ifise ubushobozi bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa 13 EH/s, bikaba bigereranya nk'ibice 1,8% vy'umubare wose wa Bitcoin Hashrate. Yacukuye ama bitcoins 1.307.188, ni ukuvuga hafi 6% vy’ama bitcoins agera ku miriyoni 21 azokwigera abaho.


### Uburyo bwo gutanga indishi


Kuva mu mpera z’umwaka w’2023, Braiins Pool yarahinduye uburyo bwo gutanga indishi kugira ngo ikoreshe uburyo bwa FPPS (Full Pay Per Share). Ivyo bisigura ko abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro baronka impembo buri musi ku bikorwa vyose bakoze kuva umusi uheze, naho nyene ico kidengeri coba kitaronse ibuye. Ivyo bitandukanye n’uburyo bwa kera aho abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro baronka gusa impembo iyo ikidengeri kibonye ibuye.


**Birabereye kumenya, [nk’uko bivugwa ku rubuga rwa Twitter rwa Mononaut](https://x.com/mononautical/status/1777686545715089605) uwo asesangura uruhererekane rw’igihe rwa Bitcoin, ko ibidengeri vyinshi vya Mining bikoresheje ubuhinga bwa FPPS vyorungika ku bidengeri vya Mining vyacukuwe bi- AntPool ni yo igenzura Hashrate y'ivyo bidengeri vyose, nk'ibice 47% vy'isi yose Bitcoin Hashrate. Iyi ni inkuru mbi cane ku bijanye n’ugusenyura urubuga.**


### Amafaranga y'ikidengeri


Amafaranga ya Braiins Pool ni 2.5%, ariko iyo ukoresheje BraiinsOS ku mashini zawe amafaranga azoba 0%

## Kwongera "umukozi"

Umukozi ni Miner uzohuza n’ikidengeri. Kugira ngo wongereko Miner nshasha, fyonda ku "Abakozi" mu rutonde rw'ibubamfu.

![signup](assets/7.webp)


Hanyuma ukande kuri buto y'ibara ry'umuyugubwe "Huza abakozi +".


Muri iri dirisha, hitamwo aho uba.


Niba Miner ushaka gufatanya ikoresha BraiinsOS, hitamwo umurongo wa "Stratum V2". Ahandiho, hitamwo "Igice ca V1".


![signup](assets/8.webp)


Uzogira amakuru yo kwinjiza kuri paji y’urubuga rwa Miner yawe (raba inyandiko za Miner yawe kugira umenye aho woshira ayo makuru).


Aha, **"umurongo+utcp://umurongo.ubwonko.com:3333"** ni URL y'ikidengeri.


**JimZap.Izina ry'Umukozi** ni ikimenyetso cawe n'izina rya Miner yawe, aho JimZap ari ikimenyetso na .Izina ry'Umukozi ni izina rya Miner. Niba ufise abacukuzi benshi, urashobora kubaha izina rimwe, muri ivyo ububasha bwabo bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none buzokwongerwa hamwe ku rutonde rw’ibikoresho, canke ukabaha amazina atandukanye kugira ngo ubakurikirane umwumwe wese ku giti ciwe.


Kandi ijambobanga ryama ari rimwe **"ikintu cose123"**


Uhejeje kwinjiza ayo makuru kuri paji yawe ya Miner kandi ikaba yatanguye Mining, uzobona aboneka inyuma y’iminota mikeyi ku rubuga rwa Braiins Pool.


## Incamake y'urubaho


![signup](assets/9.webp)


Mu bendera yo hejuru, urashobora kubona umubare wose w’ama Hashrate y’abacukuzi bawe bose mu minota 5, isaha 1, n’amasaha 24. Kandi incamake y’umubare w’abacukuzi bari kuri internet canke hanze yayo.

Hasi, hariho urutonde rugufasha gukurikirana ingene ububasha bwawe bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none bugenda buratera imbere.


![signup](assets/10.webp)


Munsi y'iki kigereranyo, ufise amakuru menshi yerekeye ingororano zawe muri Sats.


**"Ivyiza vya Mining vy'uyu musi"** Bigaragaza umubare wa Sats wacukuye uno musi. Umusi uheze, ako gaciro kazosubirwamwo kuri 0 maze Sats izorungikwa kuri konti yawe.


**"Impembo yose y'ejo"** Umubare wa Sats mwacukuye umusi umwe imbere y'aho


**"Est. Inyungu (1 TH/s)"** Ni ingereranyo y'umubare w'ama Sats uronka mu musi umwe ku bushobozi bwo guharura bwa 1 TH/s. Nk’akarorero, nimba agaciro ari 77 Sats, kandi ufise ubushobozi bwo guharura 10 TH/s ubudasiba umusi wose, rero mu vy’impwemu woba uronka 77 x 10 = 770 Sats ku musi.


**"Impembo y'Igihe Cose"** Igitigiri cose ca Sats mwacukuye na Braiins Pool


**"Igishushanyo c'impera"** Igiciro c'amahera gikoreshwa n'ikidengeri


**"Next Payout ETA"** Igereranyo ry'igihe bizofata imbere y'uko ushobora gukura Sats ku rubuga. Aha, ingereranyo ntaco yerekana kuko Mining imaze iminota mikeyi gusa iriko iragenda.


**"Account Balance"** Umubare w'ama Sats ari kuri konti yawe, ushobora gukurayo.

## Gutegura ivyo gukura

Ushobora gukuraho impembo zawe On-Chain canke biciye ku muravyo ukoresheje Address.


Hejuru, kanda kuri "Amahera". Ku mburabuzi, ufise "Konti ya Bitcoin". Ushobora kurema abandi kugira ngo basangire ingororano. Ivyo tuzobisubirako mu kigabane gikurikira.


Ubu, fyonda ku "Gutegura".


![signup](assets/17.webp)


Muri iri dirisha rishasha, ushobora guhitamwo "Ukwishura kuri uruzitiro".


Izina ry’iyi Wallet, ushireho BTC Address, hanyuma uhitemwo ubwoko bw’igitera ushaka. "Threshold" bisigura ko ukwishyura kuzotangura iyo umaze kwirundanira umubare wa BTC usobanuwe, kandi n'"Igihe c'igihe", ukwishyura kuzotangura mu bihe bisanzwe (umusi/amayinga/amezi).


Zirikana ko amafaranga makeyi ari 0.0002 BTC kandi ko munsi ya 0.005 BTC, hazokoreshwa amahera 0.0001 BTC.


![signup](assets/18.webp)

Fyonda hejuru y'idirisha kuri "Ukwishura kw'umuravyo".


Injira mu Lightning Address yawe maze ushire akamenyetso mu gasandugu "Ndatahura..." hanyuma ushire mu ngiro.


Ukoresheje ubu buryo bwo gukura, ingororano zawe zizorungikwa kuri Wallet yawe buri musi.

Braiins Pool nayo iragufasha gusangira ingororano zawe mu bikoresho vyinshi.


Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda hejuru kuri "Mining" hanyuma ibubamfu kuri "Settings".


![signup](assets/19.webp)


Kuri iyi paji, uzoshobora kwongerako iyindi "Konti y'Imari" ukanda kuri "Ongera Iyindi Konti y'Imari" maze ugatanga ingororano zawe mu % kuri izo konti zitandukanye utegerezwa gufatanya Wallet. Kugira ngo ufatanye Wallet nshasha na Konti y’Ivy’Imari, raba igice c’imbere.
