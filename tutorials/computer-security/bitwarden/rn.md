Mu gihe c’ubuhinga bwa none, turakeneye gucunga amakonti menshi yo kuri internet avuga ku bintu bitandukanye vyo mu buzima bwacu bwa misi yose, harimwo amabanki, ama platforms y’ivy’amahera, ama email, ububiko bw’amadosiye, ubuzima, uburongozi, imihora y’ubudandaji, imikino ya videwo, n’ibindi.


Kugira ngo twiyemeze kuri buri konti, dukoresha ikimenyetso, akenshi kikaba ari imeyili Address, kijana n’ijambobanga. Kubera ko umuntu adashobora gufata mu mutwe amajambo y’ibanga menshi yihariye, yoshobora kugeragezwa no gusubira gukoresha iryo jambo ry’ibanga nyene canke agahindura gatoyi ishingiro rusangi kugira ngo ashobore kuribuka bitamugoye. Ariko rero, ivyo bikorwa biratuma umutekano wa konti yawe uhungabana cane.


Ingingo ya mbere umuntu akwiye gukurikiza ku bijanye n’amajambo y’ibanga ni ukudasubira kuyakoresha. Konti yose yo kuri Internet ikwiye kurindwa n’ijambobanga ry’umwihariko ritandukanye rwose n’izindi. Ivyo birahambaye kuko, iyo uwugutera ashoboye guhungabanya rimwe mu majambo y’ibanga yawe, ntushaka ko ashobora gushika ku makonti yawe yose. Kugira ijambobanga ry’umwihariko kuri konti imwe imwe biratandukanya ibitero bishobora gutera kandi bikagabanya urugero rwavyo. Nk’akarorero, iyo ukoresheje ijambobanga rimwe ku rubuga rw’imikino ya videwo no kuri e-mail yawe, maze iryo jambobanga rikagira nabi biciye ku rubuga rw’ubuhendanyi rufitaniye isano n’urwo rubuga rw’urukino, uwo muterabwoba yoshobora rero gushika kuri e-mail yawe bitagoranye maze akagenzura izindi konti zawe zose zo kuri Internet.


Ingingo ya kabiri y’ingenzi ni inkomezi z’ijambobanga. Ijambobanga rifatwa nk’irikomeye iyo rigoye gukoresha inguvu z’agahomerabunwa, ni ukuvuga gutekereza biciye mu kugerageza no gukosa. Ivyo bisigura ko amajambo y’ibanga yawe ategerezwa kuba ari ay’impfagusa uko bishoboka kwose, maremare, kandi arimwo inyuguti zitandukanye (inyuguti nto, nini, imibare n’ibimenyetso).


Gushira mu ngiro izo ngingo ngenderwako zibiri z’umutekano w’ijambobanga (ukwiharira n’ugukomera) birashobora kugaragaza ko bigoye mu buzima bwa misi yose, kuko hafi bidashoboka gufata mu mutwe ijambobanga ry’umwihariko, ry’imburakimazi, kandi rikomeye ry’amakonti yacu yose. Aho niho umucungerezi w’ijambobanga aza.


Igikoresho co gucungera amajambo banga kiratanga amajambo banga akomeye kandi akabika mu mutekano, ivyo bikaba bigufasha gushika ku makonti yawe yose yo kuri Internet ataco ukeneye kuyafata mu mutwe umwumwe wese. Ukeneye kwibuka ijambobanga rimwe gusa, ijambobanga ry’umukuru, riguha uburenganzira bwo kuronka amajambobanga yose wabitse mu mucungerezi. Gukoresha umucungerezi w’amajambo y’ibanga biratuma umutekano wawe wo kuri Internet urushiriza kuba mwiza kubera ko bibuza gusubira gukoresha amajambo y’ibanga kandi bikazana amajambo y’ibanga ataco akora. Ariko kandi, biratuma ukoresha neza amakonti yawe ku musi ku musi mu gushiramwo amakuru yawe y’agaciro.

Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzobona ingene woshiraho no gukoresha umucungerezi w’ijambobanga kugira ngo wongere umutekano wawe wo kuri internet. Nzobamenyesha Bitwarden, kandi mu yindi nyigisho, turaza kuraba uwundi muti witwa KeePass.

## Intangamarara ya Bitwarden


Bitwarden ni umucungerezi w’ijambobanga ryiza ku batangura n’abateye imbere. Biratanga ivyiza vyinshi cane. Mbere na mbere, Bitwarden ni umuti w’ibikoresho vyinshi, bisobanura ko ushobora kuyikoresha nk’iporogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa, urubuga rwo ku rubuga, urubuga rwo kwagura umucukumbuzi, n’urubuga rwo ku biro.

![BITWARDEN](assets/notext/01.webp)

Bitwarden iragufasha kubika amajambo banga yawe kuri internet no kuyahuza ku bikoresho vyawe vyose, mu gihe ushobora gukorana n’ijambobanga ryawe ry’ibanga. Ivyo bigufasha, nk’akarorero, kuronka amajambo y’ibanga yawe kuri mudasobwa yawe no kuri telefone yawe ngendanwa, hakaba hariho uguhuza hagati y’ivyo bibiri. Kubera ko amajambo y’ibanga yawe apfutse, aguma ata muntu n’umwe ashobora kuyaronka, harimwo na Bitwarden, ata rufunguzo rwo gufungura ari ryo jambobanga ryawe nyamukuru.


Ikindi kandi, Bitwarden ni open-source, bisobanura ko iyo porogarama ishobora gusuzumwa n’abahinga bigenga. Naho ku bijanye n’igiciro, Bitwarden itanga imigambi itatu:


- Verisiyo y’ubuntu tuzokwihweza muri iyi nyigisho. Naho ari ubuntu, itanga urugero rw’umutekano rungana n’urw’amaverisiyo yishurwa. Ushobora kubika amajambo y’ibanga atagira aho agarukira no gukorana n’ibikoresho vyinshi uko wipfuza;
- Verisiyo y’imbere y’ibindi ku madolari 10 ku mwaka irimwo ibindi bikoresho nk’ububiko bw’amadosiye, gucungera ikarita ya banki, ubushobozi bwo gushinga 2FA n’urufunguzo rw’umutekano w’umubiri, no gushika ku kwemeza TOTP 2FA ataco ukoresheje Bitwarden;
- Kandi umugambi w’umuryango w’amadolari 40 ku mwaka ushobora kwagura inyungu za verisiyo ya premium ku bakoresha batandatu batandukanye.

![BITWARDEN](assets/notext/02.webp)

Njye mbona ivyo bici- ro birabereye. Verisiyo y’ubuntu ni uburyo bwiza cane ku batangura, kandi verisiyo ya premium itanga agaciro keza cane ku mahera ugereranyije n’abandi bacungezi b’amajambobanga bari kw’isoko, mu gihe itanga ibindi bikoresho. Ikindi, kuba Bitwarden ari open-source ni akarusho kanini. Rero, ni ugusenyera ku mugozi umwe gushimishije cane cane ku batangura.

Ikindi kiranga Bitwarden ni ubushobozi bwo kwiyakira umucungerezi w’ijambobanga ryawe nimba ufise nk’akarorero NAS i muhira. Mu gushinga iyo ntunganyo, amajambo banga yawe ntabikwa ku maserukira ya Bitwarden, ahubwo abikwa ku maserukira yawe bwite. Ivyo biguha ububasha bwose bwo kugenzura ukuntu amajambo y’ibanga yawe abonetse. Ariko rero, iyo nzira isaba uburongozi bukomeye bw’ububiko kugira ngo ntihagire uwutakaza uburenganzira bwo kuyironka. Rero, uburyo bwo kwiyakira bwa Bitwarden burabereye cane abakoresha bateye imbere, kandi tuzobivugako mu yindi nyigisho.
