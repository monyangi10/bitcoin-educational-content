## Imenyekanisha



Twese tumara amasaha menshi kuri Internet, kenshi tutazi ivyo umucukumbuzi wacu ariko araduhishurira. Igihe cose dufyonda, igihe cose dushaka, urubuga rwose dusura, bigaburira inganda nyinshi cane zikoranya amakuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe.



![Statistiques navigateurs 2024](assets/fr/01.webp)


*Isoko ry’umucukumbuzi w’urubuga: Chrome ni yo ihambaye n’ibice 65% vy’isoko, ikurikirwa na Safari na Edge. Inkomoko: [gs.igiharuro c'imibare.com](https://gs.igiharuro c'imibare.com/umucukumbuzi-isoko-ry'ugusangira)*



Nk’uko iyi graphe ibigaragaza, Google Chrome ni yo ihambaye cane, ifise ibice birenga 65% vy’ikoreshwa kw’isi yose. Ubwo butegetsi busobanura ko benshi mu bakoresha Internet babizigira amakuru yabo yo gusura amakuru kuri Google, ishirahamwe rifise uburyo bwo gukora ubucuruzi bushingiye ku kwamamaza abantu. Firefox, ifise 3% gusa vy’isoko, iserukira ubundi buryo bwateguwe na Mozilla, ishirahamwe ritagira inyungu ritagira inyungu y’ubudandaji mu gukoresha amakuru yawe.



Ariko guhitamwo Firefox ni intambwe ya mbere gusa. Ku mburabuzi, mbere na Firefox isaba guhindura kugira ngo ushobore gukingira cane. Iyi nkuru iragutwara intambwe ku yindi, kuva ku vyoroshe gushika ku biteye imbere, kugira ngo uhindure Firefox ibe ingabo nyayo yo gukingira gukurikirana mu gihe uzigama uburyo bwiza bwo gusura.



### Kubera iki Firefox?





- Ubuntu kandi yuguruye** (moteri ya Gecko): kode ishobora gusuzumwa, igaragara
- Ishirahamwe ridaharanira inyungu**: Ishirahamwe rya Mozilla, intumbero y'inyungu rusangi
- Uburinzi bw’imvukira bushizwemwo**: Uburinzi bwo gukurikirana (ETP), Uburinzi bwose bw’amakuru (TCP), Ugucapura kwa Leta, uburyo bwa HTTPS gusa, DNS kuri HTTPS (DoH)
- Guhindura neza**: bitandukanye na Chrome, Firefox iragufasha guhindura inyifato yayo mu buryo bwimbitse



### Ingingo ngenderwako zihambaye imbere y’uko utangura .





- Nta nzira y’ugutegura ivy’isi yose**: uko urushiriza guhindura niko urushiriza gutera ingorane zo guhagarara (ugufata urutoke). Intumbero ni ugukingirwa neza ata gutandukana n’abandi.
- Intambwe ku yindi**: Hindura ivyagezwe, ugerageze imbuga zawe zisanzwe, hanyuma ubandanye. Ntaco bimaze guhindura vyose icarimwe.
- Uburinganire bw’umuntu ku giti ciwe**: Rondera ivyiyumviro VYAWE hagati y’ubuzima bwite n’ukworohereza gukoresha.

## Uburinzi bwamaze gukoreshwa ku buryo busanzwe (buremesha)





- Kwitandukanya n’abandi (Fission)**: mu gutera imbere. Ico gikoresho gikoresha urubuga rumwe rumwe mu buryo butandukanye kugira ngo urubuga rumwe rudashobora gushika ku makuru y’urundi. Suzuma uko bimeze biciye ku `ivyerekeye:ugushigikira` (rondera "Ibisaduka"). Niba bitashobojwe, ushobora kubikoresha n'amaboko muri `ibijanye: config` na `fission.autostart = ukuri`.
- Uburinzi bwose bw'amakuru (TCP)**: burakora ku buryo busanzwe. Ama cookies n'ibindi bikoresho vyo kubika bigarukira ku rubuga rw'umuntu wa mbere ("igikombe" kimwe ku rubuga), ivyo bikaba bigabanya ugukurikirana kw'urubuga rwose. Ivyiyumviro vy’igihe gito bikorwa biciye ku Bubiko bw’Ukwinjira API iyo bikenewe (amabuto yo kwinjira yunze ubumwe).
- Uburinzi bw’Ikurikirana ry’Ibisubizo/Ibisubirwamwo**: Firefox ihita imenya kandi igasukura amakuru yasigajwe n’imbuga zisubirwamwo (amahuza akurungika biciye ku gikurikirana imbere y’aho uja), bikagabanya uwo muhora wo gukurikirana ataco ukora.

Intumbero: inyungu nini z’ubuzima bwite ata guca urubuga. Ku 90% vy’abakoresha.



Kugira ngo ugere ku mirongo, kanda kuri menyu ☰ iri hejuru iburyo, hanyuma **"Imirongo "**:

*Urupapuro rw'imiterere ya Firefox - "Rusangi" tab. Aha niho utunganya amahitamwo menshi y'ubuzima bwite bwawe*



**Ukurinda gukurikirana (ETP)**




- Guhindura **ETP** ku **Ibikomeye**. Uhagarika abandi bakurikirana (amakuru y’urubuga, ibimenyetso vy’intoke, ubucukuzi bw’amahera, ibikoresho vy’imibano...).
- Iyo urubuga ruvunitse (videwo, buto yo kwinjira...), zimya uburinzi bw’urwo rubuga gusa biciye ku ngabo 🛡️ , hanyuma usubiremwo tab.



Aha niho hari ingero zitandukanye z'umutekano wa ETP:




- Ivyagezwe** (biringaniye, bihuye cane)
  - Amabuye: abakurikirana ivy’imibano, amakuru y’urubuga (amadirisho yose), abakurikirana ibirimwo mu gushakisha mu buryo bwihariye, abacukuzi b’amahera y’ibanga, abamenya ibimenyetso vy’intoke.
  - Harimwo **Ukurinda amakuru yose** (TCP): igikombe kimwe ku rubuga.
- Bikomeye** (vyifuzwa kubera ibanga)
  - Kandi ibuza gukurikirana ibirimwo mu madirisha yose + bizwi kandi bikekwa ko bifata urutoke.
  - Bishobora guca imbuga zimwe zimwe; koresha ingabo 🛡️ ku bijanye n’ikintu kidasanzwe co mu karere.
- Umugenzo** (uwuteye imbere)
  - Gutunganya neza: ama cookies, gukurikirana ibirimwo, abana bato, gucapura urutoke (bizwi/bikekwa).

- Yongerako **Ibidasanzwe** ku mbuga 2-3 zihambaye niwabishaka (e-mail, banki).

## Urugero rwa 2 - Gukomezwa (Ugucapura n'Ihuriro)



Intumbero: gucapura ibikorwa no kugabanya ukuvuza kw’urubuga.



**DNS kuri HTTPS (DoH)**




- Igihugu ca mbere**: Bikora ubwavyo mu turere tumwetumwe (Amerika, Kanada, Uburusiya, Ukraine). Ahandi, gukoresha amaboko birakenewe.
- Itunganywa**: Ivyagezwe → Rusangiye → Ivyagezwe vy'urubuga → **Gushoboza DoH** → **Cloudflare** canke **Quad9** → **Uburinzi burengeye**.
- Uburinzi burengeye = **TRR-gusa** (nta gusubira inyuma kuri DNS ya sisitemu). Iyo urubuga rw'ishirahamwe/hoteli ruzibiye, subira kuri **Standard** canke uzimye DoH.
- Redundancy**: Niba usanzwe ukoresha VPN yizigiwe ifise DNS yayo bwite itekanye, DoH ishobora kuba redundancy.
- Igerageza ryo kugenzura**: `https://www.dnsleaktest.com/` rikwiye kwerekana gusa umutanga DoH yatowe.



![Sélection fournisseur DNS Cloudflare](assets/fr/10.webp)



**Gucapura n'ibikoresho n'imirongo**




- Ivyuma vy’amakonti menshi**: guhingura ibibanza (ivy’umuntu ku giti ciwe, ivy’akazi, ivy’amahera, ivy’imigenderanire, ivy’ugugura, ivy’ugukoresha rimwe). Gutunganya **"Igihe cose ufungure muri iki gikoresho"** ku mbuga zawe zisubiramwo. Ukwaguka kwemewe: `https://inyongera.mozilla.org/fr/firefox/inyongera/ibikoresho-vy'amakonti menshi/`.
- Ni kubera iki twobikoresha?
- Ukwitandukanya gukomeye** kw'ibifungurwa/ibiganiro/ububiko ku kibanza.
- Gukurikirana urubuga rwabo bike**: gufunga ibihangange (Facebook, Google).
- Ivyuma vyinshi bikoreshwa icarimwe** kuri serivisi imwe.
- Ivyago bike vya CSRF/XSS hagati y’ibimenyetso vy’ibice.
  - Impanuro: n’imiburiburi, ibikoresho vy’Imihora y’Imigenderanire/Google, Akazi, Ivy’Imari.
- Igikoresho ca Facebook** (ntibikenewe): ni verisiyo yoroshe yagenewe FB/Instagram.
- Itandukaniro ry'imirongo**: biciye ku `ivyerekeye:imirongo` (imirongo nyamukuru, imirongo mikeyi "itekanye cane", imirongo y'igerageza). Amakuru yose hamwe n’ugucapura kw’ibice.



**Ivyongereweko** (bizobikwa)




- Cookie AutoDelete**: ifuta amakuru y'urubuga igihe nyene uruzitiro rupfutse (ni ngirakamaro iyo Firefox yuguruye igihe kirekire).
- LocalCDN**: ikoresha amasomero y'ubu mu karere (igabanya guhamagara kuri Google/Microsoft). Uguhuza kw’igice.



**Telefone ngendanwa (Android)**




- Firefox Android + uBlock Inkomoko**: uburinzi busa n’ubwo iyo uriko uragenda.



## Urugero rwa 3 - Umuhinga (ivyerekeye: config na Arkenfox)

Hitamwo uburyo bumwe gusa muri ubu bubiri bukurikira:



**Uburyo A - Guhindura n'amaboko**: Guhindura bikeyi bigenewe biciye ku `about:config` (ugucungera kworoshe, gutomoye)


**Uburyo B - Arkenfox user.js**: Itunganywa ry'ivyuma ryuzuye (rikomeye cane, ry'uburinzi bwinshi)



➡️ **Arkenfox iramaze gushiramwo amahinduka yose yerekeye:config yavuzwe aha hepfo** + ayandi amajana. Niwahitamwo Arkenfox, wirengagize igice c'ivya:config.



### Uburyo A: Guhindura n'amaboko biciye kuri: config



Andika `ivyerekeye: config` mu murongo wa Address → Kwemera ingorane.

Ingaruka: UTC isaha, **amakete** (ubunini bw'idirisha busanzwe), Ukoresha-Umukozi/amategeko asanzwe, Canvas/WebGL/AudioContext amategeko. Uguhuza cane, ariko "quirks" nkeyi (igihe co guhindura, rimwe na rimwe n'icongereza gitoyi).

Itegeko rya zahabu: iyo hari ikintu kivunitse, subira ku mpinduka ya nyuma.



### Uburyo B: Arkenfox user.js (Imitunganyirize yikora)



Umugambi wa **Arkenfox** utanga dosiye `user.js` ibungabungwa n'abanyagihugu ikoresha amajana y'ivyo Firefox ikunda n'umutekano. Iyo usubiye gutangura, Firefox isoma iyi dosiye mu mvomero yawe maze igakoresha aya mahinduka.





- Iciyumviro ni ikihe? Tangana n'**ishingiro rikomeye ridahinduka** ata "gukanda hose"; kugabanya ubugenzuzi; kuzigama umwanya.
- Ivyo bihindura (ingero): telemetry igabanywa, amakuru/ububiko/uwurungika/HTTPS ikomezwa, **RFP** + amakete, **WebRTC irabujijwe**, DoH/TLS ihindurwa, APIs zivugana zigabanutse.
- Igihe co kuyikoresha: nimba ushaka ko Firefox ikomezwa mu minota 10 kandi wemera ibintu bikeyi bidasanzwe (DRM/ugutangaza, Web visio, SSO/ukwishyura).
- Ivyiza: vyihuta, bihoraho, **bivuguruwe** (bihuye na ESR), **vyanditswe neza cane** (wiki + ibivugwa), **bishobora guhindurwa** biciye mu guhindura.
- Ivyo bishobora gushikako: guhuza (porogarama zimwe zimwe zo ku rubuga), uguhumurizwa (UTC, ubunini bw’idirisha), n’icibutso: **si Tor** (nta n’urubuga rwo kutamenyekana).



Gushiramwo (vyiza kuri **umwirondoro wihariye**)


1. Bika urutonde/ivyo ukunda maze ushire urutonde rw’imbuga zawe ata cookies zidasanzwe.


2. Gukuraho `ukoresha.js` kuri `ukoresha.js` (ESR/verisiyo ihamye).


3. Rondera ububiko bwawe bw'umwirondoro biciye kuri `ibijanye n'umwirondoro`:




   - Amadirisha: `%IBIMENYETSO%/Mozilla/Firefox/Imirongo/...`
   - Linux: `~/.mozila/Imvugo y'umuriro/...`
   - macOS: `~/Isoko ry'ibitabo/Ibikorwa/Firefox/Imirongo/...`


4. Funga Firefox maze ushire `user.js` ku muzi wa dosiye y'umwirondoro.


5. Gusubira gutanguza; guhindura biciye ku `about:config` canke dosiye y'ihindura.



Ivyavuguruwe




- Kurikiza ibisohoka vya Arkenfox (ESR bihuye), usubirize `user.js`, wongere utangure Firefox; soma amakete y’ugusohoka.



**Guhindura ku buryo bubereye biciye ku Guhindura**



Arkenfox ni ububisha n’ibigirankana. Kugira ngo uhuze n'ivyo ukeneye (gucapura, visio, imbuga zimwe zimwe), ushobora gukora dosiye `user-overrides.js` muri dosiye imwe na `user.js`. Iyi dosiye iraguha uburenganzira bwo "guhindura" ibintu bimwe bimwe vya Arkenfox utahinduye dosiye nyamukuru.



Rema `user-overrides.js` wongereko ivyo ushaka:


```javascript
// DRM/streaming
user_pref("media.eme.enabled", true);

// Safe Browsing (si vous préférez le garder)
user_pref("browser.safebrowsing.phishing.enabled", true);
user_pref("browser.safebrowsing.malware.enabled", true);

// Historique moins restrictif
user_pref("places.history.expiration.max_pages", 200000);

// Synchronisation Firefox
user_pref("identity.fxaccounts.enabled", true);

// WebRTC (si visio nécessaire)
user_pref("media.peerconnection.enabled", true);

// Referer plus compatible
user_pref("network.http.referer.XOriginPolicy", 1);
user_pref("network.http.referer.trimOnCrossOrigin", true);
```



Ibikorwa vyiza




- Koresha urutonde rwa **"Arkenfox" rwitandukanye** maze ugume ufise urutonde "rusanzwe" kugira ngo uruhuke.
- Gabanya ivyungura (uBlock Origin OK) kugira ngo ugabanye uburebure bw’igitero n’ubudasa.
- Yongerako ibidasanzwe ku rubuga ku rubuga (ingabo 🛡️, uBO, NoScript iyo ikoreshejwe) iyo bikenewe.
- Igerageza inyuma y’ihinduka ryose: WebRTC/DNS ivuza, Gupfuka inzira zawe, CreepJS.



## Ibikorwa vyiza





- Ivyashizwe ku rubuga**: Firefox n'ibindi bishizwe ku gihe.
- Ivyungura**: bihuje n’ubwenge kandi vyizigirwa; mwirinde incungu "zikekeranya".
- Ivyo gukuraho**: kwirinda; kugerageza amadosiye y'agaciro kuri VirusTotal.
- Amajambo banga**: **umuyobozi yiyemeje** (Bitwarden, KeePassXC); **2FA** vyashobojwe; kwirinda kubika mu mucukumbuzi.
- Isuku**: gufunga Google/Facebook mu bikoresho; gufunga/gufungura ubudasiba kugira ngo "usubiremwo" ikibanza.



## Imipaka n'ubundi buryo





- Mucukumbuzi ikomeye ≠ urubuga rwo kutamenyekana: ata **VPN**, IP yawe iguma iboneka; mbere n’aho yoba ari yo, uguhuza biraguma bishoboka.
- Guhindura cane birashobora gutuma uba **uwudasanzwe**. **RFP** igira urugero; ibikoresho vyo gutoranya (nk'akarorero Chameleon) birashobora... kugutandukanya. Gerageza, gereranya, utunganye.
- Ibindi/ivyo kwuzuza:
- Tor Mucukumbuzi: urubuga rwo kutamenyekana biciye kuri Tor; buhoro buhoro. Raba **ubuyobozi bwacu bwo gushiramwo no gutunganya**:



https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/communication/tor-browser-a847e83c-31ef-4439-9eac-742b255129bb



- Mullvad Mucukumbuzi: "Tor ata Tor", kugira ngo ifatanijwe na VPN; ikirenge gishingiye ku rugero. Raba ingene woyishiramwo mu **inyigisho yacu yihariye**:



https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/communication/mullvad-browser-a16c13d6-8bf9-4cb5-9aa0-85411a9cda0e



- Ivyiza vyo gukorana: Firefox (Urwego rwa 2) + VPN yo gukoresha buri musi; Tor/Mullvad ku bikorwa vy’agaciro; imirongo itandukanye kugira ngo igere ku bice.



## Iciyumviro



Mu gukurikiza iyi ntambwe ku yindi, warahinduye Firefox iba igihome nyaco co kurwanya ubugenzuzi bwa digitale. Kuva ku miterere y’ingenzi y’Urwego rwa 1 gushika ku miterere ya Arkenfox iteye imbere, ihinduka ryose rikomeza ubuzima bwite bwawe ataco rihinduye ku bumenyi bwawe bwo gusura.



**Ubuzima bwite bwawe ubu burakingiye neza**: abakurikirana amatangazo barabujijwe, amakuru y’ibanga yaraciwemwo ibice, IP Address iracitse intege, telemetry irazimijwe. Firefox ntabwo ikiri umucukumbuzi gusa, ahubwo ni igikoresho co guhangana n’ivyo ukeneye.



**Ibuka: ibanga ntiryigera ritanga. Gerageza uburinzi bwawe ubudasiba, uhindure amasetingi yawe, kandi ntukekeranye guhindura amasetingi yawe uko ingeso zawe zihinduka. Ugutamenyekana kwawe kuri internet bivana cane n'ibikoresho vyawe nk'uko bivana n'ingene ukora.**
