VPN ("*Virtual Private Network*") ni igikorwa gishinga ubucuti butekanye kandi bufise amakuru hagati ya telefone yawe canke mudasobwa yawe na server iri kure icungiwe n’uwutanga VPN.


Mu buryo bw’ubuhinga, iyo winjiye kuri VPN, uruja n’uruza rwawe rwa internet ruca mu nzira y’amazi y’ibanga rukaja kuri server ya VPN. Ivyo bituma bigorana ko abandi bantu, nk’abatanga amakuru ya Internet (ISP) canke abakora ivy’ububisha, bashobora gufata canke gusoma amakuru yawe. Server ya VPN rero ikora nk’umuhuza ahuza na service wipfuza gukoresha mu izina ryawe. Itanga IP Address nshasha ku nzira yawe, ivyo bikaba bifasha guhisha IP Address yawe nyayo ku mbuga usura. Ariko rero, bitandukanye n’ivyo amatangazo amwe amwe yo kuri internet yoshobora kuvuga, gukoresha VPN ntibiguha uburenganzira bwo guca kuri internet ata wuzi, kuko bisaba uburyo bwo kwizigira uwuguha VPN ashobora kubona uruja n’uruza rwawe rwose.

![IVPN](assets/fr/01.webp)

Ivyiza vyo gukoresha VPN ni vyinshi cane. Ica mbere, birazigama ubuzima bwite bw’ibikorwa vyawe vyo kuri Internet ku ba ISP canke ku butegetsi, igihe gusa uwuguha VPN adasangira amakuru yawe. Ica kabiri, irakingira amakuru yawe cane cane iyo uhuye n’imihora ya Wi-Fi ya bose, iyo mihora ikaba ishobora guterwa n’ibitero vya MITM (man-in-the-middle). Ica gatatu, mu guhisha IP yawe Address, VPN iragufasha guca mu nzira y’amategeko y’aho uba n’ugucengera, kugira ngo ushikire ibintu vyari kuba bitaboneka canke bibujijwe mu karere kawe.


Nk’uko ushobora kubibona, VPN ihindura ingorane zo kwihweza uruja n’uruza ku wutanga VPN. Ku bw’ivyo, igihe uhitamwo uwuguha VPN, birahambaye ko uzirikana amakuru y’ibanga asabwa kugira ngo wiyandikishe. Iyo uwuguha amakuru asavye amakuru nk’inomero yawe ya telefone, imeyili Address, amakuru y’ikarita yawe ya banki, canke ikibi kuruta, Address yawe y’iposita, ingorane zo gufatanya akaranga kawe n’uruja n’uruza rwawe ziragwira. Iyo uwuguha amakuru ashobora gusenyuka canke akagufata mu buryo butegekanijwe n’amategeko, vyoba vyoroshe gufatanya uruja n’uruza rwawe n’amakuru yawe bwite. Ni vyiza rero guhitamwo uwutanga amakuru adasaba amakuru y’ibanga kandi yemera kwishura ata mazina, nk’aho yoba akoresheje amafaranga y’ibiceri.


Muri iyi nyigisho, ndashira ahabona umuti woroshe, ukora neza, ufise igiciro gisanzwe wa VPN udasaba amakuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe kugira ngo ukoreshe.


## Intangamarara ya IVPN


IVPN ni igikorwa ca VPN cakozwe cane cane ku bakoresha barondera uburyo bwo kwikingira. Udakunze abatanga VPN bazwi cane bakunda guterwa imbere kuri YouTube, IVPN igaragara kubera uguserukira, umutekano, n’ukwubaha ubuzima bwite.

Itegeko ry’ubuzima bwite rya IVPN rirakomeye cane: nta makuru y’ibanga asabwa igihe umuntu yiyandikisha. Ushobora gufungura konti utatanga imeyili Address, izina canke inomero ya telefone. Kugira ngo wishyure, si ngombwa ko winjiza amakuru y’ikarita y’inguzanyo, kuko IVPN yemera kwishura mu bitcoins (onchain na Lightning). Ikindi kandi, IVPN ivuga ko ata nkuru y’ibikorwa ibika, ivyo bisigura ko, mu vyiyumviro, uruja n’uruza rwawe rwa internet rutandikwa n’iyo sosiyete.

IVPN na yo ni [inkomoko yuguruye yose](https://github.com/ivpn), ku bijanye n’ubuhinga bwayo, ibikorwa vyayo, mbere n’urubuga rwabo, bituma umuntu wese ashobora kugenzura no gusubiramwo kode yiwe. Bagira kandi igenzura ry’umutekano ryigenga buri mwaka, ivyavuyemwo bikamenyeshwa ku rubuga rwabo.


IVPN ikoresha gusa ama server yikorera, gutyo igakuraho ingorane zijanye n’ugukoresha ibikorwa vy’igicu vy’abandi, nka AWS, Google Cloud, canke Microsoft Azure.


Iyi serivisi itanga ibintu vyinshi vy’ubuhinga bwa none, nk’ivyo gukoresha multi-hop, bijana uruja n’uruza biciye ku ma server menshi ari mu bihugu bitandukanye kugira ngo umuntu ntamenyekane. IVPN kandi ishiramwo umukurikirana n’umubuza amatangazo, kandi itanga uburyo bwo guhitamwo mu mirongo itandukanye ya VPN.


Birumvikana ko iyo nzira y’ubusuku izana n’igiciro, mugabo igiciro gikwiriye akenshi ni ikimenyetso c’uburyo bwiza n’ubugororotsi. Bishobora kwerekana ko iyo sosiyete ifise uburyo bwo gukora ataco ikeneye kugurisha amakuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe. IVPN rero itanga ubwoko 2 bw'imigambi: umugambi wa Standard, ushobora gufatanya ibikoresho bishika 2, n'umugambi wa Pro, ushobora gufatanya ibikoresho bishika 7 kandi ukaba urimwo "*Multi-hop*" protocol ijana uruja n'uruza rwawe biciye ku ma server menshi.


Udakunze abatanga VPN, IVPN ikora ku citegererezo co kugura umwanya wo gushika ku serivisi, aho ku kwiyandikisha gusubiramwo. Urishura mu bitcoins rimwe mu kiringo wahisemwo. Nk’akarorero, iyo uguze umwaka umwe wo gukoresha, urashobora gukoresha iyo serivisi muri ico kiringo, hanyuma uzokenera gusubira ku rubuga rwa IVPN kugira ngo ugure umwanya mwinshi wo gukoresha.

## Ni gute wokoresha no gutunganya IVPN?


Ku bijanye n’ikoreshwa n’imiterere, biroroshe cane. Kuva kuri Interface nyamukuru, urashobora gukoresha canke guhagarika iyo nzira ukoresheje gusa ikimenyetso co gutora.

![IVPN](assets/notext/21.webp)

Ufise kandi uburenganzira bwo guhagarika VPN yawe mu kiringo kinaka.

![IVPN](assets/notext/22.webp)

Niwafyonda kuri server iriho ubu, urashobora guhitamwo iyindi server mu ziriho.

![IVPN](assets/notext/23.webp)

Birashoboka kandi gukoresha canke guhagarika uruhome rw’umuriro rwinjijwe hamwe n’igikorwa co kurwanya ugukurikirana.

![IVPN](assets/notext/24.webp)

Kugira ngo ubone ibindi bikoresho, fyonda ku kimenyetso c’ivyo bikoresho.

![IVPN](assets/notext/25.webp)

Mu gice ca "*Account*", uzosangamwo ama settings ajanye na konti yawe.

![IVPN](assets/notext/26.webp)

Mu gice ca "*Rusangi*", hariho amategeko menshi y'abaguzi. Ndaguhanura ko wosuzuma amahitamwo ya "*Launch at login*" na "*On launch*" mu gice ca "*Autoconnect*" kugira ngo ushireho ubucuti na VPN iyo utanguye imashini yawe.

![IVPN](assets/notext/27.webp)

Mu gice ca "*Ihuzwa*", uzosangamwo amahitamwo atandukanye ajanye n'ihuriro. Aha niho ushobora guhindura protocole ya VPN ikoreshwa.

![IVPN](assets/notext/28.webp)

Igipande ca "*IVPN Firewall*" kigufasha gukoresha firewall mu buryo butunganye mu ntango ya mudasobwa, kugira ngo nta n'imwe ihuriye hanze ya VPN.

![IVPN](assets/notext/29.webp)

Igipande ca "*Split Tunnel*" gitanga ubushobozi bwo gukuraho porogarama zimwe zimwe mu nzira ya VPN. Amaporogarama yongerewe ngaha azobandanya akora n’uruja n’uruza rwa internet rusanzwe naho VPN yoba ifunguwe.

![IVPN](assets/notext/30.webp)

Mu gice ca "*WiFi control*", urafise uburenganzira bwo gutunganya ibikorwa vyihariye bivanye n'imihora uhuye nayo. Nk'akarorero, urashobora gushinga urubuga rwawe rwo muhira ngo rube "*Trusted*" maze ugatunganya VPN kugira ngo ntikore kuri uru rubuga, ahubwo ikore ubwayo ku rundi rubuga rwa WiFi urwo ari rwo rwose.

![IVPN](assets/notext/31.webp)

Mu "*AntiTracker*", hitamwo urutonde rwo kubuza umukurikirana wawe. Ivyo bigenewe guhagarika amatangazo, porogarama mbi be n’ibikoresho bikurikirana amakuru mu guhagarika ibisabwa ku bikorwa vyo gukurikirana amakuru igihe uriko urarondera kuri Internet. Ivyo bituma ubuzima bwite bwawe butera imbere mu kubuza amashirahamwe gukusanya no kugurisha amakuru yawe yo gusura. "*Hardcore Mode*" iraboneka kandi kugira ngo uhagarike burundu ivyicaro vyose vy'ivya Google na Meta, hamwe n'ibikorwa vyose bishingiyeko.

![IVPN](assets/notext/32.webp)
