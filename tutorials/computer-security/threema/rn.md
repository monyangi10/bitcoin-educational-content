Threema yashinzwe mu mwaka w’2012 mu Busuwisi, ni porogaramu yo gutanga ubutumwa buca bukoreshwa kugira ngo umuntu agire ubuzima bwite n’umutekano. Ubutandukanye na WhatsApp, Telegram canke Signal, Threema nta nomero ya telefone canke e-mail Address isaba kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukora konti. Uwukoresha wese afise ikimenyetso ciwe, kigatuma umuntu ashobora kwiyandikisha atazi izina ryiwe.



Ku ruhande rw’ubuhinga, ivy’itumanaho biciye kuri Threema birashirwa mu nzira y’ubuhinga. Kode y’ikoreshwa ry’amatelefone ngendanwa ryari rifunguye kuva mu 2020, ariko ibikorwa remezo vya server biguma ari ivy’umuntu kandi bihurikiye hamwe. Ama serveri akoreshwa mu Busuwisi gusa (igihugu kizwi cane kubera amategeko agenga ivy’ukurinda amakuru).



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Threema ifise abaguzi b’ishimikiro ba Android na iOS. Hariho kandi urubuga rwo gukoresha, hamwe n’ubuhinga bwo gukoresha Windows, Linux na macOS. Ariko rero, kugira ngo ubikoreshe, utegerezwa kubanza kwiyandikisha kuri telefone ngendanwa.



Igikoresho ca Threema si ubuntu. Ikora ku buryo bwo kugura rimwe: €5.99 kugira ngo ukoreshe app ubuzima bwose (canke €4.99 iyo uyiguze direct). Kugira ngo vy’ukuri ukoreshe Threema ata mazina, n’ukwishura bikwiye kuba ata mazina. Ni co gituma ushobora kugura urufunguzo rwo gukoresha mu bitcoins canke mu mafaranga kuri "*Threema Shop*" kugira ngo ukoreshe verisiyo ya Android. Ku rundi ruhande kuri iOS, ugugura bitegerezwa guca muri App Store, kubera amategeko ya Apple ku bijanye no gukoresha app.



Hariho kandi n'ubucuruzi bwihariye bwitwa "*Threema Work*". Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwibanda ku bijanye n’ivyo muhira.

## Gugura uruhusha rwa Threema



Iyo umaze gushiramwo iyo porogarama, nimba waraciye mu iduka ry’iporogarama, uba umaze kwishura uruhusha kandi ukwiye kuyironka ubwo nyene. Niba waraciye muri F-Droid canke APK, ubu urakeneye kugura uruhusha ku rubuga rwemewe.



[Genda kuri "*Iduka rya Threema*"(https://iduka.threema.ch/) ukande kuri buto ya "*Gura Threema kuri Android*".



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Hitamwo umubare w’uruhusha wipfuza kugura (rumwe gusa nimba ari rwawe gusa), uhitemwo amafaranga, hanyuma uhitemwo uburyo bwo gutanga uruhusha. Ushobora guhitamwo kwakira uruhusha biciye kuri e-mail, canke ukaja kuri uru rubuga nimba wipfuza ko utamenyekana. Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Bandanya kwishura*".



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Hitamwo uburyo bwo kwishura. Ku bijanye nanje, ngiye kwishura mu bitcoins, ivyo navyo ndagusavye kubikora, kuko bigufasha kuguma utamenyekana (igihe cose ukoresheje Bitcoin mu buryo bukwiye) kandi biraryoshe cane kuruta kwishura amahera kure. Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ibikurikira*".



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Niba udakeneye Invoice, wongere ukande kuri "*Next*" utashizemwo amakuru y'ibanga.



Hanyuma wemeze ivyo waguze ukanda kuri "*Wemeze ko wishuye*".



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Uzoca ukenera kohereza amafaranga yerekanwa kuri Bitcoin Address yatanzwe (ikibabaje, Lightning ntirashigikirwa). Igihe amafaranga yemejwe kuri Blockchain, fyonda ku "*Funga*" iruhande ya Invoice.



Uzoheza ushobore gushika ku rufunguzo rwawe rw’uruhusha. Iyumvire: nimba utarinjije e-mail Address, uru rufunguzo ruzogaragara gusa hano. Ibuka kubika URL ya paji kugira ngo uzoshobore kuyironka mu nyuma nivyaba ngombwa.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



## Rema konti kuri Threema



Ubu ko ufise urufunguzo rwawe rw’uruhusha, urashobora amaherezo gutanguza iyo porogarama. Ku ntango ya mbere, nimba utaragura Threema biciye ku iduka ry’ibikoresho, uzosabwa kwinjiza urufunguzo rwawe rw’uruhusha, waguze ku rubuga.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Set up now*".



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



Niwimure urutoke rwawe ku rubuga kuri generate isoko y'uburemere, ikenewe kugira ngo ureme "*Threema ID*" yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



"*Threema ID*" yawe izoca igaragara. Bizotuma ushobora guhura n’abandi bakoresha. Fyonda kuri "*Ibikurikira*".



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Hitamwo ijambobanga. Bizotuma ushobora gusubira gukoresha konti yawe iyo bikenewe. Ijambobanga ryawe ribe rirerire kandi ribe ridasanzwe uko bishoboka kwose, kandi ubike kopi yaryo itekanye, nk’akarorero mu gikoresho co gucungera ijambobanga.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



Hanyuma uhitemwo izina ry’ukoresha, rishobora kuba izina ryawe ry’ukuri canke izina ry’uruyeri.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Ushobora rero guhuza ID yawe ya Threema n’inomero yawe ya telefone. Ivyo bituma woroherwa no kurondera biciye mu bantu muvugana, ariko iyo ukoresheje Threema, ni ukugira ngo uzigame amazina yawe: rero ni vyiza ko utayihuza. Fyonda kuri "*Ibikurikira*".



![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)



Uhejeje iyi ntambwe, ukande kuri "*Finish*".



![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)



Ubu rero warahuye na Threema kandi urashobora gutangura kuvugana.



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



## Gushiraho Threema



Mbere na mbere, genda kuri settings ukanda ku tudodo dutatu turi hejuru iburyo, hanyuma uhitemwo "*Settings*".



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Mu gice ca "*Ibanga*", uzosangamwo uburyo bwinshi bwo guhindura ivyo ukeneye:




- Guhuza ama contacts kuri telefone yawe ;
- Kwemera ubutumwa buva ku bantu batazwi;
- Ikimenyetso co kwandika mu gihe co kwinjiza amakuru ;
- Itangazo ry'ukwakira ubutumwa..



![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)



Mu gice ca "*Umutekano*", ndagusavye gukoresha uburyo bwa "*Uburyo bwo gufunga*" kugira ngo ukinge ukwinjira muri porogaramu. Ni vyiza kandi gukoresha passphrase kugira ngo ukingire ama backups yawe yo mu karere.



![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)



Ushobora gutohoza ibindi bice vy’imiterere kugira ngo uhindure porogarama nk’uko ukunda.



![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)



## Gusubiza inyuma konti yawe ya Threema



Imbere y’uko utangura guhana ubutumwa, birahambaye ko utegura uburyo bwo kugarura konti yawe, cane cane iyo telefone yawe ihindutse canke yatakaye. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku tudodo dutatu turi hejuru iburyo bwa Interface, hanyuma ugende ku rutonde rwa "*Backups*".



![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)



Aha uzosanga uburyo bubiri bwo gukingira amakuru yawe:




- "*Igihugu ca gatatu*";
- "*Gusubiza inyuma amakuru*".



"Threema Safe* ibika amakuru yose ya konti yawe, kiretse ibiyago vyawe, ku ma server ya Threema. Aya makuru apfukwa n'ijambobanga wahisemwo igihe wakora konti yawe, kugira ngo Threema idashobora kuyaronka. Gukora backups bikorwa ubwavyo kandi bihoraho."



Na "*Threema Safe*", kugira ngo ugarure konti yawe ku gikoresho gishasha, ico ukeneye gukora n'ukwinjiza "*Threema ID*" yawe n'ijambobanga ryawe. Iyo imwe muri izo nkuru zibiri ibuze, ntibizoshoboka ko usubizaho konti yawe.



Iyi nzira igufasha gusa kugarura ID yawe, urutonde rwawe, abo muvugana, amatsinda yawe n'ibindi bimwe bimwe, ariko **si amateka y'ibiyago vyawe**.



Kugira ngo ukoreshe "*Threema Safe*", ushobora gusuzuma gusa amahitamwo ari muri "*Ivy'ububiko*".



![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)



Niba kandi ushaka gukora backup no kugarura amateka y'ibiyago vyawe, uzokenera gukoresha uburyo bwa "*Data Backup*". Ivyo bituma haba ububiko bwose bwa konti yawe, bubitswe aho hantu kuri telefone yawe. Uzokenera kwimurira iyo nkuru ku gikoresho cawe gishasha maze ukoreshe ijambobanga ryawe (kandi bishoboka ko passphrase yawe) kugira ngo ugarure konti yawe yose.



Kubera ko iyo backup ari iyo mu karere gusa, irakeneye gukopirwa ubudasiba ku binyamakuru vyo hanze. Ahandi ho, iyo igikoresho cawe kizimiye, kugisubirana ntibizoshoboka. Ubwo buryo rero burabereye cane iyo umuntu ateguwe kuva ku gikoresho kimwe akaja ku kindi, aho gukoresha ibintu vyihutirwa.



Iyumvire: "*Data Backup*" ikora gusa iyo ukoresha iyo sisitemu imwe. Ntuzoshobora kuyikoresha nk’akarorero, kugira ngo uve kuri Samsung uja kuri iPhone.

## Guhuza porogaramu ya PC



Niba ushaka gushika ku biganiro vyawe kuri PC yawe, urashobora guhuza konti yawe ya Threema na porogarama yihariye. Gukuraho porogarama y’urubuga rwawe [ku rubuga rwemewe](https://threema.ch/ru/gukuraho).



![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)



Kuri telefone yawe, ukande ku tudodo dutatu turi hejuru iburyo, hanyuma ufungure "*Threema 2.0 for Desktop*".



![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)



Fyonda kuri "*Add device*", hanyuma ukoreshe telefone yawe kugira ngo ushireko kode ya QR yerekanwa na porogarama iri kuri mudasobwa yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)



Kugira ngo wemeze ko ivyo bihuye, fyonda ku mugwi w’ibimenyetso vyerekanywe muri porogarama.



![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)



Kuri mudasobwa yawe, winjiremwo ukoresheje ijambobanga ryawe.



![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)



Uretse porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa, ubu urashobora gushika kuri konti yawe ya Threema ukoresheje mudasobwa yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)
