## Kwinjira = email = gutakaza ubuzima bwite


Mu isi ya digitale, vyacitse umugenzo kugira konti ku rubuga rwose umuntu yipfuza gukoresha.

Imwe muri izo serivisi isaba kwinjira, akenshi ifatanye n'izina ry'ukoresha_ n'ijambobanga_. Akenshi, izina ry’ukoresha ni imeyili y’ukoresha.


Iyo umuntu akoresheje imeyili yiwe bwite Address ku kwinjira kwose, biroroshe kwiyumvira ingaruka ya mbere: gutakaza ubuzima bwite, ivyo bikaba bikomeye iyo Address igizwe na _izina.izina ry'umuryango@serviceemail.com_.


Abahinguzi b’ibikoresho vy’inkomoko yuguruye bararemye urutonde rw’ibikoresho, vyavutse neza na neza kugira ngo abakoresha basubire kuronka ubuzima bwite: bazoguma binjira, ariko bakoreshe izina ry’ibanga aho gukoresha igikoresho kigaragaza akaranga kabo k’ibanga.


Icoroheje kuruta ibindi vyose, mu vyo jewe ubwanje nagerageza kandi nkiriko ndagerageza, ni co nyene [Kwinjira mu buryo bworoshe](https://simplelogin.io/).


## Izina ry'ibanga


Izina ry'ibanga ry'imeli risubirira gusa _izina.izina ry'umuryango_ igice ca imeli yawe Address n'izina ry'ikinyoma. Ku wukoresha, ntaco gihinduka; igikorwa c'amazina y'ibanga kirungika ubutumwa kuri Address nk'uko bisanzwe. Umuntu wese arashobora kubandanya akoresha inbox yiwe nk’uko yama abigira, ariko aho kwerekana izina ryiwe ry’ukuri, izokwerekana uwuyikoresha atamenyekana. Iyi serivisi irakeneye kuba nziza, kandi gushika ubu, Simple Login yaragaragaje ko ari co nyene.


Igihe usuye urubuga rworoshe rwo kwinjira ku ncuro ya mbere, utegerezwa gufungura konti (aha na ho!), ukoresheje imeyili "yemewe" Address. Aha, utegerezwa kwinjiza email, ijambobanga, no gutorera umuti captcha.

### Imeyili y'akazi


Abakoresha email ifise domain y’umuntu ku giti ciwe nk’imeli y’akazi barashobora gushinga domain yabo bwite.


![image](assets/it/10.webp)


Kuva ku rubuga nyamukuru, mu guhitamwo _Mailboxes_, birashoboka mbere kwongerako izindi aderesi za imeyili no gukoresha amazina y'ibanga azoremwa bivanye n'ivyo. Muri iyi nyigisho, nk'akarorero, nafashe ingingo yo gukora urutonde n'agasanduku k'ubutumwa _gmail.com_, hanyuma nfatanya _proton.me_ Address.


![image](assets/it/11.webp)


Kwongera Address nshasha, cane cane iyo ari iya Proton, uburyo burongowe bwerekana ubushobozi bwo kwinjira muri _sudo_ mode, super user. Simple Login izosaba kwinjiza ijambobanga ry'iyi sandugu y'ubutumwa, kugira ngo werekane ko Ownership ari yo.


⚠️ **Jewe ubwanje ndahanura ko mudakora ivyo**. ⚠️


![image](assets/it/12.webp)


**Ni vyiza ko winjiye mu gasandugu ka email -> ukope link yo kugenzura ukayishira mu murongo wa URL -> ukaronka ukugenzura utamenyesheje ijambobanga.**


![image](assets/it/13.webp)


Muri izo aderesi zibiri zishizwemwo, imwe iba ari yo ikoreshwa, iyindi na yo ikaba iya kabiri, ariko zirashobora guhindurwa bitagoranye, kandi iyo nzira iramenyekana bitagoranye ku rutonde rw’ibikoresho.


![image](assets/it/14.webp)


Tumaze kwongerako email ya kabiri Address (ntibikenewe), reka turabe ico twokora na Simple Login.


## Guhingura amazina y'ibanga


Mu gice, uburyo bwa mbere bwa menyu bwanditsweko _Alias_, aho niho ushobora kubirema. Ufise uburenganzira bwo guhindura amazina y’ibanga ya generate mu buryo butari bwo ukanda kuri buto ya Random Alias, ari yo buto ya Green yerekanwa ku ifoto ikurikira. Iki kintu kirema imeyili yihariye kandi iryoshe cane Address.


![image](assets/it/24.webp)


Niba, ariko, wipfuza gutandukanya ibikorwa mu kubiha amazina atandukanye, utegerezwa guhitamwo _New Custom Alias_. Mu kubigira, urashobora guha izina ry’ibanga ry’igikorwa ushaka gukoresha (imihora y’ubudandaji, abatanga ibikorwa, ibintu bibera kuri Internet, abanyamahanga bahuye na bo ku mpanuka, n’ibindi). Ibindi bikorwa na Simple Login.

Kubera umunezero (ariko sivyo vy’ukuri, kuvuga ukuri) naciyemwo gukora izina ry’ibanga ry’iyo banki ndayita `BANKI`. Naho ari ukuri ko banki yanje izi vyose kuri jewe, ndabona ko ari umunezero kuvugana na bo nkoresheje email Address idashobora gutahurwa kuri bo. Injira yoroshe vy'ukuri itanga izina ry'imburakimazi, ritandukanijwe n'iryo duhisemwo n'ijambo `.`


Aha, imeyili nshasha Address ishobora kuba:


- banki.ubworozi123@aleeas.com
- banki.isahani456@ubutumwa.com
- banki.guhagarika umutima789@8ingabo.net
- n'ibindi


Umuntu arashobora guhitamwo ivyicaro birenze kimwe: ivy’abantu bose biraboneka n’umugambi w’ubuntu, mu gihe ibindi, vyerekanywe ko ari ivy’ibanga (harimwo _@simplelogin.com_), vyagura amahitamwo ku bakoresha bafata ingingo yo kwiyandikisha ku mugambi w’amahera.


![image](assets/it/15.webp)


Igihe suffix n'itongo vyatowe, urashobora gushinga nimba iyi Address nshasha (kandi idasanzwe) ikwiye gukora nk'izina ry'ibanga ry'imwe gusa mu masanduku y'imeli y'umuntu ku giti ciwe, canke kuri zo zose. Izina ry'ibanga riba ryiteguye kandi rikora inyuma yo gukanda kuri _Rema_


![image](assets/it/16.webp)


Iryo kete rishasha Address ryaremewe ubu riraboneka, ryiteguye kwoherezwa (muri banki!) mu kuyikopa gusa.


![image](assets/it/18.webp)


Muri iki gihe, ushobora kwibanda ku kurema izina ry’ibanga kuri serivisi yose canke urubuga ushaka gushikako n’aho imeyili isabwa nk’ikintu nyamukuru co kurema konti.


![image](assets/it/19.webp)


Ku bakunda ubuzima bwite, hariho kandi uburyo bwo generate email Address ishingiye ku nzira ya UUID (atari ku mazina amenyekana), irema ikimenyetso kidasanzwe c'ibice 128 kitagenzurwa n'imigwi ihurikiye hamwe. Iki gikoresho, gifise akamaro ku makonti y'agaciro, gishobora kuboneka muri _Random Alias_ menu.


![image](assets/it/21.webp)

![image](assets/it/22.webp)


Nk’uko mubibona, iyi ni email Address isaba gucungirwa neza.


Niwahindura ivyiyumviro vyawe ntugishaka gukoresha izina ry'ibanga, fyonda ku _More_ itegeko ry'izina ry'ibanga ryose maze uhitemwo _Delete_.

## Ubuyobozi bw'izina ry'ibanga


Guhingura amazina y’ibanga ni ikintu coroshe, nk’uko nyene uburongozi bwayo bumeze, ivyo bikaba bisaba gusa kwitwararika no gutozwa indero. Ivy’uguca mu nzira vyose, mu vy’ukuri, bizoguma bica mu gasandugu k’iposita twasobanuye, mu ntango, nk’akazwi. Amatangazo n’amakuru ahambaye ava ku mbuga zizobandanya gushika kuri Gmail, Proton, canke ico ari co cose uwutanga imeyili.


Ariko rero, igisubizo ni uko twazigamye Address nyamukuru iyo, kuva igihe amazina y’uruyeri yaremwa, dufise umwidegemvyo wo guhitamwo uwo twoyihishurira n’uwo tutayihishurira.


Izina ry’uruyeri rikora mu kwakira no ku kohereza: uwundi mukozi azoronka inyishu iva kuri alias.preoccupy789@8shield.net, nimba ari ryo zina ry’uruyeri ryatowe kuri uwo muntu nyene.


## Ivyiza


Muri rusangi, gukoresha amazina y’ibanga ni uburyo bwiza bwo kurinda akaranga kawe n’ubuzima bwite bwawe. Amaderesi ya imeyili akenshi akusanywa n’abacuruzi b’amakuru n’imbuga ngurukanabumenyi kugira ngo bakurikirane ingeso n’inyifato z’abakoresha. Naho izina ry’uruyeri ritatuma udashobora gukurikirana rwose, gukoresha rimwe ubudasiba ni intambwe nziza yo kurinda amakuru yawe. Ikindi kandi, muri "global digital village" yacu, aho ubusuma, ugurisha amakuru, n'uguhungabanya umutekano vyose ari vyinshi cane, birashoboka cane ko email ukoresha mu kwiyandikisha ku mbuga zitandukanye yamaze guhungabanywa canke gufatwa.


Izina ry’uruyeri ry’umwihariko, rikoreshwa ku kwinjira kwose, **rituma duca dutahura urubuga rwohereza ubutumwa butari bwo (canke bubi kuruta) ku gasandugu kacu k’ubutumwa, kuko iyo imeyili imenyekana n’izina ry’uruyeri rijana na yo**. Nta ciyumviro ufise c’ingene spam na phishing biva ku mihora yitwa ko ari iyo kwizigirwa, kuko ari inzego, gushika utanguye gukoresha izina ry’ibanga ry’amabanki, rimwe ry’ibikorwa vy’iposita, canke irindi ry’ibikorwa bimwebimwe vya Leta bitegekanijwe. Igihe uwurungitse iyo spam (canke ikibi kuruta) amaze kumenyekana, uzomenya ko urubuga rwacitse intege, ufate ingingo zose zo kurinda amakuru yose yatanzwe (wiyumvire amakarata y’inguzanyo!) kuri urwo rubuga rwihariye, rushobora gutahura ko rwacitse intege haciye amayinga.


Ku bijanye n’ukwinjira mu buryo bworoshe, iki gikoresho gifise ibi bikurikira:



- mobile app (na yo nyene iva kuri F-Droid) n’ivyagutse vy’umucukumbuzi, kugira ngo ushobore gucunga amazina y’ibanga mu buryo bwose;
- ivyemezo bibiri vy’izina ry’uruyeri rishasha ryose, ivyo bikaba bituma umuntu agira ubwigenge ku bijanye n’igikorwa ubwaco;
- Infashanyo ya PGP (ku bakoresha _Premium);
- irema ryoroshe ry'ubwoko bwose bw'izina ry'ibanga (iry'akamenyero, ry'imburakimazi, na UUID);
- mu migambi y'ubuntu muri urwo rwego, ubushobozi bwo gukoresha amazina y'ibanga afise amasandugu y'iposita "yemewe" menshi. Abandi bariko barahiganwa bashika kuri umwe gusa.
