## Ni kuki hariho urufunguzo rwa USB rufise Tails?


Tails (https://tails.boum.org/) ni uburyo bworoshe bwo kugira mudasobwa itekanye igihe cose, itazosiga akamenyetso kuri mudasobwa ukoresha.


Kugira ngo ukoreshe Tails, zima mudasobwa ufise uburenganzira bwo gukoresha (ku bavyeyi bawe, ku ncuti zawe, mu kafe ka Internet...) hanyuma uyitangure ukoresheje urufunguzo rwawe rwa USB rwa Tails aho gukoresha Windows, macOS canke Linux.


Inyuma y’ivyo, uzogira ahantu ukorera n’aho ushobora guhanahana amakuru bitandukanye n’uburyo busanzwe bwo gukoresha kandi ntuzokwigera ukoresha umuduga wa Hard.


Tails ntiyigera yandika kuri Hard drive kandi ikoresha gusa RAM ya mudasobwa kugira ikore. Ukwo kwibuka kurazimangana burundu iyo Tails ifunze, gutyo hakurwaho ibimenyetso vyose bishoboka.


## Ibintu bimwe bimwe vy'ikoreshwa


Kugira ngo mubone ivyiyumviro vy’ukuri vy’ivyiza vyo kwama mufise urufunguzo rwa USB rufise Tails, ng’uru urutonde rutoyi rudaheza rwakozwe n’umugwi wa Agora256:



- Kwifatanya na Internet na Tor mu buryo butagenzuwe kandi butazwi kugira ngo ushobore gusura imbuga ataco usize;
- Gufungura PDF ivuye ku rubuga ruteye amakenga;
- Gerageza urufunguzo rwawe rw’ibanga rwa Bitcoin ukoresheje Electrum Wallet;
- Koresha ibiro (LibreOffice) maze ukore kuri mudasobwa itari iyawe;
- Fata intambwe zawe za mbere mu bidukikije vya Linux kugira umenye ingene wova mw’isi ya Microsoft na Apple.


## Ni gute wokwizigira imirizo?


Buri gihe habaho ikintu co kwizigira mu gukoresha porogarama, ariko ntibitegerezwa kuba ari impumyi. Igikoresho nka Tails kigomba kwihatira guha abagikoresha uburyo bwo kwizigirwa. Ku Mirizo, ivyo bisigura:



- Inkomoko ya bose: https://gitlab.imirizo.boum.org/;
- Umugambi ushingiye ku migambi izwi cane: Tor na Debian;
- Ivyo gukuraho bishobora kugenzurwa no gusubirwamwo;
- Impanuro zitangwa n’abantu n’imiryango itandukanye.


## Gushiramwo no gukoresha


Intumbero y’iyi nsiguro y’ugushiraho ni ukukuyobora mu ntambwe yose y’ugushiraho. Ntituzodondora ibikorwa vyo gukora kuruta uburongozi buzwi, ariko tuzokwereka inzira ibereye mu gihe tuzoguha impanuro n’ubuhinga.


Kubera imvo z’ubumenyi ngirakamaro, izo mpanuro zizoba zishingiye ku bikoresho vya macOS na Linux.

🛠️

Imbere yo gutangura ubu buryo, urabe neza ko ufise urufunguzo rwa USB rufise umuvuduko wo gusoma mutoyi wa 150 MB/s n'ubushobozi bwa 8 GB, vyiza ni USB 3.0.


Ibisabwa:



- 1 Urufunguzo rwa USB, rw'imirizo gusa, rufise ubushobozi nibura bwa 8 GB
- Mudasobwa ihuye kuri interineti ikoresheje Linux, macOS, (canke Windows)
- Nk’isaha imwe y’ubusa, bivanye n’ingene Internet yawe yihuta, harimwo n’isaha n’igice yo gushiramwo (dosiye ya 1,3 GB yo gukuraho)

## Intambwe ya 2: Suzuma ivyo waronse


![image](assets/2.webp)


🔗 **Igice c'imirizo yemewe:**


Gusuzuma ivyo wavuyeko bituma bimenyekana ko vyasohowe n’abahinguzi b’imirizo kandi ko bitarononekaye canke ngo bifatwe mu gihe c’ugukuraho.


Birashoboka ko ugenzura n’amaboko ko dosiye uherutse gukuraho ari yo witeze ukoresheje PGP, ariko ata bumenyi buhanitse, ukwo kugenzura gutanga urugero rumwe n’urwo kugenzura JavaScript kuri paji yo gukuraho, mu gihe ari ikintu kigoye cane kandi gishobora gukora amakosa.


Kugira ngo ugenzure dosiye, koresha ubuto "Hitamwo ivyo ushobora gukuraho..." buboneka mu gice cemewe!

- Linux:**
- macOS:** https://imirizo.boum.org/shiramwo/urutonde


Iyi ntambwe yo gushiramwo Tails ku rufunguzo rwawe rwa USB ni yo igoye cane muri iyo nzira yose, cane cane iyo utigeze uyikora. Iciyumviro gihambaye cane ni uguhitamwo uburyo bubereye mu gice cemewe c’umurongo wawe: Linux canke macOS.


Ivyo bikoresho bimaze gushirwaho no gutegurwa nk'uko vyasabwe, dosiye ifise .img extension irashobora gukoporwa ku rufunguzo rwawe (ugukuraho amakuru yose ariho) kugira ngo ibe "bootable" yigenga.

## Intambwe ya 5: Murakaza neza mu Mirizo!


![image](assets/6.webp)


🔗 **Igice c'imirizo cemewe:**


Haciye umunota umwe canke ibiri igikoresho co gufungura n’igikoresho co gufungura, igikoresho co kwakira kiraboneka.


![image](assets/7.webp)


Mu gicapo c’Ikaze, hitamwo ururimi rwawe n’ingene klavye yawe iteye mu gice kivuga ngo Ururimi n’Akarere. Fyonda kuri Start Imirizo.


![image](assets/8.webp)


Niba mudasobwa yawe idafise umurongo ku rubuga rwawe, usabwe kuraba amabwirizwa yemewe ya Tails kugira ngo agufashe kwifatanya n'urubuga rwawe ata Wi-Fi (igice "Gerageza Wi-Fi yawe").


Iyo umaze kwifatanya n’urubuga rwo mu karere, umuhinga mu guhuza Tor araboneka kugira ngo agufashe kwifatanya n’urubuga rwa Tor.


![image](assets/9.webp)


Ushobora gutangura gusura ata mazina yawe, ugatohoza amahitamwo n’amaporogarama ari muri Tails. Nimwinovore, mufise umwanya mwinshi wo gukora amakosa, kuko ata kintu na kimwe gihindurwa kuri USB stick... Ugusubira gutangura kwanyu gukurikira kuzoba kwibagiwe ivyo mwaciyemwo vyose!


## Mu burongozi buzoza...


Umaze kugerageza gatoyi n’inkoni yawe bwite ya Tails USB, tuzokwihweza ibindi biganiro biteye imbere mu kindi kiganiro, nk’ibi:



- Guhindura urufunguzo n'**verisiyo nshasha ya Tails**;
- Gutegura no gukoresha **ububiko buhoraho**;
- Shiraho **iyindi porogaramu**.


Gushika aho, nk'uko vyama, nimba ufise ikibazo, ntutinye kubisangiza umuryango wa Agora256. Turiko turigira hamwe kugira ejo tube beza kurusha uko turi uno musi!
