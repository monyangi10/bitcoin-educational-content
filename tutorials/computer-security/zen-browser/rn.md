Mu gihe c’ubuhinga bwa none, Google Chrome ni yo iganza n’ibice birenga 65% vy’isoko, ariko ubwo butegetsi buravyura ibibazo bihambaye ku bijanye n’ubuzima bwite n’ubudasa bw’ubuhinga. Chrome, nk'ibindi bikoresho bizwi cane, ikorakoranya amakuru menshi kugira ngo igaburire ubuhinga bwo kwamamaza bwa Google.



![Parts de marché des navigateurs](assets/fr/01.webp)



Kubera ivyo bintu vy’ukuri, ama browser mashasha ariko araseruka afise ubuhinga butandukanye: guhuza ubumenyi bw’abakoresha bo muri iki gihe n’ukwubaha ubuzima bwite. Zen Browser, yatangujwe mu 2024, ni kimwe muri iyo nzira, itanga uburyo bushasha bushingiye kuri Firefox ariko bukaba bwahinduwe ku bakoresha b’ubu.



Zen Browser igaragara kubera Interface yayo yihariye ifise amabara ahagaze, ahantu h’akazi ho gutunganya ibiganiro vyawe, n’ibiranga umusaruro nka Split View. Ariko birenze ivyo bishasha vy’ubuhinga, ni hejuru ya vyose Commitment yayo ku buzima bwite bituma iryoshe: nta telemetry, uburinzi bukomeye bwo kurwanya gukurikirana, n’iterambere ry’abanyagihugu mu mucyo.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzobona ingene Zen Browser ishobora guhindura uburyo ukoresha mu guhuza ubushobozi, guhindura umuntu n’ubuzima bwite.

## Kumenyesha umucukumbuzi wa Zen



**Zen Browser** ni umucukumbuzi w’urubuga ku buntu kandi yuguruye akomoka kuri Mozilla Firefox, yateguwe n’umuryango w’abakozi b’intahemuka kuva muri Ntwarante 2024. Bitandukanye n’umucukumbuzi wo mu masosiyete akomeye y’ubuhinga, Zen Browser ntashigikiwe n’ishirahamwe ryose ry’ubudandaji, kandi iterwa inkunga n’imiryango gusa y’intererano zivuye.



**Iciyumviro gihambaye:** Umucukumbuzi wa Zen ubu ari muri **Verisiyo ya Beta**. N'ubwo bimeze neza mu gukoreshwa buri musi, witege ivyagezwe kenshi n'ibibazo bimwe bimwe.



Filozofiya y'uwo mugambi ivugwa mu ncamake n'invugo yawo: "Murakaze kuri Internet ituje". Ubu buryo buhinduka mu mucukumbuzi yitwararika ubumenyi bwawe bw’abakoresha aho kwitwararika amakuru yawe bwite, irondera uburinganire butunganye hagati y’ubwiza, umuvuduko n’ubuzima bwite.



### Interface yahinduwe kugira ngo ikore



Zen Browser ihindura cane uburyo bwo gusura n'ibintu bishasha vyinshi:



**Ibipande bihagaze:** Bitandukanye n'ibikoresho vya kera, Zen yerekana ibipande vyawe mu ruhande ruhagaze aho kugaragaza mu buryo buringaniye. Iryo hitamwo ry’ubuhinga, ryahumekewe na Arc Browser, rituma haba ahantu heza ho kwerekana kandi rituma umuntu ashobora kugenda neza, cane cane iyo ufise amabara menshi yuguruye.



**Ibibanza vy'akazi:** Gutunganya ama tabs yawe hakurikijwe umugambi canke insanganyamatsiko mu bibanza vy'umwihariko. Nk'akarorero, umwanya wa "Work" w'ibice vyawe vy'umwuga, umwanya wa "Watch" w'ugusoma kwawe, n'ibindi. Ushobora guhindura ubwo nyene ukava mu kibanza kimwe uja mu kindi.



**Split View:** Erekana imbuga nyinshi iruhande n'uruhande mw'idirisha rimwe, ni vyiza kugereranya amakuru canke gukora ku masoko menshi icarimwe.



**Glance:** Niwihutire kubona imbere y'igihe uruja n'uruza mu idirisha ry'igihe gito ata gufungura urupapuro rushasha, ni vyiza ku bijanye no kurondera amakuru ataco utakaje.



### Ubuzima bwite ku buryo mburabuzi



Zen Browser ikoresha uburinzi bukomeye bw'ubuzima bwite:





- Ivyiza vyo kurwanya gukurikirana:** Guhagarika ubwavyo abakurikirana, amakuru y'uwundi muntu n'inyandiko z'ibimenyetso vy'intoke
- Telemetry yazimye:** Nta makuru yoherejwe kuri serveri zo hanze
- DNS kuri HTTPS:** Gushiramwo ibisabwa vyawe vya DNS kugira ngo ntukurikirane
- Kugabanya ivyerekeye Google:** Zen Browser ikuraho amahuza menshi ku bikorwa vya Google, naho bimwe bishobora kugumaho (gucukura bitekanye, gusubiramwo)



### Guhindura ibintu biteye imbere na Zen Mods



Zen itanga uburyo bwihariye bwo guhindura ibintu ku buryo bubereye n’ibidukikije bifise **Zen Mods**: urutonde rw’insanganyamatsiko n’ihinduka ryakozwe n’abanyagihugu kugira ngo rihindure ukuntu umucukumbuzi asa n’inyifato yiwe.



**Ivyiyumviro vya Zen bikundwa cane:**





- SuperPins** ⭐: Guhindura ama tabs yashizweko amabuto ateye igomwe kugira ngo uboneke nk'umwuga Interface
- Ubumwe**: Uburyo buhoraho, bubonerana buhuza umurongo wa URL, umurongo wo ku ruhande n'ibimenyetso
- Better Find Bar**: Yimurira umurongo w'ubushakashatsi hejuru kugira ngo ubone ubuhinga bwiza
- Ukwagura umurongo wo ku ruhande iyo uhagaze**: Ukwagura umurongo wo ku ruhande wihuta iyo uhagaze, kwongerera umwanya wo ku rubuga
- Better Undowned Tabs**: Bituma ubucungezi bw'ubwenge bugenda neza n'ibimenyetso bigaragara ku bitabo bitakora
- URL Bar yasukuwe**: Interface yasukuye Address bar, ikuraho Elements idakenewe.
- Transparent Zen**: ingaruka nziza z'uguca ibibatsi n'ibishushanyo bimeze neza

**⚠️ Iciyumviro gihambaye ku bijanye no guhindura umuntu ku giti ciwe no gufata urutoke:**


Uko urushiriza guhindura Zen Browser (insanganyamatsiko, ivyungura, ama mods), niko **ikirenge cawe ca digitale** kiba kidasanzwe rero kikaba gishobora gukurikirana. Ni ugusenyera ku mugozi umwe kw'ishimikiro:




- Uguhindura umuntu ku giti ciwe** = Ubumenyi bwiza bw'ukoresha ARIKO budasanzwe, bushobora kumenyekana bitagoranye
- Itunganywa mburabuzi** = Ikirenge gisanzwe ARIKO ubumenyi buke bw'umuntu ku giti ciwe



Zen Browser yahisemwo ubumenyi bw’abakoresha kuruta ukutamenyekana. Niba ikintu ushira imbere ari ukutamenyekana, hitamwo Tor Browser canke Mullvad Browser, zitegeka imiterere imwe ku bakoresha bose.



Ikindi, kuba ishingiye kuri Firefox, Zen irahuye n’ibidukikije vyose vy’ukwagura Firefox.



## Ivyiza n'ibibi



### ✅ Ibintu bihambaye





- Ubuzima bwite ku mibumbe:** Uburinzi bwo kurwanya gukurikirana burakora, telemetry irazimye, nta kwegeranya amakuru
- Innovative Interface:** Ama tabs ahagaze, ibibanza vyo gukoreramwo, Split View bitera imbere cane mu bijanye n'umwimbu
- Ivyagezwe vyihuta:** Guhuza na Firefox mu masaha atarenga 72 ku bice vy'umutekano
- Guhindura biteye imbere:** Insanganyamatsiko z'abanyagihugu, gutunganya neza, guhuza ukwaguka kwa Firefox
- Inkomoko yuguruye n'umuryango:** Kode igaragara, iterambere ry'ubufatanye, ukwigenga kuri Big Tech



### ❌ Imipaka y'ubu





- Nta verisiyo y'amatelefone ngendanwa:** Iboneka kuri PC gusa (amadirisha, macOS, Linux)
- Uguhuza kwa DRM:** Netflix, Disney+, Spotify n'izindi serivisi zo gutanga amakuru ntizikora ubu
- Umugambi muto:** Itsinda rito, infashanyo y'abanyagihugu, rimwe na rimwe ibikoko
- Ivyigwa:** Interface bitandukanye, bisaba guhindura abamenyereye gukoresha amabara aringaniye

### Gutunganya ivyagezwe mburabuzi



Kuva mu ntango, Zen Browser ikoresha uburyo bwo gucungera ubuzima bwite burengeye ubucukumbuzi bwinshi:





- Uburinzi bukomeye bwo kurwanya gukurikirana:** "Ikigereranyo" urugero rwakoreshejwe na mburabuzi, kubuza:
  - Amakuru y'ikurikirana n'amakuru akomeye
  - Inyandiko z'abakurikirana kwamamaza (Isesengura rya Google, Pixel ya Facebook, n'ibindi)
  - Amafaranga y'ibanga akoresha CPU yawe ku mafaranga y'ibanga ya Miner
  - Gucapura urutoke biciye ku gipande, WebGL, amajwi





- Ukwitandukanya kw'amakuru yose:** Ukwitandukanya kw'umuntu wa mbere kubuza urubuga rumwe gusoma amakuru y'uwundi
- Telemetry yazimye cane:** Ivyinshi mu bimenyetso vyakuweho, naho nyene hariho amahuza n'ibikorwa vya Mozilla/Google yoshobora kugumaho kandi bisaba ko umuntu akoresha ibindi bikoresho
- DNS itekanye ku buryo busanzwe:** DNS-over-HTTPS yashobojwe kugira ngo ntihagire uwutahura ibisabwa vyawe
- HTTPS-Gusa yashobojwe:** Guhatira amahuza yashizwemwo amakuru ku mbuga zose

- Harimwo **Ukurinda amakuru yose**: gutandukanya amakuru ku rubuga kugira ngo ntihagire uwukurikirana



**Bikomeye (uburinzi burengeye):**




- Uburinzi bwongerewe ariko bushobora gusenyura imbuga zimwe zimwe canke ibirimwo
- Amabuye: abakurikirana imigenderanire, amakuru y'urubuga, gukurikirana ibirimwo mu madirisha **yose**, abashakashatsi bazwi n'abakekwa
- Ni vyiza ku bakoresha bazi utuntu n'utundi



**Ihinduwe (ubugenzuzi bw'intete):**




- Hitamwo neza abakurikirana n'inyandiko zo kubuza
- Amahitamwo atandukanye: Amakuru, Gukurikirana ibirimwo, Cryptomining, Abakurikirana bazwi/bakekwa
- Ni vyiza kugira ngo utunganye neza ivyo ukeneye

Amagenamiterere > Ubuzima bwite n'umutekano > DNS kuri HTTPS (hasi kuri paji)

**Ukwongerekana kw'uburinzi (ni vyiza):**

**Uburinzi burengeye (abakoresha bateye imbere):**

- Imburi y'umutekano imbere yo gukoresha sisitemu ya DNS

## Gutahura ko DRM itaboneka muri Zen Browser



### DRM ni iki?



**DRMs (Uburongozi bw’uburenganzira bwa digitale)** ni ubuhinga bwo gukingira bushiramwo amakuru y’ubuhinga bwa none kugira ngo ntihagire umuntu ashobora gukopa. Bisaba umurongo w’umucukumbuzi (nk’uwa **Google Widevine**) kugira ngo ushobore gusobanura no gusoma ibinyamakuru birinzwe.



**Ibikorwa bisaba DRM:**




- Gutanga amasanamu:** Netflix, Disney+, HBO Max, amasanamu ya mbere ya Amazon
- Umuziki wo hejuru:** Spotify wo hejuru, Umuziki wo kuri YouTube, Deezer
- Amahugurwa yo kuri internet:** Udemy, Coursera (amasanamu akinzwe)



### Kubera iki umucukumbuzi wa Zen adafise DRM



**Impamvu nyamukuru:**


1. **Igiciro n'uburemere:** Uruhusha rwa Google Widevine si ubuntu kandi rusaba uburyo butoroshe bwo kwemererwa


2. **Ivyiyumviro vy'umugambi:** DRM ni "agasanduku k'umwirabura" k'umunyagihugu kadahuye n'uburyo bwo gukoresha inkomoko yuguruye n'ukwigenga kuri Google


3. **Ivyuma bike:** Ishirahamwe ryibanda ku buhinga bushasha bwa Interface n'ibanga



### Ingaruka ngirakamaro



**❌ Ibikorwa ntibishikwako:**


Netflix, Disney+, Spotify y'imbere, YouTube y'imbere, Udemy/Coursera amashure y'amahugurwa yishurwa



![Erreur DRM Prime Video](assets/fr/17.webp)


*Ubutumwa bw'ikosa busanzwe igihe ugerageza gukina ibirimwo birinzwe na DRM*



**✅ Ibikorwa bikora:**


YouTube y'ubuntu, Twitch, Vimeo, imbuga z'amakuru, imbuga ngurukanabumenyi, ibiganiro



**Inyishu zo guca mu nzira:**




- Koresha Firefox/Chrome ku gukurikirana gusa
- Ibikorwa vy'imvukira (Netflix, Spotify)
- Hitamwo ibirimwo bitagira DRM (Youtube, Twitch, Bandcamp, PeerTube)



**Ivyo bimeze ubu:** Ishirahamwe rya Zen ryatanguye igikorwa co kuronka uruhusha rwa Widevine, ariko nta gihe c’ibikorwa vyatanzwe. Ivyo bivana n’ukwemererwa na Google.

- Ihitamwo riri hejuru y'umurongo wo ku ruhande
- Guhingura uturere tw'insanganyamatsiko
- Guhindura vuba hagati y'ibivugwa
- Ubuhinga bushizweho buraboneka mu bibanza vyose

## Iciyumviro



Zen Browser igereranya impwemu nziza mu bidukikije vy’umucukumbuzi w’urubuga. Mu gufatanya ubuhinga bushasha kandi butanga inyungu Interface n’icubahiro gikomeye c’ubuzima bwite, itanga ubundi buryo bwo kwizigirwa bwo gukoresha ama browser y’ibigo bikomeye vy’ubuhinga.



Ivyiyumviro vyayo bihagaze n’ibibanza vy’akazi birahindura vy’ukuri ubumenyi bwo gusura ku bariko barakora imigambi myinshi. Filozofiya yayo "no Google" n'iterambere ry'abanyagihugu bituma iba ihitamwo rihuye n'abakoresha bahagaritse umutima ku busegaba bwabo bwa digitale.



Naho igifise utunenge dukeyi (nta telefone ngendanwa, DRM ibuze), Zen Browser irakuze bihagije kugira ngo ikoreshwe ku musi ku musi, kandi iriko iratera imbere ningoga kubera umuryango wayo ukora cane.



Ku ba bitcoiners n’abakoresha ubuhinga baha agaciro umusaruro n’ubuzima bwite, Zen Browser irabereye kugerageza. Ushobora neza kwemera ubwo buryo bushasha, butekanye kandi bukora neza bwo gusoma.
