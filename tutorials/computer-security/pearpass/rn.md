Imbuga ngurukanabumenyi, uburyo bwo gutanga ubutumwa, ibikorwa vy’umwuga, imbuga z’ivy’amahera: buri kimwe muri ivyo bihugu gishingiye ku banga, iyo rishobora gusenyuka bishobora kugira ingaruka zikomeye ku buzima bwawe.



Ariko naho ibitero bigenda vyiyongera, ingeso mbi ziracari zikwiragiye cane mu bantu: amajambo y’ibanga adakomeye, asubirwamwo gukoreshwa, abitswe atarakingirwa canke yibukwa mu buryo budatomoye. **PearPass ni umucungezi w’amajambo y’ibanga wa peer-to-peer, local-first kandi ufunguye, watekerejwe kugira ngo usubize umukoresha ububasha bwose ku makuru yiwe.**

## Ni kuki wohitamwo PearPass?



Umucungerezi w’amajambo banga ni porogarama itanga, ikabika kandi igatunganya amajambo banga yawe yose mu buryo butekanye. Aho gufata mu mutwe canke gusubira gukoresha amajambo y’ibanga, urafise ibanga rimwe gusa ryo kurinda: ijambo ry’ibanga ry’ingenzi, rikaba ari ryo ry’ibanga ry’ibanga ryawe ryose. Ubwo buryo buratuma bishoboka gukoresha amajambo y’ibanga yihariye, maremare, ataco akora ku gikorwa cose, bikagabanya ingorane zo kwibagirwa n’ugusenyuka, mu gihe bigabanya ingaruka z’ugusohoka kwose gushobora kubaho. Uyu musi, ni igikoresho c’ishimikiro c’ubuhinga bwa none umuntu wese akwiye kuba ariko arakoresha.



Hariho ivyiciro bibiri nyamukuru vy’umucungerezi w’ijambobanga. Ku ruhande rumwe, hariho porogarama zikoreshwa mu karere gusa, zitekanye cane kuko amakuru atigera abikwa kuri server nyamukuru, ariko ntaco zikora cane, kuko zitagufasha guhuza amakuru yawe mu buryo bworoshe hagati y’ibikoresho vyinshi (mudasobwa, telefone ngendanwa, tablette...). Ku rundi ruhande, abacungezi b’ijambobanga bakoresheje igicu babika amakuru yawe ku maserukira yabo maze bakayahuza ku bikoresho vyawe vyose. Inyungu nyamukuru zavyo ni uko zishobora gukoreshwa neza, ariko zisaba ko umuntu agira ivyo akora ku bijanye n’umutekano, kubera ko amajambo yawe y’ibanga abikwa ku bikorwa remezo udashobora kubigenzura.



PearPass iramenagura n’ibigirankana n’ivyo bigereranyo vyose bibiri. Ishira hagati y’abarongozi bo mu karere n’imiti yo mu gicu: ishoboza guhuza amajambo y’ibanga yawe, mu gihe yemeza ko atazokwigera abikwa ku maserukira yo kure. Ivyo bituma amakuru yawe yose abikwa aho hantu ku bikoresho vyawe, kandi uguhuza hagati y’amamashini menshi ni ugukorana n’abandi gusa. Iyi nyubakwa irakuraho ingingo zimwe zimwe zishobora kunanirwa zijanye n’amakuru ahuriweko: nta maserukira yo guhungabanya, kandi nta bikorwa remezo vy’uwundi muntu vyo gushika ku makuru yawe. Ivy’uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’ibikoresho vyawe birashirwa mu buryo buziguye, ivyo bikaba bituma imfunguruzo ufise zonyene zituma umuntu ashobora kuronka amakuru.



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Kugira ngo ivyo bishoboke, nk’uko izina ryayo rivyerekana, PearPass yizigiye **Pears**, ubuhinga bwo gukorana n’abandi bushingiye ku kurema no gushitsa ibikorwa bitagira server. Pears itanga ibidukikije vyo gukora, uburyo bwo gukwiragiza n’ibice vy’uruja n’uruza bikenewe kugira ngo ikoreshe ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bushitse, bishobora gukorana neza hagati y’urunganwe, ata bikorwa remezo vy’imbere. Ku bijanye na PearPass, Pears itanga ukuvumbura ibikoresho, gushinga ubufatanye bufise amakuru n’uguhuza ububiko bw’ijambobanga hagati y’amamashini yawe. Ubwo buryo buratuma PearPass iguma ikora, ishobora gukomera kandi yigenga ku butegetsi bwose bwo hanze.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/computer-security/communication/pears-6d42b312-c69f-4504-8f71-b03b56c42fdd

Uretse iyo nyubakwa nshasha, PearPass ni open-source yose, kandi ibikorwa vyayo vyose ni ubuntu. Iyi porogaramu kandi yaragenzuwe n’ishirahamwe Secfault Security ryigenga. Uretse ubuhinga bwayo bwihariye, PearPass iratanga ibintu vyose vya kera vyitezwe ku mucungerezi mwiza w’ijambobanga, ivyo tuzobibona muri iyi nyigisho yose.



Muri make, aho abarongozi b’amajambo banga ba kera bagusaba kwizigira ishirahamwe n’abakozi baryo, PearPass ifata ingingo y’ubusegaba: nta gicu, nta konti zihuriweko, nta bahuza. Uguma ugenzura gusa amakuru yawe, mu gihe wungukira ku gukorana hagati y’ibikoresho vyawe.



## None noshiramwo gute PearPass?



PearPass iraboneka ku bikoresho vyose: Windows, Linux, macOS, Android, iOS n’ibindi bikoresho vyo gukoresha mu mucukumbuzi. Ntaco bimaze gushiramwo Pears ku mashine yawe: PearPass ikora ubwayo.



### Gushiramwo kuri Windows



Ku Windows, PearPass itangwa nk’igikoresho ca kera co gushiramwo. Genda kuri [paji yemewe yo gukuraho](https://pass.pears.com/gukuraho), hanyuma ukande kuri buto `Gukuraho Windows`.



Iyo dosiye imaze gukurwako, fungura installer hanyuma ukurikize intambwe zigaragazwa n’umuhinga. Porogaramu ishobora rero gushikwako ukoresheje `Start Menu` canke biciye ku nzira ngufi yo kuri mudasobwa.



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



### Gushiramwo kuri macOS



Ku macOS, PearPass ikwiragizwa nk'ishusho ya disiki (`.dmg`). Genda kuri [paji yemewe yo gukuraho](https://pass.pears.com/gukuraho) maze uhitemwo verisiyo ihuye n’ubwubatsi bwa Mac yawe (Intel canke Apple Silicon). Uhejeje gukuraho, fungura dosiye `.dmg` maze utangure porogaramu muri dosiye ya `Iporogarama`.



Igihe macOS itanguye ubwa mbere, izokwerekana ubutumwa bw’umutekano bwerekana ko iyo porogarama yavuye kuri Internet: wemeze gusa kugira ngo ukomeze.



### Gushiramwo kuri Linux



Ku Linux, PearPass iboneka mu buryo bwa `.AppImage`, bushobora gukorana n'ibindi bikoresho vyinshi ataco bishingiyeko. Gukuraho dosiye `.AppImage` kuri [paji yemewe yo gukuraho](https://pass.pears.com/gukuraho), hanyuma uyifungure ataco uhinduye ukanda kabiri.



Bivanye n'ibidukikije vyawe, ushobora gukenera gutuma dosiye ikora biciye ku miterere ya dosiye (kanda iburyo) canke n'itegeko `chmod +x`. Iyo imaze kwemererwa, PearPass iratangura nk’ikoreshwa ryigenga.



### Gushiramwo kwagura umucukumbuzi



PearPass itanga uburyo bwo kwagura umucukumbuzi kugira ngo winjire ubwawe kandi wihute gushika ku safe yawe mu gihe uriko uracukura ku rubuga. Ukwaguka ubu kuraboneka kuri Google Chrome n'ibikoresho bihuye. Kugira ngo uyishiremwo, genda kuri [paji yemewe yo gukuraho](https://iduka ry'urubuga.google.com/ibisobanuro.



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Iyo umaze kwongerako, urashobora kuyishira ku mwanyabikoresho kugira ngo uyironke ningoga. Gukoresha iyo extension rero bisaba ko ukora ihuriro n’iporogarama ya PearPass ishizwe ahantu kuri mudasobwa yawe (ivyo tuzobigarukako mu nyuma mu nyigisho).



### Gushiramwo kuri iOS na Android



Kuri iPhone na Android, ushobora gukuraho porogaramu mu bubiko bwawe bwa porogaramu:

## None nokora gute igikapu ca PearPass?



Iyo utanguje PearPass kuri mudasobwa yawe, iyo porogarama irakuyobora mu ntambwe zibiri: gushinga ijambobanga ryawe ry’ibanze, hanyuma ugakora safe yawe ya mbere.



### Gushiraho ijambobanga nyamukuru



Igihe porogaramu itangura ku biro, ukande kuri buto ya `Skip` hanyuma `Continue` kugira ngo unyure mu gicapo c'intango maze ushikire intambwe y'ugutunganya ijambobanga ry'ingenzi.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Inyuma y’aho haza intambwe ihambaye yo guhitamwo ijambobanga ryawe ry’ingenzi. Nk’uko twabibonye mu ntangamarara, iri jambobanga rirahambaye cane, kuko riguha uburenganzira bwo kuronka ayandi majambobanga yawe yose yabitswe kuri manager. Mu buryo bw’ubuhinga, ikoreshwa mu gukuraho imfunguruzo z’ubuhinga zikoreshwa mu gupfuka amakuru yawe.



Ijambobanga nyamukuru rifise ingorane zibiri nyamukuru: kuritakaza no kurihungabanya. Niwatakaza ukuryinjiramwo, ntuzoshobora gusubira kwinjira mu bimenyetso vyawe. Mu vy’ukuri, PearPass ntizigera ibika ijambobanga ryawe nyamukuru: **niritakara, n’ibimenyetso vyawe biratakara burundu**. Nta buryo bwo kubisubirana buhari. Ku rundi ruhande, niyoba rihungabanyijwe maze umuterabwoba akaronka ukwinjira kuri kimwe mu bikoresho vyawe, azoshobora kwinjira mu makonti yawe yose.



Kugira ngo ntutakaze, urashobora gukora ububiko bw’ijambobanga ryawe nyamukuru, nk’akarorero ku mpapuro, maze ukaribika ahantu hatagira inkomanzi. Ivyiza ni uko iyo nkuru y’inyuma uyipfuka mu ibahasha kugira ngo ushobore kugenzura rimwe na rimwe ko ata n’umwe ayironse. Ku rundi ruhande, ntukigere ukora ububiko bw’iryo jambobanga.



Kugira ngo ugabanye ingorane zo gusenyura, ijambobanga ryawe ry’ingenzi ritegerezwa kuba rikomeye. Ikwiye kuba ndende uko bishoboka kwose, ikaba irimwo ibimenyetso vyinshi bitandukanye kandi igatorwa mu buryo butari bwo vy’ukuri. Mu mwaka w’2025, impanuro ntoyi zisaba ko haba nibura inyuguti 13 harimwo inyuguti nini n’into, imibare n’ibimenyetso, igihe cose ijambobanga ryoba ari ry’impfagusa. Ariko rero, nosaba ko ijambobanga rigira nibura inyuguti 20, nimba atari nyinshi, rifise ubwoko bwose bw’inyuguti, kugira ngo umutekano ubeho.



Injira ijambobanga ryawe ry'ingenzi mu kibanza catanzwe, ryemeze ubugira kabiri, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya `Continue`.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Uzoca urungikwa ku rubuga rwo kwinjira: winjize ijambobanga ryawe ry’ingenzi kugira ngo umenye ko vyose bikora neza.



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



### Rema ububiko bwawe bwambere



Umaze kwinjira, PearPass izogusaba gukora safe yawe ya mbere. Igikapu ni ikintu gipfutse aho amajambo y’ibanga yawe, ID, amakete yawe atekanye n’ayandi makuru abikwamwo. Buri safe iri ukwayo kandi ishobora kumenyekana n’izina ryihariye, bikagufasha gutunganya amakuru yawe akurikije uko ukoresha (ivy’umuntu ku giti ciwe, ivy’umwuga, ivy’imigambi yihariye...).



Fyonda kuri buto ya `Rema ububiko bushasha`.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Hitamwo izina ry'iki kibanza, hanyuma ukande `Rema ikibanza gishasha` kugira ngo uheze kurema.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Ico kigega cawe gica gica gitegurwa gukoreshwa. Ushobora gutangura kwongerako amajambo banga, kwinjira canke amajambo atekanye ubwo nyene.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



## Nokwongerako gute kwinjira muri PearPass?



Umaze gukora safe yawe, urashobora gutangura kubika ibintu vyawe muri yo. PearPass irashigikira kwandika ubwoko bwinshi bw’ibintu:




- kwinjira ku rubuga canke kuri serivisi ;
- akaranga: amakuru yawe bwite kugira ngo wuzuze ningoga amafishi, ariko kandi ubike inyandiko z’akaranga ataco uhinduye muri PearPass ;
- ikarita y’inguzanyo: inomero z’ikarata yawe y’inguzanyo kugira ngo wihute kwishura igihe ugura kuri Internet;
- Wi-Fi: amajambo banga y'imihora yawe ya Wi-Fi ;
- PassPhrase: ijambo ry’ibanga rigizwe n’amajambo menshi (imburi: ndahanura cane ko utorikoresha ku majambo y’ibanga wallet Bitcoin);
- iciyumviro: gukora amajambo atekanye ;
- umugenzo: iki kintu kigufasha kurema ubwoko bw'ibintu bimenyerewe, bihuye n'ivyo ukeneye.



Tangana n’ugufungura PearPass maze winjiremwo ukoresheje ijambobanga ryawe ry’ingenzi.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Hitamwo isandugu wipfuza kubikamwo iki kimenyetso.



![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)



Ku rupapuro rw’intango, fyonda ku buto kugira ngo wongereko ikintu gishasha, bivanye n’ubwoko bw’amakuru wipfuza kwandika. Mu gihe canje, nshaka kwongerako urubuga rwo kwinjira muri konti yanje ku rubuga rwa Plan ₿ Academy: rero ndafyonda kuri buto ya `Create a Login`.



![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)



Umaze guhitamwo ubwoko bw’ikintu wipfuza kwongerako, haca haboneka urupapuro, rugufasha kwinjiza amakuru ajanye n’ico gikorwa. Bivanye n’ivyo ukeneye, urashobora kwinjiza izina rya serivisi, kwinjira, ijambobanga be n’aho bisabwa, aderesi y’urubuga n’ibindi bimenyetso.



PearPass kandi irafise igikoresho co gukora ijambobanga, kigufasha gukora ijambobanga rikomeye uhereye ku rupapuro. Kugira ngo uyikoreshe, fyonda ku kimenyetso kigereranya utudomo dutoduto dutatu mu mwanya `Ijambobanga`, uhitemwo uburebure wipfuza, hanyuma ukande kuri `Insert password`.



Ivyo vyose bimaze kwuzuzwa, fyonda ku buto `Bika` kugira ngo ubike ikimenyetso mu gisanduku.



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Ikimenyetso ubu carazigamye. Iboneka mu rutonde rw’ibintu biri mu sandugu yatowe, kandi ushobora kuyibona ukanda kuri yo.



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Ushobora guhindura ikintu mu buryo bworoshe ukandako, hanyuma ukanda kuri buto ya `Edit`. Ushobora kandi kuyifuta ukanda ku tudodo dutatu turi hejuru iburyo bw'ikigaragaza, hanyuma ukanda kuri `Delete element`.



![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)



Ku bijanye na telefone ngendanwa, ivyiyumviro biguma ari uko biri, naho nyene interface yahinduwe. Uhejeje kwinjira, uhitemwo safe wipfuza, ukande kuri buto `+`, uhitemwo ubwoko bw’ikintu uzorema, hanyuma wuzuze amakuru akenewe.



![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)



## None PearPass yotunganywa gute?



Nk’uko twabibonye mu bice vyabanje, PearPass iragufasha kurema amazu menshi atandukanye. Ivyo bituma bishoboka gutandukanya imikoreshereze itandukanye kandi bikaba ari urugero rwa mbere rw'imitunganirize y'umucungerezi w'ijambobanga ryawe. Uvuye kuri paji y’intango, urashobora kuva mu kibanza kimwe uja mu kindi mu buryo bworoshe ukanda ku mwampi uri hejuru ibubamfu bw’ibarabara.



![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)



Ubundi buryo bwo gutunganya amajambo y’ibanga yawe, mbere no mu nzu y’ububiko, ni uguhingura amadosiye. Kugira ngo ubikore, mu nkingi y'ibubamfu y'ikigaragaza, fyonda ku kimenyetso `+` kiri iruhande ya `All Folders`, hanyuma winjize izina ry'idosiye wipfuza gukora.



![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)



Ushobora rero kubika ibimenyetso vy'uguhitamwo kwawe, haba mu gihe urema ikintu, canke mu nyuma ukanda kuri `Guhindura`. Ico ubwirizwa gukora n’uguhitamwo dosiye wipfuza iri hejuru ibubamfu bw’ifomu.



![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)



Uhejeje gufungura ikintu, nk’ugufungura, urashobora gufyonda ku kimenyetso c’inyenyeri kiri hejuru iburyo bw’ibarabara kugira ngo ugishire mu vyo ukunda. Ivyo ukunda birashobora kuronswa ningoga muri dosiye yihariye, uretse dosiye yabo y’ishimikiro.



![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, hariho umurongo wo gushakisha hejuru y’ibarabara, kugira ngo ushobore kuronka ningoga ikintu uriko urarondera, naho ububiko bwawe bwoba burimwo ibimenyetso vyinshi.



## None noshobora gute gukoranya PearPass kuri telefone yanje ngendanwa?



Ubu ko ufise ububiko bukora bufise ibintu bibitswe kuri mudasobwa yawe, birashoboka ko uzoshaka gushika ku majambo y’ibanga yawe ukoresheje telefone yawe canke ikindi gikoresho. PearPass iragufasha gukorana n'umuyobozi wawe ku mashini nyinshi ukoresheje Pears. Reka tumenye ingene bigenda.



Kugira ngo utangure, ku mashini y’inkomoko (nk’akarorero, mudasobwa yawe), winjire mu bubiko bwawe ukoresheje ijambobanga ryawe ry’ibanze. Ushitse kuri paji y'intango, fyonda ku buto `Ongera igikoresho`, buri hasi iburyo bw'ibarabara.



![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)



PearPass ica itanga uruja n’uruza rw’ubutumire, na rwo nyene ruboneka nk’ikode ya QR, kugira ngo uhuze ububiko bwatowe ku gikoresho uhisemwo.



![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)



Niba ushaka gukorana n’ibindi bikoresho ku gikoresho cawe co kuri telefone ngendanwa, banza ushiremwo iyo porogarama, hanyuma uyifungure. Uzosabwa gukora ijambobanga ry’umuyobozi wawe wa telefone ngendanwa. Ushobora guhitamwo gukoresha ijambobanga rimwe n’iryo kuri mudasobwa yawe, canke irindi ritandukanye. Mu bihe vyose, ukurikize impanuro zimwe: ijambobanga rikomeye kandi ry’impfagusa ryabitswe ku kintu gisanzwe.



![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)



Ushobora rero gukoresha ubuhinga bwa biometric authentication iyo ubishaka, kugira ngo wirinde kwinjiza ijambo banga ryawe ry’ingenzi n’amaboko igihe cose ufunguye telefone yawe ngendanwa.



![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)



Subira winjize ijambobanga ryawe, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya `Continue`.



![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)



Hitamwo `Load a vault`, hanyuma ukande `Scan QR Code` hanyuma ushireko QR code y’ubutumire yerekanwa na PearPass ku mashine yawe y’inkomoko (mudasobwa).



![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)



Ivyumba vyawe vyo kuri mudasobwa yawe no kuri telefone yawe ngendanwa ubu birahuye. Indangamuntu yose yongerewe ku gikoresho kimwe izobikwa neza kandi izosubirwamwo ku kindi.



![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)



Ku gikoresho kigendagenda, urashobora kandi, niwabishaka, gukoresha ukwuzura kwikora kw’ivyicaro. Kubigira, genda muri `Settings > Advanced`, hanyuma ukande kuri buto `Set as Default` mu gice ca `Autofill`.



![Image](assets/fr/30.webp)
