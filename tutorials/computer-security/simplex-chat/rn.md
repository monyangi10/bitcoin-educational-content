SimpleX yatangujwe mu mwaka w’2021, ni porogarama yo gutanga ubutumwa ku buntu, ifise uburyo butandukanye cane bwo gukingira ubuzima bwite. Udakunze WhatsApp, Signal n’izindi nzira zo gutanga ubutumwa zihuriweko, SimpleX iratandukanye cane n’uburongozi bw’abakoresha: nta bimenyetso vy’abakoresha, amazina y’uruyeri, inomero canke imfunguruzo za bose ziboneka. Ukwo kubura kwose kw’ibimenyetso bituma hafi bidashoboka guhuza abakoresha, bikaba vyemeza urugero rwo hejuru rw’ubuzima bwite.



Udakunze porogarama nyinshi zisaba konti canke inomero ya telefone, SimpleX iragufasha gutangura ibiyago mu gusangira uruja n’uruza canke kode ya QR y’igihe gito. Buri nzira irema umurongo wihariye ushizwemwo amakuru, kandi abahuza ntibashobora kuronka canke gusubira guhamagara uwo wohereje ata Exchange igaragara. Ubutumwa burashirwa mu mfuruka kuva ku mpera gushika ku mpera, kandi buca mu bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none (relay servers) bibufuta inyuma y’aho burungitswe, kandi ntibubona uwuburungitse canke uwuburonse, canke imfunguruzo zavyo.



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Ubwubatsi bw’urubuga rwose burashizwe ahantu hamwe kandi ntibugira ubumwe: abakozi ntibamenyana, ntibabika ububiko bw’isi yose, kandi ntibakira imirongo y’abakoresha. Ivyiza kuruta, umukoresha wese arashobora gukoresha no gukoresha server yiwe bwite, mu gihe aguma akorana n’abari ku rubuga rwa bose.



SimpleX ni inkomoko yuguruye yose (abaguzi, amategeko n’abakozi), iboneka kuri Android, iOS, Linux, Windows na macOS. Ububiko bwayo bwo mu karere burashizwemwo amakuru kandi buratwarwa, ku buryo ushobora gukura urutonde rw’ibintu ku gikoresho kimwe ukaja ku kindi utarizigiye server iri hagati.



SimpleX ihuriza hamwe ibintu vyose vya kera vy’ibikorwa vyo gutanga ubutumwa. Ariko rero, ubuhinga bwayo bwo gukora neza buraguma ari buke kuruta ubw’i WhatsApp canke Signal. Bishobora kandi kuba bigoye cane gukoresha, canecane igihe wongeyeko abo mubonana. Rero, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, ni uburyo bubereye bwo gusubirira WhatsApp canke Signal ku bakoresha bashira ubuzima bwite mu mutima w’ivyo bashira imbere, kandi biteguriye, kubera iyo mvo, gutanga uburenganzira bukeyi ku bijanye n’uguhumurizwa kw’abakoresha ku musi ku musi.

## Gushiramwo porogaramu SimpleX Chat



SimpleX Chat iraboneka ku mbuga zose. Ushobora gukura iyo porogaramu mu bubiko bwa porogaramu bwa telefone yawe:




- [Ikiganiro ca Google](https://igikino.google.com/ububiko/iporogaramu/ibisobanuro?id=ikiganiro.yoroshe.iporogaramu);
- [Iduka ry'amaporogarama](itps://amaporogarama.com/us/porogarama/intumwa-yoroshe-y'uguhanahana amakuru/id1605771084);
- [F-Droid](ubutumwa bworoshe/fdroid/).



Kuri Android, birashoboka kandi [gushiramwo biciye kuri APK](https://github.com/ikiganiro-gisanzwe/ikiganiro-gisanzwe/ibisohoka).



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwibanda ku bijanye n’ivyo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa, ariko ndabinginze mumenye ko [ivyo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa na vyo nyene biraboneka](https://simplex.chat/downloads/) (MacOS, Linux na Windows). Birashoboka guhuza verisiyo y’ibiro n’ivy’ukoresha telefone ngendanwa, ariko kugira ngo iyo nzira ikore, ivyo bikoresho vyose bitegerezwa kuba bifatanye n’urubuga rumwe rwo mu karere.

## Rema konti kuri SimpleX Chat



Iyo utanguye gukoresha porogarama, ukande kuri buto ya "*Rema urutonde rwawe*".



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Hitamwo izina ry'ukoresha, rishobora kuba izina ryawe nyaryo canke izina ry'uruyeri, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Rema*".



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Inyuma y’aho, nushireho incuro iyo porogarama izosuzuma ko hari ubutumwa bushasha. Nimba ubuzima bwa bateri ya telefone yawe atari ikibazo, hitamwo "*Instant*" kugira ngo ubone ubutumwa mu gihe nyaco. Niba ushaka kubika bateri yawe no kubuza porogaramu gukora mu nyuma, hitamwo "*Igihe porogaramu iriko irakora*": uzoheza uronke ubutumwa gusa iyo porogaramu yuguruye. Ico umuntu yoshobora gusenyera ku mugozi umwe ni uguhitamwo gusuzumwa mu bihe bitandukanye inyuma y’iminota 10 yose.



Umaze guhitamwo, ukande kuri "*Koresha chat*".



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Ubu uhuye na SimpleX Chat kandi witeguye gutangura kuganira.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



## Gutegura ikiganiro coroshe



Mbere na mbere, genda kuri settings ukanda ku foto yawe iri mu mfuruka iburyo hasi, hanyuma ukanda kuri "*Settings*".



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Ivyo ushizeho mburabuzi muri rusangi birabereye abakoresha benshi. Ariko rero, ndagusavye ko woja ku rutonde rwa "*Database passphrase & gusohora*". Aha niho ushobora kwohereza hanze urutonde rwa konti yawe ya SimpleX kugira ngo ukoreshe ububiko.



Ushobora kandi guhindura passphrase ikoreshwa mu gupfuka uru rutonde rw'amakuru. Ku mburabuzi, ivyara mu buryo butari bwo kandi bikabikwa aho hantu ku gikoresho cawe. Niba ushaka, urashobora gusobanura passphrase yawe bwite maze ugakuraho ububiko bwa passphrase bwo mu karere: uzoca ubwirizwa kuyishiramwo n’amaboko igihe cose ufunguye porogarama, hamwe n’igihe wimukiye ku kindi gikoresho.



**Iyumvire**: niwahitamwo iyi nzira, gutakaza passphrase bizotuma utakaza ubudasiba imirongo yawe yose n'ubutumwa bwa SimpleX.



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



Ndagusavye kandi ko woja kuri "*Ubuzima bwite n'umutekano*", aho ushobora gukoresha uburyo bwa "*SimpleX Lock*". Ivyo birakingira ukuntu umuntu ashobora gushika kuri iyo porogarama akoresheje kode y’ugufunga.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, "*Imenyekanisha*" na "*Igaragara*" bigufasha guhindura SimpleX Chat kugira ngo ihure n'ivyo ukunda.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



## Wohereze ubutumwa ukoresheje SimpleX Chat



Kugira ngo wihuze n'uwundi muntu kuri SimpleX, ufise uburyo bubiri:




- Koresha uruja n’uruza rw’ikoreshwa rimwe;
- Koresha Address ishobora gusubira gukoreshwa.



Address idahinduka ituma uwuyizi wese ashobora kuguhamagara kuri SimpleX. Ni Address idahinduka, ishobora gukoreshwa incuro nyinshi, n’abantu batandukanye, ata gihe gitegekanijwe. Ukwo gushikama ni kwo gutuma ishobora gushikirwa n’ibintu bitari vyo. Ariko rero, bitandukanye n’izindi porogarama zo gutanga ubutumwa, gukuraho SimpleX Address yawe birahagije kugira ngo uhagarike ubutumwa bwose butari bwo, ataco buhinduye ku biganiro vyariho. Nkako, iyo Address ikoreshwa gusa mu gushinga uruja n’uruza rw’intango, kandi ntigikenewe iyo Exchange imaze gutangura.



Ku rundi ruhande, amahuza akoreshwa rimwe gusa, ashobora gukoreshwa rimwe gusa, n’uwukoresha uwo ari we wese. Iyo umuntu amaze kuyikoresha, iyo nzira iraba idakora. Uzokenera generate nshasha ku nzira nshasha yose.



### Na Address idahinduka.



Niba wifuza gukoresha Address, fyonda ku ifoto yawe iri musi ibubamfu bwa Interface, hanyuma uhitemwo "*Create SimpleX Address*". Hanyuma wongere ukande kuri "*Rema SimpleX Address*".



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



Address yawe yo gusubira gukoresha ubu yararemwe. Ushobora kubisangiza abantu bashaka guhura nawe, mu kubereka kode ya QR, canke mu kubarungikira link.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Ivyagezwe vya Address*". Aha ushobora gutunganya uburenganzira bujanye na Address yawe. Ihitamwo rya "*Gusangira n'abo muvugana*" rituma Address yawe iboneka ku rubuga rwawe rwa SimpleX. Abo muvugana na bo bazoheza bashobore kuyirondera maze bayirungikire abandi bantu bipfuza kuvugana nawe.



Ihitamwo rya "*Kwemera*" ryemera ubwaryo amahuzwa yinjira biciye kuri Address yawe. Ivyo bisigura ko umuntu wese afise Address yawe azoshobora kubona urutonde rwawe akagurungikira ubutumwa, kiretse ukoresheje uburyo bwa "*Accept incognito*". Ivyo bihisha izina ryawe n’ifoto yawe iyo habayeho ubucuti bushasha, bikabisimbuzwa n’izina ry’uruyeri ry’imburakimazi, ritandukanye ku muntu wese muvugana.



![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)



### N'ihuriro rishobora gusubirwamwo



Uburyo bwa kabiri bwo kwifatanya n’umuntu ni ugukora uruja n’uruza rw’igihe kimwe. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku kimenyetso c'ikaramu kiri mu mfuruka iburyo hasi ya Interface, hanyuma uhitemwo "*Create 1-time link*".



Niba uwo mukundana yagurungikiye link, ukande kuri "*Scan / Paste link*" kugira ngo uyi scanner canke uyishireko.



![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)



SimpleX rero itanga uruja n’uruza rw’ikoreshwa rimwe. Ushobora kuyirungikira uwo muvugana mu buryo bwose: Exchange y’umubiri, ubundi butumwa, n’ibindi.



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Ushobora kandi guhitamwo urutonde rwo gufatanya n'iyi nzira y'ubutumire. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku rubuga rwawe ruri musi gato ya kode ya QR. Uzoheza ushobore:




- hitamwo imwe mu mirongo yawe isanzweho (tuzobona ingene wokora imirongo myinshi mu gice gikurikira);
- canke uhitemwo uburyo bwa "*Incognito*", bunyegeza izina ryawe n'ifoto yawe y'ibanga n'izina ry'uruyeri ry'umunyamakuru wawe.



Aha, ndahitamwo uburyo bwa "*Icognite*".



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Umugenzi wanje yarakoresheje iyo link. Ku ruhande rwiwe, ntiyakoresheje uburyo bwa "*Incognito*", ni co gituma mbona izina ryiwe ry'urubuga, "*Bob*". Ku rundi ruhande, Bob ntibona izina ryanje ry'ukuri "*Loïc Morel*", ahubwo ni izina ry'uruyeri, muri iki gihe "*RealSynergy*".



![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)



Nashobora gutangura kuvugana ubwo nyene, ariko mbere na mbere noshima kumenya ko ndiko ndayaga na Bob, atari umuntu w’umunyaruyeri yoba yafashe iyo nzira canke akora igitero ca MITM.



Kugira ivyo tubikore, tugiye gusuzuma urubuga rwacu rw'umutekano **hanze y'ikoreshwa**. Ivyo birahambaye, kuko iyo habaye igitero ca MITM, ukugenzura imbere mu mutima kwoba guhungabanijwe. Rero nukoreshe ubundi buryo bwo gutanga ubutumwa butekanye, canke mbere n’ikiruta ivyo, nusuzume amakode ubwawe.



Mu kiganiro, fyonda ku foto ya Bob, hanyuma kuri "*Suzuma kode y'umutekano*". Bob na we nyene ategerezwa gukora gutyo nyene ku ruhande rwiwe.



![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)



Niba uriko urakora kure, gereranya amakode ari ku yindi nzira y’ubutumwa itekanye (ategerezwa kuba asa). Canke mbere n'ivyiza, nimba mushobora guhura amaso mu yandi, scanner code ya QR y'uwo muvugana ukanda kuri "*Scan code*".



![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)



Iyo igenzura ryagenze neza, ikimenyetso c’ingabo gifise ikimenyetso co gusuzuma kizoboneka iruhande y’izina ry’uwo muvugana. Ivyo ni vyo vyemeza ko uriko urahana na Bob. Iyo igenzura ritashoboye, imburi "*Kode y'umutekano idakwiriye!*" izoboneka.



![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)



Ubu ushobora gukoresha ubutumwa bwa Exchange, guhamagara n'amadosiye ukoresheje Bob, bivanye n'uburenganzira washizeho kuri iki kiganiro.



## Guhindura imirongo yawe y'ikiganiro ca SimpleX



Kimwe mu bintu bikomeye cane vya SimpleX ni ubushobozi bwo gucunga imirongo y’abakoresha itandukanye rwose, yose ishobora gushikwako kuri konti imwe yo mu karere. Ivyo bigufasha gutandukanya neza akaranga kawe gatandukanye, ataco bigoranye mu gucunga ubutumwa.



Nk'akarorero, woshobora kurema:




- Profile iriko izina ryawe nyaryo n’ifoto nyayo y’uguhanahana amakuru yawe y’umwuga;
- Profile irimwo izina ryawe ry’ukuri n’ifoto itwenza yo guhanahana amakuru mu muryango wawe;
- Umwirondoro ufise ifoto y’ikinyoma n’izina ry’uruyeri ry’ibiyago vyawe bwite;
- Uwundi murongo w’izina ry’uruyeri wo kuganira n’abantu utazi.



Ivyo nivyo tuzokora hano. Ntangura n’ugutunganya urutonde rwanje nyamukuru, urwo rufitaniye isano n’akaranga kanje nyakuri. Kugira ngo mbikore, ndafyonda ku foto yanje iri mu mfuruka iburyo hasi, hanyuma nkafyonda ku izina ryanje ry’urubuga.



![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)



Nca nfyonda ku foto yanje kugira ngo ndayihindure, nshiremwo n’iyindi nshasha.



![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)



Kugira ngo wongereko izindi nkuru, fyonda ku "*Imirongo yawe y'ibiganiro*".



![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)



Aha uzobona amakuru yawe yose. Fyonda kuri "*Ongera urutonde*" kugira ngo ureme urutonde rushasha.



![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)



Hanyuma uhitemwo amakuru y’urutonde rwawe rushasha: izina, ifoto, n’ibindi Aha, nkoresha izina ry’uruyeri n’ishusho itandukanye kugira ngo mpishe akaranga kanje nyakuri mu guhanahana amakuru amwamwe.



![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)



Mu gufata urutoke rwawe hasi ku nkuru, urashobora kuyihisha. Ivyo bizotuma itaboneka muri porogarama, hamwe n’ibiyago vyose bijanye na yo. Ushobora kandi guhitamwo "*Mute*" kugira ngo uhagarike kwakira amamenyesha.



![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)



Umaze gukora imirongo yawe, urashobora kuyicungera wigenga. Kuva kuri paji y'intango, uhitemwo gusa urutonde rukora kugira ngo ugaragaze.



![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)



Iyo uremye uruja n’uruza rw’ubutumire canke Address idahinduka, ubu urashobora guhitamwo urupapuro rwo gufatanya na rwo. Nk'akarorero, iyo ntoye urutonde rw'abantu "*Satoshi Nakamoto*" nkarurungikira Alice, azobona gusa akaranga kanje k'uruyeri "*Satoshi Nakamoto*", atazokwigera amenya akaranga kanje nyakuri "*Loïc Morel*". Ku rundi ruhande, iyo ndamuhaye link iva ku rubuga rwanje nyarwo, nta buryo azogira bwo guhuza n’urupapuro rwanje rw’izina ry’uruyeri.



![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)
