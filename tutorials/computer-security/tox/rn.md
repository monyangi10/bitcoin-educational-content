## Tox ni iki?



Tox ni ubuhinga bwo guhanahana amakuru ku buntu (open source), bushizwe ahantu hamwe bukoresha ubuhinga bwa *Networking and Cryptography Library* (NaCl) hamwe n’imigwi y’ubuhinga bwo gushiramwo amakuru kugira ngo ababukoresha bashobore kugira umutekano n’ibanga. Tox iraguha ubutumwa bwa Exchange, guhamagara amajwi n’amasanamu, gusangira amadosiye no gusangira igicapo cawe n’abagenzi mu mutekano, mu rwego rwo hejuru kandi ata bahuza.



Ikorabuhanga iyo porotokole ya Tox ikoresha risa n’iry’imirongo y’urunganwe nk’iyitwa blockchains, ivyo bikaba bifasha mu gusenyura ibikorwa remezo vy’iyo porotokole. Udakunze imihora y’ubudandaji n’ibikoresho vyo gutanga ubutumwa bufise amakuru y’iherezo, porogarama ya Tox Chat yubatswe ku nzira yegerejwe idafise server nyamukuru. Abakoresha bose bavugana mu buryo butagira uwubuhuza, butagiramwo ugucengera. Kugira ngo umuntu wese ashobore kuvugana, amenyekana n’indangamuntu yihariye (ToxID) ikomoka ku rufunguzo rwiwe rwa bose, rubikwa mu meza Hash yakwiragijwe.

### Imigwi y'urunganwe



Abaguzi bawe ba Tox na bo nyene baragufasha kuvugana n’umugwi w’abantu mu buryo bushimikijwe rwose: ivyo vyitwa amakoraniro. Mu **Imigwi**, rema inama nshasha canke urabe urutonde rw'ubutumire kugira ngo winjire mu nama waronse.



![conferences](assets/fr/11.webp)



Inama imaze kuremwa, urashobora gutumira abagenzi bawe ngo baje muri iyo nama ku mukiriya wawe wa Tox. Mu rutonde rw’abagenzi bawe, fyonda ku izina ry’umugenzi wipfuza gutumira. Fyonda kuri **Tumira mu nama**, hanyuma uhitemwo izina ry'inama waremye. Ushobora kandi gutumira umugenzi mu gukora inama mu buryo butagaragara ukoresheje uburyo bwa **Rema inama nshasha**.



⚠️ Tox clients ziracariko zirategurwa, rero ushobora guhura n’amakosa iyo ukorana na software. Ivyo gukora inama n’uguhamagara kuri videwo ntibiraboneka ku mukiriya wa Tox Android (aTox).

### Ni igiki kigaragaza ibanga kuri Tox?



Itegeko rya Tox rikoresha Imbonerahamwe ya Hash Yakwiragijwe kugira ngo rikore uruja n’uruza rw’ibihimba vy’umubiri vy’abantu bose. Buri mukiriya wa Tox ni igice c’urubuga rwa DHT kandi abika amakuru yerekeye izindi nzira. Ku bijanye na Tox, DHT ibika amaderesi ya IP nk’agaciro gafitaniye isano n’imfunguruzo za bose za Tox (Tox ID). Ivyo bituma vyoroha kurondera igikoresho ca Tox Client ataco ubajije kuri server nyamukuru.



Ibaze kwandikira umukoresha wacu `EBC5604D9386E594CCC32943A03F96A96687FBD46788F1CD4F3337EB703C16BE3DC2CA6D9F` uwo tuzomwita **umukoresha B**. Umukiriya wawe wa Tox azosanga iki kimenyetso mu mbonerahamwe ya Hash Distributed maze aronke IP Address ijana. IP Address imaze kuboneka, umukiriya wawe wa Tox azokora umurongo w'itumanaho utaziguye, ushizwemwo amakuru n'imashini yacu ya **user B**, canke akoreshe izindi nodes nk'ibimenyetso kugira ngo ashike ku **User B**. Ivyuma vy'ugushiramwo amakuru bituma, utitaye ku nzira y'itumanaho, **Ukoresha B** ni we wenyene azoshobora gusoma ibirimwo ubutumwa bwawe.



Niba warashimishijwe no kuvumbura Tox kandi warashoboye gutahura ingene ari ngirakamaro mu gukomeza ubuzima bwite bwawe, ndagusavye ntutinye guha iyi nyigisho urukumu.
