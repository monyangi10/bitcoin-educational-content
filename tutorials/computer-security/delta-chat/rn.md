## Intangamarara - Gucungera ibiganiro n'ubuzima bwite



Mu myaka ya vuba aha, hariho ibiganiro vyinshi vyerekeye Chat Control, igitekerezo c’amategeko kigamije gushiramwo ubuhinga bwo gucapura ubutumwa bwite ku mbuga zikomeye zo guhanahana amakuru. Intumbero yavuzwe ni ukurwanya ibirimwo bitemewe n’amategeko, ingorane ni uko ubu buryo bwoba mu vy’ukuri bujanye n’ugucungera abantu benshi, bukaja guhungabanya ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera, gutyo rero n’ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha bose, atari abakoze ivyaha gusa.



Icoba ari ikibazo nyaco ni uko ama chats aba ahantu hagenzurwa, aho ubutumwa bwose, ishusho yose canke ikintu cose gifatanye n’ibindi bishobora gusuzumwa imbere y’uko mbere bishika ku muntu aburonkeye. Kandi ni ho umuti umwe ushoboka ushika: kuva ku mbuga zihurikiye hamwe tuja ku mbuga zihurikiye hamwe, zidashingiye ku muntu umwe kandi zidashobora gusuzumwa mu buryo bworoshe nk’ukwo.



Umuti umwe nk’uwo uzoshikirizwa muri iyi nyigisho: Delta Chat. Igikoresho gikomeye kandi gisanzwe gikoreshwa.




## Kubera iki Delta Chat n'ingene ikora



Delta Chat ni umuti mwiza cane wo gutanga ubutumwa ku musi ku musi: ni ngirakamaro cane mu kuvugana n’abagenzi, umuryango n’abandi bantu, nk’uko nyene bingana na WhatsApp vy’ukuri.



Ni uburyo bwo gutanga ubutumwa bushingiye ku butumwa bwose bushingiye kuri email. Mu bisanzwe ikoresha ibikorwa remezo vya email ya kera, ariko yubaka ubutumwa bugezweho n’ubumenyi hejuru yavyo. Umuntu aravye neza ivyo vyoshobora gusa n’ibiteye ubwoba, ariko mu vy’ukuri birakora neza cane kandi birakomeye bitangaje. Ushobora gukoresha amaserver y’ubutumwa yihariye yitwa ChatMail, ariko kandi arashobora gukora neza n’amaserver y’ubutumwa asanzwe. Ivyo bisigura ko ushobora kwinjira ukoresheje konti isanzweho iyo ubishaka, ata kintu gishasha ukeneye gukora.



Ikindi kintu gihambaye ni ugushigikira WebXDCs, arizo porogaramu ntoyi zo ku rubuga zishobora gukoreshwa mu biganiro, zisa n'iporogarama ntoyi ziri muri Telegram. Itandukaniro rihambaye ni uko izo porogarama zidafise Internet, rero ntizishobora gukurikirana uwuzikoresha canke ngo zirungike amakuru hanze.



Uvuye ku mutekano, Delta Chat ikoresha uburyo bwo gukingira bushingiye ku mpera kugeza ku mpera, bushingiye kuri PGP ariko bufise ivyungura vya none bituma bigereranywa mu rugero rw’uburinzi na Signal. Ico kibuze ubu ni Ibanga ry’Imbere Ritunganye, ariko uwo ni umuce uriko urahinduka.



Kubera ko ishingiye kuri email gusa, Delta Chat irayirinda rwose:




- inomero za telefone zitegekanijwe
- Indangamuntu zishizwe hamwe
- Ukwandikisha guhuye n'igikorwa kimwe



Kandi ivyo nivyo bituma iki gikoresho gishobora guhangana cane n’amategeko atera nk’aya Chat Control.

## Guhingura umwirondoro



Iyo porogarama imaze gufungurwa, Delta Chat irabaza nimba yokora urutonde rushasha canke ikoresha urutonde rwa kera.



![image](assets/it/02.webp)



Mu kurema urutonde rushasha ushobora kwinjira:




- izina
- ishusho (ntibikenewe)



Serveri ya ChatMail irasabwa ku buryo busanzwe, ariko birashoboka:




- Hitamwo uwundi mukozi wa ChatMail
- Koresha konti ya imeyili ya kera
- gutunganya IMAP na SMTP n'amaboko
- kwiyandikisha ukoresheje kode y'ubutumire ku wundi



Inyuma y’amasegonda makeyi, iyo porogaramu irategurwa, urashobora gutangura gukoresha iyo porogarama.



![image](assets/it/03.webp)




## Interface n'ibiganiro



Ikigaragaza ni coroshe cane kandi kigororotse:




- Ubutumwa bw'ibikoresho, ari bwo guhanahana amakuru mu karere
- Ubutumwa bwabitswe, busa n'ubwo muri Telegram kandi bushobora guhuzwa hagati y'ibikoresho



![image](assets/it/04.webp)



Kugira ngo wongereko umuntu wo guhamagara:




- Kwerekana kode yawe ya QR
- Scan ivy'uwundi muntu
- Ubutumire biciye ku nzira (gusangira ubutumire ku nzira).



![image](assets/it/05.webp)



Igihe iyo nzira imaze gushingwa, ubuhinga bwo gupfuka amakuru kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera burahita butunganirizwa. Ivyo gukoresha chat bisa cane n’ivyo kuri WhatsApp:




- ubutumwa bwo mu nyandiko n'ijwi
- amafoto, amasanamu n'amadosiye
- inyishu ku butumwa
- inyishu
- ubutumwa buza
- amatangazo ashobora guhindurwa.



![image](assets/it/06.webp)



## WebXDC: porogaramu mu biganiro:



Delta Chat iremeza gukoresha WebXDC, ni ukuvuga, porogaramu ntoyi zishizwe mu biganiro. Aha ni urutonde rugufi rw’ivy’ingirakamaro cane vyamenyekanye:




- ubushakashatsi
- imbaho ​​zo gushushanya
- ibiganiro vy'ibanga vy'igihe gito
- Imikino ifise amanota y'ibiganiro



Imikino irushirije kuba igoye kandi irashobora gutangura, ivyo bikaba vyerekana ukuntu ico gikoresho gishobora guhindura uko umuntu abona ibintu.



![image](assets/it/07.webp)



## Amatsinda, imirongo n'ibiranga biteye imbere



Ushobora gukora amatsinda, ukoreshe ama stickers (cane cane ku biro) kandi, mu gukoresha amahitamwo y'igerageza, mbere n'imirongo, isa n'iyo muri Telegram.



Mu mitunganyirize iteye imbere ushobora gufungura:




- ijwi ry'uguhamagara (biracariko biragerageza)
- ubuyobozi bw'umwirondoro wa imeyili
- ivy’inyuma vyuzuye.



![image](assets/it/08.webp)



## Ibikoresho vyinshi n'ububiko



Delta Chat ishigikira neza ibikoresho vyinshi:




- ushobora kwongerako ikindi gikoresho ukoresheje kode ya QR
- ushobora gukora ukwimurira kwuzuye biciye mu gusubiza inyuma.



Mu masegonda make uzosubira gusanga ama chats yawe, amatsinda yawe n’amateka yuzuye, utavyifashemwo neza kuri server nyamukuru.



![image](assets/it/09.webp)
