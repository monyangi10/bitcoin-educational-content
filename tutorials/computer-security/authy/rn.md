Muri iki gihe, ubuhinga bwo kwemeza ko umuntu ari uwundi (2FA) bwacitse ikintu gihambaye cane kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukingira amakonti yo kuri Internet kugira ngo ntabe uwushobora kuyaronka ata wemerewe. Kubera ko ibitero vyo kuri Internet vyiyongera, kwizigira ijambobanga gusa kugira ngo ukingire amakonti yawe rimwe na rimwe ntibihagije. 2FA izana Layer y’inyongera y’umutekano mu gusaba uburyo bugira kabiri bwo kwemeza umuntu uretse ijambobanga. Ukwo kugenzura gushobora gufata uburyo bwinshi, nk’akarorero kode yoherezwa biciye kuri SMS, kode ihinduka ivuye kuri porogarama yihariye, canke gukoresha urufunguzo rw’umutekano rw’umubiri. Gukoresha 2FA biragabanya cane ingorane zo guhungabanya amakonti yawe, mbere n’igihe ijambo banga ryawe ryibwe.


## 2FA biciye ku porogaramu zo kwemeza


Tuzokwihweza ibindi bisubizo nk’imfunguruzo z’umutekano w’umubiri mu zindi nyigisho, ariko muri iyi, ndasaba ko twovuga cane cane ku bikorwa vya 2FA. Ivyo bikoresho bikora ni vyoroshe cane: iyo 2FA ikoreshejwe kuri konti, igihe cose winjiye, ntuzosabwa gusa ijambobanga ryawe risanzwe ariko kandi uzosabwa kode y’imirongo 6. Iyi kode iterwa n'ikoreshwa ryawe rya 2FA. Ikintu gihambaye kiranga iyo kode y’imirongo 6 ni uko idahagarara; kode nshasha irasohoka n’iyi porogarama buri masegonda 30.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/01.webp)

Ugusubiramwo iyo kode buri masegonda 30 bituma bigorana cane ko uwugutera yinjira muri konti yawe. Uwo murongo urabuza abatera gusubira gukoresha kode yibwe canke yafashwe, kuko ihera ningoga. Gutyo, naho uwutera yoshobora kuronka iyo kode, azoshobora kuyikoresha gusa mu kiringo gito cane imbere y’uko hakenerwa kode nshasha. Vyongeye, kuba iyo kode ihinduka kenshi cane biragabanya cane umwanya umunyaruyeri ashobora gukoresha kugira ngo agerageze gutekereza kuri iyo kode biciye ku nguvu z’agahomerabunwa.


2FA biciye ku maporogarama y’ukwemeza rero igereranya uburyo bworoshe gukoresha kandi bw’ubuntu bwo kwongereza cane umutekano wa konti zawe zo kuri Internet.


Hariho porogarama nyinshi zo gushinga 2FA, muri zo Google Authenticator na Microsoft ni zo zizwi cane. Ariko muri iyi nyigisho, nipfuza kubamenyesha uwundi muti utazwi cane witwa Authy. Izo porogaramu zose zikoresha uburyo bumwe TOTP (*Ijambobanga ry’Igihe kimwe*), ivyo bikaba bituma ikoreshwa ryazo risa cane.

Authy itanga ivyiza vyinshi ku bindi bisubizo biva mu masosiyete manini manini y’ubuhinga. Mbere na mbere, biragufasha gukoranya ibimenyetso vyawe vya 2FA ku bikoresho vyinshi, bishobora kuba ngirakamaro mu gihe woba watakaje canke uhinduye telefone. Authy kandi iragufasha gukora generate ububiko bufise amakuru y’ibanga maze ukabubika kuri internet, kugira ngo ntuzokwigere utakaza uburenganzira bwo kuronka ibimenyetso vyawe, naho woba watakaje igikoresho cawe ca mbere. Kuva ku mbonerahamwe y’umukoresha Interface, jewe ubwanje mbona ko Authy na yo itanga ubumenyi bushimishije kandi busanzwe kuruta ubundi buryo bwayo.


## Ni gute woshiramwo Authy?


Kuri telefone yawe, genda ku bubiko bwa porogarama (ububiko bwa Google Play canke ububiko bwa Apple), urondere "*Ivyemezo vy'ukuri vya Twilio*".



- [Ipome](iporogaramu.ipome.com/twebwe/iporogaramu/uburenganzira bwa twilio/id494168017)

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/03.webp)

Injira muri email yawe Address kugira ngo ubone kode.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/04.webp)

Hitamwo uburyo bumwe muri ubwo bubiri buhari kugira ngo ugenzure inomero yawe ya telefone. Niwahitamwo kwakira ubutumwa bugufi, shiramwo kode y’imirongo 6 wakiriye ku butumwa kugira ngo wemeze inomero yawe.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/06.webp)

Urakoze cane, konti yawe ya Authy yarashizweho!

## Ni gute wotunganya Authy?


Kugira ngo utangure, genda kuri settings za app ukanda ku tudodo dutoduto dutatu turi hejuru iburyo bw’ibarabara.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/08.webp)

Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Imiterere*".

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/09.webp)

Mu gice ca "*Konti yanje*", urafise uburenganzira bwo guhindura konti yawe. Ndabagira inama yo kwongerako kode ya PIN kuri porogaramu mu guhitamwo "*Ukurinda porogaramu*". Ivyo vyongerako Layer y’umutekano kugira ngo ushobore gushika ku gisata cawe.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/10.webp)

Mu gice ca "*Accounts*", ushobora gushinga ububiko bw'ibimenyetso vyawe. Iyi backup ituma ushobora gusubirana ama code yawe iyo habaye ingorane. Irapfutse hakoreshejwe ijambobanga utegerezwa gusobanura. Ni ngombwa ko iri jambo banga rikomeye kandi rikabikwa ahantu heza. Gushinga iyo nkuru y’ububiko si ngombwa nimba ufise ubundi buryo bwo gusubizaho, nk’igikoresho ca kabiri gifise konti ya Authy imwe, nk’akarorero.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/11.webp)In the "*Devices*" tab, you can see all the devices synchronized with your Authy account. You have the option to disable the use of multiple devices, which restricts access to your account to that device only. If you use only one device, this can increase the security of your account, but make sure you have another backup method in case you lose that device.


Niba ushaka kwemera kwongerako ibindi bikoresho, ndaguhanura gukoresha uburyo busaba kwemezwa n’ibikoresho vyemewe ubu biri kuri konti yawe ya Authy imbere yo kwongerako ikindi gikoresho gishasha.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/12.webp)

Kugira ngo wongereko igikoresho gishasha, subiramwo gusa uburyo bwo gushiramwo bwavuzwe mu gice c’imbere ukoresheje ivyo bimenyetso nyene. Uzoca usabwa kwemeza iyo nzira nshasha ukoresheje igikoresho cawe nyamukuru.


## Ni gute woshiraho 2FA kuri konti?


Kugira ngo ushireho kode y’ukwemeza 2FA biciye ku porogarama nka Authy kuri konti, konti itegerezwa gushigikira iki kintu. Muri iki gihe, amasezerano menshi yo kuri internet aratanga iyo nzira ya 2FA, ariko ivyo si ko vyama bimeze. Reka dufate akarorero ka konti ya Proton mail nashize ahabona mu yindi nyigisho:


https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/communication/proton-mail-c3b010ce-254d-4546-b382-19ab9261c6a2

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/13.webp)

Muri rusangi uzosanga iyo nzira ya 2FA mu mitunganyirize ya konti yawe, kenshi munsi y'igice ca "*Ijambobanga*" canke "*Umutekano*".

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/14.webp)

Iyo ukoresheje iyo nzira kuri konti yawe ya Proton mail, uraronka kode ya QR. Utegerezwa rero gucapura iyi kode ya QR ukoresheje app yawe ya Authy.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/15.webp)

Kuri Authy, fyonda ku buto ya "*+*".

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/16.webp)

Fyonda kuri "*Scan Kode ya QR*". Hanyuma ushireko kode ya QR iri ku rubuga.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/17.webp)

Ufise kandi uburenganzira bwo guhindura izina ryawe nimba bikenewe. Uhejeje guhindura, ukande kuri buto ya "*SAVE*".

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/18.webp)

Authy izoca yerekana kode yawe y'imirongo 6 yihariye kuri iyo konti isubiramwo buri masegonda 30.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/19.webp)

Injira iyi kode ku rubuga kugira ngo uheze gutegura 2FA.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/20.webp)

Hari n’imbuga zizoguha ama code yo gusubirana inyuma yo gukoresha 2FA. Izo codes zituma ushobora gukoresha konti yawe iyo utakaje uburenganzira bwo gukoresha app yawe ya Authy. Ndabagira inama yo kubizigama ahantu hatagira umutekano.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/21.webp)Your account is now secured with two-factor authentication via the Authy app.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/22.webp)

Igihe cose winjiye muri konti, uzokenera gutanga kode y’inguvu yashizweho na Authy. Ubu ushobora gukingira amakonti yawe yose ahuye n’ubu buryo bwa 2FA. Kugira ngo wongereko konti nshasha kuri Authy, fyonda ku tudodo dutoduto dutatu turi hejuru iburyo bwa app.

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/23.webp)

Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ongerako konti*".

![AUTHY 2FA](assets/notext/24.webp)

Kurikiza intambwe nk’izo wakoresheje mu nkuru ya mbere. Amakode yawe atandukanye y’inguvu azoboneka kuri paji y’intango ya Authy.
