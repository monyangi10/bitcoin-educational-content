## Intangamarara: Nimuganire mu mwidegemvyo, ata n'uruzitiro


Ibaze ko worungika ubutumwa igihe interineti ihagaze, mu gihe habaye icago c’ivyaduka canke mu bibanza aho abantu badashobora guhanahana amakuru. Bitchat ni yo ituma ivyo bishoboka. Ni porogaramu y’ubutumwa yegerejwe, ishobora gusimbuka ama serveri, ireka ibikoresho bikavugana ataco bimaze, vyose bitari ku murongo bikoresheje Bluetooth n’urubuga rw’urusenga. Bitchat yakozwe mu muzirikanyi ubuzima bwite n’ukwihangana, ni nziza cane mu gukoreshwa mu bibanza aho ubuhinga bwa kera bwo gukorana n’ibindi bihugu butari bwizigirwa canke butaboneka—nk’igihe habaye ibiza, mu bibanza vya kure, canke ku bashaka kwirinda gucungera. Mu ntumbero yayo, Bitchat ikoresha ubuhinga bwo gupfuka ubutumwa kugira ngo ubutumwa bwose bube bufise ubuhinga bwo gupfuka, bugenzurwe, kandi butagira uwubuhindura.


Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwereka ingene Bitchat ikora n’ingene ushobora kuyikoresha mu guhanahana amakuru mu buryo bw’ibanga, ataco ukora.


## 🚀 Ibirango vy'ingenzi


Bitchat ishoboza ubutumwa butari ku murongo biciye muri ibi [biranga]



- Ihuye n’ibindi bikoresho**: Ihuye n’amaporotokole yuzuye hagati ya iOS na Android.
- Urubuga rw'urusenga rwegerejwe**: Ukwibonera kw'urunganwe n'ubutumwa bwinshi bujanye n'inguvu nke za Bluetooth (BLE)
- Iherezo-ku-iherezo ry'ububiko**: X25519 urufunguzo rwo guhindura + AES-256-GCM ku butumwa bwihariye
- Ibiganiro bishingiye ku mihora**: Ubutumwa bw'umugwi bushingiye ku ciyumviro bufise uburinzi bw'ijambobanga ry'ubusa
- Ububiko n'Imbere**: Ubutumwa bubitswe ku bagenzi batari kuri interineti kandi bugashikirizwa iyo basubiye kwifatanya
- Ubuzima bwite bwa mbere**: Nta konti, nta nimero za telefone, nta bimenyetso bihoraho
- Amabwirizwa y'uburyo bwa IRC: Uburyo bumenyerewe `/join, /msg, /who`.
- Ububiko bw'ubutumwa**: Ububiko bw'ubutumwa bwose bugenzurwa na ba nyir'ubutumwa
- Gukuraho ivyihutirwa**: Gukora gatatu ku kimenyetso kugira ngo ukureho amakuru yose ubwo nyene

- Insanganyamatsiko z'umwiza/umuco**: Ubwiza buhuye na verisiyo ya iOS
- Gutuma bateri ikora neza**: Gupima no gucunga ubushobozi


## 1️⃣ Uko Bitchat ikora - mu buryo bworoshe


Bitchat ishobora kugufasha kwandika ubutumwa kuri telefone ziri hafi biciye kuri Bluetooth (`BLE` nk’uko bikurikira), ata kimenyetso ca internet canke c’itelefone ngendanwa gikenewe. Iyo utanguye kuyaga, ayo matelefone arakora igikorwa co gufata ukuboko mu buryo butekanye kugira ngo akore urufunguzo rwihariye rw’igihe gito rwo gukingira ikiyago cawe. Ubutumwa bwose burakingirwa n’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera, kandi kuri buri butumwa hakoreshwa urufunguzo rushasha kugira ngo ubutumwa bwa kera bube butekanye naho telefone yawe yoba yashizwe mu kaga mu nyuma. Ubwa nyuma, iyo porogarama igabanya ubutumwa mu bice bitobito maze ikabuvanga n’amakuru y’ibinyoma kugira ngo uhishe igikorwa cawe co gutanga ubutumwa. Ku bijanye n’uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’ibikoresho n’ibindi, bikora gusa mu rugero rwo gushika kuri ~100m. Ni nk’uguca amakete apfutse mu cumba cuzuye abantu—ibikoresho biratongana ata guca ku ruhande, bikamenagura imfunguruzo inyuma y’ubutumwa bwose.


Bitchat kandi iraguha uburenganzira bwo kwifatanya n'ivyumba vy'ibiganiro bishingiye ku kibanza ukoresheje umurongo wa Nostr na `#geohashes`. Geohash ni kode ngufi, nka `#u33d`, iserukira ahantu kanaka, kuva mu karere kamwe, gushika mu gisagara cose canke akarere. Ushobora `teleport` mu cumba cose co guhanahana amakuru ca geohash kw'isi yose winjiza gusa ikimenyetso caco. Ubutumwa bwawe bwoherezwa biciye ku nzira y’ubuhinga bwo gutanga ubutumwa, ikaba irinda aho uri nyaho. Ikindi kandi, igihe cose winjiye mu cumba ca geohash, uhabwa akaranga gashasha, k’igihe gito, ukongerako ikindi kintu c’ubuzima bwite ku biyago vyawe bishingiye ku kibanza.


Kugira ngo ubone amakuru y’ubuhinga atari yo, urashobora kuraba [Igitabo c’Umweru cemewe](https://github.com/permissionlesstech/bitchat/blob/main/WHITEPAPER.md).

## 3️⃣ Ibiganiro vyo kw'isi yose n'ubutumwa bwihariye


Bitchat itanga uburyo bubiri butandukanye bwo guhanahana amakuru kugira ngo bujane n’ivyo umuntu akeneye bitandukanye:



- Imihora ya bose:** Kugira ibiyago vy’abantu bose n’abandi. Ushobora kwifatanya biciye ku rubuga rwa BLE mesh rwo mu karere ku bakoresha bari hafi canke biciye ku #geohash y’isi yose ku miryango ikoresha internet, ishingiye ku kibanza.
- Ubutumwa bwihariye:** Kugira ngo mugire ibiyago bitekanye, umuntu ku wundi. Ivyo bishinga ubucuti butaziguye kandi bufise amabanga kugira ngo ivyo muhanahana bibe ibanga.


Gutahura ubwo buryo bwompi bizokugirira akamaro mu bijanye n’ibiyago vyawe.


### Imihora ya bose: Ikibanza c'abanyagihugu


`#Imirongo y'ahantu` (hejuru iburyo) igenzura ukuntu ubona abantu bose. Guhitamwo `mesh` biguhuza n'abakoresha bose bari hafi biciye ku BLE mesh, kenshi na kenshi ibikoresho biri hagati ya metero 10-50. Ivyo bituma haba ihuriro ryuguruye aho ubutumwa bumenyeshwa umuntu wese ari mu rwego, rwiza cane ku matangazo y’ibintu canke imburi zo mu karere.


Kugira ngo ushikire abantu benshi, hitamwo `#geohash` kugira ngo winjire mu miryango ikoreshwa na internet iyunguruwe hakurikijwe aho uri. Izo nzira zikoresha ubuhinga bwa Nostr, zituma haba uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’ibisagara canke uturere mu gihe ziguma zifise akamaro bishingiye ku kibanza. Ubutumwa bwawe buraboneka ku bandi bari muri uwo muhora nyene, n’abashasha baje mu nama babona amateka y’ubutumwa bwa vuba iyo binjiye.


![image](assets/en/06.webp)


### Ibiyago vy'ibanga: Bitekanye & Bishizwemwo


Kugira ngo utangure ikiyago c'ibanga, ushobora gukora ku `izina ry'ukoresha` ryose ryerekanywe muri `Iciyumviro c'urunganwe.` Ushobora kandi gukora ku `ikimenyetso c'inyenyeri` kugira ngo ushire ikimenyetso kuri uwo mukoresha nk'uwukunda, ivyo bizotuma aguma aboneka ku rutonde rwawe rw'abantu n'igihe atari mu nzira.


![image](assets/en/07.webp)


Bitchat ica itanguye `ugufatanya ukuboko kw'umutekano` iyo utanguye ikiyago c'ibanga. Ivyo bikoresho birahanahana imfunguruzo z’igihe gito kugira ngo bikore umugende w’amazi w’ibanga wihariye w’ikiyago cawe. Iyi nzira ituma ubutumwa bwawe bwose n’amadosiye yawe yose usangiye biguma ari ibanga bivuye ku gukomeza gushiramwo amakuru kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera. Ubutumwa bwihariye burashigikira gusangira inyandiko n'amadosiye.


![image](assets/en/08.webp)

## 5️⃣ Gukora umurongo


Imirongo yo muri Bitchat irashoboza guhanahana amakuru mu buryo butunganijwe ku bijanye n’ingingo, ahantu canke imibano. Kugira ngo ukore ivyawe, ukurikize iyi nzira y'ibikorwa:


### Intambwe ya 1: Rema umurongo


Kugira ngo ureme umurongo, wandike `/j` canke `/join` ukurikiwe na `izina ry'umurongo` mu kiganiro cose (nk'akarorero `/j <izina ry'umurongo>`). Inyuma yo kurema `icon ⧉` nshasha iraboneka yerekana umurongo mushasha. Abandi bakoresha bashobora kwifatanya bandika itegeko rimwe (nk'akarorero `/j bitchat_tutorial`).


![image](assets/en/10.webp)


### Intambwe ya 2: Kugena amagenamiterere


Uretse amabwirizwa mburabuzi, ibi bikurikira biraboneka mu muhora:



- `/bika` kubika ubutumwa mu karere
- `/kwimurira` kwimurira umurongo w'ubuninahazwa na
- `/pass` kugira ngo uhindure ijambobanga ry'umurongo.


Ku miryango yigenga, iri tegeko rituma umuntu ashobora gukingira ijambobanga, risaba abagize umuryango bemerewe kwinjiza ijambobanga ry’umuntu ku giti ciwe imbere yo kwinjira.

## 7️⃣ Ivy'ubutaka


Imirongo ya Geohash ishoboza ibiyago vyihariye bishingiye ku `ahantu` aho guhuza n'urubuga rwa kera. Ico gikoresho gihindura bitchat mu gikoresho co guhanahana amakuru kimenya aho umuntu ari, kikaba ari ciza cane mu guhuza ibikorwa mu karere, mu kwubaka umuryango, no mu biganiro vyerekeye ahantu.


### Uko `#ibice vy'ubutaka` bikora


Ubuhinga bugabanya isi mu bice vy'uruzitiro hakoreshejwe [uburyo bwa Geohash](https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geohash), aho ikimenyetso cose kiri muri hash kigereranya ukuri gukomeye:



- Urugero rwa 4** (nk'akarorero, `u33d`): Rufata nk'ibilometero 25 × ibilometero 25 - ni vyiza ku biganiro vyo mu gisagara cose
- Urugero rwa 6** (nk'akarorero, `u33d8z`): Rufata nk'ibilometero 1.2 × ibilometero 1.2 - uburinganire bw'ababanyi
- Urugero rwa 8** (nk'akarorero, `u33d8z1k`): Ripfutse hafi metero 150 × metero 150 - ukuri kw'igice c'ibarabara


Guhitamwo neza bihuza ubuzima bwite n’ubuhinga: ingero zisumba izindi zituma haba uturere twihariye ariko zihishura neza aho uri.


### Kwifatanya n'imirongo ya `#geohash`


1. Ushike ku `#imirongo y'ahantu`.

2. Hitamwo urugero rw'ukuri wipfuza maze winjize `#geohash` (nk'akarorero #u33d)

3. Kanda kuri buto ya `Teleport` kugira ngo winjire mu `#umurongo w'ahantu`.


![image](assets/en/12.webp)


Canke, ushobora gukora kuri `ikimenyetso c'ikarita` kugira ngo ukoreshe ukuntu ikarita iboneka kugira ngo umenye urugero rw'ukuri maze ukande kuri `hitamwo` kugira ngo winjire mu `#umurongo w'ahantu`.


![image](assets/en/13.webp)


**Iciyumviro gihambaye**: _#geohash ikora bisaba ko umuntu akoresha interineti - bitandukanye n'urusenga rwa BLE rukora ata murongo ukoresheje Bluetooth._

## 🎯 Insozero


Bitchat ishoboza guhanahana amakuru mu buryo butekanye, bushizwe ahantu hamwe ku bihe aho ubutumwa bwa kera butashobora gukora. Ishobora gukora ata bikorwa remezo vya internet ikoresheje ubuhinga bwa BLE mesh networking, bikaba bituma ishobora gukoreshwa mu myiyerekano, mu bice vy’ivyago, no mu bibanza vya kure aho ubuhinga bwo gukorana n’ibindi bihugu bufise aho bugarukira canke bugacirwa buke. Iyi porogaramu itanga ubuzima bwite biciye mu gupfuka urufunguzo rw’igihe gito, imirongo y’ahantu ishingiye kuri geohash, n’ugukuraho amakuru mu buryo bw’ubwoba.


Iyi app iracari mu ntango z’iterambere, ariko iramaze kwerekana umuhango. Abakoresha bakwiye kwitega rimwe na rimwe ibibazo no gutanga raporo y’ibibazo biciye kuri [GitHub] (https://github.com/ubuhinga butagira uruhusha/bitchat-android/ibibazo). Ivyiza birateguwe, harimwo n'ugushiramwo `ecash` ku bikorwa vy'ubudandaji vy'abantu ku giti cabo hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwa Cashu.
