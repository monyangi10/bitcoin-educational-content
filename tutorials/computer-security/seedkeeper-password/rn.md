Seedkeeper ni smartcard yakozwe na Satochip, ishirahamwe ry’Ababirigi ryitaho ivy’ugutorera umuti ibibazo vy’ubuhinga bwo gucunga no kurinda amabanga y’ubuhinga bwa none. Satochip izwi cane kubera ubuhinga bwayo bwo gukoresha amakarata y’ubwenge y’ibidukikije vya Bitcoin, yaciye ifata Seedkeeper nk’ubundi buryo bwo kubika amajambo y’ubwenge n’ayandi mabanga y’ubuhinga bwa none.



Mu majambo nyayo, Seedkeeper ifata uburyo bw'ikarita y'ubwenge ikora vyinshi, yemejwe na EAL6, ifise umurongo w'ubuhinga n'ubuhinga bwo kwibuka bushobora guhindurwa (ico bita "Ikintu gitekanye"). Nk’uko izina ryayo rivyerekana, uruhara rwayo ni ugubika amakuru y’ivy’ibanga n’amajambo y’ibanga y’ibikorwa vya Bitcoin mu buryo buziguye kandi burindwa. Na Seedkeeper, urashobora generate, kwinjiza, gutunganya no kubika amabanga yawe ataco uhinduye mu gice gitekanye c’ikarita.



Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, Seedkeeper ifise imikoreshereze ibiri nyamukuru, tuzoyitohoza mu nyigisho 2 zitandukanye:




- Bitcoin** ububiko bw’amajambo y’ukwibuka: aho kwandika amajambo yawe 12 canke 24 ku mpapuro, urashobora kuyashira muri smartcard ukayakingira ukoresheje kode ya PIN.
- Ugucungera ijambobanga**: ushobora generate amajambobanga akomeye biciye ku rubuga rwa Seedkeeper maze ukayabika ataco uhinduye muri smartcard, bikaguha ubuhinga bwo gucunga ijambobanga bubereye kandi bworoshe gukoresha utari mu nzira.



Mu buryo bw’ubuhinga, Seedkeeper ifise ubushobozi bwa bytes 8192, bikaba biyishoboza kubika amabanga 50 atandukanye (umubare nyawo uzovana n’ubunini bwayo be n’amakuru y’imbere ajanye na buri rimwe). Seedkeeper ishobora gushikwako [biciye ku gikoresho co gusoma ikarita y’ubwenge gihuye](https://satochip.io/accessories/) kuri mudasobwa, canke biciye ku gikoresho co kuri telefone ngendanwa gihuye na NFC. Ubuhinga bwose bukora mu buryo butajanye n’umurongo, ata n’imwe ikoreshwa kuri Internet, bikaba vyemeza ko ata gitero gishobora gutera.



![Image](assets/fr/001.webp)



Ikintu gishimishije cane ni ubushobozi bwo gusubiramwo ibiri muri Seedkeeper imwe mu yindi kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukora backup. Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwereka ingene wobikora.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzovuga gusa ku gukoresha SeedKeeper ku majambobanga ya kera, mu buryo bw'umucungerezi w'amajambobanga. Niba wifuza gukoresha SeedKeeper kugira ubike amajambo y'ubwenge Bitcoin wallet, usabwe kuraba iyi yindi nyigisho:



https://planb.academy/tutorials/wallet/backup/seedkeeper-906dfff8-1826-4837-92d1-8669e216d356

## 1. Noronka gute Umucungezi w’Imbuto?



Hari uburyo bubiri nyamukuru bwo kuronka Umucungezi wawe w’Imbuto. Ushobora [kuyigura ataco uhinduye mu iduka ryemewe rya Satochip](https://satochip.io/igicuruzwa/umucungezi w’imbuto/) canke ku mucuruzi yemerewe. Ariko kuko [applet ya Seedkeeper ari inkomoko yuguruye](https://github.com/Toporin/Seedkeeper-Applet), urafise kandi uburenganzira bwo kuyishiramwo wewe nyene ku [ikarita y’ubwenge idafise ikintu]



Niba wipfuza gukoresha ubuhinga bwo gucungera amakuru bwa Seedkeeper, biragaragara ko uzokenera kugura amakarita abiri y’ubwenge.



## 2. Gushiramwo umukiriya wa Seedkeeper



Intambwe ya mbere ni ugushira iyo porogarama kuri mudasobwa yawe canke kuri telefone yawe. Kuri PC, uzokenera [gukuraho verisiyo nshasha ya Satochip-Utils](ibisohoka). Ku bikoresho vy’amatelefone ngendanwa, porogarama ya Seedkeeper iraboneka ku [Iduka rya Google Play] no ku [Iduka ry’Imbuto rya Apple] (Iduka ry’Imbuto ry’Imbuto).



![Image](assets/fr/002.webp)



## 3. Gutangura umucungezi w'imbuto



Ufungure porogaramu hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Click & Scan*".



![Image](assets/fr/003.webp)



Uzosabwa PIN code y’Umucungezi w’Imbuto yawe. Kubera ko iyi ari ikarita nshasha, nta PIN irasobanurwa. Injira kode iyo ari yo yose kugira ngo usimbuke iyi ntambwe, hanyuma ukande "*Ibikurikira*".



![Image](assets/fr/004.webp)



Hanyuma ushire iyo karata inyuma ya telefone yawe. Iryo koraniro rizomenya ko Seedkeeper itaratangura, kandi rizogusaba gushinga PIN code y’ikarita yawe y’ubwenge, ifise uburebure buri hagati y’inyuguti 4 na 16. Kugira ngo ubone umutekano mwiza, nuhitemwo kode ya PIN ikomeye ireha uko bishoboka kwose, idasanzwe kandi igizwe n’inyuguti zitandukanye. Iyi PIN ni yo yonyene ishobora gutuma umuntu adashobora gushika ku majambo y’ibanga yawe.



**Ibuka kandi kubika iyi PIN ubu**, nk'akarorero mu mucungerezi w'ijambobanga, canke ku buryo butandukanye. Mu gihe ca nyuma, ntukigere ubika umurongo urimwo PIN ahantu hamwe n’Umucungezi wawe w’Imbuto, ahandi ho uwo mutekano uzoba ubusa. Ni vyiza kugira ububiko bwo kwizigirwa: ata PIN, ntuzoshobora gusubirana amabanga yabitswe kuri Seedkeeper yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/005.webp)



Wemeze kode yawe ya PIN ubugira kabiri.



![Image](assets/fr/006.webp)



Umucungezi w’Imbuto yawe ubu yaratanguje. Ushobora kuyifungura winjije kode ya PIN uherutse gushiramwo.



![Image](assets/fr/007.webp)



Ubu uzojanwa kuri paji y’uburongozi bw’ikarita yawe y’ubwenge.



![Image](assets/fr/008.webp)



## 4. Bika amajambobanga kuri Seedkeeper



Igihe Umucungezi wawe w'Imbuto amaze gufungurwa, ukande kuri buto ya "*+*".



![Image](assets/fr/009.webp)



Hitamwo "Gutanga ibanga*". Ihitamwo rya "*Import a secret*" rigufasha kubika ibanga ririho (nk'akarorero, ijambobanga waremye kera).



![Image](assets/fr/010.webp)



Mu gihe cacu, turashaka kubika ijambobanga. Fyonda kuri "*Ijambobanga*".



![Image](assets/fr/011.webp)



Ushire "*Ikimenyetso*" kuri iri banga kugira ngo rishobore kumenyekana bitagoranye iyo ubitse amakuru menshi mu Mucungezi wawe w'Imbuto. Ushobora kandi kwongerako ikimenyetso gifitaniye isano n’ijambobanga be na URL y’urubuga.



![Image](assets/fr/012.webp)



Hitamwo uburebure n'imirongo y'ijambobanga ryawe, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Generate*", hanyuma ukande kuri "*Import*".



![Image](assets/fr/013.webp)



Shira Seedkeeper yawe inyuma ya telefone yawe y’amaboko.



![Image](assets/fr/014.webp)



Ijambobanga ryawe ryanditswe.



![Image](assets/fr/015.webp)



## 5. Ushike ku ijambobanga ryawe ry'Umucungezi w'Imbuto



Niba ushaka kumenya ijambo banga ryawe, fata Seedkeeper yawe ukande kuri buto ya "*Click & Scan*".



![Image](assets/fr/016.webp)



Injira kode yawe ya PIN, hanyuma ukande "*Ibikurikira*".



![Image](assets/fr/017.webp)



Shira Seedkeeper yawe inyuma ya telefone yawe y’amaboko.



![Image](assets/fr/018.webp)



Ivyo bigushikana ku rutonde rw’amabanga yawe yose yanditswe. Muri aka karorero, nshaka kwerekana ijambobanga rya konti yanje ya Plan ₿ Academy, nca ndarikandako.



![Image](assets/fr/019.webp)



Kanda kuri buto ya "*Hishura*".



![Image](assets/fr/020.webp)



Scan Umucungezi w’Imbuto yawe kandi.



![Image](assets/fr/021.webp)



Ijambobanga ryawe wari wabitse mbere ubu riraboneka ku rubuga. Ushobora kuyikopa ukayikoresha ku rubuga rwerekeye ivyo.



![Image](assets/fr/022.webp)



## 6. Gushigikira Umucungezi w'Imbuto



Ubu tugiye gukora backup y’Umucungezi wanje w’Imbuto ku Mucungezi w’Imbuto wa kabiri kugira ngo tugire kopi zibiri. Ukwo gusubira inyuma gushobora kuba igice c’ingene wokingira amajambo y’ibanga yawe ahambaye cane: nk’akarorero, kubika Seedkeepers zawe mu bibanza 2 bitandukanye kugira ngo ugabanye ingorane zo ku mubiri, canke kwizigira kopi incuti wizigiye.



Kugira ngo ubikore, jana n’Umucungezi wawe wa kabiri w’Imbuto (wibuke kumenya umwe muri abo babiri afise ikimenyetso kugira ngo ntihagire uwugutera urujijo). Tangana n’ugutanguza, nk’uko vyavuzwe mu ntambwe ya 3 y’iyi nyigisho. Na none, uhitemwo kode ya PIN ikomeye. Bivanye n’ingene ukoresha, woshobora guhitamwo PIN itandukanye canke ukaguma ukoresha iyo PIN nyene.



![Image](assets/fr/023.webp)



Ugurure porogaramu, ukande kuri "*Click & Scan*", winjize PIN y'Umucungezi wawe w'Imbuto n°1 (inkomoko), hanyuma uyi scanner.



![Image](assets/fr/024.webp)



Ivyo bigushikana kuri paji y’intango, harimwo urutonde rw’amabanga yawe. Fyonda ku tudodo dutoduto dutatu turi hejuru iburyo bw’ibarabara.



![Image](assets/fr/025.webp)



Hitamwo "*Kora ububiko*", hanyuma ukande "*Tangira*".



![Image](assets/fr/026.webp)



Injira muri PIN code y’ikarita yawe y’inyuma (Umucungezi w’imbuto n°2).



![Image](assets/fr/027.webp)



Hanyuma ushireko iyo karata.



![Image](assets/fr/028.webp)



Kora ukwo nyene n'ikarata nyamukuru (Umucungezi w'imbuto n°1), hanyuma ukande kuri "*Kora ububiko*".



![Image](assets/fr/029.webp)



Umucungezi wawe w'Imbuto n°2 ubu arimwo amabanga yose abitswe ku Mucungezi w'Imbuto n°1.



![Image](assets/fr/030.webp)



Ushobora gucapura Seedkeeper yawe n°2 kugira ngo umenye ko amabanga yakopiwe.



![Image](assets/fr/031.webp)
