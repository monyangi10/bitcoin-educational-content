Session ni porogaramu yo gutanga ubutumwa bufise amabanga yashizweho mu mwaka w’2020, yagenewe gutanga urugero rwo hejuru rw’ibanga kuruta ubutumwa bwa kera. Yabanje gutegurwa n'ishirahamwe *Oxen Privacy Tech Foundation*, hanyuma itegurwa n'ishirahamwe *Session Technology Foundation*.



Session irafise ibintu bimwe bimwe bishimishije vy’ubuhinga: gupfuka ubutumwa kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera, urubuga rwegerejwe rwatunganijwe kugira ngo ruboneke kandi rube rwinshi, n’ugukoresha igitunguru gihumekewe na Tor. Kandi, bitandukanye na WathsApp canke Signal, bisaba inomero ya telefone kugira ngo umuntu yiyandikishe, Session ntiyisaba amakuru y’ibanga (nta nomero, nta email, gusa imfunguruzo zibiri z’ubuhinga bwo gukingira).



Session iragufasha kohereza ubutumwa, amadosiye, ubutumwa bw’ijwi, amatelefone y’amajwi, hamwe n’imigwi y’abanywanyi bagera ku 100 (n’imiryango irenga ivyo), mu gihe ugabanya ukuvuza kw’amakuru.



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Session igenewe canecane abakoresha bashira ibanga mu mutima w’ivyo bashira imbere. Iyi serivisi yo gutanga ubutumwa igereranya ubundi buryo bukomeye bwo gusubirira WhatsApp, ifise ubuhinga bwo guhangana n’ubuhinga bwo gucungera abantu bugezweho.

## Gushiramwo porogaramu y'Ikiganiro



Session iraboneka ku mbuga zose. Ushobora gukuraho iyo porogaramu mu bubiko bwa porogaramu bwo kuri telefone yawe:




- [Ikiganiro ca Google](https://igikino.google.com/ububiko/amaporogarama/ibisobanuro?id=urubuga.loki.intumwa);
- [Iduka ry'amaporogarama](https://amaporogarama.com/us/porogarama/intumwa-y'ibanga/id1470168868);
- [F-Droid](ku rubuga rwacu).



Kuri Android, birashoboka kandi [gushiramwo biciye kuri APK](https://github.com/ishingiro-ry'ikiganiro/ibiganiro-vya-android/ibisohoka).



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwibanda ku bijanye n’ivyo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa, ariko ndabinginze mumenye ko [ivyo gukoresha mudasobwa na vyo nyene biraboneka](https://getsession.org/download) (MacOS, Linux na Windows). Mu nyuma, turaza kuraba ingene twohuza konti ku bikoresho vyinshi.



## Rema konti kuri Session



Ku gutangura kwa mbere, kanda kuri "*Rema konti*".



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Hitamwo izina ry'iyerekanwa ry'umwirondoro wawe. Iryo rishobora kuba izina ry’uruyeri canke izina ryawe ry’ukuri.



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Uzoca ubwirizwa guhitamwo hagati y'uburyo bubiri bwo gucunga amatangazo:





- Uburyo bwihuta ("*Ubutumwa bwo mu gicu bwa Firebase/Serivisi y'imenyekanisha rya Apple Push*"): bigufasha kwakira imenyekanisha ry'ubutumwa mu gihe hafi nyaco, bivuye ku bikorwa vy'imenyekanisha bitangwa na Google canke Apple (bivanye n'uko ukoresha sisitemu yawe). Kugira ngo ivyo bishoboke, IP yawe Address n’ID yawe y’imenyekanisha yihariye birungikwa kuri Google canke Apple, kandi ID ya konti ya Session na yo nyene irandikishwa kuri server ya STF (biciye kuri Tor). Ubu buryo bujanye n’ugushira ahabona (vyemewe ko ari buke) amakuru y’imbere, ariko ntibutera ingorane ibirimwo ubutumwa canke abahuye, kandi ntibushobora kwemera ko igikorwa cawe nyaco gikurikiranywa. Ubwo buryo rero burakora neza cane mu bijanye no kwishura, ariko bwishingikiriza ku bikorwa remezo bihurikiye hamwe kandi burakora neza gatoyi mu bijanye n’ibanga.





- Uburyo buhoro (*background polling*): porogaramu ya Session iguma ikora mu nyuma, iguma itora urubuga kugira ngo ibone ubutumwa bushasha. Ubwo buryo buratuma haba ibanga ryinshi kuruta ubwa mbere, kuko ata makuru arungikwa ku ba serveri b’abandi; nta makuru ya Google, Apple canke STF aronka. Ku rundi ruhande, ubu buryo burafise ingorane zibiri: amatangazo arashobora guteba (gushika ku minota myinshi), kandi inguvu zikoreshwa muri rusangi ni nyinshi kubera igikorwa c’ikoreshwa mu nyuma.



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Ubu uhuye n'iporogarama ya Session kandi urashobora gutangura guhana ubutumwa.



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



## Bika konti yawe y'igihe



Ikintu ca mbere wokora imbere yo gutangura gukoresha Session ni ukubika konti yawe kugira ngo ushobore kuyigarura iyo utakaje igikoresho cawe. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda kuri buto ya "*Continue*" iri iruhande ya "*Bika ijambobanga ryawe ryo gusubirana*".



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Session izoca yerekana ijambo Mnemonic. Uyikope neza maze uyishire ahantu heza. Iri jambo ritanga uburenganzira bwo gushika kuri konti yawe ya Session, rero birahambaye ko utayitangaza. Uzoyikenera kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha konti yawe ku kindi gikoresho, cane cane iyo telefone yawe ubu yatakaye canke isubirijwe.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Iryo jambo rikora mu buryo busa n’ubw’amajambo ya Mnemonic akoreshwa mu bipapuro vya Bitcoin. Ndagusavye rero kuraba iyi yindi nyigisho, aho ndabasigurira uburyo bwiza bwo kubika ijambo Mnemonic:



https://planb.network/tutorials/wallet/backup/backup-mnemonic-22c0ddfa-fb9f-4e3a-96f9-46e2a7954270

**Iyumvire**: Bitandukanye n'amajambo ya Mnemonic akoreshwa ku masakoshi ya Bitcoin, ku Session, **utegerezwa kubika ijambo ryose ryose**. Inyuguti 4 za mbere ntizihagije!



## Gutegura porogaramu y'Ikiganiro



Kugira ngo ubone amategeko y'ikoreshwa, fyonda ku foto yawe iri hejuru ibubamfu bwa Interface. Aha niho ushobora kwongerako ifoto y’umwirondoro.



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



Mu "*Ibanga*", urashobora gukoresha canke guhagarika ibintu bitandukanye (wirinde, bimwe bishobora gushikiriza IP yawe Address). Ndababwira kandi ngo mukoreshe uburyo bwa "*Lock App*", busaba ko umuntu yemeza ko ari we wenyene kugira ngo ashobore gushika kuri iyo porogarama.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Mu "*Imenyekanisha*", uzosangamwo amahitamwo hagati ya "*Uburyo bwihuta*" na "*Uburyo bugenda buhoro*" (raba ibice vya kera vy'inyigisho). Ushobora kandi guhindura amatangazo kugira ngo ahure n'ivyo ukunda.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, genda kuri "*Appearance*" kugira ngo uhuze Interface n'ivyo ukunda. "*Recovery Password*" ishobora kugufasha kugarura ijambo ryawe rya Mnemonic nimba wipfuza gukora ububiko bushasha.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



## Kwohereza ubutumwa hamwe n'Ikiganiro



Kugira ngo ushikire abandi bantu, fyonda ku buto "*+*" buri kuri paji y'intango.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Hariho uburyo bwinshi bwo guhitamwo. Niba wifuza gukora canke kwinjira mu mugwi, hitamwo "*Rema Itsinda*" canke "*Injira mu Muryango*".



![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)



Niba ushaka ko umuntu akwongerako nk’umuntu wo guhamagara, urashobora kumubwira ngo akore scanner ya Session ID yawe nk’uko ari kode ya QR.



![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)



Kugira ngo wohereze urupapuro rwawe rwo kwinjira kure, kanda kuri "*Tumira umugenzi*". Ushobora rero gukopa Session ID yawe ukayirungika biciye ku yindi nzira yo guhanahana amakuru. Ushobora kandi kuronka ayo makuru ukanda ku foto yawe y’ibanga kuri paji y’intango.



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Niba ufise ID y'ikiganiro c'uwundi muntu kandi ushaka kuyishiramwo, kanda kuri "*Ubutumwa bushasha*".



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Ushobora rero gushiramwo ikimenyetso caco mu nyandiko, canke ukagicapura ataco uhinduye nimba ufise nk’ikode ya QR.



![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)



Hanyuma wohereze ubutumwa bwa mbere kuri uwo muntu.



![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)



Uwo muntu akimara kwemera ivyo wasavye, uzobona izina ryiwe ry'urubuga riboneka, kandi uzoshobora kuyaga na we ataco widegemvya.



![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)



## Guhuza porogaramu ya mudasobwa



Kugira ngo konti yawe ihuzwe kuri mudasobwa yawe, urakeneye gushiramwo iyo porogarama. [Uyikure ku rubuga rwemewe](https://getsession.org/uyikure). Ndaguhanura ngo usuzume ukuri kwayo n’ubutungane bwayo imbere y’uko uyishiramwo.



![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)



Ku gutangura kwa mbere, ukande kuri "*Mfise konti*".



![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)



Injira mu nsiguro yawe ya Mnemonic, urabe ko usiga umwanya hagati y’ijambo rimwe rimwe.



![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)



Ubu urashobora gushika ku biyago vyawe ukoresheje mudasobwa yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)
