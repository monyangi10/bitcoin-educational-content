Gusuzuma ubutumwa kuri SMS vyacitse ikintu gikenewe cane ku bikorwa vyinshi vyo kuri Internet. Yaba ari ugufungura konti ku rubuga, kwemeza ko umuntu yiyandikishije canke kwemeza ko ari we, imbuga hafi zisaba inomero ya telefone. Ico gikorwa gikwiragiye cane kiratera ingorane ikomeye umuntu wese yipfuza kuzigama ubuzima bwiwe bwite: inomero yawe bwite iba ikimenyetso kidahera, igahuza ibikorwa vyawe bitandukanye vyo kuri interineti n’akaranga kawe nyakuri kandi igatuma umuntu ashobora gusaba ubudandaji udashaka.



**SMS4Sats** yishura kuri ico kibazo mu gutanga inomero za telefone z’igihe gito zo kwakira no kohereza ubutumwa bwa SMS. Iyi serivisi igaragara cane kubera uburyo bwayo bwo kutayandikisha no kwishura Bitcoin gusa biciye ku Lightning Network. Ku satoshis nkeyi, uronka inomero yo gukoresha rimwe gusa ishobora kugufasha kwemeza ko wiyandikishije ata na rimwe uhishuriye inomero yawe bwite.



Iyi nyigisho irakuyobora mu bintu bitatu vya SMS4Sats: kwakira ubutumwa bwo kugenzura, kohereza ubutumwa butazwi, no gukodesha inomero y’igihe gito amasaha menshi.



## SMS4Sats ni iki?



SMS4Sats ni ubuhinga bwo kuri internet bushobora gukoreshwa kuri [sms4sats.com](https://sms4sats.com), bushobora gucungera ubutumwa butamenyekana biciye mu kwishura muri Bitcoin Lightning. Iyo serivisi itanga ibikorwa bitatu bitandukanye: kwakira amakode yo kugenzura akoreshwa rimwe gusa, kohereza ubutumwa kuri nomero iyo ari yo yose, no gukodesha inomero z’igihe gito amasaha menshi.



### Filozofiya n'akarorero k'imikorere



Uwo mugambi ugamije gutuma haba ibanga ryinshi n’ubusegaba bw’amahera. Mu gusaba ko ata konti igira kandi yemera gusa amahera ya Bitcoin Lightning, SMS4Sats irakuraho burundu ivy’ugutanga amakuru y’ibanga. Nta aderesi ya email, nta karata y’inguzanyo, nta makuru y’ibanga asabwa. Kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kuronka ibikorwa, bisabwa kwishura Lightning gusa.



Iryo shirahamwe rifasha imbuga n’ibikorwa birenga 400 mu bihugu nka 120, bikaba bifasha mu gusuzuma vyinshi mu bikenewe. Ukwo gukwiragira kw’ibihugu vyinshi biratuma umuntu yemeza ko yiyandikishije ku mbuga zitandukanye, kuva ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi gushika ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi.



### Uburyo bwo kwishura busabwa



SMS4Sats ikoresha amafagitire y’umuravyo afise ivyangombwa (amafagitire ya hodl) ku bijanye n’imikorere yayo yo kwakira ubutumwa bugufi. Ubwo buryo buratuma ushobora kwishurwa gusa iyo SMS yakiriwe vy’ukuri. Iyo ata butumwa bushitse mu kiringo categekanijwe (iminota 20 gusa), amahera y’ukwishura arasubirwamwo ubwo nyene maze amasatoshi agasubirwamwo kuri wallet yawe. Iyi garanti ntijanye n’ibintu vyo kohereza no gukodesha, ivyo bikaba bidashobora gusubirwamwo.



## Ivyo bice bitatu vya SMS4Sats



Igikoresho ca SMS4Sats gitunganijwe hirya no hino y’ibice bitatu bihuye n’imikoreshereze itatu itandukanye: **RECEIVE** kugira ngo wakire amakode yo kugenzura, **SEND** ​​kugira ngo urungike SMS zitazwi, na **RENT** kugira ngo ukodeshe inomero y’igihe gito.



### Kwakira ubutumwa bwo kuri SMS



Igihambaye kigufasha kuronka inomero y’igihe gito kugira ngo uronke kode yihariye yo kugenzura. Iyo kode imaze kwakirwa no gukoreshwa, iyo nomero irazimwa ubuziraherezo.



### Wohereze ubutumwa



Ivyo bituma ushobora kohereza ubutumwa kuri telefone iyo ari yo yose utamenyesheje uwo uri we. Uwuzokwakira azoronka ubutumwa buvuye ku nimero itazwi.



### Gukodesha igikorwa



Ku bakoresha bakeneye kwakira ubutumwa bwinshi bwa SMS ku nimero imwe, SMS4Sats iratanga uburyo bwo gukodesha mu gihe gito. Iryo hitamwo rituma ushobora kwakira no kohereza ubutumwa bwinshi mu kiringo co gukodesha.



**Iciyumviro ku biciro** : Ibiciro vyerekanywe muri iyi nyigisho ni ivyerekana. Bitandukanye bivanye n’igihugu c’umubare, igikorwa ciwe n’ivyo umuntu asaba ubu. Nk’akarorero, ubutumwa bwo kuri Telegram France burashobora gutwara amasatoshi ari hagati ya 1.500 na 5.000, bivanye n’ingene bimeze. Imisi yose banza usuzume umubare nyawo w’invoice ya Lightning imbere yo kwishura.



## Kwakira ubutumwa bwo kugenzura



Reka turabe mu buryo burambuye ingene wokoresha SMS4Sats kugira ngo uronke kode yo kugenzura, ivyo bikaba vyerekanwa n’uguhingura konti ya Telegram.



### Intambwe ya 1: Hitamwo igihugu n'igikorwa



Genda kuri [sms4sats.com](https://sms4sats.com) maze ugume ku rubuga rwa **KWAKIRA**. Hitamwo igihugu c’umubare wipfuza mu rutonde ruja hasi. Nimba igikorwa co kwiyandikisha cawe kiri ku rutonde, gihitemwo kugira ngo ubone amahirwe menshi yo kwakira.



![Sélection du pays France et du service Telegram sur SMS4Sats](assets/fr/01.webp)



Muri aka karorero, duhitamwo Ubufaransa nk’igihugu na Telegram nk’igikorwa c’intumbero. Fyonda **NEXT** kugira ngo ugende ku ntambwe ikurikira.



### Intambwe ya 2: Wishura inyemezabuguzi y'umuravyo



Inyemezabuguzi y’umuravyo yerekanwa mu buryo bwa kode ya QR. Amahera arahindagurika bivanye n’igihugu be n’igikorwa umuntu yatoye. Scan code ya QR ukoresheje Lightning wallet yawe canke ukope invoice kugira ngo wishyure n’amaboko.



![QR code de paiement Lightning avec garantie de remboursement](assets/fr/02.webp)



Iyumvire ubutumwa buhambaye: "Nta kode ya SMS = Nta kwishura". Iyo utaronse SMS, amahera warishe azoca asubirwamwo ubwo nyene, asubizwe mu minota 20.



### Intambwe ya 3: Uronke inomero y'igihe gito



Iyo umuntu amaze kwemeza ko yishuye, inomero ya telefone y’agateganyo iraca yerekanwa. Igiharuro kigaragaza igihe gisigaye co kwakira iyo SMS.



![Numéro temporaire révélé avec timer d'attente](assets/fr/03.webp)



Kopa iyi nomero (aha +33 7 74 70 09 66) kugira ngo uyikoreshe kuri servisi wipfuza kwiyandikishako.



### Intambwe ya 4: Koresha inomero iri kuri serivisi y'intumbero



Injira inomero y’igihe gito kuri porogarama canke ku rubuga aho ushize konti yawe. Mu karorero kacu ka Telegram, shiramwo umubare, uwuvyemeze hanyuma urindire kode yo kugenzura.



![Processus d'inscription Telegram avec le numéro temporaire SMS4Sats](assets/fr/04.webp)



Ivyo bikorwa ni nk’ivyo kwiyandikisha mu buryo busanzwe: winjiza inomero, Telegram ikarungika kode yo kugenzura biciye kuri SMS, hanyuma ugaheza ugakora konti.



### Intambwe ya 5: Kugarura kode yo kugenzura



Subira ku nzira ya SMS4Sats. Iyo SMS ikimara kwakira, kode y’ugukoresha ica igaragara. Fyonda kuri **COPY CODE** kugira ngo uyikope mu buryo bworoshe.



![Code de vérification reçu sur SMS4Sats](assets/fr/05.webp)



Injira iyi kode kuri servisi y’intumbero kugira ngo uheze kwiyandikisha. Ico gitigiri c’igihe gito rero kirazimwa ubuziraherezo.



## Wohereze ubutumwa butazwi



SMS4Sats kandi iragufasha kohereza ubutumwa bwa SMS ku nimero iyo ari yo yose utamenyesheje uwo uri we.



### Intambwe ya 1: Kwandika ubutumwa



Fyonda ku rubuga rwa **SEND**. Injira inomero ya telefone iyo ujako n’ibara ryayo ryo guhamagara mpuzamakungu maze wandike ubutumwa bwawe (inyuguti 1600 zirengeye).



![Interface d'envoi de SMS avec numéro destinataire et message](assets/fr/06.webp)



Fyonda **IKURIKIYE** kugira ngo ukomeze kwishura.



### Intambwe ya 2: Urihe kandi wohereze



Ishura invoice y’umuravyo yerekanwa. Iyo umuntu amaze kwemezwa ko yishuye, iyo SMS ica irungikirwa uwuyironse.



![Confirmation d'envoi du SMS avec code de confirmation](assets/fr/07.webp)



Kode y’ukwemeza iragaragazwa kugira ngo wemeze ko ubutumwa bwarungitswe. Uwuzoronka iyo SMS azoronka iyo SMS avuye kuri nomero itazwi.



## Gukodesha inomero y'igihe gito



Ku bikorwa bisaba ubutumwa bwinshi bwa SMS ku nomero imwe, igice ca RENT kiragufasha gukodesha inomero amasaha menshi.



### Gukodesha imiterere



Fyonda ku rubuga rwa **RENT**. Hitamwo igihugu n’igihe bimara.



![Interface de location de numéro avec avertissement de non-remboursement](assets/fr/08.webp)



**Ibintu bihambaye vyo kwitwararika:**




- Ibiciro birahindagurika bivanye n'igihugu n'igihe umuntu amara
- Ugukodesha ntigusubizwa**, bitandukanye n'ubutumwa bwa SMS bukoreshwa rimwe
- Igitigiri c'abakodesha muri rusangi ntikikorana na Telegram.
- Ubu buryo burabereye kwiyandikisha ku bikorwa vyinshi bikurikirana



Uhejeje gukanda kuri **NEXT** ukishura invoice ya Lightning, uzoronka inomero ushobora gukoresha mu kiringo cose c'ubukode kugira ngo wakire no kohereza ubutumwa bwa SMS.



## Inyungu n'aho bigarukira



### Ibintu bihambaye



**Nta makuru y’ibanga asabwa**: Uburyo bwo kutayandikisha bugaragaza ko ata makuru y’ibanga yegeranywa.



**Ibindi bikorwa bitatu**: Kwakira, kohereza no gukodesha bipfuka ivyo ukeneye vyinshi.



**Ukwishura muri Bitcoin gusa** : Lightning Network iremeza gukoresha amafaranga ako kanya canke mu mazina y’uruyeri.



**Ivyishurwa ubwavyo**: Iyo wakiriye ubutumwa bwa SMS, ubuhinga bwa hodl invoices burakwemeza ko uriha gusa iyo SMS ishitse.



### Intambamyi zo kwihweza



**Umutekano w’umurongo wa SMS**: Amakode ya SMS si uburyo bukomeye bwo kwemeza kandi ntabwo akwiye gukoreshwa ku makonti y’agaciro.



**Uguhuza kw'ibihinduka**: Imbuga nyinshi zirabona kandi zigahagarika imibare y'ivy'impwemu. Bishobora kuba ngombwa ko ugerageza kenshi.



**Imibare idashobora gusubirwamwo**: Inyuma yo gukoreshwa rimwe, iyo nomero irasubirwamwo kandi ntishobora gusubirwamwo.



**Ivy’ubukode bitasubirwamwo**: Bitandukanye n’ubutumwa bwa SMS bukoreshwa rimwe gusa, ivy’ubukode ntibiza n’ingwati yo gusubizwa amahera.



## Ibikorwa vyiza



### Koresha Tor kugira ngo ubone ubuzima bwite



SMS4Sats ishobora gushikwako biciye kuri [Tor] Iyi ntunganyo ipfuka aderesi yawe ya IP igihe ukoresha iyo serivisi.



### Irinde inkuru zinegura



Ntukigere ukoresha inomero yo gukoresha rimwe gusa ku makonti yawe ahambaye (banki, email nyamukuru). Iyo izo nzira zigusaba ko wosubira kwemeza inomero yawe mu nyuma, uzotakaza uburenganzira bwo gukoresha iyo konti.



### Gutandukanya akaranga kawe k'ubuhinga bwa none



Ku bijanye na konti z’amazina y’uruyeri, ushire hamwe inomero y’igihe gito n’aderesi ya imeyili ikoreshwa rimwe gusa be n’umucukumbuzi atandukanye n’ivyo usanzwe ukoresha.



### Hitamwo 2FA ikomeye



Konti yawe imaze kuremwa, fungura inyishu zikomeye zo kwemeza: porogarama ya TOTP (Aegis, Ente Auth) canke urufunguzo rw’umutekano w’umubiri FIDO2.



## Iciyumviro



SMS4Sats ni umuti wuzuye wo gucunga ubutumwa bw’ibanga. Waba ushaka kwakira kode yo kugenzura, kohereza ubutumwa butazwi canke gukodesha inomero y’igihe gito, iyo serivisi irahuza n’ibintu vyinshi bikenewe mu bijanye n’ibanga, bivuye ku kwishura muri Bitcoin Lightning.



Ariko rero, uzirikane aho igarukira: iyo serivisi ntiyizera ko umuntu atazomenyekana, kandi ntibereye ku makuru y’ibintu bihambaye canke y’igihe kirekire. Iyo ukoresheje ubukerebutsi kandi ukamenya aho igarukira, SMS4Sats ni igikoresho ngirakamaro co gusubira kugenzura ivyo ukoresha kuri telefone.
