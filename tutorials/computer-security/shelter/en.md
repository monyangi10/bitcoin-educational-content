## Introduction


Bitcoin is an open source, transparent, decentralized project..


The deeper you go down the rabbit hole - the more interested you become in Bitcoin beyond the speculative aspect - the more you become a bitcoiner.


De facto, a natural desire emerges in you to discover, adopt and share with your peers projects that share these same values and are developing indispensable applications in our daily activities.


In this tutorial, let's discover a privacy management tool that limits permissions and access to your data to applications installed on your smartphone: **Shelter**.


**Shelter** is a free and open source (FOSS) mobile application that provides you with an isolated space where you can install or clone applications.


According to Shelter's GitHub repository, the main use cases are :


- installation of applications in the "Work Profile" to isolate them;
- freeze applications in the "Work Profile" to prevent them from running or reactivating when you're not actively using them;
- installing two copies of the same application on the same phone (cloning).


Shelter uses the **Work Profil** feature of the **Android** mobile operating system. As a result, Shelter is not available to Apple IOS users.


But what is the **Work Profile** feature?


## The Work Profile feature


In fact, it's a feature that lets you separate your business data and applications from your personal ones. This means you can use the same phone for both work and private life.

Your company can only delete the professional profile or certain applications from this profile, without affecting your personal data.


Important ❗️ Shelter depends entirely on the quality of Android's implementation of the **Work Profile** on your phone. If your system has bugs related to this feature, Shelter's operation will be affected.

## Application freeze in Work Profil


To freeze an application, go to Shelter. Go to the professional applications tab. From this tab, select the application you wish to freeze.


Press the application icon for a moment until the context menu appears. In this contextual menu, click on **Freeze**.


![screen](assets/fr/34.webp)


Once frozen, the application that was visible in the **Android Work** folder (1) becomes invisible (2). You can unfreeze it whenever you like in Shelter.


![screen](assets/fr/35.webp)


![screen](assets/fr/36.webp)


## Cloning an application


In Shelter, you can clone an application you're already using directly into your personal space.

To do this, go to the **Main Profile** tab, then click on the application you wish to clone for a moment, until the small contextual menu appears.


Let's try cloning the **Telegram** application.

When the pop-up menu appears, tap on **Cloner to Shelter (Professional Profile)**.


![screen](assets/fr/37.webp)


Accept the installation by clicking on **Install**.


![screen](assets/fr/38.webp)


**Telegram** is now installed and visible in the **Shelter** tab.


![screen](assets/fr/39.webp)


The reverse process is also possible, i.e. cloning an application available in the **professional** tab to the **main (personal)** tab.

To do this, from the Professional tab, tap on the application to be cloned to bring up the contextual menu. Then click on **Cloner to Main Profile**. Let's try cloning the GPS Local application.


![screen](assets/fr/40.webp)


Press **Install** to accept the installation.


![screen](assets/fr/41.webp)


The application is now available in your personal applications menu.


![screen](assets/fr/42.webp)


## Uninstalling Shelter


There are many reasons why you might want to uninstall Shelter. Either because your new job no longer requires you to use it, or because you've just acquired a new phone dedicated exclusively to your work..

In any case, you must first delete the **Professional Profile**. In your phone's **Settings**, go to **Passwords and Accounts*** and delete the Professional profile.


![screen](assets/fr/43.webp)


Then go back to your applications menu and uninstall the **Shelter** application now.
