## Imenyekanisha



Bitcoin ni umugambi wuguruye, uboneye, wegerejwe..



Uko ugenda umanuka mu mwobo w’inkwavu - niko urushiriza gukunda Bitcoin birenze uruhande rw’ugutekereza ku bintu - niko urushiriza kuba umunyamideri.



De facto, icipfuzo kamere kivyuka muri wewe co kuvumbura, kwemera no gusangira n’urunganwe rwawe imigambi isangira izo nzira nyene kandi iriko irategura ibikorwa vy’agaciro mu bikorwa vyacu vya misi yose.



Muri iyi nyigisho, reka tubone igikoresho co gucunga ubuzima bwite gihagarika uburenganzira n’ugushika ku makuru yawe ku bikoresho vyashizwe kuri telefone yawe: **Shelter**.



**Shelter** ni porogaramu y’ubuntu kandi yuguruye (FOSS) iguha ikibanza kiri ukwaco aho ushobora gushiramwo canke gukora clone porogaramu.



Dushingiye ku bubiko bwa GitHub bwa Shelter, ibintu nyamukuru bikoreshwa ni :




- gushiramwo ibikorwa mu "Profile y'akazi" kugira ngo bitandukanye;
- gufunga ibikorwa biri muri "Imbonerahamwe y'Akazi" kugira ngo ntibikore canke ngo bisubire gukora iyo utariko urabikoresha n'umwete;
- gushiramwo kopi zibiri z’iporogarama imwe kuri telefone imwe (cloning).



Shelter ikoresha **Igishushanyo c'akazi** c'umurongo w'ikoreshwa rya **Android**. Ivyo vyatumye Shelter itaboneka ku bakoresha Apple IOS.



Ariko none **Ivyerekeye Akazi** ni igiki?



## Ikiranga Umwirondoro w'Akazi



Nkako, ni ikintu kigufasha gutandukanya amakuru yawe y’ubudandaji n’ibikorwa vyawe n’ivyawe bwite. Ivyo bisigura ko ushobora gukoresha telefone imwe ku kazi no ku buzima bwite.


Ishirahamwe ryawe rishobora gusa gukuraho urutonde rw’umwuga canke porogarama zimwe zimwe muri iyo nkuru, ataco bihinduye ku makuru yawe bwite.



Ivy’ingenzi ❗️ Uburaro buvana n’uko Android ishira mu ngiro **Work Profile** kuri telefone yawe. Niba sisitemu yawe ifise ibibazo bijanye n'iki kintu, ibikorwa vya Shelter bizogira ico bikoze.

## Gushiramwo porogaramu mu rutonde rw'akazi



Ku **Ubuhungiro**, fyonda ku kimenyetso c'ikoreshwa rya Google Play Store umwanya mutoyi. Hanyuma, ku rutonde rw’ibintu (igicapo c’amakuru) kizoboneka, ukande kuri **Launch**.



![screen](assets/fr/23.webp)



Kanda kuri buto ya **Connect** wongereko aderesi ya e-mail y'ubucuruzi kugira ngo uhuze.



![screen](assets/fr/24.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/25.webp)



Hanyuma ugende kuri Play store ushiremwo amaporogarama y’ubudandaji ukeneye.


Nk’uko mubibona, porogarama ya **Signal**, iyo imaze gukurwako, iboneka ku rutonde rw’ibikorwa vyawe vy’umwuga ku rubuga rwa **Shelter**.



![screen](assets/fr/26.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/27.webp)



Raba ingene wotangura gukoresha Signal mu nyigisho ikurikira:



https://planb.academy/tutorials/computer-security/communication/signal-8dfb5572-6962-4f1c-bfa5-3192da4e9a4e

Ushobora kandi gushiramwo porogarama yavanwe mu buryo bwa APK (Android Package Kit) kuri telefone yawe.


Uburyo bwa dosiye **APK** ni uburyo busanzwe bukoreshwa na Android mu gukwiragiza no gushiramwo porogarama zo kuri telefone ngendanwa.



Kugira ngo ubikore, ushireko **utudomo dutatu** icon hanyuma ushireko APK mu Shelter**. Uzoca urungikwa mu bubiko bw’imbere muri telefone yawe, aho ushobora guhitamwo dosiye ya APK uzoshiramwo ukanda kuri yo.



Reka tugerageze gushiramwo dosiye APK y'iyi porogaramu **GPS local**.



![screen](assets/fr/28.webp)

![screen](assets/fr/30.webp)

## Ubusabe burahagarara muri porofayili y'akazi



Kugira ngo ufunge ubusabe, genda kuri Shelter. Genda ku rubuga rw’ibisabwa vy’umwuga. Muri iyi tab, hitamwo porogaramu wipfuza guhagarika.



Kanda gatoyi ku kimenyetso c’ikoreshwa gushika aho urutonde rw’ibintu birimwo rubonetse. Muri iyi menyu, kanda kuri **Freeze**.



![screen](assets/fr/34.webp)



Iyo imaze gukonja, iyo porogarama yari iboneka muri dosiye ya **Android Work** (1) iracika itaboneka (2). Ushobora kuyifungura igihe cose ushaka muri Shelter.

## Gukora igikorwa



Mu Shelter, urashobora gukora clone y'iporogarama usanzwe ukoresha mu kibanza cawe bwite.


Kugira ngo ubikore, genda kuri **Main Profile**, hanyuma ukande kuri porogarama wipfuza gukora clone umwanya mutoyi, gushika aho akarongo gatoyi k’ivyo uvuga kabonetse.



Reka tugerageze gukora cloning y'ikoreshwa rya **Telegram**.


Igihe urutonde ruzoboneka, nufyonde kuri **Cloner to Shelter (Ivyerekeye Umwuga)**.



![screen](assets/fr/37.webp)



Wemere gushiramwo mu gufyonda kuri **Shiramwo**.



![screen](assets/fr/38.webp)



**Telegram** ubu irashizweho kandi iraboneka mu gice ca **Ubuhungiro**.



![screen](assets/fr/39.webp)



Inzira y'uguhindura irashoboka, ni ukuvuga gukora cloning y'ikoreshwa riri mu **professional** tab ku **main (personal)** tab.


Kugira ngo ubikore, uhereye ku rubuga rwitwa Professional, nukore kuri porogarama uzokora kugira ngo ubone urutonde rw’ibintu birimwo. Hanyuma ukande kuri **Cloner ku Profile nyamukuru**. Reka tugerageze gukora cloning y’iporogarama ya GPS Local.



![screen](assets/fr/40.webp)



Kanda **Install** kugira ngo wemere gushiramwo.



![screen](assets/fr/41.webp)



Ubu iyo porogarama iraboneka mu rutonde rw’ibikoresho vyawe bwite.



![screen](assets/fr/42.webp)



## Gukuraho ubuhungiro



Hari impamvu nyinshi zituma ushobora gukuraho Shelter. Canke kubera ko akazi kawe gashasha katagusaba ko ukakoresha, canke kubera ko umaze kuronka telefone nshasha y'akazi kawe gusa..


Uko biri kwose, utegerezwa kubanza gukuraho **Ivyerekeye Umwuga**. Mu **Settings** za telefone yawe, genda kuri **Amajambo y'ibanga n'amakonti** hanyuma usibe urutonde rw'abahinga.



![screen](assets/fr/43.webp)



Hanyuma usubire muri menu ya porogaramu zawe maze ukureho porogaramu ya **Shelter** ubu.



Ubu urazi gukoresha porogarama ya Shelter kugira ngo ukore **urutonde rw'akazi** rwitandukanije kandi rutandukanye n'iporogarama zawe bwite. Niwaba wasanze iyi nyigisho ari ngirakamaro, ndagusavye unsigire urukumu rw'icatsi kibisi aha hepfo. Ndabinginze ntimwitinye kubisangiza abandi.
