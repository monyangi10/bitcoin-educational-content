> [GrapheneOS](https://grapheneos.org/) ni ubuhinga bwo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa butagira inyungu bugenewe gutanga ubuzima bwite n’umutekano mu gihe buguma buhuye neza n’ibikoresho vya Android.

GrapheneOS, yashinzwe mu mwaka w’2014 yitwa ‘CopperheadOS’ ishingiye ku nzira ya kera ya Android Code (AOSP), ariko ifise amahinduka menshi n’ivyo gutera imbere bigamije gutuma abakoresha bagira ubuzima bwite n’umutekano. GrapheneOS ishira uwuyikoresha mu bubasha bwo kugenzura telefone yiwe, atari amashirahamwe manini manini y’ubuhinga.

## Ni kuki ukoresha GrapheneOS?


Amatelefone y’ubu ni ibikoresho vyo gukurikirana no gukusanya amakuru bigura amadolari 500-1000. Igice cose c’ubuzima bwacu kiraca muri vyo, kandi ikibabaje ni uko vyinshi muri ivyo bimenyetso bisangizwa n’abandi bantu mu buryo bumwe canke ubundi.

GrapheneOS yubatswe cane cane kugira ngo igabanye uku gusangira amakuru no gutuma umutekano w’igikoresho cawe utera imbere ku bitero bishobora gutera. Nta kintu nk’ico co gukoresha konti ya GrapheneOS. Ntukeneye imwe kugira ngo ushireko apps canke ngo ukoreshe OS. Mu mvugo yoroshe, si wewe igicuruzwa.


GrapheneOS itanga umutekano wongerewe ku bumenyi bwawe bwa Android biciye ku ngingo ngenderwako zimwe zimwe z’ishimikiro zoroshe.


1. **Kugabanya uburebure bw’igitero** - Kuraho kode idakenewe (canke bloatware).

2. **Kwirinda gushikirwa n'ingorane** - Kwemerera uwukoresha uburenganzira buhagije bwo guhitamwo ivyo ashobora gukora.

3. **Gufata amasandugu y’umusenyi** - GrapheneOS ikomeza amasandugu y’umusenyi ya Android asanzweho, igakomeza gufunga ubushobozi bwa app yose bwo kuvugana n’ibindi bice vya telefone yawe.


Menya vyinshi ku bijanye n’ubuhinga bw’ibiranga GrapheneOS [hano](https://grapheneos.org/ibiranga).


### Kworohereza Impinduka


Niba umaze imyaka ushinze imizi mu bidukikije vya Google canke Apple, iciyumviro co gutakaza ivyo vyose vyiza mw’ijoro rimwe gishobora kuba ikintu giteye ubwoba. Ariko iyo umuntu afashe ingingo zimwezimwe zo gushiramwo porogarama zirimbuwe neza (zizovugwa mu nyuma), vyinshi muri ivyo bibazo vyitezwe birashobora kugabanywa canke bikakurwaho.


Naho ubundi buryo bwo guhindura ibintu buriko buraba bwiza, iciyumviro c’iryo hinduka kirashobora kuba kigutera ubwoba. Niwaba uri muri iyo nzira, impanuro yanje nziza ni ugukoresha igikoresho cawe gishasha ca GrapheneOS iruhande ya telefone yawe isanzweho mu gihe gito. Uvuye ng’aho ushobora kwicukura buhoro buhoro ku ma apps 2-3 mu ndwi gushika usanga uriko urashika ku gikoresho cawe ca GrapheneOS gusa.


Niwafata iyo nzira, nube uwukomeye kuri wewe nyene kandi ureke kwizigira ubundi buryo bugenzurwa vuba cane. Twebwe abantu turi abanebwe kandi kenshi tuzofata inzira y’ukurwanya guke. Ibuka igituma wakoze iyo switch mu ntango.


**Aho kwishura n’amakuru yawe bwite, wahisemwo kwishura n’igihe cawe, kandi rimwe na rimwe Hard yawe yararonka amahera (bivanye n’izindi porogarama ushiramwo).**


## Gutangura


GrapheneOS ubu iriko irakorwa gusa ku _(ahubwo mu buryo buteye isoni)_ [Google Pixel](ibikoresho-bifashwa) urutonde rw'amatelefone. Ivyo ntibiza ata mvo yumvikana naho. Pixel's itanga umutekano mwiza cane ushingiye ku bikoresho kugira ngo yuzuze igikorwa cakozwe co gukomeza OS. Ibi birimwo ibintu nk’ugutandukanya ibice vyihariye n’ugufungura kwagenzuwe.


### Guhitamwo igikoresho


Igihe uhisemwo Pixel ushaka gushiramwo GrapheneOS, urabe neza ko usuzuma igihe igikoresho kizobandanya kironka ivyagezwe vy’umutekano.


Mu gihe c’ukwandika, Pixel 6a ni modele ihendutse kuruta izindi zose ziboneka zifise ubufasha bwiza bw’igihe kirekire, zishobora gushika muri Nyakanga 2027. Niwahitamwo iyi modele, OEM unlocking ntizokorana na verisiyo ya OS y’isoko ivuye mu ruganda. Ukeneye kuyihindura kugira ngo izosohoke muri Ruheshi 2022 canke inyuma y’aho biciye ku guhindura ku kirere. Uhejeje kuyihindura uzokenera kandi gusubiramwo igikoresho kugira ngo ukosore ugufungura kwa OEM. Ibindi bikoresho vyose bifunguwe bizoba biteguriye GrapheneOS bivuye mu gasandugu.


Igihe uhisemwo igikoresho uzoshaka kandi kumenya neza ko ugura verisiyo idafunguwe. Hari amashirahamwe nka Verizon yohereza ama unités yabo afise bootloader locked ivyo bikaba bibuza rwose ivyo bikurikira.


### Gushiramwo GrapheneOS


GrapheneOS [igikoresho co gushiramwo urubuga](https://grapheneos.org/install/web) gituma igikorwa cose kiba nk’umuyaga, kandi gishobora kurangira n’umuntu wese mu minota itarenga 10.

Amabwirizwa akurikira ni verisiyo igabanutse yakuwe ku nzira iri hejuru.


Ivyo ukeneye gutanga vyose ni:



- Pixel
- Umugozi wa USB wo kuva kuri telefone ukaja kuri mudasobwa yawe
- Mudasobwa yo gukoresha umucukumbuzi w'urubuga (umucukumbuzi yose ishingiye kuri Chromium: Chrome, Edge, Brave, n'ibindi)


Reka tuvyinjiremwo:


1. Intambwe yambere ni ukuja kuri **Settings** > **Ivyerekeye telefone** hanyuma ugasubiramwo ukanda kuri nomero y'ubwubatsi gushika ubonye **'Developer Mode'** ikora.

2. Hanyuma genda kuri **Ivyagezwe** > **Sisitemu** > **Amahitamwo y'abahinguzi** hanyuma ushoboze **'OEM Unlocking'**.

3. Ubu rero subiramwo igikoresho maze ufate buto yo guhagarika ijwi mu gihe telefone iriko irasubira gukora.

4. Huza telefone na laptop yawe kandi iyo usabwe uruhusha, wemere ko ihuzwa.

5. Kuri paji y'urubuga, kanda kuri 'Ufungure bootloader'.

6. Uzoca ubona amahitamwo ya telefone ahinduka. Koresha buto y'ijwi kugira ngo uhindure ihitamwo kugira ngo `ufungure` hanyuma ukoreshe buto y'ubushobozi kugira ngo wemere.

7. Hanyuma ukande kuri download release kuri paji y’urubuga rwo gushiramwo.

8. Iyo umaze gukuraho vyose, genda ku ntambwe ikurikira ukande kuri 'Flash release'. Ivyo bizotwara umunota umwe canke ibiri kandi ntukeneye gukora kuri telefone na gato.

9. Ubwa nyuma, genda ku ntambwe ikurikira y’umurongo w’urubuga maze ukande **Lock Bootloader**. Uzokenera guhindura ivyo wahisemwo no kwemeza ukoresheje buto y'umuriro nk'uko wabigize mbere mu nzira.

10. Niwabona ijambo `Start`, wemeze ivyo ukoresheje buto y’umuriro maze igikoresho kizotangura muri system yawe nshasha idakoresha Google.

### Kwinjiza abahuye


**Uvuye ku yindi telefone ya Android** - Genda kuri app Contacts urondere uburyo bwo gusohora muri VCF.


**Kuva kuri iOS** - Koresha app nka Export Contact hanyuma ukoreshe 'vCard' kugira ngo urungike dosiye ya VCF.

## Ubundi porogaramu


Kugira telefone yawe ibe ngirakamaro uzoshaka gushiramwo ama application amwe amwe! Amahitamwo akurikira arashizwemwo kubera ko yose nayakoresheje jewe ubwanje canke kubera ko aronka impanuro zikomeye zivuye ku bantu benshi bajejwe ivy’ubuzima bwite. Hariho n’ibindi vyinshi vyiza bishobora gukoreshwa bitavuzwe, kandi [Ubuzima bwite buteye ubwoba](https://awesome-privacy.xyz) itanga urutonde runini cane rw’ibikorwa vyo kuzigama ubuzima bwite ku bwoko bwose bw’ibikoresho.


Kubera ko app ari Porogaramu y’Ubuntu kandi Ifunguye (FOSS) ntibisigura ko idashobora gusohoka mu vy’ubuzima bwite. Koresha [Kuvayo](https://raporo.ukuvayo-ubuzima bwite.eu.org/ru/) kugira ngo ubone ingene porogarama ukunda zikora iyo zigereranywa n’igenzura ry’ubuzima bwite bwazo.


### F-Droyide


[F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/) ni urutonde rw'ibikoresho vya FOSS bishobora gushirwako kuri Android. Umukiriya atuma vyoroha gucapura, gushiramwo no guhindura porogarama ku gikoresho cawe. Ni vyiza kuvuga ko ivyagezwe biciye kuri F-Droid rimwe na rimwe bishobora guteba kuruta uko bigenda ku yindi maduka y’amaporogarama. Ivyo ahanini bivana n’uko iyo porogarama iboneka canke itaboneka biciye ku bubiko nyamukuru bwa F-Droid canke ububiko busanzwe.


Kugira ngo ushiremwo F-Droid gusa genda ku rubuga rwabo uciye ku mucukumbuzi wo kuri telefone yawe ya GrapheneOS hanyuma ukande kuri download. Ibi bizokuraho dosiye `.apk`. Uzoheza ubazwe nimba woshima gushiramwo iyo porogarama.


Nk’uko nyene ibikorwa biboneka mu bubiko bwa mbere muri F-Droid, imigambi myinshi y’Isoko Yuguruye nayo izokwakira ububiko bwayo bwite bushobora kwongerwa mu mitunganyirize ya porogarama ya F-Droid. Niba ari uko bimeze, umugambi uvugwa uzogutwara mu ntambwe zoroshe cane zikenewe kugira ngo ivyo ubishikeko ku rubuga rwabo.

### Iduka rya Aurora


[Iduka rya Aurora] ni verisiyo ya FOSS y'iduka rya Google Play. Aurora isa cane kandi yumva isa cane n’iduka rya kera rya Play kandi iraguha uburenganzira bwo gukura no guhindura porogarama iyo ari yo yose woshobora gusanga biciye ku mahitamwo ya Google.


Ikintu cica Aurora ni ukwinjira ata mazina, ivyo bisigura ko ushobora gukuraho porogarama yose ukunda itaboneka kuri F-Droid canke APK itaziguye, ataco ukeneye kwinjira muri konti yawe ya Google.


Imbere y’uko wihuta kugira ivyo bibe uburyo bwawe bwo gushiramwo, wibuke ko porogarama nyinshi turiko turagerageza guhunga zashizwemwo zivuye muri Play Store. Kubera gusa ko zishobora gushikwako zivuye kuri Aurora ntibihindura ukuntu bamwe bashobora kuba bafise ibintu vyo gukurikirana bishizwemwo. Ntibizokwama bishoboka, ariko igihe cose ushoboye, rondera ubundi buryo bwa F-Droid imbere yo gukura kuri Aurora.


Kugira ngo ushiremwo Aurora, ushobora gusa kurondera 'Iduka rya Aurora' muri F-Droid.


Aurora irafise kandi ibitero bishobora gutera, kuko "amakonti atazwi" vy'ukuri aremwa kandi agenzurwa na Aurora. Bashobora, mu vyiyumviro, gutanga amakuru mabi canke gusunika porogarama kuri telefone yawe, naho nyene woba ubwirizwa kwemera gushiramwo ku gikoresho. Aurora nayo rimwe na rimwe irafise ibibazo bimwe bimwe n’amaporogarama atagaragara kubera ugusoma nabi kw’akarere n’ibikoresho. Ivyo akenshi bishobora gukorwa n’intambwe zikurikira.


**Impanuro yo hejuru** - Rimwe na rimwe Aurora Store izohura n'uguhagarika ibiciro bigabanya ubushobozi bwawe bwo kurondera no gushiramwo amaporogarama. Kugira ngo ubone ivyo, genda kuri **Ivyagezwe** > **Ibikoresho** > **Aurora** > **Gufungura ku buryo busanzwe**, hanyuma wongereko urwego `play.google.com`. Ubu, igihe cose uzoba uriko uraja ku rubuga rw’igicuruzwa canke rw’igikorwa rufise uruja n’uruza rwitwa ‘Download via Play Store’, ukawufyondako uzogufungurira iyo porogarama iri muri Aurora kugira ngo uyironke.

### APK Gukuraho


Porogaramu ziri kuri Android na zo nyene zirashobora gukurwako no gushirwako biciye ku dosiye `.apk`. Iyi ni uburyo bwiza cane busaba zero amaduka y’amaporogarama y’uwundi muntu, gusa gukura dosiye ku rubuga rw’umugambi canke rw’ibikorwa canke ububiko bwa GitHub.


Ikibi kiri muri iyo nzira ni uko udashobora kuronka amakuru mashasha, rero uzokenera gukurikirana inzira z’itumanaho z’iyo serivisi kugira ngo umenye ibishasha bisohotse. Ariko rero hariho umugambi munini witwa Obtanium ugamije gutorera umuti ivyo. [Obtainium](https://github.com/ImranR98/Obtainium) iragufasha gushiramwo no guhindura porogarama zikoreshwa n’inkomoko yuguruye uhereye kuri paji zazo zisohotse, no kwakira amatangazo iyo zisohotse bishasha zibonetse.

### Porogaramu z'urubuga


Ku bihe ushobora gushaka gukoresha serivisi kenshi kandi udashaka gukuraho porogarama y’ikirundi, urashobora gushika ku rubuga. Imbuga nyinshi muri iki gihe na zo nyene ziratanga ubufasha bw’urubuga rwo gutera imbere (PWA). Aha niho ushobora gushiramwo urubuga runaka (nk’akarorero Twitter.com) ku rubuga rwa telefone yawe. Hanyuma iyo ukoresheje icon irafunguka nk’iporogarama yuzuye ata bintu bisanzwe bisamaza biza n’ubumenyi busanzwe bwa browser. Ushobora kubona akarorero k’ingene ivyo bisa aha hepfo.


Kugira ngo ivyo ubishikeko muri Vanadium, umucukumbuzi wa GrapheneOS, genda gusa ku rubuga uhisemwo, ukande ku tudodo dutatu duhagaze turi hejuru iburyo bw'ibarabara hanyuma ukande kuri **'Ongera ku rubuga rw'imbere'**.


Ikibi conyene c’iyi nzira ni uko kubera ko iyi ari urubuga gusa rufise ikimenyetso, ntuzoronka uburyo ubwo ari bwo bwose bwo kumenyesha. Naho bamwebamwe bobona ko ivyo ari vyiza!

### Umucukumbuzi w'urubuga


Ntushobora vy’ukuri gukora nabi n’uburyo bwo guhitamwo imbere y’igihe, Vanadium. Iyi app ikora nk’iyindi browser yose ya telefone ngendanwa nagerageza kandi nta na rimwe nagira ingorane zo guhuza.


Ku bihe ukeneye gushika ku mbuga za Tor `.onion`, ushobora gukuraho APK y’Umucukumbuzi wa Tor uhereye ku rubuga rwabo (https://www.torproject.org/download/#android) canke biciye kuri F-Droid.


### VPNs


Kugira ngo ukinge igikorwa cawe co kuri Internet ku muntu aguha interineti (ISP), porogarama y’urubuga rwihariye (VPN) ni uburyo bwiza. VPN yohereza uruja n’uruza rwawe rwa internet mu mugende w’ibanga kuri IP Address isangiwe igenzurwa n’uwutanga serivisi ya VPN kugira ngo umenye neza ko igikorwa c’igikoresho cawe kidashobora guhuzwa nawe.


Aha hariho uburyo bubiri bwemewe bugufasha kwishura igikorwa muri Bitcoin ata makuru y’ibanga utanga. Ivyo vyose biraboneka kuri F-Droid.

### Ubutumwa


Mu myaka ya vuba aha, inyishu z’ubutumwa bufise amajambo y’ibanga zarabaye nyinshi cane. Ikibazo kiracariho naho, urashobora kugira option nziza kandi y’ibanga cane ishizwe kuri telefone yawe, ariko nimba ata contacts ufise ziyikoresha, n’ikihe ciza?


Abantu benshi batagira inyungu mu kibanza c’ubuzima bwite, birashoboka ko bakoresha WhatsApp canke iMessage. Ivya mbere bishobora gukurwa kuri Aurora Store ariko ivya nyuma ntibizokora kuri GrapheneOS (biragaragara!).



- [Signal](https://signal.org/) ni imwe mu ntumwa zizwi cane zifise amakuru y’iherezo (E2EE) zifise urutonde rukomeye rw’ibintu n’ibintu vyinshi. Signal isaba inomero ya telefone kugira ngo umuntu yiyandikishe, rero nimba utegura kuyaga n’abantu woshima ko batamenya inomero yawe ya telefone, kumbure nurondere uburyo bumwebumwe bwo kubigira. Ikimenyetso kigomba gukurwako biciye ku iduka rya Aurora.
- [Simplex]() ni intumwa nshasha cane ya E2EE. Nta ID y’ukoresha ifise, nta nomero ya telefone canke amakuru y’ibanga isaba. Abantu baragusanga mu gucapura kode yawe bwite ya QR canke mu gusura link yawe yihariye. Simplex kandi iremesha abakoresha bateye imbere gukoresha server yabo kugira ngo barushirize kugabanya kwizigira ikigo cose gihurikiye hamwe. Simplex nta mukiriya w'ibiro, rero bishobora kutaba vyiza iyo ibikoresho vyinshi biri ku rutonde rwawe rw'imbere. Simplex ya Android iraboneka kuri F-Droid.
- [Threema](https://threema.ch/ru/faq/libre_installation) itanga ubumenyi busa n’ubwo Simplex, ariko imaze igihe kirekire kandi nk’inyishu, yumva isukuye gatoyi. Threema si ubuntu, uruhusha rwo gukoresha ubuzima bwose rugura amadolari 4.99 kandi ushobora kugura na Bitcoin. Threema itanga umukiriya w’urubuga n’ibikoresho vy’ibiro vy’akavukire. Iyi porogarama ya Android iraboneka kuri F-Droid.
- [Telegram FOSS](Telegram FOSS] ni FOSS Fork idasanzwe ya porogaramu yemewe ya Telegram ya Android. Telegram ifise 'ibiganiro vy'ibanga' vya E2EE, ariko uburyo busanzwe si ubw'ibanga. Itelegaramu FOSS ishobora gukurwa kuri F-Droid.

## Ivyiza


Inkomoko yuguruye n’ubundi buryo bwo kuzigama ubuzima bwite ku bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none wamenyereye gukoresha ni vyinshi, kandi bimwe muri vyo akenshi biraruta inkomoko yugarijwe, ubundi buryo bukoreshwa n’ubutasi.


Ariko iyo wimukiye kuri GrapheneOS, hariho ivyiza vy’ibiremwa utegerezwa kureka kubera ko ata yindi nzira. Ivyo birimwo:



- Apple CarPlay/Android Auto** - Uzokenera kwizirika ku nzira nziza ya kera ya Bluetooth, USB canke Aux.
- Apple/Google Pay** - Hafi umuntu wese atwara Wallet yiwe uko biri kwose!
- Banking apps** - Si uko ivyo bidakora na gato. Bamwebamwe barabigira, mu vy’ukuri ata gatotsi. Abandi bakora gusa Google Play Services zifunguye (soma ibindi kuri ivyo aha hepfo) abandi na bo ntibakora na gato. Soma raporo iri kuri banki yawe [hano](https://privsec.dev/posts/android/ibikoresho-vy’amabanki-bihuye-n’ama-grapheneos/) kugira ngo ubone uko urukino rumeze ubu. Ntutinye nimba ivyawe biri kuri list idakora, wibuke ko ushobora kubika URL nk’urubuga ku rubuga rwawe rw’inzu.
- Amatangazo yo gusunika** - Porogaramu nyinshi zigurungikira amakuru mashasha iyo udakoresha porogaramu yihariye zizobikora biciye ku bikorwa vya Google Play. Ivyo ntibishirwaho na GrapheneOS, rero niwasanga utamenyeshwa ubwo nyene iyo umugenzi wawe akurungikiye email, birashoboka ko ari co gituma. Inkuru nziza ni uko zimwe muri izo porogarama twavuze haruguru zashizeho uburyo bwazo bwo gukorana n’inyuma kugira ngo zihora zigenzura ko hariho ivyahinduwe hanyuma ziguhe itangazo aho bisabwa .


### Google Play mu gasandugu k'umusenyi


**Urashobora kubona ko:** GrapheneOS ifise ubuhinga bwo gukorana na Layer butanga uburenganzira bwo gushiramwo no gukoresha ibisohoka vyemewe vya Google Play mu sandbox ya app isanzwe. Google Play nta n’umwe ironka uburenganzira budasanzwe canke uburenganzira kuri GrapheneOS bitandukanye n’uguca mu sandugu y’umusenyi ya app no ​​kuronka uburenganzira bwinshi cane.


Niwabona ko udashobora kubaho ata makuru y’ivyo bikoresho ukunda canke ko porogarama imwe imwe ‘utegerezwa kugira’ ataco imaze ata Serivisi zo Gukina, GrapheneOS iraguha uburenganzira bwo [gushiramwo] izo serivisi mu kibanza gifise ubushobozi bwo gukina. Iyo zimaze gushirwamwo, izo serivisi ntizisaba konti ya Google kugira ngo zikore, kandi uburenganzira bwa buri imwe burashobora kugenzurwa cane.


Imbere y’uko wihutira gushiramwo ivyo ku musi wa 1, ndaguhimiriza ngo urabe ingene ushika kure utabifise. Kumbure uzotangazwa n’ingene amaporogarama menshi akora neza ataco akora.


Niba ushaka kubishiramwo, ushobora gufyonda kuri porogaramu 'Apps' yashizwemwo mbere hanyuma ukurikiwe na 'Google Play Services'. Niwiyumvire kubishira iruhande y’izo porogarama zitari nyinshi z’ibanga udashobora kubaho udafise, imbere mu vyo ukoresha bitandukanye rwose kugira ngo ushireho iyo Layer y’inyongera y’ugutandukanya n’ibindi bice vya telefone yawe.

### Umwirondoro


GrapheneOS ishobora kugufasha kugira ubumenyi butandukanye bwo kuri telefone, muri telefone yawe. Ibindi bimenyetso birashobora gushiramwo porogarama zavyo n’ibikorwa vyavyo kandi ntibishobora gushika ku madosiye canke amakuru yose ava kuri konti ya nyen’iyo konti.

Niba ufise imwe canke zibiri gusa muri izo zitegerezwa kuba zifise porogarama zisaba Play Services, ariko zikoreshwa gake cane, gushiramwo izo iruhande ya Play Services mu rutonde rwitandukanye vyoshobora kuba iciyumviro ciza co kurushiriza gukomeza ingaruka z’ubuzima bwite z’umunota wose zisigaye mu kuzikoresha mu rutonde rwa nyirayo.


Ushobora gusoma vyinshi ku bijanye n’iyi nkuru y’ikoreshwa [hano](https://kuganira.grapheneos.org/d/168-ivyiyumviro-vy’imirongo-y’abakoresha/2).


Niwafata ingingo yo kwongerako urutonde rwitandukanye kugira ngo rujane n’ingene ukoresha, porogarama [Insular](https://f-droid.org/ru/packages/com.oasisfeng.island.fdroid/) yoshobora kuba ngirakamaro kuri wewe. Insular iragufasha guhindura mu buryo bworoshe porogaramu zawe zose zisanzwe zija ku rubuga rushasha ataco ukeneye guca mu nzira za kera zo gushiramwo zavuzwe mbere muri iyi nkuru. Insular kandi iraguha uburenganzira bwo 'gufunga' ningoga iyo porogarama yose kugira ngo uzimye burundu ibikorwa vyose vy'inyuma vy'iyo porogarama kugira ngo ntibikore.

### E-Sims


Niba ushaka gutwara ubuzima bwite bwa telefone yawe ku rundi rwego kandi ukagira ubuhinga bwa telefone ngendanwa butandukanye n’akaranga kawe k’isi nyakuri, eSIM yoshobora kuba ari yo yawe. eSIM ni SIM y’ubuhinga bwa none ushobora kugura kuri internet ukayishira kuri telefone yawe ukoresheje kode ya QR. Amashirahamwe atanga izo nsiguro zishobora kwishurwa ata wuzi umuntu akoresheje Bitcoin harimwo [Silent.Link](https://silent.link/) na [Bitrefill](https://www.bitrefill.com/gb/ru/esims/).


eSIMs ntizikwiye kurabwa nk’umuti wuzuye wo gukingira ubuzima bwite bwa telefone. Bishobora kuba igikoresho c'ingirakamaro iyo biri mu maboko y'iburyo, ariko ndagusavye ukore ubushakashatsi bwawe ku [bisubizo](https://grapheneos.org/faq#cellular-tracking) vyo gukoresha ubwoko bwose bw'ibikorwa vy'amatelefone nimba umugambi wawe ari ukugenda 'kuva ku rubuga'.


Ibikorwa vy’ugukina vy’umusenyi bitegerezwa gushirwaho kugira ngo eSIM iboneke muri GrapheneOS.


## Ububiko


Amaze kuronka telefone yawe nshasha ya de-Google'd Pixel, ni vyiza ko wikorera ububiko. Iyi backup izotuma ushobora gusubiza telefone mu buryo bumwe mu gihe woba watakaje telefone yawe canke ikabura/yibwa.


Ushobora guhitamwo kubika dosiye y'ububiko ku buryo bwose bwo kubika hanze, canke ku muti w'igicu wiyakira nka Nextcloud, naho abakoresha bamwebamwe bavuga ingero zitandukanye z'uguterimbere n'uburyo bwa nyuma.


Kugira ngo ureme ububiko bwawe bwambere:


1. Genda kuri **Imiterere** > **System** > **Backup**, hanyuma wandike kode yawe y’amajambo 12 yo gusubizaho. Iyi kode irakenewe kugira ngo ushobore gukuraho dosiye y’ububiko mu nyuma. Gutakaza kode, gutakaza uburenganzira bwo gukoresha backup ya telefone yawe.

2. Hanyuma uhitemwo aho uzobika. Nzobabwira ngo mukoreshe USB yo hanze canke ikarita microSD y’urugero rw’inganda.

3. Hitamwo amakuru uzoshiramwo. Niba ufise umwanya ku bubiko bwawe, noguhanura guhitamwo vyose.

4. Kanda ku tudodo dutatu n hejuru iburyo, hanyuma uhitemwo **Backup now**.
