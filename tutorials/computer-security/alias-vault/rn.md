Ubuzima bwite n’umutekano wo kuri Internet ni ikiganiro umuntu wese, ataravye ubucuruzi bwiwe, akwiye kwitwararika cane.



Ivyo bibazo, n’ikindi, ni igice c’isi iguma itera umuvurungano: abahinguzi benshi bariko baragira uruhara mu ciyumviro, bazana ugushirwa mu ngiro ku mirongo yashinzwe n’ibintu bishasha.



Ivyo ni ko biri kuri **Leendert de Borst** na `Alias ​​Vault` yiwe, igikoresho c’ihinduka (ica mbere mu bwoko bwaco) kigufasha gucunga no kubika amajambo y’ibanga, gukoresha amakuru y’ibanga kugira ngo wemeze ku bikorwa vy’urubuga, gutanga ukwemeza kw’ibintu bibiri, ariko ikiruta vyose generate nyayo yose _W_alia.



**Ariko Alias ​​Vault ntihagarara aho**.



## Ibirango vy'ingenzi



Alias ​​Vault ikora mu gicu ku maserukira y’umuhinguzi canke yishingira mu bikorwa remezo vyayo bwite, uburyo amadosiye ya Docker n’ishusho bishobora gushirwamwo n’igikoresho citwa scipt. Uretse urubuga Interface, hariho n’ibindi bikoresho vyo gukoresha mu gukoresha amaporogarama yose azwi cane, be n’amaporogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa yo kuri iOS na Android; iyo ya nyuma na yo nyene ishobora gukurwa kuri F-Droid, ikarenga ububiko bwa Google buzwi.



Mu nzu imwe ya Interface y'ibanga ni:




- Inkomoko y'ubuntu kandi yuguruye**
- Ijambobanga Umuyobozi**, kugira ngo ubike amajambobanga yose agoranye. Ukoresheje ukwagura umucukumbuzi, umucungerezi w'ijambobanga araheza kwinjira ku mbuga
- 2FA**, gushigikira ivyemezo bibiri
- Umuyobozi w'amazina y'ibanga afise server ya imeyili yinjijwe**: Alias ​​Vault ntirema amazina y'ibanga yohereza imeyili ku gasandugu k'ubutumwa k'ukoresha; ahubwo, irema alter-egos nyayo, yuzuye izina ry’imbere, izina ry’umuryango, igitsina, izina ry’ukoresha, ijambobanga, n’umusi w’amavuko (niba ayo makuru asabwa).



Ivyanditswe vyinshi kandi vyimbitse biri muri iyo nkuru, izoherekeza abashasha mu kuvumbura ico gikoresho gikomeye.



## Nta makuru y’ibanga!



Bitangura, nk'uko vyama, ku rubuga [aliasvault.net](aliasvault.net). Nk’uko vyavuzwe, Alias ​​Vault ishobora gukoreshwa kuri server y’umuntu bwite, canke ivuye mu gicu c’umuhinguzi kugira ngo ayimenye imbere yo kwimukira ku muti wikorera.



Urubuga rufise vy’ukuri ibishushanyo bikwegera amaso kandi vyitaweho neza, ariko ivyiza biraza iyo utanguye kurushiramwo ibiganza: **rema konti yawe**.



![img](assets/en/01.webp)



Uzotangazwa cane n’uko Alias ​​Vault idasaba amakuru y’ibanga: ivyo ukeneye vyose kugira ngo ureme iyo konti ni izina ry’ibanga iryo ari ryo ryose, ijambo umenyereye, igihe cose ririho. Wemere Amabwirizwa y’Ikoreshwa, uhitemwo ijambo, hanyuma ubandanye.



![img](assets/en/02.webp)



Tegura **`ijambobanga ry'ingenzi`** ubu, ari ryo makuru ahambaye kuruta ayandi yose muri sisitemu yawe nshasha yose. Ukoresheje iri jambobanga rimwe, mu vy’ukuri, uzoba ari wewe wenyene ushobora gushika/gusubiza iyo konti, kuko izotuma `ububiko` bwawe buguma bushizwe mu mfuruka kuri server izokwakira amakuru yawe.



![img](assets/en/03.webp)



Ukuri: Waremeje umucungerezi w'ijambobanga ryawe n'umucungerezi w'izina ry'ibanga, ariko utatanga imeyili yawe bwite ikora, imeyili y'ibanga Address.



![img](assets/en/04.webp)



Alias ​​Vault iraguha ikaze mu kibanza gitekanye, gishasha, c’umuntu ku giti ciwe ariko kandi kirimwo ubusa. Kandi ubu turatangura kuyizuza gatoyi.



Niba usanzwe ufise umucungerezi w’ijambobanga, urashobora kwinjiza dosiye muri iyo ukoresha, kugira ngo usuzume itandukaniro n’uwundi mutanga, canke kumbure ukureho umucungerezi w’izina ry’ibanga kugira ngo ushobore gucunga vyose muri porogarama imwe.



![img](assets/en/05.webp)



Alias ​​Vault ni yoroshe cane: ufise urupapuro rumwe rw'ingenzi, ari rwo `Ingoro`, rufise ibikubiyemo bibiri:




- `Ivyemezo`: bigufasha kurema no gucunga akaranga n'amazina y'ibanga
- `Email`: akazu k'ubutumwa aho ushobora gusuzuma ubutumwa bwinjira ku mazina y'ibanga washizeho.



![img](assets/en/06.webp)



Ni uguhingura `izina ry'ibanga` rya mbere dushaka gukora, rero genda hejuru iburyo bwa paji ukande _+Izina ry'ibanga rishasha_.



![img](assets/en/07.webp)



Mu ntango, ivyo kurya bisa n’ibito cane, nk’uko filozofiya ya Alias ​​Vault ibivuga. Kugira ngo ubone ibiranga iyi nzira, kanda kuri _Rema biciye mu buryo buteye imbere_.



![img](assets/en/08.webp)



Igice co hejuru c’ibarabara rya mbere, urashobora kugikoresha kugira ngo ushiremwo amakuru y’ijambobanga usanzwe ukoresha ku bikorwa ufise abonema, canke ukoresha kenshi kuri Internet.



Niba warashoboje kwemeza ibintu bibiri kuri vyose (canke vyose) muri ivyo bikorwa biri hejuru, ukoresheje Alias ​​Vault ushobora kandi gucunga iyi Layer y'inyongera y'umutekano mu kwinjiza `urufunguzo rw'ibanga`. Alias ​​Vault izokora `TOTP` ikenewe kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kuronka.



![img](assets/en/09.webp)



**Iciyumviro**: Mu kibanza cabikiwe imeyili Address, Alias ​​Vault itanga urwego rwayo bwite ku buryo busanzwe; kugira ngo ukoreshe Address ibereye wakoresheje amakonti, kanda kuri _Enter custom domain_, kugira ngo ushobore gushinga domain ibereye inyuma ya `@`.



![img](assets/en/14.webp)



Igice co hasi c’iyi skrini ni co giteye umunezero kuruta ibindi vyose. Fyonda kuri _Generate Random Alias_ maze Alias ​​Vault izogukorera akaranga gatandukanye ku bikenewe bitandukanye, imwe yose ifise isandugu yayo y’ubutumwa, amajambo y’ibanga y’urugero akomeye cane, yongeweko n’ibindi bisobanuro nk’igitsina, itariki y’amavuko, n’izina ry’ibanga ryiza.



![img](assets/en/10.webp)



Ukoresheje menyu ihuye, urashobora guhindura amasetingi agira ico akoze ku guhingura ijambobanga, nko guhitamwo inyuguti ntoya gusa no gukuraho inyuguti zishobora kuba zidasobanutse.



![img](assets/en/11.webp)



Ushobora gukoresha imeyili y'ibanga ivugwa na Alias ​​Vault, canke ugahindura ivyicaro niwafyonda ku rutonde ruja hasi.



![img](assets/en/12.webp)



Imbere y’uko ukoresha iyi imeyili ku bijanye n’injira, urashobora kugerageza ingene ikora mu kohereza ubutumwa buva kuri Address yawe bwite ku gasandugu k’ubutumwa bushasha.

## Ukwagura umucukumbuzi

![img](assets/en/15.webp)



Irashiramwo nk’uko mwamaze kubikora ku bindi bikoresho vyose, rero sinzoguma kuri ivyo bintu.



Ukwaguka kw’umucukumbuzi kuriho kugira ngo vyorohe kwinjira mu bikorwa vyo kuri interineti, canke guhingura amazina mashasha nk’uko bikenewe: iyo iyo serivisi ibitswe mu nyandiko zawe za Alias ​​Vault, ukuzuza kwikora gukora ivyo bikenewe.



![img](assets/en/17.webp)



Ico twokwirinda ni ukugenzura ko Alias ​​Vault ikora. Nkako, iyo porogarama irafise uburyo busanzwe aho ihagarara inyuma y’igihe kanaka idakora. Ivyo ni ikintu c’ingirakamaro cane, **igihe ubwirizwa guca kure ya mudasobwa yawe, nk’akarorero, ukabuza uwundi muntu kwinjira muri konti zawe**. Uburyo bubereye buzotuma usubira kwinjira winjiza `ijambobanga ry'ingenzi`, nimba igice c'imbere kikiri mu bubiko. Igihe co gusohoka ni kimwe mu bintu ushobora guhindura, ukagigabanya canke ukagirekura bivanye n’ivyo ukunda.



## Porogaramu ya telefone ngendanwa

Igihe iki kinyamakuru candika (mu mpera z’ukwezi kwa munani 2025), iyo porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa ibona ko `auto-fill` ari ikintu co kugerageza, ntikora kiretse ku mbuga nke cane. Gushika bishizwe mu ngiro, gukoresha Alias ​​Vault kuri telefone ngendanwa bisaba gukopa/gushiramwo amakuru.



Uhejeje gukura app mu bubiko, kugira ngo winjiremwo uzokwinjira gusa user na `master password` vyakozwe kuri app y'urubuga.

## Ukwiyakira: kugenzura amakuru yawe yose



Alias ​​Vault, kugira ngo tuvuge ukuri, si `umucungerezi w'ijambobanga` wa mbere ashobora kugufasha gukoresha iyo serivisi ku bikorwa remezo vyawe. Hariho n’izindi, ariko zimwe zifise aho zigarukira canke zifise igice c’inkomoko yugaye.



Akaryo ni akadasanzwe: **hagarika kwizigira abatanga serivisi bo hanze canke ibicu, ariko ukoreshe server yawe bwite yo mu karere kugira ngo urinde kandi ucunge amajambo y’ibanga, amazina y’ibanga, n’amakuru y’agaciro cane ajanye navyo vyose**. Na Alias ​​Vault ushobora kandi kugira imeyili y'iposita kuri server yawe bwite ya imeyili kugira ngo ubone ibanga ryongereweko.



Igihe kirageze co guhindukira kuri [inyandiko](https://inyandiko.aliasvault.net/gushiraho/), kugira ngo umenye ingene woja ku kwiyakira Alias ​​Vault.



![img](assets/en/23.webp)



Alias ​​Vault ikoresha Docker Compose, rero ubumenyi bukeyi kuri Linux na Docker burakenewe. Ushobora gutangura n’ugushiraho ivy’ishimikiro hanyuma ugaheza ugaheza ukoresheje inyishu ziteye imbere.



Server yawe itegerezwa kuba ikoresha imashini y’ibice 64, ifise ubuhinga bwa Linux, 1 GB ya RAM n’ububiko bwa 16 GB; verisiyo ya Docker (CE) itegerezwa kuba nibura 20.10 canke irenga, mu gihe Docker Compose isaba gusohoka kuva kuri 2.0 gushika hejuru.



Nafashe ingingo yo kugerageza Alias ​​Vault n'umukiriya mutoyi, aho DietPi ishizweho nk'ugukwirakwiza, urufatiro rwa Debian Bookworm, rwongerewe ku bintu vy'ingenzi kandi rusanzwe rukoresha `Docker` na `Docker Compose`.



Mbere, kugira ngo ugire urutonde, rema ububiko mu nzu yawe, ufungure `terminal` hanyuma ushireko itegeko ryo gukoresha inyandiko yo gushiramwo.



```bash
curl -L -o install.sh https://github.com/lanedirt/AliasVault/releases/latest/download/install.sh
```



![img](assets/en/24.webp)



![img](assets/en/25.webp)



Igihe installation izoba yarangiye, uzosanga ivyemezo vyawe vyo kwinjiramwo:


```
Admin Panel: https://localhost/admin

Password: yyy0xyx1yxy2zxx4
```



Suzuma ibirimwo mu bubiko inyuma yo gushiramwo.



![img](assets/en/29.webp)



Kugira ngo utangure Alias ​​Vault koresha itegeko:



``` Gutanguza-Izina ry'Ububiko


./shiraho.sh gutangura


```

Alias Vault adesso gira sul tuo server personale.

![img](assets/en/30.webp)

Apri un browser e digita l'url: ti comparirà la pagina per fare login come `Admin` di Alias Vault.

![img](assets/en/26.webp)

Il più è fatto! Hai davanti a te il pannello per amministrare Alias Vault in maniera personalizzata.

![img](assets/en/27.webp)

Da questa interfaccia, potrai controllare quanti e quali utenti stanno utilizzando il servizio, limitarne l'uso, cancellare gli utenti (azione irreversibile) e soprattutto controllare tutti i `log`.

Se si tratta di una nuova installazione, non avrai altri utenti oltre ad `admin`; per crearne di nuovi, apri un'altra `tab` del browser e digita:

```


umushitsi/umukoresha/intango


```

![img](assets/en/28.webp)

Da qui in poi, tutto procede come abbiamo visto in precedenza nella guida, con la differenza che adesso stai usando Alias Vault sul tuo server. Se la tua macchina è adeguatamente configurata e protetta, questo è un modo elegante e sicuro di gestire un tuo password e alias manager, senza servizi di terze parti.

Per fermare Alias Vault, torna al terminale e digita:

``` Stop-Alias-Vault
./install.sh stop

```

## Ivyiyumviro ku bijanye no gushiramwo amakuru n'umutekano



![img](assets/en/32.webp)



Dushingiye kuvyo Lanedirt avuga ku rubuga, mu nyandiko, no kuri Github, na Alias ​​Vault **amakuru yose (ibice) ushira kuri Alias ​​Vault, aguma aboshe cane ku gikoresho, apfutse kandi ntashobora gushikirwa n’umuntu wese atazi `ijambobanga ry’ingenzi`**.



Ijambobanga ry'umukuru` ni co kintu nyamukuru c'ububiko bwose. Inyuma y’aho yinjiye, ikoreshwa n’ubuhinga bwa `Argon2id`, ubuhinga bwo gukuraho urufunguzo rwo kwibuka Hard, kugira ngo ibanga ntirive muri ico gikoresho.



Ivyo vyose biguma vyihishijwe mbere no ku gicu canke ku muyobozi w’ibikorwa vyo kwakira abashitsi. Nkako, uhereye ku rubuga rwa admin ntushobora gushika ku makuru y’abakoresha, ushobora kumenya gusa nimba baremye amazina y’uruyeri, baronse ama email, n’ibindi bike.



Ibirimwo vyose bishizwe mu mfuruka kandi bikakurwa n'imfunguruzo z'ibanga zikomoka ku `ijambobanga ry'ingenzi`. **Amakuru yashizwemwo amakuru gusa ni yo abikwa kuri server, ntaco kiboneka mu nyandiko rusangi**. Iyo umukoresha yibagiwe canke atakaje `ijambobanga ryiwe ry'ingenzi`, konti ihuye nayo irazimangana ataco ihinduye, kuko server idashobora gushika ku birimwo mu nyandiko rusangi.



Ku bijanye n'ivyo kwikorera hariho inyandiko yo gusubiramwo `ijambobanga ry'umukuru`, ariko ivyo ntibibuza amakuru gutakaza.



![img](assets/en/33.webp)



Kubera ko Alias ​​Vault iri mu rwego rwa _Beta_, ushobora kugorwa no kuyironka iyo uhinduye/uhinduye ijambobanga ry'ibanze. Ndagusavye uhitemwo ikomeye kandi igoye kuva mu ntango kugira ngo ushobore kugerageza iyo serivisi maze amaherezo uhitemwo nimba uzoyikoresha. Niba ufise ingorane zo gushika ku gicu ukurikije uguhindura ijambobanga, usabwe kubwira abafasha ba Alias ​​Vault.



Kugira ngo utahure neza ubwubatsi n’umutekano vyemewe na Alias ​​Vault, ndagusavye cane ko uraba [iyi paji](https://docs.aliasvault.net/architecture/), irimwo amakuru y’ido n’ido y’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru bushingiye ku mikorere yayo.



## Ikarata y'inzira


Intumbero z’abayiteguye ni ugutuma Alias ​​Vault ikura kandi igumaho mu mpera z’umwaka wa 2025, kugira ngo ishobore gusobanura ibiranga ikoreshwa ryayo muri kazoza.



Alias ​​Vault ni kandi izokwama iguma ari inkomoko yuguruye kandi y’ubuntu, ariko kumbure si ata n’aho igarukira nk’uko biri muri beta. Hariho ibintu vyishyurwa biriko birashirwa mu ngiro, nk’uko vyamaze gutangazwa.



Hariho imigambi y’umugwi/umuryango n’ugushigikira imfunguruzo z’ibikoresho, iya nyuma ni iyo kwemeza ko ari FIDO2 canke WebAuth.



## Ninde akeneye izina ry'ububiko



**Igikoresho nk'iki ni ciza ku muntu wese ashira imbere ubuzima bwite bwo kuri internet**.



Akaranga kawe ni, mu buryo bwose, mu mutima w’ubucuruzi ukora kuri internet kandi butegerezwa kurindwa n’uburyo bwose kugira ngo ushire **ayo** makuru kure y’ibikorwa, amashirahamwe n’abacuruzi biteguye gukora ikintu cose kugira ngo bashobore gufata amaboko yabo ku nyifato yawe yo kuri internet.



Alias ​​Vault iragusubiza ububasha bwose ku makuru yawe, igabanya canke ikuraho burundu ivy’ukwizigira abandi bantu kugira ngo bakurinde kandi bashire amakuru yawe ahambaye cane.



Ubwubatsi bwa Alias ​​Vault n’ubuhinga bwo gukora amakuru y’ibanga ni vyiza ku bantu ku giti cabo, ubucuruzi buto n’ubuciriritse, imigwi y’iterambere, n’abantu bose bakunda gukoresha **inkomoko yuguruye**. Niwaba uri muri ivyo bice vyose: n’uryoherwe no kuvumbura Alias ​​Vault.
