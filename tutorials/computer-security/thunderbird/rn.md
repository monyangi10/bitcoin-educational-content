Thunderbird ni porogaramu yo gukoresha ubutumwa kuri e-mail izwi cane kubera ubushobozi bwayo bwo guhindura ibintu n’ibintu vyinshi. Itanga ubuhinga bwiza, bworoshe gukoresha kandi cane cane Interface. Izwi cane kubera ko idahinduka, ni imwe mu nzira zizwi cane zo gukoresha ubutumwa kuri e-mail ku isoko. Ingingo ihambaye yo kwibuka: ni ubuntu rwose.



## Ni kuki ukoresha Inkuba?





- Ubuntu kandi bufunguye**: bitandukanye n’ibikoresho vyinshi vyo gutanga ubutumwa, Thunderbird ni ubuntu rwose. Ariko rero, urashobora gushigikira umuryango w’abahinguzi mu kubarungikira intererano.
- Intuitive Interface kandi yoroshe kwiga**: Ivyo Thunderbird ikora, ahanini, biragororotse, rero ntubwirizwa kubihagarika umutima.
- Ibereye ubwoko bwinshi bwa **e-mail**: yaba Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook canke mbere e-mail z’ubudandaji zijanye n’izina ry’indangarubuga, iyi sandugu y’ubutumwa irashobora gukoreshwa mu buryo bwinshi.
- Multi-account**: Thunderbird ishobora kugufasha kwifatanya na konti nyinshi z’ubutumwa bwa e-mail icarimwe, bikaba bigufasha kuronka ubutumwa bwawe bwose.
- Ibintu bikora neza cane n’ubushobozi bwo guhindura**: ku buryo busanzwe, Thunderbird itanga ibintu vyinshi vyo gucunga amakonti yawe ya e-mail n’ikirangamisi c’ibintu. Ariko kandi, biragufasha kwongerako ibindi bihitamwo kubera ama extensions menshi ariho.
- Ivyuma vyinshi**: Thunderbird iboneka ku bikoresho bitandukanye (Android, Windows, Linux, macOS), biguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ubutumwa bwawe bwo kuri interineti.
- Umutekano**: Thunderbird ni porogaramu itekanye cane, ishobora kugufasha kwungukira ku gukingira amakuru kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera bishingiye kuri RSA canke ECC (Elliptic Curve) kugira ngo ushobore kumenya ibanga ry’amakuru yawe.



## Gukuraho no gushiramwo



### Gukurura


Kugira ngo ushiremwo Thunderbird, ukeneye gukura iyo porogarama ku [urubuga rwemewe](https://www.thunderbird.net/). Zirikana ko iyo porogarama iboneka ku bikoresho bitandukanye. Ariko rero, verisiyo y’imbere izoba ari yo izohita imenyekana n’amakuru atangwa n’umucukumbuzi wawe.



![0_01](assets/fr/01.webp)



Kugira ngo ubone verisiyo yihariye ya Thunderbird, genda kuri [iyi paji](https://www.inkuba.net/an/inyoni y’inkuba/yose/). Hitamwo ururimi rwawe, ushireho uburyo bwo gukoresha n’ubwubatsi bwa processeur, hanyuma utangure gukuraho.



### Gushiramwo



**Ubwoko bwo gushiramwo:**



Kuri Windows, ukoreshe executable yavanwe kugira ngo utangure gushiramwo. Fyonda kuri "Ibikurikira" kugira ngo ukomeze. Hariho uburyo bubiri bwo guhitamwo:




- Standard**: yemera gushiramwo ataco uhinduye.
- Custom**: ishobora kugufasha guhitamwo dosiye yo gushiramwo n'uko wokora canke utakora inzira ngufi zo ku biro.



![0_02](assets/fr/02.webp)



![0_03](assets/fr/03.webp)



**Gutangura gushiramwo:**



Kugira ngo utangure gushiramwo, fyonda ku buto "**Update**" canke "**Install**", hanyuma urindire gushika igikorwa kirangiye.



![0_04](assets/fr/04.webp)



![0_05](assets/fr/05.webp)



Rindira ko igikorwa co gushiramwo kirangira, hanyuma utangure porogaramu



![0_06](assets/fr/06.webp)



**Injira muri konti yawe:**



Thunderbird ishigikira ubwoko butandukanye bw’ubutumwa bwo kuri interineti. Kugira ngo winjire ku ncuro ya mbere, genda ushireho konti hanyuma uhitemwo Email.



![0_07](assets/fr/07.webp)



Idirisha rizofunguka kugira ngo ushobore kwinjiza amakuru yawe. Injira izina ryose rizogaragara mu gasandugu kawe k’ubutumwa, hamwe n’ikete ryawe ryose ry’ubutumwa Address, hanyuma ushire mu ngiro.



![0_08](assets/fr/08.webp)



**Ihitamwo rya porotokole n'imiterere y'ihuriro:**



Uzosabwa guhitamwo uburyo bwo gutanga ubutumwa bujanye n’ivyo ukeneye. Akenshi, IMAP izohagije. Ku miterere yihariye, ushobora guhindura imiterere canke ugakanda "Ibikurikira".



![0_09](assets/fr/09.webp)



Idirisha rishasha rizofunguka, rigufashe kwifatanya n’agasanduku kawe k’ubutumwa ukoresheje ijambobanga ryawe ry’injira n’ijambobanga ryawe. Wemerere Thunderbird kwinjira muri konti yawe, hanyuma ukande OK.



![0_10](assets/fr/10.webp)



Ushobora kandi gukoresha ibindi bikoresho nk’igitabu ca Address be n’ikirangamisi, canke ukabikuraho imbere y’uko ukomeza.



![0_11](assets/fr/11.webp)



![0_12](assets/fr/12.webp)



Iyo umaze kuyishiraho, konti yawe izoca yitegura ubwo nyene, biguhe uburenganzira bwo kuronka ama e-mail yawe yose. Uzoshobora kohereza, kwakira, kubika canke gufuta ubutumwa bwawe bitagoranye.

## Vumbura inyoni y'inkuba



### Uguhuzwa n'izindi konti


Thunderbird ntiyigarukira ku gucunga ubutumwa kuri e-mail gusa. Ushobora kandi kwongerako ibiganiro, amakuru n’amakonti y’imigwi. Kugira ngo wongereko konti nshasha, genda kuri settings ya konti, ukande kuri "New account" uhitemwo ubwoko ushaka. Uzuza amakuru asabwa, hanyuma wemeze kugira ngo uhuze.



![0_14](assets/fr/14.webp)



### Guhindura insanganyamatsiko


Thunderbird iragufasha guhindura insanganyamatsiko ya Interface. Kugira ngo ubikore, genda ku mirongo rusangi ya e-mail, ukande kuri "Ivyongeweko n'insanganyamatsiko", hanyuma uje ku gice ca "Insanganyamatsiko" kugira uhitemwo imwe mu nsanganyamatsiko zisanzwe ziriho.



![0_15](assets/fr/15.webp)



### Shiraho ibindi bikoresho canke insanganyamatsiko


Ushobora gukuraho izindi nsanganyamatsiko canke ibindi bikoresho kugira ngo wongereko ibintu bishasha kuri Thunderbird. Kugira ngo ubikore, genda kuri "Ibindi bice" kugira ngo ufungure ububiko bw'ivyagutse buzwi. Koresha umwanya wo gushakisha kugira uronke ikintu canke insanganyamatsiko yihariye.



![0_16](assets/fr/16.webp)



### Gutunganya ububiko kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera



Ugushiramwo amakuru kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera biratuma ushobora kwizigira ibanga ry’ubutumwa bwawe bwo kuri e-mail kandi ukabukingira kugira ngo ntihagire uwubufata. Kugira ngo uyitunganye, genda kuri settings ya konti yawe, genda ku gice ca "End-to-end encryption" hanyuma ukande kuri "Add key". Niba usanzwe ufise urufunguzo, urashobora kuruzana; nimba atarivyo, ureme uwundi mushasha. Muri ivyo, shiraho itariki y’iherezo (umwaka umwe gusa ni wo usabwa), uhitemwo ubwoko bw’urufunguzo n’ubunini bwarwo, hanyuma ushire mu ngiro.



Konti yawe ya Thunderbird izotunganirizwa bivanye n’ivyo ukeneye, ifise umutekano wo hejuru kugira ngo ukoreshe neza.



Muri make, Thunderbird ni umuti w’ubutumwa wuzuye, w’ubuntu kandi ushobora guhindurwa cane, uhuye n’ivyo abakoresha mu rugo n’abahinga bakeneye. Ukworohereza kwayo, guhuza n’ibikorwa vyinshi, ibintu biteye imbere n’umutekano wo hejuru bituma iba ihitamwo ryiza ryo gucunga neza ivy’itumanaho ryawe. Waba uriko urarondera igikoresho co kwizigirwa co gushiramwo amakonti menshi, gucungera amafaranga yawe canke gusa kwinovora ubumenyi bwiza bwa Interface, Thunderbird izoguha ivyo witega mu gihe izoguha umwidegemvyo n’uguhinduranya ivyo porogarama y’ubuntu izana.
