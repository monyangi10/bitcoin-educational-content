Thunderbird is an e-mail application renowned for its flexibility and wide range of features. It offers an elegant, easy-to-use and particularly intuitive Interface. Renowned for its stability, it is one of the most popular e-mail applications on the market. An important point to note: it's totally free.


## Why use Thunderbird?



- Free and open source**: unlike many messaging applications, Thunderbird is totally free. However, you can support the developer community by sending them a donation.
- Intuitive Interface and easy to learn**: Thunderbird's configuration is, for the most part, straightforward, so you don't have to worry about it.
- Suitable for several types of e-mail**: whether Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook or even business e-mails associated with a domain name, this mailbox is highly versatile.
- Multi-account**: Thunderbird lets you connect to several e-mail accounts at once, making it easy to access all your messages.
- High-performance features and scalability**: by default, Thunderbird offers a wide range of features for managing your e-mail accounts and events calendar. However, it also lets you add further options thanks to the many extensions available.
- Multi-platform**: Thunderbird is available on a variety of devices (Android, Windows, Linux, macOS), giving you easy access to your e-mails.
- Security**: Thunderbird is a highly secure application, allowing you to benefit from end-to-end encryption based on RSA or ECC (Elliptic Curve) to guarantee the confidentiality of your data.


## Download and installation


### Download

To install Thunderbird, you need to download the software from the [official site](https://www.thunderbird.net/). Note that the application is available for different operating systems. However, the default version will be the one automatically detected by the data provided by your browser.


![0_01](assets/fr/01.webp)


To download a specific version of Thunderbird, go to [this page](https://www.thunderbird.net/an/thunderbird/all/). Choose your language, target operating system and processor architecture, then launch the download.


### Installation


**Type of installation:**


On Windows, run the downloaded executable to start the installation. Click on "Next" to continue. Two options are available:


- Standard**: allows direct installation without prior configuration.
- Custom**: lets you choose the installation folder and whether or not to create desktop shortcuts.


![0_02](assets/fr/02.webp)


![0_03](assets/fr/03.webp)


**Launching the installation:**


To start installation, click on the "**Update**" or "**Install**" button, then wait until the process is complete.


![0_04](assets/fr/04.webp)


![0_05](assets/fr/05.webp)


Wait for the installation process to finish, then launch the application


![0_06](assets/fr/06.webp)


**Login to your account:**


Thunderbird supports different types of e-mail. To connect for the first time, go to set up an account and select Email.


![0_07](assets/fr/07.webp)


A window opens to allow you to enter your information. Enter the full name to be displayed in your mailbox, as well as your complete e-mail address, then validate.


![0_08](assets/fr/08.webp)


**Protocol selection and connection configuration:**


You will be prompted to choose the messaging protocol best suited to your needs. In most cases, IMAP will suffice. For specific configurations, you can modify the settings or simply click "Next".


![0_09](assets/fr/09.webp)


A new window will open, allowing you to connect to your mailbox using your login and password. Authorize Thunderbird to access your account, then click OK.


![0_10](assets/fr/10.webp)


You can also activate additional services such as the address book and calendar, or deselect them before continuing.


![0_11](assets/fr/11.webp)


![0_12](assets/fr/12.webp)


Once set up, your account will be automatically ready, giving you access to all your e-mails. You'll be able to send, receive, archive or delete your messages with ease.

## Discover Thunderbird


### Connection to additional accounts

Thunderbird isn't limited to e-mail management. You can also add chat, feeds and group accounts. To add a new account, go to the account settings, click on "New account" and choose the type you want. Fill in the required information, then validate to connect.


![0_14](assets/fr/14.webp)


### Change theme

Thunderbird lets you change the Interface theme. To do this, go to the general e-mail settings, click on "Add-ons and themes", then go to the "Themes" section to choose one of the available default themes.


![0_15](assets/fr/15.webp)


### Install additional plugins or themes

You can download additional themes or plugins to add new features to Thunderbird. To do this, go to "More modules" to open the official extensions store. Use the search field to find a specific plugin or theme.


![0_16](assets/fr/16.webp)


### Configuring end-to-end encryption


End-to-end encryption allows you to guarantee the confidentiality of your e-mails and protect them from interception. To configure it, go to your account settings, go to the "End-to-end encryption" section and click on "Add key". If you already have a key, you can import it; if not, create a new one. In this case, set the expiry date (one year maximum recommended), choose the key type and size, then validate.


Your Thunderbird account will be configured according to your needs, with a high level of security for optimal use.

Whether you're looking for a reliable tool for centralizing multiple accounts, securing your exchanges or simply enjoying a pleasant Interface experience, Thunderbird will meet your expectations while offering you the freedom and flexibility that free software brings.
