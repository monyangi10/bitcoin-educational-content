![cover](assets/cover.webp)



Ubuhinga bwa Android busanzwe bwashizwe kuri telefone zacu zitera ingorane zizwi cane: ugushiramwo cane ibikorwa vya Google bishikana ku gukurikirana amakuru, ibikorwa bitakenewe biterwa inkunga (bloatware) vyategetswe n'abahinguzi, no guheba gukurikirana ivyagezwe inyuma y'imyaka mikeyi, bigacira urubanza ibikoresho bikiriho.



LineageOS yiyerekana nk’inyishu nziza cane kuri izo ngorane. Igicuruzwa c’umuryango w’abakoresha ubuhinga bufunguye kandi kikaba ari co gikurikira CyanogenMod (yahagaritswe mu mpera z’umwaka wa 2016), LineageOS ni ubuhinga bwo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa bushingiye kuri Android ku buntu busubiza abakoresha mu kugenzura telefone zabo. Uwo mugambi watangujwe ku mugaragaro mu kwezi kwa kigarama 2016, ubu ufise amatelefone arenga miliyoni 4,4 kw’isi yose kandi ashigikira amajana y’amatelefone y’imigwi irenga 20 itandukanye.

## UmurongoOS ni iki?



### Filozofiya n'intumbero



LineageOS ni ubuhinga bwo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa bushingiye ku mugambi wa Android Open Source (AOSP), bwateguwe n’umuryango munini w’abanyamuryango b’intahemuka kw’isi yose. Icivugo cayo kitari co coshobora kuba "Igikoresho cawe, amategeko yawe": uwo mugambi ugamije kwagura ubuzima bw'amatelefone ngendanwa mu gihe utanga uburyo bwo gukoresha Android bubereye kandi bufise ubuzima bwite.



Uwo mugambi wavuye mu minyota ya CyanogenMod, imwe mu ROM za Android zizwi cane mu mateka. Igihe CyanogenMod Inc. yafunga imiryango yayo mu kwezi kwa kigarama 2016, umuryango warakoranije kugira ngo ureme LineageOS, ugumye impwemu y’ubuhinga bushasha n’ubuhinga bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bushasha bwari buranga uwuyibanjirije.



Udakunze OEM Android, LineageOS ntibanza gushiramwo porogarama za Google ku buryo busanzwe, kandi irakuraho burundu bloatware. Abakoresha batangura n’uburyo buke cane burimwo gusa ibikoresho vy’ingenzi (telefone, ubutumwa, kamera, umucukumbuzi) kandi barafise umwidegemvyo wo guhitamwo ivyo bongerako mu nyuma.



### Ingaruka n'abanyagihugu



Imibare yemewe yerekana urugero rw’uwo mugambi: n’ibikoresho birenga miliyoni 4,4 bikoreshwa mu bihugu 224, LineageOS iserukira imwe mu nzira za Android zikoreshwa cane kw’isi yose. Brezil ni yo irongoye inzira n’abakoresha barenga miliyoni 2, ikurikirwa n’Ubushinwa na Amerika, bikaba vyerekana ko Android y’ubuntu kandi ishobora guhindurwa uko umuntu ashaka ikwegera abantu bose.




## Ibirango nyamukuru



### Interface n'ubumenyi bw'abakoresha



**Pure Android**: LineageOS itanga ubumenyi nyakuri bwa Android hafi y’AOSP, ata n’ivyo abahinguzi bashizeko canke ibikoresho birengeje urugero. Interface iguma imenyerewe n’abakoresha Android mu gihe itanga ubuhinga bwiza cane kubera ko ata bloatware iriho.



**Google-free ku buryo busanzwe**: Nta serivisi za Google zibanza gushirwaho, kubera impamvu zijanye n'amategeko n'inyifato runtu. Ubu buryo "butagira Google" buratuma ushobora kugenzura amakuru yawe bwite kandi bugatuma ukora neza mu kwirinda ibikorwa bikoreshwa inyuma.



### Guhindura ibintu ku buryo bubereye n'umutekano



**Guhindura biteye imbere**: LineageOS ifungura amahitamwo menshi ataboneka kuri Android: gusubira gutunganya ubuto bwo kugenderamwo, insanganyamatsiko za sisitemu zishobora guhindurwa, imirongo y’ikoreshwa ihuye n’ibintu bitandukanye (akazi, umuntu ku giti ciwe, urukino).



**Outil Trust**: Ibikorwa vyinjijwe bigenzura uko umutekano w’igikoresho cawe uri kandi bikakumenyesha ibishobora gutera ubwoba, bikaguha ukuntu umutekano wa sisitemu yawe uboneka mu gihe nyaco.



**Ivyagezwe vyagutse**: Umuryango wa LineageOS wiyemeje gutanga ibikoresho vy’umutekano buri kwezi, bikaba vyemerera ibikoresho vyahagaritswe n’ababikora bishobora kubandanya bironka ibikoresho vy’umutekano vya Android bishasha.

### Ibikoresho bikundwa



Dushingiye ku mibare yemewe, ibikoresho bikoreshwa cane birimwo ibikoresho bitandukanye bifise ibiciro bitandukanye n’imyaka itandukanye, ivyo bikaba vyerekana ubushobozi bwa LineageOS bwo guhumekera ubuzima bushasha muri telefone za kera hamwe no gutuma izindi nshasha zikora neza.



### Ivyiyumviro bihambaye imbere yo gushiramwo



**Igikoresho co gufungura**: Suzuma ko uwugukora/uwugukoresha yemera gufungura. Hari amashirahamwe, nka Huawei, yakuyeho ivyo bishoboka ku bikoresho biherutse gusohoka, mu gihe ayandi ashiraho uburyo bwihariye.



**Exact model**: Ni ngombwa cane gukuraho ROM ihuye neza n’igikoresho cawe. Ivyuma bibiri bifise amazina y’ubudandaji asa bishobora gutandukanywa mu vy’ubuhinga (Galaxy S10 vs S10 5G nk’akarorero) kandi bisaba amashusho atandukanye.



**Scalable support**: Ivyuma bishasha bishobora kutafashwa ubwo nyene, kuko guhindura bisaba umuhinguzi w'umunyamwete kugira ngo abitwararike. Ku rundi ruhande, ugushigikira birashobora guhagarara iyo uwucungera igikoresho akuye mu mugambi.



## Gushiramwo



### Ibisabwa vy'ingenzi



⚠️ **Soma aya mabwirizwa yose imbere y'uko utangura** kugira wirinde ingorane!



**Subira kuri firmware (niba ari ngombwa) :**




- Igikoresho ca Android Flash**: Koresha igikoresho cemewe ca Google kugira ngo usubize mu buryo bworoshe igikoresho cawe ca Pixel kugira ngo ukoreshe Android ukoresheje umucukumbuzi wawe w'urubuga (Chrome/Edge irakenewe)
- Ubundi**: Amashusho y'uruganda n'amaboko avuye kuri [abahinguzi.google.com/android/amashusho](https://abahinguzi.google.com/android/amashusho)



**Ibibazo vy'ingenzi:**




- Gufungura igikoresho cawe nibura rimwe** ukoresheje uburyo bw'umwimerere
- Gerageza ibiranga vyose**: SMS, guhamagara, Wi-Fi, amakuru ya telefone ngendanwa
- Ivy’ingenzi**: Suzuma ko ushobora kohereza/kwakira SMS no guhamagara/kwakira amatelefone (harimwo no gukoresha WiFi na 4G/5G). Iyo idakora kuri stock system, ntizokora no kuri LineageOS!
- Ivyuma biherutse gukoreshwa**: Bimwe bisaba ko VoLTE/VoWiFi ikoreshwa n’imiburiburi rimwe kuri sisitemu y’ububiko kugira ngo IMS



**Ugutegura uburyo:**




- Kuraho konti zose za Google** kuri gikoresho cawe kugira ngo wirinde gusubiramwo uruganda, bishobora kubuza gukoresha
- Full backup** : Ivyo bizokuraho telefone yawe yose. Gusubiza inyuma amafoto, abo mubonana, amaporogarama n'amadosiye ahambaye



**Ibikoresho vya ADB na Fastboot:** Kurikiza [uburongozi buzwi bwa LineageOS](uburongozi_burongoye_gufungura-adb-n'ugufungura vuba) kugira ngo ushiremwo Ibikoresho vy'Ikibanza ca Android SDK. Genzura gushirwaho na `verisiyo ya adb` na `verisiyo ya fastboot`.



**Imiterere ya telefone:**




- Gukoresha **Amahitamwo y'umuhinguzi**: Amagenamiterere > Ivyerekeye > kanda "Umubare w'ubwubatsi" incuro 7



![android-version](assets/fr/06.webp)



*Genda kuri Settings > Ivyerekeye Telefone kugira ngo ukoreshe uburyo bw'umuhinguzi*





- Gushoboza **USB gukosora** mu mahitamwo y'umuhinguzi
- Gukoresha **OEM Unlock** (ni ngombwa kugira ngo ufungure umuyagankuba)



![developer-options](assets/fr/07.webp)



*Shoboza amahitamwo y'abahinguzi, gukosora USB no gufungura OEM*



### Gushiramwo ido n'ido

#### Intambwe ya 1: Gusuzuma porogaramu nshasha



**Firmware ikenewe**: Igikoresho cawe kigomba kuba gifise Android 13 imbere y’uko ukomeza (ku Pixel 4). Firmware yerekeza ku mashusho yihariye y’igikoresho ari muri sisitemu y’ububiko.



![pixel4-info](assets/fr/04.webp)



*Urupapuro rwemewe rwa Pixel 4 rufise amahuza yo gukuraho n'uburongozi bwo gushiramwo*



![downloads-page](assets/fr/05.webp)



*LineageOS yubatse urupapuro rwo gukuraho n'amadosiye*



**Ibikuwe kuri Pixel 4:**




- Build LineageOS**: [gukuraho.umurongo.org/ibikoresho/urubeya/yubaka](https://gukuraho.umurongo.org/ibikoresho/urubeya/yubaka)
- Dosiye zikenewe**: Kura amadosiye 3 asabwa kuri iyi paji (azokoreshwa mu ntambwe zikurikira):
  - `umuryango-22.2-YYYYMMDD-ijoro-imbeya-yashizweko umukono.zip` (ROM nyamukuru)
  - dtbo.img` (igiti c'igikoresho co gucapura)
  - `gutangura.img` (gusubizaho umurongoOS)



⚠️ **Igihambaye**: Suzuma verisiyo ya Android, ntusuzume verisiyo ya OS y’umuhinguzi. Kuba kuri ROM isanzwe (mbere na LineageOS) ntivyemeza ko ico gisabwa gishitse.



💡 **Impanuro**: Niba ukekeranya kuri firmware yawe, subira kuri stock system imbere yo kubandanya!



#### Intambwe ya 2: Gufungura bootloader



⚠️ **Iyi ntambwe irafuta amakuru yawe yose!





- Gerageza ubufatanye bwa ADB**: Huza igikoresho cawe biciye kuri USB hanyuma ugerageze n'itegeko `adb devices` kuva ku nzira ya mudasobwa yawe



![adb-devices](assets/fr/08.webp)



*Suzuma ubufatanye bwa ADB n'itegeko rya `ibikoresho vya adb`*





- Kwemerera guhuza** kuri telefone yawe



![usb-debugging-auth](assets/fr/09.webp)



*USB gukosora bishobojwe n'urutoke rwa mudasobwa RSA*





- Gufungura mu buryo bwo gufungura** :


```

```


Canke ufate **Ijwi Hasi + Inguvu** igikoresho kizimye





- Suzuma ubufatanye bwa fastboot**:


```

```

*Ikigaragaza ca Pixel 4 kigaragaza vuba gifise amakuru ya sisitemu*





- Gufungura umukoresho wo gufungura** :


```

```


Ku gikoresho, koresha amafunguro y'Ijwi kugira ngo ugende maze ukande buto ya **Power** kugira uhitemwo "Ufungure bootloader" maze wemeze igikorwa



![unlock-bootloader](assets/fr/12.webp)



*Ukwemeza gufungura bootloader ku gikoresho*

## Gutunganya n'ibikorwa



### Itanguriro rya mbere


Interface yatunganijwe neza, hafi y’iyi Android, ata Google. Gutunganya vyoroshe: ururimi, Wi-Fi, nta konti isabwa.



### Ibindi bikoresho



**Inkomoko z'ibisabwa zitekanye:**



**F-Droid** : Ububiko bw’ibikoresho vy’inkomoko yuguruye, bwashizweho mbere na LineageOS ya microG canke bushobora gukurwako ataco buvuze. F-Droid itanga gusa porogarama zifunguye zagenzuwe kandi zikoranijwe mu buryo buboneye, zikaba zitanga icemeza ko ata bikurikirana canke ibihimba bibi.



**Iduka rya Aurora**: Umukiriya atazwi kubera kwinjira mu iduka rya Google Play ata konti ya Google. Aurora iguriza amakonti atazwi, akaguha uburenganzira bwo gukuraho porogarama zisanzwe mu gihe uzigama ubuzima bwite bwawe.



**Ibindi bikoresho vy'ingenzi:**





- Kugenda**: Ikarata y'ibinyabuzima (ikarita zitari ku murongo zishingiye kuri OpenStreetMap)
- Imenyekanisha**: Ikimenyetso (ubutumwa bushizwe mu mfuruka kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera), K-9 Mail (umukiriya wa email ku buntu)
- Itangazamakuru**: NewPipe (itatangaza, YouTube idakurikirana), VLC (itangazamakuru ry'isi yose)
- Umusaruro**: Nextcloud (igicu ciyakira), Ikirangamisi coroshe (uguhuza CalDAV)
- Umutekano**: Bitwarden (umucungerezi w'ijambobanga), Umugenzuzi wa Aegis (amakode ya 2FA)



Izo porogaramu, nyinshi muri zo ziboneka biciye kuri F-Droid, zikora urutonde rw’ibidukikije rushobora gusubirira bimwe bishitse ibikorwa vya Google mu gihe zitanga ubumenyi bushasha kandi bukora.



## Gukoresha no kwitaho



### Ubumenyi bwa misi yose



LineageOS ihindura ubumenyi bwa Android, igatanga imbere y’ibindi vyose n’ugusubiza. Interface yatunganijwe neza cane cane ku bikoresho vya kera, bifise ubuzima bushasha, bifise ubushobozi bwo gukora neza kurusha ROM z’abahinguzi kubera ko ata bikoresho bikomeye vy’ugupfuka n’ibikorwa bidakenewe.



Ibikorwa vy’ishimikiro (uguhamagara, ubutumwa bwo kuri telefone, amafoto, gusoma) bikora ata nkomanzi, mu gihe ibikoresho vyo guhindura ibintu bishobora gutuma iyo sisitemu itunganywa neza bivanye n’ivyo umuntu akunda ataco bihinduye ku gushikama.



### Uburyo bwo kuvugurura OTA



LineageOS ifise uburyo bwo guhindura amakuru bworoshe cane gukoresha Over-The-Air. Verisiyo nshasha zica zitangwa ubwo nyene biciye ku makuru, kandi gushiramwo bifata gusa gukanda bikeyi, ata n’umwe akeneye gukoresha ubuhinga bukomeye. Ivyo bizigama amakuru yawe n’ibikorwa vyawe vyashizwemwo.



Ivyo bihindurwa bihoraho ni ikintu gihambaye cane cane ku bikoresho vyahagaritswe n’ababikora, bashobora kubandanya kwungukira ku bikoresho bishasha vy’umutekano vya Android.



### Ivyiza vy'ibikorwa



**Umutekano wo gushiraho:**




- Gushiraho PIN ikomeye canke ijambobanga ryo gufungura
- Suzuma ko ububiko bw'ibanga bushobojwe (kenshi na mburabuzi)
- Shiraho umucungerezi w'ijambobanga nka Bitwarden biciye kuri F-Droid



**Ukwitaho mu buryo bwo kwirinda:**




- Guhindura OTA ubudasiba ku mutekano
- Gushiramwo porogaramu ku masoko yizewe (F-Droid, Iduka rya Aurora)
- Suzuma kenshi uruhusha rwatanzwe ku bisabwa
- Gusubira gutangura rimwe na rimwe bituma ibikorwa bigenda neza kandi bikabohora ubwonko

## GrapheneOS n’umuryango: Ni itandukaniro irihe?



### Uburyo butandukanye



**GrapheneOS** yibanda cane ku mutekano mwinshi, kandi ikoresha gusa Google Pixels kugira ikoreshe ibice vyabo vy’umutekano. Ubuhinga bushiramwo uburyo bwinshi bwo kugabanya ububabare kandi bukomeza cane gukoresha sandboxing.



**LineageOS** iringaniza umutekano, ubuzima bwite n’uburyohe ku bikoresho vyinshi bishoboka. Ubwo buryo burarushiriza kuba bushingiye ku vyiyumviro, bugamije gutuma ibintu bihuza cane aho kugira ngo umuntu agire umutekano wose.



### Gucungera ibikorwa vya Google



**GrapheneOS**: Itanga ubuhinga bwo gukina bwa Google. Google Play ishobora gushirwamwo ariko ikora mu gasandugu k’umusenyi gakomeye, ata n’uburenganzira budasanzwe bwa sisitemu. Ubu buryo budasanzwe buratuma bishoboka gukoresha ibidukikije vya Google mu gihe umuntu aguma afise ubugenzuzi bukomeye bw’umutekano.



**LineageOS**: Ireka umukoresha ahitemwo gushiramwo ibikorwa vya Google (GApps), microG (ubundi buryo bwo ku buntu), canke kuguma ata Google ikoresha. Guhinduranya cane kugira ngo bihure n’ivyo ukeneye.



### Igereranyo ry'ubuhinga




| **Inzira** | **GrapheneOS** | **LineageOS** |
|------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Ubwiyunge** | Pixels gusa | Ibirahure byinshi |
| **Umutekano** | Imbangurire z'ikigali | Umutekano wa AOSP ukarutare |
| **Google Play** | Ubwiko bushobora guhitamo | Kwinjira kwa karite bishoboka |
| **Kwinjira** | Impapuro y'inyandiko + USB | Inzira y'iyo y'ubwenge |
| **Umpago** | Umutekano mbere yose | Ubwiyunge n'ubwishingizi bw'uhitamo |

### Impanuro zo gukoresha



**Hitamwo GrapheneOS** nimba ufise Pixel, nimba umutekano mwinshi ari wo ushira imbere, kandi nimba wemera inzitizi kugira ngo ukingirwe.



**Genda kuri LineageOS** nimba ufise igikoresho kitari Pixel, uriko urarondera uburinganire bwiza bw'ubuzima bwite/ubushobozi, canke ushaka umwidegemvyo wo guhitamwo urugero rwawe rwo gusenyera ku mugozi umwe n'ibidukikije vya Google.



## Iciyumviro



LineageOS itanga uburyo bukomeye bwo gusubira kugenzura telefone yawe ngendanwa ya Android. Ubumenyi butagira aho bugarukira, ubushobozi bwiza, uguhuza cane: ihitamwo ryiza ryo gufatanya ubuzima bwite n’ibikorwa vya misi yose.
