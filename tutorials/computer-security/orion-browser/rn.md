## Imenyekanisha



Mu gihe benshi mu bacukumbuzi bakorakoranya cane amakuru yacu bwite, guhitamwo umucukumbuzi ashobora gukingira ubuzima bwite biraba ikintu gihambaye cane. Chrome ni yo igenzura 65% vy’isoko ry’isi yose, ariko ubuhinga bwayo bushingiye ku gukoresha nabi amakuru yawe yo gusura. Safari, naho yinjijwe mu bidukikije vya Apple, nta bintu vy’uburinzi biteye imbere ifise kandi ntishobora gushigikira mu buryo bubereye ivyungura vy’abandi.



![Répartition du marché des navigateurs](assets/fr/01.webp)


*Isoko ry'umucukumbuzi w'urubuga: Chrome ni yo irongoye n'isoko rirenga 65%, ikurikirwa na Safari, Edge na Firefox*



**Orion Browser** yiyerekana nk’ubundi buryo bushasha ku bakoresha Apple. Iyi browser yateguwe na Kagi, ifatanya umuvuduko wa moteri ya WebKit n’ubuhinga bwa zero telemetry. Udakunze abayihiganwa, Orion nta makuru yohereza ku ma server yo kure kandi ibuza 99.9% vy’amatangazo n’abakurikirana, harimwo na YouTube.



Ikintu kidasanzwe kiyiranga? Orion ni **WebKit** yonyene ishobora gushiramwo Chrome **na** Firefox, itanga ivyiza kuruta ibindi vyose. Ukwo guhuza, hamwe n’ugukoresha ubuhinga bwo kwibuka incuro 2 gushika kuri 3 kuruta ibindi bikoresho vyo gukoresha mu gucukura amakuru be n’ugukorana neza n’ibidukikije vya Apple (iCloud, Keychain), bituma ari ihitamwo ryiza ku bakoresha Mac na iPhone bakunda ubuzima bwite.



## Ni kuki uhisemwo umucukumbuzi wa Orion?



### Inyungu nyamukuru



**Uburinzi burengeye ubundi bwose buva mu gasandugu**: Orion ibuza 99.9% vy’amatangazo (harimwo na YouTube) n’abakurikirana bose b’umuntu wa mbere n’uw’umuntu wa gatatu ku buryo busanzwe. Ikoranabuhanga ryayo rifatanya ubuhinga bwo kwirinda gukurikirana n’urutonde rwa EasyPrivacy kugira ngo bikore neza cane. Ikintu kidasanzwe: Orion ibuza inyandiko z'ibimenyetso vy'intoke **mbere y'uko zishirwa mu ngiro**, bikaba bituma gukurikirana bidashoboka - uburyo bukomeye kuruta ibindi bikoresho bigerageza gusa "gupfuka" amakuru.



**Ivyuma bishobora kugenzurwa ataco bimaze**: Orion ifata uburyo bukomeye bwo gucungera ubuzima bwite, ataco bimaze mu bijanye n’ubuhinga bwo gupima uburebure. Udakunze izindi nzira, zisaba amajana y'urubuga igihe zitangura (IP exponent, urutoke rw'umucukumbuzi, amakuru y'ibanga), Orion ntiyigera "itera telefone muhira". Iryo tandukaniro ry’ishimikiro rikuraho burundu ingorane zo gusohoka kw’amakuru ata co bigamije.



**Ibikorwa bidasanzwe**: Bishingiye kuri verisiyo ya WebKit yatunganijwe neza, Orion ingana canke mbere irenga Safari mu muvuduko kuri Mac. Ivyigwa vy’umuvuduko 2.0/2.1 biguma biyishira imbere ku bikoresho vya Apple Silicon. Guhagarika amatangazo y’akavukire bituma urupapuro rwihuta gushirwako 20 gushika kuri 40%.



**Ishigikiro ry'ivyagutse kw'isi yose**: Igikorwa gishasha, Orion ishobora kugufasha gushiramwo ivyagutse biva ku rubuga rwa Chrome **n'ivyongeweko vya Mozilla. Ubufasha bwa WebExtensions buriko burageragezwa, n'intumbero yo guhuza 100% ku gusohoka kwa beta. Ushobora gukoresha ivyungura vyinshi bikundwa nka uBlock Origin, Bitwarden, mbere no kuri iPhone - isi ya mbere kuri iOS, naho bimwe bimwe bishobora kutakora neza.



### Imipaka yo kumenya





- Ivyo bishobora gukoreshwa bike**: Ubu bigenewe macOS na iOS/iPadOS. Verisiyo ya Linux iriko irashika ku ntambwe z’iterambere (Intambwe ya 2 muri 2025), ariko nta nyubakwa ya bose iriho. Windows na Android ntibiri mu gutegurwa kubera ubukene bw’ibikoresho.
- Closed source code**: Naho ibice bimwe bimwe ari ivy’inkomoko yuguruye, Orion iguma ari iya nyen’umuntu, ikintu gitera impaka mu muryango w’ubuzima bwite.
- Ivyagutse vy'igerageza**: Infashanyo y'ivyagutse iguma muri beta, n'ukudahuza kenshi. Ivyo kwagura birashobora kugira ico bikoze ku mikorere, kandi bimwe ntibikora na gato.
- Umutekano wa WebKit**: Bitandukanye na Chromium, WebKit ntitanga mwene ukwo kwitandukanya kw’imigenderanire y’urubuga, bishobora gutera ingorane z’umutekano mu bihe bimwebimwe.
- Ivyagezwe vyo guhagarika**: Orion ikora nabi n'ibigirankana mu bigeragezo vyo kwamamaza kuri internet (26-35%), kuko Kagi abona ko ivyo bigeragezo "bifise amakosa y'ishimikiro". Ubushobozi nyakuri bwo gukoresha buri musi buraruta kure n’iyo.

## Ingingo zo kwitwararika n'umutekano



### Ibiranga umutekano bidasanzwe



**Uburinzi bw’ihinduka ry’ibimenyetso vy’intoke**: Orion ni yo browser yonyene ishobora guhagarika burundu ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’inyandiko z’ibimenyetso vy’intoke imbere y’uko zishobora gucapura sisitemu yawe. Ubu buryo bwa "nta script iriko irakora = nta n'urutoke rushoboka" buraruta uburyo bwa kera bwo gupfuka bukoreshwa n'abandi bashakashatsi.



**Urutonde rwera rubonerana**: Orion irafise urutonde rutoyi rw’imbuga za bose (browserbench.org, wizzair.com) aho guhagarika bica bihagarara kugira ngo ntihagire ibintu bikora nabi. Ukwo guseruka gutuma abakoresha batahura neza igihe n’igituma uguhagarika bigabanywa.



**Ivyagutse bitagenzuwe**: Gushigikira ivyagutse vya Chrome/Firefox bizana ibindi bibazo, kuko ivyo vyagutse bitagenewe WebKit kandi ntivyagenzuwe cane cane kuri iki kibanza.



### Gucungera no kuvugurura





- Impinduka zihita**: Orion ihita ivugurura kuri macOS biciye kuri Sparkle
- Gukurikirana ubugoyagoye**: Suzuma ubudasiba amakuru y'isohoka ku bice vy'umutekano
- Raporo y'ibibazo**: Koresha [ibisubizo.org](https://ibisubizo.org) kugira ngo umenyeshe ibibazo




## Iciyumviro



Orion Browser iserukira intambwe ikomeye imbere ku bijanye n’ubuzima bwite kuri macOS na iOS. Uburyo bwayo bwa zero telemetry, bufatanijwe n’ububiko bw’akavukire bukora neza cane be n’ugushigikira kwihariye kw’ivyungura vyose, bituma ari ihitamwo ryiza cane ku bakoresha Apple bakunda ubuzima bwite.



**Igihambaye**: Orion iguma muri beta ihoraho kuva muri 2021, n’imipaka y’uguhuza n’ibindi n’ingorane zisanzwe za WebKit. Suzuma ivyo bisubizo bishingiye ku citegererezo cawe co gutera ubwoba.



Ku gukoresha buri musi kuri Mac canke iPhone, birashoboka ko ari co kintu ciza co gusenyera ku mugozi umwe hagati y’ibanga, ubushobozi n’ukworohereza gukoresha biboneka mu bidukikije vya Apple, igihe cose wemeye aho bigarukira.



Ibuka: kurinda ubuzima bwite bwawe ntibivana gusa n’umucukumbuzi wawe. Gufatanya Orion n’ibikorwa vyiza (amajambo y’ibanga akomeye, 2FA, VPN nimba bikenewe) kugira ngo ubone umutekano mwiza wo kuri internet.
