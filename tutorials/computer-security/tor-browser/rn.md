Nk’uko izina ryayo rivyerekana, umucukumbuzi ni porogarama ikoreshwa mu guca kuri Internet. Ikora nk’irembo hagati y’imashini y’uwukoresha n’urubuga, igahindura kode y’imbuga mu mapaji ashobora gukoreshwa kandi asomwa. Guhitamwo umucukumbuzi wawe birahambaye cane, kuko bitagira ico bikoze gusa ku kuntu ukoresha mucukumbuzi ariko kandi biragira ico bikoze ku mutekano wawe wo kuri Internet no ku buzima bwite bwawe.


Urabe maso ntuvange umucukumbuzi n’umucukumbuzi. Mucukumbuzi ni porogarama ukoresha kugira ngo winjire kuri Internet (nka Chrome canke Firefox), mu gihe moteri y’ubushakashatsi ari igikorwa, nka Google canke Bing, nk’akarorero, kigufasha kuronka amakuru kuri Internet.


Ubu, Google Chrome ni yo ikoreshwa cane. Igira nk’ibice 65% vy’isoko ry’isi yose mu 2024. Chrome irashimwa cane kubera umuvuduko wayo n’ubushobozi bwayo, ariko si ngombwa ko ari yo mahitamwo meza ku muntu wese, cane cane iyo ubuzima bwite ari bwo buhambaye kuri wewe. Chrome ni iya Google, ishirahamwe rizwi cane mu gukusanya no gusuzuma amakuru menshi cane ku bakoresha bayo. Kandi vy’ukuri, umucukumbuzi wabo wo mu nzu ni wo mutima w’ingene bacungera. Iyi porogarama ni igice nyamukuru mu migenderanire yawe myinshi yo kuri Internet. Kumenya gukusanya amakuru ku mucukumbuzi wawe ni ikibazo gihambaye kuri Google.

Hariho imiryango myinshi y’ibikoresho vy’ugucukura, kimwe cose gishingiye ku moteri yihariye y’uguhindura. Mucukumbuzi nka Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, Opera, canke Vivaldi vyose bishingiye ku mucukumbuzi wa Chromium, verisiyo yoroshe kandi yuguruye ya Chrome yateguwe na Google. Izo nzira zose zikoresha moteri y’uguhindura Blink, ariyo Fork ya WebKit, ubwayo ikomoka kuri KHTML. Ugukomeye kwa Chromium kw’isoko bituma ama browser akomoka kuri yo akora neza cane, kuko abakora urubuga bakunda gutuma imbuga zabo zigenda neza cane cane kuri Blink.


Safari, ni umucukumbuzi wa Apple, akoresha WebKit, nayo ikaba iva muri KHTML.


Ku rundi ruhande, amashakisha nka Mozilla Firefox, LibreWolf, na Tor Browser yizigira Gecko, moteri itandukanye y’uguhindura, ikomoka ku mushakisha wa Netscape.


Guhitamwo umucukumbuzi mwiza bivana n’ivyo ukeneye. Ariko nimba n’imiburiburi uhangayikishijwe n’ubuzima bwite bwawe, rero n’umutekano wawe, ndagusavye kujana na Firefox kugira ngo ukoreshe muri rusangi na Tor Browser kugira ngo ubone ubuzima bwite bwinshi. Muri iyi nyigisho, nzokwereka ingene wotangura gukoresha Tor Browser mu buryo bworoshe.

## Intangamarara y'umucukumbuzi wa Tor


Tor Browser ni umucukumbuzi yagenewe canecane gukoresha Internet mu buryo butekanye kandi bwihariye uko bishoboka kwose. Mucukumbuzi ishingiye kuri Firefox, rero kuri moteri y’uguhindura Gecko.

Tor Browser ikoresha urubuga rwa Tor kugira ngo ipfuke kandi irungike uruja n’uruza rwawe biciye ku ma server menshi y’ugutanga amakuru imbere y’uko ururungika aho ruja. Uwo murongo w'inzira nyinshi, uzwi nka "*onion routing*," ufasha guhisha IP yawe nyayo Address, bikaba bigoye kumenya aho uri n'ibikorwa vyawe vyo kuri internet. Ariko rero, gusura ni ngombwa ko bigenda buhoro kuruta uko bikoreshwa n’umucukumbuzi asanzwe adakoresha urubuga rwa Tor, kuko ataco akora.

Mu buryo butandukanye n’izindi nzira, Tor Browser irashiramwo ibintu vyihariye kugira ngo ntihagire uwukurikirana ibikorwa vyawe vyo kuri Internet, nk’ugutandukanya urubuga rwose usuye no gukuraho amakuru n’amateka iyo upfutse. Igizwe kandi n’ugugabanya ingorane zo gufata ibimenyetso vy’intoke, mu gutuma abakoresha bose basa n’abasuye uko bishoboka kwose.


Ushobora gukoresha neza cane Tor Browser kugira ngo ushikire urubuga rusanzwe (`.com`, `.org`, n'ibindi). Muri ivyo, uruja n’uruza rwawe ruramenyekana mu guca mu bice vyinshi vya Tor imbere y’uko ushika ku gice co gusohoka kivugana n’urubuga rwa nyuma kuri clearnet.

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/03.webp)

Ushobora kandi gukoresha Tor Browser kugira ngo ushikire ibikorwa vyihishijwe (amaderesi ahera muri `.onion`). Muri iki gihe, uruja n’uruza rwose ruguma mu rubuga rwa Tor, ata nzira yo gusohokamwo, bikaba vyemeza ubuzima bwite bwose ku muntu akoresha no ku murongo w’aho aja. Ubwo buryo bwo gukora burakoreshwa cane cane mu gushika ku co rimwe na rimwe citwa "*urubuga rw'umwiza*," igice ca Internet kitari mu rutonde rw'ibikoresho vy'ubushakashatsi vya kera.

## Ni itandukaniro irihe riri hagati y’urubuga rwa Tor n’umucukumbuzi wa Tor?


Urubuga rwa Tor n’umucukumbuzi wa Tor ni ibintu bibiri bitandukanye bidakwiye gutera urujijo, ariko birafashanya. Urubuga rwa Tor ni ibikorwa remezo vyo kw’isi yose vy’abakozi ba relay, bikoreshwa n’abakoresha, bituma uruja n’uruza rwa Internet rutamenyekana mu kuruca mu bice vyinshi imbere y’uko ruja iyo ruja. Iyi ni yo nzira izwi cane y’igitunguru.


Ku rundi ruhande, umucukumbuzi wa Tor ni umucukumbuzi yihariye yagenewe gutuma umuntu ashobora gushika kuri iyo nzira mu buryo bworoshe. Ishiramwo ku buryo busanzwe ibintu vyose bikenewe kugira ngo umuntu yihuze n’urubuga rwa Tor kandi ikoresha verisiyo ya Firefox yahinduwe kugira ngo itange ubumenyi bumenyerewe bwo gusura mu gihe yongera ubuzima bwite n’umutekano.


Urubuga rwa Tor ntirukoreshwa gusa n’umucukumbuzi wa Tor. Ishobora gukoreshwa na porogarama zitandukanye be n’ibikoresho bitandukanye kugira ngo zikingire ivy’itumanaho ryabo. Nk’akarorero, birashoboka ko ushoboza guhanahana amakuru biciye ku rubuga rwa Tor ku nzira yawe ya Bitcoin kugira ngo uhishe IP yawe Address abandi bakoresha no kubuza gucungera uruja n’uruza rwawe rujanye n’uruja n’uruza rwa Bitcoin n’uwuguha interineti.

Mu ncamake, urubuga rwa Tor ni ibikorwa remezo bitanga ubuzima bwite mu gusura kwacu kuri internet, kandi Tor Browser ni porogarama ituma dukoresha urwo rubuga nk’igice c’ugusura urubuga rwacu.

Tor Browser ikoreshwa nk’umucukumbuzi asanzwe.

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/12.webp)

Igihe utangura, umucukumbuzi arakwereka urupapuro rugutumira kwifatanya n’urubuga rwa Tor. Fyonda gusa kuri buto ya "*Connect*" kugira ngo ushireho iyo nzira.

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/13.webp)

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/14.webp)

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/15.webp)

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/16.webp)

## Ni gute wotunganya umucukumbuzi wa Tor?


Hejuru y’ibarabara ry’umucukumbuzi wawe, uzosanga hariho uburyo bwo kwinjiza ivyo ukunda. Ivyo bigufasha kwinjiza ubwawe ibimenyetso vyo mu mucukumbuzi wawe wa kera mu mucukumbuzi wa Tor.

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/19.webp)

Ushobora kandi kwongerako ibimenyetso bishasha mu gufyonda ku kimenyetso c’inyenyeri kiri hejuru iburyo kuri paji y’urubuga uriko urasura.

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/20.webp)

Mu rutonde ruri iburyo, urashobora gushika ku mahitamwo atandukanye.

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/21.webp)The "*New identity*" button allows you to change your Tor identity. Specifically, this enables you to start a new user session on Tor, meaning changing your IP address and resetting cookies and open sessions.

"*Ibimenyetso*" bigufasha gucunga ibimenyetso vyawe.

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/23.webp)

"*History*" iraguha uburenganzira bwo kubona amateka yawe yo gusura iyo warayashoboje mu mategeko.

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/24.webp)

"*Ivyongeweko n'insanganyamatsiko*" bigufasha guhindura uko umucukumbuzi wawe asa canke ukongerako ivyungura. Kubera ko Tor Browser ishingiye kuri Mozilla Firefox, urashobora gukoresha insanganyamatsiko n’ibindi bikoresho biriho kuri Firefox.

![TOR BROWSER](assets/notext/25.webp)

Mu gice ca "*Search*", ushobora guhitamwo moteri y'ugushaka. Tor Browser ihindura DuckDuckGo, ubushakashatsi bwibanda ku kurinda ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha, ariko ushobora kandi guhitamwo Google canke Startpage, nk’akarorero.
