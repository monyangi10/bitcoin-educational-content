- Ama cookies y'uwundi muntu**: amadosiye mato mato ashizweho n'imbuga zo hanze kugira ngo agukurikirane kuva ku rubuga rumwe uja ku rundi
- Igishushanyo c'intoke**: gikorakoranya ibiranga umucukumbuzi wawe n'igikoresho cawe (uburebure bw'ibarabara, imyandikire yashizwemwo, ibikoresho, n'ibindi) kugira ngo bikumenye ata cookies
- Gukurikirana inyandiko**: amakode ya JavaScript ataboneka asesangura inyifato yawe yo gushakisha (gukanda, guca, umwanya umaze)
- Isesengura rya IP Address**: ahantu n'ikimenyetso c'uwuguha Internet



Aya makuru aca ahurizwa hamwe kugira ngo haboneke amakuru y’ido n’ido yerekeye inyifato yawe yo kuri Internet maze akagira amahera, kenshi ataco uzi. Ivyo bintu vy’ukuri biravyura ikibazo nyamukuru: woshobora gute gukoresha Internet mu gihe uzigama amazina yawe n’ibanga ryawe?



Inyishu iri ahanini mu guhitamwo kwawe kw’umucukumbuzi w’urubuga. Ico gikoresho, dukoresha buri musi kugira ngo turonke amakuru, tugure canke tuvugane, kirafise uruhara runini mu kurinda amakuru yacu bwite. Ikibabaje, ama browser azwi cane nka Google Chrome (afise hafi 65% vy’isoko ry’isi yose) akozwe hakurikijwe uburyo bwo gukora ubucuruzi bushingiye ku gukoranya amakuru menshi y’abakoresha.

Kubera iyo ngorane, abakinyi bashasha bariko baraseruka bafise ubuhinga butandukanye: ubwo gushiramwo ubuzima bwite mu mutima w’ivyo bahingura. Muri izo, Mullvad Browser iragaragara nk’umuti mushasha uhuza uburinzi bwiza bw’ubuzima bwite n’ubuhinga bwo gusura bugenda neza kandi bushoboka.



Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibikoresho vya kera bishaka guhindura ubumenyi bwawe mu kwegeranya amakuru yawe, Mullvad Browser ifata uburyo bunyuranye n’ubwo: ituma abayikoresha bose basa n’imbuga, gutyo bikaba bituma gukurikirana umuntu ku giti ciwe bidashoboka.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzobona hamwe ingene Mullvad Browser ishobora guhindura uburyo ukoresha Internet, ikaguha uburinzi bukomeye ku bijanye n’ugucungera ataco utakaje ku gukoresha neza.




## Kumenyesha umucukumbuzi wa Mullvad



**Mullvad Browser** ni urubuga rwibanda ku buzima bwite rwakozwe mu bufatanye n’umugambi wa Tor kandi rutangazwa ku buntu n’ishirahamwe ryo muri Suède Mullvad VPN. Yatangujwe muri Ndamukiza 2023, yiyerekana nk'**"Tor Browser ata rubuga rwa Tor "**, igenewe kugabanya ugukurikirana kuri internet no gufata urutoke mu gihe yemerera abakoresha gusura biciye kuri VPN yizigirwa aho gukoresha urubuga rwa Tor.



Mullvad Browser ni umucukumbuzi w’ubuntu, yuguruye ashingiye kuri Firefox ESR (verisiyo y’igihe kirekire ya Mozilla Firefox) kandi ikomezwa n’abahinga mu mugambi wa Tor. Mu majambo nyayo, irimwo vyinshi mu **ibintu vy'uburinzi vya Tor Browser**, ariko **ntiyiyobora uruja n'uruza biciye ku rubuga rwa Tor**. Ahubwo, abakoresha barashobora (kandi bakwiye) kuyihuza na VPN yizigiwe kugira ngo batamenyekane IP yabo Address.



Ku bijanye n’ubumenyi bw’abakoresha, Mullvad Browser isa n’umucukumbuzi wa kera, itanga ubuhinga bwo kugendera mu mazi. Iboneka ku buntu kuri Windows, macOS na Linux (nta verisiyo yo kuri telefone ngendanwa). Ntukeneye kuba umunyamuryango wa Mullvad VPN kugira ngo uyikoreshe; ariko, **gukoresha Mullvad Browser utapfutse IP yawe ntibitanga ubutamenyekana bwose** - rero ni vyiza cane ko uyikoresha hamwe na VPN yizigirwa.



### Intumbero: ubuzima bwite no kurwanya gukurikirana



Mucukumbuzi ya Mullvad yakozwe ifise intumbero imwe nyamukuru mu muzirikanyi: **kurinda ubuzima bwite bw’abakoresha** kuri internet no kurwanya ubuhinga busanzwe bwo gukurikirana no gutanga amakuru. Intumbero zayo nyamukuru ni:





- Kugabanya cane ugukurikirana amatangazo n’**ugukurikirana** n’imbuga n’amashirahamwe y’amatangazo. Ku mburabuzi, Mullvad Browser irabuza abakurikirana, amakuru y’ikurikirana n’inyandiko z’ibimenyetso vy’intoke zishobora kukumenya.





- Guhindura urutoke rw'umucukumbuzi wawe kugira ngo **"wivange n'abantu benshi"**. Urutoke ni nk'"ikarata y'indangamuntu" yihariye iremwe mu gufatanya ibiranga vyose vy'umucukumbuzi wawe. Mullvad Browser ikora vyose kugira ngo abayikoresha bose bafise "ikarata y'indangamuntu" imwe nyene, bikaba bidashoboka ko umuntu abatandukanya umwe wese ku giti ciwe.





- Gutanga uburinzi ubwo nyene ata yindi nkuru yongereweko. Mullvad Browser iza mu buryo "bwiteguye gukoreshwa": uwukoresha ntakeneye gushiramwo urutonde rw'ibintu vyinshi canke guhindura ibintu vyose kugira ngo akingirwe.





- Ntuhebe ikimazi ubushobozi canke ubuhinga bwo gukora neza kuruta uko bikenewe. Iyo ata nzira ya Tor iriho, Mullvad Browser itanga uburyo bwo gusura vyihuta cane kuruta Tor Browser, yegera ubushobozi bw’umucukumbuzi asanzwe afatanijwe na VPN.



### Ibirango vy'umucukumbuzi wa Mullvad



Mullvad Browser irimwo urutonde rw'ibintu vy'umutekano n'ubuzima bwite** vyahumekewe na Tor Browser:





- Gushakisha mu buryo bwihariye ibihe vyose:** Uburyo bwo gushakisha mu buryo bwihariye burakora ku buryo busanzwe kandi ntibushobora guhagarara. **Nta mateka, amakuru canke ububiko bubikwa kuva ku gihe kimwe gushika ku kindi**. Ukimara gufunga Mullvad Browser, amakuru yose yo gusura arafutwa.





- Uburinzi bwongerewe ku bimenyetso vy'intoke:** Mucukumbuzi akoresha amasetingi akomeye kugira ngo abuze ibimenyetso vy'intoke vy'ubuhinga bwa none. Ivyo birimwo:
- Ukoresha umukozi** n'umucukumbuzi verisiyo guhuza
- Isaha yashizweho kuri **UTC** ku bakoresha bose
- Letterboxing**: ubuhinga bwongera ubwabwo amabara y'umweru ku mpande z'urubuga kugira ngo ugereranije ubunini bw'ikigaragaza no kubuza kumenyana n'ingero z'igicapo cawe
- APIs zo guhagarika urutoke**: Ubuhinga bwa Canvas (igishushanyo ca 2D), WebGL (ibishushanyo vya 3D) na AudioContext (ugutunganya amajwi) burazimye kubera ko bushobora guhishura amakuru yihariye yerekeye ibikoresho vyawe
- Imyandikire ya sisitemu igezweho kugira ngo ntiyimenyekane n'imyandikire yashizweho





- Guhagarika abakurikirana n’amatangazo:** Mullvad Browser ikoresha mu buryo bw’imvukira **uBlock Origin** extension (yashizweho mbere) n’urutonde rw’uburinzi rw’inyongera kugira ngo ibuze **abakurikirana n’abandi, inyandiko z’amatangazo n’ibindi bintu bibi**. Ubwo burinzi bujana n'**First-Party Isolation**: ubuhinga bubika amakuru mu "bibindi" bitandukanye ku rubuga rumwe rumwe, bikabuza urubuga rumwe gusoma amakuru yashizweho n'urundi.





- Buto yo gusubiramwo igihe:** Nka buto ya Tor Browser "New Identity", Mullvad Browser itanga ubuto bwo **gusubiramwo vuba umucukumbuzi n'igihe gishasha, ataco kirimwo**.





- Ingero z'umutekano zishobora guhindurwa:** Ushobora guhindura ingero z'umutekano (*Isanzwe*, *Itekanye*, *Itekanye*) mu mitunganyirize, nk'uko biri muri Tor Browser.



## Ukwagura kwubatswemwo ku mburabuzi



Mullvad Browser irimwo **ibindi bitatu vyashizweho imbere** bigize umushinge w’uburinzi bwayo bwo kurwanya gukurikirana. **Ni ngombwa cane ko utazokwigera ubikuraho canke ngo uhindure imiterere yavyo**, kuko ivyo vyotuma uba uwudasanzwe mu bakoresha Mullvad Browser:



### **UBlock Inkomoko**


Iyi kwamamaza n'ugukurikirana ububisha kwagutse biza mbere vyatunganijwe n'urutonde rw'iyunguruzo ryiza** kugira ngo ububike:




- Kwamamaza bitera ubwoba
- Abakurikirana amakuru yawe y'uwundi muntu
- Inyandiko mbi
- Gukurikirana inyifato Elements.



uBlock Origin muri Mullvad Browser ikoresha ibipimo bihuye kugira ngo abakoresha bose babuze neza na neza Elements imwe, gutyo bazigame ubumwe bw’ibirenge vy’ubuhinga bwa none.



### **Ntanyandiko**


NoScript ikora inyuma kugira ngo icunge **ingero z'umutekano** za mucukumbuzi. Ibi:




- Igenzura **JavaScript** gushirwa mu ngiro hakurikijwe urugero rwatowe (Bisanzwe/Bitekanye cane/Bitekanye cane)
- Iyungurura **XSS** (Inyandiko y'Imbuga) ibitero bihita
- Ibuza akaga **ibirimwo bikora** ku mbuga zitari HTTPS
- Igishushanyo caco kinyegejwe ku buryo busanzwe, ariko gishobora kugaragazwa biciye ku "Guhindura umurongo w'ibikoresho".



### **Umucukumbuzi wa Mullvad** ukwaguka


Iyi nzira yihariye ya Mullvad itanga ibikorwa bitandukanye bivanye n’uko uri umukiriya wa Mullvad VPN canke atari we:



#### **Ata kwiyandikisha kwa Mullvad VPN:**




- Igenzura ry'ihuriro ry'ishimikiro**: yerekana IP yawe ya bose n'amakuru y'ihuriro
- Impanuro z'ubuzima bwite**: impanuro zo kunoza imiterere y'umutekano wawe (DNS, HTTPS-gusa, moteri y'ubushakashatsi)
- WebRTC** ubugenzuzi: gushoboza/guhagarika gukingira IP Address gusohoka
- Ishobora gukurwaho ata **ingaruka** ku birenge vyawe vy'ubuhinga bwa none iyo udakoresheje Mullvad VPN



#### **N'ukwiyandikisha kwa Mullvad VPN:**


Ukwaguka kwerekana ubushobozi bwayo bwose n'ibintu biteye imbere:





- Igikoresho ca SOCKS5 gishizwemwo**: gukanda rimwe guhuzwa n'igikoresho ca Mullvad VPN
- IP idahinduka Address**: bitandukanye na VPN, ishobora guhindura IP yayo Address, proxy yama yizeza igisohoka kimwe Address.
- Ihinduka ry'ukwica **: iyo VPN ivuye, uruja n'uruza rw'umucukumbuzi ruca ruhagarara
- IPv6 support**: Uguhuza IPv6 naho ubuhuza bwawe bwa VPN butagira





- Multihop (VPN ibiri)**: ubushobozi bwo guhindura ahantu proxy iri kugira ngo ureme umugende uri mu mugende
 - Ivy'urugendo rwawe bibanza guca kuri server yawe ya VPN, hanyuma "bikasimbuka" ku yindi server ya Mullvad .
 - Koresha uburyo butandukanye bwo gukoresha mu mucukumbuzi gusa





- Gukurikirana ubuhuza buteye imbere**: gukurikirana igihe nyaco c'imiterere ya VPN yawe, server ihuye, n'ugutahura ukuvuza kwa DNS





- Ushobora gushika kuri Mullvad Leta**: ubushakashatsi bwigenga bugenewe abayikoresha (naho butasabwa na Mullvad kubera imvo zifitaniye isano na konti yawe)



Ivyo bice bitatu bikorana kugira ngo bibeho uburyo bwo kwikingira, aho umukoresha wese yungukira ku vyo kwikingira bimwe ata bushobozi bwo guhindura ivyo umuntu ashaka vyotuma atamenyekana.



## Inyungu n'ibibi vya Mullvad Browser



### Inyungu





- Uburinzi bwiza cane bw’ubuzima bwite ku buryo busanzwe:** Mullvad Browser ikoresha amategeko akomeye cane y’ubuzima bwite kuva mu ntango, ata nkenerwa yo guhindura ibintu n’amaboko.





- Ibikorwa vyiza kurusha Tor Browser:** Iyo ata nzira y'igitunguru iriho, Mullvad Browser **iranyaruka cane kandi irasubiza neza** kurusha Tor Browser ku rubuga rwa kera.





- Ubworohe bwa Interface bumenyerewe:** Mucukumbuzi ya Mullvad ishingiye kuri Interface ya Firefox. Niba waramenyereye Firefox canke mbere Tor Browser, ntuzokwiyumva ko utari mu kibanza cawe.





- Ugukorana kwizigirwa n’itegeko ry’igenzura:** Mullvad Browser ivyungukira ku buhinga bw’umugambi wa Tor, kandi kode yose y’inkomoko iraboneka kugira ngo igenzurwe hanze.



### Inyungu





- Nta nzira y’ubuhinga ata VPN:** Ikintu gihambaye cane ni uko **Mullvad Browser itanyegeza IP yawe Address ubwayo** (nk’izindi browser zose, kiretse Tor Browser). IP Address yawe ni nka "Address yawe y'iposita" kuri Internet: irahishura aho uri n'ISP yawe. Biva rero cane kuri VPN** (virtuel privé réseau) kugira ngo uhishe ayo makuru ahambaye.





- Nta verisiyo yo kuri telefone ngendanwa:** Gushika ubu, Mullvad Browser iboneka kuri PC gusa (Windows, Mac, Linux).





- Ntibihuye n'ingeso zimwe zimwe:** **Uburyo bwihariye buhoraho** bisigura ko udashobora kuguma ukoresha igice kuva ku gukoresha kumwe gushika ku kundi. Ntibishoboka ko uguma uhuye na konti y’urubuga kuva ku kiganiro kimwe gushika ku kindi.





- Ibirango bibujijwe:** Kugira ngo urutoke rube rumwe, Mullvad Browser **yazimye ibirango vyinshi** biri muri Firefox kandi ntibigenewe guhindura.

Iyo utanguye gufungura Mullvad Browser, uzobona Interface isa cane n’iya Tor Browser. Mucukumbuzi iratunganijwe imbere y’igihe kugira ngo ikoreshe ubuzima bwite kandi ntibisaba guhindura ibintu bidasanzwe.

**Igihambaye:** Mucukumbuzi ya Mullvad ntiyipfuka IP yawe Address ku buryo busanzwe. Kugira ngo ukingirwe bimwe bishitse, turagusavye cane gukoresha VPN mu buryo bumwe. Ushobora gukoresha Mullvad VPN canke uwundi murongo wose wizigirwa wa VPN.



Ico gikoresho kandi kirimwo **DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH)** gikoresheje ubuhinga bwa DNS bwa Mullvad: ubu buhinga burashiramwo amakuru y’ibisabwa vyawe vya DNS (guhindura amazina y’imbuga mu ma aderesi ya IP) kugira ngo ISP yawe ntishobore kugenzura imbuga usura.

## Gukoresha buri musi



### Kugendera mu mazi bisanzwe



Mullvad Browser yitwararika nk’umucukumbuzi wa kera wo gucukumbuza buri musi. Imbuga zose zirashobora gushikira nk’uko bisanzwe, n’inyungu yongereweko yo gukingira gukurikirana.



### Ubuyobozi bwa kuki no kwinjira



Kubera uburyo bw’ibanga buhoraho, uzobwirizwa gusubira kwifatanya na konti zawe igihe cose winjiye. Ico ni co kiguzi uriha kugira ngo ubone ibanga ryinshi.



### Ukwagura



Mullvad Browser mu buryo bw'ubuhinga iraguha uburenganzira bwo gushiramwo ibindi bikoresho biva mu gitabu ca Firefox, ariko **ni ngombwa gutahura ingaruka**. Igikoresho cose congereweko gihindura ikirenge cawe ca digitale kandi kigutandukanya n’abandi bakoresha Mullvad Browser, ivyo bikaba biteye kubiri n’ingingo ngenderwako y’ishimikiro y’umucukumbuzi: gutuma abakoresha bose basa n’abasa.



Nk'uko Mullvad abisigura: *"Rimwe na rimwe, kutagira uburinzi bwihariye ni vyiza kuruta kugira uburinzi. Mu gushaka kwongerera ubuzima bwite bwo kuri interineti, ushiramwo ama extensions amaherezo atuma urushiriza kuboneka. "*



Niwahitamwo gushiramwo extensions uko biri kwose, menya neza ko uriko urema ikimenyetso c’urutoke kidasanzwe gishobora gukoreshwa mu kugukurikirana kuva ku rubuga ku rundi. Kugira ngo ukingirwe cane, ni vyiza ko wifatanya n’ivyo bikoresho bitatu vyashizweho imbere y’igihe, bikaba ari bimwe ku bakoresha bose.



## Ibikorwa vyiza n'umucukumbuzi wa Mullvad



1. **Igihe cose ukoreshe VPN:** Mucukumbuzi ya Mullvad ntiyipfuka IP yawe. VPN ni nkenerwa kugira umuntu ntamenyekane.



2. **Ntuhindure umucukumbuzi**: Irinde guhindura ama settings canke kwongerako extensions, kuko ivyo bishobora gutuma umenyekana.



3. **Koresha buto y'ikiringo gishasha**: Hagati y'ibikorwa bitandukanye, koresha igikorwa co gusubiramwo kugira ngo ugabanye ibikorwa vyawe.



4. **Hitamwo urugero rw’umutekano rujanye n’ivyo ukeneye**:




- Ivyasanzwe (vyiza)**: Ku bijanye no gusura buri musi. Amaze gutanga uburinzi bwiza cane mu gihe iguma ikora imbuga. Iyi ni yo nzira nziza cane ku 95% vy’abakoresha.
- Safer**: Niba uriko urasura imbuga zitazwi canke zishobora gutera akaga, canke kugira ngo ukingirwe cane ku nzira za Wi-Fi za bose. Hari imbuga zishobora gukora nabi.
- Itekanye cane**: Igenewe ibintu bishobora gutera ingorane nyinshi (itangazamakuru ry’itohoza, ivy’itumanaho bifise akamaro, ibidukikije vy’urwanko). Imbuga nyinshi zo muri iki gihe zizomeneka, ariko ico ni co gici- ro c’umutekano mwinshi.



5. **Uhora usuzuma ko ata mahinduka**: Gumana umucukumbuzi wawe ufise amakuru mashasha y’umutekano.



6. **Koresha ubuhinga bwo gushakisha bufasha ubuzima bwite**: Hitamwo DuckDuckGo, Urupapuro rwo gutangura canke Searx kuri Google.



7. **Gukoresha uburyo bwa HTTPS gusa**: Mu mategeko, urabe neza ko uburyo bwa "HTTPS gusa" bukoreshwa kugira ngo ukoreshe ku nguvu amahuzu atekanye.



8. **NTUHINDURE ama settings yose ateye imbere**: Bitandukanye n’izindi browser, Mullvad Browser yakozwe ku buryo abakoresha BOSE bafise configuration imwe nyene. Guhindura amategeko muri `about:config` canke guhindura amategeko ya uBlock Origin/NoScript vyotuma uba uwudasanzwe kandi ugakuraho burundu ubutamenyekana bwa mucukumbuzi.



## Impanuro ya DNS



Mucukumbuzi ya Mullvad yihita yinjiza Mullvad DNS-ku-HTTPS. Niba ukoresha Mullvad VPN, iyo extension izogusaba ko **uhagarika Mullvad DoH** kuko vyihuta gukoresha server ya DNS ya server yawe ya VPN. Niba udakoresha Mullvad VPN, guma ukoresha Mullvad DoH kugira ngo wirinde gukurikirana DNS na ISP yawe.



## Iciyumviro



Mullvad Browser ni umuti mwiza ku barondera gukoresha urubuga rwo gukoresha ubuzima bwite ata ngorane z’ubushobozi bw’urubuga rwa Tor. Ifatanijwe na VPN nziza, itanga uburinzi bukomeye ku gukurikirana no gucungera kuri internet.



Naho ifise aho igarukira (nta verisiyo yo kuri telefone ngendanwa, ibiganiro bitagumaho), Mullvad Browser ni igikoresho c’agaciro mu bubiko bw’ibirwanisho vy’umuntu wese yipfuza gusubira kugenzura ubuzima bwiwe bwite bwa digitale. Uko yoroshe gukoresha be n’uko itunganywa mbere y’igihe, bituma abayikoresha bakunda ubuzima bwite, baba abatangura canke abayizi.
