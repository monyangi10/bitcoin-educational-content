Amaduka y’ibikoresho arafise uruhara runini mu gukoresha ibikoresho vy’igikoresho cawe, kandi ahora ashirwa hamwe no gucengera bivanye n’aho uba. Ku matelefone ya Android, ububasha bwo gukoresha Google Play Store ubu ni ikintu ca kera, kandi turiko turabona ukuntu amaduka menshi y’amaporogarama akorana, agufasha kurinda amakuru yawe n’ubuzima bwawe bwa digitale - kandi vyose ku buntu.


Muri iyi nyigisho, turabona Aurora Store, uburyo bworoshe, bufunguye kandi butekanye bwo gusubirira Google Play Store.



# Gutangura n'iduka rya Aurora



Aurora Store ni igicuruzwa ca *Aurora Open Source Software*, ihuriro ry’abahinguzi n’abakunda ubuzima bwite ryibanda ku kwubaka ibikoresho vy’ubuntu, vy’inkomoko yuguruye vyubahiriza ubuzima bwawe bwa digitale n’umwidegemvyo wawe.


Aurora Store ni imwe mu maduka azwi cane y’ibindi bikoresho bishobora gukoreshwa na Google Play Store ku rubuga rwa Android kubera imvo zikurikira:




- Interface** itunganye: Interface ntigutera phase n’agatotsi, rero biroroshe kuronka ama bearings yawe.
- Ushobora gushika ku maporogarama amamiliyoni**: Aurora Store ikora nk’urugi rwo gushika ku maporogarama menshi yo kuri telefone ngendanwa. Nimba iyo porogaramu uriko urarondera iri ku iduka rya Google Play, uzoyisanga ku iduka rya Aurora.
- Nta serivisi ya Google Play ikenewe**: Kubera ubuhinga bwa MicroG, porogarama za Android zirashobora gukoreshwa kuri telefone yawe ata serivisi ya Google Play.



Ushobora gukuraho Iduka rya Aurora ku [urubuga rwemewe](https://auroraoss.com/aurora-store) ukanda kuri buto "Rekura" mu bwoko bw'inyubako. Hitamwo Vanilla nimba ukoresha telefone itari Huawei. Amatelefone ya Huawei aherutse gusohoka afise verisiyo yayo ya Aurora Store kubera uburenganzira bwayo bwo gukoresha Google kubera ibihano vya Amerika.



![download](assets/fr/01.webp)



Ushobora kandi gukuraho ububiko bwa Aurora mu yandi maduka y’ibikoresho nka F-Droid canke IzzyOnDroid.



https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/data/f-droid-2cd1aae5-7028-4c04-8fbe-95aeaf278ef4

Ariko rero, turagusavye gukura dosiye ya APK ku rubuga rwemewe kugira ngo umenye neza ko Aurora Store ari iyo ukuri kandi ko ataco ihinduye.



https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/data/integrity-authenticity-21d0420a-be02-4663-94a3-8d487f23becc

Dosiye ya APK (Android Package Kit) ni uburyo bwo gukoresha amaporogarama bukoreshwa na sisitemu ya Android mu gukwiragiza no gushiramwo porogarama. Ni nk’amadosiye **.exe** kuri Windows canke amadosiye **.dmg** kuri macOS.




Iyo porogarama imaze gukurwako, uyishiremwo n’amaboko maze uhe uburenganzira bwo gushiramwo porogarama ya Aurora Store.



![permissions](assets/fr/03.webp)



Kubera ko idasaba ibikorwa vya Google Play, urashobora kwifatanya n’iduka rya Aurora Interface ata mazina yawe maze ukaryoherwa n’ibikoresho vyose biri kuri Google Play Store. Iduka rya Aurora ni urubuga rwerekana uko iduka rya Google Play rikora. Nta n’imwe ifise, nta ruhusha canke ngo itanga amaporogarama yose.



![login](assets/fr/06.webp)



Mu guhuza ata mazina, Aurora Store irema e-mail y’igihe gito Address ihinduka buri masaha 24. Nta makuru y’ibanga abikwa kandi igihe cose wihuye n’ikiganiro kitazwi kirasohoka.


Mu mitunganyirize ya porogaramu, urashobora gusobanura incuro ivugurura n’uburyo bwo kuvugurura (bukora canke bukoreshwa n’amaboko) wipfuza gukoresha.



![update-modes](assets/fr/02.webp)



Uvuye kuri Aurora Store, urashobora gushika ku rutonde rw’ibikoresho biri kuri Google Play Store, ugasoma insobanuro zavyo, ukaronka inyishu z’abakoresha maze ukabishira kuri telefone yawe. Ariko ivyo ntibisigura ko ama application yose ari ubuntu. Nimba ubusabe bwawe butegerezwa kwishurwa kuri Play Store, uzokenera kubanza kwishura, ukoresheje ubusabe bwa Play Store canke usura urubuga rwemewe rwa Play Store.



## Utuntu dutandukanye



Aurora Store ntiyishushanya gusa Google Play Store, irafise n’ibintu biguha amakuru yose ukeneye ku bijanye n’amaporogarama ushaka gushiramwo. Ivyo bikoresho, Aurora Store iragushira mu bubasha bwo kugenzura amakuru yawe n’ubuzima bwawe bwa digitale.





- Ukwikukira kuri Google:**



Mu gihe ufata ingingo yo guhagarika ibikorwa vya Google Play kugira ngo ukinge amakuru yawe, canke ukoresheje telefone itagifasha ibikorwa vya Google, Aurora Store, kubera MicroG, umugambi w’inkomoko yuguruye, iraguha uburenganzira bwo gushiramwo no gukoresha porogarama z’amatelefone ngendanwa ziboneka kuri Google Play Store. Iyi nzira ni ngirakamaro cane cane iyo uri mu gihugu kiri mu bihano vya Amerika.



![microG](assets/fr/07.webp)





- Gukuraho ubugenzuzi:**



Aurora Store iragufasha gukuraho porogarama zo kuri telefone ngendanwa n’amaboko mu kugaragaza kode ya verisiyo ushaka gushiramwo. Gucungera gukuraho ni akarusho iyo ivyagezwe vy’iporogarama yawe bisaba amakuru menshi kuruta ayo bikeneye: urafise ububasha ku makuru yawe.



![manual](assets/fr/04.webp)





- Uruhusha:**


Mu ndondoro y’iporogarama wipfuza gushiramwo, rondera urutonde rw’uburenganzira uzoha iyo porogarama umaze kuyishiramwo.



![autorisations](assets/fr/05.webp)





- Isesengura ryo gukingira amakuru:**



Aurora Store ishiramwo Exodus, umuti usesangura kandi ukamenya abakurikirana bakoreshwa n’ibikoresho vyawe vya Android. Igikoresho co gukurikirana ni agace gatoyi gakorakoranya amakuru yerekeye wewe canke ivyo uriko urakora. Mu kugaragaza amakuru y’isesengura ry’Ukuvayo, Aurora Store iragufasha kumenya vy’ukuri ubwoko bw’amakuru ariko arakusanywa n’ikoreshwa ushaka gushiramwo.



![privacy](assets/fr/08.webp)



Ibirenze ububiko bw’ibikoresho gusa, Aurora Store ni umuti w’inkomoko yuguruye utuma ushobora kugenzura ingene amakuru yawe akoreshwa. Iraguha amakuru ngirakamaro yerekeye ibisabwa vyawe kandi ikagufasha kurinda ubuzima bwawe bwo kuri interineti kugira ngo ntibukoreshwe nabi. Kugira ngo ukomeze ibanga ryawe, menya inyigisho yacu y’umutekano w’amakuru y’ubuhinga bwa none: shiraho ikibanza c’ubuhinga bwa none c’umuntu ku giti ciwe, gitekanye kandi co kwizigirwa.
