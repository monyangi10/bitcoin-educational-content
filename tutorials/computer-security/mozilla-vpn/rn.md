Mu gihe c’ubuhinga bwa none co gukusanya amakuru, ubuzima bwite bwo kuri Internet bwacitse ikibazo gikomeye kuri twebwe abakoresha Internet. Hagati y’ugukurikirana amatangazo, ingorane zo gutera amakuru biciye ku mbuga za bose n’uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ahantu, abakoresha benshi bariko barahindukira bakoresha VPN (*Virtual Private Networks*) kugira ngo bashobore gucungera ubuhinga bwabo bwo gusura. Mu mahitamwo menshi bashobora gukoresha, hariho igikorwa kiva ku mushinge wa Mozilla, kizwi kubera Commitment yaco gushika kuri Internet y’ubuntu kandi y’inyifato runtu, kigaragara cane. Muri iyi nyigisho, turaza kuraba Mozilla VPN kugira ngo ushobore kugenzura ubuzima bwite bwawe bwa Internet.



## Mozilla VPN ni iki?



***Ihuriro ry’abantu ku giti cabo*** (VPN) ni uburyo bwo gukora uruja n’uruza hagati ya mudasobwa ziri kure zihuye n’imihora itandukanye yo mu karere. Mu yandi majambo, ni uburyo bwo gutandukanya no gupfuka ivyo uhindura n’ibindi bikoresho vyo kuri Internet. Kugira ngo umenye vyinshi ku vyerekeye VPNs, uko zikoreshwa n’ivyiza vyo gukoresha imwe, raba inyigisho ya SCU 101:



https://planb.network/courses/99c46148-7080-4915-a7e0-9df0e145cd47

Hashingiwe kuri iyo ngingo, [Mozilla VPN](ibicuruzwa) ni igikorwa ca VPN gifise inkomoko yuguruye categuwe mu 2020 n’ishirahamwe Mozilla Foundation. Iraboneka kuri:





- Android,
- iOS,
- Mac,
- Linukisi,
- Amadirisho,
- kandi nk'ukwagura umucukumbuzi wa Firefox wa Mozilla.



![download](assets/fr/01.webp)



Iboneka mu bihugu birenga 30 kandi ifise abakozi barenga 500 bajejwe gupfuka IP Address yawe kugira ngo bakwimurire mu gihe ushobora gucungera ibanga ry’ivyo ukora kuri Internet. Mozilla VPN itandukanye na:





- Ukworohereza gukoresha: igishushanyo ca Interface giteye igomwe, gifise agaciro gatoyi kikwereka ivy’ingenzi vy’ama server n’ibihugu ushobora guhitamwo.





- Ikoranabuhanga rya WireGuard: ni ubuhinga bwo guhanahana amakuru n’ubuhinga bufunguye bukoresha ubuhinga bwa none bwo gukingira amakuru kugira ngo bukore imigende y’amakuru, itanga uburyo busanzwe, bworoshe gukoresha bufise urufatiro rwa kode rutoyi kandi bushimika ku kwihuta no ku mutekano.





- Igiciro gitomoye: nk’amayero 10 ku kwezi n’amayero 5 ku kwezi ku kwiyandikisha ku mwaka.





- Ivyuma vyinshi bihuye: Huza ibikoresho bishika kuri 5 icarimwe kuri konti yawe ya Mozilla VPN.



## Gutangura na Mozilla VPN



Ushobora gukuraho [Mozilla VPN](ibicuruzwa/vpn/gukuraho/) bivanye n’uko ukoresha ubuhinga bwawe. Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko turaraba Mozilla VPN iri munsi ya sisitemu ikoresha Windows.



⚠️ Kubera ko Mozilla VPN itaboneka mu bihugu bimwe bimwe, ushobora guhura n’iryo tangazo iyo ukuye porogarama ya Mozilla VPN kuri Google Play Store.



![playstore](assets/fr/02.webp)



Iyo umaze gushiramwo neza, ukande kuri buto ya **Register** kugira ngo ufungure konti nshasha.



![inscription](assets/fr/03.webp)



Injira ijambobanga ryawe maze wemeze konti yawe mu kuzuza kode ya OTP yoherejwe kuri e-mail yawe Address. Kubera ko Mozilla VPN ari igikorwa cishurwa kiva ku kigo ca Mozilla, ukeneye kwiyandikisha kugira ngo ukoreshe Mozilla VPN ku bushobozi bwayo bwose. Fyonda kuri "Iyandikishe ubu" kugira ngo usubire kuja kuri paji y'ibiciro vya Mozilla VPN.



![tarifs](assets/fr/04.webp)



Hanyuma uhitemwo umugambi wo kwiyandikisha ubereye wewe.



![plans](assets/fr/05.webp)



Hanyuma ugende wishyure ukoresheje PayPal canke ikarita y’inguzanyo.



![paiement](assets/fr/06.webp)



Umaze gukoresha ukwiyandikisha kwawe, fungura porogarama ya Mozilla VPN maze, uko bigubereye, wemere Mozilla VPN gukusanya amakuru y’ubuhinga ku bijanye n’ugusura kwawe canke atarivyo.



![collecte](assets/fr/07.webp)



Ushobora kandi gukoresha uburyo bwo kurwanya kwamamaza, kugenzura umucukumbuzi no kubuza porogaramu mbi.



![privacy](assets/fr/08.webp)



Igihe igikorwa co gutunganya kirangiye, Mozilla VPN Interface isa n'iyi:



![home](assets/fr/09.webp)



Ushobora gukoresha VPN ukanda kuri buto ya radio iri musi, izotuma IP Address ya mudasobwa yawe ija ku rutonde rw’amaderesi ya IP mu kibanza wahisemwo. Ushobora kandi kubona urutonde rw’ibikoresho bihuye na konti yawe ya Mozilla VPN uhereye kuri paji y’intango.



![activate](assets/fr/10.webp)



Mozilla VPN iragufasha guhitamwo aho uri mu buryo bubiri:





- Single-Hop: yimurira IP Address ya mudasobwa yawe maze igashira amakuru ku server iri mu karere katoranijwe, mu karorero kacu Sofia muri Biélorussie.





- Multi-Hop: irema uruja n’uruza rw’ibanga ruva kuri mudasobwa yawe rukaja ku ma server abiri ari kure. Ivyo ni ugushiramwo amakuru kabiri: amakuru yawe ashirwako amakuru biciye kuri server A, hanyuma kuva kuri serveri A, amakuru asubira gushirwako amakuru kuri serveri B.



![hops](assets/fr/11.webp)



Mu gufyonda ku **Settings** icon, urashobora gushika ku mahitamwo atandukanye yo guhindura Mozilla VPN itanga. Mu **Imiterere y'urubuga**, ushobora gutunganya Mozilla VPN ku bikorwa vyawe vyose, canke ugahitamwo ibikoresho udashaka gukoresha Mozilla VPN. Iyi nzira ni imwe mu nshasha zitandukanya Mozilla VPN n’izindi VPN nyinshi: gucapura imigende.



![permissions](assets/fr/12.webp)



Split tunneling ni ikintu gifasha gutuma ushobora gukoresha bimwe mu bikoresho vyawe vyo kuri Internet biciye kuri VPN n’ibindi ata VPN mu gihe c’ikiganiro kimwe. Ivyo bishobora kuba ngirakamaro ku bijanye n’ugukoresha banki yo kuri internet canke ukundi gukoresha Internet kudakora neza na VPN.



⚠️Mozilla VPN itanga ubuhinga bwo gucapura ku bikoresho vyose kiretse MacOs na iOS. Mbabarira abakoresha Apple, ntimuzoshobora gukoresha iki gikoresho. Birashoboka ko bazobikora, kuko bemereye gusa iyo nzira biciye ku rubuga rwabo rwa Android ubu rero iriko iragwira ku zindi nzira zikoreshwa.



Igihe cose mu ntumbero yo gutuma abayikoresha bagira ibanga ryiza, Mozilla VPN ifise ubuhinga bwa **Kill Switch** buyishoboza guhagarika uruja n’uruza rwawe rwa Internet iyo VPN ihagaze kubera imvo iyo ari yo yose. Ivyo birinda IP Address yawe be n’ayandi makuru y’ibanga.
