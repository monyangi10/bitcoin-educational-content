Igihe cose ukanda, igihe cose ushaka, urubuga rwose usuye: umucukumbuzi wawe w’urubuga yaracitse ikintu gikomeye cane kigaburira ubuhinga bwo gucungera ubudandaji bwo kw’isi yose. Google Chrome, ikoreshwa n’abantu barenga miliyari 3, ihindura ubushakashatsi bwawe bwa misi yose bukaba amakuru y’inyungu ku bigo bikomeye vy’amatangazo. Mbere na Firefox, naho izwi nk'umucukumbuzi "w'inyifato runtu", ikoresha uburyo bwa telemetry ku buryo busanzwe burungika ingeso zawe zo gucukukura kuri Mozilla.



Ivyo bintu vy’ukuri biratuma umuntu yibaza ikibazo gihambaye: vyoba bishoboka ko umuntu ashobora guca kuri Internet ata n’umwe aguma amuta kandi akamugirako amakuru? Igishimishije, inyishu ni egome, kubera imigambi y’abanyagihugu isubiza uwukoresha hagati y’ivyo bimuhagaritse umutima.



LibreWolf irafise iyo filozofiya y’ukurwanya ivy’ubuhinga bwa none. Ubwonko bw’umuryango w’abahinguzi bigenga, iyi mucukumbuzi ihindura Firefox ikaba ingabo nyayo yo gukingira abagenzuzi bo kuri internet. Aho abashakashatsi b’ubudandaji barondera kuronka amahera, LibreWolf ikora ibihushanye n’ivyo: igutuma utaboneka ku bakurikirana mu gihe uzigama ubuhinga bwo gushakashaka bugenda neza kandi bugezweho.



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzobona ingene LibreWolf ishobora guhindura uburyo ukoresha urubuga, itanga uburinzi bukomeye bwo gukurikirana ataco ihinduye ku bushobozi canke ku bijanye n’urubuga.



![LIBREWOLF](assets/fr/01.webp)


*Isoko ry’umucukumbuzi w’urubuga: Chrome ni yo ihambaye n’ibice 65% vy’isoko, ikurikirwa na Safari na Edge. Ubu butegetsi buravyura ibibazo ku bijanye n’ubudasa bw’urubuga n’ubuzima bwite*



## Kumenyesha LibreWolf



**FreeWolf** ni urubuga rwuguruye rukomoka kuri Mozilla Firefox, rwateguwe kandi rubungabungwa n’umuryango wigenga w’abakunda porogarama z’ubuntu. Intumbero yayo nyamukuru ni ugutanga ubushakashatsi bwibanda ku buzima bwite bw’uwukoresha, umutekano n’umwidegemvyo.



Mu majambo nyayo, LibreWolf ikoresha moteri ya Gecko ya Firefox, ariko ifise ubuhinga butandukanye cane: aho Firefox itegerezwa guhuza ubuzima bwite n’ukworohereza gukoresha, LibreWolf ihitamwo ubuzima bwite ku buryo busanzwe. Umugambi ukuraho ikintu cose coshobora guhungabanya ubuzima bwite bwawe (telemetry, gukusanya amakuru, ibice biterwa inkunga) mu gihe ushiramwo imiterere y’umutekano yongerewe.



### Intumbero: ubuzima bwite n'umwidegemvyo



LibreWolf ifise intumbero yo kwongerera uburinzi bwo gukurikirana no gufata urutoke mu gihe yongera umutekano w’umucukumbuzi. Intumbero zayo nyamukuru ni:





- Kuraho telemetry yose n'ugukusanya amakuru muri Firefox
- Guhagarika ibikorwa binyuranye n'umwidegemvyo w'ukoresha, nk'ibice vya DRM
- Gukoresha **imiterere y'ubuzima bwite/umutekano** n'ibice vyihariye kuva mu ntango
- Iterambere ry'abanyagihugu ryemeza **uguseruka no kwigenga** ku nyungu z'ubudandaji



Muri make, LibreWolf yiyerekana ko ari "Firefox nk'uko yobaye iyo ubuzima bwite buba ari bwo buza imbere" - umucukumbuzi akuwubaha ku buryo busanzwe, ata yindi ntunganyo isabwa.



### Ibirango nyamukuru



Kuva mu ntango, LibreWolf itanga urutonde rw'ibintu vyerekeye ubuzima bwite:



**Nta telemetry canke gukusanya amakuru:** Bitandukanye na Chrome canke Firefox, zirungika imibare y'ikoreshwa, LibreWolf ntaco ikusanya mu gushakisha kwawe. Nta raporo z'impanuka, nta nyigisho z'abakoresha, nta vyiyumviro vyatewe inkunga.



**LibreWolf ikoresha uBlock Origin extension, imwe mu nzira nziza cane zibuza amatangazo n’izikurikirana ku isoko.** Ku buryo busanzwe, LibreWolf icungura ikintu cose coshobora kugukurikirana kuri interineti (amatangazo yinjira, inyandiko zo gukurikirana, amafaranga y’ibanga Mining).



**Igikoresho c'ubushakashatsi c'ibanga:** LibreWolf ihindura DuckDuckGo nk'igikoresho cayo c'ubushakashatsi c'intango, kidafise amateka y'ibibazo vyawe. Ibindi bikoresho vy’ubuzima bwite (Searx, Qwant, Whoogle) navyo biraboneka.



**Uburinzi bukomeye bwo kurwanya ibimenyetso vy’intoke:** Gufata ibimenyetso vy’intoke bituma umucukumbuzi amenyekana mu buryo budasanzwe biciye ku mitunganyirize yayo, mbere ata ma cookies. Kugira ngo ivyo bishoboke, LibreWolf ikoresha ubuhinga bwa RFP (Resist Fingerprinting) buva ku mugambi wa Tor, kugira ngo umucukumbuzi wawe abe rusangi uko bishoboka kwose. Ivyagezwe vyerekana ko Firefox isanzwe ari ~90% yihariye ku bikoresho nka coveryourtracks.eff.org, ugereranyije na ~10-20% gusa kuri LibreWolf (ivyo biharuro ni ivyerekana kandi bishobora guhinduka bivanye n’imiterere ya porogarama n’ibikoresho be n’ivyo gushiramwo).



![LIBREWOLF](assets/fr/07.webp)


**Urupapuro rw'ikigeragezo rwa EFF [Fuka Ivyiyumviro Vyawe](https://coveryourtracks.eff.org/) n'ubuto GERAZA UMUGAMBI WAWE.** Iyi paji ikoreshwa mu gusuzuma uburinzi ku bakurikirana n'ibimenyetso vy'intoke



![LIBREWOLF](assets/fr/08.webp)


*Funga igisubizo c'ikibazo ca Tracks zawe. Ubutumwa "ufise uburinzi bukomeye bwo gukurikirana urubuga" buragaragazwa, bugaragaza ubushobozi bw'uburinzi bwa LibreWolf.*



**LibreWolf ikoresha amategeko akomeye y'umutekano ku buryo busanzwe. Uburinzi bwo gukurikirana bwa Firefox burasunikwa ku rugero rwo gukurikirana ibihumbi, amakuru y’abandi n’ibintu bibi. Ikora kandi urubuga n'amakuru y'urubuga (*Total Cookie Protection*) kugira ngo ugabanye amakuru y'urubuga rwose, kandi igahagarika WebRTC (igabanura *abashaka ICE* no gukoresha inzira biciye ku nzira iyo hariho nzira) kugira ngo igabanye ingorane zo gusohoka kwa IP Address.**



**Impinduka zihuta za moteri:** Umugambi ukurikirana cane iterambere rya Firefox: LibreWolf yama ishingiye kuri verisiyo nshasha idahinduka ya Firefox, kandi abayibungabunga baraharanira gusohora verisiyo nshasha mu masaha 24 gushika kuri 72 kuva Firefox isohotse ku mugaragaro.



## Ivyiza n'ibibi



### Inyungu





- Nta telemetry canke amahuzu adakenewe:** LibreWolf nta makuru y'ikoreshwa irungika, igatuma ubuzima bwite bwawe bubaha.





- Umugambi ufunguye kandi ushingiye ku kibano:** Umugambi ni 100% ufunguye kandi utunganywa n’abakozi b’intahemuka. Ukwo kwigenga kuratanga icemeza ko ata citegererezo co kwamamaza kizogira ico gikoze ku bijanye n’iterambere.





- Ivyateguwe mbere ku bw'ubuzima bwite:** LibreWolf iragukiza umwanya w'agaciro: ntaco bimaze kumara amasaha ukomeza amasetingi ya Firefox, vyose vyaramaze gukorwa.





- Igikoresho co gukingira amatangazo/ugukurikirana:** uBlock Origin ishizwemwo nk’itegeko, rero ntubwirizwa gukora ikintu kugira ngo wikingire amatangazo n’ibikoko.





- Uburinzi bwiza cane bwo kurwanya ibimenyetso vy'intoke:** Kubera RFP n'imiterere myinshi y'ubuzima bwite, LibreWolf igabanya cane ibimenyetso vyawe vy'ubuhinga bwa none ku rubuga.





- Ibikorwa vyiza n'uburemere buke:** Mu gukuraho telemetry n'ibintu bimwe bimwe bitari ngombwa, LibreWolf ishobora kwihuta gatoyi kandi idakoresha ubushobozi bwinshi kuruta Firefox isanzwe.



### Inyungu n'aho bigarukira





- Nta bivugururwa vyubatswemwo:** LibreWolf ntiyivugurura. Ni wewe ushobora gushiramwo ama versions mashasha akimara gusohoka, kugira ngo ugume utekanye.





- Kugabanya uguhuza n'ibikorwa bimwe bimwe:** Kubera imiterere yayo ikomeye cane, LibreWolf ishobora guhura n'ingorane ku mbuga zimwe zimwe. Ivyuma vy’ugucapura vya Netflix na Disney+ ntibizokora, kuko LibreWolf izimya DRM ya Widevine ku buryo busanzwe.





- Nta nzira y'amazina yubatswemwo:** Bitandukanye n'umucukumbuzi wa Tor, LibreWolf ntiyobora uruja n'uruza biciye kuri Tor canke VPN ubwayo. Niba ukeneye ko urubuga rutamenyekana, uzokenera guhindura proxy/VPN n’amaboko.





- Amakuru n’ibihe bitagumaho (ivya kera):** Kubera imvo z’ibanga, LibreWolf irafuta amakuru, amateka n’amakuru y’urubuga igihe cose ufunze umucukumbuzi wawe. Uzokenera gusubira kwinjira muri konti zawe igihe cose winjiye.





- Nta verisiyo ya telefone ngendanwa canke guhuza igicu:** LibreWolf iboneka gusa ku biro (Windows, Linux, macOS). Nta porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa iriho, rero nta guhuza amakonti canke ibimenyetso vy’ibitabu biciye ku gicu.



## Gushiramwo LibreWolf



**Urubuga rwemewe:**



LibreWolf iraboneka ku bikoresho vyose bikomeye vy’ibiro: Linux, Windows na macOS. Kugira ngo ubone LibreWolf, sura urubuga rwemewe:



![LIBREWOLF](assets/fr/02.webp)


*Paje y'intango ya LibreWolf (librewolf.net) yerekana ikimenyetso c'umucukumbuzi, ubuto bw'ubururu bwo gushiramwo, n'amahuza y'Ikode y'Inkomoko n'Inyandiko. Umwampi munini w'ubururu werekana buto Install*



Fyonda kuri buto ya "Gushiramwo" kugira ngo ubone amabwirizwa arambuye kuri sisitemu yawe:

Gushiramwo biratandukanye bivanye na OS yawe:



### Kuri Linux


LibreWolf itanga inyubako ku bijanye n'ugukwirakwiza kwinshi. Ku Debian/Ubuntu n'ibiva kuri yo, ububiko bwa APT buzwi buraboneka. Ubundi, LibreWolf isohoka muri Flatpak kuri Flathub:


```
flatpak install flathub io.gitlab.librewolf-community
```



### Ku madirisha


Gukuraho installer (.exe) ku rubuga rwemewe canke ukoreshe:




- Isokoroti:** `isokoroti ishizeho librewolf`
- WinGet:** `amababa ashiraho librewolf`



### Ku macOS


LibreWolf iboneka nk'umurongo wa .dmg wa Mac. Kuvana ishusho ya disiki ku rubuga rwemewe maze ukureho porogaramu ya LibreWolf muri dosiye yawe y'Iporogarama. Canke rero, ushobora kuyishiramwo biciye kuri Homebrew.




## Gutunganya no gukoresha ubwambere



Mu gutangura kwa mbere, uzobona Firefox Interface imenyerewe, kiretse ko irengeye: urupapuro rw’intango rurimwo gusa umurongo w’ugushaka kandi nta vyiyumviro biterwa inkunga. Uzobona ikimenyetso c'inkomoko ya uBlock mu mwanyabikoresho - ikimenyetso c'uko umubuji akora.



![LIBREWOLF](assets/fr/04.webp)


*Paje y'intango ya LibreWolf ifise ivyungura n'ibikubiyemo. Umwampi w'ubururu uri hejuru iburyo werekana ikimenyetso c'ibirimwo (imirongo itatu iringaniye)*



LibreWolf ihita ishiramwo amapaji yawe mu buryo "bukomeye" (burwanya gukurikirana), kandi moteri y'ubushakashatsi izoba DuckDuckGo. Ushobora kugerageza gusura urubuga rwo gukurikirana (nk'akarorero amiunique.org) kugira ngo ubone ikirenge - bikwiye kuba rusangi kuruta uko ukoresha umucukumbuzi asanzwe.



### Amagenamiterere y'ubuzima bwite



LibreWolf irasanzwe itunganijwe neza kubera ubuzima bwite. Mu Menyu → Amahitamwo → Ubuzima bwite n’Umutekano, uzobona ko LibreWolf iteguwe ku buryo bwo Gukingira Ukurikirana: Bukomeye. Ubu buryo burabuza:




- Abakurikirana hagati y'urubuga
- Amakuki y'uwundi muntu
- Ibirimwo bizwi gukurikirana
- Gucukura amabuye y'agaciro
- Ibimenyetso vy'urutoke vy'ubuhinga bwa none



![LIBREWOLF](assets/fr/05.webp)


*Umutekano n'ubuzima bwite bw'urupapuro rwerekana LibreWolf.* amategeko y'umutekano



WebRTC irazimye (ibuza IP gusohoka), kandi Uburinzi bwose bwa Cookie buriho. Ubushakashatsi bwa Telemetry na Firefox burazimye rwose.



### Ubuyobozi bw'ibikuki n'amateka



Ku mburabuzi, LibreWolf irafuta amakuru n'ububiko bwo mu karere igihe cose ipfutse. Niba iyo nyifato igutera ubwoba, ushobora kuyihindura mu Bwite n'Umutekano → Amakuru n'amakuru y'urubuga mu gukuraho "Gukura amakuru n'amakuru y'urubuga igihe ufunga LibreWolf".



![LIBREWOLF](assets/fr/06.webp)


*Iyo paji nyene iri hasi gatoyi, yerekana uburyo bwo gukuraho amakuru y'ibanga iyo umucukumbuzi yugaye*



### Kwongerako ukwagura ngirakamaro



Nk'ingingo ngenderwako, LibreWolf iracisha bugufi kwongerako ivyungura bidakenewe, kuko ivyungura vyose bishobora kuba umurongo w'ugukurikirana. Nyamara, hariho ivyungura bishobora kwongereza ubumenyi bwawe:




- Ibikoresho vya Firefox vy'amakonti menshi** (na Mozilla) ku gushakisha mu bice
- Amaso yegerejwe** canke **LocalCDN** kugira ngo ukoreshe amasomero rusangi yo mu karere



Cane cane wirinde "VPN y'ubuntu" canke ama proxy ateye amakenga - uBlock Origin iramaze gutanga 99% vy'ivyo ukeneye.



## Gukoresha buri musi



### Gusura urubuga buri musi


Koresha LibreWolf mu bikorwa vyawe vya misi yose vyo kuri Internet. Itandukaniro rikomeye n’izindi browser ni uko usiga ibimenyetso bike cane vy’amatangazo. Emera cookie" amabendera arazimangana ku mbuga nyinshi, bivuye ku rutonde rw'ukuyungurura rwa uBlock.



### Koresha ama tab yihariye kugira ngo ushiremwo ibice


Naho LibreWolf ifuta vyose ku mpera y'ikiganiro, birashobora kuba vyiza gufungura idirisha ry'umucukumbuzi w'ibanga (Ctrl+Shift+P) ku bikorwa bimwebimwe mu gihe c'ikiganiro, kugira ngo ushobore gucapura akaranga kanaka.



### Ukoreshe neza ibikoresho vy'amakonti menshi


Gushiramwo igice c’ibikoresho vy’amakonti menshi birashobora kugufasha cane gucapura ibikorwa vyawe mu bibanza bitagira amazi. Nk'akarorero, urashobora gusobanura igikoresho ca "Banking" ku mbuga zawe za banki, igikoresho ca "Social Networks" ku Facebook/Twitter, n'ibindi. Igikoresho cose gifise ibifungurwa vyaco, ibihe n’ububiko bwihariye.



### Ubuyobozi bw'uburenganzira butunganijwe neza hakurikijwe urubuga


LibreWolf iragufasha kugenzura uruhusha uha imbuga (Ikibanza, Kamera, Mikoro, Amatangazo) ku buryo bubereye. Guha uruhusha gusa imbuga wizigira kandi aho bikenewe.



## Ibikorwa vyiza na LibreWolf



1. **Gumana LibreWolf ku gihe:** Suzuma urubuga ubudasiba kugira ngo ubone verisiyo nshasha, cane cane inyuma y'aho Firefox isohokeye.



2. **Irinde kuvanga akaranga k’umuntu n’ugucapura mu buryo bw’ibanga:** Ivyiza ni uko udakwiye kwinjira ukoresheje amakonti yawe bwite ku kiganiro kimwe uriko urakora ubushakashatsi buhambaye.



3. **Ntushiremwo LibreWolf n'ibindi bitari ngombwa:** Ivyongera vyose ushizemwo bishobora kuzana ingorane z'umutekano canke z'ibimenyetso vy'intoke.



4. **Koresha VPN canke proxy ya Tor mu kwongerako:** LibreWolf ntituma uba uwutamenyekana kuri ISP yawe. Ku bijanye n’ukutamenyekana kw’urubuga, ushobora gukoresha LibreWolf inyuma ya VPN yizigirwa.



5. **Bika amakuru yawe ahambaye:** Ibimenyetso, amajambo banga iyo abitswe aho hantu. Rimbura umucungerezi w’ijambobanga ry’inyuma (KeePassXC, Bitwarden) aho gucungera ijambobanga ry’ishimikiro ry’umucukumbuzi.



## Kugereranya n'izindi nzira



LibreWolf ni kimwe mu "bikoresho" vy'ibikoresho vy'ubuzima bwite:



**LibreWolf na Firefox:** LibreWolf ishika imaze gukomera kandi ata n'imwe ifise. Firefox iragumya akamaro ko gukorana n'ibikoresho vyinshi n'abakoresha benshi cane, ariko bisaba gutunganya n'amaboko kugira ngo ushikire urugero rw'ibanga rwa LibreWolf.



**Brave ikoresha kode ya Chrome/Chromium kandi igashiramwo uburyo bwo gukora ubucuruzi biciye muri porogarama yayo yo kwamamaza idasanzwe. LibreWolf, kuba ari umuryango Fork wa Firefox, igumya ibidukikije vya Mozilla ku buntu kandi nta sano ifise na Google.**



**LibreWolf vs Tor Browser:** Tor Browser ntishobora gusubirizwa n'ukutamenyekana mu gihe umuntu ariko aragenzurwa cane, ariko irateba kandi ntiyiyumvamwo neza mu gukoresha buri musi. LibreWolf, ku gusura buri musi ku rubuga rwa kera, iranyaruka cane kandi irakora.



**LibreWolf vs Mullvad Browser:** Mullvad Browser iraja mbere n'imbere mu gutuma ibintu bigenda neza, ariko bikaba bigabanya ubushobozi bwo gukoresha (ntibishoboka ko umuntu aguma afise amakuru yo kwinjira). LibreWolf itera uburinganire: ni iy’ibanga cane, ariko ikoreshwa ku musi ku musi.



## Iciyumviro



LibreWolf iserukira umuti mwiza cane ku barondera umucukumbuzi w'ibanga cane "wa misi yose" ataco baja kure cane ku bijanye n'ukutamenyekana kwa Tor. Ni amahitamwo meza ku barwanyi, abanyamakuru, abahinguzi canke abakoresha ububasha bashaka gukoresha urubuga rwa kera (rwihuta, rujanye n’imbuga za none) mu gihe bahunga gukurikirana amatangazo n’ubuhinga bukomeye.



Naho ifise aho igarukira (nta guhindura kwihuta, kugabanya ukuntu ishobora gukorana n’ibikorwa bimwebimwe), LibreWolf ni igikoresho c’agaciro mu bubiko bw’ibirwanisho vy’umuntu wese yipfuza gusubira kugenzura ubuzima bwiwe bwite bwa digitale. Uko yoroshe gukoresha be n’uko itunganywa mbere y’igihe bituma ari ihitamwo ryiza ku bakoresha bakunda ubuzima bwite.
