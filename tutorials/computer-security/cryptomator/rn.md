## I. Ugushikiriza



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokoresha porogarama ya Cryptomator kugira ngo dushire amakuru abitswe mu gicu, yaba kuri Microsoft OneDrive, kuri Google Drive, kuri Dropbox, kuri Box canke mbere kuri iCloud.



Gushiramwo amakuru ubika ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi nka Drive ni uburyo bwiza bwo kurinda amadosiye yawe n’ubuzima bwite bwawe. Kubera encryption, ni wewe wenyene ushobora gufungura no gusoma amakuru yawe, naho yoba abitswe kuri server yo muri USA canke mu kindi gihugu kw’isi yose.



Muri iki kigereranyo, hazokoreshwa imashini ya Windows 11 22H2 ifise OneDrive, ariko uburyo bwo kubikora burasa n’ubwo ku zindi verisiyo za Windows no ku zindi nzira zo kubika. Ico ukeneye gukora n’ugushiramwo umukiriya w’uguhuza maze ukongerako konti yawe. Ivyo bizotuma Cryptomator ishobora kubika amakuru yayo mu nzu y’ububiko.



![Image](assets/fr/020.webp)



Cryptomator ni ubundi buryo bwo gukoresha ibindi bikoresho, cane cane Picocrypt yerekanwa mu kindi kiganiro, isa n’iyindi, ariko yoroshe gukoresha. Cryptomator nayo ni **inkomoko yuguruye**, yubahiriza RGPD, kandi izo **gupfuka amakuru n'ubuhinga bwo gupfuka amakuru bwa AES-256**. Mu buryo bunyuranye, Picocrypt yizigira ubuhinga bwihuta cane bwa XChaCha20 (na bwo nyene bufise 256-bit).



https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/data/picocrypt-98c213bd-9ace-425b-b012-bea71ce6b38f

Porogaramu ya Cryptomator iraboneka kuri **Windows** (exe / msi), **Linux**, **macOS,** ariko kandi **Android** na **iOS**. N’ubundi, porogarama zose ni ubuntu, kiretse porogarama ya Android, iyo ubwirizwa kwishura (14,99 euros).



Ku mashini yawe, **Cryptomator izokora dosiye muri yo izokora safe**. Mu nzu y’ububiko, ishobora kubikwa kuri OneDrive yawe, Google Drive canke ibindi bisa n’ivyo, amakuru yawe azoba ashizwe mu nzira. Rero, iyo ubitse amakuru yawe yose mu safe hosted ku kibanza co kubikamwo Drive yawe, azokingirwa (kuko ari encrypted).



**Iciyumviro**: muri iyi nkuru, ububiko bwo kuri internet burakoreshwa nk’akarorero, ariko Cryptomator ishobora gukoreshwa mu gushiramwo amakuru kuri disiki yo mu karere, kuri disiki yo hanze canke mbere kuri NAS. Nta n’ivyo umuntu abuza, mu vy’ukuri.



## II. Gushiramwo umuduga w'ibanga



Kugira ngo utangure, ukeneye **gukuraho** no **gushiramwo** **Cryptomator**. Iyo umaze gukuraho, gukanda bikeyi ni vyo vyonyene bisaba kugira ngo urangize gushiramwo. Nka [Rclone](Rclone](rclone-iyi-gukoresha-ubuntu-gusuka-gukoresha-igicu-), Cryptomator izokwizigira WinFsp kugira ngo **ishireko umuduga w'ivyuma ku mashine yawe ya Windows**.

## III. Gukoresha ubuhinga bwo gukingira amabanga kuri Windows



### A. Rema ububiko bushasha



Kugira ngo ureme ububiko bushasha, kanda kuri buto ya "**Ongera**" hanyuma uhitemwo "**Ububiko bushasha...**". Amasafe yawe ariho kandi azwi kuri iyi mashini azoca abonekera muri Interface, ibubamfu. **Ikigega cakozwe ku mashine A gishobora gufungurwa no guhindurwa ku mashine B**, igihe cose gifise Cryptomator (kandi urufunguzo rwo gupfuka ruzwi).



![Image](assets/fr/015.webp)



Tangana n’uguha izina ikibanza c’ububiko, nk’akarorero. "**IT-Ihuza**". Ivyo bizotuma haba ububiko bwitwa "**IT-Connect**" muri OneDrive yanje.



![Image](assets/fr/011.webp)



Mu ntambwe ikurikira, Cryptomator ishobora **kumenya "Drive "** iri ku mashini yawe: Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, n'ibindi.... Kugira ngo ushobore guhitamwo uwuguha amakuru ataco uhinduye. Ivyo nabigerageje ku mashini zibiri zitandukanye za Windows 11, zifise Drives nyinshi, ntivyamenyekanye. Si ikibazo, gusa sobanura "**Ikibanza cawe**" hanyuma uhitemwo umuzi w'ububiko bwawe. Nk'akarorero: **C:\Abakoresha\<Izina ry'Ukoresha>\OneDrive**.



![Image](assets/fr/018.webp)



Igikurikira, urashobora guhindura amahitamwo ari munsi y’imiterere y’abahinga.



![Image](assets/fr/021.webp)



Igikurikira, ukeneye gusobanura **ijambobanga rihuye n'urufunguzo rwo gupfuka**. Iri jambobanga rizogufasha **gufungura safe yawe ya Cryptomator** no gushika ku makuru yayo. **Iyo uyitakaje, uratakaza uburenganzira bwo kuronka amakuru yawe**. Ubwa nyuma, uracari n'uburenganzira bwo **guhingura urufunguzo rwo gusubiza inyuma** mu gusuzuma uburyo bwa "**Ego, birarushiriza kuba vyiza kuruta kubabarira**", vyinshi mu mpwemu imwe n'urufunguzo rwo gusubizaho [BitLocker] ( Ivyo ni vyiza, ariko ntubike urufunguzo rwo gusubiza inyuma ku muzi wa OneDrive yawe!



Fyonda kuri "**Rema umutekano**".



![Image](assets/fr/019.webp)



Kopa urufunguzo rwo gusubizaho maze urubike mu mucungerezi w’ijambobanga ukunda. Fyonda kuri "**Ibikurikira**".



![Image](assets/fr/013.webp)



Nivyo, igikapu cawe gishasha cararemwe kandi giteguwe gukoreshwa!



![Image](assets/fr/014.webp)



### B. Imibare yo gushikira



Kugira ngo ushikire safe n'amakuru yayo, haba **ugusoma amakuru asanzwe canke kwongerako amakuru mashasha**, ukeneye **kuyifungura**. Cryptomator itanga urutonde rw'ibikoresho bizwi: ibikoresho vya IT-Connect biraboneka, rero uhitemwo gusa maze ukande kuri "**Ufungure**".



![Image](assets/fr/016.webp)



Utegerezwa kwinjiza ijambobanga ryawe kugira ngo ufungure iyo safe. Hanyuma ukande kuri "**Rekura umuduga**".



![Image](assets/fr/022.webp)



**Ikigega cawe gishizwe ku mashini ya Windows nk’umuduga w’ivy’impwemu.** Uwo muduga, aha uragwa urudome E, uraguha uburenganzira bwo kuronka amakuru yawe (mu nyandiko rusangi, kuko ikigega kifunguwe).



![Image](assets/fr/017.webp)



Ku ruhande rwa OneDrive, ntidushobora guca mu nzu y’ububiko bwa Cryptomator ataco duciyemwo. Ntidushobora kubona amakuru (yaba amazina ya dosiye canke ibirimwo). Ivyo bisigura ko udakeneye kwongerako amakuru ku bubiko bwawe bwa Cryptomator biciye ku nzira ngufi isanzwe ya OneDrive. **Utegerezwa kwongerako amakuru yawe ukoresheje umuduga wa Cryptomator.**



![Image](assets/fr/012.webp)



### C. Amahitamwo y'umubiri



Amategeko y'ico kigega ashobora gushikwako biciye ku buto "**Encrypted volume options**" (iyo yugarijwe) kandi azotuma ifungura ubwayo iyo ataco ukora, nk'uko ushobora kubigira ku kigega cawe c'ijambobanga. Ihitamwo rya "**Gufungura umuvuduko w'ibanga igihe utangura**", nk'uko izina ryaryo rivyerekana, rifungura umuduga ataco ukora ku ruhande rwawe, rikaduza umuduga w'ukuri. Kubera imvo z’umutekano, ni vyiza kwirinda gukoresha iyo nzira.



Biciye ku rubuga rwa "**Mounting**" ushobora kandi guhitamwo kuyishirako gusoma gusa canke gutanga urudome rwihariye.



![Image](assets/fr/024.webp)



Ikindi, mu mitunganyirize ya Cryptomator, urashobora **gukoresha gutangura kw'ikoreshwa ry'ibikorwa**.



## IV. Iciyumviro



Na **Cryptomator**, urashobora **gukora ububiko bw'amakuru** mu minota mikeyi gusa kugira ngo ukinge amakuru wipfuza kubika kuri OneDrive n'abafatanyabikorwa. Biroroshe cane gukoresha iyo bije mu "kuyifatanya" na Drive: kubera iyo ntumbero, irafise ivyo nkunda kuruta Picocrypt.
