## Ni igiki kibi iyo ukoresheje mudasobwa isanzwe?


Igihe ukora amafaranga Bitcoin, ni vyiza iyo mudasobwa yawe idafise porogarama mbi. Birumvikana.


Niwagumiza ijambo ryawe rya Bitcoin seed (kenshi amajambo 12 canke 24) kure ya mudasobwa ufise igikoresho co gusinya (nk’akarorero Hardware Wallet – intumbero yayo nyamukuru), rero woshobora kwiyumvira ko atari ngombwa cane kugira mudasobwa “isukuye” – si ukuri.


Mudasobwa ifise ubumara bushobora gusoma amaderesi yawe ya Bitcoin, igashira ahabona umubare wawe ku muntu atera – ntibashobora gutwara Bitcoin gusa mu kumenya Address, ariko barashobora kubona ingene ufise, maze bagaharura kuri ivyo nimba uri umuntu akwiriye gutera. Bashobora kandi mu buryo bumwe canke bundi gukora aho uba, nk’akarorero, bakagukuramwo inzara canke abana kugira ngo baguhe incungu.


## None umuti ni uwuhe?


Ndahimiriza benshi mu ba Bitcoiners gukoresha mudasobwa yihariye idafise malware (ifise internet) mu gukora amafaranga y’ubudandaji bwa Bitcoin. Ndasaba abantu gukoresha ubuhinga bwo gukoresha bufise inkomoko yuguruye nka Linux Mint, ariko bakoreshe Windows canke Mac nimba bikenewe – ivyo ni vyiza kuruta gukoresha mudasobwa isanzwe, ikoreshwa neza, ihora ifise ibintu bibi vyihishije muri yo.


Intambamyi imwe abantu bahura na yo ni ugushiramwo ubuhinga bushasha bwo gukoresha kuri izo orodinateri. Iyi nkuru ni iyo gufasha muri ivyo.


Hari ubwoko bwinshi bwa Linux kandi naragerageje nyinshi. Impanuro yanje ku ba Bitcoiners ni Linux Mint, kuko yoroshe gushiramwo, yihuta cane (cane cane ku gufungura no gufunga), ntivyimba (igice cose c’inyongera ca porogarama ni ingorane), kandi ntabwo ari kenshi yantera canke yigenza mu buryo budasanzwe (ugereranije n’izindi verisiyo nka Ubuntu na Debian).


Bamwebamwe boshobora guhangana cane n’uburyo bushasha bwo gukoresha, bagahitamwo Windows canke Mac OS. Ndatahura, ariko Windows na Apple ni closed source, rero dutegerezwa kwizigira ivyo zikora; Sinzi ko iyo ari politike nziza, ariko si vyose-canke-ntaco. Nzohitamwo cane ko abantu bakoresha mudasobwa ya Windows canke Mac OS iherutse gushirwamwo aho gukoresha mudasobwa ikoreshwa neza (n’uwuzi ivy’ibintu bibi vyirundaniye kuri yo). Intambwe imwe nziza ni ugukoresha mudasobwa Linux iherutse gushirwamwo, ivyo nivyo nzoba ndiko ndavyerekana.


Niba ufise ubwoba bwo gukoresha Linux kubera ibitazwi, ivyo ni ibisanzwe, ariko ni ko biri no kumara umwanya wiga. Amakuru menshi cane araboneka kuri Internet. Aha niho hari video ngufi nziza cane yerekana ivy’ishimikiro vy’umurongo w’amabwirizwa ndabashimira cane.

Hitamwo mudasobwa


Nzotangura n’ico mbona ko ari co ciza kuruta ibindi vyose. Hanyuma nzotanga iciyumviro canje ku yindi nzira.


Amahitamwo meza:


Impanuro yanje, nimba ushobora kuyigura, kandi nimba ubunini bw’ikirundo cawe ca Bitcoin bubishingira intahe, ni ukuronka laptop nshasha y’urugero rwo kwinjira. Icogereranyo c’ishimikiro kuruta ibindi vyose cubatswe muri iyi misi ni ciza bihagije kugira ngo gishobore gukora ivyo kizokoreshwa. Ivyerekeye processeur na RAM ntaco bimaze, kuko vyose bizoba vyiza bihagije.


Kwirinda:



- Igikoresho cose co gukoresha, harimwo Surface Pro
- Chromebooks – kenshi ubushobozi bwo kubika burake cane
- Mudasobwa iyo ari yo yose ifise umuduga wa eMMC; Niba ifise umuduga wa SSD, ivyo ni vyiza
- Macs – zirazimvye, kandi ibikoresho ntibikorana neza n’ubuhinga bwa Linux mu bumenyi mfise
- Ikintu cose cavuguruwe canke 2nd hand (si ikintu co gusenyura amasezerano naho)

## Ayandi mahitamwo – Imirizo


Tails ni ubuhinga bukoreshwa buva ku gikoresho ca USB, kandi bugafata mu gihe gito ibikoresho vya mudasobwa iyo ari yo yose. Ikoresha amahuzu ya Tor gusa, rero urakeneye gukoresha neza Tor. Nta n’imwe mu makuru wandika mu mutwe mu gihe c’ikiganiro cawe ibikwa kuri drive (itangura bushasha igihe cose) kiretse uhinduye ama settings maze ugakora uburyo bwo kubika ubudasiba (ku drive ya USB) – iyo uyifunga n’ijambobanga.


Si uburyo bubi kandi ni ubuntu, ariko ni ikintu giteye ubwoba ku ntumbero yacu. Gushiramwo porogarama nshasha si umuyaga. Ikintu ciza kimwe ni uko izana na Electrum, ariko ikibi c’ivyo ni uko utayishizeho wewe nyene. Urabe neza ko USB ukoresha ari nibura 8Gb.


Uguhinduranya kwawe kuragabanywa iyo ukoresheje Tails. Ushobora kutabasha gukurikiza ubuyobozi butandukanye kugira ngo ushireho ivyo ukeneye maze ubikore neza. Nk’akarorero, niwakurikiza ubuyobozi bwanje bwo gushiramwo Bitcoin core, hariho ivyo ukeneye guhindura kugira ngo ikore. Sinumva ko nzoba ndiko ndakora uburongozi bwihariye bwa Tails, rero uzokenera kwubaka ubuhinga bwawe ukabikora wenyene.


Nanje nyene sinzi neza ingene ama wallets y’ibikoresho azokorana neza n’iyi OS.


Ivyo vyose bimaze kuvugwa, mudasobwa ya Tails yo gukoresha Bitcoin ni uburyo bwiza bwo kwongerako, kandi nta gukeka ko izofasha ubuhinga bwawe bwose bwo gucungera ubuzima bwite kwiga gukoresha Tails.


## Ayandi mahitamwo – Live OS Boot


Ivyo bisa cane na Tails, kiretse ko ubuhinga bwo gukoresha butagira ubuzima bwite. Uburyo nyamukuru bwo gukoresha ivyo ni uguca flash kuri USB ifise ubuhinga bwa Linux uhisemwo maze ugatuma mudasobwa itangura kuri iyo aho gukoresha iyo drive yo mu mutima. Uko ivyo bikorwa birasigurwa mu nyuma.


Ivyiza ni uko udafise uburenganzira bwinshi kandi ibintu bizogenda ata n’ibintu biteye imbere.


Sinzi neza ingene iyo sisitemu itandukanya neza ibintu bibi biri kuri mudasobwa iriho n’ivyo ukoresha bifise iyo sisitemu nshasha. Birashoboka ko ikora akazi keza kandi birashoboka ko atari nziza nka Tails. Kuko n’uko ntazi, ico nkunda ni laptop yihariye.

Ayandi mahitamwo – Mudasobwa yawe bwite ukoresha canke mudasobwa yo ku biro


Gukoresha mudasobwa ikoreshwa si vyiza, ahanini kubera ko ntazi ingene porogarama mbi zikomeye zikora imbere mu mutima, canke nimba guhanagura umuduga bihagije kugira ngo umuntu ayikureho. Birashoboka ko ari ariko sinshaka gufata minenerwe ingene abasuma b’abanyabwenge bashobora kuba. Ushobora gufata ingingo, sinshaka gukora.


Niwahitamwo gukoresha desktop ya kera aho gukoresha laptop ya kera, ivyo bizoba vyiza, kiretse ko bizotwara umwanya ubudasiba ku bikorwa vyawe vy’ama Bitcoin bishobora kuba ari bike; ntukwiye kuba uriko urakoresha ikindi kintu. Ariko iyo ufise laptop, ushobora kuyishira gusa, mbere ukayihisha kugira ngo ugire umutekano mwinshi.


## Gushiramwo Linux Mint kuri mudasobwa iyo ari yo yose


Aya ni amabwirizwa yo gukuraho ubuhinga bwose bwo gukoresha kuri laptop yawe nshasha maze ugashiramwo Linux Mint, ariko ushobora kuyihindura kugira ngo ushiremwo verisiyo yose ya Linux kuri mudasobwa iyo ari yo yose.


Tugiye gukoresha mudasobwa iyo ari yo yose kugira ngo dushireko ubuhinga bwo gukoresha mudasobwa ku gicapo c’ubuhinga bumwe bumwe. Ntaco bimaze ko ari memory stick iyihe, igihe cose ihuye n’ibarabara rya USB, kandi ndasaba ko yoba 16Gb minimum.

Inyuma y’ivyo, uhitemwo Flash! Ushobora gukenera kwinjiza ijambobanga ryawe. Iyo irangiye, iyo drive ntabwo izoshobora gusomwa na mudasobwa yawe ya Windows canke Mac kuko yahinduwe igikoresho ca Linux. Uyikuremwo gusa.

Gutegura mudasobwa y'intumbero


Hindukiza laptop nshasha, kandi mu gihe iriko irafungura, ufate urufunguzo rwa BIOS. Ivyo ni F2, ariko bishobora kuba F1, F8, F10, F11, F12 canke Gukuraho. Gerageza kimwe cose gushika ugibonye, ​​canke urondere kuri internet modele ya mudasobwa yawe maze ubaze ikibazo kibereye.


Nk’akarorero “urufunguzo rwa BIOS ama laptops ya Dell”.


Buri mudasobwa izogira menu ya BIOS itandukanye. Gutohoza no kurondera menu iguha uburenganzira bwo gutunganya urutonde rw'ugutangura. Kubera intumbero zacu, dushaka ko mudasobwa igerageza gufungura ku gikoresho gifatanye na USB (niba hariho gihuye), imbere y’uko igerageza gufungura ku gikoresho co mu mutima ca Hard (ahandi ho Windows izofunguka). Iyo umaze kubishiraho, ushobora gukenera kubika imbere yo gusohoka canke bishobora kubika ubwo nyene.


Reboot mudasobwa kandi ikwiye gufunguka ivuye ku gikoresho co kwibuka ca USB. Ntidushobora gushiramwo Linux kuri drive y’imbere kandi Windows izokurwaho burundu.


Niwashika ku rubuga rukurikira, uhitemwo “OEM install (ku bahinga)”. Niwahitamwo “Start Linux Mint”, uzoronka igice ca Linux Mint gishizwe ku gikoresho co kwibuka, ariko iyo umaze gufunga mudasobwa, nta makuru yawe azigama – ni igice c’igihe gito kugira ngo ushobore kukigerageza.


![image](assets/10.webp)


Uzojanwa biciye ku muhinga w’ibishushanyo azokubaza ibibazo vyinshi bikwiye kuba bigororotse. Imwe izoba ari Languange settings, iyindi izoba ari urubuga rwa internet rwo muhira n’ijambobanga. Niba usabwe gushiramwo izindi porogarama, nuziyanke. Iyo ushitse ku kibazo c’ubwoko bwo gushiramwo, hari abantu boshobora gukekeranya – ukeneye guhitamwo “Gukuraho disiki maze ushiremwo Linux Mint”. Kandi, ntushire amakuru ku muduga kandi ntuhitemwo LVM.


Uzoheza ushike ku biro. Muri iki gihe, nturaheza neza. Mu vy’ukuri uriko urakora nk’umuhinguzi (i.e. umuntu yubaka mudasobwa agashiraho Linux ku mukiriya). Ukeneye gukanda kabiri ku kimenyetso co ku biro, “Shiraho Linux Mint”, kugira ngo uyirangize.


![image](assets/11.webp)


Ibuka gukuraho memory stick, hanyuma wongere ufungure. Inyuma yo gusubira gufungura, uzoba uriko urakoresha ubuhinga bwo gukoresha ku ncuro ya mbere nk’umukoresha mushasha. Urakoze cane.


Kimwe mu bintu vya mbere umuntu akwiye gukora (kandi akabikora ubudasiba), ni ugutuma urutonde ruguma rujanye n’igihe.


Gufungura porogaramu ya Terminal, wandike ibi bikurikira:


```bash
sudo apt-get update
```


hit <enter>, wemeze ihitamwo ryawe, hanyuma iri tegeko:


```bash
sudo apt-get upgrade
```


hit <enter> maze wemeze ihitamwo ryawe.


Reka bikore ivyo bishaka, vyoshobora gutwara iminota myinshi.


Igikurikira, ndakunda gushiramwo Tor (ifatanye n'inyuguti nini):


```bash
sudo apt-get install tor
```


**Impanuro:** Ushobora kandi gukoresha boot ya Linux Mint ukoresheje “OEM install” (Urabe neza ko uhuye na internet, ahandi ho woshobora kuronka amakosa). Niwabikora, mu nyuma uzokenera gukanda ku kimenyetso “ship to end user” gikwiye kuba kiri ku biro. Uca usubira gufungura maze ugatangura gukora nk’aho woba uriko urafungura mudasobwa ari bwo bwa mbere.


Iyi nkuru yasiguye igituma ushobora gukenera mudasobwa yihariye ku bikorwa vya Bitcoin, n’ingene woyishiramwo ubuhinga bushasha bwo gukoresha Linux Mint.
