Jami ni porogarama y’ubutumwa bw’ubuntu, yuguruye, ifise amateka maremare kandi ifise ibintu vyinshi. Amateka yayo asubira mu kwezi kwa kigarama 2004, igihe *Savoir-faire Linux* i Montreal yatanguza SFLPhone, umugambi w’amatelefone y’ubuhinga bwa none ku bucuruzi ushingiye gusa ku buhinga bushasha. Iyo porogarama, yari ihuye n’ingingo ngenderwako z’itumanaho (SIP na IAX), yari igaragara cane kubera ubushobozi bwayo bwo gukorana n’imirongo myinshi n’amatelefone menshi.



Mu mwaka w’2015, SFLPhone yahinduwe izina yitwa Ring, kandi yinjiza ubuhinga bwo kwubaka butagikeneye server nyamukuru. Umwaka wakurikiyeho, Ring yinjiye ku mugaragaro mu mugambi wa GNU, ikomeza ikirenge cayo mu bidukikije vy’amaporogarama y’ubuntu. Ubwa nyuma, mu kwezi kwa kigarama 2018, kugira ngo ntibavyivangane n'ibikoresho vy'ubudandaji bakoresheje ijambo ry'icongereza "*Ring*", iyo porogarama yaciye ifata izina ryayo ry'ubu: Jami. Kuva ico gihe, ryarabandanije gutera imbere nk’urubuga rwo guhanahana amakuru ku buntu, rwigenga kandi rudafise ubuzima bwite.



Ubu, Jami iraboneka ku bikoresho vyinshi. Ni izwi cane kubera ubushobozi bwayo, uburyo bugenda neza kandi yoroshe gukoresha. Iragufasha kuvugana n’abandi biciye ku butumwa buca buza, guhamagara amajwi canke guhamagara amasanamu, mu gihe uzigama ibanga ry’ibiyago vyawe biciye ku gushiramwo amakuru kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera. Gushiramwo vyoroshe n’ibintu vyinshi biyigira porogarama yuzuye yo guhanahana amakuru yoroshe kandi ibereye gukoresha ku musi ku musi.

## Ni kuki ukoresha Jami?





- Ni **open-source kandi ni ubuntu bwose**, rero urashobora kuyikoresha ataco urishe.
- Yuzuye n’ibintu vy’ingirakamaro**: iyi porogarama igufasha kwungukira ku mahitamwo menshi, bivuye ku bushobozi bwo kwongerako plugins mu buryo bworoshe uhereye mu iduka. Birashoboka mbere ko wikorera ivyungura vyawe kugira ngo ubone ibintu bihuye n'ivyo ukeneye.
- Biroroshe gukoresha kandi Interface intuitive**: naho Jami afise ibintu vyinshi, biracari vyoroshe cane gufata.
- Umutekano ukomeye**: Jami ishiramwo ubuhinga buteye imbere bwo gukingira umutekano w’amakuru yawe mu gihe wubaha ubuzima bwite bwawe.
- Ishobora gushikirwa cane kandi yihuta**: itanga ubutumwa bworoshe n’igihe bandwidth ari nke, kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukoresha neza.

### Ku bakoresha linux



Kugira ngo ushire Jami kuri Linux, ni vyiza ukoresheje umurongo w’amabwirizwa. Ni vyiza kumenya ko Jami iboneka ku bikoresho bitandukanye vya Linux. Imbere y’uko utangura gushiramwo Jami, urabe neza ko uhisemwo uburyo bwo gutanga amakuru bubereye kuri sisitemu yawe.



Umaze guhitamwo uko utanga, urashobora gushiramwo iyo sisitemu. Uzokenera gushiramwo ibikenewe kugira ngo ukoreshe Jami kuri Linux OS yawe. Amabwirizwa araboneka ataco akora kuri [iyi paji](https://jami.net/gukura-jami-linux/).



![0_06](assets/fr/06.webp)



Kugira ngo ushire Jami kuri **Ubuntu**, ushobora kubikora gutya:



```shell
sudo apt install gnupg dirmngr ca-certificates curl --no-install-recommends
```



Iri tegeko rishiramwo ibikoresho bikenewe kugira ngo ushobore gucunga imfunguruzo za GPG (gnupg na dirmngr), ivyemezo vya SSL (ca-certificates) n'igikoresho co gukuraho curl.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/computer-security/operating-system/ubuntu-78a3be56-5d51-4ec3-8629-0dd27c352ab5

```shell
curl -s https://dl.jami.net/public-key.gpg | sudo tee /usr/share/keyrings/jami-archive-keyring.gpg > /dev/null
```



Aha, curl ikoreshwa mu gukuraho urufunguzo rwa bose rwa GPG rwa Jami. Guhindura /dev/null birinda kugaragaza urufunguzo rudasanzwe ku mugaragaro.



```shell
sudo sh -c "echo 'deb [signed-by=/usr/share/keyrings/jami-archive-keyring.gpg] https://dl.jami.net/stable/ubuntu_25.04/ jami main' > /etc/apt/sources.list.d/jami.list"
```



Iri tegeko ryongera ububiko bwa Jami buzwi ku rutonde rw'inkomoko za APT.



```shell
sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get install jami
```



Ubwa nyuma, hindura urutonde rw'amapaki ariho na `apt-get update`, hanyuma ushiremwo Jami uhereye ku bubiko buzwi na `apt-get install jami`.



## Jami imiterere y'ishimikiro



Jami amaze gushirwa kuri system yawe, urashobora kuyitanguza ukoresheje menu ya system.



Uhejeje gutangura iyo porogarama, uzoba ufise uburenganzira bwo gufungura konti canke gukomeza n’iyo umaze gufungura.



![0_07](assets/fr/07.webp)



### Rema konti



Gukora konti ya Jami ni ikintu coroshe cane. Ntukeneye e-mail Address canke inomero ya telefone: Jami akoranya amakuru makeyi gusa. Niba ubishaka, urashobora kwandika izina ry’ukoresha (alias) ryerekana *Jami ID* yawe (urutoke rw’ibanga). Ishirahamwe *alias ↔ Jami ID* risohorwa kuri server y’izina ry’imbere (rishobora gusubirwamwo / ryikira), rero alias si ngombwa ngo ibe ari ngombwa.



![0_08](assets/fr/08.webp)



Kugira ngo ukinge amakuru yawe aho hantu, urashobora gushinga ijambobanga ryo gupfuka urutonde rwawe n’ivyo ufise ku gikoresho. Iri jambobanga ni ubusabe kandi ntaco rihindura ku gupfuka ivy’itumanaho kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera, ivyo bikaba bikora ku buryo busanzwe. Niwakoresha ubu burinzi bwo mu karere, hitamwo ijambobanga rirerire, ry’imburakimazi kandi ry’umwihariko, hanyuma wemeze.



![0_09](assets/fr/09.webp)



Uhejeje gupfuka konti yawe, shiraho izina ryawe ryose.

## Injira kuri konti isanzweho



Jami ntakoresha **ibimenyetso** kandi nta rutonde rw'amakuru afise rwo gufatanya na konti yawe. Amakuru yawe yose abikwa ku gikoresho cawe. Kugira ngo ushobore kwifatanya na konti yawe ya kera, ukeneye gukora **backup** ya konti yawe ya kera.



Genda kuri **Imiterere**, hanyuma **Konti**, hanyuma **Uburongozi bwa Konti**. Tera hasi ukore **backup ya konti yawe**. Hitamwo aho dosiye y'ububiko izobikwa, winjize **ijambobanga** ryasobanuwe igihe konti yawe yaremwa, hanyuma uyishire mu ngiro.



![0_12](assets/fr/12.webp)



Iyi dosiye y'ububiko izogufasha gusubira kwifatanya na konti yawe.



![0_13](assets/fr/13.webp)



### Uguhuza biciye mu bubiko



Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku buto **Importe from a archive de backup**, hanyuma uhitemwo dosiye y’ububiko bwa konti yawe ya Jami.



![0_14](assets/fr/14.webp)



Niwaba warashizeho ijambobanga igihe wakora konti yawe, urishiremwo maze wemeze. Amakuru yawe n'ubutumwa bwawe bizoboneka ubwavyo.

Ni vyiza ko uhora ukora backups za konti yawe kugira ngo amakuru yawe agume ari ku rugero rwo hejuru kandi ntutakaze ubutumwa bwawe bushasha.



### Uguhuzwa biciye ku gikoresho



Niba umaze kwinjira ku kindi gikoresho ukoresheje konti yawe ya Jami, genda kuri **Konti** hanyuma uja ku gice ca **Igikoresho gihuye** ukande kuri **Huza igikoresho gishasha**. Igikoresho co gusoma kode ya QR kizoboneka, hamwe n’aho woshiramwo kode yo guhuza.



![0_16](assets/fr/16.webp)



Tangaza Jami ku gikoresho gishasha uhitemwo **Ndasanzwe mfise konti** hanyuma **Import uvuye ku kindi gikoresho**. Uzoheza ubone **QR code** na code yo kwinjira. Kugira ngo uyihuze, urashobora gukoresha igikoresho co gupima kode ya QR kugira ngo ubone kode yerekanwa ku kindi gikoresho. Ariko rero, kenshi biroroshe gukopa kode y’ihuriro maze ukayishira mu kibanza catanzwe.



![0_17](assets/fr/17.webp)



Niwashiraho ijambobanga igihe wakora konti yawe, uzokenera kurishira ku gikoresho gishasha kugira ngo ukomeze.

Wemeze ko uhuye ku gikoresho camaze guhuzwa kugira ngo ukomeze.

## Kwongerako ukwagura kuri Jami



Kimwe mu bintu bishimishije vya Jami ni ubushobozi bwayo bwo gushiramwo amahitamwo mashasha biciye ku vyo kwagura (plugins). Ivyo gushiramwo ni ibice vy’imvukira (C/C++); SDK itanga ibikoresho n'inyandiko (cane cane muri Python) kugira ngo ifashe mu kubirema. Hariho ibikoresho bimwe bimwe biboneka mu buryo butaziguye [hano](https://jami.net/ibindi/).



Kugira ngo ushiremwo extension, kuri Desktop fungura ububiko bw’ivyongeweko buhuriweko, ushiremwo plugin ibereye, hanyuma ugende kuri **Settings → Extensions → Install** maze uyikoreshe. Kuri Android, ububiko ntibushizwemwo: ushiremwo dosiye `.jpl`, hanyuma uyishiremwo n’amaboko uhereye kuri **Imiterere → Ivyagutse → Gushiramwo**; import ni automatique kandi ushobora kuyikoresha no guhindura ama settings yayo iyo bikenewe.



![0_20](assets/fr/20.webp)



Kugira ngo ukore ibikoresho vyawe, raba ikiganiro co ku rubuga **[Vumbura SDK y’ibikoresho vya Jami maze ukore ibikoresho vyawe](https://jami.net/plugins-sdk/)**.



## Ibirango biteye imbere



Jami kandi itanga **ibintu biteye imbere** ku bakoresha bipfuza kuja kure mu gutunganya no gukoresha porogarama. Aya mahitamwo arimwo:





- Rema ahantu ho guhurira**: Iki gikoresho kigufasha kurema **ahantu ho guhurira** ku bijanye n’ivy’itumanaho ryawe, bifise akamaro mu gutunganya ibiganiro bitekanye canke guhanahana amakuru hagati y’abakoresha benshi.
- Guhuza na server ya Jami**: Ushobora guhuza Jami na **server ya Jami**, bishobora gutuma ivy’itumanaho bikora neza canke bikaboneka, cane cane mu bibanza vy’umwuga.
- Gushinga konti ya SIP**: Ushobora gushinga **konti ya SIP** (Protocole y’Itanguriro ry’Ikiganiro), bikagufasha gushiramwo Jami n’imirongo ya telefone isanzweho canke guhamagara kuri telefone.



![0_21](assets/fr/21.webp)



Ivyo bihitamwo vyagenewe abakoresha bateye imbere cane bipfuza guhindura ubumenyi bwabo bwa Jami no kwungukira ku bindi bikoresho ku bikenewe vyihariye.



Muri make, Jami ni umuti w’ivy’itumanaho wuzuye, utekanye kandi ushobora guhinduka, ubereye gukoreshwa n’umuntu ku giti ciwe no ku bikenewe vyihariye kubera **ivyongeyeko** vyawo. Gushiramwo kwayo kworoshe, gukoreshwa neza n’ibintu biteye imbere nk’**ugushiramwo amakuru**, **gukorana n’ibikoresho vyinshi** n’ubushobozi bwo **kwikorera ibikoresho vyawe** biyigira igikoresho gikomeye co gucunga ivy’itumanaho ryawe mu gihe uzigama **ubuzima bwite bwawe**.
