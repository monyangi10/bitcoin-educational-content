## I. Ugushikiriza



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba turiko turaraba Picocrypt, porogaramu yoroshe, yoroshe kandi ikora neza yo gupfuka amakuru afise umutekano mwinshi.



Ibereye **gupfuka amadosiye**, ushobora kuyikoresha mu kurinda **amakuru ari kuri mudasobwa yawe, kuri USB stick**, ariko kandi n’amakuru abitswe muri Cloud. Nk’akarorero, urashobora gupfuka amakuru ukayabika kuri **Microsoft OneDrive, Google Drive, iCloud canke Dropbox**, naho nyene kubera iyo ntumbero nkunda ikindi gice ca porogarama kizoshikirizwa mu kiganiro kizoza.



Ushobora kandi kuyikoresha iyo ukeneye **gusangira amakuru n'uwundi muntu**: kubera Picocrypt n'urufunguzo rwo gukuraho amakuru, bazoshobora gukuraho amakuru ari ku mashine yabo. Rero iyo konti yawe canke mudasobwa yawe bishizwe mu kaga, amakuru yawe arakingirwa.



Kugira ngo ukurikize umugambi wa Picocrypt, hariho Address imwe gusa:





- [Igishushanyo co kuri GitHub](https://github.com/Igishushanyo/Igishushanyo)



**Ubuntu kandi bufunguye**, PicoCrypt iraboneka ku **Madirisha,** **Linux** na **macOS**. Kuri Windows, urashobora kuyishira ku mashini yawe canke ukoreshe verisiyo y’iminwe.

Picocrypt **iraremereye cane**, ipima **3 MB** gusa, kandi ntikeneye gushirwako: ni **porogarama ishobora gutwara** ifise akamaro ko kudasaba uburenganzira bw'umuyobozi! Ariko rero, ntiyirengagiza umutekano, kuko yizigira **ubuhinga bukomeye kandi bwizewe**:





- XChaCha20** ubuhinga bwo gushikiriza amakuru
- Urufunguzo rwo guca mu nzira **Argon2**



Hejuru y’ivyiza twamaze kuvuga, ikintu gikwegera vy’ukuri ni **uko yoroshe gukoresha**!



Bikeneye ikintu kimwe gusa: **igenzura rya kode**, ariko ivyo birateguwe, nk'uko ushobora kubibona ku rutonde rw'ukugereranya ruri hejuru (umurongo wa nyuma). Ariko kuko ari open source, ntaco biguhagarika ngo uraba source code yayo.



Naho igereranywa na BitLocker mu mbonerahamwe iri hejuru, **mu vyiyumviro vyanje BitLocker na Picocrypt bigenewe gukoreshwa mu buryo butandukanye**: BitLocker yo gupfuka igitabu cose (ico ca Windows, nk'akarorero) na Picocrypt yo gupfuka umubumbe w'igiti canke ububiko bw'umwanya w'ubwoko bwa "Drive".



## III. Gukoresha Picocrypt



Ku bw’iyi yerekanwa, hazokoreshwa imashini ya Windows 11.



### A. Gushiramwo amakuru hakoreshejwe Picocrypt



Mbere na mbere, ukeneye gukura Picocrypt.exe kuri GitHub yemewe ([raba iyi paji](https://github.com/Ivyasohotse)).



Ugurure porogaramu kugira ngo ugaragaze Interface yayo y’agaciro gatoyi ku rubuga. Kugira ngo ushireho amakuru, yaba **dosiye, dosiye nyinshi canke dosiye**, **uyikurure maze uyashire muri Interface ya Picocrypt**. Ivyo bizohitamwo amakuru azoshirwa mu nzira.



![Image](assets/fr/020.webp)



Hanyuma, ku bijanye no gupfuka amakuru, urashobora gukoresha uburyo bwinshi, harimwo n’urufunguzo rwo gupfuka. Bishobora kuba **ijambobanga rikomeye** canke **urufunguzo rwo gupfuka mu buryo bwa dosiye y'urufunguzo**, canke **vyompi**. Nitwafata akarorero k’ijambobanga, urafise amahitamwo hagati yo kwihingurira ijambobanga ryawe, canke gukora ijambobanga ukoresheje Picocrypt.



Iryo jambobanga ritegerezwa kuba rikomeye, kuko rishobora gukoreshwa mu gukuraho amakuru. Injira munsi ya "**Ijambobanga**" na "**Wemeze Ijambobanga**", hanyuma ukande kuri "**Encrypt**" kugira ngo ushire amakuru! Imbere y'ivyo, ushobora gukanda kuri buto ya "**Hindura**" iri iruhande ya "**Bika igisohoka nk'**" kugira ngo ugaragaze ububiko bw'isohoka.



**Iciyumviro**: kugira ngo ukoreshe urufunguzo mu buryo bwa dosiye, kanda kuri "**Rema**" iburyo bwa "**Keyfiles**" kugira ngo ureme urufunguzo. Hanyuma uyihitemwo ukanda kuri "**Edit**" hanyuma ukureho no gutera dosiye y'urufunguzo ahantu habereye.



![Image](assets/fr/019.webp)



Izo dosiye zibiri zatowe **zishizwe mu mfuruka kandi zishizwe hamwe** muri dosiye "**Encrypted.zip.pcv**", kuko **PCV** ari yo nzira ikoreshwa na Picocrypt. Iyi dosiye ya ZIP ntishobora gusomwa kubera ubuhinga bwo gupfuka. Kugira ngo amadosiye yatowe ntashire hamwe muri dosiye imwe ya ZIP yashizwemwo amakuru, ukeneye gusuzuma uburyo "**Recursively**", kugira ngo habeho amadosiye menshi yashizwemwo amakuru nk'uko hari amadosiye akwiye gushirwamwo amakuru.



Kugira ngo turonke amakuru yacu, turakeneye kuyafungura dukoresheje Picocrypt.



![Image](assets/fr/023.webp)



Imbere y'uko tuvuga ivyerekeye gusobanura amakuru, ng'aya amakuru yerekeye bimwe mu bishobora gukoreshwa:





- Uburyo bwa Paranoid**: ukoreshe urugero rwo hejuru rw’umutekano rutangwa na Picocrypt. Ico gikoresho kizokoresha ubuhinga bwinshi bwo gupfuka amakuru (XChaCha20 na Serpent) na HMAC-SHA3 aho gukoresha BLAKE2b kugira ngo amakuru yemezwe.
- Reed-Solomon**: gushiramwo *Reed-Solomon* amakode yo gukosora amakosa kugira ngo bishobore gukosora amakosa ku makuru yononekaye. Ivyo bigufasha gushigikira urugero rw’ibiturire ruri hafi 3% rwa dosiye yawe.
- Gabanya ibice** canke **bigabanye ibice vyinshi**: nimba uriko urapfuka dosiye nini, ushobora gusaba Picocrypt kuyigabanya mu bice vyinshi. Ivyo bishobora gutuma iyo dosiye yoroha kuyirungika.
- Gufatanya amadosiye** canke **Gufata amadosiye**: gufatanya amadosiye kugira ngo ugabanye ubunini bw'amadosiye yashizwemwo amakuru.
- Dosiye zasimbujwe** canke **Fichiers supprimés**: gukuraho dosiye z'inkomoko kugira ngo ugume ufise verisiyo yashizwemwo gusa



### B. Gusobanura amakuru hakoreshejwe Picocrypt



Niba ukeneye gukuraho amakuru, ntaco bigoye kuruta kuyashiramwo amakuru. Gufungura Picocrypt gusa **ukureho dosiye ya PCV kugira ngo ifungurwe**. Hanyuma winjize ijambobanga na/canke uhitemwo dosiye y'urufunguzo imbere yo gukanda kuri "**Decrypt**".



![Image](assets/fr/021.webp)



Verisiyo idashizwemwo amakuru y'ububiko bwa ZIP "Encrypted.zip" ubu irampa uburenganzira bwo kugarura amadosiye yanje abiri mu nyandiko igaragara!



![Image](assets/fr/022.webp)



## IV. Iciyumviro



**Mwaragabishijwe: Picocrypt yoroshe cane gukoresha, kandi irakora! Naho Interface ari minimalist, irimwo uburyo bumwebumwe ngirakamaro cane bwo guhindura ubuhinga bwo gupfuka amakuru! Kandi ko iboneka mu buryo bwo gutwara, urashobora kuyijana aho uja hose, kugira ngo ushobore gukuraho amakuru yawe wizigiye**



Raba neza ko ukoresha amajambo y’ibanga akomeye kugira ngo ukinge amakuru, kandi iyo ukoresheje dosiye y’urufunguzo, utegerezwa kwibuka kuyibika, ahandi ho ntuzosubira gushobora gufungura igikoresho ca PCV cakozwe na Picocrypt. Ubwa nyuma, ukwiye kumenya ko hariho kandi [verisiyo ya CLI](https://github.com/Picocrypt/CLI) (ifise ibintu bike) ishobora kugufasha gukoresha Picocrypt ukoresheje umurongo w’amabwirizwa: aha na ho, Picocrypt ifungura urugi rw’ubushobozi bushasha.
