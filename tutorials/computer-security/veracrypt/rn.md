Muri iki gihe, birahambaye gushiramwo ubuhinga bwo kumenya ko amadosiye yawe ashobora gushikwako, akagira umutekano, kandi akagira ububiko, nk’inyandiko zawe bwite, amafoto yawe canke imigambi yawe ihambaye. Gutakaza ayo makuru birashobora gutera akaga.


Kugira ngo ivyo bibazo ntibibeho, ndaguhanura ko woguma ukora backup nyinshi z’amadosiye yawe ku bimenyeshamakuru bitandukanye. Ingamba zikoreshwa cane mu gukoresha ubuhinga bwa mudasobwa ni ingamba zo gucungera "3-2-1", zituma amadosiye yawe akingirwa:


- 3** kopi z’amadosiye yawe;
- Ibitswe nibura ku **2** ubwoko butandukanye bw’ibinyamakuru;
- Nibura **1** kopi ibitswe hanze y’aho hantu.


Mu yandi majambo, ni vyiza kubika amadosiye yawe ahantu 3 hatandukanye, ukoresheje ubuhinga butandukanye, nka mudasobwa yawe, umuduga wa Hard wo hanze, inkoni ya USB, canke ubuhinga bwo kubika kuri internet. Kandi mu nyuma, kugira kopi y’inyuma y’aho ukorera bisigura ko ukwiye kugira kopi y’inyuma ubitse hanze y’inzu yawe canke y’ubucuruzi bwawe. Iyi ngingo ya nyuma irafasha kwirinda gutakaza amadosiye yawe yose mu gihe habaye ibiza vyo mu karere nk’umuriro canke umwuzure. Kopi y’inyuma, iri kure y’inzu yawe canke ubucuruzi bwawe, iratuma amakuru yawe azorokoka ata ngorane zo mu karere.


Kugira ngo ushobore gushitsa iyo nzira y’ububiko bwa 3-2-1 mu buryo bworoshe, urashobora guhitamwo umuti wo kubika kuri interineti, mu guhuza amadosiye yo muri mudasobwa yawe n’ayo ari mu gicu cawe. Muri izo nzira zo gucungera amakuru ku rubuga, biragaragara ko hariho izo mu masosiyete manini manini y’ubuhinga bwa none uzi: Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, canke Apple iCloud. Ariko rero, ivyo si vyo bisubizo vyiza vyo kurinda ubuzima bwite bwawe. Mu nyigisho iheruka, narakumenyesheje ubundi buryo bushobora gupfuka inyandiko zawe kugira ngo ushobore kugira ubuzima bwite: Proton Drive.


https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/data/proton-drive-03cbe49f-6ddc-491f-8786-bc20d98ebb16

Mu kwemera iyo nzira yo gucungera amakuru yawe mu karere no mu gicu, uramaze kwungukira ku bwoko bubiri butandukanye bw’ibimenyeshamakuru vy’amakuru yawe, kimwe muri vyo kikaba kiri hanze y’aho uri. Kugira ngo urangize ingamba za 3-2-1, ukeneye gusa kwongerako kopi y’inyongera. Ico ndaguhanura ni ugutuma gusa amakuru yawe ariho mu karere kawe no mu gicu cawe aja hanze, nk’inkoni ya USB canke umuduga wa Hard wo hanze. Muri ubwo buryo, naho ama server y’umuti wawe wo kubika amakuru kuri Internet yoba yasambutse, mudasobwa yawe igasenyuka icarimwe, uracari n’iyo kopi ya gatatu ku buryo bwo hanze kugira ngo ntutakaze amakuru yawe.

![VeraCrypt](assets/notext/01.webp)

Ariko kandi birahambaye ko wiyumvira umutekano w’ububiko bw’amakuru yawe kugira ngo ntihagire uwundi ashobora kuyaronka atari wewe canke abakunzi bawe. Amakuru yo mu karere n’ayo kuri Internet akenshi aratekanye. Kuri mudasobwa yawe, birashoboka ko warashizeho ijambobanga, kandi ama drive Hard yo muri orodinateri zo muri iki gihe akenshi arashirwako ijambobanga. Ku bijanye n’ububiko bwawe bwo kuri internet (cloud), nakweretse mu nyigisho iheruka ingene wokingira konti yawe ukoresheje ijambobanga rikomeye n’ukwemeza ko ari we. Ariko rero, ku kopi yawe ya gatatu ibitswe ku kintu gifatika, umutekano umwe rudende ni ukuyigira ku kintu gifatika. Iyo umusuma ashoboye kwiba USB stick yawe canke Hard yawe yo hanze, yoshobora gushika ku makuru yawe yose bitagoranye.

Kugira ngo ukingire iyo ngorane, ni vyiza ko ushiramwo amakuru yawe y’umubiri. Gutyo, ukugerageza kwose kwo kuronka amakuru kuzosaba kwinjiza ijambobanga kugira ngo ushobore gukuraho ibirimwo. Iyo udafise iryo jambobanga, ntibizoshoboka ko ushobora gushika ku makuru, ugakingira amadosiye yawe bwite mbere n’igihe umuntu yokwiba USB stick yawe canke umuduga wawe wo hanze Hard.

## Intangamarara ya VeraCrypt


VeraCrypt ni porogaramu yuguruye iboneka kuri Windows, macOS, na Linux, ishobora kugufasha gupfuka amakuru yawe mu buryo butandukanye no ku buryo butandukanye.

![VeraCrypt](assets/notext/04.webp)

Iyi porogarama ishobora gutuma umuntu ashobora kurema no kubungabunga ibitabu vy’amakuru vy’amabanga mu gihe ariko aragenda, bisobanura ko amakuru yawe ahita ashirwa mu mabanga imbere y’uko abikwa kandi agafungurwa imbere y’uko asomwa. Ubwo buryo buratuma amadosiye yawe aguma akinzwe mbere n’igihe hoba ubusuma bw’ububiko bwawe. VeraCrypt ntiyipfuza gusa amadosiye ariko kandi n’amazina y’amadosiye, amakuru y’imbere, amadosiye, mbere n’ahantu h’ubusa kuri ububiko bwawe.


VeraCrypt ishobora gukoreshwa mu gupfuka amadosiye yo mu karere canke imigabane yose, harimwo na disiki ya sisitemu. Ishobora kandi gukoreshwa mu gupfuka bimwe bishitse ikintu co hanze nk’inkoni ya USB canke disk nk’uko tuzobibona muri iyi nyigisho.


Inyungu nini ya VeraCrypt ku mirongo y’ubuhinga ni uko ari inkomoko yuguruye yose, bisobanura ko kode yayo ishobora kugenzurwa n’umuntu wese.

Nk’uko uzobibona mu nyuma, igikorwa co guhingura igitabu gishasha gifise amakuru y’ibanga kuri USB canke kuri Hard kizotwara igihe kirekire cane nimba ico gikoresho gisanzwe kirimwo amakuru udashaka gukuraho. Ni co gituma ndabagira inama yo gukoresha USB stick idafise ikintu canke gukuraho ubusa imbere y’igihe kugira ngo ureme ijwi ry’ivyo bikoresho, kugira ngo uzigame umwanya.

Nk’akarorero, iyo umuntu akuhatira ku mubiri gufungura igikoresho cawe, urashobora gufungura igice gisanzwe gusa kugira ngo uhaze uwo muntu akugiriye nabi ariko ntuhishure igice canyegejwe. Hitamwo iyi nzira gusa iyo igikoresho cawe kiri ubusa; ahandi ho, uzotakaza amakuru yose arimwo. Niba wifuza kubika amakuru asanzweho, ushobora kuyarungika ahandi, uhitemwo "*Rema umubare ushizwemwo amakuru maze uwuhindure*" kugira ngo ukoreshe uburyo bwihuta bwo gukuraho vyose, canke uhitemwo "*Ushiremwo amakuru mu kibanza*". Iryo hitamwo rya nyuma rituma umuntu ashobora gupfuka igitabu ata gukuraho amakuru asanzwe ariho, ariko iyo nzira izomara igihe kirekire cane. Ku karorero, nk'uko USB stick yanje iri ubusa, ndahitamwo "*Rema volume encrypted maze uyihindure*", ni yo nzira ipfunya vyose.

Muri iyi ntambwe, uzokenera gushinga ijambobanga ryo gupfuka no gufungura igikoresho cawe. Ni vyiza guhitamwo ijambobanga rikomeye kugira ngo uwugutera ntashobora gukuraho ibintu vyawe akoresheje ibitero vy’inkomezi. Ijambobanga rikwiye kuba ry’imburakimazi, igihe cose bishoboka, kandi rikaba rigizwe n’ubwoko bwinshi bw’inyuguti. Ndaguhanura guhitamwo ijambobanga ry’imburakimazi ry’inyuguti 20 harimwo inyuguti ntoya, inyuguti nini, imibare, n’ibimenyetso.


Ndaguhanura kandi ngo ubike ijambobanga ryawe mu gikoresho co gucungera ijambobanga. Ivyo bituma vyoroha kuronka kandi bikakuraho ingorane zo kwibagirwa. Ku bijanye n’ikibazo cacu, umucungerezi w’ijambobanga ni we ahimbaye kuruta umucungerezi w’impapuro. Nkako, iyo umuntu yivye, naho igikoresho cawe co kubika amakuru coshobora kwibwa, ijambobanga riri muri manager ntirishobora kuronswa n’uwugutera, ivyo bikaba bizotuma adashobora kuronka ayo makuru. Ku rundi ruhande, iyo umucungerezi wawe w’ijambobanga ashobora guhungabanywa, gushika ku gikoresho ku buryo bw’umubiri biracari ngombwa kugira ngo ukoreshe ijambobanga no gushika ku makuru.


Kubindi bisobanuro ku bijanye no gucunga amajambo banga, ndaguhanura ngo ubone iyi nyigisho yuzuye:

Injira ijambobanga ryawe mu bibanza 2 vyategekanijwe, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ibikurikira*". ![VeraCrypt](itunga/inyandiko/25.urubuga)

VeraCrypt izoca ikubaze nimba utegura kubika amadosiye manini arenga 4 GiB mu gipimo cashizwemwo amakuru. Iki kibazo kiratuma porogarama ihitamwo uburyo bwo gukoresha amadosiye bubereye kuruta ubundi. Muri rusangi, ubuhinga bwa FAT burakoreshwa kubera ko bujanye n’ubuhinga bwinshi bwo gukoresha, ariko bushiraho umupaka w’ubunini bwa dosiye burengeye 4 GiB. Niba ukeneye gucunga amadosiye manini, ushobora guhitamwo uburyo bwa exFAT.

Izobikwa mu gice kinaka c’ivy’itangazamakuru ryawe, ubwayo ikaba ikingiwe n’ijambobanga wari warashizeho mbere. Kugira ngo generate urufunguzo rukomeye rwo gupfuka, VeraCrypt ikeneye entropi. Ni co gituma iyo porogarama igusaba guhindura imbeba yawe mu buryo butari bwo hejuru y’idirisha; ivyo binyabuzima rero bikoreshwa kugira ngo generate urufunguzo. Bandanya ugendagenda imbeba gushika igipimo c’uburemere bw’amazi cuzuye. Hanyuma, ukande kuri "*Format*" kugira utangure kurema igitabu gipfutse.
