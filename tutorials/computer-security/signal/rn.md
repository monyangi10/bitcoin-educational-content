Signal ni porogaramu y’ubutumwa bushingiye ku mpera ku mpera, yagenewe gutanga ubuzima bwite bwiza ku buryo busanzwe. Ubutumwa bwose, uguhamagara kwose n’idosiye yose birindwa n’umurongo wa Signal, wemewe nk’umwe mu mirongo ngenderwako y’ubutumwa ikomeye cane. Isubira gukoreshwa n’izindi porogarama nyinshi, harimwo WathsApp, ubutumwa bwa Facebook, Skype n’ubutumwa bwa Google ku bijanye n’itumanaho rya RCS.



Signal yatangujwe mu mwaka w’2014 na Moxie Marlinspike (izina ry’uruyeri) ikaba yateguwe kuva mu mwaka w’2018 n’ishirahamwe Signal Foundation, ishirahamwe ritagira inyungu ryashinzwe rishigikiwe na Brian Acton (uwashinze WhatsApp).



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Ugereranije na WhatsApp, Signal iratandukanye cane n’uguseruka kwayo: kode y’iyi porogarama, ku ruhande rw’umukiriya n’urw’umukozi, ni inkomoko yuguruye yose. Ivyo bituma umuntu wese ashobora kuyigenzura, cane cane akagenzura ko ubuhinga bwo gupfuka amakuru bukoreshwa nk’uko vyamamajwe.



Ariko rero, Signal yizigira gukoresha inomero ya telefone, ari yo ntege nke yayo nyamukuru iyo bije ku bijanye no kutamenyekana ugereranyije n’ibindi bisubizo. Naho ari ukwo, iyo porogarama, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, ni imwe mu zishobora kwizigirwa cane mu bijanye n’umutekano n’ubuzima bwite, kubera ubuhinga bwayo bwose bufunguye be n’umurongo w’ugushiramwo amakuru wemewe cane, rero irageragezwa kandi igenzurwa, itandukanye n’izindi porogarama ziri ku ruhande.

## Gushiramwo porogaramu y'ikimenyetso



Signal iraboneka ku mbuga zose. Ushobora gukuraho iyo porogaramu mu bubiko bwa porogaramu bwo kuri telefone yawe:




- [Ikiganiro ca Google](https://igikino.google.com/ububiko/amaporogarama/ibisobanuro?id=org.ubugizi bwa nabi bw'ivyiyumviro.umutekano);
- [Iduka ry'amaporogarama](https://amaporogarama.com/us/porogarama/ikimenyetso-intumwa-y'ibanga/id874139669);



Ku bijanye na Android, birashoboka kandi [gushiramwo biciye kuri APK].



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwibanda ku bijanye n’ivyo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa, ariko ndabinginze mumenye ko [ivyo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa na vyo nyene biraboneka](https://signal.org/fr/download/) (MacOS, Linux na Windows). Ariko rero, uzokenera kubanza gushinga porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa imbere y’uko ushobora guhuza konti yawe na verisiyo yo kuri mudasobwa.



## Rema konti kuri Signal



Iyo ufunguye porogaramu ku ncuro ya mbere, ukande kuri buto ya "*Continue*".



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Injira inomero yawe ya telefone, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ibikurikira*".



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Kode y’ukugenzura izogurungikirwa kuri SMS. Injira iyi kode mu gikorwa ca Signal.



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Hitamwo kode ya PIN kugira ngo ukingire konti yawe ya Signal. Iyi kode ipfuka amakuru yawe, kandi ishobora gukoreshwa mu kugarura uburenganzira bwo gukoresha konti yawe iyo igikoresho cawe kizimiye. Ni ngombwa rero guhitamwo kode ya PIN ikomeye kandi idasanzwe uko bishoboka kwose, kandi ukayibika mu buryo bwizigirwa.



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Wemeze iyi kode ya PIN ubugira kabiri.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Ubu ushobora guhindura urutonde rw'abakoresha bawe. Hitamwo ifoto, winjize izina ryawe canke izina ry’akataraboneka. Kuri iyi ntambwe, urashobora kandi gusobanura uwushobora kugusanga kuri Signal biciye ku nimero yawe. Hitamwo "*Bose*" nimba ushaka ko aboneka, canke "*Nta n'umwe*" kugira ngo ugume udashobora gukurikirana biciye ku nimero ya telefone (ushobora rero kwongerwako gusa n'"*Izina ry'ukoresha*" ryawe). Umaze guhitamwo, ukande kuri "*Ibikurikira*".



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Ubu uhuye na Signal kandi witeguriye ubutumwa bwa Exchange.



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



## Gutegura konti yawe y'ikimenyetso



Fyonda ku foto yawe iri hejuru ibubamfu kugira ngo ubone amaparametere y’iporogarama.



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



"*Konti*" ishobora kugufasha gucunga amategeko y'umwirondoro wawe. Ndaguhanura ngo ugume ufise ivyo ushizeho. Ushobora kandi gukoresha uburyo bwa "*Registration Lock*", bukingira konti yawe ubwoko bumwe bumwe bw'ibitero. Iyi menyu kandi irimwo amahitamwo ukeneye kugira ngo ushire konti yawe ku gikoresho gishasha.



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Usubiye gukanda ku ifoto yawe y’ibanga mu ma settings bizokujana ku mahitamwo yo guhindura urutonde rwawe. Ndagusavye ko woshiraho "*Username*": ivyo bizotuma ushobora gukorana n'abandi bantu utagaragaje inomero yawe ya telefone.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



Uhisemwo "*QR code canke link*", uzoronka amakuru ukeneye gusangira n'umuntu ashaka kukwongera kuri Signal.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Igitabu ca "*Ibanga*" ni co gihambaye cane. Aha uzosanga uburyo bwo kugenzura ukuntu inomero yawe iboneka, gucunga ubutumwa bwawe n’abo uvugana, hamwe n’uburenganzira butandukanye buhabwa ku bijanye n’ivyo usaba.



![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)



Kandi ntutinye gutohoza "*Igaragara*", "*Ibiganiro*" na "*Imenyekanisha*" kugira ngo uhuze Interface n'ingene porogarama ikora bihuye n'ivyo ukeneye.



## Huza porogaramu yo kuri mudasobwa



Kugira ngo uhuze porogarama yo kuri mudasobwa, tangura ushire porogarama kuri mudasobwa yawe (raba igice ca mbere c’iyi nyigisho). Hanyuma, kuri telefone yawe, genda kuri Settings maze ufungure igice kivuga ngo "*Ibikoresho bihuye*".



![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)



Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Huza igikoresho gishasha*".



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Kuri orodinateri yawe, fungura iyo porogarama, hanyuma ukoresheje telefone yawe ukoreshe kode ya QR yerekanwa ku rubuga. Niba wipfuza kwinjiza ibiyago vyawe, hitamwo "*Iherezo ry'amateka y'ubutumwa*".



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Ivyombo vyawe ubu birahuye neza.



![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)



## Kwohereza ubutumwa n'ikimenyetso



Kugira ngo uvugane n’umuntu kuri Signal, ubanza kumushiramwo nk’umuntu wovugana nawe. Hariho uburyo bwinshi: ushobora kuzokwongerako biciye ku nimero yabo ya telefone (niba uwo muntu yakoresheje uburyo bwo kuronka umuntu muri ubwo buryo), canke ukoreshe Signal ID yiwe.



Fyonda ku kimenyetso c'ikaramu kiri mu mfuruka iburyo hasi y'igitabu Interface.



![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)



Mu gihe canje, nshaka kwongerako uwo muntu mw’izina ry’ukoresha. Nca nfyonda kuri "*Rondera mw'izina ry'ukoresha*".



![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)



Ushobora rero gushiramwo login yayo canke ugaca scanner code yayo ya QR.



![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)



Murungikire ubutumwa kugira ngo musangire ubucuti.



![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)



Ico kiyago kizoca kiboneka kuri paji y’intango. Iyo uwo muntu yemeye ivyo usavye, uzobona izina ryiwe n’ifoto yiwe.



![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)



Urasangwa, ubu uriko urakoresha ubutumwa bwa Signal, uburyo bwiza bwo gukoresha WathsApp! Niba wabonye iyi nyigisho ari ngirakamaro, noshima cane iyo usiga urukumu rwa Green aha hepfo. Ntutinye gusangira iyi nyigisho ku mbuga zanyu zo guhanahana amakuru. Murakoze cane!
