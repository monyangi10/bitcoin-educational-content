Ico kibanza c’ubutumwa ni co gihambaye mu bikorwa vyawe vyo kuri Internet kandi kenshi gifise uruhara runini mu mutekano wawe w’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none. Iyo uwugutera ashoboye gutera mu gasandugu kawe k'ubutumwa, ashobora gushika ku zindi konti zawe bitamugoye biciye ku gikorwa ca "*forgot password*". Ivyo bishobora kumufasha kugenzura imihora yawe yo gusangirirako amakuru, amakonte yawe yo muri banki n’ibindi bikorwa vyo kuri Internet, kuko muri iki gihe, email Address akenshi ikoreshwa nk’ikimenyetso kidasanzwe c’akaranga kawe ko kuri Internet. Ku bw’ivyo, gukingira isandugu yawe y’ubutumwa ni ikintu gihambaye cane kugira ngo wikingire ibitero.



Kugira ngo ubone umutekano w’isanduku yawe y’ubutumwa, birahambaye ko ukoresha uburyo bwiza bukeyi bworoshe, ivyo tuzobitohoza muri iyi nyigisho y’abatangura gukoresha mudasobwa. Ni ngombwa kandi guhitamwo uwuguha ubutumwa kuri e-mail afise umutekano atanga uburyo bwo kwikingira buteye imbere be n’ingingo ngenderwako zikomeye z’ubuzima bwite. Ni co gituma ndiko ndababwira ngo mukoreshe ProtonMail muri iyi nyigisho. Naho woba uhisemwo kudakoresha uwo mutanga, uburyo bwiza bushikirijwe ngaha burashobora gukoreshwa ku gasandugu k’ubutumwa ako ari ko kose kugira ngo karushirizeho gutekana.



## Ni kuki ukoresha ubutumwa bwa porotoni?



ProtonMail ni umuti w’iposita utekanye, kubera ibiranga vyinshi. Ica mbere, ProtonMail itanga uburyo bwo gukingira ama e-mails yawe kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera, bisobanura ko uwuyarungitse n’uwuyakira ari bo bonyene ari bo bashobora gusoma ibirimwo. Mu vy’ukuri, mbere na ProtonMail ntishobora gushika ku ma e-mail y’abayikoresha. Iryo koraniro ry’ubuhinga rikoreshwa ubwaryo, ntirisaba ubuhinga bwihariye ku ruhande rw’abakoresha.



Ikindi, ProtonMail ishiramwo ubuhinga buteye imbere bwo kurinda ubuzima bwite bwawe, harimwo no kubuza uburyo bumwe bumwe bwo gukurikirana no gupfuka IP yawe Address. Kubera ko Proton ikorera mu Busuwisi, iraronka inyungu ku mategeko amwe amwe agenga ukurinda amakuru ataboneka mu bindi bihugu. Ikindi, ProtonMail ni opensource, ishobora gutuma abahinga bigenga bashobora gusuzuma kode ya porogarama mu mwidegemvyo.



Ubucuruzi bwa Proton bushingiye ku buryo bwo kwiyandikisha, ivyo bikaba bihumuriza kuko vyerekana ko iyo sosiyete yironsa amahera ataco ikoresheje amakuru y’abayikoresha. Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzobona ingene twokoresha verisiyo ya ProtonMail ku buntu, ariko kandi hariho n’ingero nyinshi z’ukwiyandikisha zitanga ibindi bikoresho. Uwo murongo w’ubudandaji ni mwiza kuruta ubuhinga bwo ku buntu bwose, ivyo bishobora gutuma wibaza nimba amakuru yacu bwite ariko arakoreshwa kugira ngo umuntu aronke inyungu. Igishimishije, ivyo ntibisa n’uko biri kuri ProtonMail.

## Huza n'agasanduku kawe k'ubutumwa ka Proton



Nk’uko twabibonye haruguru, urashobora gukoresha isandugu yawe y’ubutumwa ya ProtonMail biciye ku rubuga, biciye ku rubuga rwa interineti, canke biciye kuri porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa. Kugira ngo ubone iyo porogarama, genda ku rubuga rwemewe: https://proton.me/mail/download



Niba ushaka gukoresha urubuga rwa ProtonMail gusa, wibuke gushiramwo ikimenyetso kuri iyo paji mu mucukumbuzi wawe kugira ngo ushobore kuyironka mu buryo bworoshe muri kazoza no kugira ngo wirinde kugerageza kwihenda.



Kugira ngo uyironke, genda kuri URL ikurikira: https://konti.
