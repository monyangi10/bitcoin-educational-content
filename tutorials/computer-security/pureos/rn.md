## Gutangura na PureOS



PureOS ni ubuhinga bushingiye kuri Debian bwateguwe na Purism. PureOS irabereye abahinga mu vy’ubuhinga bwa none n’abakoresha barondera uburyo bwo gukwiragiza bworoshe kandi bworoshe gukoresha. Ni ikintu kidasanzwe kuko ari *Programme y'ubuntu*, kandi yibanda ku kurinda amakuru y'ibanga y'abakoresha bayo mu gushinga urutonde rushingiye ku buzima bwite, umwidegemvyo, umutekano n'ugushikama bitangwa na Debian.



### Ni kuki uhisemwo PureOS?





- Interface** yoroshe, igaragara neza: GNOME itanga ibiro bigaragara Interface, vyateguwe kugira ngo vyorohe gukoresha, mbere n'abantu batamenyereye umurongo w'amabwirizwa.





- Ubuntu**: nk’ibindi bikoresho vyinshi vya Linux, PureOS ni ubuntu bwose bwo gukoresha. Ariko rero, hariho amahera yo kwiyandikisha buri kwezi kugira ngo umuntu afashe abahinguzi.





- Umutekano n’ugushikama**: Ubwubatsi bwa PureOS n’uburyo ikora bituma iba ukwiragira gutekanye cane, bikaba vyemeza ko amakuru akingwa n’ugushikama kwa sisitemu.





- Ivyandiko n’umuryango ukora**: PureOS ifise inyandiko zitomoye, zishikira n’umuryango wiyemeje, wishura, bituma vyoroha gutorera umuti ingorane no kwiga ubuhinga intambwe ku yindi.



## Gushiramwo no gutunganya



Gushiramwo no gutunganya PureOS kuri mudasobwa yawe bizosaba ibi bikurikira:




- Urufunguzo rwa USB rw’imiburiburi 8GB kugira ngo ushireko umuco.
- 4 GB ya RAM.
- 30 GB y’umwanya w’ubuntu kuri disiki yawe ya Hard.



Genda kuri [urubuga rwemewe rwa PureOS](https://pureos.net/) hanyuma ushiremwo ishusho ya ISO y’uburyo bwo gukoresha hakurikijwe ubuhinga bw’imashini yawe.



Kugira ngo utangure gushiramwo PureOS, ukeneye gukora urufunguzo rwa USB rushobora gufungurwa ukoresheje porogarama y’umuyagankuba nka [Balena Etcher](https://www.balena.io/etcher).



Mu ntambwe zitatu zoroshe, uzoronka inkoni ya USB ifunguwe n’ubuhinga bwa PureOS.





- Nimushiremwo urufunguzo rwa USB maze mukoreshe porogarama ya Balena mwaronse.
- Hanyuma uhitemwo ishusho ya PureOS ISO .
- Hitamwo urufunguzo rwa USB nk’igikoresho co gusohora, hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya **Flash** maze urindire ko igikorwa kirangira.



![0_01](assets/fr/01.webp)



Urufunguzo rwa USB rumaze gufunguka, subiramwo mudasobwa wipfuza gushiramwo PureOS.



Igihe ufunguye, genda muri BIOS ukanda urufunguzo `ESC`, `F9` canke `F11`, bivanye n'imashini yawe. Hitamwo urufunguzo rwa USB nk'igikoresho co gutangura, hanyuma ukande `ENTER` kugira ngo wemeze.

## Ivyavuguruwe



Imbere y’uko utangura gukoresha PureOS, ni ngombwa ko uhindura ubuhinga bwawe. Ivyo bizotuma wungukira ku bintu bishasha be n’ivyo gucungera umutekano, kandi ubone ko uhagaze neza cane.





- Ivugurura biciye ku gishushanyo ca Interface:


Ugurure porogaramu ya **Software**, hanyuma ugende ku rubuga rwa **Ivyahinduwe**. Ivyagezwe biriho biragaragara ubwavyo. Fyonda kuri **Gukuraho**, hanyuma **Ushiremwo** iyo umaze gukuraho.





- Kuvugurura biciye ku nzira**:


Gufungura terminal, hanyuma winjize itegeko rikurikira kugira ngo uhindure urutonde rw'amapaki ariho:



```shell
sudo apt update
```



Injira ijambobanga ryawe maze wemeze. Hanyuma ushiremwo ivyavuguruwe na:



```shell
sudo apt full-upgrade
```



## PureOS y'iterambere



Kubera ko ari vyo, PureOS ntirimwo ibikoresho vyose bikenewe kugira ngo umuntu atere imbere.


Ushobora kubishiramwo ningoga ukoresheje iri tegeko rikurikira:



```shell
sudo apt install build-essential git curl
```



Ivyo bizoshiramwo ibikoresho vyo gukoranya **Git** na **Curl**, bifise akamaro mu bidukikije vyinshi vy'iterambere.



## Ibidukikije vya PureOS



PureOS iratandukanye n’uburyo bushasha bwo gukorana n’ibindi: uburyo bumwe bwo gukoresha buratuma habaho ugukora neza, ata nkomanzi ku bikoresho bitandukanye, harimwo ama laptops, tablettes, mini-PCs n’ama smartphones.



Ibikoresho vya PureOS vyakozwe kugira ngo bishobore guhindura: bihita bihindura ubunini bw’igicapo n’uburyo bwo kwinjiza (gukora canke kuri klavye/imbeba). Nk’akarorero, umucukumbuzi w’urubuga rwa GNOME arahindura neza Interface yayo kugira ngo itange ubumenyi bwiza ku bikoresho vy’amatelefone ngendanwa n’ivy’ibiro.



PureOS kandi irimwo **LibreOffice** ibiro, birimwo:





- Umwanditsi**: igikoresho c'amajambo yuzuye co gukora no guhindura inyandiko.
- Calc**: porogaramu ikomeye y'urupapuro rwo gucunga amakuru yawe n'imibare yawe.
- Impress**: igikoresho co guhingura ibiganiro vy’umwuga.



Iyi suite y’ubuntu iratuma ukora neza ataco ukeneye kwizigira porogarama z’abanyagihugu.



PureOS itanga ibidukikije bimwe, bitekanye kandi bishobora guhinduka, bishingiye ku gukwiragiza 100% kw’inkomoko yuguruye bitanga ubugenzuzi bwose n’ukwubaha cane ubuzima bwite. Uguhuza kwayo nyakuri kworohereza uguhingura porogarama zihuye n’ubwoko butandukanye bw’ibikoresho, nka mudasobwa, amatablette n’amatelefone ngendanwa, ataco bikeneye guhindura bikomeye.



Kubera ko PureOS ishobora gukoresha ibikoresho vy’ingenzi, abarongozi b’amapaki bakomeye be n’ibidukikije vy’ubutunzi, PureOS yorosha gutegura ibikorwa, kugerageza no gukoresha mu bidukikije bitekanye. Ubwubatsi bwayo buhamye na Commitment ku ibanga biyigira urubuga rwo kwizigirwa rwo gukoresha mu buryo butandukanye, harimwo gutegura Blockchain, gukora ibigereranyo vyihuta canke ibidukikije vyo gukora.
