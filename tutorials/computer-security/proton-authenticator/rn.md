Ivyemezo bibiri (2FA) vyongera inzitizi y’umutekano ku makonti yawe mu gusaba, uretse ijambobanga ryawe, ikimenyamenya c’inyongera c’uko ari wewe wenyene ufise. Gukoresha 2FA bigabanya cane ingorane zo gufatwa n’abantu, naho ijambobanga ryawe ryoshobora gufatwa biciye mu guhenda abantu canke mu gusohoka kw’amakuru. Iyo ata 2FA, uwugutera yokenera gusa ijambo banga ryawe kugira ngo yinjire kuri konti zawe; na 2FA, yoba akeneye kandi ikintu cawe ca kabiri, akabuza ubusuma bwinshi bwa konti.



Hariho ubwoko butandukanye bwa 2FA. Ama code ya SMS ni meza kuruta ataco amaze, ariko aguma ashobora guterwa n’ibitero vyo guhindura SIM n’ugufatwa. Ntidusaba ko SMS ari 2FA y’intango. Ivyo bikoresho vy’ukwemeza (TOTP) generate bikora amakode y’igihe gito mu karere ku gikoresho cawe, bikaba bituma bigorana cane kubifata. Imfunguruzo z’umutekano w’umubiri zitanga umutekano mwiza, ariko zisaba ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga vyihariye.



Igikoresho co kwemeza Proton ni igikoresho co kwemeza TOTP. Ni inyishu ya Proton ku mipaka y’ibikoresho biriho: vyinshi ni ivy’abanyagihugu, birimwo abakurikirana amatangazo kandi ntibitanga ububiko bufise amakuru. Proton Authenticator yitandukanya n’izindi mu gutanga porogarama yuguruye, idafise amatangazo n’abakurikirana, ifise ububiko bufise amakuru y’iherezo.



## Gutangaza umugenzuzi wa porotoni



Proton Authenticator ni porogaramu yo kwemeza TOTP yo kuri telefone ngendanwa no ku biro yakozwe na Proton, izwi cane kubera ibikorwa vyayo vyerekeye ubuzima bwite. Cokimwe n’ibikoresho vyose vya Proton, iyo porogarama ni open source kandi yarakoze igenzura ry’umutekano ry’abantu bigenga. Iboneka ku buntu ku bikoresho vyose (Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, Linux).



Igikoresho co kwemeza ko Proton itanga ibintu nyamukuru bikurikira:





- Guhingura amakode ya **TOTP** kuri konti yawe ya 2FA, ihuye n'imbuga nyinshi zikoresha Google Authenticator, Authy, n'ibindi.





- Ububiko bw’igicu bufise amakuru y’ubuhinga**: ushobora guhuza porogaramu na konti yawe ya Proton kugira ngo ukore ububiko bw’amakuru no guhuza amakode yawe n’ububiko bw’amakuru buva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera. Niwatakaza igikoresho cawe, nusubire gusa gufatanya ikindi gishasha kugira ngo ugarure amakode yawe yose.





- Guhuza ibikoresho vyinshi**: mu kwinjira muri Proton muri app, amakode yawe ya 2FA aca ahita akoranya hagati y’ibikoresho vyinshi biciye ku gupfuka amakuru kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera. Ku iOS, ubundi buryo ni uguhuza biciye kuri iCloud.





- Gufunga mu karere hakoreshejwe ijambobanga canke biometrics**: porogarama itanga PIN na/canke gufunga urutoke/Face ID. Rero naho umuntu yokwinjira ku mubiri kuri telefone yawe ifunguwe, ntazoshobora gufungura Proton Authenticator.





- Nta gukusanya amakuru canke abakurikirana**: Proton yiyemeje gukusanya amakuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe biciye ku rubuga. Nta kwamamaza canke gusuzuma inyifato bihishijwe.





- Gushiramwo/gusohora vyoroshe**: kimwe mu bintu bikomeye vya Proton Authenticator ni umuhinga wayo wo kwinjiza ibintu mu ma konti asanzweho, bihuye n’ibindi bikoresho (Google Authenticator, Authy, Aegis, n’ibindi). Ushobora kandi kwohereza amakode yawe muri dosiye iyo bisabwe.



Muri make, Proton Authenticator ifise intumbero yo kuba umuti wa 2FA udashobora gusenyuka: utekanye, wigenga, ushobora guhinduka.

## Gusubiza inyuma no kugarura



Kimwe mu bintu nyamukuru biranga Proton Authenticator ni ugucungera amakode ya 2FA kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera, kugira ngo iyo utakaje canke uhinduye igikoresho ntibisigura ko utegerezwa gusubira gutangura vyose.



### Ububiko kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera



Ku bijanye n’ugucungera amakuru y’igicu hakoreshejwe Proton Authenticator, amabanga yawe ya TOTP arashirwa ahantu ku gikoresho cawe imbere y’uko yoherezwa kuri server ya Proton. Gukuraho amakuru bishoboka kuri wewe gusa, ku bikoresho vyawe bihuye na konti yawe ya Proton. Proton AG nta rufunguzo ifise rwo gusoma ama code yawe yanditswe.



Kuri iOS, iyo uhisemwo iCloud aho guhitamwo konti ya Proton, amakuru yawe arashirwa mu buryo bujanye n’ingingo za Apple. Local backup kuri Android ishobora kugufasha gupfuka dosiye y’ububiko ukoresheje ijambobanga uhisemwo.



### Gusubizaho iyo umuntu atakaje



Iyo telefone yawe yazimiye, yibwe canke uhinduye telefone :



**Igihe Proton ikora**: Shiraho Proton Authenticator ku gikoresho gishasha. Mu gihe co gutegura ubwa mbere, winjire muri iyo konti ya Proton nyene. Ubwo nyene, iyo porogarama izokorana n’ibindi kandi ikureho amakode yawe yose ya 2FA mu vyo ukoresha mu gucungera amakuru.



**Ufise ububiko bwa iCloud (iOS)**: Huza iPhone/iPad nshasha n’iyo ID ya Apple maze urabe ko ukoresha urufunguzo rwa iCloud. Amaze gushiramwo igikoresho co kwemeza ko Proton, ushire kuri iCloud. Ama code yawe akwiye gukorana biciye kuri iCloud maze akaboneka.



**Nta n'ububiko bw'igicu**: Uzokenera kwinjiza amakonti yawe n'amaboko. Niba wararungitse hanze dosiye y'ububiko, koresha igikorwa co kwinjiza muri Proton Authenticator. Mu gihe kibi kuruta ibindi vyose, iyo utagira ububiko, uzokenera gukoresha amakode y’ububiko kuri buri serivisi, canke uhamagare abafasha.



Proton Authenticator iragufasha kwohereza hanze amakonti yawe igihe cose, haba nk’idosiye ipfutse canke nk’ikode ya QR y’amakonti menshi. Ivyo bihitamwo biraguha icizigiro congereweko.



## Ibikorwa vyiza



Gukoresha umugenzuzi wa 2FA birongereza cane umutekano wawe, ariko hariho ingendo nziza zitegerezwa gukurikizwa:



### Bika amakode yawe y'ivyihutirwa



Iyo ukoresheje 2FA kuri servisi, kenshi uhabwa urutonde rw’amakode yo gusubirana. Bigumye kure ya telefone yawe (ku mpapuro, mu gikoresho co gucungera ijambobanga ry’ibanga, n’ibindi). Mu gihe woba utakaje burundu umugenzuzi wawe, izo kode zidahinduka zizogukiza.



### Ntuvange amajambo y'ibanga yawe n'amakode ya 2FA



Biragerageza gukoresha umucungerezi w'ijambobanga nawe abika TOTPs. Ariko rero, kugumiza ijambobanga na kode ya 2FA ahantu hamwe bituma habaho ikintu kimwe co kunanirwa kandi bikagoyagoya ukwemeza kabiri. Kugira ngo umuntu agire umutekano mwinshi, abahinga benshi barasaba ko umuntu yotandukanya ivyo bintu bibiri: amajambo y’ibanga ari mu mucungerezi afise umutekano, n’amakode ya 2FA mu gikoresho gitandukanye nka Proton Authenticator. Ariko rero, gukoresha umuyobozi yunze ubumwe biracari vyiza kuruta kutagira 2FA na gato.



### Gukoresha uburyo bwinshi bwa 2FA



Ivyiza ni uko wokoresha ibintu birenze isegonda rimwe ku nkuru zawe zihambaye. Ntukekeranye kwongerako urufunguzo rw’umutekano w’umubiri nimba iyo serivisi ibivyemera. Raba inyigisho yacu ku mfunguruzo z'umubiri za Yubikey kugira uronke ibindi bisobanuro:



https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/authentication/security-key-61438267-74db-4f1a-87e4-97c8e673533e

Na wewe nyene, nugumye ufise amakode y’ivyihutirwa acapwe.



### Gumana ubwenge kandi ukinge igikoresho cawe



Ntureke ngo umuntu arondere telefone yawe ifunguye. Na Proton Authenticator, amakode yawe akinzwe na PIN/biometrics - ntumenyeshe iyo PIN. Na wewe nyene, urabe maso ku bijanye n’ubuhendanyi: mbere n’aho woba ufise 2FA, iyo uhaye kode urubuga rw’ubuhendanyi mu gihe nyaco, rwoshobora gukoreshwa n’umuntu atera.



### Ivyagezwe n'igenzura



Gumana Proton Authenticator iri ku gihe (ihindura ikosore amakosa ashoboka). Uko iyo kode ifunguye, umuryango urakora igenzura ridasanzwe, Proton na yo igatangaza ivyavuye mu igenzura risanzwe.



## Kugereranya n'ibindi bikoresho



Ni gute Proton Authenticator iteranya ibindi bikoresho vyo kwemeza? Akira incamake y'ukugereranya:



**Proton Authenticator**: Inkomoko yuguruye, ububiko bw’igicu bushizwemwo E2EE bushobora guhitamwo, gukorana n’ibikoresho vyinshi, gufunga mu karere, nta gukurikirana, biboneka kuri telefone ngendanwa no kuri mudasobwa.



**Google Authenticator**: Ni umunyagihugu, ikoreshwa kuri konti ya Google kuva muri 2023 ariko ata n’imwe ishobora gukingira (imfunguruzo zishobora kubonwa na Google), ihuriro ry’ibikoresho vyinshi ryongeweko muri 2023, nta n’imwe ifungura porogarama, irimwo abakurikirana Google.



**Aegis Authenticator**: Inkomoko yuguruye, ububiko bwo mu karere gusa, nta guhuza igicu, kode/ugufunga biometric, nta gukurikirana, Android gusa.



**Uburenganzira**: Ububiko bw'igicu bufise ijambobanga ariko kode ifunze, guhuza ibikoresho vyinshi, gufunga PIN/ikimenyetso c'urutoke, gukusanya inomero ya telefone, porogaramu yo kuri mudasobwa yahagaritswe muri Ntwarante 2024.



Uzosanga uburongozi bwacu bwa Authy aha hepfo:



https://planb.network/tutorials/computer-security/authentication/authy-a76ab26b-71b0-473c-aa7c-c49153705eb7

Proton Authenticator ni kimwe mu bisubizo vy’ukuri kandi bitekanye biriho: inkomoko yuguruye, uguhuza ibikoresho vyinshi bifise amakuru, nta gukurikirana kw’ubudandaji.
