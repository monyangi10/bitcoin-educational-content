Keet ni porogaramu yo gutanga ubutumwa buca ikora ata serveri. Yatangujwe mu mwaka w’2022 na Holepunch (isosiyete iterwa inkunga na Tether na Bitfinex), iyo porogarama ishingiye ku rubuga rw’abagenzi kandi ifise uburyo bwo kwegereza abantu bose: ubutumwa, amatelefone n’amadosiye birahanahana ataco bimaze hagati y’abakoresha, ata n’umwe abihuza.



Keet ipfuka ivy’itumanaho vyose kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera kandi ntibisaba amakuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Ukwiyandikisha ntikumenyekana, nta nomero ya telefone canke email bisabwa. Uretse guhana ubutumwa, Keet itanga amatelefone y’amasanamu y’agaciro kanini cane, hamwe no gusangira amadosiye ataco amaze. Iryo koraniro rero rishobora gukoreshwa mu buryo buvanze, ku muntu ku giti ciwe no ku muntu akora.



![Image](assets/fr/01.webp)



Muri iki gihe (Myandagaro 2025), Keet ntabwo ari inkomoko yuguruye yose. Bimwe mu bimenyetso vy’inkomoko biraboneka kuri [ububiko bwa GitHub bwa Holepunch](https://github.com/holepunchto), cane cane ibice vy’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru n’ivy’urubuga, ariko umukiriya Interface ntaraboneka. Holepunch, ariko, yatangaje umugambi wayo wo guhindura porogarama yose amaherezo ikaba open-source. Bivanye n’igihe uzobona iyi nyigisho, ivyo bishobora kuba vyaramaze gukorwa.

## Shiraho Keet



Keet iraboneka ku mbuga zose. Ushobora gukuraho iyo porogaramu mu bubiko bwa porogaramu bwo kuri telefone yawe:




- [Ikiganiro ca Google](Ibisobanuro?id=io.keet.iporogaramu&pli=1);
- [Iduka ry’Iporogarama](iporogarama.



Kuri Android, birashoboka kandi [gushiramwo biciye kuri APK]



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzoba twibanda ku bijanye n’ivyo gukoresha telefone ngendanwa, ariko ndabinginze mumenye ko [ivyo gukoresha mudasobwa navyo nyene biraboneka](https://keet.io/) (MacOS, Linux na Windows). Birashoboka kandi guhuza konti yawe ku bikoresho vyinshi.



## Rema konti kuri Keet



Ku gutangura kwa mbere, urashobora kwirengagiza amasanamu y’ivyo yerekana.



![Image](assets/fr/02.webp)



Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Ndi umukoresha mushasha*".



![Image](assets/fr/03.webp)



Wemere amabwirizwa y'ikoreshwa, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Gutegura vyihuse*".



![Image](assets/fr/04.webp)



Hitamwo izina canke izina ry'ibanga, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Heza gutegura*".



![Image](assets/fr/05.webp)



Umwirondoro wawe ubu wararemwe. Fyonda kuri "*Heza gutegura*" kandi kugira ngo uheze.



![Image](assets/fr/06.webp)



Ushobora guhindura urutonde rwawe igihe cose ukoresheje "*Urutonde*".



## Bika konti yawe ya Keet



Ikintu ca mbere wokora kuri konti yawe nshasha ya Keet ni ukuzigama ijambo ryawe ryo gusubirana. Iyi ni urutonde rw’amajambo 24 azotuma ushobora gusubira gukoresha konti yawe iyo utakaje canke uhinduye igikoresho. Iri jambo ritanga uburenganzira bwo gukoresha konti yawe ku muntu wese ayizi, ni ngombwa rero ko ukora backup yizigirwa kandi ntuzokwigere uyitangaza.



Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku rubuga rwa "*Profile*" ruri hasi iburyo bwa Interface.



![Image](assets/fr/07.webp)



Hanyuma ugende ku rutonde rwa "*Ivyagezwe*".



![Image](assets/fr/08.webp)



Hitamwo "*Ubuzima bwite n'umutekano*".



![Image](assets/fr/09.webp)



Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ijambo ry'ugusubirana*".



![Image](assets/fr/10.webp)



Kanda kuri buto ya "*Reba ijambo*" kugira ngo ugaragaze ijambo ryawe ryo gusubirana. Uyikope neza maze uyishire ahantu heza.



![Image](assets/fr/11.webp)



## Kwohereza ubutumwa na Keet



Kugira ngo uvugane kuri Keet, ukeneye gukora "*Ivyumba*". Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku kimenyetso c’ikaramu kiri hejuru iburyo bwa Interface.



![Image](assets/fr/12.webp)



Hitamwo "*Ikiganiro gishasha c'umugwi*".



![Image](assets/fr/13.webp)



Hitamwo izina n'insobanuro y'"*Icumba*" cawe, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Rema ikiganiro c'umugwi*".



![Image](assets/fr/14.webp)



"*Icumba*" cawe ubu cararemwe. Fyonda ku kimenyetso "*+*" kiri hejuru iburyo kugira ngo utumire abazoja muri iyo nama.



![Image](assets/fr/15.webp)



Sigura uburenganzira uha abanywanyi bashasha biciye ku nzira y’ubutumire, hamwe n’igihe iyo nzira imara. Hanyuma ukande kuri "*generate ubutumire*".



![Image](assets/fr/16.webp)



Keet azo generate urubuga rwo kwinjira mu "*Icumba*" cawe. Ushobora kuyikopa ukayisangiza abandi, canke ugatuma abantu wipfuza gutumira bafata kode yayo ya QR.



![Image](assets/fr/17.webp)



Ubu ushobora gutangura guhana ubutumwa n’amadosiye y’ivy’ubuhinga bwinshi. Kugira ngo utangure guhamagara, fyonda ku kimenyetso ca telefone kiri hejuru iburyo.



![Image](assets/fr/18.webp)



Uri muri iri tsinda, urashobora kandi kohereza ubutumwa bwihariye ku munywanyi kanaka. Fyonda ku ifoto y'ibanga ry'umugwi, hanyuma uhitemwo umunyamuryango wipfuza mu gice ca "*Abanyamuryango*".



![Image](assets/fr/19.webp)



Fyonda kuri buto ya "*Send DM request*" hanyuma winjize ubutumwa bwawe.



![Image](assets/fr/20.webp)



Igihe DM request yemerewe, uzosanga uwo muntu kuri page y’intango, aho ushobora kumuvugisha mu mwiherero.



![Image](assets/fr/21.webp)



## Guhuza konti yawe ku bikoresho vyinshi



Ubu ko uzi gukoresha Keet kandi ufise konti, urashobora no kuyihuza n’ikindi gikoresho, nka orodinateri. Kugira ngo ubikore, fungura porogarama kuri telefone yawe, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Profile*" maze ushike kuri "*Settings*".



![Image](assets/fr/22.webp)



Hanyuma ugende kuri "*Ibikoresho vyanje*".



![Image](assets/fr/23.webp)



Fyonda kuri "*Ongera igikoresho*". Keet azo generate uruja n’uruza kugira ngo ukoreshe igikoresho gishasha. Kopa iyi nzira.



![Image](assets/fr/24.webp)



Ku gikoresho cawe ca kabiri, shiraho Keet. Ku mugaragaro w'intango, hitamwo "*Ndi umukoresha w'ubu*".



![Image](assets/fr/25.webp)



Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Ihuza igikoresho*".



![Image](assets/fr/26.webp)



Nimushireko uruja n'uruza rwatanzwe n'igikoresho canyu ca mbere, hanyuma mukande kuri "*Start sync*".



![Image](assets/fr/27.webp)



Ku gikoresho cawe ca mbere, fyonda ku "*Wemeza ibikoresho vy'ihuza*" kugira ngo wemerere guhuza.



![Image](assets/fr/28.webp)



Ku gikoresho ca kabiri, heza igikorwa ukanda kuri "*Huza ibikoresho*".



![Image](assets/fr/29.webp)



Ubu ushobora gushika ku "*Icumba*" cawe cose n'ibiyago vyose ukoresheje iki gikoresho gishasha.



![Image](assets/fr/30.webp)
