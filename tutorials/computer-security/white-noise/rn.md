## Imenyekanisha



"Mu ngorane hari akaryo". Iryo jambo ryavuzwe na Albert Einstein ritwibutsa ko ingorane zitari izo gutorera umuti, ahubwo ko zirimwo imbuto z’imiti mishasha, y’ubuhinga bushasha.



Ivyiyumviro vy’ugutanguza umuti turiko turashikiriza muri iyi nyigisho biravyerekana neza. Ni **White Noise**, yavutse kubera ngombwa.



Mu majambo y'uwayishinze, Max Hillebrand, nk'uko vyavuzwe n'ikinyamakuru *Bitcoin*: "Twatanguje uwo mugambi kubera ata yindi nzira." Asigura ko "ibikoresho vy'ugushiramwo amakuru biriho bidakora neza ku rugero runini: kwongerako abantu 100 mu kiyago c'umugwi bigabanya cane ugushiramwo amakuru. Ama platforms yegerejwe, muri ico gihe, ntatanga ubutumwa bwihariye. ntibihagije. Twarabonye: kugira ngo turinde umwidegemvyo, twabwirizwa gufatanya izo nzira."



## White Noise ni iki?



White Noise ni porogaramu yo gutanga ubutumwa bufunguye yateguwe n’umugwi udaharanira inyungu. Iryo koraniro ritera imbere umutekano, ubuzima bwite n’ugusenyura ubutegetsi. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ibindi bikoresho bisanzwe, ntibisaba inomero ya telefone canke aderesi ya e-mail.


White Noise itandukanye n’ugushiramwo amategeko abiri y’ishimikiro - Nostr na MLS - ari yo akora ishingiro ryayo ry’ubuhinga.



Nostr (Ivyanditswe n’ibindi bintu birungikwa n’ibimenyetso) ni umurongo w’ivy’ubuhinga wegerejwe, ufunguye, wagenewe kurwanya ugucengera.  Iryo porotokole rikoresha ama relais, ama key pairs n’aba clients.



https://planb.academy/tutorials/node/others/nostr-f6d21a64-9b04-4f21-ba1c-02c98cc91f98

Na white Noise, urashobora mbere guhitamwo amasetingi yawe bwite kugira ngo ukoreshe ubuzima bwite.



Ku rundi ruhande, MLS (Messaging Layer Security) ni uburyo bwo gucungera ubutumwa bushobora gushirwa mu buryo butari bwo. Mu yandi majambo, ubutumwa bushikira gusa ku mpera, ni ukuvuga uwuburungitse n’uwubwakira. Ivyo bisigura ko ama relays akora mu gutuma ubutumwa bugenda neza adashobora gushika ku birimwo.



Aha niho tugereranya vyihuse hagati ya White Noise n’umubare w’ibikoresho bizwi cane vyo gutanga ubutumwa.

## Gutangura na White Noise



### White Noise gushirwaho



Genda ku rubuga rwa [White Noise](https://www.whitenoise.chat/), hanyuma ukande kuri **Gukuraho**.



![screen](assets/fr/03.webp)



Ubu White Noise iboneka ku bikoresho vy’amaboko gusa.


Ku rubuga rushasha ruzoboneka, kanda :





- ubuto bwa **Zapstore** canke **APK ya Android** nimba ushaka kuyikura kuri Android ;
- ku buto **Igerageza ry'Indege ya iOS** nimba ukoresha iPhone.



![screen](assets/fr/04.webp)



Kubera intumbero z’iyi nyigisho, tugiye gukora igikorwa co gukuraho Android.


Niwahitamwo gukura kuri **Zapstore**, uzosubira kuyirungika. Igihe umaze gushika kuri Zapstore, wandike **White Noise** mu kabati k’ugushaka. Hanyuma ugende ushiremwo ukanda kuri **Install**.



![screen](assets/fr/05.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/06.webp)



Niwahitamwo gukuraho dosiye ya APK, uzosubira ku bubiko bwa White Noise GitHub kugira uhitemwo verisiyo ibereye kuri telefone yawe.



Amadosiye ya APK aboneka ni :





- (57,7 MB), ibereye telefone ziherutse gukoreshwa zifise amaprocesseur y’ibice 64;
- (47,5 MB) ibereye amatelefone ya kera afise amaprocesseur y’ibice 32.



Ufise kandi amadosiye **.sha256**, ariyo checksums gusa kugira ngo ugenzure ubutungane bw'ivyo wavuyeko.



![screen](assets/fr/07.webp)



Iyo umaze gukuraho iyo porogarama, nuyishiremwo maze uyifungure.



![screen](assets/fr/08.webp)



### Gutegura konti y'ukoresha ya mbere



Ku bijanye n'uguhuza kwawe kwa mbere na White Noise, kanda kuri buto ya **Iyandikishe**.



![screen](assets/fr/09.webp)



Inyuma y’aho, ushireho urutonde rwawe mu guhitamwo ifoto y’urubuga rwawe, izina ryawe no kwongerako insobanuro ngufi y’ivyawe. Ntubwirizwa kwuzuza akaranga kawe nyakuri (izina n’ifoto).


Zirikana ko White Noise ihita iguhitamwo izina (izina ry’uruyeri), ushobora kurigumya canke kurihindura. Ubwa nyuma, ukande kuri buto ya **End**.



![screen](assets/fr/10.webp)



Uzosubira ku **home screen** ya White Noise, aho ibiyago vyawe bizoba biri ku rutonde. Konti yawe ubu irateguwe kandi yiteguriye gukoreshwa. Ushobora gusangira abandi canke ukarondera abagenzi kugira ngo utangure kuganira.



![screen](assets/fr/11.webp)

## Amagenamiterere ya konti y'ukoresha



Kanda ku kimenyetso c’umwirondoro kiri hejuru mu mfuruka y’ibubamfu kugira ngo ushikire amasetingi.



![screen](assets/fr/16.webp)



Hejuru y’ibarabara rya **Settings**, uzosanga ifoto yawe n’izina ryawe, hanyuma haze urufunguzo rwawe rwa bose, ushobora gusangira ukoresheje kode ya QR iri iruhande rwayo.



![screen](assets/fr/17.webp)



Hasi gusa, uzosanga ubuto **Hindura konti**, bugufasha kwifatanya n'iyindi nkuru iri muri porogarama.



![screen](assets/fr/18.webp)



Hanyuma ufise igice ca mbere gifise amamenyu ane (4) nka :





- Guhindura umwirondoro**



Muri iyi submenu, urashobora guhindura izina ry'umwirondoro, aderesi ya Nostr (NIP-05)... Ntukibagire gukanda kuri **Bika** kugira ngo ubike ivyo wahinduye.



![screen](assets/fr/19.webp)





- Imfunguruzo z'umwirondoro**



Aha urashobora gushika ku mfunguruzo zawe za bose n’iz’ibanga (ibanga). Nk’uko White Noise ibikwibutsa, urufunguzo rwawe rw’ibanga ntirukwiye gutangazwa uko vyogenda kwose.



![screen](assets/fr/20.webp)





- Igikoresho gikomeye



Muri iyi menu, ushobora kwongerako canke gukuraho **ivy'amakuru rusangi** (ivy'ivyuma vyasobanuwe kugira ngo bikoreshwe mu bikorwa vyawe vyose vya Nostr), **ivy'ivyuma vy'inbox** na **ivy'ivyuma vy'urufunguzo**.



Kugira ngo ubikore, nukore ku kimenyetso ca **umwanda** kiri imbere y’ibarabara kugira ngo ugifute, canke ukore ku kimenyetso ca **+** (plus) kugira ngo wongereko ikindi gishasha.



![screen](assets/fr/21.webp)





- Guca**



Fyonda kuri iyi sub-menu kugira ngo ukure konti yawe kuri porogarama. Ariko urabe neza ko wabitse imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga zitari mu nzira, ahandi ho ntuzosubira kwinjira mu nyuma.



![screen](assets/fr/22.webp)




Igice ca kabiri kiratanga ibifungurwa bitobito:





- Amagenamiterere y'ikoreshwa



Aha urashobora gusobanura ukuntu porogarama isa (insanganyamatsiko n’ururimi rwerekana), mbere ugafuta amakuru nimba wumva ko yashizwe mu kaga, canke nimba wewe ubwawe wumva ko uri mu kaga.



![screen](assets/fr/23.webp)





- Gutanga kuri White Noise



Ushobora gushigikira umugwi uri inyuma ya White Noise (ishirahamwe ritagira inyungu) n’intererano biciye ku aderesi yabo ya Lightning canke aderesi yabo yo kwishura mu gacerere ya Bitcoin.



![screen](assets/fr/24.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, aha hepfo cane hari **imiterere y'abahinguzi**.



![screen](assets/fr/25.webp)




## Gutangura ikiyago



Ubu rero reka turabe ingene twotangura ikiyago. Mu gihe c’ukwandika, White Noise itanga uburyo butatu bwo guhanahana amakuru. Natwe, tuzokwihweza **Ibiganiro vy'ibanga** (**Ibiganiro 1:1**), ni ukuvuga hagati yawe n'uwundi muntu umwe, **Ibiganiro vy'Itsinda** na **Kwohereza Amadosiye y'Ivyuma vyinshi**.




### Kat 1:1.



Kuva ku rubuga nyamukuru, kugira ngo wongereko umunyamakuru mushasha, fyonda ku kimenyetso co gutangura ikiyago gishasha.


Hanyuma ushireko QR code y’urufunguzo rwa bose (1) canke ushire urufunguzo rwa bose (2) rw’uwo mwandikiye mushasha mu gice c’ugushaka kugira ngo umuronke.



![screen](assets/fr/26.webp)



Hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya **Tangira ikiyago** kugira ngo utangure ikiyago n’uwo mumenyeshamakuru wawe. Ushobora kandi **Gukurikira** umunyamakuru wawe canke ukamutumira mu kiganiro c'umugwi ukanda kuri buto ya **Ongera mu mugwi**.



![screen](assets/fr/27.webp)



Ubutumwa bwa mbere urungikira umunyamakuru mushasha busa n’ugusaba ubutumire. Ico gisabisho kigomba kwemerwa n’uwo mwandikira imbere y’uko muvugana na we. Iyo banse, rero nta kiyago gishoboka.



![screen](assets/fr/28.webp)



Ikindi kandi, igihe cose batazokwemera ubutumire bwawe, ntibazoshobora gusoma ibirimwo ubutumwa bwawe bwa mbere.



Amaze kwemera ubutumire bwawe, ubu arashobora kukwishura, maze mwempi murashobora kuvugana ata ngorane kandi ata nkomanzi.



![screen](assets/fr/29.webp)



Ikindi, mu kiganiro, urafise ibindi bikoresho vy’inyongera.



Ushobora gukanda igihe kirekire ku butumwa bumwe bumwe kugira ngo ugaragaze amahitamwo nka :




- gusubiza ubutumwa ukoresheje emoji (1) ;
- gukora **ibivugwa bitaziguye** kugira ngo wishure ubutumwa ukanda **Ishura** (2) ;
- kopa ubutumwa ukanda **Kopa** (3).



![screen](assets/fr/30.webp)





- gukuraho ubutumwa bufise ubuto bwa **Gukuraho** iyo buvuye kuri wewe gusa.



![screen](assets/fr/31.webp)



Ushobora kurondera mu kiyago.



Fyonda kuri avatar y'umunyamakuru iri hejuru y'ibarabara kugira ngo ubone "amakuru y'ikiyago" hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya **Rondera mu kiyago**.



![screen](assets/fr/32.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/33.webp)



Mu kabati k’ugushaka kazoboneka, wandike ijambo ushaka gushaka maze utangure gushaka. Uzoca ubona amajambo ushaka ashizwe ahabona mu **buto**.



![screen](assets/fr/34.webp)




### Ibiyago vy'umugwi



Imigwi y’ibiyago irashobora kuremwa kuri White Noise.



Kugira ngo ubikore, kora ku kimenyetso kiri mu kibanza gishasha co gutangura ikiyago, hanyuma ukande kuri **Ikiyago gishasha c'umugwi**. Hanyuma, mu kabati k’ugushaka, shiramwo urufunguzo rwa bose rw’umunyamuryango wa mbere wipfuza kwongera ku mugwi wawe.



![screen](assets/fr/35.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/36.webp)



Amaherezo, iyo iyo nzira idakora, uhereye ku **Tangira ikiyago gishasha**, shiramwo urufunguzo rwa bose rw'umunyamuryango wa mbere wipfuza kwongera ku mugwi mu kabati k'ubushakashatsi. Ushobora kandi gucapura kode ya QR ijana n’urufunguzo rwiwe rwa bose.



Ico gihe, aho gukanda kuri buto ya **Tangira ikiyago**, kanda kuri buto ya **Ongere ku mugwi**.



![screen](assets/fr/37.webp)



Ku rutonde ruzoza, nufyonde kuri **Ikiyago gishasha c'umugwi**.



![screen](assets/fr/38.webp)



Hanyuma ukande **Continue** (ntukeneye gusubira kwinjiza urufunguzo rwayo rwa bose).



![screen](assets/fr/39.webp)



Nk'umuremyi w'iryo tsinda, ni wewe ubwawe uba umuyobozi waryo. Uzuza amakuru y'umugwi, nka **izina ry'umugwi n'insobanuro yawo**, hanyuma ukande kuri buto **Rema umugwi**.



![screen](assets/fr/40.webp)



Uwukoresha wongeyeko nk’umunyamuryango wa mbere, n’abandi bose wongeyeko mu nyuma, baronka itangazo ribatumira kwinjira muri uwo mugwi. Bategerezwa kwemera mu gufyonda kuri **Accept** kugira ngo binjire mu mugwi.



![screen](assets/fr/41.webp)



Birashoboka kandi kwongerako umunyamuryango mushasha usanzwe uriko urayaga nawe mu mugwi uriho. Kugira ngo ubikore, fyonda ku gishushanyo c'uwo mumenyeshamakuru kiri hejuru y'ibarabara kugira ngo ubone "amakuru y'ikiyago" hanyuma ukande kuri buto **Ongera mu mugwi**.



![screen](assets/fr/42.webp)



Ku interface nshasha izoboneka, **suzuma** umugwi wipfuza kumushiramwo hanyuma ukande kuri **Ongera mu mugwi**. Igisigaye ni ukurindira ko yemera kwinjira muri uwo mugwi.



![screen](assets/fr/43.webp)



Zirikana ko umuyobozi w'umugwi ari we wenyene ashobora guhindura amakuru y'umugwi no kwongerako canke kwirukana abanywanyi. Kandi, guhinduranya urufunguzo bibuza abanywanyi babujijwe gusobanura ubutumwa bwo muri kazoza.



Kugira ngo ukureho umunyamuryango, mu kibanza nyamukuru c'umugwi, kora ku kimenyetso c'umugwi kiri hejuru kugira ngo ugere ku kibanza c'amakuru y'umugwi.



![screen](assets/fr/44.webp)



![screen](assets/fr/45.webp)



Hanyuma ukande kw'izina ry'uwo munyamuryango hanyuma **Ukuremwo mu mugwi**. Uri muri iyo interface, urashobora no kumurungikira ubutumwa, kumukurikira canke kumushira mu rindi tsinda.



![screen](assets/fr/46.webp)

## Ingingo nyamukuru zo kwibuka



White Noise itanga urugero rwiza rw’ibanga n’umutekano mwiza cane. Ku rundi ruhande, ni ugukoresha gushasha cane, kutakwiragiye cane kandi kukiri mu ntango. Ku bw’ivyo, biracari kare cane kugira ngo umuntu ashike ku nsozero iyo ari yo yose ikora. Birashoboka ko umuntu ahura n’ibibazo bikeyi mu gihe ariko arakoresha.



Muri iki gihe, nta bintu bimwebimwe ikora (nta guhamagara amajwi canke amasanamu, nta kohereza amadosiye y’amajwi canke amasanamu) ugereranije n’ibikoresho vy’ubutumwa bikundwa cane.



Naho biri ukwo, White Noise iguma ari uburyo bushimishije bwo kuganira aho ibanga ari ryo ry’agaciro kanini (nk’akarorero, n’umuryango, abagenzi somambike canke abaharanira uburenganzira bw’abantu mu ntumbero imwe), naho nyene vyosaba utwigoro dutoduto kugira ngo ushiremwo (biciye mu bubiko bw’ibikoresho vy’abaguzi canke amadosiye ya APK) no kwiga (kumenya neza cane paiyW na con3. porotokole).



Ubu urazi gukoresha White Noise kugira ngo uvugane n’abagenzi bawe n’umuryango wawe ata nkomanzi. Turagusavye utuhe urukumu nimba ubona ko iyi nyigisho ari ngirakamaro.
