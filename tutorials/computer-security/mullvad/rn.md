VPN ("*Virtual Private Network*") ni igikorwa gishinga ubufatanye butekanye kandi bufise amakuru hagati ya telefone yawe canke mudasobwa yawe na server iri kure icungiwe n'umutanga VPN.


Mu buryo bw’ubuhinga, iyo winjiye kuri VPN, uruja n’uruza rwawe rwa internet ruca mu nzira y’amazi y’ibanga rukaja kuri server ya VPN. Ivyo bituma bigorana ko abandi bantu, nk’abatanga amakuru ya Internet (ISP) canke abakora ivy’ububisha, bashobora gufata canke gusoma amakuru yawe. Server ya VPN rero ikora nk’umuhuza ahuza na service wipfuza gukoresha mu izina ryawe. Itanga IP Address nshasha ku nzira yawe, ivyo bikaba bifasha guhisha IP Address yawe nyayo ku mbuga usura. Ariko rero, bitandukanye n’ivyo amatangazo amwe amwe yo kuri internet yoshobora kuvuga, gukoresha VPN ntibiguha uburenganzira bwo guca kuri internet ata wuzi, kuko bisaba urugero rw’ukwizigira uwuguha VPN ashobora kubona uruja n’uruza rwawe rwose.

![MULLVAD VPN](assets/fr/01.webp)

Ivyiza vyo gukoresha VPN ni vyinshi cane. Ica mbere, birazigama ubuzima bwite bw’ibikorwa vyawe vyo kuri Internet ku ba ISP canke ku butegetsi, igihe gusa uwuguha VPN adasangira amakuru yawe. Ica kabiri, irakingira amakuru yawe, cane cane iyo uhuye n’imihora ya Wi-Fi ya bose, ishobora guterwa n’ibitero vya MITM ("**man-in-the-middle**"). Ica gatatu, mu guhisha IP yawe Address, VPN iragufasha guca mu nzira y’amategeko y’aho uba n’ugucengera, kugira ngo ushikire ibintu vyari kuba bitaboneka canke bibujijwe mu karere kawe.


Nk’uko ushobora kubibona, VPN ihindura ingorane zo kwihweza uruja n’uruza ku wutanga VPN. Ku bw’ivyo, igihe uhitamwo uwuguha VPN, birahambaye ko uzirikana amakuru y’ibanga asabwa kugira ngo wiyandikishe. Iyo uwuguha amakuru asavye amakuru nk’inomero yawe ya telefone, imeyili Address, amakuru y’ikarita ya banki, canke ikibi kuruta, Address yawe y’iposita, ingorane zo gufatanya akaranga kawe n’uruja n’uruza rwawe ziragwira. Iyo uwuguha amakuru ashobora gusenyuka canke akagufata mu buryo butegekanijwe n’amategeko, vyoba vyoroshe gufatanya uruja n’uruza rwawe n’amakuru yawe bwite. Ni vyiza rero guhitamwo uwutanga amakuru adasaba amakuru y’ibanga kandi yemera kwishura ata mazina, nk’aho yoba akoresheje amahera y’ibiceri.


Muri iyi nyigisho, nzobabwira umuti woroshe, ukora neza, w’igiciro gisanzwe wa VPN udasaba amakuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe kugira ngo ukoreshe.


## Intangamarara ya VPN ya Mullvad

Mullvad VPN ni igikorwa co muri Suède kigaragara kubera Commitment ku buzima bwite bw’abakoresha. Udakunze abatanga VPN, Mullvad nta makuru y’umuntu ku giti ciwe isaba igihe wiyandikisha. Nta nkeka ko utanga email Address, inomero ya telefone canke izina; ahubwo, Mullvad aguha inomero ya konti itazwi ikoreshwa mu kwishura no kuronka iyo serivisi. Ikindi, Mullvad avuga ko ata nkuru y’ibikorwa abika biciye ku ma server yabo.

![MULLVAD VPN](assets/notext/02.webp)

Ku bijanye no kwishura, ntibisabwa gutanga amakuru y’ikarita y’inguzanyo, kuko Mullvad yemera kwishura Bitcoin (onchain gusa ku rubuga rwabo rwemewe, ariko hari uburyo butazwi bwo kwishura biciye ku Lightning). Bakira kandi amahera y’amahera biciye ku nzira y’ubutumwa.


Mullvad VPN nayo iratandukanye n’abandi biciye mu guseruka no mu mutekano wayo. Porogarama zabo zifunguye, kandi bahora bagira igenzura ry’umutekano ryigenga kugira ngo basuzume ibikorwa vyabo n’ibikorwa remezo vyabo, ivyo biva muri ivyo bikaba [bishirwa ku rubuga rwabo](https://mullvad.net/fr/blog/tag/audits). Iryo shirahamwe riri inyuma ya Mullvad riri muri Suède, igihugu kizwi kubera amategeko akomeye agenga ivy’ubuzima bwite. Bakoresha gusa amaserveri yikorera, gutyo bagakuraho ingorane zijanye n’ugukoresha ibikorwa vy’igicu vy’abandi, nk’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none AWS, Google Cloud canke Microsoft Azure.


Ku bijanye n’ibiranga, Mullvad itanga ivyo umuntu yitega vyose ku mukiriya mwiza wa VPN, harimwo n’igikoresho co kwica kikingira uruja n’uruza rwawe iyo VPN ivuye, uburyo bwo guhagarika VPN ku bikorwa vyihariye, n’ubushobozi bwo gutuma uruja n’uruza rwawe rugenda biciye ku ma server menshi ya VPN.


Birumvikana ko iyo nzira y’ubusuku izana n’igiciro, mugabo igiciro kibereye akenshi ni ikimenyetso c’uburyo n’ubugororotsi. Bishobora kwerekana ko iyo sosiyete ifise uburyo bwo gukora ataco ikeneye kugurisha amakuru yawe bwite ku bandi bantu. Mullvad VPN itanga igiciro kimwe c’amayero 5 ku kwezi, ikoreshwa ku bikoresho bitandukanye bishika kuri 5.

![MULLVAD VPN](assets/notext/03.webp)

Udakunze abatanga VPN basanzwe, Mullvad afise uburyo bwo kugura umwanya wo gushika ku serivisi aho gusubiramwo, kwiyandikisha. Ushobora gusa kwishura rimwe gusa mu ma bitcoins mu kiringo wahisemwo. Nk’akarorero, iyo uguze umwaka umwe wo gukoresha, urashobora gukoresha iyo serivisi muri ico kiringo, hanyuma ugasubira ku rubuga rwa Mullvad kugira ngo usubiremwo igihe co gukoresha.

Ugereranije na IVPN, uwundi mutanga VPN w’agaciro kanini, Mullvad irafise ubutunzi bukeyi. Nk’akarorero, mbere n’igihe uhisemwo kugura imyaka itatu ukoresheje IVPN, igiciro c’ukwezi kigera ku €5.40. Ariko rero, IVPN iratanga izindi nsiguro kandi ifise umugambi uhendutse kurusha uwa Mullvad (umugambi wa Standard), ariko ivyo bigarukira ku bikoresho 2 gusa kandi ntibirimwo umugambi wa "multi-hop".

Narakoze kandi ibipimo vy’umuvuduko bitamenyerewe kugira ngo ngerageze IVPN na Mullvad. Naho IVPN yerekanye ko isumba abandi gatoyi mu bijanye n’ugukora, umuvuduko wo muri Mullvad wari ukiri mwiza cane. Ugereranije n’abatanga VPN, IVPN na Mullvad vyerekanye ko ari n’imiburiburi bikora neza, nimba bitaruta mu bihe bimwebimwe.
