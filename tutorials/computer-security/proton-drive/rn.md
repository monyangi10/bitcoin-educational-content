Muri iki gihe, birahambaye cane gushinga ubuhinga bwo kumenya ko amadosiye yawe bwite, nk’inyandiko zawe bwite, amafoto yawe canke imigambi yawe ihambaye, ashobora gushikwako, akagira umutekano, kandi akagira ububiko. Gutakaza ayo makuru birashobora gutera akaga.


Kugira ngo ivyo bibazo ntibibeho, ndaguhanura ko woguma ukora backup nyinshi z’amadosiye yawe ku bimenyeshamakuru bitandukanye. Ingamba zikoreshwa cane mu gukoresha ubuhinga bwa mudasobwa ni ingamba zo gucungera "3-2-1", zituma amadosiye yawe akingirwa:


- 3** kopi z’amadosiye yawe;
- Yabitswe nibura ku **2** ubwoko butandukanye bw’ibinyamakuru;
- Nibura **1** kopi ibitswe hanze y’aho hantu.


Mu yandi majambo, ni vyiza kubika amadosiye yawe ahantu 3 hatandukanye, ukoresheje ubwoko butandukanye bw’ibikoresho, nka mudasobwa yawe, umuduga wa Hard wo hanze, inkoni ya USB, canke ubuhinga bwo kubika kuri internet. Kandi mu nyuma, kugira kopi y’inyuma y’aho ukorera bisigura ko ukwiye kugira kopi y’inyuma ubitse hanze y’inzu yawe canke y’ubucuruzi bwawe. Iyi ngingo ya nyuma irafasha kwirinda gutakaza amadosiye yawe yose mu gihe habaye ibiza vyo mu karere nk’umuriro canke umwuzure. Kopi yo hanze, iri kure y’inzu yawe canke ubucuruzi bwawe, iratuma amakuru yawe azorokoka ataco yitaye ku ngorane zo mu karere.


Kugira ngo ushobore gushirwa mu ngiro ubuhinga bwo gucungera amakuru 3-2-1, urashobora gukoresha ubuhinga bwo kubika amakuru ku rubuga. Ivyo bisubizo, bikunze kwitwa "igicu," bitanga uburinzi bwongereweko mu kubika amakuru yawe ku ma server atekanye ashobora gushikwako akoresheje igikoresho ico ari co cose. Ijambo "igicu" ryerekeza gusa ku kubika amakuru ku ma server yo hanze.


Abantu benshi bakoresha uburyo bwo kubika amakuru bw’amashirahamwe akomeye y’ubuhinga bwa none: Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, canke Apple iCloud.

![PROTON DRIVE](assets/notext/01.webp)

Ivyo bisubizo birabereye gukoreshwa ku musi ku musi kandi bituma amakuru yawe ashobora gushikwako, ariko ntibituma haba ibanga. Muri iyi nyigisho, ndasaba ko tworonka uwundi muti, woroshe gukoresha nk’ibikoresho vyo kubika vya Big Tech, ariko ukaba ufise izindi ngingo zo kurinda ubuzima bwite bwawe. Uwo muti ni Proton Drive, igikoresho co kubika amakuru ku rubuga rwa interineti kiva mw’ishirahamwe ry’Ubusuwisi ryitwa Proton. Turabona kandi ingene twoshira mu ngiro mu buryo bworoshe ubuhinga bwa 3-2-1 bubereye gukoreshwa ku musi ku musi.


## Intangamarara y'umuvuduko wa porotoni

Proton Drive ni umuti uryoshe cane wo kubika kuri internet kuko uhuza ukworohereza gukoresha n’umutekano w’amadosiye yawe. Udakunze ibikorwa vya kera vy’igicu biva ku bigo bikomeye vy’ubuhinga, Proton Drive ishira mu ngiro ingingo zo kurinda ubuzima bwite bwawe. Ituma amadosiye yawe yose ashobora gufungurwa kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera, bisobanura ko mbere n’imigwi ya Proton idashobora gushika ku makuru yawe. Ikindi kandi, Proton Drive ni open-source, ikaba ituma abahinga bigenga bashobora gusuzuma mu mwidegemvyo kode ya porogarama.

![PROTON DRIVE](assets/notext/02.webp)

Ubucuruzi bwa Proton bushingiye ku buryo bwo kwiyandikisha, ivyo bikaba bihumuriza kuko vyerekana ko iyo sosiyete iterwa inkunga ataco ikoresheje amakuru y’abayikoresha. Muri iyi nyigisho, nzobasigurira ingene wokoresha verisiyo ya Proton Drive ku buntu, ariko kandi hariho n’ingero nyinshi zo kwiyandikisha zitanga ibindi bikoresho. Uwo murongo w’ubudandaji ni mwiza kuruta ubuhinga bw’ubuntu mu buryo bwa Big Tech, ivyo bishobora gutuma umuntu yibaza nimba amakuru yacu bwite ariko arakoreshwa kugira ngo umuntu aronke inyungu. Ivyo bisa n’uko atari ko biri kuri Proton.


Proton Drive itanga vyinshi kuruta uburyo bwo kubika bworoshe; kandi iremesha gusangira, guhindura, no gukorana ku nyandiko kuri interineti hakoreshejwe ibikoresho vyo guhindura, bisa n’ivyo Google ikora.


Ku bijanye n’[igiciro](https://proton.me/igiciro), verisiyo y’ubuntu itanga ububiko bushika kuri 5 GB kandi irimwo ibintu vy’ingenzi. Kugira ngo ubushobozi bwo kubika bugere kuri 200 GB, ushobora kwiyandikisha kuri Proton Drive ku mahera 4 € ku kwezi. Ku rundi ruhande, iyo package ya Proton Unlimited, itanga ku € 10 ku kwezi ububiko bushika kuri 500 GB kuri Proton Drive, ushizwmwo n’ibikorwa vyose vya Proton vyishyurwa, nka VPN n’umucungerezi w’ijambobanga, hamwe n’ibindi vyiza ku bikoresho vy’ubuntu (email na kalendari).

## Ni gute woshiramwo porogarama ya Proton Drive?

Proton Drive kandi itanga porogarama ishobora gutuma amadosiye yawe yo mu karere ahuzwa n’aho ubika kuri Internet. Ivyo bifasha kandi bikaba vyoroshe gushirwa mu ngiro ingamba zacu zo gusubiza inyuma 3-2-1. Ukoresheje porogarama ya Proton Drive, uronka kopi 2 zihuye z’amadosiye yawe: imwe iri kuri mudasobwa yawe iyindi iri kuri serveri za Proton, gutyo ugashitsa ibisabwa ku bwoko 2 bw’ibinyamakuru n’ugukora backup hanze y’aho uri. Uzokenera gusa gukora kopi ya gatatu, iyo tuzoyishiraho mu nyuma.

Kugira ngo ukoreshe iyo porogarama, fyonda ku rubuga rwa "*Computers*" ruri kuri konti yawe ya Proton Drive hanyuma uhitemwo buto ihuye n'ivyo ukoresha kugira ngo ukomeze gukuraho.

![PROTON DRIVE](assets/notext/25.webp)

Iyo umaze kuyishiramwo, uzokenera kwinjira kugira ngo ufungure konti yawe, hanyuma ukande kuri "*Sign in*".

![PROTON DRIVE](assets/notext/26.webp)

Hitamwo amadosiye yo mu karere wipfuza guhuza na Proton Drive yawe.

![PROTON DRIVE](assets/notext/27.webp)

Nk'akarorero, nahisemwo gusa dosiye "*Proton Backup*". Hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Bandanya*".

![PROTON DRIVE](assets/notext/28.webp)

Uzoca ushika kuri porogarama Interface, isa n’iyikoreshwa ku rubuga.

![PROTON DRIVE](assets/notext/29.webp)

Kuva ubu, uzoba ufise dosiye yitwa "*Proton Drive*" mu karere kawe kuri mudasobwa yawe, izokwegeranya inyandiko zawe zose zibitswe kuri Proton kuri internet. Iyo wongeyeko dosiye muri iyo dosiye ukoresheje mudasobwa yawe, uzoca uyisanga kuri paji y’intango y’urubuga rwa Proton Drive, n’ibihushanye n’ivyo. Ku ma dosiye wahisemwo gukorana mu gihe co gushiramwo porogarama, urashobora kandi kuyaronka kuri internet mu kuja mu gice ca "*Computers*" ca Proton Drive hanyuma ugahitamwo mudasobwa yawe.

![PROTON DRIVE](assets/notext/30.webp)

Gutyo, amadosiye yawe yose arabikwa kandi agahuzwa n’aho uri ku mashine yawe no ku maserukira yo kuri Internet ya Proton Drive.


## Ni gute wokora backup ya Proton Drive?


Niba warakurikiye intambwe za kera, ubu ufise ahantu 2 hatandukanye ho kubika amadosiye yawe ahambaye. Kugira ngo turangize ubuhinga bwacu bwo gusubiza inyuma 3-2-1, turakeneye kwongerako kopi ya gatatu.

Ndagusavye gukora iyo backup y’inyongera ku kintu co hanze, nk’umuduga wo hanze Hard canke inkoni ya USB, nk’akarorero. Bivanye n’ingene ukoresha cane, shiraho incuro zibereye zo guhindura ububiko (buri ndwi, buri kwezi, buri gice c’umwaka...). Igihe cose wahisemwo, uzokenera gukuraho Proton Drive yawe yose kugira ngo ukore ububiko bw’amakuru ku gikoresho co hanze wahisemwo. Muri ubwo buryo, naho mudasobwa yawe yoba yibwe, n’ama server ya Proton agasambuka icarimwe, uzoguma ushobora gushika ku madosiye yawe ata nkomanzi biciye ku kopi iri ku nkoko ya USB.

Urasangwa, ubu ufise ubuhinga bukomeye cane bwo gukingira 3-2-1, bugufasha kugabanya cane ingorane zo gutakaza uburenganzira bwo kuronka inyandiko zawe bwite, uko ibintu vyogenda kwose. Mu guhitamwo Proton Drive kugira ngo ukoreshe amakuru yawe yo kuri Internet, urashobora kandi kwungukira ku gukingira ubuzima bwite bwawe.
