# Urugendo rwo ku nyandiko n'amaporogarama ya Bitcoin


Iryo shure rikomeye ry’imisi ibiri, ryigishwa na Jimmy Song, rigutwara mu mishinge y’ubuhinga bwa Bitcoin mu kwubaka ububiko bw’ibitabu bwose bwa Bitcoin kuva mu ntango. Gutangura n'imibare y'ingenzi y'ibibanza bifise impera n'imirongo y'imirongo, uzotera imbere biciye mu gusesangura ibikorwa, gushitsa inyandiko, no guhanahana amakuru ku rubuga. Biciye mu myimenyerezo yo gukora amakode mu makaye ya Jupyter, uzokwikorera Testnet Address yawe, wubake amafaranga n’amaboko, maze uyamenyeshe ku rubuga—ivyo vyose mu gihe uronka ugutahura kwimbitse kw’ingingo ngenderwako z’ubuhinga bwo gukora amakode zituma Bitcoin itekanye na Trustless.


Nimwinovore ivyo mwavumbuye!
