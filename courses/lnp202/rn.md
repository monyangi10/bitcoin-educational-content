# Intambwe yawe ya mbere yo kwigenga kuri Lightning



Waramaze kuronka sats yawe ya mbere, uraronka amahera yawe yo kwizigama kandi ushiraho urudodo rwa Bitcoin kugira ngo ube uwusegaba mu gukoresha on-chain yawe. Intambwe ikurikira ni ukuronka ubwigenge nk’ubwo ku Muco: iyo ni yo ntumbero nyayo y’iri shure.



LNP202 igenewe abakoresha bo hagati, kandi ikuyobora intambwe ku yindi mu gukoresha urudodo rwawe rwa mbere rwa Lightning, ata buhinga buteye imbere.



Uzomenya ingene woshira LND kuri Umbrel, gufungura no gucunga imirongo yawe, kugenzura node yawe no gufatanya wallet igendagenda, kugira ngo ushobore kunezerererwa ubumenyi bugereranywa n’ubwo wallet y’ububiko, mu gihe uguma ugenzura amahera yawe yose.

## Gutahura uturongo tw'umuravyo


<chapterId>8275dfd8-7a72-48cc-bf7f-bc2a46063003</chapterId>



Imbere yo gutanguza urudodo rwawe bwite, iki gice kirasubiramwo muri make inyigisho y'ishimikiro iri inyuma ya Lightning Network. Ni ngombwa vy’ukuri gutahura uburyo bukoreshwa, kuko ivyo bizokuronsa ubushobozi bwo kumenya ingorane no gufata ingendo nziza zo kuzigabanya. Ariko rero, sinzoja mu ndondoro hano, kuko iyo si yo ntumbero nyamukuru y’iri shure. Niba wifuza kwihweza cane iyo nkuru, ndagusavye cane ko woraba inyigisho ya Fanis Michalakis LNP 201, ari yo nzira yo gukoresha mu bijanye n’ivyo:



https://planb.academy/courses/34bd43ef-6683-4a5c-b239-7cb1e40a4aeb

### Node y’umuravyo ni iki?



Reka dusubire ku vy’ishimikiro: imbere yo gusobanura ico node ari co, turakeneye gutahura ico Lightning Network ari co. Ni porotokole y’urugero rwo hejuru, yubatswe hejuru ya Bitcoin, yateguwe kugira ngo ishobore gukora amafaranga y’uruzitiro rwa BTC yihuta (n’iherezo ry’aho nyene) kandi muri rusangi ntizimvye. "Offchain" bisigura ko amafaranga akoreshwa kuri Lightning atagamije kugaragara ku rubuga nyamukuru rwa Bitcoin. Imiravyo na yo ni inyishu y’igice ku gukoresha cane Bitcoin no ku gutera imbere kw’imirongo, ivyo bikaba biriko biratuma habaho ivyiyumviro ku bijanye n’uko iyo sisitemu ishobora gukoreshwa.



Kugira ngo Lightning ikore, yizigiye gufungura inzira zo kwishura hagati y’abayigize, aho amafaranga ashobora gukorwa hafi mu kanya nk’ako gukubita, kenshi n’amahera makeyi, ataco akeneye kuyandika umwe umwe ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin blockchain. Izo nzira zishobora kuguma zifunguye igihe kirekire cane, zisaba ko umuntu akora onchain gusa iyo yuguruye canke yugaye.



Igikoresho c’umuravyo ni umunyamuryango w’urubuga rwa Lightning, kigafungura imihora kandi kigatanga amahera n’ibindi bice. Mu majambo nyayo, Lightning node ni igice ca porogarama ikora kuri mudasobwa kandi ishira mu ngiro umurongo wa Lightning Network. Ingero ni LND, Umuravyo w’Ishingiro canke Eclair. Uruhara nyamukuru rw'iyi porogaramu ni:




- gufatanya n’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin kugira ngo uronke amakuru ava ku nzira nyamukuru y’uruzitiro;
- guhingura no gucunga imihora y’ukwishura y’inzira zibiri n’izindi nzira;
- guhana ubutumwa n’urubuga rwose rwa Lightning.



![Image](assets/fr/001.webp)



### Node n'umuravyo Wallet: itandukaniro rihambaye



Ku Bitcoin (onchain), "*wallet*" yerekeza kuri porogaramu icungera imfunguruzo zawe z'ibanga, ibara amafaranga yawe uhereye kuri UTXOs zawe kandi yubaka amafaranga yawe. Iyi wallet ishobora kuba ishingiye ku nzira yawe bwite ya Bitcoin canke ku y’uwundi muntu, ariko uno musi, uruhara rw’inzira n’urw’uruzitiro rwa wallet ruratandukanye cane.



Ku Lightning, biragoye cane gusubira gukoresha ubwo bwoko bw’amajambo ataco bitera urujijo. Ijambo "*Lightning wallet*" rirasobanutse neza, kuko mu vy'ukuri nta kintu nk'ico Lightning wallet yizigamira vy'ukuri, kiretse iyo ishingiye ku nzira. Ibintu bibiri gusa rero ni vyo bishoboka:



- Kugira ngo ugire urudodo nyarwo rwa Lightning (i.e. rudashobora gucungera): porogarama uriko urakoresha (nk’akarorero, porogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa nka Phoenix canke urugero rwa LND kuri Umbrel) mu vy’ukuri iriko irakoresha urudodo, kandi mu vy’ukuri ufise imfunguruzo zo kugarura amafaranga yawe. Muri iki gihe, "*Lightning wallet*" yawe mu vy'ukuri ni umurongo w'ukoresha gusa uri hejuru y'uruzitiro rwa Lightning, yaba ishizwemwo canke iri kure.



- Kugira ngo ukoreshe igikorwa co kubika: ukoresha porogarama ikwereka umubare uri muri sats kuri Lightning, ariko mu nyuma, amahera ari ku nzira y’umutanga (nk’akarorero Wallet of Satoshi). Nta mfunguruzo ufise canke ububasha bwo kugenzura imirongo. Ivyo usigaranye ni gusa ivyanditswe mu gitabu c’amakuru c’ishirahamwe. Bigereranywa no gusiga ama bitcoins yawe ku rubuga rwo guhinduranya, n’ingorane zose zijanye n’ivyo. Muri iki gihe, "*Lightning wallet*" yawe ni ugushika gusa kuri konti icungiwe n'umukoresha, na we nyene, akoresha urudodo nyarwo rwa Lightning.



Rero ntaco kiri hagati kuri Lightning: canke ufise node (mbere n'iyishizwemwo) rero uri mu kwicungera, canke ntaco ufise, rero sosiyete ni yo igira sats yawe. Ariko nk’uko tuzobibona mu bigabane bikurikira, rimwe na rimwe ivyo bikoresho bibiri birashobora kugora gutandukanya. Nk’akarorero, Phoenix ni porogarama ikoreshwa kuri telefone ngendanwa ishiramwo urudodo nyarwo rwa Lightning, ariko uwuyikoresha ntabwo ari ngombwa ko abimenya, kuko uburemere bwose bw’imikorere yayo hafi yose bunyegejwe.



### Iciyumviro c'ingene Lightning Network ikora 



Muri iki gice, nzokwibutsa vyihuse ingene Umuravyo ukora. Na none, nimba ushaka gushikiriza mu buryo bwimbitse ivyiyumviro vy’inyigisho, ndagutumiye ngo urabe inyigisho yihariye ya LNP 201.



#### Inzira zo kwishura: gufungura, kuvugurura no gufunga



Umutima w’urubuga rwa Lightning ushingiye ku nzira zo kwishura zibiri. Umurongo urashobora gufungurwa (ni ukuvuga kuremwa), guhindurwa uko amafaranga y’umuravyo agenda, hanyuma ugafungwa. Uvuye ku mbonerahamwe ya onchain, umurongo nta kindi uretse 2/2 y’isohoka ry’imikono myinshi.



![Image](assets/fr/002.webp)



Kuva ku mbonerahamwe ya Lightning, ni umuhora wo kwishura ufise amahera agabanywa hagati y’abo babiri bari muri iyo nama.



![Image](assets/fr/003.webp)





- Gufungura umurongo:**



Ivyuma bibiri bifata ingingo yo gufungura umuhora. Umwe muri bo akora bitcoins mu bikorwa vy’ugucuruza vyitwa *ugucuruza kw’amahera*. Iryo soko rituma haba igisubizo gishingiye ku nyandiko y’imikono myinshi 2 kuri 2, ivyo bikaba bisobanura ko gukoresha ayo mahera kuri Bitcoin bisaba umukono w’ibihimba vyose bibiri biri mu muhora. Imbere yo gutanga iyo nzira, uwutanga amahera asaba uwundi gusinya *itangazo ryo gukura amahera*, ridasohorwa onchain, ariko rikaba rituma rishobora gusubirana amahera yaryo iyo habaye ingorane.



![Image](assets/fr/004.webp)





- Ibikorwa vya Commitment:**



Ivyo umurongo uri (i.e. ugusangira kwa sats hagati ya A na B) bigereranywa na *commitment transaction*, izwi ku nzira zompi ariko ntihita itangazwa ku rubuga rwa blockchain. Iyi transaction idondora ingene yosubira gusangira amafaranga y’umurongo onchain hakurikijwe amahera yatanzwe kuri Lightning.



Igihe cose umuravyo urishe, izo nzira zibiri zishira umukono ku ntara nshasha isubirira iya mbere. Leta ya kera irakurwaho biciye ku buryo bw’ingenzi bwo gukurwaho: iyo umwe mu bari muri iyo nama agerageje gutangaza Leta ya kera, uwundi arashobora gusubirana amahera yose nk’igihano.



Iciyumviro gihambaye aha ni uko hama hariho ugucuruza kwa Bitcoin kwasinywe, kutamenyeshwa ku murongo, kubikwa n’ibihimba, kandi bikaba bishoboza gusubira gusangira sats y’uwundi hakurikijwe amahera yatanzwe kuri Lightning Network.



![Image](assets/fr/005.webp)





- Gufunga umurongo:**



Umurongo urashobora gupfungwa neza biciye mu gufunga kw’ubufatanye, iyo abo bompi bemeranije ku bijanye n’ingene uwo muhora uzoba umeze, canke ku ruhande rumwe (ugufunga ku nguvu) iyo umwe mu bari muri wo ahagaritse gukorana canke akabura uko ashobora gushikirwa. Mu bihe vyose, ugufunga bifata uburyo bw’ugucuruza ku ruzitiro rukoresha 2/2 c’umusaruro maze rugagabanya amahera hagati y’abaje mu nama hakurikijwe uburyo bwa nyuma bubereye bw’umurongo.



![Image](assets/fr/006.webp)



Umuravyo rero ukora nk’igice ca kabiri gishingiye kuri Bitcoin: ibintu bimwebimwe gusa (ugufungura n’ugufunga imirongo) ni vyo biboneka ku nzira nyamukuru y’uruzitiro. Ivyishyurwa vyo hagati biguma bitari mu murongo.



Imbere yo kuja kure, ng’ibi ivyiyumviro bibiri vy’ingenzi vyo gutahura ingene wocungera umurongo w’umuravyo:




- Liquidity*: igitigiri ca sats kiri ku ruhande rumwe rw’umuyoboro;
- *Ubushobozi*: ni umubare wose ufungiwe mu 2/2 multisig output, ni ukuvuga umubare w’amahera ku mpande zompi z’umuhora.



#### Urubuga rw'imirongo n'amahera



Umurongo si uwo kwishura gusa hagati y’ibice bibiri: ni igice c’uruja n’uruza rw’isi yose rw’imirongo ihuye. Node yawe irashobora gutuma abandi bakoresha bashobora kwishura biciye mu nzira zayo bwite, kandi ushobora kohereza sats ku node ya Lightning udafise inzira itaziguye, igihe cose inzira ibereye ishobora kuboneka hagati y’inzira zawe zibiri.



Buri nzira irazi, biciye ku nzira y’urusaku, ikarita y’uru rubuga: ni imihora iyihe iriho, ni iyihe nzira ihuzwa n’umurongo w’inzira zibiri, n’ubushobozi ubuhe busohorwa. Kugira ngo wohereze amahera ku muntu yakira ata murongo utaziguye, urudodo rwawe ruharura inzira igizwe n’ibice vyinshi: urudodo rwawe → urudodo X → urudodo Y → urudodo rw’uwuronka. Ku hop yose, ukwishyura guca mu muhora utegerezwa kuba ufise amahera ahagije mu bijanye n’ukwishyura.



![Image](assets/fr/007.webp)



Uguhinduka kw’amazi rero kw’umuyoboro ntiguhuye: uruhande rumwe rushobora kuba rwuzuye cane, mu gihe urundi rukaba hafi y’ubusa. Gucungera iyo nzira, ni ukuvuga kumenya aho sats iri n’aho ishobora guca, ni kimwe mu bintu bihambaye cane mu gukoresha urudodo rwa Lightning. Tuzobiraba mu buryo burambuye mu bigabane vy’ingirakamaro bizoza.



#### HTLC: gutwara amahera ataco wibwe



Kugira ngo amafaranga yishurwa ashobore guca mu bice vyo hagati ataco akeneye kwizigira, Lightning ikoresha amasezerano y’ubwenge yitwa *HTLC* (*Contracts zifunze igihe-Hashed*). Mu majambo yoroshe, HTLC ituma ukwimurira amahera bishingiye ku guhishurirwa kw’ibanga, kandi igashiramwo igihe co kurinda uwuyohereje iyo amafaranga yoba yananiwe. Igihe cose umuntu yishuye rero, ashobora gushikiriza ishusho y’imbere (ibanga rifise hash ihuye n’agaciro bemeranije). Iyo uwuherutse kwakira atanga iyo shusho y’imbere, arashobora gusaba ayo mahera, ivyo na vyo bikaba bituma buri nzira y’ubuhuza ishobora gusubirana amahera yayo.



![Image](assets/fr/008.webp)



Nzobazigama amakuru y’ubuhinga y’ingene HTLC zikora, kuko zitari ngombwa ku ntumbero z’iri shure. Uzosanga insobanuro yimbitse mu nyigisho y’inyigisho ya LNP 201. Ibuka gusa ko HTLCs zishobora gutuma haba uguhinduranya atome: canke iyo nzira iraheza nta n’umwe akomerewe mu nzira, canke bikananirwa maze umwe wese mu bari muri iyo nzira agasubirana amahera yiwe ya mbere. Nta n’umwe ari hagati.



### Ishirwa mu ngiro ry'urudodo rw'umuravyo



Nk’uko biri kuri Bitcoin, hariho uburyo bwinshi bwo gushirwa mu ngiro kw’itegeko ry’umuravyo. Amashirahamwe yigenga menshi ariko arategura amaverisiyo yayo, yose akaba akorana kuko yubahiriza ingingo zimwe (ivyo bita BOLTs). Aha niho hari ivyiyumviro nyamukuru bikoreshwa muri iki gihe.



#### LND (*Lightning Network Daemon*)



LND ni ugushirwa mu ngiro kwuzuye kw’itegeko ry’umuravyo, ryanditswe muri Go kandi ryateguwe na Lightning Labs.



![Image](assets/fr/009.webp)



#### Umuravyo w'Ishingiro (*CLN*)



Umuravyo w’Ishingiro (wahoze yitwa *C-Umuravyo*) ni ugushirwa mu ngiro kwateguwe na Blockstream. Yanditswe mu rurimi rwa C, hari n’ibihimba bimwebimwe biri muri Rust.



![Image](assets/fr/010.webp)



#### Eclair



Eclair ni uburyo bwo gushirwa mu ngiro bwanditswe mu rurimi rwa Scala kandi bwateguwe n’ishirahamwe ry’abafaransa ACINQ. ACINQ ikoresha imwe mu nzira zihambaye cane mu rubuga rwa Lightning hamwe na Eclair, kandi ikoresha iyo nzira nk’ishimikiro rya porogarama y’ibicuruzwa vyayo bwite, nk’ikoreshwa rya Phoenix.



![Image](assets/fr/011.webp)



#### LDK (*Igikoresho co guteza imbere umuravyo*)



LDK (*Igikoresho co guteza imbere umuravyo*) ni igikoresho co gutegura Rust, gicungiwe na Spiral (Block, ex-Square). Si daemon yiteguye gukoreshwa nka LND canke CLN, ariko ni ububiko bw’ibitabu bw’abahinguzi bipfuza kwinjiza Lightning mu bikorwa vyabo.



![Image](assets/fr/012.webp)



Muri iri shure rya LNP202, tuzokwibanda cane cane kuri LND: ugushirwa mu ngiro gukoreshwa cane mu gutorera umuti ibibazo vy'abaguzi b'abikorera ku giti cabo, nka Umbrel.

## Ni kuki ukoresha node yawe y’umuravyo?


<chapterId>421db24e-511c-41ed-ad68-69b0662042ea</chapterId>



Kwishura iki kibazo ni ikintu coroshe cane, kuko ari amajambo y’uruyeri: ata node yawe bwite, ntuba ugikoresha vy’ukuri Lightning, ahubwo ni ukwihenda gusa kw’Imiravyo mu bikorwa remezo vy’ishirahamwe.



Gukoresha umuyagankuba wallet bisigura ko bitcoins mu buryo bw’ubuhinga ari ivy’ishirahamwe rikoresha iyo node. Ntufata imfunguruzo z’ibanga, kandi ntugenzura imirongo. Igitigiri cawe ca wallet ni umurongo gusa uri mu rutonde rw’abaguha ibikorwa. Ivyo bintu birabereye cane abatangura, kandi ubumenyi bw’abakoresha burahinduka kenshi, ariko ikibazo nyamukuru ni iki: ni akamaro akahe ko gukoresha Bitcoin na Lightning iyo uhejeje kureka imice nyene ibatandukanya n’amahera ya kera n’amabanki?



Ivyiyumviro bibiri nyamukuru vy’agaciro vya Bitcoin ni ubusegaba bw’amahera (butagishingiye ku butegetsi bukuru bwo gutanga no kugumya) n’ukurwanya ugucengera (ukudashoboka ko uwundi muntu ashobora kubuza canke gucungera amahera yishurwa). Uburyo bwo kubika amahera kuri Lightning burajana n’izo ntumbero zompi: ntushobora kugenzura amahera yo mu mutima w’urubuga, kandi mu busobanuro, umukoresha afise amahera yose n’imirongo yose arashobora gucengera, gutevya, gushiramwo imbere canke guhagarika amahera yawe. Muri ivyo bihe, turashobora kwibaza mu buryo bubereye, **ni akamaro akahe ko gukoresha bitcoin biciye ku Lightning nimba izosubiramwo uburyo bumwe bwo kwizigira no kwizigira nk’uko bimeze mu mice y’amahera ya Leta**.



> Ico gikenewe ni uburyo bwo kwishura bushingiye ku buhinga bwa none bushingiye ku bimenyamenya vy’ubuhinga bwa none aho kwizigirana, bikaba vyemerera abantu babiri bose bashaka gukorana ataco bakeneye ata muntu agira gatatu yizigirwa.
*Satoshi Nakamoto, Bitcoin Urupapuro rweranda


Filozofiya ku ruhande, ingorane zikomeye cane kuri wewe ni izi zikurikira. Ica mbere, nta buryo ufise bwo kugenzura ko iyo sosiyete mu vy’ukuri ifise ama bitcoins ahuye n’amafaranga yerekanwa. Ishobora gukora ku bubiko bw’ibice, igafatwa, igahomba canke ikazimangana gusa. Muri ivyo, uri uwundi muntu gusa afise umwenda, ata cizigiro nyaco c’uko uzosubizwa amahera yawe.



Ica kabiri, iyo sosiyete irashobora gushikirwa n’ingorane zijanye n’amategeko: itegeko, guhagarika amahera, gusaba guhagarika abakoresha canke gukorana, gukomeza gucungera, canke mbere kubuza ata gufyina ibikorwa mu bihugu bimwebimwe. Intambamyi yose iremerera uwuguha ubufasha mu buryo bw’ubuhinga iraguzirikana.



Ku bijanye n’ibanga, ntaco bimaze. Umukozi w’ububiko abona amahera yawe yose: amahera, incuro, abayakira, amafaranga asigaye, ingeso zo gukoresha amahera. Iyo ifatanijwe n’amakuru yatanzwe n’igisata be n’isesengura ry’uruhererekane ry’inyuma kuri Bitcoin, ayo makuru arashobora gutanga urutonde rw’ibikorwa vyawe vy’ivy’amahera. Na none, ni kure cane y’intumbero ya Bitcoin yo kugabanya ukugenzura ivy’amahera.



Inkuru nziza ni uko uno musi, gukoresha node yawe bwite ya Lightning atari ikintu c’abahinga mu vy’ubuhinga, nk’uko vyari bishobora kuba vyari mu mpera z’imyaka ya 2010. Hariho inyishu zoroshe cane ku bakoresha mu rugo, ivyo tuzobisigura mu buryo burambuye mu kigabane gikurikira.




## Guhitamwo umuti mwiza w'akazi


<chapterId>615870e3-741d-4ec1-875d-a483e70f39d4</chapterId>



Uyu musi, birashoboka ko umuntu agira ubumenyi bwo gukoresha bujanye n’ubw’i Lightning custodial wallet, mu gihe aguma mu kwizigama. Intumbero y’iki gice ni ukugufasha guhitamwo inzira ibereye cane urutonde rwawe.



### Ihitamwo rya 1: Ntukoreshe umuravyo utaziguye



Umuti wa mbere ni ukudakoresha Lightning mu buryo bw’akavukire, ahubwo ni ugukoresha Bitcoin canke Liquid wallet ishiramwo ivy’uguhinduranya atome. Nk’akarorero, amaporogarama ya Aqua canke Bull Bitcoin Wallet akoresha ubu buryo, mu kugufasha kwishura amafagitire ya Lightning utakoresheje node ya Lightning wewe nyene, mu gihe uguma wicungera.



Ingingo ngenderwako iratomoye: amahera yawe aguma muri Bitcoin, yaba on-chain canke kuri Liquid, ukayaronka biciye ku wallet aho ufata imfunguruzo mu buryo bwa kera. Iyo ukoresheje scanner y’inyemezabuguzi y’umuravyo, wallet yawe itangura ibikorwa (on-chain canke Liquid) ku gikorwa co guhindura atome. Iyi serivisi rero igenzura ukwishyura kw’umuravyo biciye ku nzira yayo bwite, ikoresheje ama bitcoins watanze on-chain canke biciye ku nzira ya Liquid. Ivyo bituma udategerezwa gukorana n’imirongo yose ya Lightning wewe nyene, yamara urashobora gutanga amafagitire ya Lightning ata nkomanzi.



![Image](assets/fr/013.webp)



Inyungu nini y’iyi nzira, ugereranyije n’iyindi wallet isanzwe y’ububiko bwa Lightning, ni uko uguma ufise amahera yawe 100% ibihe vyose. Ivyo bice vy’amahera biri mu ruzitiro rwawe canke Liquid wallet, n’ijambo ryawe ry’ukwibuka. No mu gihe c’uguhinduranya, uguma ufise amahera yawe, kuko uguhinduranya ni atome. Ishingiye ku buryo bwo gukingira amakuru bugaragaza ko hariho ingaruka zibiri gusa zishoboka: canke iyo swap iraroranirwa yose, canke irananirwa kandi iyo serivisi ntishobora kwigarurira amahera yawe.



Ivyinshi mu bitabo bitanga ubwo bwoko bw’imikorere bishingiye kuri [Boltz](https://boltz.exchange/) ku bijanye n’igice c’ubuhinga c’uguhinduranya.



Uwo muti kandi uratanga inyungu zishimishije mu bijanye n’ibanga, cane cane iyo ufatanijwe na Liquid. Ku muntu atanguye, biroroshe cane gushinga no kubika: ijambo ry'intangarugero, nta mihora, nta mafaranga yo guhuza...



Ku rundi ruhande, iyo nzira irafise aho igarukira. Ica mbere, ntaco bimaze: uvana n’ukuboneka n’ubuntu bwiza bw’igikorwa co guhinduranya. Iyo itagishaka gukora kuri konti yawe, canke igahagarika gukora, ntushobora kuzosubira kwishura amafagitire ya Lightning biciye kuri yo. Hanyuma hariho amafaranga atagira uko asa: uriha amafaranga yose yo gukoresha onchain canke Liquid, n’amahera y’uguhinduranya. Kandi, iyo amafaranga y’onchain azamutse cane, birashobora kuba bizimvye cane gukoresha Lightning.



Rero ku gukoresha rimwe na rimwe, biracari vyemewe, ariko ku mukoresha w’umuravyo akora cane, ni vyiza gukora ibintu neza n’urudodo rw’umuravyo nyarwo.



### Ihitamwo rya 2: Ivyuma vy'umuravyo biri ku bwato



Urutonde rwa kabiri rw’ibisubizo rushingiye ku nzira z’umuravyo zishizwe ata guca ku ruhande mu gikorwa co kuri telefone ngendanwa. Phoenix Wallet ni yo yatanguje iyo nzira kandi iracari ikigereranyo. Muri iki gihe, iyindi migambi iratanga uburyo bugereranywa, nka Zeus (mu buryo bushizwemwo) canke BitKit.



Iciyumviro ni coroshe: mu vy’ukuri iyo porogarama ikoresha urudodo rwa Lightning, ariko ibikorwa vyose bigoranye bikorwa ubwavyo mu nyuma. Ufise "*Lightning wallet*" interface ifise ijambo ry'ukwibuka ryo gusubiza inyuma, ubona umubare w'amafaranga n'amafagitire, ariko ntucungera imirongo, amafaranga canke amaparametere menshi.



![Image](assets/fr/014.webp)



Ivyo bisubizo vyama ari ivyo kwirinda. Imfunguruzo zigenzura ayo mahera ni generated kandi zibikwa kuri telefone yawe, kandi ububiko buca kuri seed canke uburyo bungana n’ubwo. Ntugira gusa konti n’uwuguha amakuru, mu vy’ukuri ufise ama bitcoins yugarijwe mu mihora ari iyawe kandi adashobora kwibwa.



Ivyiza vy’ibikoresho vyo mu bwato vya LN ni vyinshi:




- biroroshe cane gushiramwo no gukoresha;
- ubumenyi bw’abakoresha buri hafi y’ubw’umuravyo wallet, ariko ufise uburenganzira bwo kwizigama;
- nta gucunga n’amaboko imihora canke amahera;
- gusubiza inyuma vyoroshe cane.



Ariko izo wallet zishizwemwo na zo nyene zirafise aho zigarukira cane. Ica mbere, ku bijanye n’ibanga, umukoresha w’ibikorwa (nk’akarorero ACINQ ku bijanye na Phoenix) arafise iciyumviro c’ido n’ido c’imigenderanire ica mu nzira yawe: amafaranga, incuro, abayironka, naho ivyo vyoba bitegerezwa gutera imbere, cane cane mu kwemera buhoro buhoro **T Noporam. Ica kabiri, wizigiye cane uwo mukoresha nk’umugenzi wawe nyamukuru w’umuravyo. Iyo node ya ACINQ itabonetse (mu gihe ca Phoenix), iyo sosiyete ishikiwe n’imikazo y’amategeko canke ihinduye uburyo bwayo bwo gukora, ubumenyi bwawe bwo gukoresha bushobora gusenyuka cane, canke mbere bugahungabana.



Ubwa nyuma, ukwo kworoha kuraza n’igiciro. Ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa LN bishizwemwo muri rusangi bisaba amahera yihariye ku vyo ubika, ukukura canke ibikorwa bimwebimwe vyo gucunga imirongo. Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, iki kigereranyo kirafise insiguro mu bijanye n’igikorwa gitangwa, ariko ku gukoresha cane, gishobora kuba gizimvye cane kuruta urudodo rwa Lightning rusanzwe rucungiwe neza.



### Ihitamwo rya 3: urudodo rwa kera rw'umuravyo



Umuti wa gatatu, uwo tuzoba turiko turaraba mu buryo bwimbitse muri iki cigwa ca LNP202, ni ugukoresha urudodo rwa Lightning rusanzwe kuri server canke igikoresho cihariye.



Mu kuvuga "classic" ndashaka kuvuga ko ushiramwo kandi ugatunganya ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa Lightning (nk'akarorero LND) wewe nyene hejuru y'umurongo wawe bwite wa Bitcoin. Uhitamwo abo mu runganwe rwawe, ugafungura imihora yawe, ugacungera amahera yawe yinjira n’asohoka, kandi ugashinga amategeko y’amahera y’urugendo.



Ku bijanye n’ubusegaba, ni umuti mwiza. Ntugishingiye ku sosiyete yihariye ku mihora yawe canke amahera wishyura: iyo umugenzi wawe akucengera canke akafunga umurongo, urashobora gufungura uwundi ufise urudodo rutandukanye. Iyo servisi izimye, sats yawe iguma mu mihora ugenzura, kandi urashobora kuyisubiza mu gihugu cawe onchain. Ushobora kandi gutuma ibiciro vyawe vy’igihe kirekire bigenda neza: iyo imirongo yawe imaze guhabwa ubunini bubereye kandi igacungirwa neza, igiciro cose c’ukwishura gishobora kuba gito cane.



Ku bijanye n’ibanga, biragaragara ko uriko urashikirwa n’imipaka y’akarorero ka Lightning ubwayo, ariko nturiko uratanga ubucuruzi bwawe bwose ku muntu umwe.



Mu buryo bunyuranye, gushinga node ya Lightning ya kera biragaragara ko ari ikintu gikomeye cane: utegerezwa gushiramwo, gutunganya, kubungabunga, gukurikirana ivyagezwe, gutahura uburyo imirongo n’amategeko y’ugusaba amafaranga, gucunga imirongo n’amahera, n’ibindi. Gutegura nabi, gucungera nabi canke gucungera nabi bishobora gutuma sats itakaza. Ico gice na co nyene kigomba gukora ubudasiba.



Iyi ni yo nzira nyene ndagusaba gukurikira muri iri shure, ikaguherekeza intambwe yose y’inzira kugira ngo ugabanye ingorane no gutunganya uburyo ukoresha.

## Kuzigama urudodo rwawe rw'umuravyo


<chapterId>638fa75d-62af-4bf3-ab4a-b7d10ea75815</chapterId>



Imbere yo kohereza sats yawe ya mbere ku nzira yawe, birahambaye ko utahura ingene ububiko bwayo bukora n’ingorane zijanye n’ivyo. Udakunze igitabu coroshe ca Bitcoin onchain, gushigikira urudodo rwa Lightning biragoye cane: ubuhinga butari bwo burashobora gutuma amahera yawe atakaza ubudasiba. Muri iki gice, turaza kuraba ivyo vy'ukuri bikwiye gushigikirwa, n'ingene Umbrel ifata iyo nzira na LND.



Muri iki cigwa, tuzoba turiko turakoresha uburyo bwo gushirwa mu ngiro kwa LND (*Lightning Network Daemon*). Naho ingingo ngenderwako zisa n'izindi nzira, amadosiye yo gusubizaho n'uburyo ngiye kuvuga ni vy'umwihariko kuri LND.



### Ni igiki nozigama ku nzira y’umuravyo?



Ku node ya Lightning, ntibihagije gukiza seed no kwizigira ko vyose bizosubira uko vyari. Ibintu vyinshi birafise uruhara rutandukanye, ni ngombwa rero kubitandukanya.



#### Igitabu ca seed (*cavuzwe*)



Iyo utanguje LND, uronka seed y'amajambo 24. Iyi ni uburyo bwihariye bwa LND bwitwa "*aezeed*". Si seed BIP39 ya kera, naho isa cane n’iyindi. Kuri iyi seed, LND ikuramwo imfunguruzo z’ibanga z’uruzitiro rwawe rwa wallet zijanye n’uruzitiro rwa Lightning, ni ukuvuga amaderesi ushobora kwakira canke ushobora gusubizamwo amafaranga y’ama bitcoins akurikije ugufunga kw’imirongo.



![Image](assets/fr/019.webp)



Iyi seed rero ishobora gukoreshwa mu gusubira kurema onchain wallet ifatanye n’urudodo rwawe, no kugarura amahera yari amaze gusubirwamwo onchain (nk’akarorero inyuma y’ugufunga umurongo). Ku rundi ruhande, seed yonyene ntihagije kugira ngo usubiremwo imirongo yawe ya Lightning ikiri yuguruye, kuko itarimwo amakuru akenewe ku bijanye n’ingene imirongo yawe imeze.



#### Ububiko bw'amakuru bw'umurongo (`umurongo.db`)



LND ibika ivy'imirongo yawe mu rutonde rw'amakuru rwitwa `channel.db`. Ubu bubiko bw'amakuru burimwo:




- urutonde rw’imirongo yawe yuguruye;
- abo mu runganwe rwawe n’ibimenyetso vyabo;
- commitment transactions za nyuma ku muhora wose (intara zikurikirana zisobanura uwufise iki mu muhora kandi zituma amahera yo kuri onchain ashobora gusubirwamwo iyo habaye ingorane);
- amakuru akenewe kugira ngo umuntu ahane umugenzi agerageza gutangaza raporo ya kera.



Ikibazo c’iyi database ni uko iguma ihinduka: ukwishyura kwose, inzira yose, ugufungura canke ugufunga kwose kw’umurongo birahindura ibirimwo. Ni co gituma ububiko busanzwe (nk'akarorero kopi y'igihe ca `channel.db` yawe) ari akaga. Niwagarura verisiyo ya kera cane ya `channel.db` maze ugasubira gukoranya urudodo n'iyi nzira ya kera, abo mu runganwe rwawe bashobora kubona ko uriko uratangaza inzira ya kera. Muri ivyo, iyo porotokole iratanga igihano: uwo mugenzi wawe arashobora gusubirana amahera yose y’uwo muhora, nk’aho woba wagerageje guhenda.



Mu bikorwa rero, `channel.db` si uburyo bwo gusubiza inyuma nk'uko biri. Ni ubuzima bw’urudodo rwawe. Igihe conyene bishobora gutuma uyikoresha kugira ngo usubizeho urudodo rwawe ni igihe usubizaho uru rutonde rw'amakuru uhereye ku mashini iherutse gusenyuka (nk'akarorero, disiki ishobora gusomwa). Muri ivyo, urasubirana igihugu ca kera cane kandi ushobora gusubira gutangura LND ku yindi mashini ishingiye kuri iyi nkuru, ufise umutekano mu kumenya ko iyo ntambwe iri ku gihe uko bishoboka kwose, kuko imashini ya kera itagikora. Ikindi kintu aho ubu buryo bushobora gukora nk’ububiko bubereye ni mu gutunganya amadisike abiri, afise kopi ikora, ihoraho kuva kuri imwe gushika ku yindi. Ariko rero, ubwo bwoko bw’ugutegura buragoye cane gushirwa mu ngiro.



Ariko gukora kopi z'ibihe bimwe bimwe za `channel.db` no kuzibika nk'ivyo uzosubiramwo mu nyuma ni iciyumviro kibi cane: umusi uzozikoresha, urashobora kwihana no gutakaza sats yawe yose.



#### Ububiko bw'umurongo uhagaze (SCB)



Kugira ngo gusubirana bishoboke, LND yarashizeho uburyo bwa *Static Channel Backup* (SCB). Iyi ni dosiye idasanzwe, akenshi yitwa `channel.backup`, irimwo amakuru adahinduka yerekeye imirongo yawe: abo mu runganwe rwawe ari bo, ingene wobamenyesha n’imirongo yawe ni iyihe.



Iyi dosiye ntirimwo amakuru y'ido n'ido y'imirongo canke amateka y'ivugurura. Ntibiguha uburenganzira bwo gusubira gufungura imirongo mu buryo nyabwo yarimwo, canke ngo ukomeze gukoresha iyi node ya Lightning. Ahubwo, uruhara rwayo ni ugukora nk’inanga kugira ngo usabe abo mu runganwe rwawe bagufashe gufunga neza imirongo yawe yose, gutyo ukaronka sats yawe onchain, ku mfunguruzo ushobora kuronka kubera seed. Rero, bitandukanye n’idosiye `channel.db`, ​​ihindurwa uko umuntu yishuye canke akoresheje inzira, dosiye ya SCB ihindurwa gusa iyo umurongo ufunguwe canke ufunze.



Iyo umuntu akira biciye ku SCB, inzira ni iyi:




- Ugarura seed yawe (*aezeed*), igasubira kurema wallet yawe y’uruzitiro ifatanye n’urudodo rw’Umuravyo;
- Utanga LND na SCB yawe iherutse gusohoka;
- LND ikoresha SCB kugira ironke urutonde rw’urunganwe rwawe n’imirongo ufise na bo;
- Ihamagara buri mugenzi wawe, ikamubwira ko watakaje amakuru maze ikabasaba "gufunga ku nguvu" umurongo wawe na bo, kugira ngo ushobore kugarura umugabane wawe wa onchain.



Iciyumviro ni uko abo mu runganwe rwawe, babonye ko uriko uratanga raporo y’ugutakaza amakuru, bazotangaza commitment transaction yabo ya nyuma maze bafunge umurongo w’inguvu. Ivyo bikoresho bimaze kwemezwa, amahera yawe asubira kugaragara mu gitabu cawe co ku rubuga rwa onchain (ruhuye na seed).



Ubwo buryo bwo gukira ntibutunganye, ariko. Ica mbere, mu vy’ukuri ntibigarura node yawe ya Lightning, kuko imirongo yose izopfungwa. Uzoca ubwirizwa kwubaka urudodo rushasha rw’Umuravyo kuva mu ntango. Ica kabiri, ntibitanga icemeza ko uzokira 100%, naho nyene vyongera cane amahirwe yo gusubirana amafaranga yawe yo kuri onchain iyo habaye ingorane. Nkako, iyo porotokole yo gusubirana ivana n’ugukorana n’ukuboneka kw’urunganwe rwawe: iyo umwe muri bo atari mu nzira, yatakaje amakuru yiwe bwite canke yanse gukorana, amahera yawe yoshobora kuguma abujijwe, canke mbere akabura ubuziraherezo. Ni co gituma bihambaye ko udakomeza gufungura imirongo ku nzira yawe y’umuravyo n’urunganwe rudashobora gushikirwa igihe kirekire. Iyo ushikiwe n’ugutakaza amakuru muri iyo nkuru kandi uwo mugenzi akaguma adashobora gushikirwa, gusubirana biciye muri SCB ntibizoshoboka, kandi amahera yawe azoguma atakaye gushika uwo mugenzi agarutse kuri internet (kumbure ibihe bidahera).



Mu ncamake, ubuhinga bwiza bwo gusubiza inyuma Lightning kuri LND bushingiye ku nkingi zitatu:




- seed yawe (*aezeed*), ku bijanye n’uruzitiro rw’uruzitiro;
- Ivyiyumviro vy’ukwizigirwa vy’ubuhinga bwa SCB;
- Uburongozi bwiza bw’imihora mu guhitamwo abo mu runganwe rwizigirwa no gufunga mu buryo bwo kwirinda abo akenshi badashobora gushikirwa.



### Umbrel icungera gute ububiko bw’uruzitiro rwawe rwa LND?



Umbrel itanga uburyo bworoshe bwo gusubiza inyuma urudodo rwa LND, rushingiye canecane kuri SCB. Iciyumviro ni ukugukiza ingorane zo gukoresha iyi dosiye wewe nyene, ukayigira backup no kuyikoresha mu buryo bwikora uko bishoboka kwose.



Iyo uremye node yawe kuri Umbrel, uronka seed ifise uruhara rubiri:




- iva kuri Bitcoin yawe onchain wallet ifatanye n’urudodo rwawe rw’Umuravyo;
- ikoreshwa mu kuronka ikimenyetso c'ububiko n'urufunguzo rwo gufungura rukoreshwa ku bubiko bwa SCB bwo kure.



Biciye kuri ubwo buryo, Umbrel ihita ikora backup ya SCB yawe ipfutse maze ikayibika ku maserukira yayo biciye kuri Tor. SCB ibikwa mu buryo bupfutse, kubera urufunguzo rwavuye muri seed yawe. Rero, iyo amakuru atakaye, ivyo ubwirizwa gukora n'ugusubira kurema Bitcoin na Lightning node kuri Umbrel, kuri iyo mashini nyene canke iyindi, hanyuma winjize seed yawe. Uzoheza ushobore kugarura status ya SCB nshasha ku ma server ya Umbrel, uyifungure maze utangure igikorwa co kugarura amahera yawe.



Ivyo bimenyetso vy’ububiko bishirwa mu nzira n’uruzitiro rwawe imbere y’uko birungikwa, ivyo bikaba bituma amakuru yawe aba ibanga: Umbrel ntishobora gusoma ibirimwo muri SCB. Ugutanga amakuru bica kuri Tor, ivyo bikaba bituma aderesi yawe ya IP idashobora gusohoka. Ikindi, Umbrel yawe yongera urusaku ku nzira (random padding n’ibinyoma vyoherezwa mu bihe bidasanzwe) kugira ngo server ntishobore gucapura neza igihe ufunguye canke ufunze umurongo.



Inyungu nyamukuru y’ubu buryo ni uko bworosha cane gukora backup y’urudodo rwawe rwa Lightning: ubwirizwa gukora backup ya seed yawe rimwe gusa mu gihe co gutangura urudodo. Ivyo birafise ingorane, kuko ari ugushigikira gusa SCB, ariko ku mahera abereye ni ugusenyera ku mugozi umwe kwemewe.



### Ivyiza vyo kugabanya ingorane zo gutakaza

Iyo umurongo uhambaye ukunda gufatanya n’umugenzi udashobora gushikirwa canke udashikamye, birarushiriza kuba vyiza kuwufunga neza mu gihe urudodo rwawe rukiriko rurakora. Ishirahamwe ry’ubufatanye ryo kwirinda canke gufunga ku nguvu birakuraho isoko ryoshobora gutera ingorane iyo SCB yoba yakira.





- Irinde kwibanda ku mafaranga menshi cane ku bagenzi batazwi:



Uko urunganwe rugira umurongo munini, ni ko bihambaye cane kuba uwo kwizigirwa. Hitamwo amanode akomeye, ahuye neza kandi akora, kugira ngo ugukira kwose kwo muri kazoza biciye kuri SCB gushobore kugenda neza.





- Yongerako Umbrel n'ivyo mu karere:



Uretse ububiko bwa Umbrel, urashobora kandi kubika kopi ya dosiye yawe ya SCB (`channel.backup`) ku bimenyeshamakuru vyo hanze maze ukayihindura buri gihe. Ivyiza ni uko woyisubiramwo igihe cose ufunguye canke ufunze umurongo. Ivyo biguha umuti wo gusubiza inyuma iyo, kubera imvo iyo ari yo yose, igikorwa co gusubiza inyuma c’ubuhinga bwa Umbrel kitaboneka.





- Gucungera neza seed yawe



seed Umbrel yawe ntisubiza gusa onchain wallet yawe, ariko kandi irakura urufunguzo rwo gupfuka amakuru y’inyuma. Umuntu atera afise uburenganzira bwo kuyironka rero yoshobora gutangura gusubirana maze akarungika amahera yawe kuri wallet yiwe bwite, ata n’uburenganzira bwo kuyironka afise.



Rero, niwaba ukeneye kugarura node yawe ariko ntugifise seed yawe, ntaco uzoshobora kugarura: sats yawe yose izobura. ## Watchtower: uruhara n'imiterere


<chapterId>e6c654dd-26c5-4e4d-8d11-a215bac37812</chapterId>



Mu Lightning, umurongo wose ushingiye ku rutonde rw’ibihugu bikurikirana, bigereranywa n’ibikoresho vya commitment transaction bitasohotse. Igihe cose Lightning yishuye canke irungitse inzira, abo 2 bari muri uwo muhora bubaka umugwi mushasha w’indege commitment transaction, zigaragaza ukuntu amahera ariko aragabanywa muri uwo muhora. commitment transaction za kera zica zicika ivya kera.



Iyo umwe mu bafatanyabikorwa asohoye Leta yacitse igihe, uwundi arafise uburenganzira bwo kuyihana no gusubirana amahera yose y’uwo muhora. Muri iki gice, turaza kuraba muri make ingene ubu buryo bukora, hanyuma dusigure ingene woshiraho ***watchtower***: uburyo bwo kurinda node yawe ya Lightning ku bigeragezo vyo guhenda.



### Gutahura ingene iminara y’inderetsi ikora 


Igihe cose, umuhari wose uri muri uwo muhora urafise commitment transaction iyo isohotse, yobafasha gufunga uwo muhora no gusubirana umugabane wabo w’amahera. Ivyo bimenyekana nka *ugufunga ku nguvu*. Ariko iyo bagerageza gusohora commitment transaction ya kera, ihuye n’ingene uwo muhora wari umeze kera aho wari ufise sats nyinshi, rero iyo nzira yofatwa nk’ukugerageza guhenda. Muri ivyo, uwundi muntu ashobora gukoresha urufunguruzo rwo gukuraho rujanye n’iyi leta ya kera kugira ngo asubire kuronka amahera yose ari mu muhora, mu gihe uwundi akora ubuhendanyi abujijwe by’igihe gito n’igihe co gufunga.


Iyi nzira isigura ko gutangaza Leta ya kera, ni ukuvuga kugerageza guhenda, ari akaga kanini: iyo uwundi abonye iyo nzira kuri mempool canke kuri blockchain imbere y’uko timelock ihera, barashobora gukoresha urufunguzo rwo gukuraho maze bagasubirana amahera yose. **Rero, umutekano w’umurongo wawe wa Lightning uvana n’ubushobozi ufise bwo kumenya umuntu agerageza guhenda mu gihe c’idirisha ry’igihe ryashizweho n’igihe**.



#### Ni kubera iki iminara y’inderetsi ari nkenerwa?



Uburyo bwo guhana bukora gusa iyo uwakomeretse ashoboye:




- gukurikirana igice gishasha cose ca Bitcoin kugira ngo barabe nimba hari umurongo wa commitment transaction wasohowe;
- kumenya nimba iyo nzira y’ugucuruza ihuye n’igihugu ca nyuma gifise uburenganzira canke n’igihugu cavuyeho;
- iyo habaye ikibazo cakuweho, kugira ngo utangaze ivy’ugucuruza bitegekanijwe n’amategeko mu gihe, ukoresheje urufunguzo rwo gukurwako kugira ngo ugarure amahera yose imbere y’uko igihe co gufunga gihera.



![Image](assets/fr/023.webp)



Mu gihe ciza, urudodo rwawe rw’umuravyo ruri kuri interineti 24/7, rurahuye kandi ruguma rukurikirana blockchain. Kubera iyo mvo, irashobora kumenya yonyene umuntu agerageza guhenda maze ikagira ico ikoze. Ariko mu bikorwa, urudodo rw’umuravyo rw’umuntu ku giti ciwe rurashobora guhagarara, canecane iyo umuyagankuba ucitse igihe kirekire canke iyo Internet igacitse.



Ni muri ivyo bihe vy’uguhagarara nyene aho ingorane iba nyayo: iyo umugenzi wawe w’umuhemu asohoye status ya kera mu gihe node yawe iri hanze y’umurongo, maze timelock igahera ataco ukora, ubwo buhendanyi buragira ico bukoze. Utakaza amahera yawe amwe canke yose mu muhora.



Watchtowers zashizweho kugira ngo ivyo bigabanye. Umunara w’abacungera ni igikorwa co hanze, kigenzura blockchain mu izina ryawe, kigacapura kugira ngo kibone ko hariho ugusohoka kw’imirongo ya kera kuri imwe mu nzira zawe, kandi, iyo bikenewe, kigatangaza ubwo nyene igikorwa c’igihano mu izina ryawe. Rero, naho node yawe ya Lightning yoguma itari ku murongo igihe kirekire, igihe cose umunara w’ibarabara uriko urakoresha uriko urakora, izoshobora kurinda amahera yawe mu kugenzura ukugerageza kwose kwo guhenda no gukoresha igihano gihuye, igihe nyene izoba ibonye ko hariho.



#### Ingene umunara w'inderetsi ukora 



Umunara w’inderetsi ugenewe kugabanya amakuru wiga ku bijanye n’imirongo yawe, mu gihe uwuha uburyo bwo gukora iyo habaye ingorane:




- Ku ntara yose y’umurongo mushasha ufise urunganwe, urudodo rwawe rutegura imbere y’igihe igihano gishobora gukoreshwa. Iyo habaye iyo nzira y’ubuhendanyi bw’urunganwe, iyo nzira yotuma ushobora gusubirana amahera yose ari mu muhora;
- Node yawe ica ipfuka iyo nzira y’igihano ikoresheje TXID ya commitment transaction ihuye (iyo yokoreshwa iyo uwo mubeshi agerageza gukora ubuhendanyi). Igihe cose ata gufunga kubaye, umunara w’indaba ntushobora gusobanura iyo nzira, kuko itazi neza TXID y’iyo nzira y’ubuhendanyi;
- Node yawe yohereza umunara w’abacungera ipaki irimwo igihano c’ugucuruza gipfutse n’igice ca TXID c’igikorwa co guhenda gishobora kuba.



Kubera ko TXID yoherezwa ku munara w’ibarabara idatunganye, ntishobora gusobanura ivy’ubutungane. Ariko rero, irashobora gukurikirana blockchain kugira ngo ibone TXID ihuye n’igice ifise. Iyo ibonye mwene ukwo gucuruza, ica igerageza gukoresha TXID yuzuye y’ico gicuruzwa kugira ngo ifungure ugucuruza kwawe kw’igihano. Iyo decryption ishoboye, iramenya ko ari ukugerageza guhenda maze igaca ikumenyesha igihano.



![Image](assets/fr/024.webp)



Umunara w’inderetsi rero nta n’umwe abona ido n’ido ry’imirongo yawe: nta n’akaranga k’abo mu runganwe rwawe, canke imibare, canke imiterere y’ibikorwa. Ibona gusa amapakete yashizwemwo amakuru. Amakuru yonyene ishobora gukuramwo ni urugero imirongo yawe ihindurwako, kuko ironka ipakete ku ntara yose nshasha, ariko ntishobora kumenya ibirimwo. Mu gihe co guhenda, nta gukeka ko izobona amakuru y’umurongo mu gukuraho igihano c’ugucuruza, ariko n’imiburiburi sats yawe izokizwa.



Ubwo buryo bushingiye ku gusenyera ku mugozi umwe: uha ubushobozi bwo gutangaza igihano cashizweko umukono imbere y’igihe ku munara w’inderetsi, ariko ico gikorwa kiguma kidatomoye rwose ku munara w’inderetsi gushika habayeho ubuhendanyi bumwe bumwe. Umunara w’inderetsi ntushobora guhindura abawuronka canke ngo uhindure amahera, kuko ufise gusa igikorwa camaze gusinywa, ivyo bivako bikaba bikonjeshejwe ku neza yawe. Eka mbere ntishobora kumenya ido n’ido ry’umurongo mu gufunga ku nguvu canke mu gukorana, kuko TXIDs zidahuye. Ku rundi ruhande, watchtower iguma ari umuntu wa gatatu yizigirwa cane: ukeneye kuyizigira kugira ngo ube kuri internet no gutangaza neza ubutungane bwawe igihe ubikeneye.



#### Gucika umunara w’inderetsi 



Mu vyiyumviro, urudodo urwo ari rwo rwose rwa Lightning rushobora gukora nk’umunara w’indaro ku zindi node (niba zikoresha iyo nzira nyene, nk’akarorero LND), mu gihe rwo ubwarwo rurindwa n’izindi node zirukorera uwo murimo. Mu bice bikurikira, nzokwereka ingene woshiraho ubu buryo bworoshe kuri LND yawe munsi ya Umbrel.


Nk’inkurikizi, ubuhinga bushimishije bwoshobora kuba ukwemeranya n’abagenzi bizigira ba bitcoiner kugira ngo bakore nk’umunara w’uwundi. Urakurikirana imirongo yabo, na bo bagakurikirana iyawe.

## Gufungura umurongo wawe wa mbere w'umuravyo


<chapterId>00642af7-8f3d-4a25-96d7-34e85de7bd5d</chapterId>



Niba warashitse aha, urazi ko urudodo rwa Lightning rudafise umurongo rumeze nk’urudodo rwa wallet rudafise ikintu: rurahari, ariko nta co rumaze. Kugira ngo ushobore kohereza canke kwakira amahera, urudodo rwawe rutegerezwa kuba rufatanye n’iyindi node n’imiburiburi mu rubuga rwa Lightning biciye ku muhora. Mu gihe kizoza, turahimiriza cane gufungura imihora myinshi, kubera imvo z’ukwihangana n’ugutuma inzira zigenda neza. Mu bice bikurikira, turaza kuraba kandi ingene wocungera itunga ryawe ry'amazi, ukoreshe neza ubuhinga bwawe bwo gukora no gukoresha ibikoresho biteye imbere kuruta interface ya LND kuri Umbrel.



Ariko muri iki gice c’intango, intumbero ni ugutahura gusa ingene wohitamwo umugenzi mwiza wa Lightning, aho woronkera amakuru ukeneye kugira uhitemwo abo mugenzi wawe, n’ubwa nyuma ingene wofungura umurongo wawe wa mbere biciye ku nzira y’ishimikiro ya LND.



### Ni gute wohitamwo umugenzi mwiza w’Umuravyo?



Iyo ufunguye umurongo, ukeneye guhitamwo umugenzi: uwundi murongo w’umuravyo uwo murongo wawe uzohuzwako ataco uhinduye, biciye ku murongo. Ukwo guhitamwo urunganwe ni ikintu gihambaye, kuko kuzogira ingaruka zitaziguye kuri:




- ukworoha kw’amahera yawe kugira ngo aronke inzira;
- ukwizigirwa kw’imirongo yawe uko igihe kigenda kirarenga;
- igiciro c’inzira yawe.



Nta kintu nk’ico co guhura n’umuntu wese, ariko hariho ingingo zitari nke zo kwizigirwa zo kumenya abahiganwa beza.



#### 1. Uguhuzwa kw'imitsi



Umugenzi mwiza ni urudodo rufatanye neza n’urubuga rwa Lightning. Ivyo ntibisigura gusa kugira imirongo myinshi (naho ivyo bishobora kuba ikimenyetso), ariko hejuru ya vyose kuba uhuye n’izindi nzira zishobora kwizigirwa kandi ukaba ufise ikibanza gihagije co hagati mu gicapo c’uruja n’uruza.



Igikoresho gifatanye neza kirongereza amahirwe yawe yo kuronka inzira nziza yo gushika aho wishura. Bigabanya kandi umubare w’ibihimba vy’ubuhuza bikenewe, ivyo bikaba bituma amafaranga aguma ari make.



Ku rundi ruhande, gufungura umurongo wawe wa mbere ku nzira iri ukwayo canke ihuye nabi bishobora gutuma ubushobozi bwawe bugabanuka: uzoshobora kwishura uwo mugenzi, ariko bizoba bigoye cane kwishura umurongo usigaye.



#### 2. Inyuguti nini n'ubushobozi bwo gukoresha



Urunganwe rwiza na rwo nyene ni urudodo rw’inyuguti nini zihagije. Ivyo vyerekanywe n’ubushobozi bwayo bwose bw’imirongo (umubare w’imirongo ya sats yiyemeje ku mirongo yayo yose) n’ubushobozi bw’imirongo yayo rusangi (kenshi ni vyiza kugira imirongo mikeyi gusa yanditswe neza kuruta imirongo myinshi mitoyi).



Node ifise ubushobozi buteye isoni, canke imirongo yayo yose ari mitoyi, ntizoshobora gutuma amahera yishurwa n’amahera menshi, ivyo bikaba bizotuma udakora neza.



#### 3. Amategeko agenga amafaranga



Buri nzira ishiraho amafaranga y'inzira (`amafaranga y'ishimikiro` na `igipimo c'amafaranga`). Umugenzi mwiza ntazosaba amahera menshi cane ku mihora y’ingene umuntu akora. Ku murongo wa mbere, ni vyiza guhitamwo urudodo rufise amafaranga aringaniye, kugira ngo ntubuze kwishura.



Ibuka ko amafaranga yawe bwite y’urugendo na yo nyene agira ico akoze ku kuntu abandi bakubona nk’urunganwe: urudodo ruhora ruhindura amafaranga rwarwo canke rushiraho amafaranga adasanzwe ntabwo rushimwa cane.



#### 4. Gushikama n’ubukuru



Gushikama kw’urunganwe ni ikintu gihambaye cane. Node nziza irafise igihe kinini co gukora (ni gake cane iri offline), iguma yuguruye imirongo yayo igihe kirekire kandi ntiguma ifungura/ifunga imirongo ata mpamvu.



Imirongo ya kera kandi ikiriko irakora ni ikimenyetso ciza: ivuga ko ubucuti bufise inyungu ku mugenzi, ko node izi gucunga umutungo wayo kandi ko idafunga igihe cose, rero ntubwirizwa kuguma uriha amahera ya onchain yo gufunga no gusubira gufungura.



Ku rundi ruhande, umugenzi akenshi atari ku rubuga, canke afunga imirongo ningoga, arashobora kuba isoko y’ingorane kuri wewe, n’amahera y’inyongera yo gufungura imirongo mishasha.



Naho woba ufise ivyo bipimo, ntuzohitamwo neza igihe ca mbere. Ivyo ni ibisanzwe: kamere nyakuri y’urunganwe igaragazwa n’ikoreshwa ryayo. Ni ngombwa rero:




- gukurikirana igikorwa c’umurongo (ibiharuro vy’inzira, ukuboneka, n’ibindi);
- gufunga imihora idafise akamaro canke iyo abo mu runganwe rwayo kenshi cane bataja kuri Internet;
- subira guha umutungo wawe abo mu runganwe rwawe beza uko igihe kigenda kirarenga.



Iciyumviro si ugufungura no gufunga imihora buri musi (ivyo vyoba bizimvye mu biciro vy’uruzitiro), ariko ni uguhindura buhoro buhoro ubuhinga bwawe kugira ngo ushire hamwe ku rutonde rw’urunganwe rwizigirwa, rufitaniye isano ryiza rujanye n’ivyo ukeneye.

# Gucungera urudodo rwawe rw'umuravyo

## Sigura umwirondoro wawe w'umukoresha


<chapterId>d3b2e163-50f6-4d1d-a5fc-8fd177dfac76</chapterId>



Ubu ko node yawe ya Lightning iriko irakora, intambwe ikurikira ni ugusobanura urutonde rw’umucuruzi wawe. Iryo ni ihitamwo rihambaye, kuko rigena ingamba zawe zo gufungura umuhora, ubwoko bw’urunganwe ukunda, n’ingene ucungera amahera.



Ku Lightning, kugira ivyo bikore, ukeneye amahera mu nzira ibereye. Amahera asohoka ahuye n’ubushobozi ufise bwo kwishura (sats iboneka ku ruhande rwawe rw’imirongo). Amahera yinjira ahuye n’ubushobozi ufise bwo kwakira (sats iboneka ku ruhande rw’urunganwe rwawe). Mu yandi majambo, urutonde rwawe ruca ku kibazo coroshe: kenshi, sats yawe yoba iva mu node yawe, canke iriko irinjiramwo?



### Umuguzi



Iyi ni yo nzira y’abantu benshi cane bakoresha. Ukoresha node yawe kugira ngo ukore Lightning kwishura: kugura ibintu n’ibikorwa, kwishura amafaranga, kohereza impanuro - muri make, gukoresha Lightning nk’uburyo bwihuta bwo kwishura. Ku rundi ruhande, uronka bike kuri Lightning, canke gusa uronka ku ruhande, nk’akarorero intererano nkeyi, ugusubizwa amahera hagati y’abagenzi canke amahera makeyi y’ugutanga.



Iyi profile ni yo yoroshe gucungera, kuko ico ukeneye cane ni ukugira ngo ushobore kwishura. Mu majambo ngirakamaro, ivyo bisigura ko ukeneye amahera asohoka. Uhejeje gufungura umurongo umwe canke myinshi y’ingero zibereye ku nzira zihamye, zihuye neza, amahera yawe asohoka azotuma amahera aja ku rundi ruhande rw’imirongo yawe. Ni co nyene iyo nzira irema mu buryo busanzwe umubare ubereye w’amahera yinjira. Ivyo bituma naho woba utariko urarondera kwakira ubudasiba, uzoguma ushobora kwakira rimwe na rimwe utashize mu ngiro ingamba zigoye. Rero ntukeneye kwiganyira ku bijanye n’amahera yawe yinjira.



Muri iki cigwa ca LNP202, tuzokwibanda kuri iyi nkuru y'umukoresha w'uruzitiro rw'abaguzi, kuko ari yo ihuye n'abakoresha hafi bose ba Bitcoin kuri Lightning.



### Uwugurisha



Mu buryo bumwe, umucuruzi ni ikinyuranyo c’umuguzi. Aha, intumbero nyamukuru si iyo kwishura, ahubwo ni iyo gukorakoranya. Ubucuruzi, uwutanga ibikorwa, iduka ryo kuri internet canke ikibanza co kugurisha cemera Lightning kizoronka amahera menshi yinjira, kandi kizoronka amahera make cane asohoka avuye muri iyo node.



Iyi nkuru irasaba cane, kuko kwanka kwishura kuri Lightning bishobora kuba bigereranya ugurisha kwatakaye. Ivya mbere rero bica bihinduka amahera yinjira. Iyo node yawe idafise inbound ihagije, abakiriya bawe bazobona ko amahera yabo ataco akora, naho boba bafise amahera, kubera gusa ata nzira yo kugushikanako liquidity mu nzira ibereye.



Ingorane ikomeye uwo mudandaza afise na yo nyene iva ku guhinduka kw’imirongo y’amazi. Nimba ivyo ukora vyose ari ukuronka, imirongo yawe izokwuzura buhoro buhoro ku ruhande rwawe. Ukeneye rero uburyo bwo kubungabunga no gusubiramwo amahera yawe yinjira.



Ariko rero, hariho ikintu coroshe kuruta: urutonde rw’abaguzi/abacuruzi bavanze. Iyo ukoranirije kuri Lightning, ariko kandi ugakoresha kuri Lightning (amahera y’ubudandaji, amahera y’ugutanga amahera, canke mbere amahera y’umuntu ku giti ciwe), rero amahera yawe usohoka arasubira kurema amahera yinjira. Uburongozi buragenda buraryoha, uko amafaranga agenda arahinduka, kandi ntukeneye gukoresha uburyo bw’ubuhinga bugenewe gusa kugira ngo usubire kuronka amafaranga yinjira.



### Umuyoboro



Router ni urutonde rwihariye. Ntikoresha node yayo yitwa Lightning node kugira ngo yishure canke ngo ikoranirize hamwe, ahubwo ikoresha inzira y’amahera y’abandi no gutoraniriza amafaranga. Intumbero ni ukuba inzira ngirakamaro, yizigirwa kandi ishobora guhangana n’ubutunzi mu gicapo c’umuravyo.



Kugira ngo nkubwire ukuri, kuba router kuri Lightning ni ubucuruzi bugoranye kuruta uko busa, kandi inyungu iragoye kuronka. Mbere na mbere, gufungura no gufunga imihora birazimvye mu biciro vya onchain. Kugira ngo ukoreshe neza inzira, akenshi ubwirizwa guhindura topology yawe, kugerageza, gufunga imihora idakora neza, gufungura iyindi mishasha, no gusubira gusubiramwo ubudasiba amahera yawe. Ica kabiri, uguhiganwa ni ugukomeye: ama node manini, ashizweho afata umugabane munini w’uruja n’uruza, kandi biragoye kuronka ikibanza ata kuboha umutungo munini mu mihora myinshi.



Hariho kandi n'ibisabwa vyinshi vyo gukora. Igikoresho co gucapura kigomba kuba gihagaze neza cane, kigakurikiranirwa hafi, kigashigikirwa neza, kandi kigacungirwa neza. Ubwirizwa gukurikirana ingene umurongo ukora, guhindura politike yawe y’amahera, kuguma ufise amahera aringaniye, gucunga abo mu runganwe rwawe, no gutorera umuti ningoga ingorane z’ubuhinga. Uwo murongo wo kwifatanya ushobora kuba ushimishije nk’ugukina mu vy’ubuhinga canke nk’intererano ku bikorwa remezo, ariko nimba intumbero yawe ari ugukoresha gusa Lightning mu buryo bw’ubusegaba, kwinjira mu nzira kugira uronke amahera ntaco bimaze. Ni co gituma iki cigwa ca LNP202 kitavuga ivy’iyi nkuru mu buryo bwimbitse.



### Niwishire neza aho uri imbere y’uko uja kure 



Niwaba uhuye n’ivyo umuguzi ashaka, ico uzoshira imbere ni ukugira ngo ushobore kwishura mu buryo bwizewe ukoresheje uburongozi bworoshe. Nimba uri umucuruzi, ikintu ca mbere uzokora ni ugutanga amahera ataco utakaje, ivyo bikaba bisobanura ubuhinga bwo kuronka amahera yinjira. Nimba uriko uriyumvira guca mu nzira, urakeneye kuvyifatamwo nk’igikorwa gisaba vyinshi, kidatomoye mu vy’ubutunzi kandi gitwara umwanya.



Gusobanura iyi nkuru ubu bizokufasha kwirinda umutego wa kera: gukoresha ubuhinga bw’umurongo bwagenewe umucuruzi canke umurongozi, igihe uri umukoresha gusa ashaka kwishura.

Ivyiza vy’ubwo buryo ni uko uhitamwo amafaranga y’uruzitiro (onchain) y’ugucuruza, gutyo ukirinda amahera adakenewe, kandi amahera agasubirana ningoga, ata gihe na kimwe gihagaze.



![Image](assets/fr/049.webp)



Uburyo bwa kabiri ni ugufunga ku nguvu. Muri ivyo, ntusaba amasezerano y’uwo mugenziwe maze ugatangaza ata guca ku ruhande commitment transaction ya nyuma ufise. Sinzosaba ubu buryo kiretse ari uburyo bwa nyuma, nk’akarorero iyo uwo mugenzi yanse mu buryo butegekanijwe gufunga amakoperative canke atagisubiza. Gufunga ku nguvu birafise ingorane zibiri zikomeye: amafaranga akenshi aba ari menshi cane, kuko yashizweho imbere y’igihe kugira ngo umuntu yitege ko amafaranga y’uruzitiro azoduga, kandi hariho ugucererwa mu kugarura ayo mahera, kuko abujijwe n’igihe. Iyi timelock iha uwo mugenzi wawe umwanya wo gufata ingingo iyo umuntu agerageje guhenda (ivyo bikaba bigaragara ko atari vyo aha, ariko uracariho ubwirizwa kurindira).



![Image](assets/fr/050.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, kugira ngo ufungure umurongo mushasha, genda kuri `Ingoro` hanyuma ukande kuri `Gufungura umurongo` mu gice ca `Liquidity`. Uzoheza ushobore kwinjiza ID y’uruzitiro wahisemwo, ubushobozi bw’umurongo, amafaranga y’inzira y’umuravyo wipfuza, n’amafaranga y’uruzitiro rwo gufungura.



![Image](assets/fr/051.webp)



Ivyo nivyo bikorwa nyamukuru uzokenera gukora kuri ThunderHub. Tuzobona ibindi bimenyetso uko tugenda muri iri shure rya LNP202.



## Kuronka amafaranga yinjira

Nk’uko ushobora kubibona, kugira amahera asohoka yo kwishura kuri Lightning si ikintu gikomeye cane. Gufungura gusa imirongo ku bushake bwawe ku yindi mirongo maze utangure kohereza sats. Ku rundi ruhande, kugira amafaranga yinjira kugira ngo uronke amahera kuri Lightning biragoye cane, kuko ukeneye izindi nodes kugira ngo zikugururire imihora, canke ukeneye kwimurira amahera wewe nyene mu gutanga amahera.



Iyo wemeye urutonde rw'abaguzi, muri rusangi ntaco bimaze kwiganyira ku bijanye n'amahera yinjira. Ikoreshwa ryawe ry’urudodo rwa Lightning rizoba ahanini ryerekeye kwishura, aho gukoresha amahera. Mu gukora gusa amahera makeyi y’umuravyo, uzotuma habaho amahera yinjira mu gihe.

### Amafaranga yinjira mu bufatanye



Niba utari umucuruzi, ariko ukaba ukeneye amahera yinjira (nk’akarorero, kugira ngo ushire imbere node yawe mu gihe utanguye, mu gihe urindiriye ko hari amahera yishurwa) ntubwirizwa kuvyishyura. Imwe mu nzira nkunda ni ugukorana n’abandi bakoresha node na bo nyene bakeneye amahera yinjira, kugira ngo bafungurire imihora ya Lightning. Muri ubwo buryo, gufungura umurongo biguha amahera asohoka, mu gihe nyene biguha amahera yinjira, ku buntu (amahera ya modulo onchain yo gufungura).



Ushobora, birumvikana, gutunganya ivyo ataco uhinduye n’abagenzi bawe ba bitcoiners. Ariko rero, hariho urubuga rwerekeye ubwo bwoko bw’ugufungura uruziga: [Lightning Network +](https://umuravyo.plus/). Ingingo ngenderwako si iyo gufungura imihora ibiri hagati y’abantu bamwe, ahubwo ni iyo gushinga imirongo y’uruziga irimwo abantu batatu n’imiburiburi, canke mbere n’abarenga.



Reka dufate akarorero kugira ngo dutahure ingene Lightning Network + ikora:




- Umukozi umwe akoresha node, yitwa `A`, asohora itangazo rivuga ko yipfuza gufungura umurongo wa sats miliyoni 1 ari kumwe n’abandi bantu babiri;
- Iryo tangazo riguma rikora gushika hashizwemwo abandi bantu;
- Mu nyuma, abakozi babiri, `B` na `C`, bifatanya n'itangazo ry'urudodo `A`. Ubu iyo mfuruka itatu iraheza, kandi igice co gufungura kirashobora gutangura.
- Node `A` ifungura umurongo ku node `B`;
- Node `B` ifungura umurongo ku node `C`;
- Node `C` ifungura umurongo ku node `A`.



Igihe igikorwa kirangiye, buri node irafise amahera asohoka angana na miliyoni 1 sats n’amahera yinjira angana na miliyoni 1 sats. Uwo mugambi ushobora kwaguka ku bantu bane canke batanu.



Ego ni ko, nta buryo bw’ubuhinga bwo kwemeza ko uwuzoja muri iyo nama azofungura vy’ukuri umuhora yiyemeje kurema. Ni co gituma iyo nzira yashizeho uburyo bwo kumenyekana, bushingiye ku vyo abakoresha bavuga iyo bashitse ku vyo biyemeje. Kandi mu gihe kibi kuruta ibindi vyose, iyo uhuye n’umuntu adakorana, nta mahera uzoba watakaje: uzoba gusa watakaje akaryo k’amahera yinjira.



Iyi nzira irabereye cane cane ku bijanye n'umurongo w'abaguzi, kuko igufasha kuronka amahera yinjira ku buntu, mu gihe uhuza urudodo rwawe n'abandi bakozi batobato. Ku rundi ruhande, nimba uri umucuruzi, iyo nzira muri rusangi ntaco imaze: sat yose y’amahera yinjira isaba guhagarika sat y’amahera asohoka, kandi ivyo ukeneye vyinshi vy’amahera yinjira vyoba rero birimwo gufatanya umutungo mwinshi cane.



Kugira ngo ukoreshe Lightning Network +, ufise uburyo bubiri: ukoreshe porogarama yinjijwe muri Umbrel, canke ukoreshe urubuga rwa kera maze ukore konti winjira ukoresheje node yawe. Ndabagira inama y’ivyo vya nyuma, kuko porogarama yunze ubumwe idatanga urutonde rw’ibikorwa vyose biriho.



Genda ku rubuga [Lightning Network +](https://umuravyo.plus/) hanyuma ukande kuri buto `Injira` iri hejuru iburyo bw’urubuga.

## Huza telefone ngendanwa wallet biciye kuri Tailscale


<chapterId>5fefb222-3f50-4f9d-a170-2ea628be4437</chapterId>



Nivyo, ubu ufise urudodo rwa Lightning rufatanye neza, rufise amahera yinjira n’asohoka. Rero mwese mwateguye gukoresha urudodo rwanyu rw’Umuravyo mu buzima nyakuri. Gushika ubu, twama dukoresha interface zitaziguye kuri Umbrel, haba porogaramu ya `Lightning Node` canke interface `ThunderHub`. Ivyo bikoresho bikora mu kurungika no kwakira amahera, ariko biragaragara ko bitabereye amahera ya Lightning ya misi yose. Iryo koraniro ryakozwe kugira ngo rikoreshwe kuri mudasobwa, ntirikoreshwa kuri telefone ngendanwa, kandi risaba no gufatanya n’urubuga rumwe kugira ngo rikore neza (naho mu buryo bw’ubuhinga bishoboka gufatanya kure biciye kuri Tor).



Mu bikorwa, ico turiko turarondera nk’aba bitcoiners ni urubuga rwa kera rwa Lightning wallet kuri telefone ngendanwa: ubushobozi bwo gucapura amafagitire biciye ku kode ya QR, n’urubuga rworoshe rwo kwishura no gukura amahera sats. Ivyo nyene ni vyo tuzoba turiko turashira mu ngiro muri iki gice no mu gikurikira. Iciyumviro rusangi ni ukugira porogarama y’umuyagankuba wallet kuri telefone yawe, ishobora gukoreshwa aho hose (atari urubuga rwawe rwo mu karere gusa) ariko, mu nyuma, yizigira node yawe bwite ya Lightning kugira ngo yohereze kandi yakire amahera.



### Ni ibihe bisubizo vyo guhuza umukiriya w’itelefone ngendanwa?



Ubu, hari uburyo bwinshi bwo kubikora, haba mu bijanye n’iporogarama yo kuri telefone ngendanwa be n’ubwoko bw’ihuriro hagati y’urudodo rwawe n’iyi porogarama. Uburyo butatu nyamukuru bwo guhuza ni:




- biciye ku ***Tor***;
- biciye kuri VPN ***Tailscale***;
- biciye ku ***Nostr Wallet Guhuza***.



Haciye imyaka mikeyi, nahora nkoresha ***Tor***, ariko narahagarika ningoga: igitigiri c’abananirwa n’ugucererwa kw’itumanaho vyari vyinshi cane. Mu vyiyumviro, birakora, ariko mu bikorwa, ubumenyi bw’abakoresha bwari buteye akaga. Nanje rero nogira inama yo kudakoresha iyo nzira.



Iyindi nzira naciye mfata ni iyo gukoresha ***Tailscale*** VPN kugira ngo habeho uguhanahana amakuru hagati y’iporogarama ya telefone ngendanwa n’urudodo. Uwo muti urakora neza cane: no ku nzira z’amatelefone ngendanwa zifise ubushobozi buke, ivyo ndiha vyama bica ata ngorane. Ubu ni bwo buryo ngiye gutanga ubwa mbere muri iki gice, n’ikoreshwa rya Zeus.



Mu gice gikurikira, turaza kuraba uwundi muti, uherutse gusohoka, nawo ukora neza cane: ***Nostr Wallet Connect***. Iyi nshuro, tuzokoresha porogarama ya Alby Go kugira ngo tubashikirize ubundi buryo, naho Zeus na yo nyene ihuye na NWC nimba ubishaka.



### Gushiramwo no gutunganya Tailscale



Kuri ubu buryo bwa mbere, tuzokenera Tailscale. Iyi ni umuti wa VPN ushingiye kuri WireGuard, ushobora guhuza neza ibikoresho bikwiragiye kuri Internet, mu gihe ushobora gucungera ubwawe ubuhinga bwo gupfuka, kwemeza n’uguca muri NAT. Mu mvugo yoroshe, ni nk’aho ibikoresho vyawe vyose vyari bifatanye na LAN imwe na router yawe, naho nyene vyoba biri aho hose kuri Internet.



Kugira ngo uyikoreshe, banza ukore konti. Genda ku rubuga rwa Tailscale, hanyuma ukande kuri buto `Get Started`.

![Image](assets/fr/072.webp)



Umaze kwinjira, uzobazwa ibibazo bike ku bijanye n'ikoreshwa ryawe. Mubisubize muri make kugira ngo mubandanye.



![Image](assets/fr/073.webp)

### Nostr Wallet ikora gute?



Nostr Wallet Connect ni porotokole igezweho yemerera porogaramu canke urubuga gutera ibikorwa ku nzira ya Lightning iri kure, ata gushinga uruja n’uruza rw’urubuga rutaziguye kuri iyo nzira (nta API LND yerekanwa, nta VPN, nta `.onion` service...). NWC isobanura ingene ubusabe bukora umugambi (nk'akarorero `pay_intece`) maze bukaronka igisubizo.



Bikora mu buryo bworoshe cane. Utangura ikiganiro mu gucapura kode ya QR canke biciye ku nzira y'imbere `nostr+walletconnect:`. Uyu murongo urimwo amaparametere y'ikiganiro n'ibanga ry'uburenganzira. Hanyuma, iyo iyo porogarama ishaka kwishura, irashira mu rutonde ivyo isaba, ikabishiramwo amakuru, maze ikabitangaza nk’ikintu cabaye ku nzira y’ugutanga amakuru yitwa Nostr. Ico gikoresho gisoma ivyabaye ku nzira y’ugutanga amakuru, kikagisohora, kigasuzuma ko umwanditsi yemerewe gukora iki kiganiro, kigashitsa ivyo kwishura, hanyuma kigasubiza inyishu yashizwemwo amakuru (uguterimbere n’ishusho y’imbere, canke ikosa). Ico gikoresho gikora gusa nk’umuhuza w’ivy’ugutwara abantu n’ibintu: ntigishobora gusoma ibirimwo, ariko gishobora kwihweza igihe n’incuro ivyo bisabwa.



Ugereranije n’uguhuza biciye kuri Tailscale canke Tor, akamaro nyamukuru ka NWC ni uko node yawe idashobora gushikwako ataco ihinduye ivuye hanze. Ivyo birorosha cane gukoresha telefone ngendanwa: node yawe ntikeneye kwemera ama connexions yinjira, ikeneye gusa kuba ishobora kuvugana n’umurongo w’ivyuma. Ku rundi ruhande, ushiramwo ukuntu bishingiye ku bikoresho vya Nostr: iyo bitaboneka, ubumenyi buragabanuka. Kandi, naho ubutumwa bwoba bushizwe mu nzira, iyo relay irashobora kwihweza urugero runaka rw’amakuru y’ibikorwa.



Ubutandukane bw’ibigereranyo vy’umutekano na bwo nyene burahambaye. Na Tailscale canke Tor, ugaragaza uburyo bwo gushika ku nzira yawe (biciye kuri API ya LND) irindwa n’amabanga akomeye cane. Ivyo birakomeye, kuko ushobora gutwara vyose, ariko kandi ni ikibanza co gutera co hasi. Na NWC, ukwinjira ni ugukoreshwa cane: ushinga igikorwa token cemerera gusa ibikorwa bimwebimwe.

## Ukwigenga kuramba ku Muravyo


<chapterId>691a0942-b46d-482a-8fbc-fe19b3814992</chapterId>



Ubu twashitse ku mpera y’iri shure ry’ibikorwa vy’amaboko rya LNP202. Ubu ufise ivy’ishimikiro ukeneye kugira ngo ukoreshe Lightning Network mu buryo bw’ubusegaba: uratahura uruhara nyarwo rw’uruzitiro, uguhuza kw’uburyo butandukanye, kandi warashizeho instance ya LND kuri Umbrel n’ingene ushobora gukingira no gukingira. Warafunguye kandi imihora yawe ya mbere, wize ingene wocungera amahera, kugira ngo ivyo utanga bibe ivyo kwizigirwa ku musi ku musi.



Uvuye ku bijanye n’imikorere, urudodo rwawe ubu rukwiye kwinjira mu murongo wo kubungabunga. Ikintu nyamukuru ni ukuyikurikirana (igihe co gukora, guhuza, uko umurongo umeze, ukunanirwa kwishura, n’ibindi), gukoresha ivyagezwe bishasha bitangwa na Umbrel igihe hariho amaverisiyo ahamye, no kugenzura kenshi ko ama backups yawe n’imiterere y’umunara w’ibarabara bikiriho.



Ku bijanye n’imirongo, fata uburyo bubereye: uzigame izo zigufitiye akamaro, ufunge izo zidakora ubudasiba canke zifitaniye isano n’urunganwe rudashikamye, hanyuma buhoro buhoro usubire gutanga umutungo wawe ujane ku nzira ikomeye kuruta.



**Kimwe mu mitego ikunda gushika muri iyi ntambwe ni ugutanga umutungo mwinshi cane ku node yawe ya Lightning. Zirikana ko node yawe ya Lightning idatekanye cane kuruta hardware wallet, kandi ko ukuboneka kw’amahera yemewe ku mirongo yawe bishingiye ku buryo bwo gusubiza inyuma buguma butatunganye. Ni ngombwa rero ko uguma ufise amafaranga akwiye, ushobora gutakaza iyo habaye ingorane, kandi wama uguma ufise igice kinini ca sats yawe ku gikoresho ca wallet.



Ku bijanye n’ibikoresho, ndabagira inama yo kuguma mufise amatsiko yo kumenya ibintu bishasha. Muri iri huriro, twabonye vyinshi muri vyo, haba ivyo gucunga node yawe, ukuyifatanya canke gukoresha kure kugira ngo ushobore kwishura. Ariko rero, Umuravyo ni umurima ugira inguvu cane. Buri mwaka, ibikoresho bishasha kandi bibereye birasohoka, kandi n’ibikoresho vyinshi bishasha birasohoka kuri Umbrel. Kuguma umenya ivyo bintu bishasha bishobora kugufasha kuronka inyishu zikomeye canke zikora kuruta izo zishikirijwe muri iki cigwa.



Ku bijanye n'inyigisho, nimba utarabikora, ndaguhanura cane ngo ufate inyigisho ya Fanis Michalakis yitwa LNP 201, yerekeye imikorere ya Lightning Network. Bizogufasha gutahura neza ubuhinga bukoreshwa muri iri shure rya LNP202, kandi biguhe icizigiro kinini mu bijanye n’uburongozi bwa misi yose bw’urudodo rwawe.



https://planb.academy/courses/34bd43ef-6683-4a5c-b239-7cb1e40a4aeb
