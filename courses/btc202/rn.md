# Ba umunya bitcoin w'umunyagihugu



Kumbure urazi neza imvugo ngo "Si imfunguruzo zawe, si ibiceri vyawe", iremesha kwizigama amafaranga yawe y'ibiceri. Gufata imfunguruzo zawe bwite ni intambwe ya mbere ihambaye vy’ukuri, ariko ntibihagije. Kugira ngo ushikire ubusegaba nyakuri bw’amahera, urakeneye kandi gushiramwo no gukoresha node yawe bwite ya Bitcoin. Iyi nyigisho yagenewe kukuyobora muri iyo ntambwe y’ishimikiro mu rugendo rwawe rwa Bitcoin!



BTC 202 ni inyigisho ishoboka yagenewe kukwigisha ingene wozunguruka urudodo rwawe rwa Bitcoin, naho woba utari umuhinga mu vy’ubuhinga. Tuzotangura dusobanura ico urudodo rwa Bitcoin ari co, ico rukoreshwa, n’igituma ari ngombwa cane ko wizunguruka. Nzoca ndakuyobora intambwe ku yindi mu guhitamwo ibikoresho vyawe, gushiramwo porogarama zikenewe, gufatanya Wallet yawe, no gukora ivyiza vya mbere bishoboka kugira ngo uyishire kure.



Gukoresha node ya Bitcoin si uburyo bwo guhitamwo gusa ku bahinga; ni ngombwa. Ni igikoresho co kwihangana umukoresha wese akeneye gutahura no gushira mu ngiro. Iyi course ni intango yawe yo kuba umunya bitcoiner afise ubusegaba!

## Incamake y'amashure


<chapterId>916b1f86-38a4-4ede-bdb7-83841d5a7abe</chapterId>



Ikaze muri BTC 202, aho uzokwiga gushiramwo, gutunganya, no gukoresha node ya Bitcoin mu buryo bworoshe kandi bwigenga. Ariko ivyo si vyo vyose: uzomenya kandi vyinshi ku bijanye n’ahantu n’igikorwa c’ibihimba biri muri sisitemu ya Bitcoin. Iryo shure rihinduka hagati y’insobanuro z’ivyiyumviro n’ibikorwa vy’amaboko birongowe.



### Igice ca 1 - Intangamarara



Muri iki gice ca mbere c’inyigisho, tuzotomora ivyiyumviro vy’ishimikiro hanyuma tuje ku nsobanuro zitomoye kuruta. Node ni iki? Ni ubuhe butandukaniro buri hagati ya node, Wallet, na Miner? Uzoheza umenye ivyerekeye Bitcoin core n'ingene iyo protocole ishirwa mu ngiro. Intumbero ni ukuvuga ururimi rumwe, kwirinda gutera urujijo, no gushinga umushinge ukomeye w’ivyiyumviro.



### Igice ca 2 - Kuba umunyagihugu w'umunyagihugu



Muri iki gice ca kabiri, nzotangura ndabasigurira igituma bihambaye gukoresha node yawe bwite ya Bitcoin. Tuzoheza tubone ubwoko butandukanye bw’ibihimba biriho (complete, pruned, SPV...), ingene bikora, n’ingaruka zavyo mu vy’ubuhinga.



Tuzoca tuguha icegeranyo c’ubuhinga bwo gukoresha node ya Bitcoin, harimwo n’ivyiza n’ibibi vyayo. Ubwa nyuma, tuzosozera n’impanuro zimwe zimwe zikora cane zo guhitamwo ibikoresho bibereye ivyo ukeneye n’ingene ukoresha amahera.



Iki gice rero, kirerekana inzira y’umunyabubasha wa bitcoiner: gutahura igituma bikenewe gukoresha urudodo, guhitamwo ubwoko bw’urudodo, bishingiye kuri iryo hitamwo, guhitamwo porogarama, kandi, bivanye n’urudodo rwatowe, guhitamwo ibikoresho bikwiye.



### Igice ca 3 - Gushiramwo urudodo rwa Bitcoin mu buryo bworoshe



Ivyo biteguro bimaze kurangira, ni igihe co gukorana n’Igice ca 3 kigenewe Umbrel: OS y’igicu co mu nzu yorosha kwiyakira no gushiramwo Bitcoin na Lightning node.



Inyuma y’intangamarara ngufi ya Umbrel, tuzoguha inyigisho ido n’ido kugira ngo ikuyobore mu bijanye no gushiramwo no gutunganya ku mashine yawe bwite. Intumbero y’iki gice iratomoye: kugira urudodo rwawe rwa mbere rukora neza kandi rujanye n’igihe Bitcoin.



### Igice ca 4 - Guhuza Wallet yawe n'uruzitiro rwawe



None ko mwashizeho node ya Bitcoin, ni igihe co kuyikoresha! Muri iki gice, uzomenya ingene wohuza porogarama yawe yo gucunga Wallet (nka Sparrow wallet) n’index yawe bwite ya Address (Electrs canke Fulcrum), canke ushiremwo Bitcoin core, kugira ngo ntube ugikoresha ama server ya bose.



Turaza kandi gusuzuma uruhara rw’aba indexer n’uburyo butandukanye bwo gufatanya n’uruzitiro rwawe (LAN, Tor, Tailscale, n’ibindi). Ubwa nyuma, mu kigabane ca nyuma, tuzosubiramwo ibikorwa vy’ingirakamaro cane biri kuri Umbrel ku muntu akoresha bitcoiner ku musi ku musi.



### Igice ca 5 - Ivyiyumviro biteye imbere n'ingene bigenda neza



Muri iki gice ca nyuma ca BTC 202, intumbero ni ugukomeza ubumenyi bwawe. Mbere, turaza kuraba uburyo bwiza bwo gukoresha n’urudodo rwawe rushasha rwa Bitcoin n’ingene worubungabunga mu kiringo kirekire.



Tuzoca dufata umwanya wo gusubiramwo bimwe mu vyiyumviro vyavuzwe imbere y’aho mu nyigisho, harimwo no gutahura inzira ya IBD n’ukuvumbura kw’urunganwe mu buryo burambuye, gutohoza ubuhinga bw’urudodo, no kwiga ingene wokoresha dosiye `Bitcoin.conf` kugira ngo utunganye neza ivyo ushizeho.



### Igice ca 6 - Igice ca nyuma



Nk’uko bigenda ku masomo yose ya Plan ₿ Network, mu gice ca nyuma, uzosanga ikizame ca nyuma co kugerageza ubumenyi ufise ku bijanye n’ibihimba vya Bitcoin.



None, woba witeguye gufungura node yawe ya mbere ya Bitcoin? Mushireho inzira y’ubusegaba!



## None Bitcoin ni iki?


<chapterId>0a9fd4e0-94ab-405e-924c-023397393027</chapterId>



Nk’uko vyadondowe n’uwayiremye, Satoshi Nakamoto, Bitcoin yiyerekana nk’uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera y’ubuhinga bwa none. Iri rungane ryoroshe, ari ryo mutwe w’Igitabu Cera, rifise ibimenyetso vyinshi vyerekana kamere ya Bitcoin:




- Mbere na mbere, Satoshi idondora Bitcoin nk'"uburyo", mu yandi majambo, urutonde rw'ibihimba vy'ubuhinga n'ubuhinga bikorana kugira ngo bihe igikorwa kinaka canke bikore igikorwa kinaka;
- Inyuma y’aho, asigura ko iyo nzira ishoboza gukoresha amahera y’ubuhinga bwa none, ni ukuvuga uburyo bw’amahera ataboneka;
- Ubwa nyuma, yerekana ko iyo nzira idashingiye ku kintu na kimwe co hagati: ni "peer-to-peer", bisobanura ko abakoresha ubwabo ari bo bakoresha iyo nzira.



Kubera ko Bitcoin ari ubuhinga, bitegerezwa gukoreshwa kuri mudasobwa. Kandi, kubera kamere yayo y’urunganwe, ni abakoresha ubwabo bafata inshingano yo gukoresha izo mashini. Ico twita "Bitcoin node" ni co nyene iyo mudasobwa ikoresha porogarama ishira mu ngiro umurongo wa Bitcoin (nka Bitcoin core, ariko ivyo tuzobigarukako mu nyuma). Ivyo ni vyo bituma Bitcoin ikora ata butegetsi bukuru: kwemeza bikorwa mu buryo busanzwe, n’amamashini yigenga ibihumbi n’ibihumbi ari ay’abakoresha ibihumbi n’ibihumbi.



![Image](assets/fr/047.webp)



Nakamoto, S. (2008). *Bitcoin: Uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera mu buryo bw’ubuhinga bwa none*. Ubutumwa bw'ikirundi 39.pdf



Abo bakoresha nyene ni bo bacungera umutekano wa Bitcoin. Nk’uko Eric Voskuil abisigura mu gitabu ciwe *Cryptoeconomics*, umutekano wa Bitcoin ntushingiye kuri Blockchain, canke ku bubasha bwo gukora hashing, canke ku kwemeza, ku kwegereza ubutegetsi, ku gukora amakuru y’ibanga, ku nzira yuguruye, canke ku nyigisho y’imikino. Umutekano wa Bitcoin uvana canecane n’abantu biteguriye kwishira mu kaga k’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Ukwegereza ubutegetsi abaturage bituma iyo ngorane ikwiragizwa ku bantu benshi, kandi ubushobozi bwabo bwo kurwanya ni bwo bwonyene butuma iyo nzira ikomeye.



Iryo hame ryoroshe gutahura: iyo Bitcoin iba ishingiye ku nzira imwe y’umuntu umwe, gufunga uwo muntu vyoba bihagije kugira ngo iyo nzira ihagarare, kuko bo bonyene bokwikorera ingorane zose. Kubera ko hariho ibihumbi mirongo vy’ibice bikwiragiye kw’isi yose, ingorane zirakwiragira: umwe wese muri abo bakoresha yobwirizwa gukurwamwo kugira ngo Bitcoin ihagarare.



![Image](assets/fr/048.webp)



Turashobora rero gutandukanya no gutanga amazina y’ivyiyumviro vyinshi kugira ngo dusobanukirwe ibintu mu gihe gisigaye c’iki cigwa:




- Amafaranga ya Bitcoin: igice ca konti gikoreshwa mu gukora ibikorwa muri iyo nzira;
- Igikoresho ca Bitcoin: umugwi w’ibihimba vyose bihuye;
- Bitcoin nodes: imashini zikoresha ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa Bitcoin;
- Ibikorwa vya Bitcoin: porogarama ihindura porotokole mu mabwirizwa ashobora gushirwa mu ngiro;
- Bitcoin protocol: amategeko agenga uko iyo sisitemu ikora;
- Uburyo bwa Bitcoin: ihuriro ry'ivyo vyose Elements.



### Uruhara rw'uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin



Ivyo bihimba vya Bitcoin vyose hamwe bikora ico bita urusobe rwa Bitcoin. Bituma urutonde rwose rukora rwigenga, ata buyobozi buhambaye canke ubukuru bw’abakozi.



Kuva mu ntango, Bitcoin yari igenewe gutuma uwuyikoresha wese akoresha urudodo rwiwe bwite. Ico kibazo kiracariho n’ubuhinga bwa none bwa Bitcoin core, bufatanya uruhara rwa Wallet n’urw’urudodo. Ariko muri iki gihe, iyo nzira ikunda gutandukana: ama wallet menshi ya Bitcoin yo muri iki gihe ni ama wallet gusa ahuza n’ibihimba vyo hanze (vy’umuntu umwe canke atari vyo).



### Gumana Blockchain



Igikorwa ca mbere c'uruzitiro ni ugucungera kopi y'aho hantu ya Blockchain. Kugira ngo Double-spending ibe kuri Bitcoin ata buyobozi bukuru bushizwemwo, umukoresha wese ategerezwa kugenzura ko ata n’imwe ikoreshwa muri iyo sisitemu. Uburyo bumwe rudende bwo kumenya neza ivyo ni ukumenya amafaranga yose akoreshwa kuri Bitcoin. Kubera iyo mpamvu, amafaranga yose akoreshwa arashirwako ikidodo c'igihe kandi agashirwa mu matsinda, kandi buri nzira ibika Blockchain yose.



> Uburyo bwonyene bwo kwemeza ko ata n’imwe igurishwa ni ukumenya ibikorwa vyose.

Nakamoto, S. (2008). *Bitcoin: Uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera mu buryo bw’ubuhinga bwa none*. Ubutumwa bw'ikirundi 59.pdf



Blockchain rero ni igitabu gihinduka: igihe cose igice gishasha gisohotse na Miner, iyo node irasuzuma ko ari ukuri imbere y’uko iyongera kuri kopi yayo bwite y’uruhererekane. Kuva uno musi (Mukakaro 2025), Blockchain yuzuye irarenga 675 GB, kandi iyo ngano irabandanya gukura, kuko hariho igice gishasha congerwako mu mwanya buri minota 10.



![Image](assets/fr/049.webp)



Iryo node kandi rigumya inyandiko y’aho hantu y’ama UTXO yose ariho igihe cose, azwi kw’izina rya **UTXO set**. Iryo koraniro ririmwo ibice vyose vya Bitcoin bitakoreshejwe. Turasubira gusubiramwo iyo nkuru mu buryo burambuye mu gice ca nyuma c’inyigisho.



### Kugenzura no gukwiragiza amafaranga



Uruhara rwa kabiri rw’uruzitiro ni ugukora kugenzura no gukwiragiza amafaranga. Iyo igikorwa gishasha gishitse ku nzira (biciye kuri porogarama ya Wallet canke iyindi nzira), kizosuzuma ko kijanye n’amategeko (amategeko y’uguhurizako n’amategeko y’ugutanga amakuru). Nk'akarorero:




- bitcoins zakoreshejwe zitegerezwa kubaho mu gice cayo ca UTXO (urutonde rw’ibisohoka bitakoreshejwe);
- umukono utegerezwa kuba ufise akamaro, kandi ivyangombwa vyose vyo gukoresha amahera bitegerezwa kuba vyuzuye (inyandiko ifise akamaro);
- umubare wose w’ibisohoka ntugomba kurenga umubare wose w’ibintu vyinjizwa, ivyo bisigura ko ibiciro bidashobora kuba bibi.



![Image](assets/fr/050.webp)



Inyuma y’ukwemezwa, iyo nzira ibikwa muri Mempool y’uruzitiro, ikibanza c’ukwibuka c’igihe gito kigenewe ibikorwa bitaremejwe, hanyuma igashikirizwa abandi bagenzi b’urubuga ihuriyeko. Ubwo buryo bwo gukwiragiza no kwemeza burabandanya kuva ku nzira imwe gushika ku yindi. Muri ubwo buryo, iyo nzira ikwiragizwa ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, kandi buri node ikayibika muri Mempool gushika ishizwe mu gice gifise akamaro na Miner, iyo na yo igaca ikora ku kwemeza kwayo kwa mbere.



### Suzuma kandi utange amabuye



Uruhara rwa gatatu rw’uruzitiro ni ugucungera amabuye y’agaciro. Iyo Miner ibonye igice gishasha gifise Proof of Work ikora, iratangazwa ku rubuga. Ivyo bihimba birayakira, bikagenzura ko ihuye n’amategeko yose y’itegeko, hanyuma bikayishira muri kopi yabo bwite y’aho hantu ya Blockchain nimba ari ngirakamaro. Nk’uko bigenda ku bikorwa, amabuye mashasha yemejwe aca ashikirizwa bagenzi bose bahuye n’urudodo. Ivyo birabandanya gushika aho ama node yose ari ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin amenye iyo block nshasha.



![Image](assets/fr/051.webp)



## Ni itandukaniro irihe riri hagati y’umuheto n’umuheto wa Wallet?


<chapterId>de5af634-a628-4b90-b869-468c208e178b</chapterId>



Ni ngombwa gutandukanya ubwoko bubiri butandukanye bwa porogaramu iyo ukoresha Bitcoin: node na Wallet.



Igikoresho ca Bitcoin, nk’uko twabibonye haruguru, ni igice ca porogarama gifise uruhara n’umwete mu bijanye n’uruja n’uruza rw’abantu. Ikora ibikorwa nyamukuru bitatu:




- gusubiza inyuma Blockchain,
- kwemeza ubucuruzi no gutanga,
- kwemeza ububiko n’ugutanga.



Ku rundi ruhande, Bitcoin Wallet ni porogarama yagenewe kubika no gucunga imfunguruzo zawe z’ibanga. Izo mfunguruzo zigufasha gukoresha amafaranga yawe ya bitcoins mu guhaza inyandiko zo gufunga (mu bisanzwe biciye ku musinya). Wallet ishobora kwifatanya n’urudodo (rwaba urwo mu karere canke rwo kure) kugira ngo ibone uko Blockchain imeze no gutangaza amakuru yubaka, ariko ntabwo ari, nk’uko biri, uwugira uruhara mu nzira.



Mu bihe bimwebimwe, izo nzira zibiri zikorana muri porogarama imwe, nk’uko biri kuri Bitcoin core, ikora nka Full node na Wallet. Ariko rero, porogarama nyinshi zizwi cane za Wallet (Sparrow, BlueWallet, n’izindi) zisaba ko umuntu afatanya n’uruzitiro rwo hanze (rwaba rwawe canke urw’uwundi muntu) kugira ngo atangaze amafaranga yo gukoresha no kumenya umubare w’amahera asigaye muri Wallet.



![Image](assets/fr/052.webp)



## Ni itandukaniro irihe riri hagati ya node na Miner?


<chapterId>d2992614-7ab7-4bf9-81b1-f548cda67257</chapterId>



Ivyiyumviro vyerekeye node na Miner akenshi biratera urujijo. Yamara izo Elements zibiri zikora ibikorwa bitandukanye cane muri iyo nzira.



Mu ntango, igihe Bitcoin yatanguzwa na Satoshi Nakamoto mu 2009, uwuyikoresha wese yari yitezwe ko yogira uruhara muri iyo nzira yose uko ingana. Gutyo, porogarama y’intango ya Bitcoin yarahuje ibikorwa vyinshi icarimwe: yakora nk’iyi Wallet, nk’urudodo, kandi yakora kandi nk’iyi Miner, ishobora gutuma habaho amabuye mashasha. Iciyo gihe, ingorane za Mining zari nto cane. Ico wari ufise gukora ni ugukoresha porogarama ya Bitcoin kuri mudasobwa yawe kugira uronke ama blocs maze uronke ama bitcoins nk’impera.



Ariko rero, kubera ko Bitcoin yagenda iramenyekana buhorobuhoro be n’uko abacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro bariko baragwira, uburyo bwo guhiganwa muri Mining bwarahindutse cane. Ubu, Mining yacitse igikorwa c’uguhiganwa cane, kiganzwa n’abakinyi b’inganda bafise ibikorwa remezo vyihariye. Inguvu zikenewe kugira ngo umuntu acukure ibuye rishasha ubu ni nyinshi cane ku buryo umuntu ku giti ciwe adashobora kubishikako akoresheje orodinateri isanzwe gusa. Ivyo vyatumye ubu Mining ikorwa ahanini hakoreshejwe imashini zidasanzwe zitwa ASICs (*Imirongo y’Ibikorwa Yihariye*). Izo chips zitunganijwe gusa kugira ngo zikoreshe kabiri SHA-256, ubuhinga bukoreshwa kuri Mining kuri Bitcoin.



![Image](assets/fr/053.webp)



Mu gihe c’iryo terambere, uruhara rw’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin n’urw’uruzitiro rwa Miner rwaratandukanye cane. Nk’uko vyerekanwa haruguru, uruhara rw’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin ni urw’amakuru gusa n’ukwemeza. Uruhara rwa Miner ruratandukanye:




- Ihitamwo amafaranga ategerejwe muri Mempool.
- Yubaka igice c’abakandida gishiramwo ivyo bikorwa.
- Arondera mu kugerageza no mu makosa Proof of Work ibereye.
- Iyo ibonye ikimenyamenya gikwiriye, iratangaza iyo block biciye ku nzira yayo ku zindi nzira.



Miner ikeneye urudodo rwa Bitcoin kugira ngo ikoreshe n’urubuga.



Uruhara rwa Miner na rwo nyene rimwe na rimwe ruratandukanye n’urw’ico gikoresho gicapura. Mincer ni imashini ifise igikorwa co gukora Hash template blocks zitangwa na server y'ikidengeri, irondera ama hashes ahaza ingorane zisobanuwe ku migabane, atari iya Bitcoin. Ibindi bikorwa vya Mining, birimwo kwubaka amabuye nyayo, guhitamwo ibikorwa, canke gushaka Proof-of-Work hakurikijwe ingorane za Bitcoin ubwayo, hamwe n’ugusangira, bikorwa ataco bimaze n’ibidengeri.



![Image](assets/fr/054.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, hariho itandukaniro rikomeye mu bijanye n’inkurikizi z’ubutunzi hagati ya Miner n’urudodo. Gukoresha node ya Bitcoin nta nyungu y’amahera itaziguye bitanga. Ku rundi ruhande, kwifatanya na Mining bizana ingororano (infashanyo n’amahera y’ugucuruza) ku bubiko bwose bubonetse.



Mu gice ca 2, tuzokwihweza mu buryo burambuye inyungu ngirakamaro n’iz’umuntu ku giti ciwe zo gushiramwo no gukoresha urudodo rwa Bitcoin, birenze ivy’amahera gusa.



## Bitcoin core n'ugushirwa mu ngiro kw'amasezerano


<chapterId>72381876-9317-4faa-8d41-2b252a945b8a</chapterId>



Itegeko rya Bitcoin si porogaramu: ni amategeko ataco avuga asangiye hagati y’abakoresha urubuga. Isobanura ivyangombwa vy’uburenganzira bw’ibikorwa, uburyo bwo kurema amahera, uburyo bw’amabuye, ivyangombwa vya Proof-of-Work, n’ibindi vyinshi vyerekeye. Kugira ngo ukoreshe iyi porotokole, abakoresha bategerezwa gukoresha porogaramu ishira mu ngiro aya mategeko: ivyo bizwi nka **ugushira mu ngiro** kwa Bitcoin.



Ishirwa mu ngiro rero ni porogarama y’ibihimba: porogarama ishobora gukorana n’izindi mashini ziri ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, gukuraho, kugenzura, kubika, no gukwiragiza amabuye n’ibikorwa, no gushitsa amategeko y’uguhurizako n’ugutanga amakuru mu karere. Ishirwa mu ngiro ryose ni insobanuro nyayo y’itegeko, ryanditswe mu rurimi rwa porogarama rwatanzwe, rifise ubuhinga bwaryo bwite, ubushobozi bwaryo, n’ubuhinga bwaryo bwo gukora neza. Ishirwa mu ngiro ryose rizogira kandi ishirahamwe ryaryo ry’iterambere, rifise n’ugusangira inshingano zaryo.



Muri izo nzira, imwe iraganza cane: **Bitcoin core**.



![Image](assets/fr/055.webp)



### Ishirwa mu ngiro ry'akahise ryacitse ikigereranyo .



Bitcoin core ni porogaramu y’ishingiro y’umurongo wa Bitcoin. Ikomoka ku kode y’intango yanditswe na Satoshi Nakamoto mu 2008-2009, kandi ni ugukomeza kwayo ata guca ku ruhande. Mu ntango ryari rizwi nka "*Bitcoin*", hanyuma ryitwa "*Bitcoin QT*" (kubera ko hongereweko Interface y'ibishushanyo biciye mu bubiko bw'ibitabu bwa Qt), ryahinduwe izina ryitwa "*Bitcoin core*" mu 2014 kugira ngo ritandukanye neza porogarama n'urubuga. Kuva kuri verisiyo 0.5, yarakwiragijwe n’ibice bibiri: `Bitcoin-qt` (Igishushanyo Interface) na `bitcoind` (umurongo w’amabwirizwa Interface).



Mu vyiyumviro, Bitcoin core ntiserukira umurongo wa Bitcoin; ahubwo ni ugushirwa mu ngiro kumwe gusa mu vyinshi. Ariko rero, iratandukanywa n’uko abantu benshi bayikoresha, imyaka ifise, ukuntu amategeko yayo akomeye be n’ingene itegurwa. Ku bw’ivyo, mu bikorwa, amategeko akoreshwa na Bitcoin core ni de facto ayo mu masezerano ya Bitcoin: abakoresha, abategura, abacukuzi, n’ibikorwa vy’ibidukikije ni bo bavuga hafi gusa.



### Ugukwiragizwa kw'ubu kw'ishyirwa mu ngiro



Dushingiye ku [amakuru yashizwe hamwe muri Myandagaro 2025 na Luke Dashjr] (umuhinga mu bijanye n’ugutegura ibintu azwi cane mu bijanye n’ibidukikije), ugukwiragira kw’ibikorwa hagati y’ibice vya bose vy’urubuga ni nk’uko bikurikira:




- Bitcoin core**: 87,3% vy'ibihimba
- 124**: 12,5
- Ibindi bishirwa mu ngiro**: 0,2% (btcsuite, Bcoin, BTCD...)



![Image](assets/fr/056.webp)



Mu yandi majambo, nk'ibice 9 kuri 10 vy'abantu bose bikoresha Bitcoin core. Ibindi bice vy’urubuga vyishingikiriza ku bakiriya benshi bo ku ruhande (naho umugabane wa Knots waduze cane mu mezi aheze, atari mu gihe c’impaka ku bijanye n’igipimo c’ubunini bwa `OP_RETURN`). Ivyo bikoresho bitari vyo vyo gushirwa mu ngiro akenshi bitunganywa n’umuntu umwe canke umugwi mutoyi.



**Iciyumviro:** Iyi mibare iracari ingereranyo, ariko, kuko ishingiye ahanini ku *node zo kwumviriza*, ni ukuvuga node zemera ama connexions yinjira (n’icuma 8333 kifunguye). Ivyuma bitarumviriza* biragoye cane guharura, kuko bidashoboka ko umuntu abifatanya navyo ataco bimaze: utegerezwa kurindira ko ingamba iva kuri vyo, mu buryo bw’uguhuza gusohoka. Urubuga rwa Luke Dashjr ruvuga ko ruriko ruragerageza guharura izo *node zitarumviriza* na zo nyene, ariko biraguma bidashoboka ko umuntu aronka amakuru y’ukuri ata gatotsi ku bijanye na zo, kandi ugusubiramwo iyo mibare nta kabuza birasigaye inyuma y’ukuri.



### Ibikorwa vyo mu mutima vya Bitcoin core



Bitcoin core yanditswe mu rurimi rwa C++. Ni umugambi kandi w’inkomoko yuguruye utunganywa n’umuryango w’abahinguzi b’ibintu bitanga canke barihwa n’ibigo bitandukanye (kenshi n’amashirahamwe ari mu bidukikije afise inyungu mu guteza imbere Core). [Kode iri kuri GitHub](https://github.com/Bitcoin/Bitcoin), kandi iterambere rikurikira uburyo bukomeye:




- Abatanga** batanga ivyiyumviro mu buryo bw'_ibisabwa vyo gukurura_ (PR). Mu ngingo ngenderwako, umuntu wese arashobora gutanga iciyumviro c’ihinduka, ariko ritegerezwa kugeragezwa, rikagira inyandiko, rikaca mu nzira y’isuzuma ry’abagenzi.
- Aba **abacungera** barafise uburenganzira bwo kwemeza no gufatanya ama PR. Ni bo bashingira intahe ko umugambi uzoguma uhuye kandi uhagaze neza. Muri Nyakanga 2025, hari batanu muri bo: Hennadii Stepanov, Mikaheli Ford, Andrew Chow, Gloria Zhao na Ryan Ofsky.
- Nta **umucungerezi mukuru** yariho kuva muri Ruhuhuma 2023. Uwo murimo wari waratangujwe na Satoshi Nakamoto igihe Bitcoin yatanguzwa, hanyuma na Gavin Andresen akurikije ukugenda kwa Nakamoto mu ntango za 2011, hanyuma na Wladimir J. Van204.



![Image](assets/fr/057.webp)



Iterambere rya Bitcoin core rikurikira ivyiyumviro vy’ubuhinga: abashasha batanga intererano bararemeshwa gusubiramwo no kugerageza itegeko imbere y’uko bo ubwabo batanga ihinduka. Ivyemezo bishingiye ku kwumvikana kw’abahinga, kandi ihinduka rikomeye (cane cane mu bijanye n’uguhuza) bisaba ibiganiro vyo hejuru ku nzira za bose, nk’urutonde rw’abarungika ubutumwa canke amashirahamwe y’isuzuma ry’ubucuti n’abantu.



### Ibindi bishirwa mu ngiro vya Bitcoin



Naho ari ku ruhande mu bijanye no gutora abana, hariho n’abandi bakiriya. Igihambaye ni Bitcoin Knots, yateguwe na Luke Dashjr, Fork ya Bitcoin core irimwo ubundi buryo bwo guhitamwo n’uburyo bwo guteza imbere bushingiye ku vyo umuntu akora. Ivyo birimwo n’uguhagarika cane uburyo bwo gutanga amafaranga.



![Image](assets/fr/058.webp)



Turashobora kandi kuvuga:




- Libbitcoin**: ububiko bw’ibitabu bwa C++ bwateguwe na Amir Taaki kandi bugatunganirizwa na Eric Voskuil;
- Bcoin**: ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa JavaScript, ntikugikoreshwa neza;
- BTCD/btcsuit**e: ugushirwa mu ngiro muri Go.



Iyi migambi iratuma habaho ubudasa bw’ibidukikije, ariko kwemerwa kwayo biracari bike cane, bikaba bigora ko Bitcoin core itera imbere yigenga.



### Ububasha bw'abahinguzi ba Core



Ushobora kwiyumvira ko abakora Bitcoin core bafise ububasha butaziguye kuri Bitcoin, ariko ivyo si ko biri. Ntibashobora gutegeka ko haba uguhindura protocole. Uruhara rwabo ni ugutanga kode. Ni umukoresha wese, biciye ku nzira yiwe, gufata ingingo yo gukoresha canke kutakoresha iyo kode.



Ivyo bisigura ko iyo ihinduka muri Bitcoin core ritahuye n’ivyo abantu bose bemeranije, rishobora kwirengagizwa n’ibihimba, haba mu kudasubiramwo Bitcoin core canke mu guhindura gusa ingene bishirwa mu ngiro. Ku rundi ruhande, iyo ikintu cipfuzwa n’abakoresha kibujijwe mu nzira y’iterambere ry’Ishingiro, birashoboka kwama bihindukira bikaja mu yindi nzira yo gushirwa mu ngiro canke Fork umugambi.



Nk’uko tuzobivuga mu nyuma muri iki cigwa, ni izo nzira, hakurikijwe uburemere bwazo bw’ubutunzi (ni ukuvuga abacuruzi), zitanga akamaro ku nsiguro y’amasezerano (kandi rero ku mafaranga ahuye), mu kwemera ibice vyubahiriza amategeko yayo. Ububasha nyabwo bwo gutwara Bitcoin rero buri kuri abo badandaza, si abayitegura.

Nkako, nk’uko twabibonye mu bigabane vyabanje, nta nyungu y’amahera itaziguye iboneka mu gutera urudodo. Umuntu yoshobora rero kwiyumvira yuko ata nyungu y’umuntu ku giti ciwe iri mu kubigira. ### Gukwiragiza ibanga ryinshi ry'ibikorwa

Nkako, nk’uko vyavuzwe mu kigabane c’imbere, ama wallet ntavugana n’urubuga rwa Bitcoin biciye ku bupfumu; bategerezwa gufatanya n’urudodo kugira ngo bashobore kuraba amafaranga canke amafaranga yo gutangaza. Niba utigeze ushinga node yawe, ivyo bisigura ko Wallet yawe ivana n’ibikorwa remezo vy’uwundi muntu (kenshi na kenshi ni ishirahamwe riri inyuma ya porogarama). Uwo muntu wa gatatu, cane cane iyo ari ishirahamwe, arashobora kwihweza, gukoresha nabi, canke mbere gutangaza aya makuru: haba kubera imvo z’ubudandaji, kubera uburenganzira bw’amategeko, canke kubera ubusuma bw’amakuru.

### Ibicuruzwa bidashobora guhanwa



Kubera izo mpamvu nyene twavuze haruguru, porogarama ya Wallet ishingiye ku nzira y’uwundi muntu irashobora gushikirwa n’ingorane zo gucengera: uwukoresha iyo nzira yo kure ashobora kwanka gutanga amafaranga amwamwe kubera imvo zitandukanye. Ishobora kubibona ko ari ibintu biteye amakenga canke ko biteye kubiri n’ingingo mfatirwako zayo. Ivyo bishobora kandi guhagarara iyo bidahuye n’amategeko y’ugutanga amakuru y’urudodo. Ubwa nyuma, uwo mukoresha ashobora gushingira canecane IP yawe Address kugira ngo ahagarike gutangaza amakuru yawe.



Ku rundi ruhande, ukoresheje urudodo rwawe bwite, urabona ko amafaranga yawe akwiragizwa mu rubuga rw’urunganwe. Ivyo bisigura ko uguma ugenzura vyose ku bijanye n’ugusangira amafaranga yawe, ata n’umwe wizigira uwuguhuza. Igihe cose iyo nzira izoba ihuye n'amategeko y'ugusezerana n'ugutanga amakuru y'ibice bihuye n'ivyawe, izotangazwa ku rubuga hanyuma, iyo amafaranga ahagije azoshirwamwo, izoshirwa mu gice n'igikoresho ca Miner. Kugira node yawe bwite bikwemeza ko ataco ukora, ata ruhusha rwo kwemeza amafaranga yawe.

### Gukwirakwiza neza umutekano wa sisitemu



Igipande cose gifatanya n’uruzitiro gikomeza ubushobozi bwa Bitcoin bwo gusubira inyuma no kwihangana. Bituma amakuru akwiragizwa neza kandi bikaba bituma abagenzi bashasha bashobora kwifatanya. Iyo hataba izo nzira, iyo nzira yoba gusa idashobora gukora.



Nk’uko twabibonye, ​​umutekano wa Bitcoin ntushingiye ku kwegeranya ubutegetsi, Mining, canke ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru: nk’uko bigenda ku buryo bwose, bushingiye ku bantu ku giti cabo. Mu buryo bubereye, bivana n’ubushobozi bw’abakoresha amanode bwo kunanira uguhatwa.



Igitandukanya ubuhinga bwegerejwe nka Bitcoin ni ugusangira ingorane mu bantu bose bari mu bikorwa vyabwo. Gukoresha node yawe bwite ya Bitcoin bisigura kwemera umugabane w’ivyo bibazo mu kumenya neza umutekano w’instance yawe; mu kubigira, uraremesha kandi umuzigo w’akaga ku bandi bakoresha node.



Rero si inyungu y’umuntu ku giti ciwe: gukoresha node bituma ubazwa igice c’umutekano w’urubuga. Ikiruta vyose, ni inyungu rusangi, kuko uruhara rwawe rufasha gukwiragiza ingorane. Na wewe nyene, wongerera ubushobozi bwawe bwite bwo gukoresha Bitcoin mu buryo bwo kwizigirwa.



### Gutahura cane urutonde .



Gushiramwo Full node si igikorwa c’ubusa. Birimwo gushiramwo porogarama, gutahura ingene ikora, gukurikirana uko ibintu bikorana, gusuzuma amakuru iyo habaye ingorane, eka mbere no gukoresha terminal. Ivyo bizotuma ata kabuza urushiriza gutahura ivy’itegeko. Ivyo ni akarusho kataziguye, ariko si akamaro gatoyi.



Kuronka ubwo bumenyi biratuma urushiriza kwizigira ico gikoresho kandi birashobora kugabanya ingorane zo gukora amakosa canke gushikirwa n’ibinyoma. Guzunguruka urudodo rwawe bwite na vyo nyene ni uburyo bwo kwiga.



### Guhitamwo amategeko yo gukurikiza



Ikintu gihambaye, akenshi kidatahurwa neza, ni uko gukoresha urudodo bigufasha guhitamwo amategeko ukoresha mu karere. Hari ubwoko bubiri nyamukuru bw'amategeko:





- Amategeko y'ukwemeranya**:



Aya ni yo mategeko y’ishimikiro y’amasezerano ya Bitcoin, ashingira intahe ubutungane bw’urwego no gushinga ingingo ngenderwako zo kwemeza amafaranga n’amabuye. Ivyo vyose bigurishwa bidahuye n’aya mategeko y’uguhurizako ntibishobora kwigera bishirwa mu gice gifise akamaro. Nk’akarorero, igikorwa gifise umukono udafise akamaro kuri kimwe mu bintu vyinjiyemwo kizokurwamwo mu buryo butegekanijwe.



Guhindura ayo mategeko ni nk’uguhindura umurongo w’amategeko, rero n’amahera (Hard Fork). Ariko rero, naho ata kugerageza kubihindura, ikintu coroshe co gukurikiza ata guca ku ruhande amategeko ariho kiratanga ububasha bumwebumwe: iyo igice c’ibarabara kirenganye ayo mategeko, iyo node iraca icanka.





- Amategeko yo gutanga**:



Aya ni amategeko yihariye kuri buri nzira ya Bitcoin, yongerwa ku mategeko y’uguhurizako kugira ngo asobanure imiterere y’ibikorwa bitaremejwe vyemewe muri Mempool kandi bishikirizwa abo mu runganwe. Buri nzira iratunganya kandi igashira mu ngiro ayo mategeko mu karere, ivyo bikaba bisigura igituma yoshobora gutandukanywa n’inzira imwe gushika ku yindi. Bikoreshwa gusa ku bikorwa bitaremejwe: ibikorwa bibonwa ko "bitari mu rugero" n'urudodo bizokwemerwa gusa iyo bimaze kuboneka mu bubiko bubereye. Guhindura ayo mategeko ntibikuraho urudodo muri sisitemu ya Bitcoin.



Nk’akarorero, ugucuruza ata mahera, hakurikijwe amategeko y’uguhurizako, birafise akamaro, ariko bizokwankwa ku buryo busanzwe hakurikijwe ingingo ngenderwako y’ugutanga amakuru ya Bitcoin core, kubera ko `minRelayTxFee` parameter ishizwe kuri `0.00001` (mu BTC/kB). Ariko rero, birashoboka, ku nzira yawe bwite, kugabanya iyo nzira kugira ngo ushikirize amafaranga make, canke, ibihushanye n’ivyo, wongere umupaka, nk’akarorero, ku 2 Sats/vB, kugira ngo wirinde gutanga amafaranga make.



Guhindukiza urudodo rwawe bwite bisigura kwemeza ngo: "Nshingira intahe ivyo mpisemwo kwemeza, hakurikijwe amategeko jewe ubwanje nafashe "*. Gutyo uba ubaye umukinyi mu bijanye n’uburongozi bw’urutonde, ushobora kwanka ubwihindurize busa n’ubutemera kuri wewe, canke ukemera uguhindura ibintu bishingiye ku vyo wewe ubwawe ubona.



Rero turashobora kugerageza kwihuta gutahura ububasha ufise ku mategeko kubera urudodo rwawe. Kandi urugero rw’ubwo bubasha ruzovana n’ubwoko bw’ubutegetsi.



#### Ku mategeko y'ugusubiramwo



Ku bijanye n’amategeko yo gutanga, ikintu nyamukuru ni ukugira gusa urudodo, utitaye ku gikorwa cayo c’ubutunzi. Ico kiri mu kaga hano ni uko wemera canke utemera gutanga ubwoko bumwe bumwe bw’amafaranga.



Nimba nk’akarorero wemera ko amafaranga afise amafaranga ari munsi ya 1 sat/vB akwiye kwemerwa kuri Bitcoin, urashobora guhindura iri tegeko ku nzira yawe kugira ngo itangaze ayo mafaranga, gutyo bikorohere gukwiragira ku rubuga gushika Miner amaherezo ayashize mu gice gifise akamaro. Mu vy’ukuri rero, ni ikibazo c’ububasha ku gukwiragiza amafaranga: urudodo rumwe rumwe rufise ububasha bwo gufata ingingo, kuko kwemera gutanga ubwoko bw’amafaranga ari nk’uguteza imbere ukwemerwa kwavyo ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin. Ivyo bituma iyo ukoresheje ama node menshi, uba ufise ububasha bwinshi ku ngingo ngenderwako y’ugutanga amakuru, kuko node yose ifise amasano yayo n’aho igira ingaruka ku rubuga.



Nkako, kugira urudodo rumwe canke nyinshi zitunganijwe n’amategeko yihariye y’ugutanga amakuru bisigura kumenya igice c’urubuga cemera gukwiragiza ubwoko bumwe bw’ugucuruza. Gukwiragiza ubutumwa mu gicapo c’urunganwe, nk’uko biri ku bikorwa vya Bitcoin, bikurikira inyigisho y’ugusesagura. Iyumvire ko urudodo rumwe rumwe ari urubuga rushobora gukora (`p` = ruratangaza) canke rudakora (`1-p`). Igihe igipimo `p` kijabuka urugero ruhambaye (`p_c`), igice kinini kirasohoka: igikorwa gishobora kujabuka urusobe kandi gifise amahirwe yose yo gushika ku Miner. Mu rubuga nka Bitcoin, aho urudodo rumwe rumwe rugumya umubare w’amahuza 8 asohoka, urugero rwa `p_c` muri rusangi rushirwa ku bice bike kw’ijana gusa, mbere n’aho ruri hasi iyo urudodo rumwe rumwe rufise umubare munini cane w’amahuza.



![Image](assets/fr/061.webp)



Igihe cose `p` iguma munsi ya `p_c`, igikorwa kiguma mu mifuko yitandukanije kandi ntikigera kuri Miner. Iyo iyo nzira ikimara kurenga, irakwiragira hafi mu kanya nk’ako gukubita uruja n’uruza rwose.



Mu mpera, abacukuzi ni bo bama bafata ingingo yo gushiramwo canke kutashiramwo igikorwa co gucuruza mu gice. Ariko rero, izo node zica zigira ico zikoze ku bijanye n’ugusangira amafaranga: ni zo zigena nimba abacukuzi bazomenya canke batazomenya igikorwa kinaka. Iyo igikorwa kitashikirijwe abacukuzi, biragaragara ko badashobora kugishira mu gice.



Kwongerako rero izindi nzira nkeyi bizogira ingaruka nkeyi gusa iyo urubuga rusanzwe ruri mu gihe c’ugusenyuka ku bwoko bumwe bw’ugucuruza, ariko birashobora kwemeza ko ari ngirakamaro uko urugero rw’ugusenyuka rwegereza. Kugira canke gukora ku nzira nyinshi, cane cane iyo zihuye neza, birashobora kwongerera canke kugabanya agaciro ka `p`, bigatuma mu buryo butaziguye kuyobora amategeko y’ugutanga amakuru agena amafaranga abonwa kandi amaherezo yemerwa n’abacukuzi.



#### Ku mategeko y'uguhurizako



Ku bijanye n’ingene urudodo rwawe rugira ico rukoze ku mategeko y’uguhurizako, uburemere bwarwo mu vy’ubutunzi ni co, hejuru ya vyose, kizofata ingingo. Ico ni iciyumviro gihambaye cane: agaciro k’ifaranga iryo ari ryo ryose rifitaniye isano n’ubushobozi bwaryo bwo kworohereza Exchange. Nkako, iyo ikintu kitaremewe n’umuntu n’umwe muri Exchange kubera ibicuruzwa canke ibikorwa, mu vy’impwemu nta co kimaze mu vy’amahera. Nk’akarorero, nimba ata mudandaza yemera amabuye y’agaciro nk’uburyo bwo kwishura, nta co amaze nk’amahera. Ego ni ko, akamaro kaguma ari iciyumviro c’umuntu ku giti ciwe, ariko mu karere kanaka, uko umubare w’abadandaza bemera ikintu nk’uburyo bwa Exchange urushiriza kuba mwinshi, ni ko bishoboka cane ko ico kintu gifise akamaro k’amahera ku bantu baba muri ako karere.



Reka dufate akarorero k’igisagara aho abacuruzi benshi bemera inzahabu mu Exchange ku bicuruzwa: amahirwe ni uko inzahabu ifise akamaro k’amahera ku baturage bo mu gisagara. Ivyo vyerekana ko akamaro k’amahera kavana ata guca ku ruhande n’ingingo abacuruzi bafata zo kuyemera canke kuyanka.



Ico ciyumviro ni ikintu gihambaye cane mu gutahura ubushobozi bukoreshwa muri sisitemu ya Bitcoin. Satoshi irabitomora neza: Bitcoin ni uburyo bwo gutanga amahera bukoresheje ubuhinga bwa none; mu yandi majambo, itanga igikorwa gitanga uburyo bumwe bw’amahera, Bitcoin (canke BTC). Iyo amategeko y’amasezerano ahinduwe mu buryo butajanye n’inyuma (Hard Fork), ivyo bica bihwana no kurema uburyo bushasha rero n’amahera mashasha. Uko iyo Fork izororanirwa canke izonanirwa rero bivana n’ubunini bw’ubutunzi bwayo, ivyo na vyo bikaba bigenwa n’umubare w’abadandaza bemera ubwo buryo bushasha bw’amahera.



![Image](assets/fr/062.webp)



Reka dufate akarorero: reka dufate ko Bitcoin ibabazwa na Hard Fork. Hazoba rero ubwoko 2 butandukanye bw’amahera: BTC-1 (iyi mbere, itahindutse) na BTC-2 (iyindi mafaranga mashasha afise amategeko atandukanye y’uguhurizako). Iyo abacuruzi bose bemeye BTC-1 babandanya kubigira, ariko bakanka BTC-2, rero iyo ya nyuma izogira, mu vyiyumviro, akamaro k’amahera gato cane. Nk’umukoresha, nta nyungu nogira yo kubika no gukoresha BTC-2, nzi ko ata mudandaza yoyishaka muri Exchange ku bicuruzwa canke ibikorwa. Ku rundi ruhande, iyo 50% vy’abadandaza bahisemwo kwemera BTC-2 gusa, 50% bisigaye bagafata BTC-1 gusa, rero akamaro ka BTC-1 kazoba, mu vyiyumviro, kagabanutse igice. Nkoresha ijambo "mu vyiyumviro" kuko akamaro kaguma ari ukwiyumvira ku rugero rw'umuntu ku giti ciwe kandi kavana n'ibintu vyinshi (nk'intara n'ingeso zo gukoresha) bigoye gutahura ku muntu ku muntu.



Ku Bitcoin, uruhara rwa "umucuruzi", rutahurwa nk'ikigo cose gifise uburemere bumwe bw'ubutunzi, birashoboka ko harimwo ubucuruzi (amaduka y'umubiri, imbuga zo kugurisha kuri interineti, abatanga ibikorwa, n'ibindi), ariko kandi n'ibibanza vya Exchange, kuko bemera Bitcoin muri Exchange ku yandi mafaranga mince mu Exchange, kandi bemera amahera y'i Exchange. Exchange ku bijanye n’igikorwa co gushiramwo ivy’ugucuruza mu gice.



Ku bijanye n’amategeko y’uguhurizako, urudodo rwawe ruraguha uburenganzira bwo kuyobora igikorwa cawe c’ubutunzi ku mafaranga imwe canke ayandi. Nk’akarorero, iyo ufise amanode 10 yuzuye muhira, ariko nta gikorwa gihambaye c’ubutunzi ukora, akosho kawe mu gihe ca Fork kazoba hafi ataco kamaze. Ku rundi ruhande, urudodo rumwe rukoreshwa mu gucunga uruhererekane rw’amaduka 200 yemera Bitcoin rutanga uburemere buhambaye mu vy’ubutunzi.



Rero si umubare w’ibihimba vy’umubiri bihambaye, ahubwo ni akamaro k’igikorwa c’ubutunzi bashigikira. Ikindi, iyo igikorwa cawe co mu vy’ubutunzi kivana n’urudodo udacungera, nyen’urudodo ni we azofata ingingo y’amahera ukoresha, igihe cose uzoba uguma uhuye n’urwo rudodo. Ni co gituma gukoresha no gukoresha urudodo rwawe ari ngirakamaro cane mu bijanye n'uburongozi bwa sisitemu:



> Si ipfundo ryawe, si amategeko yawe.


## Ubwoko butandukanye bw'ibihimba vya Bitcoin


<chapterId>be8f0baa-41f2-4b54-b011-092f4ccc93aa</chapterId>



Igikoresho ca Bitcoin ni rero imashini ikoresha ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’itegeko rya Bitcoin. Inyuma y’iyi nsobanuro rusangi y’ibihimba, hariho imiterere myinshi ishoboka, si yose itanga urugero rumwe rw’ubwigenge, ugukoresha ibikoresho, n’akamaro ku rubuga. Muri iki gice, tuzogerageza gutahura izo ntandukaniro kugira ngo tugufashe guhitamwo ubuhinga bwo gukora node bujanye n’ikoreshwa ryawe n’ingorane z’ibikoresho.



### Ipfundo ryose



Full node ni gusa urudodo rwa Bitcoin rukura Blockchain yose mu gice ca Genesis, rukemeza igice cose rwigenga, kandi rugabika amateka y’ivyo vyose Blockchain mu karere. Iyi ni yo nzira "isanzwe" y'urudodo rwa Bitcoin, nk'uko vyiyumviriwe na Satoshi Nakamoto.



![Image](assets/fr/063.webp)



Full node nta n’umwe ikeneye kwizigira kuko yemeza kandi ikamenya amakuru yose ari muri iyo system. Ni ubwoko bw’urudodo ruguha ivyemezo vyinshi: urazi, utazigiye uwundi muntu, nimba ukwishyura gufise akamaro, nimba ububiko bufise akamaro, nimba ugusubira gutunganya ibintu bibereye, n’ibindi.



Mu bikorwa, Full node isaba ibikoresho bitagira akamaro, harimwo amajana menshi ya gigabytes y’amadosiye y’amabuye, igikoresho gishobora kwemeza inyandiko, RAM ya Mempool n’ibibanza vy’ububiko, n’uburebure bw’uruja n’uruza buhamye. Ihuriro rya mbere (*IBD*) risoma kandi rikagenzura amateka yose: rirakomeye cane, ariko rishika rimwe gusa. Full node iragira uruhara n’umwete mu rubuga, igatanga amabuye n’ibikorwa, kandi ishobora kwemera amahuzu yinjira kugira ngo ifashe abandi bagenzi.



Bivanye n'ivyo ukeneye, urashobora kwongerako indexer kuri Full node yawe. Bitcoin core itanga indexing y’ibikorwa nk’ikintu co guhitamwo (kizimye ku buryo busanzwe), gishobora kuba ngirakamaro ku ntumbero zimwe zimwe. Ariko rero, nta indexer ya Address irimwo, iyo na yo ikaba ari yo kenshi ironderwa cane ku bakoresha ku giti cabo. Kugira ngo ushobore gutorera umuti ivyo, urashobora gushiramwo porogarama yihariye ku nzira yawe, nka Electrs canke Fulcrum, kugira ngo wihutishe ibibazo vyo kugenzura uburinganire bwa Address biva ku UTXO zijanye n’ivyo. Tuzogaruka ku ruhara rw’umurongozi mu buryo burambuye mu kigabane gitandukanye.



### Ipfundo rya pruned



Igikoresho ca pruned cemeza vyose nk'ivya Full node, kuva ku gice ca Genesis gushika ku mutwe w'uruzitiro rufise igikorwa kinini, ariko **kigumya gusa igice gishasha c'amadosiye y'igice**. Iyo amabuye ya kera amaze gusuzumwa, buhoro buhoro arayafuta kugira ngo agume munsi y’aho ushobora gushinga. Iyi ntunganyo ni nziza cane iyo ufise umwanya muto kuri disiki: uguma ufise ubwigenge bwo kwemeza amabuye, utabitse ububiko bwose bw'amateka ya Blockchain. Iryo hitamwo ni ngirakamaro canecane nimba ushaka gusa gushiramwo Bitcoin core kuri mudasobwa yawe bwite, udakoresheje imashini yihariye.



![Image](assets/fr/064.webp)



Ivyiza vy’ubuhinga vy’iyi nzira biragororotse: urudodo rwa pruned rurashobora neza gutangaza amafaranga yawe, kugira uruhara mu gutanga amakuru, kugenzura amabuye n’amafaranga, no gukurikirana uruhererekane. Ku rundi ruhande, ntishobora gukora nk’isoko y’amakuru y’akahise arenga imipaka yayo ku bindi bikorwa (nk’akarorero, abashakashatsi buzuye, abashiramwo urutonde, amasakoshi). Ibikorwa bisaba ububiko (canke urutonde rw'isi yose) rero ntibizoboneka.



Mu majambo ngirakamaro, ushobora gukoresha urudodo rwa pruned kugira ngo uhuze porogaramu y'uburongozi bwa Wallet nka Sparrow wallet. Ariko rero, ntuzoshobora gucapura amafaranga kuri Wallet yawe imbere y’igipimo co gukata. Nk’akarorero, iyo ufise igikorwa canditswe muri block 901 458, ariko node yawe iguma igumya gusa ama blocks kuva kuri 905 402 gushika hejuru (kubera ko aya kera cane yari pruned), ntuzoshobora gucapura iyo transaction. Ku rundi ruhande, nimba wari waramaze kuyipima igihe urudodo rwawe rwari rukiri n’ubu burebure bw’ibarabara, rero porogarama yawe yo gucunga Wallet izobika amakuru kandi yerekane neza uburinganire bw’ama UTXO ahuye.



Muri make, gukurikirana Wallet birakora ataco bigutera ku nzira ya pruned iyo uremye Wallet nshasha mu gihe porogarama yawe isanzwe ifatanye n’iyo nzira. Ku rundi ruhande, ushobora guhura n’ingorane iyo usubijeho Wallet ya kera, kuko amafaranga y’imbere atagigumye n’uruzitiro bigaragara ko atazoshobora gusubirwamwo.



### Ipfundo ry'umuco / SPV



Igikoresho ca SPV (*Simplified Payment Verification*), canke igikoresho gifise uburemere buke, kigumya gusa imitwe y’amabuye, atari amakuru y’ibikorwa, kandi cizigira izindi node zishitse kugira ngo kironke ikimenyamenya c’uko ibikorwa biri mu gice (ibimenyamenya vya Merkle biciye ku biti) gifise umutwe. Iciyumviro co kugenzura amahera yoroshe si gishasha, kuko cashikirijwe na Satoshi Nakamoto ubwiwe mu gice ca 8 c’Igitabu Cera.



![Image](assets/fr/066.webp)



Nakamoto, S. (2008). *Bitcoin: Uburyo bwo gukoresha amahera mu buryo bw’ubuhinga bwa none*. Igitabo c'Ivyabona vya Yehova 213.pdf



Ubwo bwoko bw’uruzitiro buragaragara ko buremereye cane mu bijanye no kubika no gukoresha CPU kuruta uruzitiro rwa Full node canke mbere rwa pruned. Igikoresho ca SPV rero kirabereye neza ibikoresho bitobito be n’uguhuza bigenda bihinduka. Nkako, akenshi ryinjizwa ataco rihinduye muri Wallet, cane cane porogarama zigendanwa nka Blockstream App.



Ico guhindura ni ukwizigirana n’ibanga: umukiriya wa SPV ntasuzuma inyandiko canke amategeko y’ukwemeza ubwiwe; rifata ko uruzitiro rufise igikorwa kinini ari rwo rufise akamaro, kandi ruvana n'urudodo rumwe canke nyinshi zuzuye ku nyishu. Gukoresha ubwo bwoko bw’urudodo rero ni uburyo bwiza kuruta gufatanya n’urudodo rw’uwundi muntu; ariko rero, biracari bifise akamaro gatoyi kuruta kugira Full node canke mbere node pruned.



![Image](assets/fr/065.webp)



### Ni iyihe node kubera ikihe gikenewe?





- Ukoresha telefone ngendanwa / uwutangura



Ku muntu atarakoresha Wallet gusa kuri porogarama ya telefone ngendanwa, gukoresha SPV node ni uburyo bwiza bwo gutangura. Gushiramwo biranyaruka, bisaba ibikoresho bike, kandi ubumenyi buraryoshe kandi buragenda neza. Ivyo bisigura ko ushobora kugenzura amakuru amwamwe wewe nyene, rero ntukizigire cane ama node y’abandi mu gihe nyene ushobora kwigenga cane iyo bije ku bijanye n’ugutangaza amakuru.





- PC / umukoresha wo hagati



Umuntu akoresha PC yo hagati arashobora gushiramwo urudodo rwa pruned kugira ngo yungukire ku vyiza hafi vyose vya Full node, ataco akoresha cane ku mashine yiwe ku musi ku musi: kwemeza neza, gukoresha disiki mu buryo buringaniye, no kuyibungabunga mu buryo bworoshe. Ni umuti mwiza wo gufatanya ama wallets yawe yo kuri desktop no kuguma wigenga mu gukwiragiza amafaranga yawe, utashize amahera mu mashini yihariye canke ngo ukoreshe cane umwanya kuri disk yawe.





- Bitcoiner / ateye imbere



Full node iguma ari umuti mwiza nimba ushaka kwigenga mu gukoresha Bitcoin kandi ntugaruke mu nyuma ku gukoresha biteye imbere nk'indexer, Lightning node, canke mbere Block explorer. Ivyo nyene ni vyo tuzokwihweza muri iki cigwa!



## Incamake y'ibisubizo vya porogaramu


<chapterId>0d48b89a-e8b5-441e-a707-537a035fc15e</chapterId>



Ku ruhande rwa porogaramu, hari uburyo 2 nyamukuru bwo gukoresha node ya Bitcoin:




- gushiramwo ataco bimaze gushirwa mu ngiro kw'amasezerano, nka Bitcoin core (vyiza), canke Bitcoin Knots,
- canke ukoreshe ugukwirakwiza kw'urufunguzo (kenshi kwitwa "_node-in-a-box_") gushiramwo ugushirwa mu ngiro kwa Bitcoin mu buryo bumwe, ariko kandi harimwo uburyo bwo gutwara Interface, ububiko bw'ibikorwa, n'ibikoresho vyiteguye gukoreshwa (Lightning, abacukumbuzi, abakozi b'urutonde, mbere n'ibikorwa vya GW2-hosting).



Ubwo buryo bwompi bujana ku ntumbero imwe: kugira node yawe bwite, ariko buratandukanye mu bijanye no gushiramwo no gukoresha Interface, kuyibungabunga, kwaguka, n’igiciro. Ivyo ni vyo tuzokwihweza muri iki kigabane.



### Ishirwa mu ngiro ry'urudodo rwa Bitcoin



Gushiramwo ishyirwa mu ngiro ritagiramwo ibintu bisigura gukoresha porogaramu y'ishyirwa mu ngiro ry'amasezerano ya Bitcoin (nka Core), ata porogaramu y'inyongera Layer. Ucungera imiterere, ivyagezwe, n’ibikorwa bijanye (indexation, API, Lightning, backups, n’ibindi) wewe nyene, bivanye n’ivyo ukeneye.



Ubu ni bwo buryo bufise ubusegaba kandi bushobora guhinduka: urazi neza ibiriko biragenda, aho amakuru ari, n’ingene vyose bikora. Ku rundi ruhande, birarushiriza kugorana igihe nyene ushaka kurenga igikorwa coroshe c’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin. Nimba intumbero yawe ari iyo kugira gusa urudodo, ugusobanuka kwayo kugereranywa n’ukw’urudodo-mu-gasandugu, canke mbere n’uguke, kuko ari ikibazo co gushiramwo porogarama gusa.



#### Bitcoin core (umukiriya mwinshi cane)



[Bitcoin core ni umukiriya w’urubuga rw’abantu benshi cane] Ikura, yemeza, kandi ikabungabunga Blockchain, itanga RPC/REST APIs, kandi ishobora gushiramwo Wallet. Niba ukunda ibikoresho bisanzwe kandi wumva ushobora kwongerako ibikorwa wewe nyene (nk’umukozi wa Electrum, explorer, na LND), urashobora gukoresha Core uko iri.



**Ivyiza:** Gushikama cane, inyifato ishobora gutegekanirwa, ubumenyi butagiramwo ivyiza, vyoroshe gushiramwo no gutunganya.



**Ibibi:** Utegerezwa kwubaka n'amaboko igice gisigaye c'ikirundo kugira ngo ureme ibidukikije vy'ibikorwa vyuzuye, aho gukora gusa urudodo rwa Bitcoin.

#### Bitcoin Knots (umukiriya nyamukuru)



[Bitcoin Knots ni Fork ya Bitcoin core], icungiwe na Luka Dashjr. Ni umukiriya nyamukuru w’ubundi buryo bwo gushitsa amasezerano ya Bitcoin. Ihuye neza n’ibindi bice vy’urubuga (ntabwo ari Hard Fork nka Bitcoin Cash), naho biri ukwo itanga ibindi bintu, harimwo n’amahitamwo y’ingingo ngenderwako y’ugutanga amakuru ataboneka muri Core, canke ikoreshwa cane cane ku buryo busanzwe kugira ngo igabanye ivyo bamwe babona ko ari spam.



Hari impamvu 2 zishoboka zo guhitamwo Knots kuruta Core:




- Ubuhinga**: Amahitamwo atandukanye ava kuri Core, cane cane mu bijanye n’uburongozi bw’ivy’ugutanga amakuru, mu kumenya amafaranga yemerwa kandi atangazwa n’uruzitiro rwawe.
- Policy**: Abantu bamwe bamwe barakunda gukoresha ubundi ba clients nka Knots kubera imvo zitari izo mu vy’ubuhinga, cane cane kugira ngo bashigikire ubundi buryo bwo gukoresha Core gutyo bagabanye ububasha bwayo bwo gukoresha. Iyo Core izokwigera ihungabana, vyoba vyiza atari gusa kugira abaguzi bakomeye kandi batunganijwe neza, ariko kandi no kumenya ingene yobakoresha neza. Abandi bakoresha Knots mu ntumbero zo kwiyamiriza, kubera ko batakaje icizigiro mu bategura Core canke ntibemera uburongozi bwinshi bw’umukiriya.

Ku bwanje, ndagusavye guhitamwo Core, ahanini kugira ngo wungukire ku bipande vy’umutekano vyihuse. Nkako, hariho utunenge tumwetumwe twavumbuwe muri Knots turakosorwa haciye igihe. Muri rusangi, inzira y’iterambere rya Core iratunganijwe neza kandi ishigikiwe n’abantu benshi batanga, mu gihe Knots itunganywa n’umuntu umwe kandi ifise umuryango muto cane. Ku rundi ruhande, amategeko y’ugutanga amakuru akunda gutakaza akamaro kayo muri iki gihe, cane cane iyo akoreshejwe n’agace gatoyi gusa k’uruja n’uruza (nk’uko bivugwa mu ciyumviro c’ugucapura).



### Ugusangira kw'urudodo mu gasandugu



_Node-in-a-box_ ifatanya Bitcoin core (canke Knots) n’uburyo bwo gukoresha bwatunganijwe mbere, Urubuga rwa Interface, n’Iduka ry’Ibikoresho vy’Ibikorwa vy’Ukwiyakira (Imiravyo, abashakashatsi, Serveri ya Electrum, Mempool, Serveri ya BTCPay, Nextcloud, n’ibindi). Mu gukanda rimwe gusa, urashobora gushiramwo, guhindura no guhuza izo module zitandukanye.



Ni umuti woroshe cane wo gutangura no gucunga ibikorwa vyinshi vy'inyongera ku musi ku musi. Ikibi ni uko iyo ingorane ibaye (nk'akarorero, amakimbirane y'ishusho ya Docker, ivugurura ry'amakosa, urutonde rw'amakuru rwononekaye), gukosora bishobora kuba bigoye cane, kuko uvana n'ugushiramwo ivy'ugukwiragiza ubwavyo. Ikindi, infashanyo y’abanyagihugu canke y’ubutegetsi akenshi iragoye.



Rero, node-in-a-box ni yoroshe cane gukoresha igihe cose vyose bikora neza, ariko iyo habaye ikibazo, utegerezwa kwitegurira gukora ubushakashatsi burebure, kurindira imfashanyo, no guhumanya ibiganza vyawe.



Vyinshi muri ivyo bisubizo biraboneka mu buryo bubiri:




- Imashini ibanza gukoranirizwa hamwe: mudasobwa yuzuye ifise OS imaze gushirwamwo. Izo mashini zihembwa uko zigenda zikeneye gusa guterwa mu muyagankuba maze zigahuzwa na Internet kugira ngo zikore. Iyo ingengo y’imari yawe ibirekura, iyo nzira irafise akamaro ko yoroshe cane gushinga, akenshi itanga infashanyo y’imbere, kandi igatanga umusanzu mu gutanga amahera y’iterambere, kuko ubuhinga bw’ubudandaji bw’ayo mashirahamwe muri rusangi bushingiye ku kugurisha ibikoresho.
- DIY: shiraho OS y’ugukwiragiza ku mashine yawe bwite (PC ya kera, NUC, Raspberry Pi, server yo muhira...). Ivyo ni vyo bishobora gutuma umuntu agira ubutunzi bwinshi, kuko ushobora gusubira gukoresha imashini ya kera canke ugahitamwo ibikoresho bihuye neza na neza n’ivyo ukeneye be n’ingene ukoresha amahera yawe. Ni n’uburyo bwo guhitamwo bushobora guhinduka, kandi bushimisha cane gutunganya. Ubwo buryo ni bwo tuzokwihweza mu gice c’ibikorwa c’inyigisho.



Aha niho hari incamake y'ibisubizo nyamukuru vy'urudodo-mu-gasanduku biriho (mu 2025):

[Start9 itanga StartOS (https://start9.com/), ubuhinga bwagenewe "ubuhinga bwo gukoresha ubuhinga bwa none": intumbero ni uko umuntu wese yogira no gucunga server yiwe bwite, ikomezwa n'isoko ry'ibikoresho vy'ubuhinga bwa none. Muri make, nimba ukeneye gusa ibikorwa vy’ingenzi vya Bitcoin, ataco wipfuza cane mu bidukikije vy’ubutunzi cane vya Umbrel, kandi uwukoresha Interface atari we abanza, rero Start9 ni uburyo bwiza. Ahandi ho, Umbrel ni yo ihitamwo ryiza.

## Incamake y'ibisubizo vy'ibikoresho


<chapterId>245d6add-9cda-46b9-9343-31dcdd70456e</chapterId>



None ko twasuzumye ubushobozi bwose bwa software, reka twibande ku bikoresho bisabwa ku node yawe. Nzoguha impanuro zitomoye ku bijanye no guhitamwo ibihimba vyawe, hamwe n’imiterere ihuye n’ingengo y’imari itandukanye. Ego ni ko, iki ni iciyumviro canje bwite n’ivyiyumviro vyanje: nta gukeka ko hariho n’izindi nzira zibereye uretse izo zishikirijwe ngaha. Ikindi, sinzosubira gusura imashini zibanza gukoranirizwa hamwe zitangwa n’imigambi y’ubuhinga bwa node-in-a-box, ivyo twamaze kubivuga mu kigabane c’imbere. Aha, tuzokwibanda cane ku mirongo y’ibisubizo vy’ubuhinga bwa none.



### Woba vy’ukuri ukeneye imashini yihariye?



Mu myaka mikeyi iheze, abakoresha ama bitcoiners bararushirije kumenya iciyumviro kitari co gisanzwe, cane cane mu gihe node-in-a-box yamenyekanye cane mu ntango z’imyaka ya 2020: node ya Bitcoin ikwiye gukoreshwa n’imashini yihariye gusa iyo ntumbero. Ariko ivyo si ukuri. Ntukeneye vy’ukuri mudasobwa yihariye kugira ngo ukoreshe node ya Bitcoin: Bitcoin core irashoboye neza gukora kuri PC yawe ya misi yose. Niba ufise umwanya uhagije wo gukoresha Blockchain canke ushobora gukata, urashobora kwemeza uruzitiro, ugahuza Wallet yawe, mbere ugafunga porogarama iyo umaze kuyikoresha. Inyungu y’iyi nzira ni nini cane: nta n’ishoramari ry’intango ririho kandi nta n’ibintu vyinshi bigoranye.



![Image](assets/fr/074.webp)



Ivyo bivuzwe, gukoresha imashini yihariye akenshi birarushiriza kuryoha. Ishobora gukora ubudasiba (24/7), ishobora gushikirwa kure ibihe vyose, ntiyigere ikoresha ubutunzi bw’imashini yawe nyamukuru, kandi, ikiruta vyose, ikoreshwa mu buryo bwihariye (ikintu ciza co gucungera umutekano: iyo PC yawe bwite ihuye n’ingorane, node yawe irabandanya gukora, n’ibihushanye n’ivyo). Rero ikibazo si "Noba nkeneye kwegurira imashini?", ahubwo ni "Noba nkeneye node iguma iri kuri internet, ishobora gushikirwa n'ibindi bikoresho, kandi ishobora gutera imbere?" (Imiravyo, indexers, ibindi bikoresho...). Nimba inyishu ari egome, guhitamwo imashini itandukanye bizokworoha cane.



### 3 uburyo bwo kuronka: gusubira gukoresha, ivya kera, n'ibishasha



#### Gusubira gukoresha PC ya kera



Ni umuti w’ubutunzi kuruta iyindi yose. Benshi muri twebwe dufise PC ya kera iteraniriza hamwe Dust i muhira, canke n’abagenzi n’imiryango: aka ni akaryo keza ko kuyigarura mu bikorwa! Kugira ngo uyikoreshe nk’uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin, wongereko gusa SSD ya 2TB, bivanye n’ivyo ukeneye, usubirize canke wongereko RAM bars kugira ngo wongere RAM. Niwitege ko uzoriha hagati y’amayero 100 n’amayero 200 ku mashine ikora neza.



Imbere yo kugura ikintu ico ari co cose, banza urabe igitigiri c’ibibanza vyo gukoresha kuri disk, ubwoko bw’ihuriro (M.2 canke SATA), uburyo RAM iteye (SODIMM canke DIMM), n’ingene ikora (DDR4, n’ibindi). Ushobora kandi gufata akaryo ko gusukura imashini, cane cane umuyaga, kugira ngo ukore neza.



Ariko rero, urabe maso nimba uriko urakoresha laptop: bateri irashobora kuba ingorane uko igihe kigenda kirarenga (ibindi kuri ivyo mu nyuma mu kigabane).



#### Yasubiwemwo canke ikoreshwa



Isoko ryuzuye ama PC mato mato y’ubudandaji yasanuwe nk’aya *Lenovo ThinkCentre Tiny*, *HP EliteDesk Mini*, canke *Dell OptiPlex Micro*. Izo mashini zirakomeye, zikomeye, ntizivuga kandi zikoresha neza inguvu. Mu vyiyumviro vyanje, iyi ni yo nzira nziza cane nimba uriko urondera imashini yihariye ku nzira yawe ya Bitcoin.



![Image](assets/fr/075.webp)



Birashoboka kandi kuronka ama PC n’ama laptops yakoreshejwe amaze imyaka mikeyi, afise imiterere iryoshe kandi afise agaciro keza cane ku mahera.



**Iciyumviro:** imashini zivuye mu mashirahamwe, nka *ThinkCentre Tiny*, akenshi zifise gusa *DisplayPort* (DP) y’ibarabara, ata n’isohoka rya HDMI. Ntimwibagire rero kuzana adapteur canke umugozi wa DP-to-HDMI nimba mukeneye.



#### Gugura ibishasha



Nimba ingengo y’imari yawe ikwemerera, urashobora kandi guhitamwo imashini nshasha. Iyi ni inzira nziza nimba ushaka kugira ibikoresho bishasha bikora neza, cane cane nimba utegura gukoresha Umbrel canke Start9 n’ibindi bikoresho hanze y’ibidukikije vya Bitcoin kugira ngo wiyakire.



### None nohitamwo imashini yo mu bwoko ubuhe?



#### PC ntoya "NUC" / igufa ritagira ikintu



Mini-PCs, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, zitanga uburyo bwiza bwo kwakira node ya Bitcoin muhira. Bizigama umwanya, birashobora gushika ku ruhande bitagoranye, bikoresha inguvu nkeyi, kandi birashobora guhindurwa mu buryo bworoshe, nk’ukwongerako RAM canke gusubirira SSD.



Ku bwanje, nkunda *Lenovo ThinkCentre Tiny*, ikwiragiye cane kw’isoko ry’ibikoresho vya kera (biva mu mato y’amashirahamwe); birakomeye cane kandi biroroshe guhindura. Hariho, vy'ukuri, vyinshi bihuye n'ivyo biva ku bandi bahinga: *Dell OptiPlex Micro*, *HP ProDesk / EliteDesk Mini / Micro*, *Intel NUC*, *Gigabyte BRIX*, *MSI Cubi*.



![Image](assets/fr/001.webp)



**Ibihambaye:** ikirenge gito, gukoresha ubushobozi buringaniye, urusaku ruto, ubushobozi bwo guhindura (bivanye n’umuderi), n’ukwizigirwa.



**Intege nke:** irazimvye gatoyi kurusha Raspberry Pi-type SBC, nta screen yubatswemwo (ushobora kuyironka kure canke biciye ku moniteur yo hanze), nta bateri (irazima bukwi na bukwi iyo umuyagankuba ucitse).



#### Laptop yihariye



Ni uburyo bwiza cane bwo gukoresha mini-PC: uno musi, urashobora gusanga ama laptops yakoreshejwe canke mbere mashasha ku bici- ro bito, afise ama processeur meza, ama ports menshi, hamwe n’ibara ry’inyuma n’ibarabara ry’ibarabara (birakora cane ku bijanye no gushiramwo ubwa mbere). Ikiruta vyose, iyo bateri ikora nk’uko UPS isanzwe: iyo umuriro ucitse, iyo node ntizima bukwi na bukwi, kandi irashobora mbere kuguma ikora amasaha menshi.



![Image](assets/fr/076.webp)



**Ibintu bihambaye:** Umuti w’ibintu vyose muri kimwe, bateri ikora nka UPS (nta n’imwe izima), gushiramwo vyoroshe bivuye ku kigaragaza n’ikibazo bifatanye, ikarita ya Wi-Fi ifatanye, n’uguhitamwo kwinshi kw’amasoko yakoreshejwe n’ayashasha (ivyo akenshi bisigura ko ushobora guhanahana ibiciro).



**Intege nke:** ikoresha ubushobozi bwinshi gato kurusha Mini-PC idafise ikintu, bateri irasambuka buhoro buhoro mu bikorwa 24/7 n’ugutakaza ubushobozi, ingorane zidasanzwe ariko nyazo zo kuvyimba canke ubushuhe bugahunga n’imyaka. Ahanini ni iki gice gituma mbona ko mini-PC ari yo nzira nziza kuruta laptop: ukuntu bateri igenda irasenyuka buhoro buhoro be n’ingorane zijanye n’ivyo.



Niwahitamwo uwo muti, ndagusavye ko uguma ubona neza uko bateri imeze kugira ngo wirinde akaga kose. Raba ubushuhe bukaze, iminuko idasanzwe, ukudashikama canke igikoko gifise ubumuga. Iyo habaye inkengeri, nuzime iyo orodinateri maze uyikuremwo, hanyuma uyishire mu kibanza kidasanzwe co gusubiramwo ibintu.



**Impanuro:** Niba BIOS/UEFI canke igikoresho c’umuhinguzi kivyemerera, shiraho umupaka w’umuzigo (nk’akarorero, 60% canke 80%) kugira ngo wongere ubuzima bwa bateri.



#### Raspberry Pi n'izindi SBC: iciyumviro kitari co



Mu ntango z’imyaka ya 2020, n’iterambere ry’ubuhinga bwa node-in-a-box, ubusazi bwa Raspberry Pi na bwo bwaje nk’uburyo bwo gukoresha node ya Bitcoin. Ico ciyumviro cari kimeze nk’ikikwegera: nticari kizimvye, cari gikomeye kandi cari gishoboka.



![Image](assets/fr/073.webp)



Mu bikorwa, nimba intumbero yawe ari ugukoresha gusa urudodo rwa Bitcoin ata bindi bikoresho, Raspberry Pi yoshobora kuba ihagije. Ariko iyo ushaka gukoresha Umbrel, Start9, canke ibidukikije bitunze kuruta (Block explorer, indexer ya Address, node y’umuravyo, porogarama zishobora kwakira...), iyo mashini irashika ku rugero rwayo ningoga.



Raspberry Pi ifise ingorane nyinshi:




- processeurs zito cane, zifise ubuhinga bwa ARM rimwe na rimwe budahuye na porogarama zimwe zimwe canke zisaba gukoreshwa cane;
- RAM ipfutse, idashoboka gusubirwamwo, ifise imiterere mike (kenshi igera kuri 8 GB);
- amasandugu yo hanze y’ama SSD ahujwe n’umugozi, amasoko akenshi y’ibikoko, bisaba kugura ikarita yihariye y’ama SSD ahamye;
- impengamiro yo gushuha ningoga be n’ingorane zo kumenya ko umuntu akonjera neza;
- ukeneye kugura ibindi bikoresho (igisandugu, umuyaga, ikarita SSD, n’ibindi);
- n’uguhuza bifise aho bigarukira cane.



Mu mateka, akamaro kanini k’ama SBC nka Raspberry Pi ni igiciro cayo: ku ma euros amajana makeyi, woshobora kuronka imashini yihariye. Ariko rero, uno musi, ibiciro vyaradutse cane, kandi iyo umaze kwongerako ibikoresho vyose vy’ingenzi vy’inyongera, igiciro kiriko cegera ic’ama PC mato mato x86 ya mbere yakoreshejwe canke yasanuwe, ayo, mu vyiyumviro vyanje, atanga inyungu nyinshi cane. Kubera iyo mpamvu, sinsaba ko umuntu ahitamwo SBC.



### Guhitamwo ibice



#### Ububiko bwa disiki: SSD ni ngombwa, 2 TB n'imiburiburi



Mu buryo bw’ubuhinga, birashoboka gukoresha node ya Bitcoin kuri HDD. Ikibazo ni uko vyose bizogenda buhoro cane cane IBD, izoca iba ndende cane kubera Bitcoin core ikoresha cane disk nk’ububiko (cane cane ku bijanye n’umugwi wa UTXO). Ni co gituma ngira inama cane yo kudakoresha HDD: irema icupa nyaryo, igabanura cane iterambere ryo muri kazoza (nk’akarorero, ku nzira y’umuravyo), kandi ishobora mbere gutuma ukudahuza kw’uguhuza n’umutwe wa Blockchain. Ikindi kandi, guhora umuntu ahagarika umutima ku disiki y’ubuhinga birongereza ingorane zo gusambuka imbere y’igihe.



SSDs zihindura cane ubumenyi bwawe bwo gukoresha: vyose bica vyihuta kandi bigenda neza, bikaba vyizigirwa cane. Gukoresha SSD rero ni (hafi) itegeko ku node yawe, kandi ntuzokwicuza, cane cane ko ibigereranyo vy’ubushobozi bwinshi ubu bishobora kugurwa.



![Image](assets/fr/077.webp)



Ku bijanye n’ubushobozi, 2TB iriko iragenda yishingira intahe buhoro buhoro nk’igipimo gishasha gishoboka. Mu ci ca 2025, Blockchain iramaze kwegera 700 GB, kandi iyo wongeyeko Umbrel, indexer ya Address, n’ibikoresho bikeyi, SSD ya 1 TB izokwihuta kwuzura. Iyo ufise 2TB, urafise urugero rwiza rw’imyaka izoza (mu kigereranyo cagutse, hagati y’imyaka 5 na 15). Ushobora kandi guhitamwo 4TB nimba utegura gukoresha porogaramu nyinshi kuri Umbrel, kubika amadosiye manini mu kwiyakira, canke nimba ushaka kwitega umwanya wawe wo kuri disiki ukeneye ku rugero runini.



![Image](assets/fr/078.webp)



Naho ku bijanye n’uburyo bwo gukora, ivyo bizovana n’ibibanza biri ku mashine yawe; ariko rero, nimba bishoboka, ndabagira inama yo gukoresha NVMe M.2 SSD.



#### Ububiko (RAM): 8 gushika kuri 16 GB



Ku Bitcoin core yonyene (ata Umbrel overlay), impanuro z’abahinguzi zirerekana ko 256 MB RAM n’imiterere ihinduwe ku mirongo yo hasi, 512 MB n’imiterere y’imbere, na 1 GB ku gukoresha mu buryo busanzwe.



Ku rundi ruhande, iyo ukoresha ubuhinga bwa node-in-a-box nka Umbrel canke Start9, ibisabwa kugira ngo ukoreshe RAM birarengeye cane. Abahinguzi ba Umbrel basaba ko RAM yoba ifise 4 GB. Ivyo bishobora kuba bihagije kugira ngo ukoreshe Core gusa, ariko uzoca ugira aho ugarukira vuba. Baca rero basaba 8 GB, ivyo nanje mbona ko ari vyo bikeyi ku bijanye n’imiterere y’ishimikiro ikikuje Bitcoin (Core, LND, indexer, n’ibikoresho bikeyi). Mu bumenyi mfise, n’Umbrel n’ibindi bikoresho bikeyi, 8 GB iracari n’agace gatoyi. Kugira ngo vy’ukuri umere neza kandi ugire margin, nogusaba 16 GB RAM.



#### Igikoresho (CPU)



Ku bijanye n’umurongo wa Umbrel, ikintu gitoyi gikenewe ni umurongo w’ivyuma bibiri w’ibice 64 uva kuri Intel canke AMD. Niba ushaka gukoresha ibikorwa bikeyi vyongeyeko Bitcoin core, quad-core (canke hejuru) izotuma habaho itandukaniro nyaryo mu bijanye n’uguhinduka kw’amazi. Nk’akarorero, amaprocesseur i5/i7 yo mu rwego rwa 6 gushika ku rwa 10 ni amahitamwo meza cane ku isoko ry’ibikoresho vyakoreshejwe.



### Ingero z'imiterere ya beton



Aha hepfo, ndatanga ivyiyumviro bitatu vy’ukuri, bihuye n’ingengo y’imari n’ivyo bikenewe bitandukanye, bifise ivyerekanwa vy’ukuri vyo kubishigikira. Ivyo bihitamwo bitanzwe nk’ingero kugira ngo vyerekane amakuru ari muri iki kigabane; ntabwo ubwirizwa guhitamwo neza neza ivyo bigereranyo. Nk’uko mbona ko Mini-PC ari yo nzira nziza mu gihe kirekire, nzoba ndiko nizigira iyi format ku bijanye n’imiterere itatu yashikirijwe.



*Ibiciro vyerekanwa aha hepfo ni ivyerekana gusa kandi bishobora guhinduka bivanye n'akarere, uwugurisha, n'igihe*



Mbere na mbere, ukeneye SSD nini bihagije kugira ngo ushobore kwakira Blockchain, mu gihe usigaye usiga umwanya wo gukora. SSDs zifise ubuzima buke mu bijanye n’ingendo zo kwandika be n’umubare wose w’amakuru yanditswe. Ariko rero, urudodo rwa Bitcoin rushira umuzigo munini kuri disiki igihe umuntu yandika. Ni co gituma ntasaba ivyitegererezo vy’urugero rw’injira; ahubwo, ndasaba NVMe SSD, itanga ubushobozi bwiza cane.



Nk’akarorero, ku bijanye n’intumbero z’iri shure, nahisemwo iki kigereranyo: *Samsung 990 EVO Plus NVMe M.2 SSD 2Tb*, kiboneka ku mahera ashika ku €120 kuri Amazon. Ushobora kandi guhitamwo izindi nzira zizwi cane nka Crucial, Western Digital, canke Kingston.

#### Imiterere y'iherezo ryo hejuru



Iyo umuntu afise ingengo y’imari nini, ubushobozi buraba bwinshi cane. Ushobora guhitamwo uburyo bwo gukora, canke mbere ugahitamwo imashini iteranijwe imbere y’igihe itangwa n’umugambi w’urudodo mu gasandugu.



Nk'akarorero, *ASUS NUC 14 Pro* iraboneka nshasha kuri Amazon ku €540. Kugiciro nk’ico, uronka processeur Intel Core Ultra 5 (iyiherutse kandi cane cane ikora neza cane), iherekejwe n’16 GB ya DDR5 RAM. Iyo ufise iyo configuration, uzoshobora kurangiza IBD mu mwanya mutoyi cane kandi ushiremwo porogarama zisaba cane ata ngorane.



Iyi ni configuration iryoshe cane, mbere n’ugurenga urugero nimba intumbero y’intango ari ugukoresha gusa urudodo rwa Bitcoin. Ku rundi ruhande, nimba ushaka kwungukira neza ku bikoresho vyose vy’ukwikira biri kuri Umbrel na Start9, urugero rw’ububasha ni rwo rubereye wewe.

Iyo ufise SSD ya 2TB, igiciro cose c’ivyo bikoresho ni €660, mu gihe iyo ufise SSD ya 4TB, ishika ku €800.



### Ibindi vyiyumviro bikeyi





- Niba wifuza kugura ibikoresho vya kera ukavyishyura mu bitcoins, n'uze mu nama iri hafi yawe! Mu kuganira n’abandi bari muri iyo nama, urazi neza ko uzoronka ibikoresho bikwiye ku giciro ciza, mu gihe ufasha kuguma ubutunzi buzunguruka bukikuje Bitcoin bukiriho. Ni akaryo kandi ko kwungukira ku mpanuro nziza zivuye mu kibano.





- Ku bijanye n’ugukoresha Internet, uzokenera umugozi wa Ethernet RJ45, n’imiburiburi ku bijanye no gushiramwo ubuhinga.





- Hari ibidukikije, nka Umbrel, rero biguha uburenganzira bwo gukoresha Wi-Fi, ariko muri rusangi ibikorwa bizoba bibi cane (na cane cane iyo ushaka gukoresha node yawe ya Lightning uri kure, kuko ivyo bishobora kugira ingaruka). Niwahitamwo Wi-Fi, urabe ko imashini yawe ifise ikarita yubatswemwo canke wongereko dongle ihuye.





- Igihe cose ukoreshe ububasha bw’umuhinguzi w’umwimerere Supply ku mashine yawe. Ibi ni ngirakamaro kugira wirinde kwonona ibikoresho vyawe no kwirinda ingorane zo gutera umuriro.





- Nimba imashini yawe idafise bateri yubatswemwo, ni vyiza ko ushiramwo inverter kugira ngo ntuzime bukwi na bukwi.





- Bivanye n’agaciro k’ibikoresho vyawe be n’aho uri, uburyo bwo guhagarika imiravyo na bwo nyene burashobora kuba bwiza, haba ku rubaho rwo guhindura umuriro canke ku nzira y’amashanyarazi ikoreshwa.





- Ubwa nyuma, wibuke gutuma imashini yawe ikonjera neza: uyisukure ubudasiba, hanyuma uyishire ahantu hakonje, harimwo umwuka mwiza, hatagira ibintu vyinshi kugira ngo wirinde gushuha cane, ivyo bikaba vyotuma igira throttling (uguhagarika umuvuduko w’ivyo ukora ku bushake).

Umbrel ni uburyo bwo gukoresha umukozi w'umuntu ku giti ciwe bugenewe gutuma umuntu ashobora kwiyakira: ushiramwo Umbrel, ugafungura umucukumbuzi kuri `umbrel.local`, maze ugacungera vyose biciye ku nzira yoroshe ya Interface.



Uwo mugambi wabanje gukwiragiza iciyumviro c'ugukanda rimwe gusa Bitcoin na Lightning node, hanyuma uragwira uba "igicu co mu nzu" nyaco: ububiko bw'amadosiye n'amafoto, gutangaza amakuru menshi, ibikoresho vy'urubuga, ubuhinga bwo mu nzu, AI yo mu karere, n'amajana y'amaporogarama ashobora gushirwako akoresheje App Store yunze ubumwe.

Intumbero ya Umbrel rero ni ukugusubiza ububasha n’ibanga ku makuru yawe, utizigiye ibikorwa vyo mu gicu, birenze gukoresha gusa urudodo rwa Bitcoin.

## Gushiramwo Full node n'umutaka

Ubu ko dufise amakuru yose akenewe, ni igihe co kwihweza neza ibintu vyose. Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwereka ingene woshiramwo node yuzuye ya Bitcoin ukoresheje UmbrelOS.



### Ibikoresho bisabwa



Aha, tuzoba turiko turakoresha ishusho ya UmbrelOS x86 (mu buryo bubereye, verisiyo ya x86_64). Uzoshobora gukurikira iyi nzira ku mashini iyo ari yo yose uhisemwo, igihe cose idafise ubuhinga bwo gukora ubuhinga bwa ARM (nta Apple Silicon, Raspberry Pi, n’ibindi). Ivyo bisigura ko mudasobwa iyo ari yo yose ifise processeur ya Intel canke AMD 64-bit izohagije, igihe cose izoba ihuye n’ibisabwa bikeyi, bivanye n’ingene wipfuza gukoresha Umbrel yawe (nibura processeur y’ibice bibiri ni vyo vyiza).



Niba warahisemwo Raspberry Pi 5 (ihitamwo ntagusaba, nk’uko vyavuzwe mu gice ca mbere), ugushiramwo biratandukanye gatoyi. Ushobora rero gukurikira iyi nyigisho yihariye maze ugasubira ku nyigisho yanje rimwe ku rubuga rwa Interface `http://umbrel.local`:



https://planb.network/tutorials/node/bitcoin/umbrel-8b0e3b5b-d3cf-4a1e-8bb8-1ad2db4dd848

Nk’uko nabibonye mu gice ca mbere, nahisemwo gukoresha iyi nyigisho kuri PC ntoyi yasanuwe nasanze kugiciro ciza: *Lenovo ThinkCentre M900 Tiny* ifise umurongo wa Intel Core i7 n’umurongo wa RAM wa 16 GB. Iyi ni uburyo bwiza cane bwo gukoresha Umbrel, cane cane ku nzira ya Bitcoin. Ariko rero, nahisemwo iyo nzira kubera ko nshaka gushiramwo urudodo rwa Lightning n’ibindi bikoresho bisaba cane mu nyuma. Nanje nyene nongeyeko SSD ya 2TB kuri ThinkCentre yanje kugira ngo ngume mfise Blockchain yuzuye kandi nkiriho mfise uruhande rwiza. Iyo ukoresheje iyo ntunganyo, igiciro cose ni €270, harimwo n’amahera yose.



![Image](assets/fr/001.webp)



Nkunda cane cane amashini ya Lenovo yitwa ThinkCentre Tiny, kuko ari imashini zikomeye, zitekanye, kandi zikomeye cane. Izo mudasobwa zirakundwa cane n’abacuruzi kandi rero ziragwiriye kw’isoko ry’ibikoresho vya kera, aho ushobora gusanga imiterere iryoshe hagati y’amayero 70 n’amayero 200.



Niba, nkanje, wahisemwo PC itagira moniteur, **uzokenera gufatanya moniteur na keyboard** gusa mu kiringo co gushiramwo. Inyuma y’aho, uzoshobora kuyironka uri kure uri ku yindi mudasobwa iri kuri iyo nzira nyene (canke ukoresheje ubundi buryo tuzovuga mu bigabane bizokurikira). Uzokenera kandi umugozi wa Ethernet RJ45 kugira ngo uhuze imashini yawe n’urubuga rwo mu karere, n’urufunguzo rwa USB rw’imiburiburi 4 GB kugira ngo ubike ishusho y’ugushiraho.



Mu gusubiramwo, ng’ibi ibikoresho bisabwa:




- Mudasobwa ifise umurongo wa x86_64 (mutoyi Dual-core, Quad-core iremezwa);
- RAM memory (4 GB n’imiburiburi, 8 GB ni vyo vyiza canke birenze kugira ngo ukoreshe igihe kirekire);
- SSD (ni vyiza + 2 TB);
- Urufunguzo rwa USB (+ 4 GB) rwo gushiramwo ishusho ya UmbrelOS;
- Monitor na keyboard (bifise akamaro gusa mu gushiramwo PC mu ntango iyo PC idafise);
- RJ45 Umugozi wa Ethernet.

### Intambwe ya 3: Gufungura mudasobwa ukoresheje urufunguzo rwa USB



Ubu ko USB stick yawe ishobora gufungurwa irimwo UmbrelOS iteguye, urashobora gufungura mudasobwa yawe kuri yo kugira ngo utangure gushiramwo system. Kura urufunguzo rwa USB kuri mudasobwa yawe nyamukuru maze urushire mu gikoresho wipfuza gushiramwo Umbrel n’urudodo rwawe rwa Bitcoin.



Nk’uko vyasiguwe mu ntango y’iki kigabane, kugira ngo urangize gushiramwo, uzokenera igikoresho co kwerekana n’igikoresho co kwinjiza. Huza ikigaragaza biciye kuri HDMI (canke ikindi kibanza, bivanye na PC yawe) hanyuma uhuze klavye biciye kuri USB ku mashine yawe. Ivyo bikoresho bisabwa gusa kugira ngo umuntu abishiremwo; ntuzobikenera inyuma, kuko Umbrel izoshobora gukoreshwa kure ukoresheje uwundi mudasobwa. Ivyo bikoresho bibiri ubihuze na PC yawe.



**Impanuro:** Niba udafise screen peripheral muhira, urashobora gukoresha TV yawe. Kubera ko ifise HDMI (canke iyindi) input, irashobora gukoreshwa nk’igicapo c’igihe gito igihe uriko urashiramwo ubuhinga bwo gukoresha.



Biragaragara ko Umbrel isaba ko umuntu afise Internet. Huza umugozi wa Ethernet RJ45 hagati y’igikoresho cawe na router yawe.



![Image](assets/fr/006.webp)



Guca imashini yawe. Akenshi, ikwiye kwibonera ubwayo urufunguzo rwa USB maze ikarufungura. Uzoca ubona igicapo co gushiramwo UmbrelOS Interface kigaragara.



Iyo igikoresho gifunguye ku yindi sisitemu canke kigaragaza ubutumwa bw’ikosa, ivyo birashoboka ko bisobanura ko kitariko kirafunguka ubwaco kivuye ku rufunguzo rwa USB. Muri ivyo, subiramwo maze winjire mu mirongo ya BIOS/UEFI (akenshi uyironka ukanda `DEL`, `F2`, `F12`, canke `ESC`, bivanye n’uwayikoze). Hanyuma, uhindure urutonde rw’ugutangura kugira ngo ushire imbere urufunguzo rwa USB. Hanyuma wongere utangure igikoresho kugira ngo utangure UmbrelOS.

Igihe gushiramwo, urudodo rwawe rwa Bitcoin ruzotanguza IBD yarwo (*Initial Block Download*): ruzokuraho no kwemeza amafaranga yose n'amabuye kuva Bitcoin yaremwa muri 2009.



![Image](assets/fr/024.webp)



Iyi ntambwe iratwara umwanya cane cane, kuko igihe imara kivana n’ibintu vyinshi, harimwo urugero rwa RAM igenewe ububiko bw’ibintu, umuvuduko wa disiki, umuvuduko wo gukoresha Internet be n’ububasha bwa processeur. Urutonde rw’igihe rero ni runini cane, bivanye n’ingene rutunganijwe. Iyo umuntu afise PC ikora neza cane (NVMe SSD, +32 GB RAM, processeur ikomeye, n’ubuhinga bwiza bwo gukoresha Internet), IBD ishobora kurangira mu masaha nk’icumi. Ku rundi ruhande, processeur ya kera, RAM ntoyi, canke, ikibi kuruta, disiki y’ubuhinga Hard (iyicitse intege cane) irashobora kwagura iyo nzira gushika ku ndwi nyinshi.



Iyo ufise PC ifise ubuhinga busanzwe (processeur ibereye, 8 gushika kuri 16 GB RAM, na SSD), ishobora gukora nk’imisi 2 gushika kuri 7.



Kugira ngo IBD yihutishe gatoyi, ushobora kwongera RAM igenewe ububiko bw'ibihimba (bukoreshwa cane cane ku bijanye n'umugwi wa UTXO, tuzosubira kuwubona mu nyuma mu nyigisho) biciye ku `dbcache` parameter. Ku Umbrel, iyo mpinduka ikorwa mu mirongo yawe, mu "*Gutunganya*" tab.



![Image](assets/fr/025.webp)

Ariko rero, sinshobora gusaba ko wosunika iyi parametere hejuru cane: mbere no kuyishira kuri 4 GiB bizotuma gusa uguhuza bigenda vyihuta nk’ibice 10%, kandi bishobora gutuma utakaza umwanya mu gihe habaye uguhagarara mu gihe ca IBD.



Urabe maso kugira ngo ntuhe agaciro kanini cane ku mashine yawe. Iyo RAM iriho kuri UmbrelOS iheze, node yawe ishobora guhagarara bukwi na bukwi, igahagarika IBD maze igasaba ko uyisubiramwo n’amaboko, bigatuma utakaza umwanya munini.

## Indexers: uruhara, igikorwa, n'imiti


<chapterId>4f93c07a-f0cb-435f-8b68-162f316d7039</chapterId>



Niba waramaze gutohoza ama node ya Bitcoin imbere yo gufata iri shure, ushobora kuba warahuye n'ijambo "indexer". Ivyo ni ibikoresho nka Electrs canke Fulcrum, bishobora kwongerwa ku nzira ya Bitcoin core. Ariko none, uruhara rwabo ni uruhe nyabuna? None mu bikorwa bakora gute? Kandi woba ukwiye gushiramwo imwe ku nzira yawe nshasha ya Bitcoin? Ivyo ni vyo tuzokwihweza muri iki kigabane.



### Index ni iki?



Mu kuvuga muri rusangi, indexer ni porogaramu isuzuma urutonde rw'amakuru atagira ishingiro, igakuramwo imfunguruzo zijanye n'ivyo (nk'amajambo, ibimenyetso, n'amaderesi), maze igakora dosiye y'inyongera, yitwa "index", aho urufunguzo rwose rwerekeza ku kibanza nyaco c'amakuru ari muri corpus. Ico gice c’imbere y’ugukora gikoresha umwanya wa CPU kandi gisaba umwanya wo kuri disiki, ariko kirakuraho ivy’ugukora igitabu cose igihe cose urutonde rw’amakuru rubajijwe; kubaza gusa urutonde rw’ibintu bituma umuntu aronka inyishu hafi ubwo nyene.



Mu majambo y’abanyabwenge, ni ingingo ngenderwako imwe n’urutonde rw’ibintu mu gitabu: iyo uriko urarondera amakuru yihariye, aho gusubira gusoma igitabu cose, uraba urutonde kugira ngo ubone ata guca ku ruhande urupapuro aho amakuru uriko urarondera aboneka.



Mu nzira ya Bitcoin, nka Bitcoin core, amakuru ya Blockchain abikwa mu buryo bwayo butagiramwo ivyiza, bujanye n’igihe. Buri bubiko burimwo ibikorwa, navyo bikaba birimwo ivyinjijwe n'ibisohoka, ata n'imwe ishirwa mu migwi na Address, ikimenyetso, canke Wallet. Iyi nteguro y'umurongo iratunganijwe neza kugira ngo yemezwe, ariko ntibereye ku bushakashatsi bushingiye ku ntumbero. Nk'akarorero, nimba ushaka kuronka amafaranga yose afitaniye isano na Address yihariye mu gice kitari mu rutonde, wobwirizwa gusubiramwo n'amaboko Blockchain yose, igice ku kindi n'amafaranga ku yindi. Aha niho nyene indexer iri ku node yawe ya Bitcoin yinjira.



![Image](assets/fr/085.webp)



Indexer ni porogarama yihariye isesangura ayo makuru menshi atagiramwo ivyatsi (Blockchain, Mempool, UTXO set) maze igakuraho imfunguruzo, nk’ibimenyetso vy’ibikorwa, amaderesi, n’uburebure bw’ibice. Ivuye kuri izo mfunguruzo, yubaka urutonde rwayo, igafatanya urufunguzo rumwe rumwe n’aho amakuru ari mu bubiko bw’urudodo.



![Image](assets/fr/086.webp)



Indexing iragufasha kurondera amakuru ku node yawe vyihuta, ataco bihinduye kandi neza. Nk’akarorero, iyo uhuje Wallet nka Sparrow ku node yawe, irashobora kwerekana uburinganire bwa Address hafi mu kanya nk’ako gukubita. Mu majambo nyayo, ibaza umukozi w'urutonde n'ikibazo nk'iki: "_Ni ibihe UTXO bifitaniye isano n'iyi nyandiko-Hash?_" Umukozi w'urutonde arishura hafi ubwo nyene, ataco akeneye gusubira gusoma Blockchain yose, kuko ayo makuru asanzwe ari mu rutonde rwiwe rw'amakuru.



### Mbega Bitcoin core irafise igiharuro?



Ata nkenegwa y’iyindi porogarama, Bitcoin core ntitanga, mu kuvuga ata gufyina, indexer yuzuye ya Address igereranywa n’iyo usanga muri porogarama nka Electrs canke Fulcrum. Naho biri ukwo, irashiramwo uburyo bwinshi bwo gukora urutonde rw’imbere, hamwe n’uburyo bwo kwagura ubushobozi bwayo bwo kubaza. Kugira ngo dutahure neza uko ibintu vyifashe, turakeneye gufata inzira y’uguca mu mateka y’uwo mugambi.



Kugeza kuri Bitcoin core verisiyo 0.8.0, kwemeza amafaranga vyari bishingiye ku rutonde rw'amafaranga kw'isi yose, ruzwi nka `txindex`. Ico kigereranyo cari kivuga ku bikorwa vyose vya Blockchain n’ivyo vyavamwo. Iyo node yaronse igikorwa gishasha, yaraba muri iyo index kugira ngo isuzume ko ibisohoka vyakoreshejwe (mu vyinjizwa) vyariho vy’ukuri kandi ko bitamaze gukoreshwa. `txindex` rero yari ngombwa cane kugira ngo amafaranga yemezwe ico gihe.



Ariko rero, iyo nzira yari ifise aho igarukira: yari igenda buhoro, itwara amahera menshi mu bijanye no kubika, kandi yari idakenewe cane mu bijanye n’amakuru. Kugira ngo ivyo bishoboke, verisiyo 0.8.0 irazana ihinduka ry’uburyo bwo kwemeza bwitwa ***Ultraprune***. Aho kubika vyose mu buryo bw'urutonde rw'ibikorwa, Bitcoin core ibungabunga urutonde rw'amakuru rworoshe rwerekeye gusa UTXOs, rwitwa `chainstate` (mu rurimi rwa misi yose, ivyo bizwi nka "UTXO set"), kandi igahindura urutonde rwayo uko ibisohoka bikoreshwa kandi bikaremwa.



Ubu buryo buranyaruka cane kandi bubika gusa uko igitabu kimeze ubu, bikaba bituma `txindex` indexer idakenewe. Ariko aho gukuraho kode ya `txindex`, abayiteguye bahisemwo kugumiza iyo mikorere inyuma y'umurongo woroshe (`txindex=1`). Mu gukoresha iyi nzira ku nzira yawe, ushobora kubaza ibikorwa vyose bivuye kuri `txid`.



Mu buryo butandukanye n’ivyo abantu benshi bemera, Bitcoin core ntitanga indexing ishingiye kuri Address nka Electrs canke Fulcrum. Hari impamvu nyinshi zituma umuntu ahitamwo:





- Uruhara rwa Bitcoin core si uguhinduka Block explorer yuzuye, canke gutanga API ihuye n’ikoreshwa ryose. Gushiramwo urutonde rushingiye kuri Address vyoba bisobanura ko Commitment ishobora gutunganirizwa igihe kirekire irenze urugero rwa mbere rwa porogarama.





- Ivyinshi mu bikoreshwa birashobora gukoreshwa mu bundi buryo. Nk'akarorero, kugira ngo ugereranye uburinganire bwa Address, urashobora gukoresha itegeko `scantxoutset`, risaba ataco rihinduye umugwi wa UTXO ataco usaba urutonde rwuzuye.





- Porogarama yose ya porogarama irafise ibisabwa vyihariye ku bijanye n’uburyo canke ubwoko bw’amakuru azoshirwa mu rutonde (Address, inyandiko ya Hash, ikimenyetso c’umunyagihugu, n’ibindi). Ni vyiza kandi bihuye n'ubwenge kureka izo porogaramu zikaba zikora index zazo kuruta gukosora umuti rusangi muri Bitcoin core.



Bitcoin core irafise urutonde rw'ibikorwa vy'ubudandaji (`txindex`), igisigazwa c'ibikorwa vyayo vya kera, ariko ntitanga urutonde rwa Address, canke Interface itaziguye ku gushakashaka gukomeye. Mu bihe bimwebimwe rero, vyoshobora kuba vyiza wongeyeko index yo hanze.



### Woba ukwiye kwongerako indexer ya Address ku node yawe?



Kwongera urutonde rwa Address, nka Electrs canke Fulcrum, si ngombwa; bivana n’ivyo ukeneye vyihariye.



Niba ushaka gusa gufatanya Wallet, nka Sparrow, n’urudodo rwawe kugira ngo ubone amafaranga n’amafaranga yo gutangaza, ivyo birashoboka vyose biciye ku Interface RPC ya Bitcoin core, haba mu karere canke kure biciye ku Tor.



Ku rundi ruhande, gukoresha porogarama ziteye imbere cane, nk'ugukoresha Mempool. Mu karere, gushiramwo indexer ya Address bica biba ivy'agaciro ku kirere Block explorer.



Indexer isaba umwanya kanaka wo gukorana (mutoyi kuruta IBD) kandi izofata umwanya w’inyongera kuri disiki. Niba SSD yawe igifise umwanya uhagije w’ubuntu umaze gukuraho Blockchain, urashobora kwongerako indexer bitagoranye.



### Ni indexer iyihe wohitamwo?



Porogarama zibiri zikoreshwa cane mu kwubaka ubwo bwoko bw’urutonde rwa Address no kurugira rushoboka: **Electrs** na **Fulcrum**. Ivyo bikoresho biratanga urutonde rwa Blockchain hakurikijwe inyandiko-Hash (amaderesi) hanyuma bikaba bitanga urutonde rwa Interface (itegeko rya Electrum), aho amasakoshi menshi, nka Electrum Wallet, Sparrow canke Phoenix, afatanya.



![Image](assets/fr/087.webp)



Mu mvugo yoroshe, Electrs ni nziza cane: ikora index Blockchain vyihuta kandi ifata umwanya muto kuri disk, ariko ikora neza gatoyi kuruta Fulcrum ku bibazo. Mu buryo bunyuranye, Fulcrum ifata umwanya munini kuri disk kandi ifata igihe kirekire kugira ngo ikore index, ariko itanga ubushobozi bwiza bwo kubaza.



Ku bijanye n’ugukoresha umuntu ku giti ciwe, ndabagira inama Electrs: ifata umwanya muto, iratunganijwe neza, kandi, naho igenda buhoro gatoyi ku bisabwa bimwebimwe kuruta Fulcrum, iracari ihagije cane ku gukoresha buri musi. Niba ufise umwanya n’umwanya kuri disk, urashobora kandi kugerageza Fulcrum, izokora neza cane cane ku ma wallets afise amaderesi menshi yo kugenzura.



Mu majambo nyayo, muri Myandagaro 2025, Electrs izosaba ububiko bugera kuri 56 GB, ugereranyije n’ububiko bwa Fulcrum bugera kuri 178 GB. Ihitamwo ryawe ry'urutonde, rero, rivana n'ubushobozi bwawe bwo kubika:




- Niba umwanya wawe kuri disk uri muto cane, uzobwirizwa gukoresha Bitcoin core ata indexer ya Address yo hanze.
- Niba ushaka gukoresha indexer, ariko ukaba ukiri n'ubushobozi, hitamwo Electrs.
- Niba ufise umwanya ubereye kuri disk, Fulcrum ishobora kuba ari co nyene uriko urarondera.



Mu gihe gisigaye c’iyi nyigisho ya BTC 202, nzoba ndiko nkoresha Electrs, ariko ushobora gukurikirana Fulcrum bitagoranye: uburyo bwo gushiramwo ni bumwe, nk’uko biri ku bijanye n’uguhuza Interface na Wallet, kuko vyose bigaragaza umukozi wa Electrum.



### Noshira gute index kuri Umbrel?



Kugira ngo ushire Electrs (canke Fulcrum) kuri Umbrel yawe, uburyo bwo kubikora buragoye: genda muri App Store, urondere porogarama ibereye (iri mu gice ca Bitcoin), hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Install*".



![Image](assets/fr/028.webp)



Igihe gushiramwo, Electrs izobandanya n’igice co gukorana (indexing), gishobora gutwara amasaha menshi.



![Image](assets/fr/029.webp)



Igihe uguhuza birangiye, urashobora gufatanya porogarama yawe ya Wallet na server yawe ya Electrum, ikaba iri kuri Umbrel.



## None nofatanya gute Wallet yanje n’uruzitiro rwanje rwa Bitcoin?


<chapterId>35519b1a-f681-4a69-a652-9fbe510cd17f</chapterId>



Ubu ko ufise node yuzuye ya Bitcoin, ni igihe co kuyikoresha neza! Mu gice gikurikira, tuzokwihweza ibindi bikoresho bishobora gukoreshwa ku nkuru yawe ya Umbrel. Ariko rero, reka dutangure n’ivy’ishimikiro: gufatanya porogarama yawe ya Wallet kugira ngo ukoreshe amakuru ava muri Blockchain yawe bwite no gukwiragiza amafaranga biciye ku nzira yawe bwite.



Nk’uko twabibonye haruguru, hariho uburyo bubiri nyamukuru bwo guhuza:




- Guhuza ata guca ku ruhande na Bitcoin core biciye ku RPC;
- Canke uhuze n’umurongo w’amashanyarazi (Electrs canke Fulcrum).



Muri iyi nyigisho, tuzokwibanda ku gufatanya n’uruzitiro rwawe biciye kuri Tor, kuko uwu ari umuti woroshe kandi utekanye ku batangura. Ndahanura cane ko utoshira ahabona icuma ca RPC c’uruzitiro rwawe mu buryo bugaragara, kuko guhindura nabi bigereranya ingorane zikomeye ku mutekano n’ibanga ry’amakuru yawe. Intambamyi nyamukuru y’ivy’itumanaho biciye kuri Tor ni ukuntu bigenda buhoro. Mu gice gikurikira, tuzoca irya n’ino uburyo bwihuta kandi butekanye bwo gukoresha Tor kugira ngo ushobore gushika kure ku nzira yawe: VPN.



Tuzokoresha Sparrow nk'akarorero muri iki gice, ariko uburyo bwo kubikora ni bumwe ku zindi porogarama zose zo gucunga Wallet zemera gukorana n'amaserveri ya Electrum. Gusa rondera ivyagezwe bihuye mu mirongo y'ikoreshwa ryawe (kenshi muri "*Server*", "*Irezo*", "*Node*"...).



Ku Sparrow, fungura urupapuro rwa "*Dosiye*" maze uje kuri "Ivyagezwe".



![Image](assets/fr/030.webp)



Hanyuma ukande kuri "*Server*" kugira ngo ushikire amaparametere y'ihuriro.



![Image](assets/fr/031.webp)



Uzoca ubona uburyo butatu bwo guhuza porogaramu yawe n'uruzitiro rwa Bitcoin:




- Server ya bose* (umuhondo): ku buryo busanzwe, iyo udafise urudodo rwa Bitcoin, iyi nzira iraguhuza n’urudodo rwa bose udafise (kenshi n’urw’ishirahamwe). Iyi nzira ntaco imaze hano, kuko ufise urudodo rwawe kuri Umbrel.
- Bitcoin core* (Green): iyi nzira ihuye n'uguhuza biciye kuri Interface RPC, ni ukuvuga, guca kuri Bitcoin core.
- Igikoresho c’amashanyarazi c’ibanga* (ubururu): iyi nzira ituma ushobora kwifatanya biciye ku gikorwa cawe c’amashanyarazi Interface (Electrs canke Fulcrum).



### Ihuzwa na Bitcoin core RPC



Niba node yawe ya Umbrel idafise indexer, iyi ni yo nzira ukeneye guhitamwo. Ku gitabu-444, kanda kuri "*igitabu-443*".



![Image](assets/fr/032.webp)



Uzoca ukenera kwinjiza amakuru menshi kugira ngo ushireho ihuriro n’urudodo rwawe. Aya makuru yose ashobora gushikirizwa kuri porogaramu ya "*Bitcoin Node*" kuri Umbrel ukanda kuri buto ya "*Connect*" iri hejuru iburyo bwa Interface.



![Image](assets/fr/033.webp)



Igipande ca "*Ibisobanuro vya RPC*" kigaragaza amakuru yose akenewe kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gukorana. Hitamwo kwifatanya na Tor Address (mu `.igitunguru`).



![Image](assets/fr/034.webp)



Injira ayo makuru mu bibanza bihuye kuri Sparrow wallet, hanyuma ukande kuri buto "*Test Connection*".



![Image](assets/fr/035.webp)



Iyo iyo nzira ishobora gukora neza, hazoboneka akamenyetso ka Green n’ubutumwa bwo kwemeza.



![Image](assets/fr/036.webp)



Ikimenyetso kiri hasi iburyo bwa Interface Sparrow wallet ubu kizoba ari Green (kigaragaza ko gifitaniye isano n’i Bitcoin core).



**Iciyumviro:** Kugira ngo iyo connection igende neza, node yawe itegerezwa kuba ihuye 100%. Niba atari uko biri, ndabinginze murindire gushika IBD irangiye.



### Huza n'amashanyarazi



Niba node yawe ifise indexer, ni vyiza ko uyifatanya na yo kuruta gukoresha Bitcoin core ataco uhinduye, kuko ibibazo vyawe bizokorwa ningoga.



Ku Sparrow, genda ku rubuga rwa "*Electrum y'ibanga*".



![Image](assets/fr/037.webp)



Uzoca ukenera kwinjiza amakuru menshi kugira ngo ushireho isano n'umurongo wawe. Uzosanga aya makuru mu gikorwa ca "*Electrs*" (canke, aho bishoboka, "*Fulcrum*") kuri Umbrel.



Hitamwo "*Tor*" kugira ngo ubone `.onion` ihuriro Address. Niba wipfuza gufatanya porogarama y’itelefone ngendanwa yitwa Wallet, urashobora kandi gucapura kode ya QR ataco uhinduye.



![Image](assets/fr/038.webp)



Niwinjize gusa Tor Address ya server yawe ya Electrum mu kibanza ca "*URL*", hanyuma ukande kuri buto ya "*Igerageza ry'Ihuza*".



![Image](assets/fr/039.webp)



Iyo ihuriro ryiza, ikimenyetso co gusuzuma n’ubutumwa bwo kwemeza bizokwerekanwa.



![Image](assets/fr/040.webp)



Ikimenyetso kiri mu mfuruka iburyo hasi ya Interface Sparrow wallet kizohinduka ubururu (ibara rijanye n’uguhuza na server ya Electrum).



**Iciyumviro:** Kugira ngo iyo nzira ikore, indexer yawe itegerezwa kuba ihuye 100%. Nimba ivyo bitabaye, rindira gushika igikorwa co gushiramwo urutonde kirangiye.

## Gutahura IBD n'uburyo bwo kuvumbura urunganwe


<chapterId>175ac9d1-ea23-45d9-9918-d3e7352435cd</chapterId>



Igikoresho cawe ca Bitcoin gitangura ata bumenyi ufise ku bijanye n’amateka y’ibikorwa. Mu ntango, ni porogarama gusa ikoresha mudasobwa (Bitcoin core canke isa n’iyo). Kugira ngo ibe urudodo rwa Bitcoin rukora neza kandi rukora, rutegerezwa gusubira kwubaka mu karere uko Ledger imeze mu kugenzura amabuye yose yasohowe kuva ku gice ca Genesis (igice ca 0, casohowe na Satoshi Nakamoto ku wa 3 Mukakaro 2009). Iyi ntambwe yitwa **IBD (_Igice ca mbere co gukuraho_)**.



IBD igizwe no gukura no kugenzura igice cose n’ibikorwa vyose ku giti cabo, hakoreshejwe amategeko y’uguhurizako, kugira ngo yubake verisiyo yayo bwite ya Blockchain. Intumbero si iyo kuronka kopi y’amakuru atagenzuwe gusa, ahubwo ni iyo gushika ku ciyumviro kimwe ataco yigenga, nk’uko benshi mu b’inzirabugunge bo muri iyo nzira babigira.



![Image](assets/fr/092.webp)



### Intumbero za IBD



Guhuza bitangura n'intambwe ya _**imitwe-ya mbere**_. Igikoresho cawe gisaba urutonde rw’imitwe y’ibice ku bagenzi benshi kandi, kuri umwe wese muri bo, kigenzura Proof of Work, uguhindura ingorane, insiguro, hamwe n’amategeko ya Timestamp n’inomero ya verisiyo. Muri make, biratuma umutwe wose waronswe uhuye n’amategeko y’uguhurizako.



![Image](assets/fr/093.webp)



Nk’ukwibutsa, igice ca Bitcoin gifise umutwe w’amabayiti 80 n’urutonde rw’ibikorwa. Urutoke rw’ibarabara ruronswa mu gukoresha SHA-256 Hash ibiri kuri uwo mutwe, urimwo ivyicaro 6:




- version
- Hash y'ibara ry'imbere
- Merkle Root y'ibikorwa
- Timestamp (irengeye igihe c’imbere c’amabarabara 11 yabanje)
- ingorane
- Nonce



![Image](assets/fr/094.webp)



Ibikorwa vy'ubudandaji bishikirizwa Merkle Tree. Iyi ni imiterere ivuga mu ncamake urutonde runini rw'amakuru (muri iki gihe, ibikorwa vyose biri mu gice) mu gukoranya ama hashes yabo buhoro buhoro abiri abiri gushika ku "muzi" umwe, gutyo bigaragaza ko ikintu ari ic'urutonde (kandi kigatahura ihinduka ryose). Muri ubwo buryo, uguhindura kwose kw’isoko na kwo nyene kurahindura umuzi wa Merkle Tree rero urutoke rw’umutwe w’ibarabara. SegWit yashizeho Commitment y’inyongera itandukanye y’ibifungurwa (imikono), ishizwe mu coinbase.



![Image](assets/fr/095.webp)



Iyi ntambwe _**headers-first**_ ishoboza node kumenya ishami rifise igikorwa kinini (ataco rifatiye ku mubare w'amabuye), ari ryo shami node za Bitcoin zikoranako. Iryo shami rihejeje kumenyekana, iyo node ikura ibirimwo mu bice bihuye bivuye mu mahuriro menshi, hanyuma ikemeza igikorwa cose: uburyo, ukuri kw’inyandiko (kiretse `assumevalid=1`), amafaranga, n’ukutagira amahera akoreshwa incuro zibiri. Igihe cose umuntu asuzuma neza, uko ibiceri bitakoreshejwe bimeze (UTXO set) birahindurwa mu rutonde rw'amakuru rwa `chainstate/`: ibisohoka vyakoreshejwe birakurwaho, mu gihe ibisohoka bishasha vy'ukuri vyongerwako.



Mempool, ku rundi ruhande, iza mu bikorwa gusa iyo yegereye isonga ry’uruzitiro: igihe cose urudodo ruguma rucerewe, nta bikorwa vy’ubudandaji bitegerejwe vyo kubika.



IBD imaze kurangira, iyo node yinjira mu gihe cayo gisanzwe: yemeza amabuye mashasha uko asohoka, ibungabunga Mempool yayo n’ibikorwa bitegerejwe hakurikijwe amategeko yayo y’ugutanga, igatanga ibikorwa n’amabuye, kandi igacungera ugusubira gutunganya uruhererekane kwose.



### Wiyumvire ko ari ukuri



Bitcoin core ishiramwo uburyo bugenewe kugabanya igihe gikenewe imbere y’uko urudodo rukora neza, mu gihe rugumya ingingo ngenderwako y’ukugenzura yigenga: AssumeValid.



Igiharuro ca `assumevalid` gishingiye ku gice c'ivya kera, Hash caco kikaba kiri muri verisiyo ya porogarama yose. Mu gihe ca IBD, iyo node yawe ibonye ko iyo block iri kw'ishami rifise ibikorwa vyinshi, irashobora kwirengagiza ukugenzura inyandiko ku bikorwa vyose vy'imbere y'iki gihe.



Ayandi mategeko yose (imiterere y’amabuye, Proof of Work, imipaka y’ubunini, amafaranga y’ibikorwa, UTXOs, n’ibindi) aguma agenzurwa neza. Guharura gusa inyandiko imbere y'iki gice c'ishingiro ni vyo vyirengagizwa. Inyungu y’ibikorwa ni nini kuri IBD, kuko ukugenzura umukono ari vyo bifata igice kinini c’umuzigo wa CPU. Inyuma y'iki gice c'ishingiro, igenzura risubira mu buryo busanzwe.



Ushobora guhatira kwemeza inyandiko zose mu guhagarika ubu buryo, ku giciro ca IBD ndende cane, ukoresheje `assumevalid=0` parameter muri dosiye `Bitcoin.conf`.



### TwiyumvireUTXO



`assumeutxo` ni ikindi gipimo kiriho, ariko bitandukanye na `assumevalid`, ntikikoreshwa ku buryo busanzwe. Ubwo buryo buratuma porogarama ishobora gushiramwo ifoto y’umugwi wa UTXO, hamwe n’amakuru yayo, maze ikayifata mu gihe gito nk’igihugu c’ishingiro, inyuma yo kugenzura ko imitwe vy’ukuri ijana kuri Blockchain ifise igikorwa kinini cane.



Iryo node rero rica ritangura gukora ningoga kugira ngo rikoreshwe mu buryo busanzwe (RPC, amasakoshi yo gufatanya, n’ibindi), mu gihe nyene ritangura gusubira kwubaka ryuzuye kandi ry’ukuri ry’iyi UTXO yaryo bwite yashizwe inyuma. Iyo ntambwe imaze kurangira, igishushanyo ca mbere gisubirizwa n’igihugu casubiwemwo mu karere. Ubu buryo butandukanya ugutanga node vyihuta n’ugusuzuma gushitse, ataco bihinduye ku vya nyuma.

Niba ukoresha umuti ushizwe mu bipapuro (nk'akarorero, mu gukwirakwiza kw'inzira), ubu bubiko bushobora gushirwa ahandi, ariko imiterere yabwo iguma ari imwe. Amadosiye n’amadosiye ahambaye adondora aha hepfo aracari hano.

### Amabuye



Blockchain rero ni igitabu c’amabuye. Full node ibika ayo mabuye nk’amadosiye akurikirana kandi ikabungabunga urutonde rumwe kugira ngo umuntu ashobore kuronka ningoga. Iyo bikenewe (gusubira gutunganya, gusubira gupima Wallet, gufasha abagenzi), ayo makuru arasubira gusomwa uko ari.



**Iciyumviro:** Gusubira gutunganya, canke gusubira gukorana, ni ikintu aho Blockchain ihindura imiterere yayo kubera ukubaho kw’ibice bihiganwa ku burebure bumwe. Ivyo bishika iyo igice ca Blockchain gisubiriwe n’urundi ruzitiro rufise igikorwa kinini cane cirundanirijwe. Ivyo gusubira gukorana ni igice gisanzwe c’ibikorwa vya Bitcoin, aho abacukuzi batandukanye bashobora kuronka amabuye mashasha hafi icarimwe, gutyo bagacapura uruja n’uruza rwa Bitcoin mu bice bibiri. Muri ivyo bihe, iyo nzira yoshobora guca mu minyororo ihiganwa mu gihe gito. Amaherezo, uko imwe muri iyo minyororo irundanya ibikorwa vyinshi, iyindi minyororo irahebwa n'ibihimba, maze amabuye yayo akamenyekana nk'"amabuye ataco amaze" canke "amabuye y'impfuvyi." Uwo murongo wo gusubirira urudodo rumwe uwundi witwa gusubira gukorana.



#### Blk*.dat dosiye (amakuru atagiramwo amakuru)



Ivyuma vyakiriwe n'ivyemejwe vyandikwa mu bikoresho bikurikirana vyitwa `blkNNNNN.dat`, bibitswe muri dosiye `blocks/`. Buri dosiye yuzuzwa mu buryo bukurikiranye gushika ishitse ku rugero rwo hejuru rwa 128 MiB, aho Core ifungura dosiye ikurikira. Imbere, buri bubiko burakurikirana mu buryo bw’urubuga, butangurirwa n’ikimenyetso c’ubupfumu n’uburebure. Iryo shirahamwe rituma umuntu ashobora kwandika vyihuta kuri disiki kandi rituma umuntu ashobora gukorana n’abandi.



![Image](assets/fr/099.webp)



Mu buryo bwa pruned, urudodo rugumya gusa idirisha rya vuba ry'ayo madosiye kugira ngo rugabanye ikirenge ca disiki. Ifuta ibikoresho vya kera cane `blk*.dat` igihe nyene intumbero y'ikibanza yatunganijwe ishitse, mu gihe igumya amateka ahagije kugira ngo igumye ihuye n'uruzitiro ruzwi cane. Index na UTXO set biguma ari ibisanzwe, bikaba bishoboza amafaranga akurikira n’amabuye bishobora kwemezwa.



#### Rev*.dat dosiye (amakuru yo gusubiramwo)



Kugira ngo ushobore gusubira inyuma mu gihe c'ugusubira gutunganya, Core ibika, bihuye na dosiye yose ya `blk`, dosiye ya `revNNNNN.dat` mu `blocks/`. Iyi dosiye irimwo amakuru akenewe kugira ngo umuntu akureho ingaruka z’ibarabara ku rutonde rwa UTXO: ku gisohoka cose gikoreshwa n’ibarabara, ivy’imbere y’ibarabara rya UTXO rihuye birabikwa (umubare, inyandiko, uburebure...). Iyo havuyeho inda, iyo node irashobora gusubira gukora nk’uko yari imbere ataco ikeneye gusubira gupima uruzitiro rwose.



![Image](assets/fr/100.webp)



#### Urutonde rw'ibice (ibice/urutonde)



Gushaka igice mu madosiye y’ibarabara vyoba ari ugutwara umwanya mwinshi cane. Core rero ibungabunga urutonde rw'amakuru rwa LevelDB muri `blocks/index/` rutanga urutonde, kuri buri bubiko buzwi, amakuru nk'aya Hash, uburebure, ikibanza co kwemeza, dosiye `blk`, n'aho iri. Iyo umugenzi asavye ububiko, canke igihe igice co mu mutima gikeneye gushika ku bubiko bumwe bumwe, uru rutonde rutanga uburyo bwo gushika ku bubiko bumwe bumwe. Iyo hataba iyo index, vyosaba ko umuntu akora ibikorwa vyinshi cane.



![Image](assets/fr/101.webp)



#### Urutonde rw'amahitamwo (urutonde/)



Index zimwe zimwe ni ubusabe kandi zizimye ku buryo busanzwe, kuko zongera ikirenge ca disiki:




- `indexes/txindex/`, twamaze kuvuga, itanga imbonerahamwe y'ikarita y'ibikorwa → ahantu, bikaba bishoboka ko umuntu ashobora kugarura ibikorwa vyose vyemejwe atazi igice kirimwo. Ivyo ni ngirakamaro ku bibazo vy'ubwoko bwa Wallet `getrawtransaction`, ariko birazimvye cane.
- urutonde/akayunguruzo k'amabuye/` gashobora kubamwo amayunguruzo y'amabuye (BIP157/158) ku bakiriya batobato. Ivyo bibanza vyihuta kugenzura ku ruhande rw'umukiriya kubera ububiko bwongerewe ku nzira y'urutonde.



### UTXO itegekanijwe (uruzitiro)



Icogereranyo ca UTXO (*Igisubizo c'Ibikorwa Bitakoreshejwe*) ni ikigereranyo c'ivy'ubuhinga bw'ibarabara ca Bitcoin: igisubizo cose kitakoreshejwe ni "Coin" iriho ishobora gukoreshwa nk'inyungu y'ibikorwa vyo muri kazoza.



![Image](assets/fr/102.webp)



Igitigiri cose c’ivyo bihimba vyose ku gihe kanaka T ni co gikora umugwi wa UTXO: urutonde runini rw’ibihimba vyose biriho ubu. Ni muri iyo leta iyo node igira inama kugira ngo ifate ingingo nimba igikorwa gikoresha amafaranga yemewe atakoreshejwe mu gicuruzwa ca kera (kugira ngo yirinde Double-spending).



![Image](assets/fr/103.webp)



Igitero ca UTXO kibikwa muri dosiye ya `chainstate/` nk'urutonde rw'amakuru rwa LevelDB. Igice cose gifatanya urufunguzo ruva kuri Hash y'ugucuruza n'urutonde rw'isohoka n'agaciro karimwo: umubare, urufunguzo rwa `scriptPubKey`, uburebure bw'igice c'irema, n'ikimenyetso c'ishimikiro ry'ibiceri.



![Image](assets/fr/104.webp)



Igikoresho kigumya ububiko bw'ubwenge hejuru ya LevelDB kugira ngo gifate ibikorwa vyo gusoma no kwandika kenshi. Igiharuro ca `dbcache` gishobora gukoreshwa mu guhindura ubunini bw'ubu bubiko: uko buba bunini, niko IBD ishobora gushika ku buhinga bwo kwibuka n'inyungu zo kwemeza ubu, ku giciro c'ugukoresha RAM nyinshi. Iyo igice gishasha kibonetse na Miner, iyo node irakuraho mu gice ca UTXO ibisohoka vyakoreshejwe (canke bikoreshwa) n’ibikorwa biri muri ico gice maze yongerako ibisohoka bishasha vyaremwe.



Mu vy’impwemu, twoshobora kwemeza igikorwa mu gusubira gupima amateka y’ibarabara kugira ngo tugenzure ko igisohoka kitigeze gikoreshwa. Ariko rero, mu vy’ukuri, ivyo vyobaye bitwara umwanya munini cane, kuko Blockchain yose yobaye itegerezwa gusuzumwa ku bijanye n’ugucuruza gushasha kwose. UTXO rero, itanga iciyumviro gitoyi gikenewe kugira ngo umuntu yerekane mu karere no mu kiringo gikwiriye ko Double-spending itahari.



Zirikana ko umugwi wa UTXO akenshi ari wo utera ivyiyumviro ku bijanye n’ugusenyuka kwa Bitcoin, kuko ubunini bwawo bugenda buragwira cane. Ivyo biva ku kwiyongera kw’igiciro ca Bitcoin, kigatuma ibice bica ibice, kandi biva ku gukura kw’ukwemera iyo nzira: uko abakoresha iyo nzira baba benshi, ni ko n’abashaka UTXO bagenda baragwira.



![Image](assets/fr/105.webp)



Ukwiyongera kw'umugwi wa UTXO na kwo nyene kuva ku miterere y'ibikorwa vy'ukwishura vyoroshe kuri Bitcoin. Nkako, iyo urishe, ukoresha UTXO imwe nk’injiza maze ugahingura UTXO nshasha 2 nk’isohoka (imwe y’ukwishura iyindi y’i Exchange). Ubwa nyuma, ubuhinga bwo gusesangura uruhererekane, bwitwa CIOH (*Ubuhinga bwo gusesangura ivyiyumviro rusangi Ownership*), buratanga ikindi kintu cotuma umuntu yirinda gufatanya Coin.



https://planb.network/courses/65c138b0-4161-4958-bbe3-c12916bc959c

Kubera ko igice caco kigomba kubikwa muri RAM kugira ngo umuntu asuzume amafaranga akoreshwa mu kiringo gikwiriye, iyo UTXO yoshobora buhorobuhoro gutuma igikorwa ca Full node gitwara amahera menshi cane. Kugira ngo iki kibazo gitorerwe umuti, hariho ivyiyumviro bikeyi bimaze kubaho, cane cane [Utreexo](https://planb.network/ibikoresho/urutonde rw’amajambo/utreexo).



### Mempool



Mempool ni urutonde rw’amafaranga y’aho hantu y’ukuri yashikirijwe ariko atararemezwa. Nk'ukwibutsa, "ugucuruza kwemejwe" ni ugushirwa mu gice gifise akamaro. Buri node igumya Mempool yayo, ishobora gutandukanywa n’iy’izindi node ziri mu rubuga bivanye n’:




- ubunini bugenewe Mempool biciye ku `maxmempool` parameter: urudodo rufise Mempool nini ruzoshobora gufata amafaranga menshi kurusha urudodo rufise Mempool nto (kiretse iyo ya nyuma iba ubusa);
- Amategeko ya gW-433: aya ni igice c’amategeko y’ugutanga amakuru y’urudodo kandi asobanura ibiranga igikorwa kitaremejwe gitegerezwa guhura kugira ngo cemerwe muri Mempool;
- transaction percolation: kubera ibintu bitandukanye, igikorwa kanaka gishobora kuba carashikirijwe igice kimwe c’urubuga, ariko ntikigeze ku kindi.



Ni ngombwa kumenya ko node mempools ata gaciro k’uguhurizako zifise. Bitcoin ikora neza cane naho node yose yoba ifise Mempool itandukanye. Mu mpera, amabuye y'ububasha yama ari ayo yongewe kuri Blockchain. Nk’akarorero, naho node yoba mu ntango yanse igikorwa kinaka muri Mempool yayo (ifise akamaro hakurikijwe amategeko y’ugusezerana), izobwirizwa kuyemera iyo amaherezo ishizwe mu gice gifise Proof of Work ifise akamaro. Iyo inanirwa kubigira, ikanka iyo block, naho yoba yubahiriza amategeko y’uguhurizako, yotuma haba Hard Fork, ni ukuvuga, kurema Bitcoin nshasha, itandukanye, iyo yoba iri yonyene.



#### Amategeko n'uburongozi bw'ukwibuka



Iyo igikorwa cakiriwe, Core ikoresha urutonde rw’igenzura ku mategeko y’uguhurizako (inyuguti, inyandiko zifise akamaro, nta gukoresha amahera kabiri, n’ibindi) n’amategeko ya Mempool, ari yo ngingo ngenderwako y’aho hantu (RBF, ibipimo vy’amahera makeyi, umupaka w’amakuru muri `OP_RETURN`, n’ibindi). Iyo iyo nzira yubahiriza ayo mategeko, irabikwa mu mutwe.



Ingano ya Mempool igabanywa n'umurongo wa `maxmempool` uri muri dosiye ya `Bitcoin.conf` (ibindi kuri ivyo mu kigabane gikurikira). Ku mburabuzi, umupaka ni 300 MB. Iyo yuzuye, iyo node iraduza umurongo wayo w'amahera makeyi maze igaca yirukana amafaranga make cane (ni ukuvuga, igumya amafaranga akwiye gucukurwa mbere). Ibikorwa vya kera cane navyo nyene birashobora guhera inyuma y’ugucerezwa kwatunganijwe.



#### Mempool gukomeza kuri disiki



Kugira ngo wihutishe gusubira gutangura, Core iguma ikurikiranya uko Mempool imeze muri dosiye `Mempool.dat` iyo node ifunze. Uretse Mempool nyayo, iguma mu ciyumviro, Core ibika iyi dosiye `Mempool.dat` kuri disiki. Igihe kizoza iyo node itanguye, isubira gushiramwo iyi foto maze igakuraho ikintu cose kitagikora kuri Blockchain iriho ubu.



### Dosiye z'inyongera n'ububiko bw'amakuru



Ayandi madosiye menshi ari ku rugero rumwe na `amabuye/`, `uruhererekane/`, na `indexes/` agira uruhara mu gukora neza kwa:




- `peers.dat` ibika igitabu ca IP Address c'abashobora kuba abagenzi, kigaburirwa n'ukuvumbura kwa DNS kwa mbere, uguhanahana amakuru, n'ivyongeweko n'amaboko. Iyo node itanguye, ishobora gukoresha kuri iyi dosiye kugira ngo ishingeho amahuzu asohoka.
- Iyo node izimye, `anchors.dat` ibika aderesi z'abagenzi basohotse, kugira ngo ushobore kugerageza kwongera guhamagara ningoga igihe uzotangura.
- `banlist.json` irimwo ibibujijwe vyo mu karere vyafashwe n'umukoresha canke n'urudodo (inyifato idakwiriye isubiramwo), kugira ngo urudodo ntirusubire gufatanya canke kwemera amahuzwa avuye kuri abo bagenzi.
- `fee_estimates.dat` ibika imibare y'igihe ku vyemezo vyerekanwa, ikoreshwa n'umugenzuzi w'amahera kugira ngo ashireho ibiciro vy'amahera bihuye n'intumbero z'ugucererwa zatowe igihe umuntu akora igikorwa.
- gW-446.conf` irimwo imiterere y'imiterere y'uruzitiro rwawe. Aho niho ushobora guhindura amategeko y’ugusubiramwo. Nzobabwira vyinshi kuri ivyo mu kigabane gikurikira.
- `imiterere.json` irimwo ibindi bipimo kuri `Bitcoin.conf`.
- `debug.log` ni igitabo c'inyandiko y'isuzuma, gishobora gukoreshwa mu gutahura igikorwa c'urudodo iyo habaye ikibazo.
- gW-448.pid` ibika ikimenyetso c'imigenderanire mu gihe co gukora, igatuma izindi porogaramu canke inyandiko zimenya bitcoind (*Bitcoin daemon*) mu buryo bworoshe kandi zigakorana na yo iyo bikenewe. Iremwa mu gutangura node igafutwa mu gufunga.
- `ip_asn.map` ni IP → ASN imeza y'ikarita (uburyo bwigenga) ikoreshwa mu gukora ububiko n'uguhinduranya urunganwe (`-asmap` uburyo).
- `onion_v3_private_key` ibika urufunguzo rw'ibanga rwa serivisi ya Tor v3 iyo uburyo bwa `-listenonion` bukoreshejwe, kugira ngo ugume ufise igitunguru gikomeye Address hagati y'ugusubira gutangura.
- `i2p_private_key` ibika urufunguzo rw'ibanga rwa I2P iyo `-i2psam=` ikoreshejwe, kugira ngo ukore amahuzwa asohoka n'ashobora kwinjira kuri I2P.
- `.cookie` irimwo ivyemezo vy'igihe gito RPC token (vyaremwe mu gutangura, bifutwa mu gihe co gufunga) iyo ivyemezo vy'ukuri vy'amakuru bikoreshwa. Ivyo birashobora gukoreshwa nk’akarorero, mu gufatanya porogarama ya Wallet.
- `.lock` ni urufunguzo rw'ububiko bw'amakuru, rubuza instance nyinshi kwandika kuri datadir imwe icarimwe.
- `imiterere.ini.bak` ni ububiko bwikora bw'imiterere ya GUI (*Bitcoin Qt*) iyo `-gusubiramwo imiterere` ikoreshwa.



Nk'uko twabibonye mu bice vya mbere vy'iri shure rya BTC 202, Bitcoin core ni porogarama ya Bitcoin na Wallet. Ariko rero, si ngombwa ko ari umuti nogusaba ku bijanye no gucunga amasakoshi yawe, kuko Interface yayo iguma ari iya mbere kandi ibikorwa vyayo birake ugereranyije na porogarama za none nka Sparrow canke Liana. Core kandi irimwo amadosiye yo gucunga amafaranga yawe:





- `wallets/` ni ububiko mburabuzi bwakira kimwe canke vyinshi;
- `amasakoshi/<izina>/Wallet.dat` ni urutonde rw'amakuru rwa SQLite rwa Wallet (imfunguruzo, ibisobanuro, amakuru y'ibikorwa, n'ibindi);
- amasakoshi/<izina>/Wallet.dat-ikinyamakuru` ni igitabo co gusubira inyuma ca SQLite.

### Inzira yo kwemeza ububiko bushasha



Iyo wakiriye ububiko bushasha, urudodo rwawe rurasuzuma Proof of Work kandi, muri rusangi, rwubahiriza amategeko y’ugusezerana. Iyo vyose bimeze neza, ikoresha amahinduka y’ugucuruza ku gucuruza ku rutonde rwayo rwa UTXO: igenzura ko ikintu cose cinjiye gikoresha UTXO zisanzweho n’inyandiko ibereye, kigafuta izo UTXO, kikongerako amasohoka mashasha. Niba vyose bifise akamaro, amahinduka ashirwa kuri `chainstate/`.



Mu buryo bumwe, amakuru yo gusubiramwo yandikwa kuri `rev*.dat` n'amakuru y'inyuma yandikwa kuri `amabuye/urutonde/` urutonde. Ico gice gica gikurikirana muri dosiye `blk*.dat` ibereye. Iyo habaye ugusubira gutunganya, iyo node isoma `rev*.dat` mu buryo buhindutse kugira ngo ikureho neza amabuye yahebwe, igarure umugwi wa UTXO, hanyuma ihuze amabuye y’uruzitiro rushasha rwiza kuruta izindi.





## Gutahura Bitcoin.conf


<chapterId>c54a629a-ddb1-41cb-9a88-21dfd9be50ca</chapterId>



Dosiye `Bitcoin.conf` ni dosiye nyamukuru y'imiterere ya Bitcoin core. Igufasha guhindura inyifato n'imirongo y'urudodo rwawe ataco ukeneye gusubira gukoranya kode y'inkomoko yayo canke guhindura umurongo w'amabwirizwa. Mu majambo nyayo, ni dosiye y'inyandiko rusangi itunganijwe mu migwi y'agaciro k'urufunguzo, bisobanura ko umurongo wose wa dosiye yerekana umurongo wihariye (urufunguzo) n'agaciro kawo, bishobora guhindurwa kugira ngo utunganye uwo murongo.



Urubuga, uguhanahana amakuru, ibikorwa, gushiramwo, gushiramwo, n'imirongo y'uburyo bwo gushika ku RPC bishobora gusobanurwa muri `Bitcoin.conf`. Ariko rero, iyi dosiye y’imiterere ntiyigera ihindura amategeko y’uguhurizako y’umurongo: ishiraho gusa politike y’aho hantu y’urudodo (amategeko yo gutanga), uburyo ihuza, igaragaza, n’ugushira ahabona ibikorwa.



### Aho hantu n'ivyo gushira imbere



Ku mburabuzi, `Bitcoin.conf` iba mu bubiko bw'amakuru bwa Bitcoin core. Iyi ni yo nzira izwi cane twavuze mu kigabane ca mbere. Ariko rero, iyo dosiye ntiyihuta kuremwa na Bitcoin core, kiretse mu bidukikije bimwebimwe, nka Umbrel. Niba itariho, uzobwirizwa kuyikora generate wewe nyene mu gukora dosiye yitwa `Bitcoin.conf`, hanyuma ukayifungura mu muhinduzi w'inyandiko kugira ngo ukore ivyo uhinduye.



Ivyagezwe vyasobanuwe muri `Bitcoin.conf` bishobora guhindurwa n'ibice 2:




- `imiterere.json` (yanditswe n'ibishushanyo vya Interface canke RPC),
- n'amahitamwo yahinduwe biciye ku mirongo y'amabwirizwa.



Zirikana ko uguhindura kwose kuri `Bitcoin.conf` bisaba gusubira gutangura kugira ngo bibeho.



### Uburyo n'imiterere



Uburyo bwa `Bitcoin.conf` rero burasanzwe cane: umurongo umwe ku mahitamwo, mu buryo `amahitamwo=agaciro`. Ivyicaro bidakenewe n'imirongo y'ubusa ntivyitabwaho, kandi ibivugwa muri kode bitangura na `#`.



Hafi amahitamwo yose ya Boolean ashobora gukurwaho n'intango `oya`. Nk'akarorero, `umviriza=0` na `nokumviriza=1` birangana bivanye n'ivyo bivugwa.



Kugira ngo ugabanye imiterere ku rubuga, ushobora gukoresha ibice: `[ikuru]`, `[igerageza]` (igerageza3), `[igerageza4]`, `[ikimenyetso]`, `[igerageza]`. Canke, ushobora gutangura izina ry'amahitamwo `regtest.maxmempool=100`.



### Ivyo Bitcoin.conf ishobora gukora n'ivyo idashobora gukora



Nk'uko vyasiguwe haruguru, amategeko y'uguhurizako ntashobora guhindurwa muri `Bitcoin.conf`, kuko ivyo bishobora gutuma haba Hard Fork. Ku rundi ruhande, hari n’ibindi bintu vyinshi bishobora guhindurwa. Hariho amashure 3 y’ingirakamaro umuntu akwiye kuguma yibuka:




- Ivyagezwe vyo mu karere gusa. Ivyo bigira ico bikoze ku nzira yawe gusa: ubunini bw'ububiko (`dbcache`), uburyo bwa pruned (`prune`), urutonde rw'amahitamwo... Bigira ico bikoze ku mikorere y'imashini yawe, ariko ntibigira ico bikoze ku rubuga.
- Ivy’ugutanga amakuru n’ivya Mempool. Ivyo bifata ingingo y’ico node yawe yemera, igumya, kandi itanga imbere y’uko yemezwa: igipimo c’amahera makeyi (`minrelaytxfee`), ubunini bwa Mempool n’igihe co kugumya (`maxmempool`, `mempoolexpiry`), gusubirira ibikorwa (RBF)... Aya mategeko ntashobora kuba atandukanye, n’ubu nyene ntabwo ari igice c’i bihuye. Ku rundi ruhande, ivyo bipimo bizogira ico bikoze ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin (nk’uko vyasiguwe mu gice ca mbere, cane cane n’inyigisho y’uguca mu mazi).
- Uguhuza urubuga. Aya mahitamwo agena ingene node yawe ironka abagenzi, yumviriza, ijabuka NAT, ikoresha Tor canke proxy, canke igabanya ubwaguke bwayo. Bihingura topologie yawe, ariko ntibihindura ugutanga amakuru.



Gutahura iyo ntandukaniro ni ikintu gihambaye cane: iyo igikorwa co gucuruza kidakurikiza amategeko y’uguhurizako, urudodo rwawe ruzovyanka uko vyogenda kwose. Ariko politike y’aho hantu ikomeye cane yoshobora kwanka gutanga amakuru y’ubudandaji afise akamaro mu buryo bw’uguhurizako.



### Urubuga n'ubuhinga



Mbere na mbere, birahambaye gutandukanya neza ubwoko 2 bw'ihuriro urudodo rwa Bitcoin rushobora kugira:




- Amahuza asohoka, atanguzwa n’urudodo rwacu ku rundi rudodo;



![Image](assets/fr/106.webp)





- Amahuza yinjira, atanguzwa n’iyindi node ku yacu.



![Image](assets/fr/107.webp)



Ubwo bwoko bubiri bw’uguhuza burashoboye neza guhanahana amakuru amwe mu nzira zompi; si ikibazo co guhagarika inzira y'uruja n'uruza, ahubwo ni itandukaniro gusa mu mutanguriro w'ihuriro. Dukurikije ukuntu urudodo rwacu rubona, amahuzu asohoka muri rusangi abonwa ko ari ay’umutekano kuruta, kuko ari twebwe tuyatanguza kandi tugahitamwo neza urudodo twohuzako, ivyo bikaba bituma bidashoboka ko iyo nzira yoba ari mbi. Ku mburabuzi, Bitcoin core igumya amahuzwa 10 asohoka (8 "*ivy'ugutanga-vyuzuye*" + 2 "*ivy'ugutanga-vy'ububiko-gusa*").



Full node yongera agaciro ku rubuga mu kwemera amahuzwa yinjira. `listen=1` parameter ishoboza kwumviriza ku nzira y'imbere 8333 y'urubuga ruvugwa, ishoboza ayo mahuriro yinjira kwakirwa kuri clearnet. Kugira ivyo bishoboke, iyo port na yo nyene itegerezwa kuba yuguruye kuri router yawe. Niba atarivyo, urudodo rwawe ruzobandanya gukorana n'amahuza asohoka gusa, ivyo ntibizogira ingaruka ku gukoresha kwawe bwite kwa Bitcoin. Ihitamwo ryo kwemera ama connexions yinjira ni ryawe; nta "mahitamwo meza" ariho.



Niba ushaka kudafungura icuma kuri router yawe, ariko ugaca wemera amahuzu yinjira, ushobora gukoresha `listenonion=1` parameter. Ivyo bizoshika ku ciyumviro nk’ico nyene, ariko biciye ku rubuga rwa Tor gusa aho gukoresha clearnet.



Ku rugero rw'urubuga, turafise kandi:




- `addnode`: yongerako umugenzi w'umugenzi wo guhamagara uretse ivyo usanzwe uvumbura (bishobora gusobanurwamwo incuro nyinshi).
- connect`: ibuza cane amahuzwa na Address yatanzwe (ishobora gusobanurwamwo incuro nyinshi). Core ntizokwifatanya n’iyindi node iyo ari yo yose.
- `seednode`: ikoreshwa gusa mu kwuzuza igitabu-Address iyo ihuye n'urudodo, hanyuma igaca.
- `maxconnections`: isobanura igipimo c'isi yose c'amahuza yinjira + asohoka. Ku mburabuzi, iyi parametere iteguwe kuri 125, bisobanura ko node yawe itazokwigera yemera amahuzwa arenga 125.
- maxuploadtarget`: ibipfukisho vy'ivyo gushiramwo kugira ngo bigabanye ubwaguke bw'umurongo ku idirisha ry'amasaha 24. Iyi nkweto ntituma ukwiragira kw'ingenzi kwa Elements.
- `onlynet`: igabanya amahuzwa asohoka ku mihora yatowe gusa (`ipv4`, `ipv6`, `igitunguru`, `i2p`, `cjdns`). Nk'akarorero, niwaba ushaka ko urudodo rwawe rufatanya n'urubuga rwa Bitcoin biciye kuri Tor gusa, urashobora gukoresha `onlynet=onion` parameter maze ugahagarika amahuriro yinjira (canke kwemera gusa amahuriro biciye kuri Tor na yo nyene).
- `dnsseed`: yemera canke ntiyemere _Imbuto za DNS_ gusaba bagenzi igihe ikigega ca Address co mu karere kawe kiri hasi (ivya kera: `1`, kiretse `-guhuza` canke `-maxconnections=0`).
- `forcednsseed`: ihatira _Imbuto za DNS_ gusaba mu gutangura, naho woba usanzwe ufise amaderesi mu bubiko (ivya kera: `0`).
- `imbuto zidahinduka`: Kwemera gukoresha *Imbuto za seed* (urutonde rwa Address rufise kode ikomeye) iyo _imbuto za DNS_ zidashoboye canke zidakora (ivya kera: `1`).
- `dns`: Yemerera inyishu za DNS muri rusangi (nk'akarorero, ku `-yongera`/`-imbuto`/`-guhuza`).



Ku mburabuzi, urudodo rwawe ruvugana kuri clearnet, Tor, na I2P. Ivyo bisigura ko abo mu runganwe rwayo bashobora kubona IP yawe ya bose Address, kandi ISP yawe izoshobora kumenya ko uriko ukoresha node ya Bitcoin (naho P2P Transport V2 ituma ISP igora cane gutega yompi). Ivyo si ngombwa ngo bibe ikibazo, ariko niwaba ushaka kwirinda gusohoka kw’ayo makuru, urashobora gufatanya node yawe gusa biciye ku rubuga rwa Tor.



Kugira ngo ushobore gukoresha Tor mu buryo bushitse, ukeneye guhatira Bitcoin core gukoresha uru rubuga gusa no gukora igikorwa cihishije c’amahuza yinjira (niba ushaka kuyakoresha). Mu `Bitcoin.conf`, ukeneye kwongerako iyi ntunganyo:




- `ku rubuga=igitunguru`,
- `uwuserukira=127.0.0.1:9050`,
- `ugutega yompi=1`,
- `ubugenzuzi=127.0.0.1:9051`,
- `ugutoranya=1`,
- `umviriza=1`,
- gufatanya=127.0.0.1`,
- `upnp=0`,
- `natpmp=0`.



Amahuza yawe yose ya P2P aca muri Tor. Node yawe ironka `.onion` Address ku mahuriro yinjira, rero nta ports zikeneye gufungurwa kuri router. ISP yawe ibona gusa uruja n'uruza rwa Tor, kandi abo mu runganwe rwawe ntibazi IP yawe ya bose Address.



Kugira ngo wirinde gutorera umuti DNS mu gusobanuka, ushobora kwongerako `dnsseed=0` na `dns=0` ku ntunganyo yawe. Uzoca ukenera gutanga n'amaboko `.onion` abagenzi biciye kuri `seednode=` canke `addnode=`, kuko kuvumbura amanode mashasha bizoba bigoye.



Biragaragara ko nimba uri umutanguzi, noguhanura ngo ureke ivyo vyose vy’urubuga ubikoreshe ubu. Ivyo utunganya mbere y’igihe akenshi birahagije.



### Mempool n'ingingo ngenderwako



#### Ibigenderwako vy'ishimikiro



Aha niho hari amaparametere y'ishimikiro ushobora guhindura kuri `Bitcoin.conf` yawe yerekeye uburongozi bwa Mempool yawe no gutanga amafaranga ataremejwe:





- `maxmempool=<n>`: Igabanya ubunini bwa Mempool bwo mu karere ku `<n>` megabaiti (ivya kera: `300`). Iyo umupaka ushitse, urudodo rwawe ruraduza cane umupaka warwo w’amahera kandi rugashira imbere ibikorwa bifise inyungu nkeyi (bishingiye ku rugero rw’amahera, atari ku gaciro nyako) kugira ngo bigume munsi y’umupaka. Ushobora gusiga iyi nzira nk'iyisanzwe. Kuvyongera birashobora kuba ngirakamaro nimba uri Mining wenyene, canke nimba ushaka kuronka iciyumviro kibereye ku bijanye n’uguhagarara kw’abantu mu Mempool no kunoza ingereranyo y’amahera. Ku rundi ruhande, kuyigabanya bizokuzigama RAM, ku rugero rutoyi, n’ibindi bikoresho vyo muri sisitemu.





- `mempoolexpiry=<n>`: Igihe co kubika amafaranga menshi ku bikorwa bitaremejwe muri Mempool (mu masaha, mburabuzi: `336`). Inyuma y’ico gihe, amafaranga akoreshwa arakurwaho naho hoba hariho umwanya.





- `persistmempool=1`: Ibika ifoto ya Mempool iyo ihagaze maze ikayisubiramwo iyo isubiye gutangura (ivya kera: `1`). Ivyo bica vyihuta gukira inyuma yo gusubira gufungura, bikaba bikirinda gusubira kwiga igihugu biciye ku rubuga.





- `maxorphantx=<n>`: Umubare munini w'ibikorwa vy'impfuvyi vyagumye (ivyinjijwe biva ku UTXOs bitaraboneka mu mugwi wa UTXO, mburabuzi: `100`). Inyuma y’ico kigero, amafaranga y’igihe ca kera cane arafutwa kugira ngo ntihagire ukura kw’ububiko kutagenzurwa.





- blocksonly=1`: Ihagarika kwemera no gusubira gutanga amafaranga ataremejwe yaronse ku bagenzi (kiretse iyo hatanzwe uruhusha rudasanzwe). Node ubu ishira gusa kandi igatangaza amabuye. Ibikorwa vyakozwe mu karere birashobora gutangazwa (kugira ngo ukoreshe urudodo rwawe na porogarama yawe ya Wallet). Ivyo bigabanya cane bandwidth na RAM bisabwa, naho nyene bishobora kugabanya akamaro k'ivyo bikoresho no kutamenya neza Mempool.





- `minrelaytxfee=<n>`: Igiciro gitoyi c'amahera (mu BTC/kvB) munsi y'aho amafaranga atemerwa muri Mempool y'uruzitiro kandi ntashikirizwa abo mu runganwe (ivya kera: `0.00001` = 1 sat/vB). Uko ako gaciro kagenda karakura, niko node yawe icungera cane amafaranga make.





- `mempoolfullrbf=1`: Kwemera ibikorwa vya RBF mbere ata kimenyetso ca RBF gitomoye mu bikorwa vyasubirijwe. Hamwe n'iyi politike ya "*full-RBF*", igikorwa gitanga igiciro kinini c'amahera gishobora gusubirira ikindi muri Mempool iyo ibindi bisabwa vyo gusubirira bishitse.



Nk’ukwibutsa, RBF ni uburyo bwo gukorana n’abandi bufasha uwurungitse gusubirira ubucuruzi n’ubundi bufise amahera menshi kugira ngo umuntu yihute kwemeza. Iyo igikorwa gifise amafaranga make cane kigumye kibujijwe, uwugirungitse arashobora gukoresha *Replace-by-fee* kugira ngo yongere amafaranga yongere ashire imbere igikorwa cabo co gusubirira muri mempools no mu bacukuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro.



#### Amagenamiterere ateye imbere kandi yihariye



Aha niho hari amasetingi ateye imbere ya Mempool n’ingingo ngenderwako y’ugutanga amakuru. Niwaba uri umutanguzi, ntukwiye guhindura aya magenamiterere:





- datacarrier=1`: Yemerera gutanga no (niba Mining biciye ku nzira) gushiramwo ibikorwa bitwara amakuru atari ay'ivy'amahera biciye ku gisohoka ca `OP_RETURN` (ivya kera: `1`). Guhagarika iyi parametere bigabanya gatoyi uburebure bw'amakuru atari ay'ivy'amahera, ku giciro co kugabanya ukuntu bihuye n'ikoreshwa rimwe rimwe. Mu bihe vyose, utegerezwa kwemera `OP_RETURN`.





- `ubunini bw'umutwazi w'amakuru=<n>`: Ubunini burengeye (mu bytes) bwa `OP_RETURN` iyo node irungika (imbere: `83`). Kugabanya aka gaciro bigabanya imizigo itwarwa biciye ku `OP_RETURN`. Iyumvire ko iyo nzira izokurwaho ku buryo busanzwe muri verisiyo izoza ya Bitcoin core.





- `bytespersigop=<n>`: Igiharuro gihindura ibikorwa vy'umukono mu bice bingana vy'isuzuma ry'imipaka y'ivy'ugutanga (ivya kera: `20`). Ivyo bizogira ico bikoze ku kwemera amafaranga y’ubutunzi bwa `sigops` hakurikijwe amategeko y’intara.





- `ukwemerera vyinshi = 1`: Kwemerera gutanga amakuru *bare-Multisig* P2MS (ivya kera: `1`). Iyi ni yo nzira ya kera cane yo gushinga ivyangombwa vy’imikono myinshi ku UTXO (yavumbuwe mu 2011 na Gavin Andresen).





- `whitelistrelay=1`: Itanga uburenganzira bwo gutanga uruhusha ku bandi bari ku rutonde rweranda (ivya kera: `1`). Abo bagenzi bafise amafaranga yabo yemerwa n’ivyo bikoresho naho node yawe yoba itari mu buryo rusangi.





- `whitelistforcerelay=1`: Itanga uruhusha rwa "*forcerelay*" ku bandi bari ku rutonde rwerurutse bafise uruhusha mburabuzi (mburabuzi: `0`). Ico gice gica kirungika amafaranga yabo naho yoba asanzwe ari muri Mempool, gutyo kikarenga uburyo bwo kurwanya ugusubira inyuma.





- `whitebind=<[uburenganzira@]addr>` / `urutonde rwera=<[uburenganzira@]CIDR>`: Iboha urutonde rwa Interface canke Address maze igatanga uburenganzira bw'intete nziza ku bagenzi bihuye: `ugusubiza`, `ugusubiza inguvu`, `G5-1 `noban`, `gukuraho`, `aderesi`, `akayunguruzo k'amashurwe`. Ivyo birashobora kuba ngirakamaro mu gutanga ubuvuzi bw’agaciro ku bagenzi bizigira (nk’amarembo, ama LAN, n’ibikorwa vyo mu mutima).





- peerbloomfilters=1`: Gushoboza gufasha amayunguruzo ya Bloom (BIP37) kugira ngo akoreshe amabuye/amafaranga yayunguruwe ku bakiriya batobato (ivya kera: `0`). Imburi: ivyo birongereza umuzigo ku butunzi bwawe.





- 1`: Ikoresha BIP157 (*Neutrino*) iyunguruzo ry'urunganwe (mburabuzi: `0`).





- `blockreconstructionextratxn=<n>`: Umubare w'ibikorwa vy'inyongera bigumye mu bubiko kugira ngo wongere wubake amabuye akomeye (ivya kera: `100`). Itera imbere mu kuroranirwa kw’ibikorwa vyo gusubira kwubaka mu gihe c’uguhuza ibintu bikomeye, ku giciro c’ukwibuka guke.



Nk’ukwibutsa, ayo mategeko yose y’ugutanga amakuru ntaco akora ku vyerekeye ukuri kw’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji biri mu gice gifise agaciro. Bifasha guhindura umusanzu wawe ku bijanye n’ugutanga, kurinda ubutunzi bwawe, no gutuma urudodo rwawe rushobora gutegekanirwa mu bidukikije bigoranye, ariko ntivyigera bikwemerera kwanka amabuye yubaha amategeko y’ugusezerana.



### Amasakoshi



Ushobora kandi guhindura uburyo amasakoshi yawe acungirwa muri dosiye `Bitcoin.conf`. Niba udakoresha Wallet mu Core, ahubwo ukoresha porogaramu zo hanze nka Sparrow canke Liana, ibi bipimo ntibizoba bifise akamaro kanini:





- aderesi=<iragi|P2SH-SegWit|bech32|bech32m>`: Isobanura uburyo bw'amaderesi ya Wallet yo kwakira.





- `uguhindura ubwoko=<iragi|P2SH-SegWit|bech32|bech32m>`: Guhatira Exchange Address uburyo (igisigaye c'inyungu ku kwishura kumwe).





- `Wallet=<inzira>`: Ishiramwo Wallet iriho mu gutangura (ishobora gusubirwamwo kugira ngo ishiremwo amasakoshi menshi).





- `walletdir=<dir>`: Ububiko burimwo amasakoshi (ivya kera: `<amakuru>/amasakoshi` nimba ariho, ahandi ho `<amakuru>`). Ivyo birashobora kuba ngirakamaro nimba wipfuza kubika ama wallet ku gitabu cihariye canke gipfutse.





- `walletbroadcast=1`: Itangaza ubwayo ibikorwa vyakozwe n'amasakoshi yuzuye (ivya kera: `1`). Tegura kuri `0` niba wipfuza gucunga gutangaza biciye ku yindi nzira.





- `walletrbf=1`: Bishoboza RBF guhitamwo kwinjira kugira ngo yerekane RBF ku bikorwa vyose (ivya kera: `1`). Iguha uburenganzira bwo kwongera amafaranga mu nyuma iyo habayeho ugucuruza kwabujijwe.





- `txconfirmtarget=<n>`: Intumbero y'ukwemeza y'ibikorwa (mu mubare w'ibice, mburabuzi: `6`). Wallet izoca ihita ishinga amahera y’ugucuruza azokwemezwa muri uwo mubare w’amabuye.





- `payxfee=<amt>`: Igiciro c'amahera adahinduka (BTC/kvB) gikoreshwa ku bikorwa vy'ubudandaji vya Wallet. Irinde muri rusangi: koresha ingereranyo ihuye biciye ku `txconfirmtarget`.





- fallbackfee=<amt>`: Igipimo co gusubira inyuma (BTC/kvB) gikoreshwa iyo umugereranyo arangije amakuru (ivya kera: `0.00`). Kuyishira kuri 0 bizibira burundu gusubira inyuma.





- `mintxfee=<amt>`: Igitigiri gitoyi (BTC/kvB) ca Wallet kugira ngo ureme amafaranga (ivya kera: `0.00001`). Wallet izokwanka kwubaka ubucuruzi buri munsi y’iyi nzira.





- `maxtxfee=<amt>`: Igitigiri c'amafaranga yose y'ugucuruza Wallet (ivya kera: `0.10` BTC). Ikingira amafaranga menshi cane adasanzwe yoshobora gusenyura bitcoins ataco bimaze.





- `kwirinda gukoresha amafaranga y'igice=1`: Ihitamwo UTXOs n'amatsinda ya Address kugira ngo wirinde gukoresha amafaranga y'igice.





- `spendzeroconfchange=1`: Yemerera UTXO Exchange itaremejwe gusubira gukoreshwa nk'inyandiko mu gucuruza gushasha (ivya kera: `1`).





- `consolidatefeerate=<amt>`: Igipimo gisumba ibindi (BTC/kvB) kirenze Wallet irinda kwongerako ibintu vyinshi kuruta ivyo bikenewe kugira ngo ihurize hamwe. Ivyo bituma habaho ugushira hamwe kw’amahirwe ku bici- ro bito kandi bikagabanya ibiciro iyo ibiciro ari vyinshi.





- `maxapsfee=<n>`: Ingingo y'imari y'inyongera (BTC, agaciro nyako) Wallet yemera kwishura kugira ngo ikoreshe uburyo bwa "*kwirinda gukoresha igice*".





- `discardfee=<amt>`: Igiciro (BTC/kvB) kigaragaza ukwihangana kwawe guta Exchange mu kuyishira ku giciro. Ivyiza vyoshobora gutwara amahera arenga ica gatatu c’agaciro kavyo kuri iki kigero biragabanywa.





- `ikigega=<n>`: Ubwinshi bw'ikigega ca Address cakozwe mbere (ivya kera: `1000`). Amafaranga ari make cane yongera ingorane zo gusubizaho ibintu bidatunganye.





- `disablewallet=1`: Itangura Bitcoin core ata n'uburyo buto bwa Wallet kandi ihagarika RPC zijanye n'ivyo. Igabanya uburebure bw'igitero n'ikirenge iyo urudodo rukoreshwa gusa mu kwemeza/kurekura.



### Ububiko, urutonde, n'imikorere



Dosiye y’imiterere iragufasha kandi guhindura amaparametere ajanye n’imashini yawe. Ivyo birashobora kuba vyiza canecane nimba ufise uburyo buke, canke, ku rundi ruhande, ubushobozi bwinshi buboneka:





- `amakuru=<amakuru>`: Ishiraho ububiko bw'amakuru nyamukuru bwa Bitcoin core.





- `blocksdir=<dir>`: Itandukanya ahantu amadosiye y'amabuye (`amabuye/blk*.dat` na `amabuye/rev*.dat`) kuva kuri `datadir`. Ivyo bishobora kuba ngirakamaro mu gushiramwo ububiko bw'amabuye ku rugero rutandukanye, mu gihe ugumya ishingiro rya Leta (`chainstate/`) ku buryo bwihuta, nk'akarorero.





- `dbcache=<n>`: Itanga `<n>` MiB ku bubiko bw'amakuru (*LevelDB*) bukoreshwa n'urutonde rw'ibice na `uruzitiro` (ivya kera: `450`). Uko agaciro kagenda karakura, niko IBD n’ugushingira intahe ubu bigenda vyihuta, ku giciro c’ugukoresha RAM nyinshi.





- `prune=<n>`: Bishoboza gukata amadosiye y'amabuye no gushinga intego y'umwanya wa disiki muri MiB (ivya kera: `0` = bibujijwe; `1` = gukata n'amaboko biciye kuri RPC; `>=550` = gukata ubwavyo munsi y'intumbero). Ntibihuye na `txindex=1`. Igikoresho kiguma ari igikoresho cemeza neza, ariko ntigishobora gutanga amateka ya kera. Iryo hitamwo ni ngirakamaro canecane iyo umwanya wawe wo kuri disiki ari muto, nk’akarorero, igihe ushizeho urudodo kuri mudasobwa yawe yo muhira.





- txindex=1`: Yubaka kandi ibungabunga urutonde rw'isi yose rw'ibikorwa vyemejwe. Ni ngombwa ku bibazo bimwe bimwe (`getrawtransaction` bitari Wallet) no ku ntumbero zo gutohoza, ariko vyongera cane ikirenge ca disiki. Ntibihuye n’uburyo bwa pruned.





- `assumevalid=<hex>`: Yerekana igice ciyumvirwa ko gifise akamaro, kiguha uburenganzira bwo gusimbuka igenzura ry'inyandiko ku ba sekuruza bayo (shinga `0` kugira ngo usuzume vyose). Raba ikigabane c’imbere kugira ngo umenye vyinshi.





- `reindex=1`: Isubira kwubaka urutonde rw'ibice n'imiterere (`uruhererekane`) kuva ku madosiye ya `blk*.dat` kuri disiki. Kandi isubira kwubaka index zikora zidasanzwe. Ivyo ni igikorwa gitwara umwanya co gukoresha mu gusanura urutonde rw'amakuru rwononekaye canke gukoresha/guhagarika neza index ziremereye.





- `reindex-chainstate=1`: Isubira kwubaka gusa `uruzitiro` kuva ku rutonde rw'ubu. Bikundwa iyo amadosiye y'ububiko ari meza.





- `blockfilterindex=<ubwoko>`: Ibungabunga urutonde rw'ibiyunguruzo vy'amabuye (nk'akarorero, `ivy'ishimikiro`) bikoreshwa n'abaguzi batobato (BIP157/158) na RPC zimwe zimwe. Izimye ku mburabuzi (`0`). Ifata umwanya wongerewe kuri disiki n'igihe co gukora index.





- `coinstatsindex=1`: Ibungabunga urutonde rw'imibare rwa UTXO rukoreshwa n'uguhamagara. Ni ngirakamaro ku bijanye n’igenzura n’ibipimo, bikakuraho ivy’ugusubira kubara bizimvye. Izimye ku mburabuzi.





- `loadblock=<dosiye>`: Izana amabuye mu gutangura kuva hanze `blk*.dat` dosiye. Ikoreshwa mu gushiramwo amateka avuye ku nzira itari mu nzira (kopi yo mu karere, ibinyamakuru vyo hanze) kugira ngo wihutishe gutangura.





- `par=<n>`: Ishinga umubare w'indodo zo kugenzura inyandiko (kuva kuri `-10` gushika kuri `15`, `0` = auto, `<0` = isiga uwo mubare w'imirongo y'ubuntu). Iguha uburenganzira bwo guhindura uburinganire bwa CPU mu gihe co kwemeza. Uburyo bw’ivyuma burabereye mu bihe vyinshi.





- `dosiye y'inyandiko=<dosiye>`: Igaragaza aho `inyandiko y'inyandiko` iri.





- `shrinkdebugfile=1`: Igabanya ubunini bwa `debug.log` mu gutangura (ivya kera: `1` iyo `-debug` idakora).





- `imiterere=<dosiye>`: Inzira ija kuri dosiye y'imiterere ihinduka `imiterere.json`.



### RPC gushika ku mutekano wo gukoresha



Ubwa nyuma, dosiye `Bitcoin.conf` nayo iraguha uburenganzira bwo gutunganya imirongo y'ukwinjira ku nzira yawe. Ube maso ku bijanye n’ivyo bintu, cane cane iyo uriko uratangura: wirinde kubihindura utatahuye neza ingaruka zavyo, kuko ivyo bishobora gutuma habaho ubugoyagoye.





- `umukozi=1`: Ikoresha umukozi wa JSON-RPC. Ni ngombwa nimba uriko uratwara `bitcoind` uciye kuri `bitcoin-cli` canke ubuhinga bw'uwundi muntu. Guhagarika (`0`) ku nzira y'ukwemeza gusa idashira ahabona API iyo ari yo yose, canke ikoresha umukozi wa Electrum.





- `rpcbind=<adr>[:icuma]`: RPC umukozi yumviriza Address/icuma. Ku mburabuzi, kwumviriza bikorwa mu karere gusa (`127.0.0.1` na `::1`). Iyi parametere irarengagizwa iyo `rpcallowip` nayo itasobanuwe. Uyikoreshe kugira ngo uhagarike Interface.





- `rpcport=<icuma>`: Icuma ca RPC (ivya kera: `8332` kuri Mainnet, `18332` kuri Testnet, `38332` ku kimenyetso c'igitabu, `18443` ku kigeragezo).





- `rpcallowip=<ip|cidr>`: Yemerera abaguzi ba RPC bavuye kuri IP canke urubuga ruto (rushobora gusubirwamwo). Koresha hamwe na `rpcbind` kugira ngo ugaragaze API gusa ku gice cizigiwe (LAN/VPN).





- `rpcauth=<IZINA RY'UMUKORESHA>:<UMUNYU>$<Hash>`: Uburyo bwo kwemeza RPC bushimikijwe (ijambobanga ry'ibanga). Kwemera kwinjira kenshi kandi kirinda kubika ibanga mu nyandiko igaragara.





- `rpccookiefile=<inzira>`: Inzira ija ku nzira y'ukwemeza (mburabuzi: `.idosiye` munsi ya `datadir/`). Ivyo bikoreshwa mu gushika ku vyo mu karere n'ukoresha umwe ata gucunga amajambobanga agumaho. Nk’akarorero, urashobora kuyikoresha kugira ngo uhuze Liana Wallet na Bitcoin core yawe iri ku mashini imwe.





- `rpcuser=<ukoresha>` / `ijambobanga rpc=<pw>`: Ivyemezo vya kera vya RPC n'ijambobanga ry'inyandiko rusangi. Irinde gukoresha ibi mu gufasha `rpcauth` canke amakuru.





- `rpcthreads=<n>`: Umubare w'indodo zo gukoresha amahamagara ya RPC (ivya kera: `4`). Wongereko nimba ufise amasonga menshi y’uguhamagara ku ruhande rw’ibikoresho vyo kugenzura/vyo hanze.





- `rpcurutonde rwera=<IZINA RY'UMUKORESHA>:<rpc1>,<rpc2>,...`: Urutonde rwera rwa APIs zemerewe. Igabanya uburebure bw’igitero mu kugabanya uburyo bwo gushikira.





- `rpcwhitelistdefault=1|0`: Inyifato y'urutonde rwera: iyo ishobojwe kandi urutonde rwera rukoreshwa, amahamagara atari ku rutonde aranka. Ivyo bishobora kandi guhatira urutonde rw'ubusa (nta guhamagara kwemerewe) igihe cose ata kintu na kimwe kiri ku rutonde.





- `rest=1`: Gushoboza REST API ya bose (ibujijwe ku mburabuzi). Kugira ngo bigaragazwe gusa ku rubuga rwizewe (ukwiyubara nk'uko biri kuri JSON-RPC).





- `conf=<dosiye>`: Igaragaza, ku murongo w'itegeko gusa, dosiye y'imiterere isomwa gusa. Ni ngirakamaro mu gukonja urutonde rw'ibikorwa (rudahinduka) ku ruhande rw'ibikorwa.





- `includeconf=<dosiye>`: Ishiramwo dosiye y'inyongezo y'imiterere (inzira ijyanye na `datadir/`). Kwemerera gutandukanya uruhara: ishingiro rusangi + umuzigo w'aho hantu urengeje urugero.





- `daemon=1` / `daemonwait=1`: Itangura `bitcoind` mu nyuma kandi, na `daemonwait`, irindira gutangura guhera imbere yo gutanga. Ivyo bifasha gukorana n’abagenzuzi (systemd, runit).





- `pid=<dosiye>`: Aho dosiye ya PID iri.





- `sandbox=<log-and-abort|guhagarika>`: Bishoboza igerageza ry'amatelefone: amatelefone yitezwe gusa ni yo yemerewe.





- `ugutangura=<cmd>` / `uguhagarika=<cmd>`: Gushitsa itegeko mu gutangura canke gufunga.





- `alertnotify=<cmd>`: Itera itegeko ku kwakira imburi.





- `blocknotify=<cmd>`: Ikora itegeko kuri buri bubiko bushasha.





- `gukosora=<urutonde>|1` / `gukosoragukuraho=<urutonde>`: Gushoboza/guhagarika ivyiciro vy'inyandiko vy'ido n'ido (nk'akarorero `urusenga`, `Mempool`, `RPC`, `kwemeza`...).





- `logips=1`: Inyandiko z'amaderesi ya IP.





- `logsourcelocations=1` / `amazina y'urudodo=1` / `ibihe vy'urudodo=1`: Yongera ahantu h'inkomoko, amazina y'urudodo, n'ibimenyetso vy'igihe nyavyo ku nyandiko, uko bikurikirana.





- `capa kuri konsole=1`: Yohereza ibimenyetso/ibikosorwa kuri konsole (*stdout*).





- `help-debug=1`: Igaragaza ubufasha bwo gukosora no guhagarika.





- `Igitekerezo=<cmt>`: Yongerako igitekerezo ku Mukozi-Umukozi P2P.



Ubu twarangije gutanga urutonde rw'ibintu vyinshi vy'imiterere. Iyi dosiye `Bitcoin.conf` rero ni yo igize urupapuro nyarwo rw'uruzitiro rwawe: isobanura imiterere y'urubuga, uburongozi bwa Mempool, ikoreshwa rya disiki n'ububiko, gushiramwo urutonde, n'uburongozi rusangi. Niba wifuza kumenya vyinshi kuri iyi dosiye maze ukayihingura ihuye n’ivyo ukeneye, ndagusavye gukoresha [umuhinguzi wa Jameson Lopp](https://jlopp.github.io/Bitcoin-core-config-generator/).



Twarashitse ku nsozero y’iri shure rya BTC 202, rizoba ryaragufashije gutahura gusa ivy’ishimikiro vy’ingene ama node akora n’ingene akorana muri systeme, ariko kandi no gushinga ayawe. Ubu uri umu Bitcoiner w’umunyabubasha, ufise uburenganzira bwawe bwite bwo kwikingira Wallet, utangaza amafaranga yawe biciye ku nzira yawe bwite. Urakoze cane!



Ubu ushobora kuja ku gice ca nyuma c’amashure, aho uzoshobora gusuzuma BTC 202, hanyuma ufate icemezo cawe kugira ngo umenye ko wamenye neza ivyiyumviro vyose vyerekanwa.



Ubu ufise uburyo bwinshi bwo guhitamwo. Intambwe ikurikira yumvikana ni ugushinga node yawe bwite ya Lightning, bikagufasha kwigenga bimwe bishitse ku bikorwa vyawe vya off-chain. Ivyo bizoca bivugwa mu nyigisho izoza, izosohoka muri iri 2025 kuri Plan ₿ Network.
