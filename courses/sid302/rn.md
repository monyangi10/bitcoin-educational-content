# Intangamarara y'igitabu Liquid Network


Gutangura urugendo rw’inyigisho rwagenewe gutanga ugutahura kwimbitse kw’umugambi wa Liquid Network n’umugambi wa Elements. Iyi bootcamp ihuza inyigisho n’ibikorwa kugira ngo ikwigishe ivy’ishimikiro vy’ubuhinga, ubwubatsi, n’ubudandaji bikenewe kugira ngo ushobore gushirwa mu ngiro no gukoresha ubushobozi bwa Liquid. Kuva kuri Confidential Transactions gushika ku guhingura ibidukikije, iri shure ni ryiza cane ku bashaka kwagura ubumenyi bwabo ku bikoresho biteye imbere mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin.


Iryo shure rizoba rifise ibiganiro vy’abahinga mu vy’ubuhinga, rivuga ku bintu nk’ubwubatsi bwa Liquid, gukoresha ibimenyetso, ivyiyumviro vy’ubuhinga vya Elements, n’ikoreshwa ry’ubuhinga bushasha nka Breeze SDK. Iryo shure ryateguwe kugira ngo rishobore gushikirwa n’abatangura n’abari hagati, kandi ritanga agaciro ku bahinga bashaka kumenya neza Liquid nk’urubuga rwo gutuma imigambi yabo igenda neza.

## Incamake y'amashure


<chapterId>3192ee7d-255b-4c4f-ba18-e08c5ab98577</chapterId>


Murakaze muri Liquid Bootcamp, amahugurwa yuzuye yateguwe kugira ngo muronke ubumenyi n’ubuhinga bwo gukoresha neza Liquid Network n’umugambi wa Elements. Iryo shure ritanga inyigisho zikomeye ku bintu bidasanzwe vya Liquid, nka Confidential Transactions, ugutanga itunga, n’imiterere y’urubuga rwayo, mu gihe kandi ritanga ivyiyumviro vy’ishimikiro vya Elements, porogarama itanga ubushobozi kuri Liquid.


Mu gihe cose c’ivyigwa, uzokwihweza ingene Liquid Network ikoreshwa, kuva ku gushinga no gukoresha ama node gushika ku gutahura ikoreshwa ryayo mu masoko y’itunga rya Bitcoin no gukoresha ibimenyetso. N’ibiganiro bivuye ku bahinga bo mu nganda, uzoronka kandi ubumenyi ku bijanye n’ingingo ziteye imbere nka HTLCs, Breeze SDK, n’umugambi wa Blockstream AMP.


Iyi bootcamp yatanguye gukorwa nk’ikintu c’abantu ku giti cabo, hakurikijwe urutonde rwatunganijwe (nk’uko vyerekanwa ku ishusho) rwagenewe ibiganiro vy’ubuzima. Ariko rero, ku bijanye n’iyi nyigisho, ibirimwo vyarasubiwemwo kugira ngo bihure neza n’uburyo bwo kuri Internet kandi bifashe abanyeshure gutahura. Urutonde rushasha ruratuma habaho ugutera imbere mu buryo bubereye kuva ku vyiyumviro vy’ishimikiro gushika ku nsiguro zijanye n’ubuhinga kandi ziteye imbere, bigatuma umuntu ashobora kwiga cane.


Uru rugendo rwatunganijwe kugira ngo rushobore kwakira abazorugiramwo bafise ubuhinga butandukanye, rutanga ubumenyi buvanze n’ubumenyi bujanye n’ibikorwa. Igihe iyi bootcamp izoba irangiye, uzoba ufise ugutahura gukomeye kw’ubwubatsi bwa Liquid, ukuntu ifatanye na Bitcoin, n’ingene wokoresha ubuhinga bwayo bushasha kugira ngo wubake kandi ukoreshe neza inyishu z’ivy’amahera.


Niwibike mw’isi ya Liquid Sidechain maze urekure ubushobozi bwayo bwose ubu nyene!
