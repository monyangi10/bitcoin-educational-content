# Kumenyesha urubuga rwa Liquid


Gutangura urugendo rw’inyigisho rwagenewe gutanga ugutahura kwimbitse ku mugambi wa Liquid Network n’uwa Elements. Iyi bootcamp ihuriza hamwe inyigisho n’ibikorwa kugira ngo ikwigishe ivy’ishimikiro vy’ubuhinga, ubwubatsi, n’ubudandaji bikenewe kugira ngo ushobore gushirwa mu ngiro no gukoresha ubushobozi bwa Liquid. Kuva ku bikorwa vy’ibanga gushika ku guhingura ibidukikije, iri shure ni ryiza cane ku barondera kwagura ubumenyi bwabo ku bikoresho biteye imbere mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin.


Iryo shure rifise ibiganiro vy’abahinga mu vy’ubuhinga, rivuga ku bintu nk’ubwubatsi bwa Liquid, gukoresha ibimenyetso, ivyiyumviro vy’ubuhinga vya Elements, n’ingene Breez ikoreshwa mu buryo bushasha nka Breez SDK. Iryo shure ryateguwe kugira ngo rishobore gushikirwa n’abatangura n’abari hagati, kandi ritanga agaciro ku bahinga barondera kumenya neza Liquid nk’urubuga rwo gutuma imigambi yabo igenda neza.

### Liquid Network Ubwubatsi n'Icitegererezo c'Ukwemeranya


Liquid Network ni uruzitiro rw’inyuma rwubakiwe ku rutonde rwa Elements, rwagenewe kwagura ubushobozi bwa Bitcoin mu gihe yizigiye umutekano wayo w’ishimikiro. Udakunze [Proof-of-Work](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/proof-of-work) ya Bitcoin, Liquid ikora ku buryo bw’[Isezerano](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/consensus) ry’Igihugu. Iryo shirahamwe ritunganijwe n’umugwi w’abanywanyi bakwiragijwe kw’isi yose, harimwo amahinguriro, amabiro y’ubudandaji, n’abatanga ibikorwa remezo. Muri ubwo bunywanyi, "abakozi" cumi na batanu baratorwa kugira ngo bakore nk'abasinya ku mabarabara.


Abo bakozi bakora [amabuye](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/block) mu buryo bw’uruziga, hakaba hari amabuye mashasha asohoka buri munota. Ico gihe nyaco gitandukanye n’ibihe vy’iminota cumi vy’ibishoboka vya Bitcoin. Kugira ngo ububiko bube ubw’ukuri, bisaba imikono y’abakozi 11 ku bakozi 15 (ibice bibiri vya gatatu vyongeweko urugero rumwe). Ubu buryo buha Liquid "iherezo ry'ibice bibiri," bisobanura ko iyo [igikorwa](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/transaction-tx) gifise ivyemezo bibiri (nk'iminota ibiri), mu mibare ntibishoboka gusubira gutunganya [uruhererekane](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/blockchain). Ukwo kwihuta n’ukudakeka ni ngirakamaro cane ku bijanye n’ugukemura ibibazo, ubudandaji bwikoresha, no gutorera umuti ibibazo vyihuta hagati y’ubudandaji.


Iryo shirahamwe rirafise inguvu. Biciye mu nzira y'Ishirahamwe ry'Inguvu (Dynafed), urubuga rurashobora guhindura abakozi canke guhindura amaparametere ataco bisaba [fork](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/fork) ikomeye. Ivyo bituma iyo sisitemu ishobora gutera imbere no gusubirira ibikoresho canke abayigize ata nkomanzi mu gihe iguma ikora ubudasiba.


### Confidential Transactions n'Ubucungezi bw'Itunga


Ikintu gisobanura Liquid ni ugushigikira kwayo kw’imvukira kuri Confidential Transactions (CT) n’itunga ryinshi. Ku ruhererekane nyamukuru rwa Bitcoin, amakuru yose yerekeye ivy’ugucuruza, ni ukuvuga uwurungitse, uwuyakira be n’amahera, aramenyekana ku mugaragaro. Mu Liquid, CT ikoresha amasezerano y’ubuhinga bwa none kugira ngo ihishe ubwoko bw’umutungo n’umubare mu gitabu ca bose mu gihe iguma yemerera urubuga kugenzura ko iyo nzira y’ugucuruza ari yo (ni ukuvuga ko ata n’ugutera imbere kw’ibiciro kwabaye). Abaje mu nama bafise imfunguruzo zihuma amaso ni bo bonyene bashobora kubona agaciro kadasanzwe, bagatanga urugero rw’ubuzima bwite bw’ubudandaji ruhambaye ku nzego zigenda zihindura ibibanza binini.


Liquid ifata ivy'ubutunzi vyose nk'abanyagihugu "bavukiye" b'uruhererekane. Ivyo birimwo Liquid Bitcoin (LBTC), amafaranga adahinduka nka USDT, n’ibimenyetso vy’umutekano. Gutanga umutungo ntibisaba amasezerano y’ubwenge asobanutse; ni igikorwa c’ishimikiro c’itegeko.


#### Itunga ry'amategeko na AMP

Ku mitungo isaba kwubahiriza amategeko, nk’ibimenyetso vy’umutekano, Liquid ikoresha urubuga rwo gucunga imitungo ya Blockstream (AMP). Ivyo bizana urugero rwemerewe aho amafaranga asaba umukono wa kabiri uvuye ku mukozi w'uruhusha. Ivyo bituma abatanga amafaranga bashobora gukurikiza amategeko—nk’ibisabwa vyo gushirwa ku rutonde rweranda canke KYC—ku rugero rw’ibintu vyinshi, bagahuza ubushobozi bw’uruzitiro rw’ibintu n’ubugenzuzi bw’amategeko bw’ivy’amahera ya kera.


### Inzira zibiri n'ibikorwa remezo vy'umutekano


Ihuriro hagati ya Liquid na Bitcoin rigumaho biciye ku nzira zibiri. Kugira ngo "ushiremwo," umukoresha yohereza Bitcoin kuri aderesi yashizweho kuri mainchain. Aya mahera arafungwa kubera ivyemezo 102 (amasaha nka 17) kugira ngo hakurweho ingorane zo gusubira gutunganya. Iyo bimaze kwemezwa, umubare ungana n’uwo LBTC uracukurwa ku ruhande rwa Liquid.


Uburyo bwa "peg-out" burashoboza abakoresha gucungura LBTC ku Bitcoin. Ivyo bisaba guturira LBTC n’uruhusha rwo gukora amakuru y’ibanga (cryptographie) ruva ku gisata c’ubuhinga. Kugira ngo ntihagire ubusuma bugira, ama peg-outs aragenzurwa cane n’imfunguruzo z’uburenganzira bwo gusohoka (PAKs) zifiswe n’abagize ishirahamwe. Ikindi, amafaranga arashobora guhindurwa ubwo nyene biciye ku bandi batanga amafaranga nka SideSwap, akarenga igihe co gutanga amafaranga kugira ngo amafaranga ashobore guhindurwa vuba.


#### Ivyuma vy'umutekano (HSMs)

Umutekano ushirwa mu ngiro cane biciye ku bikoresho vy’ubuhinga. Abakozi ntibabika [imfunguruzo z’ibanga](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/private-key) ku ma server asanzwe; ahubwo bakoresha ibikoresho vy’umutekano (HSMs). Ivyo bikoresho bifise umuyaga uva ku nzira y’ubwenge y’umurongozi w’umushitsi kandi bifise porogarama zifise amategeko akomeye. Nk’akarorero, HSM izokwanka gusinya igipande iyo uburebure bwaco butarenze ubwa mbere, ivyo bikaba bizotuma amateka adasubira kwandikwa. Iyi nzira y'umutekano "adversarial" yiyumvira ko server y'umushitsi yoshobora guhungabanywa, igatuma imfunguruzo ziguma zitekanye naho ahantu nyaho hoba hahungabanijwe.

### Elements: Umushinge wa Liquid


Elements ni urubuga rwuguruye, rw’ubuhinga bwa blockchain rukomoka ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin Core. Iragura ubushobozi bwa Bitcoin mu gutuma habaho imirongo y’inyuma—imirongo y’inyuma yigenga ishobora kwimurira ivy’ubutunzi kuri no kuva kuri Bitcoin. Mu gihe Bitcoin Core itanga ubushobozi ku rubuga rwa Bitcoin, Elements ni moteri ya porogarama iri inyuma ya Liquid Network n’izindi nzira z’inyuma zisanzwe.


Ubucuti buragororotse: Liquid ni "akarorero" kadasanzwe k'uruzitiro rw'inyuma rwa Elements, rwatunganijwe kugira ngo rukoreshwe mu guhingura n'akarorero k'uguhurizako. Abahinguzi bamenyereye Bitcoin bazosanga Elements ari nziza, kuko iguma ifise interface imwe ya RPC (Ihamagara ry’Ibikorwa vyo Kure) n’imiterere y’umurongo w’amabwirizwa (`elements-cli`, `ibintu-d`, `ibintu-qt`). Itandukaniro ry'ingenzi riri mu mitunganyirize: gushinga `chain=liquidv1` bihuza urudodo n'urubuga rwa Liquid, mu gihe `chain=elementsregtest` izunguruka ibidukikije vyo kugerageza ivy'ugusubira inyuma aho abahinguzi bashobora guhagarika generate ubwo nyene no kugerageza ata biva hanze.


#### Gutanga umutungo

Ikintu kidasanzwe muri Elements ni ugutanga umutungo w’imvukira. Udakunze Ethereum, aho ibimenyetso ari amasezerano y’ubwenge asobanutse, ivy’ubutunzi biri muri Elements ni abanyagihugu bo mu rwego rwa mbere baremwe biciye ku itegeko ryoroshe rya RPC (`issueasset`).


- Ibirango vyihariye:** Itunga ryose rironka indangamuntu yihariye y’inyuguti 64 y’inyuguti cumi n’itandatu.
- Ibimenyetso vyo gusubira gutanga:** Abatanga barashobora guhingura ibimenyetso vyo gusubira gutanga, biha uwubifise uburenganzira bwo gukora ibindi bimenyetso vy’umutungo mu nyuma (bifise akamaro ku bimenyetso vy’amahera bikomeye canke vy’umutekano).
- Igitabo c'Itunga:** Kubera ko IDs z'imirongo itandatu zidashobora gusomwa n'abantu, Igitabu c'Itunga ca Blockstream gishira izo ID ku mazina n'ibimenyetso (nk'akarorero, "USDT"), bisa na DNS y'itunga.


### Ubuzima bwite biciye kuri Confidential Transactions


Elements ivuga kuri kimwe mu bigoranye vy’intango vy’ama blockchains ya bose: ukubura ubuzima bwite bw’ubudandaji. Ku Bitcoin, amahera yose umuntu akoresha araboneka kw’isi yose. Elements izana **Confidential Transactions (CT)**, ipfuma amaso mu buryo bw’ibanga umubare n’ubwoko bw’itunga mu gihe iguma yemerera urubuga kugenzura ukuri kw’isoko.


Ivyo bishikwako hakoreshejwe **Ivyiyemezo vya Pedersen** na **Ibimenyamenya vy’Ikigereranyo**.


- Ivyemezo vya Pedersen** bisubirira umubare uboneka n’ivyemezo vy’ubuhinga bwa none. Kubera ububiko bw’ibintu bimwe, abagenzura barashobora kugenzura ko *Ivyiyumviro vy’Injira = Ivyemezo vy’Isohoka + Amahera* batigeze babona agaciro nyako.
- Ivyemezo vy'urutonde** bibuza umukoresha gukora amahera avuye mu kirere gito (nk'akarorero, akoresheje imibare mibi) mu kwemeza mu mibare ko agaciro kanyegejwe ari umubare wose mwiza mu rutonde rubereye.


Ku muntu yihweza hanze, Ibanga ry’Ibikorwa ryerekana ivyinjijwe n’ibisohoka bifise akamaro ariko bikazibira *ico* coherezwa n’*ingene*. Uwurungitse n’uwuronka (abafise imfunguruzo zihuma amaso) ni bo bonyene bashobora kubona amakuru y’inyandiko itomoye.


### Iterambere n'Ibikorwa vya RPC


Gukorana n'uruzitiro rwa Elements bikorwa cane cane biciye ku nzira yayo JSON-RPC. Ivyo bituma porogarama zanditswe muri Python, JavaScript canke Go zishobora kuvugana n’ivyo bikoresho.



- Gutegura:** Umuhinguzi atangura mu buryo bwa `regtest`. Ivyo bituma habaho uguhingura amabuye (`generateblock`) kugira ngo yemeze ibikorwa ubwo nyene, bikarenga igihe c'umunota 1 c'uruja n'uruza rw'uruja n'uruza.
- Amategeko:** Amategeko asanzwe ya Bitcoin nka `getblockchaininfo` araboneka, iruhande y’amabwirizwa yihariye ya Elements nka `dumpblindingkey` (yo gusuzuma CTs) canke `pegin` (yo kwimurira BTC mu ruzitiro rw’inyuma).
- Amazina y'ibanga:** Kugira ngo ushobore gucunga amanode menshi (nk'akarorero, "uwurungika" n'"uwakira" kugira ngo ugerageze), abahinguzi bakunda gukoresha amazina y'ibanga nka `e1-cli` na `e2-cli` yerekana ububiko bw'amakuru butandukanye, bigana urubuga rw'abagenzi ku mashini imwe.


Iyi nyubakwa iha abahinguzi ubushobozi bwo kwubaka ibikorwa vy’ivy’ubutunzi bikomeye cane—nk’ibibanza vy’ivy’ubutunzi canke amarembo y’ukwishura ku giti cabo—bakoresheje ibikoresho bikomeye kandi bimenyerewe vy’ibidukikije vya Bitcoin.

### Ibikorwa remezo vy'umusaraba-Layer na HTLCs


Ivy’ibidukikije vya Bitcoin vyahindutse ubuhinga bw’ubwubatsi bufise ibice vyinshi, aho Mainchain itanga uburaro, Lightning itanga umuvuduko, na Liquid ishoboza ubushobozi bw’itunga ry’imbere. Kwimurira agaciro hagati y’ivyo bice ata bahuza bari hagati bisaba ikintu c’intango c’ubuhinga bwa none kitagira icizigiro: [Hash](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/hash-function) Time Locked Contract ([HTLC](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/htlc)).


HTLC irema umurongo wo kwishura ufise ivyangombwa uhuza ubuhinga bwigenga mu buryo bw’atome. Ikora biciye mu nzira zibiri z'intango: **hash lock** na **time lock**.


- Igipfukisho ca Hash:** Amahera ashobora gukoreshwa ubwo nyene iyo uwuyakira ahishuye "preimage" y'ibanga ihuye n'ibara ry'ibanga ry'ibanga.
- Igihe co Gufunga:** Iyo uwuronka amahera ananiwe guhishura ibanga mu kiringo cashinzwe (uburebure bw’ibarabara), uwurungitse amahera y’intango arashobora gusubira gusaba ayo mahera.


Ico gice c’inzira zibiri kiratuma umuntu agira umutekano. Mu guhinduranya ibice, hash lock imwe ikoreshwa ku nzira zompi. Iyo uwuronka amahera ahishuriye ibanga ryo gusaba amahera ku rugero rumwe (nk’akarorero, Liquid), iryo banga riraboneka ku wurungitse, na we agashobora kurikoresha kugira ngo asabe amahera y’ugusubizanya ku rundi ruhande (nk’akarorero, Umuravyo). Iryo boha ry’ubuhinga bw’ibanga ryemeza ko iyo nzira y’uguhinduranya amahera iheza neza ku mpande zompi canke ikananirwa kuri bompi, ivyo bikaba bikuraho ingorane yo gutakaza amahera mu gihe uwundi ashobora kuyaronka.


### Taproot na MuSig2 vyavuguruwe


HTLCs za kera ([SegWit](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/segwit) v0) zakora neza ariko zari zifise ingorane z’ubuzima bwite n’ubushobozi. Bisaba gusohora [script](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/script) logic yose on-chain, bituma amafaranga y’uguhinduranya amenyekana vyoroshe ku baserukira blockchain kandi bikaba bizimvye cane kubera ubunini bw’amakuru yabo. Intango ya [Taproot](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/taproot) (SegWit v1) n’umurongo wa MuSig2 vyahinduye cane iyo nyubakwa.


Taproot iremeza **Ihuriro ry'urufunguzo** biciye kuri MuSig2. Aho guhishura inyandiko igoye ifise imfunguruzo nyinshi za bose, MuSig2 ireka abaje mu swap bagafatanya imfunguruzo zabo mu rufunguzo rumwe rwa bose.


- "Inzira y'ingenzi" y'ubufatanye:** Iyo bompi bemeye guhinduranya ("inzira y'umunezero"), barasinya kumwe ku bijanye n'uguhinduranya. Ku rubuga, ivyo bisa n’ivyo kwishura bimenyerewe, bikoreshwa umukono umwe. Ifata umwanya mutoyi cane kandi nta makuru n’amwe yerekana ku bijanye n’ivyo guhinduranya.
- Adversarial "Script Path":** Iyo uruhande rumwe rudasubiza canke rugira ububi, inyandiko iri munsi yayo (irimwo hash/time locks) irahishurirwa rero gusa. Ivyo bitunganijwe mu giti ca [Merkle](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/merkle-tree), bikaba bituma uwutagira uburyarya ashobora gushikiriza gusa ishami ry’umwihariko rikenewe kugira ngo asubire kuronka amahera, akaguma yihishije mu bijanye n’ubwenge bw’amasezerano.


### Ishirwa mu ngiro: Liquid-Imiravyo Ihinduranya


Mu bikorwa, ayo masezerano aratuma habaho uguhinduranya ata nkomanzi hagati y’ibice vya Bitcoin. Ihinduka risanzwe rya Liquid-ku-Lightning ritangura n’umukiriya asaba guhindura umukozi atanga ibikorwa. Umukiriya atanga [invoice y’umuravyo](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/invoice-lightning), kandi uwuyitanga agafunga invoice ingana na Liquid Bitcoin (L-BTC) mu aderesi ya HTLC ikoreshwa na Taproot.


Ivyo atomicity birashirwa mu ngiro iyo amahera y’ukwishyura ashitse. Kugira ngo umuntu asabe L-BTC, uwutanga iyo serivisi ategerezwa kuba afise ishusho y’imbere. Baronka iyo preimage gusa iyo bashoboye kwishura neza invoice y’umuravyo y’umukiriya. Igihe amahera ya Lightning arangiriye, ishusho y’imbere irahishurirwa, igatuma uwuyitanga ashobora gufungura amahera ya Liquid.


#### Wallet Ivyiyumviro n'uburongozi bwa UTXO

Ku bakoresha, ivyo bigoranye ni ibintu bitaboneka. Ivyuma vy’ubuhinga bwa none nka Aqua bikora ivy’uguhingura urufunguzo, uguhingura invoice, n’ugushirako umukono mu nyuma. Uwukoresha gusa "ariha" invoice ya Lightning akoresheje umubare wiwe wa Liquid. Inyuma y’ivyo, iyo serivisi iracungera ugushira hamwe kwa [UTXO](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/utxo), igakuraho ibihe bitobito, bitavuzwe, canke bisubizwa kugira ngo [wallet](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/wallet) ikomeze gukora neza no kugabanya ingaruka z’amahera mu bihe vy’uruja n’uruza rwinshi.

### Liquid Network Ubwubatsi n'Ukwemeranya


Liquid Network ikora nk'uruzitiro rw'inyuma rwubakiwe ku **Elements**. Naho Elements—fork ya Bitcoin Core—itanga umushinge wa porogarama, Liquid ni ugushirwa mu ngiro kw’urubuga rwo gukora. Udakunze Proof-of-Work ya Bitcoin, yizigiye [mining](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/mining) ishobora guhiganwa, Liquid ikoresha uburyo bwa **Uguhurizako kw'Igihugu**.


Iryo shirahamwe ritunganywa n'abakozi cumi n'itanu bakwiragijwe kw'isi yose. Izo nzego zikoresha ama server yihariye akora uruhara rubiri ruhambaye:

1. **Guhingura amabuye:** Abakozi barahindukirana bagahingura amabuye mu buryo butegekanijwe, bagahingura amabuye mashasha buri munota.

2. **Gushirako umukono ku bubiko:** Kugira ngo ububiko bube ubw’ukuri, butegerezwa gusinywa n’abakozi 11 muri 15.


Ivyo bipimo 11 kuri 15 bituma urubuga rushobora kwihanganira ukunanirwa kw’ibice bine bitahagaze. Inyungu nyamukuru y’ivyo bihuza ni **iherezo ry’ibintu**. Naho Bitcoin ivuga ku bishoboka, Liquid ishika ku cizigiro c’ugutunganya inyuma y’amabarabara abiri (nk’iminota ibiri). Iyo ibumba rifise icemezo kimwe hejuru yaryo, uruzitiro ntirushobora gusubira gutunganirizwa, bikaba bituma rugira akamaro kanini mu gukemura ibibazo no gutorera umuti vyihuse.


### Confidential Transactions n'Itunga ry'Igihugu


Igisobanura Liquid ni ugukoresha **Confidential Transactions (CT)**. Ku rupapuro rwa Bitcoin mainchain, uwurungitse, uwuronka be n’amahera bimenyekana. Liquid iranoza ivyo mu guhuma amaso mu buryo bw’ibanga umubare n’ubwoko bw’itunga, mu gihe isiga aderesi y’uwurungitse n’uwakira aboneka kugira ngo umuntu asuzumwe.


Kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gucapura amahera (nk’akarorero, mu kohereza amahera atari meza), Liquid ikoresha **Ivyemezo vya Pedersen** na **Ibimenyamenya vy’Ikigereranyo**. Ivyo bimenyetso vy’ubuhinga bwa none bituma urubuga rushobora kugenzura ko *Ibishizwemwo = Ivyo Bisohotse + Amahera* kandi ko agaciro kose ari imibare y’ukuri, ata na rimwe bihishura imibare yihariye ku gitabu ca bose. Abaje mu nama bafise imfunguruzo zihuma ni bo bonyene bashobora kubona amakuru yasohowe.


#### Gutanga umutungo

Liquid ifata ivy'ubutunzi vyose nk'ivy'imvukira. Yaba Liquid Bitcoin (L-BTC), igice c’amahera gikomeye nka USDT, canke igice c’umutekano token, vyose bifise ubuhinga bumwe. Gutanga umutungo ntibisaba amasezerano y’ubwenge—bisaba gusa guhamagara RPC yoroshe.


- Itunga ridategekanijwe:** Rishobora gutangwa ata ruhusha n’umuntu wese.
- Itunga ritegekanijwe:** Ukoresheje urubuga rwo gucunga itunga rya Blockstream (AMP), abatanga amafaranga barashobora gushitsa amategeko agenga ivy’ukubahiriza (nka KYC/AML) mu gusaba umukono wa kabiri uvuye ku mukozi w’uruhusha imbere y’uko umutungo ushobora kwimurirwa.


### Ishirahamwe ry'inzira zibiri n'iry'inguvu


Ikiraro kiri hagati ya Bitcoin na Liquid ni **Ibarabara ry’Inzira Zibiri**. Ituma abakoresha bashobora kwimurira BTC ku ruhande (Peg-in) bagasubira kuri mainchain (Peg-out).


**Inzira yo gushiramwo:**

Ivyo nta ruhusha bifise. Uwukoresha yohereza BTC kuri aderesi igenzurwa n’ishirahamwe. Kugira ngo ukingirwe Bitcoin blockchain gusubira gutunganya, ayo mahera ategerezwa kurindira **102 vyemezo** (nk’amasaha 17) imbere y’uko L-BTC ingana n’iyo icukurwa ku ruhande.


**Inzira yo gukuraho:**

Kugira ngo asubire kuri Bitcoin, L-BTC iraturirwa. Ariko rero, kugira ngo ntihagire ubusuma bw’ibikoresho vy’ububiko bwa Bitcoin biri munsi yavyo, ivyuma bifata amabuye y’agaciro (peg-outs) ntibikora vyikora. Bisaba uruhusha rw’umunyamuryango afise **Urufunguzo rw’Uruhusha rwa Peg-Out (PAK)**. Amafaranga ya Bitcoin ubwayo arakingirwa mu gikoresho ca wallet gifise imikono myinshi 11 kuri 15, gifise imfunguruzo ziri mu bice vy’umutekano vy’ibikoresho (HSMs) bifise ikirere kiva ku bikoresho nyamukuru vy’abakozi.


**Ishirahamwe ry'inguvu (Dynafed):**

Kugira ngo Liquid ibeho igihe kirekire, ikoresha Dynafed, uburyo bushobora gutuma urubuga ruhindura abakozi canke ruhindura amaparametere ata fork ikomeye. Iyo umukozi akeneye gusubirwamwo, urubuga ruratangaza ibikorwa vy’uguhindura. Inyuma y’indwi zibiri z’ugusangira, iyo ntunganyo nshasha irafata ubutegetsi, igatuma ishirahamwe ritera imbere ata nkomanzi mu gihe riguma rikora.


## Ibidukikije n'amasoko y'itunga

### Liquid Network: Ingamba z'ubudandaji n'ibidukikije


Liquid ni ikintu kirenze urudodo rw’inyuma rw’ubuhinga; ni igice c’ibikorwa remezo vyibanda ku bucuruzi gigenewe gukorana n’ibisabwa bikomeye vy’amasoko y’itunga Bitcoin mainchain idashobora gushigikira neza. Naho [Lightning Network](https://planb.academy/resources/glossary/lightning-network) ikora neza ku bijanye n’ugutanga amahera menshi, y’agaciro gato, Liquid igamije gutanga amahera y’agaciro kanini, gutanga itunga, no gusezerana hagati y’amahera.


Ivyo bidukikije birongowe na **Liquid Federation**, ishirahamwe ry’amashirahamwe ~73 harimwo amashirahamwe y’ubudandaji, amabiro y’ubudandaji, n’abatanga ibikorwa remezo. Iyi nzira y’ubufatanye igaragaza ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwa kera vy’ivy’ubutunzi ariko ikora mu buryo buboneye cane kandi igihe co gutanga amahera kigabanywa cane (iminota 2 n’imisi T+2).


### Ikimenyetso c'Itunga ry'Isi Nyakuri (RWA)


Igiharuro nyamukuru c'ubudandaji ca Liquid ni "Tokenization"—iserukira agaciro k'isi nyakuri (amafaranga, amasezerano ya mining) nk'ibimenyetso vy'ubuhinga bwa none ku rubuga rwa blockchain. Ivyo biratanga ivyiza bitatu vy’intango:

1. **24/7 Amasoko y’Isi Yose:** Gukuraho amasaha y’amabanki n’amategeko agenga ivy’aho umuntu aba.

2. **Ubushobozi bwo gukora:** Gukuraho amakosa yo gusubiza hamwe biciye mu gitabu c’amahera rusangi, kitahinduka.

3. **Atomic Settlement:** Kugabanya ingorane z’abafatanyabikorwa mu kumenya ko ugutanga bishika icarimwe n’ukwishyura.


#### Itunga ry'amategeko na AMP

Itunga ryinshi ry’inzego ntirishobora gucuruzwa ata ruhusha kubera ibisabwa n’amategeko. **Ikigo co gucunga umutungo (AMP)** ni co kigereranyo c’ubuhinga gishira mu ngiro ayo mategeko.


- Whitelisting:** Abatanga amakuru barashobora guhagarika gufata no kugurisha ku ma aderesi agenzuwe na KYC.
- Multisig Control:** Ibikorwa vyo kwubahiriza amategeko (nk’ugufunga amafaranga yibwe canke gusubira gutanga ibimenyetso vyatakaye) bicungirwa biciye ku ruhusha rw’imikono myinshi, kugira ngo ata mukozi n’umwe ashobora gukora ku ruhande rumwe.


Ivyo bikorwa remezo biriho uno musi. Ivyuma nka **Stalker** bitanga ibikorwa vy’ugutanga ibimenyetso kuva ku mpera kugeza ku mpera mu Buraya, mu gihe **Sideswap** ikora nk’uguhinduranya ibimenyetso vy’abantu bose kandi bitagira ububiko bw’ibintu, bikaba bishoboza gucuruza ibintu nk’ivyo **Blockstream Mining Note (BMN)** n’ibimenyetso vy’ubudandaji).


### Ukuroranirwa mu vy’ukuri: Icigwa ca Mayfell


Ikimenyamenya gikomeye cane c’akamaro ka Liquid kiva i **Mayfell** muri Mexique. Mu isoko aho amafaranga y’agaciro ashingiye ku mpapuro z’amasezerano—zishobora gutakaza, guhenda, no gukora buhoro buhoro—Mayfell yaciye yimurira ibikorwa remezo vyose kuri Liquid.



- Urugero:** Amakete y’isezerano arenga 25.000 yasohowe, agereranya **amadolari 1.5 miliyaridi+** ​​y’agaciro.
- Ibanga:** Hakoreshejwe Confidential Transactions ya Liquid, amafaranga y’inguzanyo abonwa gusa n’uwugurisha n’uwugurisha, bikaba bizigama ubuzima bwite bw’ubudandaji mu gihe bituma abagenzuzi bashobora kugenzura ubutungane bw’igitabu c’inguzanyo.
- Ingaruka:** Mu guhuza abaguzi batari amabanki n’amasoko y’isi yose biciye ku nzira ya blockchain, Mayfell yagabanyije ibiciro yongera yongera uburyo bwo kuronka inguzanyo ku bigo bitobito vyo muri Mexique, vyerekana ko igitekerezo c’agaciro ca Liquid kirenze kure ubudandaji bw’ibiharuro.


### Ingamba zo gushinga ahantu n'iyindi minyororo


Liquid ihanganye mw’isoko ry’abantu benshi ry’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga (Ethereum, Solana, n’ibindi), ariko ifise inyungu zitandukanye:


- Ugusobanuka kw’amategeko:** Liquid ikoresha Bitcoin (L-BTC) nk’umutungo wayo w’akavukire. Nta token canke ICO ibanza gucukurwa, irinda ingorane z'"umutekano utandikishijwe" zitera ingorane izindi blockchains za L1.
- Gushikama:** Bitandukanye n’akarorero ka konti ya Ethereum aho amafaranga y’ibikorwa ataco ashoboye aguma aturira amafaranga y’amazi, akarorero ka Liquid’s UTXO ni akagenwa kandi ko kwizigirwa ku makuru y’ivy’ubutunzi ahambaye cane.
- Ibanga:** Ibanga ry’imbere ni igisabwa ku bigo vyinshi vy’imari, ikintu Liquid itanga mu buryo bw’akavukire ko iminyororo ya Leta igorwa no gushirwa mu ngiro ata vyongeyeko bikomeye.


Ku baterambere, iki gice c’ibidukikije kiratanga akaryo gatomoye: kwubaka ibikoresho (ibipande vy’ibikoresho, amasakoshi, ubufatanye bwo kwubahiriza amategeko) bihuza amafaranga y’agaciro n’ivy’ubutunzi vya Bitcoin.


## Uguhagarika AMP


<chapterId>4f21a0a7-0dc0-44cf-8a3a-d9e2f8a3f05f</chapterId>


:::video id=f00822b4-dc1a-46ff-adfc-ff7c97a0024d:::

### Blockstream AMP: Igenzura ry'umutungo ryemerewe kuri Liquid


Blockstream AMP (Ikibanza co gucunga umutungo) ikora nk’ibikorwa remezo bihambaye kuri Liquid Network, vyagenewe canecane abatanga amafaranga y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none n’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none. Naho Liquid itanga urugero rw’ishimikiro rudafise uruhusha rwo gutanga umutungo w’imvukira, ibigo bigenzurwa akenshi bisaba ubushobozi bwo kugenzura no kwubahiriza amategeko. AMP irazibira ico kinogo mu gushiramwo urugero rwemerewe rwo kugenzura ibintu vyihariye ata gutakaza inyungu z’ubuzima bwite bwa Liquid’s Confidential Transactions.


Iciyumviro c’agaciro k’urubuga gishingiye ku bushobozi bubiri bw’intango: ukuboneka kw’abatanga n’ukwemererwa gukora. Udakunze imitungo isanzwe ya Liquid aho amafaranga n’ubwoko ari blinded kuri bose kiretse abayigize, imitungo ya AMP iremesha uwuyitanga kuguma afise urutonde rw’igenzura rwose rwa unblinded. Ukwo guseruka ni ngombwa mu gutanga raporo zijanye n’amategeko no mu gukora igenzura ry’imbere mu gihugu. Ikindi, AMP ishira mu ngiro uburyo bukomeye bwo gutanga uruhusha biciye mu buryo bwo gusinya hamwe. Igihe cose umuntu yimuriye umutungo wa AMP bisaba umukono uva kuri server ya AMP. Ivyo bituma abatanga amafaranga bashobora gukurikiza amategeko agoranye off-chain—nk’uguhagarika amakonti, gushira ku rutonde rw’abashoramari bemerewe, canke gushinga imipaka yo kwimurira amafaranga—bakora neza nk’umurinzi w’irembo ry’imbere mu gisata c’imbere.


#### Ubucuruzi bw'ibikorwa

Ubwo buhinga bwo kwubaka burazana ubudandaji bwihariye. Ubuhinga bushingiye ku kuboneka kwa server ya AMP; iyo server ikora nk'umusinye kandi akaja hanze y'umurongo, amafaranga y'itunga arahagarara. Ikindi, naho ubuzima bwite bw’ukoresha n’uwundi bugumaho, abashoramari bategerezwa kwemera ko uwutanga amakuru afise uburenganzira bwo kubona neza ivyo bafise. Iyi modele ni nziza ku bimenyetso vy’umutekano vyubahiriza amategeko ariko ntibibereye ku mafaranga y’ibanga adashobora gucengera.


### Ubwubatsi bw'iterambere n'inzira zo kwifatanya


Ubu, ubuhinga bwa AMP buriko burahinduka buva ku gusubiramwo kwa mbere buja ku buhinga bushobora guhinduka, bukorana n'abandi "Version 2". Uburyo bwa kera bwasaba abatanga amakuru kuguma bafise ama node ya Elements akora neza kandi bwahatiye abahinguzi kwizigira cane Green SDK. Naho vyari bikora, ivyo vyatumye haba intambamyi nyinshi z’ubuhinga zo kwinjira no guhitamwo wallet bike.


Ubwubatsi bw’uruvyaro rukurikira burayorohereza cane ivyo bisabwa mu guhindura ibintu bigoranye bikaja kuri server. Muri iki kigereranyo gishasha, umukozi wa AMP ni we akora igikorwa co guterura ibintu bikomeye vy’ubwubatsi bw’amafaranga, guhitamwo UTXO, no kubara amafaranga. Irerekana uwuyitanga igitabu c’Isezerano ry’Isezerano ry’Igihugu ry’Igihugu (PSET) risaba gusa ko umuntu ashirako umukono. Ubu buryo bwa "smart server, dumb wallet" burakuraho ivy'uko abatanga amakuru bakeneye gukoresha ama node yuzuye kandi bugatuma hakoreshwa ama wallets y'ibikoresho n'imiterere y'imikono myinshi isanzwe mu gucunga ububiko.


Ku bategura, iyo mpinduka bisigura kuva ku SDKs z'abanyagihugu kuja ku ndondoro zisanzwe n'imigenderanire y'ibikorwa vya PSET. Ama wallets ubu arashobora kwandika ibisobanuro vy’imikono myinshi na server ya AMP kugira ngo ashingeho uburenganzira bwo kwemererwa. Ivyo bihuza iterambere rya AMP n’ingingo mfatirwako zagutse za Bitcoin wallet, bituma ubufatanye bushikira ubuhinga bwose bushobora gukorana n’imiterere ya PSBT/PSET. Abahinguzi b’inyubako muri iki gihe bararemeshwa gukoresha ibikoresho nka Liquid Wallet Kit (LWK), bifasha izo nyubakwa zigezweho, zishingiye ku ndondoro, kugira ngo zibone ko ibikorwa vyazo bishobora gukoreshwa muri kazoza ku bijanye n’ingingo ngenderwako nshasha za AMP.


### Igikoresho ca Liquid Wallet n'Ibanga


Kubaka ibikorwa kuri Liquid bisaba kugendera mu kirundo gikomeye kuruta iterambere rya Bitcoin kubera ibiranga nk'itunga ry'imvukira na Confidential Transactions. Ivyo bidukikije bishigikirwa n'ubwubatsi buteye imbere: amasomero yo mu rwego rwo hasi nka `secp256k1-zkp` akora ivy'intango vy'ubuhinga bwa none, mu gihe ibikoresho vyo mu rwego rwo hejuru bicungera ubuhinga bwa wallet.


Mu mateka, igikoresho co guteza imbere Green (GDK) caratanze umuti wuzuye ariko ukomeye. Iyindi nzira yo muri iki gihe ni Liquid Wallet Kit (LWK), itanga uburyo bwo gukoresha ibice. LWK itandukanya ivyiyumviro mu bice bitandukanye—gufata ibisobanuro, gusinya, n’uguhanahana amakuru ku bikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwigenga—biha abahinguzi uburenganzira bwo kwubaka inyishu zisanzwe ata n’amahera y’ububiko bw’ibitabu bumwe.


#### Gufata Confidential Transactions

Ingorane igaragara cane muri iki gice c’ibidukikije ni ugucungera ubuzima buhuma amaso. Mu Liquid, ibisohoka mu bikorwa vy’ubudandaji birashirwa mu nzira hakoreshejwe ubuhinga bwo guhindura urufunguzo rwa Diffie-Hellman (ECDH). Uwurungika akoresha urufunguzo rwa bose ruhuma rw’uwuronka kugira ngo apfuke amafaranga n’ubwoko bw’itunga, akazana ikimenyamenya c’urutonde kigenzura ukuri kw’ugucuruza ata guhishura agaciro.


Kugira ngo wallet ikore, itegerezwa guhindura neza iyo nzira. Iyo wallet ibonye igikorwa cinjira, igerageza gufungura igisohoka ikoresheje urufunguzo rwayo rw’ibanga rwo gufungura. Iyo ibanga ry’abantu bose rihuye, wallet irashobora gukuraho agaciro maze ikayongera ku giciro c’uwukoresha. Iyi nzira irakoreshwa cane kandi isaba gucunga neza ibintu bihuma amaso kugira ngo amafaranga akoreshe mu mibare, ikintu gikomeye ibikoresho nka LWK bigamije gukuraho umuhinguzi.

## Breez SDK - Nta nzira


<chapterId>fb77442c-3d1e-427e-b2f5-16668ce4c643</chapterId>


:::video id=1a6289b5-fdae-4320-b5b1-41925150108c:::

### Intangamarara ya Breez Liquid SDK


Breez Liquid SDK ni igikoresho co kwikingira, gifunguye, gigenewe gukuraho ikinogo kiri hagati ya Liquid Network n’ibidukikije vyagutse vya Bitcoin. Intumbero yayo nyamukuru ni ugukuraho ibintu bigoranye vyo gucunga node za Lightning Network n’uguhindura atome, bikaba vyemerera abahinguzi kwubaka ibikorwa vy’amahera bitagiramwo amahane.


Yubatswe n'ubuhinga bwa "mobile-first", ivyiyumviro nyamukuru vyanditswe muri Rust ku bijanye n'ubushobozi n'umutekano, ariko ikoresha UniFFI (Igikorwa c'amahanga gihuriweko Interface) kugira ngo itange amabohero y'akavukire kuri Kotlin, Swift, Python, C#, Dart, na Flutter. Ivyo bituma abahinguzi bashobora kwinjiza ibikorwa vya Bitcoin mu bidukikije bakunda ata gucungera ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru ku rugero ruto.


**Ubwoko bw'ibikorwa bushigikiwe:**

SDK ikorana na Liquid nk'"ishingiro ry'i muhira." Iraruta ayandi yose mu bikorwa bitatu vyihariye:

1. **Liquid-ku-Liquid:** Uguhindura on-chain mu buryo butaziguye.

2. **Liquid-ku-Muravyo:** Kwishura amafagitire y’Umuravyo hakoreshejwe amahera ya Liquid.

3. **Liquid-ku-Bitcoin:** Guhindura amafaranga ataco akoresheje Bitcoin mainchain.


*Iciyumviro: SDK ntishigikira amafaranga ataziguye y’umuravyo-ku-muravyo canke Bitcoin-ku-Bitcoin. Ni igikoresho gishingiye canecane kuri Liquid.*


### Ubwubatsi bwo kwishura: Guhinduranya ubwato bwo munsi y'amazi


Kugira ngo Liquid, Lightning, na Bitcoin bishobore gukorana neza ata bacungezi bahurikiye hamwe, Breez yizigira **Ivy’uguhinduranya ubwato bwo mu mazi**. Ivyo ni ibikorwa vy’atome aho igikorwa co gucuruza giheza neza ku nzira zompi canke kikananirwa kuri zompi, bikaba bituma amahera atazokwigera atakarira mu nzira.


#### Umuravyo wohereje (Uguhindura ubwato bwo mu mazi)

Iyo umukoresha arishe invoice ya Lightning, SDK itangaza "ugufunga" ku Liquid Network. Ivyo bica bituma ayo mahera ashirwa mu vyo gucungera. Uwutanga amahera (Boltz) arabibona, akishura invoice ya Lightning, hanyuma akoresha ishusho y’imbere y’ukwishura (ikimenyetso c’uko umuntu yaronse amahera) kugira ngo asabe amahera ya Liquid yugarijwe.


#### Umuravyo Urakira (Uguhindura Ubwato bwo munsi y'Ubwato)

Kwakira Umuravyo ni inzira ihinduka. Uwukoresha atanga invoice, uwutanga amafaranga agafunga amafaranga kuri Lightning Network. SDK ikurikirana iyo nzira biciye ku WebSockets. Igihe urufunguzo rwemejwe, SDK ica ikora igikorwa co gusaba kugira ngo yimure amahera angana muri Liquid wallet y'ukoresha.


#### Urudodo rw'umusaraba Bitcoin

Ku bijanye n'uguhindura Liquid-ku-Bitcoin, ubwubatsi bukoresha uburyo bwo "guhindura kabiri". Ivy’ugufunga bibera ku minyororo yose icarimwe. Uwurungitse asaba amahera kuri Bitcoin, mu gihe uwuronka asaba amahera kuri Liquid. Ivyo bituma habaho gukorana ata kwizigira ata kwizigira federated peg-outs canke ubuhinga bwo guhinduranya amakuru.


### Umuhinguzi Interface n'uburongozi bwikora


Breez SDK yorosha ubumenyi bw'abahinguzi mu gukoranya amafaranga atoroshe mu nzira y'intambwe zitatu: **Guhuza, Gutegura, no Gushitsa**.


1. **Ihuza:** Itangura wallet, ikorana n’uruzitiro rw’amabuye ikoresheje Liquid Wallet Kit (LWK), kandi igashinga amahuza ya WebSocket kugira ngo igenzure mu gihe nyaco.

2. **Tegura:** Imbere yo gutanga amafaranga, iyi ntambwe iraharura kandi igasubiza amafaranga yose ajanye n’uruja n’uruza n’amafaranga y’uguhindura, bikaba vyemerera UI kwerekana umubare wose utomoye ku muntu akoresha.

3. **Execute:** Iyi ntambwe yubaka igikorwa, ikagitangaza, kigatanguza uguhinduranya.


#### Uburyo bwo gukingira bwikora

Kimwe mu bintu bihambaye cane vya SDK ni **Ugucungera amahera yishurwa**. Iyo urubuga rwacitse intege canke uwundi muntu adakorana, amahera yoshobora gufatwa n’amasezerano atagira igihe. SDK ikuraho ivyiyumviro vyo gusubirana vyose. Irakurikirana uko uguhinduranya kwose kumeze; iyo swap inaniwe canke igihe kirangiye, SDK ihita yubaka kandi igatangaza igikorwa co gusubiza amahera kugira ngo igarure amahera kuri wallet y'ukoresha.


Ikindi, SDK ikora **Igisubizo c'amakuru**. Ifatanya amakuru y’uguhinduranya off-chain (atangwa n’umuhinduzi wa Boltz) n’amateka y’uguhinduranya on-chain. Ivyo bituma amateka y’ibikorwa vy’ukoresha ashobora gusomwa n’abantu, yerekana amakuru y’inyandiko n’aho yishuye aho gukoresha gusa amakuru y’ibikorwa vy’ibikorwa vy’ubuhinga.
