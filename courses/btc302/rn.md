# Intambwe ya mbere yo kwemera Bitcoin



Muri iki gihe, ubutunzi bw’inyigisho buraboneka kuri Internet, bituma umuntu wese ashobora kumenya ivyerekeye Bitcoin n’ibidukikije vyayo.


Ariko rero, **ikintu kimwe kirabura kenshi: akaryo ko guhura, ivyiyumviro vya Exchange, gusangira ubumenyi** kandi ikiruta vyose, **kwubaka hamwe**.



Kugira ngo ivyo bikenewe bishobore gukura, benshi mu bakoresha ama bitcoin bakunda guhitamwo **gushinga imiryango yo mu karere**.

Iyo abantu bamaze gushika bihagije, baratunganya amakoraniro, kandi ayo makoraniro asubiramwo akenshi aca aba ahantu heza ho gutanga inyigisho z’ubuhinga, amahugurwa y’ibikorwa vy’amaboko be n’ibiganiro vyagutse.


Muri iri shure "**BTC302**", tuzokwihweza **igituma gushinga umuryango wa Bitcoin n'/canke guhura bihambaye**, hanyuma twinjire mu **ibikoresho n'uburyo bwo guteza imbere akaranga k'umuryango wawe k'amaso n'akaranga k'ubuhinga bwa none**.


Tuzoshikiriza kandi **imigambi n’imigambi** yoroshe gutanguza, cane cane iyo kwigisha, ishigikiwe n’ibikoresho vyinshi vyateguwe imbere y’igihe, bifunguye.


Ubwa nyuma, tuzoganira ku **iterambere ry'imiryango ya Bitcoin ku rwego rw'igihugu**, n'**ubushobozi bwayo bwo kwihutisha isi y'ejo izoba ifise amahera menshi cane**.

## Incamake y'amashure


<chapterId>e8991b82-4966-5e04-8f05-7cae46eacbaa</chapterId>



Murakaze muri BTC302!



Kuva mu mwaka w’2009, Bitcoin yarateye imbere ataco ihinduye. Ukwo kwiyongera kwafashe ishusho biciye mu vyiyumviro bishasha vyo guteza imbere Bitcoin (BIP), ugushinga ibindi bice kugira ngo urubuga rushobore gutera imbere (Lightning Network mu bindi), no guteza imbere amashirahamwe menshi n’amashirahamwe atanguye.



Iterambere ryayo riraboneka kandi muri On-Chain, aho umubare w’ibikorwa vy’ubudandaji uguma wiyongera.


Nk’akarorero, hagati y’igenekerezo rya 1 Mukakaro 2018 n’igenekerezo rya 1 Mukakaro 2024, igitigiri c’amafaranga akoreshwa kuri Layer nyamukuru ya Bitcoin kigereranywa ko [carongerekanye n’ibice 66,2%](https://dune.com/queries/3375917/5663935). Hagati aho, hagati ya Myandagaro 2021 na Myandagaro 2023, igitigiri c’amafaranga y’ubudandaji ya Leta kuri Lightning Network [caradutse n’ibice 1.212%](https://uruzi.com/kwiga/amadosiye/raporo-y’umuravyo-w’uruzi-2023.pdf).



Birumvikana ko iyo nkura ihambaye ifitaniye isano cane n’ibi:




- n’ugukura kw’abantu kw’isi yose mu gukoresha Bitcoin](https://profedustream.substack.com/p/21-raisons-dutiliser-Bitcoin), kugira ngo bibohoke mu bijanye n’ubuhinga bwa banki no/canke bakoreshe amafaranga adahinduka cane canke adahinduka cane, nk’uko biri ku banyagihugu bo muri Siriya, Misiri, A, Libani,
- ugushimira igiciro cayo uko igihe kigenda kirarenga, bikayishinga nk’ububiko bw’agaciro n’ishoramari ry’igihe kirekire, rirongowe n’ingingo ngenderwako y’amahera yayo idahinduka;
- iterambere rikurikirana ry’isoko ry’amahera y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none, cane cane mu kiringo ca 2017-2018 na 2020-2021;
- Bitcoin iriko irakura mu binyamakuru vyinshi.



![image](assets/fr/001.webp)




Naho Bitcoin ikiri mu ntango, uku kuri, ushobora kuba umaze kumenya, kuratuma twihweza ibibazo bibiri nyamukuru:




- Ni gute tworemesha aba Bitcoiners kuja kure mu nyigisho zabo no mu migambi yabo kugira ngo **bakoreshe neza ubushobozi bwa Bitcoin**?



- Twofasha gute abashasha, akenshi bakwegwakwegwa n’ugutekereza ku bintu vy’igihe gito, kwirinda gutakaza amahera ku bijanye n’ugusaza kwa memecoin gushasha, ahubwo bakibanda ku **kwiga ivy’ishimikiro vya Bitcoin**?



Umuti mwiza w’izo ngorane zompi ni uwusanzwe: **kubaho mw’isi nyakuri no kwigisha**.



Intumbero y’iri shure ni ukuguha ivyo ukeneye vyose kugira ngo ureme kandi utunganye neza umuryango canke ihuriro ry’aba Bitcoin. Waba ushaka kwubaka umugambi wo mu karere canke gukura umugwi wo kuri internet, iri shure rizokuyobora intambwe ku yindi: kuva ku kurema akaranga kawe k’amaso gushika ku gushinga ibirori bihoraho no gucunga imihora yawe yo guhanahana amakuru.



**Igice ca 1: Intangamarara


Tuzotangura tugaragaza akamaro ko guhuza abantu bakikuje Bitcoin, haba biciye mu miryango yo kuri internet canke mu makoraniro yo mu karere. Uzobona igituma n’aho wotanguza umuryango wawe bwite, hamwe n’ingene woshiraho intumbero zitomoye kugira ngo utere imbere mu gukura kwawo no kugumaho igihe kirekire.



**Igice ca 2: Gutegura akaranga kadasanzwe k'umuryango wawe wa Bitcoin**


Kugira ngo umuntu akwegere abanywanyi bashasha no gutuma umuntu yumva ko ari uwabo, ni ngombwa ko yubaka akaranga kadasanzwe. Muri iki gice, uzomenya ingene wohingura ibimenyetso, amafoto, n’amabendera yerekana impwemu y’umuryango wawe wa Bitcoin.



**Igice ca 3: Gukora inzira zo guhanahana amakuru ku muryango wawe wa Bitcoin**


Guhanahana amakuru ni umugongo w’umuryango uwo ari wo wose. Muri iki gice, tuzokwihweza imirongo myiza yo gukoresha no kukuyobora mu gushinga neza umugwi wa Telegram canke server ya Discord. Uzomenya kandi ingene woshiramwo ama robots akora vyinshi kugira ngo utunganye uburongozi bw’abanyagihugu.



**Igice ca 4: Gutanguza imihora yawe yo gusangirirako amakuru** (mu gifaransa)


Kuboneka ni urufunguruzo rwo gukura mu kibano cawe. Muri iki gice, uzomenya ingene wokora urubuga rwa Twitter rwihariye, kwifatanya n’amakoraniro yo mu karere, no gukoresha imbuga nka btcmap.org kugira ngo wongere uboneke kandi ukwegere abanywanyi bashasha.



**Igice ca 5: Gutegura inama ya Bitcoin**


Iki gice kivuga ingene umuntu yotunganya amakoraniro: kuva ku guhitamwo ahantu heza gushika ku gutegura iyo nama ubwayo. Turaza kandi gutohoza ingamba zo kwubaka ubudahemuka bw’abaje mu nama no kumenya ko umuryango wanyu ushobora kugumaho igihe kirekire.



**Igice ca 6: Ingero z'imigambi n'imigambi** (mu gifaransa)


Kugira ngo turenge amateraniro yoroshe, iki gice kirerekana imigambi nyayo kandi y’umwimerere: kuva ku bikorwa vy’insanganyamatsiko n’amahugurwa kugeza ku kurema ibintu vy’inyigisho no gukorana n’abandi mu karere. Uzomenya kandi ingene wohingura ibikoresho vy’umubiri n’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none kugira ngo utere imbere mu kibano cawe.



**Igice ca 7: Kwitega kazoza**


Aha, turaza gusuzuma iterambere ry’akameremere ry’ikibano cawe: ico wokora iyo gikuze ningoga cane, ingene wokwegeranya ishirahamwe ryawe, n’ingene wokwishura ku bidukikije vy’amategeko vy’urwanko. Iki gice kizogufasha kwitega izo ngorane no kuzitsinda.



**Igice ca 8: Guhuriza hamwe aba Bitcoin**


Igice ca nyuma gishingiye ku ntumbero y’umugambi wawe: guhuza abakunda Bitcoin bakikuje iyerekwa basangiye. Uzomenya ingene wotunganya uburyo bwawe bwo gukwegera no kugumiza abanywanyi, guha abandi ibikorwa neza, no gukura umuryango wawe mu buryo buramba.



Ni mwiteguye kwiga ingene mwokwubaka umuryango wa Bitcoin ukomeye kandi ufise ubuzima? Reka twibiremwo!



## Ni kuki wotangura umuryango canke meetup ya Bitcoin?


<chapterId>893ea248-7d5a-5b8e-948c-7031497b1031</chapterId>



### Ni itandukaniro irihe riri hagati y'umuryango n'uguhura?



Naho ata nsobanuro ikomeye y'ayo majambo mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin, umuryango utahurwa neza nk'umugwi w'abantu basangira ivyipfuzo kandi bagahanahana amakuru ubudasiba.


Ku rundi ruhande, iteraniro ni ikintu gitegurwa n’uwo mugwi kandi kigategurwa, gifise intumbero yihariye. Amakoraniro akenshi aba iyo umuryango umaze gushingwa.


Ku bijanye n'iyi nyigisho, twiyumvira ko imbere yo gutanguza inama ya Bitcoin, mwamaze guhura n'abantu bakeyi bashaka gukora ico gikorwa, mu yandi majambo, **umuryango urasanzwe uriho**.



### Ni kuki tworema umuryango wa Bitcoin?



Hari impamvu nyinshi zituma habaho umuryango wa Bitcoin:



- Gusangira ubumenyi & gukorana: Imiryango iha abanywanyi umwanya w’ivyiyumviro vya Exchange no gusangira ubumenyi. Gufashanya ni inkingi nyamukuru y’iterambere, bikaba bishoboza umwe wese kwihutisha inyigisho ziwe, cane cane bifitaniye isano na Bitcoin, bivanye n’ingene hari vyinshi vyo kuvumbura.



- Ubwubatsi bw’urunganwe: Ingamba n’imigambi myinshi irategurwa, yubakwa, kandi itegurwa muri iyo mibano.



Imiryango ya Bitcoin akenshi ikurikirana intumbero zagutse, cane cane inyigisho za bose ku:


1. Bitcoin ni iki;


2. Uko bikora;


3. Ivyo itanga mu kibano;


4. Gukuraho ibivugwa bizimiza canke bitagira ishingiro bivugwa n’abantu bamwebamwe bazwi cane muri Leta n’ibinyamakuru;


5. Itandukaniro ry’ishimikiro hagati ya Bitcoin n’ayandi mafaranga y’ivy’ubuhinga bwa none;


Mu kwubaka "ibihome" vya mbere vy'ejo, **imiryango ya Bitcoin kw'isi yose irakora nk'igihome co kurwanya amakuru atari yo n'ugucengera mu gihe itanga ahantu heza ho kwigira ivy'ishimikiro vya Bitcoin**. Batanga abashasha, mbere n’abakekeranya, ubutunzi n’amasano bikenewe kugira ngo bamenye ubushobozi nyakuri bwa Bitcoin.



![image](assets/fr/002.webp)



Ni vyiza kandi kumenya ko **umuryango ushobora kubaho wose kuri internet**, haba ari umugwi wa Telegram ("Bitcoin Londres"), umugwi wa Facebook ("Bitcoin Berlin"), umukozi wa Discord ("Bitcoin Madrid"), canke mbere urutonde rw'imeli ("Bitcoin Ubufaransa").


Mu yandi majambo, **umuryango ntukeneye amakoraniro y’umubiri kugira ngo utere imbere canke utangure imigambi. Intumbero yayo nyamukuru iguma ari imwe: gusangira, kwubaka, no kwigisha**.



### Ni kuki tworema ihuriro?



Nk’uko twabibonye, ​​**amateraniro akenshi aza inyuma y’aho umuryango ushinzwe kandi agafasha kuwuzuza**.


Uko urugendo rw’umunya bitcoiner rugenda ruratera imbere, kenshi haba icipfuzo, canke mbere n’ivyo umuntu akeneye, **guhura no gukorana n’abandi** bavuye mu bidukikije bimwe. Ivyo bishobora kuva ku kwumva uri wenyene canke gusa kutagira uwo ubwira ivyerekeye Bitcoin mu buzima bwa misi yose.


Amakoraniro aratanga ubumenyi bw’umuntu ku giti ciwe kandi bumuha ikaze kuruta guhamagara ku rubuga rw’amajwi canke rw’amasanamu. Yaba **ku vyo kunywa canke ku mfungurwa**, bararema ibibanza bitamenyerewe, vy’ubugenzi aho abantu bashobora Exchange ivyiyumviro ku mugaragaro kandi ataco baciyeko.



![image](assets/fr/003.webp)


Amakoraniro ya Bitcoin arashobora gukora vyinshi:



- Kuganira ku nsiguro z'ubuhinga canke imigambi iriko irabandanya
- Gufasha abashasha ba bitcoiners buhoro buhoro kwisuka cane mw'isi ya Bitcoin .
- Kumenyesha imigambi mishasha canke imigambi mishasha
- Guhana ibikoresho canke ibitabu
- Gusuzuma inyandiko mu gihe c'imbere
- Kwakira inama canke amahugurwa ku bintu vyihariye



Tuzovuga vyinshi muri ivyo bishoboka mu buryo burambuye mu gice ca 6/8 c'iri shure, citwa "[Ingero z'imigambi n' ingamba](1c643dc9-a15f-4f9e-93b6-cb3c58b4ee35/3213d87a-1998-5972-ab93-97a5769ba279).



Naho umuryango wose ufise umwidegemvyo wo kwishingira ivyiyumviro vyawo, gutanguza imigambi, no kugerageza mu buryo bwawo bwite, amateraniro ariko aragaragara ko ari **ingenzi ku guteza imbere Bitcoin no ku gukura kw’abakoresha amafaranga y’ibiceri**. **Kubitunganya bisaba umwanya n’inguvu**, ni co gituma iri shure risangira ubumenyi buhuriweko n’imiryango mirongo itandatu y’aba Bitcoin mu Bufaransa no mu Bubirigi](https://btcmap.org/communities/map#2/5.24949/0.20000) **kugira ngo bigufashe kwubaka umuryango wawe uko bishoboka kwose.



## None ukwiye gushinga hehe umuryango wawe?


<chapterId>72da5285-5395-592d-ab62-303a0aef3d6d</chapterId>



Ikibazo ca mbere co kwibaza ni iki: igihugu canyu gikwiye kuba hehe?

Iyi ngingo ni ngirakamaro cane nimba ushaka kuguma ufise uburenganzira bwo gutegura amakoraniro mu nyuma.


Iyo abagize ishengero bategerezwa gutwara imodoka amasaha abiri kugira ngo baje mu birori, birashoboka ko abazojayo bazoba bake. Ni co gituma guhitamwo **ahantu heza ho kuba mu kibano cawe, rero n'amakoraniro yawe yo muri kazoza, bigira ingaruka nyinshi ku gukura kwaco**.



Impanuro nkeyi zo kuyobora ingingo yawe:


1/ **Rema kandi ukuze umuryango wawe mu gisagara kiri hagati canke kinini**. Ivyo bizotuma vyoroha:



    - Kurondera ahantu heza ho gukorera amateraniro n’imigambi (ibiganiro, amahugurwa, guteza imbere kwemera Bitcoin, n’ibindi);
    - Kwongera inyiyumvo yo kwifatanya no kuba ababo mu bantu benshi;
    - Kunoza uburyo bwo gushika ku birori (ugutwara abantu bose, guhagarara, n'ibindi). Guhitamwo ahantu hafi y’ibinyabiziga vya bose n’aho abantu bahagarara ni vyiza cane.



2/ **Hitamwo izina rihuye n'igisagara cawe**. Ivyo bituma abantu boroherwa no kwifatanya n’abanyagihugu iwanyu. Ingero: Bitcoin Paris, Berlin, Satoshi Spritz Milano, n'ibindi.


3/ **Suzuma nimba hariho meet-up isanzwe iriho hafi yawe**.

Ushobora kuraba [btcmap.org](https://btcmap.org/imiryango/ikarita#0/0/0/), irimwo urutonde rw’imiryango myinshi ya Bitcoin n’amateraniro kw’isi yose.



![image](assets/fr/004.webp)



Inyuma y’ubushakashatsi, woshobora gusanga hariho iteraniro canke umuryango uriho mu gisagara cawe.

Bitcoin ni iyo kwegerejwe, rero ni ibisanzwe ko umuntu wese afise umwidegemvyo wo gukora ivyo yipfuza.


Ariko rero, **gufatanya inguvu kenshi biragira ico bivuze kuruta gucapura imiryango iri hafi no kugabanya ingaruka za buri wese**. Gutanguza umuryango mushasha aho uwusanzwe uriho birashobora gutuma haba uguhiganwa ata co bigamije.


Muri ubwo buryo, "itegeko ry'urukumu" ryoroshe rirashobora gufasha: nimba ata muryango wa Bitcoin ukora mu minota 30-45 uvuye aho uba, vyoshobora kuba vyiza utanguye uwundi mushasha. Ivyo bivuzwe, wama ufise umwidegemvyo wo gukora ivyo wipfuza.


Kandi n'ukuri, iyo umuryango w'abakuze "wateye imbere" mu nzira itagihuye n'agaciro kawe, kandi udafise umwanya canke ikigutera intege co "orange-pill", rero kwiremera Fork yawe ni uburyo bwo guhitamwo.



## Ni intumbero izihe zikwiye kuyobora abantu bo mu kibano canyu?


<chapterId>0aae1aa8-93ec-544a-ad88-22d32c619595</chapterId>



Umaze kumenya igisagara (canke akarere) k'abanyagihugu bawe, ni igihe co **gusobanura ico ushaka gushikako**.


Izo ntumbero zishobora gushirwaho neza igihe zitangura canke zigatunganirizwa mu nyuma, igihe abagize umuryango benshi bamaze kwinjira kandi umugwi wawe ukaba uriko uragira uruhara n’umwete.



Ehe ingingo zimwezimwe zo kuyobora ivyiyumviro vyawe:



1/ **Kubera iki ndiko ndarema iyi community nk'umunya bitcoiner?** Mbega ni ugusangira ubumenyi? Kwigisha abandi? Woba wigira ku bagenzi bawe bazi utuntu n’utundi kuruta? Woba uhuye n’abantu bafise ivyiyumviro nk’ivyawe? Nimwinjire mu bidukikije vya Bitcoin? Guhura n’abahinga? Canke gusa wonywa maze ukaronka abagenzi bashasha?



Inyishu zawe zizoguhindura aho uhagaze be n’ico ukora.


Nimba intumbero yawe ari **gusangira ubumenyi no kwigisha abantu ivyerekeye Bitcoin**, wibande ku **guhingura ibintu vy’inyigisho** (ingingo, ama podcast, amasanamu, ibiganiro, ama flyers, n’ibindi), ugakiza abandi amasaha atagira uko angana y’ubushakashatsi. Ushobora mbere kuja kure mu **gutegura amahugurwa, ibiganiro, n’inama**.



Nimba intumbero yawe ari uguhura gusa n’abandi ba bitcoiners no gusangira ubumenyi, urashobora gufata uburyo bwo kwiruhutsa, nk’uguhura kugira ngo unywe.

Muri ubwo buryo, uzotuma habaho ubucuti bworoshe. Ariko rero, wama wibuka ko **kunywa inzoga atari yo nzira nziza yo kwubaka isi y'ejo**.


Ni co gituma **gushinga intumbero n’imigambi nyayo ari ngombwa**.




2/ Noba nkwiye guha umuryango wanje **urugero rw’umubiri**? Nimba ari ukwo biri, ni ibirori ibihe mukwiye gutegura?



Ivyo uzogira vya mbere birashoboka ko ari uguhura. Intumbero yayo nyamukuru: gukoranya aba bitcoiners bo mu karere iwanyu.



Ariko rero, nk’uko tuzobibona mu nyuma muri [Ingero z’imigambi n’imigambi], birashobora kwubakwa mu buryo bwinshi, bifise intumbero nyinshi:**



- Gufatanya gukorana n’abandi n’ugushikiriza inyigisho ku ciyumviro ca Bitcoin;
- Gutegura iyerekanwa ry’umwuga ry’imiti ya Bitcoin;
- Ingamba zo guharira ku bacuruzi bo mu karere batera imiceri;

n’ibindi.


Bivanye n’ibintu (ibiganiro vy’ubuhinga canke amahugurwa y’ibikorwa), vyoshobora mbere kuba vyiza **gutegura ibindi birori** hanze y’aho muhora muhurira.



![image](assets/fr/005.webp)




3/ Nshaka gute ko umuryango wanje ugira ico uterereye ku bidukikije vya Bitcoin?



Iki ni ikibazo giteye imbere cane, ariko birabereye ko tuvyiyumvira hakiri kare.


Nimba iciyumviro cawe ari **ugushinga umuryango ushize hamwe, ufise intumbero** wibanda ku gutera imiti y’umuceri abantu benshi bishobotse, rero:



- Gukorana n’imiryango iriho kugira ngo ushobore kuronka amakuru yagutse, uburyo bufunguye, n’infashanyo y’agaciro iyo bikenewe.
- Gutunganya no guha abandi ibikorwa mu mugwi wawe kugira ngo wagure kandi ushire mu rugero rwiza utwigoro twawe.



**Shiraho intego z'igihe gito, hagati n'igihe kirekire**

Uburyo ngirakamaro bwo gutegura ni ugushinga intumbero z’igihe cose:



- Igihe gito: kwubaka urubuga rwo guhanahana amakuru no gushinga inzira zo guhanahana amakuru n’abanyagihugu.
- Igihe giciye hagati: guhingura ibikoresho vy’inyigisho (ingingo, amakete, amasanamu).
- Igihe kirekire: gutanguza amakoraniro y’umubiri adasanzwe.


Amateraniro amaze gushingwa, woshobora gushimikira kuri:



- Igihe gito: kwakira amahugurwa canke ibiganiro ku bintu vyihariye
- Igihe giciye hagati: gukora amasekeza yo gukangurira abantu canke kwigisha Bitcoin
- Igihe kirekire: gushinga ishirahamwe canke ubufatanye butegekanijwe n’ibarabara ryemera Bitcoin nk’ukwishurwa.


Nimwitegure: **kwubaka umuryango bisaba inguvu**


Gushinga umuryango ntibishika mw’ijoro rimwe. Bisaba umwanya, ikintu kivyurirwa umutima be n’ugutunganya ibintu.

Rimwe na rimwe, birashobora mbere gutuma wumva ucitse intege cane cane iyo ata n’umwe yerekanye, kandi ukumva ko ari wewe wenyene wikoreye iyo ntumwa ku bitugu.


Ni co gituma **iri shure ryagenewe gusangira ubutunzi n'impanuro z'agaciro, gufasha imiryango gukura neza, amaherezo igashobora kwitunga.**.



# Gutegura akaranga k'abanyagihugu Bitcoin

## Ni kuki kandi woshobora gute kwiremera akaranga kawe?


<chapterId>b48a18cc-afdf-5216-802c-32647b5cd8a3</chapterId>



Kuri iyi ntambwe, twiyumvira ko:



- Urazi aho umuryango wawe (canke ihuriro ry’ubudasiba ryo muri kazoza) rizoba rishingiye.
- Washizeho intumbero z’ibihe bitandukanye, naho nyene zitarashika ku mpera.


Intambwe ikurikira mu kwubaka umuryango wawe ni **ugusobanura akaranga kawo**, izina ryawo n’uburyo bwawo bwo kubona.


Iki gice kiratanga inyigisho n’imiti y’amaporogarama y’intango kugira ngo igufashe guhingura akaranga k’amashusho k’abanyagihugu bawe, mbere n’ubuhinga bw’intango.


**Kubera iki akaranga gahambaye?**

Abantu bumva ko bafise uruhara kandi ko biyemeje iyo bashobora kwifatanya n’umugwi. Akaranga gakomeye karafasha abantu bo mu kibano cawe gutandukana n’abandi kandi bagatuma umuntu yumva ko ari uwabo.


Vyose bitangura no guhitamwo izina, kandi igihugu cose kenshi gifise imigenderanire yaco:



- Ubufaransa: Benshi mu baturage barenga 60 bakoresha amazina nka "Bitcoin [Igisagara]" canke "[Igisagara] Bitcoin". Amatsinda yabo ya Telegram akenshi akurikiza iyi nzira: "Ikoraniro rya Bitcoin [CITY]".
- Ubudage: Igitekerezo ca Einundzwanzig (bisobanura "21") catumye habaho amazina nka "Einundwanzig [Igisagara]".
- Ubutaliyano: Urubuga rwa Satoshi Spritz rukoresha amazina nka Satoshi "Spritz [Igisagara]".

## Ni kuki wotanguza inama idasanzwe n’abanyagihugu bo mu kibano cawe?

Ivyo bizotuma ubona umwanya wawe, uwo woshobora gukoresha mu kwiyumvira gukora ubwoko bumwe bw’ihuriro. Ivyo bizorushiriza kugira akamaro igihe uzoba utanguye kwumva umunezero nyawo mu kibano cawe.


Nk'uko twabibonye, ​​**inama ya Bitcoin ishobora kubonwa nk'"urugero" rukurikira rw'umuryango**. Ku ruhande rumwe, bituma **abanywanyi bahura ubwabo**, ku rundi ruhande, bifasha **kwigisha abashasha bashimishijwe n’ivyo bidukikije**.


Igikorwa kimeze nk'iki kiragufasha, ku rugero rwawe, **gukora ubutumwa bujanye n'ivyo Bitcoin** mu gisagara cawe, bushobora gukura uko igihe kigenda kirarenga uko umuryango wawe ufata imigambi.

## Ihuriro rya mbere: ryari, kandi kubera iki?


<chapterId>f3f8a614-cfb4-5994-a2e8-d56e6f8e3347</chapterId>



Ushobora gutanguza ihuriro inyuma y’indwi nkeyi gusa umaze gushinga umuryango wawe.

Ariko rero, kuri iyo ntambwe umugwi wawe kumbure uzoba ukiri muto, ivyo bisigura ko hari amahirwe ko ata n’umwe azoza.


Ni vyiza gutegura ihuriro umaze kuronka n’imiburiburi abanywanyi bakeyi bakora cane mu mihora yawe yo guhanahana amakuru, nk’akarorero, amajana makeyi. Ivyo birashobora gushika ningoga, ariko kandi bivana n’ingeso z’abantu.


Uhejeje kwumva iyo nkuru, nusabe ko mwohura ubwawe. Niba abandi bavyishimiye, igisigaye ni **gushinga itariki muzohura ubwa mbere**.


Aho gutora itariki wewe nyene, akenshi ni vyiza **gukora ubushakashatsi nk’imisi cumi imbere y’aho ufise imisi n’ibihe vyinshi bishoboka**. Muri ubwo buryo, urashobora gukoresha amajwi kugira ngo abantu benshi baze, wubake umunezero, kandi ukoreshe neza umwanya wawe.



![image](assets/fr/029.webp)



Ni vyiza ko utokwinjira ufise ivyo witega ku bijanye n’ingene iyo nama izogenda, abantu bazoza bangahe canke ingingo zizoganirwako. Ivyo bituma abaje mu nama badacika intege kandi ntibumve ko bakozweko umukazo.


Reka ibintu bigende nk’uko bisanzwe, kumbure ku vyo kunywa. Ibiyago bikwiye kuba bitagoranye, kandi nimba bikenewe, urashobora gusubiza ikiyago mu bugwaneza kuri Bitcoin nimba gitangura guhungabana.


Nimba ushaka kwitegurira ibihe vy’ugutekana, urashobora gutegura ibikorwa bikeyi canke ingingo zo kuganirako, nk’akarorero:



- yerekana **[Mempool](https://Mempool.space/)** kuri mudasobwa canke kuri telefone ngendanwa;
- kuzana igitabu;
- kuzana **Umunyabwenge**, Bitaxe, FPGA canke mbere ASIC;
- kugira **igitabu ca Bitcoin**;
- gutegura ubwonko igishushanyo canke ikimenyetso ca Bitcoin c’abanyagihugu banyu;
- gukina **[umukino w’amakarata Bitcoin]**;
- yerekana **[btcmap.org](https://btcmap.org/)** ikarita y’imiryango n’abadandaza;
- kohereza canke kwakira Bitcoin ukoresheje **Imiravyo**.


Igihe ihuriro ryanyu ririko riratangura, kenshi si co gihe ciza co gutanga ikiganiro mu buryo bubereye. Iyo abaje ari bake, ikiyago gifunguye kandi kidasanzwe akenshi kirakora neza kuruta.



![image](assets/fr/030.webp)



Inyuma y’uguhura kwawe kwa mbere, birashoboka ko uzoshaka gutegura uwundi. Naho igisagara canyu kikiri gito, ni vyiza ko muguma muhitamwo amatariki biciye mu matora, nk’akarorero, rimwe mu kwezi canke inyuma y’amezi abiri. Ivyo murashobora kubiganirako mu nzira zanyu zo guhanahana amakuru.


Iyo umugwi usanzwe w’abakoresha bitcoiners n’abashimishijwe, birabereye gushinga urutonde rusubiramwo kubera imvo zibiri:

1) bituma umuntu wese yoroherwa no gutegura imbere y’igihe;

2) bifasha **gushinga "akamenyero"**. Naho umuntu yohagarika gusuzuma imirongo yawe ubudasiba, azomenya igihe iyo meet-up izobera kandi ashobora kuza nk’uko bisanzwe.


Iyi ngingo ya kabiri ni imwe mu mpamvu zituma, **mu Bufaransa, amateraniro menshi aba ku wa gatatu wa mbere w'ukwezi kwose** (raba umugambi w'"aba ambasaderi", watangujwe na [DecouvreBitcoin](https://decouvrebitcoin.fr/) mu 2022).

Mu Budagi no mu Butaliyano, nta musi w'igihugu ushizweho.


Ariko rero, kugira amateraniro yose mu gihugu cose ku musi umwe biratuma haba ingorane yo gushiramwo ibintu vyose hamwe: bibuza abakoresha ama bitcoin kuja mu birori mu mibano iri hafi kandi bishobora no gutuma habaho ivyiyumviro vy’umutekano. Kuganira n’abategura amakoraniro bo mu babanyi birashobora kubafasha kwemeranya ku ntunganyo nziza.


Uko umuryango wawe ukura kandi amateraniro akakwegera abantu benshi, woshobora:


- gushinga intumbero yihariye ku makoraniro yose;
- canke mbere **wongereko ikintu ca kabiri buri kwezi**.


Mu gihe ca mbere, naho nyene umuntu wese azirikana ivyo akunda, intumbero itomoye irashobora gutuma abaje mu nama bavayo bamaze kwiga ikintu gishasha kandi bagatuma ibiyago biguma vyibanda kuri Bitcoin.

Mu gihe ca kabiri, ikintu ca kabiri co mu kwezi coshobora gushikirizwa insiguro yihariye, nk’ibiganiro vy’intango canke amahugurwa (Mining, Umuravyo, gushiramwo amanode, n’ibindi).


![image](assets/fr/031.webp)



Zirikana ko **ivyo bisaba gutegura cane no gukoresha umwanya wawe mwinshi**. Ku bw’amahirwe, mu [gice ca 6/8 c’iri shure](https://planb.network/amashure/1c643dc9-a15f-4f9e-93b6-cb3c58b4ee35/3213d87a-1998-5972-ab93-97a77 ibikoresho vy’inkomoko yuguruye kugira ngo bifashe.


Uko biri kwose, vyose bivana n’umugambi w’ibikorwa w’igihe gito, hagati n’igihe kirekire mwebwe canke umuryango wanyu mwishizeho.



## Ugushaka ahantu


<chapterId>405d389e-6a32-5777-af8d-293380d2e8fd</chapterId>



Kuronka aho uzokorera ibirori vyawe akenshi si ikintu coroshe. Hejuru y'ivyo, ni ngombwa ko iki kibanza **kiba kiri mu gisagara c'aho uba**.


Hariho ibintu vyinshi umuntu akwiye kwitwararika, harimwo:

1) **Aho hantu**: mbega umuntu ashobora kuhashika bitagoranye akoresheje imodoka za bose? Mu modoka? Mbega parking irahari?

2) Mbega aho hantu hararyoshe kandi hatagira **urusaku** rwinshi? Woba uriko urarondera ahantu hasanzwe (ahantu ho gukorera hamwe, icumba c’amakoraniro) canke ikindi kintu kidasanzwe (ibarabara, iresitora)?

3) Mbega irafise ubushobozi buhagije bwo kukwakira, mbere no mw’ijoro ry’umukino wa rugby canke Champions League?

4) Yoba ifise igikoresho co gucapura amasanamu n’ahantu h’agacerere ho gushikiriza ibiganiro?

5) Kandi mu nyuma, nimba ari bar canke restaurant, menu yoba itanga ivyo umuntu wese arya n’ivyo anywa?


Vyongeye, ng’ibi ingingo zitatu zihambaye umuntu akwiye kuguma yibuka:


- Niba utegura gucapura umugoroba mu bice bibiri: ica mbere, ikiganiro mu kibanza gisanzwe kugira ngo umuntu wese yibande, hanyuma ukimukira mu kabare canke mu restora kugira ngo ubandanye mu kirere giteye umunezero, ubumenyi bwerekana ko guhindura ahantu hagati mu mugoroba kenshi bituma bamwebamwe mu bari muri iyo nama bavayo mu gihe c’akaruhuko. Ubwo buryo rero ntibubereye.
- Mu makoraniro yanyu ya mbere, birashoboka ko abazoza bazoba bake, kandi birashoboka ko mutazogira ibiganiro bimenyerewe. Muri ivyo, nta nkeka ko wimenyekanisha kuri nyen’iyo nzu canke ngo umusigurire intumbero zawe. Ariko rero, **igihe amateraniro azoba asanzwe kandi akagira abantu benshi, birabereye ko ubigira kugira ngo werekane ubuhinga kandi ugaragaze icipfuzo cawe co kugaruka buri kwezi**. Ivyo bishobora gutuma haba ibiganiro vyinshi (ugufata umwanya mu buryo bwihuse buri kwezi, gufata ibinyobwa n’/canke ivyo kurya ku buntu, gugabanya ibiciro, ibiyago vyerekeye Bitcoin, kwemera kwishura Bitcoin, n’ibindi). Biragaragara ko nimba uriko urarondera canecane ahantu harimwo igikoresho co gushiramwo amasanamu kugira ngo ushireho ibiganiro, bisa n’ibihambaye gusaba inama (amaso mu yandi, canke mbere kuri telefone) na nyen’aho hantu be n’/canke umuyobozi kugira ngo mushikirize umugambi wawe n’intumbero zawe.



![image](assets/fr/032.webp)




- uwa nyuma, hari imiryango **ishaka gusa gukorera ibirori vyayo mu bibanza vyemera amahera ya Bitcoin**, ivyo bikaba bitahurwa. Muri iki gihe, ni iciyumviro ciza **gutangura urondera ubucuruzi bwemera Bitcoin mu gisagara cawe** (kuri [btcmap.org](https://btcmap.org/map/) maze ukabamenyesha kugira ngo urabe nimba buhuye n’ivyo usaba. Nimba ata kintu kibereye kiboneka, urashobora kugerageza abaguzi benshi, Ni co gituma kenshi ari vyiza ko udashiraho ivyo nk'intumbero kuva mu ntango, ahubwo ukavyiyumvamwo igihe umuryango wawe umaze gushinga imizi neza kandi ufise "inkomezi z'umuriro" zihagije.


Kandi, igihe utanguza ihuriro ryawe, ntukekeranye **guhindura aho uzobera mu mezi make ya mbere**. Ivyo bizokugirira akamaro **kwubaka ubucuti n’abarongozi batandukanye**, gutohoza ibirere bitandukanye, no kwegeranya amakuru yerekeye abashobora kuba abashitsi muri kazoza.


Mu nyuma, iyo umugwi wawe umaze gukura, woshobora mbere gufata umuhingamo wo gusura amahinguriro menshi n’amabarabara, ukabaza nimba bafise ikibanza kibereye co kwakira ibirori vyawe vy’ukwezi n’amakoraniro yawe.



## Imbere y'uko bahura


<chapterId>c5218943-580d-5efc-89ed-6b5d728816d6</chapterId>



Mutegura gutegura inama mu ndwi ziza?

Akira urutonde rwo kugufasha kwitegura:

1/ **Wemeze aho izobera**

Baza aho uzokwakira uwo muhango maze uwubike ku mubare w’abantu ugereranywa.

2/ **Kumenyesha ivyabaye**:


    - Sangira itariki, isaha n’aho hantu ku mbuga zanyu zo gusangirirako amakuru no ku yindi mihora yo guhanahana amakuru. Nimushiremwo urubuga rwo kwiyandikisha nimba bikenewe.
    - Rema ishusho yihariye (Igicapo ca Canva nk'akarorero, raba igice ca 2/8 "[Guhingura akaranga ka Bitcoin yawe umuryango] (https://umugambi.urubuga/amashure/1c643dc9-a15f-4f9e-93b6-cb3c58b4ee35/b48a18cc-afdf-5216-802c-32647b5cd8a3)").
    - Gutuma itangazo rigaragara cane mu gushiramwo itangazo kuri Telegram na Twitter, no gukoresha "@everyone" kuri Discord.
    - Mushire amatangazo imisi 7 gushika kuri 10 imbere y’uko uwo muhango uba kugira ngo abazoba baje bashobore gutegura imbere y’igihe.



![image](assets/fr/033.webp)



3/ **Ibutse abakwumviriza**

Imisi ibiri canke itatu imbere y’uko uwo muhango uba, wongere ushire ahabona kandi ushiremwo amatangazo yawe kugira ngo abantu bayiboneke.

4/ **Guhindura ahantu**

Umusi umwe imbere y’aho (canke imisi ibiri imbere y’aho), nusuzume abanditse maze umenyeshe aho hantu umubare wa nyuma w’abazoja muri iyo nama witezwe.


Intambwe z'inyongera zo gutuma umusi mukuru wawe ugaragara:


- Sohora amakete n'amafoto
- Gutegura ikiganiro ku ciyumviro kinaka
- Gutegura igikorwa canke amahugurwa



## Umusi w'ihuriro


<chapterId>cf60067b-591f-5fdf-9b9e-d442aad0e8f9</chapterId>



Umusi munini urashitse! Isaha imwe canke zibiri imbere y’uko uwo muhango utanguye, wohereze ivyibutsa biciye ku nzira z’itumanaho z’abanyagihugu iwanyu. Subira wemeze igihe co gutangura maze umenyeshe abaje mu nama ingene bokumenya, nk’akarorero, mu kuvuga ko uzoba wambaye T-shirt n’agatambaro ka Bitcoin.


Uko ama meetups yawe azogenda arakura kandi ugatangura kwakira abantu barenga 20, gutegura aho uzobera bizorushiriza kuba ivy’agaciro.


Ehe ico wokora:

1) **Shika kare** kugira ngo ushireho ibintu.

2) **Mutunganye ikibanza** bivanye n'ivyo mukeneye n'ubushobozi bw'aho hantu. Nimba muri abantu batandatu gusa, nta setup nkeyi ikenewe. Ariko iyo umaze gushika ku bantu 10-20, ivyicaro vyuzuye birashobora gutuma abantu badashobora gukorana. Muri ivyo, nuhitemwo ahantu ho guhagarara harimwo ameza maremare ateye neza nimba ariho, mu gihe uzoba ugitanga intebe nkeyi (vyiza ni ndende). Nimba hari ikiganiro gitegekanijwe, nugumye uhagaze mu gihe kinini c’umugoroba kandi uzane intebe imbere gato y’insiguro.

3) Wongereko utuntu dutoduto:


    - Shira igicapo ku bwinjiriro kugira ngo ugaragaze aho uri (koresha Patafix ubanze uronke uruhusha rw’aho hantu).
    - Erekana amafoto afise insiguro ya Bitcoin imbere (urupapuro rwera rwa Bitcoin, itegeko ry’abanyagihugu bawe, imigambi iriho ubu, n’ibindi).
    - Shira amakete, amakete canke ibintu vyo gutangura ibiyago ku meza, nk’akarorero: Nerdminer, Hardware Wallet, laptop yerekana Mempool yayo, ASIC Miner, canke isahani y’icuma yo gukingira imfunguruzo z’ibanga.



![image](assets/fr/034.webp)



Nimba urindiriye abantu bakeyi gusa kandi utaragiranira ubucuti n’abarongoye aho hantu, wirinde kuzana no kwerekana ibikoresho vyawe vyose ubwo nyene. Igihe ukwizigirana kuzoba gushitse, uzogira umwidegemvyo mwinshi wo gutunganya ikibanza uko wipfuza.


Ubwa nyuma, fata ivyo kunywa maze urindire abantu gushika. Kandi wibuke: ntucike intege iyo ata n’umwe aza ubwa mbere. Uzane igitabu canke videwo kugira ngo urabe, kugira ngo ntihagire ico ubona.



## Inyuma y'ihuriro


<chapterId>3e881af9-9c09-5721-81ee-c5cda3b109c5</chapterId>



Iyo meetup irangiye, ni igihe co gusukura abakozi b’aho hantu. Imbere y’uko ugenda, nusabe ko wofasha abakozi canke abarongozi. Ivyo vyoshobora kuba vyoroshe nk’ugukoranya ibirahuri vyose ku meza imwe canke, nimba uri hafi y’ibarabara, ukabigarura imbere y’uko ugenda.


Iyo umaze gusubira i muhira, birashoboka ko uzomara imisi ufise Bitcoin mu muzirikanyi wawe. Ico ni co gihe ciza co gufata umutima w’abanyagihugu bawe no gukusanya inyishu z’abaje mu nama. Urashobora:


- Mushire ubutumwa bwo gukenguruka ku mihora yanyu yo guhanahana amakuru kandi musangire n’abandi amahuzu canke ibikoresho vyose vyavuzwe mu gihe c’ico gikorwa.
- Tuma abazoba bari muri iyo nama bavuge ivyo boshobora guhindura mu nama izokurikira.
- Tanga gutegura ikintu cawe gikurikira.


Uzoheza ubone amayinga makeyi yo gutegura ivyiyumviro bishasha, amashusho canke imigambi kugira ngo inama ikurikira ibe nziza kuruta.



## Akamaro ko gushiramwo no kugumiza abaje mu nama


<chapterId>f5355e35-4b07-527c-88a3-8535cba7592c</chapterId>



Roma ntiyubatswe mu musi umwe, kandi n’abanyagihugu banyu ntibazoba. Inguvu zikikuje Bitcoin ziva mu gukora amakoraniro n’ibirori bihoraho.


Kugira ivyo bishoboke, ikintu kimwe ni ngombwa: **gukomeza abaje mu nama**.


Bishobora gutangura n’umugwi mutoyi w’abakunda cane bitcoiners, bafise intumbero yo guhura ubudasiba no kuvuga ivya Bitcoin. Uko igihe kigenda kirarenga, bivanye n'uko umuntu wese ariho n'ivyo akunda, urashobora **gusangira n'umugwi ibikorwa bimwebimwe vyo gutegura**, canke ugatumira **abanyamuryango b'ishimikiro kugira ngo baje mu migambi yihariye**. Ivyo bishobora kuba ari ivy’umwihariko mu kibano canyu, mu mahuriro yanyu canke mu gisagara canyu canke mbere bikaba bihumekewe n’imigambi myinshi yashikirijwe mu [gice ca 6/8 c’iki ivyigwa] (ivyigwa] (1c643dc9-a15f-4f9e-93b6-cb3c58b4ee35/3213d87a-1998-5972-ab93-97a5769ba279). **Ivyo ntibitera imbere gusa Commitment mu bagize umuryango wawe ariko kandi birafasha umuryango wawe gukura no gutera imbere.**.


Uko meetups zawe zitera imbere, amaso mashasha azoboneka: abantu baguze BTC na/canke altcoins mu bihe vya kera, abakunda ubuhinga bashaka gukomeza gutahura, canke abatanguje buzuye bashaka kumenya ivy’iyi nzira yo kwishura idashobora gusuzumwa, yegerejwe, igaragara, kandi ibereye ikora ata n’imwe isabwa kandi ata n’umwe yizigira.


Kugira ngo umenye neza ko umuntu wese yumva ko ari mu birori vyawe, niwibande ku bintu bibiri nyamukuru:


- Ugushiramwo abantu bose**: Umuntu wese azoza akwiye kwumva ko yakiriwe neza. Niwihatire kuramutsa abashasha, ubaze igituma baje, wongere ubamenyeshe abandi. Iyo abantu bumva ko barimwo, birashoboka cane ko bagaruka kumbure mbere bagafasha mu gutegura ibintu vyo muri kazoza.
- Ukwihangana n’ugufunguka**: Ivyo bisaba umwanya n’intumbero. Niba uriko uravugana n’umuntu yiyemeje gukora shitcoiner (canke ikibi kuruta, XRP Maximalist), birashobora kuba ikigeragezo co kubarukana. Ariko mu kwifatanya n’abandi mu kiyago, woshobora gukuraho ukutumvikana canke ivyiyumviro bitari vyo. Niba bafise umutima ukunze wo kwumviriza, boshobora kuba biteguriye gutahura vy'ukuri Bitcoin.



![image](assets/fr/035.webp)



Uvuye ku vyo ukora, wiyumvire ivyabaye muri rusangi:


- Mbega **aho hantu hari heza**?
- Mbega **vyari vyoroshe kuronka?**
- Ni kuki hari abantu biyandikishije ariko ntibaje?
- Ni ibiganiro ibihe vyabaye? Mbega ivyo biganiro vyari vy’ubuhinga cane ku bantu batangura?
- None ivyo biganiro vyoba vyari vy’inzoga nyinshi cane?


Umwe wese muri bo arafise kamere yiwe, intumbero ziwe be n’ivyo akunda, ni co gituma ivyo azoshikirwa bizoba bitandukanye. Ariko iyo ahantu habereye, abantu benshi bafise umutima wuguruye, kandi imigambi ikaba ari iyo ukuri, hariho imvo zose zotuma iyo nama igenda neza.


Kubaka meetup bifata umwanya ariko ikiruta vyose, bisaba gutegura. Ni umugambi w’abantu, kandi uzokora gusa kandi ukure mu kiringo kirekire iyo uteguwe neza, wuguruye, kandi ushiramwo bose.


Mu mpera, ni wewe ushobora gufata ingingo y'aho "ugufungura no gushiramwo abantu bose" bikwiye gushika kure. Ibuka gusa: umu shitcoiner akenshi ni umu bitcoiner gusa mu gukora.

## Ni kuki woja kure y’ukunywa inzoga gusa?


<chapterId>3213d87a-1998-5972-ab93-97a5769ba279</chapterId>



Uko amabuye azogenda aratera imbere, ni ko n’umuryango wawe uzotera imbere. Amaso mashasha azokwifatanya, ayandi azogenda ariko ingingo ngenderwako ikwiye kuguma ari imwe: guteza imbere ibidukikije vya Bitcoin no gukomeza ubucuti hagati y’abagize umuryango.


Hariho imigambi myinshi n’imigambi myinshi ishobora gushirwa mu ngiro. Iki gice kiratanga urutonde rudahera rw’ivyiyumviro bishobora gushirwa mu ngiro mu kibano canke mu gihe c’inama. Ushobora guhitamwo izo zigutera intege mbere n'ivyiyumviro vyawe bwite muri iri shure [mu gukora "Igisabwa co Gukura" kuri GitHub](https://github.com/PlanB-Network/Bitcoin-ibirimwo-ivy'inyigisho).


Imbere y’uko twisuka muri vyo, reka dusubire ku ciyumviro twamaze kuvuga.


Iyo uteguye inama ya Bitcoin, uhura n’abantu bashasha. Gukorana n’abandi ni ngombwa kandi inzoga (canke inzoga muri rusangi) zirashobora gutuma haba ikirere c’ibirori. Ariko birabereye ko tubizirikana:


- Inzoga zituma ubushobozi bwo gutahura ibintu buhungabana nk’ukwiyumvira no gutahura, ivyo bikaba bishobora gutuma abaje mu nama bigorana gukurikira ibiganiro canke ibiganiro bikomeye.
- Inzoga na zo nyene zirashobora kuba umugenzo wo guhumuriza: buri kwezi, usangira "Bitcoin moment" yawe n'abagenzi mu gihe c'inzoga. Naho iyo ari uburyo bwiza bwo guhuza, mbega vy’ukuri kunywa inzoga ni co kintu nyamukuru kigamije mu nama yawe? Canke woshima ko Exchange ikora ubushakashatsi, ukigira ku bandi, ugafasha abashasha kumenya Bitcoin?


Umuntu wese afise ivyo akunda, ivyo yemera n’akaranga kiwe. Ivyo sivyo gucira urubanza meetup imwe ku yindi. Naho ari ukwo, naho kenshi ari hejuru y’ikiyeri tuvuga ivy’uguhindura isi, amahinduka ntatangura, canke ngo akure, muri ubwo buryo.


Urufunguruzo ni ukubimenya gusa.

## Gutegura amakoraniro afise insiguro, n’iterambere ry’umwaka


<chapterId>46afdbcc-a775-5c16-b20a-34956690d58f</chapterId>



Umaze kwubaka umugwi nyamukuru w’abantu nka cumi, urashobora gutangura kwongerako ibiganiro ku kintu cose. Ivyo bishobora gukurikira insanganyamatsiko yatangajwe imbere y’igihe canke bikaba bitandukanye rwose n’ibindi.


Igihe umuntu ategura ibiganiro canke amahugurwa, birafasha gutuma haba ugutera imbere gutomoye kandi gutunganijwe neza. Intumbero ni **gupfuka ivyiyumviro nyamukuru vya Bitcoin intambwe ku yindi**, bigatuma abaje mu nama babona neza iyo nsiguro bavuye mu mfuruka nyinshi.


Mu Bufaransa, mu ci c’umwaka w’2023, abategura amakoraniro barakoze ku **iterambere ry’umwaka**.

Uretse agaciro gakomeye k’inyigisho, iyo nzira ituma bishoboka gusangira inyandiko zifunguye n’ibipande vy’amashusho hagati y’imigwi, buhoro buhoro bikaba vyubaka ububiko bw’ibikoresho rusangi. Iryo shirahamwe ryakiriwe neza kandi ritegerezwa gukura mu myaka iri imbere.


Aha hepfo hariho ingene amateraniro y’igifaransa azogenda mu mwaka w’2023-2024. Ku nsanganyamatsiko yose, hariho ikiganiro gifunguye be n’amasanamu, ivyo bishobora kuguha ivyiyumviro vy’ingingo woshobora gushikiriza mu birori vyawe bwite.


Ibindi bikoresho bishobora guhindurwa birashobora kandi kuronswa muri [Igikoresho co kwigisha Bitcoin](https://planb.network/fr/resources/bet)!

## Rema ububiko bw'ibitabu


<chapterId>acc94093-f8b3-51b8-9359-348e9e075d38</chapterId>



Uwundi mugambi ushobora gushinga mu buryo bworoshe ni **gukora ububiko bw'ibitabu mu makoraniro yawe**.


Woshobora gutumira abazoja muri iyo nama bipfuza gusangira ivyo basoma ngo bazane igitabu kijanye n’igitabu Bitcoin. Iyo uwundi muntu ari muri iyo nama ashaka kuyigurana, inguzanyo yoshobora gutunganirizwa na nyen’iyo nzu muri Exchange kugira ngo umuntu ayishiremwo, nk’akarorero, amasatoshi 21.000 biciye kuri Lightning.


Ku nyen’itongo, iyo nkuru irafasha gupfuka ivyo yoshobora gutakaza canke kwiba. Ku wuguze, ni ikintu kimutera intege co kugaruka mu nama izoza kugira ngo asubire kuronka amahera yabo.


Niba ufise ishirahamwe kandi ushaka gushiramwo igice c’amahera yawe mu bikoresho vy’inyigisho, urashobora kandi kugura ibitabu vyinshi vyo gutanga mu **bubiko bwawe bw’ibitabu bwa Bitcoin**, biboneka mu nama yose.



![image](assets/fr/039.webp)


Kugira ngo dushigikire ibidukikije, **[umunyamakuru Consensus Network](https://konsensus.network/) waramaze guha amakoraniro mpuzamakungu ya Bitcoin n’imiryango igiciro c’ibitabu vyayo vyose kuva mu mpera z’umwaka w’2023, ku bijanye n’ugusaba ibitabu bitatu canke birenze**.


Kugira ngo wungukire kuri iyo nkuru y’igabanywa, **ushobora guhamagara [ikigo gisohora ibitabu ca Konsensus](https://twitter.com/KonsensusFR) kuri Twitter**.


Nimba udafise amahera, urashobora guca utanga iyo nkuru ku bantu bo mu kibano cawe.


Nimba wewe ubwawe udafise amahera, urashobora guca utanga iyo discount ku banywanyi bawe. Nk’akarorero, umuteguzi umwe yoshobora gutora amategeko y’abanywanyi, agatanga amategeko menshi ku giciro kigabanutse, maze agatanga ibitabu mw’ikoraniro rikurikira, amahera akaza gutoranywa muri Lightning ico gihe.


Woshobora kandi kwerekana igitabu c’ugutiza ibitabu mu makoraniro yawe kugira ngo usigurire neza abashasha amategeko agenga ivy’ugutiza.


Aha niho akarorero k'inkomoko yuguruye ushobora gukoresha no guhindura:

[Itegeko ry'inguzanyo y'ibitabo](ku rubuga rwa interineti)



![image](assets/fr/040.webp)



Ubwa nyuma, kugira ngo uremeshe abantu gusoma ibijanye n'igitabu ca Bitcoin, woshobora kuja mu **amasomero ya komine yo mu karere** kugira ngo urabe nimba bafise ibitabu vyerekeye igitabu ca Bitcoin.

Nibabigira, ubaze abacungezi b’ibitabo nimba hari **ameza azunguruka** yateguwe ku ciyumviro, maze ubahe amakuru y’aho muzohura.

Nimba atari vyo, nubabwire ngo baronke ibitabu bikeyi mu rutonde rwabo rwatowe imbere y’igihe. [Ihuriro ry’Ubumwe bw’abamenyeshamakuru](https://konsensus.network/) rishobora kuba isoko y’ivyiyumviro.

## Gutegura ivyo gutanga iyo umuntu apfuye

## Gutanga inyigisho mu mashure no muri kaminuza


<chapterId>65efdb44-e3d4-567c-aedf-972765753d73</chapterId>



Birumvikana ko urutonde rw’imigambi yavuzwe haruguru rutari rwose. Muri rusangi, ivyo bishobora kuba imigambi yo mu mutima y’abanyagihugu canke amakoraniro yabo, ishobora gushirwa mu ngiro mu buryo bworoshe uko itera imbere.


None reka duhindukire ku bijanye n’ugushira mu bwato.


Nk'uko mubizi, uburyo bumwe bwiza **bwo gushikira abantu benshi icarimwe ni ugutegura inyigisho ku buntu**. Ivyo bishobora gukorwa mu mashure yisumbuye no muri kaminuza, mu kumenyesha abakiri bato bose (n’abatari abakiri bato cane) Bitcoin, baba ari bashasha rwose muri yo canke barondera kurushiriza gutahura.


Gushikiriza insiguro abantu amajana canke mbere amajana bisaba ibirenze ubuhinga bwo mu vy’ubuhinga gusa. Uzokenera:


- Ukwizigira mu kuvuga imbere y'abantu
- Gucungera neza umubabaro
- Gusobanura neza
- Gucungera neza umwanya
- Inyishu canke amahugurwa yakozweko ubushakashatsi bwiza, y’agaciro kanini, ihuye n’ubuhinga bw’abakwumviriza .


Igihambaye conyene ni **kuguma ufise umutima wuguruye no kumenya igihe co kworohereza ivyiyumviro**, cane cane mu gihe c’ibibazo n’inyishu canke mu kiganiro c’abahinga. Si ukureka ingingo ngenderwako zawe, ahubwo ni ukwemera gusa ko abandi boshobora kubona ibintu mu buryo butandukanye. Rimwe na rimwe ni vyiza ko wishura mu nyuma canke ukabireka kugira ngo ikiganiro gikomeze gutera imbere. Iyo ingingo zawe zishigikiwe n’imvo zikomeye kandi zishingiye ku mvo nziza, abantu bazovyemera.


Niwumva witeguye, genda! Isi irakeneye cane inyigisho nziza ku vyerekeye Bitcoin.

Ivyo bivuzwe, iki si igikorwa gisanzwe: gisaba ikintu kivyurirwa umutima, utwigoro be n’amasano akwiriye.

Gutegura inyigisho canke amahugurwa mw’ishure canke muri kaminuza bitangura no kurondera umuntu akwiye guhura na we. Ibi bishobora kuba:


- Umuntu umuntu amenya
- Ishirahamwe ry'abanyeshure
- Ishirahamwe ry'abanyeshure ritegura urutonde rw'inyigisho canke ibirori
- Abahoze ari abigisha bo mw'ishure ryanyu ry'ubuhinga canke muri kaminuza
- Umuhuzabikorwa wa porogarama y’inyigisho, nimba ashikira ataco akora


Intambwe ya mbere akenshi ni ugushikiriza umugambi wawe canke ishirahamwe ryawe maze ugasaba ko haba inama. Mu gihe ciza, ivyo bizotuma haba ikiganiro ku bijanye na Bitcoin imbere y’abanyeshure haciye indwi nkeyi.

## Kwegera ubucuruzi


<chapterId>b730462b-7928-5d28-a14f-9ceaa773d3d2</chapterId>



Kuronka ubucuruzi bwo kwemera Bitcoin ni, ku ba bitcoiners benshi, nk’ugufungura ikintu kidasanzwe mu Pokédex yawe.


Umucuruzi wese ni uwundi, kandi kubashira mu bwato biza n’ingorane zavyo:


- Baracari bakeneye amahera ya fiat kugira ngo barihe abaguzi.
- Bisaba raporo z’ivy’ubutunzi zitomoye kandi ziteguwe n’umucungamutungo.
- Uburyo bwabo bwo kwishura butegerezwa kuba ubwo kwizigirwa, bworoshe gukoresha, kandi butagira ubusuma.
- Bashobora kuba bafise ivyiyumviro bitari vyo canke ivyiyumviro bibi ku bijanye na Bitcoin.
- Kuronka umuti w’ivy’ubuhinga butagira agasembwa birashobora kuba uruyeri.
- Gutuma umucuruzi atahura igituma bibereye kwemera Bitcoin nk’amahera yo kwishura.



![image](assets/fr/055.webp)



Gushiramwo ubucuruzi bwo mu karere ni bumwe mu buryo bwiza bwo gukura ibidukikije vy’igisagara cawe Bitcoin. Urufunguruzo ni ugukomeza intambwe ku yindi:


1/ **Saba kwishura muri Bitcoin, igihe cose ugura**

Akenshi uzoronka "oya" kandi kumbure n'ugutangara, canke mbere n'ugucira urubanza gatoyi, ariko gusa kubaza ibiterwa iciyumviro c'uko Bitcoin ari ikintu "abantu basanzwe" bashobora gukoresha kandi ko kiriko kirakundwa cane nk'uburyo bwo kwishura. Ushobora mbere gukorana n’abagenzi babiri maze ugahagarara mw’iduka rimwe ku misi itandukanye, igihe cose ugasaba kwishura Bitcoin. Vuba canke vuba, bizotuma nyen’ivyo bibanza ashaka kumenya vyinshi.


2/ **Tanga insobanuro yoroshe kandi itomoye**

Nibabaza ico Bitcoin ari co, muyigumye ngufi kandi ata majambo y’uruyeri. Nk'akarorero:

*"Bitcoin ni amahera atagenzurwa n'amabanki, akora ata bahuza, adashobora gucengera, adashobora gutera imbere, kandi afise amafaranga make kurusha amabanki asanzwe."*

Hanyuma ubahe urupapuro, nk'urwa "Impamvu 21 zo gukoresha Bitcoin" canke "Impamvu 10 zo kwemera Bitcoin mu bucuruzi bwawe", urimwo amakuru yawe yo guhamagara canke amakuru yawe yo guhura. Uko niko, iyo biteguriye demo, baramenya aho bogusanga.


3/ **Gutunganya iyerekanwa**

Igihe bagaragaje inyungu, nushireho umwanya wo kubereka umuti wo kwishura.

Ihitamwo ryiza ni **[Ubusuwisi-203-Umushahara](https://Ubusuwisi-203-Umushahara.ch/)**, kuko:


    - Bishoboza umucuruzi guhindura BTC mu fiat ku musi ku musi, kugira ngo bashobore kuriha abaguzi.
    - Ibaha uburenganzira bwo kuguma bafise ijana muri BTC iyo babishaka.
    - Itanga raporo z’ivy’itunganywa ry’amafaranga zishobora kwoherezwa hanze ku bacungamari babo.
    - Ifise urupapuro rworoshe rw’abarongozi.
    - Iha abakozi igitabu Interface gisanzwe kugira ngo bemere amahera ataco bakoze ku giciro c’amahera.
    - Ibiciro biri munsi y’amahera 2-3% asabwa n’imihora y’ukwishura ya kera.


4/ **Mubashigikire bamaze gutangura kwemera Bitcoin**

Bamaze kwinjira mu bwato:


    - Gusura ubudasiba kandi ugure.
    - Injira ubone nimba vyose bigenda neza.
    - Subira usangire amakuru yawe nimba bikenewe.
    - Mubatumire mu makoraniro yanyu.
    - Wongere kuri [ikarita y’ubusuwisi](https://btcmap.org/) maze umenyeshe [Swiss Bitcoin Pay](https://x.com/swissbitcoinpay) kuri Twitter kugira ngo wongere uboneke.
    - Nimwiyumvire kwakira amahugurwa canke amateraniro aho bari.



![image](assets/fr/056.webp)



5/ **Guhuza abacuruzi bo mu karere**

Uhejeje kugira ubucuruzi bukeyi bujanye n’ubugenzi bwa Bitcoin, n’ushireho inama yo gushinga umugwi wa Telegram w’abadandaza gusa. Ibi bishobora:


- Reka basangire ivyo baciyemwo kandi bashire mu bwato ubucuruzi bushasha.
- Gukora nk’umuhora wo gufasha mu vy’ubuhinga bwihuta.
- Gukomeza urubuga rwa Bitcoin rwo mu karere.


Gukora ubukangurambaga ni umukino w’igihe kirekire. Bisaba ukwihangana, ukwitanga n’ubuhinga. Ariko nimba umuryango wawe witeguye, woshobora gutangura gushinga umushinge w’ubutunzi nyakuri bw’uruziga rwa Bitcoin, nka [Arnhem mu Buholandi](https://www.arnhembitcoinstad.nl/) mu Buholandi.

## Gutegura ukwiyongera kw'abazoja muri iyo nama


<chapterId>8af722d4-3a9f-548c-9001-424f2142de12</chapterId>



Igihe Bitcoin yinjira mu kiringo ca kane ku nzu 840.000, abantu benshi cane baratanguye kuyimenya ko ari ihinduka ry’ubuhinga, ry’amahera n’imibano.


Cokimwe n’iterambere rya internet, iyo mpinduka izotwara umwanya, ivyo mu vy’ukuri ni akarusho. Biha abantu amahirwe yo **kwiga ivy’ishimikiro vya Bitcoin**, bagatahura ingene bayikoresha, kandi bituma ababikora n’amashirahamwe **bubaka ama protocoles n’ibikorwa** bizofungura ubushobozi bwayo bwose.


Nta n’umwe ashobora kumenya ivy’imbere, ariko turashobora **kwitega ko abantu bazokwemera Bitcoin bazokwiyongera cane**. Ivyo bisigura **kwitegurira ibintu vyose**, mbere n'ibikomeye cane, nk'ugusenyuka kw'ubuhinga bwa kera bw'ivy'ubutunzi canke ukubuza Bitcoin.


Muri iki gice ca karindwi kandi ca nyuma c’amashure, turaza kuraba ingingo nyamukuru zo kuguma mu muzirikanyi uko umuryango wawe n’amateraniro yawe bigenda birakura.


Ica mbere, kandi coroshe kwitega, ni ukwongerekana kw’abantu bifatanya n’abanyagihugu iwanyu. Ivyo bizokugirako ingaruka mu buryo bubiri nyamukuru:


1/ **Imirongo yawe yo guhanahana amakuru (Telegram, Signal, Matrix, n’ibindi) izoheza ibe yuzuye**, bitume bigorana kuguma ugendana n’ibiyago. Hari ibintu bibiri umuntu akwiye kuguma yibuka.


Ica mbere, iyo ufise abantu 500 bariko baraganira ku bintu vy’ubwoko bwose mu kiganiro kimwe, ubutumwa bwinshi cane n’amatangazo bizotuma abagize umuryango benshi bacika intege. Ico ni igihe ciza co gusubira kwiyumvira uko watunganije, nk’akarorero, mu kwimukira mu ma supergroups ya Telegram canke kuri server ya Discord, ivyo vyose bikaba bituma vyoroha gutunganya ibiganiro hakurikijwe ingingo.


Ubwa kabiri, abantu benshi mu mihora yawe bisigura ama bots menshi, ama scams menshi kandi rero n’uburinganire bwo gukora. Ivyo ubitegure mu gushinga amategeko atomoye ushobora gukurikiza igihe uriko uragenzura ubutumwa. Mu bumenyi bwacu, ivyo birafasha cane mu kwirinda amakimbirane, cane cane iyo amategeko ashobora gushikwako bitagoranye mu mihora yawe.

Akarorero keza ni **[Igitabo c’amategeko ca Bitcoin ca Lille](https://bitcoinlille.fr/reglement/) canditswe mu myaka mikeyi iheze ku bw’umugwi waco wa Telegram na server ya Discord. Ishingiye ku ngingo ngenderwako zoroheje kandi zijanye n’isi yose, kandi urashobora mbere gufata [verisiyo y’umubumbe ushobora guhindurwa . hano] (umugambi_guhindura?utm_ibirimwo=DAGHI692AD4&utm_isekeza=ugusangira_umugambi=utm_uburinganire=ihuriro2&utm_inkomoko=ubuto bwo gusangira).



![image](assets/fr/058.webp)



2/ **Amateraniro yanyu n'ibirori vyanyu bizotangura gukwegera abantu benshi, n'amaso mashasha menshi azoza kugira ngo bahuze**.

N’uku kwiyongera kw’abaje:


    - bishobora kuba vyiza **urondera ahantu hanini ho gukorera ama meetups n'ibirori vyawe**, cane cane nimba warashoboye kwakira abantu nka mirongo ibiri gusa mu bihe vya kera.
    - iyo ubonye ko umubare w’abazoja mu nama ubaye mwinshi cane, urashobora, ku makoraniro azoza, **gusaba abazoja muri iyo nama kwiyandikisha** (biciye kuri app ya Meetup canke ku rupapuro rworoshe) canke **gukora amakoraniro menshi buri kwezi** (nk’akarorero: inama y’abatanguje, iyo hagati, n’iyiteye imbere).
    - Muri ico gihe nyene, nimwitege abantu benshi bashasha mu bijanye n’ibidukikije, akenshi bakaba bafise intumbero nyamukuru yo kuronka amahera. Kugira ngo **ibiyago bigume vyibanda kuri Bitcoin** muri aya makoraniro ariko arakura ningoga, ni vyiza ko:

a) Mushiremwo **ikiganiro gifise insiguro ya Bitcoin** mu gihe c'ivyo birori, kuko ivyo bizotuma ikiyago kinini kija kuri iyo nkuru (kandi kikaja kure y'umugambi wa shitcoin uherutse kwivugwa ko ari "uw'ihinduka");

b) **Irinde kwifatanya n’abandi ba bitcoiners gusa**. Ahubwo, nushireho iciyumviro co **kwegera abashasha**, naho ivyo vyoba bisobanura guca inyuma y’icumba kugira ngo ubaramutse. Uvuye ng’aho, urashobora kuvuga igituma baje, ivyo barondera, intumbero (canke mbere ingingo ngenderwako nyamukuru) z’abanyagihugu bawe, n’ibindi.


Ubwa nyuma, naho ari ikibabaje kubivuga, abahendanyi barashobora kwiyerekana mu makoraniro. Intumbero yabo yoshobora kuba iyo kuvuga ubudasiba ku mugambi wabo wa Ponzi, MLM, canke "Ihinduka rya Blockchain rikoreshwa na AI." Birashoboka kandi ko umusi umwe, umuntu afise imigambi mibi yoshobora kuza cane cane kugira ngo asukure amahera canke agerageze kugura BTC n’amafaranga y’ibinyoma.


Mu bihe vy’uruyeri nk’ibi, uburyo bworoshe ni ugusigura mu buryo bw’ikinyabupfura ko iyo nama atari yo, ukayishigikira n’imvo iyo ari yo yose uhisemwo (nk’akarorero, ukavuga ko hoshobora kuba hariho abapolisi bambaye impuzu za gisirikare).


Iyo, ikibabaje, banse kuvaho canke kwigenza mu buryo bwubaha (egome, vyarabaye), urashobora Address iryo tsinda ataco rihinduye mu gihe c’inama kugira ngo umenyeshe bose ko umuntu , canke abantu, bariko baragerageza igikorwa c’ububisha.


Ikishimishije, ivyo bintu bishika gake kandi kenshi birateye isoni kuruta gutera ubwoba, ariko biracari vyiza kwitegura.


Kugira ngo ugire amahoro yo mu mutima: igihe cose wewe ubwawe utariko urarenga ku mategeko canke ngo ufashe mu buryo butaziguye ibikorwa bitemewe n’amategeko, ntuzobazwa mu buryo butegekanijwe n’amategeko ku bikorwa vy’abandi ku musi mukuru wawe (n’imiburiburi hakurikijwe itegeko ry’Ubufaransa).


Mu isi nziza, woba ufise **ikibanza kinini gihagije co kwakira abantu amajana n’amajana** uko umuryango wawe ukura kandi woba wubaka **ubucuti bukomeye hagati y’abacuruzi ba bitcoin** n’abazoba abacuruzi ba bitcoin mu gihe c’isoko ry’idubu, kugira ngo iyo urugendo rw’inka ruzoshika, ufise abantu benshi biteguye gufata imiti y’umukara abandi.



## Kwegeranya imiteguro y'abanyagihugu


<chapterId>bedfc110-e099-58f4-a5b7-fb50f3b19a49</chapterId>



Ibaze iki: umaze imyaka urongoye ihuriro ryawe rya Bitcoin, umusi umwe ugafata ingingo yo kureka ako kazi, kumbure ukaja kurera llamas muri Peru canke gukurikirana imipfunda itunganye muri El Salvador. None mu kibano canyu iyo muvuyeyo bigenda gute?


Urasabwa gutegura imbere y'igihe kwimurira umuryango wawe mu:

1) **Guha uburenganzira bwa admin (canke Ownership)** bw'imihora yawe yo guhanahana amakuru ku banyamuryango wizigira. Uruhara rushobora kwama ruhinduka amaboko mu nyuma.

2) **Sangira amakuru ya konti**(amazina y’abakoresha, amajambo banga, ama email ajanye) ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi zawe n’imihora yawe yo kugaragara n’umuntu umwe wizigira.


Iyo ata n’umwe afise iyo nzira, biragoye cane ko uwundi muntu yofata neza. Gutangura kuva ku ntango, mu gushinga amatsinda "forked" no gusubira kwubaka urubuga, biragenda buhoro, biraruhije, kandi kenshi biratera intege.


Niba udafise umuntu wizigira mu gihe c’ihuriro ryawe, urashobora kwama ushikira @ProfEduStream, @copinmalin, canke @gabtribe kuri Telegram. Bashobora gufasha kuronka uwuzobakurikira afise intumbero no guhuza ukwimurira amakonti n’uburenganzira bw’ubuyobozi.


**Kubera iki Ukwegereza ubutegetsi abaturage bihambaye ?**


Nimba ari wewe wenyene uri murivyo kandi ukaba ufise ububasha bwose, bigenda gute iyo uzimiye? Inyishu ni yoroshe: n’umuryango wawe uzozimangana.


Umuti: gushikiriza ibikorwa, uruhara, n'uburyo bwo kuronka kugira ngo umugwi ukomeze gukora naho umuteguzi umwe yoba avuyeho, vyaba bivuye ku ntambamyi z'igihe canke, mu gihe c'umwiza, kubera ko bafashwe kubera bafise Bitcoin itari KYC.


**Uko twosunika ukwiwegereza ubutegetsi ?**

Gufata **porogarama z’ubuntu kandi zifunguye** nka [the framasoft suite](https://framasoft.org/fr/) kugira ngo ugabanye kwizigira ama platforms ahuriweko no kwongera ubushobozi bwo kwihanganira ubugenzuzi bwa Leta canke ububisha bwa Bitcoin.

Koresha **ibikoresho vyo guhanahana amakuru bifise amakuru canke bitazwi** kugira ngo ukinge ubuzima bwite bw'abanywanyi bawe n'ibikorwa vy'abanyagihugu bawe.



## None iyo Bitcoin iba umwansi wa bose No 1?


<chapterId>c739651f-cd13-51e0-a36e-85023749b15b</chapterId>



Ku wa 5 Ndamukiza 1933, Franklin Roosevelt, Prezida wa 32 wa Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika, yarasinye **Itegeko ry’Igihugu 6102**, rituma abantu ku giti cabo badashobora kugira ibiceri vy’inzahabu, ibiceri vy’agaciro canke ivyemezo aho hose mu gihugu.


![image](assets/fr/059.webp)



Ego ni ko, Ikigoyi Kinini (ugusenyuka kw’isoko ry’ivy’ubutunzi mu 1929) si co kintu kiriho ubu. Ariko none, umusi umwe leta yoshobora gukora nk’ukwo nyene kuri Bitcoin: **kubuza Ownership y’abikorera ku giti cabo ataco ihinduye?**


Ukuri ni uko, mwene iyo ngingo ya Orwel mu vy’ukuri itazohagarika umuntu kugira BTC vy’ukuri, kuko nta tegeko rishobora kubuza umuntu gufata mu mutwe urutonde rw’amajambo.


Naho ari ukwo, vyogenda gute iyo abarongozi b’isi amaherezo batahura ubushobozi Bitcoin ifise bwo guhindura urutonde rw’isi yose, mbere no ku rwego rwa Leta? Mbega rero vyoba vyanditswe ngo "Umwansi wa Leta No 1"?


Ntidukwiye kugwa mu vyiyumviro vy’umugambi mubisha. Ariko ntaco kitubuza kwitega, kwegereza ubutegetsi, no kwigisha, guhera ubu.

## Gahunda y'ibikorwa vyihuse yo kwubaka umuryango wawe wa Bitcoin


<chapterId>1f463ec2-3f64-5641-8d60-e02a47c94f27</chapterId>



Satoshi Nakamoto yararemye igikoresho c’ihinduka risanzwe kiriko kirahindura isi yacu. Ariko ni twebwe aba bitcoiners dufata ingingo y’ingene tuzohingura kazoza.


Bitcoin ni ikinogo kinini c’inkwavu, gikora ku mutima amateka, ubutunzi, ibitabu, imibare, ubuhinga bwa mudasobwa, n’ubuhinga bwo gukingira amakuru. Nta n'umwe ashobora kuyitohoza yose, kandi ivyo ni kimwe mu vyiza vyayo.


Kubera ko umwanya wacu ari muto, ikintu nyamukuru ni ukuguma twicishije bugufi, tukaguma twiga, kandi tukagira ubumenyi bwa Exchange n’abandi. Uko ni ko umwe wese muri twebwe ashobora gushika ku rugero rw’ugutahura arondera.


**Imiryango y’isi yose rero irafise uruhara runini mu kwubaka isi y’ejo**: ibibanza aho aba bitcoiners bashobora **Exchange, kwubaka no gusangira**. **Hamwe**.



![image](assets/fr/061.webp)




Nk’uko mwabibonye muri iri shure ryose, **kwubaka umuryango wa Bitcoin no gutegura amateraniro** bisaba gutegura no gutera intege. Bivanye n’imigambi yawe be n’igihe ushobora kwiyemeza, woshobora gukora imigambi ikomeye cane canke ugatuma ibintu biguma vyoroshe.


Uko biri kwose, intumbero ni imwe: **gutera umunezero no gufasha Bitcoin gukura mu gisagara canyu**.


Kugira ngo dusozere ibice vyinshi n'ibice bitobito twavuze, ng'ibi **"umugambi w'ibikorwa"** wo kurema umuryango wawe wa Bitcoin:

1) Fata ingingo imbere y’igihe kubera iki ushaka gushinga umuryango wa Bitcoin, intumbero zawe, n’igisagara canke ahantu uzokorera.

2) Ushobora gukorana n’imiryango iriho hafi, kandi ntukekeranye kubasaba impanuro iyo uyikeneye;

3) Rema akaranga gatomoye k’abanyagihugu bawe kagaragaza abanyagihugu bawe n’aho uri (ikimenyetso, igicapo, ibendera, urupapuro rwo gutangaza, n’ibindi)

4) Gukora imihora yo guhanahana amakuru n’imihora y’ubuhinga bwa none nka Telegram, Twitter, NOSTR, Discord canke Simplex. Nimwiyumvire kandi imihora yegerejwe n’/canke idashobora gucengera.

5) Kurikirana ayandi materaniro yo mu karere n’imiryango kuri internet, maze ushire urutonde rw’umugwi wawe ku makarita atandukanye no ku mbuga zitandukanye kugira ngo uboneke.


Igihe imihora y'itumanaho y'abanyagihugu bawe izoba ikora:

6) Gutanguza ubushakashatsi kugira ngo ushireho ihuriro ryawe rya mbere umaze kwumva inyungu ihagije.

7) Sigura intumbero ushaka gushikako uvuye muri iki gikorwa c’umuntu ku giti ciwe.

8) Hitamwo ahantu heza, hakwakira abantu, hahuye n’imigambi yawe, hanyuma ubike ameza canke icumba.

9) Mumenyekanishe ico gikorwa ku mihora yanyu indwi imwe imbere y’aho, hanyuma musubire gutangaza imisi ibiri canke itatu imbere y’itariki.

10) Shira urutonde rw’ibintu vyawe ku mbuga n’ama platforms bibereye.

11) Ku musi, reka ikintu kigende nk’uko bisanzwe kandi ushiremwo n’abaje mu nama, urongore ibiganiro mu bugwaneza iyo bikenewe.


Inyuma y'aho:

12) Kwibanda ku kugumya no gushiramwo abaje mu nama kugira ngo bagaruke.

13) Gutanga ivyiyumviro vy’imigambi canke ingamba zoroshe maze utangure izo wewe ubwawe witwararika. Nihagira uwundi muntu ashaka kurongora umugambi utandukanye, numushigikire aho ushoboye.


Amaherezo:

14) Gutegura kazoza mu kwiyumvira ahantu hanini ho guhurira, amategeko agenga imihora y’itumanaho, urubuga, n’ibindi;

15) Remesha abacuruzi bo mu karere kwemera Bitcoin, mbere no kubaza gusa igihe cose uguriye ng’aho.

16) Guha ibikorwa n’uburenganzira bwo gutwara umurongo/urubuga abanywanyi bizigira.

17) Guteza imbere umuryango wawe cane kugira abantu benshi baje, kandi ushiremwo abagaragaza ko bavyishimiye.

18) Genda mbere n’imbere n’imigambi ukunda kandi igufitiye akamaro.

## Kwitwararika wewe nyene


<chapterId>cf1631cb-ddbe-55c7-be8c-26a5652979a0</chapterId>



Iyo uguye mu mwobo w’inkwavu Bitcoin, ukuntu ubona ubuzima kurahinduka.

Imbere mu mutima wawe, wumva ukwegerewe kugira ngo ugende kure, ngo uhure n’abandi bariko baraca muri iyo nzira nyene yo mu mutwe.


Izo nguvu zigutuma utanga igice cawe ku ruzitiro mu gusangira ivyo wavumbuye: Bitcoin. Bigutera kandi kwiyumvira isi y’ejo, iyo ushaka gufasha kwubaka, n’imiryango iriko irayikora.


Kubaka umuryango ni umugende muremure usaba intumbero, ingamba, ukwitanga n’igihe. Bishobora kuba igikorwa kidashimwa, ni ngombwa rero gufata ibintu intambwe ku yindi kugira ngo ntusha.


**Iyumvire wewe nyene, ku mipaka yawe**. Kandi naho Bitcoin yoshobora kugutwara ijoro n'umurango, **ntugerageze gukora vyose wenyene, canke uzokwiturira**.


Ahubwo, **rondera imfashanyo ku ba bitcoiners bamaze kugenda mu nzira imwe, batohoza inzira imwe kandi bubaka ibikoresho bimwe**. Bizogukiza umwanya mwinshi.


**Tuma abagukurikira muri iyo nzira y’urugendo ngo bagufashe**, naho nyene gutanga ibikorwa bimwebimwe bituma bifata igihe kirekire. Mu gihe kirekire, igisagara canyu kizokomera kandi kizorushiriza kwihangana.



![image](assets/fr/062.webp)



Wenyene, urashobora kwihuta guca muri imwe mu nzira nyinshi ziri mu mwobo w’inkwavu Bitcoin. Ariko wibuke,hariho izindi nzira zitari nke zo gutohoza.


**Mu kurema no/canke kwifatanya n'umuryango w'abakoresha ama bitcoin, no mu gusangira no guhanahana na bo ubudasiba, uzoba uriko uragenda mu nzira zose umanuka mu mwobo w'inkwavu igihe kimwe.**
