# Urugendo rwo mw'isi ya Frédéric Bastiat


Iryo shure rirongowe na Damien Theillier, rigutumira kwisuka mw’isi ya Frédéric Bastiat, umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi n’umunyabwenge w’Umufaransa, ivyiyumviro vyiwe bikabandanya guhindura ivyiyumviro vy’ubutunzi vyo muri iki gihe. Biciye mu mavidewo 21, Damien Theillier aratohoza ubuzima bwa Bastiat, ivyiyumviro vyiwe vy’ubwenge, abamurwanya mu vy’ingengabitekerezo, hamwe n’ivyiyumviro vyiwe vy’ubutunzi.


Iryo shure ritangura n’intangamarara ido n’ido y’ubuzima bwa Bastiat n’ivyo yaciyemwo, imbere y’uko dusuzuma abanyabwenge bashize ikimenyetso ku ciyumviro ciwe, nka Adamu Smith, Jean-Baptiste Say, Antoine Destutt de Tracy, Charles Comte, Charles Dunoyer na Richard Cobden. Hanyuma, iyo nyigisho iraba abarwanya Bastiat, harimwo Rousseau, inyigisho za kera, uburinzi, ubusosiyalisimu, na Proudhon.


Igice gihambaye c’ivyo vyigwa kijanye n’ubuhinga bw’ubutunzi Bastiat yavuga nabi, nk’akarorero «Ivyo biboneka n’ivyo bitaboneka», «Ikibazo c’abakora amabuye y’agaciro», gusahura biciye mu gutanga imisoro, n’itandukaniro riri hagati y’izo nyifato runtu zibiri z’ubutunzi. Iryo shure kandi rivuga ku bijanye n’uguhuza ubutunzi Bastiat ashigikira, harimwo n’igitangaza c’isoko, ububasha bw’inshingano, n’ubumwe nyakuri.


Ubwa nyuma, iryo shure risozerwa n'uguzirikana ku "Itegeko", rivuga ku vyiyumviro nyamukuru nk'uburenganzira bwo kuronka itunga, gusahura bitegekanijwe n'amategeko, n'uruhara rwa Leta. Insozero y’iryo shure isubiramwo iragi rya Frédéric Bastiat n’ingene yahora akora ku bukungu bwa none.


Ifatanye na Damien Theillier muri iri suzuma ry’ubutunzi ry’ivyiyumviro vya Frédéric Bastiat maze ubone ingene ivyiyumviro vyiwe bishobora gutanga umuco ku mpari z’ubutunzi n’ivya politike ziriho ubu.

## Incamake y'amashure

<chapterId>aa493f46-2d3a-4b76-ad79-ed44113a97f4</chapterId>


Intumbero y’iri shure ni ukuguha ugutahura kwimbitse ubuzima, ivyiyumviro vy’ubwenge, abamurwanya mu vyiyumviro, n’ivyiyumviro vy’ubutunzi vya Frédéric Bastiat. Biciye muri urwo rugendo rutunganijwe, uzobona ingene ivyiyumviro vyiwe vyahinduye ivyiyumviro vy’ubutunzi kandi bikabandanya guhindura impaka ziriho ubu.


**Igice ca 1: Intangamarara**

Tuzotangura n’insiguro rusangi ya Frédéric Bastiat, umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi ataco amaze. Uzomenya ivyerekeye ubuzima bwiwe, urugendo rwiwe rw’ubwenge, n’ivya kahise yashizemwo ivyiyumviro vyiwe. Gutahura ivyo bintu ni ngombwa kugira ngo umuntu atahure neza urugero rw’ivyo yanditse be n’ivyiyumviro vyiwe.


**Igice ca 2: Inkurikizi**

Tuzobandanya dusesangura abanyabwenge bahinduye iciyumviro c’ubutunzi ca Frédéric Bastiat. Uzomenya ingene abantu bakomeye nka Adamu Smith, Jean-Baptiste Say, Antoine Destutt de Tracy, Charles Comte, Charles Dunoyer na Richard Cobden bafashije mu guteza imbere ubwenge bwiwe, bagashiraho umushinge w’ivyiyumviro vyiwe ku bijanye n’ubudandaji bwigenga be n’ubutunzi bw’isoko.


**Igice ca 3: Abarwanya**

Inyuma y’aho, tuzokwihweza ivyo Bastiat anegura abamurwanya mu vy’ingengabitekerezo. Yaba Rousseau, inyigisho za kera, uburinzi, ubusosiyalisimu canke Proudhon, uzotahura igituma Bastiat yabona ko izo nyigisho ari intambamyi ku bijanye n’iterambere ry’ubutunzi n’imibano, n’ingene yishura ku mpamvu zabo afise ivyiyumviro bikomeye.


**Igice ca 4: Amakosa y'ubutunzi**

Iki gice kigenewe amakosa y'ubutunzi yashizwe ahabona na Bastiat, harimwo n'ivyo bizwi cane "*Ivyo biboneka n'ivyo bitaboneka*" na "*Ikirego c'abahinguzi b'amabuye y'agaciro*". Turaza gusuzuma ingene yerekanye n’ubuhinga, biciye mu gutwenga no mu gusesangura bikomeye, amakosa asanzwe yo mu vy’ubutunzi yo mu gihe ciwe, aguma ari ngirakamaro no muri iki gihe.


**Igice ca 5: Uguhuza ubutunzi**

Aha, uzobona iciyumviro ciza ca Bastiat ku vyerekeye ubutunzi. Tuzo Address ivyiyumviro nk’igitangaza c’isoko, ububasha bw’inshingano z’umuntu ku giti ciwe, n’itandukaniro hagati y’ubumwe bw’ukuri n’ubw’ikinyoma. Bastiat yabona ko ubutunzi ari uburyo buhuye aho inyungu z’umuntu zitahurwa neza zifasha abantu bose. Tuzokwihweza igituma.


**Igice ca 6: Ivyagezwe**

Kugira ngo dusozere iki cigwa, tuzokwinjira mu gitabu gikomeye ca Bastiat, "*Itegeko*", aho yerekana ivyiyumviro vyiwe ku burenganzira bw’itunga, ubusahuzi bushingiye ku mategeko, n’uruhara ruto rwa Leta. Muzotahura igituma iki kiganiro kibonwa ko ari kimwe mu bimenyetso bikomeye cane bishigikira umwidegemvyo w’umuntu ku giti ciwe n’ubutunzi bw’isoko.


Ni mwiteguye kumenya ingene ivyiyumviro vya Frédéric Bastiat bikiriho n’ubu? Twifatanye n’uru rugendo rw’ubwenge rushobora gutera ingorane ugutahura kwawe ku vyerekeye ubutunzi!


## Bastiat: Umunyabwenge ataco amaze


<chapterId>7f21b617-9810-5484-ad1c-befc61432126</chapterId>


Iryo shure ni intangamarara ya Frédéric Bastiat, umunyabwenge atamenyekana kandi ari umuco w’ibihe vyacu. Muri iyi ntangamarara ngufi, nzogerageza kubafasha kumenya uwo Frédéric Bastiat yari we n’insanganyamatsiko nyamukuru tuzovuga muri uru rukurikirane.

![image](assets/en/001.webp)


Nkako, Frédéric Bastiat, uwavutse mu 1801 akaba yabayeho mu gice ca mbere c’ikinjana ca 19, yaramaze igihe kanaka ari umwanditsi ahambaye. Hanyuma, buhoro buhoro, arazimangana kandi uno musi, nta n’umwe amwumva, nta n’umwe azi uwo ari we. Yamara, ikintu giteye ubwoba ni uko uwo mwanditsi yahinduwe mu ndimi nyinshi, harimwo igitaliyano, ikirusiya, igisupanyoli n’icongereza.


![image](assets/en/002.webp)


Bica bigaragara ko inyuma y’Intambara ya Kabiri y’Isi Yose, kimwe mu bitabu vyiwe carasohowe muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika. Caciye kimenyekana cane, ku buryo Ronald Reagan ubwiwe yavuze ko ari co gitabu akunda cane, kandi ico gitabu gitoyi citwa "Itegeko." Bastiat rero ni umwe mu banditsi babiri b’Abafaransa bazwi cane muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika, uwundi na we akaba azwi cane no mu Bufaransa, Alexis de Tocqueville.


![image](assets/en/003.webp)

_(Isoko ry'i Mugron mu gihugu, igisagara ca Bastiat)_


Rero, umunyabwenge atamenyekana ariko kandi umuco w’ibihe vyacu. Nkako, Frédéric Bastiat, yavukiye i Bayonne, yabanje kuba igice c’ubuzima bwiwe mu gihugu ca Landes aho yarongoye itongo ry’uburimyi yari yarazwe, amaherezo akaba ari umudandaza. Kandi rero, akiri muto cane, yarakunda cane ubutunzi, aja mu Bwongereza, ahura na Richard Cobden yari umurongozi w’umuhari w’ubudandaji bwigenga. Bastiat yarashimishijwe n’uwo muhari, yari yemera neza ko ubudandaji bw’ubuntu ari umuti w’ubufaransa aca afata ingingo inyuma y’aho yo kugerageza gukwiragiza ivyiyumviro vyiwe mu Bufaransa. Yanditse ingingo zari ziteye imbere cane aca yimukira i Paris kugira ngo arongore ikinyamakuru ico gihe citwa Journal des économistes.


![image](assets/en/004.webp)


Yari kandi umunyabwenge kandi yiyumvira ivy’imibano, urutonde rw’imibano, ubutungane, amategeko, yiyumvira uburenganzira. Kandi muri ivyo, turashobora kuvuga ko Bastiat ari umuco w’ibihe vyacu. Kandi ndashaka gusozera n’ivyo. Ni umuntu yagerageje gutahura ingene isoko rya politike rikora. Ego ni ko, ni uwuburanira kandi ubutunzi bw’isoko, uwo amaherezo ubutunzi bw’isoko ari bwo buryo bwiza bwo kurema ubutunzi. Ariko uretse ivyo, kandi niho atamenyekana, yari atahura uburyo isoko rya politike rikora.


![image](assets/en/005.webp)


Igihe yatorwa ngo abe umudepite, ni mu gihe ca Repubulika ya kabiri, kuva ico gihe, abantu ni bo bashinga amategeko. Iciyo gihe, Bastiat yarabonye ubwoko bw’ugutera imbere kw’amategeko mu nzira zose, harimwo no kurema ibikorwa vya Leta, uburenganzira bw’abantu, imisoro n’ibindi.


![image](assets/en/006.webp)


---

> **AMAHUGURWA Y'IGIHUGU cose**
> **UMUGAMBI.**
> Kubera ko abakozi banditswe mu mahugurwa y’Igihugu basavye mu buryo bubereye ko igikorwa kiriho cogabanwa hagati yabo mu buryo bungana kandi bubereye uko bishoboka kwose;
> Mu gihe akazi kariho gusa ku bagabo 16.000, kandi umubare w’abagabo banditswe urenga 50.000;
> Haremejwe ko, gushika hamenyeshejwe kandi hategerejwe intunganyo nziza, ishirahamwe ryose rizokora imisi ibiri mu ndwi guhera ku wa mbere igenekerezo rya 17.
> _Komiseri wa Repubulika, Umuyobozi w'Amahugurwa y'Igihugu,_
> **Emili TOMASI.**

---

Kandi yarabonye yuko mu vy’ukuri, ata kintu na kimwe cari carahindutse vy’ukuri. Abantu baratanga ivy’abandi biciye mu gutora no mu mategeko, ivyo yise gusahura bitegekanijwe n’amategeko. Ico kintu c’ugusahura mu buryo butegekanijwe n’amategeko cari hagati y’ibikorwa vyiwe, cane cane muri iki canditswe kigufi yanditse mu mpera z’ubuzima bwiwe, «Itegeko», aho atandukanya gusahura mu buryo butegekanijwe n’amategeko n’itunga, uburenganzira bwo kuronka itunga. Arerekana ko, mu ntango, umuti nyawo w’ingorane y’imibano ari umwidegemvyo, ni ukuvuga umutungo, ukwiyegeza n’ivyamwa vy’ibikorwa vyiwe.


Muri iki cigwa, tuzogira urugendo hamwe mu ciyumviro ca Frédéric Bastiat, dutanguriye ku nkurikizi z’abanditsi bamubumvye akiri muto cane, hanyuma turabe mu vy’ubuhinga bwiwe bw’ubutunzi, maze mu nyuma, tuzosozera n’iki canditswe gikomeye, «Itegeko», kizotumenyesha isesengura ry’isoko ry’ubuhinga bwa politike, ry’ubuhinga bwa politike.


## Ubuzima n'Ivya kahise


<chapterId>e9d92b63-83dd-552c-84e1-dd535608c109</chapterId>


Mu 1844, Frédéric Bastiat yaragize urugendo rw’ubudandaji aja muri Esupanye. Amaze kuguma i Madrid, i Seville, i Cadix n’i Lisbonne, yaciye afata ingingo yo gufata ubwato aja i Southampton, no gusura Ubwongereza. I Londres, yararonse akaryo ko kuja mu makoraniro y’ishirahamwe ryitwa Anti-Corn Law League, iryo shirahamwe yari yakurikiye igikorwa caryo ari kure. Yarahuye n’abarongozi bakuru b’iryo Shirahamwe, harimwo na Richard Cobden, uwo yari kuzoba umugenzi wiwe.


![image](assets/en/007.webp)


Aho ni ho ubuzima bwiwe bwohindukiye bimwe bikomeye. Na we ubwiwe yigana ko ihamagarwa ryiwe ryo kuba umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi ryafashwe muri ico gihe. Amaze gusubira mu Bufaransa, yari afise iciyumviro kimwe gusa mu muzirikanyi: ni ukumenyesha Ubufaransa umuhari w’abanyagihugu wariko uratera ubwoba Ubwongereza.

Frédéric Bastiat yavukiye i Bayonne kw’igenekerezo rya 30 Ruheshi 1801. Yari impfuvyi afise imyaka 9, aca akurikirana inyigisho ziwe mw’ishure ryisumbuye ry’Abagatolika ry’i Sorèze. Yari afise impano yo kumenya indimi, yariga Icongereza, Igisupanyoli, eka mbere n’Ikibasike. Ariko rero, ntiyavyuriwe umutima n’inyigisho ziwe, aca afata ingingo yo kudafata amashure yisumbuye, ahubwo ahitamwo gukora mu bucuruzi bwa se wabo bwo kwinjiza ibintu hanze n’ugushora ibintu hanze i Bayonne.


Mu mwaka w'1825, yarazwe itongo ry'uburimyi na sekuru, akaricungera nk'"umurimyi w'umunyacubahiro", mu majambo yiwe bwite. Iciyo gihe ni ho yiboneye ingorane zaterwa n’ukubura insobanuro itomoye y’uburenganzira bw’umutungo. Yaciye afata ingingo yo kuba umucamanza w’amahoro mu gisagara ciwe ca Mugron, kiri hagati mu misozi ya Landes, kikaba ari ahantu ubudandaji n’amazi bihurira hagati y’ivyambu vya Bordeaux na Bayonne. Mu nyuma, yatowe ngo abe umunyamuryanganama nkuru y’ibihugu.


![image](assets/en/008.webp)


Yaciye yihuta gukunda cane ubutunzi bwa politike, aca yiga ibitabu vya Adamu Smith, Jean-Baptiste Say, Destutt de Tracy, Charles Dunoyer na Charles Comte. Yarasomye ibinyamakuru vy’icongereza, kandi ni ho yamenye ko hariho ishirahamwe ry’Abongereza ry’ubudandaji bw’ubuntu.


![image](assets/en/009.webp)

_(Vuga, Cobden, Smith, Umusirikare, Dunoyer, Destutt de Tracy)_


Yagarutse avuye mu Bwongereza, yaranditse ingingo yitwa: «Ku bijanye n’ingene amakori y’Abongereza n’Abafaransa yagira ico akoze kuri kazoza k’ibihugu bibiri», ayirungikira ikinyamakuru Journal des Économistes co i Paris. Iyo ngingo yasohotse mu nomero yo mu kwezi kwa Gitugutu 1844, kandi yararoraniwe bimwe bishitse. Abantu bose barashima cane imvo ziwe zikomeye kandi zica intege, uburyo bwiwe bwo kuvuga buteye igomwe kandi buteye igomwe.


![image](assets/en/010.webp)


Ikinyamakuru Journal des Économistes caramusaba ko yoronka izindi ngingo, maze abagize ishirahamwe ry’ubutunzi bwa politike, cane cane Horace Say, mwene Jean-Baptiste Say, na Michel Chevalier, umwigisha rurangiranwa, baramushimira, bamuremesha kubandanya na bo mu gikorwa c’ubutunzi sprea. Ivyo vyatumye haba intango y’ubuzima bushasha i Paris.


Yabanje gusohora urutonde rwa mbere rw’ibitabu vyitwa Economic Sophisms, aho yatera abaharanira uburinzi bw’igihugu n’ubushizi bw’amanga be n’ugushinyagurira. I Paris, yaratanguye mbere n’inyigisho y’ubutunzi bwa politike mu cumba c’umuntu ku giti ciwe, abanyeshure b’abanyacubahiro bakaba bayitabira n’umwete mwinshi.


![image](assets/en/011.webp)


Umwaka wakurikiyeho, yashinze "Ishirahamwe ry'ubudandaji bwigenga" mu Bufaransa, aca yijugunya mu ntambara yo kurwanya uburinzi bw'igihugu mu Bufaransa. Yararonse amahera, arashiraho isubiramwo ry’indwi ku yindi, yongera aratanga inyigisho mu gihugu cose.

Inama ya mbere yabereye i Bordeaux kw’igenekerezo rya 23 Ruhuhuma 1846, aho hashinzwe ishirahamwe ry’ubudandaji bwigenga ry’i Bordeaux. Bidatevye, uwo muhari warakwiragiye mu Bufaransa bwose. I Paris, harashinzwe umugwi w’intango mu bagize ishirahamwe ry’abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi, abadepite, abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi n’abadandaza na bo bakawufatanya. Imigwi ihambaye na yo nyene yarashinzwe i Marseille, i Lyon n’i Le Havre.

![image](assets/en/005.webp)


Impinduka zo muri Ruhuhuma 1848 zatembagaje ubwami bwa Louis-Philippe, buzwi kw’izina ry’Ubwami bwa Nyakanga (1830-1848), maze haza Repubulika ya kabiri. Bastiat yaciye atorwa kuba umunywanyi w’inama nshingamateka nk’umuvugizi wa Landes. Yari yicaye hagati y’ibubamfu, ari kumwe na Alexis de Tocqueville, hagati y’abanyagihugu b’umwami n’abanyagihugu. Aho, yaragerageje kurwanira umwidegemvyo w’umuntu ku giti ciwe nk’umwidegemvyo w’abanyagihugu, arwanya politike zose zibuza abantu, zaba zivuye iburyo canke ibubamfu. Yatowe kuba icegera ca Komite y’Ivy’Imari, kandi yama yihatira kwibutsa abadepite bagenziwe uku kuri kworoshe, kenshi kwibagirwa mu nama nshingamateka:


> Umuntu ntashobora guha bamwe, biciye kw’itegeko, atabwirizwa kwakira abandi biciye kw’irindi tegeko.

Hafi ibitabu vyiwe vyose n’inkuru ziwe zose vyanditswe mu myaka itandatu ya nyuma y’ubuzima bwiwe, kuva mu 1844 gushika mu 1850. Mu 1850, Bastiat yaranditse ibitabu bibiri vyiwe bizwi cane: Ivyagezwe n’urutonde rw’udutabu twitwa Ivyo biboneka n’ivyo bitaboneka. Ivyagezwe vyahinduwe mu ndimi nyinshi z’amahanga, harimwo Icongereza, Ikidagi, Igisupanyoli, Ikirusiya n’Igitaliyano.


![image](assets/en/012.webp)


Yapfuye i Roma mu 1850, arwaye igituntu. Yahambwe mw’ishengero ry’i Roma ryitwa Saint Louis des Français.

## Adamu Smith na Jean-Batista bavuga


<chapterId>bcc7a12a-6cc4-5061-85e3-0e31fb1f0a49</chapterId>


Mu vy’ubutunzi, Bastiat yama yemera umwenda afitiye Adamu Smith na Jean-Baptiste Say. Ku myaka 26, yandikiye umwe mu bagenzi biwe ati: "Sinigeze nsoma kuri ivyo biganiro atari ibi bitabu bine, Smith, Say, Destutt, na Censor."


![image](assets/en/014.webp)

_(Jean-Batista Say na Adamu Smith)_


Ubutunzi bwa politike, nk’uko bwavuzwe na Adamu Smith na J.-B. Vuga, bishizwe mw’ijambo rimwe: umwidegemvyo. Umwidegemvyo w’ubudandaji, umwidegemvyo w’umuntu ku giti ciwe, umwidegemvyo w’ubudandaji, n’ukwitanga mu mwidegemvyo. Ubucuruzi bw’ubuntu bwabanje kuburanirwa n’abahinga mu vy’ubuhinga bw’umubiri, nka François Quesnay na Vincent de Gournay, hanyuma na Adamu Smith yashize hamwe ivyiyumviro vyabo n’ivyo yihweje. Ubwa nyuma, mu mpera z’ikinjana ca 18, Jean-Baptiste Say yaratomoye yongera akosora ingingo zimwezimwe zo mu nyigisho ya shebuja Adamu Smith mu gitabu ciwe c’ubuhinga citwa Traité sur l’économie politique.


![image](assets/en/015.webp)

_(Vuga, Destutt wa Tracy, Quesney, wa Gourne)_


Adamu Smith yari ashimishijwe n’uguterimbere, atari nk’intumbero ubwayo ahubwo nk’uburyo bwo gushira hejuru inyifato runtu y’abantu ku giti cabo. Kuri we, ubutunzi bw’amahanga bugizwe n’ubutunzi bw’abantu ku giti cabo. Nimba ushaka igihugu giteye imbere, ni ko Adamu Smith avuga, reka umuntu ku giti ciwe akore yidegemvya. Kandi isoko rirakora kuko rituma umuntu wese aserura ivyo akunda no gukurikirana inyungu yiwe.


![image](assets/en/016.webp)


Igishasha gikomeye c’abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri iki gihe mu ntango z’ikinjana ca 18 ni uko bakunda umuntu wese afise ubushake bwo kugarukana ubushobozi bwiwe bwo gukora mu gihe biyumvira ingene bozibira ivyipfuzo n’amatati. Umuntu mu bisanzwe ashaka gutera imbere mu bijanye n’ubuzima bwiwe n’ubw’abamukunda biciye mu Exchange y’ibintu n’ibikorwa.


Ico Adamu Smith yerekana ni uko umuntu ashobora gukorera inyungu yiwe gusa mu gukorera inyungu z’abandi:


> Nimumpe ivyo nkeneye, namwe muzoronka ivyo mukeneye mwebwe ubwanyu. (...) Si ku buntu bw’umubaji, umuhinguzi w’inzoga canke umutetsi w’umukate twiteze ivyo kurya vyacu vy’ijoro, ahubwo ni ukuntu babona inyungu zabo bwite.

![image](assets/en/017.webp)


---

> “Ikigoro umuntu wese agira kugira ngo amere neza... kirakomeye cane, ku buryo ari co conyene, kandi ata n’umwe amufasha, kidashobora gusa gutuma ikibano kibandanya gushika ku butunzi no ku guterimbere, ariko kandi kigashobora gutsinda intambamyi ijana zitagira akamaro ubupfu bw’amategeko y’abantu akenshi cane buremesha ibikorwa vyaco.”
> _Ubutunzi bw'amahanga_
> _Igitabo ca IV, igice ca V_

---

Exchange ni umukino w’amahera meza. Ico umwe aronka, uwundi na we araronka. Bitandukanye rero n’ugusubira gusangira mu vya politike aho hama hari uwutsinda n’uwutsindwa. Nitwazirikana ishure ry’icongereza, kuri Smith, kuri Ricardo, no kuri Locke imbere yabo, agaciro karafitaniye isano n’igikorwa. Ku bwa Marx, ni ko biri.


![image](assets/en/018.webp)

_(Marikisi, Rikardo, Simiti, Loki)_


Ku rundi ruhande, Bastiat azokwemera kumwe na Jean-Baptiste Say ko akamaro ari wo mushinge nyawo w’agaciro. Ugukora ntigutuma umuntu agira agaciro. Ubukene na bwo nyene ntibugira. Ivyo vyose biva ku ntumbero. Nkako, nta n’umwe yemera kwishura igikorwa kiretse abonye ko ari ngirakamaro. Umwe gusa ni we yigera atanga inyungu.

Ariko Bastiat na we nyene yaravuze Say kuri iyo ngingo. Si ivy’akamaro kari mu bintu, ni ivy’akamaro k’ibikorwa. « Agaciro ni igipimo c’ibikorwa bibiri bihindurwa », nk’uko amajambo yiwe bwite abivuga. Rero, agaciro ni ikintu umuntu yiyumvira, kandi uburyo bwonyene bwo gutahura ivyo umuntu akunda ni ukwihweza inyifato yiwe mw’isoko ry’uburenganzira. Isoko ryerekana ivyo umuntu akunda kandi ni ryo rigenzura cane ikibano biciye ku Exchange.


Ubutunzi bukurikiza amategeko menshi yoroshe akomoka ku nyifato y’umuntu. Imwe muri zo, yitwa "Itegeko rya Say", ni iyi: "Ibintu n'ibikorwa birahindurwa n'ibintu n'ibikorwa." Iciyumviro ciwe ni uko amahanga n’abantu ku giti cabo bungukira ku kwiyongera kw’urugero rw’umwimbu kuko bitanga amahirwe menshi yo guhanahana amakuru kugirira akamaro bose.


![image](assets/en/019.webp)


Nkako, ibintu bigurwa gusa mu kwitega ibikorwa umuguzi yiteze: Ndagura disiki y’umuziki nzokwumviriza, ndagura itike ya filime y’iyo filime nzobona. Kandi muri Exchange, umuhari wose ufata ingingo kubera ko ucira urubanza ko ushobora kuronka ibikorwa vyinshi mu vyo uronka kuruta ivyo uheba. Muri ivyo, amahera ni igicuruzwa gusa c’ubuhuza, arishura igikorwa umuntu yakoze kandi akagurura ibindi bikorwa.


Ku bwa Bastiat, ubutunzi bw’uguhanahana, ni ukuvuga ubw’ibikorwa vy’ugufashanya bitangwa ku buntu kandi vyemerwa, ni vyo bishigikira amahoro n’uguterimbere, bigatuma habaho uguhuza inyungu.


Ariko kuri Jean-Baptiste Say, Frédéric Bastiat na we nyene arazwe iciyumviro nyamukuru, ico gusahura. Kuko, avuga, asubiramwo amajambo ya Say ati:


> Hari uburyo bubiri gusa bwo kuronka ibintu bikenewe kugira ngo ubuzima buzigame, busharizwe kandi butere imbere: uguhingura n’ugusahura.

Abahingura ibintu bakoresha uburyo bwo kwemeza, uguhanahana amakuru be n’ubuhinga bwa Contract, mu gihe abasahura bakoresha inguvu n’ubuhendanyi. Ni itegeko rero ryo guhagarika ubusahuzi no kuronka abakozi hamwe n’itunga. Nk’uko Adamu Smith yari yaramaze kubivuga, gucungera umutekano w’abanyagihugu ni co gikorwa nyamukuru c’ubutegetsi bwa Leta, kandi ni co gituma gusaba imisoro biba vyemewe.

## Antoine Desituti wa Trasi


<chapterId>ddf64e9f-2ce0-5651-8eb8-bae578eb0b9b</chapterId>


Ntibizwi cane, ariko Destutt de Tracy yarafise uruhara runini ku bijanye n’uwazoba Prezida wa Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika, Thomas Jefferson, igihe yari intumwa i Paris mu myaka ya 1780.


> Ku muntu wese, igihugu ciwe ca mbere ni igihugu ciwe, ica kabiri ni Ubufaransa" & "Ububisha ni igihe abantu batinya intwaro yabo; umwidegemvyo ni igihe leta itinya abantu.
>

> Toma Yeferoni

![image](assets/en/021.webp)


Nkako, igitabu ciwe citwa Traité sur l’économie politique caraciriye urubanza uburinzi n’ukwaguka kwa Napoléon. Ni co gituma ryabujijwe gusohoka mu Bufaransa na Bonaparte. Ariko rero, ryahinduwe mu congereza, risohorwa muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika na Jefferson ubwiwe. Ico canditswe yaracigize igitabu ca mbere c’ubutunzi bwa politike co muri kaminuza ya Virginia, yari ahejeje gushinga i Charlottesville. Ico gitabu nticasohowe mu Bufaransa gushika mu 1819!


![image](assets/en/022.webp)


Destutt de Tracy, umunyabwenge akaba n'umuhinga mu vy'ubutunzi, yari umurongozi w'ishure ryitwa "Ideologues", ryari rigizwe n'abantu nka Cabanis, Condorcet, Constant, Daunou, Say na Germaine de Staël. Ni bo baragwa b’Abafiziyokarate n’abigishwa bataziguye ba Turgot.


![image](assets/en/023.webp)


![image](assets/en/024.webp)


Mu kuvuga ingengabitekerezo, Tracy yashaka gusa kuvuga ubuhinga bujanye n’ukwiga ivyiyumviro, inkomoko yavyo, amategeko yavyo, isano bifitaniye n’ururimi, ni ukuvuga mu majambo y’iki gihe, ubuhinga bwo kumenya ibintu. Ijambo "ingengabitekerezo" ntiryari rifise insiguro mbi Marx yari gutanga mu nyuma kugira ngo asebanye abahinga mu vy'ubutunzi ba "laissez-faire". Ikinyamakuru c’umuhari w’abanyavyiyumviro citwa La Décade filozofique et littéraire.


![image](assets/en/025.webp)


Yari iganje mu kiringo c’amahinduka kandi yarongowe na Jean-Baptiste Say. Destutt de Tracy yatowe kuba umunyamuryango w’ishure ry’ubufaransa mu 1808, n’uw’ishure ry’ubuhinga bw’inyifato runtu n’ivya politike mu 1832. Umukobwa wiwe yubatse na Georges Washington de La Fayette (umuhungu wa prezida wa mbere wa Amerika) mu 1802, ivyo bikaba vyerekana ubucuti bwa hafi bwariho muri ico gihe hagati y’Ubufaransa na wewe.


![image](assets/en/026.webp)

Intumbero y'igitabu ciwe kivuga ku bukungu bwa politike ni "ugusuzuma uburyo bwiza bwo gukoresha ubushobozi bwacu bwose bw'umubiri n'ubwenge kugira ngo turonke ivyo dukeneye bitandukanye." Iciyumviro ciwe ni uko ubudandaji ari bwo buva ku vyiza vyose vy’abantu; ni inguvu z’isi zitera imbere, zituma umuntu agira ivyiyumviro kandi zituma umuntu agira amahoro. Iciyumviro gikomeye c'ubutunzi bwa politike cashizweho na we gutya: «ubudandaji ni ikibano cose, nk'uko igikorwa ari ubutunzi bwose». Nkako, abona ko ikibano ari "urutonde rw'uguhanahana amakuru aho abo bompi bama baronka inyungu." Isoko rero ni ikinyuranyo c’ugufata ibikoko. Riratunganya bamwe ataco ribeneza abandi. Nk'uko bizovugwa mu nyuma, si "umukino w'umubare zero", ahubwo ni umukino w'umubare mwiza.


Umwanditsi wacu ntashika kure ngo asobanure ubutunzi bwa politike nk’ubuhinga bwo guhanahana amakuru. Ariko iyo nzira nyene y’uguzirikana izofatwa kandi ishikirizwe na Bastiat. Gugurisha ni Exchange y'ibintu, gukodesha ni Exchange y'ibikorwa, kandi kuguriza ni Exchange gusa yasubitswe. Ubutunzi bwa politike rero buca buba kuri Bastiat "inyigisho ya Exchange."


![image](assets/en/027.webp)


Nk’uko Destutt de Tracy abivuga, umutungo uva ata kabuza kuri kamere yacu, ku bushobozi bwacu bwo kwipfuza. Iyo umuntu ataco ashaka, nta burenganzira canke inshingano yoba afise. Kugira ngo umuntu ashobore guhabwa ivyo akeneye no gushitsa ibikorwa vyiwe, ategerezwa gukoresha uburyo aronka biciye ku bikorwa vyiwe. Kandi uburyo bw’imitunganirize y’imibano bujanye n’iyo ntumbero ni umutungo w’abantu ku giti cabo. Ni co gituma intumbero imwe rudende ya Leta ari ukurinda itunga no kwemera ko Exchange igira amahoro.


![image](assets/en/028.webp)


Kuri we, imisoro myiza cane ni iyo iringaniye, kandi yipfuza ko amahera Leta ikoresha yoba ari make uko bishoboka kwose. Aracira urubanza gusahura ubutunzi bw’ikibano na leta mu buryo bw’imyenda ya Leta, imisoro, ubutegetsi bw’amabanki n’amahera akoreshwa. Na none, itegeko rikwiye gukoreshwa gusa mu kurinda umwidegemvyo; ntibikwiye kwigera bisahura.


Mu gusozera, yongerako iyi mpanuro, itatakaje akamaro kayo:


> Reka Leta ntikore kandi ntishobore gukora imyenda itera amayaruka azoza kandi yama ijana ibihugu mu gusenyuka kwavyo.

Mu gusozera, Abanyavyiyumviro bari bafise iciyumviro gikomeye cane, ni ukuvuga ko uguhingura n’uguhana ari umuti nyawo w’ingorane za politike, n’ubundi buryo nyabwo bwo gutorera umuti intambara. Intambara zama zitera abantu, zaba izo mu mutima, nk’igihe c’Impinduka, canke izo hanze, nk’izo zarwanywe n’abami ba kera na Napoléon.


## Saroli Konte na Saroli Dunoyer


<chapterId>80bc5c4e-ac07-52c8-9dd7-e224ac291bda</chapterId>


Amateka y’amateka yose ni inkuru y’urugamba hagati y’imigwi y’abasahura n’imigwi y’abatanga umusaruro. Ivyo ni vyo vyizerwa vy’abo banditsi babiri tugiye kuganirako. Ni bo batanguje inyigisho y’ubwigenge y’urugamba rw’imigwi y’abantu, yatumye Frédéric Bastiat agira ivyiyumviro nk’uko Karl Marx yavyiyumviriye, naho uwo wa nyuma yavyigoramye.


![image](assets/en/029.webp)


Ku bwa Comte na Dunoyer, gusahura, bisobanura ubwoko bwose bw’ubukazi bukoreshwa mu kibano n’abakomeye ku bagoyagoya, ni urufunguruzo runini rwo gutahura amateka y’abantu. Ni ryo ntango y’ibintu vyose vy’ugukoresha nabi umugwi umwe n’uwundi.


![image](assets/en/030.webp)


Nimba Frédéric Bastiat afise umwenda w’inyigisho ziwe z’ubutunzi kuri Smith, Destutt de Tracy na Say, inyigisho ziwe zo mu vya politike ayikesha abarongozi b’ikinyamakuru Le Censeur, ari bo Charles Comte na Charles Dunoyer.


Iryo suzuma (1814-1819), ryahinduwe izina ryitwa Le Censeur européen inyuma y’Imisi Ijana, ryarakwiragije ivyiyumviro vy’ubwigenge vyatsinze mu 1830 n’umugumuko w’Imisi Itatu y’Ubuninahazwa be n’ugutera imbere kw’Umwami w’i Orléans, Louis-Philippe wa mbere.


![image](assets/en/031.webp)




Charles Comte, muvyara wa Auguste Comte akaba n’umukwe wa Say, ni we yashinze iyo nkuru y’isubiramwo. Bidatevye, aca afatanywa na Charles Dunoyer, umuhinga mu vy’amategeko nka we, hanyuma aca afatanywa n’umuhinga mu vya kahise akiri muto yitwa Augustin Thierry, yahora ari umunyamabanga wa Simon mweranda. Icivugo cabo ku rupapuro rwa mbere rw'inomero yose y'isubiramwo cari "Amahoro n'Umwidegemvyo".


None intumbero y’iryo subiramwo ni iyihe? Iryo zina ry’ico kiganiro riravuga: gucengera Leta. Kurwanya ububisha bw’ubutegetsi mu gutanga umuco ku vyiyumviro vya bose, kugwanira umwidegemvyo w’itangazamakuru.


![image](assets/en/033.webp)

_(Benyamini ahoraho)_


Bafata kuri Benjamin Constant itandukaniro riri hagati y’Aba kera n’Aba none, rirangwa ku ruhande rumwe n’intambara, ku rundi n’ubudandaji n’inganda. Ariko bongerako na Say ko ubutunzi bwa politike ari bwo butanga insobanuro nziza cane y’ibintu bibaho mu kibano. Baratahura canecane yuko amahanga ashika ku mahoro n’uguterimbere iyo uburenganzira bwo kuronka itunga n’ubudandaji bwigenga vyubahirijwe. Kuva ubu, kuri bo, ubutunzi bwa politike ni bwo bushingiro nyakuri kandi bwonyene bwa politike. Ku filozofiya, igarukira ku gunegura uburyo bw’intwaro mu buryo butaboneka, hategerezwa gusubirizwa n’inyigisho ishingiye ku bumenyi bw’inyungu z’ubutunzi.


> Ubutunzi bwa politike, mu kwerekana ingene abantu batera imbere n’ingene bagabanuka, bwashizeho imishinge nyakuri ya politike.
>

> Dunoyeri

![image](assets/en/034.webp)


Iyi nyigisho nshasha y’imibano irimwo imwe muri Elements yobaye ibuye ry’imfuruka ry’ubumwe bwa siyansi bwa Marx na Engels: urugamba rw’imigwi. Ariko none, inyigisho y’ubwigenge y’urugamba rw’imigwi igizwe n’iki, kandi itandukanye gute n’ubumarxiste?


Bitangura n’umuntu ku giti ciwe akora kugira ngo ashitse ivyo akeneye n’ivyo yipfuza. Kuva umuntu arema, ni ukuvuga ko yongera akamaro k’ibintu, akongera agaciro kavyo, aca yinjira mu nganda. Aha, umuhinga mu vy’inganda si nyen’inganda nk’uko ururimi rwo muri iki gihe rushobora kubivuga, ahubwo ni umuhinguzi, ataravye igikorwa bakoramwo. Ni co gituma inyigisho yabo yitwa inganda. Ivuga ko intumbero y’ikibano ari uguhingura akamaro mu buryo bwagutse, ni ukuvuga ibintu n’ibikorwa vy’ingirakamaro ku bantu.


Kuri iyo ngingo, abantu ku giti cabo barahura n’uburyo bubiri bw’ishimikiro: barashobora gusahura ubutunzi buva ku bandi, canke bagakora kugira ngo bo ubwabo baronke ubutunzi. Mu kibano ico ari co cose, umuntu arashobora gutandukanya neza ababeshwaho n’ivyo basahuye n’ababeshwaho n’ivyo bakora. Mu gihe c’Ubutegetsi bwa kera, abakuru b’igihugu baratera ata guca ku ruhande abakora cane kugira ngo babeho ku buryo bushasha bwo gutanga umusoro: umusoro. Abanyacubahiro b’abanyaruyeri bakurikiwe n’amasinzi y’abanyabiro, ata n’abanyaruyeri bari bake.


![image](assets/en/035.webp)


Mu gihe kuri Marx, ububisha bw’imigwi buri mu gikorwa c’umwimbu ubwaco, hagati y’abakozi n’abakoresha, kuri Comte na Dunoyer, imigwi irwanya ni, ku ruhande rumwe, abahingura mu kibano, bariha imisoro (harimwo abanyagihugu, abakozi, abarimyi, intiti, n’ibindi) no ku rundi ruhande, abatari umusaruro, babaho n’abatari umusaruro. idle and devouring class" (abakozi bo mu biro, abategetsi, abanyapolitike, abaronka infashanyo canke uburinzi).


Maze, mu buryo butandukanye na Marx, abanditse igitabu Censeur Européen ntibashigikira intambara y’imigwi. Ahubwo barakora isekeza ry’amahoro mu kibano. Kandi ivyo, nk’uko babivuga, bishobora gushika gusa biciye mu gukuraho politike mu kibano. Kugira ngo ivyo bishoboke, birahambaye ko babanza kugabanya icubahiro n’inyungu z’ibikorwa vya Leta. Ni ngombwa rero guha ububasha mu mugwi wa politike ababikora.


![image](assets/en/036.webp)

Ubwa nyuma, uburyo bumwe rudende bwo gukuraho isi ubusuma bw’umugwi umwe n’uwundi ni ugusambura uburyo nyene butuma ubwo busuma bushoboka: ububasha bwa Leta bwo gutanga no kugenzura umutungo n’ugutanga inyungu zijanye n’uwo mutungo ("ibibanza").


Ivyiyumviro vyabo, vyari bifise ubuhinga bushasha cane, vyari gushinga ikimenyetso iteka ryose kuri Frédéric Bastiat, we ubwiwe yari kuba umuntu yiyumvira cane ku ngorane za politike.


## Cobden n'Ishirahamwe


<chapterId>7181435c-5eae-56e4-8e55-02a24273fdd6</chapterId>


Ni mu mwaka w’1838, i Manchester, abantu bakeyi, batazwi cane gushika ico gihe, barakoranira hamwe kugira ngo barondere uburyo bwo gutembagaza ubutegetsi bw’abafise amatongo y’ingano biciye mu buryo butegekanijwe n’amategeko no kugira ngo bashitse, nk’uko Bastiat azobivuga mu nyuma,


![image](assets/en/037.webp)


> Ata gusesa amaraso, kubera ububasha bw’ivyiyumviro gusa, ihinduka rikomeye, kumbure rikomeye kuruta iryo ba sogokuruza bacu bakoze mu 1789.

Muri iyo nama hazova Ishirahamwe ry’abarwanya amategeko y’ibigori, canke amategeko y’intete, nk’uko Bastiat yovyita. Ariko vuba cane, iyo ntumbero yobaye iyo gukuraho burundu kandi ku ruhande rumwe uburinzi.


![image](assets/en/038.webp)


Iyo ntambara y’ubutunzi yo gushaka ubudandaji bw’ubuntu yari kwigarurira Ubwongereza bwose gushika mu 1846. Mu Bufaransa, uretse abantu bakeyi bari batanguye, ukubaho kw’uwo muhari munini ntikwari kumenyekanye na gato. Mu gusoma ikinyamakuru c’icongereza, ico yari yarandikishije ku mpanuka, ni ho Frédéric Bastiat yamenya ko iyo Leta iriho mu 1843. Kubera ko yari afise igishika, yarahinduye insiguro za Cobden, Fox na Bright. Hanyuma yandikirana na Cobden amakete, amaherezo mu 1845, aja i Londres kugira ngo ajane mu makoraniro maninimanini y’Ishirahamwe.


![image](assets/en/039.webp)


Iryo sekeza ryo gutera umuvurungano kubera ubudandaji bwigenga, mu bwami bwose, rifise abagize umuryango ibihumbi mirongo, ni ryo ryatumye Bastiat agira umuriro, rihindura bimwe bikomeye kandi ata gukeka inzira y’ubuzima bwiwe.


Iryo Shirahamwe rishobora kugereranywa na kaminuza y’ingendo, yigisha mu vy’ubutunzi abaja mu makoraniro yaryo mu gihugu cose, ni ukuvuga abantu basanzwe, abahinga mu vy’inganda, abarimyi n’abarimyi, abo bose iryo shirahamwe ryari ryarafashe munsi y’amababa yaryo kandi amategeko y’intete akaba inyungu zabo zihahaza. Richard Cobden ni we yari umutima w’uwo muhari kandi yari umunyamivurungano w’agatangaza.


![image](assets/en/040.webp)


Yari umuvugizi ateye umunezero kandi ateye ubwoba, yari afise ingabirano idasanzwe yo guhingura amajambo ateye ubwoba kandi ateye igomwe, atari kure y’insiguro zitagira uko zingana z’abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi.


> None umukate w’umuntu wenyene ni iki? » aca arasemerera. Ni ubukene bw’umukate. Uratangazwa no kumenya ko amategeko y’iki gihugu, kuri ivyo, ata yindi ntumbero afise atari ugutuma habaho ubukene bwinshi bushoboka bw’umukate. Kandi naho biri ukwo nta kindi. Itegeko rishobora gushika ku ntumbero yaryo gusa biciye mu kubura.

![image](assets/en/041.webp)


Mu 1845, Bastiat yarasohoye i Paris igitabu ciwe citwa Cobden and the League, ivyo yahinduye bikaba vyari biherekejwe n’ibisobanuro. Ico gitabu gitangura n’intangamarara yerekeye ubutunzi bw’Ubwongereza, yerekeye kahise k’inkomoko n’iterambere ry’Ishirahamwe. Kuva mu 1815, uburinzi bwarateye imbere cane mu Bwongereza. Hariho cane cane amategeko agenga ivy’intete biva hanze vyari bifise ingaruka zikomeye cane ku bantu. Nkako, ingano zari zikenewe kugira ngo umuntu akore umukate, kikaba cari ikintu gihambaye cane ico gihe. Vyongeye, iyo nzira yarashigikira abakuru b’igihugu, ni ukuvuga ba nyen’amatongo menshi, bakaba ari bo bayironkeramwo amahera y’ubukode.




> Bastiat yanditse ati: “Ikiba kumwe mu Bwongereza ni umubare mutoyi w’abasahura n’umubare munini w’abasahura, kandi umuntu ntakeneye kuba umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi akomeye kugira ngo ashike ku ciyumviro c’ubutunzi bw’aba mbere n’ububabare bw’aba nyuma.

Intumbero y’iryo shirahamwe yari iyo gukoranya ivyiyumviro vya bose kugira ngo bahatire inama nshingamateka gukuraho itegeko ry’intete. Mu gihe kirekire, Cobden n’abagenzi biwe bari bizigiye ko:



- Kwongerera amasoko y'inganda
- Kwongerera akazi
- Kugabanya igiciro c'umukate
- Gutuma uburimyi n'inganda bigenda neza biciye mu mahiganwa
- Guteza imbere amahoro mu bihugu .


![image](assets/en/043.webp)

_(Yeremi Bentamu)_


Cobden yari umwigishwa w’ubuhinga bwa Bentham, kandi yari yemera yuko umwidegemvyo wo gukora n’ubudandaji wari ufasha ata guca ku ruhande inyungu z’abantu benshi cane, abakene cane kandi bababara cane mu kibano. Ahubwo riho, imigenzo nk’igikoresho co kubuza abantu no guha abantu uduteka ata co bazi, yari gushobora gusa kugirira akamaro inganda zimwezimwe zikomeye cane.


Mu matora yo mu 1841, abagize iyo ligi batanu, harimwo na Cobden, baratowe mu nama nshingamateka. Ku wa 26 Rusama 1846, ubudandaji bw’ubuntu bw’uruhande rumwe bwacitse itegeko ry’ubwami. Kuva ico gihe, Ubwongereza bwari kuronka ikiringo ciza cane c’umwidegemvyo n’uguterimbere.

Ikintu gishimishije ni uko Bastiat yifatiye igice c’uburyo bwabo; yarafashe ururimi rwabo, araruhindura mu rurimi rw’igifaransa. Igitabu kivuga ivyerekeye Cobden n’Ishirahamwe ry’Ibihugu vya Leta caraciye gitera imbere ningoga, maze Bastiat yinjira mu buryo buteye ubwoba mw’isi y’abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi. Yashinze ishirahamwe i Bordeaux ry’ugushigikira ubudandaji bw’ubuntu, hanyuma arijana i Paris. Yarahawe uburongozi bw’ikinyamakuru Journal des Économistes. Uwo muhari waravutse, kandi warabandanije gushika mu 1848.

![image](assets/en/044.webp)


Inyuma y’urupfu rwa Bastiat, mu 1866, ni ho Napoléon wa gatatu yari gusinyana amasezerano y’ubudandaji bwigenga n’Ubwongereza, ivyo bikaba vyari nk’intsinzi y’inyuma y’urupfu rw’uwo mugabo yari yarahevye imyaka itandatu ya nyuma y’ubuzima bwiwe bugufi kuri ico ciyumviro gikomeye.


![image](assets/en/045.webp)

_(Misheli Chevaliye)_


Ikibazo c’ubudandaji bw’ubuntu kirabandanya kuba gihambaye no muri iki gihe. Ibitabu vy’inyigisho z’ivy’ubuhinga bw’isi mu mashure bivuga yuko ugutera imbere kw’isi yose ari kwo gutuma ibihugu bikenye bikeneye imfashanyo y’Uburengero kugira ngo bibeho. Yamara, ubukene bukaze bwaragabanutse igice mu myaka 20 iheze. Mu guhitamwo gufungura, ibihugu nk’Ubuhindi, Ubushinwa canke Tayiwani vyarashoboye guhunga ubukene, mu gihe uguhagarara ari kwo kuranga ibihugu vyugaye nka Koreya ya Ruguru canke Venezuela. Nk'uko ONU ibivuga, 36% vy'abantu babayeho mu bukene bwose mu 1990. Ubu ni "18% gusa" mu 2010. Ubukene bukaze buracari ingorane ikomeye, ariko buragenda burasubira inyuma.

## Rousseau


<chapterId>c3926110-e0b2-503c-96d9-5d3a6a661484</chapterId>


Frédéric Bastiat, uwaseruye ivyiyumviro vyiwe mu myaka ya 1840, ni we muragwa w’uruvyaro rw’abanyabwenge b’umuco barwanya ugucengera no kurwanya umwidegemvyo wo guhanahana ivyiyumviro. Iyumvire Montesquieu, Diderot, Voltaire, Condorcet, ariko kandi na Rousseau.


Kuri bo, ico ciyumviro cari coroshe: uko ivyiyumviro vyinshi vyemerwa, ni ko ukuri gutera imbere kandi amakosa akarushiriza guhakanwa bitagoranye. Siyansi yama itera imbere muri ubwo buryo.


![image](assets/en/046.webp)

_(Montesquieu, Diderot, Voltaire, Condorcet, Rousseau)_


Ahubwo, ni bake batahuye yuko ivyo vyari ukuri ku vyiyumviro vyari ukuri no ku bicuruzwa n’ibikorwa. Umwidegemvyo wo gucuruza n’abandi vy’ukuri urafise ingeso zibiri: kuba uwukora neza kandi ukajana mu gusangira mu buryo bubereye. Ivyo Rousseau ntiyabitahuye gusa, ahubwo yararwanije uwo mwidegemvyo mw’izina ry’iciyumviro c’ikinyoma c’amategeko n’uburenganzira. Bastiat avuga ko kimwe mu bintu nyamukuru bituma habaho ubusosiyalisimu ari iciyumviro ca Rousseau c’uko urutonde rwose rw’imibano rukomoka ku mategeko.


Bastiat vy’ukuri abona ko Rousseau ari we yatanguje vy’ukuri ubusosiyalisimu n’ubumwe bw’abantu bose. Mu mwanditsi w'igitabu The Social Contract, hariho ijambo risobanura neza cane filozofiya yiwe: "dutangura gusa kuba abagabo tumaze kuba abanyagihugu."


![image](assets/en/047.webp)


Mu ntango, umuntu ni umunyagihugu gusa. Ariko umunyagihugu ni umuharuro; ashaka umunezero wiwe ubwo nyene, aba umuja w’ibimenyetso vyiwe, w’ivyipfuzo vyiwe, w’inyungu yiwe yihariye. Muri make, ntagira ivyiyumviro, rero ntagira umwidegemvyo. Arakeneye kwigishwa, kugira ngo atahure ko inyungu yiwe nyakuri ari inyungu rusangi. Ni co gituma Rousseau yanditse mu kinyamakuru Contract ati:


![image](assets/en/048.webp)


---

> Uwuzokwanka kwumvira ubushake rusangi azobwirizwa kubigira n’umubiri wose: ivyo ntibisigura ikindi atari uko azobwirizwa kwidegemvya.
> (Jean-Jac Rousseau)

---

Dushingiye kuri iyo nyigisho, umuntu afise ubushake bubiri muri we: ubushake buja ku nyungu z’umuntu ku giti ciwe, ubw’abanyagihugu, n’ubushake buja ku nyungu rusangi, ubw’umwenegihugu. Kuyobora abantu, mbere n’inguvu, ngo bashake iherezo ry’ubwenge, inyungu rusangi, ni ukuyobora abantu kugira ngo babe abidegemvya. Ico bipfuza vy’ukuri ni iherezo ry’ubwenge, naho boba batabizi.


Ni vyiza rero, nk’uko Rousseau abivuga, guhatira abantu mw’izina ry’intumbero bo ubwabo iyo bazoba bafise umuco mwinshi, bari gukurikirana, ariko ntibakurikirane kubera ko ari impumyi, batazi canke bononekaye. Sosiyete ishinzwe kugira ngo ibahatire gukora ivyo bokwipfuza ubwabo iyo bazoba bamurikiwe. Kandi mu kubigira, umuntu ntabakorera ubukazi kuko abajana mu "kwidegemvya", ni ukuvuga guhitamwo neza, amahitamwo ajanye n'uko ari bo vy'ukuri.


![image](assets/en/049.webp)


Rousseau yemera neza ko ikibano ciza ari ikiremwa c’itegeko, aca rero aha ububasha butagira aho bugarukira umushingamateka. Ni we ashobora guhindura abantu ku giti cabo bakaba abagabo b’abanyabwenge, bakaba abanyagihugu.

Ariko, ni itegeko kandi rituma umutungo ubaho. Nk’uko Rousseau abivuga, umutungo ushobora kuba uw’ukuri iyo utunganijwe n’umushingamateka. Nkako, ikibi kiri mu busumbasumbane no mu buja, ivyo vyose bikaba biva ku mutungo. Ni uguhingura kw’abakomeye kwatumye haba ikibano kibi, ikibano c’abanyagihugu, imigenderanire y’ubutegetsi. Mu nsiguro yiwe yerekeye inkomoko n’imishinge y’ubusumbasumbane, yanditse iki gice kizwi cane:


> Umuntu wa mbere, amaze gukingira mu kibanza, ati: Uyu ni uwanje, akabona abantu boroshe bihagije kugira ngo bamwemere, ni we yashinze sosiyete sivile vy’ukuri. Mbega ubugizi bwa nabi, intambara, ubwicanyi, amagorwa n’ubwoba vyari gukizwa umuryango w’abantu n’uwo, akura ibiti canke yuzuza umugende, yari yasemereye bagenziwe ati: «Mwirinde kwumviriza uwo mubeshi;

![image](assets/en/050.webp)


Rero, ivy’akameremere ni vyo bitera ikibi. Kandi Marx, umusomyi akomeye wa Rousseau, ivyo yari kwibuka. None tworwanya gute ico kibi? Biciye mu gitabu Contract c’imibano, Rousseau arishura. Nkako, ikibano ciza ni ico kiva ku gitabu ca Contract kivuga ko umuntu ashobora kwitandukanya n’uburenganzira bwiwe bwose ku kibano. Kuva ico gihe, ni umuryango ushobora guha uburenganzira uwo muntu biciye mu mategeko.


Mu buryo butandukanye n'ubwo Rousseau avuga, Frédéric Bastiat avuga ko «umuntu avuka afise umutungo». Kuri we, umutungo ni ingaruka ikenewe y’akameremere k’umuntu, y’itegeko nshinga ryiwe. Yanditse ati: «Umuntu avuka afise umutungo, kuko avuka afise ivyipfuzo bimuhazwa ari ngirakamaro mu buzima, afise ingingo n’ubushobozi bikenewe cane kugira ngo ivyo bimuhaze». Ariko ubushobozi ni ukwaguka gusa kw’umuntu, kandi umutungo ni ukwaguka gusa kw’ubushobozi. Mu yandi majambo, ni ugukoresha ubushobozi bwacu mu gikorwa bituma umutungo ugira uburenganzira.


![image](assets/en/051.webp)


Nk'uko Bastiat abivuga, ikibano, abantu n'itunga birabaho imbere y'amategeko, kandi afise iri jambo rizwi cane: «Si kubera ko hari amategeko hariho itunga, ahubwo ni kubera ko hari itunga hariho amategeko». Ni co gituma itegeko ritegerezwa kuba ribi: ritegerezwa gukingira abantu n’ibintu vyabo kwinjira mu mubiri. Itunga ni _raison d’être_ y’itegeko si ukundi.


## Uburezi bwa kera


<chapterId>87d9a8c9-2352-5cb2-8b93-678118a8145c</chapterId>

Ku wa 24 Ruhuhuma 1848, inyuma y’imisi itatu y’imigumuko i Paris, Umwami Louis-Philippe wa mbere yarahevye ubutegetsi bwiwe. Ivyo ni vyo vyatumye havuka Repubulika ya kabiri.


![image](assets/en/052.webp)


Bastiat yari i Paris, ariko aribonera ivyo bintu. Mu nyuma, yari kwandika ati:


> Ku wa 24 Ruhuhuma, jewe, cokimwe n’abandi benshi, naratinya ko ihanga ritari ryiteguriye kwitegeka. Ntegerezwa kwemera, naratinya akosho k’ivyiyumviro vy’Abagiriki n’Abaroma bidutegekwa twese n’abahinga mu vy’inyigisho z’idini.

Iki gice kiratangaje. None ivya kera vy’Abagiriki n’Abaroma bifitaniye isano irihe na vyo?


![image](assets/en/053.webp)


Bastiat yerekeza kuri Repubulika ya Platon n’inyigisho yiwe y’umwami w’umunyabwenge, ariko kandi yerekeza kuri Sparta, iyo Rousseau yashima cane, ku Bwami bw’Uburoma, iyo Napoléon yari akumbuye cane. Ikibabaje ni uko nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, ivyo vyiyumviro vy’Abagiriki n’Abaroma bishingiye ku ciyumviro c’ikinyoma: iciyumviro c’ububasha bwose bw’umushinga amategeko, c’ubusegaba bushitse bw’itegeko.


Birahagije gufungura hafi igitabu cose c’ubuhinga bwa filozofiya, politike canke amateka mu buryo bw’impfagusa kugira ngo ubone iki ciyumviro, gishingiye ku muco wacu, c’uko umuntu ari ikintu kitagira ico gikora gihabwa ubuzima, imitunganirize, inyifato runtu, n’uguterimbere biva ku bubasha bwa politike. Iyo abantu basigaye bakora ivyo bashaka, bokwipfuza gutera imbere mu ntambara kandi bokizwa gusa ico kiza n’ukuboko kw’akabanga kandi gushobora vyose kw’Umushingamateka. Ariko rero, Bastiat avuga ko ico ciyumviro kimaze igihe kirekire gikomeye kandi giteguwe n’inyigisho za kera zimaze ibinjana n’ibindi.


![image](assets/en/054.webp)


Ica mbere, avuga ko Abaroma babona ko umutungo ari ikintu gisanzwe gusa, nk’ikintu c’ubuhinga c’itegeko ryanditswe. Kubera iki? Bastiat asigura mu buryo bworoshe, kubera ko babeshwaho n’ubuja n’ugusahura. Kuri bo, ivy’ubutunzi vyose vyari ivyamwa vy’ukwonona. Ntibashobora rero kwinjiza mu mategeko iciyumviro c’uko umushinge w’itunga ryemewe ari igikorwa batasenya imishinge y’ikibano cabo.

Nkako, bari bafise insobanuro y’ivy’ubutunzi, « jus utendi et abutendi » (uburenganzira bwo gukoresha no gukoresha nabi). Ariko rero, iyo nsobanuro yari yerekeye gusa ingaruka atari imvo, mu yandi majambo, inkomoko y’inyifato runtu y’itunga. Kugira ngo umuntu ashinge intahe neza umutungo, ategerezwa gusubira mw’itegeko nshinga ry’umuntu nyene, agatahura isano n’ihuriro rikenewe riri hagati y’ivyo akeneye, ubushobozi, igikorwa n’umutungo. Abaroma, bari bafise abaja, boba bari gushobora gufata iciyumviro c’uko «umuntu wese afise we ubwiwe, rero n’igikorwa ciwe, kandi, kubera ivyo, n’umusaruro w’igikorwa ciwe»? Bastiat aca yibaza.


> Bastiat asozera rero, ntidutangazwe no kubona iciyumviro c’Abaroma c’uko umutungo ari ikintu gisanzwe kandi ko ari ikintu gishingiye ku mategeko gisubira guseruka mu kinjana ca cumi n’umunani; ko, kure y’uko Itegeko ari corollary y’Itunga, ari Itunga ari corollary y’Itegeko.

Nkako, Rousseau arasangiye ico ciyumviro rusangi c’amategeko co gushingira umutungo ku mategeko. Rousseau avuga ko itegeko, n’abantu, ari ryo rifise ububasha bwose ku bantu ku giti cabo no ku mitungo. Kandi muri iyo nsiguro, ari yo nyene igize iciyumviro ca repubulika kuva mu gihe c’Impinduka y’Abafaransa, umushingamateka ategerezwa gutunganya ikibano, nk’umuhinga mu vy’ubwubatsi bw’imibano, nk’umumekanishije ahingura imashini akoresheje ibintu bitagira ico akora, canke nk’umubumvyi ahingura ibumba. Uwushinga amategeko rero yishira hanze y’ubumuntu, hejuru yabwo, kugira ngo abutunganye uko ashatse, akurikije imigambi yiyumviriwe n’ubwenge bwiwe bumurika.


![image](assets/en/055.webp)


Ahubwo kuri Bastiat, uburenganzira bwo kuronka umutungo burabanza itegeko. Ivyo ni vyo yita ingingo ngenderwako y’abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi, itandukanye n’ingingo ngenderwako y’abahinga mu vy’amategeko. Naho «ingingo ngenderwako y’abahinga mu vy’amategeko hafi irimwo ubuja, ni ko Bastiat avuga, iyo abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi irimwo umwidegemvyo».


None umwidegemvyo ni iki? Ni umutungo, uburenganzira bwo kwinovora ivyamwa vy’ibikorwa vy’umuntu, uburenganzira bwo gukora, gutera imbere, gukoresha ubushobozi bwiwe, uko abona ko bikwiye, ata Leta yinjiramwo mu buryo bundi atari mu gikorwa cayo co kumukingira.


Birababaje kwiyumvira ko filozofiya yacu y’imibano n’iya politike yagumye ifatanye n’iciyumviro c’uko umuti w’ingorane zacu zose wategerezwa kuva hejuru, ukava mw’itegeko, ukava kuri Leta. Ariko ivyo birashobora gusigurwa. Ivyo vyiyumviro bishirwa mu rubyiruko buri musi mu mashure no muri kaminuza, biciye mu kwigarurira uburezi.


![image](assets/en/056.webp)

_akarorero k'uwo mukozi w'ubutegetsi bwiwe gusa yoshobora kuba ikigo ca leta_


Ariko rero, nk’uko Bastiat abitwibutsa, ubutegetsi bw’umuntu umwe gusa burakuraho iterambere.


## Uburinzi n'ubusosiyalisimu


<chapterId>ce6cb8a8-7dc9-5ef7-939d-9a559b4d2c74</chapterId>


![image](assets/en/057.webp)

_(Rishari Kobden)_


Nk’uko twamaze kubibona, ni urugamba rwa Cobden rwo kurwanya uburinzi bw’igihugu n’ishirahamwe ry’Abongereza ryo gukuraho amategeko agenga ibigori rwatumye Bastiat yandika ingingo hanyuma akandika ibitabu.


Ugukingira igihugu, mu vy’ukuri, ni uburyo bwo gukunda igihugu mu vy’ubutunzi. Igamije gukuraho uguhiganwa n'amahanga mu gihe yigira nk'aho "aburanira inyungu z'igihugu." Baca bagerageza gutuma abategetsi ba Leta bemera urutonde rw’ibinyoma vy’ubudemagogi gusa, bigaragazwa nk’ivy’ingeso nziza: ukurwanira akazi, uguhiganwa, n’ibindi.


![image](assets/en/058.webp)

_akarorero ko kwamamaza igikoresho co gutera ivyatsi gikorerwa mu Bufaransa_


---

> Inama yacu na Arnaud Montebourg
> Yakozwe mu Bufaransa,
> aravyemera, twabigerageje

---

Iciyumviro co gukingira akazi ni co Bastiat yita ubuhendanyi. Kuko mu vy’ukuri, bingana n’ikori. Bifise ingaruka zo gutuma ibintu bizimvye cane. Reka dufate akarorero katanzwe na Bastiat ubwiwe.


Ibaze imbugita y’icongereza igurishwa mu gihugu cacu ku ma euro 2, imbugita ikozwe mu Bufaransa ikaba igura ama € 3. Nitwareka umuguzi akagura mu mwidegemvyo imbugita yipfuza, azigama euro 1, ashobora kuyishiramwo ahandi (mu gitabu, canke mu karamu).


Nitwabuza igicuruzwa c’icongereza, umuguzi azoriha uwundi unité imwe ku mbugita yiwe. Ugukingira rero bituma inganda y’igihugu ironka inyungu, n’ibihombo bibiri, kimwe ku yindi nganda (iy’amakaramu) ikindi ku baguzi. Ahubwo, ubudandaji bw’ubuntu butuma abantu babiri batsinda banezerewe.




Ugukingira igihugu na co nyene ni uburyo bwo kurwana n’abanyagihugu. Nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, ni uburyo bushingiye ku bwikunzi n’ukunzi kw’abahinguzi. Kugira ngo bongere impembo zabo, abarimyi canke abahinga mu vy’inganda basaba imisoro kugira ngo isoko ry’ibicuruzwa vyo hanze rifungwe, gutyo bagahatira abaguzi kwishura vyinshi ku bicuruzwa vyabo.


![image](assets/en/060.webp)


Bastiat ashigikiye cane abaguzi. Ku nyungu z’umugwi, ashiraho inyungu rusangi, ari yo nyungu y’umuguzi, ni ukuvuga inyungu y’umuntu wese. Igihe cose ni ukubera ivyiyumviro vy’umuguzi Leta ikwiye kwishingira igihe ikora.


Mu gihe c’ihinduka ryo muri Ruhuhuma 1848 n’inzitizi zaryo, umwansi ateye ubwoba kuruta uburinzi bw’igihugu yari guseruka, uwo busangiye n’ibintu vyinshi: ubusosiyalisimu.


![image](assets/en/061.webp)


Ni igiki? Ni umuhari wa politike usaba gutunganya abakozi hakurikijwe itegeko, inganda n’amabanki bibe ivy’igihugu, ubutunzi bugasubira gusangira biciye mu gutanga imisoro. Bastiat ubu yari gukoresha inguvu ziwe zose, ubuhinga bwiwe bwose, n’ivyanditswe vyiwe vyose kugira ngo arwanye iyo nyigisho nshasha, ivyo bikaba vyari gushobora gusa gutuma ububasha butera imbere cane, n’urugamba rw’imigwi rudahera. Gutyo, kuva mu misi ya mbere y'ihinduka, yaratanze umusanzu mu kinyamakuru c'igihe gito citwa "La République Française", kikaba caramenyekanye ningoga nk'ikinyamakuru kirwanya ihinduka. Ico ni co gihe yandika udutabu twiwe twerekeye itunga, Leta, ubusahu n’amategeko.


Ku wa 27 Ruheshi 1848, umusi ukurikira umugumuko mushasha w’amaraso wari i Paris, mw’ikete rirerire yandikiye Richard Cobden, yarashize umutima ku bintu vyari gushobora gutuma ivyo bintu bishika.



- 1° Ica mbere muri izo mpamvu ni ubujuju mu vy’ubutunzi. Ivyo ni vyo bitegura ubwenge bwo kwakira utopiya z’ubusosiyalisimu n’uburepubulikani bw’ikinyoma. Nca nsubira ku videwo iheze yerekeye imigenderanire y’inyigisho za kera n’iza kaminuza kuri iyo ngingo.



- 2° Ihanga ryarakunda cane iciyumviro c’uko ubuvukanyi n’ubumwe bishobora gushirwa mu mategeko. Ni ukuvuga ko ryasaba ko Leta iremera ata guca ku ruhande umunezero ku benegihugu bayo. Aha niho Bastiat abona intango y’igihugu c’imibereho myiza.


![image](assets/en/062.webp)


Kandi yari kubandanya gusuzuma ingaruka zavyo zigoramye inyuma y’aho. Aha ni akarorero kamwe, kavuzwe mw’ikete yandikiye Cobden:


> Kubera impengamiro z’umutima w’umuntu, umuntu wese yaratanguye gusaba Leta, ku bwiwe, umugabane munini w’ukumererwa neza. Ni ukuvuga ko Leta canke itunga rya Leta ryashirwa mu gusahura. Ivyicaro vyose vyasaba Leta, nk’aho ari uburenganzira, uburyo bwo kubaho. Utwigoro twagizwe na Leta muri iyo nzira twatumye gusa haba imisoro n’intambamyi, kandi n’amagorwa arushirizaho.


- 3° Bastiat yongerako ko mu vyiyumviro vyiwe, uburinzi ari bwo bwa mbere bwagaragaje iyo ntambara. Abanyagihugu baciye batangura gusaba ko itegeko ryobafasha kugira ngo bongere umugabane wabo w’ubutunzi. Nta gukeka ko abo bakozi na bo nyene bashaka gukora ivyo nyene.


![image](assets/en/063.webp)


---

> KURORANIRA
> GUTORA ABASOSIYALISI SFIO

---

Mu gusozera, abaharanira uburinzi n’abasosiyalisiti barasangiye iciyumviro kimwe, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga: ico barondera mw’itegeko si ugutuma umuntu wese akoresha ubushobozi bwiwe ataco yishisha, akaronka impembo ibereye kubera utwigoro twiwe, ahubwo ni ugushigikira ko uwundi mugwi w’abanyagihugu ukoreshwa nabi bimwe bishitse canke bike. Mu bijanye n’uburinzi, ni bakeyi bakoresha nabi benshi. Mu gihe c’ubusosiyalisimu, ni benshi bakoresha nabi bake. Muri ivyo bihe vyose bibiri, ubutungane burarenganywa, inyungu rusangi zirahungabanywa. Bastiat abateranya.


> Leta ni inkuru y’ibinyoma ikomeye umuntu wese yihatira kubaho kubera uwundi wese.

## Ubwibone


<chapterId>96902abd-6915-5b25-a187-a4790162b86c</chapterId>


Pierre-Joseph Proudhon ni umwe mu baserukira cane ubusosiyalisimu bw’Ubufaransa hagati mu kinjana ca 19. Azwi cane cane muri iri jambo: «Itunga ni ubusuma» mu gitabu «Itunga ni iki?» mu 1840.


![image](assets/en/064.webp)



Hariho ikintu kidasobanutse mu buryo bubereye muri ivyo vyemezo. Kuko iyo hataba umutungo umuntu aronka mu buryo bubereye, mu buryo bubereye ntihashobora kubaho igikorwa nk’ubusuma. Ni co gituma Proudhon azoheza agatomora ko ari ugusangira vy’ukuri kw’itunga abona ko ari ubusuma, atari itunga ubwaryo, ivyo adondora nk’inkomezi z’ihinduka zishingiye ku kibano c’abanyagitugu.


![image](assets/en/066.webp)


Ariko Proudhon ni umunyagitugu w’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Ntabona ko abanyagihugu, canke Leta, ari bo bafise ububasha bubereye. Aranegura cane ubukomunisita kandi akavuga ko abakozi bofatanya, uburyo bwo gukorana n’abandi mu buryo butunganijwe, bwoba bwishingikirije ku gukoranya ubutunzi ku bushake kugira ngo bafashanye. Ntibizwi cane ariko Bastiat ntiyarwanya na gato ico ciyumviro mu ngingo ngenderwako. Yaratinya gusa ko Leta yoyihindura igikorwa ca Leta gifise uburenganzira bwo gukora de facto. Amateka yokwemeza ko ari ukuri.




Ku rundi ruhande, birazwi neza ko muri « Ubukene bwa filozofiya », Marx yotera n’ubukazi Proudhon n’ubusosiyalisimu bwiwe, ubwo yise « utopique », ashigikira ico bita ubusosiyalisimu « bwa siyansi ».


![image](assets/en/068.webp)

Muri Ruheshi 1848, Proudhon yatowe mu Nteko Nkuru y’Igihugu, ari kumwe na Bastiat. Bari abagenzi kandi bari bubaha cane. Ariko rero, mu 1849, mu ntambara ikomeye cane, Bastiat yarahanahana amakete cumi n’ane mu nkingi z’ikinyamakuru La Voix du Peuple. Muri iyo Exchange ikomeye, yaratomoye ivyiyumviro vyiwe ku bibazo vy’amahera n’ivy’amabanki. Intambara yaciye ishika ku yindi nzira ikurikira: inguzanyo y’ubuntu canke inguzanyo y’ubuntu?


![image](assets/en/069.webp)


Proudhon yabonye ko inyungu ku mutungo ari yo ntambamyi ya mbere y’ubukene n’ubusumbasumbane bw’ibintu. Yaravugiye ko banki ya Leta (Exchange Bank canke Banki y'Abantu) yorema amahera ataco amaze, kandi yabonye ko "inguzanyo y'ubuntu" ari umuti w'ingorane y'imibano. Ku rundi ruhande, Bastiat yari uwushigikiye umwidegemvyo w’amabanki, bisobanura gutunganya ingendo y’amahera biciye mu mwidegemvyo wo gushika ku mwuga, bifatanijwe n’inshingano zikenewe ku mahera y’umuntu bwite, n’umwidegemvyo wo guhiganwa.


![image](assets/en/070.webp)


Bastiat yarahakanye uwo bariko bararwanya mu ntambwe zitari nke. Ubwa mbere, yarasuzumye ingaruka mbi z’inguzanyo z’ubuntu be n’uguhingura amahera. Ubwo buryo bwoshobora gusa gutera intege ibikorwa vy'akaga kandi vy'ubujuju vy'amabanki n'abakozi b'abikorera ku giti cabo kuko bazi ko bikingirwa na Leta, ni ukuvuga n'amahera y'abasoreshwa: "Ni ikintu gikomeye gushiramwo abantu bose mu gihe bavuga ngo: Reka tugerageze amahirwe yacu n'itunga ry'uwundi; Ijambo ry’imbere y’igihe nk’uko ryoshobora gukoreshwa mu gihe cacu.


![image](assets/en/071.webp)


Politike y’inyungu ntoyi ikoreshwa n’amabanki akomeye ni uburyo bwo kurema amahera mu buryo bw’ubuhinga. Kandi ingorane zikurikiranye z’urutonde rw’ivy’ubutunzi mu kinjana ca nyuma, hamwe n’imyenda y’ibihugu, ni ingaruka zavyo zitaziguye.


Hanyuma Bastiat yerekana ko bishoboka ko abakozi bashobora kugura ibintu vyinshi, ariko mu bundi buryo, bikagira ubutungane kandi bikaba vyiza. Kuri we, kugabanya inyungu na vyo nyene ni co kigamije politike y’ubwigenge. Ariko ivyo bishika mu kwibohora no mu kwirundanira umutungo, atari mu gukuraho inyungu, ni ukuvuga inguzanyo y’ubuntu.


![image](assets/en/072.webp)


Nkako, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, ugutera imbere kw’abantu guhuye n’ugushinga umutungo. Mu gatabo kiwe kitwa Umutungo n’Ubukode, Bastiat aradutahura ivyo ari kumwe na Robinson Crusoé kw’izinga ryiwe.


![image](assets/en/073.webp)

Iyo Robinson atagira umutungo canke ibikoresho vyirundanijwe, yari gucirwa urubanza rwo gupfa. Araheza asigura ko umutungo utunganya umukozi mu buryo bubiri:



- Birongera umusaruro, gutyo bikagabanya igiciro c’ibintu vyo gukoresha;
- ivyo bikaba bifise ingaruka zo kwongerera umushahara.


Mu kibano ca none, umutungo ukora nk’inguvu ziringaniza. Nkako, Bastiat avuga ati:


> Iyo umutungo wiyongera, urahiganwa na wo nyene; impembo yayo iragabanuka, canke mu yandi majambo, inyungu iragabanuka.

Mu gusozera, Proudhon na Bastiat bompi baratahuye akamaro ko kwirundanira umutungo n’impengamiro abagabo bamwebamwe bafise yo gukoresha nabi abandi. Ariko rero, ntibashitse ku nsozero imwe. Proudhon, nka Marx, yari yiteze ko abantu benshi bo mu bihugu vy’abanyagihugu bazorushiriza gukenera. Bastiat yarizera ko ubu kapitalizimu bwotuma haba uguterimbere kutagira uko kungana mu mice yose, n’iterambere ry’imigwi yo hagati iguma igira akamaro kanini. Ivyo ni vyo vy’ukuri vyabaye.


# Ubutunzi


<partId>59686d1d-58c6-59a8-9fc4-74a10d24cdbe</partId>


## Ivyo Biboneka n'Ibitaboneka .


<chapterId>25fb02a9-5d68-5c58-bd0f-d4b8e1fd91f9</chapterId>


Muri iki gice, nzobabwira ubuhinga bushasha cane, ubuhinga bushasha. Umushakashatsi umwe yarakoze amataratara abiri y’ibinyabuzima afise kamera ntoyi ikomeye cane ishizwe imbere. Ubwo buhinga buratuma umuntu abona ido n’ido adashobora kubona n’ijisho ryiwe. Mu maboko, hariho igice c’ubuhinga bwa none kirungika amashusho mu gicu biciye kuri telefone yanje yo mu bwoko bwa smartphone.


![image](assets/en/074.webp)


Uwahinguye ikigereranyo ca mbere c’ayo mabarabara ni Frédéric Bastiat mu 1850 mu gatabu kazwi cane kavuga ngo: _Ce qu’on voit et ce qu’on voit pass_. Ivyo birori ni ivy’umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi. Bituma dushobora gupima ingaruka z’ingingo abategetsi bafata ku buzima bwacu. Ni vyo birori "bituma tubona ivyo tutabona": ugusambura guterwa n'ingingo z'abaguzi n'ivyiyumviro vy'ubutunzi vy'ibinyoma. Akenshi ntitubona abahohotewe, canke abavyungukirako, muri make, ingaruka zavyo nyazo zitandukanye n'ivyo bavuga mu nsiguro z'ubutegetsi, ivyo Bastiat yita "Ubuhinga bw'ubutunzi."

Umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi mwiza, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, ategerezwa kudondora ingaruka ingingo za politike zigira ku kibano. Ariko rero, ntibategerezwa kwitwararika ingaruka z’igihe gito zigira ku mugwi kanaka, ahubwo zitegerezwa kwitwararika ingaruka z’igihe kirekire zigira ku kibano cose. Ni bande bashikirwa n’ivyo bibazo kandi ni bande bavyungukirako? Ni ibihe bintu vyihishijwe vy’itegeko kanaka canke ingingo ya politike? None abasoreshwa boba bakoze iki aho gukoreshwa na Leta n’amahera babatwaye mu misoro? Ivyo ni vyo bibazo vyabajijwe n’umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi mwiza nk’uko Bastiat abivuga.


Gutyo, mu gitabu Ibikorwa vya Leta, Bastiat yandika ati:


> Leta ifungura inzira, yubaka ingoro, igorora ibarabara, yimba umugende; kubera ivyo, bitanga akazi ku bakozi bamwebamwe, ivyo ni vyo biboneka; ariko bikabura akazi abandi bamwebamwe, ivyo nivyo bitaboneka.

Kimwe mu vyiyumviro vy’ubuhinga bwa sophisme bizwi cane ni ikinyoma c’idirisha ryavunitse. Hari abavuga ko gumena idirisha ryo mu nzu bitagira ico bikoze ku bukungu kuko bifasha uwukora ibirahuri. Ariko Bastiat azokwerekana ko ugusambura atari mu nyungu zacu kuko kutarema ubutunzi. Bitwara amahera menshi kuruta ivyo bitanga. Uwo muhungu mutoyi amena idirisha ry’umubanyi, aha akazi uwo mucuzi w’ibirahuri. Ariko rero, ng’uku ukuntu abagenzi biwe bamuhoza:


> Igicu cose gifise igipfukisho c’ifeza. Mwene izo mpanuka zituma iyo nganda iguma itera imbere. Umuntu wese arakeneye kubaho. None abakora amabarabara bobaye iki iyo amadirisho atazokwigera amena?

Gutyo, nk’uko Keynes abivuga, ugusambura itunga, mu guhatira abantu gukoresha amahera, vyotuma ubutunzi butera imbere kandi bikagira “ingaruka zigwiza” zitera inguvu ku bijanye n’umwimbu n’akazi. Ivyo ni vyo gusa bibonwa.


Ariko ikitabona ni ico nyen’iyo nzu yari kugura n’ayo mahera, ariko ubu afise ivyo akora ataco afise, n’ico afise co gukoresha kugira ngo asanure idirisha ryiwe. Ico kitaboneka ni akaryo kazimiye ka nyen’idirisha ryavunitse. Yari gushobora gutanga amahera yahawe uwo muhinguzi w’ibirahuri mu kindi kintu. Iyo atazokoresha amahera yo gusanura idirisha, yari gushobora gukoresha ayo mahera kugira ngo yikoreshe, gutyo agakoresha abantu kugira ngo akore ivyo akora.


Gutyo, nta "gukangura" ubutunzi kuzobaho n'ugumena kw'idirisha kuruta uko ataco bizoba. Ariko rero, hazoba hariho ugutakaza kw’urusenga mu gihe ca mbere: agaciro k’idirisha.


![image](assets/en/075.webp)

Icigwa ca mbere co kwigirako ni uko ingingo "nziza" canke politike "nziza" ari iyo itwara igihugu amahera make kuruta ivyo ukundi gutanga ubutunzi kwari gushobora gutwara. Uko politike ikora neza ntibikwiye gucirwa urubanza bishingiye ku ngaruka zayo gusa, ariko kandi bishingiye ku yindi nzira yari gushobora gukoreshwa. Ico ni co ciyumviro c'"igiciro c'akaryo," Bastiat akundwa cane.


Icigwa ca kabiri ni uko ugusambura kudatuma ubutunzi butera imbere nk’uko aba Keynes babibona ahubwo bituma umuntu agira ubukene. Gusambura ibintu vy’umubiri ntibigira ingaruka nziza ku bukungu, bitandukanye n’ivyo abantu benshi bemera. Kugira ngo dukoreshe amajambo asozera y'igisomwa ca Frédéric Bastiat: "ikibano kiratakaza agaciro k'ibintu vyasambutse ataco bimaze."


Reka dufate akarorero k’ubu. Igihe nyene inganda zikora imodoka ziriko ziragwana n'ingorane, abashinga amategeko bariyumvira imigambi yo gukuraho ibintu kugira ngo "basubire kuyitanguza". Ico tubona ni uko ugurisha kwa Renault na Peugeot kwiyongera. Ico tutabona ni ugutakaza ibindi bice vy’ubutunzi be n’uko imodoka zikora neza zisenyuka.


Ariko hariho n’ubundi buryo bwo guteza imbere ubutunzi. Iyo Leta yinjiye mu migambi ikomeye canke igashiramwo amahera mu nzego zimwe zimwe z’inganda kugira ngo ifashe mu gutanga akazi, mbega iyo si inkuru nziza y’iterambere? Nta kindi, Bastiat yari kwishura. Kuko amafaranga Leta yokoresha yoterwa n’iki? Mu kuduza imisoro canke mu myenda, ni ukuvuga mu biciro bitaboneka ariko vy’ukuri cane, ivyo bizogira ico bikoze ku kwiyongera. Vyongeye, intwaro nta co itanga; gusa bihindura ubutunzi buva ku gukoresha kwabwo bwite. Kandi ico tutabona ni ibintu vyinshi vyari gushobora gusohoka iyo umutungo udakurwa mu bigo vy’abikorera ku giti cabo kugira ngo ushobore gufasha porogarama za Leta.


Ubwa nyuma, hafi imyaka ijana imbere ya Keynes, turashobora kuvuga ko Bastiat yahakanye inyigisho za Keynes zivuga yuko umwenda wa Leta uremesha ubutunzi, kandi ko amahera Leta ikoresha atuma haba iterambere.


![image](assets/en/076.webp)


Icigwa gikomeye kiva muri uru rukurikirane rw’ivyanditswe ni uko Leta igira ingaruka zigoramye zitaboneka. Umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi mwiza ni we wenyene ashobora kubibona imbere y’igihe. Politike nivyo tubona. Ubutunzi ni co tutabona.


## Igisabisho c'abahinguzi b'amabuye y'agaciro


<chapterId>f4e759ed-1cb2-55c7-885e-0a60244758a4</chapterId>


Mu mwaka w’1840, Inama Nkuru y’Igihugu yatoye itegeko ryo kwongera imisoro ku bintu biva hanze kugira ngo inganda z’Ubufaransa zikingirwe. Ukwo ni kwo gukunda igihugu mu vy’ubutunzi kuzwi cane, tukaba tugihura n’ubu.


![image](assets/en/077.webp)

_hejuru: Marine Le Pen, umunyapolitike w'umufaransa_



Bastiat yaciye yandika igitabu c'ugutwenga caje kuba kimwe mu bitabu vyiwe bizwi cane: "igisabisho c'abahinguzi b'amabuye y'agaciro". Birerekana ingene imigwi imwimwe y’abahingura ibintu itunganijwe neza, ironka uduteka tudakwiriye kuri Leta, bikaba bitera ingorane abanyagihugu. Muri ico gihe nyene, ryerekana ukuntu amategeko y’uburinzi ari ubujuju kandi asenya.


![image](assets/en/079.webp)


---

> MUKINGIRE AMABUZI YACU!

---

Muri iyo nyandiko, abahinguzi b’amabuye y’agaciro basaba abadepite ko bobakingira n’amategeko umukeba w’akaga:


> Turababazwa n’uguhiganwa kudashobora kwihanganirwa kw’umukeba w’umunyamahanga, bisa n’uko ari mu mimerere myiza cane yo gutanga umuco ku buryo asesagura isoko ryacu ry’igihugu ku giciro kigabanutse cane.

None, uwo muhiganwa w’umunyamahanga atagira ubutungane ni nde? Nta wundi atari izuba. Abahinguzi baca bagaragaza akaryo ko gukingira "isoko ry'igihugu ry'abakozi b'igihugu", mu gutegeka biciye mw'itegeko gufunga "amadirisho yose, amatara yo mu kirere, ibitutu, ibipfukisho, amabarabara, amabarabara, amatara y'umuyaga, mw'ijambo rimwe, ivyuho vyose, imyobo, ibihengeri, n'imivyimba umuco w'izuba ucamwo".


Mu yandi majambo, abahinguzi b'amabuye y'agaciro bagerageza kwerekana ingaruka mbi z'"umuhiganwa w'umunyamahanga" (izuba) ku bukungu bw'Ubufaransa. Kuko izuba ntirishobora gutanga "igicuruzwa" kimwe n'amabuye y'agaciro gusa, ariko ribitanga ku buntu. Haciye imyaka amajana abiri, iyo nkuru iracari ngirakamaro bitangaje. Rimbura abashoferi ba taxi basaba itegeko ryo kubuza VTCs na Uber. Iyumvire amaduka y’ibitabu ashaka kubuza Amazon.


![image](assets/en/080.webp)


Umwansi nyakuri wa Bastiat muri iyo nkuru y’ibinyoma ni ugukingira abantu mu vya politike no mu matora, ivyo bikaba vyishingikiriza gusa ku kwikunda kw’abahinguzi no ku bujuju bw’abaguzi. Ahishura ubucuti buri hagati y’umunyagihugu mubi w’ico gihe na Leta. Aho gukora ibintu bishasha no kumenyera isoko, umunyagihugu mubi ni we arondera kuronka inyungu mu vya politike biciye mu gukingira. Ivyo vyama bituma umuguzi asahura, ni ukuvuga akarenganyo.

Muri make, uburinzi ni politike y’ibigirankana ifasha abahinguzi ku baguzi. Ariko rero, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, abaserukira vy’ukuri inyungu rusangi ni abaguzi, kuko twese turi abaguzi.


Ugukingira na kwo nyene gushingiye ku nsiguro yihishije (syllogiste) ihinduka ikinyoma:



- Uko dukora cane niko turushiriza kuba abatunzi;
- Uko ingorane zirushiriza gutsinda, ni ko turushiriza gukora;
- Rero, uko ingorane zirushiriza gutsinda niko turushiriza kuba abatunzi.


Reka tugereranye ubwo bujuju n’inkuru ngufi nkeyi zavuzwe na Bastiat. Mu kigabane ca III c’urutonde rwa kabiri rw’ibitabu vy’ubutunzi, ariyumvira umubaji yandikira umushikiranganji ikete asaba ko hashirwaho itegeko ry’uburinzi. Uwo mubaji rero arategura ivyo asaba ati: Mushingantahe Minisitiri, nshireho itegeko rivuga ko «Nta n'umwe azoshobora gukoresha ikindi kintu kiretse imigozi n'ibiti bikozwe mu shoka zidakomeye.» Mu yandi majambo, mushireho itegeko ribuza gukoresha amashoka atyaye mu Bufaransa. Gutyo, aho umuntu asanzwe atanga ibipfunsi 100 vy’ishoka, bizoba ngombwa ko atanga 300. Ababizi bazoba bakenewe cane, rero bazohembwa neza.


![image](assets/en/081.webp)


Mu kigabane ca XVI, hariho ikindi canditswe giteye isoni cane, citwa: Ukuboko kw’iburyo n’ukuboko kw’ibubamfu. Intumwa y’umwami imaze gukora itohoza, irategura raporo aho isaba umwami guca, canke n’imiburiburi abohe amaboko yose y’iburyo y’abakozi. Gutyo, arabandanya, akazi n’ubutunzi bizokwongereza. Uguhingura bizoba bigoye cane, ivyo bizotuma habaho ugukoresha abakozi benshi b’inyongera no kwongera umushahara. Ubukene buzozimangana mu gihugu.


Dukurikije iyo nzira yo gutuma abantu baronka akazi uko vyogenda kwose, ni kuki kandi tutasubiriza amakamyo n’ibigare vy’amaguru n’ibitoro n’ibiyiko vy’icayi? Ivyo bisofi vyose bifise ikintu kimwe bihuriyeko: bitera urujijo uburyo n’iherezo. Ku bwa Bastiat, intumbero y’ubutunzi si ukuzigama akazi. Ntidukwiye gucira urubanza akamaro k’igikorwa kubera igihe kimara n’uburemere bwaco ahubwo dukwiye guca urubanza ku ngaruka zaco: uguhazwa n’ivyo dukeneye, akamaro.


Ukwo gutera urujijo kw'uburyo n'iherezo kuboneka mu nsiguro ivuga ngo "amahera ni ubutunzi."

Ivyo ni vyo bigenzura politike y’amahera y’ibihugu vyinshi. Nkako, ukwo kwongerekana kw'amahera bituma amabanki aguriza amahera abantu ku giti cabo n'ibihugu kugira ngo bishobore kwishura umwenda wabo bitagoranye, ivyo ni vyo "tubona". Ariko "ico tutabona" ​​ni uko iyo nzira yo kurema amahera, itashingiye ku kurema ubutunzi nyakuri, izotuma haba ugutera imbere kw'ibiciro n'ugusenyuka kw'abazigama.




Ubutunzi nyakuri rero, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, ni urutonde rw’ibintu vy’ingirakamaro dutanga biciye ku gikorwa kugira ngo turonke ivyo dukeneye. Amahera rero ni uburyo gusa bukoreshwa cane bwa Exchange, akora gusa uruhara rw’umuhuza.


## Gusahura biciye mu gutanga imisoro


<chapterId>551fc499-2119-5a52-9114-412d29434c22</chapterId>


> Iyo abatunzi bagabanutse, abakene barapfa.

![image](assets/en/083.webp)


Iryo jambo ryavuzwe na Lao-Tzu, ridondora ingaruka zidashobora gukurwaho z’uburyo bwo gutanga imisoro bugamije gutera abatunzi cane kuruta abandi.


Yamara, woba warigeze kwumva bavuga ngo:


> Gutanga imisoro ni ishoramari ryiza: ni ikime gitera imbuto! Raba imiryango myinshi ifasha, kandi ukurikire, mu vyiyumviro, ricochets zayo ku nganda: nta n’iherezo, ni ubuzima.

Mu Bufaransa, aho amahera Leta ikoresha abonwa ko ari inyungu, imisoro irarushiriza kuba myinshi kuruta mu bindi bihugu. Ariko Bastiat aca atugabisha ubwo nyene ati: «Mu mahera yose Leta ikoresha, inyuma y'ivyiza bigaragara hariho ikibi kigoye gutandukanya».


Bivuga iki?


Ubutunzi buradondora ingaruka nziza canke mbi ingingo za politike zigira ku buzima bwacu. Ariko rero, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi ategerezwa kwitwararika, atari ingaruka z’igihe gito gusa ku mugwi kanaka, ahubwo n’ingaruka z’igihe kirekire ku kibano cose.


> Ico tubona ni igikorwa n’inyungu vyemewe n’intererano y’imibano. Ico tutabona ni ibikorwa vyova muri iyo ntererano nyene iyo isigazwa n’abasoreshwa. Ico tubona ni igikorwa n’inyungu vyemewe n’intererano y’imibano. Ico tutabona ni ibikorwa vyova muri iyo ntererano nyene iyo isigazwa n’abasoreshwa.
>

> F.Basitiyati


Kuva mu ntango, arahakana iciyumviro kikiriho c’uko amahera Leta ikoresha aterwa inkunga n’imisoro atuma abantu baronka akazi. Nkako, imisoro ntaco irema kuko ivyo Leta ikoresha ntibigikoreshwa n’abasoreshwa.


Ikindi kandi, Leta irasesagura kuruta abantu ku giti cabo. Nkako, aratwibutsa, Leta ntaco ifise; nta butunzi buvamwo. Amafaranga Leta ikoresha akenshi ni isoko y’ubusa kubera ko amahera menshi cane afatwa ku bantu ku giti cabo ahunga inshingano za ba nyen’ayo mahera, agakoreshwa mu kibanza cayo n’abanyabiro, bishingiye ku migwi y’abakosha.




Ego ni ko, nk’ukwishurwa kw’igikorwa ca Leta kingana n’ico umuntu aronka mu Exchange, ugutanga imisoro ni ikintu co gukingira rwose. Ariko mu Bufaransa, Leta yarahaye imisoro uruhara rwinshi.


Mu ntango, ryari rikwiye gufasha abantu bose. Hanyuma, imisoro na yo nyene yarahawe uruhara mu gutunganya ubutunzi. Muri ivyo, abanyapolitike n’abanyabiro barafise ububasha bugarukira gusa ku bushake bwabo bwiza. Bafise ivyiyumviro vy’ubuhinga bwabo, barahingura ubutunzi mu gusoresha no gutunganya imice myinshi canke myinshi bivanye n’ivyo bipfuza kugira ngo bayishimire canke bayishire nabi.


Ubwa nyuma, imisoro yarahawe uruhara mu kibano. Baciye bacika igikoresho c’ubutungane mu kibano. Gutyo, imisoro ntikwiye gutera abantu bose mu buryo bumwe. Imisoro itegerezwa gusubira gusangira, kuva ku "bafise vyinshi" gushika ku "bafise bike."




Ikibazo ni uko imisoro, nk’uko vyiyumviriwe, ishingiye ku bubisha bw’abafise ububasha. Bakunda canke ntibakunda abantu bamwebamwe bivanye n’uko ubutegetsi bubategereje amajwi canke atarivyo. Vyongeye, ibiciro bitera imbere ntibitanga inyungu nyinshi ku bubiko bwa Leta. Ariko rero, zireka benshi bagafata ivy’abantu bakeyi, maze mu bisanzwe zikaba abafata ibintu.


Ni co gituma Bastiat yari amaze gutahura igicapo ca Laffer. Arthur Laffer ni umuhinga mu vy'ubutunzi w'umunyamerika azwi cane kubera igitabu ciwe kizwi cane citwa "curve" (an ellipse), casohowe mu 1974, kigaragaza ko umwimbu uva ku misoro wiyongera uko igipimo c'imisoro kigabanutse. Ivyo ni vyo vyiyumviro vy’ugugabanya inyungu y’imisoro irenze urugero.


![image](assets/en/087.webp)


> Umusoro mwinshi cane wica umusoro.
>

> Artur Laferi

Abanyapolitike biyumvira ataco bazi ko hariho isano ryihuta kandi ridahinduka hagati y’ibipimo vy’imisoro n’amahera yinjira mu misoro. Biyumvira ko bashobora gukubita kabiri amafaranga yinjira mu misoro mu gukubita kabiri igipimo c’imisoro. Nk’uko Laffer abivuga, ubwo buryo burarengagiza ukuntu abasoreshwa bashobora guhindura inyifato yabo mu kwishura ku bintu bishasha bibatera intege.


![image](assets/en/088.webp)


Igiharuro ca Laffer kigaragaza ko Leta itaronka amahera iyo ibiciro vy’imisoro biri ku 100%. Ku rundi ruhande, ukugabanya imisoro kwose kuratuma habaho ibikorwa vy’ubutunzi, gutyo bigatuma Leta ironka amahera. Nkako, kugabanya ibiciro vy’imisoro y’inyuma biratuma habaho ishoramari, akazi, ubuhinga bwo kurema, gutyo bigatuma ubutunzi butera imbere. Igabanuka rihagije ryoshobora gutuma habaho ubutunzi buhagije bwo kwongerera amafaranga Leta yinjiza mu kwagura cane igitigiri c’imisoro.


Bastiat yoshobora kwongerako yuko kugabanya amahera Leta ikoresha bikwiye guhabwa akamaro nk’ako kugabanya imisoro. Naho ari ukwo, nk’uko Margaret Thatcher, umwigishwa wa Frédéric Bastiat, yabivuze neza:


![image](assets/en/089.webp)


> Intumbero si iyo guhindura abatunzi abakene, ahubwo ni iyo guhindura abakene abatunzi.

Kandi ivyo yabivuze igihe yariko arabwira abasosiyalisiti.


## Inyifato runtu zibiri


<chapterId>c518e449-f638-553c-9a49-15da48023d41</chapterId>


Abantu benshi barazi "Tartuffe ou l'Imposteur", igikino c'uruhererekane ca Molière aho umuyoboke w'umunyaruyeri agerageza guhenda Elmire no kwiba umugabo wiwe Orgon. None umuntu yokwikingira gute ubuhendanyi nk’ubwo bw’indyarya yigira nk’uwukugirira neza mu gihe akugirira umugambi mubi?


![image](assets/en/090.webp)


Bastiat avuga ko hari uburyo bubiri bwo guhagarika ubwo bwoko bw’ubuhendanyi: gukosora Tartuffe canke gutanga umuco kuri Orgon. Ego ni ko, hazokwama hariho Abatartufe, ariko ububasha bwabo bwo gukora nabi bwogabanutse cane iyo habaho Abaorgon bakeyi bo kubumviriza.


Intege nke z’ubwenge bw’umuntu ni zo zituma abantu bakoresha nabi umwidegemvyo. Ni co kintu nyamukuru kigabanya abantu kandi ni co gituma haba ibibi vyinshi. Ni ngombwa rero gumurikira ijwi ryo mu mutima ku bijanye n’ingene ibikorwa vy’abantu, vyaba ivy’umuntu ku giti ciwe canke ivy’abantu bose, bifise akamaro canke bitera ingorane, rero bikaba bibereye canke bitari vyo.


Ariko rero, hari uburyo bubiri bwo kwuzuzanya bwo gutanga umuco ku rubanza rw'abanyagihugu, nk'uko Bastiat abivuga mu kigabane ca kabiri c'urutonde rw'ivy'ubutunzi rwitwa "Inyifato runtu zibiri".


![image](assets/en/091.webp)



- Ica mbere, hariho "inyifato runtu ya filozofiya canke y'idini" ikora mu gusukura no gukosora igikorwa c'umuntu (umuntu nk'umukozi);
- hanyuma, hakaba «inyifato runtu y’ubutunzi», ikora mu kwereka umuntu «ingaruka zikenewe z’ibikorwa vyiwe» (umuntu nk’umurwayi).


Nkako, izo ni ingingo ngenderwako zibiri zihuza ata gatotsi.


1. Irya mbere rivuga ku mutima kandi riremesha umuntu ku giti ciwe gukora ivyiza; ni inyifato runtu y’idini canke ya filozofiya. Ni co gihambaye kuruta ibindi vyose. Rishinga imizi mu mutima w’umuntu ukumenya inshingano yiwe. Iramubwira ngo:


> Niwiteho imbere; wisukure ubwawe; mureke gukora ibibi; ukore ivyiza, utondeze ivyipfuzo vyawe; n’uhebe inyungu zawe; ntuhahaze mugenzawe ubwirizwa kumukunda no kumurekura; n’ube uwa mbere gusa kandi w’ubugiraneza hanyuma.

Muri make, ryigisha ingeso nziza, igikorwa co kutagira ubwikunzi. Iyo nyifato runtu, Bastiat avuga ko izoguma ari yo nziza kandi ikora ku mutima ibihe bidahera, kuko yerekana iciza kuruta ibindi vyose kiri mu muntu.


![image](assets/en/092.webp)


2. Ikindi gifasha guhanura no kurwanya ikibi biciye mu kumenya ingaruka zaco, ni inyifato runtu y’ubutunzi. Rivuga ku bwenge atari ku mutima, rikaba rigamije gukangurira uwugirizwa ingaruka mbi z’inyifato. Bikomeza ivyigwa biva ku vyo umuntu yiboneye. Rigerageza gukwiragiza ubwenge busanzwe, ubumenyi n’ukutagira icizigiro ku bantu benshi bahahazwa, ivyo bikaba bituma uguhahazwa kurushiriza kugora.


Iyo nyifato runtu y’ubutunzi yipfuza ingaruka imwe n’iyo nyifato runtu y’idini, ariko itangura ku ngaruka z’ibikorwa vy’abantu. Bitwigisha gufata ingingo ku bikorwa bitari vyo canke bitera ingorane no kugwanira ivyo bibereye canke bifise akamaro.


![image](assets/en/093.webp)


Bastiat aha ashira ahabona uruhara rwa siyansi, na cane cane ubuhinga bw’ubutunzi. Naho itandukanye n’iy’inyifato runtu y’imigenzo, uruhara rwayo ni ngombwa kugira ngo umuntu arwanye ubusuma mu buryo bwayo bwose. Inyifato runtu itera ububi mu mugambi wayo, yigisha ubushake. Ku rundi ruhande, siyansi iratera ubusambanyi mu gutahura ingaruka zabwo, gutyo bigatuma ingeso nziza zishobora gutsinda.


Mu vy’ukuri, ubuhinga bw’ubutunzi, Bastiat adondora nk’inyifato runtu yo kwikingira, bugizwe no guhakana inyigisho z’ubutunzi kugira ngo zisenyurwe burundu, gutyo zigakuraho umugwi w’abanyagihugu basahura uburenganzira n’ububasha bwawo.


Ubutunzi bwa politike rero burafise akamaro kagaragara. Birahishura ubusazi mu biguzi vyihishije, intambamyi ku mahiganwa, n’ubwoko bwose bwo gukingira.


Na none, hariho aba Tartuffe bake iyo habaho aba Orgon bake bo kubumviriza. Ehe ivyo Bastiat avuga kuri ivyo:




> Rero inyifato runtu y’idini ikore ku mitima y’Abatartufe nimba ishobora. Igikorwa kijanye n’ubutunzi bwa politike ni ugumurikira abadupe babo. Muri izo nzira zibiri, ni iyihe ikora neza cane kugira ngo abantu batere imbere? Mbega vyoba bitegerezwa kuvugwa? Nizera ko ari ubwa kabiri. Ndatinya ko abantu badashobora guhunga ivy’uko bikenewe ko babanza kwiga inyifato runtu yo kwikingira.

Ego ni ko, ubutunzi bwa politike si ubuhinga bwo kw’isi yose; ntirikuraho uburyo bwo gukora filozofiya n’ubuhinga bwo mu vy’idini. « Ariko none ni nde yigeze yerekana mwene ukwo kwivugira birenze urugero mw’izina ryayo ? Bastiat aratangara.


Ikintu kimwe nta nkeka, si politike ishobora guhindura inzira y’ibintu n’umuntu atunganye. Ahubwo, birakenewe ko politike igabanywa, ikagarukira ku ruhara rwayo rukomeye, ari rwo umutekano. Ahubwo mu bijanye n’umuco, umuryango, idini, n’imigenderanire, biciye mu bikorwa vy’ivyiyumviro, biciye mu nyigisho n’inyigisho, muri make, biciye mu gisata c’abanyagihugu, ni ho inshingano n’ubumwe bishobora gukomezwa.


# Ubutunzi buhuye

## Igitangaza c'Isoko


<chapterId>895ccd1d-7b52-5a8b-8b2c-6ec0056cf632</chapterId>


Mbega igihugu gihuje coshobora gukora ata mategeko yanditse, amabwirizwa, ingingo zo gukandamiza? Iyo abagabo basigaye bidegemvya, ntituzobona akajagari, ubuja n’ubuja, akajagari? Ni gute twokwirinda kurema ugushira hamwe gusa kw’abantu bakora hanze y’indirimbo iyo ari yo yose, nimba atari biciye mu mategeko no mu muryango wa politike ushize hamwe?


Iyi ni yo mpamvu akenshi ivugwa n’abasaba ko habaho amategeko agenga isoko canke ko ikibano conyene gishobora guhuza abantu ku giti cabo mu bumwe buhuye kandi buhuye.


![image](assets/en/095.webp)


Ivyo si vyo Bastiat abona. Nk’uko yabivuze, uburyo bwo mu kibano, cokimwe n’uburyo bwo mw’ijuru canke uburyo bwo mu mubiri w’umuntu, burakurikiza amategeko rusangi. Mu yandi majambo, ni ikintu gisanzwe gitunganijwe mu buryo buhuye. Kandi moteri y’iryo shirahamwe ni isoko ry’ubuntu.


Atubwira ko igitangaro c’isoko ry’uburenganzira ari uko rikoresha ubumenyi ata muntu n’umwe ashobora kugira wenyene kandi ko ritanga umunezero uruta kure n’iyo ikintu cose ishirahamwe ry’ubuhinga ryoshobora gukora.


Bastiat atanga ingero nkeyi kugira ngo yerekane ivyiza biva muri iryo soko. Twaramenyereye cane ico kintu ku buryo tutagitaho.

Reka twihweze umubaji wo mu gisagara, ati, twihweze ibikorwa vyose akorera abantu n’abo bose aronka:


> Imisi yose, iyo avyutse, arambara, kandi we ubwiwe nta n’imwe mu mpuzu ziwe yakoze. Yamara, kugira ngo iyo mpuzu ashobore kuronka, vyari ngombwa ko hakorwa igikorwa kinini cane, inganda, gutwara abantu n’ibintu be n’uguhingura ibintu vy’ubuhinga kw’isi yose.
>

> Hanyuma aca afungura mu gitondo. Kugira ngo umukate arya ushikire ku meza yiwe buri gitondo, amatongo yategerezwa gusukurwa, akarimwa; ivyuma, ivyuma, ibiti, amabuye vyategerezwa guhindurwa ibikoresho vyo gukora; ibintu vyose kimwe cose, gifashwe ukwaco, gifata igikorwa kinini kitagira uko kingana gishizwe mu ngiro, atari mu kirere gusa ariko no mu gihe.
>

> Uwo mugabo azorungika umuhungu wiwe kw’ishure, kugira ngo aronke inyigisho zibanza gukora ubushakashatsi, imyaka myinshi y’ukwiga imbere y’igihe.
> Asohoka hanze: asanga ibarabara ry’amabuye ry’agaciro kandi ry’umuco.
>

> Itunga ryiwe riraburanishwa: azoronka abavoka bo kuburanira uburenganzira bwiwe, abacamanza bo kububungabunga, abajejwe ubutungane bo gushitsa igihano; ibintu vyose bica bibanza gusaba ubumenyi umuntu aronka, ni co gituma habaho umuco n’uburyo bwo kubaho.

Bastiat adondora isoko nk’igikoresho c’ubufatanye gishizwe ahantu hamwe kandi kitaboneka. Biciye mu nzira y’ibiciro, itanga amakuru yerekeye ivyo umuntu wese akeneye n’ubuhinga afise, ihuza abantu bipfuza gukorana kugira ngo ubuzima bwabo butere imbere.


Bastiat asozera ati: “Igitangaje ni ukudahuza gukomeye kuri hagati y’ivyiza uwo mugabo akura mu kibano n’ivyo yokwironsa iyo agabanywa ku butunzi bwiwe bwite. Mu musi umwe, arafungura ibintu atashobora kwihingura.


![image](assets/en/096.webp)


Mu mwaka w'1958, umwanditsi w'umunyamerika Leonard Read (Ishirahamwe ry'uburezi bw'ubutunzi) yarasohoye igitabu kigufi mu kinyamakuru The Freeman, canditswe mu buryo bwa Bastiat, kikaba caramenyekanye cane: "I, Pencil". Iki canditswe ni ikigereranyo c’ico isoko ry’ubuntu ari co. Bitangura gutya:


> Ndi ikaramu y’amabuye y’agaciro, ikaramu isanzwe y’ibiti imenyerewe n’abahungu bose n’abakobwa bose be n’abantu bakuze bashobora gusoma no kwandika. Ni kimwe mu bintu vyoroshe cane mu buhinga bw’abantu. Kandi naho biri uko nta muntu n’umwe kw’isi azi uko yovyara.

![image](assets/en/097.webp)

Risubiramwo iciyumviro ca Bastiat c’ugukorana kutaboneka hagati y’abantu amamiliyoni batamenyana, bikajana mu kwubaka ikintu c’isi nk’ikaramu y’igiti. Nta n’umwe azi gukora ikaramu y’igiti ku giti ciwe. Yamara, abantu amamiliyoni baragira uruhara mu kurema iyo karamu yoroshe batabizi, bagahanahana no guhuza ubumenyi n’ubuhinga bwabo mu buryo bw’ibiciro ata bubasha burengeye ubw’abandi butegeka inyifato yabo. Iyi nkuru yerekana ko abantu bidegemvya bakora mu gukurikirana inyungu zabo zibereye bakora cane ku bw’inyungu z’igihugu kuruta ingamba z’ubutunzi zitegekanijwe kandi zishizwe hamwe.


Uwaronse agashimwe ka Nobel mu vy’ubutunzi mu 1976, Milton Friedman, na we nyene yarasubiyemwo iyo nkuru y’ikaramu kugira ngo asigurire abantu bose ingene ubutunzi bw’isoko bukora.


Mu kiganiro kimwe c’uruhererekane rwiwe rwa televiziyo rwitwa Free to Choose, arasuzuma ibice bitandukanye vy’ikintu gisanzwe kandi coroshe nk’ikaramu y’igiti, agashira ahabona igitangaro c’urutonde rwibonekeza, ruterwa n’imigenderanire ibihumbi n’ibihumbi y’ubutunzi kw’isi yose. Abantu batamenyana, badasangiye idini canke imigenzo, baracashobora guhuza kugira ngo bavemwo ico kintu. Aca asozera avuga ko isoko ry’uburenganzira ari ngirakamaro kugira ngo ntihagire uguterimbere gusa, ariko kandi habe n’uguhuza n’amahoro.


![image](assets/en/098.webp)


Friedrich Hayek, mu gitabu ciwe citwa "Ikoreshwa ry'ubumenyi mu kibano" mu 1945, yari amaze gusigura igituma ubutunzi bw'isoko n'ugushira ingingo mu bibanza bitandukanye ari ngirakamaro kugira ngo umuntu atere imbere. Nk’uko Hayek abivuga, nta muteguzi mukuru canke umukuru w’ibiro yoshobora kwigera agira ubumenyi buhagije bwo kuyobora neza ibikorwa vyose vy’ubutunzi. Uburyo bw’ibiciro bwo mw’isoko ry’uburenganzira ni bwo bwonyene butuma abantu amamiliyoni bigenga bihitiramwo ingene bogabanya neza ubutunzi.


![image](assets/en/099.webp)


Itegeko ry’ubutunzi, rivuga ko rikora neza kuruta isoko, ntirituma gusa ubutunzi bugabanwa nabi, ariko kandi rituma umugwi umwe uganza uwundi. Ni co gituma ubusosiyalisimu atari ikosa ry’ubwenge gusa ahubwo ari ikosa riheza rizana akarenganyo kanini cane.


## Umwidegemvyo n'inshingano ni urufunguruzo rw'ingorane z'imibano .


<chapterId>78baa7ef-2c80-5fc7-8881-c1be4662b96f</chapterId>


Mu kete yandikiye Alphonse de Lamartine mu 1845, Bastiat yanditse ko ubuhinga bwiwe bwose buri mu ngingo ngenderwako imwe:


> Umwidegemvyo ni wo buryo bwiza bwo gutunganya abantu.

![image](assets/en/100.webp)

Ariko rero, yongerako ikintu:


> Ko itegeko ridakwiye gukuraho ingaruka nziza canke mbi z’ibikorwa vy’umuntu wese. Iryo ni ryo hame ry’inshingano ry’inshingano.

Mu yandi majambo, umwidegemvyo n’inshingano ntibishobora gutandukanywa; ntibishobora gutandukanywa. Kuri we, uburenganzira bwo kwidegemvya buratandukanye n’ubumwe bw’abantu kubera ukwemera ko umwidegemvyo udashobora kubaho ata nshingano. Ariko none, ni ibiki vy’ukuri amajambo umwidegemvyo n’inshingano avuga vy’ukuri?


Umwidegemvyo mu vy’ukuri usobanurwa mu buryo butari bwiza: kwidegemvya ni ugukora ata n’umwe agobereye hanze mu gukoresha uburenganzira bwiwe bwite. Ariko rero, ivyo ntibisigura ko ata bintu vyose bimubuza gukora. Kuko umwidegemvyo usaba gusubizanya: uradutegeka kandi gukora ataco duhungabanya ku mutungo w’abandi, gutyo tugasanura ikibi cose cakozwe, iyo bikenewe. Ivyo ni inshingano.


Rero, inshingano igereranya, mu buryo bumwe, umuce mwiza w’umwidegemvyo: ku rugero umuntu akora mu mwidegemvyo, ategerezwa kwikorera ingaruka z’ibikorwa vyiwe bwite, vyiza canke bibi.


Inshingano z’umuntu ku giti ciwe ni co kintu nyamukuru gituma umuntu agira ubuhinga bwo kurema kandi ni co kintu kidutera kwiyubara no kubona imbere y’igihe.


![image](assets/en/101.webp)


Iyo umuntu akoresheje amahera yiwe bwite, araba maso kugira ngo ntagire umwenda mwinshi, ngo asuzume uburyo ibintu bimeze, ko ababitanga bizigirwa, akagira ingorane yo guhanwa cane. Ukwo ni ububasha bw’inshingano, bufatanije n’umwidegemvyo ni moteri nyakuri y’iterambere ry’imibano.


Ariko none ikintu co kutagira inshingano canke co gutakaza inshingano kiva hehe? Frédéric Bastiat araduha inyishu y’ico kibazo, inyishu ya politike. Avuga ati, ndasubiramwo:


> Ukwinjira kwa Leta kuradukurako ubutegetsi bwacu.

Nkako, statism iguma igabanya ubushobozi bw’abantu ku giti cabo n’uburenganzira bw’abantu bwo kwihitiramwo. Bibakorera ivyo bo ubwabo boshobora gukora kandi bikaba vyiza kuruta. Gutyo bikuramwo abantu ku giti cabo ingaruka z’ibikorwa vyabo. Bica bisenya inshingano.


![image](assets/en/102.webp)


Nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, ukugwira kw’amategeko n’ukugira uruhara birenze urugero kwa Leta biragira ingaruka zo gutuma haba urugamba rwo kurondera ubutegetsi, ubusuma, uburenganzira, ubutegetsi bw’umuntu umwe, intambara, muri make, ikintu cose kibuza iterambere ry’iterambere.

Ico kibazo co gukunda cane inzira y’amategeko canke y’ubugenzuzi bw’ubutegetsi ni uko bica intege ivyifuzo vyose mu kudutera umwuzure w’intambamyi, gutyo bikadukurako amaterambere menshi ashobora gukorwa n’ukwitanga kw’abantu ku giti cabo be n’uguhitamwo mu mwidegemvyo.


Reka tugereranye iki kibazo n’ingingo nkeyi zihambaye ziriho ubu. Akarorero ka mbere, ni ingorane zo mu 2008.


![image](assets/en/103.webp)

_(Alan Greenspan, umuyobozi wa FED, banki nkuru y'Amerika, kuva mu 1987 gushika mu 2006)_


Imyaka myinshi, abarongozi b’ivy’amahera barasiguye ko iyo inyungu zishizwe mu vy’abikorera ku giti cabo igihe vyose bigenda neza, ibihombo bizoba ari ivy’abantu bose iyo umuntu ashobora gusenyuka (ugufasha abantu, imigambi yo gutabara, gukoresha nabi inyungu, gucapura amahera, n’ibindi). Mu kubigira, baratuma haba akaga mu vy’inyifato runtu, baratuma abantu bashobora gufata ingingo zidasanzwe, kandi bararemesha isi y’ivy’ubutunzi kwigenza mu buryo butagiramwo inshingano. Baciye rero bavyura amahera mu ngorane twaciyemwo.




Kandi ico kintu kizosubira kubaho igihe cose amabanki azoguma ari munsi y’ubutegetsi bwa Leta bukuru butegerezwa kuyakingira mu gukuraho ubwigenge bwose bwo gufata ingingo no gukora.


Ikindi kigereranyo: ibikorwa vya Leta


![image](assets/en/105.webp)


Igikorwa kimwekimwe cose ca Leta gitegeka ivyo abahinga mu vy’ubutegetsi bakunda, bikaba bitera ingorane uburenganzira bw’umuntu ku giti ciwe bwo kwihitiramwo. Ivyo bituma habaho ingaruka zibiri nk'uko Bastiat abivuga: Umunyagihugu "arahagarika kwigenzura mu mwidegemvyo ku vyo yipfuza, kandi, atagifise inshingano, birasanzwe ko ahagarika kugira ubwenge." Imvo ni yoroshe: itegeko ryose ryanditswe rirahatira kandi ni kimwe kuri bose, ntirifata mu muzirikanyi ibintu vyihariye, ivyo abanyagihugu bakeneye n’ivyo bakunda.


Ubwa nyuma, igikorwa ca Leta ni co gituma umuntu adashobora gukora. Nkako, iyo ibikorwa vy’abikorera ku giti cabo bicitse ivy’abantu bose, birahunga amahiganwa. Bastiat avuga rero, ndasubiramwo ati: "umukuru w'igihugu ntaco akora co gutuma umuntu atera imbere."


![image](assets/en/106.webp)


Iyo twihweje igikorwa ca Leta c’uburezi bw’igihugu, turatahura ico Bastiat ashaka kuvuga. Birakuraho abavyeyi benshi cane umuzigo wo kwigisha abana babo, bikagabanya ishure rikagira ishure ry’abana. Ntibiremesha abigisha guhingura ibintu bishasha no gufata ivyago kuko muri iyo nzira baba ari abashitsa gusa porogarama yateguwe ata bo, n’abanyabiro. Ubwa nyuma, ryirengagiza ukuri kw’ivyo umuntu wese akeneye vyihariye.


![image](assets/en/107.webp)

Tuzobona mu kindi cigwa, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, ibikorwa vya Leta vyonyene vyemewe n’amategeko ni bitatu: igisirikare, abapolisi n’ubutungane. Ariko kugira ngo dusozere ku bijanye n’inshingano, ingorane iri mu gufasha Leta ni uko abafata ingingo atari bo bashikirwa n’ingaruka.

Mu yandi majambo, amahitamwo rusangi si amahitamwo afise inshingano kuko, ku ruhande rumwe, nta n’umwe atera ingorane abafata ingingo, ku rundi ruhande, agahatira abandi gushikirwa n’ingaruka zimwezimwe, ivyo bikaba ari bibi nk’uko ari ubusambanyi.


## Ububasha bw'inshingano


<chapterId>0c078806-6c58-53f9-a720-5fb62386e56b</chapterId>


Mu nyigisho iheruka, twabonye igituma umwidegemvyo n’inshingano ari vyo bifasha mu ngorane y’imibano. Ubu rero tuzokwihweza cane iyo ngingo mu kwerekana ingene Frédéric Bastiat abona ibibi bitera imibano n’umuti wavyo.


Abanyagihugu b’abanyagihugu rimwe na rimwe baranegurwa kubera ko birengagiza ikibi, bubaka utopia y’umwidegemvyo utyoroye kandi utunganye mw’isi nziza. Ivyo binegura nta shingiro bifise na gato ku bijanye n’umwanditsi wacu.


Nta n’umwe ashobora kwirengagiza ikibi kiganza muri kahise k’imibano y’abantu: akarenganyo, intambara be n’imibabaro. Twipfuza ko twoshobora gukuraho ivyo bibi. Ivyo ni vyo, ikirenze ivyo, igice kinini c’ubuhinga bwa filozofiya bwo muri iki gihe, kuva kuri Rousseau gushika kuri Heidegger, gushika kuri Hegel na Marx.


Ikibi si ikintu nyakuri gitomoye gusa ariko kandi gifise uruhara muri kahise no mu bikorwa vy’abantu, ni ko Frédéric Bastiat avuga. Bishobora kugabanywa ariko nta gukeka ko bitazokurwaho burundu kuko ivyo vyoba bisobanura kwica umwidegemvyo n’inshingano. None ikibi kiva hehe, uruhara rwaco ni uruhe, kandi cokwirindwa gute?


Kugira ngo Bastiat yishure ivyo bibazo, azobandanya gusuzuma ibikorwa vy’umuntu. Ivyo, vy’ukuri, birashobora gutuma haba ivyiza n’ibibi.


![image](assets/en/108.webp)


Ikibi kibanza kuva ku kudatungana kwacu. Bastiat avuga ko guhitamwo mu mwidegemvyo ari ugushira mu kaga ko guhitamwo nabi. Nkako, turashobora guhendwa mu buryo bwinshi, mbere no ku bijanye n’ivyo dukeneye be n’inyungu zacu. Umuntu arashobora gukosa, arakunda gukosa mu gutahura urukino rw’amategeko y’ubutunzi canke mu kuyakevya akava ku mpera yayo.


Ni ukudatungana kw’ubwenge rero ari kwo guhagarara kw’abantu kandi ni kwo kuguma ku ntango y’imibabaro yacu.

Nimba ikibi kiva ku ntege nke z’umuntu atari ku mwidegemvyo ubwawo, canke ku bucuruzi bw’ubuntu, umuti ntabwo uri mu guhagarika umwidegemvyo canke Exchange ahubwo uri mu nshingano ubwazo, kuko ari yo isoko y’ubumenyi bwose. Iryo hame ry’inshingano ni iri, ndasubiramwo amajambo ya Bastiat:


> Umuntu wese akora aronka ingororano canke igihano c’ibikorwa vyiwe.

Biciye muri ico gihano c’akameremere, umuntu ariga, akavumbura, akikosora, akitera imbere, kandi akitera imbere. Mu yandi majambo, inshingano ni ingingo ngenderwako y’ugutungana n’ugutera imbere nk’uko twabibonye mu nyigisho iheruka.


![image](assets/en/109.webp)


Iyo umuntu yikoreye ingaruka nziza canke mbi z’ingingo afata, azokwipfuza gutera imbere mu kwigira ku vyo yiboneye. Rero, inshingano y’umuntu ku giti ciwe, ari yo mwigisha akomeye w’ibihugu nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, ingingo ngenderwako y’ishimikiro y’ugutunganya inyifato n’imibano yose, itegerezwa kwemererwa gukora.


Ikibi kivyara imibabaro, kandi imibabaro ituma dutahura ikosa canke ikosa, ikadusubiza mu nzira igororotse. Ni mu kumenya ikibi dutera imbere.


Ni kubera ko umuntu ashobora gukora amakosa canke gukora nabi maze akagira ingaruka mbi, ni co gituma aremeshwa kubazwa. Azoca yihatira kubona imbere y’igihe ingorane zoshobora kumushikira kugira ngo yikingire.


![image](assets/en/110.webp)


---

> Gukosa ni umuntu.

---

Biragaragara rero ko Bastiat ari kure y’ukuba impumyi. Ntahakana ko ikibi kiriho. Umuntu ni intege nke, akunda gukosa no gukosa. Nta hantu umuntu azobona Bastiat ahakana ko gukoresha umwidegemvyo w’umuntu ku giti ciwe bifitaniye isano n’ugushobora gukora amakosa, ubushobozi bwo guhitamwo ata co bimaze canke ata co bimaze.


Avuga gusa ko nimba isoko y’ikibi iri mu kubura umwidegemvyo, umuti uri mu mwidegemvyo ubwawo, kandi cane cane mu gukoresha neza kandi mu buryo bushitse inshingano z’umuntu ku giti ciwe.


![image](assets/en/111.webp)


Ariko nimba gukoresha nabi umwidegemvyo ari vyo bituma tugira amagorwa, gukoresha neza umwidegemvyo ni wo muti, ni ukuvuga gukoresha neza kandi mu buryo bushitse inshingano z’umuntu ku giti ciwe, zishingiye ku burenganzira bwo kugira itunga. Itegeko ry’imibano rero rica mu nshingano, ntirica mu kwivanga kwa Leta mu bintu vyose, ari ryo rimwe mu masoko akomeye y’ugusahura, rero n’ibibi.


![image](assets/en/112.webp)

Mu buryo butandukanye n’ubwa Rousseau, arondera kurandura ikibi biciye mu nzego rusangi, Frédéric Bastiat arwanira ubushobozi bw’ikibi n’amakosa, iyo ata mwidegemvyo canke inshingano y’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Kuko ivyo ni vyo vyonyene bituma, biciye mu nzira y’ukuvumbura, umuntu atera imbere kandi akagabanya ibibi vyo mu kibano.

Bitegerezwa gutomorwa ko iyo nzira y’iterambere biciye mu nshingano ataco ikora. Si na gato, nk’uko vyagendeye Hegel canke Marx, ubwoko bw’ugushinga intahe kw’ibidukikije canke kw’amateka, kwoshobora gutuma haba uguhuza n’iterambere ku gitangaro canke ku buryo bw’ubuhinga. Ni ivyerekeye ukugabanya buhoro buhoro kandi ataco bimaze ububi kandi ntibizokwigera bikurwaho burundu.


## Ubumwe bw'ukuri n'ubw'ikinyoma


<chapterId>fa2172e9-22fa-5c01-a3c8-1e8316c064a4</chapterId>




Frédéric Bastiat, mu gatabo kiwe kazwi cane citwa «Itegeko», aragaya ukugoranya itegeko gushingiye ku gutuma, mw’izina ry’«ubumwe», ivyo vy’ukuri bitegerezwa kwitwa gusahura bibe ivy’amategeko. Nkako, hariho ukuvuguruzanya mu gushaka gutegeka ubuvukanyi biciye mu mategeko, ivyo twokwita uno musi "ubutungane bw'abantu" canke ubumwe.




Kuko inyifato runtu isobanurwa nk’inyifato y’ubushake. Iyo umuntu ku giti ciwe agoberewe gutanga ikintu adashaka gutanga, yama ari umunyavyaha w’ubusuma.


![image](assets/en/115.webp)


Nkako, iyo intererano itegekanijwe n’itegeko, ntiba ikiri inyifato runtu. Inyifato y’inyifato runtu yo gutanga isubirizwa n’ugusaba «uburenganzira», ari vyo gusaba ku gikorwa c’abandi. Ubumwe bw’ikinyoma ni uguhamagara umuntu ngo abeho kubera abandi.


Ivyo ni vyo Bastiat yita "ubuhinga bw'ubuvukanyi bushingiye ku mategeko." Reka tumusubiremwo kuri iyi ngingo:


> Ubuvukanyi buravyuka, canke ntabwo ari bwo. Kuyitegeka ni ukuyisambura.

Kandi na none:


> Intwaro zikora gusa igikorwa gihanwa n’Inguvu. None, biraremewe guhatira umuntu kuba umugororotsi, ntimuhatire kuba umugiraneza. Itegeko, iyo rirondera gukora ku nguvu ivyo inyifato runtu ishikako biciye mu kwemeza, kure y’ukuduga mu bwami bw’Urukundo, rigwa mu rwego rw’Ubusahuzi.
> Yamara rero ukwo kugoreka amategeko kurafise izina, ni ubusosiyalisimu, ni ukuvuga ingengabitekerezo yo gusubira gusangira ubutunzi ku nguvu na Leta. Ubusosiyalisiti, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, burangwa n’ingengabitekerezo y’ugusahura bitegekanijwe n’amategeko. Ariko ubuhendanyi bw’iyo ngendo ni uko ipfuka ubukazi bwayo munsi y’ugukoresha nabi ururimi: uguhamagara ubumwe canke ubuvukanyi.

![image](assets/en/116.webp)


---

> Gufashanya
> Ishirahamwe rya GUISY
> 1899

---

Ariko rero, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, hariho ubundi buryo bwo gusubirira ubumwe bwa Leta butegekanijwe: «société mutuelle d’aide», ni ukuvuga ugufashanya kw’abagabo hagati yabo biciye ku mashirahamwe y’ugufashanya. Ariko kandi yarabonye imbere y’igihe ko Leta izoheza igafata izo mutuals kugira ngo zibe inzego zidasanzwe kandi zihurikiye hamwe, ziremeshe gukoresha amahera no gusesagura.


![image](assets/en/117.webp)


Mu gatabo kitwa "Ubutungane n'Ubuvukanyi", Bastiat aratohoza kandi iciyumviro c'uburyo bworoshe kandi bubereye bwo gutanga imisoro mu gutanga amahera y'ivyo abantu bose bakeneye (abapolisi, ubutungane, ingabo): inyungu n'inyungu vyobaye bishingiye ku rugero rumwe kandi ruringaniye rw'imisoro. Ivyo nivyo bizwi uno musi kw'izina rya "Flat Tax".


![image](assets/en/118.webp)


---

> Imishinge –
> **agaciro kongerewe**
> ku **ikibano**
>

> Ishirahamwe ry'Ubusuwisi

---

Nkako, ubumwe bwo mu miryango, ubumwe bwo mu karere, canke ubugiraneza buteguwe buratera imbere cane mu bihugu bifise uburyo bwo gutanga imisoro yoroshe kandi bufise umwidegemvyo mu vy’ubutunzi, nk’Ubusuwisi na Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika, mu gihe buhagarara cane mu bihugu aho Leta yasubiriye cane inshingano z’umuntu ku giti ciwe nk’Ubufaransa canke Ubudage.


Kenshi ni umuco kwidodomba "ubwikunzi" bwobayeho mu bihugu vy'ubwigenge. Ariko ni ibihushanye n’ivyo. Iyo igihugu kiremerewe n’imisoro kandi abantu ku giti cabo batagifise ivyabo, ntibaremeshwa gutanga ahubwo bakikuramwo.




Mu vy’ukuri, sosiyete sivile yidegemvya ntiruhukira ku bwikunzi: ubutunzi bw’isoko bukora bushingiye ku gukorera mugenziwe no gusubizanya. Umuntu ashobora gukorera inyungu yiwe gusa mu gukorera inyungu z’uwundi, mu guha uwundi umugenzi ajana ku Exchange y’inyungu bose. Mu yandi majambo, ni Exchange y’ubushake irema ubumwe nyakuri.


Gusubira gusangira ku nguvu ntaco bihuriyeko n’ubumwe nyakuri bw’abantu, bukaba ari ubw’ibanga canke bw’ubushake kandi buboneka mu miryango, canke hagati y’abagize ishirahamwe.


Ni ku ruhara rw’itegeko rero Bastiat aha arwanya abasosiyalisiti. Yandika ati:

Amategeko arashobora guhatira umuntu kuba umugororotsi, ariko ntashobora kumuhatira kwihebera Imana. Ubumwe bw’ikinyoma bw’abasosiyalisiti burakuraho ukwihebera Imana mu gushigikira uguhatira kwa Leta gusukuye, ari kwo gushingira intahe ubutegetsi bw’agahomerabunwa.


# Ivyagezwe

## Uburenganzira bwo kuronka umutungo


<chapterId>a48a0616-2105-5520-8312-e21a0b6489c7</chapterId>


Ku bijanye n’itunga, ntidukwiye gutahura ubutaka hano. Bisigura "uburenganzira bw'umukozi ku gaciro yaremye biciye mu gikorwa ciwe." Bastiat asobanura:


> Nbona ko uburenganzira bwo kuronka umutungo bushingiye ku mwidegemvyo wo kubanza gukoresha umuntu wiwe bwite, hanyuma akawukoresha mu bikorwa vyiwe, hanyuma akawukoresha mu bikorwa vyiwe - ivyo bikaba bigaragaza, ikindi, ko, umuntu abona ibintu mu buryo bumwe, umwidegemvyo n’uburenganzira bwo kuronka umutungo bidashobora gutandukanywa.

Amaze gushinga iyo ngingo, kugira ngo Bastiat atahure umushinge w’inyifato runtu w’itunga, atangura ku ngingo ngenderwako yoroshe y’ubuhinga bw’abantu, ivuga ko kuva mu ntango, umuntu ategerezwa gukora kugira ngo abeho kandi ko ivyamwa vy’ibikorwa vyiwe ari ukwaguka kw’ubushobozi bwiwe, ni ukuvuga umuntu wiwe.


> Umuntu, Umwidegemvyo, Itunga, — uwo ni umuntu. Ni muri ivyo bintu bitatu umuntu ashobora kuvuga, ata n’ubuhendanyi bw’ubudemagogi, ko ari vyo bibanza kandi bisumba itegeko ryose ry’abantu.

Iyo umuntu atahuye muri iyo nsiguro, uburenganzira bw’Itunga buri muri ubwo burenganzira butava ku mategeko meza ariko bukayibanziriza kandi ari bwo raison d’être yayo. Vy'ukuri,


> Itegeko ni umuteguro rusangi w’uburenganzira bw’umuntu ku giti ciwe bwo kwiregura mu buryo bubereye.
> Ivyagezwe

Intumbero yayo ni ukugwanira uwo muntu n’itunga ryiwe.


![image](assets/en/120.webp)

_(François Quesnay, umurongozi w'abafiziyokarate)_


Uburenganzira rero si kimwe n’itegeko. Uburenganzira ntibumenyekana n’ijambo ry’umukuru w’igihugu, eka mbere ntibuvana gusa n’uko yemerewe. Ni igiterwa n’umugenzo, urutonde rw’amategeko rubanza kandi rusumba itegeko, rwitegeka umushingamateka nk’uko rwitegeka umunyagihugu wese asanzwe.


Uburenganzira "ntibwaremwe". Ntibivumbuwe bivuye ku ciyumviro ciza c’ingene amategeko y’ikibano akwiye kuba; rivumburwa muri kamere y’umuntu no mu mategeko y’ubugwaneza, rikarungikwa n’ubwenge bw’imigenzo.

Abantu ku giti cabo barafise uburenganzira bwa kamere bubaho imbere y’itegeko: Itunga, Umwidegemvyo, Umuntu. Uruhara rw’Itegeko rukwiye kuba urwo kuzigama ubwo burenganzira kavukire bw’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Ku bw’ivyo, Leta itegerezwa kuba ifise aho igarukira. Uyu musi, twovuga ko Bastiat ari umuvugizi wa Leta y’agaciro gatoyi.


![image](assets/en/121.webp)


Mu nzira ya Rousseau, twavuganye mu nyigisho iheruka, igikorwa c’umushingamateka ni ugutunganya, guhindura, mbere no gukuraho umutungo iyo abonye ko bikwiye. Kuri Rousseau, umutungo si ikintu gisanzwe ahubwo ni ikintu gisanzwe, nk’uko nyene ikibano ubwaco kimeze. Ico ciyumviro kiva ku mategeko y’Abaroma, ayo Rousseau yari amenyereye cane.


![image](assets/en/122.webp)


Robespierre na we nyene, ashiraho ingingo ngenderwako y'uko "Umutungo ari uburenganzira bw'umwenegihugu wese bwo kwinovora no gukoresha umuce w'ibintu vyemewe n'itegeko."


Ku bwa Rousseau, umutungo ntubanza itegeko; ni isezerano gusa rishirwaho n’ubushake rusangi kandi mu mipaka rifata ingingo. Ivyo bituma ata mwidegemvyo canke uburenganzira bibaho bitandukanye n’ikibano n’ubushake bwiza bw’abashingamateka. Ariko iyo umuntu atandukanije uburenganzira bwo kuronka itunga, bica vyoroshe gushingira intahe uburenganzira bw’ikinyoma, ubwo burenganzira umuntu aronka gusa mu kurenga uburenganzira bw’abandi.


Nk’akarorero: uburenganzira bwo gukora canke uburenganzira bwo kuba mu nzu.




Kugira ngo nshobore kuronka ikintu ku buntu, umuntu ategerezwa kwishura mu izina ryanje. Kandi nimba ari Leta iriha, kuko itazana ubutunzi, ishobora kubigira gusa mu gufata inzu ku muntu, canke ikingana na yo, ngo ayimpe.


Ico ciyumviro c’uko uburenganzira bwo kuronka umutungo ari ikiremwa c’itegeko rero kijana, nk’uko Bastiat abivuga, mu gufungurira ikibanza kitagira aho kigarukira abanyabwenge b’ivy’ubuhinga bw’idini bipfuza gushushanya ikibano nk’uko babiteguye.


Mu rutonde rw’umwidegemvyo w’akameremere, hariho itegeko ry’akameremere, ritandukanye n’ivyo abashinga amategeko bashaka. Ni ngirakamaro ku bantu bose kandi irabanziriza ikibano ico ari co cose. Kandi ni inshingano ya Leta gucungera uburenganzira bwa muntu wese. Ikibano c’ubutungane ni ico uburenganzira bw’umutungo bwubahirizwa bimwe bishitse, bisobanura ko umuntu akingirwa n’abandi boshobora kumugirira nabi.


![image](assets/en/120.webp)

Aha, Bastiat yifatanya n’iragi ry’abafiziyokarate, kandi birenze ivyo, n’umugenzo wa filozofiya y’amategeko ya Ciceron na Aristote. Itegeko ntirirema uburenganzira. Intumbero yayo ni ukubirwanira gutyo igakingira itunga, ry’umuntu ubwiwe, iry’ubunyankamugayo bw’umuntu, n’iry’ivyamwa vy’ibikorwa vyiwe.



_(Sisero)_


## Ugusahura mu buryo bushingiye ku mategeko: Ugugoreka Itegeko


<chapterId>b4122847-e477-578e-ba34-d35844ac4715</chapterId>


Iciyumviro nyamukuru ca Bastiat muri "Itegeko", agatabu kiwe kazwi cane ko mu 1850, ni ukwerekana igituma n'ingene itegeko ryacitse ubusahuzi, ni ukuvuga isoko y'uburenganzira, ubukode bushingiye ku mimerere, n'ububisha bw'ivy'ubutunzi.


![image](assets/en/125.webp)


None ivyagezwe vy’ukuri bimeze gute?


Bastiat atangura ashinga imishinge y’itegeko ry’ubuhinga bw’abantu: ubuzima, umwidegemvyo n’itunga.


Uburyo bw’inzego z’umwidegemvyo kavukire ni bwo igihugu, abantu ku giti cabo n’itunga bibaho imbere y’amategeko. Muri iyo nzira, Bastiat yongerako ati:


> Si kubera ko hari amategeko hariho imitungo, ahubwo ni kubera hari imitungo hariho amategeko.
> Itunga n'Itegeko

![image](assets/en/126.webp)


Umuntu wese aremerewe kurwanira ubuzima bwiwe no gukoresha ubushobozi bwiwe. Kandi itegeko ni ryo ry’ishirahamwe rusangi ry’ukwo kwiregura kwemewe. Itegeko riburanira ubutungane. Si ubutungane bwiza bwotunganya ubuvukanyi n’ubumwe, ahubwo ni ubutungane bubi bugarukira ku kubuza uburenganzira bw’umuntu umwe kwigarurira ubw’uwundi.


Ariko iyo itegeko rihevye kuba ribi rikagira ryiza, inyiyumvo y’ubusumbasumbane irarushirizaho mu kibano, igatuma haba amakimbirane. Yanditse ko iyo twaguye ubuziraherezo igice c’Itegeko, ni ukuvuga inshingano ya Leta, tuba dufunguye urugi rw’“urutonde rudahera rw’ukwidodomba, urwanko, imivurungano n’imigumuko.”


Bastiat avuga ko ubugiraneza bw’ikinyoma ari kimwe mu bintu nyamukuru bituma itegeko rigoranywa. Abagabo bamwebamwe bibona ko bari hejuru y’abandi bantu kandi ko bashoboye guhitamwo neza kuruta abandi.


![image](assets/en/127.webp)

Barazi neza ivyiza ku bandi kandi bazotegeka umuntu wese iciyumviro cabo c’ivyiza; abo ni bo bagiraneza. Baciye barema uburenganzira bw’ikinyoma uno musi bwitwa uburenganzira bw’imibano. Uburenganzira bwo mu kibano nta kindi ariko ni uburenganzira ku bikorwa vy’abandi, uburenganzira bwo gukoresha ivy’umuntu, ivyamwa vy’ibikorwa vyiwe: uburenganzira bwo kuba mu nzu, uburenganzira bwo kuronka amagara, uburenganzira bwo kwiga, gukora, umushahara mutoyi, n’ibindi.


None gusahura ni iki? Ni ikinyuranyo nyaco c’itunga, ni ko Bastiat atubwira. Gusahura biva mu kilatini _spoliare_, bisobanura kwambura. Twarabonye ko umuntu ashobora kubaho gusa mu kwifatanya n’ibintu, mu gukoresha ubushobozi bwiwe ku bintu, ni ukuvuga mu gukora. Alas, arashobora kandi kwigarurira umusaruro w’ubushobozi bwa mugenziwe, ni ukuvuga kumusahura.


![image](assets/en/128.webp)


Intumbero yose y’itegeko ni ugukingira ubwo busahu burengeye ubw’amategeko, ni ukuvuga kurwanira umutungo n’umwidegemvyo, ibintu bibiri bidatandukanye.


Igihe nyene vyemewe mu ngingo ngenderwako ko itegeko rishobora gukevya rikava ku ntumbero yaryo nyakuri, ko rishobora guhungabanya imitungo aho kuyishingira intahe, rikurikira ata kabuza urugamba rw’imigwi, haba rwo kwikingira ubusahuzi canke rwo kuritunganya na ryo nyene ku nyungu y’umuntu.


![image](assets/en/129.webp)


Aho kurwanira uburenganzira bw’abantu, itegeko rihinduka rikagira ukurinda inyungu z’amashirahamwe n’iz’imigwi. Ugusahura gutunganijwe n’itegeko, ku nyungu y’imigwi ibigira n’abagenzi babo canke abaguzi babo. Bastiat rero ariteze ishure ry’uguhitamwo kwa bose mu kinjana ca 20 aho itegeko ari ingaruka y’«isoko rya politike» aho imigwi y’abantu irondera guhazwa n’inyungu zabo kubera abandi.


![image](assets/en/130.webp)


Kuri we, intumbero y’Ivyagezwe itegerezwa kuba gusa iyo “guhagarika ubusahu bwose.” Iyo Leta itagize ico ikoze mu buzima bw’umuntu ku giti ciwe, abantu ni bo bafise ubuzima kandi ni bo bajejwe ubuzima bwabo. Bagira umunezero wabo bwite. Bikorera ingaruka nziza canke mbi z’ivyo bakora.


Barazi neza ko uburenganzira bwabo bwa kavukire bushingiye kandi ko butashobora gukorwako. Uburenganzira bw’umutungo butekanye buha abantu ubushobozi bwo gukora imigambi y’igihe kirekire kuko bazi ko umutungo wabo udashobora gusahurwa.


> Kubura Ubusahuzi, — ni ingingo ngenderwako y’ubutungane, amahoro, urutonde, ugushikama, ugusubiza hamwe, ubwenge busanzwe nzomenyesha n’inkomezi zose, n’agahinda! ntibihagije, vy’amahaha yanje, gushika impwemu yanje ya nyuma

Bastiat yanditse iyo nteruro iri hejuru mu kinyamakuru The Law, igihe kanaka imbere y’uko apfa.


![image](assets/en/131.webp)


Haciye imyaka ijana Frédéric Bastiat apfuye, ubusahuzi bushingiye ku mategeko buragaragara neza mw'Itangazo ry'Uburenganzira bwa Muntu ryo mu 1948, cane cane mu ngingo zaryo 22 ("umuntu wese afise uburenganzira bwo kwishingira imibereho myiza"), 23 ("umuntu wese afise uburenganzira bwo gukora (restyone the"), 2 25 ("umuntu wese afise uburenganzira bwo kuronka ubuzima bubereye ku buzima n'imibereho myiza"), 26 ("umuntu wese afise uburenganzira bwo kwiga").


## Uruhara rw'Itegeko na Leta


<chapterId>52258229-7c7c-592b-aa27-203b03aa41c9</chapterId>


Mu mwaka w’1848, Bastiat yari umudepite. Yaciye ashingwa kuba icegera ca prezida wa komisiyo y’ivy’amahera. Yari rero afise ikibanza ciza cane co kwishura iki kibazo: Leta ni iki? Turahindukirira Leta kugira ngo tubone ko tumererwa neza. Ariko Bastiat atwibutsa ko Leta ataco ishobora guha abanyagihugu itabanje kubatwara.


Bastiat atangura guhindura ingano yemewe na bose: ni Leta ibungabunga ihanga. Ariko Leta ntishobora gutunga abanyagihugu kuko itazana ubutunzi; rirayizunguruka gusa, ikayisubiramwo. Ahubwo, abanyagihugu ni bo batunga Leta biciye mu kurema ubutunzi.


![image](assets/en/132.webp)


Ikindi kandi, Leta ubwayo ntabwo iriho; hariho gusa abagabo bagize Leta, batwara, barongora, babeshwaho na Leta mu buryo butaziguye canke butaziguye. Abagabo rero barongora Leta ni nk’abandi; barondera guhazwa n’inyungu zabo bwite.


![image](assets/en/133.webp)


Kandi ko igikorwa ca Leta ari ugusubira gusangira gusa, gishikirwa n’imikazo y’imigwi y’inyungu zidasanzwe. Nkako, hari imigwi idasanzwe y’inyungu yaratahuye yuko vyari vyoroshe kuronka amahera biciye mu kwifatanya na politike kuruta biciye mu nyifato zitanga inyungu. Barondera kwiba amahera y’abandi biyoberanya ngo ni Leta, bagahungabanya ubushobozi bw’isoko bwo gukora ibintu biciye mu kugwiza amategeko, imisoro n’intambamyi z’ubutegetsi.


![image](assets/en/134.webp)


Mu yandi majambo, Leta ikurikira gusa intumbero z’aba clients, kandi iciyumviro c’inyungu rusangi nta co kimaze. Inyungu iyo ari yo yose bamwebamwe baronka iba ari iyo gutakaza abandi: si urukino rw’umubare zero ahubwo ni urukino rw’umubare mubi.

Bastiat rero ariteze, imbere y’imyaka ijana, isesengura ry’ingene isoko rya politike rikora ryari guseruka mu mpera z’imyaka ya 1950 n’ishure ryitwa Public Choice rya James Buchanan, umuhinguzi w’igihembo ca Nobel mu vy’ubutunzi, n’uwo bakorana Gordon Tullock.

![image](assets/en/135.webp)



Ikindi kandi, Bastiat yemeza ko Leta ata burenganzira ifise butabanza kubaho mu muntu ku giti ciwe. Ni kuki Leta ifise uburenganzira bwo kwishingira, mbere n’inguvu, umutungo w’umuntu wese? Kubera gusa ko ubwo burenganzira bubaho imbere y’igihe mu muntu ku giti ciwe. Umuntu ntashobora kwima abantu ku giti cabo uburenganzira bwo kwikingira, uburenganzira bwo gukoresha inguvu iyo bikenewe kugira ngo yirukane ibitero bikorerwa abantu babo, ubushobozi bwabo n’itunga ryabo. Ubwo burenganzira busanzwe bwo kwikingira, buba mu benegihugu bose, burashobora gufata uburyo rusangi, bugatuma inguvu rusangi zigira uburenganzira.


Kugira rero umuntu amenye nimba Leta ifise uburenganzira mu buryo bubereye, ategerezwa kwibaza nimba ubwo burenganzira buba mu muntu ku giti ciwe kubera ishirahamwe ryiwe kandi ata ntwaro iyo ari yo yose iriho.


Ni co gituma Leta idashobora mu buryo ubwo ari bwo bwose guhungabanya uburenganzira bw’umuntu; ahubwo ritegerezwa kubashingira intahe.


![image](assets/en/137.webp)


Iratuma haba Umutekano, wo mu gihugu no hanze, n’Ubutungane. Ishobora kuba ikomeye kandi ikora neza mu vyo ikora. Ariko itegeko ntirishobora gusohoka hanze y’urwo ruhara rukomeye cane kuko rica rihinduka igikoresho co gusahura kuri bamwe kugira ngo abandi bavyungukireko. Iyo Ivyagezwe bigoramye, bikora nk’igikoresho c’akarenganyo. Ugugoramya amategeko kwama kujana mu gusahura, nk’uko twabibonye mu nyigisho iheruka. Ni ikintu gica gica, kivyihuta, kidashobora gukurwaho kandi kidakeka. Gukura itegeko mu bubasha bwaryo bishobora gusa guhungabanya uburenganzira bwa muntu. Sosiyete sivile rero irakurwako ububasha bwayo (inzego z’akameremere, amasezerano, amashirahamwe, amashirahamwe) igashigikira uburongozi bwa Leta, ni ukuvuga uburongozi bw’ubuhinga n’uburongozi bw’uburongozi.


![image](assets/en/107.webp)


Ivyo bituma ibikorwa vya Leta vyemewe vyonyene vy’igihugu nk’uko Bastiat abivuga ari bitatu: igisirikare, abapolisi n’ubutungane. Mu yandi majambo, Leta itegerezwa gukingira abantu, umwidegemvyo wabo n’itunga ryabo imbere n’inyuma. Ni ibisanzwe rero ko umuntu wese atanga umusanzu muri ubwo burinzi. Ariko rero, uretse izo nshingano zitegekanijwe n’amategeko, iyindi ntererano yose umuntu ashobora gutanga mu kindi gikorwa gikorwa na Leta, irasuzumwa. Hanze y’uwo muzingi, Bastiat yanditse ati:


> Idini, uburezi, ishirahamwe, ibikorwa, uguhanahana, vyose biri mu rwego rw’ibikorwa vy’abantu ku giti cabo, munsi y’ijisho ry’ubutegetsi bwa Leta, bukwiye kugira gusa ubutumwa bwo gucungera no guhana.

Ku bijanye n’ibikorwa vya Leta, avuga ingingo ngenderwako yoroshe:

Niba ushaka kurema igikorwa, yerekane akamaro kaco. Erekana ko bibereye ibikorwa bitanga, bingana n’ivyo bigura. Ni co gituma asozera avuga ko bibereye kwemera urwego rwa Leta gusa ivyo urwego rw’abikorera ku giti cabo rudashobora gushikako rwose.


Mu ncamake, iyo Leta irenze ubutumwa bwayo bwo kurwanira abantu n’itunga, iremesha imigwi y’inyungu kurondera uburenganzira no guhindura ububasha kugira ngo ironke inyungu kubera abasoreshwa n’abaguzi.


> Leta ni ikinyoma gikomeye umuntu wese yihatira kubaho kubera uwundi wese.

Yanditse Frédéric Bastiat mu gatabu gatoyi kitwa Leta.




## Iragi rya Frédéric Bastiat


<chapterId>2a2a181a-e477-5be1-ba1f-af59490c364e</chapterId>


Inkurikizi za Bastiat zatanguye kugabanuka mu mpera z’ikinjana ca 19, aho ubusosiyalisimu na cane cane ubumarxiste bwadutse muri kaminuza no mu bigo vy’ubushakashatsi, vyaterwa inkunga n’amahera ya Leta.


Mu kinjana ca 20, ubumenyi n’ukumenyekana kwa Bastiat vyarazimiye. Ntiyasubiye kuvugwa mu bitabu vy’ubutunzi. Intambara ya Kabiri y’Isi Yose irangiye, ni ho abantu bosubira gukunda ivyiyumviro vy’umwidegemvyo, ivyo bikaba bigaragara ko vyari vyibagiwe cane na Roosevelt be n’intwaro z’ubutegetsi bw’agahomerabunwa zo mu Buraya. Umwe mu bashinze iyo nzira y’ubuzima ni umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi w’Umunya Otirishiya yitwa Ludwig von Mises, umwe mu banyeshure benshi b’abanyaburaya bahunze Uburayi baciye muri Esupanye na Portigal kugira ngo bashike muri Amerika.


Mises yatuye i New York mu 1943, yarateguye amakoraniro yakwegereye abantu b’ubwenge budasanzwe: George Stigler, Milton Friedman, bompi bakaba bari bazoronka agashimwe ka Nobel, na Murray Rothbard, uwo gihe yari umunyeshure muri kaminuza ya Columbia. Muri ayo mahugurwa ni ho yavuze ku ncuro ya mbere ivya Bastiat nk’umwe mu bamenyeshamakuru bakuru b’uburenganzira bwa kera. Yarashikirije abamwumviriza udutabu twa Bastiat, ari two Itegeko na Leta, tukaba tutarahindurwa mu congereza. Mu mwaka w'1953, umwe mu bari muri iyo nama, Leonard Read, yari gutuma igitabu "The Law" ca Bastiat gihindurwa mu congereza, aca afata igikorwa co gukwiragiza ico gitabu mu gihugu cose biciye ku mushinge wiwe: Umushinge w'uburezi bw'ubutunzi.


![image](assets/en/139.webp)

Ariko uwuzokwimenyekanisha kandi ivyiyumviro vya Frédéric Bastiat yari umwanditsi w’ivy’ubutunzi mu kinyamakuru New York Times, Henry Hazlitt, mu gitabu gitoyi citwa: “Ubutunzi mu cigwa kimwe,” casohowe mu 1946. ingaruka.

![image](assets/en/140.webp)


Hazlitt atangura igitabu ciwe mu gusubiramwo inkuru ya Bastiat yerekeye idirisha ryavunitse. Aca avuga mu ncamake iyo nkuru mu cigwa coroshe kandi kidasanzwe:


> Ubuhinga bw’ubutunzi bushingiye ku kuraba gusa ingaruka zica ziza vuba ariko kandi n’ingaruka zimara igihe kirekire z’igikorwa canke politike iyo ari yo yose; ni ugukurikirana ingaruka z’iyo politike atari ku mugwi umwe gusa ariko no ku migwi yose.

Hazlitt aca akoresha ico cigwa ku ngorane nyinshi zitandukanye z’ubutunzi: ukugenzura ubukode, amategeko agenga umushahara mutoyi, inyungu zifatwa nk’iz’intambara, ibikorwa vya Leta n’ikinyuranyo c’ingengo y’imari, ugutera imbere kw’amahera, amakori, n’ubwa nyuma ukuzigama.


Bastiat yari umwe mu bahinga b’ubutunzi Ronald Reagan yakunda cane, kera cane imbere y’uko atorwa kuba umukuru w’igihugu. Ntibizwi cane, ariko imyaka umunani, Reagan yarakoreye ishirahamwe General Electric nk’umurongozi w’ikiganiro caryo ca televiziyo kuri CBS, kandi nk’umuntu ashinzwe gutoza abakozi b’iryo shirahamwe. Porogarama yiwe y’amahugurwa yubatswe ku nyandiko z’intango z’ubutunzi bw’isoko. Ivyo bitabu vyatowe ni ivy’Abanyaotrishe babiri, Hayek na Mises, Abongereza babiri, Cobden na Bright, n’Umufaransa umwe, Frédéric Bastiat.


Icigwa Reagan yigishije abakozi biwe, akurikije Bastiat, ni uko ata nkuru y’imigani ikomeye kuruta iyo gutuma abantu bagira iterambere n’akazi biciye mu gukoresha amahera ya Leta.

![image](assets/en/141.webp)


Ni John Maynard Keynes cane cane yasavye ko amafaranga Leta ikoresha yongerera umusaruro kubera ikintu kigwiza: iyo Leta yubatse ikiraro, abakozi b’ico kiraro bazoshobora kugura umukate, hanyuma umutetsi ashobora kugura inkweto, n’ibindi. Iyo inganda z’abikorera ku giti cabo ziriko ziragabanuka, zirashobora gukosorwa n’ibikorwa bikomeye. Iyo hari ubushomeri, Leta irashobora gutuma abantu bose baronka akazi.


Ariko nk’uko Bastiat yavyerekanye neza, ukugira ico Leta ikora biragira ingaruka mbi zitaboneka. Umuhinga mu vy’ubutunzi mwiza ni we wenyene ashobora kubibona imbere y’igihe. Reka dufate akarorero: Ni ukwihenda kwizera ko Leta ishobora "gushiraho akazi" kuko ku kazi kose ka Leta karemwe, gasenya akazi mw'isoko. Nkako, akazi ka Leta karihwa n’imisoro. Akazi ka Leta ntikaremwa; biratahurwa. Ideni ryose Leta ikoresha ritegerezwa kuronswa ata kabuza biciye ku deni ry’ikori canke ry’imyenda.

![image](assets/en/142.webp)


Turavye ibintu muri iyo nzira, Reagan yasiguriye abakozi ba GE, ivyo bita ibitangaro vy’amahera Leta ikoresha bigaragara mu buryo butandukanye rwose. Kuko imisoro igabanya ubushobozi bwo gukora, kandi ubutunzi buvugwa n’amahera Leta ikoresha ntibushobora gusubiza bimwe bishitse ivyo bwabujijwe kuvuka n’imisoro yashizweho kugira ngo umuntu yishure ayo mahera.



Mu vy’ukuri, Reagan yafashe kuri Bastiat ingingo nyinshi z’ingenzi zijanye n’iyerekwa ry’ubwigenge ry’imibano n’abantu: ubukuru bw’imibano ku gihugu, agaciro k’uguhitamwo n’inshingano z’umuntu ku giti ciwe, akamaro k’rwiyemezamirimo mu kurema ubutunzi, akamaro k’amasezerano y’ishimikiro y’amategeko, ategerezwa kwemerera ubutunzi kuba imbere y'uko ishobora gukwiragizwa, icipfuzo co guha umuntu wese amahirwe mu masoko y'amahiganwa...
