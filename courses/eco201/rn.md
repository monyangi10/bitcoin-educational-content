# Urugendo rwo mu vy'ubutunzi


Murakaze mu nyigisho ya Théo Mogenet! Kubera ko akunda cane ubutunzi, amateka, ubuhinga, ubuhinga bwa politike, n’ubuhinga, yahisemwo kubabwira ubumenyi afise mu vy’ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya. Iri shami, ritazwi cane mu vy’ubutunzi, rishingiye ku gutahura kw’umuntu no ku nyifato y’umukinyi yidegemvya. Ntibikomeye cane mu biharuro, ni ikibazo c’ubwenge n’ivy’imibano hejuru ya vyose.


Iri shure ry’ivyiyumviro ryaramaze ibinjana vyinshi inyuma yaryo kandi rifise urutonde rwose rw’abanditsi, ivyiyumviro n’abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bari inyuma yaryo. Amazina akomeye mu vy’ubutunzi nka Hayek, Rothbard, Mises, Bastiat canke Menger, baramaze igihe kirekire barwanira uwo muhari. Mu buryo butandukanye n’ubu Keynes buri hose bwo muri iki gihe cacu, ishure ry’i Otirishiya risubiza umuntu ku giti ciwe hagati y’ivyo biganiro n’uburyo bwo kwidegemvya, ubw’abanyagihugu, mbere n’ubunyagitugu.

## Incamake y'amashure


<chapterId>eae3de7b-cce6-516d-83d9-28fbd582c0ca</chapterId>


Murakaze mu mahugurwa ya ECO201!


Muri iki cigwa catanzwe na Théo Mogenet, uzosanga ishure ry’ivyiyumviro vy’ubutunzi ritandukanye cane n’inyigisho ya Keynes yari ihambaye. Kugeza ubu, ushobora kuba warigishijwe yuko ugucungera amahera n’ingingo mfatirwako z’ubutunzi ari vyo ahanini bijejwe amabanki akomeye, n’iciyumviro c’uko gucapura amahera n’ugukoresha amahera ya Leta bituma ubutunzi butera imbere. Ariko rero, hariho uburyo bundi buhuye kuruta: **Ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya**.


Kubera ko iryo shure ry’ivyiyumviro ryamaze ibinjana birenga bibiri ry’ubushakashatsi, ivyiyumviro vya filozofiya, n’ivyanditswe n’abanditsi bazwi cane nka Carl Menger, Ludwig von Mises, na Friedrich Hayek, rifata uburyo butandukanye, rishigikira uburyo bwo kubona ubutunzi bushingiye ku vyiyumviro vy’umuntu ku giti ciwe n’ivy’umuntu ku giti ciwe.


Ubutunzi ni, mu vy’ukuri, igice c’imibano kandi gikomeye cane, gifise abakozi benshi bigenga bakorana mu mwidegemvyo kugira ngo bagire ikintu kimwe gihuye. Kugira ngo umuntu atahure iyo nzira y’inguvu, ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya burakunda isesengura ry’uburyo, rishingiye ku nsiguro y’abantu, ku vyerekeye imibano, no ku kwiga ingene isoko rigenda, aho gushingira ku mibare ikomeye.


Muri iki cigwa, uzokwihweza ingingo ngenderwako z’ishimikiro z’iryo shure ry’ivyiyumviro. Théo Mogenet, umwigisha wawe, ni umuvugizi w’iyi nzira y’ubutunzi kandi azokuyobora mu vyiyumviro nyamukuru vy’ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya, akwereke ingene ivyo vyiyumviro bikora cane cane kw’isi ya Bitcoin.


**Igice ca 1: Intangamarara y'UBUTUnzi**

Tuzotangura n’intangamarara rusangi y’Ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya, dutohoze inkomoko yayo y’amateka n’imishinge y’ivyiyumviro vyayo. Iki gice kandi kivuga ku vyiyumviro vy’ingenzi nk’amahera, inguzanyo, amabanki, n’amabanki nkuru. Muzotahura igituma izo nzego zifise uruhara runini mu vyiyumviro vy’Abanyaotirishiya, cane cane mu kunegura ivyo bikorwa vy’amahera.


**Igice ca 2: Ishingiro ry'Ivyiyumviro**

Iki gice kizokwihweza cane ivyiyumviro nyamukuru vy’ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya, nk’inyigisho y’agaciro, isigura igituma agaciro k’ikintu katagira intumbero ahubwo kavana n’akamaro umuntu wese abona. Uzobona kandi ingene amahera asohoka mu buryo busanzwe nk’ikintu gisanzwe mu kibano, hamwe n’ivyiyumviro vy’uguhitamwo umwanya, inyungu, n’umutungo kamere biri mu mutima w’inyigisho y’isoko ry’uburenganzira ryo muri Otirishiya.


**Igice ca 3: Ivyiyumviro vy'ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya**

Aha, turaza kuraba ingene inyigisho y’Abanyaotirishiya ikoreshwa mu buryo ngirakamaro. Uzomenya mu buryo burambuye ivyerekeye inyigisho y’ingendo y’ubudandaji yo muri Otirishiya, isigura ingene gukoresha nabi amahera bituma habaho iterambere ry’ubudandaji ry’ubuhinga rikakurikirwa n’ugusubira inyuma kw’ubutunzi. Turabona kandi igituma uguharura ubutunzi kudashoboka mu gihe c’ubutegetsi bw’abasosiyalisiti n’ingene uburyo bwo muri Otirishiya bushingiye ku buhinga (praxeologie) (inyigisho y’ibikorwa vy’umuntu), butanga uburyo bwihariye kandi buhuye bwo gutahura ibintu vy’ubutunzi.


Iryo shure ni ihuriro ry’ubutunzi n’ubuhinga, rirongowe n’ikiganiro gifunguye hagati yanje na Théo (Rogzy). Ndashimira cane Théo Mogenet kubera yaremye iri shure. Twararyohewe cane no gutegura ivyo birimwo, vyateguwe kugira ngo umuntu wese ashobore kubibona. Iryo shure rikora nk’intangamarara y’ingenzi kandi rizoshinga umushinge w’amashure yacu y’ubutunzi azoza, ateye imbere cane.


Kandi vyogenda gute nimba urufunguruzo rwo gutahura ubutunzi bwo muri iki gihe ruri mu ciyumviro kimaze ibinjana vyinshi? Reka tuvyivumbure twese hamwe!


## Amahera, Inguzanyo, Amabanki, n’Amabanki Nkuru

> "Ikibazo nyamukuru c'amafaranga asanzwe ni ukwizigira kwose gukenewe kugira ngo akore. Banki nkuru itegerezwa kwizigirwa kugira ngo ntiyigere igabanya amafaranga, ariko amateka y'amafaranga yuzuyemwo ukurenga kuri ukwo kwizigira. Amabanki ategerezwa kwizigirwa kugira ngo afate amafaranga yacu kandi ayayohereze mu buryo bw'ubuhinga bwa none, ariko arayagurisha mu buryo butari bwo. mwizigire ubuzima bwacu bw’ibanga, mwizigire ko batareka abasuma b’akaranga ngo baduhe amakonti.”
>

> Satoshi Nakamoto, uwahinguye igikoresho citwa Bitcoin, yitwa izina ry’uruyeri.


### Uko amahera aremwa


Mu nzira y'amahera yacu y'ubu, amahera aremwa ahanini biciye mu migenzo ya banki izwi nka "banki y'ububiko bw'ibice." Iri jambo risobanura ahanini ko amabanki adasabwa kugira amafaranga menshi nk’ayo aronka mu bizigamirwa. Ku bw’ivyo, barashobora kurema ubushobozi bushasha bwo kugura igihe batanga inguzanyo, kandi, ku ruhande rumwe, bagagabanya ubushobozi bwo kugura igihe abakiriya bariha inguzanyo zabo.


Nk’akarorero, iyo wegera banki yo mu karere kawe kugira ngo ubone ingwati yo kugura inzu, amahera iyo banki yakugurije yobayeho nk’inyandiko yo mu gitabu. Mu vy’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubuhinga, mu bisanzwe duserukira ubutunzi bw’umuntu ku giti ciwe dufise urutonde rw’ubutunzi, rufise impande zibiri: uruhande rw’umutungo, harimwo umutungo uwo ari wo wose, amasezerano y’ivy’ubutunzi, ububiko, canke ubundi bwoko bw’ubutunzi afise, n’uruhande rw’imyenda, rwerekana inkomoko y’amahera yakoreshejwe mu kurema umutungo uri ku ruhande rw’umutungo. Itandukaniro hagati y'itunga n'imyenda ryitwa "uburinganire" kandi rishobora kwiyumvirwa nk'ubutunzi rusangi bw'ishirahamwe.


Iyo ikigo c'ivy'imari gifise uruhusha rwo gukora muri banki, bisigura mu vy'ukuri ko imyenda yanditswe nk'"amahera y'abaguzi" afatwa nk'amahera yemewe mu gihugu canke mu karere k'amahera. Rero iyo urondera inguzanyo yo kugura inzu muri banki, uwo mu banki ntaguriza amahera yashizwemwo n’uwundi mukiriya. Ahubwo, banki irashira amahera waguze kuri konti yawe, icarimwe ikandika inguzanyo yawe Contract nk’itunga rya banki. Uko wishura inguzanyo yawe, amahera arazima neza, kandi agaciro k’inguzanyo ihuye n’iyo Contract karagabanuka, banki ikagumana inyungu gusa y’inguzanyo.


Uhejeje kugura iyo nzu, urategeka uwugukorera muri banki ngo arungike amahera kuri konti y’uwugurisha. Iyo konti y’umuguzi iri muri banki itandukanye, umukozi wawe wa banki amenyesha umukozi wa banki ahuye n’iyo banki kugira ngo abone ko konti y’umuguzi ishobora gushirwamwo amahera nk’uko bibereye, mu gihe akuraho amahera kuri konti yawe amahera ahuye n’ayo.

Ivyo bituma amabanki ashobora kwandika amahera yose akoreshwa, harimwo n’ayoherezwa ku mbuga ngurukanabumenyi, ayo agura ku ikarita y’inguzanyo be n’ayo atanga amasheki, mu kiringo kinaka (akenshi ni indwi canke ukwezi). Baca baheza bagaheba ivyo bikorwa hagati yabo bakoresheje amafaranga yo mu mabanki, ayo na yo akaba ari ubundi buryo bw’amahera y’amahera (fiat) atigera akoreshwa na bose. Amafaranga y’amabanki abikwa muri banki nkuru kuri konti idasanzwe ishobora gukoreshwa n’amabanki n’ibigo vy’imari bifise uruhusha gusa.


### Uguhagarara kw'amabanki y'ububiko bw'imice n'umuguzi w'uburyo bwa nyuma


Ikibazo nyamukuru kiri muri iyo nzira y’ububiko bw’amahera y’imice ni uko gukura amahera menshi muri banki kanaka bishobora gutuma ishobora gusenyuka. Kubera ko amabanki ategerezwa gushitsa ivyo abakiriya basaba mu gihe afise gusa amahera makeyi yo mu banki, iyo abakiriya benshi bihutiye gukura amahera icarimwe, birashobora gutuma iyo banki idashobora gushitsa ivyo basaba, ivyo bikaba bituma ishobora gusenyuka. Kubera ko abantu benshi, amashirahamwe menshi be n’ibigo vyinshi bifise amahera yabo abika mu mabanki, kureka banki igasenyuka vyoshobora kugira ingaruka zikomeye ku vy’ubutunzi, nk’ugusubira inyuma kw’ubutunzi canke mbere ugucika intege.


Ico kibazo c’uruyeri ni co catumye habaho amabanki akomeye yo muri iki gihe. Mu kinjana ca 19 mu Bwongereza, gusubiramwo amabanki vyatuma ubutunzi buhungabana, ivyo bikaba vyatumye hashingwa Banki y’Ubwongereza nk’“inguzanyo ya nyuma.” Banki y’Ubwongereza yari yahawe igikorwa co kuguriza amahera amabanki ari mu bibazo mu bihe vy’amagume kugira ngo ntihagire ikintu gishobora gutuma ubuhinga bwose bw’ivy’ubutunzi buhagarara. Ico ciyumviro c’uko amabanki akomeye ari yo atanga inguzanyo mu buryo bwa nyuma, kuva ico gihe carakwiragiye kw’isi yose, kikaba ari ikintu gisanzwe.


Uretse kubungabunga ugushikama kw’ivy’ubutunzi, amabanki nkuru ni yo ashinzwe gushinga ibiciro vy’ingenzi vy’amategeko. Ivyo bipimo ni vyo bigena igiciro amabanki afise uruhusha ashobora kugurana amafaranga muri banki nkuru, mu vy’ukuri bikaba bisobanura igiciro c’amahera y’ibigo vy’imari bifise uruhara runini mu gutanga inguzanyo mu bukungu bwacu. Ivyo bipimo rero birakora nk’ikigereranyo c’urutonde rw’ivy’ubutunzi rwose. Nk’umuntu ku giti ciwe, inyungu wishura ku ngwati yawe zishobora guca mu giciro c’inyungu n’inyungu ya banki.

Mu gihe c’amagume akomeye y’ivy’ubutunzi yo mu 2008, Lehman Brothers, banki nini y’ishoramari, yaratangaje ko yacitse intege inyuma y’aho ihomvye cane ku bijanye n’ivy’ingwati yari ifise, kandi ikaba yarakuwemwo amahera menshi cane ku bakiriya bayo. Mu kwishura kuri iyo ntambara y’ivy’ubutunzi itari bwigere ibaho, abakozi bo mu mabanki akomeye kw’isi yose barashize amahera menshi mu masoko y’ivy’ubutunzi, bafatanya amabanki y’ishoramari yariko aragwana n’amabanki y’ubudandaji, bongera bagabanya ibiciro vy’amategeko gushika hafi kuri zero kugira ngo ntihagire ubutunzi busenyuka.


Naho izo ngingo zabujije ko haba umupfunda w’ugusenyuka kw’ubutunzi, nta co zakoze kugira ngo ubutunzi bube buteye akaga bwakurikiyeho. Abantu amamiliyoni baratakaje akazi n’amazu yabo, amahera abaguzi bakoresha yaragabanutse cane, ubudandaji buragabanuka, amabanki na yo aratakaza amahera menshi. Naho inyungu zari ntoyi mu mateka, bake ni bo bari biteguriye kugurana, ivyo bikaba vyatumye haba uruzitiro rubi aho ukugabanya kw’intango kw’amahera akoreshwa n’ishoramari kwari gukomeje. Ku bw’ivyo, abakozi bo mu mabanki nkuru barateye intambwe zindi mu gushirwa mu ngiro porogarama zo kworohereza amafaranga (QE). Izo porogarama zari zirimwo amabanki nkuru agura amabondi ya Leta be n’amafaranga y’ingwati ashigikiwe n’ingwati mu mabanki y’ubudandaji afise amafaranga y’ububiko bwa banki nkuru.


![image](assets/en/03.webp)

Mu buryo butandukanye n’ivyo benshi bari biteze, porogarama za QE ntizasubiye gutuma ubutunzi butera imbere cane ariko zatumye umutungo w’ubutunzi ushika ku rugero rwa kera. Ivyo vyagiriye akamaro canecane abatunzi n’ibigo vy’imari, kuko vyari bisanzwe bifise ivyo bintu vyinshi cane, ivyo bikaba vyatuma habaho ubusumbane mu vy’ubutunzi. Turavye ingene ubuhinga bwo gukoresha amabanki buteye bwasiguwe imbere y’aho, ivyo ntibikwiye kudutangaza. Kubera ko amafaranga y’amabanki adashobora kwinjira mu bukungu nyakuri mu buryo bworoshe, porogarama za QE ahanini zatumye ibiciro vy’itunga vyiyongera ataco zihinduye ku bijanye n’ubutunzi bw’abantu basanzwe.


### Ingaruka za Cantillon


Naho biri ukwo, ingingo ngenderwako y’ubutunzi y’ingenzi irashobora gukugwa muri iki gice: iyo amahera mashasha aremwe, mu ntango agira akamaro ku bari hafi cane y’aho ayo mahera akomoka, akagirira akamaro abari kure cane. Ubwo bumenyi bw'ubutunzi bwatanguye mu kinjana ca 18 igihe Richard Cantillon yabuvuga mu gitabu ciwe "Inyandiko ku mico y'ubudandaji muri rusangi." Ubu mu rurimi rw’ikirundi ryitwa “Effet Cantillon”.


![image](assets/en/04.webp)


Igishushanyo4: Ingaruka za Cantillon muri make / Inkomoko: Ivy'Imari y'Uruzi


Muri iyo nkuru, abakozi bo mu mabanki, abarongozi ba banki, abafise amasheke n’amabondi, abahinga mu vy’amazu, abaguzi b’amazu, n’umuntu wese afise itunga ry’amahera canke amazu, bararonka umuyaga w’amahera, mu gihe umuzigo wari ku bandi bose. Ivyo vyagumyeho imyaka myinshi kandi ahanini bisigura ubusumbasumbane bw’ubutunzi buriko burakura, ukuntu abantu bakora cane bumva ko ata burenganzira bafise, be n’ukuntu ibiciro vy’itunga bisa n’ibidashobora guhagarara naho nyene iterambere ry’ubutunzi ryari ririko riratera imbere buhoro.


Mu vy’ukuri, iyo nzira iragoramye. Amabanki ntaco akora, yamara iyo ananiwe, birashobora gutuma ubutunzi bwose buhungabana. Ico kibazo co mu vy’inyifato runtu gituma abarongozi ba banki bafata ingingo zirengeje urugero kugira ngo banki yabo ishobore kuronka amahera menshi, bazi ko amaherezo banki nkuru izobakiza, ivyo bikaba bizotuma ivyo bihembo bishika ku bariha imisoro. Muri ivyo bihe, abakozi ba banki nkuru bararema ivyangombwa vyo guhindura cane ububasha bwo kugura buva ku bantu bakora cane no ku bazigama buja ku bafise itunga n’abafitaniye isano n’ubuhinga bw’ivy’ubutunzi, gutyo bagaca inzira yo kurema ubutunzi n’ukwirundanira ubutunzi.

### Inkurikizi z'ingingo ngenderwako z'inyungu zitagira inyungu


Mu bihe birebire vy’Itegeko ry’Inyungu Zitagira Inyungu (ZIRP), amabanki aragira amahirwe make yo gusubira kwubaka uburinganire bwayo kubera ko inyungu zayo zigenda zigabanuka. Amabanki akenshi aronka amahera mu kugurana ku bipimo vy’igihe gito no mu kugurana ku bipimo vy’igihe kirekire. Ariko rero, iyo amabanki akomeye aguze amabondi menshi cane, agashiraho ibiciro kuri zero, amabanki ntaco agira ngo agurize, cane cane abacuruzi n’abandi bashobora gufata ingorane. Ahubwo, batanga ubutunzi bwabo kugira ngo bakingire umutungo uriho canke batange inguzanyo zijanye n’ingwati kugira ngo bashobore gushitsa ivyo abaronka inyungu ku ngaruka ya Cantillon basaba.


Iyindi ngaruka itategekanijwe ya ZIRP ni uko iremesha intwaro gukoresha amahera menshi. Kubera ko intwaro zitagira amafaranga yo kugurana kandi zishobora kwizigira amabanki nkuru kugira ngo zigure amabondi yazo biciye mu migambi ya QE, zifise ikintu gisanzwe zituma zikoresha amahera menshi bishoboka, cane cane mu bihe vya demokarasi aho gukoresha amahera bishobora gutuma abantu batora. Ivyo bimenyetso akenshi ntivyitaho ingaruka z’igihe kirekire z’ukwo gukoresha nabi amafaranga, bikaba bituma habaho ukwiyongera gukomeye kw’imyenda ya Leta mu bihugu vyateye imbere kuva mu gihe c’Ikibazo c’Ivy’Imari kw’Isi Yose (GFC).


![image](assets/en/06.webp)


Igishushanyo ca 6: Ideni rya Leta n'iry'abikorera ku giti cabo nk'% y'umusaruro w'igihugu (Isi, igereranijwe n'umusaruro w'igihugu ku gihugu) / Inkomoko FMI


Kubera ko inflation iriko iraduga bivuye ku kurema amafranga menshi mu kwishura ku bikorwa vy'ugufunga bitewe na COVID, abakozi ba banki nkuru ubu bariko baraduza ibiciro vy'amategeko mu kugerageza guhagarika inflation. Ariko rero, ivyo biratera ingorane ikomeye urutonde rwose. Amabanki arafise amahera menshi kuruta uko vyari bimeze kera, intwaro zifise imyenda myinshi cane mu mateka, iterambere ry’ubutunzi riragenda buhoro, amadeni aragenda aragwira, kandi abaguzi, bariko barahangana n’uguduga kw’ibiciro vy’ibintu vy’agaciro, bariko baragorwa no kuronka ivyo bakeneye. Kugenzura inflation vyosaba ko ibiciro biduzwa ku rugero rushobora gutuma intwaro zihungabana, mu gihe amabanki ashobora gutakaza abazigamye kuko abantu bakoresha amahera yabo mu bintu vy’ingenzi bigenda birazimvye canke barondera ubuhungiro mu mitungo ya Hard n’amahera yo mw’isoko ry’amahera kugira ngo bakingire inflation.


### Iciyumviro


> “Biciye muri ubwo buryo (fractional reserve banking), intwaro zirashobora mu mpisho kandi zitabonetse, zigafata ubutunzi bw’abantu, kandi nta muntu n’umwe mu miliyoni yomenya ubusuma”
>

> Yohani Maynard Keynes


Mu vy’ukuri, urutonde rwacu ruriko rurashikirwa n’ingorane zikomeye, kandi Bitcoin iraseruka nk’ubundi buryo bwonyene bwo kwizigirwa. Ariko rero, Bitcoin yonyene ntishobora Address ibibazo biri mu bijanye n’amahera yacu. Ikiruta vyose, turakeneye abantu batahura ingingo ngenderwako z’ishimikiro z’ubutunzi mu bakunzi ba Bitcoin, bikaba bishoboza ubumenyi bwagutse n’ubwenge busanzwe bw’ubutunzi kugira ngo bituyobore kure y’ukwubaka uwundi mushinge w’ubutunzi ugoyagoya w’iterambere ryacu. Intumbero y’intango y’iri shure ni ukwigisha abashasha bakunda Bitcoin ingingo ngenderwako nziza z’ubutunzi.


Kugira ngo turonke iyo ntumbero, tuzosigura ingingo ngenderwako z'ishimikiro z'"Ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya", ishure ry'ivyiyumviro vy'ubutunzi rifise umugenzo w'uburyo bwo gukora ivyo bintu kuva mu kinjana ca 16, ritanga ubumenyi ku bikorwa vy'umuntu mu gihe afise ingorane z'ubutunzi. Ubu ukoresheje iyi ntangamarara, uratahura ivy’ingenzi vyo kurema amahera be n’ingene ubuhinga bwacu bw’ivy’ubutunzi n’ivy’amahera buri.


Mu kigabane kizoza, tuzokwihweza cane ibuye ry’imfuruka ry’ishure ryose ry’ivyiyumviro ry’ubutunzi: ni inyigisho y’agaciro. Ibice bikurikira bizoca irya n’ino amahera nk’ikigo c’imibano, inyigisho y’umutungo kamere n’ingendo y’ubudandaji, ingorane yo kubara ubutunzi, n’insiguro ngufi y’amateka n’uburyo bw’ishure ry’ubutunzi ryo muri Otirishiya.

## Ivyiyumviro vy'agaciro


<chapterId>eb1608d4-5d36-56a0-bcfc-ed8c03dfa906</chapterId>


> “Agaciro kariho gusa mu mutima w’umuntu”
>

> Carl Menger, Ingingo ngenderwako z'ubutunzi bwa politike


### Impinduka yo ku ruhande


Ku muzi w’uguzirikana mu vy’ubutunzi hari ikibazo c’agaciro. None tumenya gute agaciro k’ikintu? Mbega agaciro ni umutungo w’ibintu uvukana? Canke, ahubwo, ni ikintu umuntu yiyumvira? None tugereranya gute agaciro k’ibintu bibiri? None agaciro kava hehe?


Mwene ivyo bibazo vyaramaze ibinjana vyinshi bihangayikishije abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi no mu vy’ubuhinga bwa filozofiya kandi vyaronse inyishu zitandukanye nyinshi. Mu buryo bwinshi, ugutera imbere kw’ubumenyi bw’ubutunzi kwaciwe n’ugutera imbere kw’inyigisho z’agaciro.


Inyuma y'aho inyigisho y'aba physiocrates yerekeye agaciro k'ubutaka, ivuga ko agaciro kose kava ku butaka, yari yarahakanwe n'inyigisho y'agaciro k'akazi k'abahinga mu vy'ubutunzi, bavuga ko agaciro k'ikintu kava ku rugero rw'abakozi baja mu guhingura, ni ho inyigisho y'agaciro k'inyuma yasubiriye ivya nyuma. Mu myaka ya 1870, hakurikijwe Marx, uwa nyuma mu bahinga b'ubutunzi ba kera, amashure atatu mashasha y'ivyiyumviro vy'ubutunzi yaraserutse hafi icarimwe akikuje inyigisho y'agaciro: ishure rya Lausanne ryari kumwe na Léon Walras, ishure ry'ubu canke ry'ubuhinga bushasha ryari kumwe na William Stanley Jevons, n'ishure ryo muri Otirishiya ryari kumwe na Carl Menger. Iryo hinduka mu vyiyumviro vy’agaciro ryatumye habaho ugusubira gusubirana gukomeye kw’ivyiyumviro vy’ubutunzi.


![image](assets/en/07.webp)


Kuva ibubamfu uja iburyo: William Stanley Jevons, Carl Menger, Léon Walras


Iciyumviro c’agaciro co ku ruhande kivuga ko agaciro k’ubutunzi gahuye n’ico umukozi w’ubutunzi yishura ku bushake ku gice gikurikira c’ikintu canke igikorwa. Nk’uko iyo nyigisho ishimika ku vy’uko ibiciro bishingwa ku ruhande, ni ukuvuga ku gice gikurikira c’ikintu kinaka, vyitwa “ugushira ku ruhande”.


Ni ibisanzwe gushikiriza ukuntu ayo mashure atatu ashirwa ku ruhande nk’aho asa. Nkako, Walras na Jevons barahuye cane, ariko ivyiyumviro vya Menger biratandukanye n’ibindi mu buryo bukomeye. Mu gitabu ciwe, ubu kibonwa ko ari co gishingiyeko inyigisho y'ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya, citwa "Grundsätze des Volkswirtschaftlehre" (Ingingo ngenderwako z'ubutunzi bwa politike), casohowe mu 1874, Menger, aratanga insobanuro y'agaciro, ariko ahanini ishingiye ku muntu, itandukanye n'ivyo Walras na Jev babona ko ari agaciro.


### Agaciro k'umuntu


![image](assets/en/08.webp)


Umuhinga mu vy'ubutunzi wo muri Otirishiya arahakana iciyumviro c'abakurikiye Adamu Smith maze aheba iciyumviro c'uko agaciro k'ikintu kava ku rugero rw'abakozi bakoreshwa mu kugikora, ashigikira iciyumviro c'uko agaciro kaco kagenwa n'umuntu ku giti ciwe, uwo, muri buri gihe, akora igikorwa co mu mutwe co gupima agaciro ku bijanye n'ingero yihariye y'ikintu. Iryo simbuka ry’ubwenge ryakozwe na Menger rirwanya intumbero y’agaciro: kuri we, agaciro si umutungo w’intumbero w’ibintu; ni ingaruka gusa y'ubucuti umuntu afise n'ico kintu: «agaciro ntikabaho hanze y'ubumenyi bw'umuntu».


Mu yandi majambo, Menger aradutumira kwihweza ko agaciro kabaho gusa nk’ikintu co mu mutwe c’umuntu, ko agaciro atari umutungo w’ibintu, ahubwo ko rikomoka ku ciyumviro c’umuntu ku bijanye n’akamaro ashobora kuronka muri ivyo bintu.


Dushingiye kuri iyo mbonerahamwe, litiro y’amazi yo kunywa nta gaciro k’intumbero ifise. Umuntu afise uburyo bwo kuronka amazi yo kunywa kandi atanyotewe muri iki gihe, kumbure yoha agaciro gato cane iyo litiro y’amazi y’inyongera, mu gihe umuntu afise inyota hagati mu bugaragwa, abona ko ari itandukaniro hagati y’ubuzima n’urupfu, yoba ata gukeka yiteguriye guha agaciro hafi y’ubuzima ataco amaze.


Mu ncamake, Menger yabonye ko agaciro k’ikintu c’ubutunzi ata kindi ari atari agaciro k’umuntu ku giti ciwe atanga ku kindi gice c’ico kintu canke c’ico gikorwa.


### Exchange y'ubushake: Urukino rw'Ivyiza


Uhereye kuri ivyo, Menger afata ingingo y’uko Exchange y’ubushake hagati y’abantu babiri iba kubera ko umwe wese mu bagize uruhande yizera ko izokwongerera akamaro kabo. Kuri we, Exchange ntiyishura ko hariho ukungana kw’agaciro, bitandukanye n’ivyo abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi ba kera bemera. Nk’uko uwo munyabwenge w’Umunyaotirishiya abivuga, iyo habaho ukungana kw’akamaro hagati y’ibintu vyahinduwe, nta mvo yotuma abo babiri bokwiruhira guhinduranya mu kibanza ca mbere. Iyo hariho Exchange, ni kubera ko buri ruhande rubona ko ari mu nyungu zabo (subjective), kandi kubera ivyo, Exchange yose y’ubushake itanga inyungu ku kibano.


### Gupima agaciro nk'ikintu co gutunganya ivyipfuzo vy'umuntu


Ariko rero, mwene iyo nyungu yo mu kibano, canke agaciro k’umuntu ku kintu ciza, ntibishobora gupimwa. Ku bwa Menger, agaciro ni ikintu c’ubumenyi co kugereranya (ordinal) aho kuba ingero (cardinal). Si nk’uko abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi b’abanyagihugu bavyiyumviriye kuva kuri Walras na Jevons, Assignment y’umuntu ku giti ciwe y’agaciro k’imibare yerekana akamaro bayikuramwo, ahubwo ni igikorwa co gutegeka ivyipfuzo vy’umuntu aho umuntu aserura ko yipfuza cane umubare w’ivyiza A kuruta umubare w’ivyiza B.


Umukozi wese arashobora kuvuga nimba akunda ibitoke 2 kuruta ivyigwa vy’ubutunzi, ariko nta n’umwe ashobora kuvuga ataco akora ko aha agaciro ibitoke 2 ku 3.1416 utils, mu gihe aha agaciro ivyigwa vy’ubutunzi ku 3 utils, kandi rero, akunda kugira ivyo bitoke. Mwene iyo ndondoro y’ivyo umuntu akunda, ishingiye ku bikorwa vy’ukuri bikomeza, ntihuye n’ukuri kw’imigenderanire y’ubumenyi tubona mu buzima bwacu bwa misi yose. Umuntu nta na rimwe asuzuma ibintu bamushikirizwa mu kubigereranya n’urugero rudasanzwe rw’akamaro. Ahubwo, agereranya mu buryo bw’ukwiyumvira ingendo zitandukanye z’ibikorwa, izo adashobora gucira urubanza mu buryo bushitse ariko naho ari ukwo ashobora kuzishira ku rutonde ashingiye ku kuntu zikenewe.


Ico ciyumviro c’agaciro, gitahurwa nk’ubucuti bwo mu mutwe umuntu afise n’imigambi yiwe be n’uburyo bubereye bwo kuzishitsa, na co nyene kiratuma abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya basigura ikintu c’ugucapura ibikorwa.


### Ugucapura kw'akazi


![image](assets/en/09.webp)


Gusura uruganda rw’imisumari, Léonard Defrance (mu kinjana ca 18)


Umuntu wese ni uwudasanzwe kandi afise ivyabaye vyiwe bwite. Umuntu wese rero arafise ubushobozi bwo gukora ibikorwa bimwebimwe kuruta abo mu runganwe rwiwe (akarusho katagira uko kangana) canke ubushobozi bwo gukora ibikorwa bimwebimwe kuruta abandi (akarusho kagereranywa). Ntibishobora kuba ukundi; guhakana ico kintu c’intango vyoba ari ukuvuga yuko abantu bose bangana mu mice yose.


Mu gihe umuntu afise ubushobozi burengeye ubw’abo mu runganwe rwiwe mu guhingura ikintu kinaka (akarusho katagira uko kangana), barafise inyungu yo kwihariza mu guhingura ico kintu hanyuma bagahindura amasaranganya yabonye n’ibintu bipfuza. Mu kubigira, barahazwa n’akamaro kabo k’umuntu ku giti ciwe mu vy’ubutunzi kuruta iyo bogira uruhara mu guhingura ibintu vyose bipfuza.


Ariko kandi, birashobora kuba ari uko uwo muntu atagira akamaro gashitse mu guhingura ikintu ciza ico ari co cose. Muri ivyo, hazoba hakiriho ubwoko bw’ibikorwa umuntu ashobora gukora neza kurusha abandi (inyungu y’ukugereranya), kandi kubera iyo mvo, baracari n’inyungu yo kwihariza.


Nta gukeka ko hari abantu boshobora gutanga ico ciza bahawe mu buryo bumuha inyungu kuruta we, ariko kubera ko abo bantu kumbure bashobora gutanga inyungu mu kindi gikorwa kuruta muri iki, kandi ko badashobora gukora ivyo bikorwa vyose icarimwe, ntibibagira inyungu gukora kuri ico gikorwa aho gukora ku kindi bashobora gukora cane. Mu kwihariza igikorwa bafise umwimbu mwinshi, bazoronka inyungu nyinshi kuruta iyo batagira ivyo bakora, kandi rero, biciye ku Exchange, boshobora kuronka umubare mwinshi w’ivyo bindi bintu, naho ivyo bintu baronka vyoba vyakozwe neza na bo nyene kuruta ababikora babikuyeko.


Fata akarorero k’umuganga. Ashobora kuba ari mwiza mu kwandika ama email no gutegura amasaha yo guhura n’abandi kuruta umunyamabanga wiwe (akarusho kagereranywa). Ariko umwanya wose amara akora ivyo bikorwa ni umwanya adakoresha mu gukiza abarwayi. Ni co gituma, nk’uko ari we ashobora gukiza abantu, ni mu nyungu ziwe gushinga uwundi muntu ibikorwa vy’ubutegetsi naho yoba ari mwiza muri ico gikorwa kurusha umuvugizi wiwe, kuko bimufasha gushika ku rugero rwo hejuru agaciro kavuye ku bandi, gutyo n’ubutunzi bwayo bwite.


Mu vy'ukuri, hariho inyungu yo kwimenyereza, mbere no ku bantu badafise inyungu zishitse, kuko umwanya ari umutungo udasanzwe kandi uhanganye: igice cose c'igihe umuntu amara mu gikorwa kitari ico umuntu ashobora gutanga cane gisobanura igiciro giserukirwa n'igikorwa yari yarahevye (igiciro c'akaryo).


Uwo muntu amaze kwimenyereza cane igikorwa kinaka, arashobora rero kubika igitigiri c’ibintu abona ko ari ngombwa kugira ngo akoreshe we ubwiwe, Exchange na yo akayibika igitigiri c’ibintu bisigaye kugira ngo akoreshe ibindi bintu yipfuza. Mu kubigira, barahazwa n’icipfuzo cabo c’ibintu bo ubwabo bakora, ivyo bikaba bisobanura ko ibice bisigaye vy’ivyo bakora ata co bimaze kuri bo. Ni co abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bita ugugabanya akamaro k’inyuma: igice cose c’inyongera c’ikintu kirakenewe cane kuruta icabanje. Ku bandi babuze ivyo bintu, ni inkuru itandukanye: kubera izo mpamvu nyene, bakunda kwipfuza cane ivyo badakora kuruta ivyo bakora. Ivyo bituma habaho ukudahukana gukomeye hagati y’agaciro gatandukanye k’abantu ku giti cabo, ivyo bikaba bifasha cane mu guhinduranya: buri ruhande rufise inyungu yo guhinduranya umusaruro wabo w’amasaranganya kuko ivyo bituma bongera akamaro kabo k’umuntu ku giti ciwe.


Iciyumviro c’isesengura ry’imbere ni uko abantu bama bamererwa neza iyo bize cane igikorwa cabo kandi bagakorana n’abandi. Ni co gituma abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya, cane cane Ludwig Von Mises, bashika ku ciyumviro c’uko inyungu y’umwimbu iva mu kugabanya ibikorwa ari yo ituma habaho inzira y’ugufashanya mu kibano. Aha, vyoshobora kuba vyiza tumusubiyemwo ata guca ku ruhande:


"Ibintu vy’ishimikiro vyatumye haba ugufashanya, ikibano, n’iterambere kandi bihindura umuntu w’igikoko akaba umuntu ni ukuri kw’uko igikorwa gikorwa mu gihe c’ugucapura ibikorwa gitanga inyungu kuruta igikorwa co kwitandukanya n’abandi kandi ko imvo y’umuntu ishobora kwemera uku kuri. ni vyiza gufasha inyungu zabo bwite."


### Iciyumviro


> "Umuntu niyabona ko ashobora kubaho neza amanitse ku giti kuruta kwicara ku meza, yoba ariko akora nk'ikijuju kugira ngo ntiyimanike."
>

> Baruki Sipinoza


1871-1874 ni imyaka itangaje y’ubutunzi bwa none: ico kiringo carabonye ibikorwa vy’abanyabwenge batatu bigenga b’ishimikiro ry’ubutunzi bwa none. Mu gushimika ku gaciro k’ibintu, abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya bazotegura ivyiyumviro vyose vy’ubutunzi bibatandukanya n’abo basangiye. Ibikorwa vy’abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya bazirikana ku bikorwa vy’umuntu mu gihe c’ubukene bizoguma bitandukanye cane n’inyigisho z’ubutunzi zatangujwe na Jevons na Walras zishingiye cane ku mibare zihagaze inyuma y’iciyumviro c’uko agaciro gashobora gupimwa mu buryo butagiramwo uburyarya kandi kakavamwo nk’igikorwa gikomeza.


Yubakiye ku bumenyi bw'agaciro k'urutonde rw'ibintu, Menger yasiguye ukuntu havutse ugusangira ibikorwa n'ubushake Exchange. Yamara nk’uko tuzobibona mu kigabane gikurikira, Exchange itaziguye ni ubuhinga bubi ku bakozi b’ubutunzi barondera gutuma bashobora gukoresha neza akamaro kabo. Se w’Ishure ry’i Otirishiya rero yararushirije gutera imbere mu vyiyumviro vyiwe kugira ngo asigure igituma amahera yaje nk’ikigo c’imibano.


Ibice bikurikira bizoba vyerekeye ukuntu amahera yaserutse nk’ikigo c’imibano, inyigisho y’umutungo kamere n’inyungu, ivyo bikaba ari vyo bizoshingirako inyigisho y’ingendo y’ubudandaji, n’ubwa nyuma uruhara rw’ibiciro mu kubara ubutunzi.


## Uguseruka kw'amahera nk'ikintu gikomeye mu kibano


<chapterId>14ded794-0578-5478-ba5b-b2106c74f3ef</chapterId>


Naho abantu ku giti cabo bafise inyungu imwe yo kwimenyereza no gutuma ibikorwa bigabanywa cane, haracariho ingorane zo guhuza ibikorwa zigabanya ukwo kwaguka.


Ica mbere, birahambaye kumenya ko kubera ko ibikorwa vyo guhingura ibintu bifise igihe kandi akenshi bitajanye n’igihe (bitajanye n’igihe kimwe), akenshi hazoba itandukaniro ry’igihe hagati y’intererano y’intango y’umuntu n’iyo uwo mugenziwe azoronka. Kwiyemeza gukora igikorwa kinaka ubu tutabanje kwizigira yuko abandi bazoronka ivyo dukeneye muri kazoza birashobora kudutera akaga.


Mu kugabanya ibikorwa, umwe wese mu bagize uruhande araheza akaronka inyungu mu gukorana neza, ariko umuntu ku giti ciwe, yoshobora kugeragezwa no kunezerererwa igikorwa c’abandi ataco asubiye, kuko muri ubwo buryo, baronka ikintu c’agaciro ataco baciye. Mwene ivyo bihe, aho ugukorana bivamwo inyungu nke ku muntu ku giti ciwe ariko inyungu nyinshi ku mugwi, bivugwa mu vyiyumviro vy'urukino nk'"ingorane y'infungwa."


### Ingorane y'infungwa


Mu ntango, ingorane y’uwo mufungwa yari iteguwe gutya: Abakekwako ivyaha babiri, Alice na Bob, badashobora kuvugana, bari mu kaga ko gufungwa, n’ibihano bishobora guhabwa nk’ibi:



- Iyo Alice areze Bob, Bob akaguma acereje, Alice aragenda ku buntu, Bob akaronka imyaka 3.
- Iyo Alice na Bob bompi bareganya, umwe wese aronka imyaka 2.
- Iyo bompi bacereje, umwe wese aronka umwaka 1.


Ivyo bishobora guserurwa mu rutonde (ibisubizo vy’imibare vyerekana umubare w’imyaka umuntu amaze afungiwe):


| Alice / Bob       | Accuse | Remain Silent |
| ----------------- | ------ | ------------- |
| **Accuse**        | 2, 2   | 0, 3          |
| **Remain Silent** | 3, 0   | 1, 1          |

Muri uwo mukino, nta karyo ko guhuza ibikorwa (uguhanahana amakuru ntibishoboka) kugira ngo haboneke inyungu nziza ku mpande zompi. Ku bw’ivyo, Alice na Bob barafise ikintu kimwekimwe cose kibatera intege co kuregana, naho nyene bitajana ku ngaruka nziza kuri uwo mugwi. Ingamba nziza kuri bompi ni uguceceka, umwe wese akaronka igihano c’umwaka 1.


Uyu mukino ugaragaza ingorane ikunda guhura n’abantu mu buzima nyakuri: iyo ata buryo bwo guhuza ibikorwa buhari, abantu bakunda guhitamwo ingamba zituma baronka inyungu nyinshi ku giti cabo, ataco bafatiye ku ngamba abandi bahisemwo (ubusuma, ubuhendanyi, ubuhemu, ubukazi, n’ibindi), mbere n’igihe uguhuza ibikorwa vyifuzwa cane bishoboka biciye mu guhuza ibikorwa/uburinganire.


### Amahera yo gutorera umuti ingorane zo guhuza ibikorwa


![image](assets/en/10.webp)


Ico kibazo gifise ingaruka nke mu mibano mito mito (nk’akarorero, umuryango, imigwi y’abagenzi) kuko, muri ivyo bihe, umuntu wese aramenyana ataco yishisha, bikaba bishoboka kwibuka intererano z’uwundi. Dufashe ko kuva mu kibano (guhunga) bitera igiciro, uburyo bwo kumenyekana bushingiye ku kwibuka kw’abakozi ku giti cabo akenshi burahagije kugira ngo umuntu yirinde imitego iterwa n’ingorane y’infungwa.


Ariko rero, iyo umuntu ariko arakorana n’imiryango minini yungukira cane ku gucapura ibikorwa, ingorane zo guhuza ibikorwa zirasubira kuvyuka. Ivyo biva ku mpamvu zibiri nyamukuru:


Ica mbere, abantu barafise ubushobozi bwo gutahura ibintu buke. Ntibishoboka ko umuntu agumana kandi yibuka ubucuti buhamye afise n’abantu barenga 150, ivyo bikaba bituma uburyo bwo kumenyekana budahagije kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gutsinda ingorane y’infungwa ku rugero runini.


Ubwa kabiri, ingero yemewe n’abantu y’agaciro k’intererano muri Exchange (ugugereranya) ni ingorane idasanzwe. Nk’akarorero, iyo umuntu atanga inyama zivuye mu guhigira, agasaba ibikoresho vyo kwiberamwo mu gusubiza, ni gute inyama zitangwa zishobora gusuzumwa mu buryo bungana n’ibikoresho asavye? Ni ko biri no ku bijanye n’uburyo – mbega inyama z’imbwebwe zifise agaciro kanini canke gato kuruta inkwi?


Naho vyoshoboka gushinga igipimo gishimishije ca Exchange ku bicuruzwa bibiri bibiri, kubungabunga ayo makuru biraca bihinduka bidashoboka. Mu buryo butaziguye bwa Exchange bujanye n’ibicuruzwa N, hariho ibiciro vya N(N-1)/2 Exchange vyo kwibuka. Ku bukungu bw’ibicuruzwa 50, ivyo bisigura kwibuka ibiciro vya 50\*49/2, canke 1225 Exchange, bitandukanye n’ibiciro 50 gusa mu guhinduranya ibicuruzwa bitaziguye. Ku bukungu bw’ibicuruzwa 100, uwo mubare uraduga ukaja kuri 4950. Mwene ukwo gufatanya kw’ibice bine gushiraho umupaka w’inyongera ku bijanye n’ugushobora gukoresha Exchange (uguhinduranya) itaziguye.


Ikindi kandi, kubera ko ivyo biganiro bitaba ubwo nyene ahubwo bikaba bigenda biva mu gihe, gusuzuma intererano mu gihe birarushiriza kugorana gusuzuma intererano. Uretse gusuzuma igipimo ca Exchange hagati y’ibintu bibiri biriho ubu, biraba ngombwa gusuzuma agaciro k’intererano ya kera ugereranije n’iyizoza.


Muri iki gihe, naho mwene ubwo buryo budakora, twoshobora gukoresha ubuhinga bwo kwandika canke bwo kubika amakuru mu buryo bwa digitale kugira ngo twibuke ayo makuru yose maze dushireho uburyo bwo gutanga inguzanyo (gukurikirana intererano za kera, harimwo n’igipimo ca Exchange c’izo ntererano, ni ugushinga ahanini uburyo bwo gutanga inguzanyo).


Mu bihe vy’imbere y’ubutekerezi, izo tekinoloji ntizariho. Gutyo, ba sogokuruza bacu bategerezwa kurondera ibindi bisubizo kugira ngo bashobore kwinovora inyungu z’ugusangira ibikorwa batishize mu kaga k’ingaruka mbi z’ingorane y’infungwa. Umuti w’ico kibazo ca Exchange itaziguye wari Exchange itaziguye yoroshejwe n’amahera.


### Guhura kabiri kw'ivyo umuntu ashaka n'ivyo ashobora kugurisha


![image](assets/en/11.webp)


Amahera ashobora kubonwa nk'umuti wavumbuwe na ba sogokuruza bacu ku kibazo ca Address ico abahinga mu vy'ubutunzi bita "uguhura kabiri kw'ivyo umuntu yipfuza". Ico kibazo gifise ivyiyumviro bitatu: ivy’ahantu, ivy’igihe n’ivy’abantu.


Mu Exchange (barter) itaziguye hagati ya Alice na Bob, bompi barakeneye kugira ikintu uwundi yipfuza mu gihe kimwe no mu kibanza kimwe. Mu gukoresha Exchange itaziguye, ni ukuvuga, biciye ku mahera, Alice arashobora kugura kuri Bob, na Bob ashobora gukoresha iyo nkuru y’amahera ahandi, mu kindi gihe, kandi n’uwundi muntu (igihe cose uwo wundi yemeye ubwo bwoko bw’amahera).


Kugira ngo ikintu gikore nk’amahera, gitegerezwa kuba gifise ubushobozi bwo kugurisha cane, bisobanura ko gikwiye kwipfuzwa n’abantu benshi bishobotse, kenshi. Mu gukoresha ikintu kigurishwa cane, ingorane y’uguhura kabiri kw’ivyo umuntu yipfuza iratorwa umuti mu bijanye n’ahantu n’imigenderanire hagati y’abantu: nimba ikintu nkoresha nk’amahera cipfuzwa hose kandi n’abantu benshi, ndashobora gutandukanya bitagoranye igikorwa co kugurisha n’igikorwa co kugura mu bijanye n’aho umuntu ari be n’imigenderanire y’abantu.


Ariko rero, ingorane y’ugucuruzwa mu gihe c’igihe irarushiriza kugorana gutorera umuti kubera imvo zibiri:


Ica mbere, entropie (izwi cane kw'izina rya "ingaruka y'igihe") ihindura buhoro buhoro kamere z'ibintu vyinshi bifise akamaro kataziguye. Ku bw’ivyo, kugira ngo ikintu kigume kigurishwa mu gihe kirekire bisaba ko kigumaho cane canke kidashobora gushikirwa n’ibintu bishobora gusenyuka.


Ubwa kabiri, ubukene bw'ikintu ciza ku gihe "t" ntibushobora kwemeza ko buzoba buke cane muri kazoza. Mu gutanga ubutunzi buhagije ku kibanza kinaka c’ubuhinga, abantu barashobora kwongerera Supply y’ivyiza vyose. Ico gusa cotuma umuntu yongera umusaruro w’ikintu ni igiciro c’akaryo kijanye n’ico kintu. Ku bw’ivyo, ubukene bw’ikintu buriho ubu ntibushobora kwemeza ko buzoba buke cane muri kazoza. Ibintu vyonyene bishobora kwongerwa umusaruro wavyo ku ruhande ku giciro kinini cane bishobora kuba bike cane, ni co gituma ivyo ari ikintu kiranga ibicuruzwa vy’amahera vyavutse mu mwidegemvyo mu mateka yose y’abantu.


Mu bihe vy’imbere y’ubutekerezi, ibintu bitandukanye nk’ibikoko vyo mu kiyaga, amabuye y’agaciro, amabuye y’agaciro canke amabuye y’agaciro vyakoreshwa nk’amahera. Ivyo bintu vyari vyoroshe gutwara, ntivyari bifise akamaro kanini karenze agaciro kavyo k’ugushaza, vyari bihanganye n’ugusenyuka (ni ukuvuga ko bitagenda birasenyuka uko igihe kigenda kirarenga), vyari bike cane kandi/canke vyasaba abakozi benshi cane kugira ngo bishobore gukorwa. Kubera ko urugero rw’ugusangira ibikorwa rwari ruto ico gihe, kandi rero, igiciro c’akaryo kijanye n’uguhingura ibintu vy’ugushaza cari kinini, ivyo bintu ntivyari gushobora gukorwa mu bwinshi. Gutyo, abakoresha ivyo bintu nk’amahera boshobora kwizigira ko muri kazoza bazoba bake cane.


Kuba ba sogokuruza bacu b’abahigi n’abakoranirizi bariko barakora ivyo bikorwa bikoresha ubutunzi bwinshi, naho ata kintu na kimwe bashobora gutanga, vyerekana inyungu nyinshi bari biteze mu kwagura ikibanza, imibano n’igihe ca Exchange. Iyo ivyo bitaba ari ukwo, kandi bikaba ari ngirakamaro kuri bo gukoresha ivyo bintu mu kwubaka amazu y’uburaro, mu guhigira canke mu bindi bikorwa, aho gukoresha mu guhingura ibintu vy’amahera, kumbure ntitworonka ibimenyamenya vyinshi vy’ubucukuzi vy’ivyo bikorwa vy’ubuhinga. Ayandi mashirahamwe akoresha neza ubutunzi bwayo yari gutera imbere neza no guterimbere cane, kandi ivyo bikorwa vy’ubuhinga vyari guca bizimangana ningoga, bikaja mu bikorwa vy’uguhingura ibintu bifise akamaro ataco bimaze.


Muri ubwo buryo, uguhingura ibintu vy’amahera, mu guteza imbere ukwaguka kw’ugusangira ibikorwa, vyari bigereranya ugukoresha neza ubutunzi (mu bijanye n’akamaro k’abantu ku giti cabo) kuruta ubundi buryo bwose (kwongerera ubuhigi, uburovyi, gukorakoranya, uguhingura inkwi, kwubaka inzu, guhingura ubuhigi n’uburovyi bwinshi n’ibindi).


### Ukudakeka


Kugira ngo dusozere isesengura ryacu ry’ikigo c’amahera, turakeneye Address ikibazo c’ibikorwa vy’ubutunzi mu gihe c’ukudakeka ataco bimaze ku bijanye n’ivyo muri kazoza.


Nk’uko abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya babivuze, ibikorwa vy’umuntu birafise igihe kandi vyama vyerekeye kazoza. Iyo umuntu akoze ikintu, arahindura uko ameze ubu afise icizigiro co kuronka ukunyurwa muri kazoza. Ukwo gutegura mu mutwe gushobora kwerekezwa ku bijanye n’ivyo muri kazoza ka hafi canke ka kure, ariko kugira ngo umuntu ashobore gutegura ivy’igihe kirekire, ategerezwa kubanza kuronka ivyo akeneye mu gihe gito kuko uko azoba ari muri kazoza ka hafi bigira ico bikoze ata guca ku ruhande ku kuntu azoba ari muri kazoza ka kure.


Ivyo biva ata guca ku ruhande ku ntumbero y’umuntu; nta n’umwe ashobora kwirengagiza urutonde rw’ibintu vy’igihe n’ukuntu bikurikirana biva kuri vyo kuko ni kimwe mu bintu nyamukuru bibuza ubuzima bw’umuntu. Kubera rero ko kazoza k’abantu kaguma katamenyekana, bazorondera gukingira ubuzima bwabo bw’igihe kirekire igihe gusa bazoronka ubuzima bw’igihe gito.


Muri ivyo, amahera, mu kwemera ko agaciro kabikwa muri iki gihe no kugajana mu vyo umuntu azoba muri kazoza, arafise uruhara runini mu guhuza ibikorwa vy’umuntu hagati y’ibihe. Mu kubika amahera, ni ukuvuga kuzigama, abantu barinda ukudakeka muri kazoza, gutyo bagashobora kuyobora ibikorwa vyabo mu gihe kirekire. Ariko ivyo bashobora kubishikako gusa iyo amahera akoreshwa ari ububiko bw’agaciro, bisobanura ko afise ubushobozi bwo kugurishwa mu gihe c’igihe, ivyo bikaba nk’uko twabibonye, ​​ari ikintu kiranga ibintu bihoraho kandi bike cane.


Mu kigabane gikurikira tuzokwihweza cane iciyumviro co guhitamwo umwanya no gusigura ukuntu abanya Otirishiya babona inyungu n’umutungo kamere, ivyo bikaba bizoba ishingiro ry’igice gikurikira kivuga ku ciyumviro c’ingendo y’ubudandaji.


## Igihe Ukunda, Inyungu, n'Itunga


<chapterId>37732a5c-4f66-5e2d-bc2c-cc8d29693af7</chapterId>


### Igihe ukunda


Twarasozereye igice ca nyuma mu gusigura ingene abakozi b’ubutunzi bakoresha ivyiza bigurishwa cane, ni ukuvuga amahera kugira ngo bakingire ukudakeka muri kazoza. Twarasiguye kandi ko ukuntu ibintu vy’igihe bikurikirana bituma turwanya ukudakeka buhorobuhoro: iyo tuzi ko ivyo tuzobaho mu ndwi ikurikira ni ho gusa dushobora kwibanda ku migambi iri kure cane muri kazoza.


Canke, tubivuge mu buryo butandukanye: nk’abantu turagabanya agaciro k’ibintu vyo muri kazoza.


Ukwo gusuzuma umuntu ku giti ciwe agaciro k’ibintu vyo muri kazoza ugereranyije n’ibintu vyo muri iki gihe vyitwa uguhitamwo igihe. Ibindi vyose bikaba bingana, ivy’ubu birakundwa cane kuruta ivy’imbere. Kubera ko turi abantu bapfa, kandi kazoza kaguma katamenyekana, birasanzwe ko duhitamwo kuronka iciza ubu aho kuronka mu nyuma. Naho igihe umuntu akunda gishobora gutandukanywa, kubera ibintu vyinshi cane nk’umuco, ubutunzi, uburezi, umubiri, n’ibindi, igihe umuntu akunda cama ari ciza, bisobanura ko ibintu vyose bingana, twama duha agaciro ivyiza vy’ubu kuruta ivyiza vyo muri kazoza.


Ico ciyumviro co gupima agaciro k’ibintu vyo muri kazoza ku bintu vyo muri iki gihe ni co gishingiyeko ikintu c’inyungu. Nkako, mu bukungu bufise amasoko y’itunga atakoreshwa, inyungu z’ishingiro (zibonwa ko zidafise ingorane zo kudatanga) zigenwa ku mahuriro y’itunga Supply n’ivyo umuntu asaba. Rero, ivyo bipimo bigereranya uko igihe umuntu akunda ku bukungu bwose kimeze: ukwiyongera kw’inyungu kuva ku kwiyongera kw’abasaba umutungo ugereranije na Supply, bikaba vyerekana ko umuntu akunda igihe kinini. Ku rundi ruhande, ukugabanya inyungu bishika kubera ukwiyongera kw’amahera azigamiwe, ivyo bikaba ari ukwiyongera kwa Supply y’umutungo kamere, bikaba vyerekana ko igihe umuntu akunda kigabanuka.


![image](assets/en/12.webp)


Mu bukungu aho inyungu zidakoreshwa na banki nkuru, dukunda kubona umurongo w’inyungu ugenda uraduga: uko umwenda umara igihe kirekire, ni ko inyungu igenda irakura. Ivyo bihushanye n’ivyo ntibishobora kubaho kuko vyotuma kazoza kazoba gatomoye kuruta ubu, ivyo bikaba ari ibintu bidashoboka.


Iciyumviro co guhitamwo umwanya n’ingene duserura umwanya wacu bwite dukunda biciye mu gikorwa co gukoresha no kuzigama ni ngirakamaro mu migenderanire yo gutanga umutungo n’uguhingura. Reka duhindukirire umunyeshure wa Menger, Eugen von Böhm-Bawerk, n’inyigisho yiwe y’ubutunzi kugira ngo dutahure neza ingene uguhitamwo umwanya bigira ico bikoze ku bijanye n’imitunganirize y’ibikorwa.


### Ivyiyumviro vy'umutungo


![image](assets/en/13.webp)


Mu ntango y’iri shure, twabonye ko, kuri Carl Menger, ibintu bifatwa gusa nk’ibintu vy’ubutunzi (bifise agaciro) kuko bikora nk’uburyo bwo gushika ku ntumbero zitowe kandi zihabwa agaciro n’abantu ku giti cabo. Dushingiye kuri iyo mbonerahamwe, isesengura ryose ry’ubutunzi rizunguruka ugukoresha kuko ari yo ntumbero y’iherezo ituma umuntu akora ibikorwa vyose vy’ubutunzi. Rero, kuri Menger, intango y’isesengura ry’ubutunzi ni ibicuruzwa vy’abaguzi, canke ibicuruzwa vya nyuma, kuko ari vyo bigereranya intumbero nyamukuru y’igikorwa c’ubutunzi. Ibindi bicuruzwa vyose vyo mu bukungu, ivyo twokwita "ibicuruzwa vyo hagati", bifise agaciro gusa kuko bishoboza abantu ku giti cabo kuronka ivyo bicuruzwa vy'abaguzi: ni ibicuruzwa bikoreshwa mu guhingura ibindi bicuruzwa.


Kugira ngo abacuruzi bashobore guhingura ibintu vy’ugukoresha, bafatanya ivyo bintu bitandukanye vyo hagati n’ibintu vy’intango vy’uguhingura (abakozi, ubutaka n’umutungo kamere) hakurikijwe uburyo butuma umusaruro uvamwo urushirizaho kuba mwinshi. Iyo ntunganyo, ikorwa n’abanyamigambi, canke ubuhinga bwo guhingura, irimwo intambwe zitandukanye aho ibintu vyo hagati bihinduka gushika aho amaherezo bihinduka ibintu vy’abaguzi.


Gutyo, nka Menger, turashobora gusobanura ibintu vy’abaguzi nk’ibintu vy’urutonde rwa mbere, ibintu vyari mu ntambwe ya mbere nk’ibintu vy’urutonde rwa kabiri, ivyo mu ntambwe y’imbere nk’ibintu vy’urutonde rwa gatatu, n’ibindi, gushika dushitse ku bintu vy’intango (ubutaka, abakozi, umutungo). Umubare w’intambwe turimbura ahanini uvana n’imiterere y’ubuhinga yemejwe n’abanyamigambi kandi ntibikwiye gufatwa nk’ikintu kigaragaza intumbero y’imiterere y’ubuhinga. Ahubwo, intambwe z’uguhingura n’ibintu vyo hagati bibaho gusa mu gihe c’ubuhinga bwa teleoloji: umukinyi yiyumvira urutonde rw’ibikorwa azocamwo kugira ngo ashike ku ntumbero yipfuza, maze mu bwenge bagaca bagabanya igikorwa cabo mu ntambwe zikurikirana.


Ico kiranga ugucapura mu bwenge kw’ibikorwa mu buryo bukurikirana gitegekwa n’ingene igikorwa c’umuntu gifata igihe. Igikorwa kimwekimwe cose abantu bakora gifata umwanya; gukora ubwo nyene ntibishoboka. Ku bw’ivyo, umukinyi yama afise amahitamwo mu bikorwa bifata umwanya munini canke muto.


Kuva ubu, kubera ko abantu bafise ata kabuza ivyiza bakunda mu bijanye n’igihe, bisobanura ko bakunda ivyiza vy’ubu kuruta ivyiza vyo muri kazoza, bazohitamwo gusa inzira ndende iyo igisubizo baronka gifise agaciro kanini kuri bo kuruta ivyo boba barashitseko mu gufata inzira itaziguye. Ahandi ho, nta n’umwe yofata uburyo butwara umwanya mwinshi: mu gihe c’ivyavuyemwo bingana, inzira ngufi kuruta izindi zose iguma ari yo ihitamwo.


Kubera urutonde rw’ibikorwa vy’umuntu, ayo mahitamwo hagati y’ibihe yama afise ingaruka ku rutonde rw’ibikorwa. Mu yandi majambo, ibikorwa vy’igihe gito nkora biri munsi y’imigambi y’igihe kirekire nshizeho, kandi ibikorwa vyanje vy’igihe gito bizogira ico bikoze ku vyo nshobora gukora muri kazoza. Ico iki ciyumviro kigaragara kijanye n’ibikorwa vy’uguhingura kivuga ni uko ugukevya kwose kw’uguhingura, ni ukuvuga, ugukura kwose kw’imiterere y’uguhingura, bisaba kuzigama imbere y’igihe. Iyo mfashe ingingo yo gutanga amafaranga menshi muri iki gihe kugira ngo nshobore gushika ku ntumbero ya kazoza, ntegerezwa kubanza gushiramwo ivyo bizotuma nkomeza mu kiringo ishoramari ryanje rizofata.

### Robinson Crusoe n'Ibarabara ry'Ibikorwa:


![image](assets/en/15.webp)


Amaduka ya Robinson Crusoe avuye mu bwato bwasambutse, Yohani Alegizandere Gilfillan (1793-1864)


Mu gitabu ciwe, uwo muhinga mu vy’ubutunzi wo muri Otirishiya aradutumira kwihweza ivy’uguhuza ibintu hagati y’ibihe biva mu guhinduranya ibikorwa biciye mu ngeragezwa y’ivyiyumviro ishingiye kuri Robinson Crusoe wenyene kw’izinga ryiwe.


Robinson, cokimwe n’umuntu wa kera, yizigiye kurondera ivyo kurya no guhigira kugira ngo abone ivyo afungura. Reka twiyumvire ko Robinson ashobora kwegeranya ibitoke bihagije kugira ngo yigaburire umusi wose mu masaha umunani. Mu bihe nk’ivyo, nta mwanya agira wo gukora ibindi bikorwa. Ariko rero, Robinson yiyumvira yuko mu gukora igiti c’ibiti, yoshobora gutera hasi ivyo bihimba vy’umubiri bitamugoye, akaronka ivyo arya ku musi ku musi mu masaha ane gusa akora. Vyongeye, agereranya ko bizomutwara imisi itanu, akora amasaha abiri ku musi, kugira ngo akore iyo nkingi. Ni co gituma ashika ku ciyumviro c’uko akwiye kuzigama 1/5 c’ibitoke vyiwe mu misi itanu, canke akamara amasaha 2 y’inyongera ku musi mu gukorakoranya imisi 5, kugira ngo azigame ibitoke bihagije kugira ngo yibeho mu kiringo amara akora igiti.


Iyo atakoze iyo prior saving, Robinson ntazoshobora kurangiza igiti ciwe kandi yoshobora gupfa muri ico gihe.


Rero, mu misi itanu, aratanga amasaha abiri y’ikiruhuko ciwe kugira ngo yegeranye ibindi bihimba vy’umubiri. Ico kiringo kirangiye, araronka ivyamwa bihagije maze agatangura gukora igiti c’ibiti, akora amasaha abiri ku musi mu kiringo c’imisi itanu. Iyo akazi kiwe karangiye, arashobora kuronka ibitoke bihagije vy’ivyo arya ku musi mu masaha 4 aho kuronka amasaha 8, ivyo bikaba bimufasha gukoresha amasaha 4 asigaye ku musi mu bindi bikorwa.


Mu gukora gutyo, Robinson afata inzira y’uguhingura: aho kwegeranya ata guca ku ruhande ivyo bihimba vy’umubiri, ashiramwo inguvu mu guhingura ivyiza vy’agaciro bizotuma arushiriza kugira umwimbu muri kazoza. Ariko rero, ategerezwa gutanga ikimazi c’igihe gito, ni ukuvuga gukiza, kugira ngo ivyo abishikeko. Iyo atabikora, ntiyari gushobora kurangiza ivyiza vyiwe vy’umutungo. Ariko rero, ico kimazi c’igihe gito kiramuha akamaro kanini kuko iyo amaze gukoresha igiti ciwe, aronka amasaha 4 ku musi (gushika igiti kibe ica kera). Ayo masaha 4 y’inyongera ku musi aramushoboza kurema ibintu vyinshi vy’agaciro, nk’ibikoresho vyo guhigira canke insenga zo kuroba, buhoro buhoro bigatuma ivyiwe bigenda neza.


### Iciyumviro


Mu yandi majambo, mu bukungu bw’umuntu umwe bwa Robinson Crusoé, kuzigama biciye mu kwitanga kw’ukunyurwa kw’ubu ni co kirundanira umutungo wongerera umusaruro. Muri ivyo, kuzigama, ni ukuvuga gusubiza inyuma ukunyurwa kw’ubu, ni co kiguzi co kwishura kugira ngo umuntu arushirize kunyurwa muri kazoza. Ivyo bisigura ko, muri ivyo, kuzigama ari co kintu gikenewe kandi gikenewe kugira ngo ubutunzi butere imbere iyo ari yo yose.


Ico ni iciyumviro giteye ubwoba, naho coroshe: ukwagura kwose kw’imiterere y’umwimbu bisaba ko umuntu azigama imbere y’igihe (kuko ibintu bikenewe kugira ngo ivyo bihingure ntibizova mw’ijuru), kandi rero, uko tuzozigama cane, ni ko tuzoshobora kwirundanira umutungo mwinshi, ivyo na vyo bikazotuma turonka inyungu z’umwimbu zituma turonka ibintu vyinshi. Gutyo, abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya babona ko ugugabanya igihe umuntu akunda ari intango y’uruziga rwiza rwo kuzigama –> ivyiza vyinshi  umusaruro mwinshi  ivyiza vyinshi = ubuzima buhambaye –> igihe gito.


Ubu, nk’uko vyacitse intege mu kigabane ca mbere, inyungu zamaze imyaka mirongo zikoreshwa na banki nkuru mu gihe amabanki y’ubudandaji yatanga inguzanyo ata n’imwe yizigamiye, bisobanura ko inyungu zidagereranya igihe dukunda kandi zitanga iciyumviro c’uko dushobora kuzigama amahera menshi.


Ivyo vyerekanywe neza n’ikigereranyo kiri aha hepfo: ibiciro vy’igihe kirekire biri hasi y’ibiciro vy’igihe gito. Ica mbere, ivyo nta co bimaze na gato, kuko vyotuma kazoza kazoba gatomoye kuruta ubu. Ubwa kabiri, birakenewe ko haba itohoza ku ngaruka zishobora guterwa n’ugutanga umutungo: iyo umuntu wese atera intege gukora nk’aho amafaranga azigamye ari menshi, mu gihe abazigama ataho bari kubera ko badahembwa kubera bazigamye, ivyo vyoshobora kugira ingaruka izihe ku bukungu?

# Ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya


<partId>ad0fce42-2556-56b8-a093-5b4fcacc7cf3</partId>


## Ivyiyumviro vy'Abanyaotirishiya ku bijanye n'ingendo y'ubudandaji


<chapterId>718afaa8-ce78-58aa-9477-073eef0bd137</chapterId>


> "Uko iterambere ry'inguzanyo za banki ziterwa n'ugutera imbere rikomeza, ni ko urugero rw'ishoramari ribi mu bicuruzwa vy'ubutunzi rugenda ruragwira, kandi ni ko bikenerwa cane gusesa izo nzira zitari nziza. Iyo ukwaguka kw'inguzanyo guhagaze, gusubira inyuma, canke mbere kugabanuka cane, ishoramari ribi rigaragara"
>

> Luduwiki voni Mises


Ludwig Von Mises, umunyeshure wa Böhm-Bawerk yari afise ubuhinga bwinshi kuruta abandi bose kandi ata gukeka ko ari we muhinga mu vy’ubutunzi wo muri Otirishiya ahambaye kuruta abandi bose mu kinjana ca 20, ni we yakoresheje ivyiyumviro vya Böhm-Bawerk kugira ngo asigure imvo n’imvano n’ingene ubutunzi bugenda. Friedrich A. Hayek, uwurindwa na Mises, mu nyuma yarashizeho iyo nzira gushika ku nsozero zayo zijanye n'ubwenge mu bitabu vyamuhaye agashimwe ka Nobel mu vy'ubutunzi mu 1974.


Mises na Hayek batanguye isesengura ryabo n’ukwiyongera kw’amahera bazigamye nk’intango. Nk’uko twabibonye mu bigabane vyabanje, ukwongerekana kwose kw’amahera azigamiwe gutuma n’ivyo umuntu akoresha bigabanuka, gutyo bigatuma ibiciro vy’ibintu bikoreshwa bigabanuka. Ivyo bituma habaho ingaruka zibiri: iya mbere, ugusaba ibintu vy’agaciro vyiyongera bitewe n’ugutera imbere kw’imishahara nyayo biva ku kugabanya ibiciro vy’ibintu bikoreshwa; n’ica kabiri, ukwiyongera kw’inyungu z’abanyamigambi ku ntambwe z’ubuhinga ziri kure cane y’ugukoresha (urutonde rwo hasi). Uko umushahara nyawo ugenda uraduga, abashoramare baraterwa intege zo kuzigama abakozi mu gukoresha ibintu vyinshi vy’agaciro, ivyo bikaba bituma habaho ugushaka gukomeye kw’ibintu vy’agaciro n’inyungu nyinshi ku bashoramare bakora ivyo bintu vy’agaciro gatoyi. Gutyo, mu gihe c’ukwiyongera kw’ubuzigama, ni ukuvuga ukugabanya igihe umuntu akunda, inyungu ziragabanuka, bigatuma habaho uguteza imbere inzira z’inyongera z’umwimbu n’ukwiyongera kw’umwimbu. Ivyo ni inzira y’ubuhinga bwa kera ya Böhm-Bawerkian, kandi ni ingaruka nziza cane.


Ariko rero, abo bahinga babiri b’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya bariyumviriye ivyoshobora kuba iyo ukugabanya inyungu, ari vyo bifasha iyo nzira y’ugukevya mu bijanye n’uguhingura, kutava ku kwiyongera kw’amahera azigamiwe, ahubwo kuva ku kwagura inguzanyo.


Mu bijanye n’amabanki y’ububiko bw’ibice, kwagura inguzanyo ntibisaba ko amafaranga azigamiwe yongerekana bihuye n’ivyo. Rero, abashoramare barashobora gukorana umutungo mwinshi no kwifatanya n’abandi mu bikorwa vy’uguhingura ibintu naho nyene igihe bakunda coba kidahindutse, ni ukuvuga ata kugabanya kw’ivyo bakoresha. Ku bwa Hayek na Mises, ivyo bintu bikwiye gutuma haba ingorane zikomeye zo guhuza ibikorwa hagati y’abakozi b’ubutunzi. Kubera ko ata nyungu z’isoko ry’uburenganzira zihari, izo ngorane zishobora kutaboneka ubwo nyene, ariko mu gihe kirekire, ivyo biva ku gutanga nabi umutungo bikwiye gutuma habaho ingaruka zigaragara: ugusubira inyuma kw’ubutunzi.


Kugira ngo tudondore neza iki kintu c’uguhuza nabi igihe n’ingaruka zaco, tuzokwizigira akarorero k’imiterere y’ibikorwa maze twihweze ingene bigira ico bikozeko, ubwa mbere n’ugugabanya inyungu biva ku kwiyongera kw’amahera azigamiwe, hanyuma n’ugugabanya inyungu biva ku kwaguka kw’inguzanyo.


### Igabanuka ry’inyungu bitewe n’ukwiyongera kw’amahera azigamiwe:


Kugira ngo tworohereze insobanuro yacu, tuzosubira ku rutonde rwa Menger rw’ibintu maze tugaragaze imiterere y’umwimbu ku kigereranyo gifise umubare w’intambwe zidasanzwe:


![image](assets/en/17.webp)


Mu kigereranyo kiri hejuru, ibikoresho vy’intango bica mu ntambwe zitandukanye z’uguhingura, bikagira amahinduka bituma bikwegeranya uko ibintu vy’iherezo bikoreshwa (biciye mu gukorana n’ibintu vy’intango vy’uguhingura: igihe, ubutaka, abakozi). Uburebure bw’uruhande rw’iburyo rw’inkomoko itatu bugereranya mu buryo bw’igishushanyo umusaruro w’igihugu kuko bugaragaza umubare w’ibintu vyose bigurishwa mu kiringo kinaka. Ico kinogo kiri hagati y’umurongo umwe umwe gihuye n’agaciro kongereweko (mu mafaranga) kava ku ntambwe yose y’urugendo. Iryo tandukaniro rishobora kandi kubonwa nk’inyungu ijana n’intambwe imwe imwe (inyungu - amafaranga).


Iyo, ku rugero rwose, abakozi b’ubutunzi bongera ivyo bazigama, igitigiri c’ibintu vya nyuma bikoreshwa kizogabanuka - ibindi vyose bikaba bingana, kuzigama birimwo vy’ukuri gusubiramwo igice c’ivyo umuntu akoresha ku musi ukurikira. Ivyo bizotuma inyungu zigabanuka - kuko Supply y’umutungo iriko iragwira, bikaba bituma abashoramare bakoresha iyo nzira y’umutungo kugira ngo bareme ibintu bishasha vyo gushora imari mu bikorwa, gutyo bagatuma urutonde rw’ibikorwa ruramba.


Tuzoheza turonke urutonde rw’ibikorwa rwagutse, ihinduka rishobora guserurwa mu buryo bw’uburyo n’iki kigereranyo gikurikira:


![image](assets/en/18.webp)


Aha, agaciro k’amahera k’ibicuruzwa vy’abaguzi bisabwa karagabanutse, bituma habaho uburyo bwo kurema intambwe y’inyongera y’uguhingura. Muri iki gihe aho ukugabanya inyungu ari ingaruka y’ukugabanya ugukoresha, ni ukuvuga, ukwongerekana kw’amahera azigamiwe, uburebure bw’inkomoko y’inkomoko y’inkomoko, bugereranya ubwinshi bw’amahera ariko arakoreshwa, ntibuhinduka. Ihinduka ry’imiterere y’ibikorwa (ugukura) riva gusa ku kwimurira ububasha bwo kugura buva ku gice kimwe c’imiterere bukaja mu kindi.


Biragaragara kandi ko ukugabanya ivyipfuzo vy’ibicuruzwa bizotuma, mu kiringo giciye hagati, ibiciro vy’ibicuruzwa bigabanuka aho kugabanya igitigiri c’ibicuruzwa vya nyuma bitangwa. Ivyo ni kubera ko igice ca nyuma c’imiterere y’ubuhinga kitazoca gihinduka ubwo nyene inyuma y’aho ukugabanuka kw’abasaba ibicuruzwa vy’abaguzi kubaye; abashoramare bazofata umwanya wo guhindura imigambi yabo n’ishoramari ryabo. Uko bazoba bafise ibiharuro, ukugabanya ivyo basaba bizobahatira kugurisha ivyo biharuro ku giciro gitoyi, kandi, kubera ivyo, amasaranganya y’amahera bazigamye azotuma mu ntango ibiciro vy’ibicuruzwa vy’abaguzi bigabanuka (ni ukuvuga, ukwongerekana kw’imishahara nyayo).


Ku rundi ruhande, ibicuruzwa vy’ishoramari bizobona ibiciro vyavyo biduga kuko guha abashoramari ububasha bwo kugura bibashoboza kwongerera amahera bakoresha mu gushora imari mu bikorwa. Ivyo bizigamirwa, birungikwa n’abazigama ku bacuruzi, bimaze gukoreshwa n’abo ba nyuma, inyungu zizosubira kuduga (bivuye ku kugabanya Supply y’umutungo kamere), ivyo na vyo bikazotuma ibiciro vy’ibintu vyo gushora imari bigabanutse. Nkako, ku mpera y’iyi nzira y’uguhinduranya, ibiciro bizoguma nk’uko vyari kera. Ariko abakozi b’ubutunzi muri rusangi barahungukiye: ukwongerekana kw’umwimbu biva ku kuramba kw’imiterere y’umwimbu bizotuma abaguzi bashobora kuronka ibintu vyinshi ku bici- ro bitobito; ubushobozi bwo kugura bw’abazigama buzokwongerekana, bimwe bivuye ku nyungu zizoronka, ibindi bivuye ku biciro vy’abaguzi bigabanutse; mu gihe abashoramare, iyo bafashwe muri rusangi, ntibazoronka inyungu canke ngo bahombe. Abakora ibikorwa vyegereye cane ugukoresha bazotakaza inyungu, mu gihe abakora mu kurema inzira nshasha z’uguhingura bazoronka inyungu zihuye n’ivyo bazoronka. Mu gihe nk’ico, nta n’inyungu nshasha y’amahera iremwa; ni umusaruro wiyongera, gutyo agaciro nyakuri k’amahera yinjira kakaduga.


### Igabanuka ry’inyungu bitewe n’ukwiyongera kw’inguzanyo (Nta kwiyongera kw’amahera azigamiwe):


Ubu, iyo twihweje ukugabanya inyungu biva ku kwagura inguzanyo zitangwa n’amabanki, turaronka ishusho itandukanye cane y’imiterere y’ibikorwa.


Kubera ko inyungu ziri hasi, abashoramare barashobora kugurana ubutunzi bwinshi, gutyo bagatuma habaho inzira z’uguhingura zifise urutonde rwo hejuru. Muri ivyo, ukwo kwagura imiterere y’ubuhinga ntikuzotuma ugukoresha kugabanuka kuko ata gusubiramwo kw’abaguzi kw’ugukoresha ubu. Mu yandi majambo, GDP irakura. Ku bw’ivyo, inkomoko yacu itatu izoreha mu gihe izoguma ifise uburebure nk’ubwo, bisobanura ko uburebure bwayo buzokwongerekana.


Zirikana ko ivyo ari ingaruka zihuye n’ubwenge rwose z’ukwaguka kw’inguzanyo. Kubera ko amabanki atanga ibinyamakuru vy’ubudandaji mu gutanga inguzanyo, umuntu akwiye kwitega ko ubushobozi bwo kugura muri rusangi buzokwongerekana.


![image](assets/en/19.webp)


Uko inguzanyo zija mu bukungu biciye mu ngurane zihabwa abashoramare, dukwiye kubona ko inyungu zigenda zirushirizaho mu nzego z’ubuhinga ziri kure y’ugukoresha, n’inyungu zigereranywa zigabanuka mu nzego ziri hafi y’ugukoresha. Uwo nyungu myinshi rero urashigikira ugusubira gutanga umutungo uja muri izo ntambwe nshasha, zikoresha umutungo mwinshi (ubwubatsi bw’ubwato, imodoka, ubwubatsi, ubuhinga buteye imbere, n’ibindi), no kugabanya ishoramari mu nzego ziri hafi y’ugukoresha.


Ubu, abacuruzi bari muri izo ntambwe zihambaye z’ubuhinga bararonka amahera menshi, kandi, uko igihe co guhitamwo caguma ari co kimwe, dukwiye kandi kubona ko hariho ugushaka kw’ibintu vy’abaguzi. Ariko nk’uko, muri iki gihe c’iterambere, inyungu y’umutungo ushizwemwo yari nyinshi mu mice iri kure y’ugukoresha, habayeho ukwimurira umutungo mu bikorwa biri hafi y’ugukoresha ukaja mu bikorwa biri kure cane. Ku bw’ivyo, abashoramare bari mu bice vyo hasi vy’umwimbu ntibagira uburyo bwo guhabwa ivyo basaba vyiyongereye. Ivyo bituma haba uguhuzagurika hagati y’ivyo bice bibiri vy’imiterere y’umwimbu: kimwe cose kigerageza kuronka umutungo ku giciro c’ikindi, kandi kubera ko ugusaba ugukoresha bigereranya ivya nkenerwa vyihutirwa cane, mu gihe kanaka, abashoramare bakora ibikorwa biri kure y’ugukoresha bazobura ubutunzi bukenewe kugira ngo barangize ishoramari ryabo. Igitigiri c’inyungu muri izo nzego rero gica gitangura kugabanuka, ubudandaji burasenyuka, kandi ukwiyongera kw’ibiciro vy’abaguzi bituma habaho ugusubira gutanga umutungo vyihuse kugira ngo hakorwe ibintu vy’agaciro gatoyi. Iyo iyo nzira yo gusubira gutanga ubutunzi mu buryo butunguranye igaragaye, ubutunzi buraca buja mu bihe vy’ugusubira inyuma: ibiciro vy’itunga biragabanuka, umushahara nyawo uragabanuka, ibiciro vy’abaguzi biragabanuka, kandi ibikoresho vy’ububiko birarundanywa.


Ku bwa Friedrich Hayek na Ludwig von Mises, ugusubira inyuma kw’ubutunzi ni ikimenyetso c’ugutanga nabi umutungo kuva mu gihe c’ukwagura. Uko ibiciro vy’amahera yazigamiwe n’amahera yakoreshwa nabi, abashoramare barateguye imigambi idashobora kurangira kubera ukubura ubutunzi, no/canke kwubaka igenamigambi ry’ubushobozi bwo gutanga umusaruro ku rugero rw’ugukoresha rwo muri kazoza rudashobora gukomeza kubera ukubura amahera yazigamiwe.


Biciye mu gusenyuka kw’ubutunzi, ni ukuvuga kugabanya ibiciro vy’itunga n’imishahara, inyungu nyinshi, no gusesa imigambi itarangiye, ni ho gusa ubutunzi bushobora gusubira gutunganya no gutera imbere buja mu nzira iramvye. Rero, ugusubira inyuma kw’ubutunzi ni ugukwiragira kw’ivyo vyiyumviro vy’uguterimbere bitera inzira y’uguhindura ibintu mu buryo bw’ubukazi.


Muri rusangi, iyo nzira y’ugusubira inyuma kw’ubutunzi iterwa n’urwego rw’amabanki ubwarwo. Igihe cose inguzanyo zizoba ziriko ziraduga ku rugero rwihuta, ibiciro birabandanya kwiyongera, kandi abacuruzi barahiganwa kugira ngo baronke ubutunzi bushobora gutuma umuntu agira ico akora. Ariko nk’uko vyavuzwe na Hyman Minsky, harashika aho urwego rw’amabanki rufata ingingo yo kugabanya ingorane zarwo, bigatuma rero rugabanya uruja n’uruza rw’inguzanyo. Ku bw’ivyo, iyo ntambara ituma abantu benshi bacika intege, inguzanyo zigakomera, ubushobozi bwo kugura buriho bukagabanuka, n’amahera agahungabana.


Mwene ukwo guhindura gushobora gufatwa nk’igihe aho ugukoresha nabi, n’ugushiramwo amahera make bishirwa mu ngiro kugira ngo umuntu asubire kwubaka amahera yazigamye yari yabuze. Ku bwa Hayek, iyo ntambwe y’agahinda, naho ibabaje, irakenewe cane kuko ituma ibikorwa vy’ubutunzi bisubirana bishingiye ku miterere y’ibiciro bihuye vyerekana ubukene nyakuri bw’ibintu bifasha mu guhingura. Iyo iyo ntambara ihagaze, ubutunzi ntibushobora gusubira mu nzira yipfuzwa kuko, iyo ata nzira y’amakuru ishobora gutuma abakozi b’ubutunzi bashobora gushingira intahe ingingo zabo, ugutanga nabi ubutunzi kuzobandanya gusa.


Ikibabaje ni uko ubwo buryo bwo gutera agahinda kenshi bucikamwo n’ububasha bwa politike be n’amabanki akomeye arondera “gutera imbere” ubutunzi biciye mu gukoresha amahera ataco amaze no gukoresha politike yoroshe y’amahera.


![image](assets/en/20.webp)


Ku bakunda amahera no ku bakunda Keynes, imvo y’ugucika intege ni ukudahagije kw’abasaba vyose hamwe, rero nta n’umwe muri bo yitwararika iterambere ry’ibiciro bihuye, nk’uko twabibonye, ​​ari ryo shingiro ry’ingorane. Gutyo, bemera ko gutanga insiguro y’ukwagura inguzanyo (kugabanya inyungu) no gukoresha ubushobozi bwa Leta bwo gutanga amadeni kugira ngo yongere ivyifuzo bizotuma habaho ugusubirana. Mu gihe gito, izo ngingo zishobora gusa n’izitanga ingaruka zikenewe: ikinyuranyo gifasha mu gukoresha amahera, mu gihe ukugabanya inyungu bituma ibiciro vy’itunga biduga, ivyo na vyo bikaba bituma abafise itunga bongera amahera bakoresha. Ariko rero, mwene ukwo gutera imbere kuraheza kugabanuka, mu gihe ingorane y’imibumbe igumaho, canke mbere igakomera cane uko ugutanga nabi kw’itunga kubandanya bivuye ku nyungu ziri hasi cane.


Mu bihe vya none, amabanki nkuru n’intwaro vyaragize umwete mwinshi mu kubuza ko iyo nzira yo guhindura ibintu igaragara ku buryo duheza tukagira ubushomeri bwinshi bushingiye ku mibumbe n’ukwirundanira imyenda idahera. Ubuyapani buratanga akarorero muri ivyo. Inyuma y'aho ubutunzi buteye imbere mu 1989-90, Banki y'Ubuyapani (BoJ) n'intwaro zitandukanye zari ku butegetsi zakoresheje uburyo bwose buvugwa aha kugira ngo zigerageze "gusubira gutanguza ubutunzi bw'Ubuyapani." Uretse ugutera imbere gutoyi kwakurikiye porogarama zo gukoresha amahera n’ugugabanya inyungu, Ubuyapani bwagumye mu gihe c’iterambere ry’ubwonko n’imyenda myinshi cane mu myaka 30.


### Insozero ku ciyumviro c’ingendo y’ubudandaji:


Mu gushimika ku rutonde rw’ibikorwa vy’umuntu no kwitwararika canecane ingaruka z’uguhinduka kw’inyungu ku guhuza ibikorwa vy’ubutunzi hagati y’ibihe, Ludwig Von Mises na Friedrich Hayek basiguye inzinguzingu z’ubutunzi nk’inguvu z’imbere mu gihugu z’uburyo bw’amabanki y’ububiko bw’ibice. Itandukaniro hagati y’isesengura ry’abanya Otirishiya n’iry’abakunda amafaranga n’abanya Keynes ahanini riri mu kuba uwa mbere yitwararika canecane intambwe zitandukanye z’umwimbu n’imiterere y’ibiciro, mu gihe uwa kabiri ahagarara ku bintu bihinduka vyose nk’ingero z’akazi, umusaruro w’igihugu, canke urutonde rw’ibiciro vy’abaguzi. Nkako, kuko babuze inyigisho y’ubutunzi, abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bakuru bakunda gushingira intahe imvo z’ugusubira inyuma kw’ubutunzi ku “mpwemu z’ibikoko” canke “ibintu vyo hanze”.


Kuruta ayandi mashure yose y’ubutunzi, Ishure ryo muri Otirishiya rishimika ku kamaro k’ibiciro bihuye kugira ngo abakozi b’ubutunzi bashobore guhuzwa. Abagize ishure ry’i Otirishiya baramaze imyaka irenga ijana bakwegwakwegwa n’impaka ku bijanye n’ico kibazo, cane cane kuva Mises asohora igitabu ciwe ku bijanye n’ukuntu ubutunzi budashoboka mu butunzi bw’abasosiyalisiti mu 1919.


Ivyo ni vyo bizokwigwa mu kigabane gikurikira kandi ca nyuma c’iri shure.


## Ubudashoboka bwo kubara ubutunzi mu gihe c'ubusosiyalisimu


<chapterId>2578a9d8-90e9-58dd-a8c5-6366948564c7</chapterId>


> “Aho ata biciro vy’isoko vy’ibintu bifasha mu guhingura ibintu biriho kubera ko bitagurwa canke ngo bigurishwe, ntibishoboka ko umuntu akoresha ubuhinga bwo kubara mu gutegura ibikorwa vyo muri kazoza no mu kumenya ivyavuye mu bikorwa vya kera. nk’aho vyoba ari ugusesagura ibintu bikeyi vyo guhingura ibintu vy’umubiri n’ivy’abantu (akaduruvayo n’ubukene kuri bose bizovamwo ata kabuza” .
>

> Ludwig von Mises, akajagari kategekanijwe


### Ubudashoboka bwo kubara ubutunzi mu gihe c'ubusosiyalisimu


Naho intwaro za Marxiste zasubiyemwo ukunanirwa mu kinjana ca nyuma, impaka zijanye n’uguharura ubutunzi ziracari ngirakamaro kubera imvo zibiri zihambaye:


1. Ivyiyumviro bigereranywa biracariko birashigikirwa n’abaterambere n’abandi bashaka gufasha abantu.

2. Gushinga ibiciro, haba mu masoko y’itunga biciye mu bikorwa vy’abakozi ba banki nkuru canke mu yandi masoko biciye mu bigo vya Leta, amategeko, n’ugufasha kwa komite zigenzura, birabandanya kuba vyinshi.


### Impaka z'Ibara ry'Ubutunzi


Ivyo biganiro vyatangujwe n’imwe mu mpapuro z’ubutunzi zari zifise akosho kanini mu kinjana ca 20, “Ibara ry’ubutunzi mu gihugu c’Ubumwe bw’Abasosiyalisiti,” ryanditswe na Ludwig von Mises, risohoka mu 1920. (Ubudage), n’imigambwe y’abasosiyalisiti n’abakomunisita iriko iratera imbere mu Buraya bwose.


Imbere y’ingingo ya Mises, impaka zijanye n’ubusosiyalisimu n’ubu kapitalizimu zari zikikuje canecane imvo z’inyifato runtu be n’ingorane y’inkurikizi. Naho umuntu yokwiyumvira ko ikibano gitunganijwe gikikuje ingingo ngenderwako y’abamarxiste y’uko « kuva kuri buri wese nk’uko ashoboye, kuri umwe wese nk’uko akeneye » ari co gisumba ibindi mu vy’inyifato runtu, ikibazo ngirakamaro c’uko « ni nde azokura imyanda » cari gikeneye gutorwa umuti. Inyishu rusangi yari iyo ko ubusosiyalisimu bwovyara abantu batagira kamere y’ubu kapitalizimu, bagakorera bagenzi babo n’umutima ukunze mbere n’aho ata mahera yobatera intege.


Mises akoresheje ingingo yiwe, yarashizeho urugero rushasha mu bijanye n’impaka. Uwo muhinga mu vy’ubutunzi wo muri Otirishiya ashize ku ruhande ivyiyumviro vy’ubuhinga bwa none vyerekeye ubushobozi bw’ubutunzi bwa politike bwo kurema “umuntu mushasha”, yerekanye ko gutunganya ubutunzi mu buryo bubereye bidashoboka ata bici- ro vy’ibintu vyo hagati mu guhingura. Na n’ubu, ingingo yiwe iracari itahurwa neza n’abamunegura, eka mbere n’abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bamwebamwe b’abanyagihugu. Ni vyiza rero ko tubisigura mu buryo burambuye kuruta.


### Gusigura ukuntu ububaruzi bw'ubutunzi budashoboka


![image](assets/en/21.webp)


Ivyiyumviro vyinshi bitari vyo ku bijanye n’imvo za Mises biva ku gutahura nabi uruhara rw’imigwi y’abarongozi n’abanyamigambi mu bukungu bw’abanyagihugu. Mises ntiyigeze yirengagiza ubushobozi bw’abarongozi bwo gutegura imigambi myiza y’uguhingura mu bikorwa vyabo bwite. Ahubwo yashimikiye akamaro k’abanyamigambi n’abanyamigabane, abo, nk’abafise uburyo bwo gukora, bagatanga umutungo mu nganda zitandukanye, gutyo bagahingura ibiciro bikora nk’ibikoresho mu biharuro vy’ubutunzi vy’abarongozi.


Iyo ata masoko y’itunga n’amahera, biraca bidashoboka ko umuntu akoresha neza ikoreshwa ry’ubutunzi mu nganda zose. Ivyo bisigura ko naho hoba hariho imitunganirize itunganye mu kigo cose canke mu gice c’ubutunzi, ubutunzi bwose ntibushobora guhura neza n’ihinduka ry’ukuntu ibikoresho biboneka, uko ibintu bigenda, n’ivyo abaguzi bakunda. Mu majambo ya Mises:


> "[...] ikinyoma gikomeye kivugwa mu vyiyumviro [vy'abanyagihugu b'isoko] ni uko baraba ingorane y'ubutunzi bafatiye ku mbonerahamwe y'umunyamabanga wo mu rwego rwo hasi afise ubwenge butarengeye ibikorwa vy'ubuhinga. Bafata imiterere y'umwimbu w'inganda n'ugutanga umutungo ku mashami atandukanye y'ubuhinga nk'uko bimeze guhindura iyo mibumbe kugira ngo ihuze n’ihinduka ry’ibintu.... Bananirwa gutahura ko ibikorwa vy’abarongozi b’amashirahamwe bishingiye gusa ku gushitsa ata buryarya ibikorwa bajejwe n’abakuru babo, abafise imigabane.... Ibikorwa vy’abarongozi, ugugura n’ugucuruza kwabo, ni igice gitoyi gusa c’ibikorwa vy’isoko ryose. gutanga ibicuruzwa vy’ubutunzi ku mashami atandukanye y’inganda.Abashoramari n’abanyamigabane bashinga amashirahamwe n’ayandi mashirahamwe, bakayagura canke bakayagabanya ubunini bwayo, bakayasesa, canke bakayafatanya n’ayandi mashirahamwe bagura kandi bagurisha imigabane n’amabondi y’amashirahamwe asanzwe ariho n’ayandi mashirahamwe mashasha, bagatanga inguzanyo yose; ivyo vyose vyitwa isoko ry'umutungo n'amahera. Ni ivyo bikorwa vy'ubutunzi vy'abaterambere n'abacuruzi bijana umusaruro muri iyo nzira aho bihaza ivyipfuzo vyihutirwa vy'abaguzi mu buryo bwiza bushoboka."
>

> Mises, Ibikorwa vy’umuntu, urupapuro rwa 703-04.


Mu vy’ukuri, Mises avuga ko uburenganzira bw’umutungo, bushira abafise umutungo mu kibanza c’inyungu n’ibihombo, bubatera intege zo gutanga ubutunzi bwabo ku nganda ubu zikeneye cane ubutunzi kugira ngo zihaze ivyo abaguzi basaba. Iyo baroraniwe, bararonka inyungu, ariko iyo bananiwe, barahomba amahera. "Urukoba rwabo mu rukino" rubatera intege zo gutekereza ku bijanye n'ugutanga umutungo mwiza ku bijanye n'ingene ubutunzi buriko buragenda. Ivyo bituma habaho uguhinduka gushingiye ku isoko aho ingaruka rusangi z’ibikorwa vyabo zitanga amakuru ahambaye ku bijanye n’ugukoresha neza ubutunzi.


Ibice vy’imbere vyasiguye ko agaciro gashingiye ku muntu, ibikorwa vy’ubutunzi vyerekana ibiciro vy’amahirwe, kandi ibiciro vy’abaguzi bigaragaza urutonde rw’ivyo abaguzi bashaka. Abanyamigambi barahiganwa ku bijanye n’ibintu bifasha mu guhingura kugira ngo bubake imibumbe y’uguhingura ituma inyungu zirushirizaho kuronka amafaranga menshi, zigahaza ivyipfuzo vy’abaguzi neza kuruta ubundi buryo bwo guhitamwo. Rero, ibiciro vy’ibintu vy’umwimbu biva ku bici- ro vy’abaguzi: iyo ikintu c’umwimbu gishobora generate inyungu nyinshi z’amahera (guhazwa neza n’ivyo abaguzi bipfuza) mu yindi nganda canke mu mugambi utandukanye, abashoramari bazorusha nyen’ico kintu ubu, bagatuma igiciro caco kiduzwa ku rugero rw’umwimbu waco wo ku ruhande. Ukwo guhiganwa hagati y’abanyamigambi ku bintu vy’umwimbu, bigaragaza umwimbu wabo munini cane, ni inzira itanga amakuru akwiriye yerekeye ugutanga umutungo.


Uwo murongo ni uwuhambaye cane kuko wemeza canke ugakuraho ubushobozi bw’ibikorwa bitandukanye, ugatuma ibintu vy’umwimbu bishirwa ku mikoreshereze yavyo itanga umwimbu mwinshi. Isoko rikora iyo nshingano nk’igikorwa gikomeza. Mu isi iguma ihinduka—aho ivyo abaguzi bakunda, ivy’uguhingura, ubuhinga, amabwirizwa, igitigiri c’abantu n’ibindi biguma bihinduka—ibiciro vy’ibintu vyo hagati mu guhingura biguma bihinduka biciye ku bikorwa vy’abanyamigambi n’abanyagihugu bihuza n’ibintu bihinduka. Kubera ko ayo mahinduka aba mu karere, amakuru ategerezwa gukwiragizwa ku bakozi b’ubutunzi badashobora kugira ubumenyi bushitse ku isi yose. Uwo ni wo murimo w’isoko: utuma abashoramare bakora ku makuru yo mu karere, akenshi ari ay’uburyo, kandi agoranye mu gutanga ivyiyumviro vy’imiterere y’ubutunzi y’umwimbu, ivyo bikaba bica vyemezwa canke bikurwaho n’isoko. Muri ubwo buryo, amakuru akwiriye akomoka kuri iyo nzira iva hasi ija hejuru arahurizwa hamwe maze agakwiragizwa mu bukungu bwose biciye ku nzira y’ibiciro. Uwo murongo wo gutanga amakuru no kuyakwiragiza ni ngombwa cane mu gutanga ubutunzi kuko ushoboza abakozi b’ubutunzi, bafise ubumenyi buke kw’isi, gukora imibare y’ubutunzi no gutegura imigambi y’ubutunzi ihuye bizigira ibiciro.


Uvuye muri iyo nzira, ubutunzi butegekanijwe hagati buzoshikirwa ata kabuza n’ugutanga nabi umutungo. Mu gihe gito canke giciriritse, mwene ukwo gutanga nabi vyoshobora kutamenyekana kubera ko ata bici- ro vy’isoko canke gusenyuka kw’amahera bishobora kubihishura. Ariko rero, kubera ko ata n’inyishu (ibiciro) n’uburyo bwo gusubira gutanga (ugusenyuka), amakosa azokwirundanira gushika aho ubusazi bugaragaye biciye mu kugabanya cane ubuzima.

### Ivyo Otirishiya ibona n’ivyo ayandi mashure y’ubutunzi yananiwe


Umuntu yoshobora kuvuga yuko gushushanya mwene iyo nkuru nziza umuntu abona inyuma ari ikintu coroshe. Nakare, twese turazi ama etageri ata co amaze muri URSS, ingorane za Venezuela, n’ivyago vy’abantu vyabaye muri Cambodge. Ariko Mises yari yarabonye ivyo bintu kuva mu 1920. Yamara, gushika URSS isenyutse mu 1989, abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi benshi, harimwo n’abantu benshi baronse agashimwe ka Nobel, bariko barashima igitangaro c’ubutunzi c’Ubusoviyeti, kandi bakavuga yuko ubutunzi bw’Ubusoviyeti buzoca burengera ubwa Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika.


Naho ivyo bimenyetso bitangaje be n’ibimenyetso vyinshi vyerekana ko ubutunzi budashoboka mu gihe c’ubusosiyalisimu, abarongozi ba politike kw’isi yose barafise umushasharo mwinshi kuruta uko vyari bimeze kera wo gushinga ibiciro, guhindura inganda zose ngo zibe iz’igihugu, no gutanga imigambi y’imyaka itanu, akenshi ishimwa cane n’abantu batazi ivy’ubutunzi. Inkurikizi z’ukwo kwivanga mu bikorwa zirumvikana cane ku bantu bo mu bihugu vy’Uburengero vyahora biteye imbere, bakaba bariko barabona buhorobuhoro ukuntu ubuzima bwabo buriko buragabanuka.


### Ivyiyumviro vy'ingendo y'ubudandaji vyo muri Otirishiya nk'ikibazo kidasanzwe c'ukudashoboka kw'ibara ry'ubutunzi mu gihe c'ubusosiyalisimu


![image](assets/en/23.webp)


Mu kigabane c’imbere, twasobanuye neza ingene ishoramari rirenze urugero n’ugutanga nabi umutungo biva ku gukoresha nabi inyungu n’amabanki akomeye. Mu vy’ukuri, ivyo twasiguye birashobora gufatwa nk’ikibazo kidasanzwe c’ukudashoboka kw’ibara ry’ubutunzi mu gihe c’ubusosiyalisimu, bikoreshwa mu bijanye n’amasoko y’amahera. Iyo ibiciro bishizwe hanze y’agaciro kavyo kw’isoko, abashoramare n’abatanga umutungo baraterwa intege zo kwinjira mu bikorwa vy’ishoramari bidashobora kugumaho mu kiringo kirekire kubera ukubura amahera bazigamye. Mu kwivanga mu nzira y’ibiciro, abategura imigambi nkuru (muri iki gihe, abakozi bo mu mabanki nkuru) baratuma haba uguhuza nabi hagati y’abakozi b’ubutunzi. Muri iki gihe, ukudahuza neza hagati y’ibihe bijana n’ugushiramwo amahera menshi mu bicuruzwa vy’ishoramari vy’urugero rwo hejuru n’ugushiramwo amahera make mu bicuruzwa vy’ishoramari vy’urugero rwo hasi, ivyo bikaba bigaragaza ikimenyetso kidasanzwe c’ugutanga nabi umutungo mu nganda zose.


Inkurikizi z’ukwo gutanga nabi ni nk’ingorane z’ivy’ubutunzi n’ivy’ubutunzi, ukugabanya ibikorwa vy’ubutunzi, n’ugutakaza imyenda. Izo ngaruka z’ubutunzi buhambaye zikomoka ku kudahukana hagati y’amahera azigamiwe n’amahera ashirwamwo biva ku kwagura inguzanyo. Mu ntwaro ya URSS no mu zindi ntwaro z’abakomunisita, gushinga ibiciro vyatuma haba uguhuza nabi nk’ukwo nyene, ivyo bikaba vyatuma ibintu bimwebimwe bibura, ibindi na vyo bikaba bihingurwa birenze urugero. Muri ivyo bihe vyose, ibiciro ntivyerekana ivyo abaguzi bakunda vy'ukuri, haba mu bijanye n'igihe bakunda canke ivyo bakunda, abashoramare bakuru canke abategura imigambi bajejwe gutanga umutungo kugira ngo bashore umutungo mu "nganda zitari zo."


Ubu, impaka z’imibare y’ubutunzi zisubira kuvyuka ahanini mu biganiro vyerekeye inguvu, aho ishoramari ribi rirongowe n’umugambi wa Green ririko rirushiriza kugaragara. Bizuka kandi mu biganiro vyerekeye amasoko y’amahera, aho abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya bagaragaza ko ingorane zo mu 2008, izo abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bakuru bananiwe kuvuga, zari ingorane za kera z’iterambere n’ugusenyuka zirangwa n’ugushiramwo amahera menshi mw’isoko ry’amazu kubera ibihe birebire vy’inyungu nto. Ikindi kandi, aba neo-marxiste n’iyindi migwi y’abasosiyalisiti barakwiragiza iciyumviro c’uko ukuza kw’ubuhinga bwa none kwoshobora gutorera umuti ingorane y’uguharura ubutunzi. Ariko rero, iyo nzira y’ukubona ibintu iva ku gutahura nabi ikibazo; ingorane y’uguharura ubutunzi si ikibazo c’ububasha bwo guharura ahubwo ni ikibazo co gutanga no gukwiragiza amakuru ajanye n’uguhingura n’ugutanga ubutunzi. Aya makuru ashobora gushikirizwa gusa mu karere n’abakozi bafise ubumenyi bwihariye kandi bafise inyungu mu vyo bazovamwo. AI ntishobora gusubirira iyo nzira iva hasi ija hejuru kandi, rero, ntishobora gufasha abategura imigambi yo hagati Address ingorane yo gutanga ubutunzi. Ikibabaje, kubera imyaka ijana y’ukutumvikana, turiteze ukwiyongera kw’ibivugwa ko AI izozana ikiringo gishasha c’uguterimbere mu vy’ubutunzi kirongowe n’abategura ivy’ubuhinga bwa none bafise umuco, bafashijwe na AI, bashobora gukosora ukunanirwa kw’amasoko yidegemvya.


Kugira ngo ubone neza ingene ingorane y’uguharura ubutunzi ikoreshwa mu buryo bubereye mu gihe ca none, urashobora kuraba iyi ngingo ivuga ku ngorane yo gutanga ubutunzi mu Bushinwa bwa none.

### Iciyumviro


Muri iki gice ca nyuma, twasuzumye ukuntu ibara ry’ubutunzi ridashoboka mu gihe c’ubusosiyalisimu, ari ryo ry’agaciro ry’ishure ry’ubutunzi ryo muri Otirishiya. Iciyumviro c’Ubudagi cashikirijwe muri iki cigwa kirashika ku ntumbero y’uwo mwanzuro kandi kiratanga imvo ikomeye y’ingingo ngenderwako zitagira ico zikora. Mu ntumbero yayo, ivyiyumviro vyose vy’Ubudagi bizunguruka akamaro k’ibiciro mu guhuza ubutunzi. Mu gushimika ku kamaro k’ibiciro vy’amahirwe n’uguharura ubutunzi kugira ngo umuntu akoreshe neza ubutunzi, abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya berekana ukuntu ibikorwa vy’umuntu bitoroshe kandi biteye ubwoba mw’isi iguma ihinduka.


Abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi n’abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo hagati akenshi ntibakunda abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya kubera ko bagaragaza ukuntu kazoza katamenyekana, ukuntu ubuhanuzi bw’ubutunzi bushingiye ku rugero butari bwo, be n’ingaruka mbi z’ugufasha mu vy’ubutunzi. Muri make, ubutunzi bwa Otirishiya burashimika ku kutagira akamaro n’ingaruka mbi z’ibikorwa vy’abanyagihugu.


Umugenzo w’Abanyaotirishiya urimwo uburyo bwo kwicisha bugufi ku bikorwa vy’umuntu, ugakuramwo insiguro zikomeye ku vyiyumviro vy’agaciro k’umuntu, ukudakeka, uburenganzira bwo kwihitiramwo, n’ugusobanuka. Irasigura ingene urutonde rw’isoko, naho rutari igihimba c’umugambi w’umuntu, ruhagaze nk’inzego nyamukuru y’iterambere n’uguterimbere kwacu. Nimba hari ikintu kimwe gihambaye co gukura muri iri shure, ni uko ubu kapitalizimu bwacitse ubutunzi buhambaye kubera ubushobozi bwabwo bwo kumenyera amahinduka mw'isi ihinduka kandi idatekanye ituwe n'abantu bidegemvya.


## Uburyo bwa Otirishiya


<chapterId>419129c1-82ba-54e3-b385-95d4d89a447e</chapterId>


Ishure ry’ubutunzi ryo muri Otirishiya ryitandukaniye n’ayandi mashure kubera uburyo bwaryo bwo kwigisha bushingiye ku vyiyumviro (axiomatic-deductive), butandukanye n’uburyo bwo kwigisha ivyiza bukoreshwa kenshi mu vy’imibano. Uburyo bw’abanyabwenge bushingiye ku mategeko ashizweho ashingiye ku makuru y’ivyashikiye abantu, bugakoresha uburyo busa n’ubw’ubuhinga bw’umubiri. Irahingura ivyiyumviro bivuye ku vyo umuntu abona, hanyuma bikemezwa canke bikahakanwa n’ivyageragejwe vy’igihe gito. Uburyo bwa siyansi bushingiye ku kugumya iciyumviro gisobanura neza amakuru no kubandanya kuyitohoza gushika habonetse iciyumviro gitomoye kuruta.


Ariko rero, mu vy’imibano, biragoye gutandukanya ibintu bihinduka nk’uko biri mu vy’ubuhinga bwa fizike, kuko umwanya wose muri kahise ni uwudasanzwe kandi hari ibintu vyinshi biza. Igerageza ry’ubutunzi ntirishobora gusubirwamwo mu laboratwari, kandi birahambaye kumenya ko kwihweza isano hagati y’ibintu bibiri bihinduka bitagaragaza isano ry’inkomoko hagati yavyo. Abanya Otirishiya, cane cane Ludwig von Mises, barateye imbere uburyo bundi bwitwa uburyo bwa a priori canke uburyo bwa axiomatic-deductive bwo kwiga ivy’imibano. Ubwo buryo bushingiye ku vyiyumviro vy’ishimikiro vyitwa axiomes, bisa n’ivyo bikoreshwa mu biharuro. Nk'akarorero, ubuhinga bwa Euclide ni akarorero k'uburyo bw'ubuhinga bw'ubuhinga bw'ubuhinga bw'ubuhinga bw'imibare.


Mu bukungu bwa Otirishiya, ivyiyumviro vy’ishimikiro birimwo uguhitamwo umwanya mwiza, ivyo bikaba bishingiye ku mahitamwo y’umuntu ku giti ciwe y’ibintu canke ibikorwa uno musi aho guhitamwo ejo, kubera ukudakeka ku bijanye n’ivyo azokora muri kazoza. Ivyo vyiyumviro vy’ukuri ntibishirwako ibibazo, kuko bibonwa ko bigaragara kandi bihuye n’ubuzima bwa misi yose. Bakoresheje izo nyigisho z’ishimikiro, abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya barakoresha amategeko y’ubwenge kugira ngo bashobore kuronka amajambo atanga amakuru yerekeye ingene ibintu vy’ubutunzi bikora. Nk’akarorero, basigura yuko ingorane z’ubutunzi ziterwa n’ukudahukana hagati y’amahera azigamiwe n’ayo umuntu ashora, ivyo bikaba bituma abantu bakoresha nabi inyungu mu buryo bw’ubuhinga. Abantu bafise umwanya mwiza bakunda basaba inyungu nziza kugira ngo bashobore kwishura ingorane zo kuguriza. Abanya Otirishiya bavuga ko imigenderanire yo gupima agaciro ishingiye ku muntu, rero inyungu zishobora guhinduka bivanye n’umuntu ku giti ciwe be n’ivyo abayemwo.


Ibiciro birafise uruhara runini mu gutunganya abantu mu buryo bubereye bafise amakuru y’igice. Inyungu ziringaniza Supply n’ugusaba umutungo mw’isoko, gutyo bigatuma ubutunzi butera imbere. Abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya barashimika ku vy’uko gushinga inyungu ataco bimaze bishobora gutuma haba ingorane mu vy’ubutunzi kandi bikaba bituma umuntu adashobora guharura mu butegetsi bw’abasosiyalisiti.


### Abahinga mu vy'ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya n'uburyo butandukanye


Abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya barakunda guhura n’ingorane iyo bariko baraganira n’ayandi mashure y’ivyiyumviro, kuko badakoresha uburyo bumwe bwo gusesangura. Naho Abanyaotirishiya bafata ingingo zishingiye ku vyiyumviro vy’ishimikiro, nk’uko agaciro k’ibintu bishingiye ku muntu, kugira ngo bashobore gutahura ingaruka zijanye n’ubwenge, abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi b’abanya Keynes canke b’abanyabwenge b’amahera bakunda kwizigira amakuru y’ivyashikiye abantu kugira ngo bashireho amategeko rusangi y’ubutunzi.


Akarorero k’itandukaniro ry’uburyo ni ikibanza c’ababuranira inyigisho y’amahera y’ubu (MMT) bashigikiye gucapura amahera kugira ngo umuntu ashike ku ntego za politike, bakoresheje ukuntu ata n’imwe ishobora gutera imbere hagati y’umwaka wa 2008 n’uwa 2019 nk’imvo. Abahinga mu vy’ubutunzi bo muri Otirishiya n’ababuranira MMT ntibavuga ururimi rumwe kandi ntibemeranya ku ngingo ngenderwako zo kumenya ko itegeko ry’ubutunzi rifise akamaro. Ivyo bituma impaka hagati y’ayo mashure atandukanye zigorana kandi kenshi zitagira ico zivamwo.


Ni ngombwa kumenya ko gutoragura ivyatsi, birimwo guhitamwo amakuru kugira ngo haboneke ubucuti hagati y’ibintu bihinduka, ari uburyo butagiramwo ubuhinga kandi butagiramwo uburindutsi mu vy’ubutunzi. Nk’akarorero, kurema amahera ntibituma vy’ukuri haba ugutera imbere kw’ibiciro, kandi uburyo buteye igomwe burakenewe kugira ngo umuntu atahure uburyo butoroshe bwo gukora ubutunzi. Ivyiyumviro vy’ukuri birafise uruhara runini mu gutekereza ku vy’ubutunzi muri Otirishiya. Ni Elements z’ishimikiro umuntu ashobora gukuramwo ibintu bihuye n’ubwenge. Ariko rero, birahambaye kwemera ko gutegekanya neza ivy’imbere mu vy’ubutunzi akenshi bigorana kubera ukuntu ibintu vy’ubutunzi bikomeye cane be n’ukudakeka bisanzwe.


Uburyo ni ikintu gihambaye cane mu vy’ubutunzi no mu vy’imibano muri rusangi. Bigira ico bikoze ku kuntu ibibazo bibazwa, ku kuntu ivyiyumviro bishirwaho no ku kuntu amakuru asobanurwamwo. Gutahura uburyo butandukanye hagati y’amashure y’ivyiyumviro vy’ubutunzi birashobora kudufasha gutahura uburyo butandukanye bwo kubona ibintu no gutegura ivyiyumviro vyacu bwite ku bijanye n’ingingo zavuzwe mu bice vyabanje.
