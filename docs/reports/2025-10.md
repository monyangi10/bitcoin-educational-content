# Bitcoin Educational Content - October 2025 Report

## TL;DR

October 2025 was an exceptionally productive month for Bitcoin educational content with **massive expansion** in tutorial coverage and significant translation progress:

- **316 commits** from **42 contributors** across the globe
- **~1.4M lines added** (net +1.1M) - substantial content expansion
- **50+ new tutorials** added covering wallets, nodes, Lightning, and privacy tools
- **70+ proofreading initiatives** completed across 15+ languages
- **Major hardware wallet tutorial expansion**: SeedSigner, Seedkeeper, Satochip, COLDCARD
- **Lightning Network focus**: BTCPay Server, LNbits, Amboss, Zeus integrations
- **3 batch translation efforts** covering Korean, Rundi, and tutorial sections
