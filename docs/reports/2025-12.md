## Summary

December 2025 was a highly productive month for the Bitcoin Educational Content repository:

- **New Educational Content**: Two major courses were added - LNP202 "Lightning Payments in Depth" provides comprehensive coverage of Lightning Network mechanics, while SOC104 introduces Bitcoin sociology concepts. Draft security courses SID402 and SID406 are also in development.

- **Tutorial Expansion**: 20 new tutorials were added, with a strong focus on communication privacy tools (Matrix, Bitchat, Shelter, White Noise), wallet diversity (Silentium, Heritage, Coin Wallet), and inheritance planning (Bitcoin inheritance plan, Bitcoin Keeper Inheritance v2).

- **Thai Language Launch**: December saw a comprehensive Thai language rollout with batch translations for courses, tutorials, professors, and resources - significantly expanding reach in Southeast Asia.

- **Bulgarian Excellence**: The Bulgarian proofreading team led by @dnabg completed reviews for 9 courses with quizzes, demonstrating exceptional dedication.

- **Global Proofreading**: 52 proofreading commits touched 16 different languages, with French receiving the most attention (11 commits), followed by Bulgarian (7 commits) and Spanish (4 commits).

- **Infrastructure Improvements**: The proofreading dashboard received visual improvements, and new scripting tools were added for UUID management and language team leaderboards.

Thank you to all 41 contributors who made December 2025 another milestone month for Bitcoin education!
